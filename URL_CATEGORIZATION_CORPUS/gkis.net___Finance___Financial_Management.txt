          <h3>
           GAINSKEEPER PRODUCTS
          </h3>
          <br/>
          GainsKeeper offers a suite of automated 'tax smart' trading tools to brokers, fund companies (mutual funds &amp; hedge funds), institutions and individual traders &amp; investors.  GainsKeeper calculates, tracks and reports corporate actions, cost basis, capital gains &amp; losses and wash sale.  Plus, GainsKeeper generates the
          <a href="http://www.irs.gov/pub/irs-pdf/f1040sd.pdf" target="_blank" title="Show IRS Schedule D">
           IRS Schedule D
          </a>
          and
          <a href="http://www.irs.gov/pub/irs-pdf/f8949.pdf" target="_blank" title="Show Form 8949">
           IRS Form 8949
          </a>
          <!--<a href="http://www.irs.gov/pub/irs-pdf/f1040sd1.pdf" title="Show Schedule D-1" target="_blank">Schedule D-1</a>-->
          and reports on broad-based
          <a href="http://www.irs.gov/pub/irs-pdf/f6781.pdf" target="_blank" title="Show Form 6781">
           section 1256 (Form 6781)
          </a>
          options.
Active traders, including those with mark-to-market election, can generate Income/Loss reports supplementing
          <a href="http://www.irs.gov/pub/irs-pdf/f4797.pdf" target="_blank" title="Show IRS Form 4797">
           IRS Form 4797
          </a>
          .
          <br/>
          <br/>
          BasisPro automates
          <a href="us/BasisProInstitutions.aspx">
           cost basis
          </a>
          calculations and tracking, essential to coming cost basis legislation requirements.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <a href="us/FundTaxInstitutions.aspx">
           FundTax®
          </a>
          leverages GainsKeeper's tax lot accounting system for transaction processing and reporting to help avoid wash sales and to automate straddle and REIT basis adjustment tracking.
