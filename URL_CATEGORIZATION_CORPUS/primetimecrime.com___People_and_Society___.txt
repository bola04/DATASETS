      <a class="auto-style188" href="https://mexiconewsdaily.com/news/may-homicide-numbers-worst-on-record/">
       <span class="auto-style667">
        May a record month
       </span>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p class="auto-style971">
     </p>
     <p class="auto-style971">
      MEXICO CITY - Homicide numbers in May were the 
		highest ever recorded, according to the National Public Security System 
		(SNSP).
      <span style="mso-spacerun: yes">
      </span>
      There were 2,530 
		reported cases of intentional homicides during the month, breaking
      <a href="https://mexiconewsdaily.com/news/october-homicides-break-junes-record/">
       the previous record of 2,371 set last Oct
      </a>
      .
      <span style="mso-spacerun: yes">
      </span>
      The total number of victims was 2,890, a figure that is 360 more 
		than the number of cases because some investigations related to 2 or 
		more deaths. In its latest crime rate report, the SNSP also said there 
		were 11,437 intentional homicide investigations in the 5-month period to 
		the end of May which related to the murder of a total of 13,298 people.
      <span style="mso-spacerun: yes">
      </span>
      (Mexico News Daily)
