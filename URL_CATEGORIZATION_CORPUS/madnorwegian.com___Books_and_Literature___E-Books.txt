         <h2 class="entry-title">
          <a href="http://www.madnorwegian.com/1108/news/ahistory-4-details-patreon-offer/" rel="bookmark">
           Ahistory 4 details, Patreon offer
          </a>
         </h2>
         <div class="entry-info">
         </div>
         <div class="entry-content clearfix">
          <p>
           Mad Norwegian Press is proud to announce the forthcoming publication of
           <em>
            Ahistory
           </em>
           — its foundational
           <em>
            Doctor Who
           </em>
           work, as written by
           <strong>
            Lance Parkin
           </strong>
           and
           <strong>
            Lars Pearson
           </strong>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            Ahistory
           </em>
           Fourth Edition, almost undoubtedly the largest timeline to
           <em>
            Doctor Who
           </em>
           ever written, will be published as three volumes…
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Volume 1
            </strong>
            : September 18, 2018
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Volume 2
            </strong>
            : December 4, 2018
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Volume 3
            </strong>
            : March 12, 2019
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
           <em>
            Ahistory
           </em>
           now incorporates nearly 2,500
           <em>
            Doctor Who
           </em>
           -related stories (the TV show, the books, the audios, the comics and much more) onto a single timeline, from the beginning of the Universe and working its way forward to the universe’s heat death. This Fourth Edition of
           <em>
            Ahistory
           </em>
           includes
           <em>
            Doctor Who
           </em>
           titles up through the end of 2017 (the finale of the twelfth Doctor era, with
           <em>
            Twice Upon a Time
           </em>
           ), as well as a host of spin-offs (
           <em>
            Torchwood
           </em>
           ,
           <em>
            The Sarah Jane Adventures
           </em>
           ,
           <em>
            Class
           </em>
           and more).
          </p>
          <p>
           Cumulatively,
           <em>
            Ahistory
           </em>
           is now well over a million words.
          </p>
          <p>
           The three volumes range from 448 to 482 oversized pages each. Each will retail for $29.95. A full list of the titles being included in
           <em>
            Ahistory
           </em>
           will be available before publication of Volume 1.
          </p>
          <p>
           Readers can pre-order the new
           <em>
            Ahistory
           </em>
           direct from the publisher (and receive additional perks) on Patreon, at the newly launched
           <a href="https://www.patreon.com/madnorwegian">
            Patreon.com/madnorwegian
           </a>
           . The Patreon pre-orders are ideal for US customers, with tip jars available for overseas readers who wish to support Mad Norwegian’s lunatic books.
          </p>
          <p>
           Otherwise,
           <em>
            Ahistory
           </em>
           Fourth Edition is up for preorder on the likes of Amazon and Barnes &amp; Noble. In the UK, it’s best attainable from Amazon UK and Forbidden Planet.
          </p>
          <p>
           There will be no digital version of
           <em>
            Ahistory
           </em>
           Fourth Edition for at least two years after publication, possibly more.
          </p>
          <p>
           To request a review copy of
           <em>
            Ahistory
           </em>
           Fourth Edition, or to schedule an interview with the book’s authors, please email: MadNorwegian@gmail.com
          </p>

           <i>

           <i>
            Chicks Dig Time Lords
