       <h2>
        <span class="mw-headline" id="Moorcock.27s_Miscellany">
         Moorcock's Miscellany
        </span>
       </h2>
       <p>
        Welcome Moorcock's Miscellany, the official website of Michael Moorcock &amp; friends.
       </p>
       <p>
        Alas the forums are now offline. One can still enjoy the recent good old days by visiting
        <a class="external text" href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150905103023/http://www.multiverse.org/" rel="nofollow">
         The Miscellany at The Way Back Machine
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        Multiversal connections can also be maintained via:
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         Twitter
        </b>
        :
        <a class="external text" href="https://twitter.com/MoorcocksMisc" rel="nofollow">
         @MoorcocksMisc
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Zlogdan has created a GoodReads forum here:
        <a class="external text" href="https://www.goodreads.com/group/show/176000-multiverse--michael-moorcock" rel="nofollow">
         GoodReads forum
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Facebooking members may encounter one another through the
        <a class="external text" href="https://www.facebook.com/groups/1645810829019452/" rel="nofollow">
         Miscellanist's Redoubt
        </a>
        with regards to Jason Block for setting it up.
       </p>
       <h2>
        <span class="mw-headline" id="Announcements">
         Announcements
        </span>
       </h2>
       <p>
        May 21st, 2017
       </p>
       <p>
        The
        <a href="/../index.php?title=Wikiverse:Categorical_index" title="Wikiverse:Categorical index">
         Categorical index
        </a>
        has been updated with the latest titles with book covers.
       </p>
       <hr/>
       <p>
        April 10th, 2017
       </p>
       <p>
        John from Jayde Design says:
       </p>
       <p>
        "John &amp; Maureen Davey announce the launch of JaydeDesign.com.
       </p>
       <p>
        More than eighteen months in the making, it’s our new bookselling website.
       </p>
       <p>
        Although predominantly selling books -- and those mostly (but not exclusively) Moorcockian -- we also sell a selection of artwork, C.D.s, comics, D.V.D.s, games, graphic novels, magazines, records, videos and more.
       </p>
       <p>
        Go to:
        <a class="external text" href="http://www.JaydeDesign.com" rel="nofollow">
         www.JaydeDesign.com
        </a>
        ".
       </p>
       <p>
        Please be so kind as to forward this e-mail to anyone who you think might be interested; any social-media mentions would also be very much appreciated.
       </p>
       <p>
        Please also note:-
       </p>
       <p>
        ·         All prices include shipping within the U.K.
       </p>
       <p>
        ·         If outside of the U.K., please e-mail us and a quotation can be provided (in sterling &amp;/or U.S.$), including international shipping.
       </p>
       <p>
        ·         All images on the site are illustrative only, and (because of varying conditions) not always a precise representation of the items themselves.
       </p>
       <p>
        ·         Items’ varying conditions appear (where applicable) in drop-down lists.
       </p>
       <p>
        ·         All newly added or amended items appear (in date order) under a “What’s New?” section, which can be found at the left-hand sidebar of all pages.
       </p>
       <p>
        ·         This is a brand-new website; please bear with any errors and/or teething problems that might arise, and do also please let us know if there’s anything you feel might improve it.
       </p>
       <p>
        Any queries -- you know where to find us..."
       </p>
       <hr/>
       <p>
        The
        <a class="external text" href="http://www.multiverse.org/imagehive" rel="nofollow">
         Imagehive
        </a>
        is back up.  We've heard that recently our friends have not had the access to it.  Well, it's back up to its upgraded state, which isn't great, but still useful.  Maybe the wiki images have come back.  We are actively upgrading this week and next.
       </p>
       <p>
        05/15/2016
Berry Sizemore
       </p>
       <hr/>
       <p>
        Hello everyone, this is Berry Sizemore AKA Reinart der Fuchs.  I am sorry for the recent disruption to the ordinary state of Moorcock's Miscellany, which is generally very good.  Due to the continuous march of technology into the future, drastic changes will unfold at the site, one day restoring us to our former glory.  Please be patient as I determine our next steps.
       </p>
       <p>
        Your login may or may not work on this particular part of the site and rest assured that all your old posts haven't gone away.  I have it all safe and sound, and we'll do what we can to make them available again.  By now you've guessed the website is so disrupted that communication with Mr. Moorcock is unavailable at this time.  It may be so for a while.
       </p>
       <p>
        Thank you for understanding.
       </p>
       <p>
        11/04/2015
Berry Sizemore
       </p>
