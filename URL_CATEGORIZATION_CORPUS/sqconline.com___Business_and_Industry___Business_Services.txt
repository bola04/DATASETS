                <h2>
                 Acceptance Sampling: Accept or Reject Batches
                </h2>
                <p>
                </p>
                <div style="text-align:center">
                 <div class="adsense">
                  <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">

                <a class="lexicon-term" href="/glossary#Sample_Size" title="The number of items that should be randomly chosen from a batch.">
                 sample size

                <a class="lexicon-term" href="/glossary#Batch" title="A batch is a collection of items from which a sample will be drawn, for deciding on its conformance to the acceptance inspection. The batch size is the number of items in a lot or a batch.">
                 batch
                </a>
                based on the quality of a sample, using statistical principles.
