       <h1>
        The Best Lottery Data Feeds Available.
       </h1>
       Our lottery data feeds are the highest quality and most comprehensive lottery data feeds available to digital publishers.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Today's online and digital consumers demand high quality content.  As a wholly owned subsidiary of TinBu, LLC, we are pleased to be part of the world's leading lottery data distribution platform.  Clients using our lottery data feeds around the world include AOL, MSN, BING, AP Wire, Fox, USA Today, Tribune, ABC Television, Motricity, DirecTV, AccuWeather, Univision, Rogers, The New York Post, Media General, Media News Group, Town News, and many others.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Our lottery data feeds use our patent-pending proprietary system that delivers accurate and timely lottery results around the clock.  Covering every lottery game in North America, including all U.S. state lottery results and Powerball and Megamillions lottery results, we also cover all lottery games in the EU, AU, U.K., Ireland, Nordic regions, Mexico, and most of Latin America.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Our lottery data feeds are available as turnkey  HTML solutions or in flexible XML feeds or as TEXT files.  We support multiple languages and our lottery data platforms are feature rich. We supply the current winning lottery numbers as well as other important information such as previous winning numbers, current jackpot amounts, payout information, next draw dates, time until next draw, frequency analysis, hot and cold number charts, and more.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Our lottery data feeds are used in search engines, such as AOL and BING, and are integrated into web sites large and small, including USA Today, MSN Local, People PC and more.  Our lottery data feeds are also used to power digital sign networks, and for mobile application.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Pricing on our lottery data feeds is flexible. We work with clients large and small, and our technical support team is available around the clock.  Contact us today to discuss how our lottery data feeds can support and grow your digital platform.
