        <h2>
         Dream interpretation courses, podcasts, articles, and services
        </h2>
        <p>
         You dreams, once interpreted, offer deep insight and wisdom into your self, your life, and why you experience life in the way that you do. Dreams are windows into your mindset, highlighting your limiting beliefs as well as talents and gifts that you have yet to discover. They reveal your unconscious patterns, thoughts, beliefs, and feelings, and since your unconscious mind powerfully influences the way you live your life, this information is gold.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Are you letting your dreams slip away, night by night, week by week, year by year, when you could be using all that insight to create positive change in your life?
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Jane Teresa Anderson by Lannie McTiernan Jan 2018" class="alignleft wp-image-429" height="496" sizes="(max-width: 330px) 100vw, 330px" src="https://dream.net.au/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Jane-Teresa-Anderson-by-Lannie-McTiernan-Jan-2018-682x1024.jpg" srcset="https://dream.net.au/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Jane-Teresa-Anderson-by-Lannie-McTiernan-Jan-2018-682x1024.jpg 682w, https://dream.net.au/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Jane-Teresa-Anderson-by-Lannie-McTiernan-Jan-2018-200x300.jpg 200w, https://dream.net.au/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Jane-Teresa-Anderson-by-Lannie-McTiernan-Jan-2018-768x1154.jpg 768w, https://dream.net.au/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Jane-Teresa-Anderson-by-Lannie-McTiernan-Jan-2018.jpg 800w" width="330"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         You’re in the right place to find intelligent, meaningful insight into your dreams, whether that’s through exploring all the free dream resources you’ll find here –
         <a href="https://www.janeteresa.com/category/podcasts/">
          podcasts
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="https://www.janeteresa.com/category/blog/">
          articles
         </a>
         , and, if you sign up for the
         <a href="https://www.janeteresa.com/newsletter-sign-up/">
          newsletter
         </a>
         , a free ebook, Top Dream Tips – as well as
         <a href="https://dream-academy-online.com">
          online courses on dream interpretation
         </a>
         , dream alchemy, and dream therapy. You can also
         <a href="https://www.janeteresa.com/dream-consultations/dream-consultation-by-phone-or-skype/">
          consult me
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          I’m Jane Teresa Anderson BSc Hons, dream analyst, dream therapist, mentor, author of six books about dreams and dreaming, creator of The Dream Academy online courses, and host of The Dream Show.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         I’m also a frequent guest on national television and an accomplished radio dream interpretation expert, interpreting callers’ dreams.
        </p>
        <p>
         My work is about giving you the tools you need to transform your life in wonderful ways through interpreting and working with your dreams.
        </p>
        <p>
         <i>
          <a href="https://www.janeteresa.com/about/about_jane_teresa/">
           My bio
          </a>
          .
         </i>
        </p>
        <p>
         I’ve been
         <a href="https://www.janeteresa.com/dream-consultations/">
          consulting
         </a>
         as a dream analyst and dream therapist for 26 years, including 20 years online through this website.
        </p>
        <p>
         I help you to see solutions to problems, resolve issues, gain insightful perspective, and improve your life at every level, through intelligent, meaningful insight into your dreams.
