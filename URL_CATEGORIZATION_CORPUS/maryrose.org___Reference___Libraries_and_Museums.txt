    <div class="two-column__subsection">
     <div class="image-section--columns-wrapper" data-component-id="component-5b2ddab967464" data-component-prefix="image-section--columns-wrapper" id="component-5b2ddab967464">
      <div class="image-section--column" data-component-id="component-5b2ddab9669c2" data-component-prefix="image-section--column" id="component-5b2ddab9669c2">
       <div class="image-section--column--title">
        Citation by the European Museum of the Year 2018 judging panel
       </div>
       <div class="image-section--column--text">
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         "In its final state, the Mary Rose Museum is on exceptional achievement - a museum that has finally reached its last stage of its progressive evolution which started 35 years ago (1982). It is close to perfection in fields as different as the presentation of historical research, underwater archeology, conservation techniques, experimental archeology, architecture, scenography and virtual reality. The ship is now revealed totally, freed from all the technical drying equipment, with various light effects and film screenings on or above the ship itself.
        </p>
        <p>
         Ship, Objects and People are the three elements now shaped into in a fascinating history and an impressive display. The emphasis given to the daily activities of the crew on board and the projections over the decks of the original ship's structure strengthen the human dimension of the Mary Rose."
        </p>
