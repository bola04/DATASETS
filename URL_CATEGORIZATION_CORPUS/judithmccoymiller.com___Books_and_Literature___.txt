        Welcome
       </h1>
       <div class="home_content">
        <p class="first-child ">

          <span>
           T
          </span>
         </span>
         hank you for taking the time to visit my website!
        </p>
        <p>
         I am a great lover of history, and you’ll soon discover that my love of history influences my characters, their ethnicity, and the locale of
         <a href="http://judithmccoymiller.com/books/">
          my books
         </a>
         . I am consistently drawn to another time and place, yearning to tell the stories of forgotten voices from long ago and the impact of God’s love and grace upon our country as it was shaped. If you want additional information, just visit the
         <a href="http://judithmccoymiller.com/about-judy/">
          About Me
         </a>
         page.
        </p>
