             <h1 class="nlf-postheader">
              Fitness - Way to Stay Young, Healthy and Beautiful
             </h1>
            </div>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="nlf-content-layout">
           <div class="nlf-content-layout-row">
            <div class="nlf-layout-cell" style="width: 100%">
             <img alt="Fitness Exercise" id="postheader-logo" src="images/fitnessexercise.jpg"/>
             <p>
              <span style="color: #D6217F; font-size: 18px; font-family: Verdana, Segoe, sans-serif; font-weight: 600">
               Why should fitness become part of your life?
              </span>
              Because fitness is an integral part of healthy lifestyle! It is an excellent way to stay fit and healthy and this is probably its main benefit. The other benefit of fitness which is of paramount importance for women in particular is beautiful shape they get when exercising.
             </p>
             <p>
              Young women often torture themselves with strict diets and express methods of losing weight in the hopes of getting well shaped body. But without exercising this goal is impossible to meet. Only fitness will make the body not only slim but also leaner and more sculpted.
             </p>
             <img alt="Consequences of Sedentary Lifestyle" class="rt_img" src="images/consequencesofsedentarylifestyle.jpg" title="Consequences of Sedentary Lifestyle"/>
             <p>
              These days most people have a sedentary lifestyle that has a negative impact on their life, causing various health problems, low vitality, apathetic condition, poor stamina. And this is not just words! Still lots of people don't even thinks about exercising at all.
             </p>
             <p>
              Sports has long proved to be an effective way to cheer up. After exercising a person always feels himself full of energy and even though he has got physically tired his morale is high, he feels great and his vitality is at maximum. Sports is considered the best cure for depression for a reason as it helps forget about problems and troubles for a while. Sport helps look at problems from a different angle and this gives the psyche some rest.
             </p>
             <p>
              Many people associate fitness with success in life and youth. Because it is not just sport, it is a way of life! Fitness includes attending gym, aerobics and healthy diet.
             </p>
             <img alt="Celebrity Training" class="lt_img" src="images/celebritytraining.jpg" title="Celebrity Training"/>
             <p>
              If you want to make fitness part of your life you need to choose the right clothing for exercising. The thing is that some people when
              <a href="http://www.nolimitsfitness.com/how_to_choose_clothing_for_fitness.html">
               choosing clothing for fitness
              </a>
              just decide to wear cheap apparel they don't like very much. This is wrong. When you don't feel comfortable, sport will never bring results you expect. Clothing should bring pleasure and enjoyment from any moment of life, including training. Many world famous brands offer their collections of sports clothing and footwear that is not only comfortable but also beautiful and stylish. Just think where else you could show off those tight fitting breeches and a short top? Don't forget to buy new workout clothing and
              <a href="http://www.nolimitsfitness.com/choosing_a_workout_trainers.html">
               workout trainers
              </a>
              for you from time to time.
             </p>
             <p>
              Don't neglect fitness accessories as well.
              <a href="http://www.nolimitsfitness.com/how_to_choose_fitness_tracker.html">
               Fitness tracker
              </a>
              is a very useful device that will help you track your activity 24 hours a day, monitoring your fitness results, the quality of your sleep and the number of calories burnt. Using a special app you can view the data presented in the form of easy to understand charts on your smartphone.
             </p>
             <img alt="Fitness App" class="rt_img" src="images/fitnessapp.jpg"/>
             <p>
              Smartphone is also a great helper for those who are engaged in fitness! If your busy schedule doesn't allow you to attend the gym and hiring a personal fitness trainer is too expensive for you
              <a href="http://www.nolimitsfitness.com/top_health_and_fitness_apps.html">
               fitness apps
              </a>
              are what you need. These days there is a wide selection of various apps for fitness lovers so choosing the right app from so many options available can be a real challenge.  In addition, a fitness app is an excellent way to
              <a href="http://www.nolimitsfitness.com/save_money_on_fitness.html">
               save money on fitness
              </a>
              .
             </p>
             <p>
              Of course, the beginners had better start exercising under the guidance of a qualified trainer. Your success depends on your
              <a href="http://www.nolimitsfitness.com/best_fitness_programs.html">
               choosing the right fitness program
              </a>
              - it should match your physical training.
              <a href="http://www.nolimitsfitness.com/top_american_gyms.html">
               The best gyms in America
              </a>
              offer their customers a fitness testing supervised by a professional sports doctor. A customer should notify the doctor about his health condition, as well as all diseases and injuries ever had. After that a specialist will develop a special program right for you and will tell you which exercises are bad for you and which will bring the best results.
             </p>
             <p>
              Whether you choose to attend the gym or exercise at home you can not only shape your body and lose excess weight, but even change your life! Fitness will help you forget about many deceases and systemic disorders.
             </p>
