   <h2 style="text-align: center;margin-top:60px;">
    The Guide Pick™ Difference
   </h2>
   <p class="center squeeze">
    We’ve spent a lifetime in the mountains. When it comes to gear we won’t compromise. Leveraging the collective knowledge of the RMI Guide Team, we select and direct you to top mountain gear. Guide Pick™ is outfitting simplified.
   </p>
   <div class="clear">
   </div>
   <div class="step-box third">
    <div class="step-circle">
     Step
     <br/>
     1
    </div>
    <p>
     RMI Guides work and play on the world’s highest mountains where their gear is exposed to the most extreme conditions – collectively over 10,000 days of use annually.
    </p>
    <a href="https://www.rmiguides.com/about/guides/" target="_self">
     <img alt="Peter Whittaker, Whittaker Mountaineering Guide Team" src="https://www.whittakermountaineering.com/images/web-assets/peter_everest.jpg" style="width: 378px; height: 205px;"/>
    </a>
   </div>
   <div class="step-box third">
    <div class="step-circle">
     Step
     <br/>
     2
    </div>
    <p>
     With almost 70 opinionated guides giving input, complete agreement rarely happens, but alignment does. This feedback process allows the best gear in each category to be revealed.
    </p>
    <a href="https://www.whittakermountaineering.com/shop-by-activity" target="_self">
     <img alt="Peter Whittaker, Whittaker Mountaineering Guide Team" src="https://www.whittakermountaineering.com/images/web-assets/wm_climbers.jpg" style="width: 378px; height: 205px;"/>
    </a>
   </div>
   <div class="step-box third">
    <div class="step-circle">
     Step
     <br/>
     3
    </div>
    <p>
     The top products in each category are identified and stamped Guide Pick™. You’ll see this designation on the RMI Equipment Lists and throughout Whittaker Mountaineering.
    </p>
    <a href="https://www.whittakermountaineering.com/brands/la-sportiva/nepal-cube-gtx" target="_self">
