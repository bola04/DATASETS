            <h2>
             <em>
              Voices of Women in AA
             </em>
            </h2>
            <p class="MsoNormal">
             <span style="color: #333333; font-family: verdana, geneva;">
              Voices of Women in AA is a collection of 61 stories from Grapevine. The book begins with articles by or about women who contributed to AA early in its history, followed by stories by some of the program’s earliest female members. Sections are devoted to spirituality, sponsorship, life changes, relationships, family, careers and friendships. The collection concludes with a chapter devoted to women’s meetings. The stories demonstrate the various ways women alcoholics—often with great courage—find sobriety in AA and embrace the program to live rich and rewarding lives
             </span>
             <span style="color: #333333; font-family: verdana, geneva; font-size: x-small;">
              .
             </span>
            </p>
            <p class="MsoNormal">
             <span style="border-width: 1pt; border-style: none; border-color: windowtext; padding: 0in;">
              <span style="background-color: #fbf8f6; color: #333333; font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-size: 18px;">
              </span>
              <strong>
               <a href="https://store.aagrapevine.org/" style="font-family: 'Lucida Grande';">
                Order your copy today.
               </a>
              </strong>
             </span>
            </p>

            </p>

             <em>
              Carry the Message Project
             </em>
            </h2>
            <p class="p1">
             The Carry The Message Project focuses on getting Grapevine and La Viña subscriptions to alcoholics in need. It's not necessary to know the recipient. Sponsor a subscription for someone behind bars, in a treatment facility, nursing home, or that is homebound.
