    <h3>
     The LED won't light and/or the beeper won't make a sound.  Are they burned out?
    </h3>
    Likely not.  Reverse the connection wires and the LED should light and the beeper should "beep." LEDs are marvelous light
sources, but only work one way in a circuit.
    <h3>
     Can I used my own dough / Play-doh?
    </h3>
    Yes! All brands and colors work just fine!
    <h3>
     What about dough sticking to the metal contacts?
    </h3>
    You should wipe them off, as it helps the electrical conductivity.  We just wait until the dough dries a bit on the contacts,
then it crumbles right off.
