        <p class="style4">
         <span class="style12">
          Greg Alexander
         </span>
         - Tattoo Artist Extraordinaire
        </p>
        <p class="style4">
         December 13, 1950 - June 17, 2016
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="subHeader style6">
          Greg and Maggie Maye's Tattoo
         </span>
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Greg and Susan Alexander moved to Tucson in 1998 with their infant daughter, Maggie. They opened Maggie Maye's Tucson Tattoo Studio and fell in love with Tucson and the people that live here. In a couple of years along came William. Greg was devoted to his wife and children. Next came his passion for his art which was expressed in many ways - tattooing, painting, sculpting and creating the complex with is now Placita de la Luna.
        </p>
        <p>
         We miss his gentle soul, his humor, his wisdom, his warmth and his fiercly protective love.
        </p>
        <p>
         Susan continues to work at Placita de la Luna as a tattoo artist (Studiobtattoo), a coffee roaster (Rain Crow Coffee) and a gallery owner (Rain Crow Gallery). All of these ventures came out of the creative mind spring that Greg possessed.
        </p>
