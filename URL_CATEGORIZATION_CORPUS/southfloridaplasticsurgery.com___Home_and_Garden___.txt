     <h1>
      Welcome!
     </h1>
     <p>
      My practice  is based on honesty, integrity, and a dedication to excellence in plastic  surgery. I promise to provide you with knowledgeable, compassionate care in a  very warm, friendly, professional office. Every patient has unique needs and  desires. We want to get to know you, and together we will come up with a plan  to reach your goals!
     </p>
     <p>
      I offer the  latest cosmetic surgery procedures and specialize in saline breast  augmentation, silicone breast augmentation, breast augmentation revisions and  breast lifts. In the interest of your safety, I only perform surgery in a full  service hospital with board certified M.D. anesthesiologists.
     </p>
     <p>
      Please give  us a call to visit my office for a complimentary consultation. If you are from  out of town, I invite you to email me directly with photos for an email  consultation. I promise a prompt, detailed reply within 24 hours. After surgery I will also give you my cell phone number to make reaching  me even easier!
     </p>
     <p>
      Please enjoy  my website, and do come again — and next time bring a friend!
     </p>
