          <h1 class="headline" itemprop="headline">
           Euphemia Haye | The Best Sarasota Restaurant &amp; Most Romantic Destination for Anna Maria Dining
          </h1>
         </div>
         <div class="post_content" itemprop="articleBody">
          <p>
           Longboat Key is known as a beach-lover’s paradise on Florida’s west coast, but it is also home to one of the finest and most romantic restaurants in the country — Euphemia Haye.  Also on site is the Haye Loft Lounge and Dessert Bar where you’ll find the best live music Sarasota and Anna Maria locals have already discovered.
          </p>
          <p>
           Please note:  Downstairs at Euphemia Haye fine dining restaurant, reservations are highly recommended (941-383-3633).  The Haye Loft, located upstairs, is designated for walk-ins only and the menu contains “lighter bites” including pizzas and a daily “sandwich” special.
          </p>
          <p>
           Chef owned and operated since 1980, Euphemia Haye Restaurant, is locally thought of as the best restaurant in Sarasota. Located inside a historic, wooden cottage, Euphemia Haye is hidden amongst a scenic “jungle” of tropical greenery where palm trees are studded in twinkly lights and birds of paradise are blooming. Chef Raymond Arpke, co-owner and cookbook author, along with his wife D’Arcy, are the restaurant’s inspirations.  Come, dine with us and “let go.” Enjoy a glass of wine and feast on our award-winning global and American food while you look out onto our beautiful grounds.
          </p>
          <p>
           If you are here on vacation, this is the Sarasota restaurant you won’t want to miss and the Anna Maria dining experience you’ll never forget. Located in the convenient location of Longboat Key,  Euphemia Haye is a pleasant and scenic short drive from Bradenton, Anna Maria Island, Sarasota and St. Armands Circle. If you are visiting Anna Maria Island, this is the most exquisite and intimate dining you will find. We look forward to providing you with exemplary service in our charming and friendly atmosphere.
          </p>
