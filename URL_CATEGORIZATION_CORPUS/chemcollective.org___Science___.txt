     <h1 id="welcome">
      Resources to Teach and Learn Chemistry
     </h1>
     <div id="main_content" style="float:left;">
      <p id="intro">
       The ChemCollective is a collection of
       <a href="http://chemcollective.org/vlabs">
        virtual labs
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="http://chemcollective.org/scenario_based">
        scenario-based learning activities
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="http://chemcollective.org/tutorials">
        tutorials
       </a>
       , and
       <a href="http://chemcollective.org/tests">
        concept tests
       </a>
       . Teachers can use our content for pre-labs, for alternatives to textbook homework, and for in-class activities for individuals or teams. Students can review and learn chemistry concepts using our virtual labs, simulations, and tutorials. The ChemCollective is organized by a group of faculty and staff at Carnegie Mellon
	   who are interested in using, assessing, and creating engaging online activities for chemistry education.
      </p>
      <p id="more info">
      </p>

       <div id="feature_box">

          </a>

         <img src="assets/common/images/general/chemvlabpict.png"/>
        </a>
        <p>
         With support from the US Department of Education, the ChemCollective has teamed with WestEd to create a set of high school classroom activities in stoichiometry, thermochemistry, equilibrium and acid/base chemistry. The activities support your instruction by allowing students to practice and apply their knowledge to real world contexts, while receiving online tutoring and feedback.
        </p>
