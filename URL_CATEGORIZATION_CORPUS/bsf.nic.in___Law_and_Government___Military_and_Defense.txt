           <p>
            The
            <strong>
             Border Security Force (BSF)
            </strong>
            is a Border Guarding Force of India.
                                            Established on December 1, 1965, it is a paramilitary force charged with guarding
                                            India's land border during peace time and preventing transnational crime. It
                                            is a Union Government Agency under the administrative control of Ministry of Home
                                            Affairs.It is one of many law enforcement agency of India.It currently stands as
                                            the world's largest border guarding force.
