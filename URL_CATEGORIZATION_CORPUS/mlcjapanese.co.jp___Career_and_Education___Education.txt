         </h1>
         <p class="paragraph">
          The
          <strong class="character">
           Japanese language school
          </strong>
          , Meguro Language Center (in Tokyo) was established in 1990 and since then
has been attended by students from all over the world. We can offer English-speaking
students part-time group lessons (conversation, business, JLPT), one-to-one
lessons, intensive course, Online lesson (by Skype), and on-site lessons.
         </p>

         <h2 class="paragraph">
          <span class="character" style="color : #000000;">
           Features of the MLC Meguro Language Center in Tokyo
          </span>
         </h2>
         <ul class="sp-part-top" id="sp-list-1">
          <li>
           <strong class="character">
            Japanese Language School in Tokyo
           </strong>
           ,
           <span class="character" style="color : #f91772;">
            for English speakers
           </span>
           .
          </li>
          <li>
           <span class="character" style="color : #f91772;">
            No registration fee
           </span>
          </li>
          <li>
           <span class="character" style="color : #f91772;">
            Free trial lesson
           </span>
          </li>
          <li>
           <span class="character" style="color : #f91772;">
            Private, Group, On-site and Online(Skype) lessons are available.
           </span>
          </li>
          <li>
           You can download lots of
           <span class="character" style="color : #f91772;">
            free study materials
           </span>
           (Worksheets, audio files, Free E-mail lesson)
          </li>
          <li>
           <span class="character" style="color : #f91772;">
            3 min. from Meguro station.
           </span>
           (Easy to commute from Tokyo, Shibuya, Shinjuku, Roppongi, Hiroo, Azabu,
  Shinagawa, Gotanda, Ebisu.)
          </li>
         </ul>
         <ul class="sp-part-top" id="sp-list-2">
          <li>
           <span class="character" style="color : #0000ff;">
            No worries even if you are beginner!
           </span>
           <br/>
           Since we use English at first to make lessons more effective and productive,
  you can take a class in a less stressful atmosphere. As the lessons progress,
  we use much more Japanese.
          </li>
          <li>
           <span class="character" style="color : #0000ff;">
            Original materials
           </span>
           <br/>
           We use lots of MLC-produced materials to practice more effectively besides
  the textbooks. Lots of original worksheets, homework and quizzes are also
  used.
          </li>
          <li>
           <span class="character" style="color : #0000ff;">
            We can offer you the most suitable course.
           </span>
           <br/>
           We have an interview with each student at first. So we ask you about your
  learning experience, objectives, course of interest, your availability
  and so on. Then we check your grammar level, speaking level and Kanji level.
  After the interview, we suggest the most suitable course and lesson plan
  for you.
          </li>
          <li>
           <span class="character" style="color : #0000ff;">
            Daily conversation, business conversation, preparation for JLPT
           </span>
           <br/>
           We can offer various kinds of courses to suit a student's needs. For example,
  those who want to study Japanese for the first time, those who want to
  improve total communication in Japanese, those who require business Japanese,
  or those who want to pass JLPT. You can choose the course based on your
  own needs.
          </li>
          <li>
           <span class="character" style="color : #0000ff;">
            Convenient location
           </span>
           <br/>
           Japanese Language School, MLC Meguro Language Center is located three minutes'
  walk from Meguro station in Tokyo. It is more conveniently located when
  compared to other schools in Tokyo. You can drop by and take a lesson on
  your way home or while you are sightseeing.
          </li>
          <li>
           <span class="character" style="color : #0000ff;">
            Free Wi-Fi
           </span>
          </li>
         </ul>
         <h2 class="paragraph" id="spb-bookmark-1" style="color : #e8235b;">
          <span class="character" style="color : #ff3d7f;">
           Free Study Materials
          </span>
         </h2>
         <div class="sp-part-top sp-block-container" id="sp-block-container-4">
          <p class="paragraph">
           For Japanese learners all over the world! You can download study materials
for free. Plenty of free study materials such as worksheets(PDF files),
quizzes, lists, audio files are available for free.We also share worksheets
to help you pass the JLPT (Japanese Language Proficiency Test). Level check,
worksheets, grammar quizzes and Kanji quizzes are available. Let's learn
Japanese!
          </p>
