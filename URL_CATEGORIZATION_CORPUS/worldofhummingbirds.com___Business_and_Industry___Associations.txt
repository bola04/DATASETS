    <p>
     The World of Hummingbirds .com is designed to the ultimate resource center for everything related to hummingbirds.  Any information about hummingbirds you would ever want can be easily accessible with just a few clicks.  This information is completely free.  You can use it to create a hummingbird farm right outside your own home.  You, your family, your friends, and your neighbors can enjoy hummingbirds with their daily antics.  Everything from information from how to
     <a href="feeding.php">
      feed hummingbirds
     </a>
     and how to make
     <a href="nectar.php">
      hummingbird food
     </a>
     , to useful information and do-it-yourself projects like making your own
     <a href="feedersmake.php">
      homemade hummingbird feeder
     </a>
     or
     <a href="uninvitedwasps.php">
      wasp trap
     </a>
     .  Anything associated with hummingbirds can be found here.
    </p>
    <p>
     Hummingbirds are wonderful little creatures and each one has its own personality, not to mention
     <a href="behavior.php">
      attitude
     </a>
     .  They are the
     <a href="facts.php">
      smallest bird in the world
     </a>
     , and are the only bird to be able to fly forwards as well as backwards.  The maneuvers they make mid-air are incredible and they are known the world over for their flying acrobatics.  Hummingbirds are the original helicopter and are considered by many to be little clowns as they dash and dart amongst each other.  They have the ability to give us hour upon hour of entertainment.  The price of admission, a little
     <a href="nectar.php">
      sugar water
     </a>
     and the
     <a href="habitat.php">
      right location
     </a>
     .
    </p>
    <p>
     We here at the World of Hummingbirds .com work hard to bring you accurate and up-to-date information on the likes and dislikes of hummingbirds as well as useful information about hummingbirds that may come in handy to attract and enjoy them. As you look though this website don't forget to check out our
     <a href="typescatalog.php">
      Types of Hummingbirds Catalog
     </a>
     ,
     <a href="gardencatalog.php">
      Garden Catalog
     </a>
     ,
     <a href="habitatmap.php">
      Habitat Maps
     </a>
     , and
     <a href="migrationmap.php">
      Migration Maps
     </a>
     . We also have a terrific
     <a href="shop.php">
      Hummingbird Shop
     </a>
     . Here you can find a wonderful
     <a href="shopfeeders.php">
      hummingbird feeder
     </a>
     or even that perfect
     <a href="shop.php">
      hummingbird gift
     </a>
     for your favorite hummingbird enthusiast. So, whether you live in a city, the county, apartment, or house, the World of Hummingbirds is here to bring you all the information you need to enjoy hummingbirds and all of their antics.
    </p>

     The World of Hummingbirds hopes you enjoy this site and that you will have fun bringing these wonderful little creatures to your home.  With the data found on this site you to will have the knowledge and information needed to create your own Hummingbird Farm for you and your family to enjoy for years to come.
