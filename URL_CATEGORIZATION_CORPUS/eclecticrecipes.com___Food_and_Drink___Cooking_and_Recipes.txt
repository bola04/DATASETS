     <p>
      The kitchen is the center of our home. It’s where everything lands the moment it comes through the door. It’s where homework gets done, projects get started and important conversations begin.
     </p>
     <p>
      Because so much happens in our kitchen, it has to stay clean. There’s so many things that end up on my counters and kitchen table. Dirty things from the garage, mail that’s been in I don’t know whose hands and gross kid stuff constantly lands on kitchen surfaces that are supposed to stay clean.
