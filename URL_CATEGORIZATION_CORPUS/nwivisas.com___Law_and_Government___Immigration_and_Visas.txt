      </a>
     </h2>
     <p class="date">
      22 June 2018
     </p>
     <p>
      Canada employs an immigration system which successfully selects applicants for migration based on merit.  The system is called the Express Entry utilizes a system whereby anyone interested in migrating to Canada creates an online profile with their immigration department.  The profile contains personal and academic details and a complete CV of their professional career and experience.  This system ensures that candidates who are most likely to succeed economically – not simply those first in line – are able to immigrate to Canada.
