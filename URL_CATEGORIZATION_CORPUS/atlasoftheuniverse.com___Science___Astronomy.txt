  <h1 class="header">
   <img alt="An Atlas of The Universe" height="100" src="atlas.gif" width="500"/>
  </h1>
  <p>
   This web page is designed to give everyone an idea of what our universe actually
looks like.  There are nine main maps on this web page, each one approximately
ten times the scale of the previous one.  The first map shows the nearest stars
and then the other maps slowly expand out until we have reached the scale of the
entire visible universe.
