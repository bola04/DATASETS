       <li class="se" style="color:black;">
        <h2>
         <a href="http://www.mcponline.org/content/17/1/1.full">
          Your technological advances belong here
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         The
         <em>
          Molecular &amp; Cellular Proteomics
         </em>
         editorial leadership team gathered over the summer of 2017 to discuss the journal's plans for the new year. At that strategy
                           session, we determined it is necessary to re-establish that MCP is where your technological advances belong, and we have made
                           other policy changes about which you should be aware.
         <a href="http://www.mcponline.org/content/17/1/1.full">
          Click here to read more
         </a>
        </p>
        <hr/>
        <h2>
         <a href="http://www.mcponline.org/content/16/3/327">
          Targeted Mass Spec Guidelines - June 2017
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         Beginning June 26, 2017, MCP implemented
         <a href="http://www.mcponline.org/site/misc/MCP_Targeted_Mass_Spec_Guidelines_1.27.17.pdf" title="Guidelines to Authors for Publication of Manuscripts Describing Development and Application of Targeted Mass Spectrometry Measurements of Peptides and Proteins and Submission Checklist">
          new guidelines and requirements
         </a>
         for papers describing the development and application of targeted mass spectrometry measurements of peptides, modified peptides
                           and proteins. Please read
         <a href="http://www.mcponline.org/content/16/3/327" title="New Guidelines for Publication of Manuscripts Describing Development and Application of Targeted Mass Spectrometry Measurements of Peptides and Proteins">
          this editorial
         </a>
         for more information about the guidelines.
        </p>
        <p>
         MCP has changed its copyright policy and pricing structure for new manuscripts. The highlights:
         <a href="/site/misc/policy.xhtml#copyright">
          Authors keep their copyright
         </a>
         and
         <a href="/site/misc/ifora.xhtml#PubCharges">
          don’t pay color figure fees
         </a>
         .
        </p>
       </li>
