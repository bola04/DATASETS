      First Steps on the Snow
     </h1>
     <p class="intro">
      Whether you’re a beginner, main slope ready, expert or anything in between, we have a
      <a href="/lessons">
       Ski Lesson
      </a>
      suitable for you.  As Britain’s newest
      <a href="/info">
       Indoor Ski Centre
      </a>
      we deliver the alpine conditions every skier and snowboarder dream of.
     </p>
     <p>
      Here you’ll find a huge 160m main  slope as well as the largest indoor ski lesson slope in the UK. Both ski and snowboarding slopes are over 30m wide and maintained with the perfect snow surface- perfect for all your indoor skiing and snowboarding needs!
     </p>
     <p>
      After your session on the slope be sure to visit our Schuss Bar on the slope, enjoy FREE tea &amp; coffee during your session.
     </p>
     <p>
      Off the slope we have an alpine themed restaurant - The Lodge Bar &amp; Kitchen which offers panoramic views over the slope and an all new menu combined with traditional favourites and delicious alpine dishes too. It’s also a great place to come and enjoy live Sky Sports on one of our many big-screen TVs, and you don’t have to go on the slope to enjoy our Cafebar.
     </p>
