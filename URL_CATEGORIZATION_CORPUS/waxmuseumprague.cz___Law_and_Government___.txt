      </h2>

Připojte se k Tayloru Lautnerovi, přisedněte si k Georgi Clooneymu, udělejte si selfie s Tomem Cruisem anebo si zazpívejte s Lady Gaga.       </p>
      <p>

Shake hands with Taylor Lautner, sit down with George Clooney, take a selfie with Tom Cruise, or sing along with Lady Gaga. To top all of this - entry to this exhibition is FREE with your ticket to the Prague Wax Museum. Are you ready for your starring role?
