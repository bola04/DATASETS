       <h3 style="text-align: center;">
        Save Time with the
        <a href="https://www.rescuegroups.org/services/pet-adoption-portal/" title="Pet Adoption Portal">
         <strong>
          Pet Adoption Portal
         </strong>
        </a>
        ,
        <br/>
        <a href="https://www.rescuegroups.org/services/data-management-service/" title="Data Management Service">
         <strong>
          Animal Data Management Service
         </strong>
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="https://www.rescuegroups.org/services/website-service/" title="Website Service">
         <strong>
          Website Service
         </strong>
        </a>
        , and more services designed for Animal Welfare Organizations
       </h3>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="clearfade">
       </div>
       <br/>
       <div class="ezcol ezcol-one-quarter">
        <img alt="Free uploads to the major adoption listing websites with the Pet Adoption Portal, Data Management Service and Website Service" class="size-full wp-image-1416 alignnone" height="64" src="https://www.rescuegroups.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/arrow-up.png" title="Free uploads to the major adoption listing websites with the Pet Adoption Portal, Data Management Service and Website Service" width="64"/>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Exports to Hundreds of Adoption Websites and Services - Free!
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         When you post your adoptable pets with us, we can send them out to
         <strong>
          <a href="https://www.rescuegroups.org/adoption-listing-websites/" title="Adoption Listing Websites">
           over 200 adoption listing websites
          </a>
          .
         </strong>
         You have full control of where your pets appear, and
         <strong>
          it's all done for free.
         </strong>
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="ezcol ezcol-one-quarter">
        <img alt="RescueGroups.org is a non-profit organization" class="size-full wp-image-1425 alignnone" height="64" src="https://www.rescuegroups.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/circle-love1.png" title="RescueGroups.org is a non-profit organization" width="64"/>
        <p>
         <strong>
          We are a Non-Profit
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         That means that our one-and-only mission is to help the community and no one is profiting personally from what we are doing. You won't find any ulterior motives in what we do.
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="ezcol ezcol-one-quarter">
        <img alt="RescueGroups.org has over 3,500 organizations using our services." class="size-full wp-image-1417 alignnone" height="64" src="https://www.rescuegroups.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/chat-quote.png" title="RescueGroups.org has over 3,500 organizations using our services." width="64"/>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Over 5,600 Partner Organizations
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         We receive many
         <a href="/testimonials" title="Testimonials">
          <strong>
           testimonials from organizations
          </strong>
         </a>
         who want to let others know of their great experiences using RescueGroups.org services.
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="ezcol ezcol-one-quarter ezcol-last">
        <img alt="RescueGroups.org takes security very seriously. We use SSL to protect your account." class="size-full wp-image-1419 alignnone" height="64" src="https://www.rescuegroups.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/lock-closed.png" title="RescueGroups.org takes security very seriously. We use SSL to protect your account." width="64"/>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Secure, Web-based Services
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         We take security very seriously. Our data management website uses SSL to protect your account information, and we require every user to have their own user name and password.
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="ezcol-divider">
       </div>
       <br/>
       <div class="clearfade">
       </div>
       <br/>
       Most volunteers don't have time to update more than one or two websites. Our
       <strong>
        Pet Adoption Portal,
       </strong>
       <strong>
        Animal Data Management Service
       </strong>
       , and
       <strong>
        Website Service
       </strong>
       can automatically send your animal data and pictures to a
       <strong>
        <a href="https://www.rescuegroups.org/adoption-listing-websites">
         long list of websites
        </a>
        .
       </strong>
       You update only one pet list, and the other websites get updated!
       <p>
       </p>
       <div class="clearfade">
       </div>
