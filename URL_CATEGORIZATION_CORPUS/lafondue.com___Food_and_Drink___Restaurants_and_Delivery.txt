        <h3>
         <span style="color: #ffffff;">
          Large Party Reservations
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p>
         La Fondue gladly accommodates large groups for dinner. We provide all parties of 9 or more the full fondue experience with our 4 course set menu:
        </p>
        <p>
         First course –
         <strong>
          Salad
         </strong>
         <br/>
         Second course –
         <strong>
          Any two (2) cheese fondues of your choice
         </strong>
         <br/>
         Third course –
         <strong>
          Variety of chicken, beef, seafood, and vegetables
         </strong>
         <br/>
         Fourth course –
         <strong>
          Any two (2) chocolate fondues of your choice
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Prices begin at $69/guest, and we are happy to accommodate any dietary requirements where possible. For parties who require or prefer a private room, the minimum spend for such rooms begin at $1500
         <em>
          ($2500 weekends and holidays)
         </em>
         , plus applicable fees, taxes, and gratuities. The $500 deposit required to book your reservation will be applied toward your final bill for the evening.
