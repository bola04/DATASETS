            <h2>
             Out in front, setting the pace
            </h2>
            <p>
             <a href="node/591">
              A one-on-one interview
             </a>
             with Brooks Jackson, MD, MBA, Vice President for Medical Affairs
            </p>
            <h2>
             <a href="https://medcom.uiowa.edu/theloop/news/bradley-haws-named-associate-vice-president-and-cfo-for-ui-health-care">
              UI Health Care names new CFO
             </a>
            </h2>
            <p>
             Bradley Haws, MBA, chief executive officer of the University of Virginia Physicians Group, has been named associate vice president and chief financial officer for University of Iowa Health Care. He will begin Aug. 20.
            </p>
            <h2>
             Changing Medicine. Changing Lives.
             <sup>
              ®
             </sup>
            </h2>
            <p>
             University of Iowa Health Care (UI Health Care) represents the integrated health-care enterprise of the University of Iowa, which includes:
            </p>
            <ul>
             <li>
              <a href="https://uihc.org/">
               UI Hospitals and Clinics
              </a>
             </li>
             <li>
              <a href="http://www.uichildrens.org/">
               UI Stead Family Children's Hospital
              </a>
             </li>
             <li>
              <a href="http://medicine.uiowa.edu/">
               UI Roy J. and Lucille A. Carver College of Medicine
              </a>
             </li>
             <li>
              <a href="http://www.medicine.uiowa.edu/uiphysicians/">
               UI Physicians
              </a>
             </li>
            </ul>
            <p>
             UI Health Care is committed to excellence in:
            </p>
            <h2>
             <a href="/education">
              Education
             </a>
            </h2>
            <p>
             Through its mission, UI Health Care trains the next generation of leaders in patient care, medical research, and education.
            </p>
            <ul>
             <li>
              <a href="http://gme.medicine.uiowa.edu/">
               Graduate Medical Education
              </a>
             </li>
            </ul>
            <h2>
             <a href="http://www.medicine.uiowa.edu/research">
              Research
             </a>
            </h2>
            <p>
             Our research has led to some of the most important discoveries in treatments for cancer, heart attack, stroke, eye diseases, hearing loss, genetic diseases, birth defects, and many other conditions.
            </p>
            <h2>
             <a href="https://uihc.org/primary-and-specialty-care">
              Patient Care and Service
             </a>
            </h2>
            <p>
             Comprehensive multidisciplinary care is a hallmark of an academic medical center. The full spectrum of specialty and subspecialty care draws patients from Iowa, the region, and around the world.
            </p>
            <ul>
             <li>
              <a href="https://uihc.org/nursing-iowa">
               Department of Nursing Services and Patient Care
              </a>
             </li>
            </ul>
