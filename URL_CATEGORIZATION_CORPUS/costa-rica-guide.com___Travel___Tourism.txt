           Food &amp; Drink
          </a>
         </h3>
         <p>
          Costa Rican culinary traditions include
          <a href="https://costa-rica-guide.com/travel/food/cuisine/#bocas">
           Bocas
          </a>
          which are small snacks served with drinks and casual “
          <a href="https://costa-rica-guide.com/travel/food/typical-costa-rican-menu/">
           tipico menu
          </a>
          ” restaurants called
          <a href="https://costa-rica-guide.com/travel/food/fast-food/">
           Sodas
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          A simple recipe of beans and rice called
          <a href="https://costa-rica-guide.com/travel/food/gallo-pinto-recipe-costa-rica/">
           Gallo Pinto
          </a>
          is Costa Rica’s national dish but seafood, tropical fruit, chocolate and of course
          <a href="https://costa-rica-guide.com/activities/coffee-tours/">
           coffee
          </a>
          are specialties too.
         </p>
