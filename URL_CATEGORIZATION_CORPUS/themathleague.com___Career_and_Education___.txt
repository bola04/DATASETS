                <p>
                 We will be holding our annual summer program for students in grades 6, 7, 8 and 9 at The College of New Jersey in Ewing Township NJ. The program consists of six days of math contests (individual questions, team questions, relay questions, and speed questions) as well as a series of lectures on various mathematical topics. The program will be held from July 24th through July 29th. Last summer, approximately 250 students (Elementary School students and Middle School students combined) from China, South Korea, Canada, and the United States attended our program. Eligibility requirements are as follows: for grades 6, 7, and 8, a score of 31 or higher on our contests for those grade levels or a score of 24 or higher on our algebra 1 contest; for grade 9, a cumulative score of 30 or higher on our six high school contests or a score of 24 or higher on our algebra 1 contest. Please note: We will also consider applications from students who have not participated in our contests but have demonstrated mathematical talent via other contests or activities.
