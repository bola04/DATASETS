       <p align="justify">
        <font color="#FFFFFF" face="Verdana" size="2">
         Robert Stanek is the author of more than 
100 books and is best known for the Ruin Mist novels 
featured on this site. For adults, Robert Stanek recommends the Ruin Mist 
Chronicles, which includes
         <i>
          Keeper Martin's Tale
         </i>
         ,
         <i>
          Kingdom Alliance
         </i>
         ,
         <i>
          Fields of 
Honor,
         </i>
         and
         <i>
          Mark of the Dragon
         </i>
         .
         <i>
          Elf Queen's Quest
         </i>
         is 
book one in the Ruin Mist Dark Path, an alternate telling of the original 
Ruin Mist Chronicles books meant for devoted fans who have 
already finished the RMC and want to experience the original story from a 
different viewpoint.
        </font>
       </p>
       <p align="justify">
        <font color="#FFFFFF" face="Verdana" size="2">
         For young adults, Robert Stanek recommends
         <i>
          The 
Kingdoms and the Elves of the Reaches
         </i>
         books 1 to 4 and the
         <i>
          In the Service of Dragons
         </i>
         books 1 to 4. These books include illustrations, character studies, and extended 
		background on the world of Ruin Mist.
        </font>
       </p>
       <p align="justify">
        <font color="#FFFFFF" face="Verdana" size="2">
         <i>
          Dragons of 
the Hundred Worlds
         </i>
         and
         <i>
          Battle for Ruin Mist
         </i>
         are the two most recent Ruin Mist series.
         <i>
          Breath of 
Fire
         </i>
         and
         <i>
          Living Fire
         </i>
         , fantasy adventure novels for all ages, are the first
         <i>
          Dragons of the Hundred Worlds
         </i>
         books.
         <i>
          A Daughter of Kings
         </i>
         , a graphic novel for all ages, is the 
first
         <i>
          Battle for Ruin Mist
         </i>
         book.
        </font>
       </p>
