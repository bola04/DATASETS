            <b>
            </b>
            <p>
             Take a deep breath and relax; we're here to help you. Cat diabetes is a treatable,                        manageable condition.  You can maintain your sanity and your cat's health, all on a reasonable budget. Medical doctors and veterinarians and many diabetic cat caretakers contribute to this site with knowledge you can trust.
            </p>
            <p>
             First-time users, make sure to check out the links to the right for introductory articles on feline diabetes. We also have an extensive library of veterinary articles for those who need references or want to learn more.  It is important to us that you, as the caretaker, know the latest information on feline diabetes.
            </p>
            <p>
             Our message board (the FDMB) is an invaluable resource                          for cat owners, just like you. There is always                        someone online, even in the middle of the night, that                        can help talk you through a difficult time.  Information is shared, support groups abound, treatment is optimized.
            </p>
            <br/>
            <p>
             Although primarily volunteers, including the site founder, run this website, costs are quite high for hosting, supplies, bandwidth, and technical help.  We have shopping affiliates that provide small commissions, we sell some logo merchandise, and accept donations. Proceeds from these areas help support this site  along with small contributions to feline rescue &amp; feline education sites.
            </p>
