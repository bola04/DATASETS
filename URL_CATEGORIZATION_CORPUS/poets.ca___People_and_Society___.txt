             NEW ADDRESS AS OF MAY 1, 2018
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            We’ve moved! As of May 1, 2018, please find us at 2 Carlton Street Suite 1519, Toronto ON, M5B 1J3.
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: right;">
            <a href="http://poets.ca/2018/06/17/2018-league-award-winners/">
             <strong>
              2018 LEAGUE AWARD WINNERS ANNOUNCED
             </strong>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            We are beyond elated to announce the winners for our 2018
            <a href="http://poets.ca/lampert/">
             Gerald Lampert
            </a>
            and
            <a href="http://poets.ca/lowther/">
             Pat Lowther Memorial
            </a>
            Awards,
            <a href="http://poets.ca/souster/">
             Raymond Souster Award
            </a>
            , and
            <a href="http://poets.ca/goldenberet/">
             Sheri-D Wilson Golden Beret Award,
            </a>
            along with our
            <a href="http://poets.ca/lifemembers/">
             Life
            </a>
            and
            <a href="http://poets.ca/honorarymembers/">
             Honorary Member
            </a>
            designations. Revealed at
            <a href="http://www.canadianwriterssummit.com/">
             Canadian Writer’s Summit
            </a>
            on June 16, 2018, here are the 2018 League Award winners:
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="https://gooselane.com/products/otolith">
             <em>
              Otolith
             </em>
             by Emily Nilsen (Goose Land Editions)
            </a>
            , Gerald Lampert Memorial Award winner;
            <br/>
            <a class="broken_link" href="http://arpbooks.org/books/detail/indianland">
             <em>
              Indianland
             </em>
             by Lesley Belleau (ARP Books)
            </a>
            , Pat Lowther Memorial Award winner;
            <br/>
            <a href="https://uofrpress.ca/Books/C/Cloud-Physics">
             <em>
              Cloud Physics
             </em>
             by Karen Enns (University of Regina Press)
            </a>
            , Raymond Souster Award winner;
            <br/>
            <a href="http://dwaynemorgan.ca/">
             Dwayne Morgan
            </a>
            , Sheri-D Wilson Golden Beret Award winner;
            <br/>
            Marylin Dumont, Life Member and;
            <br/>
            The University of Regina Press, Honorary Member.
           </p>

            <a href="http://poets.ca/2018/06/17/2018-league-award-winners/">

           <p>
            <a href="http://poets.ca/poemaday/">
             <strong>
              CALL FOR SUBMISSIONS: POEM-A-DAY PILOT PROJECT
             </strong>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Introducing: Poem-a-Day, presented by Poets.ca! Poem-a-Day’s name tells it like it is: one poem shared by the League, Monday-Sunday, each month, all year – starting late 2018. Until now, our call for submissions has been exclusive to members of the League but, in the spirit of National Poetry Month, we are happily opening the call to all Canadians!
           </p>
           <div class="text_exposed_show">
            <p>
             To view our submissions guidelines or sign up early to receive a Poem-a-Day directly to your inbox, visit our website:
             <a data-ft='{"tn":"-U"}' data-lynx-mode="asynclazy" data-lynx-uri="https://l.facebook.com/l.php?u=http%3A%2F%2Fpoets.ca%2Fpoemaday%2F&amp;h=ATMalDEMmURcEWGQBEwCEfd--z-S_JRM6W4j0V3vIo8sX8GUc6j2KtkR5m0KLTlIMxxzSeqarbpHOVEtFv_slJAHcIgKihmILwF1jBjG91Bd3Og7hdlSpt6e8WFAGNYW8ngbFsw" href="http://poets.ca/poemaday/" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">
              http://poets.ca/poemaday/
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Thank you for helping us build this exciting venture in Canadian poetry!
            </p>

           <p style="text-align: right;">
            <strong>
             <a href="http://poets.ca/2018/04/09/2018-jessamy-stursberg-poetry-prize/">
              2018 JESSAMY STRUSBERG POETRY PRIZE FOR CANADIAN YOUTH WINNERS
             </a>
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            We are pleased to announce the winners of the 2018 Jessamy Stursberg Poetry Prize for Canadian Youth: Aminah Attar, Stephanie Chang, Stephanie Cui, Leeloo Legange, Melanie Thompson, and Connie Xie! Congratulations to all winners as well as the honorable mentions (which you can find here)! Don’t miss the chance to read the winners’ full poems, interviews, and jurors’ comments! Thank you to our jurors for the junior category — Heidi Greco, Robin Richardson, and Carol Casey — and the senior category — Richard Osler, Bren Simmers, Cheryl Antao-Xavier, and Greg Santos!
