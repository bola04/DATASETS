                <h2 class="title">
                 <a href="/news/house-representatives-passes-farm-bill">
                  House of Representatives Passes a Farm Bill
                 </a>
                </h2>
                <div class="content clearfix">
                 <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
                  <div class="field-items">
                   <div class="field-item even">
                    <p>
                     <img alt="" src="/sites/ofrf.org/files/staff/IMG_2342.jpg" style="width: 240px; height: 180px; margin: 4px 1px; float: right;"/>
                     While the House Farm Bill was wholly opposed by Democrats, who were joined by some Republicans in their opposition, the House of Representatives passed
                     <a href="https://agriculture.house.gov/uploadedfiles/agriculture_and_nutrition_act_of_2018.pdf">
                      their version of the Farm Bill,
                     </a>
                     by a vote of 213-211 with two Republicans and two Democrats not voting.
                    </p>
                    <p>
                     The U.S. Senate is currently working on their text for the Farm Bill (
                     <a href="http://ofrf.org/news/farm-bill-draft-includes-much-needed-investment-organic-research-and-education">
                      read about it here
                     </a>
                     ), which is different than the House version. If the House and Senate pass different bills, the differences will be parsed out in conference committee, and if approved, will go to the President to be signed into law.
                    </p>
                    <p>
                    </p>
