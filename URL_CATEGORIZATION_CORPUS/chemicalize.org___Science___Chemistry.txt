         <strong>
          What is this credit system? Could you make Chemicalize free?
         </strong>
         <p>
          The credit system doesn't mean you can't use Chemicalize for free. We provide some features free of
                                    charge and give free credits monthly for every user. These possibilities should be enough for typical
                                    use cases. However, more intensive usage is also possible by paying some reasonable compensation for the
                                    extra resources. We believe that all Chemicalize users will benefit from the credit system since we can
                                    speed up the development and updates to provide more valuable features and content.
         </p>
