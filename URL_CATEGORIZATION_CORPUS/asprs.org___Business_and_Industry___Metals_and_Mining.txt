                   Featured: Forensic Working Group
                  </h3>
                  <p>
                   Is having the ability to accurately determine the size, position or speed of objects and people important to your career?  We invite you to join the practitioners, scientists, scholars, and litigators of the Forensic Working Group.  Our members apply peer reviewed science and best practices to real world scenarios involving image, video and survey data.  We are the most respected Photogrammetric group serving the courts, government, business and general public through analysis, reporting, testimony, collaboration and educational outreach.
