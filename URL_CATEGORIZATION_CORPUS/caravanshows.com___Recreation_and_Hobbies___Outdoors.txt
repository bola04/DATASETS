          <h1 class="page_title">
           Welcome to The Caravan &amp; Motorhome Show
          </h1>
          <div class="tabs">
          </div>
          <div class="region region-content">
           <div class="block block-system" id="block-system-main">
            <div class="content">
             <div about="/welcome-caravan-motorhome-show" class="node node-page node-promoted clearfix" id="node-1" typeof="foaf:Document">
              <span class="rdf-meta element-hidden" content="Welcome to The Caravan &amp; Motorhome Show" property="dc:title">
              </span>
              <div class="content">
               <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
                <div class="field-items">
                 <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
                  <p>
                   <strong>
                    The largest Caravan and Motorhome Show of its kind, explore the BIGGEST choice of caravans, motorhomes and holiday homes anywhere in the North of England at The Caravan &amp; Motorhome Show, 17 – 20 January 2019, at Manchester’s EventCity.
                   </strong>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   With the greatest choice available of the latest models on display from
                   <a href="http://www.caravanshows.com/exhibitors" target="_self">
                    <strong>
                     over 200 exhibitors
                    </strong>
                   </a>
                   , The Caravan &amp; Motorhome Show is the perfect place to kick-start your UK caravanning adventures.
                   <strong>
                    Compare caravan layouts, motorhome interiors and holiday home specifications of the latest models
                   </strong>
                   and take advantage of some superb show-only discounts from the region’s leading dealerships. Don't miss our exciting, free outdoor features where you can get behind the wheel of a motorhome or hitch up a caravan to a tow car and take a test drive with expert advice and tuition - perfect for newcomers and those with years of experience. Plus, don't miss
                   <a href="http://www.caravanshows.com/attractions/my-first-towing-experience" target="_self">
                    <strong>
                     My First Towing Experience
                    </strong>
                   </a>
                   where kids aged 3-10 can get behind the wheel of a tow car and take a caravan for a spin around our track!
                  </p>
                  <h3>
                   <a href="/node/2" style="color:#FF5500;">
                    Many great reasons to visit:
                   </a>
                   <br/>
                  </h3>
                  <ul>
                   <li>
                    <strong>
                     Huge choice
                    </strong>
                    of new model touring caravans, motorhomes and holiday homes
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    <strong>
                     Save £££’s
                    </strong>
                    on thousands of essential new season accessories
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Enjoy
                    <strong>
                     free car parking
                    </strong>
                    at Manchester's EventCity with over 3,000 spaces
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    <strong>
                     Free Caravan &amp; Motorhome show guide
                    </strong>
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    <strong>
                     Kids under 16 go free
                    </strong>
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Free entry to the co-located
                    <a href="/node/155">
                     Destinations: The Holiday &amp; Travel Show
                    </a>
                    , with live cookery demonstrations on the Food &amp; Travel Stage
                   </li>
                  </ul>
                  <p>
                   <b>
                    Enjoy a great day out for the whole family at The Caravan &amp; Motorhome Show, Manchester, EventCity.
                   </b>
                  </p>
