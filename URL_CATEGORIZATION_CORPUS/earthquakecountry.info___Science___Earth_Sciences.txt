         </p>

          <a href="https://www.earthquakecountry.org/prepare/">
           prepared
          </a>
          to
          <a href="https://www.earthquakecountry.org/survive/">
           survive
          </a>
          and
          <a href="https://www.earthquakecountry.org/recover/">
           recover
          </a>
          quickly, by following the
          <a href="https://www.earthquakecountry.org/sevensteps/">
           <i>

           </i>
          </a>
