     <a href="/forum/dyeing/25768/color-suggestions-that-can-cover-faded-fushiaberry-red/" title="Color Suggestions That Can Cover Faded Fushia/Berry Red">
      <div class="box">
       <div class="author">
        <img alt="hxix" height="100" src="https://cdn.haircrazy.com/avatars/no_avatar.png" width="100"/>
       </div>
       <p>
        Hello! this is my first post on the forum as I am a new member.
I'm bored with my hair and am looking for hair colors preferably from the adore range that can cover this faded fushia/berry red color I did a month ago. However, I have no desire to bleach my hair anymore. The colors I am interested in achieving are either a dark blue (which I've never done before), a nice purple that isn't pink toned, or a pretty cinnamon or red toned brown that doesn't give off too much orange. However, if these colors are not attainable, I'd really appreciate any color suggestions that would compliment my skin tone. I prefer the Adore brand as it is easily accessible and easy on my college student pockets lol.
       </p>
       <p>
        To give a little background about my hair, it has been previously pre-lightened and dyed orange using a mix of Adore's Ginger and Adore's Sunrise Orange back in February (pictured below is this colored faded). After fading that over the course 3 months, I put another semi permanent color on top by the brand
        <strong>
         Kiss Colors
        </strong>
        from their tintation line in the color
        <em>
         Berry Red
        </em>
        . It is a beautiful color and this new kiss line is great, the
        <strong>
         same price
        </strong>
        as adore with
        <strong>
         more
        </strong>
        product, but I'm just over it and want something new.
       </p>
