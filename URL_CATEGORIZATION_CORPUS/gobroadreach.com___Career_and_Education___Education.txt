   <h1>
    Broadreach Teen Summer Adventure Camps,
    <a href="http://www.gobroadreach.com/summer-programs-for-high-school-students.asp" onclick="setCookie('g_ageid',17,null);">
     High School Summer Study Abroad Programs
    </a>
    ,
    <a href="http://www.gobroadreach.com/summer-programs-for-college-students.asp" onclick="setCookie('g_ageid',18,null);">
     College Study Abroad Summer Programs
    </a>
    and
    <a href="http://www.gobroadreach.com/summer-programs-focused-on-marine-biology-and-marine-science.asp" onclick="setCookie('g_interestid',8,null);">
     Marine Biology Summer Programs
    </a>
   </h1>
   <p>
    Since 1993, Broadreach has offered
    <a href="http://www.gobroadreach.com/summer-programs-for-high-school-students.asp" onclick="setCookie('g_ageid',17,null);">
     educational adventure summer programs for high school students
    </a>
    and
    <a href="http://www.gobroadreach.com/summer-programs-for-middle-school-students.asp" onclick="setCookie('g_ageid',16,null);">
     middle school students
    </a>
    , as well as
    <a href="http://www.gobroadreach.com/summer-programs-for-college-students.asp" onclick="setCookie('g_ageid',18,null);">
     summer study abroad programs for college students
    </a>
    . They have taken thousands of students on a wide variety of adventures in over 35 countries including the islands of the Caribbean, Central and South America, Australia, Fiji, the Solomon Islands, India, China, Egypt, South Africa, France, Canada and the Bahamas.
   </p>
   <p>
    Broadreach’s adventure travel summer camps for teens offer more exploration, learning and discovery than traditional
    <a href="http://www.gobroadreach.com/teen-tour-travel-summer-camps.asp">
     teen tours
    </a>
    or traditional teen summer camps. Their
    <a href="http://www.gobroadreach.com/teen-tour-travel-summer-camps.asp">
     teen trips
    </a>
    include
    <a href="http://www.gobroadreach.com/caribbean-summer-camps.asp">
     Caribbean summer camps
    </a>
    ,
    <a href="http://www.gobroadreach.com/teen-scuba-diving-summer-camps.asp">
     scuba diving summer camps
    </a>
    ,
    <a href="http://www.gobroadreach.com/sailing-summer-camp.asp">
     sailing summer camps
    </a>
    ,
    <a href="http://www.gobroadreach.com/costa-rica-summer-camps.asp">
     Costa Rica summer camps
    </a>
    and
    <a href="http://www.gobroadreach.com/community-service-summer-camps.asp">
     teen community service camps
    </a>
    worldwide. Broadreach’s
    <a href="http://www.gobroadreach.com/academic-summer-camps.asp">
     academic summer camps
    </a>
    all qualify for
    <a href="http://www.gobroadreach.com/college-credit.asp">
     college credit
    </a>
    and include
    <a href="http://www.gobroadreach.com/marine-biology-summer-camps.asp">
     marine biology summer camps
    </a>
    as well as a variety of
    <a href="http://www.gobroadreach.com/teen-language-summer-camps.asp">
     foreign language summer camps
    </a>
    including
    <a href="http://www.gobroadreach.com/spanish-summer-camps.asp">
     Spanish summer camps
    </a>
    and
    <a href="http://www.gobroadreach.com/french-summer-camps.asp">
     French summer camps
    </a>
    .
   </p>
   <p>
    Broadreach’s summer programs and
    <a href="http://www.gobroadreach.com/summer-programs-for-college-students.asp" onclick="setCookie('g_ageid',18,null);">
     college study abroad programs
    </a>
    combine the finest elements of experiential learning, traditional classroom learning, and service learning with international travel and cultural exchange to create extraordinary expeditions. This is how we take our students to a new latitude every summer.
   </p>
