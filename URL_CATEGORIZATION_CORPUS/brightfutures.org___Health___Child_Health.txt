          Bright

                that
          <br/>
          optimal
                health involves a trusting relationship between the health professional,
                the child, the family, and the community as partners
                  in health practice.

              Futures initiative is focused at the American Academy of Pediatrics
        <br/>
        and a collaborative of other federally- and State-funded Bright
              Futures projects (click below for select projects).
