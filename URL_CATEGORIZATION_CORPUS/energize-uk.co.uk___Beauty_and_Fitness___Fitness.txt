     <h1>
      Energize offers a personal and friendly service..
     </h1>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Energize Personal Training Ltd was founded in 2000 with the aim of providing an honest and truly personal service to its customes.
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      Our MORE MOTIVATION, MORE GUIDANCE AND MORE RESULTS philosophy has helped Energize Personal Training build a successful and loyal client base.
     </p>
     <p>
      Over the past 18 years the Energize Personal Training team of friendly, experienced and qualified personal trainers have helped hundreds of people from Wolverhampton and its surrounding areas lose weight, tone up and achieve their specific health and fitness goals.
     </p>
     <p>
      Based at Wolverhampton Lawn Tennis &amp; Squash Club on the outskirts of Wolverhampton city centre in the West Midlands, Energize Personal Training offers a range of bespoke personal fitness training services to individuals, couples, small groups and the Corporate Health and Fitness market.
     </p>
     <p>
      More recently the Energize Personal Training team have expanded their provision under the System Energize banner where they now offer a range of exciting classes a yearly detox diet and fitness programme and our successful active weekends.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      We pride ourselves in offering our personal training clients a more private and intimate training environment with an un-intimidating atmosphere and a friendly ambience and clients of our group sessions can enjoy fun, dynamic and effective classes.
     </p>

           <p>
            System Energize Detox
           </p>
           <p>
            Following the huge success of our the System Energize Detox Camps in 2015 we will be running two programmes again in 2016, the first will commence on 9th January 2016 and the second in late May/June. In addition to our two scheduled programmes electronic copies of the Detox Plan will be available via our online shop from 15th December 2015
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-family:helvetica; font-size:10.000000pt">
             The System Energize Detox is not a short term fix, not a gimmick from a Sunday magazine. The participants of this lifestyle revolution will develop nutritional habits that will stay long after the plan has finished.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-family:helvetica; font-size:10.000000pt">
             It is a fresh, whole food, natural and clean eating, calorie burning plan that will put you on the path to a cleaner, healthier and happier you. It has been designed to reset the body's systems, purge harmful toxins, boost respiratory health, burn body fat and provide the perfect stepping stone for continued health and fitness gains.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-family:helvetica; font-size:10.000000pt">
             The food plan is designed with seasonal fruit and vegetables in mind. This allows the participant to eat locally sourced food emphasising maximum nutrients, shorter shelf time, less chemical intake due to less preservation and a lower carbon foot print.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Benefits of the System Energize Detox include:
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-family:helvetica; font-size:10.000000pt">
             • Weight loss • increased energy • better digestion • clearer skin • sharper mind • fewer infections and clearer sinuses • increased cardiovascular fitness • greater muscle tone and flexibility.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Remove Toxins
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-family:helvetica; font-size:10.000000pt">
             The Primary goal is to reset the body's systems and purge toxins from the internal organs. Therefore foods selected have antioxidant, anti inflammatory and cleansing properties benefiting participants with more energy, healthy skin, improved digestion.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Fitness
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-family:helvetica; font-size:10.000000pt">
             Exercise plays an important role in the detox plan as participants will be pushed to the limit with two functional and intense workouts a day. This will provide as a great calorie burner ridding the body of excess body fat and boosting the efficiency of the respiratory system.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Weight loss
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-family:helvetica; font-size:10.000000pt">
             If your main goal is weight loss then you are going to have make a few adaptions to your detox plan. Designed primarily to cleanse and rejuvenate your body so you are going to have to sacrifice some parts of the plan.
            </span>
            <strong>
             Smoothies and one snack per day will need to be omitted from the diet to help you achieve your goal.
            </strong>
           </p>
