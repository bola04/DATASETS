      Literature Project is a collection of free classic books, poems, speeches, and
plays. For each piece of literature, we offer a free online eBook
that can be easily read and searched.  In addition, each online book includes links to
a
downloadable eBook
version. We also offer Text-to-Speech
software that can be downloaded from our site.
     </p>

      Our mission is to provide resources for the
appreciation of electronic literature. We care about digital content and eBooks
because we care about the environment. Save a tree and read an eBook. You can
help save our planet.
