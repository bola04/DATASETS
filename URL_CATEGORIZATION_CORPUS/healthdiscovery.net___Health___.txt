     </h1>

           <h2>
            Restaurant Nutrition and WWPoints
           </h2>
           <p>
            Our
            <strong>
             interactive menus
            </strong>
            make it easier to choose healthy restaurants and fast food by comparing
            <strong>
             dietary values and nutrition facts
            </strong>

             fat

             cholesterol

            </em>

            <em>
             carbs
            </em>

            <em>
             fiber
            </em>
            , or
            <em>
             protein
            </em>
            the food contains. We have all
            <strong>
             nutrition information for restaurants
            </strong>
            that we could find - including vitamins, minerals, and allergens.
           </p>
           <p>
            As a tradition, we've tailored special
            <strong>
             Weight Watchers menus
            </strong>
            with Points for all restaurants, including the new Smart-Points.

            <em>
             high fiber
            </em>
            and
            <em>
             high protein
            </em>
            recipes.
