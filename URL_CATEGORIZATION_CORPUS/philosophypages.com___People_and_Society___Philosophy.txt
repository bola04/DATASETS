      <h2>

      <br/>
      <br/>
      <p>
       This site offers helpful information for students of the Western philosophical tradition.
The elements you will find on this site include:
      </p>
      <dl>
       <dd>
        The
        <a href="dy/index.htm">
         Dictionary of Philosophical Terms and Names
        </a>
        .
        <dd>
         A survey of the
         <a href="hy/index.htm">
          History of Western Philosophy
         </a>
         .
         <dd>
          A
          <a href="dy/zt.htm">
           Timeline
          </a>
          for the intellectual figures discussed here.
          <dd>
           Detailed discussion of several major
           <a href="ph/index.htm">
            Philosophers
           </a>
           <dd>
            Summary treatment of the elementary principles of
            <a href="lg/index.htm">
             Logic
            </a>
            <dd>
             A generic
             <a href="sy.htm">
              Study Guide
             </a>
             for students of philosophy.
             <dd>
              Links to other philosophy
              <a href="links.htm">
               Sites
              </a>
              on the Internet.
             </dd>
            </dd>
           </dd>
          </dd>
         </dd>
        </dd>
       </dd>
      </dl>
