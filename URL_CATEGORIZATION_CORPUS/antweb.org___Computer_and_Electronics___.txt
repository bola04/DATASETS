       <p>
        AntWeb is the world's largest online database of images, 
specimen records, and natural history information on ants. It is community driven and open to contribution from anyone with specimen records, natural history comments, or images.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        Our mission is to publish for the scientific community 
high quality images of all the world's ant species.  AntWeb 
provides tools for submitting images, specimen records, annotating species 
pages, and managing regional species lists.
