     <div style="font-size: small; margin: 1.5em 4em 0 0;">
      <p>
       Some tips:
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        Fantasy surnames work well only as
        <span class="italic">
         short
        </span>
        names, as medium or long ones will probably sound ridiculous (e.g. Stormwolfblade). Unless that's your intention, of course.
       </li>
       <li>
        Apostrophes (') and dashes (-) only appear in medium and long names.
       </li>
       <li>
        English names are in the format "
        <span class="italic">
         name surname
        </span>
        " (short) or "
        <span class="italic">
         name middle-name surname
        </span>
        " (medium or long, which in this case are the same).
       </li>
       <li>
        For place names, fantasy titles, superhero / supervillain names, rock band names, or military operation names, the length selection doesn't affect anything.
       </li>
       <li>
        A list of suggestions may not have exactly what you're looking for, but may inspire you to think of it on your own -- possibly a variation of one of the suggestions.
       </li>
      </ul>
