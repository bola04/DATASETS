       <h2 class="entry-title">
        Welcome
       </h2>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         AkLA is a nonprofit professional organization for the employees, volunteers and advocates at academic, public, school and special libraries of all sizes in Alaska, as well as library products and services vendors.
        </p>
        <p>
         The goals of the organization are to promote vibrant libraries that deliver outstanding services to their users and to provide professional development opportunities.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Strategic Plan
        </h2>
        <p>
         The Alaska Library Association has finalized its 2017-2019 Strategic Plan.
         <a href="http://akla.org/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/AkLA_Strategic_Plan_FINAL.pdf">
          More ..
         </a>
        </p>
        <h2>
         Membership Drive
        </h2>
        <p>
         The Association needs your membership! To continue to be the effective organization, we need your support. It’s easy to
         <a href="http://akla.org/join/" title="Join AkLA or Renew">
          join or renew online
         </a>
         with our new membership management system!
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
