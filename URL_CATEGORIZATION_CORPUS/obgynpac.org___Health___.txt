       <h2>
        Ob-GynPAC: Your Voice, Amplified.
       </h2>
       <div class="content">
        <div class="content-wrapper">
         <p>
          Ob-GynPAC, ACOG’s political action committee, is the only federal PAC exclusively dedicated to electing representatives who support our specialty. During the 2016 election cycle, our members contributed more than $1.2 million to our PAC, enabling us to support over 180 state and federal candidates, including 24 medical professionals. We support people, not parties, who work to protect and promote women's health.

The stakes in these elections just keep getting higher for our specialty -- for our practices and our patients. We must elect Members of Congress, State Legislators, and Governors who understand the issues most important to us. Every dime you contribute to Ob-GynPAC goes directly to help elect worthy candidates—or to help defeat our opponents! Not a penny goes to staffing, overhead, or administrative costs.

Please join us today to help defend our specialty, and make sure that we are able to stand strong for women’s health.
         </p>
