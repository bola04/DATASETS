            <h2>
             One Senior Place
            </h2>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             One Senior Place is a dedicated, comfortable, and inviting area within the library which is specifically designed for people age 50 and over. A collection of books (fiction, non-fiction &amp; large print), magazines, and other resources can be found in that area. Look for the "One Senior Place" sign the next time you are in the library and then spend some time browsing at your leisure.
            </p>

             V.I.P Pass
            </h2>
            <p>
            </p>

             Visit the library, borrow a V.I.P. pass and explore! Here's how it works:  Visit the library and check out a V.I.P. pass - just like a book! After you have enjoyed a great day, return the V.I.P. pass to the library so another person can check it out.
