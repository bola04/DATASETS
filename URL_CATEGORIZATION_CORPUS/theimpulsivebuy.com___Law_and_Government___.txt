             <p>
              Usually, with these Weekend Giveaway posts there’s a list of all the things that’ll be given away to one lucky reader. But this time there isn’t one because we’re not sure what we’re giving away.
             </p>
             <p>
              There might be a gift card. There might not. There might be candy. Okay, there definitely will be candy, and if you
              <a href="https://www.instagram.com/theimpulsivebuy/">
               follow TIB on Instagram
              </a>
              you might get an idea of what candy we could be giving away. There might be a snack that you can get only in Hawaii. There might be a stuffed toy. But, there will definitely be shredded paper.
             </p>
             <p>
              <b>
               RULES:
              </b>
             </p>
             <p>
              To enter The Impulsive Buy’s Mystery Box Prize Drawing, leave a comment with THIS post. You can write whatever you want, but please don’t forget to fill out the email field because we’ll be emailing the randomly selected winner for his or her mailing address.
             </p>
             <p>
              We will stop accepting entries on Sunday, June 24, 2018 11:59 p.m. Hawaii Standard Time. Only one comment allowed per person, and it’s only open to U.S. residents 18 years old or older.
             </p>
             <p>
              The winner will be announced at the top of this post a few days after we stop accepting entries.
             </p>
             <p>
              Good luck!

           <a href="https://www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/2018/06/22/quick-review-taco-bell-watermelon-freeze/" title="QUICK REVIEW: Taco Bell Watermelon Freeze">
           </a>
           <div class="post-inner-content">
            <header class="entry-header page-header">
             <h2 class="entry-title">
              <a href="https://www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/2018/06/22/quick-review-taco-bell-watermelon-freeze/" rel="bookmark">
               QUICK REVIEW: Taco Bell Watermelon Freeze
              </a>
             </h2>
             <div class="entry-meta">
              <span class="posted-on">
               <i class="fa fa-calendar-alt">
               </i>
               <a href="https://www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/2018/06/22/quick-review-taco-bell-watermelon-freeze/" rel="bookmark">
                <time class="entry-date published" datetime="2018-06-22T06:00:00+00:00">
                 June 22, 2018
                </time>
                <time class="updated" datetime="2018-06-21T22:58:31+00:00">
                 June 21, 2018
                </time>
               </a>
              </span>
              <span class="byline">
               <i class="fa fa-user">
               </i>
               <span class="author vcard">
                <a class="url fn n" href="https://www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/author/denis/">
                 Denis
                </a>
               </span>
              </span>
              <span class="comments-link">
               <i class="fa fa-comments">
               </i>
               <a href="https://www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/2018/06/22/quick-review-taco-bell-watermelon-freeze/#comments">
                3 Comments
               </a>
              </span>
             </div>
             <!-- .entry-meta -->
            </header>
            <!-- .entry-header -->
            <div class="entry-content">
             <p>
              <img alt="Taco Bell Watermelon Freeze" border="0" data-lazy-src="https://i0.wp.com/www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Taco-Bell-Watermelon-Freeze.jpg?resize=550%2C919&amp;ssl=1" data-recalc-dims="1" height="919" src="https://www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/wp-content/plugins/jetpack/modules/lazy-images/images/1x1.trans.gif" style="display:block; margin-left:auto; margin-right:auto;" title="Taco Bell Watermelon Freeze.jpg" width="550"/>
             </p>
             <noscript>
              <img alt="Taco Bell Watermelon Freeze" border="0" data-recalc-dims="1" height="919" src="https://i0.wp.com/www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Taco-Bell-Watermelon-Freeze.jpg?resize=550%2C919&amp;ssl=1" style="display:block; margin-left:auto; margin-right:auto;" title="Taco Bell Watermelon Freeze.jpg" width="550"/>
             </noscript>
             <h3>
              What is it?
             </h3>
             <p>
              The latest flavor creation in Taco Bell’s Freeze line of slush drinks, this variation adds some candy “seeds” to the mix to give it a unique look.
             </p>
             <h3>
              How is it?
             </h3>
             <p>
              Gimmicky but good. The watermelon flavor is about as expected. It’s a bit milder than some previous Freeze offerings, like the Strawberry Skittles flavor, and it’s not too sweet nor too tart.
             </p>
             <p>
              But what about the candy seeds? Well, they really don’t bring much to the table, other than slowing down the slush-making process. Your friendly Taco Bell barista must first open a little package of seeds, fill the cup about a third of the way with slush, sprinkle in some seeds, fill more of the cup, sprinkle in more seeds, and so on.
             </p>
             <p>
              <img alt="Taco Bell Watermelon Freeze 2" border="0" data-lazy-src="https://i1.wp.com/www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Taco-Bell-Watermelon-Freeze-2.jpg?resize=550%2C823&amp;ssl=1" data-recalc-dims="1" height="823" src="https://www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/wp-content/plugins/jetpack/modules/lazy-images/images/1x1.trans.gif" style="display:block; margin-left:auto; margin-right:auto;" title="Taco Bell Watermelon Freeze 2.jpg" width="550"/>
             </p>
             <noscript>
              <img alt="Taco Bell Watermelon Freeze 2" border="0" data-recalc-dims="1" height="823" src="https://i1.wp.com/www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Taco-Bell-Watermelon-Freeze-2.jpg?resize=550%2C823&amp;ssl=1" style="display:block; margin-left:auto; margin-right:auto;" title="Taco Bell Watermelon Freeze 2.jpg" width="550"/>
             </noscript>
             <p>
              Eerily, the result for my drink looked like some sort of weird candy seed face starting me down, just daring me to drink it. I took the dare, and while the watermelon flavor was nice, the seeds didn’t add much flavor at all. The black coating was almost flavorless, and the inside only had a faint sugary taste, like an unflavored Smartie.
             </p>
             <h3>
              Is there anything else I need to know?
             </h3>
             <p>
              <img alt="Taco Bell Watermelon Freeze 3" border="0" data-lazy-src="https://i0.wp.com/www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Taco-Bell-Watermelon-Freeze-3.jpg?resize=550%2C594&amp;ssl=1" data-recalc-dims="1" height="594" src="https://www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/wp-content/plugins/jetpack/modules/lazy-images/images/1x1.trans.gif" style="display:block; margin-left:auto; margin-right:auto;" title="Taco Bell Watermelon Freeze 3.jpg" width="550"/>
             </p>
             <noscript>
              <img alt="Taco Bell Watermelon Freeze 3" border="0" data-recalc-dims="1" height="594" src="https://i0.wp.com/www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Taco-Bell-Watermelon-Freeze-3.jpg?resize=550%2C594&amp;ssl=1" style="display:block; margin-left:auto; margin-right:auto;" title="Taco Bell Watermelon Freeze 3.jpg" width="550"/>
             </noscript>
             <p>
              Nothing frustrates me more than a clogged straw caused by candy pieces mixed into an ice cream or slush drink, but there’s no need to worry about that here. The straw provided (in watermelon rind green) is of ample circumference to allow multiple seeds to easily travel from the cup to your piehole without fear of obstruction. Be forewarned though, the larger volume straw also increases the probability of brain freeze.
             </p>
             <p>
              <img alt="Taco Bell Watermelon Freeze 4" border="0" data-lazy-src="https://i1.wp.com/www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Taco-Bell-Watermelon-Freeze-4.jpg?resize=600%2C375&amp;ssl=1" data-recalc-dims="1" height="375" src="https://www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/wp-content/plugins/jetpack/modules/lazy-images/images/1x1.trans.gif" style="display:block; margin-left:auto; margin-right:auto;" title="Taco Bell Watermelon Freeze 4.jpg" width="600"/>
             </p>
             <noscript>
              <img alt="Taco Bell Watermelon Freeze 4" border="0" data-recalc-dims="1" height="375" src="https://i1.wp.com/www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Taco-Bell-Watermelon-Freeze-4.jpg?resize=600%2C375&amp;ssl=1" style="display:block; margin-left:auto; margin-right:auto;" title="Taco Bell Watermelon Freeze 4.jpg" width="600"/>
             </noscript>
             <p>
              And weirdly, the seeds seem to lose their coating after a few minutes of swimming in the slush, so by the time I got to the bottom many of them had a bit of a speckled egg look.
             </p>
             <h3>
              Conclusion:
             </h3>
             <p>
              Even though the candy seeds seem like a missed opportunity —imagine if they were kiwi flavored or something else to complement the watermelon slush — overall the Watermelon Freeze still a refreshing summer drink and quite a bargain if you score one during Happy Hour.
             </p>
             <p>
              <b>
               Purchased Price:
              </b>
              $2.29 (or $1 during Happy Hour from 2 p.m. to 5 p.m.)
              <br/>
              <b>
               Size:
              </b>
              16 oz.
              <br/>
              <b>
               Rating:
              </b>
              7 out of 10
              <br/>
              <b>
               Nutrition Facts:
              </b>
              220 calories, 0 grams of fat, 60 milligrams of sodium, 59 grams of carbohydrates, 0 grams of fiber, 58 grams of sugar, and 0 grams of protein.
             </p>

           <a href="https://www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/2018/06/22/quick-review-krispy-kreme-strawberries-kreme-and-banana-pudding-doughnuts/" title="QUICK REVIEW: Krispy Kreme Strawberries &amp; Kreme and Banana Pudding Doughnuts">
           </a>
           <div class="post-inner-content">
            <header class="entry-header page-header">
             <h2 class="entry-title">
              <a href="https://www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/2018/06/22/quick-review-krispy-kreme-strawberries-kreme-and-banana-pudding-doughnuts/" rel="bookmark">
               QUICK REVIEW: Krispy Kreme Strawberries &amp; Kreme and Banana Pudding Doughnuts
              </a>
             </h2>
             <div class="entry-meta">
              <span class="posted-on">
               <i class="fa fa-calendar-alt">
               </i>
               <a href="https://www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/2018/06/22/quick-review-krispy-kreme-strawberries-kreme-and-banana-pudding-doughnuts/" rel="bookmark">

                 June 22, 2018
                </time>
                <time class="updated" datetime="2018-06-21T22:31:18+00:00">
                 June 21, 2018
                </time>
               </a>
              </span>
              <span class="byline">
               <i class="fa fa-user">
               </i>
               <span class="author vcard">
                <a class="url fn n" href="https://www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/author/sean-sullivan/">
                 Sean
                </a>

              <span class="comments-link">
               <i class="fa fa-comments">
               </i>
               <a href="https://www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/2018/06/22/quick-review-krispy-kreme-strawberries-kreme-and-banana-pudding-doughnuts/#respond">
                Leave a comment
               </a>
              </span>

            <!-- .entry-header -->
            <div class="entry-content">
             <p>
              <img alt="Krispy Kreme Strawberries  Kreme and Banana Pudding Doughnuts" border="0" data-lazy-src="https://i2.wp.com/www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Krispy-Kreme-Strawberries-Kreme-and-Banana-Pudding-Doughnuts.jpg?resize=550%2C733&amp;ssl=1" data-recalc-dims="1" height="733" src="https://www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/wp-content/plugins/jetpack/modules/lazy-images/images/1x1.trans.gif" style="display:block; margin-left:auto; margin-right:auto;" title="Krispy Kreme Strawberries &amp; Kreme and Banana Pudding Doughnuts.jpg" width="550"/>
             </p>
             <noscript>
              <img alt="Krispy Kreme Strawberries  Kreme and Banana Pudding Doughnuts" border="0" data-recalc-dims="1" height="733" src="https://i2.wp.com/www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Krispy-Kreme-Strawberries-Kreme-and-Banana-Pudding-Doughnuts.jpg?resize=550%2C733&amp;ssl=1" style="display:block; margin-left:auto; margin-right:auto;" title="Krispy Kreme Strawberries &amp; Kreme and Banana Pudding Doughnuts.jpg" width="550"/>
             </noscript>
             <h3>
              What are they?
             </h3>
             <p>
              Summer has arrived at the doughnut shop! The all-new Strawberries and Kreme Doughnut is a strawberry frosted yeast shell filled with both strawberry and KK’s signature Kreme. The returning Banana Pudding doughnut is a yeast shell filled with a mixture of banana custard and Kreme, topped with yellow icing and Nilla Wafer pieces.
             </p>
             <h3>
              How are they?
             </h3>
             <p>
              <img alt="Krispy Kreme Strawberries  Kreme Doughnut 2" border="0" data-lazy-src="https://i2.wp.com/www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Krispy-Kreme-Strawberries-Kreme-Doughnut-2.jpg?resize=550%2C550&amp;ssl=1" data-recalc-dims="1" height="550" src="https://www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/wp-content/plugins/jetpack/modules/lazy-images/images/1x1.trans.gif" style="display:block; margin-left:auto; margin-right:auto;" title="Krispy Kreme Strawberries &amp; Kreme Doughnut 2.jpg" width="550"/>
             </p>
             <noscript>
              <img alt="Krispy Kreme Strawberries  Kreme Doughnut 2" border="0" data-recalc-dims="1" height="550" src="https://i2.wp.com/www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Krispy-Kreme-Strawberries-Kreme-Doughnut-2.jpg?resize=550%2C550&amp;ssl=1" style="display:block; margin-left:auto; margin-right:auto;" title="Krispy Kreme Strawberries &amp; Kreme Doughnut 2.jpg" width="550"/>
             </noscript>
             <p>
              Well, it looks like someone at Krispy Kreme didn’t get the memo because my Strawberries and Kreme Doughnut is completely devoid of any strawberry filling. I was really excited at the idea of a dual-filling and the way a jelly texture would work with a creamier one. That being said, it is BURSTING with Kreme and as a huge fan of the thick sweet stuff, I am still enjoying this immensely.
             </p>
             <p>
              <img alt="Krispy Kreme Strawberries  Kreme Doughnut" border="0" data-lazy-src="https://i1.wp.com/www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Krispy-Kreme-Strawberries-Kreme-Doughnut.jpg?resize=550%2C550&amp;ssl=1" data-recalc-dims="1" height="550" src="https://www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/wp-content/plugins/jetpack/modules/lazy-images/images/1x1.trans.gif" style="display:block; margin-left:auto; margin-right:auto;" title="Krispy Kreme Strawberries &amp; Kreme Doughnut.jpg" width="550"/>
             </p>
             <noscript>
              <img alt="Krispy Kreme Strawberries  Kreme Doughnut" border="0" data-recalc-dims="1" height="550" src="https://i1.wp.com/www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Krispy-Kreme-Strawberries-Kreme-Doughnut.jpg?resize=550%2C550&amp;ssl=1" style="display:block; margin-left:auto; margin-right:auto;" title="Krispy Kreme Strawberries &amp; Kreme Doughnut.jpg" width="550"/>
             </noscript>
             <p>
              The strawberry icing has a surprisingly authentic and slightly tart berry flavor that’s much more real and much less Nesquik than I was expecting. The yeast shell is soft and fresh and even though I’m a bit letdown, I’ll take Kreme over jelly any day, and even as is it’s pretty delicious.
             </p>
             <p>
              <img alt="Krispy Kreme Banana Pudding Doughnut 2" border="0" data-lazy-src="https://i2.wp.com/www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Krispy-Kreme-Banana-Pudding-Doughnut-2.jpg?resize=550%2C550&amp;ssl=1" data-recalc-dims="1" height="550" src="https://www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/wp-content/plugins/jetpack/modules/lazy-images/images/1x1.trans.gif" style="display:block; margin-left:auto; margin-right:auto;" title="Krispy Kreme Banana Pudding Doughnut 2.jpg" width="550"/>
             </p>
             <noscript>
              <img alt="Krispy Kreme Banana Pudding Doughnut 2" border="0" data-recalc-dims="1" height="550" src="https://i2.wp.com/www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Krispy-Kreme-Banana-Pudding-Doughnut-2.jpg?resize=550%2C550&amp;ssl=1" style="display:block; margin-left:auto; margin-right:auto;" title="Krispy Kreme Banana Pudding Doughnut 2.jpg" width="550"/>
             </noscript>
             <p>
              The Banana Pudding Doughnut came as advertised and the banana custard is AMAZING. It has the runny-ness of custard with the slight sugary grit of the classic Kreme and a big super delicious banana flavor that exceeded my expectations. It’s sweet, but rich and eggy and I can’t imagine any way it could be better.
             </p>
             <p>
              <img alt="Krispy Kreme Banana Pudding Doughnut" border="0" data-lazy-src="https://i0.wp.com/www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Krispy-Kreme-Banana-Pudding-Doughnut-1.jpg?resize=550%2C550&amp;ssl=1" data-recalc-dims="1" height="550" src="https://www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/wp-content/plugins/jetpack/modules/lazy-images/images/1x1.trans.gif" style="display:block; margin-left:auto; margin-right:auto;" title="Krispy Kreme Banana Pudding Doughnut.jpg" width="550"/>
             </p>
             <noscript>
              <img alt="Krispy Kreme Banana Pudding Doughnut" border="0" data-recalc-dims="1" height="550" src="https://i0.wp.com/www.theimpulsivebuy.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Krispy-Kreme-Banana-Pudding-Doughnut-1.jpg?resize=550%2C550&amp;ssl=1" style="display:block; margin-left:auto; margin-right:auto;" title="Krispy Kreme Banana Pudding Doughnut.jpg" width="550"/>
             </noscript>
             <p>
              What could be better are the Nilla wafers, which are stale and don’t do much to elevate the experience, and kind of bring it down – making it taste and feel less fresh. Although it isn’t great, it’s easy to overlook, because once again the ratio of filling inside is damn fine.
             </p>
             <h3>
              Is there anything else I need to know?
             </h3>
             <p>
              What I got was essentially a Chocolate Iced Kreme Filled with strawberry on top and a fancy little Hostess-y squiggle. Since the Kreme filled is my number two favorite right after KK’s Original Glazed, I really dug it, but the potential for it to be even better is there.
             </p>
             <h3>
              Conclusion:
             </h3>
             <p>
              The Banana Pudding doughnut is a worthy returning flavor with an immaculate filling that’s up there with KK’s best. It may have been better with some sparingly placed white chocolate chips, but I can’t deny the necessity of wafers for the pudding profile.
             </p>
             <p>
              <b>
               Purchased Price:
              </b>
              $1.89
              <br/>
              <b>
               Size:
              </b>
              N/A
              <br/>
              <b>
               Rating:
              </b>
              8 out of 10 (Strawberries and Kreme, but could be 9 or better if made properly)
              <br/>
              <b>
               Rating:
              </b>
              8 out of 10 (Banana Pudding)
              <br/>
              <b>
               Nutrition Facts:
              </b>
              <b>
               Strawberries &amp; Kreme
              </b>
              – 330 calories, 17 grams of fat, 7 grams of saturated fat, 0 grams of trans fat, 0 milligrams of cholesterol, 135 milligrams of sodium, 41 grams of carbohydrates, 0 grams of fiber, 23 grams of sugar, and 4 grams of protein.
              <b>
               Banana Pudding
              </b>
              – 360 calories, 19 grams of fat, 8 grams of saturated fat, 0 grams of trans fat, 0 milligrams of cholesterol, 160 milligrams of sodium, 45 grams of carbohydrates, 0 grams of fiber, 24 grams of sugar, and 4 grams of protein..
             </p>
