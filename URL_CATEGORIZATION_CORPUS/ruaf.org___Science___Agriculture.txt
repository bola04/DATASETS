         </h2>
         <div class="boxes-box" id="boxes-box-front_why_urban_agriculture">
          <div class="boxes-box-content">
           <p>
            Urban agriculture is increasingly recognised by city authorities and civil society organisations for its capacity to:
           </p>
           <p>
            - strengthen the resilience of the urban food system
            <br/>
            - enhance access of the urban poor to nutritious food
            <br/>
            - generate (self-) employment and income
            <br/>
            - help cities to adapt to climate change and reduce their ecological foot print.
           </p>

            Read more on
