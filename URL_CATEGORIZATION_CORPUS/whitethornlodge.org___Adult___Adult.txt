     White Thorn Lodge is a private family nudist
club nestled in the hills of Western Pennsylvania, an hour or so from
     <b>
      Pittsburgh, Cleveland, Erie, Canton
     </b>
     and
     <b>
      Wheeling
     </b>
     .
Since White Thorn's inception as a member-owned cooperative club in
1961, we have worked to create a relaxing natural haven in our more
than 200 wooded acres. We are proud of our friendly family atmosphere,
our
     <b>
      campgrounds
     </b>
     , our
     <b>
      sports and recreational facilities
     </b>
     , our
     <b>
      weekend events
     </b>
     , and especially the opportunity for renewal that is unique to the nudist experience.
     <p>
      Since 1962, White Thorn has been affiliated with the
      <a href="http://www.aanr.com/">
       AANR
      </a>
      (American Association for Nude Recreation) and its regional
association
      <a href="http://www.aanr-east.com/">
       AANR-East
      </a>
      (American Association for Nude Recreation - Eastern Region, Inc.) as a full-member club.
     </p>
     <p>
      White Thorn Lodge is a private membership club; however, we welcome
guests during our summer season from Memorial Day through the weekend
after Labor Day. Use the
      <a href="public/message.html">
       message page
      </a>
      to tell us about yourself or send
      <a href="mailto:wtl@whitethornlodge.org">
       e-mail
      </a>
      to wtl@whitethornlodge.org, and we can invite
you for a visit.
     </p>
