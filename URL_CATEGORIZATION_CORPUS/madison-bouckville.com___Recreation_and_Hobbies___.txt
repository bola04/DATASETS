                         Newsletter News
                        </strong>
                        : We are pleased to welcome Jim Ford on board as editor of our Madison Bouckville Antique Week newsletter. Jim is a well known local historian and author of three very interesting books on the history of Madison and Bouckville. Jim and his Wife Joan are life long residents of the area. Jim is a retired history teacher from Madison Central school, He currently remains very busy researching and documenting Madison county history. We look forward to
                       </em>
                      </span>
                      <em>
                       <span style="font-family: times new roman">
                        <a href="http://www.madison-bouckville.com/newsletters.html">

                         </strong>
                        </a>
                        which will be posted and emailed monthly to those who have signed up for the newsletter. Jim will be updating us on the countdown to Antique week, Madison Bouckville Promotions board meeting news and some interesting history memo's. Jim has written several books on the area, including:  The Pride of Cidertown -- A History of the Bouckville Summits Baseball Team, Sweet Cider Days -- A History of Mott's in Bouckville, New York, and Grove -- His Life and Legacy in the Madison Area.
