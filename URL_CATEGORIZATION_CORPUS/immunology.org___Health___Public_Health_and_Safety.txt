    <h1 class="main-page-title">
     Home
    </h1>
    <div class="region region--content">
     <div class="region-inner">
      <div class="block block-block block--homepage-intro " id="block-block-8">
       <div class="block-inner">
        <div class="block-content">
         <p>
          We promote excellence in research, scholarship and clinical practice in immunology in order to improve human and animal health.
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>
      </div>
      <div class="block block-block d-span-n-4 block--cta-block blue " id="block-block-9">
       <div class="block-inner">
        <h2 class="block-title">
         What is immunology?
        </h2>
        <div class="block-content">
         <p>
          Immunology is the study of the immune system and a very important branch of the medical and biological sciences. We have a whole suite of resources that can help you learn more about immunology and your immune system, including our online learning platform "BiteSized Immunology".
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="https://www.immunology.org/public-information/what-is-immunology">
           See more
          </a>
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>
      </div>
      <div class="block block-block d-span-n-4 block--cta-block pink " id="block-block-10">
       <div class="block-inner">
        <h2 class="block-title">
         Regional and Affinity Groups
        </h2>
        <div class="block-content">
         <p>
          All BSI members are encouraged to join and become active in the work of the many Regional and Affinity Groups supported by the BSI. These groups organise a number of activities with BSI support, including seminars and meetings as well as one-off larger events.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="https://www.immunology.org/about-us/our-people/regional-and-affinity-groups">
           See more
          </a>
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>
      </div>
      <div class="block block-block d-span-n-4 span-last block--cta-block purple " id="block-block-11">
       <div class="block-inner">
        <h2 class="block-title">
         Not a member?
        </h2>
        <div class="block-content">
         <p>
          It's easy to become a member of the BSI. Sign up today to connect with the largest network of immunologists in Europe and to enjoy discounts at Congress and other BSI meetings, free access to our journals, Immunology News, grants, bursaries and much more.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="https://www.immunology.org/membership">
           See more
          </a>
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>
