      <h4>
       Czech Academy of Agricultural Sciences
      </h4>
      <p>
       The Czech Academy of Agricultural Sciences is a specialized and professional scientific agricultural institution operating in the Czech Republic.
       <a href="https://www.agriculturejournals.cz/web/about/">
        More info …
       </a>
      </p>
     </div>
     <div class="homepage_section">
      <h4>
       <strong>
        Editorial Boards
       </strong>
       <strong>
        ’ Statute
       </strong>
      </h4>
      <p>
       Editorial Boards of the scientific journals of the Czech Academy of Agricultural Sciences (CAAS) are established by the CAAS.
       <a href="https://www.agriculturejournals.cz/web/wp-content/uploads/Statute_of_the_Editorial_Boards_CAAS.pdf">
        More info …
       </a>
      </p>
      <h4>
       Personal Data Processing and It‘s  Protection
      </h4>
      <p>
       The Czech Academy of Agricultural Sciences processes and protects personal data in accordance with the applicable legislation.
       <a href="https://www.agriculturejournals.cz/web/personal-data-processing-and-its-protection/">
        More info …
       </a>
      </p>
      <h4>
       Licence terms
      </h4>
      <p>
       All contents of the journals is freely available for non-commercial purposes, users are allowed to copy and redistribute the material, transform, and build upon the material as long as they cite the source.
      </p>
      <h4>
       Open Access Policy
      </h4>
      <p>
       The journals provides immediate open access to their content on the principle that making research freely available to the public supports a greater global exchange of knowledge.
      </p>
      <h4>
       Similarity Check
      </h4>
      <p>
       All the submitted manuscripts are checked by the
       <a href="https://www.crossref.org/crosscheck/index.html">
        CrossRef Similarity Check
       </a>
       .
      </p>
