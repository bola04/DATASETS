       </a>
      </h2>
      <div class="content">
       <p>
        Following the nomination of two additional new Board members, there is an Addendum to the agenda for the 88th Annual General Meeting (AGM) of the Freshwater Biological Association. Biographies and a nomination form can be found
        <a href="https://drive.google.com/file/d/0B3GNzXyzhDbFQndGNjhmV2x6Ymc/view?usp=sharing" target="_blank">
         here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        Our Honorary Research Fellows will  deliver a series of presentations on their recent scientific work during the afternoon, immediately before the AGM (Monday 23 October from 13.15 hrs). The agenda for the afternoon can be found
        <a href="https://drive.google.com/file/d/0B3GNzXyzhDbFQzlYTG5yRUhMX0k/view?usp=sharing" target="_blank">
         here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        We would be delighted to welcome members to this stimulating opportunity to hear from our distinguished colleagues. Please let
        <a href="mailto:membership@fba.org.uk">
         membership@fba.org.uk
        </a>
        know if you plan to attend, in order that we can provide sufficient refreshments.
       </p>

        We look forward to seeing many of you on the 23rd.
