         <span style="font-weight: bold; text-transform: uppercase; font-size: 16px; padding:5px 0 5px 0!important;">

           Career Center

         - Search for current DCAA openings at
         <a href="https://www.usajobs.gov/Search/?a=DD10&amp;p=1&amp;s=location" target="_blank" title="Visit USAJobs.gov for a list of current DCAA openings">
          www.usajobs.gov
         </a>
         <p>
          There's  no other career in the financial world like a career with DCAA. Before an  aircraft carrier puts out to sea for the first time, we've been on board.  Before a rifle is put in the hands of a  soldier, we've seen it being made. Before any of hundreds of contractors  delivers anything to the DoD, we've been involved. We are auditors, but not in  the traditional sense. We audit DoD contracts of all sizes – up to billions of  dollars – to make sure our military and the taxpayer get what they are paying  for.
