             <h4>
              <strong>
               Florida Romance Writers
              </strong>
              <img alt="" class="alignright size-full wp-image-18" height="131" src="http://frwriters.org/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/logo32.png" title="logo3" width="142"/>
             </h4>
             <p>
              Florida Romance Writers, Inc. (FRW) is the South Florida chapter of Romance Writers of America®, a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting excellence in romance writing and to helping writers establish long-term careers. FRW encompasses Dade, Broward and Palm Beach Counties in southeast Florida. Our meetings take place in Fort Lauderdale.
             </p>
             <p>
              Florida Romance Writers offers a wealth of networking and educational opportunities to help writers of all levels improve their craft and become better informed about the publishing business. Members have won contests and awards, celebrated first book sales, and achieved the New York Times Bestsellers list.
             </p>
             <p>
              If you’re interested in joining FRW, visit our
              <a href="http://www.frwriters.org/membership/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Membership">
               <strong>
                Membership Page
               </strong>
              </a>
              for more details.
             </p>
             <p>
              Check out our
              <strong>
               <a href="http://www.frwriters.org/events/monthly-meetings/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Monthly Meetings">
                Monthly Meetings
               </a>
              </strong>
              for exciting new programs. Non-members are welcome to attend two meetings as a guest.
             </p>
