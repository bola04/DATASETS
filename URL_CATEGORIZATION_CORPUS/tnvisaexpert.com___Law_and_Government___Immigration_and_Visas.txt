     <h2>
      What Is the TN Visa?
     </h2>
     <p>
      The TN Visa (or TN Status) is a temporary working permit allowing Canadians, Americans and Mexicans to work in the United States, Canada and/or Mexico. It is a visa/permit that can be obtained at the border upon entering the respective country. This is based on NAFTA, the North American Free Trade Agreement.
     </p>
     <h2>
      How Much is a TN Visa?
     </h2>
     <p>
      The cost to get a TN Visa varies depending on whether you are applying at the border or mailing in your application. The cost of applying at the border is $50 and $6 for the I-94 fee. Completing the application by mail costs $325 for the I-129 Form.
     </p>
     <h2>
      How Long is the TN Visa Valid For?
     </h2>
     <p>
      The TN Visa or TN Status can be obtained for as short as 1 day and for as long as 3 years. The best part is it can be renewed as many times as you want. You just have to ensure you have a valid application, that you qualify under NAFTA and your job continues to qualify.
     </p>
     <h2>
      Will My Application be Denied?
     </h2>
     <p>
      <b>
       Applications are denied because you have not clearly shown how you and the job meet the NAFTA requirements.
      </b>
      In most NAFTA Occupations, the applicant will need to address the educational requirement and/or work experience.
     </p>
     <p>
      Another important aspect of avoiding being denied is
      <b>
       showing how the job meets the NAFTA requirements
      </b>
      . This included the job duties, eductional requirements, various other details as specified by NAFTA. If done correctly, it is also the reason why the job title is not as critical as many people think.
     </p>
     <p>
      The next important factor is to ensure
      <b>
       you have the correct supporting documents
      </b>
      . Originals and copies should be gathered to be presented at the time of applying for the visa.
     </p>
     <p>
      Finally,
      <b>
       being prepared for the border interview
      </b>
      . Many people think this is a matter of presenting the application documents, but an Immigration Officer can take this opportunity to ask questions to verify the validity of the application.
      <b>
       If you do not answer the questions correctly, they can deny your request
      </b>
      for the visa, so be prepared.
     </p>
