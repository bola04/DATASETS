        <p align="left" style="margin-left: 0pt; margin-right: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;">
         <font color="#000080" face="Verdana" size="1">
          Welcome
        to the premier Islamic philosophy resource on the Web. We are
        dedicated to the study of the philosophical output of the Muslim world.
         </font>
        </p>
        <p align="left" style="margin-left: 0pt; margin-right: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;">
         <font color="#000080" face="Verdana" size="1">
          Islamic philosophy is also sometimes referred to as Arabic philosophy or 
		Muslim philosophy.
         </font>
        </p>
        <p align="left" style="margin-left: 0pt; margin-right: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;">
         <font color="#000080" face="Verdana" size="1">
          This
        site contains hundreds of full-length books and articles on Islamic philosophy,
        ranging from the classical texts in the canon of Islamic philosophy to
        modern works of Muslim philosophy.
         </font>
        </p>
        <p align="left" style="margin-left: 0pt; margin-right: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;">
