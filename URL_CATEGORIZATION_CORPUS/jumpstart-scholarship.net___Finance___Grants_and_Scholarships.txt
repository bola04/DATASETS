         <p>
          <strong>
           <span style="font-size: 150%;">
            Welcome to the College JumpStart Scholarship Program
           </span>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          The College JumpStart Scholarship is an annual, merit-based competition -- financial need is not considered -- that is open to 10th-12th graders, college students and non-traditional students. The main requirement is that you are committed to going to school and can express your goals for getting a higher education. The first place prize is a
          <strong>
           $1,000 scholarship
          </strong>
          to help cover educational expenses. This award can be used at any college or university in the United States.
         </p>
         <p>
          The deadline for the competition is October 17, 2018. When you are ready to apply, please
          <strong>
           <a href="application-us">
            SUBMIT YOUR APPLICATION TODAY
           </a>
          </strong>
          .
         </p>

Unfortunately, we don't have an easy way to edit already submitted applications. However, you can always submit a new application at any time. All applications are judged individually and anonymously. Please don't abuse this and submit dozens of applications.               We have opened our spring scholarship! The deadline is April 15, 2018. However, we encourage all applicants to apply as early as possible. There is no advantage to applying late. As you can imagine many applicants wait until the last minute and we get a flood of applications near the deadline which means our judges have less time to review those submissions. So get a jumpstart and apply today! Good luck!
