     <h1>
      <img align="right" alt="Barefoot Boy" height="129" src="images/photos/Barefoot_Boy.jpg" width="110"/>
      A monument to Haverhill’s great poet and abolitionist
     </h1>
     <p>
      <a href="pdf/Whittier_Brochure-WEB.pdf" target="_blank">
       <img align="left" alt="" height="248" src="images/brochure_preview.jpg" width="117"/>
      </a>
      <a href="pdf/Whittier_Brochure-WEB.pdf" target="_blank">
       (Download Brochure)
      </a>
      The John Greenleaf Whittier Birthplace is substantially the same as when the poet lived there. Best known for his poetry—in particular his poem, Snow-Bound—Whittier was also a prime mover in the abolitionist movement.
     </p>
