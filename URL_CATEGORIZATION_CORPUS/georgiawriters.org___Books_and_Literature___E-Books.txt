            <h4>
             Welcome to the Georgia Writers Association!
            </h4>
            <p>
             We are here to help you negotiate the writing life—Georgia style! Our readers and writers are important to us, and we want to be the go-to resource for Georgia’s premier literary community. And that is why we’re deeply involved in helping all writers build community, become better at the craft, and connect with fellow writers and professionals in the field.
            </p>
            <p>
             Whether you are a novice writer looking to improve and get published or a well-established professional writer with a long list of accolades, we are here to support, educate, promote, and offer camaraderie. We bring you
             <em>
              —
             </em>
            </p>
            <ul>
             <li>
              Member-only benefits
             </li>
             <li>
              Workshops and events:
              <a href="http://visitor.r20.constantcontact.com/d.jsp?llr=i6byw7bab&amp;p=oi&amp;m=1101548750760&amp;sit=6cwrfidcb&amp;f=535712e9-eed4-4532-aece-354a5d5cc7d2" rel="noopener" target="_blank" title="Join Our Mailing List">
               Click this link to join our mailing list
              </a>
              .
             </li>
             <li>
              <a href="http://www.georgiawriters.org/online-literary-resources/literary-events-grant-georgia/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
               Literary Events Grant of Georgia
              </a>
             </li>
             <li>
              <a href="http://www.authoroftheyear.org/">
               Georgia Author of the Year Awards
              </a>
             </li>
             <li>
              <a href="http://www.redclayconference.org/">
               Red Clay Writers Conference
              </a>
             </li>
            </ul>
            <p>
             Thank you for stopping by. We appreciate your continued support and participation.
