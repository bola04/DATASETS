             <h2 class="panel__title">
              <span>
               About Learning Disability Week 2018
              </span>
             </h2>
             <p class="call-to-action__desc">
             </p>
             <p>
              Learning Disability Week 2018 is from
              <strong>
               18 to 24 June
              </strong>
              and is all about health - with a big focus on
              <a href="https://www.mencap.org.uk/treatmewell">
               Treat me well
              </a>
              .
             </p>
             <p>
              We want this to be a chance for people with a learning disability and NHS staff to celebrate and share good practice, and to work together to improve care that isn't good enough.
             </p>
             <p>
              In order to do this we've asked people to hold events in their local hospital in England and Northern Ireland throughout the week.
             </p>
             <p>
              Find out more and how you can get involved!
             </p>
             <a class="button-stroked button-stroked--white" href="/get-involved/learning-disability-week-2018">
              Go

             </p>
