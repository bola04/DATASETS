      <p>
       Welcome to Fatty’s Custom Tattooz official website.  Since 1994, Fatty’s has established itself as Washington DC’s leader in custom tattooing, body piercing, high-end jewelry, and fine art.
      </p>
      <p>
       All tattooists at Fatty’s are multi-faceted, mixed media artists who strive for perfection in their various art forms. We specialize in high quality, custom tattooing. You will notice when entering Fatty’s that our walls are not covered with the typical, corny “pick em and stick em” tattoo flash that you see everywhere else. Our walls proudly display fine art paintings by our artists. There is no weak link at Fatty’s Custom Tattooz.
      </p>
      <p>
       Fatty’s only sells high quality, internally threaded jewelry for the best piercing results possible. Most of our competitors are able to offer piercing rates cheaper than ours due to the fact that they are using low-cost, low-quality, externally threaded body jewelry. Our large selection of exotic body jewelry is like no other in DC, and we are always happy to make special orders for your jewelry needs.
      </p>
      <p>
       All of our piercers are experienced, professionally trained, and are constantly striving to learn more and stay up to date with with all the current piercing information and skills.
      </p>
      <p>
       Fatty’s has been featured in many media outlets including The Washington Post, NPR, Newsweek, and Tattoo Magazine to name a few. Fatty himself has won over a dozen awards in national/international competitions making him one of the most highly recognized and decorated tattoo artists in the mid Atlantic region.
      </p>
      <p>
       Make sure to join our blog to stay informed and up to date as well as the chance to win free Fattybucks and Fattygear. You can also join us on Facebook and Twitter.
      </p>
      <p>
       Feel free to contact us by phone, Email, or mail with your comments, compliments, questions, and yes, even complaints. Your feedback is important and we want to hear it! The quickest way to have your questions answered is to either call during operating hours, or to come on down to Fatty’s in person. Please note that it may take a few days to respond to your Email and we can only give accurate tattoo estimates in person.
      </p>
