       <h2>
        About SGD
       </h2>
       <p>
        The
        <em>
         Saccharomyces
        </em>
        Genome Database (SGD) provides comprehensive integrated biological information for the budding yeast
        <em>
         Saccharomyces cerevisiae
        </em>
        along with search and analysis tools to explore these data, enabling the discovery of functional relationships between sequence and gene products in fungi and higher organisms.
       </p>
