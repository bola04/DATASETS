             <p align="justify">
              Laserase is a professional and friendly clinic that offers laser tattoo removal and laser hair removal for both men and women.
             </p>
             <p align="justify">
              We have been established in Plymouth for over 20 years and have clients from many areas of Devon and Cornwall including Saltash, Looe, Liskeard, Callington, Tavistock, Launceston, Newton Abbot, Torquay, Exeter and Okehampton.
             </p>
             <p align="justify">
              We see many people who, having been tattooed, later regret the decision. It may lead to problems obtaining work, progression in a career, low self esteem or even psychological distress.
             </p>
             <p align="justify">
              So if you want a cover up tattoo, clear skin, have a dated design, a wrong name or just a tattoo that no longer suits your tastes, contact our experts to discuss your options.
             </p>
             <p align="justify">
              Q-switched lasers are considered to be the most effective, safe and reliable way to remove unwanted multicolour tattoos.
             </p>
             <p align="justify">
              We now operate both the Q-switched Ruby and Q-switched Nd:YAG lasers. This enables us to offer 3 wavelengths of high powered energy so we can truly recommend the treatment that is best suited to your tattoo and skin type.
             </p>
             <p align="justify">
              Hair Removal can also have a positive impact on self esteem and confidence. Almost any area on the face or body can be treated.
             </p>
             <p align="justify">
              Women most commonly seek hair removal in facial areas, legs, bikini line and under the arms. Men have found laser hair removal beneficial for excessive hair on the chest, shoulders, neck and back.
             </p>
             <p align="justify">
              Lasers are well adapted to treating large surface areas, making the treatment an excellent and more permanent alternative to waxing and other conventional removal methods. At Laserase we use an Alexandrite laser which has shown through numerous clinical studies to work quickly and effectively with very little discomfort to give long lasting effects.
             </p>
