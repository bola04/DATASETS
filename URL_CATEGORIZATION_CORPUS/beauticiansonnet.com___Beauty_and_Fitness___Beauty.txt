               <font color="#CC0000">
                <b>
                 <font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="1">
                  Beauty 
                          lies in what we portray and how we project ourself. 
                          Images of beauty are everywhere.There is no proper definition 
                          of beauty. Beauty is in the eyes of the beholder &amp; 
                          could be anything from misty mountains to lines on grandmother's 
                          face. It's perception.
                 </font>
                </b>
               </font>
              </p>
              <p align="justify">
               <font color="#CC0000">
                <b>
                 <font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="1">
                  In 
                          the developed world the preoccupation with the body 
                          and with Beauty is intensifying and the Beauty Industry, 
                          despite nearly thirty years of feminism, is a multi-billion 
                          dollar a year business.
                 </font>
                </b>
               </font>
              </p>
              <p align="justify">
               <font color="#CC0000">
                <b>
                 <font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="1">
                  It’s 
                          a well known fact that stress and the rigours of daily 
                          life can affect the way you look. For decades women 
                          have worked hard at keeping their exteriors beautiful. 
                          They have religiously exfoliated, cleansed, toned and 
                          moisturised their skin, but according to the latest 
                          beauty gurus, beauty really does come from within.
                 </font>
                </b>
               </font>
              </p>
              <p align="justify">
               <font color="#CC0000">
                <b>
                 <font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="1">
                  For 
                          some time now, the New-Age jargon has taught us that 
                          looking good comes from feeling good about oneself. 
                          If a woman feels healthy and happy, her skin has a better 
                          chance of glowing. Which is why, these days, hands-on 
                          beauty treatments are designed to pamper the person 
                          as well as her skin. As a result, at least 50% of treatment’s 
                          effectiveness is down to the relaxation, not just creams 
                          on the skin.
                 </font>
                </b>
               </font>
