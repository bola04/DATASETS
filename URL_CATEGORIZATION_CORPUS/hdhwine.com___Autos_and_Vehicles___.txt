Sought-after wines from Pomerol are also now available, including Vieux Château Certan, which brought in a score of 96-98 points from the Wine Advocate, and the equally highly-rated Trotanoy and Latour à Pomerol.

       <p>
        <a href="/053018">
         Château Haut-Brion at HDH Retail
        </a>
        <span class="newsController 3">
         [+]
        </span>
       </p>
       <div class="newsExpanded item3">
        Hart Davis Hart Retail is excited to offer a selection of vintages from Château Haut-Brion. With vineyards dating as far back as the early 1500s, the Château has a rich legacy and is considered one of the finest wines in the world. One of only four vineyards deemed worthy of the title of First Growth under the Classification of 1855, the wine continues to live up to that honor today and is defined by its aromatic complexity, elegance, and supple earthiness. Our inventory includes the esteemed 2005, 2009, 2010, 2015, and 2016 vintages. Take advantage of our selection and add these highly sought, pristine bottles to your cellar today.

         see more

Robert Parker wrote that Jean-François Coche-Dury “produces some of the region’s longest-lived, most aromatically complex, and compellingly textured white wines.” These wines are highly sought and have extremely limited quantities, so don’t miss the chance to add to your collection today.
