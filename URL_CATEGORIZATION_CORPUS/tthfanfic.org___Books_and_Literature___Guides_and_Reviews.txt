        <a name="n555">
         Quarantined? Things to know.
        </a>
       </div>
       <div class="paragraphs">
        Just a reminder to those who have stories in Quarantine --
        <br/>
        <br/>
        The notice that you get if your story has been put in quarantine explains
        <b>
         why and how to fix it
        </b>
        .
        <br/>
        <br/>
        (And you SHOULD get a message. If not you need to check your email/spam folder. Of course, if your email address in your profile is not valid, you aren't going to get anything from the site and we can't guess where to send it.)
        <br/>
        <br/>
        The Mods
        <b>
         always
        </b>
        give a reason for quarantining a story. It isn't random.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Breaking
        <a href="https://www.tthfanfic.org/rules.php">
         the Site Rules
        </a>
        - will likely lead to quarantine.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        The top 3 reasons?
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <span class="quote">
         <br/>
         <b>
          Rule 10:
         </b>
         No disclaimer. (
         <i>
          Or in the wrong place. Every story, not exception.
         </i>
         Disclaimers? Aren't optional.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <b>
          Rule 8:
         </b>
         Crossover quota. We're a Buffy crossover site and as such our rules encourage the posting of Buffy crossovers. While there are exceptions, they ARE exceptions to the rule, not the expected norm.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <b>
          Rule 17:
         </b>
         Bad grammar &amp; spelling (so bad it makes a story unreadable), etc.
         <br/>
        </span>
        <!--quote-->
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Runner-Up:
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <b>
         Rule 13:
        </b>
        Summary or title not being a summary or title (or non-summary verbiage in the summary.)
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Note: For actual Official Rule wordage - go to
        <a href="https://www.tthfanfic.org/rules.php">
         the Rules Page
        </a>
        .
        <br/>
        <br/>
        And if you want your story out of quarantine in a reasonable amount of time (hours/days instead of weeks), you
        <b>
         must
        </b>
        notify the Mods that/when you've fixed the problems. We don't sit there refreshing your story page waiting for you to fix story problems. Sorry.
        <br/>
