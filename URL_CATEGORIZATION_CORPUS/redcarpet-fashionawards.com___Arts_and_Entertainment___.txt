           Pom Klementieff In Altuzarra – amfAR GenCure Solstice 2018
          </a>
         </h1>
        </header>
        <section class="entry">
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-441366" height="478" sizes="(max-width: 620px) 100vw, 620px" src="http://www.redcarpet-fashionawards.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Pom-Klementieff-In-Altuzarra-amfAR-GenCure-Solstice-2018.jpg" srcset="http://www.redcarpet-fashionawards.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Pom-Klementieff-In-Altuzarra-amfAR-GenCure-Solstice-2018.jpg 620w, http://www.redcarpet-fashionawards.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Pom-Klementieff-In-Altuzarra-amfAR-GenCure-Solstice-2018-150x116.jpg 150w, http://www.redcarpet-fashionawards.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Pom-Klementieff-In-Altuzarra-amfAR-GenCure-Solstice-2018-300x231.jpg 300w" width="620"/>

         <p>
          Pom Klementieff attended the amfAR GenCure Solstice 2018 on Thursday (June 21) in New York City.
         </p>
         <p>
          The actress wore an Altuzarra blue tie-dyed dress from Barneys XO Capsule Collection styled with a black velvet ‘Kiss’ bag from the Fall 2018 collection.
         </p>
         <p>
          I love the dress for its vibrant mix of colours but Pom, surprisingly, isn’t really selling the look to me.
         </p>
         <p>
          Better grooming was needed here.
         </p>

          I love how the off shoulder silhouette with a fitted top and full skirt plus adorable bow detail at the sleeves is not only charming, but also channels a warm-weather spirit.
