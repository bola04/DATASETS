     <h3>
      Start your career with the right partner: Navitas Professional
     </h3>
     <p>
      Navitas Professional is a professional development and education expert specialising in career focused programs for outstanding employment outcomes.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Navitas Professional has been delivering career readiness solutions to tertiary students, graduates and young professionals since 2008. A leader in the private education industry, we recognise that graduates need a competitive edge to gain employment.
     </p>
     <p>
      Our programs encompass professional workplace training and mentoring, internships, industry networking, business communication, team-building and leadership skills. We deliver training in
      <a href="locations/adelaide/">
       Adelaide
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="locations/brisbane/">
       Brisbane
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/future-students/campuses/darwin/">
       Darwin,
      </a>
      <a href="/future-students/campuses/hobart/">
       Hobart
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/future-students/campuses/melbourne/">
       Melbourne
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/future-students/campuses/perth/">
       Perth
      </a>
      and
      <a href="/future-students/campuses/sydney/">
       Sydney
      </a>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
      Participation in one of our internship placements will help you gain practical industry experience within your chosen profession. Our programs provide professional workplace training and mentoring as well as provide opportunities to network with industry professionals, enhance your communication skills and add value to your CV.
     </p>
     <p>
      Our staff are experienced professionals,
      <a href="mailto:info@navitas-internships.com">
       contact us
      </a>
      to discuss how we can assist you to achieve your professional and personal goals.
