             <h3>
              <strong>
               Welcome...
              </strong>
             </h3>
             <p>
              to the webspace of suspense novelist
              <a href="/mmarshall">
               <strong>
                Michael Marshall
               </strong>
              </a>
              — and his alter ego, horror and sf writer
              <a href="/mms">
               <strong>
                Michael Marshall Smith
               </strong>
              </a>
              . Michael's work includes the
              <em>
               New York Times
              </em>
              ,
              <em>
               Sunday Times
              </em>
              and internationally-bestselling
              <strong>
               Straw Men
              </strong>
              thrillers, modern sf classics like
              <strong>
               Only Forward
              </strong>
              and
              <strong>
               Spares
              </strong>
              , and the recently-televised
              <strong>
               Intruders,
              </strong>
              starring Mira Sorvino, John Simm, and Millie Bobby Brown.
             </p>
             <p>
              Links will take you to information about the novels and short stories, a place to find
              <strong>
               <a href="/novels">
                eBooks
               </a>
              </strong>
              , news on
              <strong>
               <a href="/happening">
                current projects and events
               </a>
              </strong>
              , and a
              <a href="/blog">
               <strong>
                blog
               </strong>
              </a>
              . Icons at the bottom will take you to social sites like
              <a href="https://twitter.com/ememess" target="_blank">
               <strong>
                Twitter
               </strong>
              </a>
              . And why not sign up for the very occasional and totally worthwhile
              <a href="/newsletter">
               <strong>
                newsletter
               </strong>
              </a>
              , too. Pictures throughout the site are taken from Michael's
              <a href="https://instagram.com/ememess" target="_blank">
               <strong>
                Instagram
               </strong>
              </a>
              .
             </p>

               <h3>
                Introducing:
                <strong>
                 MICHAEL RUTGER
                </strong>
               </h3>
               <p>
                As part of a longterm goal to eventually become every author with the first name "Michael", June 19th (US) and August (UK) sees the publication of a novel under the name
                <a href="http://www.michaelrutger.com" target="_blank">
                 Michael Rutger
                </a>
                .
               </p>
               <p>
                Titled THE ANOMALY, it's a suspense thriller and available for order now in the
                <a href="https://www.hachettebookgroup.com/titles/michael-rutger/the-anomaly/9781538761847/" target="_blank">
                 US
                </a>
                and
                <a href="https://www.amazon.co.uk/Anomaly-DARE-gripping-terrifying-thriller/dp/1785763989" target="_blank">
                 UK
                </a>
                .
               </p>
