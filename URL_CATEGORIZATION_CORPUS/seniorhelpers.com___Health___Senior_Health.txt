        <h1>
         Welcome to Senior Helpers, Leading Senior Care Providers!
        </h1>
        <p>
         Senior Helpers is pleased to partner with entertainment news host and pop culture icon Leeza Gibbons and renowned occupational therapist Teepa Snow to join in a revolutionary program called the Senior Gems®. The Dementia Care program helps caregivers and families care for their elderly loved ones battling this devastating disease.
        </p>
        <p>
         Having lost both her grandmother and mother to Alzheimer's, Leeza knows all too well the highs and lows of being a caregiver for those with the disease. Leeza is joined by Teepa Snow, a nationally renowned certified occupational therapist and dementia expert. Teepa has over 30 years of experience as a dementia care and dementia education specialist. Partnering with Leeza and Teepa has enabled us to expand our range of care and become one of the nation’s leading senior care providers.
        </p>
