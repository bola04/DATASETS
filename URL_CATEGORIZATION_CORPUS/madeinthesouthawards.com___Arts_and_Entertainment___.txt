           <span class="font-size: 1.3em!important;">
            A celebration of Southern craftsmanship, the Made in the South Awards is our annual hunt for the best Southern-made products in six categories—
            <a href="https://madeinthesouthawards.com/crafts-category/">
             <strong>

             <strong>
              outdoors
             </strong>
            </a>
            . A panel of
            <em>
             Garden &amp; Gun
            </em>
            editors and guest judges will choose a winner and three runners-up in each category—as well as one overall winner. Those selected will appear in the pages of our December 2018/January 2019 issue.
            <strong>
             The overall winner will claim a $10,000 prize.
            </strong>
           </span>
