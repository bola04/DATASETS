      <h4>
       USA Swimming Introduces New Membership Category
      </h4>
      <hr/>
      <p>
       In September 2018, USA Swimming will be offering a new membership category
		for those who want to try the sport of competitive swimming.  The Flex Membership 
		will be offered at a rate of $20 and allow the athlete to compete in up to two
		meets per year.  Potomac Valley Swimming will be offering this new membership option.
      </p>
      <p>
       To read more about this new opportunity, visit the
       <a href="https://www.usaswimming.org/news-landing-page/2018/05/29/usa-swimming-to-introduce-flex-swim-membership-in-september-2018">
        USA Swimming Website
       </a>
       .
		If you have any questions, contact the PVS Administrative Vice Chair,
       <a href="mailto:tim.husson@gmail.com?subject=Flex Membership">
        Tim Husson
       </a>
       .
      </p>
