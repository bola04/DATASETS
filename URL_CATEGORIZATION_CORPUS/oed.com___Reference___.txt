             <h2 style="margin: 18pt 0cm 4.8pt">
              Celebrate the OED's 90th birthday with us!
             </h2>
             <p>
              This year sees the 90th anniversary of the publication of the completed First Edition of the
              <em>
               Oxford English Dictionary
              </em>
              .
              <a href="https://public.oed.com/oed90th">
               Find out more about our birthday celebrations &gt;
              </a>
             </p>
             <h2 style="margin: 18pt 0cm 4.8pt;">
              Words where you are
             </h2>
             <p>
              Which English words are used where you are? Help us add more regional words to the dictionary.
              <a href="https://public.oed.com/appeals/words-where-you-are/">
               Submit your word &gt;
              </a>
             </p>
             <h2 style="margin: 18pt 0cm 4.8pt">
              June 2018 update
             </h2>
             <ul>
              <li>
               <b>
                What's new:
               </b>
               More than 900 new words, senses, and subentries have been added to the
               <em>
                Oxford English Dictionary
               </em>
               in our latest update, including
               <em>
                binge-watch
               </em>
               ,
               <em>
                impostor syndrome
               </em>
               , and
               <i>
                silent generation
               </i>
               .
               <a href="https://public.oed.com/updates">
                Find out more &gt;
               </a>
              </li>
              <li>
               <strong>
                New article
               </strong>
               : Coinciding with the 90th anniversary of the publication of
               <em>
                The House at Pooh Corner
               </em>
               , several words from Winnie-the-Pooh have been added to the
               <em>
                OED
               </em>
               in this update.
               <a href="https://public.oed.com/blog/winnie-pooh-oxford-english-dictionary/">
                Read more &gt;
               </a>
              </li>
              <li>
               <b>
                Videos:
               </b>
               <a href="https://www.youtube.com/user/OxfordDictionaries">
                Oxford Dictionaries
               </a>
              </li>
             </ul>
             <p>
             </p>
