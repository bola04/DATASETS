         The Ukulele Hall of Fame
Museum was founded in 1996.  The Museum is a
non-profit organization with federal 501 (c) non-profit status.  Our
Mission is to document, preserve and promote the history of the ukulele, its players, and makers, as well as to inspire and engage people
everywhere in the perpetuation of the ukulele and ukulele music through
performances and exhibits.  The Museum is in search of an
appropriate public site to house the collection of books, tapes,
records, CDs, photographs, and uke ephemera.  We welcome your input and acknowledge your support through
