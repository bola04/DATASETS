       <h2 class="block__title">
        How Does It Work?
       </h2>
       <div class="node node-block view-mode-full clearfix" id="node-15526">
        <div class="content">
         <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
          <div class="field-items">
           <div class="field-item even">
            <ol>
             <li>
              Bike tourists and hosts sign up on the site.
             </li>
             <li>
              The interactive map and other search tools let travelers find hosts on their route.
             </li>
             <li>
              Travelers send a private message to potential hosts through the site.
             </li>
             <li>
              Hosts may offer hospitality consisting of a couch, a room, or a place to camp.
             </li>
            </ol>
            <p>
             Read more in the
             <a href="/faq">
              Frequently-Asked Questions
             </a>
             .
            </p>
