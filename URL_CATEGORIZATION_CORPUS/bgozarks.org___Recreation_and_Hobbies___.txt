           <h1>
            <span style="color: #6e7d36;">
             Learn. Play. Grow.
            </span>
           </h1>
           <p>
            The
            <strong>
             Botanical Garden of the Ozarks
            </strong>
            is a unique destination in Northwest Arkansas that draws more than 80,000 visitors a year. The Garden is a collection of twelve themed gardens and includes the region’s only butterfly house. Our mission is to provide educational, entertainment, and recreational opportunities for students, residents, and Garden visitors to increase their appreciation of the native and natural ecosystem of the Ozarks.
           </p>
           <p>
            Our
            <strong>
             education
            </strong>
            programs include workshops, classes, and lectures for adults in horticulture, conservation, and other topics of interest to gardeners of all skill levels and others who admire the beauty and science of the natural world. The majority of our education programs are for children of all ages. We work with area school districts to provide hands-on nature and science learning experiences. Our programs such as
            <a href="https://bgozarks.org/learn/education-classes/little-sprouts/" title="Little Sprouts">
             Little Sprouts
            </a>
            , Earth Day, Butterfly Days, and Firefly Fling offer unique outdoor experiences to thousands of children each year.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="https://youtu.be/HUUG42S4Vck">
             Learn more about the botanical garden’s history and programs
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Admission:
            </strong>
            $7 for adults (ages 13 &amp; over); $4 for children (ages 5-12); free for children 4 and under.
            <br/>
            Always
            <strong>
             FREE
            </strong>
            for
            <a href="https://bgozarks.org/support/become-a-member/">
             BGO Members
            </a>
            !
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
             <strong>
              4703 N. Crossover Road • Fayetteville, AR • 72764
             </strong>
            </span>
           </p>
