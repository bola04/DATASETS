       A loss and a plea for help...
      </span>
      <span class="details">
       [Posted 1/22/2017]
      </span>
     </h3>
     <div class="content">
      <p>
       Madison is part of our family here at TransFur, and one of the life partners of our former Community Relations Director, Snowy Hare. On Sunday, a tragedy struck that touched us all.   Madision and Jess (her other partner) had just returned from a walk,  when she complained of something being wrong, and then collapsed on the floor.
      </p>
      <p>
       She never again regained consciousness, passing on Tuesday, January 17th, from complications.
      </p>
      <p>
       Madision is now trying to pick up the pieces of her life, and part of that is raising money to purchase the car in which they made so many memories traveling the UK.
      </p>
      <p>
       If you feel moved to,
       <a href="https://t.co/kZqT9TboRd">
        please consider helping
       </a>
       . I know times are hard, and I am making no demands of anyone, but I am certain the kindness in this dark time would mean a lot to her.
      </p>
      <p>
       Thank you for reading.
      </p>
      <p>
       -Waggs
      </p>

      <span class="title">
       Under New Management
      </span>
      <span class="details">
       [Posted 1/1/2017]
      </span>
     </h3>
     <div class="content">
      <p>
       Hello! Everyone, I'm Aurali; and I shall be taking over for Waggs and Cayoosh. If you don't know me, That's fine.
      </p>
      <p>
       I've been a lurker here for years, and have been a lover of the transformation community for my entire life It seems.
      </p>
      <p>
       I have plenty of plans for TransFur, community upgrades, more contests and bringing the site up to the current year.
      </p>
      <p>
       My experience has been varied, built both bleatr and the current version of e621, adminning quite a few sites and owner more. I'll also be bringing in a few trusted friends and members of the community to help make the transition as smooth as possible.
      </p>
      <p>
       Contact me via telegram of the same name or here directly. I would love to get to know each and every member of this community and make TransFur the shining pinnacle of the transformation community once again.
      </p>
