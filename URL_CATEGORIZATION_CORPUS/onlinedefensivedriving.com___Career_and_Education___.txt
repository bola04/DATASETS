     <article class="post-10 page type-page status-publish hentry" id="post-10">
      <h1>
       Welcome to Texas Online Defensive Driving!
      </h1>
      <p>
       Are you looking for a quick, easy online defensive driving Texas course? Search no further, you’ve found it! Whatever your reason — getting a Texas traffic ticket dismissed and keeping your Texas driving record clean, or just brushing up on safe driving habits — our Texas online defensive driving school provides the driver safety training you need. Since 1996 we have been serving Texans with our
       <strong>
        Texas Department of Licensing and Registration approved
       </strong>
       100% online course. With our
       <strong>
        defensive driving course
       </strong>
       you’re just a few hours away from:
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        Dismissing a ticket and avoiding having points added to your license
       </li>
       <li>
        Preventing insurance increases
       </li>
       <li>
        Brushing up on your driving skills
       </li>
      </ul>
      <h2>
       Why Choose Our Texas Defensive Driving Course?
      </h2>
      <p>
       You can complete our TDLR-certified course 100% online around your schedule. Our course offers interactive videos and graphics which provide you with the information you need in 6 easy chapters. Each chapter ends with a quiz you can’t fail, since you can take it as many times as you need to get a 70% or higher.
      </p>
      <p>
       Start the
       <a href="/registration.php">
        traffic course
       </a>
       today!
      </p>
      <h2>
       Texas Traffic Tickets
      </h2>
      <p>
       Taking a traffic course will help keep your driving record clean. Texas operates on the Driver Responsibility Program, also called DRP, which requires you to pay a $100 “assessment surcharge” if you collect 6 points during a year. Continual violations can result in a license suspension. Keeping that in mind, note that a moving violation will add 2 points to your Texas Driver’s License. If that violation resulted in an accident, it will add 3 points. Points will also increase your insurance, usually exponentially.
      </p>
      <p>
       Here’s the good news: taking our online Texas defensive driving course will
       <strong>
        keep points off your license
       </strong>
       . In fact, taking this course may lower your car insurance costs instead of increasing it.
      </p>
      <h2>
       Texas Traffic Ticket Dismissal
      </h2>
      <p>
       If you receive a ticket in Texas, you have three options:
      </p>
      <ol>
       <li>
        Pay for your ticket which results in three points on your license
       </li>
       <li>
        Contest the ticket in course which could cost you court fees and lawyer expenses with no guarantee of success
       </li>
       <li>
        Take a traffic school course which
        <strong>
         prevents points from being added to your Texas Driver’s License
        </strong>
       </li>
      </ol>
      <p>
       Only those who have not taken a defensive driving course in the past 12 months to dismiss a ticket are eligible for this option, which is considered a privilege. Those with a commercial driver’s license (CDL) cannot take the course to dismiss a ticket. Certain violations (speeding over 25 mph, passing a school bus, leaving the scene of an accident, no car insurance, and construction zone violations) are also not eligible. However, if you were cited for a minor moving violation, you can take a defensive driving course for ticket dismissal, avoiding the hassle, cost, and stress of the other two methods.
      </p>
      <p>
       On top of knowing points won’t be added to your license, our Texas defensive driving course could also earn you a
       <strong>
        10% discount on your car insurance
