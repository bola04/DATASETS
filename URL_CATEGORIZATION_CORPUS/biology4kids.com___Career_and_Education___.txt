      What is
      <strong>
       biology
      </strong>
      ? Well... Here's our best definition: Biology is the study of
      <strong>
       life
      </strong>
      and the changes that take place with and around all living things. The study of life extends far beyond Earth. Biology covers every planet and object in the Universe.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      In the same way that everything on Earth is made of atoms, everything that is alive on Earth is made of
      <strong>
       cells
      </strong>
      . An entire living thing can be one cell or it can be billions. Most cells on Earth have similar pieces and parts. Let's get started and look inside
      <a class="contentLink" href="files/cell_main.html" title="cell structure">
       cell structure
      </a>
      . Go take a look!
