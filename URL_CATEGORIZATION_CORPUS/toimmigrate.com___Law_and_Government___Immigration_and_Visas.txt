         </p>

          </li>

          </li>

          </li>
          <li>

          </li>
          <li>
           Your reason for wanted to come to Canada.
          </li>

         <h2>
          Was your Application Refused?
         </h2>
         <p>
          There is nothing worse than doing your very best to gather your documents, compile your application, pay your fees to submit it… only to be unsuccessful. If you have been refused on your application, this is not necessarily the end of the road, there are always options available to you.
         </p>
         <h4>
          Assessment
         </h4>
         <p>
          With any refusal, you should receive the reason for your refused application. You will need to take this into consideration and make sure it is properly addressed in any further applications. Common reasons for refusals are:
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           Missing documentation
          </li>
          <li>
           Lack of supporting evidence
          </li>
          <li>
           You did not comply with requests from Citizenship and Immigration Canada
          </li>
         </ul>
         <h4>
          Reapply
         </h4>
         <p>
          If you have been refused on a minor detail or your situation has substantially changed since your refusal, you can reapply or request reconsideration on your matter. Basically, you would be updating your previous application with new information and asking for the officer to review the previous decision. At the same time, you can reapply and start the process over again.
         </p>
         <h4>
          Takeover
         </h4>

Once they retain the Firm, we would review their application for its weaknesses and make sure these are properly address and Citizenship and Immigration Canada has been properly informed of all the current and proper information. Once we have strengthened your case, we will follow up with the Immigration Officer and keep an eye on your application, ensuring that it is processed within a timely fashion.
         </p>
