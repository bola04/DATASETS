             </div>
             <p>
              The basic blackjack strategy is the starting point for more advanced blackjack strategies - such as blackjack card counting. The basic idea behind card counting is that a deck full of 10's are to the advantage of the player - while a deck full of small cards is to the advance of the dealer.
             </p>
             <p>
              <em>
               Why is that?
              </em>
              Because a 10 rich deck increases the odds of the dealer busting - whereas the player can choose to stand. Similar a deck full of small cards makes the odds of the dealer busting smaller. If you can count the cards and figure out if the deck is 	full of 10's or small cards - you have the advantage of adjusting your playing strategy (the size of your bets), whereas the dealer must always hit until they reach soft 17.
