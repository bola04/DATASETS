            <p>
             <img id="myPicture"/>
             I am a philosopher at New York University. Officially I am University Professor of
             <a href="http://as.nyu.edu/content/nyu-as/as/departments/philosophy.html">
              Philosophy
             </a>
             and
             <a href="http://www.cns.nyu.edu/">
              Neural Science
             </a>
             and co-director of the
             <a href="https://wp.nyu.edu/consciousness/">
              Center for Mind, Brain, and Consciousness
             </a>
             .  I’m also Honorary Professor of Philosophy at the
             <a href="http://philrsss.anu.edu.au/">
              Australian National University
             </a>
             and co-director of the
             <a href="http://philpapers.org/">
              PhilPapers Foundation
             </a>
             . I am interested in the philosophy of mind (especially consciousness) and the foundations of cognitive science, as well the philosophy of language, metaphysics and epistemology, and many other areas.
            </p>
