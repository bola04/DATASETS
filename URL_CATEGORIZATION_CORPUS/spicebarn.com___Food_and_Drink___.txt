          <span style="color:#C00000; ">
           Cranberry Orange Cheese Spread
          </span>
         </strong>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         12 ounces dried cranberries
         <br/>
         1 Â¼ cup red wine
         <br/>
         Â½ cup sugar
         <br/>
         5 sage leaves
         <br/>
         1/8 teaspoon cinnamon
         <br/>
         5 fresh thyme sprigs
         <br/>
         Â¼ teaspoon ground allspice
         <br/>
         1 8 ounce cream cheese
         <br/>
         Â½ teaspoon grated orange zest
        </p>
        <br/>
        Combine cranberries, wine, sugar, sage, thyme, cinnamon, and allspice in a saucepan.  Bring to a boil, reduce heat, simmer for 20 minutes until one fourth liquid remains.  Remove from heat, strain, cool.
Bring cream cheese to room temperature.  Combine with orange zest.  Spread cream cheese in 8 inch spring form pan or into a log.  Top with cranberries and chill.  Serve with assorted crackers.
