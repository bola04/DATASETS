     <ul>
      <li>
       <h3>
        December 2017
       </h3>
       <p>
        We have just published a
        <a href="../criticism/bibliography/search.html?annotation=polish+translation*">
         bibliography of over 100 translations of Whitman into Polish
        </a>
        , annotated by Marta Skwara (University of Szczecin).
       </p>
      </li>
      <li>
       <h3>
        September 2017
       </h3>
       <p>
        We have just published
        <a href="../published/periodical/journalism/index.html">
         transcriptions of 39 editorials
        </a>
        likely written by Whitman as the editor of the
        <em>
         New York Aurora
        </em>
        . We have also created a new section of the
        <em>
         Archive
        </em>
        devoted to Whitman's
        <a href="../published/periodical/editing/index.html">
         newspaper editing.
        </a>
       </p>
      </li>
      <li>
       <h3>
        August 2017
       </h3>
       <p>
        With support from the National Historical Publications and Records Commission, we've published 252 letters from the
        <a href="../biography/correspondence/old-age/index.html">
         Old Age
        </a>
        period.
       </p>
      </li>
