      <h2>
       Welcome to NNIN
      </h2>
      <div class="tabs">

      <div class="region region-content">
       <div class="block block-system" id="block-system-main">
        <div class="content">
         <div class="node node-page clearfix" id="node-705">
          <div class="content">
           <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
            <div class="field-items">
             <div class="field-item even">
              <p>
               <span class="highlight-red">
                <strong>
                 Important Notice:
                </strong>
               </span>
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               NNCI Announcement September 16.2015:
               <a href="http://www.nsf.gov/news/news_summ.jsp?cntn_id=136211">
                NSF has announced awards to support the formation of the National Nanotechnology Coordinated Infrastructure
               </a>
               , a network that will build upon the base formed by the former NNIN.   NNCI will consist of 16 primary sites, with a total of 27 institutional partners involved. Eight former NNIN sites are part of the new network. Like NNIN, NNCI will offer open access to advanced nanotechnology resources across a broad range of nanotechnology areas.
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               NNCI was formed as 16 separate awards.  A competition will be held during 1Q2016 to establish the coordinating office of NNCI. A central web site for NNCI will not be available until then. Details of networked programs such as REU will not be available until later.
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               More detailed
               <a href="http://www.nnin.org/news-events/news/nnci-award">
                information on NNCI is available here
               </a>
               , including links to the
               <a href="http://www.nsf.gov/news/news_summ.jsp?cntn_id=136211">
                NSF press release
               </a>
               , individual site press releases, and individual site web sites.
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               The
               <strong>
                web site
               </strong>
               of NNCI is located
               <a href="http://www.nnci.net">
                here
               </a>
               . Please consult it for more information.
              </p>
              <p>
               <strong>
                <span class="highlight-red">
                 Important Notice:
                </span>
               </strong>
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               Funding for NNIN came to end end on Sept 1, 2014 after almost 12 years. Sites will be rapidly winding down their efforts under NNIN.
               <strong>
                This web site will no longer be updated.
               </strong>
               Content will gradually be deleted, become obsolete, or be transferred to the new (pending) NNCI web site. See above for important NSF announcement about the successor nanotechnology network.
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>
