           <p class="title">
            <a href="/blog/poetry-aloud/" title="Read more">
             <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
              Poetry Aloud
             </span>
            </a>
            :
            <span class="date">
             06/16/2018
            </span>
           </p>
           <div class="description">
            <p>
             <span>
              Come share your favorite poems with others the
              <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
               third Thursday of each month at 7:00 pm
              </span>
              . Participants can bring a poem about the designated topic or theme and read it out loud. This informal reading can include traditional, classic, contemporary and/or modern poetry. Even if you don’t have a poem to share, you’re welcome to come and listen!
