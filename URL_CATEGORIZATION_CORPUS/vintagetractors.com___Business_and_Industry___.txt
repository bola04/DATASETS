   <p>
    Rusty Acres Ranch is a working English walnut ranch located
  in the Sacramento Valley of California just north of the town
  of Woodland. In addition to producing walnuts, I collect and
  restore old tractors. Most of my collection are John Deere's
  from the 1930's through the 1950's, but I also have some Oliver
  and Massey-Harris tractors from the same era, as well as one
  lunger engines and a 1924 Fageol.
