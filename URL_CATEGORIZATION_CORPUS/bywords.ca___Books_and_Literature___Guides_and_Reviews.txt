    <p>
     <b>
      Bywords
     </b>
     publishes on a monthly basis poems on the web, a
     <em>
      Calendar of Literary Events
     </em>
     , and the
     <em>
      Bywords Quarterly Journal
     </em>
     . Its aims are to publish emerging and established poets who reside, study or work in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada.
     <b>
      Bywords
     </b>
     will reflect Ottawa's rich multicultural diversity.
    </p>
    <p>
     <b>
      Bywords
     </b>
     was founded in the Fall of 1990 by a group of  editors, staff, students, and alumni of the UniversityÂ of Ottawa. The group   was brought together by Heather Ferguson of Agawa Press, Seymour Mayne of the Department of English, and Gwen Guth, a doctoral graduate in the Department.
    </p>
    <p>
     The
     <b>
      bywords.ca
     </b>
     team is a dedicated group of motivated volunteers who would like to make
     <b>
      Bywords
     </b>
     a strong literary force in Ottawa.  Our goal is to turn
     <b>
      bywords.ca
     </b>
     into the focal point for Ottawa poetry, literary information, and events.
    </p>
