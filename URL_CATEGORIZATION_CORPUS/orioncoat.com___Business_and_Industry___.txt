           <h1>
            Orion Can Transform Your Ordinary Surface into a Functional Surface
           </h1>
           <p>
            Orion Industries is a ISO 9001:2008 registered, AS 9100C certified, DuPont/Chemours Licensed Industrial Applicator of Teflon® coatings, PTFE coatings, Xylan®, Emralon®, XylaMed® and more than 500 proven functional and industrial coatings. We provide an evaluation of your specific application and produce coated prototypes &amp; a quotation quickly. Orion's investment in extensive automation provides our customers with repeatability, coating uniformity, prototyping processed on production machinery, high volume production and on-time delivery. Orion does not sell the functional coatings that we apply.
           </p>
