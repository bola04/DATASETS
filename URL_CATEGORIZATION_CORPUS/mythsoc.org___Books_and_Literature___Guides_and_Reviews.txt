    <div class="intro-segment" id="intro-society">
     <p>
      The Mythopoeic Society is a non-profit organization devoted to the study of mythopoeic 
		literature, particularly the works of members of the informal Oxford literary circle known
		as the “Inklings.”
     </p>
     <div class="intro-more">
      <a href="/about.htm">
       Learn More »
      </a>
     </div>
    </div>
    <div class="intro-segment" id="intro-funding">
     <p>
      The society’s activities — books, periodicals, annual conferences, discussion groups,
		awards, and more — are made possible by membership dues and donations.
     </p>

      <h3>
       <a href="http://www.mythcon.org">
        Mythcon 49
       </a>
      </h3>
      The Mythopoeic Society's 49th Annual Conference is coming up July 20-23, 2018, in Atlanta, Georgia. The conference theme is
      <em>
       On the Shoulders 
of Giants
      </em>
      and our Scholar Guest of Honor is Robin Anne Reid. The first
      <a href="/assets/MC49ProgRep.pdf">
       <strong>
        Progress Report
       </strong>
      </a>
      is now 
available, as are
      <a href="/mythcon/mc49-registration.htm">
       <strong>
        registration and banquet sales
       </strong>
      </a>
      . The
      <a href="/mythcon/mc49-CFP.htm">
       <strong>
        Call 
for Papers
       </strong>
      </a>
      deadline is May 15th.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Please join us as we continue our three year celebration of the
      <strong>
       50th Anniversary
      </strong>
      of the Mythopoeic Society by commemorating  
the initial Call For Papers for the first
      <em>
       Mythlore
      </em>
      back in 1968, first published in 1969.
