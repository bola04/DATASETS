           <h1>
            Wisconsin Girls Summer Camp for Kids and Teens
           </h1>
           <p>
            Located in the northwoods of Wisconsin, Camp Agawak is a premier girls' summer camp focusing on improving the skills and personal development of each Agawak girl who passes through our forested gates. Since 1921, our rich traditions, creative programming, nurturing environment, and fun activities have given thousands of campers the chance to discover themselves, develop character and form meaningful friendships.  Agawak as an overnight girls' summer camp will be an experience that enhances self confidence, leadership and promotes teamwork.
           </p>
           <p>
            We offer over 45 camp activities which campers select based on personal interest. Agawak girls are in great hands - all of our camp instructors have professional experience in their activity area, and have been well-trained to ensure your daughter has a rewarding, fun and safe overnight camping experience at Agawak.
           </p>
