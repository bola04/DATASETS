                  <br/>
                  <br/>
                  You can submit poll ideas here

                  <br/>
                  Previous Poll Results:
                  <br/>
                  Question:
                  <b>
                   Most important quality of a scanlation
                  </b>
                  <br/>
                  Choices:
                  <br/>
                  Volume vs. magazine raws - votes: 98 (2.2%)
                  <br/>
                  Regularity of releases  - votes: 964 (21.3%)
                  <br/>
                  Speed of release between raw and scanlation  - votes: 321 (7.1%)
                  <br/>
                  <b>
                   Accurate translation of the original language  - votes: 1540 (34.1%)
                  </b>
                  <br/>
                  Readability of the English  - votes: 1070 (23.7%)
                  <br/>
                  Redrawing of the images  - votes: 37 (0.8%)
                  <br/>
                  "Clean" images (lack of artifacts)  - votes: 207 (4.6%)
                  <br/>
                  Fonts  - votes: 35 (0.8%)
                  <br/>
                  Color (or grayscale) balance  - votes: 23 (0.5%)
                  <br/>
                  Image resolution  - votes: 227 (5%)
                  <br/>
                  There were 4522 total votes.
                  <br/>
                  The poll ended: May 12th 2018
                  <br/>
                  <br/>
                  Even though accurate translation got the most votes, it's also the least likely quality that the average person would be able to evaluate
