         <h3>
          Apr 15 2018: Coastal Pet Rescue to Celebrate 15th Anniversary on April 28th
         </h3>
         <p style="margin-left:25px;">
          Coastal Pet Rescue will hold its 15th anniversary bash Saturday, April 28th from 10 am to 2 pm at Grayson Stadium. Sponsored by The Hipster Hound and Bentley's Pet Stuff, the event will feature live entertainment by The Mandrakes, pet portraits by Photos by Becky, photos with the Star Wars 501st Georgia Garrison, and games by Savannah Sport and Social. Pets will be able to run the bases with their people, and CPR will have an alumni section to reconnect adopted pets with their rescuers. Attendees can shop from local vendors as well as purchase this season's Bark in the Park tickets for upcoming Savannah Bananas games. Coastal Pet Rescue will also have adoptable pets on site.
