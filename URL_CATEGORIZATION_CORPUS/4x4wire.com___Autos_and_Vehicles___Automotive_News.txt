                     <p>
                      <a href="http://r20.rs6.net/tn.jsp?f=0019qyEb_fXHN4qqVbHrxgmvFz-n0dWEylsPSz7wbPWlcXQPHDimUoPrRtRUNngRDEIbL6YDdXcWfaDIrD3PP36v-eu6lmrjCNHqBNS5KbeoGr3ljjYcq2EvMhxIpMHqSjlSJhTGrrU-41ml9H0vExG74tfnxoCxqUezP0l36PuKevIXsMpa7V_2g==&amp;c=XBKhL-xk6Ll8nGAoSEbe8EtOHfolmlUkXKG4Pke03xnMQGJVasrzJg==&amp;ch=BW_x1oCq2Jzkv1p5fSmSMc_VjUMhWmNvmv-V_YQUNO6ChIoDZcHMOg==" rel="nofollow" style="text-decoration: underline; color: #506f71; font-style: normal; font-weight: normal;">
                       <img alt="Shooters Help Prevent Wildfires - Wildfires have many causes, Don't be one of them" border="0" class="" hspace="0" src="http://files.constantcontact.com/0305d9a9001/cb7c6b19-66af-4348-a998-02e5116cd6ed.png" style="height: auto !important; max-width: 100%; float: right;" vspace="0" width="182"/>
                      </a>
                      NEWTOWN, Conn. — Wildfires have many causes, and the National Shooting Sports Foundation is urging shooters and hunters to not be one of them.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      NSSF’s “Help Prevent Wildfires”
                      <a href="http://r20.rs6.net/tn.jsp?f=0019qyEb_fXHN4qqVbHrxgmvFz-n0dWEylsPSz7wbPWlcXQPHDimUoPrRtRUNngRDEIbL6YDdXcWfaDIrD3PP36v-eu6lmrjCNHqBNS5KbeoGr3ljjYcq2EvMhxIpMHqSjlSJhTGrrU-41ml9H0vExG74tfnxoCxqUezP0l36PuKevIXsMpa7V_2g==&amp;c=XBKhL-xk6Ll8nGAoSEbe8EtOHfolmlUkXKG4Pke03xnMQGJVasrzJg==&amp;ch=BW_x1oCq2Jzkv1p5fSmSMc_VjUMhWmNvmv-V_YQUNO6ChIoDZcHMOg==" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" style="text-decoration: underline; color: #506f71; font-style: normal; font-weight: bold;" target="_blank">
                       infographic
                      </a>
                      contains sobering facts about the scale and costs of wildfires, noting that 90 percent of all wildfires are human-caused — accounting for more than 60,000 per year. Wildfires threaten animal and human life and burn millions of acres land. Fighting wildfires is dangerous work, and the costs of suppressing wildfires is staggering — more than $2 billion in 2017. We often think of the largest, most destructive wildfires taking place in the arid landscapes of the West, but as the infographic points out, the southern and eastern parts of the United States have a large number of wildfires as well.
                     </p>

                      NSSF reminds shooters and all outdoor recreationists to be mindful of their surroundings and actions to avoid being the cause of a wildfire. Precautions include:

                     </h4>
                     <p>
                      This July, Americans will celebrate the 242nd anniversary of the signing of the Declaration of Independence.  Drafted by Thomas Jefferson, the document was adopted by the Second Continental Congress in Philadelphia on July 4, 1776, formally declaring that America’s 13 colonies viewed themselves as free and independent states.  In honor of Independence Day, the American Battlefield Trust — a national nonprofit organization that preserves battlefields of the Revolutionary War, War of 1812 and Civil War — recommends these eight family-friendly Fourth of July events at historic destinations nationwide:

                       Kaibab National Forest, Grand Canyon National Park to implement additional fire restrictions
                      </a>
                     </h2>
                     <div class="eb-post-meta text-muted">
                      <div class="eb-post-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
                       <i class="fa fa-user">

                    <div class="eb-post-body type-standard">
                     <p>
                      <img align="right" data-style="clear" height="110" src="/images/easyblog_images/64/usfs-logo.jpg" width="100"/>
                      Fredonia, Ariz., June 5, 2018 - The U.S. Forest Service and National Park Service will implement additional fire restrictions, also known as Stage II fire restrictions, across the North Kaibab Ranger District, and the South Rim, North Rim and Inner Canyon of Grand Canyon National Park beginning at 8 a.m. this Friday, June 8, 2018. Visitors should be mindful that these new fire restrictions will limit certain activities and will remain in effect until rescinded, which typically occurs after significant and widespread moisture arrives and fire danger levels subside.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      As a reminder, the Williams and Tusayan Ranger Districts are already in Stage II fire restrictions and will also remain in effect until lifted.
                     </p>

                      BLM LAKE HAVASU FIELD OFFICE AND NIC INC. PARTNER TO OFFER ELECTRONIC RECREATION PERMITS AND ANNUAL PASSES
                     </h3>
                     <p>
                      LAKE HAVASU CITY, Ariz. – Recreationists will now be able to easily attain electronic recreation permits and annual passes for Lake Havasu shoreline sites, making access to public lands more convenient.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      The permits and passes will be available through the digital marketplace,
                      <a data-hasqtip="0" href="https://www.yourpassnow.com/parkpass" rel="nofollow nofollow noopener noreferrer" style="box-sizing: border-box; background: transparent; color: #800080;" target="_blank">
                       YourPassNow
                      </a>
                      . This innovation is made possible through a partnership between the Bureau of Land Management and NIC Inc., a digital government services provider.
                     </p>
