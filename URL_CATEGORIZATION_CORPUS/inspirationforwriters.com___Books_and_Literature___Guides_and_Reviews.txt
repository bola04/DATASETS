          <h1 class="entry-title">
           Welcome
          </h1>
         </header>
         <!-- .entry-header -->
         <div class="entry-content">
          <p>
           Welcome to Inspiration for Writers, Inc.  We’re so glad you stopped by. We hope you stay and explore the many pages of our site–and we hope you’ll mark us as a favorite and return often to see what’s new.
          </p>
          <p>
           Our goal is simple: to make you the best writer you can be. To that end, we start you off with a heavy dose of encouragement. Visit
           <a href="http://inspirationforwriters.com/the-writing-life/">
            The Writing Life
           </a>
           for
           <a href="http://inspirationforwriters.com/the-writing-life/the-writing-life-the-writers-prayer-by-sandy-tritt/">
            The Writer’s Prayer
           </a>
           or to read essays and articles on what it means to be a writer. Then, spend some time studying the many
           <a href="http://inspirationforwriters.com/writing-tips/">
            Writing Tips
           </a>
           that cover everything from character traits to controlling point of view to grammar advice–and everything in between. If you want even more tips, read one of our Newsletters, follow our
           <a href="http://www.inspiration4writers.blogspot.com/">
            Blog
           </a>
           , or download our Workbook. Or, invite one of our editors to give a
           <a href="http://inspirationforwriters.com/workshops/">
            Workshop
           </a>
           at a location near you.
          </p>
          <p>
           When you’re ready to get serious about taking your writing to that next level, check out our
           <a href="http://inspirationforwriters.com/editorial-services/">
            Editorial Services
           </a>
           . We offer one of the most comprehensive services available at a very competitive price–and we provide
           <a href="http://inspirationforwriters.com/editorial-services/free-sample-edit/">
            a free, no-obligation sample edit
           </a>
           so you can see our quality for yourself. If you want to know what others have to say about our work, check out our Testimonials–or see how we’ve helped other writers become published authors on our
           <a href="http://inspirationforwriters.com/success-stories/">
            Success Stories
           </a>
           page.
          </p>
          <p>
           Or, if your goal is simply to be published, we offer
           <a href="http://inspirationforwriters.com/ghostwriting/">
            Ghostwriting Services
           </a>
           that turn your idea into a ready-to-publish manuscript–complete with your name as the author.
          </p>
          <p>
           If you still have questions, contact us. We’re always here for you.
          </p>
          <h4 style="padding-left: 90px;">
           <span style="color: #993300;">
            “You were always available–whether I needed an early morning phone chat or a late night e-mail.”
           </span>
          </h4>
          <h4 style="padding-left: 90px;">
           <span style="color: #993300;">
            ~ Bob Fortune, Illinois.
           </span>
          </h4>

          </p>
