        <h3>
         Featured partner
        </h3>
        <h4>
         The Tusi Mathematical Research Group
        </h4>
        <p>
         The Tusi Mathematical Research Group (TMRG) is a non-profit professional scholarly organization based in Mashhad, Iran and authenticated by the Iranian Ministry of Science. Three TMRG journals are hosted on Project Euclid and edited by TMRG president, Dr. M. S. Moslehian of Ferdowsi University of Mashhad:
         <a href="/aot">
          <em>
           Advanced Operator Theory
          </em>
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="/afa">
          <em>
           Annals of Functional Analysis
          </em>
         </a>
         , and
         <a href="/bjma">
          <em>
           Banach Journal of Mathematical Analysis
          </em>
         </a>
         .
         <em>
          AFA
         </em>
         and
         <em>
          BJMA
         </em>
         are published by
         <a href="/publisher/euclid.publisher.dup">
          Duke University Press
         </a>
         on behalf of TMRG.
        </p>
        <p>
         Among its many activities to promote mathematics research, TMRG sponsors the annual Functional Analysis Award and recently honored the 2018 winner, Dr. Maryam Soleimani-Mourchehkhorti from Isfahan University. Dr. Soleimani-Mourchehkhorti co-authored the winning paper published in the
         <em>
          Journal of Functional Analysis
         </em>
         . For further reading in the same field, please see Project Euclid's holdings on
         <a href="/search_result?type=index&amp;q.a.subject=46B20">
          geometry and structure in normed linear spaces
         </a>
         (MSC 46B20).
        </p>
