              New CW MARS logo!
             </a>
            </h2>
            <span class="rdf-meta element-hidden" content="New CW MARS logo!" property="dc:title">
            </span>
            <span class="rdf-meta element-hidden" content="0" datatype="xsd:integer" property="sioc:num_replies">

           </header>
           <div class="content node-admin-article">
            <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
             <div class="field-items">
              <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
               <p>
                <span id="docs-internal-guid-25b10b9c-580f-af68-4e2e-1160bb0b8274" style="font-size:9.5pt;font-family:Arial;color:#222222;background-color:#ffffff;font-weight:400;font-style:normal;font-variant:normal;text-decoration:none;vertical-align:baseline;white-space:pre;white-space:pre-wrap;">
                 CW MARS is pleased to unveil a new and modern logo that better represents the services and resources we provide for member libraries and their patrons throughout central and western Massachusetts.
                </span>
               </p>
               <p align="center">
                <img alt="[CW MARS Logo]" src="http://www.cwmars.org/sites/default/files/public_website_CWMars_Logo-no-tag.png"/>
               </p>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size:9.5pt;font-family:Arial;color:#222222;background-color:#ffffff;font-weight:400;font-style:normal;font-variant:normal;text-decoration:none;vertical-align:baseline;white-space:pre;white-space:pre-wrap;">
                 <span id="docs-internal-guid-25b10b9c-580f-e1ad-a8bd-f526bd37e49e" style="font-size:9.5pt;font-family:Arial;color:#222222;background-color:#ffffff;font-weight:400;font-style:normal;font-variant:normal;text-decoration:none;vertical-align:baseline;white-space:pre;white-space:pre-wrap;">
                  The logo has been updated to clearly indicate the iconic image of a book, while representing the ease of sharing and access to materials with the arrow.  The logo incorporates our new tagline, “Libraries Connecting Communities”,  highlighting how membership benefits libraries and communities in central and western Massachusetts.  These benefits include access to a shared collection of more than 2.5 million titles including a large number of ebooks.  Patrons also are able to use their CW MARS card at libraries across Massachusetts.
                 </span>
                </span>
               </p>

           <header>
            <h2 class="title">
             <a href="/content/text-messaging-online-catalog">
              Text Messaging in the Online Catalog
             </a>
            </h2>
            <span class="rdf-meta element-hidden" content="Text Messaging in the Online Catalog" property="dc:title">
            </span>
            <span class="rdf-meta element-hidden" content="0" datatype="xsd:integer" property="sioc:num_replies">
            </span>
           </header>
           <div class="content node-page">
            <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
             <div class="field-items">
              <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
               <div>
                C/W MARS introduced two new features on Monday, February 3 in the online catalog:
               </div>
               <ul>
                <li>
                 <strong>
                  Text call number to phone
                 </strong>
                 --  This allows patrons or staff from the OPAC to text a call number to a cell phone.  When viewing an individual record, patrons can click on the link labeled “text” which will take them to a form to enter a cell phone and receive a text with call number and location information for the individual copy.
                 <br/>
                </li>
                <li>
                 <strong>
                  Text Notices for Holds Pickup
                 </strong>
                 -- Patrons can now opt to receive a text message when their hold is ready for pickup.  When placing a hold, the patron can choose to enter a cell phone number and when their hold becomes available, they will receive a text message.
                </li>
               </ul>
               <div>
                Please be aware that standard text charges will apply.
               </div>
              </div>
             </div>
            </div>
           </div>
