Magdy worked with a New Mexico-based photo lab, Gamma Tech, that was able to make the high-quality duplicates the museum required, and Magdy and Warda collaborated over email to bring these backup sets into the collection. Magdy worked with a New Mexico-based photo lab, Gamma Tech, that was able to make the high-quality duplicates the museum required, and Magdy and Warda collaborated over email to bring these backup sets into the collection.
