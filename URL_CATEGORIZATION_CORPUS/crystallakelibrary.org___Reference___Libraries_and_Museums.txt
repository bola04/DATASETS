    <h1 style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom: 0;">
     "Reading Takes You Everywhere" Summer Reading Program
     <br/>
     June 11- August 
  5
    </h1>
    <h2 style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom: 0;">
     <img alt="srp" height="437" src="images/srplogoping.jpg" width="400"/>
    </h2>
    <p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom: 0;">
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom: 0;">
     <span class="style14">
      Join the more than 4,500 people who participate in the Crystal Lake Public Library's annual Summer Reading Program. The program is for readers of all ages: children, teens and adults.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      The Summer Reading Program offers participants fun prize incentives, which are donated by local business sponsors. It's a great way to motivate children to keep reading over the summer months, and gives teens and adults extra incentives to read, as well.
     </span>
    </p>
    <p class="style14" style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom: 0;">
    </p>
    <p class="style1" style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom: 0;">
     <span class="style1">
      <span class="style14">
       Register in person at the Library, pick up your first reading log and start your summer reading adventure.
      </span>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p class="style14" style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom: 0;">
    </p>
    <p class="style14" style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom: 0;">
     <strong>
      <span class="style1">
       <span class="style14">
        Pilot Program for 2018:
       </span>
      </span>
     </strong>
    </p>
    <p class="style14" style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom: 0;">
     <span class="style1">
      I
      <span class="style14">
       n addition to CLPL cardholders, the 2018 Summer Reading Program will be open to students (and their families) who attend a school in the city of Crystal Lake.
      </span>
     </span>
    </p>
