            <h2>
             Kim Kardashian: Hair of the Rich and Fabulous
            </h2>
           </div>
           <div class="contentpaneopen">
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             Kim Kardashian is so huge in the media today. The show "Keeping Up With the Kardashians" has gotten a totally positive response from the public. The gorgeous star is more popular among Americans than some of the best actors and actresses in the United States. Those addicting reality shows...
            </p>
            <div style="text-align: center;">
             <img alt="Kim Kardashian Hair" height="200" src="http://www.contactmusic.com/newsimages/kim_kardashian_1158916.jpg" width="200"/>
             <img alt="Kim Kardashian Blonde Hair" height="200" src="http://www.popcrunch.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/04/kim_kardashian_blonde.jpg" width="164"/>
            </div>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             Kim was born on October 21, 1980 which makes her 30 years old now. Her late father, Robert Kardashian was a high-powered attorney in Los Angeles, where Kim was born and raised. Her mother, Kris Jenner remains close to Kim and her siblings.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             Kim Kardashian's career started in 2007 when the family's reality TV show debuted and was a success. "Keeping Up With the Kardashians" is a show that is widely recognized and watched in America. There are several fans of Kim as well as several websites and social networks that support her. She is not only a reality TV star, though. She also has entered into the retail business. She co-owns D-A-S-H, a clothing boutique, with her sisters Kourtney and Khloe. In 2010 she and her sisters also released a clothing line for Bebe, a popular store seen throughout the country. That is quite an accomplishment! Kim also endorses ShoeDazzle, an online shoe company. She has been a busy woman in the retail arena!
            </p>
            <div style="text-align: center;">
             <img alt="Kim Kardashian Short Hair" height="200" src="http://www.popcrunch.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/06/kim-kardashian-short-hair-photo.jpg" width="200"/>
             <img alt="Kim Kardashian Hair Bangs" height="200" src="http://www.fashionstyleme.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/02/Kim-Kardashian-long-straight-cut-with-bangs_hairstyle.jpg" width="150"/>
             <img alt="Kim Kardashian Updo" height="200" src="http://www.usmagazine.com/uploads/assets/photo_galleries/regular_galleries/1063-kim-kardashians-10-best-hairstyles-ever/photos/1303325922_updo-lg.jpg" width="164"/>
            </div>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             Ok, so here's what you've been waiting for: the discussion of her beautiful hair and hairstyles. Her long, jet black hair is shiny and flawless. She wears it all kinds of different ways. The star usually wears her hair down and the style is long and wavy as shown in the first picture of her. Many women are using extensions to get this look because her hair is so long. She hasn't always had long hair or even black hair for that matter. The second picture of Kim shows her with a short brown bob-like haircut. This haircut makes her look completely different, don't you think? Also, Kim went blonde at one point in her career. Her hair stylist probably used three different shades of blonde to get this look. Her hair is long and wavy as usual, but the color change makes a huge difference in her overall appearance. At one point, Kim was sporting bangs. Not a lot of people can pull bangs off, but Kim looks pretty as usual with bangs and long, wavy hair. What about updos that the star has worn at glamourous events? In the last picture, Kim is shown with her hair up in an updo that produces major volume. Her hair is all tucked in and appears to be short although it isn't. This is a very glamourous updo that can also be used for business meetings since the hair looks more sleek than messy.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             If you are interested in any of Kim's haircuts, hairstyles or updos shown above,
             <a href="/find-hair-stylists.html">
              find a hair stylist
             </a>
             who can help you put your mark on what kim has showcased!
            </p>
