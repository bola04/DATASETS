   <h1 style="font-size: 26px">
    Welcome to Caring Medical Regenerative Medicine Clinics!
   </h1>
   <br/>
   <p>
    We have been a leader in the world for Comprehensive Prolotherapy and other Regenerative Medicine therapies since 1993. Our treatments are designed to find and correct the underlying causes of chronic pain, osteoarthritis, and sports injuries. Caring Medical is an oasis of hope for patients who are trying to avoid joint surgery, as well as those who have already tried and failed multiple procedures, surgeries, and other therapies.
    <a href="https://www.caringmedical.com/chronic-pain/" style="color: #22a6c9; text-decoration: underline">
     See what make our clinics unique and why people travel from all over the world to specifically see our providers.
    </a>
    Learn more about our treatments, technology, and how we achieve outstanding patient results because of our unique,
    <em>
     Comprehensive
    </em>
