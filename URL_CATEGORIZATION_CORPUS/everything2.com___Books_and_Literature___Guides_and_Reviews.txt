        <a class="title" href="/title/You%2527re+here+to+save+the+world.+Unfortunately%252C+you+live+in+a+virtual+world%252C+detached+from+reality.?author_id=1252419#gate">
         You're here to save the world. Unfortunately, you live in a virtual world, detached from reality.
        </a>
        <span class="type">
         (
         <a href="/user/gate/writeups/You%2527re+here+to+save+the+world.+Unfortunately%252C+you+live+in+a+virtual+world%252C+detached+from+reality.">
          place
         </a>
         )
        </span>
        <cite>
         by
         <a class="author" href="/user/gate">
          gate
         </a>
        </cite>
       </div>
       <div class="content">
        <p>
         <a class="populated" href="/title/I+didn%2527t+go+there" title="I didn't go there">
          I didn't go there
         </a>
         often. Maybe
         <a class="populated" href="/title/once+a+month" title="once a month">
          once a month
         </a>
         or so, by previous agreement.
        </p>
        <p>
         The entrance bridge was long. And the wind made it chilly, throwing my hair and clothes around me. Today the sky was bright blue, unlike its
         <a class="populated" href="/title/usual+stormy+self" title="usual stormy self">
          usual stormy self
         </a>
         . The sun reflected off the polished marble walls of the citadel and made it hard to see just how high the architecture stretched.
        </p>
        <p>
         A few scattered and lonely visitors were already in the high-ceilinged lobby, the size of which made them seem even more isolated.
         <a class="populated" href="/title/It+was+never+very+crowded" title="It was never very crowded">
          It was never very crowded
         </a>
         here, which made each individual footstep echo all the more loudly.
        </p>
        <p>
         In some ways, this was a bit of
         <a class="populated" href="/title/a+small+vacation" title="a small vacation">
          a small vacation
         </a>
         for me. A chance to take
         <a class="populated" href="/title/a+break+from+my+usual+habits" title="a break from my usual habits">
          a break from my usual habits
         </a>
         , and meet with
         <a class="populated" href="/title/people+I+didn%2527t+usually+get+to+see" title="people I didn't usually get to see">
          people I didn't usually get to see
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         A large staircase led upstairs and I wandered into another entrance hall. I waved briefly to the now familiar receptionist. The flames flowing around her calmed a bit as she nodded back, before consuming her again as she returned to…
        </p>
       </div>

         more
        </a>
        )
       </div>

       <div class="contentinfo contentheader">
        <a class="title" href="/title/There+are+things+that+happen+and+there+is+no+reason+for+it?author_id=1946526#moeyz">
         There are things that happen and there is no reason for it
        </a>
        <span class="type">
         (
         <a href="/user/moeyz/writeups/There+are+things+that+happen+and+there+is+no+reason+for+it">
          personal
         </a>
         )
        </span>
        <cite>
         by
         <a class="author" href="/user/moeyz">
          moeyz
         </a>
        </cite>
       </div>
       <div class="content">
        <p>
         Friday morning I finally threw out an angel with the kitchen trash, the head having been knocked off years ago by my dearly departed husband during his morning rush to get to work on time. He secretly glued the angel's head back on and tied a red ribbon to conceal the crack
         <i>
          then never told me
         </i>
         . He had broken other angels and knew how much this bothered me for whatever reason. The
         <a class="populated" href="/title/You+are+like+red+strings+tied+on+my+fingers+tonight" title="You are like red strings tied on my fingers tonight">
          red satin
         </a>
         sagged and faded, then perhaps months after last…
        </p>

       <div class="contentinfo contentheader">
        <a class="title" href="/title/Mr.+Tickles+saves+the+world?author_id=2018863#thalio">
         Mr. Tickles saves the world
        </a>
        <span class="type">

       <div class="content">
        <p>
         My name is Ben Hansen (no, not the actor).
I used to write for a
         <a class="populated" href="/title/zine" title="zine">
          zine
         </a>
         called Bust Out that covered millennial culture with
a focus on social media trends. About a year ago now, out of the blue, I received
an invitation to do a live, on camera interview with Mr. Tickles.
         <i>
          The
         </i>
         Mr. Tickles. The
         <a class="populated" href="/title/calico" title="calico">
          calico
         </a>
         cat whose
fame, even by standards of internet giants like Grumpy Cat and Boo the Dog, is
truly mind-blowing. This is the story of what happened that day and a transcript
of the interview…
