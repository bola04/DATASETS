          <div class="tex2jax_process">
           <h1>
            The IMU is an international non-governmental and non-profit scientific organization. IMU's objectives are:
           </h1>
           <p>
            To
            <strong>
             promote
            </strong>
            international cooperation in mathematics.
           </p>
           <p>
            To
            <strong>
             support
            </strong>
            and assist the International Congress of Mathematicians and other international scientific meetings or conferences.
           </p>
           <p>
            To
            <strong>
             encourage
            </strong>
            and support other international mathematical activities considered likely to contribute to the development of mathematical science in any of its aspects, pure, applied, or educational.
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
       </section>
