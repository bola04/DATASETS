       <h2>
        In 1997, Bugaboo Tattoo opened…
       </h2>
       <p>
        and became Hammond, Indiana’s first tattoo studio. They operated on Calumet Avenue for four years until moving to their current and permanent location on Kennedy Avenue, also in Hammond. With dedication, education and commitment to the art, this shop has quickly become recognized as one of the premier studios in the Chicagoland area, producing many award winning tattoos year after year.
        <br/>
        Inside Bugaboo Tattoo, you will find five private tattoo rooms as well as a piercing studio. The atmosphere is relaxed, yet professional with a staff that is always available to help clients with their questions and needs. Because of the graphic nature of some of our work, no one under 16 is permitted in the studio.
       </p>
