       <h3>
        <font color="#0000A0" face="Arial">
         What is a SwedishBrick
        </font>
        <font color="#0000A0" face="Arial" size="1">
         <sup>
          SM
         </sup>
        </font>
        <font color="#0000A0" face="Arial">
         anyway?
        </font>
       </h3>
       <p style="font-family: Comic Sans MS">
        <font color="#0000A0" face="Arial">
         Since most Volvos are
        "boxy but safe" - shaped with aerodynamics
        somewhat similar to that of a brick, and they're
        Swedish...the discussion group focused on Volvos was
        named "SwedishBricks".
        </font>
       </p>
       <p>
        <font color="#0000A0" face="Arial">
         <em>
          The name stuck.
         </em>
        </font>
       </p>
       <h3>
        <font color="#0000A0" face="Arial">
         The SwedishBricks
        Discussion Group
        </font>
       </h3>
       <p>
        <font color="#0000A0" face="Arial">
         Sometimes the group
        is referred to as Volvo.net or S'bricks...No matter what
        it's called, though, we're all Volvo lovers. Many of us
        have owned more than one (or 10) Volvos, some dating back
        to models built in the 1950's (PV 444 anyone?).
        </font>
       </p>
       <p>
        <font color="#0000A0" face="Arial">
         Many of us own 240
        series cars, and the discussions on the mailing list tend
        to focus on maintenance and performance enhancements for
        these cars first built in the early '70s and on through
        the early '90s. The 240 series vehicle is considered
         <em>
          <strong>
           the
          </strong>
         </em>
         classic Volvo...one everyone thinks of when
        they hear "Volvo".
        </font>
       </p>
       <p>
        <font color="#0000A0" face="Arial">
         <img align="right" alt="1972 Volvo 1800e, ch # 39,121" height="116" hspace="0" src="http://www.swedishbricks.net/images/1800E.gif" width="260"/>
        </font>
        <font color="#0000A0" face="Arial">
         Other Volvos have become
        famous too, of course. Robin Williams in
         <em>
          <strong>
           Garp
          </strong>
         </em>
         drove an older Volvo, a PV 544, Roger Moore as the Saint
        drove an 1800, with the beautiful bull horns of the early
        English-built cars, and re-issue of the Saint featured a
        new C-70.
        </font>
       </p>
       <p>
        <font color="#0000A0" face="Arial">
         We acknowledge that
        not all bricks are square :) But we don't have to like
        it!
        </font>
       </p>
       <p>
        <font color="#0000A0" face="Arial">
         Anyway, welcome to
        the site. Browse around, let us know what else you'd like
        to see here...And if you have content or pictures to
        contribute, please let us know. We'd like to post good
        original pictures of Volvos, particularly clean original
        older ones.
        </font>
       </p>
       <p align="center">
        <font color="#0000A0" face="Arial">

            New York

           </a>
          </b>
         </i>
         article about performance Volvos.
        </font>
       </p>
