        Hidden Hurt - Domestic Abuse Information, based in the UK
       </h1>
       <hr class="solid"/>
       <p>
        No matter who you are, you are likely to have some experience of 
            Domestic Abuse at some stage in your life.
       </p>
       <div class="testamonial-box-left">
        For a general overview of domestic 
            violence,
        <a href="http://www.hiddenhurt.co.uk/domestic_violence.html" onclick="return FIX.track(this);">

            relationship, wondering whether it is, or simply confused by mixed 
            emotions regarding your partner; you may have escaped and are trying 
            to make sense of what happened; you may know someone who is being 
            abused and want to understand what they are going through; or you 
            may be worried that you are abusive towards the person you love.
       <div class="full-width-box2">
        Please note that I am not a counsellor, 
            or hold any qualifications apart from having been there and survived 
            myself. The information is neither comprehensive, nor infallible!

        Being Politically Correct
       </h3>
       <p>
        <img alt="Victorian woman being hit" class="img-float-left" height="181" src="http://www.hiddenhurt.co.uk/image-files/xvictorian.jpg.pagespeed.ic.cE0LVfxxrM.jpg" width="225"/>

            most of this site I have referred to the victim of abuse as female, 
            the abuser as male. This is to simplify the grammar (ie avoiding 'she/he', 
            since we don't have a common word for both) and make the information 
            more readable, though I realise that there are many men who are either 
            current victims of domestic abuse or have survived and escaped an 
            abusive relationship.

            try to consider the experience of abuse from both a male and a female 
            perspective.

       <h3>
        Are you in an abusive relationship?
       </h3>
       <div align="right" class="img-float-right">
        <img alt="abusive-man" height="318" src="http://www.hiddenhurt.co.uk/image-files/abusiveman.jpg.pagespeed.ce.LH3ofEbNwN.jpg" width="141"/>
       </div>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Do 
            you:
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        ... sometimes feel afraid of your partner?
        <br/>
        ... feel as though the boundaries keep shifting?
        <br/>
        ... feel as though you can never do the right thing?
        <br/>
        ... often feel guilty for something you have not done?
        <br/>
        ... feel as though you are walking on eggshells?s
        <br/>
        ... often change your social engangements because of your partner?
        <br/>
        ... wish he/she would change and be nicer to you?
        <br/>
        ... spend more time thinking of what your partner wants than what 
            you do?
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Does your partner:
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        ... get jealous easily, even without reason?
        <br/>
        ... nag or belittle you frequently?
        <br/>
        ... always want to know where you have been and what you have been 
            doing?
        <br/>
        ... threaten to hurt himself or the children if you don't comply?
        <br/>
        ... sometimes just seem to totally ignore you for hours or days?
        <br/>
        ... insist on having sex even if you don't really want to?
        <br/>
        ... embarass you in front of friends or family?
        <br/>
        ... belittle your accomplishments or trash your ideas?
       </p>
       <p>
        If you can
        <strong>
         answer yes to most of those
        </strong>
        , then chances 
            are, you are in an abusive relationship. Read more about the
        <a href="http://www.hiddenhurt.co.uk/warning_signs.html" onclick="return FIX.track(this);">
         Warning 
            Signs of an Abusive Personality
        </a>
        and also have a look at the
        <a href="http://www.hiddenhurt.co.uk/abuse_victim_characteristics.html" onclick="return FIX.track(this);">
         Characteristics 
            of domestic abuse victims
        </a>
        .
       </p>
