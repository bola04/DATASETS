            <h2>
             Right Here, Right Now: San Antonio
            </h2>
            <div class="content-wrap">
             <h3>
              On View: April 28 – August 5, 2018
             </h3>
             <p>
             </p>
             <h4>
              Brown Foundation Gallery
              <br/>
              On View April 28–August 5, 2018
             </h4>
             <p>
              The third iteration of CAMH’s
              <em>
               Right Here, Right Now
              </em>
              series focuses on 19 artists and artist collectives living and working in San Antonio, Texas. The artworks brought together in
              <a href="https://camh.org/event/right-right-now-san-antonio/">
               <span style="color: #808080;">
                <em>
                 Right Here, Right Now: San Antonio
                </em>
               </span>
              </a>
              attest to the richness of the city’s creative community. Rather than attempting to provide a generalized, lay-of-the-land exhibition—which is a fraught notion—
              <em>
               Right Here, Right Now: San Antonio
              </em>
              instead looks to the rich dialogues that are possible when extraordinary artworks are brought together in constellation.
             </p>
