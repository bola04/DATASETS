     <div id="slideShowViewPort">
      <div class="slideShowPanel" style="background-image:url(/images/slideShow/groundwaterSampling.jpg);" title="Sampling a borehole for methane concentrations.">
       <h1>
        Unconventional hydrocarbons
       </h1>
       <p>
        A BGS-led research team is setting out to define the combined environmental and health risks over the whole life cycle of
        <a href="/research/highlights/2018/UnconventionalHydrocarbons_UK.html">
         shale gas operations in the UK
        </a>
        . The team will investigate the surface and near-surface processes that affect pollutant behaviour in water and air, and earthquake hazard associated with shale gas development in the UK.
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="slideShowPanel" style="background-image:url(/images/slideShow/AChadwick750_414.jpg);" title="MBE awarded to Andy Chadwick">
       <h1>
        MBE awarded to Andy Chadwick
       </h1>
       <p>
        <a href="/staff/profiles/0130.html">
         Dr Andy Chadwick
        </a>
        has been awarded the MBE in recognition of his services to the science of
        <a href="/science/CO2/home.html">
         carbon capture and sequestration
        </a>
        . The BGS is a world leader in the science of underground carbon dioxide storage and Andy's MBE reflects the high quality and high profile research of Andy and his colleagues.
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="slideShowPanel" style="background-image:url(/images/slideShow/InnovatorOfTheYear2018_MR0223.jpg);" title="Innovator of the Year 2018">
       <h1>
        Innovator of the Year 2018
       </h1>
       <p>
        Profesor Martin Broadley, jointly with Dr Louise Ander of the British Geological Survey (BGS), were presented with a presitgious
        <a href="/news/ANNOUNCEMENT/GeonutritionAward.pdf">
         Innovator of the Year award
        </a>
        for international impact, in recognition of their world-leading research into solving hidden hunger.
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="slideShowPanel" style="background-image:url(/images/slideShow/Bryan-Lovell-2019.jpg);" title="Bryan Lovell meeting 2019">
       <h1>
        Bryan Lovell meeting 2019
       </h1>
       <p>
        On 21–23 January 2019, BGS are co-convening
        <a href="/news/item.cfm?id=8768">
         this meeting
        </a>
        at the Geological Society in London entitled 'Role of geological science in the decarbonisation of power production, heat, transport and industry'.
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="slideShowPanel" style="background-image:url(/images/slideShow/UKGEOSInce-Marshes-038.jpg);" title="UKGEOS Science Community Meeting 2018">
       <h1>
        UKGEOS Science Community Meeting 2018
       </h1>
       <p>
        <a href="https://nerc.ukri.org/funding/available/capital/ukgeos/community-event/" target="_blank" title="UKGEOS Science Community Meeting 2018">
         The meeting
        </a>
        will give information about the UK Geoenergy Observatories sites, facilities and science vision, including practicalities concerning delivery of UKGEOS research. It will also explain how scientists and early career researchers can get involved.
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="slideShowPanel" style="background-image:url(/images/slideshow/landslide-Freetown_Sierra-Leone.jpg);" title="New projects see UK space firms tackle global challenges">
       <h1>
        New projects see UK space firms tackle global challenges
       </h1>
       <p>
        Improved disaster response in Commonwealth states and tropical disease control are among the goals of
        <a href="https://www.gov.uk/government/news/new-projects-see-uk-space-firms-tackle-global-challenges" target="_blank">
         10 new projects
        </a>
        involving British space organisations, Science Minister Sam Gyimah announced on 8 February. A new BGS-led project is: Modelling Exposure Through Earth Observation Routines (METEOR): EO-based Exposure, Nepal and Tanzania Grant: £2.8 million.
       </p>
      </div>
