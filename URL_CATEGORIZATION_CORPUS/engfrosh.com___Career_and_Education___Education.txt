     <h1>
      Congratulations on being accepted to Carleton University!
     </h1>
     <p>
      Welcome to Engfrosh: the Fall Orientation program for new students in Engineering, Architecture, Industrial Design, and Information Technology. Our goal is to help you get adjusted to your new life at Carleton, meet your peers and classmates in the Faculty of Engineering and Design, and have an awesome time in the process!
     </p>
     <br/>
     <h2>
      EngFrosh Registration and Sign-in
     </h2>
     <p>
      Registration is scheduled to open on July 11th. Once you've registered be sure to stop by our registration desk to pick up your kit between 9:00 AM and 6:00 PM on Saturday, September 2nd or Sunday, September 3rd in Fenn Lounge (in Residence Commons) to pick up all of your gear!
     </p>
     <br/>
     <h2>
      Late Sign-In
     </h2>
     <p>
      While EngFrosh will only be set up in Fenn Lounge from 9AM-6PM on Saturday and Sunday, there will be other Carleton University staff to assist you between the hours of 6PM and 9PM in Fenn Lounge. We do strongly recommend that you pick up your kit before Opening ceremonies at 6:30 PM on Sunday night though if at all possible!
     </p>
     <br/>
     <h2>
      Late-Night Shuttle Bus Service (Sunday and Monday)
     </h2>
     <p>
      To accommodate Fall Orientation participants and volunteers who will require a convenient, safe way to get home on these evenings, late-night shuttle buses will be offered at no extra cost to all students.

       Click here for the routes, pick-up locations, times and drop-off spots.
