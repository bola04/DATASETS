         <h1>
          Welcome to Austin Poetry Slam!
         </h1>
         <p>
          #AustinSlam
         </p>
         <p>
          You have found Austin’s premiere showcase of slam poetry! We hold a competitive spoken word poetry show every Tuesday night at the Spider House Ballroom on 29th Street!
         </p>
         <h4>
          Tuesdays:
         </h4>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Location:
          </strong>
          Spider House Ballroom,
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Time:
          </strong>
          Doors/Sign-up at 7:15pm, Draw at 8pm, Slam Starts at 8:15pm
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Price:
          </strong>
          $6 on Tuesday nights for regular slams, and occasionally $8 or $10 on special nights, such as Quarter Finals, Semi-Finals, IWPS Finals, WOWPS Finals, Slam Bowl or the Nationals Slam Scrimmage.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Slamming:
          </strong>
          Poets wanting to slam on Tuesdays must sign up at the door before 8pm. All audience members and competing poets pay the same $6 cover.
         </p>
         <h4>
         </h4>
         <p>
          Our most active social media channel is Facebook.  Find us here:
          <a href="https://www.facebook.com/austinpoetryslam" title="Austin Slam on Facebook">
           Austin Slam on Facebook
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          Otherwise, click around on the menu above for more info
          <a href="http://austinslam.com/about/" title="About">
           about APS
          </a>
          and
          <a href="http://austinslam.com/events/" title="Events">
           our events
          </a>
          , and please come out and join us this Tuesday or Sunday!!!
         </p>
