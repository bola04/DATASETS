       <h3>
        New York Style Arizona Pizza
       </h3>
       <p>
        No disrespect to you and yours, but when we moved here from Long Island, we just couldn’t sink our teeth into the AZ pizza we ordered. That’s why we founded NYPD Pizza in 1996 – to show Arizona how pizza is supposed to taste!
       </p>
       <p>
        Hot out of our NYPD Pizza family kitchen, enjoy authentic New York flavor in every hand-tossed pie we bake. From the freshest, whole-milk mozzarella to our unrivaled family sauce recipe, our Arizona pizza is true to its East Coast roots. We even filter our water exactly how they do it in Yonkers, NY – giving our dough the distinctly delicious flavor we were raised on.
       </p>
       <p>
        And while our pizza and assorted Italian dishes are outstanding, an amazing dining experience is about much more than the food. That’s why, when you step inside any of our
        <a href="https://www.aznypdpizza.com/nypd-pizza-locations-az" title="Locations">
         12 Valley locations
        </a>
        to share our passion for flavorful AZ pizza, you’re a part of our New York family. Really – we mean it! Come in when
        <a href="https://www.aznypdpizza.com/kids-at-nypd-pizza" title="Kids at NYPD Pizza">
         kids eat free
        </a>
        and bring your gluten-free or vegan cousins. Everyone will find something delicious on our menu! And don’t believe everything you hear about New Yorkers – we’re actually incredibly social. So be sure to keep in touch on Facebook and Twitter. Otherwise, Nona might worry.
       </p>
