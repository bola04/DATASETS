     This website is dedicated to providing you with barbecue information including BBQ recipes and techniques, grilling recipes and techniques, smoker recipes, information about bbq recipes, barbecue mops, and barbeque rubs; basically anything concerning BBQ. If you do not find the what you need here, please email me and I will answer any question you have.  We look forward to providing you with tons of information about smoker recipes and techniques for cooking barbeque chicken, bbq sausage, instructions teaching you how to perfectly smoke a brisket and other food like pork shoulders, and ribs. There are many grilling recipes too, so please feel free to browse around.  Also, please join our message board. You can learn quite a bit from the great BBQ novices on the board.  We have had lots of traffic and questions related to designing and building BBQ smokers and trailers. I have posted information about building barbeque pits and rigs which you can find in the menu at the top of the page.
