       <h2>
        Answers to Common Questions
       </h2>
       <div class="toggle">
        How Do I Proceed?
        <div>
         <p>
          Easy. Just follow the three steps above. The free materials in the first step give you all the basics and, for many, this will be all you need. Professionals and motivated students may wish to explore step two - our Optimal EFT Course - which is an advanced, on-line training. From there you can investigate step three, our hands-on Complete EFT Certification which is customized to your needs.
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="toggle">
        What is EFT (or "Tapping," as it is commonly known)?
        <div>
         <p>
          EFT stands for (Emotional Freedom Techniques) and I first introduced it to the public in 1995. It integrates the Chinese meridian system into the therapy process by tapping on meridian points with your fingertips. Properly done, it reduces the conventional therapy process from weeks, months or years to a fraction of that time (often to minutes or hours). Numerous internet versions of it are in use by millions worldwide. However, the official, in-depth process is given free in our
          <a href="/english/eft-tutorial-en/eft-tapping-tutorial-en.html" hreflang="en">
           Gold Standard Official EFT Tapping Tutorial
          </a>
          on our website.
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="toggle">
        What are Optimal EFT and The Unseen Therapist?
        <div>
         <p>
          Optimal EFT is our newest EFT advancement and introduces
          <a href="https://www.unseentherapist.com" rel="alternate noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">
           The Unseen Therapist
          </a>
          , a spiritual presence of unlimited healing power Who provides healing that transcends all man-made methods. Before 2017, our EFT Tapping procedure (see the question above) was the mainstay of the EFT process. Starting in 2017, however, the tapping has been replaced to accommodate the new advancement. See my free e-book,
          <a href="https://www.unseentherapist.com" rel="alternate noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Where the impossible becomes routine">
           The Unseen Therapist
          </a>
          , for details.
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="toggle">
        Is the EFT Tapping process obsolete?
        <div>
         <p>
          In time… yes! it will be obsolete. It is becoming so even now. This is because
          <a href="https://www.unseentherapist.com" rel="alternate noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">
           The Unseen Therapist
          </a>
          has vastly superior powers when compared to any man-made healing method (including EFT Tapping). However, for best results with The Unseen Therapist, a new form of thinking is required... an ultimate psychology... to fully utilize Her powers and that, of course, involves time and experience. Many people will still embrace EFT Tapping for a while (that's why I still teach it) but serious health professionals and highly motivated students will likely embrace these new skills.
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="toggle">
        Can anyone utilize The Unseen Therapist?
        <div>
         <p>
          Yes. She does not restrict Her benefits to anyone. She doesn't care about your educational background or your IQ, popularity, status in life, religion (if any) or anything else. You must, however, learn to communicate with Her and that pursuit is the focus of my
          <a href="https://www.unseentherapist.com" rel="alternate noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">
           Unseen Therapist book
          </a>
          , Optimal EFT and other instructional materials.
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="toggle">
        Why do you give away so much for free?
        <div>
         <p>
          Because I want these precious processes in the hands of as many people as possible. Everyone benefits when this happens. 90% of the public (my estimate) should be perfectly satisfied with our free foundational materials and need not go further. For professionals and motivated students (the other 10%), we provide top-quality advanced training and certification designed to place you into that upper echelon where the world's top healing practitioners reside. We refer to this level as, "A Sky With No Ceiling."
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="toggle">
        Why are there so many EFT Tapping spin-offs on the internet?
        <div>
         <p>
          When I introduced EFT to the public in 1995, I had no idea it would be in the hands of millions. Accordingly, I offered it with an "open hand" wherein there were no royalties or legal fences of any kind. That generosity, however, did not include a license to change it.
         </p>
         <p>
          As human nature unfolded, however, enthusiastic people filtered EFT through their own belief systems and, as a result, changed it and recreated EFT in their own images. Accordingly, you will find hundreds of EFT websites on the internet and over one thousand EFT books on Amazon.com. No two are the same. For more info see
          <a href="/eft-tutorial-en/before-begin-en/eft-tapping-spinoffs-en.html">
           About Those EFT Tapping Spin-Offs
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          To address this problem, I have trademarked Official EFT™, Gold standard EFT™, Optimal EFT™ and The Unseen Therapist™ and appointed "Gary Craig Official EFT Centers." I granted the Gary Craig Official EFT Centers the exclusive rights to offer Official EFT information and trainings in English and various other languages worldwide.
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="toggle">
        Are EFT Certifications by the spin-offs valid?
        <div>
         <p>
          You must answer this one for yourself. While I honor the enthusiasm with which the spin-offs offer their teachings, their various EFT certification and training courses vary dramatically and do not adequately represent Official EFT. Further, none of them have been given authorization to offer our most important advancements, Optimal EFT and
          <a href="https://www.unseentherapist.com" rel="alternate noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">
           The Unseen Therapist
          </a>
          . Nonetheless, you may find one or more of these offerings to your liking. If so, I encourage you to explore them, take their trainings, buy their products, etc. This may fulfill your needs. If not, return here for more authentic training.
         </p>
         <p>
          To help you understand whether or not you are dealing with the real, official, EFT, I have trademarked Official EFT™, Gold standard EFT™, Optimal EFT™ and The Unseen Therapist™. To help you choose a Center which is authorized by me to offer certification in Official EFT, I have appointed Gary Craig Official EFT Centers. These Centers are endorsed by me and are the only organizations authorized to offer Official EFT information and trainings in English and various other languages worldwide.
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="toggle">
        Can the spin-offs join in this new movement?
        <div>
         <p>
          Sure. Interested EFT promoters need only contact me to discuss the many rewards involved. This will allow them to blend harmoniously with the movement instead of being seen as dividing it.
         </p>
