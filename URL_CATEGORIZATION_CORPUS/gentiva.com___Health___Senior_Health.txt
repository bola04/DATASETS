         <h2>
          Bringing Compassionate Care Home
         </h2>
         <p>
          Gentiva Health Services is now Kindred at Home and Kindred Hospice.  We are better together creating the nation’s leader in home care services. Our nurses, therapists and other healthcare professionals are dedicated to improving quality of life through extraordinary service, compassionate care and clinical excellence in home health, hospice and community care services.
         </p>
         <p>
          Our full range of clinical services includes:
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           Skilled nursing
          </li>
          <li>
           Physical, occupational and speech-language therapies
          </li>
          <li>
           Cardiac and pulmonary care
          </li>
          <li>
           Neurorehabilitation
          </li>
          <li>
           Wound care
          </li>
          <li>
           Disease and pain management
          </li>
          <li>
           Medication management and education
          </li>
          <li>
           Patient education to promote self-management
          </li>
          <li>
           Treatment for balance problems that can lead to fall risks
          </li>
          <li>
           Hospice services, including help with physical, emotional and spiritual needs
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          More information on the geographic coverage area of our services is
          <a href="/locations" title="Find a Location">
           available here
          </a>
          .
