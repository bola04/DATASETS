                <td class="messagecellbody" colspan="4">
                 Good looking cigar,a few minor vein lines,solid cannon,nice sheen to the wrapper which has a nice earthy tobacco and molasses aroma pre light.cuts well and has a open draw.
                 <br/>
                 Takes a flame well and gives nice spice in the retro with a sweet hint in the finish.Noticing a hint of cream and some tobacco in the aromatics and a nice mocha.Nice smooth start with good complexity.
                 <br/>
                 The second third has a strong leather presence with the mocha still around but the cream has disappeared as has the sweet note in the finish.Still well balanced and very enjoyable.Ash held well past an inch and is solid and mostly grey in color with some black flecks.
                 <br/>
                 The last third has some of the cream and sweet hints back and wood is working off the leather,still nice spice in the retro and earthy tobacco making up the background.
                 <br/>
                 Really nice cigar,good balance and decent complexity with good smoke production. Definitely a smoke worth trying.
                </td>
