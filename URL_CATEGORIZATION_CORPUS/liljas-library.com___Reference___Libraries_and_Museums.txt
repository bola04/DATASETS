       <h3>
        <a href="article.php?id=6080">
         Mr Mercedes At Comic Con
        </a>
       </h3>
       <span class="datesection">
        Posted: June 22, 2018, 03:16:55
       </span>
       <p>
        <iframe allow="autoplay; encrypted-media" allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/PMy9Fpj3btk" width="560">
        </iframe>
        <br/>
        <i>
         <br/>
         EL SEGUNDO, Calif., June 21, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- The AT&amp;T* AUDIENCE Network series "Mr. Mercedes," produced by Sonar Entertainment, is returning to San Diego Comic-Con July 19-21 with the cast and creators of season 2 for the "Mr. Mercedes Immersive Experience" and show panel.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         The "Mr. Mercedes Immersive Experience" will give fans a glimpse into the past and a peek into the future of the AT&amp;T AUDIENCE Network original series based on the trilogy by Stephen King. Fans can be the first to play the virtual reality (VR) escape room game, "Mr. Mercedes: Lair Escape," on the HTC Vive Pro  the next generation of VR headsets. In the "Mr. Mercedes: Finders Keepers" augmented reality (AR) adventure game, they'll collect clues to unlock never-before-seen content and special prizes.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         The "Mr. Mercedes Immersive Experience" will include:
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <b>
          Augmented Reality Adventure Game
         </b>
          Download the app to embark on an AR-driven investigation helping Detective Bill Hodges crack the case and claim a reward if you succeed.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <b>
          Virtual Reality Escape Room Game
         </b>
          Be fully immersed in a virtual escape room inside Brady's lair. Escape before time runs out or you're trapped forever!
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <b>
          Recreating the Physical World of "Mr. Mercedes"
         </b>
          The show's set designers are recreating Finders Keepers, Mercy General Hospital and Brady's lair using actual props.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <b>
          Bringing Brady to Life Through Projection Mapping
         </b>
          3-D light projection will creepily bring Brady to life in his hospital bed. Is he in a coma or did you just see him move?
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <b>
          Lenticular Photo Experience
         </b>
          This photo op will transform the participant's face into Brady's along with a socially shareable GIF.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <b>
          Private Screening Room
         </b>
          Media, VIP and lucky fans will get the chance to watch a sneak peek of season 2.
         <br/>
        </i>
        <br/>
        <a href="https://mrmercedesexperiencefastpass.splashthat.com/" target="_blank">
         Sign up here
        </a>
       </p>

       <h3>
        <a href="article.php?id=6081">
         More On Season 2 Of Mr Mercedes
        </a>
       </h3>
       <span class="datesection">
        Posted: June 21, 2018, 23:12:57
        <br/>
        Section:
        <b>
         TV
        </b>
        »
        <a href="section.php?id=116">
         Mr Mercedes
        </a>
       </span>
       <p>
        Here is a plot summary for season 2 of
        <b>
         Mr Mercedes
        </b>
        .
        <br/>
        <i>
         <br/>
         The series picks up a year after Brady Hartsfield's thwarted attempt to perpetrate a second mass murder in the community of Bridgton, Ohio. Since the incident, Hartsfield has been hospitalized in a vegetative state. Retired Detective Bill Hodges has done his best to move on from his Brady obsession, teaming up with Holly Gibney to open Finders Keepers, a private investigative agency. But when unexplainable occurrences begin to affect hospital staff members attending to Brady, Hodges is haunted by the feeling that Brady is somehow responsible.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         The original cast, which includes Brendan Gleeson ("Harry Potter") as Detective Bill Hodges, Harry Treadaway ("Penny Dreadful") as Brady Hartsfield, Jharrel Jerome ("Moonlight") as Jerome Robinson, Scott Lawrence ("Avatar") as Detective Peter Dixon, Breeda Wool ("Unreal") as Lou Linklatter, Justine Lupe ("Francis Ha") as Holly Gibney, Nancy Travis ("How I Met Your Mother") as Donna Hodges, and Holland Taylor ("The Truman Show") as Ida Silver, is joined by Jack Huston ("Boardwalk Empire") as Brady Hartsfield's doctor Dr. Felix Babineau, Tessa Ferrer ("Grey's Anatomy") as Dr. Babineau's wife Cora Babineau, and Maximiliano Hernandez ("Sicario") as Assistant DA Antonio Montez for season 2.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Season 2 of "Mr. Mercedes" will premiere on AT&amp;T AUDIENCE Network on Wednesday, Aug. 22 at 10 p.m. ET/PT.
         <br/>
        </i>
        <br/>
       </p>

         Castle Rock World Premieres At Comic-Con
        </a>
       </h3>
       <span class="datesection">
        Posted: June 20, 2018, 08:24:41
        <br/>
        Section:
        <b>
         TV
        </b>
        »
        <a href="section.php?id=131">
         Castle Rock
        </a>
       </span>
       <p>
        <a href="https://deadline.com/2018/06/stephen-king-j-j-abrams-castle-rock-comic-con-premiere-hulu-sissy-spacek-1202413675/" target="_blank">
         Deadline
        </a>
        reports that
        <b>
         Castle Rock
        </b>
        is heading for Comic-Con.
        <br/>
        <i>
         <br/>
         Castle Rock doesnt premiere on Hulu until July 25, but San Diego Comic-Con attendees will get a lot more than a sneak peek at the psychological-horror drama. The entire opening episode of the J.J. Abrams-produced series based on Stephen Kings stories will be played for fans at the confab next month.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Abrams is working on the next Star Wars movie over in the U.K., so he wont be in Ballroom 20, but a Castle Rock panel with stars Sissy Spacek, Bill Skarsgard, Melanie Lynskey and more will be there. Although it is not 100% yet that Andre Holland will be there, that panel is where the special screening is set to take place for fans too. With all that, King himself will not be in the house in any form or fashion as of right now. Yet, there is a chance that SDCC vet Abrams might show his face via video, but that is still under discussion and a matter of scheduling.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         San Diego Comic-Con runs from July 19-22.
         <br/>
        </i>
        <br/>

         Thanks to Lou Sytsma
        </span>
