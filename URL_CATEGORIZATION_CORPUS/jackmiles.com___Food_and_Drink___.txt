                      <img alt="Jack Miles" border="0" height="600" src="http://www.jackmiles.com/_/rsrc/1472072165384/Home/jack-miles-2016.jpg?height=600&amp;width=400" width="400"/>
                     </font>
                    </div>
                    <p style="line-height:26.6667px">
                     <font size="4">
                      Welcome to the quiet. Most of the time, my wife and I are the only two people here. I read and write. Catherine reads and tends the orchard. The world roars by, but now that I am retired (as of 7/16), we engage it at a certain distance—or we try to, despite the current danger.
                     </font>
                    </p>
                    <p style="line-height:26.6667px">
                     <font size="4">
                      What you will find collected on this website are writings of mine on religion, literature, culture, and politics that have accrued over fifty years, along with an eclectic assortment of reviews and interviews. In the years ahead—time, strength, cash, and patience availing—I intend to write a book on the ancient Greek translation of the Jewish Bible as the greatest translation ever written; a book on the Jewish writers who dared to imagine God himself as a village Jew; and a book of more personal reflections on the religious literatures of the world, to be entitled
                      <i>
                       The Library of the Gods.
                      </i>
                      These are labors of love, undertaken in quiet.
                     </font>
                    </p>
                    <p style="line-height:26.6667px">
                     <font size="4">
                      Yet the world does have a way of walking in on us. These days, when I leave the peace of my retreat, it is most often to speak about one of two pressing global issues:
                     </font>
                    </p>
                    <p style="line-height:26.6667px">
                     <font size="4">
                      First, the dream of inter-religious peace. I will spend the 2018-2019 academic year as
                      <a href="http://www.bc.edu/research/cjl/centerscholarship/CorcoranVisitingChair.html" target="_blank">
                       Corcoran Visiting Professor of Christian-Jewish Relations at Boston College
                      </a>
                      . In November 2018, Knopf will publish my
                      <i>
                       <a href="http://www.jackmiles.com/Home/books/god-in-the-qur-an">
                        God in the Qur’an
                       </a>
                      </i>
                      .
                     </font>
                    </p>
                    <p style="text-align:left;line-height:26.6667px">
                     <span style="font-size:large;background-color:transparent">
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-size:large;background-color:transparent">
                     </span>
                     <font size="4">
                      Second,
                     </font>
                     <span style="background-color:transparent">
                      <font size="4">
                       the crisis of climate change and the promise of religion as just possibly an ally that may be rallied to help meet it--for example, my three-part contribution
                      </font>
                     </span>
                     <span style="background-color:transparent">
                      <font size="4">
                       to "Bending the Curve," a new video course to be offered on all campuses of the University of California.
                      </font>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-size:large;background-color:transparent">
                      [
                     </span>
                     <span style="background-color:transparent">
                      <font size="4">
                       <a href="http://www.jackmiles.com/Home/other-works/on-terrorism-war-and-public-policy/religion-and-climate-change">
                        Here's the shooting script for the three modules
                       </a>
                       .]
                      </font>
                     </span>
                    </p>
                    <p style="line-height:26.6667px">
                     <font size="4">
                      So, yes, it’s quiet here, but within the quiet a lot is happening, and I hope to have more to report as work progresses. Stay as long as you like. No one will disturb you. And come back soon.
                     </font>
                    </p>
