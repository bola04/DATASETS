    <div class="isiTitleDIv">
     <span class="isiHeading">
      IMPORTANT SAFETY INFORMATION
     </span>
    </div>
    <div class="isiCnt">
     <div class="isiRightTxt">
      <span>
       What is ORTHO TRI-CYCLEN
       <span class="regSup">
        ®
       </span>
       Lo?
      </span>
      <p>
       ORTHO TRI-CYCLEN
       <span class="regSup">
        ®
       </span>
       Lo is a birth control pill
       <span class="oral">
        (oral contraceptive)
       </span>
       used by women to prevent pregnancy.
      </p>
     </div>
     <div class="isiLeftTxt">
      <span class="pinkHeading">
       What is the most important information I should know about ORTHO TRI-CYCLEN
       <span class="regSup">
        ®
       </span>
       Lo?
      </span>
      <span>
       Do not use ORTHO TRI-CYCLEN
       <span class="regSup">
        ®
       </span>
       Lo if you smoke cigarettes and are over 35 years old.
      </span>
      <span>
       Smoking increases your risk of serious cardiovascular side effects from hormonal birth  control pills, including death from heart attack, blood clots, or stroke.
      </span>
      <span>
       This risk increases with age and the number of cigarettes you smoke.
      </span>
     </div>
     <span class="pinkHeading">
      Who should not take ORTHO TRI-CYCLEN
      <span class="regSup">
       ®
      </span>
      Lo?
     </span>
     <p class="orthoMargin">
      Do not take ORTHO TRI-CYCLEN
      <span class="regSup">
       ®
      </span>
      Lo if you:
     </p>
     <ul>
      <li>
       smoke and are over 35 years of age
      </li>
      <li>
       had blood clots in your arms, legs, lungs, or eyes
      </li>
      <li>
       had a problem with your blood that makes it clot more than normal
      </li>
      <li>
       have certain heart valve problems or irregular heart beat that increases your risk of having blood clots
      </li>
      <li>
       had a stroke
      </li>
      <li>
       had a heart attack
      </li>
      <li>
       have high blood pressure that cannot be controlled by medicine
      </li>
      <li>
       have diabetes with kidney, eye, nerve, or blood vessel damage
      </li>
      <li>
       have certain kinds of severe migraine headaches with aura, numbness, weakness or changes in vision, or any migraine headaches if you are over 35 years of age
      </li>
      <li>
       have liver problems, including liver tumors
      </li>
      <li>
       have any unexplained vaginal bleeding
      </li>
      <li>
       are pregnant
      </li>
      <li>
       had breast cancer or any cancer that is sensitive to female hormones
      </li>
     </ul>
     <span>
      If any of these conditions happen while you are taking ORTHO TRI-CYCLEN
      <span class="regSup">
       ®
      </span>
      Lo, stop taking ORTHO TRI-CYCLEN
      <span class="regSup">
       ®
      </span>
      Lo right away and talk to your healthcare professional. Use nonhormonal contraception when you stop taking ORTHO TRI-CYCLEN
      <span class="regSup">
       ®
      </span>
      Lo.
     </span>
     <span class="pinkHeading">
      What are the possible serious side effects of ORTHO TRI-CYCLEN
      <span class="regSup">
       ®
      </span>
      Lo?
     </span>
     <ul>
      <li>
       <span>
        Like pregnancy, ORTHO TRI-CYCLEN
        <span class="regSup">
         ®
        </span>
        Lo may cause serious side effects, including blood clots in your lungs, heart attack, or a stroke that may lead to death. Some other examples of serious blood clots include blood clots in the legs or eyes.
       </span>
      </li>
     </ul>
     <p class="seriousTxtMargin">
      Serious blood clots can happen especially if you smoke, are obese, or are older than 35 years of age. Serious blood clots are more likely to happen when you:
     </p>
     <ul>
      <li>
       first start taking birth control pills
      </li>
      <li>
       restart the same or different birth control pills after not using them for a month or more
      </li>
     </ul>
     <span>
      Call your healthcare professional or go to a hospital emergency room right away if you have:
     </span>
     <ul>
      <li>
       leg pain that will not go away
      </li>
      <li>
       sudden severe shortness of breath
      </li>
      <li>
       sudden change in vision or blindness
      </li>
      <li>
       chest pain
      </li>
      <li>
       a sudden, severe headache unlike your usual headaches
      </li>
      <li>
       weakness or numbness in your arm or leg
      </li>
      <li>
       trouble speaking
      </li>
     </ul>
     <span class="pinkHeading">
      Other serious side effects include:
     </span>
     <ul class="boldlist">
      <li>
       <span>
        Liver problems, including:
       </span>
       <ul>
        <li>
         rare liver tumors
        </li>
        <li>
         jaundice (cholestasis), especially if you previously had cholestasis of pregnancy. Call your healthcare professional if you have yellowing of your skin or eyes
        </li>
       </ul>
      </li>
      <li>
       <span>
        High blood pressure.
       </span>
       You should see your healthcare professional for a yearly check of your blood pressure.
      </li>
      <li>
       <span>
        Gallbladder problems
       </span>
      </li>
      <li>
       <span>
        Changes in the sugar and fat (cholesterol and triglycerides) levels in your blood
       </span>
      </li>
      <li>
       <span>
        New or worsening headaches including migraine headaches
       </span>
      </li>
      <li>
       <span>
        Irregular or unusual vaginal bleeding and spotting between your menstrual periods, especially during the first 3 months of taking ORTHO TRI-CYCLEN
        <span class="regSup">
         ®
        </span>
        Lo.
       </span>
      </li>
      <li>
       <span>
        Depression
       </span>
      </li>
      <li>
       <span>
        Possible cancer in your breast and cervix
       </span>
      </li>
      <li>
       <span>
        Swelling of your skin especially around your mouth, eyes, and in your throat (angioedema).
       </span>
       Call your healthcare professional if you have a swollen face, lips, mouth, tongue or throat, which may lead to difficulty swallowing or breathing. Your chance of having angioedema is higher if you have a history of angioedema.
      </li>
      <li>
       <span>
        Dark patches of skin around your forehead, nose, cheeks, and around your mouth, especially during pregnancy (chloasma).
       </span>
       Women who tend to get chloasma should avoid spending a long time in sunlight, tanning booths, and under sun lamps while taking
       <span class="orthoTxt">
        ORTHO TRI-CYCLEN
        <span class="regSup">
         ®
        </span>
       </span>
       Lo. Use sunscreen if you have to be in the sunlight.
      </li>
     </ul>
     <span class="pinkHeading">
      What are the most common side effects of ORTHO TRI-CYCLEN
      <span class="regSup">
       ®
      </span>
      Lo?
     </span>
     <ul>
      <li>
       headache (including migraine)
      </li>
      <li>
       nausea and vomiting
      </li>
      <li>
       breast problems
       <ul>
        <li>
         tenderness, pain, and discomfort
        </li>
        <li>
         enlargement and swelling
        </li>
        <li>
         discharge
        </li>
        <li>
         nipple pain
        </li>
       </ul>
      </li>
      <li>
       stomach pain
      </li>
      <li>
       pain with your periods (menstrual cycle)
      </li>
      <li>
       mood changes, including depression
      </li>
      <li>
       acne
      </li>
      <li>
       vaginal infections
      </li>
      <li>
       bloating
      </li>
      <li>
       weight gain
      </li>
      <li>
       fatigue
      </li>
     </ul>
     <p class="possibleTxt">
      These are not all the possible side effects of ORTHO TRI-CYCLEN
      <span class="regSup">
       ®
      </span>
      Lo. For more information, ask your healthcare professional or pharmacist. You may report side effects to the FDA by visiting www.fda.gov/medwatch, or calling 1-800-FDA-1088. You may also report side effects to Janssen Scientific Affairs, LLC at 1-800-526-7736.
     </p>
     <span class="pinkHeading">
      What should I tell my healthcare professional before using ORTHO TRI-CYCLEN
      <span class="regSup">
       ®
      </span>
      Lo?
     </span>
     <span class="bold_txt">
      Tell your healthcare professional if you:
     </span>
     <ul>
      <li>
       are pregnant or think you may be pregnant. If you become pregnant, stop taking
       <span class="orthoTxt">
        ORTHO TRI-CYCLEN
        <span class="regSup">
         ®
        </span>
       </span>
       Lo.
      </li>
      <li>
       are depressed now or have been depressed in the past
      </li>
      <li>
       had yellowing of your skin or eyes (jaundice) caused by pregnancy (cholestasis of pregnancy)
      </li>
      <li>
       are breastfeeding or plan to breastfeed. ORTHO TRI-CYCLEN
       <span class="regSup">
        ®
       </span>
       Lo may decrease the amount of breast milk you make; this is less likely to occur if breastfeeding is well established. A small amount of the hormones in ORTHO TRI-CYCLEN
       <span class="regSup">
        ®
       </span>
       Lo may pass into your breast milk. Talk to your healthcare professional about the best birth control method for you while breastfeeding.
      </li>
      <li>
       Tell your healthcare professional about all the medicines you take, including prescription and over-the-counter medicines, vitamins, and herbal supplements.
      </li>
     </ul>
     <p class="possibleTxt" style="margin-bottom:10px;">
      ORTHO TRI-CYCLEN
      <span class="regSup">
       ®
      </span>
      Lo may affect the way other medicines work, and other medicines may affect how well ORTHO TRI-CYCLEN
      <span class="regSup">
       ®
      </span>
      Lo works.
     </p>
     <ul>
      <li>
       Especially tell your healthcare professional if you take any of the following medicines:
       <ul>
        <li>
         barbiturates, carbamazepine, bosentan, felbamate, griseofulvin, oxcarbazepine, rifampicin, topiramate, rifabutin, rufinamide, aprepitant, and over-the-counter medicines containing St. Johns wort. Using these medicines with ORTHO TRI-CYCLEN
         <span class="regSup">
          ®
         </span>
         Lo may lead to irregular or unusual vaginal bleeding and/or spotting between your menstrual periods, and may make your contraceptive less effective. Talk to your doctor about using a back-up or alternative method of contraception when these medicines are used with ORTHO TRI-CYCLEN
         <span class="regSup">
          ®
         </span>
         Lo.
        </li>
        <li>
         Colesevelam
        </li>
        <li>
         HIV protease inhibitors such as: nelfinavir, ritonavir, darunavir/ritonavir, (fos)amprenavir/ritonavir, lopinavir/ritonavir, and tipranavir/ritonavir.
        </li>
       </ul>
      </li>
     </ul>
     <p class="possibleTxt">
      Know the medicines you take. Keep a list of them to show your healthcare professional and pharmacist when you get a new medicine. Ask your healthcare professional if you are not sure if any of your medicines are listed above.
     </p>
     <span class="pinkHeading">
      What else should I know about taking ORTHO TRI-CYCLEN
      <span class="regSup">
       ®
      </span>
      Lo?
     </span>
     <p>
      If you are scheduled for any lab tests, tell your healthcare professional you are taking
      <br/>
      ORTHO TRI-CYCLEN
      <span class="regSup">
       ®
      </span>
      Lo. Certain blood tests may be affected by ORTHO TRI-CYCLEN
      <span class="regSup">
       ®
      </span>
      Lo.
     </p>
     <p class="orthoTopSpace">
      ORTHO TRI-CYCLEN
      <span class="regSup">
       ®
      </span>
      Lo does not protect against HIV infection (AIDS) and other sexually transmitted infections.
     </p>
     <span class="pinkHeading">
      What should I know about my period when taking ORTHO TRI-CYCLEN
      <span class="regSup">
       ®
      </span>
      Lo?
     </span>
     <p>
      Your periods may be lighter and shorter than usual. Some women may miss a period. Irregular vaginal bleeding or spotting may happen while you are taking ORTHO TRI-CYCLEN
      <span class="regSup">
       ®
      </span>
      Lo, especially during the first few months of use. This usually is not a serious problem. It is important to continue taking your pills on a regular schedule to prevent a pregnancy.
     </p>
     <p class="possibleTxt">
      If you miss two or more of your periods in a row tell your healthcare provider because you may be pregnant.
     </p>
     <p class="possibleTxt">
      You are encouraged to report negative side effects of prescription drugs to the FDA. Visit
      <a href="http://www.fda.gov/medwatch" target="_blank">
       www.fda.gov/medwatch
      </a>
      , or call 1-800-FDA-1088. You may also report side effects to Janssen Scientific Affairs, LLC at 1-800-526-7736.
     </p>
