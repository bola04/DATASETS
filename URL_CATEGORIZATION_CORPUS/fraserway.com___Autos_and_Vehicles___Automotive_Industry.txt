         <h2>

          </span>
         </h2>
         <h2>
          Fraserway, Canada's only national RV dealer
         </h2>
         <p>
          Fraserway RV is Canada's favourite RV dealer &amp; premiere RV renter. With locations in Vancouver (Delta), Abbotsford, Kamloops, Prince George, Whitehorse, Lacombe County, Leduc, Edmonton, Calgary (Airdrie), Toronto (Cookstown) and Halifax (Bedford), we certainly have a lot in-store for you. We're here to help base camp your outdoor adventures; from Tent Trailers to Travel Trailers, Fifth Wheels to Toy Haulers, Class C motorhomes to Class A motor coaches, we’ve got you covered. And our RV Rentals, RV Sales, RV Parts and RV Service departments exist to serve you better.
         </p>
         <p>
          Find out what makes us more than just another RV company; more than just product and price, more than just great selection and trusted RV brands, more than just a knowledgeable and friendly staff.  We are a first-rate customer value experience, and proud to be a part of the pleasant surprise Canada holds for you.
         </p>
         <p>
          So come explore Canada with us; from the east coast to the west coast, from the south to the north, and everywhere in between.
         </p>
         <p>
          Fraserway RV, the place for everything RV since 1969.
