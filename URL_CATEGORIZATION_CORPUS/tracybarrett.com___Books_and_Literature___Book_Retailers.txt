                  <a href="http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/tg/detail/-/0828320004/qid=1111094504/sr=1-15/ref=sr_1_15/103-3644800-7632608?v=glance&amp;s=books">

                   <i>

                   </i>
                  </a>

                  <a href="https://www.amazon.com/Song-Orpheus-Greatest-Greek-Myths/dp/1535144505/ref=sr_1_1_twi_pap_1?s=books&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1468770372&amp;sr=1-1">
                   <i>
                    The Song of Orpheus
                   </i>
                  </a>
                  , retells seventeen little-known (but authentic) Greek myths.
