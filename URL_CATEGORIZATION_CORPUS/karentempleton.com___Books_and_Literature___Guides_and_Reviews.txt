        <p>
         I’m thrilled to announce the release of the last two books in my  Wed in the West series for Harlequin Special Edition! Set in my own backyard in  beautiful northeastern New Mexico, where the Sangre de Christo mountains tower  over majestic rolling plains and lush pine forests, we’re talking horses and  cowboys, oh, yeah…as well my usual assortment of quirky characters, down home  settings and rowdy little boys (and girls!). And dogs. I have loved writing  this series, beginning with
         <a href="http://www.karentempleton.com/books/motherswish.php">
          A MOTHER’S WISH
         </a>
         in 2008, which went on to win a  RITA award from Romance Writers of America…followed by another RITA for
         <a href="http://www.karentempleton.com/books/welcomehomecowboy.php">
          WELCOME  HOME, COWBOY
         </a>
         in 2011. But all good things must come to an end, in order to make  room for other good things down the road.
        </p>
        <p>
         But as for these books…in
         <a href="books/rancherschristmas.php">
          THE RANCHER’S EXPECTANT CHRISTMAS
         </a>
         (October 2016, ebook and print) ranch manager Josh Talbot is stunned to  discover that, upon his boss’s death, he’s inherited half of the sprawling New  Mexico ranch he’s called home his entire life. Only the other half has gone to  Deanna Blake, the boss’s daughter…and the gal Josh knew better than to even  think about getting cozy with when they were teenagers. Except jilted, and very  pregnant, Deanna hasn’t thought of the Vista Encantada as “home” for more than  ten years. Nor is she about to now – she wants to sell it to provide for her  unborn daughter. Only she hadn’t counted on how being around her old crush, and  his adorable little boy, would make her reconsider what home really means…
        </p>
        <p>
         Following in February 2017 is
         <a href="books/reboundbride.php">
          FALLING FOR THE REBOUND BRIDE
         </a>
         . When  Deanna’s cousin Emily Weber seeks temporary refuge at the Vista after narrowly  escaping marriage to her cheating fiancé, the peace she’s so desperately  seeking is shattered by the strong, silent, and oh-so-tempting presence of  prodigal son Colin Talbot, who’s unexpectedly returned home after many years  away to deal with his own demons. Alone. But can these two shattered souls find  solace in each other’s arms, at least until Colin leaves again? Or does fate  have something entirely different in mind…?
        </p>
        <p>
         in other news,
         <a href="http://karentempleton.com/books/santasplaybook.php">
          SANTA’S PLAYBOOK
         </a>
         , which  earned the rare 4.5 Top Pick GOLD designation from Romantic Times when it  debuted in 2014, also went on to win RT’s Reviewer’s Choice Award for Best  Special Edition for that year. Since I absolutely love this story, about a  drama teacher whose biggest role turns out to be healing the hearts of a  widowed football coach and his four children, I’m thrilled with how well  received has been. And thanks to the wonderful world of digital publishing,  this, as well as my entire backlist, is still available at Amazon.com, bn.com,  another other etailers for years to come.
        </p>
