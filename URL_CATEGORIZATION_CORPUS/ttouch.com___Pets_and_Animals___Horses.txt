     <h4>
      A World of Possibilities
     </h4>
     <p>
      Tellington TTouch Training is about a world of possibilities. The TTouch transformational body work and lessons in accelerated learning open revolutionary new relationships with animals. These respectful and meaningful interactions are full of intent, cooperation, discovery, partnership, and magic.
      <a href="javascript:;" onclick="P7_swapClass(1,'f1','showfaq','hidefaq','div')">
       Learn more ...
      </a>
     </p>
     <div class="hidefaq" id="f1">
      <p>
       The four components of Tellington TTouch Training -- body work, ground exercises, equipment and envisioning the highest thought of how we want our animals to be -- become our guided pathway. From these spring the activation of the body's cells and the enhancement of the body's and mind's innate ability to heal. A healthy body and mind become possible, along with self-confidence, improved body motion, positive behavioral patterns, and a feeling of calm wholeness.
      </p>
      <p>
       Tellington TTouch Training changes undesirable behavior, enhances performance, health and well-being, and deepens the relationship between animals and their people.
    The Tellington method has its own road map that is easy to understand and follow. Within the contents of the journey lies a sweet mystery that is intriguing and richly productive.
