        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Small Publishers, Artists &amp; Writers Network
        </h1>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <h4 style="text-align: center;">
         <em>
          <span style="color: #800000;">
           <strong>
            The Small Publishers, Artists &amp; Writers Network (SPAWN) provides information, resources and opportunities for anyone involved in or interested in publishing, whether they are an author, freelance writer, artist or own a publishing company.
           </strong>
          </span>
         </em>
        </h4>
        <p>
         <a href="http://spawn.org/gdpr-for-indie-authors/">
          <img alt="" class=" wp-image-5806 alignright" data-attachment-id="5806" data-comments-opened="1" data-image-description="" data-image-meta='{"aperture":"0","credit":"","camera":"","caption":"","created_timestamp":"0","copyright":"","focal_length":"0","iso":"0","shutter_speed":"0","title":"","orientation":"0"}' data-image-title="fd32c52e-26fc-41ad-9a56-d9992716d49d" data-large-file="https://i1.wp.com/spawn.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/fd32c52e-26fc-41ad-9a56-d9992716d49d.png?fit=625%2C391" data-medium-file="https://i1.wp.com/spawn.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/fd32c52e-26fc-41ad-9a56-d9992716d49d.png?fit=300%2C188" data-orig-file="https://i1.wp.com/spawn.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/fd32c52e-26fc-41ad-9a56-d9992716d49d.png?fit=640%2C400" data-orig-size="640,400" data-permalink="http://spawn.org/gdpr-for-indie-authors/fd32c52e-26fc-41ad-9a56-d9992716d49d/" data-recalc-dims="1" height="208" sizes="(max-width: 333px) 100vw, 333px" src="https://i1.wp.com/spawn.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/fd32c52e-26fc-41ad-9a56-d9992716d49d.png?resize=333%2C208" srcset="https://i1.wp.com/spawn.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/fd32c52e-26fc-41ad-9a56-d9992716d49d.png?w=640 640w, https://i1.wp.com/spawn.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/fd32c52e-26fc-41ad-9a56-d9992716d49d.png?resize=300%2C188 300w, https://i1.wp.com/spawn.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/fd32c52e-26fc-41ad-9a56-d9992716d49d.png?resize=624%2C390 624w" width="333"/>
         </a>
         Do you love history? How can you incorporate it into your book without turning it into the dreaded Information Dump? And if you’re using family history, there is the problem of angering friends and relatives and the risk of being disinherited.
         <a href="http://spawn.org/weaving-history-into-contemporary-novels/">
          <strong>
           Lea Wait
          </strong>
         </a>
         and
         <a href="http://spawn.org/an-outrageous-reality-writing-family-history/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
          <strong>
           Constance Hood
          </strong>
         </a>
         explain how they use history to make their books come alive and how to keep on the good side of those around you.
        </p>
        <p>
         Still confused by GDPR and what the new EU laws mean to you as an author?
         <a href="http://spawn.org/gdpr-for-indie-authors/">
          <strong>
           Penny Sansevieri
          </strong>
          spreads some light on the subject
         </a>
         . For a more in depth discussion about data protection, listen to
         <a href="http://spawn.org/gdpr-what-all-authors-need-to-know-with-gemma-gibbs/">
          Mark Dawson’s Self Publishing Formula podcast that gives clarity to GDPR
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         When you’ve had a success, be sure to share your story with the rest of us. A short bio and link, or an article if it was more involved.
        </p>
        <p>
         What would you like to learn? See more of or less? Feedback is essential, so send a note to
         <a href="mailto:editor@spawn.org">
          editor@spawn.org
         </a>
         and let us know. Do you have an idea? An area of expertise? Drop me a line!
        </p>
