        <h2 class="title">
         <a href="/news/2012-06-20/sword-of-truth-ebook-matrix" title="Sword of Truth eBook Matrix">
          Sword of Truth eBook Matrix
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         Wednesday, June 20, 2012 - 10:53 am
        </p>
        <div class="content">
         <!-- google_ad_section_start -->
         <p>
          With Terry Goodkind publishing "The First Confessor: The Legend of Magda Searus" as eBook only (except for a very limited printed Collectors Edition), we've gathered links to the other eBooks for the most popular eBook readers. iBooks and Sony are missing a few titles, which hopefully will be available in the near future. Debt of Bones is only available as part of a collection. As most eBooks come locked with DRM, they can only be read with the platform they have been bought with (for example an iBook eBook cannot be read on the Kindle, however an Amazon Kindle eBook can be read on the Kindle, and on Kindle for iOS and the other Kindle readers).
         </p>
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0051VVOB2/ref=as_li_tf_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;tag=swordoftruthc-20&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B0051VVOB2">
          Kindle
         </a>
         From $79 - $379
         <br/>
         There are free readers for Windows PC, Mac, Windows Phone 7, Android, Blackberry, and iOS available. Amazon even offers a free online Kindle Cloud Reader for browsers.
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/feature.html/ref=sv_kstore_1?ie=UTF8&amp;docId=1000493771">
          Link to Free Readers
         </a>
         <a href="http://www.barnesandnoble.com/nook/">
          Nook
         </a>
         From $99 - $249
         <br/>
         Free Nook readers are also available for Windows PC, Mac, Windows Phone 7, Android, and iOS available
         <a href="http://store.apple.com">
          iOS
         </a>
         devices are available from $199 for the most recent iPod Touch, $199 for the iPhone with contract, to the iPad starting at $399 for the New iPad
         <br/>
         <a href="http://itunes.apple.com/us/app/ibooks/id364709193?mt=8">
          iBooks
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://itunes.apple.com/us/app/kindle-read-books-magazines/id302584613?mt=8">
          Kindle Reader
         </a>
         , and
         <a href="http://itunes.apple.com/us/app/nook-by-barnes-noble/id373582546?mt=8">
          Nook Reader
         </a>
         are available for free and let you read books from the other platforms.
         <a href="http://ebookstore.sony.com/reader/">
          Sony Reader
         </a>
         are available from $129.99
         <br/>
         Unfortunately Sony does not offer free readers for other platforms.
         <a href="http://books.google.com">
          Google Books
         </a>
         has a free reader for Android and iOS. Books can also be read online with a browser.

        <h2 class="title">
         <a href="/news/2012-06-18/the-first-confessor-limited-collectors-edition-revealed" title="&quot;THE FIRST CONFESSOR&quot; Limited Collector's Edition Revealed">
          "THE FIRST CONFESSOR" Limited Collector's Edition Revealed
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         Monday, June 18, 2012 - 08:26 am
        </p>
        <div class="content">
         <!-- google_ad_section_start -->
         <p style="text-align:center">
          <img alt="The First Confessor Logo" src="/sot/news/2012-06-18/the_first_confessor.jpg" width="480"/>
         </p>
         THE FIRST CONFESSOR Limited Collector's Edition will be available for $300 and will be including several great collectibles such as a Sword of Truth pendent.
         <br/>
         Pre-sale for the Collector's Edition begins this Wednesday at 12:00 p.m. / Noon, PST.
         <br/>
         Of the 300 made a few will be available via contests.
         <br/>
         As there are currently no plans to offer this novel as mass-market or hard cover, this will be the only way to get 'The First Confessor' in a printed form.

More details are available at
         <a href="http://terrygoodkind.com/theoracle/ce/collectors.html">
          http://terrygoodkind.com/theoracle/ce/collectors.html
         </a>
         .

The eBook is scheduled to be released July 2nd, 2012 and will be available for $9.99 ($8.99 during the first week).
