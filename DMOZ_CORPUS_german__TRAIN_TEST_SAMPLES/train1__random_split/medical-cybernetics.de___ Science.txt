           <p>
            <font color="#990000" size="+2">
             M
            </font>
            odelling
                      and reasoning in health sciences are currently
                      enjoying growing interest. This is paralleled by
                      increased understanding that it are primarily
                      the information processing systems within the
                      organism's intercausal networks that provide the
                      fundamentals of life.
           </p>
           <p>
            Medical Cybernetics [-&gt;
            <a href="definition.html">
             preliminary
                      definition
            </a>
            ] searches for quantitative
                      descriptions of biological dynamics primarily in
                      the intact, but beyond also in the diseased
                      organism in order to gain new insights into the
                      principles of life and its perturbations and to
                      gather evidence based foundations for clinical
                      decision making.
           </p>
           <p>
            This portal is to provide scientific
                      reference information and introductory notes on
                      several fields of Medical Cybernetics as well as
                      links to working groups, institutes, societies
                      and associations, readings and events
                      worldwide.
           </p>
