      <h2>
       Ports Of Call 2018
      </h2>
      <p>
       Trade- and shipsimulation. Our popular classic game Ports Of Call comes to new life.
      </p>
      <p>
       After so many years POC (more than 20 years sice the first Amiga Version in 1986) is still very popular and we still develop new versions of this games, with new technical features as 3d ship simulation and new features with more ships
 and harbours, multi freights and many more for the trading/ economic part. The new Ports Of Call (economic part) is called POC XXL  works with two parts, the trade part (called POC XXL)
 and the optional simulation part (POC Sim3d/Sim3dII). POC XXL will call POC Sim3dII when a actual ship simulation for steering is wanted. Also a 2d simulator is contained in POC XXL. But POC Sim3dII can also be played as a standalone game.
      </p>
