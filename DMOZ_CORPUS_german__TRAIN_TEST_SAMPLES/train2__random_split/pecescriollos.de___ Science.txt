           <td valign="top">
            <h1>
             welcome at PecesCriollos
            </h1>
           </td>
          </tr>
          <tr>
           <td valign="top">
            <p>
             <font class="editorstd12">
              The aim of this page is
             </font>
             <font class="editorstd12">
              to provide a permanently updated list of the freshwater fishes from Argentina, Paraguay, and Uruguay for the ichthyological community and anyone else interested in the subject.
             </font>
            </p>
            <p>
             <font class="editorblk12">
              <strong>
               PecesCriollos
              </strong>
             </font>
             <font class="editorstd12">
              is based on the following national faunal lists:
             </font>
             <br/>
             <font class="editorstd12">
              for Argentina: Mirande &amp; Koerber (2015):
              <strong>
               CLOFFAR
              </strong>
              <br/>
              for Paraguay: Koerber, Vera-Alcaraz &amp; Reis (2017):
              <strong>
               CLOFPY
              </strong>
              <br/>
              for Uruguay: Litz &amp; Koerber (2014):
              <strong>
               CLOFFUY
              </strong>
              .
              <br/>
              All systematical changes that have been published since then, will allways be included in www.pecescriollos.de as soon as possible.
             </font>
            </p>
            <p align="center">
             <a href="http://www.pecescriollos.de/go/epactionotus-aky" target="_blank">
              <img alt="Epactionotus aky, dorsolateral view (photo Ingo Seidel)" border="0" height="228" src="https://media.hotelwebservice.com/media/pecescriollos/img/dyn/39e5eeb2508baf43910dd68aab520d83.jpg" width="350"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             <font class="editorstd12">
              Regular updates will be provided as pdf-download
             </font>
             <font class="editorstd12">
              s. Notification about new versions will be send to anyone interested upon request.
             </font>
            </p>
            <p>
             <font class="editorstd12">
              Please check our partner section. Without their financial and technical support www.pecescriollos.de would not have been possible.
             </font>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
