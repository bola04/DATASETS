       Welcome @ Galointsch

     <p>
      Zven and Leif have been Galointsch since 2003. 
 The name may be completely obscure, but the chemistry
  between these two musicians creates an unmistakeable groove!
   Musical influences range from blues and hip hop to drum 'n'
    bass and minimal techno, combined with a large pinch of soul,
     and based on varying binary 4-step or broken beats. The result is Galointsch.
 Galointsch cannot be pigeon-holed, 
 they are to be found everywhere - but especially somewhere 
 near the dancefloor! Their fancy live-equipment is enough to convince 
 even the most sceptical listeners that making electronic music is not just 
 about pushing buttons, but about working the controls with enough passion to 
 turn the audience on.
