           <p>
            IPCO is a new name in Industrial Process solutions but a business partner with whom many will already be familiar. Previously operating as Sandvik Process
            <br/>
            Systems, we are now an independent company owned by FAM AB – part of the Wallenberg group.
           </p>
           <p>
            We continue to develop customized solutions for the processing industry, with the same people, skills and process systems – but under a new name and brand.
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             35+ Sales and service offices globally
            </li>
            <li>
             Production facilities in Americas, Asia and Europe
            </li>
            <li>
             Number of employees: 600
            </li>
            <li>
             200 M EURO annual sales
            </li>
            <li>
             President: Henrik Furhoff
            </li>
            <li>
             Owner: FAM AB
            </li>
           </ul>
