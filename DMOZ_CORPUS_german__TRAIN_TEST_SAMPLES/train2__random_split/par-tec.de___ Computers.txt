         ParTec Cluster Competence Center
        </h2>
        <p class="bodytext">
         <i>
          ParTec
         </i>
         <i>
          Cluster Competence Center
         </i>
         delivers the software, consultancy and support services necessary to achieve new heights in productivity and availability on todays commodity based supercomputers.  Parastation, an opensource project developed and maintained by ParTec, has unique features  specifically designed to address the challenges of scalability and reliability on extremely large clusters.  ParTec's unrivaled expertise in delivering professional services, consultancy and support has made it the  partner of choice in some of the leading HPC sites across Europe.

        <h2>
         Customer Reference: Research Center Jülich
        </h2>
        <div class="csc-textpic csc-textpic-intext-left csc-textpic-caption-l">
         <div class="csc-textpic-imagewrap">
          <dl class="csc-textpic-image csc-textpic-firstcol csc-textpic-lastcol" style="width:330px;">
           <dt>
            <a href="http://www.fz-juelich.de/jsc/juropa/" target="_blank">
             <img alt="" border="0" height="229" src="typo3temp/pics/b3b617a9f0.jpg" width="330"/>
            </a>
           </dt>
           <dd class="csc-textpic-caption">
            picture source: Forschungszentrum Jülich.
           </dd>
          </dl>
         </div>
         <div class="csc-textpic-text">
          <p class="bodytext">
           In June 2009,
           <a class="internal-link" href="products/parastationv5.html" title="Opens internal link in current window">
            ParaStationV5
           </a>
           propelled the 3288 node JuRoPA cluster  at the
           <a class="external-link-new-window" href="http://www.fz-juelich.de/jsc/en" target="_blank" title="Opens external link in new window">
            Jülich Supercomputer Centre
           </a>
           to an  impressive
           <b>
            274,8 Teraflops
           </b>
           of sustained performance with a parallel efficiency of
           <b>
            91,6%
           </b>
           . At that time JuRoPA
           <b>
            Ranked No. 10 in the World and
           </b>
           <b>
            No. 1 in  Europe
           </b>
           of the most powerful general purpose supercomputers (
           <a class="external-link-new-window" href="http://top500.org/lists/2009/06" target="_blank" title="Opens external link in new window">
            top500.org
           </a>
           ).
           <a class="internal-link" href="products/parastation-mpi.html" title="Opens internal link in current window">
            ParaStationMPI
           </a>
           was shown to scale to more than
           <b>
            25,000 MPI processes
           </b>
           (without threading) on this machine.
          </p>
          <p class="bodytext">
           <span lang="EN-US">
            JuRoPA was inaugurated May 26, 2009 by  the German Federal Minister for Education and Research, Prof. Dr. Annette Schavan, the Prime Minister of North Rhine-Westphalia, Dr. Jürgen Rüttgers, and Prof. Dr. Achim Bachem, Chairman of the Bord of Directors at Research Center Jülich as well as high-ranking international guests from academia, industry and politics.
           </span>
          </p>
