            <p>
             <b>
              Where do we come from? Where do we go? Where do we lay our hat - where is our heart? Where is our home?
             </b>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             When we launched
             <b>
              <a href="http://www.piranha.de/piranha/heimatklaenge" rel="nofollow">
               HeimatKlänge festival
              </a>
             </b>
             in 1988 to present
             <i>
              "urban folk and dance music - Home Sounds from Home Planet Earth"
             </i>
             in Berlin, there was no House of World Cultures (launched in 1989), no radiomultikulti (1994 - 2008), no YAAM (launched in 1994), no Karneval der Kulturen (launched in 1996). Even the wall was still up...
             <br/>
             <br/>
             With the occasion of
             <b>
              HeimatKlänge's 30th anniversary
             </b>
             , we'd like to rise up its quetions once again and bring them on the forefront of the current 2018 situation where, the topic of "Heimat" ("homeland") is as controversial as it is omnipresent and where the diversity of species in international music is a global commodity.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             <img alt="[http://www.piranha.de/image/cms/piranha_records/events/HKcampaign/1996_HK_lomosWEBSITE.jpg]" src="http://www.piranha.de/image/cms/piranha_records/events/HKcampaign/1996_HK_lomosWEBSITE.jpg" title="http://www.piranha.de/image/cms/piranha_records/events/HKcampaign/1996_HK_lomosWEBSITE.jpg"/>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             <b>
              Heimatklänge
             </b>
             was a Berlin World Music...
            </p>
