        <p>
         Resulting from a reorganisation of Scouting in Scotland as a whole, 
         the Ayrshire Area Scout Council was merged into a new organisation
         <a href="http://www.scouts-southwestscotland.org.uk">
          South West 
         Region Scouts
         </a>
         which comprises of the merger of Ayrshire, Dumfriesshire 
         and Galloway. The new Region comprises of 5 Districts, Ayrshire 
         East,  Dumfriesshire, Galloway, Kyle and Carrick and North Ayrshire 
         &amp; Arran.
        </p>
        <p>
         The new Region came into effect on 1st April 2008.
        </p>
