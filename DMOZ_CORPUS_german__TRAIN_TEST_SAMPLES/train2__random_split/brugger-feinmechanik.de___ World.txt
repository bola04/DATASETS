            <p>
             Life is like a box of chocolates. You never know what you're going to get. You can, however, be certain of what state the box will be in – as long as it has been tested using our devices. Brugger Feinmechanik has been manufacturing various material testing devices since 1860 and is a specialist in the quality control of films in the food, pharmaceutical and packaging industry. We offer permeation testers for gas and steam, thermal contact sealing devices and testing devices for determining the sealed seam properties as well as other special film testing devices. We do everything we can to meet the highest standards – our own. A product that tests how perfect something is must be absolutely perfect itself. We are continually further developing our innovations and guarantee that our devices will have a particularly long service life. Alongside our testing devices, we offer a free introduction at our company site as well as in-depth software and hardware training on request. So you know exactly what you're getting.
