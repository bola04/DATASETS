We work to advance the principles of our party by spreading the Democratic message to US voters in and around Berlin and encouraging them to vote for Democratic candidates back home.

               </a>

               <a href="http://demsinberlin.de/n2/events/category/monthly-political-pub-night">
                political pub night
               </a>
               (every third Wednesday of the month). We also host
               <a href="http://demsinberlin.de/n2/?post_type=tribe_events">
                other events
               </a>
               for you to become engaged!
