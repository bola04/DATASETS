        <h3 class="widget-title">
         Welcome to the Weltempfänger Hostel &amp; Café
        </h3>
        <div class="textwidget">
         <div class="two-columns-one">
          <p>
           Our backpacker hostel is located on the outskirts of Cologne’s city centre in the Ehrenfeld district, between Bahnhof West and Bahnhof Ehrenfeld. Located directly at the subway station ‘Piusstraße’, we are easily accessible by public transport.
          </p>
          <p>
           We offer you a cheap and cosy accommodation, with nice people in the best location, from where you can explore the city and the surrounding area or stay and meet interesting new people, world travellers and locals.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="two-columns-one last">
          <p>
           Our well-equipped kitchen will be at your disposal as well as our Café Weltempfänger, where you can have breakfast, snack, drink and listen to music and enjoy the city and Veedel.
          </p>
          <p>
           In the surrounding area there are many other nice cafés and restaurants, cosy pubs and bars, clubs for dancing and listening to music, theater, cinema, green meadows for sunbathing and dozing, shops for shopping and much more.
          </p>
          <p>
           We would be very pleased to welcome you in our hostel!
          </p>
          <p>
           The WELTreceivers
          </p>
         </div>
