       "Berlins freshest trio" (soultrade.de) famous for their high-energy live performances, provide a huge club-event with fresh live music and a stirring blend of jazz/house and elektro! While the crowd is dancing "like they were on trampolines" (nickodemus/nyc) the three YE:SOLARs make you forget that they're basically a trio with rhodes, doublebass and drums on stage.
       <br/>
       <br clear="left"/>
       At the beginning of the new millennium Random Rhodes, Steve Bass and Groovemaster7 found themselves studying jazz in Berlin and playing lots of gigs as a jazztrio. Realizing that they also share the love for electronic clubmusic YE:SOLAR started improvising and creating their own material with one vision in mind - combining both the jazz background with styles like deep-house and elektro.
       <div class="absatz">
       </div>
       In fact, another important reason for this change was not to play in front of wine-drinking mid-fifties, but to have serious crowds dancing to their music - a rough and fresh sound you could call elektrojazz or even punkjazz! Also production-wise they found a genuine sound which blends up-to-date production skills with their unique musical ideas.
       <div class="absatz">
       </div>
       But honestly - the real shit is YE:SOLARs live performance and raw energy! There's no doubt you will forget anything you ever heard about jazztrios. Expect Random Rhodes doing real headbanging while wearing special working clothes, Steve Bass driving his doublebass into your stomach plus providing freaked-out film-snippets with YE:SOLAR as main actors, which Groovemaster7 hasn't seen yet because he is concentrating on playing kick, snare, kick, snare, kick...
