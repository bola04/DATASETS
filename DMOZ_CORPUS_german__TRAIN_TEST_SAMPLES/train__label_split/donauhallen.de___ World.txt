         </h1>

It doesn't matter if you plan congresses, fairs, conferences, seminars, concerts or galas, the 'Donauhallen' in Donaueschingen is the conference centre in the South! Take a look behind the scenes of the 'Donauhallen' and you'll see that each room and each hall are captivating due to  their individual room characteristics.
