          <h2>
           Art conservator search and directory for clients
          </h2>
          <p>
           Are you looking for a restorer who performs a conservation or restoration expertly and according to current standards? 
 			Our network contains a detailed directory where you can find the right contractor.
 			It allows you to perform a targeted restorer search in several categories.
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            <a href="/en/region/">
             Countries and Regions
            </a>
            : Here you will find restorers in over 30 countries worldwide. Most users currently come from Germany, followed by Austria and Switzerland.
           </li>
           <li>
            Main Assignment and Qualification: Select restorers based on their degrees or level of education. For example, the search for restorers includes the groups of "restorers with diplomas or masters" or "state-examined restorers".
           </li>
           <li>
            <a href="/en/field/">
             Areas of expertise
            </a>
            : To find a restorer specializing in the field of "painting conservation" or "gilding", look no further. Furthermore, references and articles are presented according to subject areas.
           </li>
           <li>
            <a href="/en/organization/">
             Organizations / Association
            </a>
            : This category is for anyone who wants to find someone who is already a member of an organization like the german "VDR" or the ICOM.
           </li>
           <li>
            Working status: Which users are currently employed, operate a business or are self-employed? Here you can find out.
           </li>
           <li>
            <a href="/en/job/">
             Apprenticeship / Interns
            </a>
            : Upcoming restorers will find suitable employers who offer a training or internship position.
