       <h2 class="ce_headline" style="margin-top:20px;">
        Welcome to Zedler-Group – your experts in bicycles and e-bikes
       </h2>
       <div class="ce_text block">
        <p>
         The bicycle is more than a (dream) job, to us it’s a philosophy. We are aware of its growing importance on public roads, as it doesn’t take up much space, strengthens the health of its users and hardly affects the environment. This is why we do our utmost in promoting this environmentally friendly means of travel and hereby ensure operational safety of the bicycle during your ride.
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="ce_text block">
        <p>
         Our different business branches develop and provide knowledge of the construction and use of different bicycle types, such as mountain bikes, road bikes, kid’s bikes or trekking bikes. We deal with carbon, aluminium, steel and titanium frames and also care about add-on parts, such as wheels, forks, handlebars, gear systems, brakes, saddles and seat posts. In addition, since the beginning of the pedelec boom our team has focussed on the e-bike and its specific components.
        </p>
        <div>
         Zedler-Group comprises three specialist fields which are independent business branches:
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="ce_text block" style="margin-top:30px;">
        <figure class="image_container float_left" style="margin-bottom:50px;">
         <a href="http://www.zedler-institut.de/de/index.html" title="Zedler – Institut für Fahrradtechnik und -Sicherheit GmbH">
          <img alt="Zedler – Institut für Fahrradtechnik und -Sicherheit GmbH" height="36" src="files/kunde/global/images/zedler_Institut_Logo_orange-mobile.png" title="Zedler – Institut für Fahrradtechnik und -Sicherheit GmbH" width="200"/>
         </a>
        </figure>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Zedler – Institut für Fahrradtechnik und -Sicherheit GmbH
         </strong>
         focus on
         <a href="http://www.zedler-institut.de/en/index.html" target="_blank">
          testing
         </a>
         of bicycles and components in the company's own test laboratory. They also develop and build testing systems and equip the test laboratories of bicycle manufacturers. In addition, Zedler-Institut design, prepare and print
         <a href="http://www.zedler-fahrrad.de/en/index.html" target="_blank">
          user manuals
         </a>
         as well as operating instructions for e-bikes and all types of bicycles in accordance with EN and ISO standards in force.
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="ce_text block" style="margin-top:20px;">
        <figure class="image_container float_left">
         <img alt="Gesellschaft der Fahrrad-Sachverständigen mbH (GDFS)" height="75" src="files/kunde/global/images/GdFS_Logo_2016-03-01_Grau-mobile.png" title="Gesellschaft der Fahrrad-Sachverständigen mbH (GDFS)" width="200"/>
        </figure>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Gesellschaft der Fahrrad-Sachverständigen mbH
         </strong>
         (GDFS) prepare all kinds of
         <a href="http://www.gdfs.bike/en/index.html" target="_blank" title="bicycle reports">
          bicycle reports
         </a>
         for insurers, lawyers, manufacturers and retailers as well as for private accident victims and claimants. Our team members draw up comprehensive expert’s reports for bicycles, pedelecs and components involved in accidents or subject to burglary or technical failure.
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="ce_text block" style="margin-top:30px;">
        <figure class="image_container float_left">
         <img alt="Sachverständigenbüro für Fahrradtechnik Zedler" height="66" src="assets/images/e/Ingenieurb%C3%BCro_Logo-3790d18e.jpg" title="Sachverständigenbüro für Fahrradtechnik Zedler" width="200"/>
        </figure>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Sachverständigenbüro für Fahrradtechnik Zedler
         </strong>
         was founded by graduate engineer Dirk Zedler in 1993. The founder works on behalf of the courts all over Germany and Europe as publicly appointed and sworn in expert for bicycles and electric bicycles (CCI Stuttgart).
        </p>
       </div>
