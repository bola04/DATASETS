     <h2 id="post-1602">
      <a href="https://www.cyberpunk2020.de/2017/08/31/wir-akzeptieren-nur-den-wopper-coin/" rel="bookmark">
       Wir akzeptieren nur den Wopper-Coin
      </a>
     </h2>
     <p class="comments">
      <a href="https://www.cyberpunk2020.de/2017/08/31/wir-akzeptieren-nur-den-wopper-coin/#comments">
       with one comment
      </a>
     </p>
     <div class="main">
      <p>
       Ist dein Dark-Future-Setting noch nicht abgefahren genug? Der Frikadellen-Brater Burger King hilft.
      </p>
      <blockquote>
       <p>
        Die Schnellrestaurant-Kette Burger King geht in Russland neue Wege in Sachen Kundenbindung: Käufer sollen für jeden umgesetztem Rubel (umgerechnet rund 0,014 Euro) ein Kryptowährungstoken namens
        <a href="http://www.wavesgo.com/assets.html?ANTz8NnpfbEcDFXo4gwd7UL5ugc9bdTcPGbEPktRPZw2" rel="external noopener" target="_blank">
         Whoppercoin
        </a>
        erhalten.
        <br/>
        (Quelle:
        <a href="https://www.heise.de/newsticker/meldung/Whoppercoin-Kryptogeld-als-Treueprogramm-bei-Burger-King-Russland-3816066.html">
         Heise.de
        </a>
        )
       </p>
      </blockquote>
      <p>
       Hat man in dem Settingbuch “Hardwired” schon erkannt, dass staatliches Geld nichts mehr wert sein wird, so ist die Realität schon weiter. Mit dem Woppercoin sehen wir hier wohl den Beginn der ersten Konzernwährung.
      </p>

     <h2 id="post-1599">
      <a href="https://www.cyberpunk2020.de/2017/08/30/r-talsorian-to-work-on-cyberpunk-2020-again/" rel="bookmark">
       R. Talsorian to work on Cyberpunk 2020 again?
      </a>
     </h2>
     <p class="comments">
      <a href="https://www.cyberpunk2020.de/2017/08/30/r-talsorian-to-work-on-cyberpunk-2020-again/#respond">
       without comments
      </a>
     </p>
     <div class="main">
      <p>
       I don’t know much, since I wasn’t at Gencon 2017. But James Gray, who works for Fat Goblin Games, who licence Castle Falkenstein from R.Talsorian, writes:
      </p>
      <blockquote>
       <p>
        <b>
         Cyberpunk Red
        </b>
        will be
        <b>
         Cyberpunk 2020
        </b>
        1.5. Some small rule changes and an update to the technology so it makes more sense from a modern perspective (your real world cellphone shouldn’t be half the size of the cellphone your character uses in game. They’re hoping to release that within a year. A
        <b>
         Cyberpunk 2077
        </b>
        put out to coincide with the video game is possible but there are currently no promises there.
       </p>
       <p>
        (
        <a href="https://forum.rpg.net/showthread.php?809153-Any-more-news-on-R-Talsorian-Cyberpunk&amp;p=21320945#post21320945">
         Source
        </a>
        )
       </p>
      </blockquote>
      <p>
       I kind of wonder… Will “Cyberpunk Red” just replace the core rulebook? Will all the sourcebooks checked, modified and reprinted (unlikely, I would guess). Will the game be able to have a comeback? In the sense of being a relevant near future game, I mean, not as a retro-future game. Will it even be played again?
      </p>
      <p>
       And why would they put in the effort? Mike has the licence for the Witcher RPG. That has huge potential, if they are fast enough. The game has been out there quite a while now, and the buzz is fading. Why divert resources? Especially if they could probably reboot the franchise with the Cyberpunk ’77 computergame and have the rulebook on the shelves, when the crpg comes out?
      </p>

     <h2 id="post-1583">
      <a href="https://www.cyberpunk2020.de/2017/01/27/coole-neue-sachen-fur-cyberpunk-2020/" rel="bookmark">
       Coole neue Sachen für Cyberpunk 2020
      </a>
     </h2>
     <p class="comments">
      <a href="https://www.cyberpunk2020.de/2017/01/27/coole-neue-sachen-fur-cyberpunk-2020/#respond">
       without comments
      </a>
     </p>
     <div class="main">
      <p>
       Es gibt ein paar sehr coole Sachen, die in jüngerer Zeit für Cyberpunk als Genre rausgekommen sind. Da ist zum einen eine Vornheim-inspirierte Settinghilfe namens
       <a href="https://neuralarchive.blogspot.de/2017/01/augmented-reality-city-kit-now.html">
        Augmented Reality.
       </a>
      </p>
      <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_1584" style="width: 167px">
       <a href="https://neuralarchive.blogspot.de/2017/01/augmented-reality-city-kit-now.html">
        <img class="wp-image-1584 size-full" height="220" src="https://www.cyberpunk2020.de/wp-content/uploads/sites/9/2017/01/AR-Front-Small.jpg" width="157"/>
       </a>
       <p class="wp-caption-text">
        Awesome Setting Help for all kinds of Cyberpunk games.
       </p>
      </div>
      <p>
       Und dann bin ich über eine tolle Serie gestolpert, die Jobs auflistet, die für jene sind, die keinen gut bezahlten Konzernjob kriegen. Das Gegenstück zum Burger wenden beim Macces könnte es sein, die Drone zu polieren, die deinen alten Job macht. Mein Favorit:
      </p>
      <blockquote>
       <p>
        <span style="font-family: Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;">
         <b>
          Rental Smart-Car Attendant:
         </b>
        </span>
        <span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif;">
         across the city, travellers rent their driver-less smart-cars by the hour or subscribe to commuter vehicles. For all their efforts, the corporations have yet to create the drone that can reliably pick the used condoms and burger wraps out of the lining between the car seat and the door.
        </span>
       </p>
      </blockquote>
      <p>
       Es gibt drei Teile:
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        <a href="https://vircadesproject.blogspot.de/2014/03/wage-slave-entry-level-jobs-for-your.html">
         Wage slave – entry level jobs for your near future dystopia!
        </a>
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="https://vircadesproject.blogspot.de/2014/03/wage-slave-part-2-restructuring.html">
         Wage slave part 2: the restructuring!
        </a>
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="https://vircadesproject.blogspot.de/2016/10/wage-slave-3-redundancy.html">
         Wage slave 3: the redundancy!
        </a>
       </li>
      </ul>

     <h2 id="post-514">
      <a href="https://www.cyberpunk2020.de/2008/05/27/you-are-being-watched/" rel="bookmark">
       You are being watched
      </a>
     </h2>
     <p class="comments">
      <a href="https://www.cyberpunk2020.de/2008/05/27/you-are-being-watched/#comments">
       with one comment
      </a>
     </p>
     <div class="main">
      <p>
       The closed circuit camera in the top right corner is part of an online demostration against german laws which focus on digitally spying on its citizens – i.e. forcing the providers to hoard data like where your mobile phone logged in, what sites you surfed to, when you phoned, who you phoned, how long you phoned. It adresses my fellow Germans to protest against this spying.
