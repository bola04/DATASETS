        <div class="span8">
         <p>
          <strong>
           Silvana Koch-Mehrin
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          <span class="s1">
           Silvana is the President and Founder of the
           <a href="http://www.womeninparliaments.org/">
            <span class="s2">
             <b>
              Women Political Leaders Global Forum (WPL)
             </b>
            </span>
           </a>
           , the worldwide network of female Politicians. WPL is an independent, non-partisan and not-for-profit foundation. Its mission is increase the number and influence of women in political leadership.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          <span class="s1">
           Silvana is a former Vice-President of the European Parliament (2009-2011) and a former MEP (2004-2014). She served in the committees on budget, on trade and on gender equality.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          <span class="s1">
           Before her time in politics she founded and ran a public affairs consultancy in Brussels, which later merged with a larger US firm.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          <span class="s1">
           In addition to her work for WPL, Silvana acts as Senior Advisor to EY Ernst &amp; Young, Senior Policy Advisor to gplus europe, and provides guidance on EU regulatory issues to a range of companies and governments.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          <span class="s1">
           She is a regular speaker at institutions such as the UN, OECD, World Bank and other international fora.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          <span class="s1">
           With her three kids and their Irish father she lives in Brussels, Belgium.
          </span>
         </p>
