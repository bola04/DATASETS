         </h2>

          <p>
           <img alt="" class="alignright wp-image-30317 size-medium" height="166" sizes="(max-width: 250px) 100vw, 250px" src="https://www.djk-dom-minden.de/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/Pressfoto-250x166.jpg" srcset="https://www.djk-dom-minden.de/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/Pressfoto-250x166.jpg 250w, https://www.djk-dom-minden.de/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/Pressfoto-100x66.jpg 100w, https://www.djk-dom-minden.de/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/Pressfoto-768x511.jpg 768w, https://www.djk-dom-minden.de/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/Pressfoto-500x332.jpg 500w" width="250"/>
