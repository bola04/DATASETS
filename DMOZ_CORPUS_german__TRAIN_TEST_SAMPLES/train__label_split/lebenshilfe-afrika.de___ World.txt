       <h2>
        Aid for orphans in Afrika
       </h2>
       <p>
        In 1997,a private initiative began with the aid of Werner Schwarz for orphans in Tanzania in the village Kamishango (Kagera region). The will to extend such aid led to the foundation of our association "Lebenshilfe für Afrika e. V." in 2000. All members of the association are volunteers.
        <br/>
        Originally starting with 11 founding members, we now have attracted over 140 members.
       </p>

       <h3>
        <strong>
         Support Lebenshilfe für Afrika for free through online shopping
         <br/>
        </strong>
       </h3>
       <p>
        Through the cooperation with the social enterprise clicks4charity.de  you can now collect money for L.f.A. when shopping online - completely  free of charge! Please also let the younger generation know about this  opportunity to donate.
       </p>
       <p>
        clicks4charity cooperates with well-known Internet companies who  voluntarily donate a percentage of the sales value to a charity of your  choice. There is no additional cost, no registration is required and  furthermore the donation is only three mouse clicks away.
        <br/>
        Companies participating are, for example Amazon, Karstadt.de, Conrad, Neckermann and many more!
       </p>
       <p>
        How 	it works:
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         Visit 	the website at
         <a href="http://www.clicks4charity.net/index.php?cid=514&amp;sid=152" target="_blank">
          clicks4charity.net/lebenshilfe-afrika
         </a>
         . You will find 	the L.f.A. Logo in "1. Charity" already selected
        </li>
       </ul>
       <ul>
        <li>
         Choose 	under "2." the desired company (your shop) and
        </li>
       </ul>
       <ul>
        <li>
         3. 	"Yes, I want to help" and "buy now".
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        You are then redirected to the company's website and can start shopping.
        <br/>
        Please test this method and pass on this information to friends and friends.
        <br/>
        Thank you for your support.
       </p>
