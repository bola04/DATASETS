      <h4>
       Dr. Jezdinsky Patient Letter
      </h4>
      <p>
       Toronto, Nov.-23-15
      </p>
      <p>
       Dear Patient,
      </p>
      <p>
       Let me share some exciting developments underway at Kingsway Dental! We are relocating my practice and joining Dr. Schindler, a fellow dentist who has practiced in this neighbourhood for more than 16 years.
      </p>
      <p>
       My patients always come first. To that end I have spent a lot of time with Dr. Schindler and his staff and am excited to team up with these great professionals. I will continue to deliver the high level of restorative dentistry - from general dentistry to cosmetic, crown and bridge and implants - with the meticulous attention to detail for which I have earned a reputation. My hygienist, Doris  Marusic will continue to look after your hygiene needs. This move allows me to focus more on what I do best – great dentistry, and less on overseeing administration and the upkeep of a large office.
      </p>
      <p>
       Here are the important changes you need to know:
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        <b>
         New Location
        </b>
        : The new office is at
        <u>
         <a href="https://www.google.ca/maps/place/Toronto+West+Professional+Centre,+2425+Bloor+St+W,+Toronto,+ON+M6S+4W4/@43.6493759,-79.4859231,17z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m2!3m1!1s0x882b3688261c369b:0xc7781c2a0b345847">
          2425 Bloor St. West, Suite 310
         </a>
        </u>
        (3rd floor, has two elevators)
        <i>
         which is just around the corner
        </i>
        . Up-to-date equipment and experienced staff.
       </li>
       <li>
        <b>
         Parking
        </b>
        : Continue to park at the church on South Kingsway!
       </li>
       <li>
        <b>
         Reception
        </b>
        : Reach reception at
        <u>
         416-767-5459
        </u>
        . Dr. Schindler’s receptionist will have all your information on file and take care of your booking, whether it’s a routine cleaning, check-up or continuing treatment with me.
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       We look forward to your next visit at the new location. Thank you for making us your trusted partner when it comes to your oral health. We value your business, your trust and your loyalty. Feel free to drop me a line at
       <a href="mailto:dentistry@drschindler.ca">
        dentistry@drschindler.ca
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       Sincerely Yours,
       <br/>
       Dr. Richard F.Jezdinsky
       <br/>
       <a href="http://kingsway.dental">
        www.kingsway.dental
