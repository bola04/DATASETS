       <h2>
        Isabella Devinast, Sculptress, Berlin
       </h2>
       <p>
        Steel caught my heart when I was sixteen.
        <br/>
        Fourteen years later, I modeled in wax and made my first bronze. In 2010, I added chocolate and acrylic plaster, and in 2017, I discovered clay for myself and further started my first stucco.
       </p>
       <!--<p>If you ever had the possibility to see how steel is milled, have seen how a blast furnace plant works, this vast impression will never leave your heart again. It is an act of pure creation, extremely impressive, sheer energy.</p>
