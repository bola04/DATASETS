          <div class="ce_text block">
           <h1 style="text-align: center;">
            Innovative part cleaning for development and industry
           </h1>
           <h2 style="text-align: center;">
            <br/>
            <em>
             "The genius of a construction lies in its simplicity.
            </em>
            <br/>
            <em>
             Everyone can build complex."
            </em>
           </h2>
           <h3 style="text-align: center;">
            Sergei Pawlowitsch Koroljow, Russian aerospace pioneer, 1907 – 1966
            <br/>
            <br/>
            <br/>
           </h3>
          </div>
          <div class="ce_text keinabstand block">
           <p style="text-align: center;">
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            Growing quality requirements for components call for increasingly sophisticated solutions for industrial part cleaning processes. Ch.Batsch Verfahrenstechnik is taking an alternative approach.
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            Our innovative and simple degreaser and part cleaning equipment meets the highest expectations for cleanliness requirements from a wide range of industries, such as mechanical part manufacturing, aerospace, medical engineering, food processing, electrical and electronic industry, or lab engineering.
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            Highly efficient precision and maximum precision cleaning due to vapor degreasing with
            <a href="de/teilereinigung">
             IDO 36
            </a>
            and
            <a href="de/teilereinigung">
             IDO 72
            </a>
            and maximum efficiency solvent reclaiming with vaporizers from the
            <a href="de/loesungsmittelrecycling/vakuumdestillationsanlage-desta">
             DESTA
            </a>
            and
            <a href="de/wasseraufbereitung/atmosphaerische-destille-sld">
             SLD
            </a>
            type series
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            Residual solvent in the concentrate: &lt;1%!
           </p>
          </div>
