         Patent Search, Patent Monitoring, Legal Status Monitoring, Patent Analysis - a software solution for all questions regarding the monitoring of third party IP rights
        </h1>
        <p>
         One of the world's largest patent databases for research and for regular competitor monitoring. Well engineered workflow components for the display of the individual workflow in a company (comments, evaluations). A work environment tailored to the tasks and experience of every user. Analysis of patent portfolios (without limitations), of public data or of evaluated IP rights. Standalone versions with optimized export options for the researcher. Company-wide solutions for the collabrotaion across all teams in a company.
        </p>
