         <h2>
          History
         </h2>
         <p>
          Daume Regelarmaturen GmbH is a medium-sized company in Isernhagen near Hanover focussing on the development and manufacturing of control valves for the whole energy supply field and for many fields of process engineering, such as for the chemical industry, processes in power plant engineering, the mineral oil processing industry, pharmaceutical industry and the steel industry.
         </p>
         <p>
          Daume Regelarmaturen GmbH valves are used throughout the world. The company's product range is based on a module system enabling the customer to combine the various valve types with the most varied types of actuators.
         </p>
         <p>
          We particularly specialize on regulating water and steam and on special-purpose valves.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Our Range of Work and Services
         </h2>
         <p>
          We provide you with comprehensive engineering in the development/design, manufacturing, testing and fitting of special-purpose valves.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="product_development-(1).jpg" src="/getattachment/About/Daume-Regelarmaturen-GmbH/product_development-(1).jpg" style="margin: auto 20px; float: left;" title="product_development-(1).jpg"/>
          <strong>
           Product Development
          </strong>
          <br/>
          We come up with the most economic solution for any field of application – be it with standard modules or individually designed products.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="precision_manufacturing.jpg" src="/getattachment/About/Daume-Regelarmaturen-GmbH/precision_manufacturing.jpg" style="margin: auto 20px; float: left;" title="precision_manufacturing.jpg"/>
          <strong>
           Precision Manufacturing
          </strong>
          <br/>
          <span style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.5;">
           Product selection is ensured from the use of the most up-to-date manufacturing facilities and highly skilled workers.
          </span>
         </p>

         </p>
