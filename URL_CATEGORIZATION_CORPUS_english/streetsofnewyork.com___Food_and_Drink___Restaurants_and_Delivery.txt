       <h1>
        Phoenix Old World Italian Restaurant
       </h1>
       <img alt="Something for everyone" class="img-responsive" src="https://www.streetsofnewyork.com/uploaded_files/images/FoodImages/Something-for-everyone.jpg?1526419579620" style="border-style:solid; border-width:0px; float:right; margin:0px 5px" title="Something for everyone"/>
       Since 1976 Streets of New York has been serving authentic east coast taste to Phoenix, Arizona. Our goal regardless of whether you are at home with friends, ordering a delivery, picking up on your way home from work or dining in one of our stores, we want to be your favorite local Italian restaurant.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       From our delicious chopped antipasto salads and pasta to Toscana garlic chicken and New York style pizza we are committed to serving the finest ingredients in every dish we serve.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       The vision of serving great tasting Italian food and being the neighborhood gathering spot helped us create our family business, “Streets of New York”.
       <h2>
        When it comes to quality, we’re not just talk…we’re about taste!
       </h2>
       Streets of New York is committed to serving the finest of ingredients to you and your palate. This starts with choosing vendors that represent healthy; safety tested and consistent products to ensure quality ingredients that we deliver to your table.
       <ul>
        <li>
         Sausage is non-GMO and antibiotic free
         <br/>
        </li>
        <li>
         Our Italian pasta is chosen for healthier, higher quality with increased protein levels
         <br/>
        </li>
        <li>
         Pizza sauce and marinara recipes are made with the finest grade Italian tomatoes imported from Naples Italy
         <br/>
        </li>
        <li>
         Tomatoes are freshly packed in aluminum free cans with no added salt or preservatives
         <br/>
        </li>
        <li>
         We proudly offer an herb flavor “Gluten Free” crust
         <br/>
        </li>
        <li>
         We use the highest quality, 100% real mozzarella cheese
         <br/>
        </li>
        <li>
         The extra effort even trickles down to the Tuscan EVOO offered in our restaurants and used in recipes.
        </li>
       </ul>
       <h2>
        Phoenix and Pizza go hand in hand!
       </h2>
       <img alt="New York Style Pizza" class="img-responsive" src="https://www.streetsofnewyork.com/uploaded_files/images/FoodImages/Pizza-300.jpg?1526419685324" style="border-style:solid; border-width:0px; float:right; margin:0px 5px" title="New York Style Pizza"/>
       Did you know that Phoenix is one of the nation’s premiere pizza towns? At Streets of New York, we have been proudly serving our New York Style pie for over 40 years.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       From the Mediterranean to the Classic Margherita, you can even create your own New York Pizza.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       From traditional crust to thin and crispy all of our pizza crust is brushed with mouth-watering garlic butter and it’s topped with our original, 41-year-old pizza sauce which is made with the finest grade Italian tomatoes imported from Naples Italy.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       From freshly made dough made daily to 100% real mozzarella cheese, dine it take it home or have it delivered.
       <h2>
        Gluten Free Pizza Crust
       </h2>
       Streets of New York proudly offers an herb flavor “Gluten Free” crust. The official “Gluten Free Organization ” backs us in keeping up with their standards.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       The
       <a href="http://www.gfco.org/about-us/who-we-are/">
        Gluten-Free Certification Organization (GFCO)
       </a>
       , is an industry program of the Gluten Intolerance Group (GIG). Dedicated to providing certification services to producers of gluten-free products to provide assurance to consumers of the safety of their foods.
       <h2>
        Italian dishes for every palate
       </h2>
       <img alt="Cheese Ravioli" class="img-responsive" src="https://www.streetsofnewyork.com/uploaded_files/images/FoodImages/Cheese-ravioli.jpg?1526419974995" style="border-style:solid; border-width:0px; float:right; height:401px; margin:0px 5px; width:600px" title="Cheese Ravioli"/>
       From “old world” meatballs to chicken parmigiana, spaghetti calzones, chopped antipasto and luscious lasagna
       <a href="https://www.streetsofnewyork.com/streets-of-new-york-menus.cfm">
        our menu
       </a>
       is chalk full of wonderful delights including broasted chicken. We only use Tuscan EVOO (Extra virgin olive oil) and herbs and spices that are custom blended exclusively for us to create a taste sensation in every dish.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Craving a delectable chopped antipasto with our robust and zesty Italian Dressing? We will take good care of your taste buds with our 41-year-old secret recipe.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       After the plates have been cleared try our traditional Italian cannoli’s that are prepared by an east coast bakery!
       <h2>
        Catering to more than just your palate
       </h2>
       With more than 30 years of
       <a href="https://www.streetsofnewyork.com/catering-and-parties.cfm">
        catering experience
       </a>
       , we can help you make a lasting impression on your special event with our freshly made pastas and tantalizing salads. Of course if pasta isn’t what you’re looking for we do offer an extensive menu of catering options to choose from that suit any taste.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       From
       <a href="https://www.streetsofnewyork.com/streets-of-new-york-catering-parties/wedding-catering.cfm">
        Weddings
       </a>
       to
       <a href="https://www.streetsofnewyork.com/streets-of-new-york-catering-parties/corporate-catering.cfm">
        Corporate events
       </a>
       and even school lunch programs and
       <a href="https://www.streetsofnewyork.com/streets-of-new-york-catering-parties/special-events-catering.cfm">
        special events
       </a>
       experience our stress-free, professional, quality catering services.
       <h2>
        Streets of New York locations to serve you
       </h2>
       Since our humble beginning, we have proudly grown to cover not just the Phoenix Metro area, but Prescott, AZ and Las Vegas, NV as well. View our
       <a href="https://www.streetsofnewyork.com/store-locations.cfm">
        26 convenient Streets of New York locations
       </a>
       . We welcome you to stop in for a satisfying slice and a taste of New York.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Live well, maintain health and celebrate life!
