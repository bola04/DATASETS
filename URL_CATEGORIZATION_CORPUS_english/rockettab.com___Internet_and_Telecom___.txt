      <h2>
       What Is RocketTab?
      </h2>
      <p>
       RocketTab is the free local search enhancer on the web. Once you download RocketTab for free and install it, you will be able to conveniently search popular local sites without having to open additional windows or tabs, going to multiple sites and then performing the same searches on each of them. One search and voila! The RocketTab browser application is and will always remain FREE to download and use. RocketTab is supported via text advertising.
      </p>
      <h3>
       How Does It Work?
      </h3>
      <p>
       Simply search and RocketTab results will appear at the top of the search results page. Enhance your regular search experience by clicking on any of the tabs on the right hand side and customized, local results will appear. It’s that easy!
      </p>
      <ol>
       <li>
        RocketTab’s proprietary technology searches top local sites’ content by passing on your searches anonymously to those sites, grabbing the results and then displaying those relevant local results all in one place.
       </li>
       <li>
        RocketTab seamlessly layers its results and ads above the search engine’s unaltered results, enhancing your search experience by making it easier to find what you’re looking for.
       </li>
       <li>
        RocketTab is invisible unless you search.
       </li>
       <li>
        Easily disable or uninstall RocketTab directly from the app or from your operating system’s control panel.
       </li>
      </ol>
      <h3>
       Does RocketTab store user information?
      </h3>
      <p>
       No, RocketTab does not store any personal information or browsing habits. RocketTab acts as a proxy for your HTTP/S traffic and does transmit limited information about URLs visited and keywords in order to retrieve ads.
      </p>
      <h3>
       How much does RocketTab cost?
      </h3>
      <p>
       Nothing! It’s free to download at anytime for anyone.
      </p>
      <h3>
       Can you tell me more about the ads?
      </h3>
      <p>
       In order to continue to provide our users with new and cutting edge applications, RocketTab relies on minimal advertising.  Our ads are relevant and unobtrusive text ads that appear below RocketTab’s enhanced searches.  We sometimes partner with 3rd parties to distribute RocketTab.  We clearly label which product we are part of.  If you do not want RocketTab advertising, you must uninstall the product that we have partnered with.
      </p>
      <h3>
       How do I download RocketTab?
      </h3>
      <p>
       <a class="download-button" href="#">
        Download
       </a>
      </p>
      <h3>
       What browsers is RocketTab compatible with?
      </h3>
      <p>
       RocketTab is currently compatible with Internet Explorer, Firefox, and Chrome.
      </p>
      <h3>
       How do I uninstall RocketTab?
      </h3>
      <p>
       To uninstall RocketTab you must remove “RocketTab” from your list of programs. Below are the instructions on how to remove RocketTab.
      </p>
      <ol>
       <li>
        Click the Windows Start button on the taskbar
       </li>
       <li>
        Go to the Control Panel
       </li>
       <li>
        Click ‘Uninstall a Program’
       </li>
       <li>
        Identify RocketTab on the list of programs
       </li>
       <li>
        Select RocketTab and click ‘Uninstall’
       </li>
       <li>
        Complete the uninstallation process
       </li>
      </ol>
      <h3>
       Why am I getting update notifications from RocketTab?
      </h3>
      <p>
       Occasionally, our software requires updates to bring you the latest functionality. These updates will be offered to you free of charge. If you wish to decline the update, click “Advanced” and then “Stop Reminder”.
      </p>
