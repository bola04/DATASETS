             <h1 data-magellan-destination="hit-the-road-with-pride">
              <div class="field field-name-field-copy-headline field-type-text field-label-hidden field-wrapper">
               Exceeding Your Expectations is a Passion
              </div>
             </h1>
             <div class="body-copy">
              <div class="field field-name-field-body-copy field-type-text-long field-label-hidden field-wrapper">
               <p>
                Whether it’s finding new and smarter solutions or putting the finishing touches on our unique designs, we stand apart through our relentless pursuit of excellence. We know your investment, passengers’ safety, and peace of mind are top priorities. A product of those priorities, a Prevost coach is the ultimate partner regardless of whether you are looking for a premium touring or intercity commuter motorcoach or an unsurpassed quality conversion coach ready to be transformed into a high-end motorhome or specialty purpose vehicle.
               </p>
               <p>
                Prevost excellence begins at the design stage, extends through our manufacturing operations, and continues throughout the ownership experience at our best in the industry, Parts and Service Centers. A Prevost coach is a vehicle you can count on.
               </p>

             <div class="row first">
              <div class="copy medium-3 small-6 columns">
               <a name="news-feature">
               </a>
               <h2 data-magellan-destination="news-feature">
                News Feature
               </h2>
               <div class="text">
                <p>
                 <strong>
                  Prevost Ride and Drive 2018 acquainted operators with the people, products and processes
                 </strong>
                </p>
                <p>
                 Prevost took pleasure in hosting its signature Ride and Drive 2018 event in late April in Hagerstown, MD.  45 operators from 30 coach companies throughout the U.S. and Canada attended.
                </p>
                <p>
                 The Ride and Drive brings invited Prevost and Volvo 9700 operators and prospective owners together with Prevost sales and service representatives, executive staff members and suppliers for three days of company updates, new product demonstrations, maintenance and service updates; and to share in a bit of industry fellowship.
                </p>
                <p>
                 “Ride and Drive 2018 presented unique opportunities for our customers to become better acquainted with the Prevost company,” says Prevost Vice President and General Manager, Francois Tremblay. “Not only do they get to experience our coaches and innovative features first hand, they gain a sense of the Prevost culture and our core values.”
                </p>
                <p>
                 The packed agenda showcased new components and systems available on Prevost and Volvo 9700 coaches and gave operators the opportunity to participate in Prevost AWARE and Electronic Stability Program (ESP) demonstrations.
                </p>
                <p>
                </p>
