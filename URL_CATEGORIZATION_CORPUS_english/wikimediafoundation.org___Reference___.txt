        <div id="mf-intro" style="padding:.5em 1em; background:#eaf3ff; border:1px solid #a2a9b1; font-size:120%;" title="Introduction">
         <p>
          The
          <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Frequently_asked_questions" title="Frequently asked questions">
           Wikimedia Foundation
          </a>
          , Inc. is a
          <a href="#nonprofit">
           <span id="return">
            nonprofit
           </span>
          </a>
          charitable organization
          <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mission" title="Mission">
           dedicated
          </a>
          to encouraging the growth, development and distribution of
          <a class="extiw" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/en:free_content" title="w:en:free content">
           free
          </a>
          , multilingual, educational content, and to providing the full content of these
          <a class="extiw" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/wiki" title="en:wiki">
           wiki
          </a>
          -based projects to the public free of charge. The Wikimedia Foundation operates some of the
          <a href="/wiki/Our_projects" title="Our projects">
           largest collaboratively edited reference projects
          </a>
          in the world, including
          <a class="external text" href="//www.wikipedia.org">
           Wikipedia
          </a>
          , a top-ten internet property.
         </p>
         <p>
          Imagine a world in which every single human being can freely share in the sum of all knowledge. That’s our commitment.
         </p>

            <h2>
             <span class="mw-headline" id="Transparency">
              Transparency
             </span>
            </h2>
            <p>
             The Wikimedia Foundation is
             <a href="/wiki/Values" title="Values">
              proud
             </a>
             to be one of the most transparent non-profit organizations in the world. For updates about our program activities and our Annual Report, see the other links on this page. Additionally, we provide the following information:
            </p>
            <ul>
             <li>
              <b>
               Core financial information.
              </b>
              On the
              <a href="/wiki/Financial_reports" title="Financial reports">
               financial reports
              </a>
              page, you will find audited financial statements, detailed financial plans, and our form 990.
             </li>
             <li>
              <b>
               Board meeting minutes and outcomes.
              </b>
              The Wikimedia
              <a href="/wiki/Board_of_Trustees" title="Board of Trustees">
               Board of Trustees
              </a>
              is the ultimate corporate authority in the Wikimedia Foundation. On the
              <a href="/wiki/Meetings" title="Meetings">
               meetings
              </a>
              and
              <a href="/wiki/Resolutions" title="Resolutions">
               resolutions
              </a>
              pages, you will find information about its decisions.
             </li>
             <li>
              <b>
               Policies and procedures.
              </b>
              Beyond key policies like the
              <a href="/wiki/Privacy_policy" title="Privacy policy">
               privacy policy
              </a>
              ,
              <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Donor_privacy_policy" title="Donor privacy policy">
               donor privacy policy
              </a>
              and
              <a href="/wiki/Trademark_policy" title="Trademark policy">
               trademark policy
              </a>
              , we publish all other key
              <a href="/wiki/Policies" title="Policies">
               policies and procedures
              </a>
              governing Wikimedia operations.
             </li>
            </ul>
            <p>
             Wikimedia Foundation staff regularly participate in open
             <a class="extiw" href="https://meta.wikimedia.org/wiki/IRC_Office_Hours" title="m:IRC Office Hours">
              office hours
             </a>
             on IRC (an online chat system). Our fundraising work is public and community-driven as well. If you'd like to jump right in, visit the
             <a class="extiw" href="https://meta.wikimedia.org/wiki/Fundraising" title="m:Fundraising">
              community fundraising landing page
             </a>
             .
            </p>
