          </h2>
          <div class="entry-category-cemeteries entry-category-local_news entry-category-volunteerism entry-author-lisa_burks entry-type-post entry" id="entry-6a00d83454de4669e2013485c9d5fb970c">

            <h3 class="entry-header">
             <a href="http://lisaburks.typepad.com/home/2010/07/grand-view-memorial-park.html">
              Grand View Memorial Park
             </a>
            </h3>
            <div class="entry-content">
             <input name="zemanta-related" type="hidden" val=""/>
             <div class="entry-body">
              <p>
               I continue to volunteer at
               <strong>
                Grand View Memorial Park
               </strong>
               in
               <strong>
                Glendale, Calif
               </strong>
               . The class action suit has been settled, claimants have been paid from insurance monies. The court also assigned $500,000 from those same monies to be used to restore records to the office, install a new watering system and other maintenance with funds left over.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="http://lisaburks.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83454de4669e2013485c9cbff970c-pi">
                <img alt="IMG01279" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d83454de4669e2013485c9cbff970c " src="http://lisaburks.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83454de4669e2013485c9cbff970c-450wi" style="width: 450px; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"/>
               </a>
              </p>
              <p>
               Court ordered visitations continue while the landscape architect finalizes plans for the irrigation system. Grounds are purposely being left to dry out to enable ease of vegetation removal prior to trenches being dug and pipes being laid.
              </p>
              <p>
               The next visitation date is this
               <strong>
                Sunday, August 1st from 12-4pm
               </strong>
               .
              </p>
              <p>
               For more information, visit the website I created and maintain to keep the public informed about news there at
               <strong>
                <a href="http://www.grandviewmemorialpark.info" target="_blank">
                 GrandViewMemorialPark.info
                </a>
               </strong>
               .
               <a href="http://lisaburks.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83454de4669e2013485c9cbff970c-pi">
                <br/>
               </a>
               <br/>
               .
              </p>
