          <h2 class="entry-title">
           <a href="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/emo-hairstyles-defined/" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to Emo Hairstyles Defined">
            Emo Hairstyles Defined
           </a>
          </h2>
          <p class="meta-line">
           <a class="meta-label meta-date" href="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/2015/10/30/" rel="bookmark" title="11:29 pm">
            <i class="icon-left icon-calendar">
            </i>
            <time class="entry-date" datetime="2015-10-30T23:29:18+00:00">
             October 30, 2015
            </time>
           </a>
           <span class="meta-label meta-publisher vcard">
            Published by:
            <a href="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/author/admin/" rel="author" title="Posts by admin">
             admin
            </a>
           </span>
          </p>
         </header>
         <!-- .entry-header -->
         <div class="row">
          <div class="columns sixteen">
           <div class="thumbnail-outer format-ico post-ico">
            <a href="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/emo-hairstyles-defined/">
             <img alt="Sweet-Emo-Guys-Hairstyles" class="attachment-single-post-small size-single-post-small" height="200" src="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/Sweet-Emo-Guys-Hairstyles-266x200.jpg" width="266"/>
            </a>
           </div>
           <div class="entry-content">
            <p>
             An emo image was traditionally associated with angsty teenagers. Loneliness, melancholy and emotional mood swings were often associated with it too. You can say an emo image was associated with pessimistic feelings. However, this is no longer true in the modern world. Emo teenagers are no longer depressive and emo hairstyles now extend even to the working class.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/how-to-get-an-emo-hairstyle/" target="_blank">
              Emo hairstyles
             </a>
             now express one’s individuality to the core and convey a certain vulnerability or sensitivity that shows courage and strength. Emo hairstyles are no longer confined in a box as they are versatile and can mean many different styles.
            </p>
            <p>
             Some key characteristics remain the same though. They are the following:
            </p>
            <h2>
             1. Bangs
            </h2>
            <p>
             <img alt="19c635749978124d_medium_length_haircuts" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-36" height="300" sizes="(max-width: 225px) 100vw, 225px" src="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/19c635749978124d_medium_length_haircuts-225x300.jpg" srcset="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/19c635749978124d_medium_length_haircuts-225x300.jpg 225w, http://www.myemohairstyles.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/19c635749978124d_medium_length_haircuts.jpg 300w" width="225"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             It’s all about the bangs. And by bangs, we mean not the wispy kind but full bangs swept to the side or just right on the forehead. Emo bangs are created by cutting several layers of hair and sweeping it to the side. Usually longer than traditional bangs so the eyes and a cheekbone are partially covered. Messy, straight – you can make it your own!
            </p>
            <h2>
             2. Layers
            </h2>
            <p>
             <img alt="Emo-Boys-Hairstyle-with-Bangs" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-50" height="300" src="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/Emo-Boys-Hairstyle-with-Bangs-256x300.jpg" width="256"/>
             <img alt="Sweet-Emo-Guys-Hairstyles" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-52" height="300" sizes="(max-width: 210px) 100vw, 210px" src="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/Sweet-Emo-Guys-Hairstyles-210x300.jpg" srcset="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/Sweet-Emo-Guys-Hairstyles-210x300.jpg 210w, http://www.myemohairstyles.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/Sweet-Emo-Guys-Hairstyles.jpg 472w" width="210"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             Emo hairstyles are all about creating layers. The mullet is a perfect example of an emo hairstyle. Choppy cuts – hair at the crown is cut shorter than the rest – are characteristic of this style. Bob hair cuts get an emo twist by making the front hair longer with the hair at the back gradually becomes shorter.
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <h2>
             3. Color
            </h2>
            <p>
             <img alt="Short-Colored-Emo-Hairstyle" class="size-medium wp-image-37 aligncenter" height="300" sizes="(max-width: 221px) 100vw, 221px" src="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/Short-Colored-Emo-Hairstyle-221x300.jpg" srcset="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/Short-Colored-Emo-Hairstyle-221x300.jpg 221w, http://www.myemohairstyles.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/Short-Colored-Emo-Hairstyle.jpg 461w" width="221"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/coloring-an-emo-hairstyle-tips-and-tricks-of-the-trade/" target="_blank">
              Color highlights an emo hairstyle
             </a>
             – literally and figuratively. Black and brunettes work great with emo highlights such as green, blue or red.
            </p>
            <h2>
             4. Styling
            </h2>
            <p>
             <img alt="e3aee713eff50b9accda89d07cc80700" class="size-medium wp-image-38 aligncenter" height="300" src="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/e3aee713eff50b9accda89d07cc80700-197x300.jpg" width="197"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/fashionable-emo-hairstyles/" target="_blank">
              Styling
             </a>
             further defines an emo hairstyle. Backcombing – or combing hair towards the face (and not away) is a trademark style. Teasing the upper portion of the hair is normal, generally held together by wax.
            </p>
            <p>
             Little things you can do with your hair to get an emo look:
            </p>
            <ol>
             <li>
              Add a chunky hair color highlight.
             </li>
             <li>
              Snip off hair edges by thinning them out at the tips – a razor cut does this trick.
             </li>
             <li>
              Play around with bangs! Usually, just styling with bangs create an emo look already. Trim a thick section of your hair at the crown, so that your bangs can cover half the forehead and usually one eye.
             </li>
             <li>
              Straighten your hair, especially at the edges. Emo looks are usually tousled at the top ending with straight edges to the side of the face (you could always
              <a href="https://www.amazon.co.uk/dp/B0151CNZX0" target="_blank">
               try vitamin c serum to reduce wrinkles
              </a>
              !).
             </li>
            </ol>
           </div>

          <h2 class="entry-title">
           <a href="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/fashionable-emo-hairstyles/" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to Fashionable Emo Hairstyles">
            Fashionable Emo Hairstyles
           </a>
          </h2>
          <p class="meta-line">
           <a class="meta-label meta-date" href="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/2015/08/15/" rel="bookmark" title="11:25 pm">
            <i class="icon-left icon-calendar">

         <div class="row">
          <div class="columns sixteen">
           <div class="thumbnail-outer format-ico post-ico">
            <a href="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/fashionable-emo-hairstyles/">
             <img alt="2355" class="attachment-single-post-small size-single-post-small" height="200" src="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/2355-266x200.jpg" width="266"/>
            </a>
           </div>
           <div class="entry-content">
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/how-to-get-an-emo-hairstyle/" target="_blank">
              Emo hairstyles
             </a>
             are usually constrained to rock groups and emo teeners but the truth is, you easily find these styles in the runway, sported by celebrities and even donned by career women (and even men!) and executives.
             <a href="http://www.cosmopolitan.com/style-beauty/news/a42632/fire-color-hair/" target="_blank">
              Cosmopolitan
             </a>
             reports women are even setting their hair on fire by dyeing their strands fiery red these days.
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             Here are four great examples of emo hairstyles that have gone mainstream:
            </p>
            <p>
             <img alt="2347" class="alignnone wp-image-44 size-medium alignleft" height="300" sizes="(max-width: 200px) 100vw, 200px" src="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/2347-200x300.jpg" srcset="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/2347-200x300.jpg 200w, http://www.myemohairstyles.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/2347.jpg 600w" width="200"/>
             <img alt="2355" class="alignnone wp-image-45 size-medium alignleft" height="300" sizes="(max-width: 250px) 100vw, 250px" src="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/2355-250x300.jpg" srcset="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/2355-250x300.jpg 250w, http://www.myemohairstyles.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/2355.jpg 520w" width="250"/>
             <img alt="2334" class="alignnone wp-image-43 size-medium alignleft" height="300" sizes="(max-width: 264px) 100vw, 264px" src="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/2334-264x300.jpg" srcset="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/2334-264x300.jpg 264w, http://www.myemohairstyles.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/2334.jpg 528w" width="264"/>
             <img alt="2330" class="alignnone wp-image-42 size-medium alignleft" height="300" sizes="(max-width: 240px) 100vw, 240px" src="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/2330-240x300.jpg" srcset="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/2330-240x300.jpg 240w, http://www.myemohairstyles.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/2330.jpg 560w" width="240"/>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             From left to right:
            </p>
            <h2>
             1. Auburn Emo Hairstyle
            </h2>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/coloring-an-emo-hairstyle-tips-and-tricks-of-the-trade/" target="_blank">
              Black base hair color
             </a>
             s are the norm for emo looks, but you can always try this auburn hue. It is still dark enough and with the right makeup, you can pull off a rock vampire chic look like the one pictured above. It is also formal enough to pull off wearing in an office environment. Bonus? Bangs in a wispy curvy style are good for softening a square face (strong jaw) or reducing a high (large) forehead in heart-shaped faces.
            </p>
            <h2>
             2. Platinum Blonde Stacked Bob
            </h2>
            <p>
             Bob haircuts can instantly turn emo with how you style it. By backcombing layers towards your face, you instantly get an emo effect. Platinum blonde is always glamorous. Coupled with an asymmetrical cut, this is flattering for round-shaped faces. Easily add height and face length by teasing top layers.

            </p>
            <p>
             Make them shine by using a hair serum to make for a glamorous look. You can also use red and purple accents to further highlight your fringe.
            </p>
            <h2>
             4. Textured Bob
            </h2>
            <p>
             Not ready to go all out emo yet? Try this textured and angled bob with long heavy bangs. Your layers can be cut to the side and when you style your hair, tousle them slightly with wax to make it edgy.

            How to Get an Emo Hairstyle
           </a>
          </h2>
          <p class="meta-line">
           <a class="meta-label meta-date" href="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/2015/03/23/" rel="bookmark" title="11:21 pm">
            <i class="icon-left icon-calendar">
            </i>

            </a>
           </div>
           <div class="entry-content">
            <p>
             While emo hairstyles back in the day were trademark hairstyles of rockers, musicians and anime (Japanese, Taiwanese and Korean pop, manga/comics) lovers, these are now considered mainstream hairstyles, with most celebrities rocking the ‘do in one form of another.
            </p>
            <h2>
             What makes a hairstyle ‘emo’?
            </h2>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             <img alt="143" class="aligncenter wp-image-33 size-large" height="391" sizes="(max-width: 625px) 100vw, 625px" src="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/143-1024x640.jpg" srcset="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/143-300x188.jpg 300w, http://www.myemohairstyles.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/143-768x480.jpg 768w, http://www.myemohairstyles.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/143-1024x640.jpg 1024w, http://www.myemohairstyles.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/143-624x390.jpg 624w" width="625"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             Fringe bangs, sheared asymmetrical haircuts and bold colors are trademarks of emo hairstyles.
            </p>
            <h2>
             How to Get Emo Hair
            </h2>
            <p>
            </p>
            <ol>
             <li>
              Get a haircut. Emo haircuts usually have chunky layers that are bluntly cut with side-swept full bangs that extend to the eyebrows.
             </li>
            </ol>
            <ol start="2">
             <li>
              Ask for a razor trim. Emo haircuts usually feature super-sharp, straight edges. You can ask the hairstylist to use a razor comb on the ends of your hair to achieve this cut.
             </li>
            </ol>
            <ol start="3">
             <li>
              Thin hair on the bottom. Most emo haircuts feature styles that are teased and thick on top but thin and straight at the bottom. To achieve this, ask your hairstylist to think out the bottom part of your hair, about three to four inches.
             </li>
            </ol>
            <ol start="4">
             <li>
              Color your hair. This is an optional step but emo hair colors usually include bold hues – jet black, blonde, neon streaks, etc.
             </li>
            </ol>
            <ol start="5">
             <li>
              Do it gradually. you can always make it shorter, but growing hair, especially when it is cut in layers takes a while. Stay a bit longer if you are trying the emo cut for the first time. You can have it shorter next time.
             </li>
            </ol>
            <ol start="6">
             <li>
              To style your emo hair, tease your top layers and apply hairspray. Use a fine-toothed comb to backcomb your hair (moving comb towards your scalp, not away from it). You can use a hair straightener to achieve perfectly straight hair at the edges.
             </li>
            </ol>
            <h2>
             How to take care of your Emo Hairstyle
            </h2>
            <p>
             Dying, teasing and straightening your hair to achieve an emo hairstyle can be damaging to your strands. Here are a few ways to take care of your hair:
            </p>
            <ol>
             <li>
              Watch out for split ends. To prevent split ends, have your hair trimmed every 6 to 8 weeks.
             </li>
            </ol>
            <ol start="2">
             <li>
              Use a heat protectant before you use a hair straightener when styling.
             </li>
            </ol>
            <ol start="3">
             <li>
              If you wish to style your hair everyday, train your hair so that it can be washed once or twice a week. Or you can use natural organic shampoo products that are guaranteed to be gentle with your strands.
             </li>
            </ol>
            <ol start="4">
             <li>
              Use the right hair care products. Use products that don’t contain sodium sulfates.
             </li>
            </ol>
            <ol start="5">
             <li>
              Take a hair styling break at least once a week to let your hair rest from products. You can wear a hat over it instead or tie your hair back.
             </li>
            </ol>

          <h2 class="entry-title">
           <a href="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/what-is-emo/" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to What is Emo?">
            What is Emo?
           </a>
          </h2>
          <p class="meta-line">
           <a class="meta-label meta-date" href="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/2007/12/13/" rel="bookmark" title="5:01 am">
            <i class="icon-left icon-calendar">
            </i>
            <time class="entry-date" datetime="2007-12-13T05:01:20+00:00">
             December 13, 2007
            </time>
           </a>
           <span class="meta-label meta-publisher vcard">
            Published by:
            <a href="http://www.myemohairstyles.com/author/admin/" rel="author" title="Posts by admin">
             admin
            </a>
           </span>
          </p>
         </header>
         <!-- .entry-header -->
         <div class="row">
          <div class="columns sixteen">
           <div class="entry-content">
            <p>
             <strong>
              Emo hair
             </strong>
             ,
             <strong>
              Emo hairstyles
             </strong>
             and
             <strong>
              emo haircuts
             </strong>
             are characterized by often black hair with random splurges of bright coloured highlights. The emo haircuts may also have asymmetrical lines and different colour background hair but the large majority of people with emo hairstyles prefer to dye it black.
            </p>
