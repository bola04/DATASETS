      <p>
       The OpenBSD project produces a
       <b>
        FREE
       </b>
       , multi-platform 4.4BSD-based
       UNIX-like operating system. Our efforts emphasize portability,
       standardization, correctness,
       <a href="security.html">
        proactive security
       </a>
       and
       <a href="crypto.html">
        integrated cryptography
       </a>
       .  As an example of the
       effect OpenBSD has, the popular
       <a href="https://www.openssh.com">
        OpenSSH
       </a>
       software comes from OpenBSD.
      </p>
      <p>
       OpenBSD is freely available from our download sites.
      </p>
      <p>
       The current release is
       <a href="63.html">
        OpenBSD 6.3
       </a>
       , released Apr 15, 2018.
      </p>
      <p>
       OpenBSD is developed entirely by volunteers.
       The project's development environment
       and
       <a href="hackathons.html">
        developer events
       </a>
       are funded through contributions collected by
       <a href="https://www.openbsdfoundation.org">
        The OpenBSD Foundation
       </a>
       .
       Contributions ensure that OpenBSD will remain a vibrant and
       <a href="policy.html">
        free
       </a>
       operating system.
      </p>
