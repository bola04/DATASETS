         <h3>
          We are gathering and investigating information about interfaces of modern and obsolete electronic hardware including pinouts of interface ports, expansion slots, other connectors of computers and other electronic devices.  
 Information about functions of pins in connector (pinout) is essential for everyone who wants to investigate modern computer hardware, explore the way it's works, connect various devices to computer, produce DIY electronic devices.
         </h3>

         <p>
          Equipment described herein includes modern and obsolete computers, peripherals,
 mobile phones and other electronic devices. We are not publishing pinouts of integrated circuit (IC) or their datasheets.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          The content of site is divided in some categories.
          <br/>
          Pinouts of computer hardware components: videocards, power supply units, harddisks are in "Computer Hardware". It also includes information about some standard electrical interfaces.
          <br/>
          Pinouts of external connectors of various devices (cellular phones,GPS,UPS) and their peripheral (headsets, PSU) are in "Devices Connectors".
          <br/>
          Section "Cables and Adapters" covers schemes of cables for a wide variety of electronic equipment. Please note that schemes of cables may be placed into other sections also.
          <br/>
          The "Pinouts by Vendor" covers mostly cellular phones, other portable devices and cars. It's not possible to find a pinout of computer components here.
          <br/>
          If you know view of your connector you may search for it in "Connectors" section. Please note that drawing can differ from original connector (count pins and search for similarly shaped connector).
         </p>
         <p>
          Sometimes pinouts of electronic devices interfaces are available in open-access documentation, but often this information is hidden and not officially published. Most of our documents are created by our visitors, written by pinouts.ru(pinoutguide.com) team or collected from public available sources (with link(s) to origin(s) included).
         </p>

          Content of this site is the result of our collective work started in 2000 year.
