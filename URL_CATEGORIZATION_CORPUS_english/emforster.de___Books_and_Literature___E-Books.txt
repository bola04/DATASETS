               <p>
                Welcome to
                <i>
                 Aspects of E.M. Forster
                </i>
                . This site has been on-line since March 2000. My aim is to fit you out with information about E. M. Forster, his life and literature. Contributions are always welcomed.
               </p>
               <p>
                This site contains an extensive
                <a href="template.php3?t=life">
                 biography
                </a>
                of E.M. Forster. Moreover, you can find a list of
                <a href="template.php3?t=pictures">
                 annotated images
                </a>
                of Forster, his family and friends, the places of his life etc. Another important element is the
                <a href="template.php3?t=work">
                 writings
                </a>
                section, in which you can read about both Forster's fictional and his non-fictional texts and their film adaptations. This includes
                <a href="template.php3?t=work">
                 summaries
                </a>
                ,
                <a href="template.php3?t=work">
                 character lists
                </a>
                ,
                <a href="template.php3?t=work">
                 interpretations
                </a>
                and
                <a href="template.php3?t=work">
                 selected essays
                </a>
                . If you want to do research on Forster, you can rely upon the
                <a href="template.php3?t=bibliography">
                 bibliography
                </a>
                section, which marries available printed literature to on-line texts. If this is insufficient, you can use the
                <a href="template.php3?t=board">
                 message board
                </a>
                to discuss problems concerning Forster's life and literature with other users of this site, or you can try to find the respective piece of information at another site, linked to through the
                <a href="template.php3?t=link">
                 annotated links
                </a>
                list. The
                <a href="template.php3?t=numb">
                 numbers
                </a>
                area provides the results of occasional polls concerning various aspects of Forster's life and writings.
               </p>
