        <h2>

        </h2>
        Welcome to the home of Rainbow Landing™ Nectar. This diet is especially formulated to meet the intensive needs of an interactive Lorikeet feeding experience as well as to provide these special birds with all of the nutritional requirements needed for optimal health, beautiful plumage and successful breeding. With the assistance of an avian nutritionist this diet was formulated to meet the specific nutritional needs of Lories and Lorikeets.
 
 Rainbow Landing™ Nectar diet is one with the lowest in iron content found on the market, it contains no added Vitamin A and only the freshest of ingredients. Where possible, natural, organic and fair trade ingredients have been sourced out to be included. With a professionally recommended calcium to phosphorus ratio, fresh bee pollen, and the supplementation of vitamins, minerals and amino acids, it is the most nutritious diet available for members of the family, Loriidae.
