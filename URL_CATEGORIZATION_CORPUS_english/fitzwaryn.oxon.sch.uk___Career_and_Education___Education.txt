        <div class="Radius" id="About">
         <h1 id="PageTitle">
          Welcome
         </h1>
         <div>
          <p>
           Fitzwaryn School is an academy in a multi academy trust (MAT). We are a special school for children from 3 - 19 with an outstanding OFSTED report.
          </p>
          <p>
           <span>
            The school is organised into nine classes and a post 16 centre. We cater for pupils with autism spectrum disorders, moderate learning difficulties, severe learning difficulties and profound and multiple learning difficulties.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span>
            Fitzwaryn has recently undergone a substantial rebuild and now has state of the art facilities including a purpose built Post 16 Centre.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            Stephanie Coneboy - Headteacher
           </em>
          </p>
         </div>
