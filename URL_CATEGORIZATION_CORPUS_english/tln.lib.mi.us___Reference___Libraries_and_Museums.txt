         <h2>
          Serving 75 Libraries in Southeast Michigan
         </h2>
         <img src="../images/tln-building.png" style="float: right;"/>
         The Library Network is a public library cooperative serving 75 libraries in southeast Michigan. Our mission is to provide and facilitate quality services developed through collaboration of our member libraries. We are funded by the state and by our member libraries.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <h2>
          From the Director's Desk
         </h2>
         Welcome to The Library Network (TLN). From acquisitions to cataloging, circulation control to account management, delivery of materials, purchasing or Wide Area Network administration, we have one mission â facilitate the process of our Libraries Working Together. Like a multi dimensional restaurant, TLN is a menu driven library cooperative, offering a wide range of services for member selection. From appetizers to a full course meal, our member libraries select their level of service use. Enjoy our rich array of services as you review our website. Bon appetit and Happy Reading!
         <br/>
         <b>
          - Jim Pletz, Director
         </b>
