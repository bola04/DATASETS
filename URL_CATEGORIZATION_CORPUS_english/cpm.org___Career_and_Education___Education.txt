               <div class="sqs-block-content">
                <h3>
                 <strong>
                  CPM'S INTERVENTION COURSE
                 </strong>
                </h3>
                <p>
                 CPM is developing an intervention course for students who are taking Core Connections, Course 3, but need additional support in mathematics with a concurrent math class. The course is unique in that it focuses on problem solving, building relationships, building student confidence, while also focusing on some key 8th grade standards like ratio and proportion, solving equations, and numeracy. For more information, you can listen to a recent podcast about the development of the course or write to
                 <a href="mailto:intervention@cpm.org" target="_blank">
                  intervention@cpm.org
                 </a>
                 .
                </p>
                <p>
                 CPM will be piloting the intervention course during the 2018-19 school year, and the course will be available to the general public Summer 2019.
