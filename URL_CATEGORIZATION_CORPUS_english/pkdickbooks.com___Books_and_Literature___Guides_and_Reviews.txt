        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          <strong>
           Mark your calendar!
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://pkdickbooks.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/pkd-fest-2017-logo.jpg">
           <img alt="pkd-fest-2017-logo" class="size-medium wp-image-92 alignleft" height="300" sizes="(max-width: 274px) 100vw, 274px" src="http://pkdickbooks.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/pkd-fest-2017-logo-274x300.jpg" srcset="http://pkdickbooks.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/pkd-fest-2017-logo-274x300.jpg 274w, http://pkdickbooks.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/pkd-fest-2017-logo.jpg 466w" width="274"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          The next Philip K. Dick Festival will take place in Fort Morgan, CO, the resting place of Philip K. Dick and his twin sister.
         </p>
         <p>
          Come and join us to celebrate the life and work of science fiction master Philip K. Dick on the 35th anniversary of his passing,
         </p>
         <p>
          March 3rd and 4th, 2017 in
          <br/>
          FORT MORGAN, COLORADO
         </p>
         <div class="paragraph">
         </div>
         <div class="paragraph">
          <ul>
           <li>
            <span style="font-size: medium;">
             <strong>
              Produced
             </strong>
             by the City of Ft. Morgan and Morgan County, Colorado
            </span>
           </li>
           <li>
            <span style="font-size: medium;">
             <strong>
              Hosted
             </strong>
             by Lord Running Clam
            </span>
           </li>
          </ul>
         </div>
         <p>
          For more information visit our
          <a href="http://wide-books.com/philip-k-dick-festival-2017.html">
           Philip K. Dick Festival Page
          </a>
          at Wide-Books.com.
         </p>

        <h2 class="entry-title">
         <a href="http://pkdickbooks.com/blog/2016/02/15/philip-k-dick-conference-2016/" rel="bookmark">
          PHILIP K DICK Conference 2016
         </a>
        </h2>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">
          Posted on
         </span>
         <a href="http://pkdickbooks.com/blog/2016/02/15/philip-k-dick-conference-2016/" rel="bookmark" title="12:48 am">
          <span class="entry-date">
           February 15, 2016
          </span>
         </a>
         <span class="meta-sep">
          by
         </span>
         <span class="author vcard">
          <a class="url fn n" href="http://pkdickbooks.com/blog/author/administrator/" title="View all posts by Administrator">
           Administrator
          </a>
         </span>
        </div>
        <!-- .entry-meta -->
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          The 2016
          <a href="https://sfatcsuf.wordpress.com/philip-k-dick-conference/">
           Philip K Dick Conference
          </a>
          will take place in Fullerton, CA on April 29-30.
         </p>
         <p>
          For information about registration and accommodation
          <a href="https://sfatcsuf.wordpress.com/registration-and-accommodation/">
           visit this page.
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          The program has not been communicated yet, but we know that there will be a keynote talk by Dr. Ursula Heise and a plenary talk by Jonathan Lethem.  James Blaylock, sf authors and alums of CSUF will be there and many more guests to come!
         </p>
         <p>
          In 1972,  Philip K. Dick moved to Fullerton, California, in Orange County, at the behest of Dr. Willis McNelly, Professor of English at California State University, Fullerton. Dick lived in the OC for ten years until his death in 1982, and left his manuscripts and papers to CSUF’s Special Collections in the library
         </p>
