       </font>
      </a>
     </b>
     Freegal (which stands for "free" + "legal") is a downloadable music service that works with your FSPL library card. Freegal offers hundreds of thousands of songs from over 100 genres of music and many record labels from the Sony Music Entertainment catalog -- all without any digital rights management (DRM) issues to deal with or any special software required! (If you prefer, however, you may download the Freegal App for your
     <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/freegal-music/id508036345?mt=8">

      Android phone
     </a>
     .)

     <p>
      You are limited to
      <b>
       5
      </b>
      songs per week -- Freegal's download week begins at 11:00p.m. on Sunday night when your downloads available counter is reset.  Access to Freegal from this website is limited to Fort Smith Public Library cardholders with accounts in good standing (current address with fines under $2.00 and no overdue materials).

      <h2>
       <font color="#0066cc">
        <i>
         What is the TumbleBook Library?
        </i>
       </font>
      </h2>
     </center>
     <a href="http://www.tumblebooklibrary.com/autologin.aspx?userid=O3M1jfEdbaPTlNPTgR2AWw%3d%3d" onmousedown="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'FSPLindex', 'DigDnld', 'Tumblebooks2']);">
      <img align="right" alt="New! TumbleBooks Library @ FSPL!" border="2" height="70" src="TumbleBookLibrary.jpg" width="256"/>
     </a>
     <br/>
     The
     <a href="http://www.tumblebooklibrary.com/autologin.aspx?userid=O3M1jfEdbaPTlNPTgR2AWw%3d%3d" onmousedown="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'FSPLindex', 'DigDnld', 'Tumblebooks2']);">
      <font color="#0066cc" size="+1">
       <b>
        <i>
         TumbleBook Library
        </i>
       </b>
      </font>
     </a>
     is a fun and interactive library of animated, talking story books for preschoolers, beginner readers and older independent readers.  Read along and listen, or read on your own. TumbleBooks are created by taking existing story books, adding animation, sound, music and narration to produce an electronic (e-book) story book. TumbleBooks will help teach kids the joy of reading in a format theyll love!
     <p>
      <b>
       Our TumbleBook Library is a collection of:
      </b>
     </p>
     <ul>
      <li>
       <b>
        Story Books
       </b>
        entertaining and amusing picture story books to listen to, or read on your own.
      </li>
      <li>
       <b>
        Tumble Readers
       </b>
        large print
       <b>
        <i>
         Read-On-Your-Own
        </i>
       </b>
       story, chapter and teen books.
      </li>
      <li>
       <b>
        Life Learning
       </b>
        books that teach kids about important social and health issues in a fun, educational manner.
      </li>
      <li>
       <b>
        Puzzles &amp; Games
       </b>
        jigsaw puzzles, word fun, memory games and more to play after reading a book!
      </li>
      <li>
       <b>
        Language Learning
       </b>
        read a growing collection of Spanish, French, Russian and Chinese original story books, or translations of English story books.
      </li>
      <li>
       <b>
        Audio Books
       </b>
        listen to tales of Aesops, fairy tales, and classics such as Black Beauty, Winnie-the-Pooh, and more.
      </li>
      <li>
       <b>
        Index
       </b>
        A-Z listing of the all story books in the TumbleBook Library.
      </li>
     </ul>
     <p>
      <b>
       Check out our TumbleBooks today!
      </b>
      The TumbleBooks Library at the Fort Smith Public Library are being funded by the
      <b>
       Friends of the Library
      </b>
      . For more information, please call the Main Library at 479-783-0229.
