           <section class="callPap1">
            <h2 class="papers">
             Authorship Standards
            </h2>
            <p class="papers">
             In order to respect the authorship right of authors and uphold scientific honesty, the authorship of CMJ should be complied as follows:
            </p>
            <ol>
             <li>
              Author names should not be added, removed and changed the order after submitted the manuscript.
             </li>
             <li>
              Each article could have one corresponding author.
             </li>
             <li>
              Authors are not recommended to be co-first authors. Authors in the same institution could not be the co-first authors.
             </li>
            </ol>
            <p>
             Chinese Medical Journal reserves the right of final explanations to the Authorship standard.
            </p>
            <div style="width:200px;float:left;text-align:right;">
             - Chinese Medical Journal
            </div>
