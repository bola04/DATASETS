              </span>
              <strong>
               Did you know that
               <em>
                vehicle price
               </em>
               is the most important part of negotiating a car lease deal?
              </strong>

              <em>
               only
              </em>
              part of a lease that a dealer can negotiate. All other factors such as money factor, residual value, and acquisition fee are already set by the lease finance company. Therefore, it’s important to know that you should always get the best price possible on a vehicle before you agree to lease. One way to do that is by using an
              <strong>
