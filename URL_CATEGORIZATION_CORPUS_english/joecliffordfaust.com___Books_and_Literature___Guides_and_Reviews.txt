           <a href="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/education-school-system-singapore" rel="bookmark">
            How Education Differs From the US in Countries Like Singapore?
           </a>
          </h2>
          <div class="entry-meta">
           <span class="meta-date">
            <a href="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/education-school-system-singapore" rel="bookmark" title="8:08 pm">
             <time class="entry-date published updated" datetime="2018-04-16T20:08:55+00:00">
              April 16, 2018
             </time>
            </a>
           </span>
           <span class="meta-author">
            <span class="author vcard">
             <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/author/admin/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Heidi Hayes">
              Heidi Hayes
             </a>
            </span>
           </span>
           <span class="meta-category">
            <a href="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/category/history/" rel="category tag">
             History
            </a>
           </span>
          </div>
         </header>
         <!-- .entry-header -->
         <div class="entry-content clearfix">
          <p>
           Singapore is one of the developing countries where growth rate is lower than developed countries like the US. There are many reasons due to which the growth rate is lower in these countries but education is the major problem due to which these countries are far away from the developed countries. There is no doubt that they are trying hard to compete with these developed countries but they are still miles away from these countries.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="" class="size-medium wp-image-97 alignright" height="225" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/1-300x225.jpg" srcset="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/1-300x225.jpg 300w, http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/1.jpg 750w" width="300"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           However, there is nothing wrong in making some efforts and they should keep on trying otherwise, they will be left far behind in the race because the world is continuously growing and it is never going to stop for a single person or even for a country. So, the countries need to focus on their improvement instead of waiting for others to do something for them.
          </p>
          <p>
           Today, we are going to talk about the differences that are found in the education system of Singapore and US. We believe that the
           <a href="https://www.cis.edu.sg/">
            Singapore international school
           </a>
           administrations would take it seriously and they would definitely do something about it. If they did not take any steps today, they’d regret their decision in the future. So, it’s now or never. The decision is all yours. Here is the information about how education is different in US and Singapore.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Lack of guidance
          </h2>
          <p>
           The major problem in Singapore is that the students do not get enough guidance from the teachers due to which they are unable to choose an ideal career. Although students are in a position where they can take their decisions on their own, they still need guidance from the teachers and from their elders as well.
          </p>
          <p>
           So, it’s not only the responsibility of the teachers to guide the students but the parents should also participate in building a bright career for their child. The situation in the US is completely different because the teachers provide complete guidance to the students according to their preferences. And that is the reason why students in the US are more successful than Singapore or other developing countries.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Flaws in the education system
          </h2>
          <p>
           <img alt="" class="size-medium wp-image-98 alignright" height="168" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/2-300x168.jpg" srcset="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/2-300x168.jpg 300w, http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/2-768x431.jpg 768w, http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/2-1024x574.jpg 1024w, http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/2.jpg 1243w" width="300"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           The Singapore education system has some flaws in it and these flaws need to be addressed on time otherwise, they will lead the next generation to a devastating career. The authorities are working hard on changing their education system. This may take some time but they will definitely succeed in this effort. On the other hand, the American education is at its peak and it continuously growing. Therefore the students have more opportunities in the US instead of Singapore.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Lack of entertainment
          </h2>
          <p>
           The lack of entertainment is the major reason due to which the institutes in Singapore aren’t producing experts like the US. It is a fact that education is important for a successful career but the entertainment is also a part of education. So, the schools should focus on providing several entertaining opportunities to the students.
           <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Education_in_the_United_States">
            Here
           </a>
           is more information about how education differs from the US in countries like Singapore.
          </p>

         <header class="entry-header">
          <h2 class="entry-title">
           <a href="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/tips-for-writing-on-boring-subjects-like-geometry/" rel="bookmark">
            Tips for writing on boring subjects like geometry
           </a>
          </h2>
          <div class="entry-meta">
           <span class="meta-date">
            <a href="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/tips-for-writing-on-boring-subjects-like-geometry/" rel="bookmark" title="7:49 pm">
             <time class="entry-date published updated" datetime="2017-12-16T19:49:50+00:00">
              December 16, 2017
             </time>
            </a>
           </span>
           <span class="meta-author">
            <span class="author vcard">
             <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/author/admin/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Heidi Hayes">
              Heidi Hayes
             </a>
            </span>
           </span>
           <span class="meta-category">
            <a href="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/category/writing-tips/" rel="category tag">
             Writing Tips
            </a>
           </span>
          </div>
         </header>
         <!-- .entry-header -->
         <div class="entry-content clearfix">
          <p>
           Writing an article is a very tough project as you need to conduct plenty of research before you start writing a paper. However, when you use your writing skills properly, you can easily write an article on any topic you want but it takes a lot of effort to reach that position. Once you have mastered the research skills you can easily write a creative and engaging article on any topic.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/3uYe0LO-QgI" width="560">
           </iframe>
          </p>
          <p>
           But there are some complicated subjects that may still bring a thought to your mind that you’re still a beginner because those subjects are so much boring that you get tired immediately after starting your research. Subjects like geometry are so much boring that even the expert writers avoid taking the subjects that involve geometry in them.
          </p>
          <p>
           Geometry is such a boring subject that you’ll sometimes even find some lecturers requesting that will you please
           <a href="http://homeworkdoer.org/math/geometry-homework-help.html">
            do my geometry
           </a>
           paper research for me. However, you can turn this weakness to your greater strength by following the tips we have shared in this article. The most important thing about boring subjects is that there isn’t enough competition and you can easily win as many projects as you want because most the writers usually avoid working on such subjects.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="https://www.copyblogger.com/boring-topic-content-marketing/">
            Here
           </a>
           are some amazing tips that can be used to write interesting content on boring subjects.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Fun and entertainment
          </h2>
          <p>
           The best way of writing on boring subjects like geometry is to write in a fun and entertaining way because these subjects are so much boring that people do not want to read boring content to understand the difficulties of these subjects. You must learn the art of converting these boring subjects to some fun and entertainment so the readers may appreciate your work.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="" class="size-medium wp-image-92 aligncenter" height="215" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/1-300x215.jpg" srcset="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/1-300x215.jpg 300w, http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/1.jpg 390w" width="300"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           It may take some time to take command over this skill but once you have learned the art of converting boring subjects to the entertainment, you’ll become the best writer of this field and people will even agree to pay your desired rates for completing their projects.
          </p>
          <h2>
           More research
          </h2>
          <p>
           The research is the most important element for writing excellent quality articles but when it comes to writing about the boring subject, you need to conduct more and more research as compared to the easier subjects. This may take more than the expected time but you can also charge a higher amount for the time you’ve consumed to research a topic. Most of the people understand the difficulties of such subjects and they happily agree to pay you more money once you provide them the best quality work.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Watching videos
          </h2>
          <p>
           The videos are the best way of understanding the boring subjects. So, you can easily watch a video about a topic that is assigned to you and then you can simply start writing your piece of paper about that topic. This will help to make this process easier for you.
          </p>
         </div>

        <article class="post-85 post type-post status-publish format-standard sticky hentry category-writing-tips" id="post-85">
         <a class="post-thumbnail-small" href="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/traveling-writer-awesome-content-experience-touring-maui" rel="bookmark">
         </a>
         <header class="entry-header">
          <h2 class="entry-title">
           <a href="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/traveling-writer-awesome-content-experience-touring-maui" rel="bookmark">
            Are you a traveling writer? How to come up with awesome content based on my experience touring Maui
           </a>
          </h2>
          <div class="entry-meta">
           <span class="meta-date">
            <a href="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/traveling-writer-awesome-content-experience-touring-maui" rel="bookmark" title="2:35 am">
             <time class="entry-date published updated" datetime="2017-10-22T02:35:13+00:00">
              October 22, 2017
             </time>
            </a>
           </span>
           <span class="meta-author">
            <span class="author vcard">
             <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/author/admin/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Heidi Hayes">
              Heidi Hayes
             </a>
            </span>
           </span>
           <span class="meta-category">
            <a href="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/category/writing-tips/" rel="category tag">
             Writing Tips
            </a>
           </span>
          </div>
         </header>
         <!-- .entry-header -->
         <div class="entry-content clearfix">
          <p>
           Being a traveling writer is one of the most valuable jobs in the world as you get the opportunity to travel to different countries and you get the chance to meet people from different cultures and nations and most important of all you get the opportunity to see the incredible beauty of nature.
          </p>
          <p>
           But only a writer can understand that it is not all about fun and entertainment but it is one of the major responsibilities that are on your shoulders as a writer.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="" class=" wp-image-86 aligncenter" height="183" sizes="(max-width: 275px) 100vw, 275px" src="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/1.jpg" srcset="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/1.jpg 705w, http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/1-300x200.jpg 300w, http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/1-120x80.jpg 120w, http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/1-360x240.jpg 360w, http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/1-600x400.jpg 600w" width="275"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           It is a fact that you get the chance to enjoy the amazing moments but you need to take a deep look at everything when you’re visiting a country or location for writing about. You need to research a lot and find all the information about the place that you’re visiting because you can’t afford to make a mistake while writing about your experience of visiting a place.
          </p>
          <p>
           You cannot think about breaking the trust of thousands of people who believe each and every word that you write in your research. This is the only behavior that has helped you become a successful traveling writer. There are many writers who copy the work of others and post it as their research but everybody knows that they fail to get the success that loyal writers can.
          </p>
          <p>
           So, you have experienced the
           <a href="https://www.liveyouraloha.com/maui-tours">
            Awesome Tours in Maui
           </a>
           and now you want to write about those tours but don’t have any idea about where to start because there are so many things to watch that you can’t even think of missing any of them.
          </p>
          <p>
           So, here are some tips about how can you convert your feelings of Maui tour to the incredible words so that your readers may get attracted to this beautiful place.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Natural Beauty
          </h2>
          <p>
           You should start your article with the beauty of nature and express all the beautiful spots you saw in Maui. You can also attach the pictures of those places to build the interest of the readers. The pictures are more attractive as compared to the writings.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Romantic places
          </h2>
          <p>
           <img alt="" class=" wp-image-87 aligncenter" height="188" sizes="(max-width: 282px) 100vw, 282px" src="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/2.jpg" srcset="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/2.jpg 550w, http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/2-300x200.jpg 300w, http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/2-120x80.jpg 120w, http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/2-360x240.jpg 360w" width="282"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           The next thing you must write about is the visit to romantic places because most of the people go to Maui for a Honeymoon tour because there are lots of romantic places where they can spend amazing moments with their partner. You can also describe the way they can enjoy the romantic moments in those places. It will also help build their interest.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Adventures
          </h2>
          <p>
           Youngsters prefer going to Maui to enjoy the adventurous activities so you must mention the activities they can participate in. Don’t forget to mention that they’ll also get the opportunity to
           <a href="https://www.liveyouraloha.com/swim-with-dolphins-hawaii">
            swim with dolphins
           </a>
           in Maui.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="" class=" wp-image-88 aligncenter" height="210" sizes="(max-width: 373px) 100vw, 373px" src="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/3.jpg" srcset="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/3.jpg 1280w, http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/3-300x169.jpg 300w, http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/3-768x432.jpg 768w, http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/3-1024x576.jpg 1024w" width="373"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           This will be more interesting for them and they’ll definitely decide to visit this amazing place.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Basic tips
          </h2>
          <p>
           Along with all these things, you can share several tips with them such as how to book the room and where should they spend their time and most importantly how can they save money on traveling etc.
          </p>
         </div>

        <article class="post-61 post type-post status-publish format-standard sticky hentry category-writing-tips tag-become-a-confident-writer" id="post-61">
         <a class="post-thumbnail-small" href="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/simple-and-easy-ways-to-grow-confidence-as-a-writer/" rel="bookmark">
         </a>
         <header class="entry-header">
          <h2 class="entry-title">
           <a href="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/simple-and-easy-ways-to-grow-confidence-as-a-writer/" rel="bookmark">
            Simple and easy ways to grow confidence as a writer?
           </a>
          </h2>
          <div class="entry-meta">
           <span class="meta-date">
            <a href="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/simple-and-easy-ways-to-grow-confidence-as-a-writer/" rel="bookmark" title="4:52 pm">
             <time class="entry-date published updated" datetime="2017-07-09T16:52:36+00:00">
              July 9, 2017
             </time>
            </a>
           </span>
           <span class="meta-author">
            <span class="author vcard">
             <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/author/admin/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Heidi Hayes">
              Heidi Hayes
             </a>
            </span>
           </span>
           <span class="meta-category">
            <a href="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/category/writing-tips/" rel="category tag">
             Writing Tips
            </a>
           </span>
          </div>
         </header>
         <!-- .entry-header -->
         <div class="entry-content clearfix">
          <p>
           Building confidence as a writer sometimes appears to be a tedious task for many writers. This does not only happen with beginners but the experienced contractors also suffer from such difficulty. But once you learn to build writing confidence, you become an efficient writer.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="" class="size-medium wp-image-62 aligncenter" height="157" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/07/1-300x157.jpg" srcset="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/07/1-300x157.jpg 300w, http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/07/1.jpg 600w" width="300"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           Today we’re going to share some valuable tips with you that will help you become a confident writer and win appreciation from a great number of readers.
          </p>
          <h2>
           <strong>
            Practice more
           </strong>
          </h2>
          <p>
           You can grow your writing confidence unanimously if you keep practicing your skills. It is inevitable. You do not only gain advanced knowledge but you also get the chance the learn the writing style of other writers. The more you practice your skills the more you build confidence.
          </p>
          <p>
           You get the chance to learn from your own mistakes. And the feedback from your readers also builds confidence in you.
          </p>
          <h2>
           <strong>
            Participate in competitions
           </strong>
          </h2>
          <p>
           Joining some competitions is also a good idea. Participating in the competitions helps you gain new valuable experience and demonstrate your abilities. Those who do not step into competition due to the fear of failure can never become confident writers. Because every failure helps you learn some new things.
          </p>
          <h2>
           <strong>
            Find a writing job
           </strong>
          </h2>
          <p>
           If someone is paying for writing, you must grab that outstanding opportunity immediately because it will help you become a confident writer. You can look for professional writer opportunities in the newspaper or other resources.
          </p>
          <p>
           So, if you don’t have confidence in your writing then finding a job to build some confidence will be an ideal option for you.
          </p>
          <h2>
           <strong>
            Read more
           </strong>
          </h2>
          <p>
           <img alt="" class="size-medium wp-image-63 aligncenter" height="225" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/07/2-1-300x225.jpg" srcset="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/07/2-1-300x225.jpg 300w, http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/07/2-1.jpg 728w" width="300"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           Reading different books and genres is a great way to become a better writer. However, some people get so much engaged in writing that they can’t even manage to spare some time to read more and learn from others.
          </p>
          <p>
           Therefore, we suggest you read blogs and books by talented writers because it will help you gain new experience. You will be able to learn the new ways to write.
          </p>
         </div>

        <article class="post-66 post type-post status-publish format-standard sticky hentry category-writing-tips tag-writing tag-writing-tips" id="post-66">
         <a class="post-thumbnail-small" href="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/how-to-become-a-better-writer/" rel="bookmark">
         </a>
         <header class="entry-header">
          <h2 class="entry-title">
           <a href="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/how-to-become-a-better-writer/" rel="bookmark">
            How to become a better writer
           </a>
          </h2>
          <div class="entry-meta">
           <span class="meta-date">
            <a href="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/how-to-become-a-better-writer/" rel="bookmark" title="4:52 pm">
             <time class="entry-date published updated" datetime="2017-07-09T16:52:04+00:00">
              July 9, 2017
             </time>
            </a>
           </span>
           <span class="meta-author">
            <span class="author vcard">
             <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/author/admin/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Heidi Hayes">
              Heidi Hayes
             </a>
            </span>
           </span>
           <span class="meta-category">
            <a href="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/category/writing-tips/" rel="category tag">
             Writing Tips
            </a>
           </span>
          </div>
         </header>
         <!-- .entry-header -->
         <div class="entry-content clearfix">
          <p>
           Congratulations, you are on your way to success if you are looking for
           <a href="https://www.themuse.com/advice/10-simple-ways-to-become-a-better-writer">
            tips on how to become a better writer
           </a>
           . Because a writer has the power to do wonders in the world. A writer can conquer the hearts of people by using emotional phrases in his articles and stories.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="How to become a better writer" class="size-medium wp-image-58 aligncenter" height="164" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/07/2-300x164.jpg" srcset="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/07/2-300x164.jpg 300w, http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/07/2-768x419.jpg 768w, http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/07/2-1024x559.jpg 1024w, http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/07/2.jpg 1375w" width="300"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           But that’s only possible if you have a catchy and interesting writing style. And if your writing style is dull and boring then you must look for some ways on how to become a better writer otherwise, you’ll not only be wasting your time and effort but it will also affect your overall reputation as a writer.
          </p>
          <p>
           Being a writer people might also ask you about how to become a better writer. And it would be an embarrassment if you couldn’t answer their question properly.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/po-qkiwmjTk" width="560">
           </iframe>
          </p>
          <p>
           If you are a writer or want to become one then you must know some ways that can help you become a better writer.
          </p>
          <p>
           We have tried to find the answer to the most difficult question of your life. And we have gathered many helpful
           <a href="http://www.lifehack.org/articles/communication/a-guide-to-becoming-a-better-writer-15-practical-tips.html">
            tips on how to become a better writer
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Here are the tips:
           </strong>
          </p>
          <h2>
           <strong>
            Enrolling in various writing courses: –
           </strong>
          </h2>
          <p>
           You should take writing classes regularly to learn more about a particular writing technique or to polish your general writing skills. You will get an inspiration by associating with people who have the same passion as you do.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="How to become a better writer" class="size-medium wp-image-59 aligncenter" height="200" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/07/2-300x200.png" srcset="http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/07/2-300x200.png 300w, http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/07/2-120x80.png 120w, http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/07/2-360x240.png 360w, http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/07/2-600x400.png 600w, http://www.joecliffordfaust.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/07/2.png 650w" width="300"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           You’ll be able to boost your confidence after hearing the comments of experts about your work. Search for free online courses on the internet if you can’t afford to take one.
          </p>
          <h2>
           <strong>
            A catchy title is always awesome: –
           </strong>
          </h2>
          <p>
           The title plays an important role in the writing. Most of the readers buy a book or start reading a story due to an interesting title. So, try to be creative about deciding the title of the story.
          </p>
          <h2>
           <strong>
            Start with a story: –
           </strong>
          </h2>
          <p>
           Beginning with a story is a great way of grabbing the attention of your readers. Try to illustrate your point with a catchy story and make it easy for the reader to understand that what you are talking about.
          </p>
          <p>
           Try to emerge the visualization in your content so that the reader may feel all the incredible moments that you have stated in your piece of writing. Great writers are creating fictional scenes with colors and details to engage their audience.
          </p>
          <p>
           These tips on how to become a better writer are incredible enough to help you win the race. Follow all these steps and increase your audience significantly.
          </p>
