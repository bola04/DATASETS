   <h4>
    Inspired Speakers Series
    <br/>
    Howard Paul: ARMY Officer, Jazz Guitarist, President &amp; CEO of Benedetto Guitars
    <br/>
    Sunday, July 29, 2 p.m.
   </h4>
   <p>
    This talk features Howard Paul — from jazz guitarist to Army officer to corporate executive, he is a man of many talents. A gifted musician and business leader, for years he has maintained dual careers in music and business. In 2006, he was named President/CEO of Benedetto Guitars in Savannah, GA.
    <a href="https://upto.com/e/bOa8O" target="new">
     Click here
    </a>
    for more information.
   </p>
   <p>
    Free for Appleton members and CF students; $10 for nonmembers. Advance reservation (for members) and purchase (for nonmembers) is recommended as events regularly sell out.
    <a href="https://cffoundation.wufoo.com/forms/m1hxp3cf1cc9iy9/" target="new">
     Click here to get your ticket now!
    </a>
   </p>
   <hr/>
   <div class="column exhibit-pic">
    <div align="center" style="padding-right:5px">
     <a href="http://www.appletonmuseum.org/exhibits/current/">
      <img alt="Free Museum Day" height="214" src="/images/museumday18.jpg" width="200"/>
     </a>
    </div>
   </div>
   <h4>
    Museum Day &amp; Education Open House
    <br/>
    Saturday, July 7, 10 .m.-5 p.m.
   </h4>
   <p>
    Join us for the annual Museum Day, where all ages are invited to enjoy the permanent collection, special exhibitions and ARTSpace with FREE ADMISSION all day. This means you get to see
    <em>
     Medieval to Metal: The Art &amp; Evolution Of The Guitar
    </em>
    ," plus paintings by Mel Fiorentino and other special exhibitions, all for free!
   </p>
   <p>
    New this year, the education department will host an open house. See art-making demonstrations in our studios and plein air painting in the courtyard, meet the staff and teachers, and create art in the ARTSpace.
   </p>
   <p>
    Appleton members always receive free admission but we encourage you to bring your nonmember friends!
   </p>
