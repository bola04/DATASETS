      <p>
       Welcome to Same Difference, the online home of Samedifference1. I am a physically DisAbled writer and blogger living in the UK. Here at my website, you will find all kinds of fun and useful sections related to disability and DisAbility. The main feature at the moment is my blog where I write and comment about issues relating to all disabilities.
      </p>
      <p>
       I invite you to visit my forum, which I have recently started. This is meant to be a messageboard for enquiries, extended discussions and notification of events and points of interest.
      </p>
      <p>
       You can also
       <a href="https://samedifference1.com/poetry-ebook/" title="Poetry Ebook">
        buy my e-book of poetry
       </a>
       . This is an original collection of poems written by me about disability issues.
      </p>
      <p>
       Please see above my portfolio of articles contributed to other print and online media.
      </p>
      <p>
       Any donations received would be welcome. These would be used for the maintenance of this site.
      </p>
      <p>
       Feel free to come in and have a look around! Enjoy your stay. Suggestions for improvement can be emailed to me at samedifferenceone@hotmail.co.uk
      </p>
      <p>
       Thanks for being here!
      </p>
