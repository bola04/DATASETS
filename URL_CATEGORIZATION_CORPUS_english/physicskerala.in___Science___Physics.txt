                 <br/>
                 Regional Institute of Education (RIE) has invited applications for admission to the various Education Programmes offered by it at Ajmer (affiliated to Maharshi Dayanand Saraswati University), Bhubaneswar (Utkal University), Bhopal (Barkatullah University), Mysuru (University of Mysore) and Shillong (North Eastern Hill University (NEHU)) during 2018-19.
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 The Programmes offered, seat availability and eligibility for admission are as follows:
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 <strong>
                  <br/>
                 </strong>
                 <strong>
                  B.Sc. B.Ed.
                 </strong>
                 (4 year integrated): Programme is offered at different RIE centres in Physical Science Group and Biological Science Group as follows: RIE Ajmer, RIE Bhubaneswar: 50 for each stream; RIE Bhopal, Mysuru: 40 for each stream. Applicant must have passed Plus 2 / Higher Secondary/Senior Secondary or any equivalent examination in 2016, 2017 or should be appearing /appeared in 2018 with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics/Statistics, for admission to Physical Science Group, and with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology/Biotechnology for admission to Biological Science Group securing at least 50% marks in aggregate of qualifying examination with relaxation of 5% marks for SC/ ST/PWD applicants.
                 <br/>
                 <strong>
                  <br/>
                 </strong>
                 <strong>
                  B.A. B.Ed
                 </strong>
                 (4 -year integrated): Applicant should have Passed +2/ Hr. Secondary/ Sr. Secondary or equivalent examination in Science/Commerce/ Arts stream with at least 50% marks in aggregate (relaxation of 5% marks for SC/ ST/PWD candidates) in 2016, 2017 or should be appearing /appeared in 2018. There are 50 seats each at RIE Ajmer, Bhubaneswar and 40 each at Bhopal and Mysuru.
                 <br/>
                 <strong>
                  <br/>
                 </strong>
                 <strong>
                  M.Sc. Ed (6 year)
                 </strong>
                 : This is an integrated Programme of Teacher Education leading to the Post-graduate degree, M.Sc.Ed. in Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry with an aim to prepare Senior Secondary School teachers of Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics without any option of intermediate exit before completing six years of study. There are 15 seats available for each of the M.Sc.Ed. Programme in Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics at RIE Mysuru. Applicant must have passed Plus 2 / Higher Secondary/Senior Secondary or any equivalent examination in 2016, 2017 or should be appearing /appeared in 2018 with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics/Statistics, securing at least 50% marks in aggregate of qualifying examination with relaxation of 5% marks for SC/ ST/PWD applicants.
                 <br/>
                 <strong>
                  <br/>
                 </strong>
                 <strong>
                  B.Ed
                 </strong>
                 (2 year): The seat availability is as follows at different RIEs for (i) Science and Mathematics Group and (ii) Social Science and Language Group: Ajmer, Bhubaneswar-50 each; Bhopal-40 each; Mysuru, Shillong- 25 each. Those with at least 50% marks in Bachelor’s Degree and /or in the Master’s Degree in Science/ Bachelor’s in Engineering or Technology with specialization in Science or Mathematics or in Bachelor’s Degree and/or in the Master’s Degree in Social Science/ Humanities are eligible for admission to the programme (relaxation of 5% marks for SC/ ST/ PWD candidates).
                 <br/>
                 <strong>
                  <br/>
                 </strong>
                 <strong>
                  M.Ed.
                 </strong>
                 (2 year): There are 30 seats available at each of the RIEs at Ajmer, Bhubaneswar, Bhopal and Mysuru. Applicant should have obtained at least 50% marks or an equivalent grade (relaxation by 5% for SC/ST/PWD Candidates) in one of B.Ed OR B.A.B.Ed./ B.A.Ed./ B.Sc.B.Ed./ B.Sc.Ed OR B.El.Ed. OR D.El.Ed with a Bachelor’s degree in Arts/Science (with 50% marks), from any University / College / Institute recognized by affiliating universities
                 <strong>
                  <br/>
                 </strong>
                 <strong>
                  <br/>
                 </strong>
                 <br/>
                 <strong>
                  Integrated B.Ed- M.Ed
                 </strong>
                 (3 year): This is a professional programme in education, without any option of intermediate exit before completing three years of study. There are 40 seats for the course at RIE, Bhopal. Applicant should have a post graduate degree in Science / Social science/ Humanities from a recognized institution with a minimum of 55% marks or equivalent grade (relaxation by 5% for SC/ST/PWD candidates).
                 <strong>
                  <br/>
                 </strong>
                 <strong>
                  <br/>
                 </strong>
                 <br/>
                 <strong>
                  <br/>
                 </strong>
                 <strong>
                  Selection:
                 </strong>
                 <br/>
                 <a href="https://www.blogger.com/blogger.g?blogID=8705083632203739705" imageanchor="1" style="clear: right; float: right; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-left: 1em;">
                 </a>
                 Selection will be based on the Performance in an Entrance Examination and aggregate scores in Qualifying examination in the ratio 60:40. The Common Entrance Examination (CEE) will be of 2 hours duration will be held from 9 am to 11 am on 10
                 <sup>
                  th
                 </sup>
                 June 2018 at centres as per the list given at https://ncert-cee.kar.nic.in/ExamCenterList.aspx. CEE will have questions from Language Proficiency (English) (20 questions, 40 marks), Teaching Aptitude/Attitude (30, 60), Reasoning Ability (30, 60).
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 <div style="text-align: center;">
                  The manner in which aggregate for various courses will be computed is given at
                  <br/>
                  <a href="https://ncert-cee.kar.nic.in/" target="_blank">
                   https://ncert-cee.kar.nic.in/
                  </a>
                 </div>
                 <br/>
                 <strong>
                  <br/>
                 </strong>
                 <strong>
                  Eligibility for applying to various RIEs
                 </strong>
                 :
                 <br/>
                 Only applicants from specified states are eligible to apply to a particular Centre. However, at some centres for some courses, admissions are made at All India level. For B.Ed at NERIE, Shillong; applicant should be from Arunachal Pradesh, Sikkim, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Manipur, Tripura, Assam, Nagaland. B.Sc. B.Ed. / B.A. B.Ed./M. Ed at RIE Bhubaneswar: Arunachal Pradesh, Sikkim, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Manipur, Tripura, Assam, Nagaland. B.Sc. B.Ed. / B.A. B.Ed. / B.Ed./ M.Ed at RIE Ajmer: Delhi, Haryana, Punjab, Chandigarh, Himachal Pradesh, Jammu And Kashmir, Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh, Uttarakhand B.Sc. B.Ed. / B.A. B.Ed. / B.Ed. / M.Ed. / B.Ed.- M.Ed.(Integrated) at RIE Bhopal: Chhatisgarh, Goa, Gujarat, Daman &amp; Diu, Dadra &amp; Nagar Haveli, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra
                 <a href="https://www.blogger.com/blogger.g?blogID=8705083632203739705" imageanchor="1">
                 </a>
                 B.Sc. B.Ed. / B.A. B.Ed./ B.Ed. / M.Ed at RIE Bhubaneswar: Andaman &amp; Nicobar Islands, Bihar, Jharkhand, Odisha, West Bengal B.Sc. B.Ed/ B.A. B.Ed/ M.Sc.Ed / B.Ed/ M. Ed at RIE Mysuru: Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, Kerala, Lakshadweep, Tamil Nadu, Puducherry, Telangana M.Sc.Ed at RIE Mysuru: All India B.Ed- M.Ed. (Integrated) at RIE Bhopal: All India Prarambh School for Teacher Education Jhajjar, Haryana: BSc.B.Ed/ B.A.B.Ed: All India.
                 <br/>
                 <strong>
                  <br/>
                 </strong>
                 <strong>
                  Application:
                 </strong>
                 <br/>
                 Application can be registered at
                 <a href="https://ncert-cee.kar.nic.in/" target="_blank">
                  https://ncert-cee.kar.nic.in/
                 </a>
                 , latest by 6 pm on 9
                 <sup>
                  th
                 </sup>
                 May 2018 as per instructions at the website. The fee for entrance examination is Rs 800/- for General/OBC Category Students, Rs 400/- for SC/ST Category Student and Rs 400/- for PWD of any category Students. Application fee can be paid online using Credit card/Debit card/Net banking or through CASH by printing the e-Challan of SBI and crediting the fee amount in any branches of SBI. The challan is valid only for 6 days. Once fee payment is through, applicant can print/save the application form. Those selected through the entrance examination, have to submit to the respective RIEs at the time of admission, the printed application form along with all the enclosures including the photo copy of the mark sheet. Applicants can download their admit card after 14
                 <sup>
                  th
                 </sup>
                 May 2018. Those who are appearing in the qualifying examination in 2018 must enter their qualifying examination marks as per the schedule given in the website.
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 The result of B.Sc.B.Ed./B.A.B.Ed./M.Sc.Ed programmes will be declared on 4
                 <sup>
                  th
                 </sup>
                 July 2018 and the result of B.Ed./B.Ed.-M.Ed./M.Ed programme will be declared on 19
                 <sup>
                  th
                 </sup>
                 July 2018.

              <h1 class="post-title">
               <a href="https://www.physicskerala.in/2018/04/iiser-tirupati-admission-to-integrated.html">
                ►
                             IISER, Tirupati, Admission to Integrated PhD (I-PhD) Program August 2018
               </a>
              </h1>
              <div class="post-header-line-1">
              </div>
              <div class="post-body">
               <table align="right" border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" style="margin-right:49.5pt;border-collapse:collapse;margin-right:5.4pt; margin-left:5.4pt;margin-top:5.4pt;margin-bottom:5.4pt">

               <p>
               </p>
               <div id="summary2967327165047664891">
                <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
                 <div style="text-align: center;">
                  <a href="http://feedburner.google.com/fb/a/mailverify?uri=PhysicsPhD">
                   <img src="https://lh5.googleusercontent.com/-Gre0hHq7g88/TmdSBhufTkI/AAAAAAAAAz8/waSa6D4IkBI/s288/PhD_Position.gif"/>

                 </div>
                 <h2 style="text-align: center;">
                  <span style="font-size: small;">
                   ADMISSION TO INTEGRATED PHD (i-PhD) PROGRAM AUGUST 2018
                  </span>
                 </h2>
                 <div style="text-align: justify;">
                  The integrated Ph.D. (i-Ph.D.) programme at IISER Tirupati is aimed for highly motivated students who wish to pursue a career in research. Students with an excellent academic record can apply for the i-Ph.D. programme upon completion of a bachelor's degree in any branch of science. The course combines curricula for master's and doctorate degrees into a single integrated programme with a blend of course work and research projects.
                 </div>
                 <div style="text-align: justify;">
                  IISER Tirupati offers modern research laboratories and computational facilities as well as hostel facilities for students. Students will also have the opportunity to participate in a broad range of seminars, meetings and workshops, giving them a chance to interact with people from other research organizations within the country and abroad.
                 </div>
                 <b>
                  Important Dates:
                 </b>
                 <br/>
                 <div style="text-align: center;">
                  Opening date of Online Application : February 17, 2018
                 </div>
                 <div style="text-align: center;">
                  Closing date of Online Application : April 15, 2018
                 </div>
                 <div style="text-align: center;">
                  Interview Dates (Tentative) : June 1, 2018 to June 6, 2018
                 </div>
                 <br/>
                 <b>
                  Overview of the i-Ph.D. programme
                 </b>
                 <br/>
                 <div style="text-align: justify;">
                  IISER Tirupati offers Integrated Ph.D. (i-Ph.D.) as a research programme with a blend of course work (2 years) and research projects. On successful completion of the doctoral research project, students are awarded a degree in Master of Science and a Ph.D. degree. The first four semesters consist of core courses, elective courses and advanced courses specializing in one subject, and combined with research work. On successful completion of course work, related project work and a comprehensive exam, the student continues with the research leading to a Ph.D.
                 </div>
                 <div style="text-align: justify;">
                  The i-Ph.D. program is to be offered from August 2018 in the Biological, Chemical, Mathematical and Physical Sciences.
                 </div>
                 <div style="text-align: justify;">
                  <b>
                   Eligibility Criteria:
                  </b>
                 </div>
                 <div style="text-align: justify;">
                  1. Students with minimum 60% marks in aggregate (overall percentage with all subjects) or CGPA 6 out of 10-point scale in B.Sc./B.Tech/B.E. or any equivalent degree.
                 </div>
                 <div style="text-align: justify;">
                  2. In addition to the above, students must have qualified any of the national level exams listed in the following table for the respective departments.
                 </div>
                 <table>
                  <tbody>
                   <tr>
                    <th>
                     Discipline
                    </th>
                    <th>
                     Exams
                    </th>
                   </tr>
                   <tr>
                    <td>
                     Biology
                    </td>
                    <td>
                     JGEEBILS - 2017 or JAM -2018 (BL - Biological Sciences or BT - Biotechnology)
                    </td>
                   </tr>
                   <tr>
                    <td>
                     Chemistry
                    </td>
                    <td>
                     JAM (Chemistry) 2018
                    </td>
                   </tr>
                   <tr>
                    <td>
                     Mathematics
                    </td>
                    <td>
                     JAM 2018 (MA - Mathematics or MS-Mathematical Statistics) or Valid NBHM MA/MSc Scholarship
                    </td>
                   </tr>
                   <tr>
                    <td>
                     Physics
                    </td>
                    <td>
                     JAM (Physics) 2018 or JEST (Physics) 2018
                    </td>
                   </tr>
                  </tbody>
                 </table>
                 General Information to the Applicants:
                 <br/>
                 <ol>
                  <li>
                   The candidate MUST have qualified at least ONE of the above mentioned Additional eligibility criteria.
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   The scores/ranks must be valid at least till August, 2018. Please note that the admission criteria can change during the next round of admission.
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   SC/ST, PD and KM candidates Percentage may be relaxed with 5% or 0.5 in case of CGPA.
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   Candidates in the final year of their qualifying degree are also eligible to apply. However, their final selection is subject to their successful completion of the qualifying degree with the above-mentioned requirements at the time of admission. A provisional degree certificate is mandatory at the time of admission (Scheduled to be 1st August 2018).
                  </li>
                 </ol>
                 <b>
                  Application Procedure:
                 </b>
                 <br/>
                 Only online applications will be accepted. Applicants can submit their forms for the respective departments using the following links:
                 <br/>
                 <div style="text-align: center;">
                  <a href="https://form.jotform.me/80450337769463" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                   Biology
                  </a>
                 </div>
                 <div style="text-align: center;">
                  <a href="https://form.jotform.me/80452263269458" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                   Chemistry
                  </a>
                 </div>
                 <div style="text-align: center;">
                  <a href="https://form.jotform.me/80451533169456" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                   Mathematics
                  </a>
                 </div>
                 <div style="text-align: center;">
                  <a href="https://form.jotform.me/80451890669467" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                   Physics
                  </a>
                 </div>
                 <div style="text-align: justify;">
                  The candidates must carefully fill and submit the online application form on or before April 15, 2018. Only short-listed candidates will be intimated further about the date of interview. Candidates belonging to reserved category will have to submit relevant certificates, if selected.
                 </div>
                 <div style="text-align: justify;">
                  <br/>
                 </div>
                 <div style="text-align: justify;">
                  <b>
                   Selection Procedure:
                  </b>
                  The shortlisted candidates will be called for interview and final selection is based on interview performance. The respective departments may use additional criteria (based on academic performance and/or performance in the national level tests) in order to shortlist candidates for interviews. Fellowship: Financial assistance will be provided by the institute to the selected candidates. Please Note: All communication with candidates will be through emails only. Kindly check this website for periodic updates.
                 </div>
                 <div style="text-align: justify;">
                  <br/>
                 </div>
                 <div style="text-align: justify;">
                  <b>
                   Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs):
                  </b>
                 </div>
                 <ol>
                  <li>
                   I am in my first year of the Master's degree, am I eligible to apply for the i-PhD program?
                   <br/>
                   Yes, you are eligible.
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   Can I apply for i-PhD in more than one discipline?
                   <br/>
                   If you meet the eligibility criteria for more than one discipline, you can apply for both the disciplines by filing their respective application forms.
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   What is the fellowship structure?
                   <br/>
                   Institute will provide decent fellowship to an i-PhD student during the first two years, after which they will be receive a higher fellowship at par with a regular PhD student.
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   Will I be paid for my travel expenses for attending the i-PhD written test/interview?
                   <br/>
                   Institute will NOT be able to reimburse for your travel tickets.
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   Is it possible to leave the programme after MS?
                   <br/>
                   There is no exit option after MS.
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   Will I get an MS degree? If yes, how many years after joining the programme?
                   <br/>
                   The MS degree will be provided together with the PhD degree, however it will carry a date same as the date of passing the comprehensive examination, which is usually conducted before registering for a PhD.
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   How long does it take to complete the degree?
                   <br/>
                   Usually, it takes around six years to get a PhD degree in this program.
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   If I am selected in one department, am I allowed to choose research supervisor/mentor from other departments? Is it possible to pursue research with multiple supervisor/mentor?
                   <br/>
                   You may be allowed to pursue research with multiple faculty and faculty from other departments, provided your primary supervisor/mentor is affiliated to your parent department.
                  </li>
                 </ol>
                 <div style="text-align: right;">
                  For more information visit
                  <a href="http://www.iisertirupati.ac.in/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                   http://www.iisertirupati.ac.in/
                  </a>
                 </div>

                             LIGO-IndIGO Summer Students Program
               </a>
              </h1>
              <div class="post-header-line-1">
              </div>
              <div class="post-body">

               <div id="summary7018604213626722226">
                <p dir="ltr">
                 LIGO Laboratory at California Institute of Technology hosts a 10-week summer student research program every year, called the LIGO SURF Program. In consideration of the upcoming LIGO-India project, LIGO has graciously agreed to host a few talented and motivated undergraduate students from Indian institutions, pre-selected by IndIGO, as part of this program.
                </p>
                <p dir="ltr">
                 The program encourages undergraduate students to participate in the development of gravitational-wave astronomy through the LIGO Project. Selected students will work on summer research projects either at the Caltech campus in Pasadena, California or at the LIGO Observatory sites in Washington or Louisiana. The program runs from June 19 through August 24, 2018. Considering the fact that the summer vacation period in major Indian undergrad institutions start early, LIGO/Caltech has agreed to host the Indian students during the period May 15 - July 30, 2018. The dates are non-negotiable.
                </p>
                <p dir="ltr">
                 Online applications should be submitted before the deadline of 4 February 2018. The applicants should also arrange two recommendation letters to be sent directly to the e-mail ligosurf (at) gw-indigo (dot) org prior to the deadline.
                </p>
                <p dir="ltr">
                 For more information visit:
                 <a href="http://jobs.gw-indigo.org/ligosurf">
                  http://jobs.gw-indigo.org/ligosurf
                 </a>
                </p>
