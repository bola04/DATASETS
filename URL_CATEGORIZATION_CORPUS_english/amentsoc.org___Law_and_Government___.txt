     <h1 class="homepage-title">
      Amateur Entomologists' Society
     </h1>
     <p>
      The Amateur Entomologists' Society (
      <abbr title="Amateur Entomologists' Society">
       AES
      </abbr>
      ) is one of the
      <abbr title="United Kingdom">
       UK
      </abbr>
      's leading organisations for people
      <span class="highlighttext">
       interested in insects
      </span>
      . Entomology involves learning about
      <span class="highlighttext">
       insects and the environment
      </span>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
      The society produces
      <a href="/publications/">
       four publications
      </a>
      for members. We also publish and regularly update a highly regarded
      <a href="/publications/books/">
       series of books
      </a>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
      We run an annual
      <a href="/events/exhibitions.html">
       exhibition
      </a>
      , a
      <a href="/events/members-day.html">
       Members' Day
      </a>
      , and we organise
      <a href="/events/">
       field trips
      </a>
      for both adult and junior members.
     </p>
