         <h2 class="purple">
          <img src="/img/layout/ask_expertANIMATED11.14.gif" style="float:right;"/>
          Ask the Expert
         </h2>
         <div class="user-content">
          <p>
           <strong>
            Q: What is post-operative delirium?
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span>
            <strong>
             A:
            </strong>
            Delirium is a term meaning “sudden confusion.” Post-operative delirium is delirium that happens after an older adult has an operation (surgery) and is the most common post-operative complication in older adults.
           </span>
          </p>
