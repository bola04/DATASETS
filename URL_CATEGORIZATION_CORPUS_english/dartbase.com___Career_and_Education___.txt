      <h2>
       Information and help for Dart players
      </h2>
      This is a collection of articles I wrote and published online during the latter years of my darts career, from about 1996 to 1999. I have been a semi-professional darts player from about 1991 to 1997.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      I have been Austrian darts champion twice, in 1991 and 1993, played several times for the Austrian national team, and was the first Austrian darts player to achieve a
      <a href="http://www.dartswdf.com/" target="_blank">
       WDF
      </a>
      world ranking (#92). You can find out
      <a href="aboutit.htm">
       more about me here
      </a>
      .
      <h3>
       Articles I recommend
      </h3>
      <a href="slump.htm">
       <img border="0" src="ph1.gif" style="float:left;"/>
      </a>
      <a href="slump.htm">
       The first slump
      </a>
      <br/>
      <br/>
      An explanation of the different phases of a player's development. Highly recommended, especially when you are in a slump and feel terrible about your game. This has turned out to be the most popular article I wrote.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      <a href="archive.htm">
       Articles about the mental game
      </a>
      <br/>
      <br/>
      I always liked to explore this part of the game the most, and I think this won me a lot of matches - and lost me a lot before I discovered that what goes on in your brain during play matters a lot! I especially recommend the articles about
      <a href="tdt4.htm">
       Energy
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="tdt6.htm">
       Visualizing
      </a>
      and
      <a href="tdt1.htm">
       Tension
      </a>
      .
      <br/>
      <br/>
      <a href="technics.htm">
       Dart technique
      </a>
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Throw, grip and stance - the basics of a successful dart technique.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      <a href="new.htm">
       Practicing
      </a>
      <br/>
      <br/>
      What you should do to improve, and how you should approach practicing.
      <br/>
      <br/>
