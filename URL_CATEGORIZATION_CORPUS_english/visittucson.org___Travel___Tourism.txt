            <h2>
             Tucson is calling you...
            </h2>
            <p>
             Calling all travelers, the free thinker and the free at heart. Discover a place where you can explore without boundaries.
            </p>
            <p>
             Our natural landscape draws visitors
             <a href="http://www.visittucson.org/things-to-do/outdoors">
              outdoors
             </a>
             where there is plenty of room to roam.
            </p>
            <p>
             A bit off the beaten path, Tucson’s unique
             <a href="http://www.visittucson.org/things-to-do/categories/attractions">
              attractions
             </a>
             and
             <a href="http://www.visittucson.org/visit/accommodations">
              accommodations
             </a>
             beckon you to discover and explore and our
             <a href="http://www.visittucson.org/things-to-do/arts">
              arts and culture
             </a>
             scene makes Tucson a place that’s authentic and comfortable.
            </p>
            <p>
             Savor the unapologetic passion for food that inspires Tucson’s
             <a href="http://www.visittucson.org/food-drink/restaurants">
              cuisine
             </a>
             , or comes as you are and experience our vibrant
             <a href="http://www.visittucson.org/things-to-do/restaurants/nightlife">
              nightlife
             </a>
             .
            </p>
            <p>
             An oasis from the unoriginal and pretentious, Tucson is a place that you want to be. Let Tucson inspire the free spirit in you.
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
           </div>
           <p class="blockquote">
            <span>
             Tucson had opened my eyes to the world and given me... a taste for the sensory extravagance of red hot chiles and five-alarm sunsets.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p class="byline">
            - Barbara Kingsolver
           </p>
