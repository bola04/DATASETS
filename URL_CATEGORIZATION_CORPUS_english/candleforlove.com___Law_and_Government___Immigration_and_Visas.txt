 can take 1 to 2 years for our fiancees and spouses to obtain their
 
 visa. During the visa process many issues and questions may arise
 
 from procedure, paperwork, and unclear or conflicting instructions.
 
 While the Department of Homeland Security - DHS, United States
 
 Citizen and Immigration Service (USCIS), and State Department have
 
 the final say, we provide an additional resource to aid and support
 
 each other along the way.

 family based visa or have already succeeded, you will find Candle for

 Candle for Love.
