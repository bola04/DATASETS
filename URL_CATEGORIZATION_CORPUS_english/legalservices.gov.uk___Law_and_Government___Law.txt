          <section class="what-we-do" id="what-we-do">
           <div class="content">
            <h1 class="label">
             What we do
            </h1>
            <div class="overview">
             <p class="description">
              We provide civil and criminal legal aid and advice in England and Wales to help people deal with their legal problems.
             </p>
             <p class="parent_organisations">
              <abbr title="Legal Aid Agency">
               LAA
              </abbr>
              is an executive agency, sponsored by the
              <a class="brand__color" href="/government/organisations/ministry-of-justice">
               Ministry of Justice
              </a>
              .
             </p>
             <p>
              <a href="/government/organisations/legal-aid-agency/about" id="read_more_link">
               Read more about what we do
              </a>
             </p>
            </div>
           </div>
