         <p>
          <b>
           WikiSummaries
          </b>
          provides
          <a class="external text" href="http://www.wikisummaries.org/wiki/Category:Summaries" rel="nofollow">
           <i>
            free book summaries
           </i>
          </a>
          that anyone can contribute to! The current list of summaries is
          <a href="/wiki/Category:Summaries" title="Category:Summaries">
           here
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          <br/>
          The community is open to everyone. WikiSummaries and its contributers have the goal of helping to bring knowledge to the world, for
          <i>
           free
          </i>
          , and even
          <i>
           <b>
            you
           </b>
          </i>
          can help! Want to help?
          <b>
           <a class="external text" href="http://www.wikisummaries.org/index.php?title=Special:Userlogin&amp;type=signup" rel="nofollow">
            join our community
           </a>
          </b>
          and get started. Need help editing or writing summaries?
          <a href="/wiki/WikiSummaries:Manual_of_Style" title="WikiSummaries:Manual of Style">
           Click here
          </a>
          ! Don't know what summaries are but would like to know?
          <a href="/wiki/WikiSummaries:Writing_summaries" title="WikiSummaries:Writing summaries">
           Click here
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <h2>
          <span class="mw-headline" id="Readers.2C_Authors.2C_and_Publishers:_Help_Us_Add_Summaries.21">
           Readers, Authors, and Publishers: Help Us Add Summaries!
          </span>
         </h2>
         <p>
          Great books need your help! If there's a book you love that's not here then start a summary! Even a paragraph to get the summary off the ground helps a lot. If you are an author, this is a great place to give readers more information about your work. If you are a publisher, adding summaries here will help people find your titles online.
         </p>
         <p>
          <br/>
          <b>
           We need your help to create new summaries. To create a new summary, type the title in the box below.
          </b>
         </p>
