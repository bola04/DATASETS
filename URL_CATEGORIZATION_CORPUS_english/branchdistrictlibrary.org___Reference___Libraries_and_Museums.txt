               <strong>
                <img alt="Libraries Rock! Logo" height="328" src="https://www.branchdistrictlibrary.org/sites/default/files/Slogan%202-small.png" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="400"/>

                  Family
                 </a>
                </li>
               </ul>
              </div>
              <p>
              </p>
              <p>
               REGISTER HERE - Summer Reading for the whole Family starts June 4th and runs through July 21. Children, Teens, and Adults can read to win cool prizes, attend fun programs, and enjoy an awesome Summer at the Library. New for 2018 is the opportunity to register and log your reading time using Wandoo Reader. From the comfort of your home you can read and win!
              </p>
              <p>
               Sign up online starting May 29 or pick up a paper Registration Form at your nearest BDL Branch. Log your Summer Reading, starting on June 4, and be entered into the chance to win great Prizes!
