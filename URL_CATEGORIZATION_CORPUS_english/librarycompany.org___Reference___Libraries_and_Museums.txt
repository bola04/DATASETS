                       <p class="BasicParagraph">

                       <p>
                        Join us as we bring Birch’s Views to life! Enjoy a two-mile, two-hour tour of Birch’s Views in Philadelphia’s Historic District! On this tour, you will encounter Congress Hall, the State House (now Independence Hall), the back of the State House, and Pennsylvania Hospital! You will also learn about scenes depicted in Birch’s Views that display buildings that are no longer standing, such as the Alms House, New Theatre, and Surgeons Hall; places that, today, can only be seen through the eyes of William Birch. The Walking Tour will begin at 10:00 am. This tour will depart from Congress Hall, and will conclude outside of LCP.

                       <h2 class="BasicParagraph">
                        William Birch Bicycle Tour
                       </h2>
                       <p class="BasicParagraph">
                        <strong>
                         Saturday, September 15
                        </strong>
                       </p>
                       <p class="BasicParagraph">
                        <strong>
                         10:00 am
                        </strong>
                       </p>
                       <hr/>
                       <p>
                        Join us on a special bicycle tour as we bring to life Birch’s
                        <em>
                         Country Seats
                        </em>
                        ! For one day, and one day only, enjoy a leisurely, informative, and exciting tour wherein you will cycle to, and learn more, select sites featured in Birch’s
                        <em>
                         Country Seats
                        </em>
                        such as
                        <em>
                        </em>
                        two of Birch’s houses -Woodlands and Solitude- in addition to the sites of demolished houses such as Echo, Lansdowne, Belmont, Prospect Lodge, Mendenhall, and nearby extant houses not pictured by Birch such as Cedar Grove, Laurel Hill, Lemon Hill, Historic Strawberry Mansion, Mount Pleasant, and Woodford. The tour will begin at 1 Boathouse Row, Philadelphia, PA 19130. The tour will leave promptly at 10 o’ clock AM. Please arrive ten minutes prior to the departure time. Bikes will not be provided by the Library Company of Philadelphia. If you do not have a bicycle, we recommend renting a bike from Indego, https://www.rideindego.com/ or Wheel Fun Rentals https://wheelfunrentals.com/pa/philadelphia/boathouse-row/. Please bring water, and please wear a helmet! Your tour guide will be standing directly outside Wheel Fun Rentals.
