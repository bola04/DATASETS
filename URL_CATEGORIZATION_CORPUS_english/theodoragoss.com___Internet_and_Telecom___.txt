        <h2 class="entry-title">
         About
        </h2>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          Welcome!
         </p>
         <p>
          I’m a writer of novels, short stories, essays, and poems. You can read more about me on my Press page. The Novels, Stories, Essays, and Poems pages list my publications, including some that are available to read online. The Purchase page tells you where you can buy my work. The Free page links to writing of mine that is available online for free. This page is about what’s happening now. Look below to see what I have coming out, where I will be appearing this year, and where you can find more information about me. This page also tells you how to contact me.
         </p>
         <p>
          My Blog is updated regularly, so if you would like to know what I’m working on or thinking about, you can always check there. I hope you enjoy browsing!
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Publications
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          My first novel,
          <a href="https://www.amazon.com/Strange-Case-Alchemists-Daughter/dp/148146650X/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
           <em>
            The Strange Case of the Alchemist’s Daughter
           </em>
          </a>
          (The Extraordinary Adventures of the Athena Club #1), came out from Saga Press last summer.  Here is the fabulous cover:
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="final-cover" class="alignnone size-thumbnail wp-image-9005" height="150" sizes="(max-width: 99px) 100vw, 99px" src="https://theodoragoss.files.wordpress.com/2010/11/final-cover.jpg?w=99&amp;h=150" srcset="https://theodoragoss.files.wordpress.com/2010/11/final-cover.jpg?w=99&amp;h=150 99w, https://theodoragoss.files.wordpress.com/2010/11/final-cover.jpg?w=198&amp;h=300 198w" width="99"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          The second book in the series,
          <a href="https://www.amazon.com/European-Monstrous-Gentlewoman-Extraordinary-Adventures/dp/1481466534/ref=asap_bc?ie=UTF8">
           <em>
            European Travel for the Monstrous Gentlewoman
           </em>
          </a>
          (The Extraordinary Adventures of the Athena  Club #2), will be coming out this summer, on July 10th. The cover is equally fabulous:
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-thumbnail wp-image-9258" height="150" sizes="(max-width: 99px) 100vw, 99px" src="https://theodoragoss.files.wordpress.com/2018/05/european-travel-for-the-monstrous-gentlewoman-1000.jpg?w=99&amp;h=150" srcset="https://theodoragoss.files.wordpress.com/2018/05/european-travel-for-the-monstrous-gentlewoman-1000.jpg?w=99&amp;h=150 99w, https://theodoragoss.files.wordpress.com/2018/05/european-travel-for-the-monstrous-gentlewoman-1000.jpg?w=198&amp;h=300 198w" width="99"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          The third book in the series will be coming out in the summer of 2019. These novels are based on a novella of mine originally published on
          <em>
           Strange Horizons
          </em>
          , which you can read online: “
          <a href="http://www.strangehorizons.com/2010/20100118/daughter-f.shtml" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
           The Mad Scientist’s Daughter
          </a>
          .”
         </p>
         <p>
          Here are some short stories, essays, and poems of mine that came out recently:
         </p>
         <p>
          “
          <a href="http://dailysciencefiction.com/fantasy/fairy-tales/theodora-goss/snow-blood-fur" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
           Snow, Blood, Fur
          </a>
          ,” on
          <em>
           Daily Science Fiction
          </em>
          , is a “Little Red Riding Hood” retelling that I wrote while teaching a writing workshop. I asked all my students to write a piece of short fiction, and I wrote one as well — this story grew out of that exercise.
         </p>
         <p>
          “
          <a href="http://www.tor.com/2017/03/09/come-see-the-living-dryad/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
           Come See the Living Dryad
          </a>
          ,” on Tor.com, is about an unusual woman with a very unusual disease, and the great-great granddaughter who tries to find out what actually happened to her.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-thumbnail wp-image-9085" height="150" sizes="(max-width: 100px) 100vw, 100px" src="https://theodoragoss.files.wordpress.com/2010/11/come-see-the-living-dryad.jpg?w=100&amp;h=150" srcset="https://theodoragoss.files.wordpress.com/2010/11/come-see-the-living-dryad.jpg?w=100&amp;h=150 100w, https://theodoragoss.files.wordpress.com/2010/11/come-see-the-living-dryad.jpg?w=200&amp;h=300 200w" width="100"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          “
          <a href="http://www.tor.com/2016/05/04/red-as-blood-and-white-as-bone/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
           Red as Blood and White as Bone
          </a>
          ,” also on Tor.com, is about a girl who believes in fairy tales until one comes knocking on her door.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Tor.com Cover for Red as Blood" class="alignnone size-thumbnail wp-image-8656" height="150" sizes="(max-width: 100px) 100vw, 100px" src="https://theodoragoss.files.wordpress.com/2010/11/tor-com-cover-for-red-as-blood.jpg?w=100&amp;h=150" srcset="https://theodoragoss.files.wordpress.com/2010/11/tor-com-cover-for-red-as-blood.jpg?w=100&amp;h=150 100w, https://theodoragoss.files.wordpress.com/2010/11/tor-com-cover-for-red-as-blood.jpg?w=200&amp;h=300 200w" width="100"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          An essay of mind, “Professor Tolkien and the Fairies,” was published in the Tolkien-themed spring issue of
          <a href="https://www.faeriemag.com/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
           <em>
            Faerie Magazine
           </em>
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          I had a poem published recently in
          <em>
           Uncanny Magazine
          </em>
          : “
          <a href="https://uncannymagazine.com/article/persephone-in-hades/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
           Persephone in Hades
          </a>
          .”
         </p>
