          </h3>
          <p>
           At Claire Wallace Wedding Hair and Makeup, we are passionate about what we do: We pride ourselves in providing you with a very personal service so that you have an amazing wedding day experience! We are here to offer you a professional mobile wedding hair and makeup service, working in and around Southern counties to include Central London, East Anglia (Norfolk), South East and South West of England.

Our team uses their unique experience to explore a wide range of possibilities, allowing you to choose a style and image which makes you feel elegant and sophisticated. We embrace the experience of working with the entire wedding party and take pleasure in including your bridesmaids, flower girls and the mother of the bride on this special day.
