        <h2>
         <span class="mw-headline" id="What_is_Lojban.3F">
          What is Lojban?
         </span>
        </h2>
        <div class="thumb tright">
         <div class="thumbinner" style="width:242px;">
          <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QdlGxgKt7nc" target="_blank">
           <img alt="" class="thumbimage" height="160" src="/images/7/70/yt-lightning_talk.png" width="240"/>
          </a>
          <div class="thumbcaption">
           <div class="magnify">
            <a class="internal" href="/papri/File:yt-lightning_talk.png" title="Enlarge">
            </a>
           </div>
           a «lightning talk» introduction to Lojban by Timo Paulssen
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <p>
         Lojban is a carefully constructed spoken language. It has been built for over 50 years by dozens of workers and hundreds of supporters.
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Lojban's grammar is simpler than of most natural languages, but complex in its own unique way. Lojban is an experiment in thought, as well: its users aim to see how its unique language structures affect thought and understanding.
         </li>
         <li>
          Lojban allows the expression of tiny nuances in emotion, using words called
          <a class="mw-redirect" href="/papri/attitudinals" title="attitudinals">
           attitudinals
          </a>
          , which are essentially spoken emoticons.
         </li>
         <li>
          Lojban allows you to communicate concisely without unnecessary or undesired details. For example, you don't have to always think of what tense (past, present or future) to use in a verb when it's already clear from context.
         </li>
         <li>
          Lojban is machine parsable, which allows potential new explorations in the fields of artificial intelligence and machine translation.
         </li>
         <li>
          Lojban has a live community of speakers expanding its vocabulary day by day.
         </li>
         <li>
          Lojban is designed aiming at being neutral between cultures.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <h3>
         <span class="mw-headline" id="Lojban_means_different_things_to_different_people">
          Lojban means different things to different people
         </span>
        </h3>
        <ul>
         <li>
          a linguistic curiosity - a test-bed for language experimentation
         </li>
         <li>
          a challenging way to expand their minds or discipline their thoughts
         </li>
         <li>
          a new perspective on languages
         </li>
         <li>
          an entertaining medium to communicate with friends or create art
         </li>
         <li>
          a domain for exploring the intersection of human language and software
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         What will Lojban mean for you?
        </p>

        <h2>
         <span class="mw-headline" id="Learning_Lojban">
          Learning Lojban
         </span>
        </h2>
        <p>
         These are learning resources that are made by people speaking Lojban and that represent personal views of those authors:
        </p>
        <div class="thumb tright">
         <div class="thumbinner" style="width:242px;">
          <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yZjSTUK3hFI" target="_blank">
           <img alt="" class="thumbimage" height="160" src="/images/8/8c/yt-lesson_in_lojban_i.png" width="240"/>
          </a>
          <div class="thumbcaption">
           <div class="magnify">
            <a class="internal" href="/papri/File:yt-lesson_in_lojban_i.png" title="Enlarge">
            </a>
           </div>
           «Lessons in Lojban» by Dustin Lacewell
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <ul>
         <li>
          A set of
          <a href="/papri/pronunciation" title="pronunciation">
           pronunciation guides
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          <a class="external text" href="https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1Yrxt9gAtqA_oSDD76Toq8VLSZjjOxJ5rkKsxFs3Cw8E/pub?start=true&amp;loop=false&amp;delayms=5000" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
           Lojban in simple phrases
          </a>
          , an introduction (also available in
          <a class="external text" href="http://mw.lojban.org/images/d/d1/Lojban._How_to_quickly_create_sentences.pdf" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
           PDF
          </a>
          )
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="/papri/Lojban_Wave_Lessons" title="Lojban Wave Lessons">
           Wave Lessons
          </a>
          , a popular textbook
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="/papri/The_Crash_Course_(a_draft)" title="The Crash Course (a draft)">
           "The Crash Course"
          </a>
          , a draft of a textbook
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="/papri/la_karda" title="la karda">
           la karda
          </a>
          , a rapid-fire overview of the grammar in small pieces
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         This is the resource officially approved by The Logical Language Group:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <a class="external text" href="https://lojban.org/publications/cll/cll_v1.1_xhtml-section-chunks/" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
           "The Complete Lojban Language", a reference grammar of Lojban
          </a>
          . This book will help those who already understand some Lojban. It's the most complete reference of Lojban language by far. It does not, however, teach vocabulary, style, or colloquial language. Please note that since the initial publication of the reference grammar, some language reforms such as
          <a href="/papri/How_to_use_xorlo" title="How to use xorlo">
           xorlo
          </a>
          have become widely adopted.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <h3>
         <span class="mw-headline" id="Dictionaries">
          Dictionaries
         </span>
        </h3>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <a class="external text" href="http://jbovlaste.lojban.org/" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
           la jbovlaste
          </a>
          is a prescriptive, editable, multilingual dictionary. It is considered by many to be the current de-facto standard dictionary of Lojban. Many other dictionaries and tools are based on it.
         </li>
         <li>
          <a class="external text" href="http://vlasisku.lojban.org/" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
           la vlasisku
          </a>
          is an online English—Lojban dictionary, based on data of la jbovlaste; intended to be more user-friendly (note: it is only irregularily updated, la jbovlaste is more up-to-date).
         </li>
         <li>
          <a class="external text" href="https://la-lojban.github.io/sutysisku/en/#" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
           la sutysisku
          </a>
          is an online multilingual Lojban dictionary (including English) that also works offline. Updated at least once a month.
         </li>
         <li>
          <a class="external text" href="https://sites.google.com/site/slovlacukt/file-cabinet" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
           la slovlacukt
          </a>
          , an alternative offline dictionary.
         </li>
         <li>
          <a class="external text" href="http://guskant.github.io/lojbo/stardict.html" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
           Stardict dictionaries in some languages
          </a>
          (usable with
          <a class="external text" href="http://goldendict.org/" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
           GoldenDict
          </a>
          ,
          <a class="external text" href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/dictionary-universal/id312088272?mt=8" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
           Dictionary Universal
          </a>
          , etc.)
         </li>
         <li>
          <a class="external text" href="http://guskant.github.io/lojbo/gismu-cmavo.html" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
           Multilingual gismu (root word) and cmavo (particle) lists in tsv and html formats
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          <a class="mw-redirect" href="/papri/Word_frequency_lists" title="Word frequency lists">
           Word lists ordered by frequency of use
          </a>
         </li>
        </ul>

        <p>
         Lojban is spoken by a vibrant community, producing literature, music, and art, and discussing questions about language and thought. The web comic
         <a class="external text" href="https://www.xkcd.com/191/" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
          xkcd
         </a>
         said that, if you spoke Lojban, you'd have to speak with Lojbanists. That may not be such a bad thing!

        <h3>
         <span class="mw-headline" id="Instant_messaging_.28text_chat.29">
          Instant messaging (text chat)
         </span>
        </h3>
        <h4>
         <span class="mw-headline" id="Connecting_quickly">
          Connecting quickly
         </span>
        </h4>
        <p>
         To get connected to Lojban chat use any of the following methods (your and others' messages will be available in all of them no matter which method you use).
        </p>
        <p>
         People involved in Lojban community usually read each day's backlog and will answer your questions within several hours and often even within minutes.
        </p>
        <h4>
         <span class="mw-headline" id="Telegram">
          Telegram
         </span>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Using this method you will be able to read the history of the chat since the beginning of 2016.
        </p>
        <ol>
         <li>
          Install the free
          <a class="external text" href="https://telegram.org" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
           Telegram messenger
          </a>
          on your Apple, Android or your desktop device
         </li>
         <li>
          join main Lojban chat groups by following these links:
          <ul>
           <li>
            <span class="plainlinks">
             <a class="external text" href="https://telegram.me/joinchat/BLVsYz3hCF-CtYw0-2IkqQ" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
              Lojban
             </a>
             ,
             <a class="external text" href="https://telegram.me/joinchat/BLVsYz4hC9ulWahupDLovA" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
              ckule
             </a>
             ,
             <a class="external text" href="https://telegram.me/joinchat/BLVsYz20Boixl0xN-0TrPw" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
              jbosnu
             </a>
            </span>
           </li>
          </ul>
         </li>
         <li>
          Ask questions on Lojban in
          <b>
           ckule
          </b>
          group, talk in or about Lojban in
          <b>
           Lojban
          </b>
          group and talk only in Lojban in
          <b>
           jbosnu
          </b>
          group.
         </li>
        </ol>
        <h4>
         <span class="mw-headline" id="Slack">
          Slack
         </span>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Using this method you will be able to read the history of the chat for the last ~7 days or so.
        </p>
        <ol>
         <li>
          Open
          <span class="plainlinks">
           <a class="external free" href="https://slaka.herokuapp.com" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
            https://slaka.herokuapp.com
           </a>
          </span>
          Submit your email and get an invite to
          <span class="plainlinks">
           <a class="external free" href="http://lojban.slack.com" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
            http://lojban.slack.com
           </a>
          </span>
          to your e-mail inbox.
         </li>
         <li>
          Open
          <span class="plainlinks">
           <a class="external free" href="http://lojban.slack.com" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
            http://lojban.slack.com
           </a>
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          Ask questions on Lojban in #ckule channel, talk in or about Lojban in #lojban channel and talk only in Lojban in #jbosnu channel.
         </li>
        </ol>
        <h4>
         <span class="mw-headline" id="Direct_web_access">
          Direct web access
         </span>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Using this method you will be able to see messages that are sent only while you are connected.
        </p>
        <ol>
         <li>
          Navigate to
          <span class="plainlinks">
           <a class="external text" href="https://kiwiirc.com/client/chat.freenode.net/#lojban,#ckule,#jbosnu" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
            the web-based IRC client (Kiwi)
           </a>
          </span>
          , choose a nickname you want yourself to be known as, and click "Start". Easy!
         </li>
         <li>
          Click on the channel you want (tabs on the top of the page), and type your text in the text bar at the bottom - the last line at the bottom of the chat page.
         </li>
         <li>
          Ask questions on Lojban in #ckule tab, talk in or about Lojban in #lojban tab and talk only in Lojban in #jbosnu tab.
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p>
         You may alternately try
         <a href="/papri/Lojban_Live_Chat#Direct_web_access" title="Lojban Live Chat">
          other methods of connecting
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <h3>
         <span class="mw-headline" id="Mailing_lists">
          Mailing lists
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p>
         The
         <a class="external text" href="http://groups.google.com/group/lojban-beginners/topics" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
          lojban-beginners mailing list
         </a>
         is for questions about learning and understanding Lojban. You can subscribe to the list by sending a question to
         <a class="external text" href="mailto:lojban-beginners@googlegroups.com" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
          lojban-beginners@googlegroups.com
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         The general-purpose
         <a class="external text" href="http://groups.google.com/group/lojban/topics" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
          lojban mailing list
         </a>
         , which covers anything else related to Lojban, was founded in 1989, and has about 500 subscribers. You can subscribe to it by sending an email to
         <a class="external text" href="mailto:lojban@googlegroups.com" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
          lojban@googlegroups.com
         </a>
         .
        </p>

        <h2>
         <span class="mw-headline" id="What.27s_next.3F">
          What's next?
         </span>
        </h2>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Try reading Lojban
          <a href="/papri/te_gerna_la_lojban" title="te gerna la lojban">
           texts
          </a>
          . Start with something shorter and easier such as "
          <a href="/papri/la_.teris._po%27u_lo_tirxu_cu_vitke_zi%27o_le_barda_tcadu" title="la .teris. po'u lo tirxu cu vitke zi'o le barda tcadu">
           Terry the Tiger
          </a>
          " (audio is available) or "
          <a class="mw-redirect" href="/papri/The_Berenstain_Bears_and_the_Prize_Pumpkin" title="The Berenstain Bears and the Prize Pumpkin">
           The Berenstain Bears and the Prize Pumpkin
          </a>
          " (
          <a class="external text" href="http://guskant.github.io/gerna_cipra/maltufa-1.1.html" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
           a parser suitable to those two texts
          </a>
          ). When you're ready, move on to "
          <a href="/papri/le_cmalu_noltru" title="le cmalu noltru">
           The Little Prince
          </a>
          " (suitable parser:
          <a class="external text" href="http://lojban.github.io/ilmentufa/camxes.html" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
           camxes
          </a>
          ) and "
          <a class="external text" href="http://selpahi.de/oz.html" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
           The Wonderful Wizard of Oz
          </a>
          " (suitable parser:
          <a href="/papri/maftufa" title="maftufa">
           maftufa
          </a>
          ).
         </li>
        </ul>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Try your hand at translating into Lojban, or write your own original texts: Poetry, prose, blogs, tweets, etc.
         </li>
         <li>
          Continue learning vocabulary using
          <a href="/papri/Lojbanic_Software" title="Lojbanic Software">
           memorization software
          </a>
          .
         </li>
         <li>
          Help expand the
          <a class="external text" href="http://jbovlaste.lojban.org/" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">
           dictionary
          </a>
          by translating Lojban words into other languages, or coin new compound words and add them.
         </li>
        </ul>
