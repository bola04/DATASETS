        </h1>

        <div class="content clear-block">
         <p>
          At Allegheny Health Network (AHN), we put our patients first. You get the right care at the right time in your neighborhood and throughout western Pennsylvania. And it’s personalized according to your needs.
         </p>
         <p>
          Our doctors, nurses, and staff are experts in the latest medical research and technology, but they specialize in compassion. Visit any of our hundreds of care centers and eight hospitals across the region and see for yourself.
         </p>
         <p>
          At AHN, we’re committed to providing you with the best possible care that’s affordable. Our multidisciplinary teams share knowledge and collaborate on your care plan.
         </p>
         <p>
          And we are here when you need us — even if you call the same day or need to come in after hours. You are the Living Proof of why we do what we do. And the difference we can make.
         </p>
