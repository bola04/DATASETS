          <span style="color: #0c0c0c;">
           NATIONAL AWARD WINNER – ADULT FICTION    |
          </span>
          <span style="color: #323232;">
           <b>
            AMAZON #1 BESTSELLER
           </b>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          Kathy Lynn Harris is the author of two Amazon bestselling novels:
          <a href="http://www.kathylynnharris.com/blue-straggler-contemporary-fiction/" title="Blue Straggler | Contemporary Fiction">
           <i>
            Blue Straggler,
           </i>
          </a>
          which remained in the top 10 in comic fiction for 12 months,
          <i>
          </i>
          and
          <a href="http://www.kathylynnharris.com/good-kind-knowing-contemporary-fiction/" title="A Good Kind of Knowing | New Fiction">
           <i>
            A Good Kind of Knowing
           </i>
          </a>
          , which earned top literary honors in 2013 from the National Federation of Press Women. Her most recent children’s book,
          <a href="http://www.kathylynnharris.com/higgenbloom-dancing-grandmas-childrens-picture-book/" title="Higgenbloom &amp; the Dancing Grandmas | A Children’s Picture Book">
           <i>
            Higgenbloom and the Dancing Grandmas
           </i>
          </a>
          , is a growing favorite with both the rockin’ grandma and honey bee hipster scenes.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.kathylynnharris.com/about/">
           Kathy’s a native Texan
          </a>
          , and like the late, great Molly Ivins, she’s mostly proud of it. Her favorite things include soft dogs, good friends, smooth vodka and cheese.
         </p>
