      <a id="about">
      </a>
      <p align="justify">
       Please note Maree has been offered a once in a lifetime opportunity to work on the paradise island of Bermuda for a year and will return to the UK in summer 2018.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Maree is based at her Bristol studio where she teaches both private and equipment lessons, she also teaches group matwork classes in Tockington and Thornbury. Maree has been a Body Control Pilates® trained instructor since 2001 and is qualified to teach advanced level matwork, Reformer, Cadillac, Chair and Ladder Barrel. She also had the privilege to study under Lolita San Miguel (Pilates Elder) in the USA and graduated with a Master Mentor certification in October 2010. Maree is one of only two Pilates Instructors based in the UK to achieve this prestigious qualification.
 
 Highly passionate about her ongoing training Maree continues to enjoy her own professional development, please see opposite about her completion of the Master Mentor Program. Before becoming involved in Pilates, Maree was a dance teacher and worked with many diverse groups in the community. This experience gives her particular skills in tailoring her teaching approach to individuals needs. She is highly experienced at rehabilitating clients suffering from both acute and chronic injury.
      </p>

       PILATES MASTER MENTOR PROGRAM
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Lolita San Miguel is known as one of the 'Elders' of Pilates as Joseph Pilates himself trained her. She is one of only two, herself and Cathy Grant, who were actually certified by Joseph Pilates to teach the method. Having practiced Pilates for over 50 years Lolita now runs the Pilates Master Mentor Program. She created this course to pass her wealth of knowledge to a new generation of personally chosen Pilates Instructors.
 Maree feels very honored to be one of the first 40 Instructors worldwide to have completed this program, and one of only two based in the UK.
 Lolita is a truly fascinating, inspirational person, so please click the following link to read more about her and the Master Mentor Program
       <a href="http://lolitapilates.com" target="_blank">
        http://lolitapilates.com
