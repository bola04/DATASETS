     <p>
      What is The Cameron Collector? It's a place to learn and share knowledge for:
     </p>
     <ul>
      <li>
       Hard-core Cameron collectors
      </li>
      <li>
       Enthusiasts
      </li>
      <li>
       Even golfers planning to buy their first Cameron putter.
      </li>
     </ul>
     <p>
      Various members are knowledgeable on many aspects of collecting, including:
     </p>
     <ul>
      <li>
       Pre-Titleist models
      </li>
      <li>
       Hand made Scott Cameron putters
      </li>
      <li>
       Garage putters
      </li>
      <li>
       Tour models
      </li>
      <li>
       Titleist production models
      </li>
      <li>
       Limited editions
      </li>
      <li>
       Headcovers
      </li>
      <li>
       Pivot tools
      </li>
      <li>
       Heck, even left handed models!
      </li>
     </ul>
     <p>
      The Cameron Collector is all about participating in conversation about Scotty Cameron putters. You'll find that Cameron enthusiasts are typically a generous, fun loving group of people. We have collectors 15-75 years of age, male and female, scratch golfers, and well, a few people who could use some lessons.
     </p>
     <p>
      While many of us collect other golf items and even other putters, since this is The Cameron Collector, when we talk about putters, it's all about Scotty's work.
     </p>
     <p>
      We don't accept advertising and we don't sell anything (although once you're established as a member, you can use our buy-sell-trade section) in order to keep The Cameron Collector a great place to get involved in this wonderful hobby.
     </p>
     <p>
      So, what do we do here at The Cameron Collector?
     </p>
     <ul>
      <li>
       Talk about Cameron putters and accessories
      </li>
      <li>
       Ask questions and share answers
      </li>
      <li>
       Share photos of our collections
      </li>
      <li>
       Buy/sell/trade
      </li>
      <li>
       Get insight from the top collectors
      </li>
      <li>
       Just chat about, well, almost anything in The Club House
      </li>
     </ul>
     <p>
      We even share regular conversations with Scotty Cameron!
     </p>
