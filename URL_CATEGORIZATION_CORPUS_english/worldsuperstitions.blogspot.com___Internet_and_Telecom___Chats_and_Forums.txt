              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header">
              <div class="post-header-line-1">
              </div>

             <div class="post-body entry-content">
              In Vietnam, when giving gifts for brides and grooms on their wedding day, they are usually given in pairs, including blankets. Avoid giving a single item because it is believed that it will cause bad luck that the marriage is not expected to last long. Always opted for two less expensive items than a single more expensive one.

             <h3 class="post-title entry-title">
              <a href="http://worldsuperstitions.blogspot.com/2013/04/india-death.html">
               India - Death
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header">
              <div class="post-header-line-1">
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="post-body entry-content">
              It is believed that in India, when there is a birth or death in the family, the family members should not go to a temple or light a lamp at home, for 15 days. For death, they don't attend any marriages and do not celebrate any festivals for a year.
