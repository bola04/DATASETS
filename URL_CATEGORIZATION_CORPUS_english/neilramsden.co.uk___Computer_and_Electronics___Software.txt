             consultancy, built on thirty years of experience.

             looking for.
      </p>
      <p>
       I have a special interest in renewable energy technologies. In
             particular, I've longstanding experience of working in the wind
             turbine industry.
      </p>

       My aim is to use a versatile mix of skills to get the right
             solution to your mathematics or computer based needs.
      </p>

             engineers, and computer software development in a variety of coding
             languages, including Python and C++.

      <h2>
       Word Building and Spelling
      </h2>
      <p>
       A growing proportion of my time has been spent in looking at ways
             of applying computers to understanding and teaching English
             vocabulary and spelling, particularly by applying the approach
             known in linguistics as
       <em>
        morphology
       </em>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       This web-site includes
       <a href="spelling/index.html" title="Spelling and vocabulary micro-site">
        free web resources
       </a>
       for
             investigating the structures making up the English writing system.
      </p>
      <p>
       They include an
       <a href="spelling/glossary/glossary.html">
        Orthographic Glossary
       </a>
       , a
             powerful
       <a href="spelling/searcher.html">
        Word Searcher
       </a>
       ,
             an interactive
       <a href="spelling/suffixing.html">
        Suffix Checker
       </a>
       and
       <a href="spelling/matrix/index.html">
        Mini Matrix-Maker
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       You can also get a copy of
       <a href="microscope/index.html">
        Word
             Microscope
       </a>
       , which is a downloadable PC program for peering into
             the structure of a chosen specimen word.
      </p>
      <p>
       Word Microscope guides you in creating your own linked word
             families to help with vocabulary and spelling.
      </p>
