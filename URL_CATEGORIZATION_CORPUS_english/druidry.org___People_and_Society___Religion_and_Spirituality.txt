        Druid Grove, Order of Bards, Ovates and Druids

                 <div id="home-page-circle">
                 </div>
                 <div id="home-page-text">
                  <p>
                   Druidry is a vital and dynamic nature spirituality
                   <br/>
                   that is flourishing all over the world.
                   <br/>
                   It unites our love of the earth with our love of creativity
                   <br/>
                   and the arts.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   Flowing through all the exciting new developments in
                   <br/>
                   modern druidism
                   <br/>
                   is the power of an ancient tradition:
                   <br/>
                   The love of land, sea and sky - The love of the
                   <br/>
                   earth our home.
                  </p>
                 </div>
                </div>
               </div>

                    <h2>
                     Shamanism
                    </h2>
                    <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
                     <div class="field-items">
                      <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
                       <p>
                        The Order of Bards, Ovates &amp; Druids works with Druidry as a spiritual way and practice that speaks to three of our greatest yearnings: to be fully creative in our lives, to commune deeply with the world of Nature, and to gain access to a source of profound wisdom.
                       </p>
                      </div>
                     </div>
                    </div>

                    <h2>
                     Magic
                    </h2>
                    <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
                     <div class="field-items">
                      <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
                       <p>
                        Druidry teaches the art of how we can open to the magic of being alive, the art of how we can bring ideas into manifestation, and the art of journeying in quest of wisdom, healing and inspiration.
                        <br/>
                       </p>

                    <h2>
                     Philosophy
                    </h2>
                    <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
                     <div class="field-items">
                      <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
                       <p>
                        Our Bardic teachings help nurture the singer, the artist or storyteller within: the creative self. Ovate teachings help foster the shaman, the lover of Nature, the healer within. The Druid teachings help develop our inner wisdom: the sage who dwells within each of us.
                       </p>
