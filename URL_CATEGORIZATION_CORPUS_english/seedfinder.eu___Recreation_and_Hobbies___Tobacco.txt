       <h2>
        Welcome to SeedFinder.eu
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img alt="Logo" class="floatleft" height="58" src="pics/banner/seedfinder-code.png" title="Welcome to SeedFinder.eu" width="234"/>
        SeedFinder.eu collects and standardizes information about thousands of cannabis varieties. We offer an interface for cannabis breeders, seedbanks, cultivation and hemp-forums, communities, growers and cannabis-enthusiasts all around the world. With the help of our SeedFinder-System - especially made for this purposes - we are able to collect all important information about individual strains in a compact and searchable way. We enable you to find the tips and tricks for cultivation and growth who are scattered all around the web or to find pictures, profiles and opinions. Help us to expand and advance the single strain-descriptions with your knowledge.
        <span class="highlight fett">
         Important:
        </span>
        <span class="RoTUnter">
         The SeedFinder is not a shop
        </span>
        , we do not sell any cannabis seed and are not addicted to a breeder or shop.
       </p>
      </div>

       <section>
        <h1 class="bigRound1">
         Database
        </h1>
        <div class="partInnerDiv">
         <p>
          Behind the SeedFinder there is a strain-database with momentary
          <a href="database/strains/" title="view all cannabis-strains">
           <span class="highlight">
            13034 different cannabis varieties
           </span>
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <h2 class="topEm smallah">
          Breeder &amp; Strains
         </h2>
         <p>
          12318 of the listed strains are from
          <a href="database/breeder/" title="Browse strains by breeder / seedbank and find links to all available seedbanks.">
           679 known breeders
          </a>
          - connected and expanded with
          <a href="database/strains/origin/" title="List of species sorted by origin, local land races and their hybrids.">
           92 pure local breeds
          </a>
          . You also can find
          <a href="database/strains/cloneonly/" title="Clone Only's">
           125 clone-only strains
          </a>
          and last but not least
          <a href="database/strains/unknown-or-legendary/" title="Unknown / Legendary">
           499 various unknown or legendary varieties
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <h2 class="topEm smallah">
          Lineage / Hybrids
         </h2>
         <p>
          Our unique genetic-database will enable you to allocate related crosses, all descendants and the parents of each variety.  Browse through the strains with our dynamic HybridMaps - from the multi-hybrids via F1-Hybrids up to the landraces - and back!
         </p>
         <h2 class="topEm smallah">
          SeedShop Database
         </h2>
         <p>
          Our SeedPolice is monitoring seedbanks and collects information about reliability, service and shipping of this shops. Here you can find
          <a href="seedpolice/seedcops-list.html" title="SeedCops List">
           383 Shops
          </a>
          at the moment. 47 are also integrated into the SeedFinder, including offers and actual prices.
         </p>
         <h2 class="topEm smallah">
          Cup-Winner
         </h2>
         <p>
          The winners of the most important cannabis-cups are connected with our cannabis database. Check out the best rated and
          <a href="database/cup-winner/" title="Winners of different cannabis-contests">
           most famous strains
          </a>
          worldwide!
         </p>
         <h2 class="topEm smallah">
          Forums &amp; Threads
         </h2>
         <p>
          With the help of our
          <a href="threadfinder/threads.html" title="SeedFinders ThreadFinder">
           ThreadFinder
          </a>
          we connected the 38 largest and most popular cannabis communities to our page.
         </p>
         <h2 class="topEm smallah">
          Cannabis Gallery
         </h2>

          Into our
          <a href="cannabis-gallery.html" title="Strain Gallery">
           Cannabis-Gallery
          </a>
          you can find strain-related pictures. They are sorted by in- and outdoor plants and also by the flowering-time.

         </h2>
         <p>

          <a href="search/lineage/" title="Find hybrids and strains with specific genetics / parents.">
           family tree search-engine
          </a>
          to look out for special hybrids!

         <h2 class="topEm smallah">
          Pure strains
         </h2>
         <p>
          All strains come from somewhere, if known we store the heritage as exact as possible. Here you can list all
          <a href="database/strains/origin/" title="List of species sorted by origin, local land races and their hybrids.">
           pure varieties
          </a>
          by origin and countries.
         </p>
         <h2 class="topEm smallah">
          Strain Lists
         </h2>
         <p>
          You also can have a look onto our
          <a href="database/strains/alphabetical/" title="All our strains in an alphabetically order.">
           alphabetical
          </a>
          list of strains, or view only
          <a href="database/strains/autoflowering/" title="All autoflowering cannabis varieties">
           autoflowering
          </a>
          varieties - or the
          <a href="database/strains/cloneonly/" title="Clone Only Strains">
           clone-only
          </a>
          ones.
         </p>
         <h2 class="topEm smallah">
          Prices &amp; Radar
         </h2>
         <p>
          Into our
          <a href="seedshop-radar.html" title="SeedShop Radar :: Actual Seed-Offers">
           SeedShop-Radar
          </a>
          you will find actual reduced strains or offers seperately, we monitor only serious shops - a good place to grab a bargain! You also can use the search to find strains by commercial availibility and prices.
         </p>
         <h2 class="topEm smallah">
          Aroma, Taste &amp; High
         </h2>
         <p>
          With our Review-Function we also collect data about the aroma, the taste and the effect of the cannabis strains. Thanks that you are able to look out for strains with a special
          <a href="cloud/" title="Browse the varieties according to aroma, taste or effect.">
           effect, taste or aroma
          </a>
          and to visualize it with a nice pie-chart!
         </p>
         <h2 class="topEm smallah">
          Outdoor Strains
         </h2>
         <p>
          Get weather-data and possible varieties for your area! Simply add your geo-coordinates to
          <a href="research/climate/" title="SeedFinder Climate Info">
           get climate- and grow info
          </a>
          for your spot!
         </p>
         <h2 class="topEm smallah">
          CannaFox Add-On
         </h2>
         <p>
          Get our
          <a href="cannafox/" title="Browse the varieties according to aroma, taste or effect.">
           Mozilla-Add-On
          </a>
          , search for strains directly from your browser and get some additional and discreet SeedFinder options in a sidebar.
         </p>

         Research
        </h1>
        <div class="partInnerDiv">
         <p>
          SeedFinder collects, combines and provides a lots of useful data with the help of its users worldwide.
         </p>
         <h2 class="topEm smallah">
          Strain Reviews
         </h2>
         <p>
          We collect the main grow- and plant data directly from the grower with our multilingual
          <a href="research/reviews/" title="SeedFinder's Plant-Profile Functions">
           Strain Review Function
          </a>
          . Onto this way we can standardize grow-info, tips and tricks, smoke reports and valuations for the different varieties - and even for their phenotypes.
         </p>
         <h2 class="topEm smallah">
          Comparisons
         </h2>
         <p>
          With our special
          <a href="research/strain-vs-strain/" title="Direct Comparisons">
           Strain VS Strain
          </a>
          function we can directly compare the most important characteristics of individual cannabis-varieties.
         </p>
         <h2 class="topEm smallah">
          Medical Strains
         </h2>
         <p>
          For the medicinal properties of individual cannabis varieties we have installed the
          <a href="research/medical/" title="Our special database for medicinal cannabis strains.">
           Medicinal Strain Finder
          </a>
          . This helps in the search for certain varieties, and also stores information about their optimal dose for certain diseases.
         </p>
         <h2 class="topEm smallah">
          Climate Zones
         </h2>
         <p>
          SeedFinder uses its own ecophysiological climate specificiation system. Together with our database for historical weather data we can provide very detailed
          <a href="research/climate/" title="SeedFinders climate zone specifications for outdoor strains.">
           climate zones and weather data
          </a>
          even for your area! In combination with our reviews we are able to provide specific strains and detailed info for this zones.
         </p>
