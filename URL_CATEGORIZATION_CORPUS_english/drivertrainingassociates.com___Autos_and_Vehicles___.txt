         your
        </i>
        schedule by logging in and out of the course whenever you want to. All courses are available 24 / 7 / 365.

        Discount Pricing
       </h2>
       <p>

         New York Defensive Driving

         Georgia Driver Education
        </a>

       <h2 class="title_blue_sm">
        No Hidden Fees
       </h2>
       <p>
        We show all payment options up front so that you won't receive a "surprise" after you've paid for your course. DTA is a member of the Better Business Bureau and we are proud of our reputation as one of the few honest traffic school providers in the business.
       </p>
       <h2 class="title_blue_sm">
        Easy, User Friendly Courses
       </h2>
       <p>
        From traffic school to driver education, every course that we create is designed with the user in mind. Concepts are explained using clear, simple language that anyone can understand. This makes it easier for you to learn the material and retain it longer, so that you will use our defensive driving techniques in your everyday driving.
       </p>
       <h2 class="title_blue_sm">
        More Video and Animations, Less Reading
       </h2>
       <p>
        DTA's courses feature more video, animations and graphics to keep students interested in learning. We have raised the bar for interactive education by actually showing defensive driving maneuvers and crash scenarios rather than just talking about them. Many other online defensive driving courses are 100% reading.
       </p>
       <h2 class="title_blue_sm">
        100% Money-Back Guarantee
       </h2>
       <p>
        We care about customer satisfaction. If you are dissatisfied with your DTA course for any reason, you can contact us to request a refund as long as you have not passed the course, or are in a disqualified status.
       </p>
       <h2 class="title_blue_sm">
        U.S. Based Customer Support
       </h2>

        Whether you have a technical question or just need help deciding which course to sign up for, we are here for you. Contact us and one of our professionally trained, U.S. based support team members will assist you.

        More Courses
