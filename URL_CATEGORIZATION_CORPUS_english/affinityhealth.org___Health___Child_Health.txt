         <h2>
          We're here for you
         </h2>
         <h5>
          At Ascension St. Elizabeth Hospital, Ascension Mercy Medical Center and Ascension Calumet Medical Center, you'll get advanced care close to home.
         </h5>
         <h2>
          <strong style='color: rgb(68, 68, 68); line-height: 20px; font-family: "Droid Sans", sans-serif; font-size: 14px;'>
           <em>
            Why travel for expert care?
           </em>
          </strong>
         </h2>
         <p>
          You'll find everything you need right here at Affinity Health System.
         </p>
         <p>
          Our hospitals in Appleton, Oshkosh and Chilton offer the latest technology and genuine compassion. When you meet our experts in
          <a href="/Affinity/Services/Cancer.htm" linktype="2" target="_self">
           cancer care
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="/Affinity/Services/Obstetrics-PregnancyChildbirth.htm" linktype="2" target="_self">
           childbirth
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="/Affinity/Services/Robotic-Surgery.htm" linktype="2" target="_self">
           robotic surgery
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="/Affinity/Services/Neurosciences.htm" linktype="2" target="_self">
           neurosciences
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="/Affinity/Services/Orthopaedics.htm" linktype="2" target="_self">
           orthopedic
          </a>
          and more, you’ll discover they’re not just skilled at healing your body. They’re committed to lifting your spirits, too.
         </p>
         <p>
          For primary and specialty care at every age and stage of life, our
          <a href="/Affinity/Find-a-Location.htm" linktype="2" target="_self">
           26 convenient clinics
          </a>
          throughout the Fox Valley welcome you like family. We promise to listen, treat you with respect, and put your needs and interests first.
         </p>
        </div>
