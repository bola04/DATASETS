     <h2>
      About me
     </h2>
     <p>
      I've lived in Vermont since 1997 and I've been helping people learn to use computers at a Drop-In Time at the local tech center for the past 13 years. I have a Masters in Librarianship, my main interest area is the intersection of technology and civic engagement. I believe the digital divide is just one more symptom of an increasing distance between haves and have-nots in the United States.
     </p>
