           </h4>
           <p>
            June 2- September 2- Demuth Museum Location
           </p>
           <p>
            The exhibition and accompanying catalog focus on the Demuth Museum's collection of original art by Charles Demuth.  The collection is now the largest in the world with 52 works of art as well as it is the largest repository of original archival material on the artist. The exhibit will feature highlights from the collection, where his primary subject matter was the city of Lancaster and his mother's garden.    |
