     <h2>
      Are You Up For the 7-Day Vegan Challenge?
     </h2>
     <div class="post">
      <h3>
       What is it?
      </h3>
      <ul>
       <li>
        A fun, easy way to try veganism, and in the process learn about your health, reduce your carbon footprint, and find compassion towards all other creatures.
       </li>
      </ul>
      <h3>
       Why do the challenge?
      </h3>
      <ol>
       <li>
        <strong>
         Environment
        </strong>
        :
        <a href="http://www.culinaryschools.org/yum/vegetables/">
         Reduce your carbon footprint
        </a>
        .
       </li>
       <li>
        <strong>
         Health
        </strong>
        :
        <a href="http://7dayvegan.com/health/">
         Add plants and get healthier
        </a>
        .
       </li>
       <li>
        <strong>
         Compassion
        </strong>
        : Withdraw from the
        <a href="http://7dayvegan.com/compassion/">
         cruel treatment of animals
        </a>
        .
       </li>
       <li>
        <strong>
         Growth
        </strong>
        : Show yourself that
        <a href="http://7dayvegan.com/possible/">
         your limits aren’t really limits
        </a>
        .
       </li>
       <li>
        <strong>
         Fun
        </strong>
        : Being vegan isn’t a sacrifice —
        <a href="http://7dayvegan.com/fun/">
         it’s lots of fun
        </a>
        !
       </li>
      </ol>
      <p>
       But most of all, to experience what we believe:
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Veganism is love
       </strong>
       .
      </p>
      <h3>
       How does it work?
      </h3>
      <ol>
       <li>
        No meat, poultry, seafood, dairy or eggs for 7 days (any 7 straight days).
       </li>
       <li>
        Announce to your friends, family, followers that you’re doing the challenge.
        <a href="http://7dayvegan.com/join/">
         Add your name to the list of Challengers
        </a>
        .
       </li>
       <li>
        Use the recipes/meal plans on this site if you like.
       </li>
       <li>
        Tell people how the challenge went at the end of the 7 days.
       </li>
      </ol>
      <p>
       You don’t have to refrain from other animal products for now —  like honey, leather, fur, sugar refined with bone char, etc.
      </p>
      <p>
       This challenge is so easy and fun, there’s no reason not to do it.
      </p>
      <p>
       You can do it anytime during the year — there’s no specific dates set for this challenge. You can do it multiple times, or go for 14 days, 21 days, a month, if you want to have even more fun.
      </p>
      <p>
       And it’s entirely free — no payment required, no email address needed.
      </p>
      <h3>
       How to start
      </h3>
      <ul>
       <li>
        Ready to start? Just make a commitment, and choose a meal plan! Here’s the
        <a href="http://7dayvegan.com/start/">
         quickstart guide
        </a>
        .
       </li>
      </ul>
