       <h3 class="post-title">
        <a href="https://sanfranciscosantarchy.wordpress.com/2015/12/10/santacon-2015-is-here/" rel="bookmark">
         SantaCon 2015 is Here!
        </a>
       </h3>
       <small>
        December 10, 2015
        <!-- by sanfranciscosantarchy -->
       </small>
       <div class="entry">
        <p>
         This just in from the North Pole:
         <br/>
         SantaCon is this Saturday December 12th starting at Union Square at Noon.
        </p>
        <p>
         As with years past, there are a number of neighborhoods which Santa may enjoy, with no single set route. Please remember as you make your way through the city that SantaCon is more than just a bar crawl! Take some time to do something creative, helpful or fun! Santa loves to give out gifts to children and those less fortunate. Please bring a new, unwrapped toy
         <span class="text_exposed_show">
          for the San Francisco Fire Department’s Toy Program, which will be collected at Union Square.
         </span>
        </p>
        <div class="text_exposed_show">
         <p>
          Making the most of your SantaCon:
          <br/>
          With so many neighborhoods to explore, Santa is encouraged to spread out and explore different parts of the city. If a bar is too crowded, try going down the street and exploring somewhere new. Please be respectful of those working to serve you and remember, Santa should always tip!
         </p>
         <p>
          Don’t Be That Santa.
          <br/>
          Also, remember not everyone is participating in SantaCon. Don’t fuck with those who live in the neighborhood. Don’t urinate in public. SFPD will issue citations for bad behavior and no one wants that. Don’t make folks hate Santa.
         </p>
         <p>
          Remember the Five Fucks:
          <br/>
          Don’t Fuck with police.
          <br/>
          Don’t Fuck with kids.
          <br/>
          Don’t Fuck with security.
          <br/>
          Don’t Fuck with bartenders.
          <br/>
          Don’t Fuck with Santa.
         </p>
         <p>
          Please don’t drink and drive. Use public transportation, taxis, ride sharing, etc. to get around safely.
         </p>
         <p>
          Have a great time! For more official SF SantaCon information, check the official site here:
          <a href="https://www.facebook.com/l.php?u=https%3A%2F%2Fsanfranciscosantarchy.wordpress.com%2F&amp;h=iAQGj8nVnAQGH2qqr5qbFuW1llQvFcI5eWHEsHtncnmcsZQ&amp;enc=AZOmM3Wv9xQkCOYl4RbbUTPeJ7qL0bXTuU44oXbgHEJ7Vo3yS4-FD-Nr9mK9j1P5XkU_yMy4XAL0i-nM6DyupRuMNpgn2CUUoF21HRh9s21N00Rcns3UqyQrTC_yDFJaBqMd5TyMuXNmJCMDtMqqQFxYXVRtVl3Bqc-_G4qBpjOKdg&amp;s=1" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           https://sanfranciscosantarchy.wordpress.com/
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          -SF Santa
         </p>
        </div>

       <h3 class="post-title">
        <a href="https://sanfranciscosantarchy.wordpress.com/2014/12/01/santarchy-2014-toy-drive/" rel="bookmark">
         Santarchy 2014 Toy Drive!
        </a>
       </h3>
       <small>
        December 1, 2014
        <!-- by sanfranciscosantarchy -->
       </small>
       <div class="entry">
        <p>
         <strong>
          Once again Santa will be collecting toys for the SFFD’s Toy Program 2014!
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Here’s what you need to do. Bring a toy to with you to Union Square at the start of Santarchy.
         <strong>
          There will be a SFFD fire truck parked outside of the Handlery Union Square Hotel, located at 351 Geary Street.
         </strong>
         This is about five doors away from Union Square (
         <a href="http://goo.gl/maps/RSZ1D">
          here is a map
         </a>
         if you need a visual aid) and conveniently just a few doors away from Lefty O’Douls.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          You drop your toy off at the fire truck.  The truck will be parked there from 11:30 AM to 1:30 PM  so do this first, as soon as you get to Union Sq.
         </strong>
         Folks from the SFFD will be on hand to collect your toy.  Cash donations are OK.
         <strong>
         </strong>
         Just make sure you
         <strong>
          only give cash to uniformed SFFD personnel.
         </strong>
         Here are the guidelines for toys.
        </p>
        <ol>
         <li>
          Bring something for a kid 12 years old or under.
          <br/>
          2. Bring a
          <strong>
           NEW
          </strong>
          toy, not some piece of crap you have lying around the house. Put some effort into it OK?
          <br/>
          3. Don’t wrap it.
          <br/>
          4. For girls they especially need gifts for older girls. Arts and Crafts kits and dolls are in high demand. For boys they need sports equipment.  Don’t get all PC and lecture Santa about toys and gender roles. Give the kids what they want.
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p>
         The San Francisco Firefighter’s Toy Program is the city’s largest and the nation’s oldest program of its kind. Since 1949, it has evolved from a few firefighters repairing broken toys and bikes for 15 families to, in 2011, 300 firefighters and friends volunteering their time to distribute over 200,000 toys to more than 40,000 disadvantaged children. Besides helping individual families in need, the Toy Program serves many community organizations, including shelters for abused women and children, inner-city schools, children’s cancer wards, and pediatric AIDS units. They also respond on a year round basis to displaced children who become victims of fires, floods and other such disasters. They are way cool and some of them are extremely hot (the fire women too).
        </p>
