         <p>
          BAAL is
          <span class="C-5">
           a professional association, based in the UK, which provides a forum for people interested in language
          </span>
          <span class="C-7">
           and applied linguistics. We have
          </span>
          <span class="C-6">
           an international professional membership of around 1000 members.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="C-6">
           <a href="https://baal.org.uk/news/">
            For our latest news click here
           </a>
           . To read more about what applied linguistics is and does,
           <a href="http://baal.org.uk/about/what-applied-linguistics-is-and-does/">
            click here
           </a>
           .
          </span>
         </p>
         <p class="Normal P-4">
          <span class="s1">
           BAAL organises scientific meetings, and publishes a
           <a href="http://baal.org.uk/newsletter/">
            newsletter
           </a>
          </span>
          <span class="s1">
           and
           <a href="http://baal.org.uk/annual-conference/">
            conference
           </a>
          </span>
          <span class="s1">
           proceedings. We support applied linguistic activity via
          </span>
          <span class="s3">
           <a href="http://baal.org.uk/funding/">
            funding
           </a>
          </span>
          <span class="s1">
           ,
           <a href="http://baal.org.uk/special-interest-groups/">
            special interest groups
           </a>
          </span>
          <span class="s1">
           and an annual
           <a href="http://baal.org.uk/book-prize/">
            book prize
           </a>
          </span>
          <span class="s1">
           . We bring together the applied linguistics community via
           <a href="http://baal.org.uk/baalmail/">
            BAALmail
           </a>
          </span>
          <span class="s1">
           , our dedicated webmail list, through which members share and discuss applied linguistics-related news, achievements and opportunities. We forge and maintain close links to institutions and organisations with a vested interest in applied linguistics. We also send representatives to national and international meetings of interest to our members.
          </span>
         </p>
