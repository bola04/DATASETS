          <h2>
           Hot Topics in Sustainable Agriculture
          </h2>
          <p>
           Visit SARE's
           <a href="/Learning-Center/Topic-Rooms" target="_self" title="">
            Topic Rooms
           </a>
           for in-depth resources on important topics in sustainable agriculture, including:
          </p>
          <a id="eztoc1799_1_2" name="eztoc1799_1_2">
          </a>
          <h3>
           <a href="/Learning-Center/Topic-Rooms/Cover-Crops" target="_self" title="">
            Cover Crops
           </a>
          </h3>
          <p>
           The many
           <a href="/Events/Cover-Crop-Conferences/National-Conference-on-Cover-Crops-and-Soil-Health/Cover-Crop-Innovators-Video-Series" target="_self" title="">
            benefits of cover crops
           </a>
           are increasingly appreciated among farmers. They play an important role in
           <a href="/Learning-Center/Topic-Rooms/Cover-Crops/Cover-Crops-Soil-and-Fertility-Management" target="_self" title="">
            erosion control
           </a>
           ,
           <a href="/Learning-Center/Topic-Rooms/Cover-Crops/Cover-Crops-Pest-Management" target="_self" title="">
            weed control
           </a>
           ,
           <a href="/Learning-Center/Topic-Rooms/Cover-Crops/Cover-Crops-Soil-and-Fertility-Management" target="_self" title="">
            soil conservation and soil health
           </a>
           . With careful
           <a href="/Learning-Center/Topic-Rooms/Cover-Crops/Cover-Crops-Selection-and-Management" target="_self" title="">
            selection and management
           </a>
           , they fit into any
           <a href="/Learning-Center/Topic-Rooms/Cover-Crops/Cover-Crops-Crop-Rotations" target="_self" title="">
            crop rotation
           </a>
           or cropping system, such as
           <a href="/Learning-Center/Topic-Rooms/Cover-Crops/Cover-Crops-No-Till" target="_self" title="">
            no-till farming
           </a>
           , and are integral to organic farming.
          </p>
          <a id="eztoc1799_1_3" name="eztoc1799_1_3">
          </a>
          <h3>
           <a href="/Learning-Center/Topic-Rooms/High-Tunnels-and-Other-Season-Extension-Techniques" target="_self" title="">
            Season Extension
           </a>
          </h3>
          <p>
           Find in-depth information about
           <a href="/Learning-Center/Topic-Rooms/High-Tunnels-and-Other-Season-Extension-Techniques/Season-Extension-An-Overview#Hightunnels" target="_self" title="">
            high tunnels
           </a>
           ,
           <a href="/Learning-Center/Topic-Rooms/High-Tunnels-and-Other-Season-Extension-Techniques/Season-Extension-An-Overview#Hightunnels" target="_self" title="">
            hoop houses
           </a>
           and other season extension techniques, including
           <a href="/Learning-Center/Topic-Rooms/High-Tunnels-and-Other-Season-Extension-Techniques/Season-Extension-Types-and-Construction" target="_self" title="">
            high tunnel construction
           </a>
           ,
           <a href="/Learning-Center/Topic-Rooms/High-Tunnels-and-Other-Season-Extension-Techniques/Season-Extension-An-Overview#Greenhouses" target="_self" title="">
            commercial greenhouse production
           </a>
           ,
           <a href="/Learning-Center/Topic-Rooms/High-Tunnels-and-Other-Season-Extension-Techniques/Season-Extension-Cultivar-Selection-and-Variety-Trials" target="_self" title="">
            high tunnel vegetable production
           </a>
           and more.
          </p>
          <p>
           Explore additional SARE Topic Rooms on
           <a href="/Learning-Center/Topic-Rooms/Farm-to-Table-Building-Local-and-Regional-Food-Systems" target="_self" title="">
            local and regional food systems
           </a>
           , and for Southern audiences, on
           <a href="http://www.southernsare.org/Educational-Resources/Topic-Rooms/Small-Ruminants" target="_self" title="">
            small ruminants
           </a>
           and
           <a href="http://www.southernsare.org/Educational-Resources/Topic-Rooms/Pastured-Poultry" target="_self" title="">
            pastured poultry
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <a id="eztoc1799_2" name="eztoc1799_2">
          </a>
          <h2>
           <a href="/Learning-Center/SARE-Program-Materials/National-Program-Materials/What-is-Sustainable-Agriculture" target="_self" title="">
            What is Sustainable Agriculture?
           </a>
          </h2>
          <div class="object-left">
           <div class="content-view-embed" style="width:170px;">
            <div class="class-image">
             <img alt="SARE Folder_03_lowres" height="113" src="/var/ezflow_site/storage/images/media/images/outreach-images/promo-pubs/sare-folder/sare-folder_03_lowres/1032773-1-eng-US/SARE-Folder_03_lowres_articleimage.jpg" style="" title="SARE Folder_03_lowres" width="170"/>
            </div>
           </div>
          </div>
          <p>
           Every day, farmers and ranchers around the world develop new, innovativestrategies to produce and distribute food, fuel and fiber sustainably. While these strategies vary greatly they all embrace three broad goals, or what SARE calls the 3 Pillars of Sustainability:
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <div>
           
           <b>
            Profit
           </b>
           over the long term
          </div>
          <div>
           
           <b>
            Stewardship
           </b>
           of our nation's land, air and water
          </div>
          
          <b>
           Quality of life
          </b>
          for farmers, ranchers and their communities
          <p>
           There are almost as many ways to reach these goals as there are farms and ranches in America.
          </p>
          <p>
           To learn more, check out the award-winning
           <i>
            <a href="/Learning-Center/SARE-Program-Materials/National-Program-Materials/What-is-Sustainable-Agriculture" target="_self" title="">
             What is Sustainable Agriculture?
            </a>
            .
           </i>
          </p>
         </div>
