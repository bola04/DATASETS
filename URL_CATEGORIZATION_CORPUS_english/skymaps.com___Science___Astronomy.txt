           <b>
            Do you enjoy looking at the night sky?
           </b>
          </font>
          <br/>
          <font face="Verdana,Arial,Helvetica" size="2">
           Stargazing is a wonderful pastime enjoyed by millions of people all around the world. Anyone can join in. Here's how...
           <p>
            Find a dark area away from lights and with a good view of the night sky. After about 10 minutes your eyes will adapt to the dark and you will begin to see many stars. Use a good pair of binoculars (
            <a href="http://www.skymaps.com/a1/B00008Y0VN/" target="_blank">
             No. 1 Best Seller at Amazon.com
            </a>
            ) to see even more stars and other celestial objects.
           </p>
