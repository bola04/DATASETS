       <h1>
        The Lexington Network of Care
       </h1>
       <p>
        <br/>
        As one of Illinois’ Premier Providers of Post-Acute and skilled long term care services, Lexington Health Network is with you every step of the way on your road to recovery or at any level of your care journey.
       </p>
       <p>
        Lexington Health Network proudly offers a full
        <a href="?p=57&amp;t=the-network">
         network
        </a>
        of health care services, including but not limited to
        <a href="http://www.lexingtonhealth.com/?p=45&amp;t=post-acute-services">
         post-acute care
        </a>
        and rehabilitation, medically complex services, skilled nursing, as well as long-term care services, specializing in memory care programming for those with Alzheimer’s and dementia-related diseases. Additionally, Lexington’s senior care services include respite care,
        <a href="http://www.lexingtonhealth.com/?p=72&amp;t=lexington-square">
         assisted as well as independent living campuses
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        In addition, Lexington's diverse network also offers
        <a href="http://www.lexingtonhealth.com/?p=56&amp;t=home-care-services">
         home health care
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://www.lexingtonhealth.com/?p=80&amp;t=hospice">
         hospice care
        </a>
        and
        <a href="http://www.lexingtonhealth.com/?p=81&amp;t=private-care-services">
         private care services
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        At Lexington, we know finding the right care can be difficult and we’re here to help you every step of the way. We’re dedicated to providing the highest level of quality care for our patients, residents, and family members. With 10 Post-Acute &amp; Skilled Nursing and rehabilitation centers, 2 independent and assisted living buildings housed within 2 distinct retirement communities, coupled with diverse home health, hospice and private duty nursing services, we’re sure to be just around the corner.
       </p>
       <p>
        As a family-owned comprehensive health care network, our patients and residents are just that – family.
       </p>
