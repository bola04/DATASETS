              <h2>
               Poker Video Lessons
              </h2>
              <p>
               Our poker videos aim to teach you poker strategies that are used by pros in tournaments and cash games.
              </p>
              <ul>
               <li>
                Optimal betting strategy alongside the strategic moves pros use to manipulate opponents into making mistakes.
               </li>
               <li>
                Effective hand reading so you always have a good idea of what your opponents cards are. Then we show you how to use this information to adjust your play on each hand.
               </li>
               <li>
                Correct starting hand considerations that dynamically change during tournaments depending on Position, other players actions, stack sizes, and hand strength.
               </li>
               <li>
                Execution of bluffs that work a high percentage of the time.
               </li>
               <li>
                Timely steals during multi-table tournaments and sit and go's. Strong focus on bubble play strategy.
               </li>
               <li>
                Ideal use of position during hands.
               </li>
              </ul>
              <h5>
               <strong>

              <p>
               Poker Video Instructors narrate their poker videos with play by play decisions in detail with clear, easy to follow concepts that you can apply when you play. Watch, listen and ask questions. The free online poker video lessons will help you improve your game at the poker tables. Our Poker School Video Library has over 35 free poker instructional videos taught by poker pros. Brett unveils the secrets he uses to beat cash games, crush single table tournaments, and consistently make final tables. These exclusive videos give you an in-depth and thorough understanding of the strategies and techniques he uses everyday to be successful playing online poker tournaments. The videos show real online poker games and real online poker tournaments. You not only see Brett's hands and how he plays them, but added play by play audio commentary that lets you learn the thought process behind each poker decision he makes.
              </p>
              <h2>
               Poker Lessons Covering Most Forms of Poker
              </h2>
              <p>
               <img alt="Free Poker Videos" src="/images/gameplay.jpg" style="float: left;"/>
              </p>
              <p>
               At Pro Poker School our skilled poker instructors aim to teach you a very high level of poker. We accomplish this through a series of instructional videos that establish a strong understanding of the fundamental poker concepts and then build upon that to more advanced concepts. Through our instruction you will see your confidence increase as well as your mental toughness. Our goal is to turn you into a complete poker player with courses in many different forms of poker. The poker games we teach here at Pro Poker School include no limit Texas holdem, pot limit omaha, omaha hi-lo and 7 card stud.
              </p>
              <h4>
               No Limit Texas Holdem Video Lessons
              </h4>
              <p>
               No Limit Texas Hold'em Video Lessons, you will learn the strategies for tournaments, sit and go single table tournaments and cash games. The world's most popular poker game is broken down in detail in various free poker videos offered by Pro Poker School instructors.
              </p>
              <h4>
               Limit Texas Holdem Video Lessons
              </h4>
              <p>
               Limit Texas Holdem Video Lessons: Learn the keys that will improve your fundamental understanding of Hold'em concepts before playing the No Limit Texas Holdem.
              </p>
              <h4>
               Pot Limit Texas Holdem Video Lessons
              </h4>
              <p>
               Pot Limit Holdem Video Lessons: This increasingly popular style of poker allows experienced players to outplay less experienced opponents by having the option to bet up to the size of the pot which makes mistakes more costly.
              </p>
              <h4>
               Omaha Hi-Lo Video Lessons
              </h4>
              <p>
               Omaha Hi-Lo Video Lessons: Once you have learned the fundamental concepts of limit omaha hi-lo, you can apply your knowledge towards pot limit omaha hi/low, which is one of the most popular online poker games alternatives to Texas Holdem. With plenty of action in omaha hi-lo knowing which starting hands to select and using hand reading skills to figure out your opponents hand are key concepts that are taught. These skills will improve your game skill in other forms of poker like Hold'em.
              </p>
              <h4>
               Pot Limit Omaha Video Lessons
              </h4>
              <p>
               Pot Limit Omaha Video Lessons: Enjoyable action game similar to hold'em but played with four cards in your hand instead of two. You must use two cards from your hand and three from the board to make your best five card hand. The instruction focuses on Pot Limit Omaha tournaments and Cash Games in which big hands are the norm since having four starting cards creates more multi-way action compared to Holdem.
              </p>
              <h4>
               HORSE Video Lessons
              </h4>
              <p>
               HORSE Video Lessons: The poker game of HORSE is actually a rotation of 5 different poker games. Holdem, Omaha hi-lo, Razz, Seven Card Stud and Seven Card Stud hi-lo make up the HORSE games which are played in tournaments and cash games.
              </p>
              <h4>
               Seven Card Stud Video Lessons
              </h4>
              <p>
               Seven Card Stud Video Lessons:Extremely popular before the Hold'em revolution, seven card stud games are still very relevant.
              </p>
              <h4>
               Seven Card Stud Hi-Lo Video Lessons
              </h4>
              <p>
               Seven Card Stud Hi-Lo Video Lessons: Hi-Lo games attract weak opponents who consistently make poor decisions. Learn how to take advantage of these opportunities through solid play. Another game included in HORSE, seven card stud hi/low is a fun game with plenty of profit potential.
              </p>
              <h4>
               Lowball Video Lessons
              </h4>
              <p>
               Lowball Video Lessons: Draw poker variation.
              </p>
