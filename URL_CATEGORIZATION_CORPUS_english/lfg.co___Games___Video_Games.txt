      <h2 class="post-title">
       GenCon! GenCon! GenCon!
      </h2>
      <p>
       Yup, that’s right kids! Everyone’s favourite show of the year,
       <a href="https://www.gencon.com/">
        GenCon
       </a>
       , is fast approaching! It’s The Best Four Days in Gaming™ and we have a ton of awesome and secret stuff lined up for you all *cough*newgame*Coughcough*expansion*cough* to enjoy!
      </p>
      <p>
       But what’s better than The Best Four Days in Gaming™? How about The Best Four Days in Gaming™ and US!
      </p>
      <p>
       This year, we are in need of 4 people to join Sohmer, Lar, Will, Greg, Costello and Myself at GenCon and give us a hand demoing our games, taking to folks, working the booth, etc. We always have an amazing time at GenCon and would love for you to join us. For those interested, here are the details:
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        Join us for all 4 days of GenCon!
       </li>
       <li>
        Food and Boarding will be covered.
       </li>
       <li>
        Transportation to Indianapolis or the venue is not covered.
       </li>
       <li>
        You will receive 1 Exhibitor Badge for the show.
       </li>
       <li>
        You will work the booth with us running demos, making sales and having fun!
       </li>
       <li>
        The gig is unpaid (aside from what’s mentioned above).
       </li>
       <li>
        Hang out with us during the weekend including intimate, candlelit dinners and more than likely a trip to the mall!
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       And that’s the short and skinny of it! To apply, inquire or whatever, send an e-mail to:
       <a href="mailto:gencon@blindferret.com">
        gencon@blindferret.com
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Have fun and hope to see you at GenCon!
      </p>
      <p>
       -Moss
