          <h2 class="date-header">
           Saturday, June 16, 2018
          </h2>
          <div class="entry-category-see_what_show_movies_and_tv entry-author-mrbrown entry-type-post entry" id="entry-6a00d83451b52369e2022ad39946ef200b">
           <div class="entry-inner">
            <h3 class="entry-header">
             <a href="http://www.mrbrown.com/blog/2018/06/see-what-show-mrbrowns-mini-movie-reviews-mostly-watched-on-the-plane.html">
              See What Show: mrbrown’s Mini-movie Reviews (mostly watched on the plane)
             </a>
            </h3>
            <div class="entry-content">
             <input name="zemanta-related" type="hidden" val=""/>
             <div class="entry-body">
              <p>
               <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://www.mrbrown.com/.a/6a00d83451b52369e2022ad39946e8200b-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
                <img alt="F2FA4ED7-08F7-4E1F-A75F-FC33A9AD4CA9" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d83451b52369e2022ad39946e8200b img-responsive" src="http://www.mrbrown.com/.a/6a00d83451b52369e2022ad39946e8200b-700wi" style="width: 670px;" title="F2FA4ED7-08F7-4E1F-A75F-FC33A9AD4CA9"/>
               </a>
              </p>
              <p>
               I was on a plane for many hours. I was on planes A LOT. Here is another bunch of  mrbrown's Short-Attention-Span Movie Reviews.
              </p>
              <div class="entry-content" style="box-sizing: border-box; caret-color: #333333; color: #333333; font-family: 'Source Sans Pro', Arial, sans-serif; -webkit-tap-highlight-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0); -webkit-text-size-adjust: 100%;">
               <div class="entry-body" style="box-sizing: border-box; font-size: 1em;">
                <p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px 0px 10px;">
                </p>
               </div>
              </div>
              <p>
               1. 12 Strong. Thor is a Special Forces soldier sent to whack Al-Qaeda with a team of 12, after 9-11. Reasonably entertaining but very America Rah Rah.
              </p>
              <p>
               2. The 15:17 to Paris. Based on the true story of three Americans who stopped a terrorist from shooting up a Paris train. Clumsy movie that used the real fellas acting as themselves. They can’t act. And the movie felt too long to tell the story of something that was over in a few seconds.
              </p>
              <p>
               3. Den of Thieves. Gerard Butler as Major Crimes badass vs a bunch of professional bank robbers. Better than Geostorm, lousier than Olympus Has Fallen.
              </p>
              <p>
               4. Ocean’s 8. Watched this on the last day of my time in SF, at an AMC cinema. Entertaining heist movie. The all-female cast was fun to watch. Awkwafina was a hoot. Not as good as Ocean’s Eleven (hard to beat the original) but way better than Ocean’s Twelve.
              </p>
              <p>
               5. The Mountain Between Us. Kate Winslet and Idris Elba try to survive crashing into a snow-covered mountain. The Revenant this ain’t. But Kate held the show together. A little too long and draggy. Watch only if stuck on a plane with terrible movie selection (I’m looking at you, EVA Air).
              </p>
              <p>
               6. Molly’s Game. Jessica Chastain is Molly Bloom, who was arrested by the FBI for running poker games for the rich and famous. Based on a true story. If you like Aaron Sorkin’s writing and dialogue, this movie will entertain you. Also stars Idris Elba, as her lawyer.
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>
              <p>
               #seewhatshow
              </p>

             </a>
            </h3>
            <div class="entry-content">
             <input name="zemanta-related" type="hidden" val=""/>
             <div class="entry-body">
              <p>
               <iframe class="exposure-post-cover-embed" src="https://brown.exposure.co/qing-ming/embed/cover?embed=true" style="width:100%;height:600px;margin-bottom:5px;border:solid 1px #ccc;border-radius:2px;">

               <a href="https://brown.exposure.co/qing-ming">

              <p>
               <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://www.mrbrown.com/.a/6a00d83451b52369e201b7c961d921970b-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">

              <p>
               Quietly, my family celebrated Qing Ming again this year.                It is a time for all of us to gather as an extended family to reflect and remember, regardless of our religious beliefs.

           <div class="entry-inner">
            <h3 class="entry-header">
             <a href="http://www.mrbrown.com/blog/2018/04/a-drawing-by-my-youngest.html">
              A drawing by my youngest
             </a>
            </h3>
            <div class="entry-content">
             <input name="zemanta-related" type="hidden" val=""/>
             <div class="entry-body">
              <p>
               <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://www.mrbrown.com/.a/6a00d83451b52369e201bb0a037947970d-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">

               </a>
              </p>
              <p>
               The wife sent me a photo of this drawing by my youngest daughter recently. I think Joy captured my wife and me perfectly.
              </p>
              <p>
               Obviously, I am a tough cookie that melts in contact with milk of my wife's love. Gosh, that sounded really mushy.
              </p>
              <p>
               Our kids often make us laugh, make us cry, and make us lose our collective minds.
              </p>
              <p>
               But once in a while, they make us feel warm and fuzzy inside.
              </p>
              <p>
               I am humbled my 12-year-old looks at the relationship that my wife and I have, and sees this kind of love. We must be doing something right.

            <h3 class="entry-header">
             <a href="http://www.mrbrown.com/blog/2018/04/mrbrown-and-kim-huat-goes-to-apples-chicago-event-2018.html">
              mrbrown (and Kim Huat) goes to Apple's Chicago Event 2018
             </a>
            </h3>
            <div class="entry-content">
             <input name="zemanta-related" type="hidden" val=""/>
             <div class="entry-body">
              <p>
               <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://www.mrbrown.com/.a/6a00d83451b52369e201b8d2e792cc970c-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">

               </a>
              </p>
              <p>
               I spent last week in Chicago, on the invitation of Apple, to check out their new education offerings. The event took place at Lane Tech High School in the Windy City, and we got to see a new entry-level iPad with Pencil support, in addition to the new school-based ecosystem that Apple made to make put Apple back into the classroom again.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://www.mrbrown.com/.a/6a00d83451b52369e201b8d2e79290970c-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
                <img alt="DSCF1902" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d83451b52369e201b8d2e79290970c img-responsive" src="http://www.mrbrown.com/.a/6a00d83451b52369e201b8d2e79290970c-800wi" style="width: 800px; " title="DSCF1902"/>
               </a>
              </p>
              <p>
               Cheaper Chromebooks have made inroads into school environments, so this was a needful move on Apple's part. Besides making the Apple Pencil available for non-Pro iPad users via the cheaper new iPad, the Apple Schoolkit and other school apps aimed to make life easier for teachers to manage their classes, issue handouts, manage grades, and mark scripts digitally.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://www.mrbrown.com/.a/6a00d83451b52369e201bb0a0085c1970d-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
                <img alt="DSCF1862" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d83451b52369e201bb0a0085c1970d img-responsive" src="http://www.mrbrown.com/.a/6a00d83451b52369e201bb0a0085c1970d-800wi" style="width: 800px; " title="DSCF1862"/>
               </a>
              </p>
              <p>
               I will leave it to Kim Huat to give you the overview of the event via
               <a href="https://youtu.be/aQylfJlHPf4">
                this video
               </a>
               :
              </p>
              <p>
               <iframe allow="autoplay; encrypted-media" allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="450" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/aQylfJlHPf4" width="800">

              <p>
               And since iOS 11.3 also came out soon after, here are
               <a href="https://youtu.be/5j2QEscE1Hw">
                the four new Animojis on iPhone X
               </a>
               :
              </p>
              <p>
               <iframe allow="autoplay; encrypted-media" allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="450" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/5j2QEscE1Hw" width="800">
               </iframe>
              </p>
              <p>
               I had a great time in my short stay in Chicago. The Apple Store at Michigan Avenue is lovely, and I got to partake of the city's deep dish pizza, tacos, and blues music.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://www.mrbrown.com/.a/6a00d83451b52369e201bb0a008650970d-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">

               </a>
              </p>
              <p>
               <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://www.mrbrown.com/.a/6a00d83451b52369e201b7c95d55b0970b-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
                <img alt="DSCF1939" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d83451b52369e201b7c95d55b0970b img-responsive" src="http://www.mrbrown.com/.a/6a00d83451b52369e201b7c95d55b0970b-800wi" style="width: 800px; " title="DSCF1939"/>

               </a>
               <br/>
              </p>
              <p>
               <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://www.mrbrown.com/.a/6a00d83451b52369e201b8d2e79352970c-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">

              </p>
              <p>
               I wish I had more time in Chicago. The last time I was here,
               <a href="http://www.mrbrown.com/blog/2012/10/usa-2012-riding-chicago.html">
                it was 2012
               </a>
               . I did stop by in 2016, on
               <a href="http://www.mrbrown.com/blog/2016/09/mrbrowns-great-us-train-adventure-day-23.html">
                Great US Train Adventure
               </a>
               from SF to NYC but that was only for a few hours.
              </p>
              <p>
               It was cold here but I had the right clothes. 0ºC to 2ºC at night, and surprisingly, 8ºC-12ºC during the day on the days I was there. Which is warm by Chicago standards.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://www.mrbrown.com/.a/6a00d83451b52369e201b8d2e793f7970c-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
                <img alt="IMG_5879" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d83451b52369e201b8d2e793f7970c img-responsive" src="http://www.mrbrown.com/.a/6a00d83451b52369e201b8d2e793f7970c-800wi" style="width: 800px; " title="IMG_5879"/>
               </a>
              </p>
              <p>
               The kids have been asking me for a pen-based device to draw and create stuff on. The iPad Pro was too pricey to be given to the kids. And a Wacom tablet would mean being tethered to the iMac at home. This new entry-level iPad with Pencil support came at the right time.
              </p>
              <p>
               Now if only I can get over the jet lag sooner. Shuttling between Singapore, Austin (for the
               <a href="https://www.instagram.com/p/BgMilRYhkre/?taken-by=mr_brown">
                Ready Player One premiere at SXSW
               </a>
               ), and Chicago in the space of three weeks really wore me out. I will share
               <a href="https://www.instagram.com/p/BgUIBVnBHmB/?taken-by=mr_brown">
                the Austin photos soon
               </a>
               , but do go watch
               <a href="https://www.instagram.com/p/BgNszBahFED/">
                Ready Player One
               </a>
               . It is a fun little movie by Steven Spielberg.
              </p>

          <div class="entry-category-see_what_show_movies_and_tv entry-author-mrbrown entry-type-post entry" id="entry-6a00d83451b52369e201b7c959bb87970b">
           <div class="entry-inner">
            <h3 class="entry-header">
             <a href="http://www.mrbrown.com/blog/2018/03/see-what-show-pacific-rim-uprising.html">
              See What Show: Pacific Rim: Uprising
             </a>
            </h3>
            <div class="entry-content">
             <input name="zemanta-related" type="hidden" val=""/>
             <div class="entry-body">
              <p>
               <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://www.mrbrown.com/.a/6a00d83451b52369e201bb09fcef17970d-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
                <img alt="Pacific_rimmed" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d83451b52369e201bb09fcef17970d img-responsive" src="http://www.mrbrown.com/.a/6a00d83451b52369e201bb09fcef17970d-800wi" style="width: 800px; " title="Pacific_rimmed"/>
               </a>
              </p>
              <p>
               Just how bad was Pacific Rim: Uprising? After the screening, a few of us fans of the first movie, had to hug each other for comfort.
              </p>
              <p>
               It had dodgy CG and lacked Guillermo del Toro’s heart. And the sequel felt like one of those pro-China Jackie Chan movies. It may as well be titled Pacific Rim: 中国万岁.
              </p>
              <p>
               It had its fun robot moments, but even those couldn’t make up for the rest of the movie.
              </p>
              <p>
               John Boyega just didn’t have Idris Elba’s gravitas.
              </p>
              <p>
               I mean, Elba could make cheesy lines like “Today, we are canceling the apocalypse!” sound epic.
              </p>
              <p>
               At one point of the movie, Boyega says in a speech, “I am not my father…” and I found myself agreeing rigorously.
              </p>
              <p>
               Boyega was okay when he was being witty. But when he tried to be heroic, it didn’t quite work.
              </p>
              <p>
               And why do all the Jaegers look so alike? Macam Transformers liddat. After a while, you don’t even know which robot is which.
              </p>
              <p>
               Chinese actress Jing Tian completes her hat trick of stinkers with The Great Wall, Kong: Skull Island, and this movie. I think the next time you see a Hollywood movie with Jing Tian in it, run.
              </p>
              <p>
               As one of my readers, Dinesh, commented: " Looks like Jing Tian’s Hollywood career won't make it to Ming Tian."
              </p>
              <p>
               No more sequels please. Pacific-rimmed by one sequel is enough.
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>
