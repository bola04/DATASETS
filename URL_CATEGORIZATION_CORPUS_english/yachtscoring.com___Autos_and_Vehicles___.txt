               <b style="font-size: 14px">
                Welcome to Yacht Scoring
               </b>
               <p>
                <b>
                 Yacht Scoring
                </b>
                is a featured packed 100% web based yacht racing management and scoring system that simplifies the task of competitor registration, event management, competitor and media communications while providing results in near-real time to competitors and the World following your event on the internet.  With
                <b>
                 Yacht Scoring
                </b>
                your event can be up and running with a complete event web site in 5 minutes!
               </p>
               <p>
                <b>
                 Yacht Scoring
                </b>
                , works 100% over the internet with a single database for all of your event information eliminating the need to keep the entry list in one program (or more), transferring entries into a scoring program, scoring and then having to upload the results to the internet and the media.
                <b>
                 Yacht Scoring
                </b>
                also provides you with a single central data source that everyone in your Race Committee, using individually setup Access Codes, can access to manage the event from just about anywhere - home, office, club, etc.
               </p>
               <p>
                And most important for you... No Advertizing!  Only your sponsor's logos which you get to post and link!
               </p>
               <p>
                Yacht Scoring Capabilities and Functionalities:
               </p>
               <table>
                <tr>
                 <td align="75%">
                  <p>
                   <b>
                    Prior to event:
                   </b>
                  </p>
                  <ul>
                   <li>

                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Multiple event registration with one time data entry
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Competitor login &amp; entry maintenance
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Complete Crew List Information
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Crew Waiver online signing
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Competitor Crew email communication
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    World Sailing Classification and USSailing Membership Reports
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Event registration maintenance
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Event logo Upload (brand the event pages with your race logo)
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Event sponsor's logo upload and add web links
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Event documents upload and automated linking
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Required document tracking and reporting
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    One-click email competitor notification of missing documents
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Manage all communication with competitors and event subscribers
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Course/Division/Class splits setup
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Generate Scratch Sheets, Event Roster, RC Work Sheets
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Crew / Boat sign-up board
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    And more...
                   </li>
                  </ul>
                  <p>
                   <b>
                    During the event:
                   </b>
                  </p>
                  <ul>
                   <li>
                    Post race updates directly to the web site
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Email race news to competitors and subscribers
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Enter finish times and generate results directly on the Internet (results available immediately!)
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Real time "Current Standing" Results (Race and Cumulative)
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Real time detailed race and cumulative results
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Real time media formatted results (AP format and many others)
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Real time scrolling results updated as finish times are entered
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Print out final results before boats return to the dock
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Results are immediately available to the Media in Media AP Format
                   </li>
                  </ul>

                   <b>
                    At end of event:
                   </b>

                   <li>
                    Complete results already posted on the internet (done during the race)
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Write-up and email distribution of results to selected sailing media list
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Static copy of results file for your archive
                   </li>
                   <li>
                    Copy of your competitor's data for club use.
