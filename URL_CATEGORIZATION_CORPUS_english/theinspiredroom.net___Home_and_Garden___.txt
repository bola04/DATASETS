            coffee table
           </a>
           is actually on a crazy good sale right now during their big
           <a href="http://bit.ly/2M4OfEV" rel="nofollow noopener" target="_blank">

           </a>
           , so you can get it for just $111.99. I KNOW! That is an insanely awesome price (and I found a LOT of other things I like on sale that are just as amazing, too).
          </p>
          <p>
           I love that World Market offers such a big bang for your design buck in price, function, style
           <em>
            and
           </em>
           quality.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            You can see some of my favorites from the indoor furniture sale
            <a href="http://bit.ly/2JYKHn9" rel="nofollow noopener" target="_blank">
             here
            </a>
            . I have several items in my cart right now!
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Seattle Townhouse Changes + Coffee Table + Furniture Sale" class="alignnone wp-image-112086 size-full aligncenter" data-jpibfi-post-excerpt="" data-jpibfi-post-title="Seattle Townhouse Changes + Coffee Table + Furniture Sale" data-jpibfi-post-url="http://theinspiredroom.net/2018/06/19/seattle-townhouse/" data-jpibfi-src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Living-Room-Kylee-Townhouse.jpg" height="975" sizes="(max-width: 650px) 100vw, 650px" src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Living-Room-Kylee-Townhouse.jpg" srcset="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Living-Room-Kylee-Townhouse.jpg 650w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Living-Room-Kylee-Townhouse-200x300.jpg 200w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Living-Room-Kylee-Townhouse-400x600.jpg 400w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Living-Room-Kylee-Townhouse-600x900.jpg 600w" width="650"/>

          <p>
           The coffee table is still in such great shape after all these years, too. It’s super solid and wears well, so you’d never know we use it all the time for many of our meals (since we don’t have a dining table!).
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <img alt="Seattle Townhouse Changes + Coffee Table + Furniture Sale" class="alignnone wp-image-112087 size-full aligncenter" data-jpibfi-post-excerpt="" data-jpibfi-post-title="Seattle Townhouse Changes + Coffee Table + Furniture Sale" data-jpibfi-post-url="http://theinspiredroom.net/2018/06/19/seattle-townhouse/" data-jpibfi-src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Seattle-townhouse-living-room.jpg" height="860" sizes="(max-width: 650px) 100vw, 650px" src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Seattle-townhouse-living-room.jpg" srcset="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Seattle-townhouse-living-room.jpg 650w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Seattle-townhouse-living-room-227x300.jpg 227w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Seattle-townhouse-living-room-453x600.jpg 453w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Seattle-townhouse-living-room-600x794.jpg 600w" width="650"/>
           <em>
            <a href="http://bit.ly/2JXnAJE" rel="nofollow noopener" target="_blank">
             Coffee Table
            </a>

          <p style="text-align: left;">
           <em>
            <strong>
             What is always changing:
            </strong>
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           One thing that
           <em>
            seems to change quite often
           </em>
           in our living room is the wall behind my sofa. I feel like my biggest home decor struggle is always art. I’m pretty picky and about it, so up until this point I have put temporary things in my frames like wrapping paper or pieces I painted myself.
          </p>
          <p>
           Recently, I decided to switch it up and turn this wall into a gallery with some art I have been collecting. What you see here is still in progress–I’m not quite happy with it just yet so stay tuned because it’s probably going to change again. ;) Do you have walls like that? One of these days I hope I’ll get it just right and it can stay that way. But then again, change is fun, isn’t it?
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Seattle Townhouse Changes + Coffee Table + Furniture Sale" class="aligncenter wp-image-112112 size-full" data-jpibfi-post-excerpt="" data-jpibfi-post-title="Seattle Townhouse Changes + Coffee Table + Furniture Sale" data-jpibfi-post-url="http://theinspiredroom.net/2018/06/19/seattle-townhouse/" data-jpibfi-src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Kylee-Townhouse.jpg" height="812" sizes="(max-width: 650px) 100vw, 650px" src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Kylee-Townhouse.jpg" srcset="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Kylee-Townhouse.jpg 650w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Kylee-Townhouse-240x300.jpg 240w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Kylee-Townhouse-455x568.jpg 455w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Kylee-Townhouse-480x600.jpg 480w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Kylee-Townhouse-600x750.jpg 600w" width="650"/>

          <p>
           <strong>
            <em>
             What I’m thinking of changing:
            </em>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           One big project that I’m seriously considering is a kitchen makeover. I’ve actually been thinking about it FOREVER, ever since we moved in. Our kitchen is very dark, so I’d love to lighten it up. A kitchen remodel obviously won’t be quite as easy (or affordable) as changing out a gallery wall or rearranging furniture, so there’s a lot to consider. I’ll write another post sometime soon to share the makeover ideas I’m mulling over for the kitchen. I’d love to hear your thoughts on it (you guys always have the best ideas!), so stay tuned.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Seattle Townhouse Changes + Coffee Table + Furniture Sale" class="alignnone wp-image-112084 size-full aligncenter" data-jpibfi-post-excerpt="" data-jpibfi-post-title="Seattle Townhouse Changes + Coffee Table + Furniture Sale" data-jpibfi-post-url="http://theinspiredroom.net/2018/06/19/seattle-townhouse/" data-jpibfi-src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Kylee-coffee-table.jpg" height="975" sizes="(max-width: 650px) 100vw, 650px" src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Kylee-coffee-table.jpg" srcset="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Kylee-coffee-table.jpg 650w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Kylee-coffee-table-200x300.jpg 200w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Kylee-coffee-table-400x600.jpg 400w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Kylee-coffee-table-600x900.jpg 600w" width="650"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            <em>
             What big change could be on the horizon:
            </em>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           We’d eventually love to buy a house, we’d love to have a bit more space and a bigger yard. That’s my long term dream. But with Seattle being as crazy expensive as it is with so few houses for sale, finding one we’d like (and could get) seems like an expensive and daunting process.
          </p>
          <p>
           In light of that, sometimes we think about staying here in this townhouse for another five years. If we stay, I’d definitely want to make this townhouse more of what we want it to be while we live here. Townhouses sell at premium prices here, so thankfully resale value isn’t a huge concern to us. Even if the market went down in the next few years, we’d be OK with the types of updates I’d want to make.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Seattle Townhouse Changes + Coffee Table + Furniture Sale" class="alignnone wp-image-112088 size-full aligncenter" data-jpibfi-post-excerpt="" data-jpibfi-post-title="Seattle Townhouse Changes + Coffee Table + Furniture Sale" data-jpibfi-post-url="http://theinspiredroom.net/2018/06/19/seattle-townhouse/" data-jpibfi-src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/wood-coffee-table-world-market.jpg" height="975" sizes="(max-width: 650px) 100vw, 650px" src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/wood-coffee-table-world-market.jpg" srcset="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/wood-coffee-table-world-market.jpg 650w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/wood-coffee-table-world-market-200x300.jpg 200w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/wood-coffee-table-world-market-400x600.jpg 400w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/wood-coffee-table-world-market-600x900.jpg 600w" width="650"/>

             What changes I’ll be sharing with you soon:

I’ll be back soon to update you on some master bedroom changes I made recently, as well as to share my upcoming office makeover and my kitchen makeover inspiration and ideas.

         <h2 class="entry-title">
          <a class="entry-title-link" href="http://theinspiredroom.net/2018/06/13/trex-deck-building-progress/" rel="bookmark">
           Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek
          </a>
         </h2>
         <div class="entry-content">
          <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <img alt="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" class="aligncenter wp-image-112015 size-full" data-jpibfi-post-excerpt="" data-jpibfi-post-title="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" data-jpibfi-post-url="http://theinspiredroom.net/2018/06/13/trex-deck-building-progress/" data-jpibfi-src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-Saddle-The-Inspired-Room.jpg" height="975" sizes="(max-width: 650px) 100vw, 650px" src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-Saddle-The-Inspired-Room.jpg" srcset="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-Saddle-The-Inspired-Room.jpg 650w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-Saddle-The-Inspired-Room-200x300.jpg 200w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-Saddle-The-Inspired-Room-400x600.jpg 400w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-Saddle-The-Inspired-Room-600x900.jpg 600w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-Saddle-The-Inspired-Room-300x450.jpg 300w" width="650"/>
           <em>
            This deck project is in partnership with
            <a href="http://theinspiredroom.net/2018/06/07/trex-deck-project-lowes/" rel="nofollow noopener" target="_blank">
             Trex and Lowe’s
            </a>
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="float: left; font-size: 60px; line-height: 30px; padding-top: 2px; padding-right: 5px; font-family: times,Georgia; color: #d4d4c7;">
            O
           </span>
           ur backyard was a big selling point for us in buying our home three years ago, so we were so thrilled to finally be creating an inviting deck where we could gather with friends and family to really enjoy it! If you aren’t familiar with Trex, it’s a beautiful composite decking that (unlike wood) won’t crack, stain, or splinter. It’s also low maintenance, which was a huge deal for me. I prefer to enjoy my home and yard, not be slave to the maintenance of it.
          </p>
          <p>
           Even as you begin to design the features of a new deck, start to visualize it in your head, and even see the dimensions all drawn out on paper, until you’re actually
           <em>
            seeing it in person
           </em>
           you may not realize what tremendous difference an improvement like this will make! A new deck becomes like an additional room, so it’s a great way to expand your livable space. It’s an investment that adds value to your home, but it also improves how you live in your home which is always a win for me with any project.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" class="aligncenter wp-image-111935 size-full" data-jpibfi-post-excerpt="" data-jpibfi-post-title="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" data-jpibfi-post-url="http://theinspiredroom.net/2018/06/13/trex-deck-building-progress/" data-jpibfi-src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Patio-Before-Trex-Deck.jpg" height="433" sizes="(max-width: 650px) 100vw, 650px" src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Patio-Before-Trex-Deck.jpg" srcset="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Patio-Before-Trex-Deck.jpg 650w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Patio-Before-Trex-Deck-300x200.jpg 300w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Patio-Before-Trex-Deck-455x303.jpg 455w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Patio-Before-Trex-Deck-600x400.jpg 600w" width="650"/>
           <br/>
           We started with an old cracked concrete patio. Not the worst situation in the world, of course, but definitely not the most inspiring. It was ready for a refresh.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" class="aligncenter wp-image-111996 size-full" data-jpibfi-post-excerpt="" data-jpibfi-post-title="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" data-jpibfi-post-url="http://theinspiredroom.net/2018/06/13/trex-deck-building-progress/" data-jpibfi-src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Deck-Framing-Progress.jpg" height="975" sizes="(max-width: 650px) 100vw, 650px" src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Deck-Framing-Progress.jpg" srcset="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Deck-Framing-Progress.jpg 650w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Deck-Framing-Progress-200x300.jpg 200w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Deck-Framing-Progress-400x600.jpg 400w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Deck-Framing-Progress-600x900.jpg 600w" width="650"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           A really cool thing about our project is that we were able to leave the entire existing patio in place. I’m all about simplification in every way possible, so it was wonderful that the deck framing was able to be constructed right over it!
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <img alt="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" class="aligncenter wp-image-111997 size-full" data-jpibfi-post-excerpt="" data-jpibfi-post-title="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" data-jpibfi-post-url="http://theinspiredroom.net/2018/06/13/trex-deck-building-progress/" data-jpibfi-src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Deck-progress-the-inspired-room-blog.jpg" height="433" sizes="(max-width: 650px) 100vw, 650px" src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Deck-progress-the-inspired-room-blog.jpg" srcset="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Deck-progress-the-inspired-room-blog.jpg 650w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Deck-progress-the-inspired-room-blog-300x200.jpg 300w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Deck-progress-the-inspired-room-blog-455x303.jpg 455w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Deck-progress-the-inspired-room-blog-600x400.jpg 600w" width="650"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           Since my husband and I are definitely not skilled DIY’ers when it comes to construction, it was wonderful to be able to have a Lowe’s PSE (Project Specialist Exterior) build the deck.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" class="aligncenter wp-image-112003 size-full" data-jpibfi-post-excerpt="" data-jpibfi-post-title="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" data-jpibfi-post-url="http://theinspiredroom.net/2018/06/13/trex-deck-building-progress/" data-jpibfi-src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Lowes-worker-deck.jpg" height="975" sizes="(max-width: 650px) 100vw, 650px" src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Lowes-worker-deck.jpg" srcset="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Lowes-worker-deck.jpg 650w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Lowes-worker-deck-200x300.jpg 200w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Lowes-worker-deck-400x600.jpg 400w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Lowes-worker-deck-600x900.jpg 600w" width="650"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           One of the things I usually dread about home improvement projects is the whole process of looking for quality contractors you can trust to take your vision and make it a reality. Being able to line up a qualified, skilled and experienced contractor for our deck through a Lowe’s was a HUGE plus for us! I highly recommend taking a look at (and benefitting from!)
           <a href="https://lowes.trex.com/?utm_source=inspiredroom&amp;utm_medium=blog&amp;utm_campaign=post2&amp;utm_term=lowespse#installation" rel="nofollow noopener" target="_blank">
            the Lowe’s scheduler service
           </a>
           for your projects. It’s definitely worth it.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <img alt="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" class="aligncenter wp-image-111998 size-full" data-jpibfi-post-excerpt="" data-jpibfi-post-title="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" data-jpibfi-post-url="http://theinspiredroom.net/2018/06/13/trex-deck-building-progress/" data-jpibfi-src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Deck-progress.jpg" height="488" sizes="(max-width: 650px) 100vw, 650px" src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Deck-progress.jpg" srcset="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Deck-progress.jpg 650w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Deck-progress-300x225.jpg 300w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Deck-progress-455x342.jpg 455w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Deck-progress-600x450.jpg 600w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Deck-progress-280x210.jpg 280w" width="650"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" class="aligncenter wp-image-112006 size-full" data-jpibfi-post-excerpt="" data-jpibfi-post-title="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" data-jpibfi-post-url="http://theinspiredroom.net/2018/06/13/trex-deck-building-progress/" data-jpibfi-src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-Framing.jpg" height="532" sizes="(max-width: 650px) 100vw, 650px" src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-Framing.jpg" srcset="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-Framing.jpg 650w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-Framing-300x246.jpg 300w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-Framing-455x372.jpg 455w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-Framing-600x491.jpg 600w" width="650"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           Our deck was built in the winter (thus the missing leaves on our Japanese Maple tree!). Every day I’d look out to see the progress (and of course dream about when it would be filled with furniture and flowers!).
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" class="aligncenter wp-image-112007 size-full" data-jpibfi-post-excerpt="" data-jpibfi-post-title="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" data-jpibfi-post-url="http://theinspiredroom.net/2018/06/13/trex-deck-building-progress/" data-jpibfi-src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-deck-process-screws.jpg" height="433" sizes="(max-width: 650px) 100vw, 650px" src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-deck-process-screws.jpg" srcset="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-deck-process-screws.jpg 650w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-deck-process-screws-300x200.jpg 300w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-deck-process-screws-455x303.jpg 455w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-deck-process-screws-600x400.jpg 600w" width="650"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           Our Trex deck color is called Saddle, which was an in stock option at Lowe’s. There are so many colors you can choose from! Even if you
           <em>
            think
           </em>
           you have an idea of what color you want before you begin, I still recommend you bring samples home first so you can see how natural surroundings impact your decision.
          </p>
          <p>
           We found that the wood hue created a cozy, inviting, organic feel that really complemented our natural “Northwest” surroundings, too. When you consider your home’s location in the country, and even style features found in the neighborhood homes, you have the opportunity to bring together elements that will create a harmonious mood.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" class="aligncenter wp-image-112004 size-full" data-jpibfi-post-excerpt="" data-jpibfi-post-title="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" data-jpibfi-post-url="http://theinspiredroom.net/2018/06/13/trex-deck-building-progress/" data-jpibfi-src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-The-Inspired-Room-blog-backyard-progress.jpg" height="433" sizes="(max-width: 650px) 100vw, 650px" src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-The-Inspired-Room-blog-backyard-progress.jpg" srcset="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-The-Inspired-Room-blog-backyard-progress.jpg 650w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-The-Inspired-Room-blog-backyard-progress-300x200.jpg 300w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-The-Inspired-Room-blog-backyard-progress-455x303.jpg 455w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-The-Inspired-Room-blog-backyard-progress-600x400.jpg 600w" width="650"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" class="aligncenter wp-image-112009 size-full" data-jpibfi-post-excerpt="" data-jpibfi-post-title="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" data-jpibfi-post-url="http://theinspiredroom.net/2018/06/13/trex-deck-building-progress/" data-jpibfi-src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-enhance-railing-and-baluster-kit.jpg" height="867" sizes="(max-width: 650px) 100vw, 650px" src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-enhance-railing-and-baluster-kit.jpg" srcset="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-enhance-railing-and-baluster-kit.jpg 650w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-enhance-railing-and-baluster-kit-225x300.jpg 225w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-enhance-railing-and-baluster-kit-450x600.jpg 450w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-enhance-railing-and-baluster-kit-600x800.jpg 600w" width="650"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           We were especially excited about having black railings installed around our deck. While our deck isn’t high off the ground, we wanted to utilize railings to give the space a cozier feeling. I find railings bordering a deck establishes a backdrop that can make furniture placement easier, too.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" class="aligncenter wp-image-112017 size-full" data-jpibfi-post-excerpt="" data-jpibfi-post-title="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" data-jpibfi-post-url="http://theinspiredroom.net/2018/06/13/trex-deck-building-progress/" data-jpibfi-src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-Sneak-Peek.jpg" height="463" sizes="(max-width: 650px) 100vw, 650px" src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-Sneak-Peek.jpg" srcset="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-Sneak-Peek.jpg 650w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-Sneak-Peek-300x214.jpg 300w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-Sneak-Peek-455x324.jpg 455w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-Sneak-Peek-600x427.jpg 600w" width="650"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           Trex offers so many styles of railings and color combinations! Our railings are called Trex Enhance in Charcoal Black. I love the classic style. The black of course makes a nice statement and contrast with a white house, yet it allows the natural greenery around the yard to stay in focus.
          </p>
          <p>
           The added architectural detail as well as the diagonal layout of our railings make the deck design feel so special.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" class="aligncenter wp-image-112013 size-full" data-jpibfi-post-excerpt="" data-jpibfi-post-title="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" data-jpibfi-post-url="http://theinspiredroom.net/2018/06/13/trex-deck-building-progress/" data-jpibfi-src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Deck-Details-Trex.jpg" height="975" sizes="(max-width: 650px) 100vw, 650px" src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Deck-Details-Trex.jpg" srcset="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Deck-Details-Trex.jpg 650w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Deck-Details-Trex-200x300.jpg 200w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Deck-Details-Trex-400x600.jpg 400w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Deck-Details-Trex-600x900.jpg 600w" width="650"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           Another detail I absolutely LOVE is the layout of the diagonal deck boards and the border around the perimeter, it makes it feel so custom and unique. I love how a diagonal design element creates a pleasing sense of movement for your eye.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" class="aligncenter wp-image-102389 size-full" data-jpibfi-post-excerpt="" data-jpibfi-post-title="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" data-jpibfi-post-url="http://theinspiredroom.net/2018/06/13/trex-deck-building-progress/" data-jpibfi-src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/The-Inspired-Room-Small-Kitchen-Reveal-Herringbone-Gray-Tile-Floors.jpg" height="1000" sizes="(max-width: 650px) 100vw, 650px" src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/The-Inspired-Room-Small-Kitchen-Reveal-Herringbone-Gray-Tile-Floors.jpg" srcset="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/The-Inspired-Room-Small-Kitchen-Reveal-Herringbone-Gray-Tile-Floors.jpg 650w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/The-Inspired-Room-Small-Kitchen-Reveal-Herringbone-Gray-Tile-Floors-195x300.jpg 195w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/The-Inspired-Room-Small-Kitchen-Reveal-Herringbone-Gray-Tile-Floors-390x600.jpg 390w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/The-Inspired-Room-Small-Kitchen-Reveal-Herringbone-Gray-Tile-Floors-600x923.jpg 600w" width="650"/>
           <br/>
           Creating that movement and design interest is also the reason why we decided to lay our tile in a
           <a href="http://theinspiredroom.net/2017/02/27/small-kitchen-remodel-reveal-before-after/" rel="nofollow noopener" target="_blank">
            diagonal herringbone pattern in our kitchen!
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" class="aligncenter wp-image-112014 size-full" data-jpibfi-post-excerpt="" data-jpibfi-post-title="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" data-jpibfi-post-url="http://theinspiredroom.net/2018/06/13/trex-deck-building-progress/" data-jpibfi-src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/The-Inspired-Room-Deck-Sneak-Peek.jpg" height="433" sizes="(max-width: 650px) 100vw, 650px" src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/The-Inspired-Room-Deck-Sneak-Peek.jpg" srcset="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/The-Inspired-Room-Deck-Sneak-Peek.jpg 650w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/The-Inspired-Room-Deck-Sneak-Peek-300x200.jpg 300w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/The-Inspired-Room-Deck-Sneak-Peek-455x303.jpg 455w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/The-Inspired-Room-Deck-Sneak-Peek-600x400.jpg 600w" width="650"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           As soon as the deck was complete the whole backyard felt like a new place! The railings and deck established such a wonderful outdoor gathering space, but I’ve also noticed that somehow the new deck made the yard feel like an even more inviting destination, too.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" class="aligncenter wp-image-112016 size-full" data-jpibfi-post-excerpt="" data-jpibfi-post-title="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" data-jpibfi-post-url="http://theinspiredroom.net/2018/06/13/trex-deck-building-progress/" data-jpibfi-src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-Sneak-Peek-The-Inspired-Room-Blog.jpg" height="975" sizes="(max-width: 650px) 100vw, 650px" src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-Sneak-Peek-The-Inspired-Room-Blog.jpg" srcset="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-Sneak-Peek-The-Inspired-Room-Blog.jpg 650w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-Sneak-Peek-The-Inspired-Room-Blog-200x300.jpg 200w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-Sneak-Peek-The-Inspired-Room-Blog-400x600.jpg 400w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Trex-Deck-Sneak-Peek-The-Inspired-Room-Blog-600x900.jpg 600w" width="650"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           We are setting up the deck right now, so I cannot wait to show you the finished and furnished reveal (Lowe’s has some really great furnishings and decor pieces for your outdoor spaces. If you missed the look we’re going for, you can see our vision board and find sources for some the furniture we’ll have on the deck
           <a href="http://theinspiredroom.net/2018/06/07/trex-deck-project-lowes/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
            here
           </a>
           ).
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" class="aligncenter wp-image-112002 size-full" data-jpibfi-post-excerpt="" data-jpibfi-post-title="Trex Deck Building Progress and Sneak Peek" data-jpibfi-post-url="http://theinspiredroom.net/2018/06/13/trex-deck-building-progress/" data-jpibfi-src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Jack-Trex-Deck-Progress.jpg" height="975" sizes="(max-width: 650px) 100vw, 650px" src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Jack-Trex-Deck-Progress.jpg" srcset="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Jack-Trex-Deck-Progress.jpg 650w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Jack-Trex-Deck-Progress-200x300.jpg 200w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Jack-Trex-Deck-Progress-400x600.jpg 400w, http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Jack-Trex-Deck-Progress-600x900.jpg 600w" width="650"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           Oh, and in case you’re wondering what the dogs think of this change, Jack was quite obsessed with the deck from the first day he could walk on it. If I can get a video of him on the deck, you’ll laugh! He loves taking a running leap off from the deck to the grass. He clearly thinks we designed this as his own personal playground. (Notice Lily way off in the yard, always sitting somewhere outside enjoying the sea breeze in her fur! She’s waiting for the furniture to be set up, she’s all about the comfort.).
          </p>
          <p>
           Stay tuned for the big reveal, friends! I can’t wait to invite you over for our first virtual gathering on the deck right here on the blog.
          </p>
          <p>
           Leave me a comment below to tell me what you’re bringing to the (virtual) deck reveal party at my house! I’ll give away a copy of my book
           <a href="http://theinspiredroom.net/books/" rel="nofollow noopener" target="_blank">
            Simple Gatherings
           </a>
           to five commenters at random, or to whoever is offering to bring the tastiest dishes. ;-)
          </p>
