         <h1>
          Science for Kids ~ How to make a barometer
         </h1>
         <p>
          Did you know that you can make your own barometer using items from around your home?  I had no idea!  My kids love science experiments, so I can’t wait to try it! Today, Joanna from
          <a href="http://www.bluebarnliving.blogspot.com" target="_blank">
