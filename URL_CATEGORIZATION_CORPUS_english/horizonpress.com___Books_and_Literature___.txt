      <h1 class="pad1">
       Caister Academic Press
      </h1>
      <b>
       Books and eBooks in Microbiology and Molecular Biology
      </b>
      <p>
       Caister Academic Press is a leading specialist publisher of books and ebooks in microbiology and molecular biology with a focus on current research, latest technology, new applications and emerging trends. Founded in 1993 we currently publish around fifteen new titles each year comprising review volumes, practical manuals and reference texts for research scientists, postgraduates and bioscience professionals. Our books are multiauthor volumes with internationally renowned authors chosen from a wide range of countries. Our editors and authors are highly respected leaders in the field with worldwide reputations in their specialist area.  We aim to produce our books within six to eight months of receiving the manuscripts ensuring the publication of up-to-date, authoritative and cutting-edge volumes. Our range of books and ebooks includes titles in microbiology, molecular biology, virology, biotechnology and PCR.
      </p>
