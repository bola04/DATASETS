         <h2>
          THE WILDLIFE TRADE MONITORING NETWORK
         </h2>
         <p>
         </p>
         <div class="ss-slideshow-v2" id="squarespace-slideshow-wrapper-1486476228" rel="5899d3ea0a6d03da24234a4b">
         </div>
         <div style="background-color: #a5a5a5; padding: 11px 0 2px 0; width: 810px; text-align: center; font-size: 13px;">
          <p style="font-family: 'Raleway Light', Verdana, sans-serif; color: #fff !important;">
           <strong>
            we work to ensure that trade in wild plants and animals is not a threat to the conservation of nature
           </strong>
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
         </p>
         <h2 style="line-height: 17px !important;">
          HOW WE WORK
          <br/>
          <span style="color: #969595!important; font-size: 16px !important; line-height: 17px !important;">
           PATHWAYS TO CATALYSING CHANGE
          </span>
         </h2>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p style="font-family: 'Raleway', Verdana, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; color: #3a3a3a;">
          TRAFFIC believes in a world where wildlife trade maintains wildlife populations and contributes to sustainable human development. Through research, analysis, guidance and influence we
          <span style="color: #66bb5e;">
           promote sustainable wildlife trade
          </span>
          and
          <span style="color: #f86c6c;">
           combat wildlife crime and trafficking
          </span>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="full-image-float-left ssNonEditable">
           <span>
            <a href="traffic-programme/?#prog-analysis">
             <img alt="" src="/storage/images/analysis.jpg?__SQUARESPACE_CACHEVERSION=1486738998777" style="width: 190px;"/>
            </a>
           </span>
          </span>
          <span class="full-image-float-left ssNonEditable">
           <span>
            <a href="traffic-programme/?#prog-research">
             <img alt="" src="/storage/images/research.jpg?__SQUARESPACE_CACHEVERSION=1486566723497" style="width: 190px;"/>
            </a>
           </span>
          </span>
          <span class="full-image-float-left ssNonEditable">
           <span>
            <a href="/traffic-programme/?#prog-guidance">
             <img alt="" src="/storage/images/guidance.jpg?__SQUARESPACE_CACHEVERSION=1486742425836" style="width: 190px;"/>
            </a>
           </span>
          </span>
          <span class="full-image-block ssNonEditable">
           <span>
            <a href="traffic-programme/?#prog-influence">
             <img alt="" src="/storage/images/influence.jpg?__SQUARESPACE_CACHEVERSION=1486566770449" style="width: 190px;"/>
            </a>
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <div style="width: 810px;">
          <div style="width: 48%; border: 1px Solid #CCC; display: inline-block;">
           <div style="background-color: #515151; color: #fff; text-align: center; font-family: 'Anton', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; padding: 8px 0;">
            <span>
             <span style="color: #f86c6c;">
              RED STREAM:
             </span>
             WILDLIFE CRIME AND ILLEGAL TRADE
            </span>
           </div>
           <p style="color: #5b5b5b; text-align: center; font-size: 12px; padding: 10px 30px; font-family: 'Raleway Light', Raleway, Verdana, sans-serif; line-height: 19px;">
            We work towards a sustained reduction in illegal wildlife trade by increasing the risks and reducing the reward associated with trading in wildlife contraband, reducing criminal motivation and engagement.
           </p>

         <h2>
          ENCOURAGING SUSTAINABILITY
         </h2>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          As human populations have grown, so has demand for wildlife resources. Whether for food, medicine, construction or lifestyle, wildlife trade is an increasingly important component to the economies and lifestyles of societies across the world. The trade in timber and seafood for example is estimated to be worth a combined total of USD300 billion a year.
         </p>
         <p>
          Given the scale of such demand, it has become critically important to ensure that responsible practices, sustainability and traceability are promoted throughout supply chains. We work with governments, international organizations and local communities to ensure that legal wildlife trade brings benefits without impacting on the conservation of nature.
         </p>
