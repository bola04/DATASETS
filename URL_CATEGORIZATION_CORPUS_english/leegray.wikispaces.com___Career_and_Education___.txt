               Thank you for visiting my Wikispaces page. Since beginning my educational career in 1987, many changes have come to the classroom I was a product of. Gone are the days of chalkboards and overheads. The traditional classroom has given way to web-based instruction, interactive classrooms, and virtual public schools. Students in schools now have opportunities once only dreamed of. It is my purpose as an educator to ensure all students obtain the best education career geared toward their individual goals and needs. Education has become a marketplace, where the student becomes the raw material and the post-high school careers become the consumer. Whether career holds college, military, trades, or work force opportunities, it is the challenge of educators to prepare a student to be successful in the next phase of their life.
