      <h2>

      </h2>
      <h3>
       Give Every Dollar A Job
      </h3>
      <p>
       The secret is just to be intentional about what you want your money to do
       <em>
        before
       </em>
       you spend it. (Have we given away the secret sauce?) Take only the money you have on hand and ask yourself, “What should this money do before I’m paid again?” With just four simple steps, you’ll begin to take back control.
