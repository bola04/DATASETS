       <h2 class="content style5">
        <strong>
         Coffee Education
        </strong>
       </h2>
       <p class="content">
        Our intention is to improve coffee quality through education and research. This 300 page coffee Web site was developed as a platform to share our passion for specialty coffee.
       </p>
       <p class="content">
        We recommend beginning your journey learning about coffee by learning about the agriculture of coffee since the harvesting and processing of coffee plays a major role in how coffee should be roasted and brewed. After learning about the
        <a href="agriculture/coffeeplant.htm">
         coffee plant
        </a>
        , we move to coffee
        <a href="coffee/grinding.htm">
         grinding
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="coffee/brewing.htm">
         brewing
        </a>
        , and
        <a href="coffee/roasting2.htm">
         roasting
        </a>
        . Once you have a grasp on the basics of single origin coffees, explore our
        <a href="espresso/potential.htm">
         espresso section.
        </a>
       </p>
       <p class="content">
        The espresso section brings together the agriculture and coffee sections by drawing a link between preparation methods and flavor profile.
        <a href="espresso/blending.htm">
         Blending
        </a>
        espresso is an art form that necessitates a complete understanding of coffee harvesting and production methods.
       </p>
       <p class="content">
        The final sections about coffee science, coffee politics, and the coffee market are more detailed views of the coffee world. The market section gives coffee consumption statistics as well as coffee production statistics. The politics section gives an overview of current social issues of coffee including
        <a href="politics/fairtrade.htm">
         fair trade
        </a>
        coffee,
        <a href="politics/organic.htm">
         organic
        </a>
        coffee, and bird safe coffee.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Pour yourself a cup of joe and enjoy our library, coffee
        <a href="picturegallery.htm">
         photo galleries
        </a>
        , and original coffee training videos.
       </p>
