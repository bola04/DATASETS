         <p>
          If you are in the market for
          <b>
           tattoo or piercing supplies
          </b>
          , 
               you’ve come to the right place. We can provide you with all 
               the information you need to use, buy, and sell
          <b>
           <a href="tattoo.html">
            equipment
           </a>
          </b>
          ,
          <b>
           <a href="piercing.html">

           <a href="bodyjewelry.html">
            body jewelry
           </a>
          </b>

               tattoo equipment, it’s important to research the
          <b>
           types 
               of equipment
          </b>
          that are out there and the pros and cons to using 
               each type.
          <b>
           <a href="tattooguns.html">
            Tattoo guns
           </a>
          </b>
          ,
          <b>
           needles
          </b>
          ,
          <b>
           tubes
          </b>
          ,
          <b>
           <a href="tattoomachinecomparison.html">
            machines
           </a>
          </b>
          , and
          <b>
           inks
          </b>
          are all key elements to starting 
               a studio, shop, parlor or business. The
          <b>
           guns and machines
          </b>
          are 
               usually
          <b>
           hand crafted or homemade
          </b>
          .
         </p>

         <p>
          As far as
          <b>
           piercing supplies
          </b>
          go, the selection is unlimited. 
               You can buy g
          <b>
           old, silver, or titanium body jewelry
          </b>
          in all 
               types of
          <b>
           exotic designs
          </b>
          and shapes. As a supplier or piercing 
               shop, you will want to carry all types of piercing supplies so your 
               customers have options to choose from. The
          <b>
           <a href="bodypiercingsupplies.html">
            piercing 
               guns
           </a>
          </b>
          and
          <b>
           needles
          </b>
          are just as important as the
          <b>
           exotic 
               body jewelry
          </b>
          because your customers will want their piercing 
               to be as painless and infection-free as possible. Make sure to sterilize 
                 guns and
          <b>
           chairs
          </b>
          after every use. Piercing needles 
               should be thrown away, assuming they’re disposable.

         <h1>
          Tattoo guns, machines, power supplies and pigment inks
         </h1>
         <p>
          When it comes to needles, tubes, and inks for
          <b>
           designs
          </b>
          , 
               make sure you know what products go together. For tubes and needles, 
               they usually come in
          <b>
           disposable forms
          </b>
          , a great option for 
               the customer because they know their experience will be hygienic 
               and pleasant. As for the inks, it is crucial to carry a wide variety 
               of colors so you as the artists can create rich and
          <b>
           unique designs
          </b>
          . 
               Another important piece of equipment to purchase is the
          <b>
           thermal 
               image copier
          </b>
          . This machine will help create a beautiful and 
               consistent tattoo from any hand drawn design or stencil.
          <img align="right" height="224" src="images/dragontattooblack.gif" width="307"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          As a tattoo artist, you go through a lot of supplies. One of these 
               supplies is tattoo ink. For tattoo artists of all experience levels, 
               tattoo ink is the lifeblood of your business or hobby, obviously. 
               If tattoo ink is starting to break your budget, then read on for 
               tips on finding cheap tattoo ink.
         </p>
