         </h2>
         <div id="HPText">
          <p>
           Arizona Sun® provides perfect skin protection products for everything fun under the sun! The sunscreen lotion and sunblock lotion are perfect for mountain biking, skiing, pushing a stroller or doing some serious pool time.
          </p>
          <p>
           Inspired by the same ingredients Native Americans used to protect themselves from the desert sun, Arizona Sun® combines the Southwest's natural ingredients - including aloe, sage, wild roses, cacti and jojoba - with today's most advanced skin sciences to bring you a line of skin care and sunscreen lotion and sunblock lotion products worthy of the sun's most harmful ultraviolet rays.
          </p>
          <p>
           From moisturizers and advanced sunscreens, to sun tan lotion and body care products for adults and kids, each sunblock lotion and sun tan lotion or sunscreen lotion product feels great going on and evokes the delicious, world famous scent of fragrant desert flowers. A special experience before you even step outside!
          </p>

           We offer a unique assortment of
