          <h3>
           INSTRUCTIONS FOR YOUR FIRST DISNEY WORLD VISIT
          </h3>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           Follow the instructions that follow for your first family visit to Walt Disney World
           <strong>
            <span style="color: #ff0049;">
             to save thousands of dollars,
            </span>
           </strong>
           and
           <strong>
            <span style="color: #ff0049;">
             avoid dozens of hours waiting in line,
            </span>
           </strong>
           while seeing all the best of Walt Disney World!
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           Want a second opinion?  Then
           <a class="ext-link" href="https://www.amazon.com/easy-Guide-Disney-World-Visit/dp/1683900839/ref=as_li_ss_tl?s=books&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1503744089&amp;sr=1-1&amp;keywords=The+easy+guide+Walt+Disney+2018&amp;linkCode=sl1&amp;tag=playoufirfamv-20&amp;linkId=b888db402dc50b5059bb14654484c7ba" rel="external nofollow" target="_blank" title="">
            buy the book
           </a>
           !
          </p>
          <h3>
           <a href="https://yourfirstvisit.net/wp-content/uploads/2008/01/yourfirstvisit.net_...helps-you-make-great-Disney-World-plans-quickly.jpg">
            <img alt="yourfirstvisit.net...helps you make great Disney World plans quickly!!" class="dave" height="249" src="https://yourfirstvisit.net/wp-content/uploads/2008/01/yourfirstvisit.net_...helps-you-make-great-Disney-World-plans-quickly-300x249.jpg" title="yourfirstvisit.net" width="300"/>
           </a>
           1.
           <a href="https://yourfirstvisit.net/planning-your-first-family-trip/when-to-go/">
            <span style="color: #ff0049;">
             When to Go to Walt Disney World:
            </span>
           </a>
          </h3>
          <p>
           If you can, arrive at Walt Disney World one of
           <strong>
            the first three Saturdays after Thanksgiving
           </strong>
           .
           <a href="https://yourfirstvisit.net/planning-your-first-family-trip/when-to-go/">
            <strong>
             More…
            </strong>
           </a>
           <a href="https://yourfirstvisit.net/planning-your-first-family-trip/when-to-go/next-best-dates/">
            <strong>
             Next best dates to go…
            </strong>
           </a>
          </p>
          <h3>
           2.
           <a href="https://yourfirstvisit.net/planning-your-first-family-trip/how-long-to-stay/">
            <span style="color: #ff0049;">
             How Long to Stay:
            </span>
           </a>
          </h3>
          <p>
           <strong>
            You can have a wonderful trip spending as few as 5 days.
           </strong>
           However, if you can
           <strong>
            , be in Orlando for 9 days
           </strong>
           –a Saturday through a Sunday.
           <a href="https://yourfirstvisit.net/planning-your-first-family-trip/how-long-to-stay/">
            <strong>
             More…
            </strong>
           </a>
           <strong>
           </strong>
           <a href="https://yourfirstvisit.net/planning-your-first-family-trip/how-long-to-stay/other-trip-lengths/">
            <strong>
             Going for a shorter trip…
            </strong>
           </a>
          </p>
          <h3>
           3.
           <a href="https://yourfirstvisit.net/planning-your-first-family-trip/how-oldtall-your-children-should-be/">
            <span style="color: #ff0049;">
             How Old/Tall Your Children Should be:
            </span>
           </a>
          </h3>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Kids of any age can have a wonderful time at Disney World.
           </strong>
           However, if this may be your kids’
           <strong>
            <span style="color: #ff0049;">
             only
            </span>
           </strong>
           childhood visit, consider going when your
           <strong>
            youngest child is at least 8 or 9,
           </strong>
           and your
           <strong>
            shortest child at least 48 inches tall
           </strong>
           .
           <a href="https://yourfirstvisit.net/planning-your-first-family-trip/how-oldtall-your-children-should-be/">
            <strong>
             More…
            </strong>
           </a>
           <strong>
           </strong>
           <a href="https://yourfirstvisit.net/planning-your-first-family-trip/how-oldtall-your-children-should-be/what-if-theyre-too-shortyoung/">
            <strong>
             Planning for going with younger/shorter kids…
            </strong>
           </a>
          </p>
          <h3>
           4.
           <a href="https://yourfirstvisit.net/planning-your-first-family-trip/where-to-stay/">
            <span style="color: #ff0049;">
             Where to Stay at Walt Disney World:
            </span>
           </a>
          </h3>
          <p>
           Stay at Disney’s
           <strong>
            Polynesian Resort
           </strong>
           if you can afford it, or at Disney’s
           <strong>
            Art of Animation
           </strong>
           <strong>
            Resort
           </strong>
           if you can’t.
           <a href="https://yourfirstvisit.net/planning-your-first-family-trip/where-to-stay/">
            <strong>
             More…
            </strong>
           </a>
           <strong>
           </strong>
           <a href="https://yourfirstvisit.net/planning-your-first-family-trip/where-to-stay/next-best-resort-choices/">
            <strong>
             Next best resort options…
            </strong>
           </a>
          </p>
          <h3>
           5.
           <a href="https://yourfirstvisit.net/planning-your-first-family-trip/what-to-budget/">
            <span style="color: #ff0049;">
             How Much to Budget:
            </span>
           </a>
          </h3>
          <p>
           <strong>
            You can have a wonderful first visit for $3,000 or less.
           </strong>
           However, to see
           <strong>
            all
           </strong>
           the best of Walt Disney World,
           <strong>
            budget if you can $8,400
           </strong>
           if you are staying at
           <strong>
            the Polynesian
           </strong>
           , or
           <strong>
            $5,300
           </strong>
           if at
           <strong>
            Art of Animation
           </strong>
           .
           <a href="https://yourfirstvisit.net/planning-your-first-family-trip/what-to-budget/">
            <strong>
             More…
            </strong>
           </a>
           <strong>
           </strong>
           <a href="https://yourfirstvisit.net/planning-your-first-family-trip/what-to-budget/estimating-budgets/">
            <strong>
             Developing a much lower or a different budget…
            </strong>
           </a>
          </p>

           <div class="format_text">
            <p>
             Hi Sarah, it rains almost every day (often just briefly) in the summer in Orlando, and most simply ignore it and continue with their park plans. There’s no refunds because of rain.  The Disney World restaurants begin booking 180 days out, and the most popular–like Cinderella’s’ Royal Table–sell out months before. I would check availability for it, for Crystal Palace (WInnie the Pooh), for Chef Mickey’s (Mickey and friends at the nearby COntemporary) and 1900 Park Fare (Cinderella in the evenings, Mary Poppins at Breakfast) in the nearby Grand Floridian.
            </p>
            <p>
             Magic Kingdom is busy in July.  The best days there are Tuesday and Thursdays, so the 10th and 12th are your best options!
            </p>
            <p>
             Once you have set your dates you need to book your
             <a href="https://yourfirstvisit.net/2016/02/21/the-basics-the-disney-world-theme-parks-and-fastpass/">
              FastPass+
             </a>
             ASAP, as otherwise you will face needless waits.
            </p>
