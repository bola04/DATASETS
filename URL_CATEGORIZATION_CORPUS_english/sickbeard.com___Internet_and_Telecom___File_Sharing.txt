       <h2>
        Welcome to Sick Beard's Official Website
        <br/>
        <span>
         The ultimate PVR application that downloads and manages your TV shows
        </span>
       </h2>
       <p>
        Sick Beard is a PVR for newsgroup users (with limited torrent support). It watches for new episodes of your favorite shows and when they are posted it downloads them, sorts and renames them, and optionally generates metadata for them. It currently supports NZBs.org, NZBMatrix, NZBs'R'Us, Newzbin, Womble's Index, NZB.su, TVTorrents and EZRSS and retrieves show information from theTVDB.com and TVRage.com.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <h2>
        Feature List
        <br/>
        <span>
         Quick list of some of the major features
        </span>
       </h2>
       <div class="block">
        <img alt="picture" height="53" src="images/img_1.gif" width="53"/>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Automatic Episode Downloads
         </strong>
         Finds new episodes and downloads them using your favorite client.
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="block">
        <img alt="picture" height="53" src="images/img_2.gif" width="53"/>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Generate Episode Metadata
         </strong>
         Connects to TheTVDB and downloads TV Show information, including episode screenshots.
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="block">
        <img alt="picture" height="53" src="images/img_3.gif" width="53"/>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Rename Your TV Episode Files
         </strong>
         Specify your naming convention and Sick Beard will rename your files for you.
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="block">
        <img alt="picture" height="53" src="images/img_4.gif" width="53"/>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Communicate via XBMC, Growl, Prowl, Twitter, Notifo, and more
         </strong>
         Sick Beard will notify you of newly downloaded episodes via your PC or phone and can update your XBMC library.
        </p>
