           <p>
            Communicating effectively with citizens through a variety of channels, transforming service delivery through digital and delivering long-term savings.
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="container copy-video">
          <div class="content left-col">
           <p>
            Public Sector organisations are under constant pressure to deliver more for less. Understanding the environment and
            <a href="http://blog.wlt.com/transforming-public-services-guide" target="_blank">
             the needs of end users
            </a>
            of central or local government is vital.
           </p>
           <p>
            With our publishing division TSO, we have over 50 years of experience of working with the public sector, such as The Gazette, The Financial Conduct Authority and Medicines and Healthcare Products Regulatory Agency. Williams Lea Tag is also
            <a href="https://www.wlt.com/news/2017/03/14/williams-lea-tag-achieves-cyber-essentials-plus-accreditation" target="_blank">
             Cyber Essentials Plus accredited
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <p>
            To view the government frameworks that we operate under please
            <a href="https://www.wlt.com/government-framework" target="_blank">
             click here
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="https://www.tsoshop.co.uk/" target="_blank">
             <strong>
              TSO Shop
             </strong>
            </a>
            specialises in official and regulatory publications and products.
           </p>
           <p>
            Our information management and publishing solutions focus on consultancy, infrastructure efficiencies, and offer best-in-class services to deliver solutions to citizens.
           </p>
