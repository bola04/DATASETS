       Welcome to Tau Online
      </h2>
      <div class="entry">
       <p>
        Welcome to Tau Online, our online community for Warhammer 40,000. We cater for just about all races and factions, ranging from Tau, Eldar, Chaos, Space Marines or even the Imperial Guard. Feel free to visit our thriving and friendly
        <strong>
         <a href="http://forums.tauonline.org">
          forums
         </a>
        </strong>
        , or browse our site to find help, advice or just plain inspiration from other gamers.
       </p>
       <p>
