                <h2>
                 Your money, day-to-day
                </h2>
                <p>
                 Let's face it; we think about money the same way we spend it, day-to-day, not summarized by month or in a long list of transactions. CalendarBudget, the best personal money management software, tracks and forecasts your use of money in the same way you actually use it, day-to-day, on a calendar as financial planners have long recommended.
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 Also, avoidable problems like bounced checks, overdraft charges, insufficient funds and over-use of credit can be predicted and prevented since CalendarBudget allows you to plan ahead and see when the funds will be available in your account.
                </p>

                 <li>
                  reminds you when bills are due and encourages you to keep your budget up to date
                 </li>
