             <h4>
              Welcome to Ink Jam Tattoo Studio, Arlington's first and only tattoo studio. Ink Jam is a relaxed, friendly clean environment where quality and detailed work is our number-one priority. A custom tattoo can be designed for you, or choose from our extensive collection of designs. Whether you are getting your first tattoo or fifteenth, large or small, you will walk out with a showcase tattoo.
             </h4>
             <h4>
              Ink Jam's owner, Jim Quinn, is an experienced tattoo artist. He attended Butera School of Art in Boston, Mass and has worked as an artist in the New England area for the past twenty years. Jim was awarded First Place for a commemorative 9/11 back piece in the 2002 Boston Bike and Tattoo Show. Come to our studio and
