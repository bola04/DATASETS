                     <h2 class="field field--name-field-project-heading field--type-string field--label-hidden field--item">
                      <a href="/youth-justice">
                       Youth Justice
                      </a>
                     </h2>
                     <div class="field field--name-field-project-body field--type-text-with-summary field--label-hidden field--item">
                      <p>
                       Over one million cases involving youth are heard by our juvenile courts annually. Youth need experienced, dedicated advocates to fight for them in courts, legislatures, and city halls.
                      </p>
                     </div>
                    </div>
                   </div>
                  </div>
                  <div class="field--item">
                   <div class="row jlc-paragraph paragraph paragraph--type-program-project paragraph--view-mode-work" id="paragraph-63">

                     <h2 class="field field--name-field-project-heading field--type-string field--label-hidden field--item">
                      <a href="/children-prison">

                      </a>
                     </h2>
                     <div class="field field--name-field-project-body field--type-text-with-summary field--label-hidden field--item">
                      <p>
                       Solitary confinement is nothing short of state-sanctioned child abuse. Strip searches and shackling are inhumane. No child deserves this treatment.
                      </p>
                     </div>
                    </div>
                   </div>
                  </div>
                  <div class="field--item">
                   <div class="row jlc-paragraph paragraph paragraph--type-program-project paragraph--view-mode-work" id="paragraph-64">
                    <div class="col-xs-12 bs-region bs-region--main">
                     <h2 class="field field--name-field-project-heading field--type-string field--label-hidden field--item">
                      <a href="/foster-care">
                       Foster Care
                      </a>
                     </h2>
                     <div class="field field--name-field-project-body field--type-text-with-summary field--label-hidden field--item">
                      <p dir="ltr">
                       Over 415,000 youth are in foster care, and around 20,000 young adults “age out” of care without permanent family connections. They deserve better from the institutions meant to support them.
                      </p>
                     </div>
                    </div>
                   </div>
                  </div>

                      <a href="/youth-advocacy">
                       Youth Advocacy
                      </a>
                     </h2>
                     <div class="field field--name-field-project-body field--type-text-with-summary field--label-hidden field--item">

                      </p>
