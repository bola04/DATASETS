     <h1 align="center" class="style62">
      <br/>
      Ecology Action: Biointensive Farming
      <br/>
      Growing Ecosystems of Hope  for over 40 Years!
      <br/>
     </h1>
     <hr/>
     <h1 align="center" class="SubheadGreen style10 style14 style15">
      <span class="style7">
       <em>
        Ecology Action teaches people worldwide to better feed themselves
        <br/>
        while building and preserving the soil and conserving resources.
       </em>
      </span>
      <br/>
     </h1>
     <p class="style8">
      Aware of intensifying world challenges and the basic need of people to feed themselves, we have been working for 40 years to develop an elegant, small-scale agricultural system
      <strong>
       — GROW BIOINTENSIVE® Sustainable Mini-Farming —
      </strong>
      that when practiced correctly, nurtures healthy soil fertility, produces high yields, conserves resources and can be used successfully by almost everyone. Our goal is to help this system be known and used locally...on a worldwide basis.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      To learn more about the GROW BIOINTENSIVE method, and how it works,
      <a href="grow_main.html">
       click here
      </a>
      .
     </p>

     <p align="center" class="style6">
      <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="253" mozallowfullscreen="" src="https://player.vimeo.com/video/176675689?color=ffffff&amp;title=0&amp;byline=0&amp;portrait=0" webkitallowfullscreen="" width="450">

      <span class="style8">

     <p align="center" class="style8">
      In  2014, Ecology Action held a special 2-week Farmers Course in Willits, CA. During this amazing event, twelve different sustainability experts with over 250 years of combined experience lectured on a variety of topics essential for sustainable farming. Dozens of these presentations were captured on film so we could share this unique educational experience in farming and sustainability. These films are now online, and are offered as part of Ecology Action's educational mission.
     </p>
