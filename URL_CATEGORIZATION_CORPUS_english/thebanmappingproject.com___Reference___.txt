       <p>
        <b>
         August 2012  Progress Report
        </b>
        <br/>
        Read about the latest activities of the Theban Mapping Project in our
        <a class="link" href="about/TMP-August-2012-Progress-Report.pdf" target="_blank">
         Progress Report
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Photo Database
        </strong>
        offers over 8,000 
   images with Zoomify technology.  We are still working on the categories and 
   file information (IPTC) for the database images. This work will be performed by 
   TMP staff as time permits.
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.tmpimagegallery.com">
          Click here to see work in progress
         </a>
        </strong>
        ,
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Bibliography of Theban West Bank
        </strong>
        <strong>
         Archaeological Sites
        </strong>
        offers over 5,000 references that describe tombs in the Valley of   the Kings, Valley of the Queens, outlying wadis and the Tombs of the Nobles,   plus all the memorial temples, shrines, villages, graffiti, predynastic remains,   and Christian sites. We will regularly update the
        <a href="http://www.tmpbibliography.com">
         <strong>
          Bibliography of Theban West Bank Archaeological Sites
         </strong>
        </a>
        , and we  hope to add a search engine to make it an even more useful tool in the near  future.
        <br/>
        <br/>

         site management Masterplan
        </a>
        for the Valley of the Kings produced by the TMP.
