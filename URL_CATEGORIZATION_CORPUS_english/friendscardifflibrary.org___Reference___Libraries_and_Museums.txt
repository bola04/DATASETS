          <h1 class="post-entry" style="text-align: center;">
           Welcome to the Friends of the Cardiff-by-the-Sea Library
          </h1>
          <hr/>
          <section class="post-entry">
           The Friends of the Cardiff-by-the-Sea Library is a volunteer organization that aids and supports the Cardiff-by-the-Sea branch of the San Diego County Library, by:
           <ul>
            <li>
             Maintaining membership of persons interested in the library and the community;
            </li>
            <li>
             Focusing public attention on library services, facilities, and needs;
            </li>
            <li>
             Helping the library to be responsive to the needs of the community; and
            </li>
            <li>
             Expanding the resources of the library
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p>
            We are actively involved with the literary and artistic activities of Cardiff-by-the-Sea and integrating those activities with the operations of the library.
           </p>
          </section>
