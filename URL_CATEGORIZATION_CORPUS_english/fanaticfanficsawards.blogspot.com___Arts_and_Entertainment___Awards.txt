                     <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                      <a href="http://fanaticfanficsawards.blogspot.com/2016/02/nominations-are-now-open.html">
                       Nominations are now open!
                      </a>
                     </h3>
                     <div class="post-header">
                      <div class="post-header-line-1">
                      </div>
                     </div>
                     <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-7409352736607102747" itemprop="description articleBody">
                      <h2 style="text-align: center;">
                       Fanatics, the time has come!
                      </h2>
                      This is your opportunity to recognize all those talented people that have taken their time to write those stories that captured you from the start, for authors to recognize those who helped them fix their grammatical faux pas, and for anyone to shine light into the graphic artists in every fandom.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      So, put on your thinking hats and nominate now! We look forward to reading your submissions.
                      <div>
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div>
                       <div style="text-align: center;">
                        <a href="http://awards.fanaticfanfics.com/index.php/nominations" target="_blank">
                         CLICK HERE TO NOMINATE!
                        </a>
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: center;">
                        SUBMISSIONS WILL CLOSE MARCH 6th, 2016 - 12:00 PST
                       </div>
                       <div>
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       To check the categories and requirements please visit the
                       <a href="http://awards.fanaticfanfics.com/index.php/categories" target="_blank">
                        Categories
                       </a>
                       page. Please make sure the information you submit is correct, or your nomination will not be counted.
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       If you have any questions, check the
                       <a href="http://awards.fanaticfanfics.com/index.php/rules-faqs" target="_blank">
                        Rules + FAQs
                       </a>
                       page or
                       <a href="http://fanaticfanfics.com/index.php/contact-us" target="_blank">
                        contact us
                       </a>
                       .
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       When providing a story link, any fanfiction site is valid
                       <span style="font-size: x-small;">
                        (Fanfiction.net, FictionPad, Twilighted, The Writer's Coffee Shop, Archive of Our Own, etc.)
                       </span>
                       . Blogs and tumblrs are also welcome. As long as you can read the story online with the author's permission, then the link will be valid.
                      </div>
                      <div>
                       <br/>
                       Thank you to everyone that has helped with this event so far, and may the odds be ever in your favor.
                      </div>
                      <div>
                       <br/>
                       <i>
                        -Fanatic Fanfics
                       </i>
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       P.S. We'd like you to be aware that this site has moved! The Multi-Fandom Awards can now be found at
                       <a href="http://awards.fanaticfanfics.com/" target="_blank">
                        http://awards.fanaticfanfics.com
                       </a>
                       ,where all past and future nominees and award winners will be displayed.

                     <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                      <a href="http://fanaticfanficsawards.blogspot.com/2015/06/we-will-announce-winners-tomorrow.html">
                       We Will Announce Winners TOMORROW!
                      </a>
                     </h3>
                     <div class="post-header">
                      <div class="post-header-line-1">
                      </div>
                     </div>
                     <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-2825740669522995362" itemprop="description articleBody">
                      Hey Fanatics,
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      We want to inform you all that the winners will be announced tomorrow
                      <span style="color: orange;">
                       <b>
                        June 26th, 2015
                       </b>
                      </span>
                      at
                      <span style="color: orange;">
                       <b>
                        3pm GMT-5.
                       </b>
                      </span>
                      We will be posting live from our
                      <a href="https://www.facebook.com/fanficfanatics" target="_blank">
                       <b>
                        Facebook
                       </b>
                      </a>
                      and it will immediately repost on
                      <a href="https://twitter.com/FanaticFanfics" target="_blank">
                       <b>
                        Twitter
                       </b>
                      </a>
                      . Follow us on either to participate on the reveal. Afterwards, the winners and their banners will be posted here on the
                      <a href="http://fanaticfanficsawards.blogspot.com/" target="_blank">
                       <b>
                        Blog
                       </b>
                      </a>
                      .
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      It's been a very busy week for our banner maker. She's been up almost 26 hours straight and we encouraged her to take a break. It's not easy to make 123 banners in a couple of days, so I hope you guys understand why we are taking an extra day to make sure everything is done correctly.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      Thank you for understanding and may the odds be ever in your favor.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>

              </span>
              <b>
               <a href="https://www.facebook.com/fanficfanatics">
                Fanatic Fanfics
               </a>
              </b>
              promotes and the readers love. Authors, betas and banner makers play an important part in those stories, and these awards are about thanking and honoring them for making the fandom you follow so special. Each year we will ask for new fandoms to add to our list, as well as new categories you want to see. Stay tuned and remember to participate in all the polls, help us spread the word among your fandom, and get those thinking caps on to nominate anyone and everyone you'd like per the category's
              <a href="http://fanaticfanficsawards.blogspot.com/p/categories.html">
               guidelines
              </a>
