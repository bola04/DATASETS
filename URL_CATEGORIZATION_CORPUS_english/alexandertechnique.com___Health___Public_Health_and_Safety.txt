       <p>
        <br/>
        The
        <b>
         Alexander Technique
        </b>
        is a way to feel better, and move in a more relaxed and comfortable way... the way nature intended.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        An
        <b>
         Alexander Technique
        </b>
        teacher helps you to identify and lose the harmful habits you have built up over a lifetime of stress and learn to move more freely.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        The
        <b>
         Alexander Technique
        </b>
        is for you if you are ready to feel more comfortable in your own body.
       </p>
       <p>
        The
        <b>
         Alexander Technique
        </b>
        can also help you if:
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         You suffer from repetitive strain injury or carpal tunnel syndrome.
        </li>
        <li>
         You have a backache or stiff neck and shoulders.
        </li>
        <li>
         You become uncomfortable when sitting at your computer for long periods of time.
        </li>
        <li>
         You are a singer, musician, actor, dancer or athlete and feel you are not performing at your full potential.
        </li>
       </ul>
