       GEARED PROSHARES HAVE DAILY PERFORMANCE OBJECTIVES
      </span>
      <br/>
      Geared (leveraged or short) ProShares ETFs seek returns that are a multiple of (e.g.,  2x or -2x) the return of an index or other benchmark (target)
      <strong>
       <em>
        for a single day
       </em>
      </strong>
      , as measured from one NAV calculation to the next. Due to the compounding of daily returns, ProShares' returns over periods other than one day will likely differ in amount and possibly direction from the target return for the same period. Investors should monitor their holdings consistent with their strategies, as frequently as daily. For more on correlation, leverage and other risks, please read the
      <a href="/resources/prospectus_reports.html">
       prospectus
      </a>
