           About NFOA
          </h2>
          <p>
           The Voice of Nebraska Firearms Owners
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="row">
        <!-- About Text Start -->
        <div class="col-lg-7 ">
         <div class="about-text">
          The Nebraska Firearms Owners Association is the “Voice of Nebraska Firearms Owners” leading the efforts to preserve your natural rights as guaranteed in Article 1 of our State Constitution and the Second Amendment to the U.S. Constitution.
          <p class="color-text">
           We stand for firearms freedom and work through educational campaigns, lobbying, and special events to educate our elected representatives and fellow citizens to ensure legislation that recognizes the law-abiding citizen’s right to keep and bear arms.
          </p>
          <p class="pad-bottom-sm">
           Members of NFOA are assured their voices are heard in the Unicameral and that their elected officials will be held accountable. Members also benefit from being a part of a 10,000+ community of firearms owners in Nebraska, an excellent network of resources, information, training and education. Basic membership is free as we believe that every firearm owner should be afforded the same opportunities no matter their financial means. We’ve worked to secure additional benefits for our other levels of membership as those funds support the work of this all volunteer, all local organization.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a class="footer-btn margin-top-sm" href="https://www.nebraskafirearms.org/wp-login.php?action=register">
