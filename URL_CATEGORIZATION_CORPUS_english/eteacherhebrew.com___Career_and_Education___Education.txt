                  <h2>
                   eTeacher Hebrew Program Overview
                  </h2>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="views-field-body">
                 <div class="field-content">
                  <p>
                  </p>
                  <div id="cke_pastebin">
                   Hebrew is a language spoken by over 7 million people around the world, the majority of which are in Israel. It is one of the oldest languages in existence, that had almost become extinct until its revival in the early 20th century as a precursor to the modern State of Israel.
                  </div>
                  <div id="cke_pastebin">
                  </div>
                  <div id="cke_pastebin">
                   At eTeacher, we enable you to learn Modern Hebrew – the everyday language of millions of Israelis as well as numerous  Jewish people across the globe. eTeacher offers courses for adults, and special classes for children ages 7-17.
                  </div>
                  <div id="cke_pastebin">
                  </div>
                  <div id="cke_pastebin">
                   eTeacher’s approach is teaching
                   <strong>
                    Hebrew in Hebrew
                   </strong>
                   . Instruction is given in Hebrew only, regardless of the class's level or students' background. The Hebrew in Hebrew approach has been proven to be
                   <a href="http://eteacherhebrew.com/how-we-teach" target="_blank">
                    the most effective method for teaching this language
                   </a>
                   . eTeacher is proud to bring you
                   <a href="http://eteacherhebrew.com/faculty">
