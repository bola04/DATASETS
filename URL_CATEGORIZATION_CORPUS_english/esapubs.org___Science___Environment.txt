       The Mission  of the Publications of the
  Ecological Society of America is to:
      </p>
      <p>
       <br/>
       ? Publish and make broadly available the most significant results of
 ecological  research, particularly those that increase the understanding
  and applications  of general ecological concepts;
       <br/>
       <br/>
       ? Publish research results that employ theoretical, modeling,
 analytical,  experimental, observational, synthetic, historical, and
 descriptive approaches,  including those of allied natural and social
 sciences;
       <br/>
       <br/>
       ? Publish articles that are accurate, clear, and concise, while
 providing  supplementary information in accessible, digital formats;
       <br/>
       <br/>
       ? Publish a suite of journals, in suitable forms, that address the
 interests of  all appropriate readers, including scientists, students,
 educators, resource  managers, and other users of ecological
 knowledge.
