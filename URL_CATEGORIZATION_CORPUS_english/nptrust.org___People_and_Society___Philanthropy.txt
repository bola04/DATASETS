         <h1>
          We make it easier to achieve your philanthropic goals.
         </h1>
         <p>
          We offer uniquely flexible donor-advised funds that will help you:
         </p>
         <p>
          • Contribute the widest range of assets
          <br/>
          • Make international and specialized grants
          <br/>
          • Choose from a wide range of investment options
          <br/>
          ​• Establish a lasting giving legacy
         </p>
         <p>
          <!-- Removed casestudy-links class from below div -->
         </p>
         <p class="intro">
          With NPT as your partner, you can spend less time dealing with the complicated, administrative side of giving and more time focusing on what you want to achieve.
         </p>

     <div class="ov-caseStudy">
      <div class="caseStudy-slider">
       <img alt="case study image" src="https://www.nptrust.org/images/uploads/Williamson-School.jpg" width="1000"/>
       <img alt="case study image" src="https://www.nptrust.org/images/uploads/Illiquid-Assets-Rowan-1.jpg" width="1000"/>
       <img alt="case study image" src="https://www.nptrust.org/images/uploads/Illiquid-Assets-Rowan-3.jpg" width="1000"/>
       <img alt="case study image" src="https://www.nptrust.org/images/uploads/Illiquid-Assets-Rowan-4.jpg" width="1000"/>
      </div>
      <ul class="caseStudy-Captions">
       <li>
        <p>
         A philanthropist wanted to donate 88 acres of private property to benefit the Williamson Free School of Mechanical Trades (pictured).
        </p>
       </li>
       <li>
        <p>
         The donor also wanted to protect his donated land from future real estate development.
        </p>
       </li>
       <li>
        <p>
         He gifted it into an NPT donor-advised fund account and we sold the property to a municipality that preserved it for public use.
        </p>
       </li>
       <li>
        <p>
         NPT worked with the Williamson School to develop a matching grant program agreement that would help it attract new donors.
        </p>
       </li>
      </ul>

     <div class="ov-caseStudy">
      <div class="caseStudy-slider">
       <img alt="case study image" src="https://www.nptrust.org/images/uploads/International-Grants-1.jpg" width="1000"/>
       <img alt="case study image" src="https://www.nptrust.org/images/uploads/DAF-Flowchart.jpg" width="1000"/>
       <img alt="case study image" src="https://www.nptrust.org/images/uploads/International-Grants-3.jpg" width="1000"/>
       <img alt="case study image" src="https://www.nptrust.org/images/uploads/International-Grants-4.jpg" width="1000"/>
      </div>
      <ul class="caseStudy-Captions">
       <li>
        <p>
         A global hotel corporation wanted to reward their best facilities by allowing them to make grants to their local communities.
        </p>
       </li>
       <li>
        <p>
         Instead of creating a corporate foundation, they established a donor-advised fund at NPT.
        </p>
       </li>
       <li>
        <p>
         From this donor-advised fund, hotels recommended grants that provided disaster relief and fulfill a variety of local needs.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: rgb(173, 216, 230);">
          <span style="font-family: times new roman,times,serif;">
           <em>
            Photo: Haiti Earthquake Relief  | Miguel Samper for Mercy Corps
           </em>
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
       </li>
       <li>
        <p>
         NPT handled all of the management, due diligence, compliance and reporting that international grantmaking requires.
        </p>
       </li>
      </ul>

     <div class="ov-caseStudy">
      <div class="caseStudy-slider">
       <img alt="case study image" src="https://www.nptrust.org/images/uploads/Investment-Options-1a.jpg" width="1000"/>
       <img alt="case study image" src="https://www.nptrust.org/images/uploads/Investment-Options-2.jpg" width="1000"/>
       <img alt="case study image" src="https://www.nptrust.org/images/uploads/Investment-Options-3.jpg" width="1000"/>
       <img alt="case study image" src="https://www.nptrust.org/images/uploads/Investment-Options-4a.jpg" width="1000"/>
      </div>
      <ul class="caseStudy-Captions">
       <li>
        <p>
         A donor wanted to consolidate over $50 million in donor-advised fund assets from three other charities.
        </p>
       </li>
       <li>
        <p>
         A large portion of the assets were in alternative investments and the donor hoped to retain his investment approach.
        </p>
       </li>
       <li>
        <p>
         After NPT's independent experts performed rigorous due diligence on the proposed assets...
        </p>
       </li>
       <li>
        <p>
         ...we accepted the investments and created a single donor-advised fund account.
        </p>
       </li>
      </ul>

     <div class="ov-caseStudy">
      <div class="caseStudy-slider">
       <img alt="case study image" src="https://www.nptrust.org/images/uploads/Giving-Legacies-1.png" width="1000"/>
       <img alt="case study image" src="https://www.nptrust.org/images/uploads/Giving-Legacies-2.png" width="1000"/>
       <img alt="case study image" src="https://www.nptrust.org/images/uploads/Giving-Legacies-3.png" width="1000"/>
       <img alt="case study image" src="https://www.nptrust.org/images/uploads/Giving-Legacies-4a.jpg" width="1000"/>
      </div>
      <ul class="caseStudy-Captions">
       <li>
        <p>
         Upon his death, a donor wanted to gift the bulk of his assets to a donor-advised fund that allowed his wife and children to be advisors.
        </p>
       </li>
       <li>
        <p>
         The donor was worried that his family's diverse interests and locations would make shared advising of the fund too complicated.
        </p>
       </li>
       <li>
        <p>
         To alleviate his concerns, we worked with the family's lawyer and helped the donor customize an advisor succession plan.
        </p>
       </li>
       <li>
        <p>
         In the final plan, family members serve one-year rotations as the fund's advisor and may grant out an equal percentage of the assets.
        </p>
       </li>
      </ul>
