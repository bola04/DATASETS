          <h1>
           <strong>
            Restricted Countries &amp; Jurisdictions:
           </strong>
          </h1>
          <p>
           Due to laws &amp; regulation within certain countries, Sportsbet does not allow the residents of or persons located in certain countries to open or operate an account to otherwise place bets, or transact with Sportsbet.
          </p>
          <p>
           If you are staying or holidaying in any of the Restricted Jurisdictions below, you will not be able to access your Sportsbet account whilst you're there.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Alternative options within the Paddy Power Betfair Group:
           </strong>
           <br/>
           United States -
           <a href="http://tvg.com/">
            TVG.com
           </a>
           (State dependent) or
           <a href="http://betfair.com/">
            Betfair.com
           </a>
           (NJ Only)
           <br/>
           United Kingdom -
           <a href="http://paddypower.com/">
            Paddypower.com
           </a>
           or
           <a href="http://betfair.com/">
            Betfair.com
           </a>
           <br/>
           Bulgaria -
           <a href="http://betfair.com/">
            Betfair.com
           </a>
           <br/>
           Denmark -
           <a href="http://betfair.com/">
            Betfair.com
           </a>
           <br/>
           Romania -
           <a href="http://betfair.ro/">
            Betfair.ro
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           If you are staying or holidaying in any of the Restricted Jurisdictions below, you will not be able to access your Sportsbet account whilst you're there.
