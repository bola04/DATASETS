             <div class="textwidget">
              <h2>
               Japanese Hair Straightening
               <br/>
               (Thermal Reconditioning)
              </h2>
              <div style="background-image: url('http://www.leresh.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/icon1.png'); height: 150px; width: 150px; padding: 5px; margin: 5px; float: right;">
              </div>
              <p>
               <strong>
                Japanese Hair Straightening
               </strong>
               or
               <strong>
                Thermal Reconditioning
               </strong>
               is a method of permanently straightening and defrizzing hair types that include: overly curly, wavy, frizzy, kinky, colored, highlighted and bleached hair.
              </p>
              <p>
               The process simultaneously straightens and repairs hair by realigning the cystine bonds, and improves the hair structure by sealing in the highest quality proteins/emollients through the heat and compression of a flat ironing process.
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>
              <div class="su-button-center">
               <a class="su-button su-button-style-default su-button-wide" href="http://www.leresh.com/?services=japanese-hair-straighteningthermal-reconditioning-trc" style="color:#FFFFFF;background-color:#aa5f27;border-color:#884c1f;border-radius:0px;-moz-border-radius:0px;-webkit-border-radius:0px" target="_self">
                <span style="color:#FFFFFF;padding:6px 16px;font-size:13px;line-height:20px;border-color:#c48f68;border-radius:0px;-moz-border-radius:0px;-webkit-border-radius:0px;text-shadow:1px 1px 1px #000000;-moz-text-shadow:1px 1px 1px #000000;-webkit-text-shadow:1px 1px 1px #000000">
                 <i class="fa fa-star" style="font-size:13px;color:#FFFFFF">
                 </i>
                 Thermal Reconditioning
                </span>
               </a>
              </div>
              <h2>
               Lisco &amp; Yuko
              </h2>
              <p>
               The leading producers of
               <strong>
                Japanese Hair Straightening
               </strong>
               or
               <strong>
                Thermal Reconditioning
               </strong>
               systems include
               <strong>
                Liscio
               </strong>
               and
               <strong>
                Yuko
               </strong>
               systems.
              </p>
              <p>
               I have been educated in both systems, as of June 2017 I have successfully completed over 2800 hair straightening procedures and 2,300
               <strong>
                Brazilian Blowout
               </strong>
               procedures in the greater Portland, Oregon area. The quality and results of both systems never cease to amaze me.
               <strong>
                Japanese Hair Straightening
               </strong>
               or
               <strong>
                Thermal Reconditioning
               </strong>
               literally transforms the lives of clients.
              </p>
              <p>
               The
               <strong>
                Liscio
               </strong>
               system, which I use the most, was introduced, manufactured and received approval by the Japanese government in 1996, for its unique thermal reconditioning formula. In 1998, it was patented in the United States and since then, has developed the reputation of being the leading
               <strong>
                Japanese Hair Straightening
               </strong>
               or
               <strong>
                Thermal Reconditioning
               </strong>
               system.
              </p>
              <p>
               In contrast,
               <strong>
                Yuko
               </strong>
               system was developed in Japan and patented in the United States in 1997. I find the
               <strong>
                Yuko
               </strong>
               system more effective on coarse, defiant hair.
              </p>
             </div>
            </div>
           </div>

             <div class="textwidget">
              <p>
               <img alt="woman1" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-100" src="http://www.leresh.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/woman1.jpg" style="max-width:960px; width:98%; box-shadow:1px 1px 10px 1px rgba(0,0,0,0.5); border: 1px dotted #aa5f27; margin-top:20px;"/>
              </p>
              <h2>
               Brazilian Keratin Treatment (BKT)
              </h2>
              <p>
               Categorized as a temporary smoothing treatment and conditioning treatment, was originally developed in Brazil during the 1990's. Since then, the process has reached perfection with the
               <strong>
                Brazilian Blowout
               </strong>
               (
               <strong>
                BBO
               </strong>
               ) formulation that has sent Hollywood spinning.
              </p>
              <div style="background-image: url('http://www.leresh.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/icon4.png'); height: 150px; width: 150px; padding: 5px; margin: 5px; float: right;">
              </div>
              <p>
               The
               <strong>
                Brazilian Blowout
               </strong>
               version of
               <strong>
                Brazilian Keratin Treatment
               </strong>
               is universal, suitable for all types of chemically treated hair, including: bleached, highlighted, colored, relaxed, permed and previously straightened hair. The
               <strong>
                BBO
               </strong>
               calms, revitalizes and resurrects curly, frizzy, damaged, dull, lifeless and mistreated hair.
              </p>
              <p>
               Unlike competing
               <strong>
                Brazilian Keratin Treatments
               </strong>
               ,
               <strong>
                Brazilian Blowout
               </strong>
               has become the industry's leader and smoothing treatment of choice.
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>
