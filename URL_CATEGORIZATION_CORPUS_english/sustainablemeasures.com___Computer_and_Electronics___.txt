                    <div class="enhanced">
                     <b>
                      Sustainable Measures
                     </b>
                     ,was founded and lead by Maureen Hart from 1993 until 2014 when she became the Executive Director of the
                     <a href="http://www.sustainabilityprofessionals.org" target="_blank">
                      International Society of Sustainability Professionals (ISSP)
                     </a>
                     .
                     <img height="200" src="sites/default/images/New England Farm.jpg" style="float:right; border:2px solid black; margin:8px" width="200"/>
                     Although she is no longer available for private consulting, she can be reached at ISSP where she works with the ISSP staff and members in their quest to make sustainability standard practice. Her passion for sustainability indicators burns stronger than ever. She can direct you to additional resources, both on this website and through the ISSP network if you are looking for any of the following services.  She encourages you to join
                     <a href="https://www.sustainabilityprofessionals.org/civicrm/contribute/transact?reset=1&amp;id=1" target="_blank">
                      ISSP
                     </a>
                     and become involved in a global network of sustainability practitioners working to make sustainability standard practice.
                    </div>
                    <div class="homepage">
                     <ul>
                      <li>
                       <b>
                        Training
                       </b>
                       – Both introductory workshops and more advanced training in such topics as general sustainability issues, indicators and performance measurement and community and organizational processes for furthering sustainability.
                      </li>
                      <li>
                       <b>
                        Sustainability Assessment
                       </b>
                       -  Sustainability Competency &amp; Opportunity Rating &amp; Evaluation (SCORE)   Determine where your organization is on the road to sustainability and identify new opportunities and assess your organizational practices.
                      </li>
                      <li>
                       <b>
                        Community, Organizational and System Development
                       </b>
                       – Clarify and develop goals based upon sustainability principles, review existing measurement and feedback systems, create a set of indicators appropriate to needs and resources and develop a process to encourage on-going use.
                      </li>
                      <li>
                       <b>
                        Evaluation
                       </b>
                       – Work to analyze indicators and processes to ensure they reflect and support principles of sustainability, move the community or organization toward a more sustainable state and can be consistently used regardless of change and development.
                      </li>
                      <li>
                       <b>
                        Data Analysis, Reporting and Editing
