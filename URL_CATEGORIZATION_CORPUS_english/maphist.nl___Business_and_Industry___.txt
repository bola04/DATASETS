   <h1>
    MapHist
   </h1>
   <h3>
    Forum on the history of cartography
   </h3>
   <hr/>
   MapHist, the Map History Forum, is a forum whose primary focus
 is historical maps, atlases, globes and other cartographic documents. The
 list is open to all persons interested in the history of cartography
 and discussion is encouraged on all aspects of this broad subject.
   <p>
    The primary purpose of MapHist is to encourage individuals to communicate
 current research; evaluate methods and tools of analysis; announce important
 acquisitions and news; announce position vacancies; announce new publications;
 investigate library holdings; and to share information between conferences
 and the appearance of relevant journals.
   </p>
