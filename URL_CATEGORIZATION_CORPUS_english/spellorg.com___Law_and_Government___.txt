         <h1>
          Check Out a Spell Checker
         </h1>
         <p>
          If learning to spell was not your strong suit in school, it is tempting to use popular custom writing services such as
             thepensters.com or
          <a href=" http://www.customwritings.com/">
           CustomWritings
          </a>
          . Or check your spelling of English words by reading about English spelling challenges.
             But the Internet technology can take care of this in another way.
             Whether	constructing a newsletter for your students as a teacher, writing a	research paper or custom essay,
             developing an advert in an advertising position or typing a memo while employed as a business professional,
             spell check is a commonly used tool to prevent misspellings,
             catch typing errors and allow a written piece to appear professional and scholarly.
         </p>
         <p>
          An online spell checker and grammar checker requires the user
             to copy and paste an authored work from any word-processing program into an Internet text field or box.
             After pressing the spell check button, the results are either displayed on a
             new page or in a pop-up window. The program offers advice and options to alter the misspelled word, inserting it
             into the text.
         </p>
         <p>
          Avoid writer's regret and looking like you need a refresher course in learning to spell by using an online spell
             checker. Not only are the programs easy to use but save valuable time.
         </p>
