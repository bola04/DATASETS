                <h3>
                 <a href="/book/9780525520375">
                  <img align="right" alt="There There: A Novel by Tommy Orange" data-entity-type="file" data-entity-uuid="eabcb517-ee7a-4246-a38d-c33bba2c4823" src="/sites/default/files/inline-images/June%202018-featured-book-ib-322x385.jpg"/>

                <p>
                 <b>
                  <a href="/indie-next-list" target="_blank">

                  <br/>
                  <i>
                   <b>
                    View from a bookseller
                   </b>
                  </i>
                 </b>
                </p>
                <div>
                 “
                 <em>
                  There There
                 </em>
                 is the kind of book that grabs you from the start and doesn’t let go, even after you’ve turned the last page. It is a work of fiction, but every word of it feels true. Tommy Orange writes with a palpable anger and pain, telling the history of a cultural trauma handed down through generations in the blood and bones and stories of individual lives. He also writes with incredible heart and humor, infusing his characters with a tangible humanity and moments of joy even as they are headed toward tragedy. There There has claimed a permanent spot in my heart despite having broken it, or maybe because it did. I think this may be the best book I’ve ever read.”
                 <p>
                  —
                  <b>
                   Heather Weldon,
                   <a href="https://www.changinghands.com/">
                    Changing Hands Bookstore
                   </a>
                   , Tempe, AZ
                  </b>
                 </p>

        <section class="block block-block-content block-block-contentf562213b-47ef-4a31-b2b7-46bbba5bc9a2 clearfix" id="block-whyshopindie">
         <div class="field field--name-body field--type-text-with-summary field--label-hidden field--item">
          <hr/>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            Why shop Indie?
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <p>
           When you shop at an independently owned business, your entire community benefits:
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            The Economy
           </strong>
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Spend $100 at a local-owned business and $52 of that stays in your community.
           </li>
           <li>
            Spend $50 at a national chain and keep $6.50 in the local community.
           </li>
           <li>
            Spend $50 online with a remote vendor with no sales tax collected and keep not one penny in your local community.
           </li>
           <li>
            Local businesses create higher-paying jobs for our neighbors.
           </li>
           <li>
            More of your taxes are reinvested in your community--where they belong.
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
           <strong>
            The Environment
           </strong>
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Buying local means less packaging, less transportation, and a smaller carbon footprint.
           </li>
           <li>
            Shopping in a local business district means less infrastructure, less maintenance, and more money to beautify your community.
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
           <strong>
            The Community
           </strong>
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Local retailers are your friends and neighbors—support them and they’ll support you.
           </li>
           <li>
            Local businesses donate to charities at more than twice the rate of national chains.
           </li>
           <li>
            More independents means more choice, more diversity, and a truly unique community.
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
           Now is the time to stand up and join your fellow individuals in the IndieBound mission supporting local businesses and celebrating independents.
          </p>
