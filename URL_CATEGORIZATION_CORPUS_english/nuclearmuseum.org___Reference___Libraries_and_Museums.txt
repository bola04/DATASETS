      <h1>
       Welcome to the National Museum of Nuclear Science &amp; History
      </h1>
      <div class="copy">
       <p>
        The nation’s only congressionally chartered museum in its field. An intriguing place to learn the story of the Atomic Age.
        <br/>
        Exhibits spanning the early research of nuclear development through today’s peaceful uses of nuclear technology.
       </p>

      <div class="tout">
       <a href="http://www.nuclearmuseum.org/learn/kids-camp/science-is-everywhere-summer-camp-2018/">
        <img alt="" src="/assets/uploads/general/cache/Camp_Footer2-300x190.jpg"/>
       </a>
       <h2>
        Summer Camp - May 29 - August 10
       </h2>
       <p>
        The National Museum of Nuclear Science &amp; History will host "Science is Everywhere" Summer Camp 2018 for children who are 6 to 12 years old. Experience the wonders of science in week-long sessions, May 29 - August 10. Sessions include "Code of the Robot," "Chemistry and Intrigue," "Science of the X-scape," "STEMinists," "Spy Kids" and many more!
       </p>
       <a class="more" href="http://www.nuclearmuseum.org/learn/kids-camp/science-is-everywhere-summer-camp-2018/">
        Learn More
       </a>
      </div>

     <div class="grid_4">
      <div class="tout">
       <a href="http://www.nuclearmuseum.org/visit/events/june-22-100.3-the-peak-free-summer-fun-road-show/">
        <img alt="" src="/assets/uploads/general/cache/Peak_FREE_2018_footer1-300x190.jpg"/>
       </a>
       <h2>
        100.3 The PEAK FREE Summer Fun Road Show
       </h2>
       <p>
        100.3 The PEAK, an iHeartRadio station, will host its 2018 Free Summer Fun Road Show at the National Museum of Nuclear Science &amp; History on Friday, June 22, from 6 am to 10 am. All members of the community are welcome to visit the museum for FREE from 6 am to 10 am on the morning of Friday, June 22, as Albuquerque's own Jackie, Tony and Donnie host their popular morning show inside the museum. Doors will open at 6 am.
       </p>
       <a class="more" href="http://www.nuclearmuseum.org/visit/events/june-22-100.3-the-peak-free-summer-fun-road-show/">
        Learn More
       </a>
      </div>

     <div class="grid_4">
      <div class="tout">
       <a href="http://www.nuclearmuseum.org/support/operation-preservation-the-campaign-to-restore-the-planes/b-47e-stratojet-restoration/">
        <img alt="" src="/assets/uploads/general/cache/B47_footer-300x190.jpg"/>
       </a>
       <h2>
        B-47E Stratojet Restoration
       </h2>
       <p>
        Help us restore New Mexico’s only Boeing B-47E Stratojet, serial number 53-2280, one of only 23 surviving B-47 airplanes in existence! This airplane will be the focus of a special initiative within “Operation Preservation” – a multi-year campaign to repaint and refurbish the iconic aircraft in the National Museum of Nuclear Science &amp; History’s nine-acre outdoor exhibit area, Heritage Park.
       </p>
       <a class="more" href="http://www.nuclearmuseum.org/support/operation-preservation-the-campaign-to-restore-the-planes/b-47e-stratojet-restoration/">
