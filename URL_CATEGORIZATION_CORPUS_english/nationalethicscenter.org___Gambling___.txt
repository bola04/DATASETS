               Invitation to take a survey to help research into ethical decision-making
 The Project Management Institute (PMI) is partnering with my colleague, Alan Richter, President of QED Consulting to conduct a survey to explore global responses to ethical issues and dilemmas, and examine the influence of culture (as well as gender, age, education, profession, etc.) on ethical decision-making.
