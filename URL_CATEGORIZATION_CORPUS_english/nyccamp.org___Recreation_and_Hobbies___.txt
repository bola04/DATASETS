             <h2 class="views-field views-field-title node-title hidden">
              <span class="field-content">
               <a href="/articles/drupal-nyc-camp-2017" hreflang="en">
                Drupal NYC Camp, 2017
               </a>
              </span>
             </h2>
             <div class="views-field views-field-body">
              <div class="field-content">
               <br/>
               <p>
                Drupal NYC Camp was hosted on
                <strong>
                 Sat Nov 18 2017
                </strong>
                as part of
                <a href="http://www.OpenCamps.org" target="_blank">
                 Open Camps
                </a>
                .
               </p>
               <h2 class="block-title">
                Recap of the Presentations
               </h2>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 <a href="/sessions/anatomy-page-request">
                  Anatomy of a Page Request
                 </a>
                </strong>
                <br/>
                Speaker: Charles Novick
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 <a href="/sessions/drupal-and-ethereum-blockchain">
                  Drupal and the Ethereum Blockchain
                 </a>
                </strong>
                <br/>
                Speaker: Thorsten Krug
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 <a href="/sessions/life-edge-cdn-and-https-delivery-2017">
                  Life on the Edge: CDN and HTTPS Delivery in 2017
                 </a>
                </strong>
                <br/>
                Speaker: David Strauss
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 <a href="/sessions/drupal-and-21st-century-workforce-development">
                  Drupal and 21st Century Workforce Development
                 </a>
                </strong>
                <br/>
                Speaker: Doug Hardwick
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 <a href="/sessions/webform-drupal-8-training">
                  Webform for Drupal 8 Training
                 </a>
                </strong>
                <br/>
                Speaker: Jacob Rockowitz
               </p>
               <h2 class="block-title">
                Drupal NYC Camp 2018 Planning
               </h2>
               <p>
                We're aiming to host Drupal NYC Camp in 2018 as part of Open Camps again. Our planned dates are
                <strong>
                 Nov. 16-19, 2018
                </strong>
                . Let's collaborate and plan for NYC Camp 2018 and beyond. We have a template plan we can use as a starting point. However, we'd ideally like to set some objectives and priorities for 2018; in particular audience size, presenters for a full-day single-track event, and related logistics.
               </p>
               <p>
                NYC Camp is
                <strong>
                 100% free
                </strong>
                for attendees, run
                <strong>
                 solely by volunteers
                </strong>
                and is a not-for-profit event.  Our conference is only made possible by the generosity of our partners and the commitment, vision and energy of our volunteers.  We would love your help before and during the camp! If you are interested in volunteering, please reach out to
                <a href="mailto:info@nyccamp.org">
                 info@nyccamp.org
                </a>
                .
               </p>
