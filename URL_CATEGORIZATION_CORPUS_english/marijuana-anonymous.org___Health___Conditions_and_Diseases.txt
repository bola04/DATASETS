       <div class="page-header">
        <h1>
         Preamble to
         <cite title="Life with Hope: A return to living through the 12 steps and 12 traditions of Marijuana Anonymous">
          Life with Hope
         </cite>
        </h1>
       </div>
       <p class="lead">
        <strong>
         Marijuana Anonymous
        </strong>
        is a fellowship of people who share our experience, strength, and hope with each other that we may solve our common problem and help others to recover from marijuana addiction.
       </p>
       <p class="lead">
        The only requirement for membership is a desire to stop using marijuana. There are no dues or fees for membership. We are self-supporting through our own contributions.
        <acronym title="Marijuana Anonymous">
         MA
        </acronym>
        is not affiliated with any religious or secular institution or organization and has no opinion on any outside controversies or causes. Our primary purpose is to stay free of marijuana and to help the marijuana addict who still suffers achieve the same freedom. We can do this by practicing our suggested
        <a href="/how-it-works/twelve-steps" title="The Twelve Steps of Marijuana Anonymous">
         Twelve Steps
        </a>
        of recovery and by being guided as a group by our
        <a href="/how-it-works/the-twelve-traditions" title="The Twelve Traditions of Marijuana Anonymous">
         Twelve Traditions
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p class="lead">
        <strong>
         Marijuana Anonymous
        </strong>
        uses the basic 12 Steps of Recovery founded by Alcoholics Anonymous, because it has been proven that the 12 Step Recovery program works!
       </p>
       <h3>
        Who is a marijuana addict?
       </h3>
       <p class="lead">
        We who are marijuana addicts know the answer to this question. Marijuana controls our lives! We lose interest in all else; our dreams go up in smoke. Ours is a progressive illness often leading us to addictions to other drugs, including alcohol. Our lives, our thinking, and our desires center around marijuana—scoring it, dealing it, and finding ways to stay high.
       </p>
       <blockquote class="pull-right">
        <p>
         <strong>
          The only requirement for membership is a desire to stop using marijuana.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <small>
         <acronym title="Marijuana Anonymous">
          MA
         </acronym>
         <cite title="The Twelve Traditions of Marijuana Anonymous, Tradition Three">
          Tradition Three
         </cite>
        </small>
       </blockquote>
