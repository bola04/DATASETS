        <div class="home_content">
         <p class="homeh1">
          Welcome to the Gaming &amp; Leisure Association of Ireland (GLAI)
         </p>
         <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
          The Gaming and Leisure Association of Ireland is a non profit trade association established to represent the interests of its members around Ireland, both nationally and at EU level. The Association represents
          <a href="http://glai.ie/about-us/members/" title="Members">
           private members’ gaming clubs
          </a>
          and online operators who provide casino-like services to their members.
         </p>
         <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
          <strong>
           To view the latest GLAI press release,
           <a href="http://glai.ie/news/" title="News">
            click here
           </a>
           .
          </strong>
         </p>
         <hr style="padding-left: 30px;"/>
         <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
          <strong>
           Casino Regulation and Casinos in Ireland:
          </strong>
          The casino sector in Ireland is currently not subject to regulation. The GLAI is committed to securing regulation for its members, by engaging with stakeholders, both public and private, who have a part to play in bringing about the regulation of the casino &amp; gaming sector in Ireland. The GLAI also provides advice on best-practice as gleaned from other regulated jurisdictions.
         </p>
         <hr style="padding-left: 30px;"/>
         <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
          <a href="http://glai.ie/about-us/code-of-practice/" title="Code of Practice">
           <img alt="" class="alignright" height="85" src="http://glai.ie/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/GLAI-Code-of-Practice-LOGO-SMALL.jpg" title="GLAI Code of Practice" width="128"/>
          </a>
          Our philosophy is that only through a regulated environment can we assist in promoting
          <a href="http://glai.ie/responsible-gambling/" title="Responsible Gambling">
           responsible gaming
          </a>
          in Ireland, protecting the young and vulnerable and ensuring public safety. Our aims are to secure regulation for private members’ gaming clubs, promote
          <a href="http://glai.ie/about-us/code-of-practice/" title="Code of Practice">
           best practice in gaming
          </a>
          in Ireland and assist members in the transition to a regulated environment while providing an identifiable voice for the casino sector.
         </p>
         <hr style="padding-left: 30px;"/>
         <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
          <strong>
           Land-based and online gaming in Ireland:
          </strong>
          in a properly regulated environment, there is
          <a href="http://glai.ie/about-us/gambling-in-ireland/" title="Gambling in Ireland">
           significant potential for growth
          </a>
          in the gaming and gambling industry, thus creating employment and substantial increases in tax revenues.
         </p>
