       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="col-md-4 col-sm-4">
       <i class=" fa-4x">
       </i>
       <h2>
        Supportive
       </h2>
       <h4>
       </h4>
       <p>
        We only pick leaders who lead with love. We hire exceptional group leaders who are adept at engaging small groups of writers. We screen them for their expertise, their diplomacy, and their belief in positive reinforcement. We believe writers need a safe space to grow.
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="col-md-4 col-sm-4">
       <i class=" fa-4x">
       </i>
       <h2>
        Personal
       </h2>
       <h4>
       </h4>
       <p>
        Our workshops are never about lectures with hundreds of people. Instead, our workshops are always small and geared toward individual attention. We also offer one-on-one coaching for writers working on a first draft or editing for writers with a completed manuscript.
       </p>
