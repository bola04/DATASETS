          <b>
           mission
          </b>
          of the Hawaiian Ecosystems at Risk project (HEAR) is to provide technology, methods, and information to--and to facilitate communication among
          <!--added 20070328/pt@hear.org-->
          --decision-makers, resource managers, and the general public to help support effective science-based management of harmful non-native species in Hawaii
          <!--next part added 20010612/pt-->
          and the Pacific.
