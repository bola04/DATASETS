     <div class="intro">
      <h2>
       Sheffield Industrial Museums are the showcase of Sheffield’s industrial story from early industrialisation to modern times, where ‘Made in Sheffield’ remains a mark of craftsmanship and quality known worldwide.
      </h2>
      <h2>
       Discover how steelmaking forged the city of today and its impact on the modern world!
      </h2>
      <p>
       Our three unique heritage sites and museums across Sheffield reveal more about the growth of the steel city.
      </p>
      <p>
       Kelham Island Museum’s interactive galleries tell the story of light trades and skilled workmanship to mass production. Catch a unique glimpse of life at home and at work at Abbeydale Industrial Hamlet, an 18
       <sup>
        th
       </sup>
       century scythe and steelworks or discover Shepherd Wheel, a unique example of Sheffield’s Knife grinding industry.
      </p>
      <p>
       Enjoy a great family day out full of nostalgia and innovation!
      </p>
      <h2>
       Please note, due to essential replacement works, the River Don Engine will not be running week commencing Monday 2nd July.
      </h2>
      <h2>
       The lower Transport Gallery will also be closed this week.
      </h2>
