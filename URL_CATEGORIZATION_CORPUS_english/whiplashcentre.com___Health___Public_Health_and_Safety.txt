           <div class="one-half">
            <h2>
             How we can help you
            </h2>
            <p>
             The most common question asked of us here at the Melbourne Whiplash Centre is: “How can you help me?” Our simple answer to this question is, “Once we have performed our detailed two hour evaluation, we will know whether you are a suitable candidate for our program, or not”.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a class="btn-secondary left" href="http://www.whiplashcentre.com/about-us/">

             <a class="catchall" href="#footer">

            </p>

         <div class="row zebra">
          <div class="wrap">
           <div class="one-half first">
            <h2>
             Treatment of whiplash
            </h2>
            <p>
             There are many manual techniques that have evidence of assisting ease symptoms of patients suffering from chronic neck pain. We employ the majority of the techniques listed below on an as need basis with our patients. However, there is no more important or potent treatment than a graduated strengthening and conditioning program for the cervical spine. This is accomplished at our centre via our computerised technology and sophisticated equipment known as the MCU (Multi cervical Unit).
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.whiplashcentre.com/patient-information/assessment-treatment/">
              Learn more »
             </a>
            </p>
           </div>
           <div class="one-half">
            <h2>
             Whiplash and Chronic Neck Pain
            </h2>
            <p>
             Our staff are expertly trained to assist patients suffering from ongoing neck issues. 85% of our patients are experiencing their neck symptoms stemming from a motor vehicle accident. But chronic neck pain can also arise from direct trauma, assaults or simply by sitting at a desk all day.
             <strong>
              Whiplash symptoms
             </strong>
             can vary enormously. This will depend on the type of accident, the speed at impact, car type and the position of your neck and body at impact.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.whiplashcentre.com/patient-information/">
              Learn more »
             </a>
            </p>
           </div>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="row last">
          <div class="wrap">
           <div class="three-fourths first success-rate">
            <div class="content">
             <h2>
              Our success rate
             </h2>
             <p>
              All our patients experiencing chronic neck pain are screened via a 2 hour evaluation on our computerised Multi Cervical Unit. If you are deemed appropriate for our advanced neck rehabilitation program, we are achieving a 75% success rate in significantly reducing our patients symptoms.
             </p>
             <p>
              <a href="http://www.whiplashcentre.com/patient-information/" title="Patient Information">
