        <p class="home_page_paragraph">
         WELCOME to Pilates Works Studio in Weybridge, Surrey. We have been teaching Pilates at the highest level for the past 12 years and have an unrivalled reputation throughout Surrey and beyond. Our large bright and modern studios occupy space over two floors and are all fully equipped with traditional Pilates equipment as well as state of the art clinical reformers for rehabilitation.
        </p>
        <p>
         We offer group mat, reformer, barre and rehabilitation classes organised by ability so that there is the perfect class for you. We also offer 1:1 private tuition for a very personalised session. Our team of highly experienced teachers, all of whom hold Body Control Pilates® certification are waiting to welcome you and provide you with outstanding and inspirational tuition. When you come and visit you will discover our intention is simple. We want to show you how Pilates changes the way you look and feel so that you leave the studio feeling confident in your body and refreshed in your mind.
        </p>
