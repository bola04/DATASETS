           <h2>
            <font color="blue" size="-1">
             <b>
              About Mapcruzin
             </b>
            </font>
           </h2>
           <p>
            <img hspace="8" src="/xdot.gif.pagespeed.ic.jAVPhXi7CK.png" vspace="0"/>
            MapCruzin is an
 independent firm specializing in innovative Geographic Information
 System (GIS) projects, environmental and sociodemographic research, website development and hosting. In 1996 we created the first U.S. based
 interactive toxic chemical facility maps on the internet and we have been online ever since.
           </p>
           <p>
            <img hspace="8" src="/xdot.gif.pagespeed.ic.jAVPhXi7CK.png" vspace="0"/>
            Our lives and those of all living things on this earth are at a turning point. We believe that to plan for an equitable and just future, we must
            <a href="/environmental-inequality.htm">
             analyze present inequalitites through an historical lense
            </a>
            to assure that they are never repeated again.
           </p>
           <p>
            <img hspace="8" src="/xdot.gif.pagespeed.ic.jAVPhXi7CK.png" vspace="0"/>
            Our specific focus is on
            <a href="/environmental_justice.htm">
             environmental justice and equality
            </a>
            especially as it pertains to pollution, climate change, peak oil, energy, water, housing, food and technology. We look forward to working with groups as we look towards reinventing our economies, cultures and societies in an equitable and just manner.
           </p>
           <p>
            <img hspace="8" src="/xdot.gif.pagespeed.ic.jAVPhXi7CK.png" vspace="0"/>
            Our hope is that our efforts will help speed you on your journey of re-imagining our relationships with each other and the earth.
           </p>
