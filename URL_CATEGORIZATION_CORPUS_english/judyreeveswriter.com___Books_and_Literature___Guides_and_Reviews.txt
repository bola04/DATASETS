          Home
         </h1>
        </header>

        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          <img alt="Judy Reeves" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-3681" height="197" sizes="(max-width: 175px) 100vw, 175px" src="http://judyreeveswriter.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/JR-author-introa1.jpg" srcset="http://judyreeveswriter.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/JR-author-introa1.jpg 175w, http://judyreeveswriter.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/JR-author-introa1-133x150.jpg 133w" width="175"/>
          Welcome to my online home. I’m a writer, teacher, and writing practice provocateur. I blog as
          <a href="http://judyreeveswriter.com/the-lively-muse-blog/">
           <strong>
            The Lively Muse
           </strong>
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          I’m a writer so information about my
          <a href="http://judyreeveswriter.com/books-and-kits/">
           books
          </a>
          resides here too, as well as other writing, maybe even the
          <a href="http://servinghousejournal.com/ReevesThreePoems.aspx" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
           occasional link
          </a>
          to a poem or two.
         </p>
         <p>
          I love to teach and lead workshops, so you can find info about my teaching schedule and other writerly events, such as retreats, groups, readings, and presentations. Future plans include podcasts, online workshops, and downloadable DIY workshops.
         </p>
         <p>
          As for the Provocateur title—that’s what I hope I do when I engage with others who love to write, or want to write, or know they have a writer somewhere within that’s longing to get out. A provocateur is one who stirs the pot and I want to help make some literary soup.
         </p>

        <p>
         My newest book came out of the Wild Women writing workshops I led for more than a decade. Writing with women of all ages and varied life experiences revealed a commonality among us: a yearning to reclaim a true nature that resides below the surface of our daily life and to give it voice. The longing to express this wild, authentic nature informed the workshops and now this book. Both intuitive and practical, the book responds to this deep need for expression with specific and inspiring activities, exercises, and writing prompts.
