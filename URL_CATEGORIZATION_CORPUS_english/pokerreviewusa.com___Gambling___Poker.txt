        <h3>
         USA Legal and Regulated Poker Rooms
        </h3>
        <p>
         As of December 2017 there are five legal and regulated online poker rooms in the United States.  All of which are only available in Nevada, New Jersey and Delaware.  The Pokerstars and WSOP operated online poker rooms are by far the largest regulated online poker rooms in the United States.
         <br/>
         In Nevada you have
         <a href="http://www.wsop.com/">
          WSOP.com
         </a>
         , which shares traffic with Delaware's
         <a href="http://www.delawarepark.com/igaming/how-to-play/multi-hand-poker/multi-hand.php">
          DelawarePark.com
         </a>
         .  This is the largest regulated room in the U.S.  There is also a poker room called Real Gaming, but traffic is virtually non existant.
         <br/>
         In New Jersey you have
         <a href="http://www.wsop.com">
          WSOP.com
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://www.pokerstarsnj.com/">
          Pokerstars NJ
         </a>
         and
         <br/>
         <a href="http://nj.partypoker.com/">
          NJ Partypoker
         </a>
         competing for the online poker market in New Jersey.  All three poker rooms are growing around the same pace, with Pokerstars and WSOP having significantly more players than Partypoker.
