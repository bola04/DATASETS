    Plant Of The Month
   </h2>

     <div class="col-sm-5">
      <a href="https://www.houseplantsexpert.com/flowering-maple.html">
       <img alt="Light purple flowers" class="img-responsive center-block" src="assets/images/abutilon_flower_close_up.jpg"/>
      </a>
     </div>
     <div class="col-sm-6 col-sm-offset-1">
      <hr class="hr-divider-ghost"/>
      <h3 class="text-theme title-sm">
       Flowering Maple - Abutilon x hybridum
      </h3>
      <p class="text-theme">
       The flowering maple is an excellent plant for growing around this time of the year. It adapts well to temperatures during summer indoors and outdoors.
      </p>
      <p class="text-theme">
       During the summer the Abutilon will need plenty of bright light and watering to thrive and flower.
      </p>
      <ul class="text-theme list-unstyled list-md">
       <li>
        <i class="fa fa-check colored">
        </i>
        Attractive bell shaped flowers bloom.
       </li>
       <li>
        <i class="fa fa-check colored">
        </i>
        Cuttings are easily propagated.
       </li>
       <li>
        <i class="fa fa-check colored">
        </i>
        Attention should be given in regards to plenty of light throughout the day, frequent watering, and autumn - winter care.
       </li>
      </ul>

      Benefits For The Indoor Grower
     </h2>
     <div class="row">

        <li>
         <b>
          Look and feel:
         </b>

        </li>
        <li>
         <b>
          Air quality:
         </b>
         A major benefit is how much a house plant can improve air quality. Some improve the quality more than others and a study was produced by NASA to discover plants that remove pollutants.
        </li>
        <li>
         <b>
          Your well being:
         </b>
         This is not an obvious one. Taking care of anything living, such as people or animals provides that person with a sense of spiritual and emotional well being and, I believe this applies to plants.
        </li>
       </ul>
