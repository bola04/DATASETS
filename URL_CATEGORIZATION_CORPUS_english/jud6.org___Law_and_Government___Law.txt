       <h1>
        Sixth Judicial Circuit of Florida
       </h1>
       <p>
        In Florida, trial courts of general jurisdiction are aligned in 20  circuits. The
        <a href="GeneralPublic/AboutTheCircuit.html">
         Sixth  Judicial Circuit
        </a>
        serves citizens of
        <a href="http://www.pascocountyfl.net/" target="_blank">
         Pasco
        </a>
        and
        <a href="http://www.pinellascounty.org/" target="_blank">
         Pinellas
        </a>
        counties. The circuit is comprised of two county courts, which have  jurisdiction only in their home county, and a circuit court, with jurisdiction  in both counties. All cases filed in these two counties are assigned to one of  nine
        <a href="/GeneralPublic/CourtDivisions.html">
         different  court divisions
        </a>
        .
       </p>
