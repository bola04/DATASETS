        <h1 class="page-title">
         Welcome
        </h1>
       </header>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <a class="thickbox no_icon" data-rel="gallery-1252" href="https://i0.wp.com/www.rawaimuaythai.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/rawaimuaythai_training.jpg" title="">
          <img alt="group shot" class="aligncenter wp-image-1527 size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" sizes="(max-width: 950px) 100vw, 950px" src="https://i0.wp.com/www.rawaimuaythai.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/rawaimuaythai_training.jpg?resize=950%2C549" srcset="https://i0.wp.com/www.rawaimuaythai.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/rawaimuaythai_training.jpg?w=992 992w, https://i0.wp.com/www.rawaimuaythai.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/rawaimuaythai_training.jpg?resize=300%2C173 300w, https://i0.wp.com/www.rawaimuaythai.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/rawaimuaythai_training.jpg?resize=768%2C444 768w, https://i0.wp.com/www.rawaimuaythai.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/rawaimuaythai_training.jpg?resize=500%2C289 500w"/>
         </a>
         <a class="thickbox no_icon" data-rel="gallery-1252" href="https://i2.wp.com/www.rawaimuaythai.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/summer-special.jpg" title="">
          <img alt="training discount" class="wp-image-2093 size-full aligncenter" data-recalc-dims="1" sizes="(max-width: 915px) 100vw, 915px" src="https://i2.wp.com/www.rawaimuaythai.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/summer-special.jpg?resize=915%2C394" srcset="https://i2.wp.com/www.rawaimuaythai.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/summer-special.jpg?w=915 915w, https://i2.wp.com/www.rawaimuaythai.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/summer-special.jpg?resize=300%2C129 300w, https://i2.wp.com/www.rawaimuaythai.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/summer-special.jpg?resize=768%2C331 768w, https://i2.wp.com/www.rawaimuaythai.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/summer-special.jpg?resize=500%2C215 500w"/>
         </a>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Welcome to Rawai Muay Thai
        </strong>
        , one of the best training camps in Thailand. At Rawai Muay Thai we are committed to offering our guests the first rate Muay Thai training that has made us well-known around the world. Our Muay Thai trainers are the best at what they do, patient, friendly and gifted.
       </p>
       <p>
        We pride ourselves on teaching the proper Muay Thai techniques. We are a traditional Muay Thai gym and we teach all aspects of Thai Boxing, including
        <a href="http://www.rawaimuaythai.com/training/muay-boran/">
         Muay Boran
        </a>
        and the Wai Kru ceremony, which is performed before every Muay Thai fight.
       </p>
       <p>
        Many of the people who come to train at Rawai Muay Thai come for the following reasons:
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         improve fitness
        </li>
        <li>
         increase stamina
        </li>
        <li>
         lose weight
        </li>
        <li>
         increase muscle
        </li>
        <li>
         get fighting experience
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        It’s OK is you have done little to no Thai Boxing before.
        <strong>
         We accept students of all levels and ages
        </strong>
        . The Muay Thai classes offer an intense workout to help you get in shape and lose weight and increase muscle mass. Muay Thai technique is fun, and before you know it, the class is over and you are sweating and tired and amazed at what you just achieved.
       </p>
       <p>
        Some people come because they are interested in fighting professionally and want to improve their skills. We offer students a chance to fight in Thailand. Tuk, the owner of the camp, fought professionally for 15 years and knows pretty much everyone in the sport. He is well connected and
        <strong>
         can organize fights
        </strong>
        for anyone who wants to train hard.
       </p>
       <p>
        Our sponsored boxers in the gym have fought all over the country.  These young boys and girls live in the camp. When you train at Rawai Muay Thai, part of your fee goes to support these local fighters. We have a Rawai Muay Thai
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.rawaimuaythai.com/about-us/rawai-kids-fund/">
          Kids Fund
         </a>
        </strong>
        that you can support.
       </p>
