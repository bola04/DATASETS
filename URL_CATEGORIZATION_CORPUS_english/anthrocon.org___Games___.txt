          <div class="sqs-block-content">
           <h1 class="text-align-center">
            Anthrocon is Pittsburgh's Premier Furry Convention
           </h1>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="row sqs-row">
          <div class="col sqs-col-2 span-2">
           <div class="sqs-block spacer-block sqs-block-spacer sized vsize-1" data-block-type="21" id="block-0cc0234efa52c572af15">
            <div class="sqs-block-content">
            </div>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="col sqs-col-8 span-8">
           <div class="sqs-block html-block sqs-block-html" data-block-type="2" id="block-a8e7c052f3e6249d5b9d">
            <div class="sqs-block-content">
             <p>
              Anthrocon's 22nd annual convention will celebrate anthropomorphics, which are humanlike animal characters, such as have fascinated mankind since the dawn of human imagination.
             </p>
             <p>
              We are a collection of artists,
              <em>
               animators, writers, costumers, puppeteers
              </em>
              ... and just everyday fans who enjoy cartoon animals and their kin.
             </p>
             <p>
              Membership is open to any and all who like to imagine what it would be like if animals could walk and talk as we do — and no, you do
              <em>
               not
              </em>
              have to wear a costume to attend!
             </p>
             <p>
              The festivities will begin on
              <strong>
               Thursday, July 5th, 2018
              </strong>
              , and will run through
              <strong>
               Sunday, July 8th, 2018
              </strong>
              in downtown Pittsburgh, PA.
             </p>
             <p>
              <a href="/registration">
               Registration
              </a>
              for the convention is now open,
              <a href="/hotel">
               hotel reservations
              </a>
              at our special discounted rates will be available for the general public in early February, 2018. Watch this space for announcements regarding our special guests!
             </p>
             <p>
              If you are looking for the old forums,
              <a href="http://forums.anthrocon.org">
               they are over here
              </a>
              .
