                         <h2>Find Snail Mail Pen Pals</h2>
                      </header>
                      <article>
                          <p>Find snail mail pen pals through the listings in <span class="magazinename">The Letter Exchange</span>. When you subscribe, we'll assign you a Lex Number. With your number you can:</p>
                         <ul>
                             <li>Submit listings to be printed in the magazine in categories like Art & Photography, Daily Life, Literature, and Work & Careers &mdash; 30 in all. </li>
                             <li>Browse the Lex Number-coded listings to find interesting correspondents. </li>
                             <li>Use the confidential forwarding service that connects letter writers anonymously through Lex Numbers (we don't print names or addresses) until you feel comfortable exchanging addresses. Or you may correspond entirely using the forwarding service, if you wish.</li>
                         </ul>
                         <aside class="inneraside">
                               <h5>Ghost Letters</h5>
                             <p>Respond to the listings in the <a href="listings-categories.html">"Ghost Letters"</a> section by pretending to be the fictional or historical character the listing is addressed to, creating a story through letters.</p>
