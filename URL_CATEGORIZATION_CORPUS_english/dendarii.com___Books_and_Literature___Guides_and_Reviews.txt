   <h1>
    Lois McMaster Bujold Home Page
   </h1>
   This is the official and authorised Lois McMaster Bujold website. 
 It is devoted to the award winning Science Fiction and Fantasy author 
 Lois McMaster Bujold. Most of Lois's stories are set in the future and
 concern the Vorkosigan family and the planet of Barrayar. Her recent novels 
 are from two different fantasy worlds.
