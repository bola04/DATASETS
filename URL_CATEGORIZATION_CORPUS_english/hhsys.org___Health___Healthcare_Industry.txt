          <p style="font-size: 120%; line-height: 170%;">
           Huntsville Hospital is the second largest hospital in Alabama. With 971 licensed beds on three campuses, Huntsville Hospital-Main, Women &amp; Children Hospital and Madison Hospital.
          </p>
          <p style="font-size: 120%; line-height: 170%;">
           It serves as the regional referral center for North Alabama and southern Tennessee. In recent years, the hospital has expanded its service throughout the region with the development of
           <a href="/affiliates">
            Huntsville Hospital Health System
           </a>
           , making it the third largest publicly owned hospital system in the nation with more than 1,800 beds and 13,000 employees.
