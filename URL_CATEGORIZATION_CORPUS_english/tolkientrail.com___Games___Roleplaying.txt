           Welcome to the Tolkien Trail, traveller! You are about to embark upon a journey that takes you across Middle Earth, as described in J.R.R. Tolkien's masterpiece,
           <i>
            The Lord of the Rings
           </i>
           . Your guide on this tour is the famed wizard Gandalf. Along the way, you will encounter games, fan fiction, music, original artwork, the popular
           <a href="http://entmoot.tolkientrail.com">
            Entmoot discussion board
           </a>
           and much more.
          </p>

           Your
           <a href="bree.shtml">
            journey begins outside Bree
           </a>
           . If you are new to the Trail, it is strongly recommended that you
           <i>
            visit Bree first
           </i>
           , to get the full experience of this site. Otherwise, returning visitors may go directly to an area by clicking on its name on the sidebars.
          </p>
