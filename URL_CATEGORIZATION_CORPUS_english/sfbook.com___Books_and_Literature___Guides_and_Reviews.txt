        <h1>
         Science fiction, fantasy &amp; horror book reviews
        </h1>
        <p>
         SFBook.com is one of the oldest
         <strong>
          book review
         </strong>
         sites on the internet, founded back in 1999 in an age before phones became smart and poking someone would get you told off. A non profit site primarily aimed at the
         <strong>
          Science fiction, fantasy and horror
         </strong>
         genres, we strive to feature only the very best in Science fiction, fantasy, horror and speculative novels. We like to think we write personal, unique and constructive reviews.
        </p>
        <p>
         We are constantly evolving and expanding the web site and strive to make the site a richer experience for the visitor. If you have any suggestions, or constructive feedback we'd love to hear from you.
        </p>
        <p>
         We have dedicated pages for
         <a href="https://sfbook.com/sci-fi-books.htm">
          Science fiction book reviews
         </a>
         as well as
         <a href="https://sfbook.com/fantasy-books.htm">
          fantasy book reviews
         </a>
         and
         <a href="https://sfbook.com/horror.htm">
          horror book reviews
         </a>
         but you can also browse by author, publisher, name, series or
         <a href="https://sfbook.com/sub-genres.htm">
          Sub Genre
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         We try our very best to prevent giving away any spoilers so that reading the book we are reviewing isn't spoiled in any way and as such some reviews may seem shorter than those elsewhere on the internet. Many reviews you will see on the website lean towards the positive and this is simply due to the fact that we try and only read the very best books for the simple reason that life is too short to read a bad book. If we do end up reading a bad one we will make sure we tell you.
        </p>

         Currently SFBook has a number of guest reviewers and five regular reviewers (3 in the UK, 2 in the USA). These are
