     <p style="text-align: center; font-size: 10px;">
      P.S:  I was thinking that it would be neat to have a tagline.  I mean, Wheaties has a tagline
      <br/>
      (“The Breakfast of Champions”), Kashi has a tagline (“Seven whole grains on a mission”).
      <br/>
      Even California Almond Growers have a tagline (“A can a week, that’s all we ask”).
      <br/>
      I wanted one, too.  So I chose:
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center; font-size: 10px;">
      <span style="color: #008000;">
       <em>
        April Halprin Wayland: ½ poet, ½ author, ½ not good at fractions
        <br/>
       </em>
      </span>
      because it made me laugh.  I hope it makes you laugh, too.
