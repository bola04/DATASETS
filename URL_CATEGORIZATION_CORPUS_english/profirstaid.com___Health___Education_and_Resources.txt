       <h3 class="visible-xs">
        Common Questions
       </h3>
       <div class="row">
        <div class="col-xs-12 col-sm-3">
         <h4>
          Can I use ProFirstAid for my initial certification?
         </h4>
         <p>
          Yes, whether it is your first time or your 15th time, ProFirstAid is a great way to get CPR certified. We recommend that anyone being certified for the first time do a hands-on skill evaluation.
         </p>
        </div>
        <div class="col-xs-12 col-sm-3">
         <h4>
          Is this approved by the American Heart Association?
         </h4>
         <p>
          The American Heart does not review or approve third party CPR providers (we're their competition). However, ProFirstAid does adhere to the AHA's guidelines and is a perfectly acceptable certification.
         </p>
        </div>
        <div class="col-xs-12 col-sm-3">
         <h4>
          Does ProFirstAid meet OSHA requirements for CPR &amp; First Aid?
         </h4>
         <p>
          Yes, the
          <a href="/blended_program">
           ProFirstAid blended course
          </a>
          does meet OSHA requirements. However, it is still a good idea to check with your employer first to make sure they will accept ProFirstAid.
         </p>

         <h4>
          How many tries do I get to pass the test?
         </h4>
         <p>
