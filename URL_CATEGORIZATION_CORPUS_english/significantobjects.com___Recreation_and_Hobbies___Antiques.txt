      <p>
       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Significant-Objects-Joshua-Glenn/dp/1606995251/signifobject-20/">
        <img alt="" class="size-medium wp-image-10122 alignleft" src="http://significantobjects.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2012/05/sigobj-two-covers.jpg" style="margin: 1px 5px;" title="SOBookBunnyCover" width="200"/>
       </a>
       <strong>
       </strong>
       <em>
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       Significant Objects, a literary and anthropological experiment devised by Rob Walker and Joshua Glenn, demonstrated that the effect of narrative on any given object’s subjective value can be measured objectively.
      </p>
      <p>
       The project auctioned off thrift-store objects via eBay; for item descriptions, short stories purpose-written by over 200 contributing writers, including
       <a href="http://significantobjects.com/2009/10/26/wooden-animal/" target="_blank" title="Wooden Animal">
        Meg Cabot
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="http://significantobjects.com/2009/10/02/hawk-ashtray/" target="_blank" title="“Hawk” Ashtray">
        William Gibson
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="http://significantobjects.com/2010/04/30/corked-bottle-ben-greenman-story/" target="_blank" title="Corked Bottle">
        Ben Greenman
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="http://significantobjects.com/2009/07/29/cape-cod-shoe/" target="_blank" title="Cape Cod Shoe">
        Sheila Heti
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="http://significantobjects.com/2009/12/01/rabbit-candle/" target="_blank" title="Rabbit Candle">
        Neil LaBute
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="http://significantobjects.com/2009/11/13/missouri-shotglass/" target="_blank" title="Missouri Shotglass">
        Jonathan Lethem
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="http://significantobjects.com/2010/02/12/mermaid-figurine/" target="_blank" title="Mermaid Figurine">
        Tom McCarthy
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="http://significantobjects.com/2009/07/06/chili-cat/" target="_blank" title="Chili Cat">
        Lydia Millet
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="http://significantobjects.com/2010/03/24/miniature-turkey-dinner-jenny-offill-story/" target="_blank" title="Miniature Turkey Dinner">
        Jenny Offill
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="http://significantobjects.com/2009/07/31/metal-boot/" target="_blank" title="Metal Boot">
        Bruce
       </a>
       <a href="http://significantobjects.com/2009/07/31/metal-boot/" target="_blank" title="Metal Boot">
        Sterling
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="http://significantobjects.com/2010/04/08/birthday-candles-scarlett-thomas-story/" target="_blank" title="Birthday Candles">
        Scarlett Thomas
       </a>
       , and
       <a href="http://significantobjects.com/2009/08/28/wooden-mallet/" target="_blank" title="Wooden Mallet">
        Colson Whitehead
       </a>
       , were substituted. The objects, purchased for $1.25 apiece on average, sold for nearly $8,000.00 in total. (Proceeds were distributed to the contributors, and to nonprofit creative writing organizations.) All the project’s stories are archived on this site.
      </p>
      <p>
       Glenn and Walker are now pleased to announce that a collection of 100 of the project’s finest stories has been published by Fantagraphics in a highly impressive volume, masterfully designed by
       <a href="http://www.unflown.com/" target="_blank">
        Jacob Covey
       </a>
       .
       <strong>
        It is available now via
        <a href="http://www.powells.com/biblio/1-9781606995259-4">
         Powell’s
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Significant-Objects-Joshua-Glenn/dp/1606995251/signifobject-20/">
         Amazon
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://www.barnesandnoble.com/w/significant-objects-joshua-glenn/1105583614">
         B&amp;N
        </a>
        , or wherever finer books are sold.
       </strong>
      </p>

       Follow the latest news and other interesting notes on objects, stories, and value at
