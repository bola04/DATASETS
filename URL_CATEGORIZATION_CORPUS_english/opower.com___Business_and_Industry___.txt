     <div class="c81w1">
      <p>
       On May 2, 2016, Oracle announced that it entered into a definitive agreement to acquire Opower, the leading provider of customer engagement and energy efficiency cloud services to utilities. The transaction has closed.
      </p>
      <p>
       Opower’s solutions enable over 100 global utilities, such as PG&amp;E, Exelon and National Grid to deliver a modern digital customer experience. Opower’s big data platform stores and analyzes over 600 billion meter reads from 60 million utility end customers, enabling utilities to proactively meet regulatory requirements, decrease the cost to serve, and improve customer satisfaction.
      </p>
      <p>
       Oracle Utilities offers a complete suite of operational applications and cloud services for global electric, gas and water utilities that automate core operational processes and enable compliance. Together, Oracle and Opower will provide the industry with the most complete, modern and integrated cloud platform for the entire utility value chain, from meter to grid to end-customers.
      </p>
