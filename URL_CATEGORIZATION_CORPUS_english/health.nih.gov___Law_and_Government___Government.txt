              </div>
              <h4 class="teaser-title">
               <a href="https://nei.nih.gov/health/pinkeye/pink_facts">
                Protect Yourself From Pink Eye
               </a>
              </h4>
              <p class="teaser-description">
               Has your eye been red and felt itchy or like it was burning? Pink eye is a common eye condition. One way to prevent pink eye is to avoid sharing eye makeup.
              </p>
             </div>
             <div class="teaser teaser-clickable">
              <div class="teaser-thumbnail">
               <img alt="A mature man with headphones on" height="150" src="https://www.nih.gov/sites/default/files/styles/thumbnail/public/health-info/mature-man-listening-headphones-outside.jpg?itok=ln4h032b" title="A mature man with headphones on" typeof="foaf:Image" width="150"/>
              </div>
              <h4 class="teaser-title">
               <a href="https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/healthy-moments">
                Healthy Moments Radio
               </a>
              </h4>
              <p class="teaser-description">
               Get tips on preventing and managing diseases from Healthy Moments Radio.
              </p>
             </div>
             <div class="teaser teaser-clickable">
              <div class="teaser-thumbnail">
               <img alt="Three young women reading books together in a library" height="150" src="https://www.nih.gov/sites/default/files/styles/thumbnail/public/health-info/three-young-women-reading-books-together-library.jpg?itok=aoInkY-x" title="Three young women reading books together in a library" typeof="foaf:Image" width="150"/>
              </div>
              <h4 class="teaser-title">
               <a href="https://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/uterine/conditioninfo/default">
                What Are Uterine Fibroids?
               </a>
              </h4>
              <p class="teaser-description">
               Uterine fibroids are growths found in the wall of the uterus, or womb. They are not cancer. Most women don’t need treatment.
              </p>
