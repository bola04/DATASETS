        </h1>

       <div class="entry-content post_content">
        <p>
         <a class="autohyperlink" href="http://ISpeakHindi.com" target="_blank" title="http://ISpeakHindi.com">
          ISpeakHindi.com
         </a>
         was a fun adventure meeting with Hindi speakers, learners, teachers.  It was a great family project with my mother in law, father in law, and wife.  And the friends that were part of it were great!.    Back when
         <a class="autohyperlink" href="http://ISpeakHindi.com" target="_blank" title="http://ISpeakHindi.com">
          ISpeakHindi.com
         </a>
         got started, there were far fewer Hindi Learning resources.  That has changed.  And now I think Hindi leaners would be better served by looking at those resources.
        </p>
        <p>
         Shipra Singh has helped on
         <a class="autohyperlink" href="http://ISpeakHindi.com" target="_blank" title="http://ISpeakHindi.com">
          ISpeakHindi.com
         </a>
         and offered to keep it going.  But I am interested in making a clean break.  She has started her own site
         <a href="http://ISpeakHindi.in">
          ISpeakHindi.in
         </a>
         Also check out her Facebook page:
         <a href="https://www.facebook.com/Learn-Hindi-159919120705319/">
          www.facebook.com/Learn-Hindi-159919120705319/
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The Hindi-Urdu Flagship program at the University of Austin has a list of some wonderful Hindi learning resources: http://hindiurduflagship.org/resources/
        </p>
        <p>
         Also,
         <a href="https://www.hindipod101.com/">
          HindiPod101
         </a>
         is worth checking out.  (The related
         <a href="https://www.japanesepod101.com/">
          JapanesPod101
         </a>
         was the original inspiration for ISpeakHindi)
        </p>
        <p>
         Best wishes on your Hindi learning journey.
        </p>
