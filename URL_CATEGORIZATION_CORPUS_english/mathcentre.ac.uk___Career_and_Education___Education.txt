       <h1 class="title">
        Mathematics resources  -  www.mathcentre.ac.uk or http://sigma.coventry.ac.uk
       </h1>
       <div class="content_box_text">
        <p>
         <img alt="News icon" src="images/icons/new-anim.gif"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         New
         <b>
          math
         </b>
         centre resources have been contributed to the Community project.
         <a href="../../../../news/">
          Find out more.....
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          math
         </b>
         centre has evolved to become a well-used and valued online drop-in centre for mathematics resources.  Find out more about
         <a href="../../../../resources/uploaded/who-uses-mathcentre-1dec13-to-30nov14.pdf">
          who used mathcentre
         </a>
         in 2013/14 and which were the
         <a href="../../../../resources/uploaded/most-used-mathcentre-resources1314.pdf">
          most popular resources
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="../../../../search/?q=Numbas">
          <b>
           Numbas
          </b>
          revision and diagnostic exercises
         </a>
         for students on basic and other topics in mathematics are accessible from
         <b>
          math
         </b>
         centre.
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <a href="http://www.mathcentre.ac.uk:8081/mathseg/" target="_blank" title="maths eg">
           Maths eg
          </a>
         </b>
         is an open-access formative assessment system made available to the
         <a href="../../../../communities/" title="Community Project">
          <b>
           math
          </b>
          centre Community Project
         </a>
         by Brunel university
         <a href="../../../../resources/uploaded/mathseg.pdf">
          Find out more.....
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          math
         </b>
         centre offers mathematics support materials, free of charge, to students, lecturers and everyone looking for post-16 maths help.  We have several types of resources to help you:
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Quick Reference Leaflets icon" src="images/icons/pdf.png"/>
         <a href="../../../../types/#h2">
          Quick Reference leaflets
         </a>
         - key topics in a nutshell - brief and easily accessible resources
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Mathtutor video tutorials icon" src="images/icons/video.png"/>
         <a href="../../../../types/#h32">
          <b>
           math
          </b>
          tutor Video tutorials
         </a>
         - watch and listen to a tutor working through important topics
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Teach-yourself booklets icon" src="images/icons/pdf.png"/>
         <a href="../../../../types/#h4">
          Teach Yourself booklets
         </a>
         - written to accompany the videos, these provide in-depth treatment of important topics, with theory, worked examples and exercises
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Practice and revision booklets icon" src="images/icons/pdf.png"/>
         <a href="../../../../types/#h1">
          Practice &amp; Revision booklets
         </a>
         - hundreds of practice exercises with answers covering important topics in algebra and calculus
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="On-line diagnostic tests icon" src="images/icons/tick.png"/>
         <a href="../../../../types/#h8">
          On-line Test Yourself diagnostics
         </a>
         - on specific topics to enable you to gauge your competency and decide whether further work is required
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="On-line exercises icon" src="images/icons/tick.png"/>
         <a href="../../../../types/#h8">
          On-line Test Yourself exercises
         </a>
         - with answers provided, to enable you to practice key techniques
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="STACK icon" src="images/icons/stacklogo4.png"/>
         on-line exercises and fully worked solutions generated from the
         <a href="search/?q=STACK&amp;saudience=students&amp;cat%5B%5D=4">
          STACK
         </a>
         computer-assisted assessment system and linked directly from selected Teach Yourself booklets
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="iPod video segments icon" src="images/icons/ipod.png"/>
         <a href="../../../../types/#h64">
          iPOD Video segments
         </a>
         - short video clips that you can download to your video iPod for support on the move
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="3GP mobile phone downloads icon" src="images/icons/web.png"/>
         <a href="../../../../types/#h513">
          3GP Mobile Phone downloads
         </a>
         - short video clips that you can download to your mobile phone
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Facts &amp; Formulae Leaflets icon" src="images/icons/pdf.png"/>
         <a href="../../../../types/#h512">
          Facts &amp; Formulae Leaflets
         </a>
         - electronic versions of the very popular leaflets distributed by the Higher Education Academy MSOR Subject Centre
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Motivating Mathematics icon" src="images/icons/video.png"/>
         <a href="../../../../types/#h518">
          Motivating Mathematics
         </a>
         - videos showing practical applications of mathematics in everyday life
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="../../../../types/#h16">
          Staff Resources
         </a>
         - a selection of case studies, resource packs, links to key reports and research on mathematics support, and other valuable resources for staff who offer mathematics support
        </p>
        <p>
         Help on how to use
         <b>
          math
         </b>
         centre can be found under
         <a href="faq/">
          FAQ
         </a>
        </p>
