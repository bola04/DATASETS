        </p>

         You can explore our inventory online first. Use the search filters, where you can easily narrow down your options by body type, MPG, or features. When a specific model piques your interest, you’ll want to schedule a test drive to explore it in person. We’ll get you behind the wheel to get a feel for the car’s performance and features, and we’ll gladly answer any questions that you may have.
        </p>

         Professional Finance Center
        </h2>
        <p>
         The experts at our
         <a href="https://www.kiastore.com/finance/">

        </p>

         After that’s completed, you’ll meet with one of our friendly and knowledgeable staff members. They’ll go over your budget and driving needs, and will come up with a payment plan that works for you. Additionally, regardless if you wish to lease or buy your next car, we’ll get you the loan or lease terms that you deserve.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Expert Service Center
        </h2>
        <p>
         Buying a new car is a big investment, so you want to make sure it lasts for many years and miles. By bringing your car to our state-of-the-art
         <a href="https://www.kiastore.com/service/">
          service center
         </a>
         , it’ll get the kind of treatment it deserves. With the Kia Extra Mile Service, you’ll be protecting your investment and maintaining your warranty. But more importantly, you’ll ensure its reliability for the duration of its life.
        </p>
        <p>
         Our certified technicians have years of experience working on all types of vehicles. Because of this, they can easily diagnose and remedy any issue with your car. Whether you need a simple oil change or a more complex engine repair, we’ll work quickly and efficiently to get you back on the road as soon as possible. Plus, we use only genuine parts, so your car will stay in tip-top shape for a long time.
        </p>
        <p>
         To schedule service or maintenance, either call us or make an appointment online. Supply us with some info about your car and the work it needs, and we’ll confirm a day and time that’s convenient for you.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Parts Center
        </h2>

         <a href="https://www.kiastore.com/parts/">
          parts department

         . We’re always fully stocked with genuine OEM parts. These parts are what our technicians use for any needed repairs on your vehicle.
        </p>

Whether you want to change the vehicle’s upholstery, or upgrade the sound system, we have the parts and accessories on hand to make that possible. Simply give us a call or order the parts online, and we’ll let you know when they’re available to be picked up.
