       <p class="alt">
        "Mr. Patrick. He called it
        <strong>
         Delicious Death
        </strong>
        . My cake!
        <br/>
        I will not have my cake called that!"
        <br/>
        "It was a compliment really," said Miss Blacklock.
        <br/>
        "He meant it was worth dying to eat such a cake."
       </p>
       <p>

        <i>
         A Murder is Announced, 1950
