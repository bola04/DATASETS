    </p>

 	  instructors with a passion for Pilates, it is the reason other teachers 
 	  come to us to workout!
    </p>

     Small classes follow the dynamic style of Classical Pilates with 
 	  flowing transitions to build strong and healthy bodies. Come and discover 
 	  how real Pilates should feel with us. Our classes will give you a vigorous 
 	  and challenging workout and we welcome men and women. Mat classes utilise 
 	  small pieces of equipment to teach the body how to correctly recruit the 
 	  right muscles and at intermediate/advanced level to provide challenge to 
 	  the exercises . We run classes for every level of Classical Pilates 
 	  throughout Worthing, Angmering ,Ferring, East Preston and Lancing using 
 	  personalised tuition in a group environment to maximise the benefit to 
 	  you. Find out why our clients stay with us and how to integrate Pilates 
 	  into your everyday life.
    </p>
