       <h1 class="text-beige">
        Official Author Site for John Passarella
       </h1>
       <p>
        Welcome to Passarella.com, the official website for Bram Stoker Award-Winning supernatural (and paranormal) thriller author John Passarella. He is the author of the original novels
        <em>
         Wither
        </em>
        (co-authored, Bram Stoker Award-Winner for First Novel),
        <em>
         Wither's Rain
        </em>
        ,
        <em>
         Wither's Legacy
        </em>
        ,
        <em>
         Kindred Spirit
        </em>
        and
        <em>
         Shimmer
        </em>
        , and the fiction collection
        <em>
         Exit Strategy &amp; Others
        </em>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        Passarella also wrote several original media tie-in novels, including his forthcoming
        <em>
         Supernatural: Joyride
        </em>
        (OCT 2018) ,
        <em>
         Supernatural: Cold Fire
        </em>
        , as well as
        <em>
         Supernatural: Rite of Passage
        </em>
        ,
        <em>
         Supernatural: Night Terror
        </em>
        and the Scribe Award Finalist,
        <em>
         Grimm: The Chopping Block
        </em>
        , all from Titan Books. Previously, he authored three media tie-in novels for Simon &amp; Schuster,
        <em>
         Buffy the Vampire Slayer: Ghoul Trouble
        </em>
        ,
        <em>
         Angel: Avatar
        </em>
        and
        <em>
         Angel: Monolith
        </em>
        .
       </p>

           <h3>
            About Matthew &amp; Matthew's Miles
           </h3>
           <p>
            My son Matthew was diagnosed with an inoperable brain stem tumor in 2001 at the age of seven and given 6 to 9 months to live. (As of this writing, he is nearly 23.) To date he's had four brain surgeries—3 in 2005 alone—and six weeks of radiation therapy, completed April 3, 2006. Read
            <a class="text-link-light" href="http://caringbridgeclassic.org/nj/matthew/" target="_blank">
             his story
            </a>
            here and find out why my wife and I started
            <a class="text-link-light" href="http://www.matthewsmiles.org/" target="_blank">
             Matthew's Miles
            </a>
            to raise money for pediatric brain tumor research.
