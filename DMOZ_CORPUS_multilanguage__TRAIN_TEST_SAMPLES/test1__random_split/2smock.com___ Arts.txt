      Welcome to 2smock!
     </h1>
     <blockquote>
      <p>
       <b>
        Closing Down Sale:
       </b>
       It is with much regret that Diane and I have decided to close down 2smock. Between Diane’s recent completion of her PHD and an exciting new career and my own Motherhood/Study commitments 2smock has been pushed further and further down the priority list and frankly it deserves more than that. As such we are releasing our remaining stock in preparation for closing down 2smock and wanted to offer all previous customers first chance at what’s left as a thank you for your previous support.
       <br class=""/>
       <span style="color: #5856d6;">
        <br class=""/>
       </span>
       The
       <a class="" href="http://2smock.com/">
        2smock.com
       </a>
       site has been updated with the current stock levels and at this time we will not be putting things onto our Etsy or eBay stores in an effort to avoid order/stock availability conflicts across various platforms. All previous customers can use the discount code emailed out to you to receive a massive 80% off the product total. Shipping will still be the same as that cost goes straight to Australia Post unfortunately. New Customers will find all remaining stock will be placed on sale from the 1st of July 2017, this seemed fairest to everyone as stock is limited.
       <a href="http://2smock.com/?p=1236&amp;preview=true">
        You can find out more about this on our blog.
       </a>
      </p>
     </blockquote>
     <p>
      2smock is the ready to smock and wear blank canvas your next smocking and embroidery project has been looking for. Our products are ready to send right now to get your next smocking project under way. From our Classic Yoke and Bishops Round Yoke Girls Dresses to our Low Cut Round Yoke Ladies Nightwear we hope you find what you are looking for. Sold as ready to smock garments or as smocking kits we offer flexibility and affordability. As part of our relaunch (find out more about who we are in
      <a href="http://2smock.com/our-blog/" title="Our Blog">
       our blog
      </a>
      and
      <a href="http://2smock.com/our-story/" title="Our Story">
       our story
      </a>
      ) we are offering all our existing stock at unbeatable prices while our production of the new Baby and Girls Nightwear range is under way. Have a look around and make yourself at home, we look forward to supporting your next smocking project.
     </p>
     <p>
      <em>
       Diana Raward &amp; Diane Spencer-Scarr – the 2smock Team
      </em>
