                Marilyn and Gary Deeds
               </font>

              <p>
               <font face="Comic Sans MS">
                <font color="#000080">
                 <font size="-1">
                  A new
                                   life began for Marilyn and Gary Deeds
                                   in 1990 when they retired.
                 </font>
                </font>
               </font>
               <br/>
               <font face="Comic Sans MS">
                <font color="#000080">
                 <font size="-1">
                  They
                                   find their lives so full now, they
                                   wonder how they had time to work!
                 </font>
                </font>
               </font>
              </p>
             </center>
             <p>
              <br/>
             </p>
             <table border="0" cols="1" width="879">

                <td>
                 <center>
                  <b>
                   <i>
                    <font face="Comic Sans
                                           MS">
                     <font color="#000080">
                      <font size="+2">
                       About the Good
                                               Deeds
                      </font>
                     </font>
                    </font>
                   </i>
                  </b>
                 </center>
                 <p>
                  <font face="Comic Sans MS">
                   <font color="#000080">
                    <font size="-1">
                     People
 often
 
                                           ask us how we got started in
                                           our music ministry.  It
                                           was an outgrowth of our
                                           experiences with bluegrass
                                           music.  Why we got
                                           started in bluegrass music is
                                           still a mystery to us! 
                                           We don't know what prompted us
                                           to attend our first bluegrass
                                           festival, but we were
                                           immediately attracted to this
                                           spirit-filled music.
                    </font>
                   </font>
                  </font>
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <font face="Comic Sans MS">
                   <font color="#000080">
                    <font size="-1">
                     Attending
 bluegrass
 
                                           festivals and jams sparked our
                                           desire to play bluegrass
                                           music.  At first Marilyn
                                           and our twin boys started
                                           playing bluegrass music
                                           together.  Gary had never
                                           played an instrument in his
                                           life, but not wanting to be
                                           left out he figured he needed
                                           to get involved.  Since
                                           the "family band" didn't have
                                           a bass, that was Gary's chosen
                                           instrument.
                    </font>
                   </font>
                  </font>
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <font face="Comic Sans MS">
                   <font color="#000080">
                    <font size="-1">
                     We
 
                                           played together as a family at
                                           bluegrass jams and
                                           festivals.  As we were
                                           drawn into the music we found
                                           ourselves playing more and
                                           more "gospel bluegrass".
                    </font>
                   </font>
                  </font>
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <font face="Comic Sans MS">
                   <font color="#000080">
                    <font size="-1">
                     Then
 it
 
                                           happened!  In 2002 God
                                           called us to share our gospel
                                           bluegrass music.  We were
                                           not sure at first if this was
                                           a true calling, but soon we
                                           began to receive
                                           confirmations.
                    </font>
                   </font>
                  </font>
                 </p>
                 <blockquote>
                  <font face="Comic Sans MS">
                   <font color="#000080">
                    <font size="-1">
                     *
 
                                           Friends, bands and festival
                                           promoters gave us words of
                                           encouragement to get out and
                                           share our music.
                    </font>
                   </font>
                  </font>
                  <p>
                   <font face="Comic Sans MS">
                    <font color="#000080">
                     <font size="-1">
                      *
 
                                             People commented on
                                             Marilyn's beautiful
                                             voice.
                     </font>
                    </font>
                   </font>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <font face="Comic Sans MS">
                    <font color="#000080">
                     <font size="-1">
                      *
 
                                             Another confirmation is an
                                             interesting story.  We
                                             were in Toronto, Canada at
                                             an RV campground playing our
                                             music outside our motor
                                             home.  A lady came over
                                             to talk to us and asked if
                                             we had ever been to the
                                             Bible Conference in Indio,
                                             California.  Here we
                                             were 2500 miles from home
                                             and were told about a
                                             conference practically in
                                             our own back yard!  Was
                                             God speaking to us through
                                             this stranger?  The
                                             following January we
                                             attended this conference and
                                             the speaker really spoke to
                                             us.  His four main
                                             points were:
                     </font>
                    </font>
                   </font>
                   <br/>
                   <font face="Comic Sans MS">
                    <font color="#000080">
                     <font size="-1">
                      Always
 
                                             follow God's leading with
                                             faith
                     </font>
                    </font>
                   </font>
                   <br/>
                   <font face="Comic Sans MS">
                    <font color="#000080">
                     <font size="-1">
                      Always
 
                                             believe God's promises
                     </font>
                    </font>
                   </font>
                   <br/>
                   <font face="Comic Sans MS">
                    <font color="#000080">
                     <font size="-1">
                      Be
 
                                             patient to wait for God's
                                             timing
                     </font>
                    </font>
                   </font>
                   <br/>
                   <font face="Comic Sans MS">
                    <font color="#000080">
                     <font size="-1">
                      Always
 
                                             keep God first
                     </font>
                    </font>
                   </font>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <font face="Comic Sans MS">
                    <font color="#000080">
                     <font size="-1">
                      Imagine, being so
                                             far from home and having
                                             someone tell us about a
                                             conference near us,
                                             attending it and hearing the
                                             message that we needed to
                                             hear?
                     </font>
                    </font>
                   </font>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <font face="Comic Sans MS">
                    <font color="#000080">
                     <font size="-1">
                      *
 
                                             Another confirmation: One of
                                             Marilyn's bridesmaids from
                                             over 40 years ago started
                                             emailing us and wanted to
                                             know if she could send us a
                                             copy of
                      <u>
                       The Purpose
                                               Driven Life.
                      </u>
                      At
                                             the time we had not heard of
                                             the book.  Upon reading
                                             it our desire to follow
                                             God's purpose for our life
                                             was reinforced.
                     </font>
                    </font>
                   </font>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <font face="Comic Sans MS">
                    <font color="#000080">
                     <font size="-1">
                      *
 
                                             Probably the most amazing
                                             confirmation was when
                                             Marilyn started receiving
                                             songs.  She would wake
                                             up in the middle of the
                                             night or early morning hours
                                             with melodies and lyrics
                                             running through her
                                             mind.  Marilyn had
                                             never written poetry or
                                             songs in her life. 
                                             These songs came to her
                                             totally complete.  At
                                             first she dismissed this as
                                             merely a dream, but the
                                             songs kept coming.  So
                                             she began writing them
                                             down.  She really
                                             believes they are a gift
                                             from God.  Over a three
                                             year period she has received
                                             over 150 songs!
                     </font>
                    </font>
                   </font>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <font face="Comic Sans MS">
                    <font color="#000080">
                     <font size="-1">
                      Before stepping
                                             out into the music ministry
                                             we were busy raising five
                                             children, serving in the
                                             United States Marine Corps,
                                             starting several business,
                                             teaching elementary school,
                                             painting in watercolors,
                                             sailing on San Francisco
                                             Bay, converting a Greyhound
                                             bus into a motor home and
                                             building a house.
                     </font>
                    </font>
                   </font>
                  </p>
                 </blockquote>
                 <font face="Comic Sans MS">
                  <font color="#000080">
                   <font size="-1">
                    As
                                         you can see, we have done a
                                         variety of things, but we are
                                         finding that our retirement
                                         years are some of the most
                                         fulfilling.  To be serving
                                         God through our music ministry
                                         is so rewarding.  We share
                                         our music at churches,
                                         retirement homes, assisted
                                         living facilities, skilled
                                         nursing facilities, gospel
                                         jamborees, bluegrass festivals,
                                         campgrounds and community
                                         centers.
                   </font>
                  </font>
                 </font>
