               We're building the path to a future without type 1 diabetes.
              </h2>
              <p>
               We are an international network of leading academic institutions, physicians, scientists and healthcare teams dedicated to the prevention of type 1 diabetes. We offer risk screening for relatives of people with type 1 diabetes and innovative clinical studies to preserve insulin production.
              </p>

               <a href="/t1d-facts">
                Know your family's risk
               </a>
              </h4>
              <p>
               Pathway to Prevention is a risk screening study to detect your family member's risk of developing T1D. Screening results can show an increased risk years before symptoms appear.
               <a href="/t1d-facts">
                More
               </a>
              </p>
             </div>

              </a>
              <h4>
               <a href="/participate">
                Risk screening is important
               </a>
              </h4>
              <p>
               If you have type 1 diabetes in your family, you are in a unique position to help prevent it. Family members are 15 times more likely to develop type 1 diabetes.
               <a href="/participate">
                More
               </a>
              </p>
             </div>

              <h4>
               <a href="/about-us/trialnet-locations">
                Find a location near you
               </a>
              </h4>
              <p>
               We provide flexible options for type 1 diabetes risk screening. Blood draws are available from over 200 TrialNet locations worldwide and partner sites throughout North America.
               <a href="/about-us/trialnet-locations">
                More
