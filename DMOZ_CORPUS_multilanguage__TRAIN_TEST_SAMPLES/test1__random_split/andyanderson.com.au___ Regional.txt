            <a href="http://www.andyanderson.com.au/look-good-commercial-landscaper/" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to What To Look For In A Good Commercial Landscaper">
             What To Look For In A Good Commercial Landscaper
            </a>
           </h1>
           <div class="entry-meta">
            <a href="http://www.andyanderson.com.au/look-good-commercial-landscaper/" rel="bookmark" title="2:50 am">
             <time class="entry-date" datetime="2017-12-01T02:50:09+00:00" pubdate="">
              December 1, 2017
             </time>
            </a>
            <span class="byline">
             by
             <span class="author vcard">
              <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.andyanderson.com.au/author/editor/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Lola McGuigan">
               Lola McGuigan
              </a>
             </span>
            </span>
            <span class="cat-links">
             Posted in
             <a href="http://www.andyanderson.com.au/category/business/" rel="category tag">

             <a href="http://www.andyanderson.com.au/category/news/" rel="category tag">

           <!-- .entry-meta -->
          </header>
          <!-- .entry-header -->
          <div class="entry-content">
           <p>
            Hiring a commercial landscaper isn’t a difficult task, provided that you know what to look for in a good contractor. There are many types of landscapers, so you need to find one who has a wealth of experience with your specific type of project. Once you narrow down your list of potential contractors, you should start assessing their skills and capabilities, in order to find the one that best suits your needs.
           </p>
           <p>
            A
            <a href="http://www.allscapeswa.com.au/commercial-landscaping-perth/">
             good commercial landscaper
            </a>
            should have enough experience and a solid work portfolio to show you. Besides, they should have all needed licenses and certifications required to do this job. All these things are very easy to check. Most landscaping companies have presentation websites where they showcase their best work, their current projects and perhaps some client testimonials. This information should help you gain a better understanding of the ability of a certain commercial landscaper to help you with your project.
           </p>
           <p>
            The landscaper should know the basic principles of designing a landscape in such a way that it looks great all year round. This means they have to know how to mix and match plants, rocks, ponds and other design elements for creating a landscape able to attract looks regardless the season of the year. In addition, the best experts in commercial landscape design know that low maintenance is one of the keys to enjoying their work for a very long time. Building managers and business owners are usually too busy to bother with the regular maintenance of this space. While they would surely pay someone to cater to the plants, they would be happier if they didn’t need to increase their expenses that much by hiring a permanent gardener.
           </p>
           <p>
            The best landscaping professional should also be able to understand the specific of your business and the profile of your customers. He or she should know how to make your building fit into the overall look and feel of the surroundings, thus making your business memorable. A high-end landscape design is always going to impress potential clients coming to your business headquarters for the first time. You have only one chance to make a positive first impression, so you need to make sure your landscaper understands how to add value to your business by choosing the right features.
           </p>
           <p>
            Last but not least, great landscapers would have happy clients who would be willing to endorse the quality of their services. By asking all contractors on your shortlist to provide you a few references of their clients, you can find out immediately whether these professionals know how to listen and how to implement what they hear in their work. Good communication between you and the landscaping company is mandatory for the success of your project, whether you’re creating your landscape from scratch or you’re only improving something that’s already there.
           </p>
           <p>
            By taking all the above into consideration when you search for the best service provider, you are surely going to make the best choice right off the bat. This will save you a lot of trouble along the way.
           </p>
          </div>

           <h1 class="entry-title">
            <a href="http://www.andyanderson.com.au/online-vs-offline-marketing-one-focus/" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to Online Vs Offline Marketing- Which One Should You Focus On?">
             Online Vs Offline Marketing- Which One Should You Focus On?
            </a>
           </h1>
           <div class="entry-meta">
            <a href="http://www.andyanderson.com.au/online-vs-offline-marketing-one-focus/" rel="bookmark" title="2:39 am">
             <time class="entry-date" datetime="2017-09-19T02:39:12+00:00" pubdate="">
              September 19, 2017
             </time>
            </a>
            <span class="byline">
             by
             <span class="author vcard">
              <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.andyanderson.com.au/author/editor/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Lola McGuigan">
               Lola McGuigan
              </a>
             </span>
            </span>
            <span class="cat-links">
             Posted in
             <a href="http://www.andyanderson.com.au/category/business/" rel="category tag">
              Business
             </a>
             ,
             <a href="http://www.andyanderson.com.au/category/marketing/" rel="category tag">
              Marketing
             </a>
            </span>
           </div>
           <!-- .entry-meta -->
          </header>
          <!-- .entry-header -->
          <div class="entry-content">
           <p>
            Everyone wants to win in this world. Everyone wants to pick the right business opportunities, the right partners and for those who market products and services, the right strategy.
           </p>
           <p>
            A part of choosing the right business marketing strategy is finding out where you’re going to do the marketing(either online or offline). You have to figure out whether your audience is mostly online or offline. The ideal venue usually depends on the business or product, but the answer for most cases is usually both. As such, you need to balance the benefits of both online and offline marketing in order to create an ideal strategy.
           </p>
           <p>
            The internet currently reaches over 40% of the world’s population, and the numbers will continue to rise, given the innovation, advancement and growth in the information industry. Research shows that people will research a product or a company they have seen online, but over 70% of those will purchase the product offline.
           </p>
           <p>
            Now that we have seen how many people have access to the internet in relation to the global population, let us now look at the benefits of both online and offline marketing and which one you should focus on.
           </p>
           <h2>
            The Pros of Online Marketing
           </h2>
           <p>
            One of the primary benefits of marketing online is that you can easily make changes to your advertising. There’s an array of tools to help with tracking marketing campaigns, and so, it’s easy to find out what is working and what is not. You can easily change the graphics or wording of your ads, blogs, websites etc.
           </p>
           <p>
            Another benefit is the cost.
            <a href="https://www.duxdigital.com.au/">
             Online marketing in Perth
            </a>
            is way cheaper than offline advertising. You can create sites and blogs for very little or even for free. There’s also the benefit of free article marketing.
           </p>
           <p>
            The third benefit is the turnaround time. For a print advertisement, you need to contact a newspaper or magazine ad office, discuss the pricing, design the ad and pay to have it published. However, if you use the internet, you could have your ad up on the desired location within a couple of days or hours. You could also get leads faster compared to publishing ads on the newspapers or billboards.
           </p>
           <h2>
            The Pros of Offline Marketing
           </h2>
           <p>
            Marketing offline might be expensive, but it isn’t without a share of its benefits as well.
           </p>
           <p>
            First and foremost, your prospects do not need internet access in order to view your ad. They can be reached via TV, newspapers,
            <a href="http://www.adweek.com/creativity/10-great-magazine-ads-dont-just-sit-there-looking-pretty-132401/">
             magazine ads
            </a>
            , billboards, etc. These mediums tend to be expensive, but they are traditionally known to have excellent results.
           </p>
           <p>
            There’s also direct mail. You can send samples of your products through direct mail. Touching and experiencing the product hands down beats virtual experience.
           </p>
           <p>
            Finally, there is the impact made through business cards. These are excellent as most people have business cards in some kind of Rolodex or wallets. They still are a source of contacts especially when a need for emergency services arises.
           </p>
           <p>
            Finding a balance between offline and online marketing is the best strategy. However, it is not possible for some companies, especially startups to find this balance due to the costs involved. As such, the best thing to do is first to find success through online marketing. Once you have gained adequate money, you can then venture into billboards, newspapers and TV ads.
           </p>
          </div>

             Tips for Booking Accommodation for Your Business Trips
            </a>
           </h1>
           <div class="entry-meta">
            <a href="http://www.andyanderson.com.au/tips-booking-accommodation-business-trips/" rel="bookmark" title="4:19 am">
             <time class="entry-date" datetime="2017-08-04T04:19:20+00:00" pubdate="">
              August 4, 2017
             </time>
            </a>
            <span class="byline">
             by
             <span class="author vcard">
              <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.andyanderson.com.au/author/editor/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Lola McGuigan">

             </span>

           <!-- .entry-meta -->

          <!-- .entry-header -->
          <div class="entry-content">
           <p>
            If you have a business trip coming up, you’re going to want to find the appropriate accommodation for your excursion. You won’t want to book just any hotel for your trip; you’ll want to stay in a place that meets or exceeds your needs.
           </p>
           <p>
            The accommodation that you stay in can have a significant impact on your trip. These tips will help you to find the right hotel for your next business trip.
           </p>
           <h3>
            Book Ahead Of Time
           </h3>
           <p>
            It’s a smart idea to book the accommodation for your business trip in advance. If you’re traveling for work, you need to make sure you are able to acquire a room in the right location. If you book your accommodation at the last minute, you could wind up in a hotel that doesn’t provide that you need.
           </p>
           <p>
            Booking your hotel early on can also be a great way to save. When there are plenty of rooms available, hotels tend to charge lower prices. Booking early will allow you to book a

            </a>
            at a very reasonable price.
           </p>
           <h3>
            Research Your Options
           </h3>
           <p>

            <a href="http://www.traveller.com.au/reviews/accommodation-reviews">

            </a>

           </p>

           </h3>
           <p>

           <p>
            If you have a business which needs transport equipment, you will be faced with two options. The first is to buy the transport equipment and the second is to rent it. Many business owners assume that purchasing their equipment is the best option, but this is not always the case. There are many reasons why
            <a href="http://www.rentco.com.au/">
             renting your transport equipment
            </a>
            is better than buying and you need to know what these are to make an informed decision.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             The Costs
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            The primary reason why you should rent your equipment is the costs. Transport equipment can be very expensive depending on the specs and the type of equipment you need. The high costs are something that many new, small and medium-sized businesses will not be able to carry. If you do not have the capital to pay for the equipment, renting would be the best option.
           </p>
           <p>
            When you rent your transport equipment, you will be paying either a monthly fee or a fee for the days that you need the equipment. This will be easier for most businesses to handle and you will not have to worry about your assets standing when you are not using them.
           </p>
           <h3>
            Higher Standards Of Equipment
           </h3>
           <p>
            The equipment that you are able to buy will generally not be the top of the range unless you have a lot of starting capital. This means that your purchased transport equipment may not be the best for your business. When you rent the equipment, you can get the top of the range without too much effort. Of course, when you rent the top of the range equipment, you will need to pay a higher rental fee, but this will not be close to the price of the equipment if you purchased it outright.
           </p>
           <h3>
            Taxable Income Deduction
           </h3>
           <p>
            In many countries, when you rent or lease your business equipment you will be able to deduct the costs from your taxable income. This will lower the amount of tax that you need to pay. This is something that you will generally not be able to do with purchased equipment as this is seen as an asset of your business.
           </p>
           <h3>
            No Breakdown Risks
           </h3>
           <p>
            Transport equipment comes with the risks of breakdown and this will cost you more to cover. This is not something that you have to worry about when you rent the equipment. This is due to the fact that the rental company will carry these risks. If the equipment breaks down, you will need to contact the rental company and they will have to sort out the repairs.
           </p>
           <p>
            Of course, when it comes to these risks, you need to read the terms of the rental agreement. There are times when you might be liable for some costs depending on why the equipment has failed.
           </p>
           <p>
            There are many reasons why you should look at renting your transport equipment instead of buying it outright. The lower costs are the greatest reason with the higher standard of equipment being a close second.
           </p>

            <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-37" height="310" sizes="(max-width: 740px) 100vw, 740px" src="http://andyanderson.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/22.jpg" srcset="http://www.andyanderson.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/22.jpg 740w, http://www.andyanderson.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/22-300x126.jpg 300w, http://www.andyanderson.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/22-2x1.jpg 2w" width="740"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <em>
             You should personalize your emails. There are many advanced email marketing service available that has many built-in features to send personalized emails. You should be careful in including the details. You should not use any personal financial information. The customers should not feel like they have been exposed.
            </em>
           </p>
           <p>
            <em>
             You should put your customers first. You should try to give value to customers through your products or services. Even if it costs you up front, it will provide great customer satisfaction and a greater percentage of repeat sales. So, you must try to give personalized experience to your customers all the time.
            </em>
           </p>

         <article class="post-11 post type-post status-publish format-standard has-post-thumbnail hentry category-business category-marketing category-news" id="post-11">
          <div class="blog-image">
           <img alt="post" class="imagerct" src="http://www.andyanderson.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/feat3-670x300.jpg"/>
          </div>
          <header class="entry-header">
           <h1 class="entry-title">
            <a href="http://www.andyanderson.com.au/top-5-e-commerce-website-design-trends-follow/" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to Top 5 e-commerce website design trends you should follow">
             Top 5 e-commerce website design trends you should follow
            </a>
           </h1>
           <div class="entry-meta">
            <a href="http://www.andyanderson.com.au/top-5-e-commerce-website-design-trends-follow/" rel="bookmark" title="6:33 am">
             <time class="entry-date" datetime="2017-04-12T06:33:16+00:00" pubdate="">
              April 12, 2017
             </time>
            </a>
            <span class="byline">
             by
             <span class="author vcard">
              <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.andyanderson.com.au/author/editor/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Lola McGuigan">
               Lola McGuigan
              </a>
             </span>
            </span>
            <span class="cat-links">
             Posted in
             <a href="http://www.andyanderson.com.au/category/business/" rel="category tag">
              Business
             </a>
             ,
             <a href="http://www.andyanderson.com.au/category/marketing/" rel="category tag">
              Marketing
             </a>
             ,
             <a href="http://www.andyanderson.com.au/category/news/" rel="category tag">
              News
             </a>
            </span>
           </div>
           <!-- .entry-meta -->
          </header>
          <!-- .entry-header -->
          <div class="entry-content">
           <p>
            <em>
             The world of e-commerce changes very frequently. Consumers always look for user-friendly website designs. They want sites that will let them search for the product they want and check out fast. Here are some e-commerce trends that you should follow.
            </em>
           </p>
           <h3>
            <em>
             Material Design
            </em>
           </h3>
           <p>
            <em>
             It is a content-focused style that was released by Google in 2014. This design has been later adopted by many e-Commerce companies. The design has a minimalistic appearance. It uses shadow effects and concepts of depth and motion. This design will keep your user engaged for long hours with your site.
            </em>
           </p>
           <h3>
            <em>
             Responsive Design
            </em>
           </h3>
           <p>
            <em>
             People now shop on multiple screens, like TVs, mobile, tablets, etc. So, it is essential for websites to be responsive. Companies should design the site for large screens and optimize it for the small screens like mobile and tablets.
            </em>
           </p>
           <h3>
            <em>
             Great animations
            </em>
           </h3>
           <p>
            <em>
             Animation can connect you with the users easily. It will give the users a more enjoyable shopping experience. Customers will feel great if you can use the right animation at the right time and place. You can include things like button spins, icon rotation, loading bars, etc. These will create a long lasting image in the customers’ mind.
            </em>
           </p>
           <h3>
            <em>
             Large Backgrounds
            </em>
           </h3>
           <p>
            <em>
             Now the trend is to have large backgrounds. If you have large product photos, it will be more appealing to the customers. It also gives a very professional look to the site.  It comes to the customers to place an order when they enter the site.
            </em>
           </p>
           <h3>
            <em>
             Storytelling
            </em>
           </h3>
           <p>
            <em>
             Storytelling will give you an extra edge over the others. Customers enjoy listening to stories. It is a great way to stand out from the crowd. You should have great interface design to attract the users.
            </em>
           </p>
           <p>
            <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/UmheGRy2sgs" width="560">
            </iframe>
           </p>
           <p>
            <em>
             These are the latest trends of e-commerce sites. You should develop your site according to these trends to keep up with the competition. These trends will help you get more customers to your e-commerce site.
            </em>
           </p>
          </div>
