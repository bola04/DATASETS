               <p>
                <font color="red">
                 <strong>
                  Guest Notice
                 </strong>
                </font>
                <br/>
                <em>
                 Please be advised that the Lion Square Lodge will be undergoing renovations and construction through mid-September. While open for business, some guest rooms may not be available during the process. The renovations taking place in our meeting rooms should be completed by mid-July. Construction work of our parking area and parking garage will continue through mid-September between the hours of 9am and 5pm daily. During this period, guests may experience some noise or light disturbances, and on-property parking may be limited.
                </em>
               </p>
               <hr/>
               <p>
                Nestled in the Rocky Mountains at the base of Vail Mountain and the Gore Creek, the picturesque Lion Square Lodge is perfect for family vacations, couples retreats, group events and more. Conveniences such as shopping, dining and entertainment abound from this location within Lionshead Village. Our ski in and ski out access is second to none! Walk out our door and you are ready to step into your skis and head to the Eagle Bahn Gondola - putting world-class skiing at your door.
               </p>
               <p>
                Lion Square Lodge accommodations range from cozy, inviting, private hotel rooms to spacious, deluxe condominiums, all conveniently located within a ski-in/ski-out lodge in Vail. Superb guest services at Lion Square Lodge range from complimentary daily maid service to daily newspaper delivery, and include all the fine services you'd expect from a first-class hotel.
               </p>
               <p>
                An extensive $53 million property renovation at Lion Square Lodge was recently completed, with the final phase reopened for the 2013/2014 ski season. Major renovations over the course of the project at Lion Square Lodge include significant interior and exterior upgrades throughout all three buildings of the property, the new ski-in/ski-out
                <strong>
                 <a href="https://wvr-d7-files-prod.s3.amazonaws.com/VAI-lion-square-lodge-el-sabor-menu_0.pdf" target="_blank">
                  El Sabor restaurant
                 </a>
                </strong>
                on the lobby level, new elevators, and a number of expanded units in the East Building all with patios or balconies with stairs offering access directly to the ski slope.
               </p>
               <p>
                All units are non-smoking and feature special touches you'll appreciate, from complimentary voicemail and high-speed Internet access to built-in hair dryers and soft terry robes. Plus, guests enjoy full access to all of the resort amenities, including a sparkling pool, hot tubs, dry sauna, state-of-the-art fitness center and more.
               </p>
