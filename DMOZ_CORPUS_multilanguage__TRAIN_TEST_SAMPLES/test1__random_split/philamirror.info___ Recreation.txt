        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          <a href="http://philamirror.info/?attachment_id=53181" rel="attachment wp-att-53181">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-53181" height="266" sizes="(max-width: 545px) 100vw, 545px" src="http://philamirror.info/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/viral-hepatitis-control-programme-fdc.jpg" srcset="http://philamirror.info/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/viral-hepatitis-control-programme-fdc.jpg 545w, http://philamirror.info/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/viral-hepatitis-control-programme-fdc-300x146.jpg 300w" width="545"/>
          </a>
          The stamp was released on 28
          <sup>
           th
          </sup>
          July 2018 at New Delhi.A National Viral Hepatitis control programme aimed at eliminating the deadly condition by
          <a href="http://philamirror.info/?attachment_id=53182" rel="attachment wp-att-53182">
           <img alt="" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-53182" height="178" src="http://philamirror.info/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/viral-hepatitis-stamp.jpg" width="241"/>
          </a>
          2030 was launched on Saturday by the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare in collaboration with the World Health Organisation.The programme was launched on World Hepatitis Day with an aim to combat viral hepatitis and reduce mortality and morbidity associated with it, Union Health Minister J P Nadda said. Terming it a historic day, he said the programme has been developed in association with all stakeholders who have worked at the grass-root level and has been launched in accordance with Sustainable Development Goals (SDG), 2030.

GEF-SGP, UNDP to date has supported over 130 projects in the GEF focal areas of biodiversity conservation, climate change and land degradation. Currently, SGP projects are spread over 19 districts in Bhutan GEF-SGP support is geared towards developing rural communities, and ensuring long term benefit for these communities through small grants. In Bhutan, GEF-SGP supports over fifty small projects that make a difference to the people of rural Bhutan.
