       <h1 align="center">
        Anchorage Alaska Real Estate
       </h1>
       <p style="text-align: left;">
        When you're seeking homes in Alaska, you want a balanced blend of metropolitan
        <a href="/alaska-local-information-guide.php" title="Alaska local information guide">
         amenities
        </a>
        , untouched natural beauty, economic opportunity and easy access to outdoor recreation -- and that's exactly what you'll find in Anchorage. The largest city in the largest state in the U.S., Anchorage is home to more than 298,600 people within the city limits and more than 380,800 residents within the Anchorage metropolitan area, which includes the cities of
        <a href="/eagle-river-alaska-real-estate.php" title="home for sales in Eagle River Alaska">
         Eagle River
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="/palmer-alaska-real-estate.php" title="homes for sale in Palmer Alaska">
         Palmer
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="/wasilla-real-estate.php" title="home for sales in Wasilla">
         Wasilla
        </a>
        and
        <a href="/chugiak-alaska-real-estate.php" title="Home For sale in Chugiak">
         Chugiak
        </a>
        in the Matanuska-Susitna Borough just to the north and the resort town of
        <a href="/girdwood-alaska-real-estate.php" title="Home for sale in Girdwood">
         Girdwood
        </a>
        to the south.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Fur Rondez" height="207" src="/images/fur_rondez_400.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="438"/>
       </p>
       <div id="dcDisplay">
        <p>
         <span class="P">
          You can search everything that the
          <a href="/anchorage-ak-real-estate.php" title="Home for sales in Anchorage">
           Anchorage Alaska
          </a>
          real estate market has to offer, right here on our site! Don't miss our Feature Homes. We update our listings daily so if you are l
          <span class="P">
           ooking for homes in Alaska, Anchorage, Palmer and the surrounding areas, you will find every listing on the market right here.
          </span>
          You can also register on our site - It's free - and enter your want list for your ideal home. As soon as a home comes onto the market that meets your want list, you are notified immediately. Then at a click of a button or a quick phone call you could be walking through your next home within 24-48 hours.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="P">
          <img alt="northern lights above hillside" height="222" src="/images/northern_lights_above_hillside_518.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="438"/>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="P">
          Need local real estate information? We've summarized much of what is important about the Anchorage, Eagle River, Palmer, Girdwood and Wasilla real estate market on this site.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="P">
          Need expert advice? No web site, large or small, can take the place of a real estate agent. Not even ours! This site is filled with ways that you can contact us for one-on-one expert help and advice. That's our job and we have been doing it successfully for over 25 years.
          <br/>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="P">
          Contact us with confidence! We guarantee your privacy will be respected, and we offer the services on this site for free and without obligation. Why? We want to be YOUR real estate agents. As your trusted, professional real estate partners, we will help you find the best home in your area within your price range. And together we will sell your home, for as much as the market will bear, and as quickly as possible.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="P">
          <a href="/buyers-guide.php">
           Buying
          </a>
          and
          <a href="/sellers-guide.php">
           selling
          </a>
          a home is one of the biggest events in your life. we have the experience and track record you are looking for.
         </span>
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        While Alaska's indigenous Dena'ina have called the region along Knik Arm home for thousands of years, settlers first populated the city in the early 20th century with the construction of the Alaska Railroad. After Anchorage was incorporated in 1920, military bases, the aviation industry and the oil boom of the 1960s drew adventure-seeking people. Anchorage continues to grow today, with a healthy economy based on petroleum extraction, transportation, military and tourism, and an almost 15 percent gain in population since 2000.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="alaskan  grown" height="155" src="/images/alaskan_grown_400.jpg" style="float: left; margin-top: 5px; margin-bottom: 5px; margin-left: 10px; margin-right: 10px;" width="236"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        More than 40 percent of those who make their homes in Alaska live in Anchorage. The Anchorage MLS and Alaska real estate market reflects these upward trends. Home values in the Anchorage metropolitan area have increased by 7.1 percent over the past year, with an average value of $354,871. Though the average sales price of homes in the Anchorage Alaska real estate market has declined by 4.84 percent to $344,273 over the past year, unemployment numbers in Anchorage remain lower than the state and the nation as a whole, and the Municipality of Anchorage forecasts steady housing market growth – along with an increasing population -- over the next few decades.
       </p>
       <p>
        It's no surprise that Anchorage continues to draw those seeking homes in Alaska. Nested between the rugged Chugach Mountains and the scenic shores of Cook Inlet, Turnagain Arm and Knik Arm, Anchorage residents enjoy convenient access to the Chugach State Park, which offers almost half a million acres of wilderness crisscrossed by hiking trails, salmon-filled rivers, 155 mountain peaks to climb, camping sites and -- of course -- that famous Alaskan wildlife habitat. And speaking of wildlife, those with homes in Alaska expect to catch frequent glimpses of the other local residents such as moose, fox, mountain goats, Dall sheep, bald eagles, whales and even the occasional bear or lynx.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>

       <p>
        But Anchorage is packed with metropolitan attractions, as well. From world-class eateries to eclectic art galleries and shopping, the city offers rich cultural experiences at destinations such as the Alaska Native Heritage Center, the Anchorage Museum of History and Art, the Anchorage Opera and the Alaska Center for the Performing Arts. Sports fans can follow the University of Alaska Anchorage Sports, Alaska Baseball League, Alaska Aces Hockey, as well as the annual Iditarod Dog Sled Race. No matter where interests may lie, when you're seeking to buy or sell homes in Alaska, Anchorage offers something for everyone.
       </p>

        Whether you're buying or selling your Alaska home, thinking of relocating or want to explore local Anchorage Alaska real estate trends on the Anchorage MLS, you want an experienced Realtor on your side. The Mary Cox team is on your side, every step of the way.
