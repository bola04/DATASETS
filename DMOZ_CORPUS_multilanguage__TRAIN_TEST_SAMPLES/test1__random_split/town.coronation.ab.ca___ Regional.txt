         <h1 style="text-align: left;">
          Town of Coronation
         </h1>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          Welcome to the Town of Coronation's website. We are very excited and proud to show off our many assets that include an abundance of services, supports and facilities that are enjoyed and appreciated by young and old. We believe it is a great place to raise a family and a quiet and affordable community in which to spend your retirement years. As you browse through our site, we hope you will be amazed and impressed with all that we have to offer. We are the located within the
          <a href="http://www.countypaintearth.ca/" target="_blank">
           County of Paintearth
          </a>
          , with a population of 940. Our residents are proud of their community, and we offer many services and programs to enrich our resident's lifestyle. For more information, please go to our
          <a href="/history/">
           history page
          </a>
          .
         </p>
