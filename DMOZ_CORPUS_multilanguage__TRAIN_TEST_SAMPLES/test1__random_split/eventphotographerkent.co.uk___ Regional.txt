       <h1>
        Pink Spot Photography
       </h1>
       <h2>
        Kent’s finest wedding, special occasion and portrait photographer
       </h2>
       <p>
        When it comes to your special day, your loved ones or simply a birthday celebration, only professionally taken and high quality event photographs will do. You don’t want the hassle of having to take photos for yourself, or even the blurry or discoloured photos that some cameras still produce.
       </p>
       <p>
        Offering crisp, natural and timeless photos, I work throughout Kent, ensuring that every client has beautiful printed or digital keepsakes to treasure for many years to come.
       </p>
       <h3>
        Memories encapsulated in stunning photographs
       </h3>
       <p>
        Using the very latest camera equipment, I take beautiful photographs of any wedding, family gathering, birthday celebration or Christmas party. A disc/USB of fully edited photos will be supplied, copyright free after the event so that you always have those photos as a stunning reminder.
       </p>
       <h3>
        I come to you
       </h3>
       <p>
        All photographs are taken in the comfort of your own surroundings or if for a wedding, at the church, reception hall or anywhere you choose. I am completely flexible; after all, it is all about you, your family and the people you care about
       </p>
       <h3>
        Fair Pricing
       </h3>
       <p align="left">
        You will find my prices to be very reasonable. Wedding packages range from £350 to £850, family portraits are usually around £120 and any special occasion photography starts from £150.
       </p>
       <p>
        I work throughout Kent and London, taking stunning photographs at a variety of different events, including weddings, family gatherings, birthday celebrations and Christmas parties.
       </p>
       <h3>
        Based in Ramsgate, Kent
       </h3>
       <p>
        I have recently taken photos at events in Medway, Canterbury, Ashford, Dover, Folkestone, Gravesend, Dartford, Maidstone, Sevenoaks, Tunbridge Wells, Thanet, Gillingham, Rochester and Chatham.
       </p>
      </section>
