         <h1>
          Greetings!
         </h1>
         <img align="right" src="me-suit.jpg"/>
         <div class="dimmer">
          Hello, My name is Phill Phelps. I am an electroaccoustic musician from Bristol in the UK.
          <br/>
          I teach audio programming and research at the the [
          <a href="http://www.uwe.ac.uk">
           University West Of England
          </a>
          ] where I studied
          <i>
           Music Systems Engineering
          </i>
          . In addition to this, I always seem to be involved in a lot of obscure music projects, most of which are in collaboration with fellow (ex)students.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          I have a strong interest in the Japanese language and culture and I am very interested in
 	getting involved in [
          <a href="video.shtml">
           film projects
          </a>
          ] - a lot of the music I write has
 	strong atmospheric qualities which would enhance any imagery set alongside them. I can, and
 	will work for free, so if you are interested - please email me: 
 	[
          <a href="mailto:zenpho@zenpho.co.uk">
           zenpho@zenpho.co.uk
          </a>
          ]
         </div>
