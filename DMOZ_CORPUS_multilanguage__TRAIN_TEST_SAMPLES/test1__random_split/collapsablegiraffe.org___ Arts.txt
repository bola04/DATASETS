        <h2>
         What is The Collapsable Hole?
        </h2>
        <p>
         The Collapsable Hole is performance space at Westbeth Arts in New York City’s 
 West Village. It is run by a group of eight artists and artist-run organizations. Originally founded in Williamsburg, 
 Brooklyn in 2000 by Collapsable Giraffe and Radiohole in what was a run down garage, the first Hole was a home for 
 "an approximate inventory of New York's most innovative artists” (Village Voice) and had a run of 13 years. After a 
 few years in the wilderness, the Hole has been resurrected in new form but with its irrepressibly unruly ethos intact.
        </p>
