               <p style="text-align:center">
                <em>
                 <em>
                  <strong>
                   Welcome to Eglinton Veterinary Facilities
                  </strong>
                 </em>
                </em>
               </p>
               <h3 class="text-align-center">
                <strong>
                 183 Eglinton Avenue West, Toronto, Ontario  416-487-1533
                </strong>
               </h3>
               <p style="text-align:center">
               </p>
               <p>
                <em>
                </em>
                <strong>
                 Caring for North Toronto pets for eight decades, Eglinton Veterinary Facilities is a full-service animal hospital located near Yonge and Eglinton in the heart of Toronto. Along with our professional and courteous team, our veterinarians seek to provide the best possible medical, surgical and dental care, while promoting responsible pet ownership, preventative health care and health-related educational opportunities for our clients. We all recognize that pets are cherished parts of the families who visit us!
                </strong>
               </p>
               <p class="text-align-center">
                We welcome you to browse these pages to learn more about our veterinary practice and to find out more about how Eglinton Veterinary Facilities can serve both your needs, and those of your pet.  Please keep an eye on our
                <strong>
                 <a href="https://beverley-bateman.squarespace.com/config/#module=content&amp;collectionId=52023c79e4b068e11031b3c2" target="_blank">
                  Journal
                 </a>
                </strong>
                page for announcements and other newer items of interest, and feel free to
                <strong>
                 <a href="https://beverley-bateman.squarespace.com/new-page/" target="_blank">
                  contact us
                 </a>
                </strong>
                at any time.
               </p>
               <p style="text-align:center">
                We are especially excited to announce that Eglinton Vet is one of only a few clinics in the Greater Toronto Area to offer laparoscopic spays and other procedures using our new, state-of-the art laparoscope. Please visit the information page entitled
                <a href="/laparoscopic-spays/">
                 <strong>
                  laparoscopic procedures
                 </strong>
                </a>
                <em>
                </em>
                to find out more.
               </p>
               <p class="text-align-center">
                Complimenting our commitment to new technology, we are pleased to also be able to offer our patients the benefit of our therapeutic laser program. This assists us with the treatment of chronic pain, injuries and other medical problems. Further information can be found on our
                <strong>
                 <a href="https://beverley-bateman.squarespace.com/laser-therapy/" target="_blank">
                  laser therapy
                 </a>
                </strong>
                page.
               </p>
               <p class="text-align-center">
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <p style="text-align:center">
                <strong>
                 183 Eglinton Avenue West -- Between Yonge Street and Avenue Road
                </strong>
               </p>
               <p style="text-align:center">
                <strong>
                 Close to Eglinton Subway - proud to be part of the vibrant Yonge/Eglinton neighbourhood
                </strong>
               </p>
