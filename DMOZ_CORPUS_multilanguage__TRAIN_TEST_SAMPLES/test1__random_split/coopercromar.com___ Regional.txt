        <p>
         Cooper Cromar is a leading and multi award winning UK Architectural Practice which operates throughout all sectors of the property and construction industries.
        </p>
        <p>
         The Company provides a comprehensive architectural, urban design, master planning, interior design, space planning, graphic visualisation and construction management service on a diverse range of singular and mixed use projects and building types.
        </p>
        <p>
         Cooper Cromar strives to create the best quality buildings and developments and to provide the highest level of service on each and every project in order to satisfy and indeed exceed all of our Clients objectives and expectations.
        </p>
        <p>
         Over the years since establishment in 1984 the Practice has developed an acknowledged reputation for innovative and creative architectural design solutions coupled with astute commercial awareness and proactive management thus ensuring timeous and cost effective delivery of major construction projects.
        </p>
        <p>
         Through a combination of the above qualities Cooper Cromar’s aim on every project is to transcend our Clients aspirations and build long standing and mutually rewarding business relationships.
        </p>
