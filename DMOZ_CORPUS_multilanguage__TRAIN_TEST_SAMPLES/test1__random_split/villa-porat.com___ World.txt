       <a href="http://villa-porat.com/" rel="bookmark" title="Villa Porat">
        Villa Porat
       </a>
      </h2>
      <div class="info">
      </div>
      <div class="entry">
       <p>
        <strong>
         Welcome
        </strong>
        to the web pages of “
        <strong>
         Villa Porat
        </strong>
        ”
       </p>
       <p>
        And during this virtual visit we want to show you hospitality and be, as always, good hosts, tell you about the beauty of our area, the pleasant climate, and we will try to show you clear and untouched nature.
        <br/>
        Of course, we will inform you about the settlement that we can offer to you, and also about other contents.
       </p>
       <p>
        You will find all the information you should know before coming to Croatia, and all your possible dilemmas and questions we will solve with a quick answer by e-mail or telephone.
       </p>
       <p>
        We will try to show you the harmony of nature and hospitality that are with the location of our villa, direct by the sea, major trump of our recognition.
       </p>
       <p>
        In this small authentic Mediterranean paradise, with long touristy tradition, soul and body are coming in absolute harmony with nature. As your hosts we will try to fulfil all your wishes and demands, and we will try to make your vacations perfect, and our villa will certainly be the place where you will want to come back again.
       </p>
