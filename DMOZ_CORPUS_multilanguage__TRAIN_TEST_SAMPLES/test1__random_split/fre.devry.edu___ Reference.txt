         <h1 class="rte-basic title">
         </h1>
         <p>
          DeVry University Newark Campus
         </p>
         <div class="component-text-block fluid" data-component-name="Video / Image Highlight with Text">
          <div class="fluid-wrap">
           <div class="description rte-content ">
            <p>
             Conveniently located in metropolitan San Francisco Bay Area, with easy access to local freeways, DeVry University Newark Campus offers undergraduate and
             <a href="/graduate-programs.html">
              graduate degree programs
             </a>
             in some of today's growing career fields. In a metropolitan area with the largest concentration of IT and biotech companies in the nation and a vibrant professional services industry, DeVry University Newark enables you to explore possible career opportunities in a wide variety of industries.
            </p>
            <p>
             The Newark Campus offers:
            </p>
            <ul>
             <li>
              Spacious classrooms
             </li>
             <li>
              Fully wired computer lab
             </li>
             <li>
              Comfortable commons area
             </li>
             <li>
              Ability to take courses at
              <a href="/universities/california/san-jose-center.html">
               San Jose Center
              </a>
              *
             </li>
             <li>
              Ability to take courses
              <a href="/online-education.html">
               online
              </a>
              or in one of our innovative new extended classrooms*
             </li>
            </ul>
            <p>
             Explore below to see the degree programs offered at the Newark Campus. Then
             <a href="/forms/request-information-campus-tour.html?vc=160053" target="_blank">
              schedule a campus tour
             </a>
             and talk to an advisor today.
            </p>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="rte-content disclaimer">
           <p>
            *Program availability varies by location and delivery method.
           </p>
           <p>
            <i>
             All students enrolled in site-based programs will be required to take some coursework online and, for some programs and locations, a substantial portion of the program may be required to be completed online.
            </i>
           </p>
