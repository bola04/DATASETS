            <p class="p2">
             I know, I know. Not many people associate those words with the idea of
             <a href="http://www.drwinans.com/about-us/meet-the-doctor/">
              seeing the dentist
             </a>
             . My patients do though… and thank goodness, they tell me so.
            </p>
            <p class="p3">
             You see, I love being a dentist. Not because it’s my job – which of course is nice – but because I have a daily opportunity to make someone smile.
            </p>
            <p>
             I see all kinds of people, from kids who have super-healthy teeth, to people who have a whole lot of dental problems. And while I wish I could prevent every dental problem instead of treating it, sometimes I also enjoy putting on my engineer/artist cap and getting down to the business of bringing a smile back, well, pretty much from the dead.
            </p>
            <p>
             So, no matter where you live in
             <a href="http://www.drwinans.com/contact-us/">
              Central PA
             </a>
             , or what you need,
             <a href="http://www.drwinans.com/about-us/meet-our-team/">
              my team
             </a>
             and I are here for you, to make you comfortable, get you healthy, and keep you smiling.
            </p>
            <p>
             While you’re here, why not check out my list of dental services by
             <a href="http://www.drwinans.com/dental-services/">
              clicking here
             </a>
             . You’ll see I offer everything from dental cleanings and gum disease treatments, to crowns, bridges, and
             <a href="http://www.drwinans.com/dental-services/implant-dentistry/">
              implants
             </a>
             . Oh, and did I mention? I also have a special passion for
             <a href="http://www.drwinans.com/dental-services/cosmetic-dentistry/">
              cosmetic dentistry
             </a>
             like teeth whitening, veneers, and even rejuvenating treatments.
            </p>
            <p>
             And, by the way, I have a family too and I understand how crazy life can get. I try to do whatever I can to make your life easier. Here’s how:
