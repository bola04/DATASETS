      <h1>
       Welcome And What's New At DAK
      </h1>
      <!-- InstanceEndEditable -->
      <div id="content">
       <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="River Description" -->
       <p>
        <span style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0;">
         We think there's nothing better than slipping into river time and returning
         to what is truly significant. It's very likely that you do too. Whether
         you are new to the sport of whitewater kayaking, interested in making plateau breakthroughs,
         or wanting an exceptional wilderness or international trip, join us in
         reaching your destination. You'll find our company encourages your progress
         and your confidence, and ensures that you'll have fun along the way.
         If this is what you're looking for, we look forward to paddling with
         you!
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        The
        <a href="owyhee.html">
         <strong>
          Lower Owyhee
         </strong>
        </a>
        is an outstanding desert canyon trip. It offers fun whitewater in stunning canyons of constantly changing geology. Although it's only been on the books for 2 years with DAK, folks are signing up for a second go at this remote and amazing river!
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="mainsalmon.html">
         <strong>
          Idaho's  Main Salmon
         </strong>
        </a>
        was a great success! This big-water, Class 3 run is famous for it's huge sandy beaches, great wave trains and excellent surf waves. From 18 to 81, everyone on this first trip agreed that it's a gem of an overnighter. For either of these trips email
        <a href="mailto:info@momentumriverexoeditions.com">
         <strong>
          Momentum
         </strong>
        </a>
        or call 541-488-2525.
       </p>
       <p style="margin-bottom: 0;">
        It is our goal to provide the best in whitewater guiding and instruction
         to groups and individuals. What sets us apart? Our experience. For over
         30 years we've been teaching and guiding, and we love it! Our students
         will tell you that we individualize what we teach and how we guide to
         meet each persons needs. Join us and see for yourself!
       </p>
