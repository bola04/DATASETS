     <section class="first" id="content">
      <h3>
       <span style="color: #003366;">
        <strong>
         Welcome to The Concord Regional Crimeline!
        </strong>
       </span>
      </h3>
      <p>
       The Concord Regional Crimeline is a non-profit organization organized under the laws of the
       <br/>
       State of New Hampshire.  The purpose of this organization is to promote community education
       <br/>
       and awareness of criminal activities in order to deter and prevent their occurrence.  Crimeline
       <br/>
       funds rewards for anonymous information that leads to arrest(s) or indictment for crimes that
       <br/>
       have been committed.
      </p>
      <p>
       Currently The Concord Regional Crimeline is serving the following NH communities:
       <br/>
       Allenstown, Barnstead, Boscawen, Bow, Canterbury, Chichester, Concord, Deering, Dunbarton, Epsom, Franklin, Henniker, Hillsboro, Hopkinton, Loudon, Pembroke, Pittsfield, Salisbury, Webster and the Merrimack County Sheriff’s Department.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.concordregionalcrimeline.com/wp-content/uploads/Crimeline-Application-Town-or-City.pdf" target="_blank">
        <span style="color: #003366;">
         <b>
          CRIMELINE TOWN OR CITY MEMBERSHIP APPLICATIO
         </b>
        </span>
        <b>
        </b>
        <span style="color: #003366;">
         <b>
          N
         </b>
        </span>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.concordregionalcrimeline.com/wp-content/uploads/Crimeline-Application-Individual.pdf" target="_blank">
        <span style="color: #003366;">
         <b>
          CRIMELINE INDIVIDUAL MEMBERSHIP APPLICATION
         </b>
        </span>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="color: #003366;">
        <b>
         <a href="http://www.concordregionalcrimeline.com/?attachment_id=859">
          CRIMELINE GOLF TOURNAMENT INFORMATION – SEPTEMBER 13, 2018
         </a>
        </b>
       </span>
      </p>
      <h3>
       <span style="color: #003366;">
        <strong>
         How Crimeline Works
        </strong>
       </span>
      </h3>
      <p>
       You can report a tip 3 ways:
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        #1
        <span style="color: #003366;">
         By Phone
        </span>
       </strong>
       :
       <strong>
        Call 603-226-3100
       </strong>
       . The line is staffed 24 hours a day. You will be given a code number to use in all subsequent calls. You do not have to identify yourself.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        #2
        <span style="color: #003366;">
         On-line
        </span>
       </strong>
       <span style="color: #003366;">
        :
       </span>
       <a href="https://www.tipsubmit.com/WebTips.aspx?AgencyID=234" target="_blank">
        Submit information through the website link.
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        #3
       </strong>
       <span style="color: #003366;">
        <strong>
         Text
        </strong>
        :
       </span>
       Submit information by text messaging
       <strong>
        TIP234
       </strong>
       and your message to:
       <strong>
        CRIMES (274637)
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       If your information is approved for a reward, arrangements will be made for cash payment.
       <br/>
       You are responsible for tracking your tip through this website via your Crimeline Identification
       <br/>
       Number.
      </p>
      <p>
       A reward of up to $1,000 is offered to anyone providing information which leads to an arrest or
       <br/>
       indictment for a crime. Rewards may also be made for information leading to the recovery of
       <br/>
       stolen property, the seizure of illegal drugs, or an arrest on an outstanding warrant.
      </p>
     </section>
