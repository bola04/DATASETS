    <h1>
     NuWay Speaker - Precision Components for Quality Sound
    </h1>
    <p>
     NuWay Speaker has been in the business of making speaker components for all segments of the loudspeaker industry since 1953.
    </p>
    <h2>
     Coming Together of 2 Great Ones!
    </h2>
    <p>
     In 2010 Loudspeaker Components, LLC, located in Lancaster, Wisconsin, USA, purchased the assets of NuWay Speaker.  NuWay brand products continue to be manufactured under the management of Loudspeaker Components in Nogales, Mexico and Lancaster, Wisconsin.
    </p>
    <div class="floatl">
     <img alt="Nuway Speaker products" class="border" height="96" src="i/nuway-products.jpg" style="margin:0 12px;" title="Nuway Speaker products" width="150"/>
    </div>
    <p>
     Since 1953 NuWay Speaker has been in continuous pursuit of higher technology.  Since 1965 Loudspeaker Components has been doing the same.  A coming together of trade secrets borne of the greatest, progressive thinkers in the industry brings a unique offering to the market.
    </p>
    <p>
     Speaker Cones, dust caps, spiders, gaskets and other die-cut parts are competitively priced as a result of innovative manufacturing methods developed by Loudspeaker Components and NuWay or by the talented staff based in Nogales, Mexico since 2003.  Simply put, highest speaker industry technology and quality sold worldwide at competitive prices.
    </p>
    <p>
     Thousands of dies, formulations and machines used to produce the world-famous NuWay products are available to the world under the same sales and marketing program as Loudspeaker Components.
    </p>
    <p>
     A worldwide leader in the industry, Loudspeaker Components wrote the
     <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Speaker_cone" target="_blank" title="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Speaker_cone">
      Wikipedia Speaker Cone article
     </a>
     .
    </p>

     Most noteworthy of the NuWay additions to the Loudspeaker Components lineup include the following capabilities:
