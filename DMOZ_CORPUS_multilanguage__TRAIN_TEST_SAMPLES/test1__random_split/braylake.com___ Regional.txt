      <h2>
       Berkshire's Premier Windsurfing, Sailing and Paddlesports Centre
      </h2>
      <p>
       <span style="font-weight: 400;">
        Bray Lake Watersports started in 1979, now nearly 40 years later, we are recognised for windsurfing and sailing by the Royal Yachting Association (RYA), Canoeing and Kayaking by British Canoeing (BC), Stand Up Paddle Boarding by The British Stand Up Paddle Boarding Association (BSUPA) and for junior activities by the Adventurous Activities Licensing Authority (AALA) for excellence. The Centre offers courses, instruction and hire for all levels in a safe and friendly environment.
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-weight: 400;">
        Our picturesque 50 acre lake is surrounded by a mixture of grassy banks and a mature tree line, and open seven days a week, our pine log clubhouse provides comfortable and enjoyable facilities, including a dedicated water sports shop and café - perfect whatever the weather.
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-weight: 400;">
        In a world that is becoming more automated, computerised and stressful, we provide the perfect alternative for your leisure time. We offer a multitude of outdoor activities for both young and old, for just about any occasion with the options for individuals, schools, groups, junior parties and corporate events. Browse our website and book today or why not come down in person for coffee or tea and check us out.
       </span>
      </p>
      <h2>
       <span style="font-weight: 400;">
        Registration
       </span>
      </h2>
      <p>
       <span style="font-weight: 400;">
        To use the facilities at Bray Lake, whether you are attending a course, hiring, swimming or any other activity, you are required to become a Friend of Bray Lake.  If you have previously been a customer at Bray Lake, you will already be registered, however, you will require an access code to complete registration, please call the Centre on 01628 638860.
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       For new users please register for FREE as a Friend of Bray Lake today.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <a class="download" href="https://customerweb.microshadeapplications.co.uk/BrayLake/common/scubaWebLogin.php?scubaAppName=stellar" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">
        New User Registration

      </p>
