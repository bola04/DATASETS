      <h1>
       A Charity for the Children of Africa
      </h1>
      <p>
       Abaana is a Christian based charity which invests in Children in Africa, helping them to break the chains of poverty, through education.
      </p>
      <p>
       Most of our projects are in Uganda, working in 14 locations supporting the children and parents in these communities. Thanks to our Donors, we have been able to build 14 schools, support churches and sponsor over 1,500 children as well as continuing our work with children on the streets and other projects.
      </p>
      <p>
       Thank you for caring for children in Africa. We can make a difference!
      </p>
