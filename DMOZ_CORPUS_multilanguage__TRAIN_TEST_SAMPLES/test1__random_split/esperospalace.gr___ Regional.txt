          <h3>
           How to reach Esperos Palace Hotel and Faliraki village
          </h3>
          <p>
           Esperos Palace Hotel is located in Faliraki just 12 km from the city of Rhodes, 11 km from the port and 18 km from the International Airport "Diagoras". The nearby location of Esperos Palace to the capital of Rhodes ensures easy access in any way you choose to reach the hotel Esperos Palace.
          </p>
          <ul class="slick">
           <li>
            <h4>
             Arriving at Esperos Palace from the port of Rhodes
            </h4>
            If you are in Rhodes harbor follow signs leading to Faliraki and Lindos via the ring road from the port to Faliraki. The Esperos Palace Hotel is at your left just 2 km before entering in Faliraki.
           </li>
           <li>
            <h4>
             Arriving at Esperos Palace from Rhodes airport
            </h4>
            Coming from Rhodes airport turn right at the first traffic lights and follow the signs directing you to Faliraki and Lindos. Prefer the ring road that does not pass through the center of Rhodes, to reach the hotel Esperos Palace quickly and comfortably.
           </li>
           <li>
            <h4>
             Getting around by bus
            </h4>
            Faliraki is located on the east coast of Rhodes and served by several bus routes that follow this direction. Also, Faliraki has very good connection with Lindos, while the bus stop is right outside the hotel Esperos Palace. For more information and exact itineraries visit the bus of Rhodes website (www.ktelrodou.gr (link is external)) or contact +30 22410 27706 or address to the reception.
           </li>
           <li>
            <h4>
             Getting around by taxi
            </h4>
            In Rhodes there are many points that you can order or hire a taxi to visit different places. If you are in Faliraki and want taxi services please call +30 22410 85444, while if you are in the city of Rhodes, please call +30 22410 27666.
           </li>
           <li>
            <h4>
             Getting around by car
            </h4>
            Rent the car you want to explore and enjoy the island of Rhodes from Esperides Rent-A-Car, located within the Esperos Palace Hotel that has the best prices.
           </li>
