          <p>
           Boy Scout Troop of Wind Lake, Norway and Tichigan Wisconsin has been active since 1963. With the Cub Scout Pack 332, we work together to instill leadership and a sense of service to the boys of these communities.
          </p>
          <p>
           The mission of our troop and the Boy Scouts of America is to prepare boys to make ethical and moral choices over their lifetimes by instilling in them the values of the Scout Oath and Law.
          </p>
