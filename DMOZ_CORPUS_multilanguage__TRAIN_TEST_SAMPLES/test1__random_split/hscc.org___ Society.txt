             On behalf of Father Henry Carmona, our deacon and parish staff, we welcome you to Holy Spirit Catholic Community and invite you to join the life of this vibrant Catholic Community.
            </strong>
           </h5>
           <p>
            Holy Spirit Catholic Community was formed from the merging of three parishes; St. Joseph, St. Anthony and St. Paul's. There is something for everyone here, there are ways to serve, pray and to worship, to learn and to grow in friendship and fellowship.
