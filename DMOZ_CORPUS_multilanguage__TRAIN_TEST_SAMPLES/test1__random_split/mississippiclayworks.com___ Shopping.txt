      <div class="readytheme-contentsection" id="footer_about_us">
       <p>
        Forty four years ago I started using a potters wheel to create hand-thrown stoneware pottery for my customers. I designed a gas fired kiln that holds over one hundred pieces of pottery and I formulated all of the glazes I use on my pots. Since that time, I've made many improvements in style, technique and materials. I believe the pottery I produce is some of the finest to come off a potters wheel!
       </p>
       <p>
        In 1991, I started Mississippi Clayworks, located in Historic Downtown Hastings, Minnesota.
