                  <span class="imgPusher" style="float:left;height:0px">

                   Built in 1927 by the Shubert Organization, the Forrest has played host  to hundreds of theatrical productions over the years, including Broadway  musicals, comedies, dramas, Yiddish theatre, dance programs, Gilbert  &amp; Sullivan productions, and the Philadelphia Orchestra. Still owned  and operated by the Shubert Organization for over eight decades, the  Forrest Theatre continues to be a shining star in Philadelphia's theatre  community.
