    <h2 style="margin-top: 0px; height: 40px;">
     Welcome
    </h2>
    <div style="text-align: justify;">
     <big>
      <font>
       <font face="Arial, Geneva" size="4">
        <small>
         Looking for a choir in Reading?
         <br/>
         <br/>
         The Reading Concert Singers are a moderately sized, enthusiastic choir
 who enjoy presenting a wide diversity of choral music, from traditional
 church music to modern shows and film music, and also themed concerts,
 such as our recent presentation celebrating our Planet Earth. The
 proceeds from most of our performances are donated to charity.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         We rehearse on Wednesday evenings at the Carey Centre in Reading from
 7.45pm to 9.45pm with a half-time refreshment break. We are always
 delighted to welcome new members in all parts and no voice tests are
 required.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         For more details, please contact our Musical Director, Tim Munday on 0118 9622676,
         <br/>
         <br/>
         or email:
         <a href="mailto:info@readingconcertsingers.org.uk">
          info@readingconcertsingers.org.uk
         </a>
