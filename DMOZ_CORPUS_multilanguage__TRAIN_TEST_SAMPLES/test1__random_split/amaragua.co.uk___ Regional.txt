     <h2>
      Guest House in Edinburgh
     </h2>
     <p>
      Within 30 minutes walking distance to centre.
     </p>
     <p>
      AmarAgua is a comfortable Victorian residence, conveniently located around the corner from a main bus route, 10 minutes from the main tourist attractions, such as “Edinburgh Castle” and the famous “Royal Mile”.
     </p>
     <p>
      Situated in a quite residential area, the guest house is within 5 minutes walking distance to The Pollock Halls and the Commonwealth Swimming Pool.
     </p>
     <p>
      Near the Guest House you also find many recreational facilities include a golf course and tennis courts.
     </p>
     <p>
      The majestic “Arthur Seat”, situated in the Holyrood Park in the middle of the city where you can hike to the top which takes about 40 minutes from us and enjoy the 360 degrees view of the capital of Scotland.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       AmarAgua Guest House has just been awarded a HotelsCombined Recognition of Excellence for 2017!
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      AmarAgua has been recommended for many years in the prestigious American Travel Guide “Rick Steves” as well as in “Frommer’s” and “Fodor’s”.
