            <b>
             So what is Blood Bowl?
            </b>
            <br/>
            <br/>
            The game of Blood Bowl is an epic conflict between two heavily armed teams of psycho warriors. Just like Gridiron, players pass, throw and run the ball to try to get it to the End Zone. This strive to get a Touchdown is battered and bashed down by the opposing team and their arsonal of weapons. As with all great sports, the team with the most points at the end of it wins and is crowned the Blood Bowl Champions.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            The original board game has been ported over by a bunch of raving fans, and can now be played completely online. So grab a copy of the rulebook, get yourself a Blood Bowl Client, and find yourself a League in the links section!
