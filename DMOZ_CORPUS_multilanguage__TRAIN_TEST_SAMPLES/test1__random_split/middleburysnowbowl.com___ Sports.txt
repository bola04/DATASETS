          <h2 style="text-align: center">
           Welcome to Middlebury Snow Bowl
          </h2>
          <p style="text-align: center">
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            We are a family-friendly winter adventure destination in the heart of the Green Mountains of Vermont.  Located just 20 minutes from the classic New England village of Middlebury, we offer skiing and riding on a wide variety of piste and off-piste terrain, and we can accommodate all abilities from rank beginner to experienced expert.  Here are some other features of the winter experience we offer:
           </strong>
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Four lifts serving an extensive
             <a href="http://www.middleburysnowbowl.com/the-mountain-2/">
              trail
             </a>
             system with a 1,000 foot vertical
            </strong>
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Efficient snowmaking on a large proportion of our trail system—you’ll always be able to ski
            </strong>
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Professional
             <a href="http://www.middleburysnowbowl.com/snow-school__trashed/private-clinics-and-packages/">
              snow school
             </a>
             to serve your every need for instruction
            </strong>
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             A comfortable, drive-up base lodge with free day-storage of gear
            </strong>
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             On-site dining every day, with a great selection of reasonably priced food
            </strong>
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             On-site ski and snowboard
             <a href="http://www.middleburysnowbowl.com/ski-shop/">
              rentals, tuning, services and retail
             </a>
            </strong>
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Nearby
             <a href="http://www.middleburysnowbowl.com/lodging/">
              lodging
             </a>
             ranging from B&amp;B’s and Inns to simple motels in all price ranges
            </strong>
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
           <strong>
            If you have any questions at all, please call us at 802-443-7669.  We’d love to have you join us for a wonderful
            <a href="http://www.middleburysnowbowl.com/packages/">
             winter experience
            </a>
            !
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            In addition, if you are a Nordic skier, you can indulge your passion at the
            <a href="http://www.rikertnordic.com/">
             Rikert Nordic Center
            </a>
            , located less than a mile away.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
