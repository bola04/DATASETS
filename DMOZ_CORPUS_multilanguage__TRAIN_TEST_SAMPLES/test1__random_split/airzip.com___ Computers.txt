          <h2>
           Why choose AirZip FileSECURE?
          </h2>
          <p>
           AirZip FileSECURE offers one of the most effective 
 						security solutions for preventing unauthorized access to 
 						your confidential documents, engineering drawings and 
 						other electronic files. We go several steps further than 
 						others to actively limit who, how, where and when 
 						information can be accessed as well as implementing 
 						methods to discourage and thwart those who are 
 						intent on actively attempting to steal your sensitive information 
 						and intellectual property - whether they be your 
 						employees, suppliers, business partners or hackers.  
 						Read about some of the methods used in FileSECURE to 
 						protect you
           <a href="FileSECURE_security_features.htm" target="_self">
            here
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           FileSECURE is arguably the most powerful and secure 
 						product of its type available. We persistently secure 
 						Microsoft Office documents, PDFs, all major CAD formats, 
 						images, media and other files as well as the output of ANY 
 						application that can print. We ensure almost perfect 
 						viewing fidelity by rendering secured content directly in 
 						Microsoft Office or using Adobe's PDF, Oracle AutoVue, Dassault eDrawings, 
 						Kisters 3D Viewstation or OpenText Content Viewer 
 						Toolkit technology.
          </p>
          <p>
           Once a file is secured, you no longer have to worry 
 						whether someone accidentally forwards it to an 
 						unauthorized person as an email attachment, copies it to 
 						a USB drive and carries it out the door or places it on 
 						a publicly accessible server. The file can only be 
 						viewed if the user is able to authenticate from an 
 						approved workstation and has explicit permission to view 
 						the file. The protected information displayed to the 
 						user can further be dynamically watermarked/fingerprinted with user, 
 						network and system specific information to discourage 
 						photographing the content, or to provide forensic 
 						evidence if a picture is taken.
          </p>
          <p>
           AirZip continues to add features and functions at a 
 						steady pace and is one of the last remaining companies 
 						whose primary focus is persistent control of access to 
 						files and documents both inside and outside the 
 						organization.
          </p>
          <p>
           The major features of our recent releases can be 
 						viewed
           <a href="FileSECURE_release_history.htm" target="_self">
            here
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           Our FileSECURE Clients are available in English, 
 						Japanese, Simplified and Traditional Chinese making it 
 						the only real solution when sending sensitive 
 						information to low or no enforcement jurisdictions. 
 						AirZip's support staff provide technical support on a 
 						7x24x365 worldwide basis.
          </p>
          <p>
           Whether you are a large multi-national corporation with 
 						tens of thousands of users or a small business with just 
 						a few, there is an affordable FileSECURE security solution for 
 						you - with both on-premises installation and cloud based 
 						service options.
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            FileSECURE is Made in the USA
           </b>
           .
          </p>

           Call us today to arrange a webinar, demonstration and/or 
 						an evaluation of the most comprehensive rights 
 						management solution available.
