    <article class="content" id="aboutRestoring-text">
     <h2>
      About Photo Restoration
     </h2>
     <p>
      <b>
       Photo restoration
      </b>
      is so important because you are restoring the captured moments of your family history for future generations to enjoy.
     </p>
     <p>
      It is likely that you have a collection of old photos but over the years they will fade and deteriorate - however by acting now an old photo can be restored. By having your photos restored, it will not only create a backup but it will give your grandchildren and great grandchildren a visual insight into their family tree. Restoring your photos is particularly rewarding if you have an interest in genealogy, as the newly restored photo may reveal information you had once never noticed.
     </p>
     <p>
      With over 10 years' photo restoring experience, your photos are in safe hands and will not affect the original. Your photo will be scanned in at a high resolution which will ensure as much detail is preserved. The scan will then be digitally restored and a proof supplied to you for your approval.
     </p>
     <br/>
     <p class="bigtext">
      Don’t let those moments fade away, have them restored today!
     </p>
     <br/>

      <h4 class="no-outline">

      </p>

     <h2 class="styleh1">
      <a class="noLinkStyle" href="https://www.photorestoration.services/blog/how-much-does-photo-restoration-cost" target="_blank">
       How much does Photo Restoration cost?
      </a>
     </h2>
     <p>
