We’re also located near Octopus Islands Marine Park, and world-renowned standing wave “Okisollo”. We are intimately familiar with this Vancouver Island wilderness kayaking mecca: Let us show you safe and easy Quadra Island sea kayaking tours, forest trails, canoe routes, and kayak camping beaches. Enjoy comfortable accommodation after a day of sea kayaking or Quadra Island outdoor adventures, or as a starting place for multi-day sea kayak tours.

You can learn basic flatwater kayak skills or high performance whitewater and tidal rapids sea kayaking. We offer kayak daytrips (everyone welcome!) in spectacular Surge Narrows Marine Park, canoe rentals, and sea kayak rentals for kayak touring and performance kayaking.
