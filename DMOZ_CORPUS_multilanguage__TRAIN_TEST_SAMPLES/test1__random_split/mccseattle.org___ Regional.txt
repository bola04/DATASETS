             <h1>
              Who We Are
             </h1>
             <p>
              More than 45 years ago in 1972, a small group of brave LGBTQ+ (Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transgender, Queer, etc.) Christians opened our doors to worship together.  It was an era when gathering in public as LGBTQ+ Christians could mean risking your life.
             </p>
             <p>
              How times have changed!  LGBTQ+ rights have advanced beyond what many original MCC members could imagine.  And yet our church still thrives. The Church and the world need our prophetic witness!
             </p>
             <p>
              Emerald City MCC continues to worship together because we preach, teach and practice
              <strong>
               unconditional inclusivity
              </strong>
              and desire for
              <strong>
               radical change
              </strong>
              not found elsewhere.  Even here in Seattle, where many churches "affirm" gay people as their members, it's still not easy sharing your WHOLE self with a congregation.  Here at MCC, we welcome and celebrate ALL of you!
             </p>
