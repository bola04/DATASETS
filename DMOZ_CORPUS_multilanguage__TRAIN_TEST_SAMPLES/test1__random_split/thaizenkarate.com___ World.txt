        <h1>
         Get Paid To Shop On Kate Thornton’s New Website
        </h1>
        <p>
         <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9vGAecDG1Hw-">
          Kate Thornton
         </a>
         is a well-known UK TV presenter. Many people will know her best from her role
         <a href="http://www.womanandhome.com/life/news-entertainment/loose-women-presenters-axed-itv-205369/">
          fronting Loose Woman
         </a>
         and The X Factor. This year, she has teamed up with some of her celebrity friends to launch a new online venture. Some of the high-profile pals helping Thornton are Myleene Klass, Amanda Byram,
         <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shaznay_Lewis">
          Shanznay Lewis
         </a>
         , Nicole Appleton, Natalie Appleton,
         <a href="https://twitter.com/denise_vanouten">
          Denise Van Outen
         </a>
         , Lisa Faulkner and Julie Graham.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Kate Thornton TBSeen
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="https://vimeo.com/249771917">
          Kate Thornton’s
         </a>
         new website is called TBSeen.com and it is an innovative cashback shopping portal that is set to compete with sites like
         <a href="https://www.topcashback.co.uk/">
          TopCashback
         </a>
         and
         <a href="https://www.quidco.com/">
          Quidco
         </a>
         . Visitors to the site can effectively get paid to shop. By clicking through to various brand websites, shoppers are awarded cash back for placing an order. The amount of cash back awarded ranges from around 5% to 15% of the order total. According to Thornton, the average family could see savings topping £800 each year by shopping through the site.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eXoEw7Is64Y">
          Kate Thornton’s new website
         </a>
         was setup as a business venture with Edd Royal, said to be the brother of one of Thorton’s friends, who was despite to create an upmarket version of current popular cashback websites like Quidco. The website’s goal was to combine a digital
         <a href="https://lifestyle.org/">
          lifestyle magazine
         </a>
         with cashback shopping from carefully chosen brands. If you visit the site now, you will see that there are already lots of lifestyle articles uploaded under the magazine tab. Articles are sorted into the following categories: style, beauty, home and life. There is also a section of the online magazine dedicated to competitions, giveaways and freebies from selected brands.
        </p>
        <h3>
         Thornton’s Success
        </h3>
        <p>
         To start earning cashback on TBSeen.com, you first need to register an account and provide your bank account details. The site needs your bank details in order to pay you any earned cashback. You can also opt to receive payouts via PayPal. Financial products, such as insurance, credit cards, loans and energy supplier tariffs, are set to payout the largest amount of cashback. You can also earn up to 12% cashback on your next
         <a href="https://www.expedia.co.uk/Holidays">
          holiday booking at Expedia
         </a>
         .

         Some of the retailers currently featured on TBSeen are Boots, Marks and Spencer, Boden,
         <a href="http://www.thewhitecompany.com/">
          The White Company
         </a>
         and Bobbie Brown. The website has a handy app that visitors can download in order to get notifications when they visit online stores where cashback is available. Visitors can also subscribe to the website’s newsletter to get updates about new deals, videos and updates.

       <h1 class="entry-title">
        <a href="http://thaizenkarate.com/taking-an-access-to-psychotherapy-course/" rel="bookmark" title="Taking an Access to Psychotherapy Course">
         Taking an Access to Psychotherapy Course
        </a>
       </h1>
       <div class="before-content">
       </div>
       <!-- .before-content -->
       <div class="content">
        <p>
         Taking an Access to Psychotherapy Course
        </p>
        <p>
         If you are a mature student who wants to study psychotherapy at university, then going on an access to psychotherapy course could be a good option.
        </p>
        <p>
         Access to psychotherapy courses are useful for people who left school a long time ago, and who need to revisit their study skills and update their qualifications in order to gain entry to a degree course. The courses cover learning skills, basic psychology, and often the ‘allied health professions’, so you might cover ethics, biology, chemistry, or behavioural issues.
        </p>
        <p>
         Typically, an access to psychotherapy course would take one year, and would prepare you to study a BSc in Psychotherapy. They can be studied part time, and the tutors will give assistance with UCAS preparations.
        </p>
        <p>
         Psychotherapy is a rewarding but challenging profession, and there is a lot to think about. You need to have an eye for detail and be a good listener and be able to maintain a professional distance from the people that you are working with. This is one area where many people fall down. If you are not able to manage your attachments or become too invested in the people you are treating, then the job could become very difficult.
        </p>
        <p>
         There are NHS jobs in Psychotherapy, and there are jobs in commercial environments too, including
         <a href="http://www.nhshealthatwork.co.uk/what-is-oh.asp">
          occupational health
         </a>
         , and counselling for people who have
         <a href="https://www.salary.com/the-top-10-most-stressful-jobs/">
          high-stress jobs
         </a>
         such as 999 responders, paramedics, firemen, or armed forces.
        </p>
        <p>
         The field is one that is varied and exciting in some ways, and that’s why so many people opt to go into it as mature employees. If you’re looking for something where no two days will feel the same, and where you’re always improving your skills and dealing with different people, then psychotherapy could be the field for you.
         <br/>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/6nEL44QkL9w" width="560">
         </iframe>
         <br/>
         It can be high stress – many people have a lot of patients, and are seeing a lot of different people each day to the point that they may worry they’re not giving enough attention to each patient. That is simply a struggle that a lot of healthcare professionals are facing, and one that each individual will have to find ways of dealing with. If there were more qualified
         <a href="http://thaizenkarate.com/neurologists-discuss-psychoanalysis/">
          psychotherapists
         </a>
         , then it is entirely possible that there would be more people able to serve those in need, and that the quality of care overall would increase in the NHS and the private sector.
        </p>
       </div>
