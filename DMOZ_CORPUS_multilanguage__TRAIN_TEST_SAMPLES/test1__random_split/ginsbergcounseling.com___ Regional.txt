          <h3>
           <strong>
            Peace.  Strength.  Fufillment.  Happiness.  Recovery.
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <h4>
           Part of being human is attempting to understand and improve our lives, to be content with our present and look boldly to a future full of possibility.
          </h4>
          <h4>
           I am a licensed psychologist, based in Denver, Colorado, helping
           <a href="http://ginsbergcounseling.com/?page_id=105" title="Individual Therapy ">
            individuals
           </a>
           ,
           <a href="http://ginsbergcounseling.com/?page_id=111" title="Couples Therapy">
            couples
           </a>
           and
           <a href="http://ginsbergcounseling.com/?page_id=113" title="Family Therapy ">
            families
           </a>
           . As a counseling psychologist and therapist committed to the well-being of my clients, I fully believe in the process of counseling to help individuals confront and overcome issues in their life and start new beginnings. I am a psychologist who naturally approaches all clients from a strength-based perspective grounded in respect, believing that this style best enhances client dignity and gives people the greatest chance at reaching their goals.
          </h4>
          <h4>
           I offer help for individuals and couples dealing with emotional, relational, physical, and social issues. Counseling is available for adults, adolescents, couples, and families.
          </h4>
          <h4>
           Counseling (also referred to as “therapy”) is an investment in yourself, and reflects a decision to work toward obtaining greater strength, peace, and fulfillment. People come to therapy from all walks of life, for a variety of reasons, but each of them is taking a step toward self-improvement and challenging themselves to find support and growth by engaging in the process.
          </h4>
          <h4>
           Sometimes clients are unsure of how they can be assisted by counseling. I take the time to explain the process to anyone who needs reassurance, whether they have been in therapy for years or sitting down for their first session.  I believe in de-mystifying counseling because it should be a comfortable place for everyone, and one in which the client has a great deal of control and say in how it unfolds.
          </h4>
          <h4>
           Meeting with a trained, professional psychologist can have an enormous positive impact on people’s lives and I am honored to be a part of people’s journeys toward increased personal power and renewed ability to make meaning of, and tackle, the ups and downs of daily living.
          </h4>
          <h4>
           I invite you to look at the services I offer and to contact me with any questions you have about counseling in general and my practice specifically.
          </h4>
