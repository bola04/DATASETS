         <p>
          Founded in 1996 by
          <a href="/about-us">
           Lynn Hayden
          </a>
          , Dancing for Him Ministries is a Spirit Filled, biblically based organization whose primary purpose is to minister to the body of Christ the heart of God through creative expressions of worship, prophesy and dance.
         </p>
         <p>
          As artistic ministers who transcribe the heart of God into an acceptable life changing form, Dancing for Him Ministries exists to teach others about this unique art form through which to spread the gospel of Jesus Christ and set captives free!
         </p>
         <p>
          We welcome and invite you to explore our website. Find out more about what prophetic dance is, the online
          <a href="/school/">
           praise dance school
          </a>
          , how you can
          <a href="/conferences/">
           join us for a worship dance conference
          </a>
          to learn how to do liturgical dance, or how to
          <a href="/conferences/">
           host a life changing conference
          </a>
          in your church for many to enjoy.
         </p>
         <p>
          If you're not able to join us for a conference, we have made it easy for you to learn how to praise dance through the many different
          <a href="/shop/">
           resources
          </a>
          offered in our online bookstore.  The Shop is filled with
          <a href="/shop/dvds-on-praise-dance-1.html">
           worship dance DVD's
          </a>
          , and
          <a href="/shop/books-on-prophetic-dance-1.html">
           books on praise/prophetic dance
          </a>
          and choreography. The online store has many
          <a href="/shop/">
           more resources
          </a>
          , now offering
          <a href="/shop/worship-flags.html">
           Flags
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="/shop/worship-flags-praise-dance-streamers-billow-banners-1.html#.U_tun0tgvzM">
           Streamers and Billows
          </a>
          , as well as a nice selection of
          <a href="/shop/praise-dance-wear-1.html">
           praise dance wear
          </a>
          . Browse the Shop today!
         </p>
