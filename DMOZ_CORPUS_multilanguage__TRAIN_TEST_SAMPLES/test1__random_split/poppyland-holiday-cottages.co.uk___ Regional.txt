      <h1>
       Poppyland Holiday Cottages, north Norfolk
      </h1>
      <h2>
       Four star, family-run self catering holiday accommodation in the villages of
       <a href="https://www.poppyland-holiday-cottages.co.uk/overstrand">
        Overstrand
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="https://www.poppyland-holiday-cottages.co.uk/wickmere/">
        Wickmere
       </a>
       &amp;
       <a href="https://www.poppyland-holiday-cottages.co.uk/aylmerton">
        Aylmerton
       </a>
       in north Norfolk
      </h2>
      <p>
       <img alt="Norfolk Countryside" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-1696" data-lazy-src="https://www.poppyland-holiday-cottages.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/norfolk_countryside.jpg" height="451" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="300"/>
      </p>
      <noscript>
       <img alt="Norfolk Countryside" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-1696" height="451" src="https://www.poppyland-holiday-cottages.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/norfolk_countryside.jpg" width="300"/>
      </noscript>
      Poppyland Holiday Cottages offer 9 individual properties in unspoilt rural North Norfolk where you can relax and enjoy the slower pace of life that is still a feature of this part of the world.
      <p>
       Offering both coastal and countryside locations, our properties are completely different in style with all of their character retained. You will always find the property of your choice comfortable, clean, warm and very well equipped so that you can enjoy your holiday.
      </p>
      <p>
       North Norfolk offers plenty to see and do for all the family. Our properties are in ideal locations and provide you with a home to self-cater or to spoil yourselves and dine at some of the many local restaurants and quaint Norfolk pubs. Whatever you wish to do, you’re sure to find it in North Norfolk. Visit and see for yourselves – you won’t be disappointed.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Please note that smoking is not permitted within any of the rooms of our properties. Dogs welcome in Swallow Cottages only, please see our terms and conditions.
       </strong>
      </p>
