      <h2>
       09.05.2018
       <a href="https://iguana.bandcamp.com/album/iguana-radio-t-live-session" target="_blank">
        via Facebook
       </a>
      </h2>
      <div class="p">
       A new live album is out now on:
       <br/>
       <br/>
       iguana.bandcamp.com/album/iguana-radio-t-live-session
       <br/>
       for those who just like to listen.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <a href="http://www.facebook.com/263188547464395" target="_blank">
        The Obelisk
       </a>
       reviewed the live video:
       <br/>
       <br/>
       "... the quality is exceptional and the performance by the band (...) is spot on..."
       <br/>
       " Three of the four cuts on Cult of Helios — “Josiah,” “Albedo” and “A Deadlock Situation” — make an appearance, and only the 15-minute title-track is left off (...), so if you haven’t heard the record, Iguana offer a chance to get introduced to their spacious but earthy flow and the peaceful vibe they present across its span while also locking into heavier nod in cuts from the debut like “Vague as a Mirage” and “Madinat al Yasmin,” which unfolds a Tee Pee-style roll and gives the oft-smiling Alexander a chance to showcase his tone alongside the two guitars."
       <br/>
