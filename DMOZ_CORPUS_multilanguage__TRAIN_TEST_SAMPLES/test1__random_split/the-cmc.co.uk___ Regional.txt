      <div class="entry clear">
       <p>
        <strong>
         What do we do
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Image front_body3.jpg" class="photo" src="/images/front_body3.jpg" style="float: right;"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Chelmsford Mountaineering Club, (The CMC) is a relatively small (approx. 70 members) but very friendly and highly active mountaineering club, with members across a wide range of ages and abilities. Our club is BMC affiliated and our ethos is to
        <strong>
         actively participate in mountaineering pursuits, in a safe and enjoyable way.
        </strong>
        We meet every week, at either Harlow Climbing Wall, the Basildon Climbing Wall. Where we are meeting next can be found on the club calender on our
        <a href="/meets.php">
         meets page.
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Our membership has a strong rock climbing interest, and we regularly run trips to the
        <strong>
         Lakes, North Wales, Peaks, Scotland
        </strong>
        and
        <strong>
         Cornwall
        </strong>
        . We actively have a go at anything from sports routes to sea stacks, to multi pitch mountain routes.
       </p>
       <p>
        Plus, we also have (and actively encourage) members who enjoy mountain walking and have trips to
        <strong>
         North Wales, Peaks,
        </strong>
        and
        <strong>
         Scotland
        </strong>
        (to name a few!) in winter and summer.
       </p>
       <p>
        Members also arrange their own trips, anything from a day down at Swanage to Alpine climbing trips, to trekking in the Himalaya, and with some members summiting in the greater ranges. See our
        <a href="/meets.php">
         meets list
        </a>
        for details of club meets.
        <strong>
         Then come along, join up and get involved.
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Image front_body.jpg" class="photo" src="/images/front_body.jpg" style="float: left;"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        There is a wealth of experience within our club that you can learn from, and we have organised training for club members including
        <strong>
         Scottish Winter Skills
        </strong>
        courses,
        <strong>
         First Aid
        </strong>
        ,
        <strong>
         Self Rescue
        </strong>
        and
        <strong>
         Navigation Skills
        </strong>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Your willingness to come along and get involved in the club and have a good time is more important than the grade you climb.
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        We also have a number of social events during the year. Ranging from going out for a Curry, Summer BBQ to our annual Christmas dinner….
        <strong>
         not to be missed!
        </strong>
       </p>
       <h3>
        <strong>
         Upcoming Club Trips…
        </strong>
       </h3>
       <p>
        28th-29th July – Peak District
        <br/>
        25th-27th August – Yorkshire
        <br/>
        22nd-23rd September – Peak District
        <br/>
        17th-18th November – Lake District
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         For more information on any of our trips, either take a look at the
         <a href="http://www.the-cmc.co.uk/meets">
          meets page
         </a>
         or
         <a href="mailto:chelmsfordmountaineeringclub@gmail.com">
          email the CMC
         </a>
         .
        </strong>
       </p>
