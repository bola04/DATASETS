                 <p style="text-align: left;">
                  <span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
                   <span style="color: #000000;">
                    The Central Shenandoah Planning District Commission (CSPDC) represents and serves the local governments of Augusta, Bath, Highland, Rockbridge, and Rockingham counties and the cities of Buena Vista, Harrisonburg, Lexington, Staunton and Waynesboro as well as the 11 towns within the Central Shenandoah region.
                   </span>
                  </span>
                 </p>
                 <p style="text-align: justify;">
                  <span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">

Covering everything from land use planning, transportation, water and waste-water utilities, natural resource management, affordable housing, economic and community development, disaster mitigation and preparedness, agritourism to human services, the CSPDC is an invaluable asset to the quality of life in the Shenandoah Valley.
