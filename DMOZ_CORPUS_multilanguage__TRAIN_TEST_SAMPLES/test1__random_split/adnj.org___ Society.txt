        <span class="style76">
         About the Academic Decathlon
        </span>
       </font>
       <span class="style29">
        <br/>
        The Academic Decathlon is a series of interscholastic competitions for high school teams nationwide. The Decathlon encourages, recognizes
 and rewards academic effort and excellence.
        <br/>
        <br/>
       </span>
       <font color="#800000">
        <span class="style76">
         It’s All Teamwork
        </span>
       </font>
       <span class="style29">
        <br/>
       </span>
       <font class="style29" size="2">
        The Decathlon tests knowledge and academic skills - but isn’t just for the class genius. One of the unique features of the Decathlon is that each
 team represents a range of achievement levels. The team consists of three students who have "A" averages, three with "B" averages and three
 with "C" averages. To prepare for competition, the team members begin working and studying together early in the fall. On competition day, all
 nine team members take the same tests, participate in the same events, and are judged by the same criteria. The team’s final score depends
 on the performances of ALL its members.
        <br/>
       </font>
       <span class="style29">
        <br/>
       </span>
       <font color="#800000">
        <span class="style76">
         The Lineup
        </span>
       </font>
       <span class="style29">
        <br/>
        The Decathlon competition consists of the following 11 events:
       </span>
      </p>
      <span class="style75">
       <ul>
        <li>
         <font class="style29" size="2">
          Seven written, multiple-choice tests (mathematics, science, 
 	music, social science, economics, literature, and fine arts)
          <br/>
         </font>
        </li>
        <li>
         <font class="style29" size="2">
          Speech (prepared and impromptu)
          <br/>
         </font>
        </li>
        <li>
         <font class="style29" size="2">
          Written essay
          <br/>
         </font>
        </li>
        <li>
         <font class="style29" size="2">
          Interview by a panel of business persons
          <br/>
         </font>
        </li>
        <li>
         Super Quiz (the only public portion - a truly exciting climax to the day-long competition)
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        <font color="#800000">
         <span class="style76">
          State and National Competition
          <br/>
         </span>
        </font>
        <font class="style29" size="2">
         Three regional 
 competitions are held New Jersey in late January or February. The regional winners take part in the State Decathlon 
 about a month later. The highest scoring team represents New Jersey at the 
 United States Academic Decathlon. Other high scoring teams can compete in 
 national online competition.
         <br/>
         <br/>
        </font>
        <font color="#800000">
         <span class="style76">
          The New Jersey Experience
         </span>
        </font>
        <span class="style29">
         <br/>
         High schools in New Jersey have been participating in the Academic Decathlon for 
 more than 30 years. Newcomers are welcomed each year.
         <br/>
         <br/>
        </span>
        <font color="#800000">
         <span class="style76">
          The Funding Process
         </span>
        </font>
        <span class="style29">
         <br/>
         ADNJ is supported through registration fees paid by participating high schools and corporate contributions. We are grateful for the support of
 our friends in business and industry who make education a high priority.
        </span>
       </p>
