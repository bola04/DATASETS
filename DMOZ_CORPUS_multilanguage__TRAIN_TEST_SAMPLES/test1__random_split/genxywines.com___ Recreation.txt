      <h2>
       Explanation of GenXY Wine Review Rankings
      </h2>
      <p>
       (*****) National or regional benchmark for variety,
 gold medal standard, a prima donna that demands worship
      </p>
      <p>
       (****1/2) Outstanding quality and style, silver
 medal standard
      </p>
      <p>
       (****) Stands out from the pack, bronze medal
 standard
      </p>
      <p>
       (***1/2) Better than average, good varietal
 expression or interesting innovation
      </p>
      <p>
       (***) The Pack, solid boutique wine
 making level showing consistent varietal
 expression and free of faults, generally will complement rather than dominate food
      </p>
      <p>
       (**1/2) Competent winemaking at the bulk
 commercial wine level, price is important, late night drinking after you have drunk the more expensive stuff
      </p>
      <p class="paragraph">
       (** and less) Showing technical faults or nasty
 edges, only buy to give to people you really dont like
      </p>
