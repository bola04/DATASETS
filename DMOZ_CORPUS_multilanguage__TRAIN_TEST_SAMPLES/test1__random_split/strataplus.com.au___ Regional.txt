     <h1>
      Strata Plus – a personal approach to strata management
     </h1>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
      Strata Plus specialises in strata management in Sydney and throughout NSW. We provide tailored management solutions for strata and community schemes and building management committees. We care about our clients and are transparent in all our business transactions. Our clients are at the heart of everything we do and their needs are the focus of our personal approach and tailored service.
     </p>
     <p>
      We have
      <a href="http://www.strataplus.com.au/contact/">
       five offices
      </a>
      located in Central Sydney, Parramatta, Newcastle, Tweed Heads and Port Stephens, employing a team of fifty led by Managing Director, David Ferguson, and Executive Director, Olivera Ferguson.
     </p>
     <p>
      Over the past few years, our commitment to excellence has been recognised through Strata Community Australia (NSW) Awards. We have been selected as recipients of three strata awards, have won highly commended Large Strata Management Business for two years running, and have had a number of finalists in the owner engagement category.
