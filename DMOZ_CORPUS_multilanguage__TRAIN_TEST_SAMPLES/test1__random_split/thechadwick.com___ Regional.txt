         <h1>
          <span class="nowrap">
           Welcome to
          </span>
          <span class="nowrap">
           The Chadwick
          </span>
          <span class="nowrap">
           Bed &amp; Breakfast
          </span>
         </h1>
         <h2 style="text-align: center;">
          The New Standard of Bed &amp; Breakfast Luxury
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          The perfect getaway in Portland, Maine, The Chadwick Bed &amp; Breakfast provides thoughtful touches that invite a sense of pampering and romance in unsurpassed comfort and elegance. With endless events and activities offered in the
          <a href="/recommendations">
           Greater Portland
          </a>
          area, you can keep active or spend time relaxing in the comfort of this classically appointed home-away-from-home.
          <em>
           Do as little or as much as you’d like.
          </em>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Whether you are visiting Maine for a long weekend, vacation, honeymoon, anniversary, wedding, or on business, The Chadwick Bed &amp; Breakfast strives to provide a level of personal service that goes above and beyond.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Innkeepers &amp; Owners E Scot Fuller-Beatty &amp; Jason Fuller-Beatty, Innkeeper Erin Abraham, Assistant Innkeeper Carney Brewer, and the rest of The Chadwick Bed &amp; Breakfast staff take great pride in providing tastefully appointed
          <a href="/rooms">
           guest rooms
          </a>
          —each with a private bathroom—beautifully landscaped grounds, and a five-star gourmet
          <a href="/breakfast">
           breakfast
          </a>
          each and every morning. We can’t wait to have you experience it for yourself!
         </p>
