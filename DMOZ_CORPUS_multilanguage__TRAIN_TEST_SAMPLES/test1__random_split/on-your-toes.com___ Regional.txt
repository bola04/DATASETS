             <div class="sqs-block-content">
              <h3>
               For the love of:
              </h3>
              <h1>
               Dance
              </h1>
              <p>
               Our goal is to provide the finest in dance programming for students ages 2 - 18.  We take great pride in our community and we believe that our dance school is more than just a place to learn dance.  We also hope we can inspire and motivate students to set high standards for themselves in and out of the studio, and we aim to help them reach those goals.  It is a testament to our sense of community and our commitment to the whole child that many of our dancers have taken their first steps with Miss Tricia in her Parent &amp; Tot classes and have continued to dance at
               <em>
                On Your Toes
               </em>
               through high school.
              </p>
             </div>
            </div>
