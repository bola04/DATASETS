        <h2>
         Clearwell Caves, a uniquely special home to so many
         <strong>
          amazing
         </strong>
         things!
        </h2>
        <div class="cushycms">
         <p>
          Set in a beautiful valley, this extensive natural cave system has been mined for iron ore and pigments for more than 4,500 years, making Clearwell Caves one of the most ancient, complex and fascinating mine systems in Britain. Today, you explore impressive caverns, with exciting displays throughout and immerse yourself in the hidden world lying deep beneath the Forest of Dean.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          "No visit to the Forest of Dean is complete without seeing the Caves" - a recent visitor comment.
         </p>
         <h2 style="text-align: center;">
          Lots of dates have been added to our special TRY CAVING events. Join an experienced mine guide for a fun and exciting trip, venturing far beyond the show Caves, on an exciting caving trip. Booking details on our
          <a href="http://www.clearwellcaves.com/deep.html">
           events page
          </a>
          .
         </h2>
