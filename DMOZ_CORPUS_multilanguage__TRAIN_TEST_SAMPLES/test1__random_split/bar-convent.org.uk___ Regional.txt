      <h1>
       THE BAR CONVENT is England's oldest living  convent, founded in 1686 as a school for girls. It is still home to members of  the Congregation of Jesus today.
      </h1>
      <p>
       The Grade 1 Listed eighteenth-century buildings house a state-of-the-art
       <a href="exhibition.htm" title="Exhibition">
        Exhibition
       </a>
       , an  award-winning
       <a href="cafe.htm" title="Cafe">
        Cafe
       </a>
       and highly-rated
       <a href="accommodation.htm" title="B&amp;B Guest House">
        Guest House
       </a>
       , plus newly  refurbished
       <a href="meetings_functions.htm" title="Conference Facilities">
        Conference facilities
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       Whether you're here to visit the Exhibition, to relax in the Cafe and  garden or to enjoy a city break in historical
       <a href="local_area.htm" title="Historic York">
        York
       </a>
       , we're sure you'll enjoy your time with us.
      </p>
      <p>
       The Bar Convent welcomes those of all faiths and none.
      </p>
