        The Environmental Health Foundation of Canada (EHFC) is a not-for-profit organization established in 1989, for advancing environmental public health in Canada and internationally through charitable, education and research initiatives.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        The activities of EHFC are coordinated by a Board of Trustees.  Members come from the environmental public health profession, educational institutions, government and industry.  Trustees give their time, experience and expertise to the administration of the Foundation.
