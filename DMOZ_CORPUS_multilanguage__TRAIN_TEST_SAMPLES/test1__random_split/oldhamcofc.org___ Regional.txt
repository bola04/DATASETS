       <h1>
        ABOUT OLDHAM COUNTY
       </h1>
       <p>
        Oldham County, rich in the history of Route 66 and Old Tascosa, is located 20 miles west of Amarillo. We have huge herds of cattle, miles of crops, wide-open ranchland and prairies, lots of cowboys, oil and wind energy, genuine people, and home to the small communities of
        <a href="http://www.oldhamcofc.org/index.php?page=vega-tx">
         Vega
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://www.oldhamcofc.org/index.php?page=adrian-tx">
         Adrian
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://www.oldhamcofc.org/index.php?page=wildorado-tx">
         Wildorado
        </a>
        , and
        <a href="http://www.oldhamcofc.org/index.php?page=boys-ranch-tx">
         Boys Ranch
        </a>
        . The stars really do shine brighter here! Come see for yourself.
       </p>
       <p>
        While here, make sure to visit our many
        <a href="http://www.oldhamcofc.org/index.php?page=museums">
         AREA ATTRACTIONS
        </a>
        , eat at one of our unique home-spun
        <a href="http://www.oldhamcofc.org/index.php?page=resaurants-and-cafes">
         RESTAURANTS
        </a>
        , shop at our
        <a href="http://www.oldhamcofc.org/index.php?page=gift-shops">
         SPECIALTY STORES
        </a>
        , and make plans to attend one of our
        <a href="http://www.oldhamcofc.org/index.php?page=events">
         ANNUAL EVENTS
        </a>
        .
       </p>
