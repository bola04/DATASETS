          <h2>
           EMS in short
          </h2>
          <p>
           The EMS is the association of Meteorological Societies in Europe. The network consists of 37 Member Societies and 30 Associate Members. The EMS is a non-profit-making organisation. The
           <a href="/meetings-events/ems-annual-meetings/">
            EMS Annual Meetings
           </a>
           attract some 600 people each year from all sectors of the field. With a number of
           <a href="/awards/" title="Awards">
            Awards
           </a>
           outstanding contributions to the science, its applications and communication are honoured;  young scientists are supported through
           <a href="https://emetsoc.org/awards/award-category/young-scientist-travel-awards/" title="Young Scientist Travel Awards">
            travel grants
           </a>
           .
          </p>
