         <h2>
          River rafting fun
         </h2>
         <p>
          The Colorado River through the Grand Canyon has intrigued the minds and lives of men since first being conquered by John Wesley Powell in 1869. We offer whitewater rafting trips through the Grand Canyon, Cataract Canyon and the Salmon River from three to 12 nights long.
         </p>
         <p>
          The desert beauty found along the Colorado River in Grand Canyon and Cataract Canyon are unmatched. The rapids along the Colorado River are some of the biggest, most exciting rapids in the world!
         </p>
         <p>
          In one of America’s most scenic wilderness areas lies the Main Fork of the Salmon River, also known as the “River of No Return.” The Salmon River flows through the continent’s second deepest gorge surrounded by majestic pine trees, clean sandy beaches, and plenty of exciting whitewater.
         </p>
        </div>
        <div class="col-md-4 about-content">
         <h2>
          Whitewater rafting adventure
         </h2>
         <p>
          Rafting trips are an exciting adventure and have proven to be great get-togethers for both family and friends alike. The Grand Canyon, Cataract Canyon, and the Salmon River are three of the premier rafting trips in North America.
         </p>
         <p>
          We have taken many people on whitewater rafting trips for several different occasions. Some combined their family reunions with a rafting trip, while others came on company retreats. No matter what the occasion we would love to share this experience with you.
         </p>
