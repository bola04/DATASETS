       <h1>
        Psiholog In Bucuresti
       </h1>
       <p>
        <span style="color: #0000ff;">
         <strong>
          <span style="font-size: 14px;">
           <span style="font-family: verdana,geneva,sans-serif;">
            <span style="line-height: 1.6em;">
             Deviza noastra: Orientare catre client, profesionalism, confidentialitate !
            </span>
           </span>
          </span>
         </strong>
        </span>
       </p>
       <div class="review-item-container ng-scope">
        <div class="review-text-container">
         <div class="ng-binding">
          <span style="font-family: verdana, geneva; font-size: 14px;">
           <span style="line-height: 1.6em;">
            Cabinetul este dotat cu programe de testare psihologica avizate de c
           </span>
          </span>
          <span style="line-height: 1.6em; font-family: verdana, geneva; font-size: 14px;">
           atre Colegiul Psihologilor din Romania
          </span>
         </div>
        </div>
       </div>
       <p>
        <strong style="text-align: justify; line-height: 1.6em;">
         <span style="line-height: 1.6em; font-size: 14px;">
          Cabinetul este agreat de ca
         </span>
        </strong>
        <strong style="text-align: justify; line-height: 1.6em;">
         <span style="line-height: 1.6em; font-size: 14px;">
          tre Ministerul Transporturilor si Infrastructurii – certificat nr.67/2012
         </span>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <span style="color: #ff0000;">
          <span style="font-size: 14px;">
           <span style="line-height: 1.6em;">
            Pentru a oferi beneficiarilor nostrii servicii eficiente si complete, colaboram cu centrul medical autorizat pentru eliberarea fisei medicale pentru angajare, a fisei medicale permis auto, a fisei
           </span>
          </span>
         </span>
        </strong>
        <strong>
         <span style="color: #ff0000;">
          <span style="font-size: 14px;">
           <span style="line-height: 1.6em;">
            medicale portarma si a avizului medical pentru soferi profesionisti/functii siguranta circulatiei.
           </span>
          </span>
         </span>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <span style="font-size: 14px;">
          <span style="line-height: 1.6em;">
           <span style="color: #ff0000;">
            Acelasi program, aceeasi locatie!
           </span>
           <a href="https://www.testpsihologic.info/fise-si-avize-medicale/" title="Fise si Avize Medicale">
            <span style="color: #ff0000;">
             Detalii
            </span>
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: 14px;">
         <span style="line-height: 1.6em;">
          Cabinetul Psihologic Davidescu Mihaela va ofera o gama larga de servicii psihologice:
         </span>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: 14px;">
         <span style="line-height: 1.6em;">
          <strong>
           <a href="https://www.testpsihologic.info/test-psihologic-auto-bucuresti/" title="Aviz psihologic auto">
            Psihologia transporturilor
           </a>
           :
          </strong>
          se adreseaza angajatorilor care au ca obiect de activitate transporturile sau persoanelor fizice care doresc sa obtina permisul de conducere auto si presupune testare psihologica a conducatorilor auto.
         </span>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: 14px;">
         <span style="line-height: 1.6em;">
          In cadrul psihologiei transporturilor, efectuam si testari psihologice pentru
          <strong>
           <a href="https://www.testpsihologic.info/test-psihologic-soferi-profesionisti/" title="Soferi profesionisti">
            soferi profesionisti
           </a>
          </strong>
          / functii implicate in
          <strong>
           <a href="https://www.testpsihologic.info/test-psihologic-soferi-profesionisti/" title="Siguranta circulatiei">
            siguranta circulatiei rutiere
           </a>
          </strong>
          , la scolarizare, angajare si mentinere functie.
         </span>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: 14px;">
         <span style="line-height: 1.6em;">
          <strong>
           <a href="https://www.testpsihologic.info/aviz-psihologic-angajare-bucuresti/" title="Examinare psihologica angajare">
            Psihologia muncii si organizationala:
           </a>
          </strong>
          vizeaza colaborarea cu sectorul de medicina muncii in concordanta cu legea privind securitatea si protectia muncii. Metodele psihologice in acest domeniu sunt utilizate in vederea evaluarii, selectiei si expertizei psihologice la angajare/mentinere in functie sau in scopul avizarii psihologice, pentru diagnoza si intenventie organizationala, precum si realizarea consultantei psihologice pentru managementul resurselor umane.
         </span>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: 14px;">
         <span style="line-height: 1.6em;">
          <strong>
           <a href="https://www.testpsihologic.info/aviz-psihologic-arma/" title="Testare psihologica port arma">
            Psihologia aplicata in domeniul securitatii nationale:
           </a>
          </strong>
          se adreseaza in special angajatorilor care au personal care necesita aviz de port arma, firmelor de paza si protectie sau persoanelor fizice ce doresc sa obtina permisul port arma si presupune test psihologic port arma.
         </span>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: 14px;">
         <span style="line-height: 1.6em;">
          <strong>
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            <a href="https://www.testpsihologic.info/psihologie-clinica/" style="font-family: verdana, geneva;" title="Psihologie Clinica">
             Psihologie clinica:
            </a>
           </span>
          </strong>
          se focalizeaza pe promovarea sanatatii psihice si preventia patologiei, acordand atentie factorilor psihologici implicati in starea de sanatate si de boala  (psihodiagnostic si evaluare clinica) si culminand cu interventia si tratamentul psihoterapeutic si al tulburarilor cu etiologie de ordin psihologic si/sau psihosomatic. Sunt implicate toate etapele de varsta (copil, adolescent, adult, varstnic), fiecare cu particularitatile proprii, care tin mai ales de dezvoltarea intelectuala si a personalitatii.
         </span>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: 14px;">
         <span style="line-height: 1.6em;">
          <strong>
           <a href="https://www.testpsihologic.info/consiliere-psihologica-si-psihoterapie/" title="Consiliere psihologica si psihoterapie">
            Psihoterapie cognitiv comportamentala:
           </a>
          </strong>
         </span>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        –  situatii de criza existentiala (deces, divort, somaj, toleranta redusa la schimbare, etc)
       </p>
       <p>
        –  depresie, anxietate, fobii, atacuri de panica
       </p>
       <p>
        –  adictii, tulburari de alimentatie (anorexie, bulimie, obezitate)
       </p>
       <p>
        –  evaluare/testare psihologica (in sensul autocunoasterii si dezvoltarii personale, orientare scolara si profesionala)
       </p>
       <p>
        –  tulburari de comportament ale copiilor.
       </p>

         <span style="font-size: 14px;">
          <span style="line-height: 1.6em;">

            Supervizare profesionala in domeniul psihologiei muncii si transporturilor
           </a>
          </span>
