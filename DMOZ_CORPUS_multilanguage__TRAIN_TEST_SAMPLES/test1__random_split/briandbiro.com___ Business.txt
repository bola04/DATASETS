          <h3 class="module-title">
           Notes From the Road
          </h3>
          <p>
           Starting in 2016, instead of publishing a calendar we thought it would be fun to publish thoughts and comments from Brian experiences on the road. He's one of America's busiest and in-demand motivational speakers so there are always lots of things to report. Here's a full run-down of his most recent Notes from the Road.
          </p>
          <hr/>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h5 style="line-height: 15.808px;">
           June 28, 2016
          </h5>
          <p>
           Last week I had the joy and privilege to do my 30th presentation for PriceWaterhouseCoopers at the Hilton in downtown Atlanta. The energy was supercharged as 500 top recruits for the dynamic firm along with almost 100 PWC staff broke through with me to end their three day Elevate event. This is one of the most exciting, fun, and fast-paced events I do each year and I LOVE it!  The PWC team is first class and the recruits are the best of the best!
          </p>
          <p>
           Two days later I did another event I do each year for young people. These are high school students at the Rotary Youth Leadership event. Several years ago a wonderful long-time Rotary volunteer named Rod Webb asked if I would work with the high school students. He was so passionate about the kids, so kind and giving, I happily agreed to gift the event. Rod passed away not long after that first event and I have continued to gift this event each year when my schedule allows. Rod's daughter Jennifer has taken up the mantle for Rod and it is an honor to serve her and these awesome young leaders in memory of Rod.
          </p>
          <p>
           Happy Independence Day to all!
          </p>
