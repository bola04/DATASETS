             2018-2019 Committee Selection
            </font>
           </h2>
           <p>
            The Women’s Bar Association of Maryland, Inc. has several Standing Committees. If anyone is interested in serving on any of these Committees in 2018-2019, please contact Maria Twigg at execdirector@wba-md.org or 888-858-9958.
            <a href="http://www.wba-md.org/fileupload/Committee Selection.pdf">
             See attached form for committee descriptions and more information.
            </a>
           </p>

         <tr>
          <td>
           <h2>
            <font color="932400">
             WBA Capturing History
            </font>
           </h2>
           <p>
            Women report being subjected to overt and subtle gender bias and discrimination. The WBA wants to capture these stories to educate others and to learn effective techniques in battling gender bias and discrimination. We are looking for both current stories and events that have occurred in the past. Therefore, we are seeking your stories of incidents of gender bias and/or discrimination.
            <a href="http://www.wba-md.org/fileupload/Capturing History (revised).pdf">
             If you have a story, please complete the form and send it to Maria L. Twigg at mlt.wba@gmail.com.
            </a>
           </p>
