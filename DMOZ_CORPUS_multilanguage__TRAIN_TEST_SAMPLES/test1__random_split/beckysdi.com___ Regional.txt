      <div id="home_intro">
       <div>
        <p>
         Becky's was voted "Best Drive-In Theater" in the Lehigh Valley area in the
         <a class="external" href="http://www.mcallcommunity.com/flipbooks/ReadersChoice/2016/index.html#p=22" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">
          Morning Call's Readers Choice Awards
         </a>
         since the category was created in 2007.  Becky's was also recognized in 2016 in
         <a class="external" href="http://www.10best.com/awards/travel/best-drive-in-movie-theater" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">
          USA Today's 10 Best Drive-In Theaters
         </a>
         , coming in as the 5th best drive-in theater in the USA.  Becky's was also recognized by being voted "The Best of the Best" in Showmanship by the Drive-In Theater Fan Club in 1996, and was also included in the New York Times' "
         <a class="external" href="http://www.nytimes.com/2001/05/13/arts/10-drive-ins-worth-a-detour.html" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">
          10 Drive-Ins Worth a Detour
         </a>
         " by Don and Susan Sanders in 2002. This family run business is located in Walnutport PA, (Lehigh Township) on Route 248 approximately 11 miles North of Allentown, PA, 20 miles West of Easton, PA and 15 miles from Bethlehem, PA.
        </p>

      <h1>
       Q&amp;A
      </h1>
      <dl id="faq_list">
       <dt>
        When do your movies start?
       </dt>
       <dd>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Movies start
         <em>
          approximately
         </em>
         at dusk.  Exact showtimes are typically posted a few days in advance.  Please check our
         <a href="../schedule" title="Schedule">
          schedule
         </a>
         for exact showtimes.
        </p>
       </dd>
       <dt>
        How do I get sound?
       </dt>
       <dd>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         We broadcast sound for the movies over FM radio.  You can get sound using your car radio or a portable radio.  Tune to 90.7 FM for screen 1 and 106.5 FM for screen 2.
        </p>
        <p>
         We also have portable radios available for rent.  These can be rented from our screen 1 projection booth.  A radio rental costs $3.00, and we require a valid driver's license or other form of identification to hold while the radio is rented.
        </p>
       </dd>
       <dt>
        Do you accept credit cards?
       </dt>
       <dd>
        <p>
         Yes, we accept all forms of major credit cards at our box office and refreshment centers.
        </p>
       </dd>
