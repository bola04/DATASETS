        <span id="u9341">
         Stefan Maier Tracker Organs
        </span>
        offers high quality pipe organ service to clients all over New England, and beyond.
       </p>
       <p class="Main-headings-H1" id="u9341-9">
        We care for some of the
        <span id="u9341-5">
        </span>

         <span id="u9341-6">
          more renowned organs
         </span>
        </a>
        in our home state of Massachusetts, such as the Old North Church, Mechanics Hall, Harvard University, and Wellesley College.
       </p>

        Our services range from
        <a class="nonblock" data-href="page:U92" href="servicing.html">
         regular servicing and tuning
        </a>
        to building, repairs, and full restorations. Our shop is fully equipped to do
        <span id="u9341-14">
        </span>
        <a class="nonblock" data-href="page:U98" href="rebuildrestore.html">
         <span id="u9341-15">
          tonal, wood, leather, metal, and electrical work
         </span>
        </a>
        <span id="u9341-17">
         .
        </span>
        We are also experienced in moving and rigging large instruments.
       </p>

        We enjoy rendering our services to the small local country church as much as to the more prominent institutions, and strive to keep services customized precisely to a client's needs and budget.
