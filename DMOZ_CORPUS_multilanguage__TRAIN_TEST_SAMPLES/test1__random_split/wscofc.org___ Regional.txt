         </h1>

        <div class="entry-content">
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://wscofc.org/site/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/Slide07.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-989 size-large" height="450" sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" src="http://wscofc.org/site/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/Slide07-600x450.jpg" srcset="http://wscofc.org/site/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/Slide07-600x450.jpg 600w, http://wscofc.org/site/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/Slide07-300x225.jpg 300w, http://wscofc.org/site/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/Slide07.jpg 720w" title="Slide07" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          Welcome to the website of West Side Church of Christ in Elkton, KY(
          <small>
           <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?oe=utf-8&amp;client=firefox-a&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;q=715+West+Main+Street+Elkton,+KY+42220&amp;fb=1&amp;gl=us&amp;hnear=&amp;cid=0,0,13412732173035522922&amp;ei=Ic0ZS6_VNM2Ttgff2MzNBA&amp;ved=0CAgQnwIwAA&amp;hq=725+West+Main+Street+Elkton,+KY+42220&amp;ll=36.812995,-87.16939&amp;spn=0.008246,0.013733&amp;z=16&amp;iwloc=A&amp;source=embed">
            View Map
           </a>
          </small>
          ).  We have recently undertaken changes to our site, and we are very hopeful you will enjoy the revisions made. In the
          <a href="http://wscofc.org/site/3183/calendar/calendar/" title="Calendar">
           Calendar
          </a>
          link above,  you will find current birthdays and anniversaries of our members along with most of our scheduled church activities. We also invite you to attend any of our services and/or activities at any opportunity you may have. We are located just one mile west of the historic Elkton Town Square.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          The first services of the West Side Church of Christ were held in the former Elkton Elementary School on March 29, 1964. Construction of the present building began in 1965 with the first service held on September 12, 1965. Later an addition was made to the current church building to enlarge the auditorium and provide much needed classroom space. In 1999, a Fellowship Hall was erected on adjoining property. To read more about the history and work of West Side, click on the
          <a href="http://wscofc.org/site/blog-2/" title="Blog">
           Blog
          </a>
          link above for a recent article.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          We hope sincerely that God will richly bless you in all that you do!
         </p>
