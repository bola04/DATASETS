             <td style="width: 350px;">
              <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
              </h2>
              <p>
               Reverend Diana Kuhl has served as Part Time Visitation Pastor of Holy Cross from June 1, 2013 to September 15, 2016. She also served as the Associate Pastor at Peace Presbyterian Church in Elkhorn, NE. Prior to ordination and her call as Associate Pastor at Peace she had served as their Christian Educator. Pastor Diana had served at a Lutheran church in Fremont for seven years as the Youth, Family, and Hispanic Ministry Director.
              </p>
              <p>
               She is a graduate of Midland Lutheran College in Fremont, Ne. and received her Seminary Education at Lutheran Seminary Program of the Southwest in Austin, Texas, with a focus in Hispanic Ministry and Outreach.
              </p>
              <p>
               She and her husband, David, a Senior Account Analyst for Ford Motor Credit in Omaha, have three grown children and three grandchildren. Their daughter Lisa, and her husband Adam, and their son Blaine (5
               <sup>
                th
               </sup>
               grader) live in Valley. Their daughter, Heather and her husband Brian, and their children Levi (3
               <sup>
                rd
               </sup>
               grader) and Lillian (age 4) live in Fremont, and their son, James lives in Lincoln.
               <br/>
               Pastor Di loves spending time with family and friends.

              <h1>
               Welcome to Shepherd of the Hills Lutheran Church
              </h1>
             </td>
            </tr>
            <tr>
             <td>
              <p style="text-align: justify;">
               Our prayer is that you would be blessed and strengthened by the power of Jesus Christ and that you will live a life of abundance in fellowship, joy and liberty.  Shepherd of the Hills Lutheran Church is here for you. It is more than a church. Shepherd of the Hills is a fellowship of believers coming together to declare the glory of the Lord and celebrate Jesus as King. We study the Word, practice what we learn, and in the process, grow together.
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: justify;">
               We fulfill our mission by reaching out to our surrounding community through monthly community dinners (3rd Tuesday of the Month) and providing gardening plots for surrounding neighbors.   We partner with
               <a href="http://www.projecthopeomaha.org/">
                <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
                 <strong>
                  Project Hope
                 </strong>
                </span>
               </a>
               as they use our building and distribute pantries from 11 a.m. to 2:45 p.m. every Monday, Wednesday and Friday.
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: justify;">
               We also have many youth and adult activities designed to be fun and informative in the name of the Lord.  We hold dinners, gardening groups, women's groups and more.   For more information, see our
               <a href="/images/2016_Annual_Report-Shepherd-of-the-Hills.pdf">
                <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
                 Annual Report for 2016 activities
                </span>
                .
               </a>
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               <strong>
                COME JOIN US
               </strong>
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               <strong>
                SUNDAYS AT 9:00 AM FOR CHRISTIAN EDUCATION AND 10:00 AM FOR WORSHIP SERVICES
                <br/>
               </strong>
              </p>
