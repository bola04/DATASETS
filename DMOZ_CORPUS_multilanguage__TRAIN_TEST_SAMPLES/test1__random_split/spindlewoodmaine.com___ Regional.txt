         <h1 class="entry-title">
          Home
         </h1>
        </header>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <h2 style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://spindlewoodmaine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Spindlewood-Logo.jpg">
           <img alt="Spindlewood Kindergarten Logo" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-61" height="56" src="http://spindlewoodmaine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Spindlewood-Logo.jpg" width="256"/>
          </a>
         </h2>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <strong>
           A Waldorf Kindergarten from 1986 – 2016.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <strong>
           Now hosting overnight guests through Airbnb!
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="https://www.airbnb.com/rooms/14968123?s=51">
           https://www.airbnb.com/rooms/14968123?s=51
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <strong>
           _______________________________________________________
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          The Spindlewood experience is a balanced program that weaves active, outside movement and quiet, inward activities into a beautiful fabric that brings comfort and security to the children, and develops a foundation for lifelong learning.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://spindlewoodmaine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_00000061.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="alignright wp-image-319 size-medium" height="225" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="http://spindlewoodmaine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_00000061-300x225.jpg" srcset="http://spindlewoodmaine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_00000061-300x225.jpg 300w, http://spindlewoodmaine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_00000061.jpg 330w" width="300"/>
          </a>
          The outdoor play yards include a shallow frog pond, a flowing stream, plenty of shade trees as well as plenty of space for running, digging, gardening, swinging and climbing. Well-designed play structures and a quiet, shaded sand box provide a balanced outdoor play environment. When outdoors, the teachers might do a gardening project with the children, or lead them on a nature walk through our beautiful woodlands.
         </p>
         <p>
          During circle time, the teacher leads the children on movement journeys to verse and song, strengthening their physical development and language acquisition. At story time, the teacher creates a magical environment that allows the children to delve into their imagination and travel on a rich, picturesque journey through the spoken word. During indoor play time, the teachers are often actively engaged in purposeful work, allowing the children to enter deeply into imaginative play, while the teacher observes and facilitates if needed. The children help prepare and serve a wholesome mid-morning meal of grains, fruits and vegetables.
         </p>
         <p>
          Spindlewood’s cottage is on a quiet road. Walking paths lead past
          <a href="http://www.silverioarchitectureanddesign.com/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Silverio Architecture and Design">
           <strong>
            John Silverio’s architecture and painting studio
           </strong>
          </a>
          . Also on the property are Susan and John’s home and gardens, as well as a barn and pasture with three sheep and three hens. The peaceful and natural setting invites children to explore and interact with the natural world and with each other at their own developmental pace.
         </p>
         <p>
          Located in the center of Lincolnville, Maine, Spindlewood began in 1986 as the first home of
          <a href="http://ashwoodwaldorf.org" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Ashwood Waldorf School">
           <strong>
            Ashwood Waldorf School
           </strong>
          </a>
          , which now has a separate campus in Rockport.
         </p>
         <p>
          Spindlewood invites and welcomes children and families of any race, religion, sexual orientation, gender identity, and national or ethnic origin. ________________________________________________________________________________________________
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Susan Silverio 105 Proctor Road, Lincolnville, ME 04849- 207-763-4652
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Member of the
          <a href="http://maineindependentschools.com" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Independent Schools Association of MIdCoast Maine">
           <strong>
            Independent Schools Association of Mid-Coast Maine
           </strong>
          </a>
         </p>
