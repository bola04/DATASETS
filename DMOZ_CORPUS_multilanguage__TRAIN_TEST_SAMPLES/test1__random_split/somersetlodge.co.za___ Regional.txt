          <h2>
           Welcome to Somerset Lodge
          </h2>
          <div class="news-text">
           <p style="text-align: justify;">
            <img alt="" class="alignleft" height="93" src="http://somersetlodge.co.za/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/temp_img_2.jpg" width="140"/>
            Somerset Lodge is a spacious family owned hotel and guesthouse with a warm friendly atmosphere and comfortable en suite bedrooms. It is the oldest recognised resting spot for travellers in the area, the original Cape Dutch farmhouse dates back to 1850. What was the original horse and carriage changing station in Somerset West in 1895, Somerset Lodge is now a popular destination for both business and holiday travellers.
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: justify;">
            Somerset Lodge offers a warm welcome to all our guests and is ideally located for business or pleasure. Each of the 14 rooms is individually decorated and has its’ own charm and character. All have telephones, satellite television, en suite bathrooms and tea and coffee making facilities. Friendly staff,secure parking and free Wi-Fi all add to your enjoyable stay at Somerset Lodge. Choose from our choice of single, twin, double or family rooms. Also available is our popular honeymoon suite.
           </p>

          <h2>
           BED &amp; BREAKFAST
          </h2>
          <div class="news-text">
           <p style="text-align: justify;">
            <img alt="" class="alignleft" height="93" src="http://somersetlodge.co.za/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/breakfast.jpg" width="140"/>
            Somerset Lodge is graded as a 3 star accommodation venue by the Tourism Grading Council of South Africa and is on the AA Travel recommended accommodation list. It combines a hotel and guest house feel and is ideal for business travelers with all the facilities you require under one roof- accommodation, restaurant, bar, secure parking, and free Wi-Fi. Breakfast is healthy and hearty and we are noted for our home style cooking and tasty meals. Enjoy breakfast, lunch or dinner with us. Lunch packs for business travelers can be pre-ordered.
           </p>
