        <h2>
         Our History
        </h2>
        <p>
         In 1984, Feller, Finch and Associates, Inc. was founded by Donald Feller and Richard Finch. They wanted to create an engineering firm that would be known in the industry for providing quality, precise and dependable services with long term client satisfaction as their primary goal. They achieved this by retaining a staff of experienced professionals committed to maintaining their clients‘ schedules within budget guidelines. Through “word of mouth” referrals, FFA has grown steadily. The firm is now licensed in 5 states and handles approximately 1000 work assignments per year.
        </p>
        <p>
         We are licensed in: GA, KY, MI, OH, PA, SC, TN
        </p>
