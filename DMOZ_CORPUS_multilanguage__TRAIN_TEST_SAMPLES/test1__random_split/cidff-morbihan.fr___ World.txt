        L'agenda du CIDFF Morbihan
        <span class="agenda">

        </span>
       </h3>
       <div class="event_home vevent">
        <a class="type_event category event_6" href="/l-association/prochains-evenements?tid=6" title="Voir les événements Action collective">

          <span class="value-title" title="2018-01-23">
          </span>

          </span>

         <p>
          Atelier de "Préparation à l'Engagement Professionnel et Social"
         </p>
         <div class="details_event">
          <p style="background-color: transparent; color: #4c4e51; font-family: &amp;quot; arial&amp;quot;; font-size: 14.73px; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: 22.54px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; margin-top: 0px; orphans: 2; text-align: left; text-decoration: none; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; white-space: normal; word-spacing: 0px; padding: 0px;">
           Cette action collective est destinée :
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <ul style="background-color: transparent; color: #000000; font-family: &amp;quot; arial&amp;quot;; font-size: 12.8px; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; margin-bottom: 15px; margin-left: 15px; margin-right: 0px; margin-top: 10px; orphans: 2; text-align: left; text-decoration: none; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; white-space: normal; word-spacing: 0px; padding: 0px;">
           <li style="background-attachment: scroll; background-clip: border-box; background-color: transparent; background-image: url('http://www.cidff-morbihan.fr/sites/all/themes/cidff/images/puce_li.jpg'); background-origin: padding-box; background-position-x: left; background-position-y: 7px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-size: auto; color: #4c4e51; font-size: 15px; list-style-image: none; list-style-position: outside; list-style-type: none; margin-bottom: 5px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; margin-top: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; padding-left: 13px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;">
            <span style="font-family: Calibri; font-size: 16px; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
             A des femmes très éloignées de l’emploi, sans idée de projet mais dans l’expression d’une volonté de travailler,
            </span>
           </li>
           <li style="background-attachment: scroll; background-clip: border-box; background-color: transparent; background-image: url('http://www.cidff-morbihan.fr/sites/all/themes/cidff/images/puce_li.jpg'); background-origin: padding-box; background-position-x: left; background-position-y: 7px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-size: auto; color: #4c4e51; font-size: 15px; list-style-image: none; list-style-position: outside; list-style-type: none; margin-bottom: 5px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; margin-top: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; padding-left: 13px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;">
            <span style="font-family: Calibri; font-size: 16px; padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
             A des femmes en difficulté dans un accompagnement social ou professionnel en cours
            </span>
           </li>
           <li style="background-attachment: scroll; background-clip: border-box; background-color: transparent; background-image: url('http://www.cidff-morbihan.fr/sites/all/themes/cidff/images/puce_li.jpg'); background-origin: padding-box; background-position-x: left; background-position-y: 7px; background-repeat: no-repeat; background-size: auto; color: #4c4e51; font-size: 15px; list-style-image: none; list-style-position: outside; list-style-type: none; margin-bottom: 5px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; margin-top: 0px; padding-bottom: 0px; padding-left: 13px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;">
            <span style="padding: 0px; margin: 0px;">
             A des femmes ayant été suspendues du dispotif
            </span>
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p style="background-color: transparent; color: #4c4e51; font-family: &amp;quot; arial&amp;quot;; font-size: 14.73px; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: 22.54px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; margin-top: 0px; orphans: 2; text-align: left; text-decoration: none; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; white-space: normal; word-spacing: 0px; padding: 0px;">
           Le but de cette action est de se projeter dans l'accompagnement social et professionnel. Pour ce faire, on vise à :
          </p>
