         <h1>
          Maine Saltwater Fishing Charter
         </h1>
         <p>
          Diamond Pass Outfitters is your premier Maine saltwater fishing charter offering a range of saltwater fishing trips for your enjoyment! We offer both
          <a href="/inshore-fishing/">
           inshore fishing trips
          </a>
          and
          <a href="/deep-sea-fishing/">
           offshore deep sea fishing trips
          </a>
          depending on what you desire and the best conditions at the time. This will give you several different fishing trip options depending on the seasons, the type of fishing you prefer, and your experience. This is your fishing trip and we can tailor the trip to ensure that you have the best trip possible.
         </p>
         <p>
          Of course, the fishing is what we are there for, but the boat ride itself is an experience not to be missed with the spectacular scenery and beauty of the rugged Maine coast as our backdrop. Add to that spectacular fishing for a variety of species and what more can you ask for? We offer
          <a href="/inshore-fishing/striped-bass/">
           striped bass fishing trips
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="/inshore-fishing/bluefish/">
           bluefish fishing trips ,
          </a>
          <a href="/inshore-fishing/">
           mackerel and Pollack fishing trips
          </a>
          <a href="/inshore-fishing/bluefish/">
           ,
          </a>
          <a href="/cod-haddock/">
           cod and haddock fishing trips
          </a>
          <a href="/inshore-fishing/bluefish/">
           ,
          </a>
          <a href="/offshore-fishing/sharks/">
           shark fishing trips
          </a>
          <a href="/inshore-fishing/bluefish/">
           , and
          </a>
          <a href="/offshore-fishing/bluefin-tuna/">
           bluefin tuna fishing trips
          </a>
          <a href="/inshore-fishing/bluefish/">
           . Depending on the time of year and the conditions, you have a lot of options. So climb aboard and let Captain Luis Tirado show you the fantastic Maine Saltwater fishing that awaits!
          </a>
         </p>
