        <div id="about-us-more-wrapper">
         <p class="large-orange">
          Our Story
         </p>
         <p>
          Back in 1986, pursuing a career in the great outdoors Colin and his wife Kirstie were travelling in a their Landrover towing a 4m-long caravan to provide catering for a Toyota commercial in New Zealand’s back-country with the late Barry Crump.
         </p>
         <p>
          Colin drove around a sweeping corner near Glenorchy and a trestle strapped to the roof flew off. It landed perfectly set up in an adjacent paddock, so the company name ‘Flying Trestles’ was born!
         </p>
        </div>
        <div id="about-us-more-wrapper">
         <p class="large-orange">
          Mobile Kitchens
         </p>
         <p>
          Our fleet of professional mobile kitchen trucks, “Flying Kitchens”, enable us to operate effectively anywhere in New Zealand providing cuisine to match any restaurant, in places where nobody else can. We have specially designed kitchen trucks to suit any event, style or function, enabling our chefs to prepare Extraordinary food where you’d least expect it.
         </p>
         <p>
          Our Flying Kitchens are totally self-contained, equipped with generators, water, gas and waste tanks, walk-in refrigerator, six hobs, gas ovens and dishwashers. This enables us to go anywhere from our Queenstown and Auckland locations.
         </p>
         <p>
          Our Chefs are highly professional and talented with extraordinary repertoire of food knowledge, style and creativity.
         </p>
        </div>

           <h2>
            TSS Earnslaw
           </h2>
           <p>
            <strong>
             TSS Earnslaw – celebrated 100 years – 1912-2012
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            The TSS Earnslaw was launched on Lake Wakatipu in 1912 and is the oldest coal-fired passenger steamship in the Southern Hemisphere, she celebrated 100 years of outstanding service on 18th October 2012 with commemorative sailings which re-enacted the maiden voyages from Kingston to Queenstown and from Queenstown to Glenorchy, including a nostalgic visit to the high country lakeside stations which the vintage steamer serviced for almost 70 years.
           </p>
           <p>
            Flying Trestles was overwhelmed to be given the opportunity to cater for
            <strong>
             The Woolshed Party at Mount Nicholas Station
            </strong>
            on the far shores of Lake Wakatipu for a special 100th birthday bash.

         </h2>
         <p>
          We have many talented chefs with amazing repertoire and knowledge of food and cultural styles.
         </p>

It’s such a wonderful experience having your own personal chef, describing each dish whilst creating a work of art in front of you.

          <h2>
           Our Food
          </h2>
          <p>
           <strong>
            We can provide any style of food for your event, function or private party. We’ve built  up an amazing repertoire over the years, everything from traditional French through to Asian and European. We love to work with ingredients from every continent and often you’ll see a fusion of ideas from our chefs working on a menu.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           We always use fresh seasonal and local produce wherever possible and make our own sauces, dressings and marinades. We’re passionate about flavours and and love creating new ideas. Our menus incorporate only the best products, such as Wakanui and Savannah beef, Harmony free range chicken &amp; pork and NZ premium free range lamb (1stClass) usually only available to the export market and very sought after.
          </p>
          <p>
           Please do take a moment to look at our sample menus to give you inspiration (help inspire your taste buds) or let our head chef and event stylist help design a tantalising menu specifically for you or to perfectly match your event.
          </p>
