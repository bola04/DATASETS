              <h1>
               DVD Production, Web Video, Corporate &amp; Training DVD, &amp; Multi Media
              </h1>
              <br/>
              Aura Production's award winning team are characterised by excellence in creative thinking and project management in an integrated style.
              <br/>
              Aura Productions produce corporate, training and educational programmes, TV adverts, animation and documentary programmes. In recent times there is an increasing emphasis on producing web video.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              All of these are output for broadcast, DVD, web and other digital media. We are a fully equipped production house with a diverse client base both within Ireland and internationally.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              Operating since 1994 the company has developed vast expertise in film production with an environmental themes.
              <br/>
              Our long time clients include; Department of Education &amp; Skills, ESB Networks, The Dept. of Agriculture Food &amp; the Marine, Kepak, the HSA, Boston Scientific, Bord Bia, Bord na Mona, Teagasc to name just a few.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              New clients to Aura Productions include AIB, tourism groups, Novartis, and Landrover, Ogra Skincare.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              Our full 'in house' set up allows us to offer a wide range of services from full commercial and broadcast productions to dynamic visual presentations. We also record for radio using our state of the art studio recording facilities.
