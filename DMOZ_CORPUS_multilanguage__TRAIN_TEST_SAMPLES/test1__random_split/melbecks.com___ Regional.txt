         <h1 class="entry-title">
          Bassenthwaite Cottages
         </h1>
        </header>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <div class="at-above-post-homepage addthis_tool" data-url="https://melbecks.com/">
         </div>
         <p>
          Choose from our selection of five pet-friendly Bassenthwaite cottages at Melbecks. All of our holiday cottages welcome pets – we just vary the number of dogs each property accommodates according to its size. Melbecks is situated at the foot of Skiddaw and just 1 mile from Bassenthwaite, and just 7 miles from Keswick. Our self-catering cottages sleep between 2 and 6, providing combined accommodation for 20 people.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Pet-Friendly Cottages
         </h2>
         <p>
          <a href="http://melbecks.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Little-Dorrit-1.jpg">
           <img alt="Little Dorrit, one of Gracie's puppies" class="size-medium wp-image-297 alignright" height="300" sizes="(max-width: 160px) 100vw, 160px" src="https://melbecks.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Little-Dorrit-1-160x300.jpg" srcset="https://melbecks.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Little-Dorrit-1-160x300.jpg 160w, https://melbecks.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Little-Dorrit-1.jpg 546w" width="160"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          At Melbecks we look forward to welcoming you and your dog(s). The Lake District is a great place to bring your dog and we can provide advice on where you can walk your dog locally, on and off the lead. If you have stayed with us and have photographs of you and your dog holidaying in the Lake District, then just forward them to davidmelbecks@gmail.com
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           Uldale – sleeps 6 people and up to 3 dogs
          </li>
          <li>
           Skiddaw – sleeps 4 people and up to 2 dogs
          </li>
          <li>
           Dash – sleeps 4 people and up to 2 dogs
          </li>
          <li>
           Dodd – sleeps 4 people and up to 2 dogs
          </li>
          <li>
           Randel – sleeps 2 people and 1 – 2 dogs
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          If you don’t find the cottage you are looking for here, then try our sister website that has many more cottages and farmhouses,
          <a href="http://www.lakelandcottages.co.uk/petfriendly.html" rel="noopener" target="_blank" title="Dog friendly cottages in the lakes">
           click here for dog-friendly Lake District cottages
          </a>
          . They have a selection of pet-friendly holiday cottages in Keswick, Borrowdale and the surrounding villages of Braithwaite, Bassenthwaite.
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_451" style="width: 910px">
          <a href="http://melbecks.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/Bassenthwaite-Cottages-At-M.jpg">

           Bassenthwaite Cottages – Dodd Cottage

         <p>
          Our Bassenthwaite cottages have also accommodated Guinea Pigs, Cats, Rabbits and Parrots. If you have a particular request then please do
          <a href="http://melbecks.com/contact-us/" title="Contact Us">
           get in touch with us
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <h2>
          Bassenthwaite
         </h2>
         <p>
          Bassenthwaite is a working village situated on the old coaching route between Keswick and Carlisle. It is approximately 8 miles from Keswick and 1 mile from Bassenthwaite Lake. It has a thriving local community, with several working farms in the village itself. There is a seasonal camp shop at the Caravan Park. The village pub is the Sun Inn and is pet-friendly. A small stream runs through the village, alongside which is a small children’s playground. There is a village green where the local children play football and other games.
         </p>
