                       New Umbrella Brigade
                      </a>
                     </h3>
                     <div class="post-header">
                      <div class="post-header-line-1">
                      </div>
                     </div>
                     <div class="post-body entry-content">
                      Whitey has teamed up with members of the Brickbats (with whom Stiffs Inc split a 7" single and often performed back in the day) to release a new
                      <a href="http://www.umbrellabrigade.com/">
                       Umbrella Brigade
                      </a>
                      album called Marchen. The previous Umbrella Bridade wasn't really to my taste, but I
                      <i>
                       love
                      </i>
                      the new one - it's still more electronic than anything by Stiffs Inc, but sounds like a logical next album after
                      <i>
                       Electric Chair Theatre
                      </i>
                      would have sounded. I feel almost as though I'm listening to a new Stiffs Inc album with this one!
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      From the
                      <a href="http://www.umbrellabrigade.com">
                       website
                      </a>
                      you can get a special version that includes a book with a CD, or you can get it for 9.99 on iTunes. Â I hope to have more info about this soon; for now, here's the video for "Take Back the Witch."
