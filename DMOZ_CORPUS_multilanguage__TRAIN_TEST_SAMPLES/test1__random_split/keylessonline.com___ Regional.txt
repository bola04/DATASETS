     <p class="just">
      Welcome to KeylessOnline.com! The idea of creating this website started back in 1999 with the intention of providing notes and chords for famous indian songs (films, devotional, pop) - to share keyboard notes to the public, add newer songs to quench the thirst of song requestors - best of all, to make them all FREE!. Most of the songs in this site have been through song requests from all of you. I am extremely grateful for your patronage over the years. It is this wonderful response that gives me the energy and zest to continue providing this service.
     </p>
     <p class="just">
      If you would like to request notes and/or chords for any song, please use the
      <a href="/request.php">
       Request-A-Song
      </a>
      link. Since I do this as a hobby in my spare time (which keeps shrinking by the day), I may not be able to get to your request right away. But I will try my level best to get to your song request sometime in the future. Please do provide your email-id at the time of song request so I can try to notify you, once your requested song is published on my website. It takes a lot of time and effort to publish these and is done completely on a voluntary basis. I would appreciate your cooperation and patience.
     </p>
     <p class="just">
      If you have any questions, please use the
      <a href="/contact.php">
       Contact Us
      </a>
      link to submit your queries. Before submitting though, please make sure your question is not already answered in the
      <a href="/faq.php">
       FAQ
      </a>
      section. I have made a sincere attempt of compiling frequently asked questions in there, which you may find useful.
     </p>
     <p class="just">
      Thanks for visiting and your continued patronage. Any comments, suggestions and feedback are most welcome.
     </p>
     <p class="just">
      If you like and find the contents of this site useful, your invaluable donations to some non-profit charity organizations like Sankara Eye Foundation and Udhavum Karangal would be most appreciated. Small drops do make a big ocean.
     </p>
     <p class="just">
      Thanks,
      <br/>
      VK
     </p>
