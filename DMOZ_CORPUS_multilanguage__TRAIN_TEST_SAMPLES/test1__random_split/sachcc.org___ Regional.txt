       The Mexican American Chamber of Commerce was founded in 1972 to address California’s changing population, its economy and the impact each had on the local Hispanic community. It focused on educational and economic opportunities in California and the United States. In 1985, the Mexican American Chamber became the Sacramento Hispanic Chamber of Commerce to reflect the growing population of a diverse Mexican/Latino community.
