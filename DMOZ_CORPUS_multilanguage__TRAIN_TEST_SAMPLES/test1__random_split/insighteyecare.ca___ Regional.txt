       <h1 class="entry">
        SEE the facts about your vision and eye health
       </h1>
       <ul>
        <li>
         Did you know that
         <strong>
          every 12 minutes in Canada, someone starts to lose their vision.
         </strong>
         <em>
          When was the last time you had your eye health checked?
         </em>
        </li>
        <li>
         Did you know that
         <strong>
          1 in 9 Canadians suffer from irreversible vision loss by the age of 65
         </strong>
         – that is equal to the number of women affected by breast cancer.
        </li>
        <li>
         Did you know that
         <strong>
          <strong>
           75% of vision loss can be prevented or treated.
          </strong>
         </strong>
         <p>
          <strong>
          </strong>
          <strong>
           <strong>
           </strong>
          </strong>
         </p>
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        By visiting your Optometrist in Surrey BC on a yearly basis for your
        <a href="http://www.insighteyecare.ca/eye-examinations">
         routine eye examination
        </a>
        , eye diseases such as glaucoma and macular degeneration can be detected earlier before damage occurs to your vision. At Insight Eyecare – Doctors of Optometry, our eye doctors are committed to maintaining the best vision and eye health in our patients. At our South Surrey BC eye clinic, we have state of the art equipment, including a digital retinal camera and a
        <a href="http://www.insighteyecare.ca/visual-field-testing">
         visual field analyzer
        </a>
        , that aid in earlier detection of eye diseases.
       </p>
       <p>
        Our highly advanced
        <a href="http://www.insighteyecare.ca/digital-retinal-photography">
         retinal camera
        </a>
        takes a picture of the back of your eye and can help detect the early stages of diseases such as glaucoma, macular degeneration, retinal diseases, diabetes, high blood pressure, high cholesterol and much more. Our retinal camera is capable of stereo-fundus photos which are beneficial in diagnosing the early stages of glaucoma. Approximately 250,000 Canadians suffer from some form of glaucoma and only half of them are actually aware that they have the disease.
       </p>
       <p>
        At Insight Eyecare, our Surrey Optometrists and eye doctors recommend that all children should be examined by age of 6 months and yearly thereafter because 80% of a child’s learning is based on how they see. Did you know that 1 in 4 children have a vision disorder? This is the main reason why our eye doctors in Surrey BC at Insight Eyecare do not charge any additional fees for
        <a href="http://www.insighteyecare.ca/childrens-vision-care">
         children’s eye exams
        </a>
        . A child’s vision is crucial in their development and learning.
       </p>
       <p>
        Insight Eyecare carries the latest
        <a href="http://www.insighteyecare.ca/designer-eyewear">
         designer frames
