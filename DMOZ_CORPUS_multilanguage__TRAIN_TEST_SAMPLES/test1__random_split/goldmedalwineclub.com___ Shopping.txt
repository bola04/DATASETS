      <h2>

       <h3>
        The Gold Wine Club
       </h3>
       <p class="lead">
        Established in 1992, the Gold Medal Wine Club was one of the first
        <a href="/wine-club-history">
         wine of the month clubs
        </a>
        in existence and remains as one of the oldest, most respected, independent wine clubs in the country. Monthly
        <a href="/wine-club/gold">
         Gold Wine Club
        </a>
        members are offered a wide variety of award-winning wines from California‚Äôs best, family owned,
        <a href="/blog/post/beauty-of-small-batch-winemaking-130">
         boutique wineries
        </a>
        in each of their shipments.  Our various wine of the month clubs feature wines from over 150 different wineries each year. Since inception, we have introduced our wine club members to over 2,000 wineries and over 3,000 different wine selections from every
        <a href="/regions">
         wine region
        </a>
        throughout California.
       </p>
       <h3>
        The Platinum Wine Club
       </h3>
       <p class="lead">
        In the summer of 1993, the
        <a href="/wine-club/platinum">
         Platinum Wine Club
        </a>
        was introduced alongside the existing Gold Wine Club as an option for members to experience an even higher level of wine quality. This ultra-premium wine of the month club features rare, collectable wines from California's top winemakers and only includes wines with 90+ ratings. On occasion, special pre-release wines and
        <a href="/store/item/bouch√©-2004-champagne-exclusive-direct-import-from-france">
         exclusive offerings
        </a>
        are made available only to Wine Club members. Through this monthly wine club, over 1,000 different wines have been featured since its inception and continues to grow each month.
       </p>
       <h3>
        The Diamond Wine Club
       </h3>
       <p class="lead">
        The third stage of growth for Gold Medal Wine Club happened in 1995 with the addition of the
        <a href="/wine-club/diamond">
         Diamond Wine Club
        </a>
        , taking member experience to the next level. Only 93+ rated, top-of-the-line, collectable wines with impeccable credentials are delivered to Diamond Wine Club members. These legendary wines are made by some of the world's top winemakers and due to the sparsity and limited production of these wines, the Diamond Wine Club is available to only 500 fortunate members. Over 260 incredible wines have been featured in this quarterly wine club so far.
       </p>
       <h3>
        The International Wine Club
       </h3>
       <p class="lead">
        In September of 2005, Gold Medal Wine Club began exclusively importing international wines from small, hidden wine estates around the world, thereby starting the
        <a href="/wine-club/international">
         International Wine Club
        </a>
        . Each shipment showcases two awesome red wines and one amazing white wine, and since initiating the international adventure in
        <a href="/region/italy">
         Italy
        </a>
        , it has since taken members on a wine tasting journey around the world. The
        <i>
         Taste the Adventure
        </i>
        newsletter includes information about the featured wines, winery history,
        <a href="/regions">
         wine regions
        </a>
        ,  and more. The International Wine Club was elevated to another level when our exclusive,
        <a href="/special/international-wine-club-australia-adventure-package">
         Gourmet Adventure Package
        </a>
        was created. One of the very first subscription boxes of its kind, this collection of tasty treats is comprised of imported food items from the same country as the wines. Since inception, this club continues to be a member favorite.
       </p>
       <h3>
        The Pinot Noir Wine Club
       </h3>
       <p class="lead">
        Since its debut in 2006, the
        <a href="/wine-club/pinot-noir">
         Pinot Noir Wine Club
        </a>
        quickly became a go-to wine club for new and existing members alike who share a passion for Pinot Noirs. Two different bottles of highly-rated
        <a href="/varietal/pinot-noir">
         Pinot Noirs
        </a>
        were originally shipped every-other-month. However, after its enthusiastic reception, this wine club is now shipped on a monthly basis. Today, members of the Pinot Noir Wine Club enjoy hard-to-find, limited-production Pinot Noirs that are some captivating and world-class. From the
        <a href="/store/search?bog=us&amp;_filter:RegionID=12&amp;SearchKeys=RegionID:Russian River Valley AVA:12">
         Russian River Valley
        </a>
        to the
        <a href="/region/sta.-rita-hills-ava">
         Sta. Rita Hills AVA
        </a>
        in California, or the
        <a href="/region/willamette-valley-ava">
         Willamette Valley
        </a>
        in Oregon to even a international import now and again, this wine of the month club is definitely worthy of your attention.
       </p>
       <h3>
        The Garagiste Wine Club
       </h3>
       <p class="lead">
        In 2014, Gold Medal Wine Club launched its newest wine club program: The
        <a href="/wine-club/garagiste">
         Garagiste Wine Club
        </a>
        . This unique wine club not only embraces independent winemakers, but also celebrates their creative spirit that has resulted in the production of fascinating wines in extremely small quantities. Due to this, the wines in the Garagiste Wine Club are virtually impossible to find in the marketplace. In order for a winery to be featured in this intriguing wine of the month club, the overall winery production cannot exceed 1,500 cases annually. If you love the thought of finding under-the-radar, fascinating wines
        <i>
         before
        </i>
        they are discovered, the Garagiste wine of the month club is the perfect choice. Garagiste Wines are mostly from California but occasionally from Oregon and Washington.
       </p>

     var updateExpirationDate = (function() {
     expiration = $('#updateCreditCard').find('input.cc-exp');
 		expiration.parents('.form_row').removeClass('error');
     if(!$.payment.validateCardExpiry(expiration.payment('cardExpiryVal'))) {
       expiration.parents('.form_row').addClass('error');
     } else {
       var ccExp = expiration.payment('cardExpiryVal');
       var eDate = new Date( (new Date(ccExp.year, ccExp.month,1))-1 );
       $('.cc-exp-hidden').val( eDate.getFullYear() + '-' + (eDate.getMonth() + 1) + '-' + eDate.getDate() );
       $.ajax({
         url:'/api/input/submit', 
         type:'post',
         data: $('#updateCreditCardForm').serialize(),  
         dataType:'json'
               }).done(function(res) {
         if (res.Successful) {
                   window.location.reload();
         } else {
           var msg = res.Message;
           alert(msg);
         }
       });
     }
     return false;
   });
