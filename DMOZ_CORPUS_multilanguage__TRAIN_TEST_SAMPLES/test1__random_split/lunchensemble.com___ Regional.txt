            <p align="center">
             <font size="2">
              If there's a fire burning, or a lesson needs 
 some learning, or a tide that needs some turning to
              <br/>
              a better world somewhere,  the Old Folkie's there
              <i>
               ...
              </i>
              -
              <i>
               Harry 
 Chapin
              </i>
             </font>

            <p>
             <font face="Verdana" size="2">
              Our Mission and Outreach: (1) addressing issues 
 of hunger and poverty through awareness and financial support; (2) involving 
 children and youth in programs which show them they can make a difference; (3) 
 helping kids to grow into tomorrow's leaders who will shape a world free of 
 intolerance, injustice, and indifference.
             </font>
             <font face="Verdana" size="2">
              Through our partnerships with the
              <font color="#BA500E">
               <b>
                <a href="http://www.billpere.com/SongwritingAcademy/connecticut_songwriting_academy.htm" target="_blank">
                 <u>
                  Connecticut Songwriting Academy
                 </u>
                </a>
               </b>
              </font>
              and the
              <u>
               <b>
                <a href="http://www.ctsongs.com" target="_blank">
                 <u>
                  Connecticut Songwriters 
 Association
                 </u>
                </a>
               </b>
              </u>
              , we help young people develop their potential in all 
 areas of music, theater, and community leadership. Our flagship programs are the 
 LUNCH Ensemble and the Harry Chapin Legacy Concerts.
             </font>
            </p>
            <p align="left">
             <img align="left" border="0" height="50" src="images/wheat-bullet.jpg" width="20"/>
             <font face="Verdana" size="2">
              LUNCH is a non-profit, all-volunteer, tax exempt 
 501(c)(3) charitable organization.  LUNCH is non-denominational, 
 encompassing all faiths, beliefs, and cultures.
             </font>

            <p align="justify">
             <img align="left" border="0" height="50" src="images/wheat-bullet.jpg" width="20"/>
             <font face="Verdana" size="2">
              Founded in 1989 by
             </font>
             <font face="Times New Roman">
              <a href="http://www.billpere.com" target="_blank">
               <b>
                <u>
                 Bill Pere
                </u>
               </b>
              </a>
             </font>
             <font face="Verdana" size="2">
              as a community service outreach of the Connecticut 
 Songwriters Association, and spun off as an independent charitable organization 
 in 1999, LUNCH was inspired by the work of the late songwriter Harry Chapin, who 
 founded World Hunger Year and who devoted much of his music career to the fight 
 against hunger, receiving a posthumous Congressional Medal. Our first event, The 
 Harry Chapin Legacy Show, was held in 1991.
             </font>
