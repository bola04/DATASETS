      <h5>
       This site contains black &amp; white and color industrial photography of 
 		
 		abandoned and active subway trains and tunnels, power plants, mills/factories, railroad yards, and other unusual places. My photos are about intense light in dark spaces and forms materializing from that darkness. 
 		
 		Abandoned places are my cathedral. I am at home here, I feel alive, remorseful, accepted. Each visit is like passing through myself.
