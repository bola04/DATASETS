          <div>
           <p>
            Welcome to eera – the epidemiology, economics and risk assessment group.
           </p>
           <p>
            We are based at the
            <a href="http://www.roslin.ac.uk/" target="_self">
             Roslin Institute
            </a>
            , part of
            <a href="http://www.ed.ac.uk/" target="_self">
             Edinburgh University’s
            </a>
            <a href="http://www.vet.ed.ac.uk">
             veterinary school
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <p>
            We study disease in animal populations to improve animal and human health
           </p>
           <p>
            Our work involves field studies in the tropics, Europe, farms and markets throughout the United Kingdom; laboratory analysis and computer based modelling and simulations.
           </p>
           <p>
            We answer questions as simple as ‘Is the population diseased?’  to as complex as long time-scale exotic disease import risk predictions.
           </p>
          </div>
