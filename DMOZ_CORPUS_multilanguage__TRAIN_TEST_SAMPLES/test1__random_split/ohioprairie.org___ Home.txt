       <span class="C-6">
        <span class="C-7">
         The Ohio Prairie Association is a non-
         <wbr>
          profit volunteer organization that promotes knowledge, appreciation, conservation, restoration, management, and expansion of Ohio prairie communities and their native plant and animal species to individuals, conservation organizations, public agencies, educational institutions, and others with an interest in native ecosystems.
         </wbr>
        </span>
       </span>
      </span>
     </p>
     <p class="Body">
      <span class="C-5">
       The OPA Vision Statement
       <span class="C-7">
