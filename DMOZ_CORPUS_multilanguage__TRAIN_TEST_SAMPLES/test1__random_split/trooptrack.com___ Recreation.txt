        TroopTrack: for Scouters, by Scouters
       </h1>
       Our staff know scouting and software development. We're not just
 technologists who are experts in the software industry. We also have
 a wealth of experience in Scouting that we use every day to make TroopTrack better.
       <div class="row-fluid">
        <div class="span4">
         <img alt="Dave" class="img-polaroid" src="https://d3thk2na9w8m59.cloudfront.net/assets/dave-a6ada3b17ac5542eaedb4e98d5c61f851b60d9c9dc8f232e396c8731cc61978b.jpg"/>
         Dave, our CEO, is a former Boy Scout and is currently an associate Venturing advisor. He
 has been a Varsity coach, pack committee chair, and troop committee chair. He has been
 writing software professionally and for fun since he was 13. He has written articles for
 major software development magazines and spoken at conferences around the country on
 software development.
        </div>
        <div class="span4">
         <img alt="Ryan" class="img-polaroid" src="https://d3thk2na9w8m59.cloudfront.net/assets/ryan-1e54c0c4090e9cd50a41a27da582031c374091aafd09ce06c1ec2ab32c22260b.jpg"/>
         Ryan, our Chief Technology Officer, is an amazing software developer
 who also happens to be capable of kicking virtually anything to pieces. He recently was a Gold Medalist in the
 USAT Taekwondo Nationals and a Bronze Medalist in the US Open Taekwondo Championships.
 He has made significant contributions in the software industry and is a recognized
 expert of web application development.
        </div>
        <div class="span4">
         <img alt="Interns" class="img-polaroid" src="https://d3thk2na9w8m59.cloudfront.net/assets/interns-81e535695ff2f7c9d0285713493be5f5542bfc3ba8408d527e75b107f3db8458.png"/>
         Spencer, shown above, was the first Scout to join our crew of interns. He is an
 Eagle Scout and recipient of the Honor Medal Lifesaving Award for risking his own
 life to save his scout leader's life. Our other interns include Sam, Coleman, and
 Melissa. Sam and Coleman are both former Boy Scouts. Melissa is the mother of
 a Boy Scout and has served as a Wolf Den Leader and Bear Den Leader.

       Records Management Doesn't Have to Be a Pain
      </h1>
      <p>
       As a scout leader, you know the challenge of keeping track of everything you need to know about your Troop or Pack. It's easy to lose track of contact information, medical forms, equipment borrowed by a scout or scouter, books in your library, training, etc. TroopTrack has lots of features that make keeping up-to-date easier, including:

       <li>

       </li>

       </li>
       <li>

       </li>
       <li>
        Customizable reports
       </li>
       <li>
        Money management at the unit and scout level
       </li>

       <h1>
        Need to send an email to the wolf den? Piece of cake!
       </h1>
       <p>
        Your TroopTrack subscription includes a customizable email list server that makes it easy to communicate with the whole troop or just parts of it. TroopTrack automatically creates a few email lists for you to make it easy to communicate with parents, leaders, scouts, patrols, and dens, but you can also add your own. Better still, you can control who can use the lists so that your email lists don't get taken over by spammers.
       </p>
       <p>
        TroopTrack has an announcement feature you can use to display a headline whenever a troop member logs in. Some units use this feature to communicate a monthly theme, other's use it to remind members of an upcoming event or deadline.
