         <h1>
          The Colonnade Hotel in Boston
         </h1>
         <h2>
          Experience Modern Luxury in Back Bay
         </h2>
         <p>
          Centrally located and exquisitely appointed in the iconic Back Bay neighborhood, The Colonnade Hotel in Boston is the contemporary luxury hotel of choice for the modern traveler. Nestled at the intersection of a quaint residential community and Boston’s main shopping districts in Copley Square and Newbury Street, our Back Bay address offers prime access to the Boston Symphony, Hynes Convention Center, Northeastern University and nearby transit, allowing you to get out and
          <a href="/location" onclick="">
           explore the best of Boston
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          The Colonnade Hotel in Boston wants to offer a heightened experience to each of our guests, whether you're
          <a href="/rooms/business-travelers" onclick="">
           traveling for business
          </a>
          or pleasure. Upon arrival, be greeted by a variety of luxurious
          <a href="/about">
           amenities
          </a>
          you’re unlikely to find at other hotels in Boston, including our gourmet on-site restaurant,
          <a href="/brasserie-jo" onclick="">
           Brasserie JO
          </a>
          , which offers flavorful cuisine for every palate, a fully-equipped
          <a href="/about/fitness-center" onclick="">
           fitness center
          </a>
          , and our renowned
          <a href="/roof-top-pool" onclick="">
           rooftop pool
          </a>
          , known as RTP, which offers an idyllic escape from the city alongside sweeping views of the Boston skyline.
         </p>
         <p>
          After days and nights exploring major
          <a href="/location/boston-attractions" onclick="">
           Boston attractions near The Colonnade
          </a>
          , enjoy a peaceful retreat to one of our modern and spacious
          <a href="/rooms">
           guestrooms
          </a>
          where you’ll discover an abundance of natural sunlight and unobstructed views of the Prudential Center and Back Bay. Enjoy an indulgent night’s rest atop a plush pillow-top mattress and when you awake be sure to enjoy a variety of other in-room comforts including a 37-inch flat-screen LCD TV, Keurig coffee brewers, dimmable lighting, an abundance of outlets and complimentary
          <strong>
           ULTRA high-speed wired &amp; wireless Internet
          </strong>
          access to help you stay connected while you’re away from home.
         </p>
         <p>
          Experience a new kind of hotel in Boston, imbued with New England hospitality, East Coast charm, and accessible luxury. Only at The Colonnade.
         </p>
