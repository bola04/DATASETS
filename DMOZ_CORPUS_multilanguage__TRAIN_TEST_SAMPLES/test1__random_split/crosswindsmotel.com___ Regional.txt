       Convenient Location
      </h3>
      <p>

 			to offer. The world famous mile-long Rehoboth Beach Boardwalk, Funland and Dolle‘s Candyland, are just 3 blocks

       <a href="http://crosswindsmotel.com/rehoboth-beach-dogfish-head/">
        we‘re within stumbling distance of Dogfish Head Brewpub
       </a>
       (only 2 doors down!).
      </p>
      <p>
       We know you didn‘t come to Rehoboth Beach to hang out inside. Just a short walk in any direction and you‘ll 
 			be playing in the waves and the sand, strolling through downtown shops and boutiques or sampling delicious 
 			delights at local restaurants. Instead of wondering what to do, you‘ll be deciding what to do next!
      </p>
