 I began my career as photographer in 1980. Very soon I dedicated myself to advertising photography, working with cameras of large format, lighting equipments, and learning the maximal rigor and precision demand in the production and accomplishment of the photographic captures. This background has determined the search for Excellency in each one of my professional actions  
 In the mid eighties I started to explore in special effects in photography; this proved to be very useful at the moment when I switched, about the mid nineties, to the digital world. 
 Today, all of my work evolves around the digital world, from the initial capture to the post-production. 
 Furthermore, in addition to the commercial space, I have regained my inclination for a more personal and artistic photography.
 
 Marcelo Kohn Fotografía es un estudio de fotografía digital publicitaria dedicado a la producción y realización de encargos fotográficos para campañas publicitarias en formato digital, desde la captura hasta la post producción. Productores especializados en diversas areas  como: alimentos, modelos, productos, locaciones e industria, posibilitan una gran diversificación
