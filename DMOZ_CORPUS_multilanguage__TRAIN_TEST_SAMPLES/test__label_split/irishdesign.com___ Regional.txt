       Welcome

      <div class="swirl wow fadeInUp">
      </div>

     <article class="post-22 page type-page status-publish has-post-thumbnail hentry" id="post-22">
      <div class="entry-content">
       <p>
        <img alt="" class="size-medium wp-image-2810 alignright" height="300" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="http://www.irishdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/97d49128-3ddd-46cd-ad68-1b4906d30bde-300x300.png" srcset="http://www.irishdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/97d49128-3ddd-46cd-ad68-1b4906d30bde-300x300.png 300w, http://www.irishdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/97d49128-3ddd-46cd-ad68-1b4906d30bde-150x150.png 150w, http://www.irishdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/97d49128-3ddd-46cd-ad68-1b4906d30bde-768x768.png 768w, http://www.irishdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/97d49128-3ddd-46cd-ad68-1b4906d30bde-90x90.png 90w, http://www.irishdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/97d49128-3ddd-46cd-ad68-1b4906d30bde-260x260.png 260w, http://www.irishdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/97d49128-3ddd-46cd-ad68-1b4906d30bde.png 1024w" width="300"/>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        We can’t believe it’s been 30 years since we emigrated from Ireland to Canada and opened our shop in Niagara-on-the-Lake. We started small, near the clock tower on Queen Street, and then took over the two stores on either side of us. 18 years were spent at that location and some of the most wonderful memories were made.
       </p>
       <p>
        Of course everything is authentically Irish as we travel to Ireland to do our buying every year. We specialize in Aran knit sweaters, winter coats, jewellery, Official Guinness Merchandise, and much more.
       </p>
       <p>
        In 2006 we moved across the street and built the larger shop we are at now. At the time this included The Irish Tea Room, run by Maureen’s sister Pat. It was only in September of 2016 that Pat decided to retire and we expanded again to make more room for our lovely Irish products.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <img alt="IMG_8627" class="size-medium wp-image-1611 alignleft" height="225" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="http://www.irishdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/IMG_8627-300x225.jpg" srcset="http://www.irishdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/IMG_8627-300x225.jpg 300w, http://www.irishdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/IMG_8627-768x576.jpg 768w, http://www.irishdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/IMG_8627-1024x768.jpg 1024w, http://www.irishdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/IMG_8627.jpg 1200w" width="300"/>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        We couldn’t be happier with where we are at with our business. Without our wonderful staff, we would not be able to do what we do. We have some of the best people working with us since the very beginning and we would like to thank them for their hard work and dedication to amazing customer service. We are truly one big family and we’re very lucky to have them.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        Of course we are also very thankful for our customers whom we have grown to know and love over the years. Without you we wouldn’t be where we are today, and we can’t thank you enough for all of your continuous support for us and what we bring to the beautiful town of Niagara-On-The-Lake.
       </p>
       <p>
        We hope to see you soon!
       </p>
       <p>
        <em>
         Sláinte
        </em>
        <br/>
        Paul &amp; Maureen Dickson
       </p>
