         <p>
          Had a great weekend at Ramblin’ Roots Revue, an excellent Americana roots music festival at Bucks Student Union. Saw so much amazing live music including storming sets by Danny and the Champions, Robert Vincent, The Magic Numbers and a fun Tom Petty tribute. It was also lovely to meet Romeo Stodart of The Magic Numbers before their headline set.
         </p>

          </a>
         </h1>
         <div class="entry-meta">
          <span class="sep">
           Posted on
          </span>
          <a href="http://lisaredford.com/?p=2167" rel="bookmark" title="9:38 pm">

        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          <a href="http://lisaredford.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/IMG_1705.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-2168" height="300" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="http://lisaredford.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/IMG_1705-300x300.jpg" srcset="http://lisaredford.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/IMG_1705-300x300.jpg 300w, http://lisaredford.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/IMG_1705-150x150.jpg 150w, http://lisaredford.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/IMG_1705-768x768.jpg 768w, http://lisaredford.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/IMG_1705-1024x1024.jpg 1024w" width="300"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          It’s been a busy few days! Had a wonderful night performing at Norwich Waterfront Studio opening for Sarah Darling. It was exciting to perform two brand new songs and lovely to have the brilliant Noel Dashwood joining me on exceptional dobro and harmony vocals.
         </p>
         <p>
          New song “I Just Can’t Forget”
         </p>
         <p>
          <iframe allow="autoplay; encrypted-media" allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="329" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/5o7wKDw_3BE?feature=oembed" width="584">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <p>
          Rehearsing new song “Anything But Easy” before the show:
         </p>
         <p>
          <iframe allow="autoplay; encrypted-media" allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="329" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/Lezb_zgVUBM?feature=oembed" width="584">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <p>
          Sarah sounded absolutely sublime and it was a joy to support her.
         </p>
         <p>
          “With dobro player Noel by her side, she opened up the evening with a great array of original songs which she has written over her substantial career as a musician.
         </p>
         <p>
          It was easy to be drawn in by her elegant and classy deliverance” Huge thanks to the EDP Newspaper for this great
          <a href="http://www.eveningnews24.co.uk/what-s-on/sarah-darling-review-at-the-waterfront-studio-norwich-1-5438053">
           review.
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          “Lisa performs numbers from her recent Another Place and Time EP, several brand new songs, and also one of my favourites, Carolina Rain from the earlier Slipstream album. Lisa  has always moved seamlessly between the worlds of country and folk, but tonight’s set understandably steers more towards the former, and provides a great opener to tonight’s show.” And thanks also to
          <a href="http://www.outlineonline.co.uk/content/sarah-darling/live-reviews-/120560/2486">
           Outline Magazine
          </a>
          for their kind words.
         </p>
         <p>
          Prior to the show I was interviewed by
          <a href="http://www.fatea-records.co.uk/magazine/news/LisaRedford/">
           Fatea Magazine
          </a>
          about songwriting, favourite artists and my career. Huge thanks to Nic Rigby.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://lisaredford.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/IMG_1721.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-2169" height="300" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="http://lisaredford.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/IMG_1721-300x300.jpg" srcset="http://lisaredford.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/IMG_1721-300x300.jpg 300w, http://lisaredford.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/IMG_1721-150x150.jpg 150w, http://lisaredford.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/IMG_1721-768x768.jpg 768w, http://lisaredford.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/IMG_1721-1024x1024.jpg 1024w" width="300"/>
          </a>
          The following day I had a lovely afternoon recording my second Under the Apple Tree session for Whispering Bob TV. It’s always special catching up with Bob Harris and for this session it was so nice to be reunited with pedal steel player Alan Cook who played on my second album “Lost Again”.
         </p>
         <p>
          Bob Harris and I between two pedal steel greats! Chris Hillman and Alan Cook.
          <a href="http://lisaredford.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/IMG_1722.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-2170" height="300" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="http://lisaredford.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/IMG_1722-300x300.jpg" srcset="http://lisaredford.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/IMG_1722-300x300.jpg 300w, http://lisaredford.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/IMG_1722-150x150.jpg 150w, http://lisaredford.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/IMG_1722-768x768.jpg 768w, http://lisaredford.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/IMG_1722-1024x1024.jpg 1024w" width="300"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>

        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          <a href="http://lisaredford.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/Ramblin-Roots-main-poster.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-2155" height="300" sizes="(max-width: 212px) 100vw, 212px" src="http://lisaredford.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/Ramblin-Roots-main-poster-212x300.jpg" srcset="http://lisaredford.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/Ramblin-Roots-main-poster-212x300.jpg 212w, http://lisaredford.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/Ramblin-Roots-main-poster.jpg 678w" width="212"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          So excited to be chosen to play The Ramblin’ Roots Revue Festival and the EarBelly Acoustic Stage on Saturday 7th April! Many wonderful artists performing at this festival in High Wycombe which has the incredible The Magic Numbers headlining – going to be a lovely celebration of Americana
         </p>
         <p>
          <a aria-controls="js_2df" aria-describedby="js_2dg" aria-haspopup="true" data-hovercard="/ajax/hovercard/event.php?id=2075046119395489&amp;extragetparams=%7B%22directed_target_id%22%3Anull%2C%22groups_location%22%3Anull%7D" data-hovercard-prefer-more-content-show="1" href="https://www.facebook.com/events/2075046119395489/?acontext=%7B%22action_history%22%3A%22null%22%7D" id="js_2dh">
           The Ramblin’ Roots Revue 2018
          </a>
          is a three-day celebration of Americana, Roots, Alt-Country and Folk music. It’s all indoors at an award-winning venue in South Bucks with a great selection of craft ales, fine whiskies and artisan food.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://lisaredford.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/ear-belly-stage.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-2156" height="300" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="http://lisaredford.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/ear-belly-stage-300x300.jpg" srcset="http://lisaredford.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/ear-belly-stage-300x300.jpg 300w, http://lisaredford.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/ear-belly-stage-150x150.jpg 150w, http://lisaredford.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/ear-belly-stage-768x768.jpg 768w, http://lisaredford.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/ear-belly-stage.jpg 960w" width="300"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          It’s a stellar line-up of live music across three stages with weekend tickets at £32.50.
         </p>
         <p>
          Tickets here:
          <a data-lynx-mode="origin" href="https://l.facebook.com/l.php?u=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.seetickets.com%2Fgo%2FRRR2018&amp;h=ATPSgbhuocjuL3BjD5J8K9Y11Xj568-h05CCqT9br00PgBJCJKyeUZzzVoNAVMELpsMQJOrr5BG0LMw9xeNuvC8Btu8q6ACdXPEl9Kt356n3ousihmXyx_ufGA" rel="nofollow noopener" target="_blank">
           www.seetickets.com/go/
           <wbr>
           </wbr>
           RRR2018
          </a>

          With my support to country star Sarah Darling coming up, it was lovely to also interview her for
          <a href="http://www.isingmag.com/sarah-darling/">
           iSing Magazine
          </a>
          . Talking about her inspirations, new material and vocal care.
         </p>
