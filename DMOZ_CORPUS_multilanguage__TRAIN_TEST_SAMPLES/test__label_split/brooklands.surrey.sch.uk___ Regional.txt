       <p>
        Brooklands is a primary school for pupils with severe and complex needs including autism aged 2-11. If you require further information about the school, including admissions, please contact Teresa Prior, the Headteacher. Alternatively contact the Surrey South-East Area SEN management team on 01737 737990. if you require a copy of any documents available on our website please contact the school office, these will be provided free of charge to parents. Brooklands School is committed to safeguarding and promoting the welfare of children and expects all staff, parents and visitors to share this commitment.
