   <h2>
    The Club
   </h2>
   <p>
    Bedford are a friendly, sociable, yet competitive club. Our players range
   from youngsters to seasoned veterans
    <br/>
    that  share a desire to enjoy the game of Water
   Polo. Training is structured yet relaxed, and on Fridays there is
    <br/>
    also the opportunity to enjoy a well deserved pint  in the Kent Arms afterwards!
   </p>
   <h2>
    The Sport
   </h2>
   <p>
    One of the original Olympic disciplines, Water Polo is acknowledged as one
   of the toughest sports  going. Teams of 7
    <br/>
    battle it out trying to hurl the ball into the opponents floating goal. 
   So whether you are a keen swimmer
    <br/>
    looking for more excitement in the water,
   or a sportsman/woman looking for a new challenge,
    <br/>
    we would love to hear from you...
   </p>
