          SEC Football Sports is a fan site that delivers updated news and information about Southeastern Conference Sports.  Southeastern Conference Football or SEC Football was founded in December 1932.  Currently there are twelve member institutions from nine contiguous southern states that compete in SEC Football.  The Southeastern Conference is divided into an Eastern Division and a Western Division.  The two regular season winners play each other in the SEC Football Championship Game every December.  The winner of the SEC Football Championship receives the invitation to play in the Sugar Bowl unless invited to play in the BCS Championship Game.  Everyone in the South is passionate about SEC Football and SECFootball Sports is passionate about bringing the best of SEC Football to the fans.  Thanks to fans like you, SECFootball Sports is celebrating its 15th anniversary this year.  Pick your favorite SEC Football School logo below and find the best SEC Football Fan Sites, SEC Football Message Boards and Forums, SEC Football Tickets, SEC Schedules, SEC Football TV and Radio, and SEC Football Scores and Standings.
