                  <p class="pagetitle">
                   Welcome
                  </p>
                  <p class="bodytext">
                   <font color="#800000" size="3">
                    <b>
                     I
                    </b>
                   </font>
                   f you are looking for a Greek island 
                     full of fun, hospitality, warmth and relaxation then look no 
                     further than Crete!
                  </p>
                  <p class="bodytext">
                   <br/>
                  </p>
                  <p class="bodytext">
                   <b>
                    <font color="#800000" size="3">
                     C
                    </font>
                    rete
                   </b>
                   has everything you want from an island:
                   <b>
                    Fine sandy 
                     beaches
                   </b>
                   ,
                   <b>
                    traditional villages,
                   </b>
                   <b>
                    dramatic mountains
                   </b>
                   ,
                   <b>
                    remarkably rich history
                   </b>
                   , remains from ancient 
                     civilizations and even more.
                  </p>
                  <p class="bodytext">
                   Its the fifth largest island in the Mediterranean and the 
                     largest in Europe. Its climate, which is one of the best in 
                     Europe, is mild in winter and sunny though spring, summer 
                     and autumn with a cooling breeze on even the hottest of 
                     days.
                   <br/>
                   <br/>
                  </p>
                  <p class="bodytext">
                   <br/>
                   <b>
                    <font color="#800000" size="3">
                     V
                    </font>
                    illa Dina
                   </b>
                   is only a short drive away, almost
                   <b>
                    23 km from Chania
                   </b>
                   once the island capital.
                  </p>
                  <p class="bodytext">
                   The city of Chania is 
                     located on the
                   <b>
                    west coast of Crete
                   </b>
                   and its the most 
                     mountainous area of the island. The city splits up to the 
                     old city, with Venetian walls, the labyrinthine lanes, the 
                     Venetian small streets and the new city which was build the 
                     last years far away from the old city with good street 
                     layout , new buildings squares, streets etc.
                  </p>
                  <p class="bodytext">
                   With its famous Venetian harbor, picturesque waterfront 
                     cafés in front and the Agora Market, which is in the shape 
                     of the cross, what a better place to relax and to enjoy the 
                     sunny days.
                  </p>
                  <p class="bodytext">
                   <br/>
                   <br/>
                   <b>
                    <font color="#800000" size="3">
                     C
                    </font>
                    hania
                   </b>
                   is the gateway to the far west of Crete, where the 
                     most remote and most spectacular spots are to be found.
                  </p>
                  <p class="bodytext">
                   South are the
                   <b>
                    Lefka Ori
                   </b>
                   or White Mountains and the famous
                   <b>
                    Samaria Gorge
                   </b>
                   , the longest in Europe.
                  </p>
                  <p class="bodytext">
                   East are situated the 
                     quiet peaceful villages of
                   <b>
                    Apokoronas
                   </b>
                   with its narrow roads 
                     full and a landscape of wild nature full of light and sea, 
                     which capture the visitor and detach the returns promise.
                  </p>
                  <p class="bodytext">
                   <br/>
                   <br/>
                   <br/>
                   <b>
                    <font color="#800000" size="3">
                     O
                    </font>
                   </b>
                   ne of those villages is
                   <b>
                    Almyrida
                   </b>
                   , a
                   <b>
                    small fishing village
                   </b>
                   and although more and more people have gradually discover Almyridas charms and the village with its countryside have 
                     seen changes which include some additional buildings, 
                     nothing has spoilt the lovely bay and Almyrida still retains 
                     the archetypical Greek fishing village atmosphere which make 
                     it a favorite summer holiday resort.
                   <br/>
                   <br/>
                   <br/>
                   <br/>
                  </p>
