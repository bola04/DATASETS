A visit to Branson would not be complete without visiting Silver Dollar City, Celebration City, White Water, or the Shepherd of the Hills and Outdoor Drama. The drama started over 40 years ago and is based around the book Harold Bell Wright wrote. You will not want to miss Worlds Largest Toy Museum.
                <br/>
                <br/>
                You certainly do want to visit Table Rock Lake with over 800 miles of shore line, which is the home of small boats to large cabin cruisers with many Lake Resorts around the Table Rock Lake shore from the mom and pop property to the multi-million dollar Chateau On The Lake Resort. If you are fishing, taking picture, water skiing, scuba diving or just joy riding on Table Rock Lake, the clear water of Table Rock Lake is an ideal location.
                <br/>
                <br/>
                Lake Taneycomo, a 20 mile long lake starts at Branson from the spillway of the Table Rock Lake. The cold water maintains the Rainbow and German trout in Lake Taneycomo. Lake Taneycomo is the 2nd best trout fishing lake in the United States. Two new state records over 27 pounds were recently taken from Lake Taneycomo.
                <br/>
                <br/>
                When it comes to recreation Branson Missouri. Table Rock Lake and Lake Taneycomo has it all. Near by areas such as Village of Indian Point, Kimberling City, Branson West, and Lampe which are all known for their recreation resorts and their hospitality while Lake Taneycomo flows through Branson.
                <br/>
                <br/>
                The Branson Family Entertainment centers welcome the kids. From Silver Dollar City, the Tracks (go carts and other entertainment), local caves including Fantastic Caverns, Talking Rocks Cavern, Marvel Cave and many others located nearby, Table Rock Lake cruises, ride the ducks, miniature golf courses, movie theaters, museums, theme parks and water rides.
                <br/>
                <br/>
                Guys, you may not want to tell your wife, Branson is home to 3 large discount shopping malls with hundreds of discount stores and dozens of smaller malls, just waiting for them to buy something for you at discounted prices.
