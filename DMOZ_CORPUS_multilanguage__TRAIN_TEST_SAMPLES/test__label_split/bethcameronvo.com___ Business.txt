      <h1>
       About Beth Cameron
      </h1>
      <p>
       Beth has been providing  female voice over and audio production for over 10 years. She works full time from her  professional home studio making voice-over affordable for nearly any budget.
      </p>
      <p>
       Beginning her career in  Boston radio, Beth’s interest in Educational Media led her to San Francisco  where she spent several years working in the fields of educational software,  interactive and retail media, as well as commercial production. In 2000 Beth  returned to Boston where she earned her Master’s degree in Educational Media.
      </p>
      <p>
       Her extensive background  and training in all areas of the media industry give Beth a unique approach to  the art and business of voice over.
      </p>
