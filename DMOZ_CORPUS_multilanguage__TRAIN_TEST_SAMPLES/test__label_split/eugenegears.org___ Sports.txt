           <img alt="bRamble Logo" class="alignnone wp-image-6663 size-thumbnail" height="150" src="http://eugenegears.org/wp-content/uploads/wheelart_2018_10.png" width="150"/>

         <div style="width: 35%; margin-top: 15px; float: left;">

          </a>
         </div>
         <div style="width: 35%; margin-top: 15px; float: left;">
          <a href="https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSe4V8XJ9VDraz-nQ2ElBuCsJZocLuU868LUs4_Fqq8SR7GH9g/viewform?c=0&amp;w=1" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
           <br/>
           <img alt="Volunteer" src="http://eugenegears.org/wp-content/uploads/Volunteer_6_scaled.png"/>
           <br/>

         <h2>
          25 Years and Still Riding
         </h2>
         <p>
          Welcome to GEARs. The Greater Eugene Area Riders is a non-profit organization with
          <a href="/membership">
           the mission
          </a>
          to promote and encourage bicycle riding for transportation and recreation. We coordinate weekly rides, provide safety and skill education, defend the rights and responsibilities of bike riders, and work to enhance the bicycle culture in Eugene and surrounding area. Membership in GEARs has its rewards! In addition to all of the rides and advocacy work we do, there are additional benefits. Local bike shops and other businesses are now offering discounts for GEARs members. Go to our
          <a href="http://eugenegears.org/programs/bikerewards/">
           Bike Rewards
          </a>
          page to see a list of participating business members.
          <a href="/membership">
           Join GEARs Now.
          </a>
         </p>
         <div class="info_box">
          <h3>
           Ride Schedule Change
          </h3>
          <p>
           I don’t know what I was thinking offering Quartzville Creek rd on a weekend during the summer!  There will be so much traffic with boaters and campers to make a beautiful ride miserable. I will lead a 12-15 mph ride following same route as Garry on Saturday, July 21st (ride 227c Applegate Trail).
          </p>
          <p>
           I will offer the Quartzville Creek rd ride on Tuesday, 24 July, with same directions:  meet at 0700 at ABP to carpool to White’s Park  in Sweet Home.  RSVP to Jackie at 541-345-3941.
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          If you would like to receive our Monthly Digital Newsletter you can sign-up
          <a href="http://eugenegears.org/newsletter-sign-up">
           HERE
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <div class="info_box">
          <h2>
           Next Meetings:

          <p>
           Our next
           <strong>
            Board Meeting

           will be
           <strong>
            Tuesday, August 14th at 6:30 pm.
           </strong>
           The meeting will be at Stellaria Building, 150 Shelton McMurphey Blvd, Eugene, in the second floor conference room. Members and the public are encouraged to attend.
          </p>
