          <p align="center">
           <strong>
            Holmes Bible College
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p align="center">
           <strong>
            2018 Graduation Convocation
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p align="center">
           <strong>
            May 5, 2018, 10:30 am
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p align="center">
           <strong>
            Holmes Memorial Church Sanctuary
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p align="center">
           <strong>
            Speaker: Bishop Greg Amos
            <img class="fr-imgright fr-dii fr-draggable" src="/Websites/holmescollege/images/Bishop%20Greg%20Amos.jpg" style="width: 214px; height: 273.444px;"/>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <!--[if !vml]-->
           <!--[endif]-->
           Bishop Greg Amos is currently the Bishop of the South Carolina Conference of the IPHC. He has served as President of Fire &amp; Ice, Inc. (now called “Accelerant”) since 1998. He is currently serving on the National Discipleship Ministries Council of the IPHC, as Chairman of the Falcon Children's Home and Family Services Board, on the Emmanuel College Board of Trustees, Holmes Bible College Board of Trustees, and on the Southwestern Christian University Board of Regents.
          </p>
          <p>
           Bishop Amos served as the Discipleship Ministries Director of the South Carolina Conference for twelve years. Before that he served as Senior Pastor for over 5 years, and as Youth Pastor or Associate Pastor in various churches for 12 years.
          </p>
          <p>
           Bishop Amos has a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Religion from Emmanuel College. He received a Master of Ministry Degree from Southwestern Christian University. He and his wife of 37 years, Tamara Amos, have two children.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
