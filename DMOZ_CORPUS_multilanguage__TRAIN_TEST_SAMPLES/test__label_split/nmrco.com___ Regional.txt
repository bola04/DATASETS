          <h3>
           Group Health
          </h3>
          <!-- Para -->
          <p>
           Requests can be submitted for medical necessity, experimental/investigational, fraud, waste &amp; abuse, and quality of care. NMR will assign a physician to review the medical records of the patient whose claim is being disputed. After a review of the submitted medical records, including performing any requested teleconference(s) with associated treating provider(s), the reviewer will create a detailed report which summarizes their findings and addresses all requested questions specific to the claim. Findings are supported by recent and relevant references from policy/plan language, peer literature, publications, or national criteria. Each report receives a thorough quality assurance check performed by a nurse before being returned to the client. As a result of NMR's best in class policies, procedures and workflows virtually any requested turn-around time can be accommodated.
          </p>
