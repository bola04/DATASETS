 amateur sports photographer based in Suffolk, UK. View the photo galleries for images

      <a href="http://www.mikewatts.com/hdt/mt1/mt.html">
       Hdt marathon time calculator
      </a>
      which you can run on a mobile device - iphone or android or Kindle (or PC).
      <br/>
      Simply follow the link - then on an iphone/ipod etc use the phone's menu to Add Bookmark to home page and you can then use it whether or not you're on a network.
      <br/>
      Enter the marathon length, then speed or time allowed and it will calculate your km times for section A or E, then click "Min and Max" or Mid time to get maximum and minimum times, or the mid time, whichever you use.
