       Astwood Bank Angling Club (ABAC) is a well established fishing club with over 500 members from across the UK.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       The club has a wide range of rivers and still waters that cater for all aspects of pleasure, match and specimen fishing ranging from "bagging up" venues to tougher lower stocked waters.  All of our venues are located within the beautiful Warwickshire and Worcester countryside where buzzards, king fishers and muntjac deer can regularly be seen.  We also have a large stretch of the Warwickshire Avon that is popular with Barbel anglers with regular reports of double figure fish being caught, as well as a stretch of the Arrow at Wixford.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       All our venues are well maintained and host a wide range of species including Roach, Rudd, Perch, Pike, Eel, Gudgeon, Crucian Carp, Tench, Bream, Barbel, Chub, Common and Mirror Carp.  A number of our venues have access and pegs for disabled anglers.  For more information  please visit our
