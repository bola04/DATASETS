      We are a working beef and quarter horse farm. Our riverside campsites, rustic cabins, and open spaces are perfect for family-centered activities. On-site canoe and kayak rentals make it easy to enjoy the beauty of the Upper Iowa River. The new office offers a modern shower and restroom. When you visit be sure to ask about our free farm tours!
