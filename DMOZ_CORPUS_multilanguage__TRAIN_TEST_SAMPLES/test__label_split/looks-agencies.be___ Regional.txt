       <h2>
        About us
       </h2>
       <div class="cms-text">
        <p>
         Looks Agencies leading fashion agency was founded in 2002 by two driving forces: Dennis Thijssens and Marie-Laure Romanski.
         <br/>
         From the start the agency represented
         <strong>
          fashionable and qualitative footwear and accessories collections
         </strong>
         within the middle to high price range.
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal">
         Through the years the company had long lasting and successful relationships with prestigious names such as
         <em>
          Guess, Moschino, Ugg Australia, Patrizia Pepe, Janet &amp; Janet, Stella Luna, Scholl Footwear, Lola Cruz, Audley London, Candice Cooper, Fiorucci, Fiamme Footwear, Roberto d’Angelo and What For.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal">
         The showrooms are located in the heart of the Antwerp fashion area and have a surface of 800m².
         <br/>
         Our distribution network consists of a well balanced
         <strong>
          high profile network
         </strong>
         of the most upmarket footwear, fashion and accessories retailers.
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal">
         <strong>
          Long lasting and loyal relationships
         </strong>
         with solid and important companies take an important place in our agency.
         <br/>
         This makes of Looks Agencies a unique showroom in the Benelux, that tends not only to sell a product but also a certain vision on footwear and accessories with a sensibility for the fashion of tomorrow.
        </p>
