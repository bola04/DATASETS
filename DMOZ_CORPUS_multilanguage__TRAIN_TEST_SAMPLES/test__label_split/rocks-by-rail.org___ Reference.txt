       <p>
        Welcome to an outdoor adventure, cast in an ironstone quarry. Covering 19 acres of reclaimed quarry, railway tracks and nature trails,
        <strong>
         Rocks by Rail is a journey back in time
        </strong>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        It’s an unmissable opportunity to experience an
        <strong>
         awesome industrial landscape
        </strong>
        . Explore fascinating nature trails. Take a train trip. Drive a classic loco. Tour the workshop and loco yard. View the ironstone exhibition and learn about its importance throughout history.
       </p>
       <p>
        Then sit back with a cuppa in a 1960s style café, or browse the Museum shop.
        <br/>
        Rocks By Rail is a
        <strong>
         big value day out
        </strong>
        . Whether you’re a family seeking informative fun, a rail and digger enthusiast, or a teacher looking for ways to bring lessons to life – you’ll find the
        <strong>
         living ironstone museum
        </strong>
        so involving
        <strong>
         you’ll wonder where the hours went!
        </strong>
       </p>
