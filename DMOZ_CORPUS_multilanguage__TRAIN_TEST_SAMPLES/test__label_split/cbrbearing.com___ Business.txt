      <h2>
       Why Ceramic Bearings?
      </h2>
      <p>
       The heart of the ceramic hybrid ball bearing is the ceramic ball itself. Poor quality balls can fail, sometimes with disastrous results, but the high-quality, aerospace-grade balls used by CBR will not chip, fracture, or fail, even under the harshest of conditions. All of our ceramic hybrid ball bearings are electrically non-conductive, last longer, and perform with 40% less friction than traditional steel ball bearings.
      </p>
