           <p>
            Vanderbilt Bone &amp; Joint cares for all your moving parts: back and hips, elbows and knees—everything involving bones and the muscles and connective structures that help them work.
           </p>
           <p>
            When they don’t work so well, because of disease or injury, we have a team of highly trained specialists to help you build strength and stability, reduce pain and keep you moving.
           </p>
           <p>
            Our experts offer a full continuum of care, including diagnosing your problem, assessing it with X-ray or MRI images, creating a customized care plan and prescribing physical therapy.
           </p>
           <p>
            And because injuries sometimes happen when you least expect it, Vanderbilt Bone &amp; Joint offers
            <a href="https://myhealthwalkin.com/program/wic-vanderbilt-bone-joint-after-hours-clinic" target="_blank">
             walk-in care
            </a>
            from 8 a.m. to 8 p.m., Monday through Friday—no appointment necessary.
           </p>
