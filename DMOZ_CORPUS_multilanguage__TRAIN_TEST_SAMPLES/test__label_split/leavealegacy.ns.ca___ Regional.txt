      <h1>
       Welcome to LEAVE A LEGACY™ Nova Scotia
      </h1>
      <p>
       <br/>
       Whether you fondly remember the good old days or can't stop thinking about tomorrow...arrange a gift in your will and you can make a difference in the lives that follow.
       <br/>
       Contact your favourite charity today.
      </p>
      <h2>
       Leave Your Legacy
      </h2>
      <p>
       When choosing to leave a gift in your will, you are providing critical financial support to benefit future generations. While realizing tax savings, you can also feel good knowing your gift will have an enormous impact on your community today and tomorrow. Leaving a gift in your will may be one of the most fulfilling decisions you will ever make and it’s easier than you think.
      </p>
      <p>
       LEAVE A LEGACY™ Nova Scotia promotes the importance and financial benefits of leaving a charitable gift in your will to provide future support to the causes that mean the most to you.
      </p>
      <p>
       LEAVE A LEGACY™ is a public awareness initiative that aims to bring together professionals and planners to help people become knowledgeable about the many benefits of leaving a gift to a charity through their estate planning process.
      </p>
      <h3>
      </h3>
     </div>
     <div class="row">
      <h2>
       The aim of LEAVE A LEGACY™ is two-fold:
      </h2>
      <div class="col-sm-6">
       <div class="homeBox">
        <div class="cms content">
         <img class="box-img" src="/images/box-1-image.png"/>
         <p>
          Ensure every adult in Canada has an up-to-date will
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>
      </div>
      <div class="col-sm-6">
       <div class="homeBox">
        <div class="cms content">
         <img class="box-img" src="/images/box-2-image.png"/>
         <p>
          Encourage individuals to leave a gift for their favourite registered charity in their will.
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="cms content">
      <p>
       The vision of
       <strong>
        LEAVE A LEGACY™
       </strong>
       <strong>
        Nova Scotia
       </strong>
       is to ensure every Nova Scotian is aware of the personal and financial benefits of leaving a gift for their charities of choice in their will. Preparing an estate plan that includes a charitable gift provides tremendous personal satisfaction and significant tax benefits for donors.
      </p>
      <p>
       Everyone benefits from
       <strong>
        LEAVE A LEGACY™
       </strong>
       – the individual, the family and the community. Whatever your reasons, your gift makes a difference!
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <strong>
        LEAVE A LEGACY™
       </strong>
       is a program of the
       <a href="https://www.cagp-acpdp.org/">
        Canadian Association of Gift Planners (CAGP-ACPDP™)
       </a>
