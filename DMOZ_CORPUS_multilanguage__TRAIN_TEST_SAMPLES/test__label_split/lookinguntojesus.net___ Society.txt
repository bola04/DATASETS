        <h2 class="entry-title">
        </h2>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <div class="pf-content">
          <p>
           <strong>
            a NEW TESTAMENT church!
            <br/>
           </strong>
           <a href="http://lookinguntojesus.net/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/quote_line.jpg">
            <img alt="quote_line" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-66" height="50" sizes="(max-width: 960px) 100vw, 960px" src="http://lookinguntojesus.net/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/quote_line.jpg" srcset="http://lookinguntojesus.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/quote_line.jpg 960w, http://lookinguntojesus.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/quote_line-300x15.jpg 300w, http://lookinguntojesus.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/quote_line-940x50.jpg 940w" width="960"/>
           </a>
           We’re an assembly of Christians meeting together in Kingston, Ontario who enjoy regular Bible study and fellowship with one another. Our desire is to be a New Testament church, just like you can read about in the Bible. We have no source of religious authority except the Bible (God’s blueprint) for doctrine and practice. We use no creed, no catechism, no manual, etc.. God’s word is perfect, and thus we ought to obey what He has revealed. We cannot improve upon His pattern for salvation, worship and life. We make it our aim to follow His plan in all things we do, and encourage others to do the same, to the glory of God. If that appeals to you, we look forward to seeing you. Click
           <a href="http://youtu.be/9FxxfBxQoLc" target="_blank" title="Invitation video">
            here
           </a>
           to see a short invitation video.
           <strong>
            <br/>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            come WORSHIP with us…
           </strong>
           <br/>
           <a href="http://lookinguntojesus.net/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/quote_line.jpg">
            <img alt="quote_line" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-66" height="50" sizes="(max-width: 960px) 100vw, 960px" src="http://lookinguntojesus.net/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/quote_line.jpg" srcset="http://lookinguntojesus.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/quote_line.jpg 960w, http://lookinguntojesus.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/quote_line-300x15.jpg 300w, http://lookinguntojesus.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/quote_line-940x50.jpg 940w" width="960"/>
           </a>
           <br/>
           You are invited to come and worship with us. Here is a Google Map to help you find your way to our meeting place. See you soon!
           <br/>
           <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="300" src="https://www.google.com/maps/embed?pb=!1m18!1m12!1m3!1d22860.067957056013!2d-76.54349255449219!3d44.258357350962875!2m3!1f0!2f0!3f0!3m2!1i1024!2i768!4f13.1!3m3!1m2!1s0x4cd2ac15166f61eb%3A0x24d2e6b17aef5509!2s935+Sydenham+Rd%2C+Kingston%2C+ON+K7M+3L8!5e0!3m2!1sen!2sca!4v1440423553283" style="border: 0;" width="600">
           </iframe>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            let’s STUDY…
           </strong>
           <br/>
           <a href="http://lookinguntojesus.net/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/quote_line.jpg">
            <img alt="quote_line" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-66" height="50" sizes="(max-width: 960px) 100vw, 960px" src="http://lookinguntojesus.net/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/quote_line.jpg" srcset="http://lookinguntojesus.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/quote_line.jpg 960w, http://lookinguntojesus.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/quote_line-300x15.jpg 300w, http://lookinguntojesus.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/quote_line-940x50.jpg 940w" width="960"/>
           </a>
           <br/>
           In addition to our weekly Bible classes and worship, we also offer the opportunity for folks to set up private Bible classes through the week to look at Bible questions or topics of interest. If you’d be interested in doing so, contact us and we’d be glad to set up a time at a mutually agreeable location (ie. a coffee shop). Call 613.896.0498 or e-mail us
           <a href="http://www.lookinguntojesus.net/e-mail-us-here">
            here
           </a>
           .
