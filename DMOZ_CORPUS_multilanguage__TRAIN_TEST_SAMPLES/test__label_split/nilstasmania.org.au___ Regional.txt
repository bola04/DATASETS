      <div class="column">
       <h4 class="title">
        What do we do
       </h4>
       <p>
        No Interest Loans Tasmania is overseen by a Committee of Management comprising of stakeholders and supporters who guide the organisation. Day-to-day management is headed by the Coordinator who is supported by Loans Officers, Administration Staff, a Micro-Business Loans Coordinator and others.
       </p>
       <div class="segment">
        <p>
         Our program also has an educational impact, teaching valuable financial literacy. Through our relationship focus, combines with the successful repayment of one or several loans, the program improves emotional well-being by adding to confidence, self-worth and self-esteem of the individual. The program further connects these individuals and families to their communities.
        </p>
