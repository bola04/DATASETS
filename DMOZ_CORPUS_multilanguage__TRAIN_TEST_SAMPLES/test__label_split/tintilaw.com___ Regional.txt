         <span class="white-text">
          One of the preeminent law firms in Massachusetts
         </span>
         located on the North Shore, Tinti, Quinn, Grover &amp; Frey, P.C. has been representing individuals and businesses throughout the Commonwealth of Massachusetts since 1982. The attorneys and professional staff at Tinti, Quinn, Grover &amp; Frey combine years of experience with a cost-effective, team-driven approach to provide our clients with expert legal representation and advice. The firm’s attorneys have specialized experience in business law, residential and commercial real estate transactions, litigation, estate planning and probate, real estate development, environmental law, creditor’s rights and bankruptcy, family law, tax planning, banking law and specialized financing. The firm and its lawyers have been recognized for superior achievement by the receipt of various awards, commendations, and professional designations.
