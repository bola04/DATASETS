                      <div class="uabb-infobox-text uabb-text-editor">
                       <p>
                        <span class="style2">
                         A balloon provides you with a privileged platform from which to observe the splendor that is Cappadocia. Imagine as you effortlessly drift over unique rock formations and rich fertile valleys, all set against the dramatic volcanic landscape that formed the region over two  million years ago.
                         <br/>
                         In a hot air balloon there is no sense of motion, the balloon is a part of the airstream in which it travels.
                        </span>
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        <strong>
                         How do balloons fly?
                        </strong>
                        <br/>
                        Balloons operate through the basic principles of gravity and heat transfer. As air inside the balloon is heated, it rises. As the air inside the balloon cools, it descends. To make the balloon fly, an inflator fan fills it with air, which is then heated.
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        <strong>
                         What is included?
                        </strong>
                        <br/>
                        We pick you up from your hotel. You will have a light breakfast before departure. At the end of the flight you will celebrate with a glass of original Cappadocia Balloons champagne, awarded your flight certificate.
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        You come to earth gently, flown by our expert national and internatıonal pilots; your mind and camera bursting with images that will last a lifetime.Whether adventurous or merely curious, this is a spectacular experience for people of all ages.Join us in Cappadocia and allow us to show you some of the warm Turkish hospitality that has made our company famous for almost a decade.
