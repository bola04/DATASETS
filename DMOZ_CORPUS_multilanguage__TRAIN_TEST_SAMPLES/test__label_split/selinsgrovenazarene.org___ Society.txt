          <p>
           <span class="larger">
            Selinsgrove, Pennsylvania
           </span>
           — Worship with us at Selinsgrove Church of the Nazarene.
          </p>
          <p>
           <span class="larger">
            Traditional Service
           </span>
           provides an opportunity for the congregation to corporately join together in a call to worship and in song. Singing is led by a vocal ensemble accompanied by both the piano and organ. Ministry highlights, pastoral prayer, and a sermon will also be experienced during this service.
          </p>
          <p>
           <span class="larger">
            Contemporary Service
           </span>
           is similar to the traditional service with contemporary music. Singing is accompanied by our Praise and Worship Team.
