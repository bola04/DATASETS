             <h3>
              At the end of the Hana Highway
             </h3>
             <p>
              In the heart of Hana, Joe’s Place is moments from Hana Bay and the best swimming beaches Hana has to offer, as well as Hana Cultural Center and Museum, and Hana park. Red Sand Beach is walking distance. All the wonderful Hana food trucks — Thai, Ethiopian, BBQ Chicken, Tacos and so much more — are nearby. Newly re-opened and refurbished under new management, we offer nightly rentals in rooms that are newly remodeled, clean, comfortable, and affordable. Family rates and high speed wireless Internet (100 Mb/Second) are available. Please view our rooms and availability on the new
              <a href="http://joesplacehana.checkfront.com/reserve/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
               booking site at Checkfront
              </a>
              .
             </p>
             <h3 style="margin-top: 30px;">
              About Joe’s
             </h3>
             <p>
              It no longer makes sense to drive to Hana and back in the same day. Instead, stay over and immerse yourself in the old Hawaii, but with modern amenities.
             </p>
             <p>
              Joe’s place was originally established in 1923. It was completely rebuilt in 1982 into its current shape and rooms. After being purchased by the Franklin family in May, 2018, it was restored again — all floors, doors, fixtures, switches, faucets, toilets, bathroom vanities and sinks replaced, with new beds and new furnishings.
             </p>
             <p>
              The property is a one acre parcel edged on both sides and the upper side with Banana trees, palms, and many native flowers. A view of Hana Bay and the outer coastline can be seen from the upper part of the property as well as rooms 5, 6 and 7. The common living room is for the use of all guests, and has new flooring, furnishings and HD Cable/TV in the common TV room. The common kitchen, shared by ‘The House’, has had less work structurally or cosmetically, but does have new pots and pans, so feel free to cook.
             </p>
             <h3 style="margin-top: 30px;">
              Location
             </h3>
             <p>
              Located on Uakea Road, the street that skirts Hana Bay, it is a 4 minute walk to get your feet wet at Hana Bay, swim, or jump off the pier. Have breakfast at the Barefoot cafe, and catch the dolphins playing in the early morning swells. Walk 4 minutes along Uakea road in the other direction from Joe’s Place (back towards civilization – the way you came), and you are on a beautiful black rock beach, perfect for viewing the sunrise. The three Bay View rooms also enjoy the Hana Bay sunrise view, with the outer coast of the Hana Bay in the background. The power of the Hawaiian Hana sun at sunrise is so powerful we have put additional black-out drapes at the large windows, so it is possible to sleep in – although sunrise over Hana Bay, and sunrise at Haleakala crater are both moments you won’t want to miss. Get in the car and you are less than 5 minutes from Hana Ranch bar and restaurant, Hasegawa’s General Store, and the famous Hana Food Trucks. Another few minutes drive from Hana and you’re in Hamoa Beach. How to pick a beach to go to? Black sand beach, white sand Koke Beach, red sand beach, the refreshing fresh water Venus Pools… all within a few minutes drive of our property.
             </p>
             <h3 style="margin-top: 30px;">
              Getting here
             </h3>
             <p>
              We are only 3.7 miles from Hana Airport, and can arrange a flight for you from Kahalui OGG to Hana. The flight takes about 15 minutes, and flies at low altitude along the coast. The drive along the Hana Highway is world-famed for its beautiful tropical nature, stunning vistas, numerous waterfalls and swimming holes, and curving paths through the mountains. The drive is at least 2 hours to travel the 49 miles. But figure extra time, as you will want to stop at many of the look-out points.
             </p>
