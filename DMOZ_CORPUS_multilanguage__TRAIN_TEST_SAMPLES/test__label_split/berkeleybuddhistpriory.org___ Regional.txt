         <div class="atblock container">
          <h2 class="widget-title" style="color:#202529;">
           <span class="title-decoration">
           </span>
           About
          </h2>
          <div class="textwidget">
           <p>
            Berkeley Buddhist Priory is a Buddhist temple following the tradition of
            <a href="https://berkeleybuddhistpriory.org/?page_id=12">
             Serene Reflection Meditation
            </a>
            , which was known as Soto Zen in Japan and Ts’ao-Tung (Chan) in China. The teaching and our practice emphasize meditation, guiding our lives by the moral Precepts of Buddhism, awakening the heart of compassion, and expressing it through selfless activity in everyday life.
           </p>
           <p>
            The Priory is a temple of the
            <a href="http://obcon.org/index.html" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
             Order of Buddhist Contemplatives (OBC)
            </a>
            , an international Buddhist Order, whose North America office is at Shasta Abbey located in Mt. Shasta, California. The Founder of both OBC and Berkeley Buddhist Priory is the late
            <a href="https://berkeleybuddhistpriory.org/?page_id=13">
             Rev. Master Jiyu-Kennett.
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            The Priory was established in 1973. It offers a regular
            <a href="https://berkeleybuddhistpriory.org/?page_id=11">
             schedule
            </a>
            of meditation, Dharma classes, Buddhist services, and day-retreats, as well as spiritual guidance. It also offers meditation instruction and workshops for beginners. There are no fees for participating in above activities or any other services that the Priory offers. Like other affiliated temples of OBC, the Priory is financially independent; we are supported entirely by
            <a href="https://berkeleybuddhistpriory.org/?page_id=1325">
             donations
            </a>
            of our congregation and friends.
           </p>
           <p>
            The Prior of the temple is
            <a href="https://berkeleybuddhistpriory.org/?page_id=1575">
             Rev. Kinrei Bassis
            </a>
            , a Dharma heir of Rev. Master Jiyu-Kennett. Rev. Kinrei was ordained as a Buddhist monk by Rev. Master Jiyu-Kennett in 1979, and trained at Shasta Abbey. He received Dharma Transmission in 1982 and was named Master by Rev. Master Jiyu-Kennett in 1987. He has been the Prior of the temple since 1997. A selection of his
            <a href="https://berkeleybuddhistpriory.org/?page_id=16">
             Dharma talks
            </a>
            and
            <a href="https://berkeleybuddhistpriory.org/?page_id=15">
             articles
            </a>
            are available for download.
           </p>
