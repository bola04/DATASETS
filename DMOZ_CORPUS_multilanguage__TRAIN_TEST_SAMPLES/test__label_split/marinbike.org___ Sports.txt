           <small>
            What
           </small>
           We Do
          </h2>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="row">
         <div class="item">
          <a href="http://www.marinbike.org/news/">
           <img alt="" class="icon-round" src="https://i2.wp.com/www.marinbike.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/icon-round-bike-bubble.png?fit=216%2C215"/>
          </a>
          <h3>
           <a href="http://www.marinbike.org/news/">
            Give Cyclists a Voice
           </a>
          </h3>
          <hr/>
          <p>
           Advocacy brings cyclists' collective voice to life. We advocate vigorously for increased bicycle and pedestrian funding, interconnected routes, and policies to enable a great ride. We are always looking forward, working to build
 a bike-friendly Marin.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="item">
          <a href="http://www.marinbike.org/classes/">
           <img alt="" class="icon-round" src="https://i0.wp.com/www.marinbike.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/icon-round-bike.png?fit=216%2C215"/>
          </a>
          <h3>
           <a href="http://www.marinbike.org/classes/">
            EDUCATE THE NEXT GENERATION
           </a>
          </h3>
          <hr/>
          <p>
           Confidence leads to a great experience on the bike.
 We help build new rider confidence through safety and skills education from classes teaching parents how to share the joy of cycling with their children, to programs demonstrating the benefits of green transportation.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="item">
          <a href="http://www.marinbike.org/get-involved/volunteer/">
           <img alt="" class="icon-round" src="https://i0.wp.com/www.marinbike.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/icon-round-group.png?fit=216%2C215"/>
          </a>
          <h3>
           <a href="http://www.marinbike.org/get-involved/volunteer/">
            BUILD THE CYCLING COMMUNITY
           </a>
          </h3>
          <hr/>
          <p>
           Every new cyclist is a power for good. We provide the opportunities and environment to support Marin's diverse cycling community.
 Our network of events is a central hub for cyclists of all ages and skill levels to connect, learn, ride, and have fun.
          </p>
         </div>

    <div class="vertical-center">
     <div class="container">
      <div class="row">
       <div class="col-md-8 col-md-offset-2">
        <h2 class="text-center color-white">
         <small>
          Our
         </small>
         Programs
        </h2>
       </div>
      </div>
      <div class="row">
       <div class="item">
        <a href="http://www.marinbike.org/our-programs/road-advocacy/">
         <img alt="" class="icon-round" src="https://i0.wp.com/www.marinbike.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/icon-round-mtns.png?fit=216%2C215"/>
        </a>
        <h3>
         <a href="http://www.marinbike.org/our-programs/road-advocacy/">
          Road
         </a>
        </h3>
        <hr/>
        <p>
         The Road Advocacy Program builds the political support needed to identify, promote and fund bike and pedestrian improvements in Marin. Safety and connectivity between communities are the driving themes. We work closely with our public agency partners toward completing the North-South and East-West Greenways, Marin's primary bicycle and pedestrian routes.
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="item">
        <a href="http://www.marinbike.org/our-programs/off-road/">
         <img alt="" class="icon-round" src="https://i2.wp.com/www.marinbike.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/icon-round-trees.png?fit=216%2C215"/>
        </a>
        <h3>
         <a href="http://www.marinbike.org/our-programs/off-road/">
          Off-Road
         </a>
        </h3>
        <hr/>
        <p>
         The Off-Road Program expands mountain biking opportunities through education, environmental stewardship and trail development. We are committed to bridging gaps in Marin's trails network, while also maintaining and restoring existing trails. This effort is guided by the vision of one day riding from the Golden Gate Bridge to Point Reyes, all off-road.
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="item">
        <a href="http://www.marinbike.org/our-programs/safe-routes-to-school/">
         <img alt="" class="icon-round" src="https://i2.wp.com/www.marinbike.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/icon-round-bike-lane.png?fit=216%2C215"/>
        </a>
        <h3>
         <a href="http://www.marinbike.org/our-programs/safe-routes-to-school/">
          Safe Routes To Schools
         </a>
        </h3>
        <hr/>
        <p>
         The Safe Routes to Schools Program improves the quality of life for entire school communities through education, encouragement and infrastructure improvements. Presented in partnership with the Transportation Authority
 of Marin, Safe Routes teaches cycling skills and promotes healthy lifestyles, while championing green transportation.
        </p>
       </div>
