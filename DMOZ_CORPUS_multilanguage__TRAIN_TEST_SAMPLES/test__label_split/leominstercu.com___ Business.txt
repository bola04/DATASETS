          <h1 style="font-size: 24px !important; color: #707070 !important;">

          </h1>
          <div class="container_12 clear">
           <h2 style="color:#76232f !important;">
            <a data-wpel-link="internal" href="https://leominstercu.com/funds-are-still-available-through-the-equity-builder-program/">
             Funds are still available through the Equity Builder Program!
            </a>
           </h2>
           <p>
            LCU, through the Federal Home Loan Bank’s Equity Builder Program, still has funds available to provide assistance to first-time homebuyers and displaced homemakers.
