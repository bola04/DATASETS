     <h1>
      Space to Work. Rest. and most importantly Play!
     </h1>
     <br/>
     <p>
      That's what we give you! Our rooms give you the space to follow your passion, especially if it's a noisy one. We manufacture a range of sound attenuating booths and garden rooms which can be used indoors as well as outdoors. We also make garden rooms which aren't soundproof which just provide an attractive and comfortable garden room, suitable for many uses. All our rooms come in panel form and can be put up quickly. Some can be self assembled, or we can install them for you.
     </p>
     <br/>
     <p>
      There are a number of options to help customize your Green Room to your own specification. Our range of acoustic rooms offer some of the best sound attenuation available, and with the ability to put our external studio down the end of the garden, you should have no worries about disrupting the neighbours.
     </p>
     <br/>
     <p>
      Our acoustic rooms can be used for all sorts of applications, such as music practise, music teaching, classrooms, sound recording and production, voice over, film and television, broadcasting, audiology, and scientific testing to name but a few.
     </p>
