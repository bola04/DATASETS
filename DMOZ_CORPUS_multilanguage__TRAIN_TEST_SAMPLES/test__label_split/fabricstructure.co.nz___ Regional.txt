       <h1>
        <strong>
         Fabric Structures
        </strong>
       </h1>
       <h2>
        Fabric Structures (FSS) is a leader in
        <em>
         “creative fabric engineering.”
        </em>
       </h2>
       <p>
        From our New Zealand base we specialise in the design and fabrication of bespoke fabric
        <a href="/architectural-structures/">
         architectural
        </a>
        and
        <a href="/event-art-structures/">
         event structures
        </a>
        . We also specialise in the rigging and application of a range of
        <a href="/products/">
         proprietary architectural membrane products.
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        We work with and are experts in the use and implementation of a range of materials including ETFE, Lycra, Mesh and PVC.
       </p>
       <p>
        From tensioned membrane structures, to canopies and inflatables we have an enviable track record of delivering projects of great beauty and technical difficulty.
       </p>
       <p>
        Our unique fabric structure solutions extend to a diverse range of disciplines and projects including education and commercial buildings, events and exhibitions, facades and recent projects include:
        <a href="/projects/hagley-cricket-pavilion.html" title="Hagley Cricket Pavilion">
         Hagley Cricket Pavilion
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="/projects/darling-harbour-pavillon.html" title="Darling harbour Pavilion">
         Darling Harbour Pavilion
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="/projects/the-cloud.html">
         The Cloud
        </a>
        and
        <a href="/projects/the-zoo-kea.html">
         zoo aviary enclosures at Auckland Zoo
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        Renowned for our creativity and innovation, we are passionately committed to the continuing development of architectural textile fabrication and design.
       </p>
