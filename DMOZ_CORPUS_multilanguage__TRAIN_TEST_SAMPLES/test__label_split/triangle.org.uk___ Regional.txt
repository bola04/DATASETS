          Welcome!
         </h3>
        </div>
        <div class="panel-body">
         <p>
          <strong>
           We ran a celebratory event on Friday 13 July at Teesside University. This included a presentation on Children and Young People’s Evidence by
           <a href="https://www.triangle.org.uk/person/kimberly-collins">
            Dr Kimberly Collins
           </a>
           .
          </strong>
         </p>
         <h5>
          <a href="https://www.triangle.org.uk/gallery/triangle-in-the-north" target="_blank">
           Click here to see photos from the event
          </a>
         </h5>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <img alt="" src="/files/General Images/untitled.jpg" style="width: 400px; height: 219px;"/>
          </strong>

         <p>
          Read about Triangle's work in a 2018 article in the Guardian
          <strong>
           <a href="https://www.theguardian.com/society/2018/feb/17/child-witnesses-one-girl-evidence-hamster" target="_blank">
            here
           </a>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Triangle is an independent organisation enabling children to communicate about important things, especially in legal proceedings. Our vision is to reposition children and young people in society as competent communicators and active agents in their own lives. In the last 20 years we have worked with over 10,000 children and young people across the UK, through intermediary, advocacy, consultancy, specialist support, interviewing and expert opinion services
         </p>
         <div class="aa-content collapse" data-content-a="&lt;p&gt;Triangle provides&lt;strong&gt; skilled support for children and young people in different settings including legal proceedings&lt;/strong&gt; and enables children's communication when it matters most, for example when children or young people’s evidence is required by the courts, when their views are needed to inform decisions about their lives, and when adults are trying to understand and respond to concerning behaviours.&lt;/p&gt;
 
 &lt;p&gt;Triangle is an &lt;strong&gt;independent organisation&lt;/strong&gt;; not part of any government department, charity or voluntary agency.&lt;/p&gt;
 
 &lt;p&gt;We have a strong&lt;a href=&quot;http://triangle.org.uk/who-we-are&quot;&gt; multidisciplinary team&lt;/a&gt; with a shared value base, skilled at communicating with children and young people with a wide range of needs.&lt;/p&gt;
 
 &lt;p&gt;We have particular expertise with very young children and those with complex communication needs arising from impairment, neglect or trauma. We aim to set the standard in the UK and beyond for careful and effective communication with children and young people.&lt;/p&gt;
 
 &lt;p&gt;We are regularly instructed in the most complex and high profile situations, and have been involved in many landmark developments in children’s evidence.&lt;/p&gt;
 
 &lt;p&gt;We also provide &lt;a href=&quot;http://triangle.org.uk/service/trainers&quot;&gt;training&lt;/a&gt; and &lt;a href=&quot;http://triangle.org.uk/resources&quot;&gt;resources&lt;/a&gt; and contribute to national policy and guidance.&lt;/p&gt;
 
 &lt;p&gt;&lt;a href=&quot;/page/about&quot;&gt;Find out more about Triangle&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;" data-content-c="&lt;p&gt;Triangle does different things. Triangle makes sure young people are listened to about important things – things that have happened, or things that matter a lot. Triangle supports young people with their feelings and their behaviour. Triangle helps young people to stay safe.&lt;o:p&gt;&lt;/o:p&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
 
 &lt;p&gt;Triangle is independent. That means we are free to do things our way. We aren't part of social services, the government, the police or the courts. Young people work with Triangle to help us do things better. We always do things in a calm and safe way.&lt;o:p&gt;&lt;/o:p&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
 
 &lt;p&gt;On this website you can find out all about Triangle and what we do.&lt;o:p&gt;&lt;/o:p&gt;&lt;/p&gt;" id="intro-expand-target">

          <p>
           Triangle provides
           <strong>
            skilled support for children and young people in different settings including legal proceedings
           </strong>
           and enables children's communication when it matters most, for example when children or young people’s evidence is required by the courts, when their views are needed to inform decisions about their lives, and when adults are trying to understand and respond to concerning behaviours.
          </p>
          <p>
           Triangle is an
           <strong>
            independent organisation
           </strong>
           ; not part of any government department, charity or voluntary agency.
          </p>
          <p>
           We have a strong
           <a href="http://triangle.org.uk/who-we-are">
            multidisciplinary team
           </a>
           with a shared value base, skilled at communicating with children and young people with a wide range of needs.
          </p>
          <p>
           We have particular expertise with very young children and those with complex communication needs arising from impairment, neglect or trauma. We aim to set the standard in the UK and beyond for careful and effective communication with children and young people.
          </p>
          <p>
           We are regularly instructed in the most complex and high profile situations, and have been involved in many landmark developments in children’s evidence.
          </p>
          <p>
           We also provide
           <a href="http://triangle.org.uk/service/trainers">
            training
           </a>
           and
           <a href="http://triangle.org.uk/resources">
            resources
           </a>
           and contribute to national policy and guidance.
          </p>
