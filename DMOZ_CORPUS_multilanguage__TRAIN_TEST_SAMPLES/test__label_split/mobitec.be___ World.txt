     <h2>
      Mobitec: manufacturer from chairs, seats and tables
     </h2>
     <p>
      Founded 1990, Mobitec Systems SA is a Belgian family business specialized in the production of
      <strong>
       high quality furniture
      </strong>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
      Office chairs, wooden tables, office tables, seats or professional furniture: Mobitec provides its output to an exclusively professional clientele.
     </p>
     <p>
      Mobitec strives to anticipate and meet its customers’ specific needs: a comprehensive collection of
      <strong>
       innovative design
      </strong>
      , a wide range of finishes, a flexible production, meticulous quality control and after-sales service.
     </p>
     <p>
      With the confidence of its customers, Mobitec is now the
      <strong>
       first chair manufacturer in Belgium
      </strong>
      (wooden chair, office chair, seats). The company employs nearly 500 people and distributes its products throughout Europe.
     </p>
