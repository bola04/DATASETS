        <h1 class="title">
         Heritage Railway with Train Rides on the 1st and 3rd Sunday of each month
        </h1>
        <div class="buildmode-full">
         <div class="node node-type-page odd full-node node-type-page clear-block">
          <div class="nd-region-middle-wrapper nd-no-sidebars">
           <div class="nd-region-middle">
            <div class="field field-body">
             <p>
              <span style="line-height: 1.5em; font-size: 1em;">
               Silver Stream Railway is a working heritage railway
              </span>
              <a href="/information-and-map" style="line-height: 1.5em; font-size: 1em;">
               located on Reynolds Bach Dr in Upper Hutt
              </a>
              <span style="line-height: 1.5em; font-size: 1em;">
               , Wellington, New Zealand and is open for train rides on
              </span>
              <strong style="line-height: 1.5em; font-family: inherit; font-size: 1em; font-style: inherit;">
               the 1st and 3rd Sundays of each month and selected public holidays, 11am to 4pm.
              </strong>
              <span style="line-height: 1.5em; font-size: 1em;">
               The railway provides entertainment for all the family and is one of Upper
              </span>
              Hutt's
              <span style="line-height: 1.5em; font-size: 1em;">
               biggest attractions.
              </span>
             </p>
             <h2>
              <strong>
               <span style="font-size: 1em;">
                Our Next Operating day is:
               </span>
              </strong>
             </h2>
             <p>
              <a href="/sites/default/files/5aug.jpg">
               <img alt="" height="260" src="/sites/default/files/resize/5aug-400x260.jpg" style="font-family: Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, &amp;quot;Bitstream Vera Sans&amp;quot;, sans-serif; font-size: 12.8px; font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: 19.2px; width: 400px; height: 260px;" width="400"/>
              </a>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 1em; line-height: 1.5em;">
               Silver Stream Railway's mission is to restore and preserve heritage railway memorabilia - in particular items
              </span>
              <span style="font-size: 1em;">
               that relate to the Wellington region.
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               We are open to the public for train rides on the 1st and 3rd Sun
              </strong>
              <strong style="font-family: inherit; font-size: 1em; font-style: inherit; line-height: 1.5em;">
               days of each month with a
               <a href="/locomotives/steam-locomotives">
                steam locomotive
               </a>
               in service, supplemented by
               <a href="/locomotives/diesel-locomotives">
                diesel locomotive
               </a>
               or
               <a href="/locomotives/railcars">
                railcar
               </a>
               .
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="120" scrolling="no" src="//www.youtube-nocookie.com/embed/hHo37MFa1e8?rel=0" width="220">
              </iframe>
             </p>
             <h2>
              About
             </h2>
             <p>
              Silver Stream Railway (named after the silver coloured stream {Hull's Creek} that runs beside the railway) is a heritage railway near the suburb of Silverstream in the Hutt Valley 30km from Wellington, New Zealand. It regularly operates former New Zealand Goverment Railway and ex industrial steam and diesel locomotives along a restored section of the Wellington to Upper Hutt railway that was bypassed in 1954.
             </p>
             <h2>
              History
             </h2>
             <p>
              The beginnings of Silver Stream Railway were in 1967 when the Wellington branch of the New Zealand Railway and Locomotive Society (which changed is name to Silver Stream Railway Incorporated in 1983) began a collection of locomotives and rolling stock. Tracklaying on the old formation of the Hutt Valley Line did not begin until 1977. The collection of
              <a href="/locomotives">
               locomotives
              </a>
              and
              <a href="/rolling-stock">
               rolling stock
              </a>
              had previously been stored at a site by the Gracefield Branch in Seaview, and this was transferred to the present Silverstream site in 1984. The official opening of the full 1.5 kilometre track took place on 15 February 1986.
             </p>
