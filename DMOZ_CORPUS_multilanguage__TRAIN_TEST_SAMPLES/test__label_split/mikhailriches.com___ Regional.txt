         <h3>
          <a href="http://www.mikhailriches.com/2018/07/double-win-at-the-housing-design-awards/">
           Double win at the Housing Design Awards
          </a>
         </h3>
         <p>
          We are delighted to have won two awards at the
          <a href="http://hdawards.org/schemes?_sft_scheme_year=2018&amp;_sft_scheme_award=winner&amp;_sft_scheme_architects=mikhail-riches">
           Housing Design Awards
          </a>
          last night. Both
          <a href="http://www.mikhailriches.com/project/st-andrews-riverside-tiverton/">
           St Andrew’s Riverside
          </a>
          in Tiverton for 3 Rivers Developments and
          <a href="http://www.mikhailriches.com/project/croydon/">
           Croydon Smaller Sites
          </a>
          for Brick X Brick won project awards. You can see all the winners from last night
          <a href="http://hdawards.org/schemes/?_sft_scheme_year=2018&amp;_sft_scheme_award=winner">
           here.
          </a>

           Good presentational / verbal communication skills

           , stating ‘Architectural Assistant Part I or 2’ in the subject line.
