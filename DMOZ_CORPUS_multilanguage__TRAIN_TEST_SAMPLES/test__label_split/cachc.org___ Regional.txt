      <h1>
       Help, hope &amp; healing.
      </h1>
      <div class="xpagebodytext">
       <p>
        The Children’s Advocacy Center: The Emmy Haney House is dedicated to serving those affected by alleged child abuse or neglect by offering help, hope, and healing.
       </p>
       <p>
        The CAC facilitates the teamwork essential for effective intervention and healing and is essentially the "child's office" - a location where all professionals and agencies responsible for investigating and providing services can provide a coordinated response on a child's developmentally appropriate level.
       </p>
