       <p>
        <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">

       <p>
        Welcome to Standing Frames, part of the Active Linx group. We are specialists in proving the latest products in the UK to help people with a range of disabilities - we have the knowledge and experience to enable the user to get the most out of using a standing frame.
       </p>
       <p>
        Paul Moore, founder of Active Linx, is an active wheelchair user of 30 years with invaluable first hand experience of the benefits standing frames can bring - combined with 20 years of providing frames to people across the UK. You may have reached our website from a personal recommendation or just searching for a standing frame because you have told that this could benefit you, a friend or relative.
       </p>
       <p>
        The evidence and research is there for all to read and see with many studies carried out over the years across the World - using standing frames to 'stand' brings with it many health benefits and decreases the risk of secondary problems. Just to name a few standing can greatly reduce the risk of pressure sores, digestive complications and help with spasticity that is experienced on a daily basis.
       </p>
       <p>
        However it's not necessarily the case that one standing frame fits all - there are many different models available and over the last 20 years Paul has seen and been involved in the development of new and exciting frames that using the latest technology further increase the benefits. Feel free to contact Paul to discuss any questions you may have or to arrange a free demonstration.
