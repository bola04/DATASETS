       <h3>
        Venice, Florida Estate Planning and Probate Attorneys
       </h3>
       <p>
        Klingbeil &amp; Roberts, P.A., has provided knowledgeable personal service and legal guidance to clients in Venice, Florida and surrounding areas since 1993. We work to maintain personal contact with all of our clients as we guide them through challenging legal issues in estate administration, estate planning, real estate and other matters.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Florida Estate Planning, Elder Law and Probate
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Our estate planning and administration practice includes helping people plan for the future and helping family members with difficult legal issues in
        <a href="/Practice-Areas/Probate-Administration.shtml">
         probate
        </a>
        after a loved one has died. We assist our clients with creating
        <a href="/Practice-Areas/Estate-Planning-Wills-Trusts.shtml">
         wills or trusts
        </a>
        , and address concerns involving
        <a href="/Practice-Areas/Incapacity-Guardianship.shtml">
         guardianships
        </a>
        when a family member suffers an illness, injury or other condition that renders them incapable of making important decisions on their own.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Personal Representation in Real Estate Matters
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Our lawyers also advise clients in a full spectrum of residential and commercial
        <a href="/Practice-Areas/Real-Estate.shtml">
         real estate
        </a>
        , including addressing
        <a href="/Practice-Areas/Title-Insurance.shtml">
         title insurance
        </a>
        in real estate transactions and
        <a href="/Practice-Areas/Land-Use-Planning.shtml">
         land use planning
        </a>
        . Additionally, we regularly advise clients on business organization plans. Having practiced law in Venice since the early 1980s, our attorneys have substantial experience in the area and with the applicable Florida laws.
       </p>
       <p style="background-color: #c0c0c0; padding: 8px">
        Our lawyers assist clients from A to Z through the Florida real estate process, from contract to closing, including issuance of title insurance, preparation of loan documents, and all other details of the real estate transaction.
       </p>
       <p>
        Our substantial knowledge of title insurance and real estate transactions in Florida helps us to support our clients. When contract or title issues come up, we work quickly to ensure that our clients receive clear title.
       </p>
       <p>
        Clients often return to us for additional assistance through the estate administration process. They seek our help when a loved one dies leaving a will we drafted or when they have questions related to a trust we set up.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Contact our Florida Law Firm
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Please contact Klingbeil &amp; Roberts, P.A., in Venice, for more information about estate planning, probate administration, real estate matters, title insurance, or guardianship. We can be reached by
        <strong>
         phone at (941) 882-1327
        </strong>
        ,             E-mail , or by filling out the
        <a href="/Contact.shtml">
         intake form
        </a>
        on our Contact Us page.
       </p>
