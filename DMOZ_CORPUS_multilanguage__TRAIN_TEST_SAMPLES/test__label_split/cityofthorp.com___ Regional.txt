         <h1 align="center" style="text-align: center;">
          Official Website of the City of Thorp, Wisconsin
         </h1>
         <p>
          Welcome to the official website of the City of Thorp, Wisconsin. Located in the northwestern corner of Clark County on Highway 29, Thorp is the gateway to Wisconsin's agricultural epicenter. Thorp has a healthy local economy and a busy main street, where you'll find a unique blend of quaint horse-drawn wagons and modern culture and technology. Whether you're looking for a local event, or information about city government--you'll find it here. Please visit our website often to keep informed about what's happening in Thorp -- and please remember to patronize our local businesses. Welcome!
         </p>
