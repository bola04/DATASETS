        <div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;display:block;">
         <font color="#990000">
          Welcome,fellow Brighton High School alums!
          <br/>
          <br/>
          The purpose of this website is to provide a place where we can all go, visit and remember those BHS classmates that are no longer with us. I intend it to be place where you can check your graduating class to see who has left us and to think of the good times.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          To that end, I am attempting to list any person who attended Brighton High School, regardless if they graduated. A failure to graduate did not minimize the impact a person had on our lives.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          You can help! If you see I am missing a person, or have a person, but am missing information, please drop me a line or send me a picture. I can be reached at info@fallenbulldogs.com.See also the
          <a href="/do-you-have-information.html" title="">
           Do You Have Information?
          </a>
          page.
         </font>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <font color="#990000">
          Be on the lookout for links on any given memorial. I might have found an obituary, online memorial, or information about how this person passed away. Just click on any different colored text and it will take you to the information. Also, try clicking on a picture. If the original picture was bigger, clicking on it will bring it up to full size.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          If you have a question, please check Frequently Asked Questions. If it isn't addressed, drop me a line. But, be patient. There arethousands of BHS alum and only one of me.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Bill Kretzer
          <br/>
          Brighton High School
          <br/>
          Class of 1981
