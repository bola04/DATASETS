         <article about="/home" class="node node-content-page article odd node-full clearfix" id="article-1" role="article" typeof="sioc:Item foaf:Document">
          <span class="rdf-meta element-hidden" content="Welcome to Mingarry, Moidart and The Ardnamurchan" property="dc:title">
          </span>
          <span class="rdf-meta element-hidden" content="0" datatype="xsd:integer" property="sioc:num_replies">
          </span>
          <div class="node-content">
           <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden view-mode-full">
            <div class="field-items">
             <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
              <h1 style="text-align: center;">
               Welcome to Mingarry, Moidart and The Ardnamurchan
              </h1>
              <p>
               <a class="colorbox colorbox-insert-image" data-colorbox-gallery="gallery-all" href="/sites/mingarrylodges.com/files/content/castle_tioram_210612.jpg" title="Castle Tioram and the beach at Dorlin at low tide in Autumn">
                <img alt="Castle Tioram and the beach at Dorlin at low tide in Autumn" class="image-medium" src="/sites/mingarrylodges.com/files/styles/medium/public/content/castle_tioram_210612.jpg?itok=_u0btwCF" style="width: 220px; height: 147px; margin: 5px 10px; float: left;" title="Castle Tioram and the beach at Dorlin at low tide in Autumn"/>
               </a>
               Find time for nature by taking a holiday at Mingarry Lodges in the heart of Moidart, in the scenic West Highlands of Scotland, at the gateway to The Ardnamurchan peninsula.
              </p>
              <p>
               The area is renowned for its spectacular scenery, stunning beaches and wild, beautiful, unspoilt landscapes, populated with some of Scotland's most iconic wildlife.
              </p>
              <p>
               In addition, Fort William (the Outdoor Capital of the UK) and the Ben Nevis range, as well as The Small Isles, Isle of Mull and the Isle of Skye are within reach, allowing you to explore some of the best rugged landscapes and seascapes in the Western Highlands.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a class="colorbox colorbox-insert-image" data-colorbox-gallery="gallery-all" href="/sites/mingarrylodges.com/files/content/pine_marten_201612a.jpg" title="A pine marten in the woods behind Mingarry Lodges">
                <img alt="A pine marten in the woods behind Mingarry Lodges" class="image-medium" src="/sites/mingarrylodges.com/files/styles/medium/public/content/pine_marten_201612a.jpg?itok=TPx9h2ot" style="width: 220px; height: 147px; margin: 5px 10px; float: left;" title="A pine marten in the woods behind Mingarry Lodges"/>
               </a>
               Stay in one of two exclusive woodland cabins with views of Loch Shiel to the front and access to the woods, hills and moorland behind. The mixed woodland support a thriving population of woodland birds, red squirrels and pine martens, that are regularly seen from the lodges, as well as affording occasional sightings of red and roe deer. You may even be lucky and see the rare and elusive wild cat.
              </p>
              <p>
               The two bedroom self catering lodges are equipped to the highest standard and combine rustic charm with all the traditional home comforts.
              </p>
              <p>
               There are some secluded lovely sandy beaches nearby, including the lovely beach at Dorlin, which affords access to the Eilean Tioram at low tide, a tidal island surmounted by the Castle Tioram, the former stronghold of MacDonald of Clan Ranald, and now a romantic ruin.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a class="colorbox colorbox-insert-image" data-colorbox-gallery="gallery-all" href="/sites/mingarrylodges.com/files/content/over_the_dunes.jpg" title="One of the secret beaches near The Singing Sands">
                <img alt="One of the secret beaches near The Singing Sands" class="image-medium" src="/sites/mingarrylodges.com/files/styles/medium/public/content/over_the_dunes.jpg?itok=GkdcpDUK" style="width: 220px; height: 147px; margin: 5px 10px; float: left;" title="One of the secret beaches near The Singing Sands"/>
               </a>
               Loch Shiel is a good place to look for raptors, including golden eagle, white-tailed eagle, hen harrier, osprey and buzzard, as well as black throated divers. Look out for otters and common and grey seals from the Garbh Eilean Wildlife Hide at Ardery, a short drive away, situated on the shores of Loch Sunart.
              </p>
              <p>
               There are also some lovely coastal and woodland walks nearby, as well as a number of Corbetts and Marilyns to climb.
              </p>
              <p>
               The cabins provide the perfect Highland retreat for couples, families and family groups who wish to explore the great outdoors; with scenic walks, secluded sandy beaches, sheltered sea lochs, romantic castles, towering peaks and abundant wildlife nearby.
              </p>
              <p>
               Find out more by exploring what Moidart and the Ardnamurchan have to offer on our website and view full details of our
               <a href="/ardnamurchan-self-catering">
                self catering accommodation
               </a>
               ,
               <a href="/activities">
                things to do
               </a>
               and
               <a href="/activities/wildlife">
                wildlife
               </a>
               spotting opportunities.
              </p>
              <hr/>
              <p>
              </p>
