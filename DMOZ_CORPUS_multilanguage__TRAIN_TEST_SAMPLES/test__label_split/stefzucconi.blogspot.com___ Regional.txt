             <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
              <a href="http://stefzucconi.blogspot.com/2011/11/mainstream-conspiraloons-202-james.html">
               Mainstream Conspiraloons #204 James Delingpole
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header">
              <div class="post-header-line-1">
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-5972168357879541459" itemprop="description articleBody">
              <span style="font-size:130%;">
               <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-cRl5Uq2VN4g/TsXDc-QqNoI/AAAAAAAAGaU/oz5_AC2Za4Y/s1600/Adam-Blog--James-Delingpo-007.jpg" onblur="try {parent.deselectBloggerImageGracefully();} catch(e) {}" style="font-family: arial;">
                <img alt="" border="0" id="BLOGGER_PHOTO_ID_5676157807887005314" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-cRl5Uq2VN4g/TsXDc-QqNoI/AAAAAAAAGaU/oz5_AC2Za4Y/s400/Adam-Blog--James-Delingpo-007.jpg" style="display:block; margin:0px auto 10px; text-align:center;cursor:pointer; cursor:hand;width: 400px; height: 240px;"/>
               </a>
              </span>
              <span style="font-size:130%;">
               <br/>
               <span style="font-family:arial;">
                Until recently, the single greatest contribution made to public discourse by
               </span>
               <span style="font-style: italic; font-family:arial;">
                Daily Telegraph
               </span>
               <span style="font-family:arial;">
                columnist and professional tory irritant about town, James Delingpole, has been to help polarise the debate about alleged Anthropogenic Global Warming along conventional Right vs Left lines; making it a whole lot easier for the average Jane or Joe on the street to adopt a position on AGW without having to know or think very much about the scientific basis for AGW at all
               </span>
              </span>
              <div style="font-family: arial;">
               <span style="font-size:130%;">
                <span class="Apple-style-span">
                 <br/>
                </span>
               </span>
              </div>
              <div>
               <span style="font-size:130%;">
                <span class="Apple-style-span">
                 <span style="font-family: arial;">
                  Left Wing = AGW proponent. Right Wing = Denier. Simples
                 </span>
                 <br/>
                </span>
                <br/>
                <span style="font-family:arial;">
                 And when it comes to economic or foreign policy matters James can be most succinctly described as an almost perfect Melanie Phillips clone, but with a cock (though I am making unsubstantiated morphological assumptions here)
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                </span>
               </span>
               <div style="text-align: center;">
                <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-uBa7A1F_OMk/TsXMkJQ0Z2I/AAAAAAAAGag/OVxPfNoSlWg/s1600/_46813386_melanie_phillips_qt.jpg">
                 <img alt="" border="0" id="BLOGGER_PHOTO_ID_5676167826704197474" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-uBa7A1F_OMk/TsXMkJQ0Z2I/AAAAAAAAGag/OVxPfNoSlWg/s400/_46813386_melanie_phillips_qt.jpg" style="display:block; margin:0px auto 10px; text-align:center;cursor:pointer; cursor:hand;width: 160px; height: 138px;"/>
                </a>
                <span style="font-style: italic;font-size:100%;">
                 <span style="font-family:arial;">
                  James Delingpole
                 </span>
                 <br/>
                </span>
               </div>
               <span style="font-size:130%;">
                <br/>
                <span style="font-family:arial;">
                 That was until James'
                </span>
                <a href="http://blogs.telegraph.co.uk/news/jamesdelingpole/100118071/goldman-sachs-rules-the-world/" style="font-family: arial;">
                 most recent column
                </a>
                <span style="font-family:arial;">
                 on the subject of the Goldman's coup currently underway in Western Europe...
                </span>
                <br/>
                <br/>
                <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-B-L6ngtlJr4/TsXDWF7GMdI/AAAAAAAAGaI/ZZJbv6e6t0g/s1600/goldmans.jpg" style="font-family: arial;">
                 <img alt="" border="0" id="BLOGGER_PHOTO_ID_5676157689684963794" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-B-L6ngtlJr4/TsXDWF7GMdI/AAAAAAAAGaI/ZZJbv6e6t0g/s400/goldmans.jpg" style="display:block; margin:0px auto 10px; text-align:center;cursor:pointer; cursor:hand;width: 400px; height: 150px;"/>
                </a>
               </span>
               <span style="font-size:130%;">
                <br/>
                <span style=" font-style: italic; color: rgb(51, 51, 255);font-family:arial;">
                 "...We need to find common ground here, I think â€“ those of us who believe (as the Occupy crowd do) that the current crisis is caused by the failure of capitalism and those of us who believe (as I do) that it is caused by the failure of corporatism, welfarism and â€“ ultimately â€“ the fiat money system. While we are busy being distracted by our particular ideological hobby horses, we are being shafted by a political bankster corporatist elite which doesn't give a stuff about us because we are the little people and we are of no consequence to the New World Order they wish to impose on us.
                </span>
                <br/>
                <br/>
                <span style=" font-style: italic; color: rgb(51, 51, 255);font-family:arial;">
                 Beware the Vampire Squid: whether you are on the Left or the Right it will feast on your blood and suck you dry all the same."
                </span>
                <br/>
                <br/>
                <br/>
                <span style="font-family:arial;">
                 My oh my, how those corporate journalists enjoy bashing internet-based conspiraloonery, yet are gradually finding themselves drawn to the language of net-based loondom as their own lexicon becomes less and less capable of describing the shit that's going down
                </span>
                <br/>
                <br/>
                <span style="font-family:arial;">
                 though, obviously, James wouldn't want the Little People to become
                </span>
                <span style="font-style: italic; font-family:arial;">
                 too
                </span>
                <span style="font-family:arial;">
                 focused on the crimes of the Big People at the expense of those distractive ideological hobby horses as, let's be honest here, they're a nice little earner
                </span>
                <br/>
               </span>
               <div style="text-align: right;">
                <span style="color: rgb(255, 255, 204);font-size:130%;">
                 <span style="font-family:arial;">
                  .
                 </span>
                </span>
                <br/>

             <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
              <a href="http://stefzucconi.blogspot.com/2011/11/i-cant-quite-put-my-finger-on-what-it.html">
               That does not compute
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header">
              <div class="post-header-line-1">
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-3475627703006979396" itemprop="description articleBody">
              <span style="font-size:130%;">
               <span style="font-family:arial;">
                I can't quite put my finger on what it is exactly but something about Deek Jackson's
               </span>
               <a href="http://deekjackson.com/">
                <span style="font-style: italic; font-family:arial;">
                 Conspiracy Clown
                </span>
               </a>
               <span style="font-family:arial;">
                project suggests to me, in the gentlest of ways, that Deek is currently tussling with the teensy weensiest of logical paradoxes
               </span>
               <br/>
               <br/>
              </span>
              <div style="text-align: center; font-family:arial;">
               <span style="font-size:130%;">
                <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="213" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/SGM7lLy1Pb4?rel=0" width="360">
                </iframe>
                <br/>
               </span>
              </div>
              <span style="font-size:130%;">
               <br/>
               <span style="font-family:arial;">
                Deek appears to be suggesting that anyone who gets angry when someone disagrees with them is a dick. He appears to be quite angry that people behave like this
               </span>
               <br/>
               <br/>
               <span style="font-family:arial;">
                Fans of
               </span>
               <span style="font-style: italic; font-family:arial;">
                Star Trek
               </span>
               <span style="font-family:arial;">
                will recognise this as being the same strategy William Shatner routinely used to destroy otherwise impregnable machine intelligences through force of
                <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Changeling_%28Star_Trek:_The_Original_Series%29#Plot">
                 paradoxical argument
                </a>
                alone
                <br/>
                <br/>
               </span>
              </span>
              <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-B-aFXF3ETIM/TsWkyYh6WeI/AAAAAAAAGZ8/JRDpeMdr3gg/s1600/320x240.jpg">
               <img alt="" border="0" id="BLOGGER_PHOTO_ID_5676124090855479778" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-B-aFXF3ETIM/TsWkyYh6WeI/AAAAAAAAGZ8/JRDpeMdr3gg/s400/320x240.jpg" style="display:block; margin:0px auto 10px; text-align:center;cursor:pointer; cursor:hand;width: 320px; height: 240px;"/>
              </a>
              <br/>
              <span style="font-size:130%;">
               <span style="font-family:arial;">
                Human intelligence is, of course, clearly superior to machine intelligence as it is (usually) more than capable of processing contradictory data with the aid of contradictory beliefs, without melting or destroying any mining colonies
                <br/>
                <br/>
                As it happens I agree with what Deek is trying to say (which is why I'm posting his video) but he comes out with almost as much bullshit as many of the truthers he's criticising, and he defends his bullshit just as aggressively as they defend theirs
                <br/>
               </span>
              </span>
              <div style="text-align: right;">
               <span style="color: rgb(255, 255, 204);font-size:130%;">
                .
