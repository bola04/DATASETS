            <font size="3">
             <font color="#000000" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">
              This
             </font>
            </font>
            <font color="#000000" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">
             is the home page of POG - the Pan Oceanic Owners Group. POG 
                   is an association of owners of Pan Oceanic sailboats with members 
                   around the globe - we now have 35 members with boats in 6 different 
                   countries. Several of the boats have circumnavigated, and POG 
                   members include those cruising the waters of the Caribbean, 
                   Europe, North America and Asia.
             <br/>
             <br/>
            </font>
            <font color="#000000" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">
             If 
                   you are a Pan Oceanic owner just finding POG - welcome. POG 
                   was just formed in 2000 and is still finding boats &amp; owners 
                   on a regular basis. POG provides a forum to exchange information 
                   about our boats.....the improvements and changes we have made, 
                   the stories of cruising we have enjoyed.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             <img align="left" border="1" height="49" src="pogpics/ted_designed2.gif" width="282"/>
             If 
                   you are a prospective owner, please consider yourself welcome 
                   also. Those searching for a comfortable pilot house cruising 
                   boat will find the Pan Oceanics deserve attention. While many 
                   pilot house sailboats are really motorsailors and poor sailing 
                   boats, the Ted Brewer designed Pan Oceanics are truly sailboats 
                   that happen to enjoy pilot houses. The hull design is moderate/heavy, 
                   providing a suitable compromise between cruising comfort and 
                   sailing performance.
             <br/>
             <br/>
            </font>
            <font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">
             <img align="left" height="196" hspace="15" src="pogpics/drawing3.gif" width="155"/>
            </font>
            <font color="#000000" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">
             There 
                   are three core models - the Pan Oceanic 38, 43, and 46. The 
                   boats have a distinctive pilot house / flush deck appearance. 
                   Pan Oceanic yachts feature heavily built fiberglass hulls and 
                   rich interior woodwork.
            </font>
           </p>
           <p>
            <font color="#000000" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">
             All 
                   the boats do share a fine pedigree - they were all designed 
                   by renowned naval architect Ted Brewer.
            </font>
           </p>
           <p>
            <font color="#000000" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">
             Enjoy 
                   cruising around the site - you will find much information about 
                   the boats including a history of the design, specifications 
                   and lots of photos.
            </font>
