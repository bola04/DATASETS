        <p>
         <strong>
          Atul Kulkarni
         </strong>
         is an Indian Actor born in Belgaum, Karnataka, India on 10th Sept 1965. He has worked in more than 60 films in 7 languages. An alumni of
         <strong>
          National School of Drama (N.S.D.)
         </strong>
         ,  New Delhi , Atul has won many awards including 2 national awards. He enjoys the public and critical acclaim for his work not only nationally but also internationally. He is one of the very few actors who are also known for their articles, interviews and comments on social and political views. Details of his active social work in the fields of education
         <strong>
          (QUEST)
         </strong>
         and environment can be found on this site along with of course his Acting carrier...
         <br/>
         Happy Viewing!!
        </p>
