      <h3 class="headline">
       Brookwood Strategic Partnership with
       <img alt="Polartec" src="images/polartec.png" style="display: inline; margin-top: 3px;"/>
      </h3>
      <span class="line">
      </span>
      <div class="clearfix">
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="four columns imageholder">
      <a href="/brookwood-military/strategic-partnership">
       <img alt="Military Picture" src="images/military.jpg"/>
      </a>
     </div>
     <div class="twelve columns">
      <p style="margin-right: 5px;">
       Combining forces and expertise, Brookwood and Polartec have entered into a strategic partnership to provide a superior weather protection system that optimizes operational efficiency in all environments. The system is based on the U.S. Military’s Generation III Extended Cold Weather Clothing System and proven proprietary technology. Base layers excel at moisture management while outer layers keep out even the roughest weather to protect against any climate threat.
      </p>
