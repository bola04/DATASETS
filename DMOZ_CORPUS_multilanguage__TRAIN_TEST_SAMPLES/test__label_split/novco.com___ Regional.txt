                                  Innovations are always welcomed. MYBRANDS retail chain, which constantly makes its customers happy with innovations, has renewed the design and terms of discount cards. The following changes have been made in the issue, transfer and storage processes of the renewed discount cards: New terms are as follows: It is possible to get 5% MYBRANDS discount card by making purchase of 50 AZN at any store of MYBRANDS retail chain with a receipt in a day.  There is no discount for such purchases. Discount is only made after a card is issued for upcoming purchases. Cards are issued without charge, and card owners get a 5% discount for the goods of MYBRANDS retail chain.      To get a 10% discount card, you need to make a purchase of 1,000 AZN in a year. This amount may be accumulated after one or several purchases. If any client doesn’t make purchases of 1,000 AZN in a year, card balance will be zeroed out, but the card will not be blocked.  To get a 10% discount card, clients need to make a purchase of 1,000 AZN from MYBRANDS retail chain in a year after getting a 5% discount card. This amount may be accumulated after one or several purchases. When switching to the new card, 1,000 AZN will be deleted from the balance, and the remaining amount will be transferred to the balance of a 10% discount card.  To get a new card, 3 AZN will be charged, and owners of this card will get a 10% discount for the goods of MYBRANDS retail chain.      If a card holder, after getting a 10% discount card, doesn’t make purchases of 3,000 AZN in a year which is the upper limit for this card and can’t switch to a 15% discount card, he/she should make purchases of 1,000 AZN in a year, which is the lower limit, to maintain the card interest.    If a card holder makes purchases of 1,000 AZN in a year which is the lower limit for the 10% discount card, his/her balance for the next year will be cut off by 50%, and the card interest will not be changed.  If a card holder can’t reach the lower limit of a 10% discount card in a year, balance of the card will be deleted, and the card will be replaced by a 5% discount card.  To get a 15% discount card, clients need to make a purchase of 3,000 AZN from MYBRANDS retail chain in a year after getting a 10% discount card. This amount may be accumulated after one or several purchases. When switching to the new card, 3,000 AZN will be deleted from the balance, and the remaining amount will be transferred to the balance of a 15% discount card.  To get a new card, 3 AZN will be charged, and owners of this card will get a 15% discount for the goods of MYBRANDS retail chain.      If a card holder, after getting a 15% discount card, doesn’t make purchases of 6,000 AZN in a year which is the upper limit for this card and can’t switch to a 20% discount card, the card owner should make purchases of 3,000 AZN in a year which is the lower limit to maintain the card interest.    If a card holder makes purchases of 3,000 AZN in a year which is the lower limit for the 15% discount card, his/her balance for the next year will be cut off by 50%, and the card interest will not be changed. If the card holder can’t reach the lower limit of a 15% discount card in a year, balance of the card will be deleted, and the card will be replaced by a 10% discount card.  To get 20% discount card, clients need to make a purchase of 6,000 AZN from MYBRANDS retail chain in a year after getting a 15% discount card. This amount may be accumulated after one or several purchases. When switching to the new card, 6,000 AZN will be deleted from the balance, and the remaining amount will be transferred to the balance of a 20% discount card.  To get a new card, 3 AZN will be charged, and owners of this card will get a 20% discount for the goods of MYBRANDS retail chain.     If a card holder, after getting a 20% discount card, doesn’t make purchases of 20,000 AZN in a year which is the upper limit for this card and can’t switch to a VIP 20% discount card, the card holder should make purchases of 6,000 AZN in a year which is the lower limit to maintain the card interest.    If a card holder makes purchases of 6,000 AZN in a year which is the lower limit for a 20% discount card, his/her balance for the next year will be deleted completely as 50%, and the card interest will not be changed.  If the card holder can’t reach the lower limit of 20% discount card in a year, balance of the card will be deleted, and the card will be replaced by 15% discount card.  To get a VIP card, clients need to make a purchase of 20,000 AZN from MYBRANDS retail chain in a year after getting a 20% discount card. VIP 20% cards are issued free of charge, and owners of these cards will get a 20% discount for the goods of MYBRANDS retail chain. If a card holder, after getting a VIP 20% card, doesn’t make purchases of 6,000 AZN in a year which is the lower limit for this card, the card balance will be deleted, and the card will be replaced by a 20% discount card. If a card holder makes purchases of 6,000 AZN in a year which is the lower limit for a VIP 20% card, his/her card will be maintained for the next year, and the card balance will be zeroed out.  It should be noted that MYBRANDS retail chain includes more than 38 stores of 20 global brands. Throughout the years, MYBRANDS stores have become the most favorite place for the buyers in Azerbaijan with the famous brands, campaigns and loyalty card system. For further information *0114
