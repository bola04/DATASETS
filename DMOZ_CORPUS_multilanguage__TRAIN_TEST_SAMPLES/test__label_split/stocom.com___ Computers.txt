         <h1 class="entry-title">
          Stoddard Communications
         </h1>
        </header>
        <!-- .entry-header -->
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p class="Body" style="text-align: left;">
          Stoddard Communications is a New Mexico based production company with a focus on developing documentary, narrative and educational programming. We support the film and video industry with services in pre-production, production, postproduction and training, with a specialty in production/postproduction workflows.
         </p>
         <p class="paragraph_style" style="text-align: left;">
          <span style="color: #33cccc;">
           <strong>
            film &amp; video workflow consulting
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p class="Body" style="text-align: left;">
          Workflow consulting encompasses a wide range of services for establishing a practical workflow, development through distribution. Services include workflow research and design for Digital imaging that best fits your project and budget and technical support in data handling, storage, camera systems, postproduction systems and integration.
         </p>
         <p class="Body" style="text-align: left;">
          Which digital format best communicates your ideas? Which technology is within your budget and best targets your audience? Consult with us to develop a production/postproduction workflow that is efficient and viable. We will help you meet your production, distribution and budgetary goals.
         </p>
         <p class="paragraph_style" style="text-align: left;">
          <span style="color: #33cccc;">
           <strong>
            production
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p class="Body" style="text-align: left;">
          Production services begin in the project development stage with research and script writing, and pickup in the preproduction, or production phase. Whatever level of involvement you choose to involve us, you will receive our full attention and quality service.
         </p>
         <p class="paragraph_style" style="text-align: left;">
          <span style="color: #33cccc;">
           <strong>
            post-production
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p class="Body" style="text-align: left;">
          <span class="style_1">
           We offer extensive postproduction services in video and audio editing, sound editing, color grading, motion graphics , finishing as well as postproduction systems consulting. Postproduction starts in the development and preproduction phase as a workflow consultation. Include a post consultant/manager in your development stage and you will save your budget from a possible disaster.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p class="paragraph_style_1" style="text-align: left;">
          <span style="color: #33cccc;">
           <strong>
            <span class="style_2">
             training
            </span>
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p class="Body" style="text-align: left;">
          Brad Stoddard is an Apple Certified Final Cut Pro X Trainer. FCPX classes are available at the University of New Mexico Continuing Education, Albuquerque, NM. One-on-one instruction is available in video editing, encoding, lighting for film, animation and motion graphics. Brad’s Cinema Techniques for Film, Animation and Motion Graphics, notes are  available here
          <a href="http://stocom.com/basic_lighting" rel="noopener" target="_blank" title="http://stocom.com/basic_lighting">
           Basic Lighting for Film and Video
          </a>
          .
         </p>
        </div>
