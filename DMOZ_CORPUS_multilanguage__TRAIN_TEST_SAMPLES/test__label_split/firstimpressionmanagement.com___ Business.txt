       <h1 class="cufon">
        Customized Corporate
        <br/>
        Learning Solutions
       </h1>
       <p>
        First Impression Management offers the benefits of
        <strong>
         customized quality training
        </strong>
        to your company at a convenient time and in the most effective format for you and your employees. We’ll
        <strong>
         create and design programs according to your needs and objectives.
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        For businesses, government agencies, academic and non-proﬁt organizations  First Impression Management provides the most comprehensive training available. Our training facilitators provide interactive learning experiences to empower your team with knowledge and skills in all aspects of business etiquette, professionalism and personal branding, pertinent to your particular industry and work environment. Our audiences range from new professionals in the workplace to seasoned executives.
       </p>
       <p>
        First Impression Management has designed hundreds of unique programs for clients across a wide range of industries. Our process for custom work is handled in four phases: Discovery, Design, Development and Implementation. We
        <strong>
         leverage adult learning principles and motivational theory
        </strong>
        to create programs with the right mix of cognitive and experiential learning activities for each situation.
       </p>
