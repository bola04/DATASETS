         <p>
          Welcome to the North Slope Borough, America’s farthest north municipal government! Our borough encompasses an area of nearly 95,000 square miles across northern Alaska and includes some of the most spectacular landscapes found in North America. On this website, you can access information about the services we provide along with important upcoming community events and the proceedings of the North Slope Borough Assembly. Visitors can also find information useful in planning a trip to see this beautiful part of the world that we call home. Quyanaqpaq!
         </p>

          Harry K. Brower Jr.

          Mayor
