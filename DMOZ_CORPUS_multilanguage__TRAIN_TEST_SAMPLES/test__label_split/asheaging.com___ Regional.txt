      <h2>
       Our intent is to make this website a comprehensive listing for all programs and services serving Senior Adults in Ashe County, North Carolina.
      </h2>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Ashe Services for Aging
       </strong>
       provides a wide range of services to Seniors and Disabled Adults of Ashe County, North Carolina including (among many other programs) Delivered Meals, Adult Day Health Care, Child Day Care, Assisted Living, Respite and In-Home Aide.
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       ASA also operates a Senior Center where older Adults can directly access a wide range of activities and services including health screenings, exercise classes, games, and trips.
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       Generations Day Care, adjacent to the Senior Center is one of three intergenerational day care programs in North Carolina. Elderly housing is also available and in walking distance of the Senior Center.
      </p>
