        <p>
         Villa Dianna Restaurant began 10 years ago when brother-in-laws Richie Hodza and Ron Iljazi sold their New Jersey pizzeria to bring their authentic Italian recipes to Burleson, Texas. Together with Ron's sister Michelle and wife Vee, our little family restaurant is so very grateful to our local community. On November 15, 2014 our lives changed forever with a devastating fire. Ten years of hard work was gone within hours. We can't thank the community of Burleson enough for all the love and support you have given us. We are humbled and thrilled to once again be open and bring delicious food to our customers.
