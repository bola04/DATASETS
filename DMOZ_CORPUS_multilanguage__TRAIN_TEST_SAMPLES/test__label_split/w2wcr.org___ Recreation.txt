            <h2>
             Welcome!
            </h2>
            <p style="padding-left: 30px; ">
             <strong>
              It shall be our purpose to further the exchange of information and cooperation between members, to promote radio knowledge, fraternalism and individual operating efficiency, and to so conduct club programs and activities as to advance the general interest and welfare of Amateur Radio in the community.
             </strong>
            </p>

            <h2 style="text-align: left; ">
             Please join us on July 30th 2018 at the Queensbury Middle School - 455 Aviation Rd, Queensbury, NY for our July Meeting. This month we will be hosting Mike Lisenco N2YBB the Hudson Division Director of the ARRL. This is an open invitation, you do not need to be a member of the Warren County Radio Club to attend. Meeting begins at 7pm. Talk in on the 146.73 repeater.

            <h2>
             Repeater Etiquette
            </h2>
            <p>
             <strong>
              When using the system, please leave gaps in between transmissions to allow the system to reset to avoid timing out. Also leaving gaps in between transmissions will eliminate stations constantly "doubling" or transmitting at the same time. Thank you for your cooperation.
             </strong>
            </p>
            <h2>
             Repeater Information
            </h2>
            <ul>
             <li>
              Prospect Mt. repeaters:
             </li>
             <ul>
              <li>
               - 146.730 PL 100
              </li>
              <li>
               + 443.450  (serves as link repeater)
              </li>
              <li>
               - 224.780 NO PL (will only be linked during nets and if needed in emergencies)
              </li>
             </ul>
             <li>
              Gore Mt. repeater:
             </li>
             <ul>
              <li>
               + 147.135 PL 123
              </li>
             </ul>
             <li>
              Burch Hill repeater is now listening on -146.775 PL 146.2  The hope for this repeater is to provide coverage in Whitehall and Ticonderoga, however it also provides an alternative to Prospect at I87 Exit 15.
             </li>
             <li>
              <a class="external-link" href="http://www.broadcastify.com/listen/feed/11730" target="_blank" title="W2WCR Internet Scanner Feed">
