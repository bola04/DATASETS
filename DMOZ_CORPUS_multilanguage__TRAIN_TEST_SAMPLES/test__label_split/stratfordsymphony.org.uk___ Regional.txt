      <p>
       Welcome to the Stratford-upon-Avon Symphony Orchestra website.
      </p>
      <p>
       The Stratford-upon-Avon Symphony Orchestra was founded over fifty years ago.  Its size and standards have made steady progress; there are currently nearly sixty playing members.
      </p>
      <p>
       Programmes consist of a wide variety of well-known (and some less well-known), music ranging from the 17th to the 20th century.
      </p>
      <p>
       We rehearse weekly on Friday evenings in Shottery Village Memorial Hall and give 3 concerts a year in Stratford-upon-Avon.
      </p>
      <p>
       For further information on our future concert programmes and membership of the orchestra please visit our Programme and Membership pages, or why not sign up to our
