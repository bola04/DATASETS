     <h1>
      Welcome!
     </h1>
     <p>
      For over two decades, HealthActCHQ (HACHQ) has developed and licensed industry-leading Patient Reported Outcomes (PRO) surveys to assess health-related quality of life and its impact on everyday functioning and well-being. Our Company's focus has been to give voice to the patients' and families' perspectives about their physical, emotional, and social well-being in clinical practice and research settings.
     </p>
     <p>
      HACHQ's Patient Reported Outcomes measures are translated and licensed worldwide for use in academic research, pharmaceutical clinical trials, patient registries, school systems, clinical care networks, and population-based quality of life outcomes research. More than 575 peer-reviewed manuscripts documenting the development, validation, uses, and effectiveness of the Company's surveys are presented at our website
      <a href="/bibliographies.php">
       Bibliography
      </a>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
      Our outcomes surveys include the
      <a href="/chq.php">
       Child Health Questionnaire (CHQ)
      </a>
      and
      <a href="/itqol.php">
       Infant Toddler Quality of Life Questionnaire (ITQOL)
      </a>
      - pediatric overall quality of life measures. HACHQ has developed condition-specific outcomes surveys for use in clinical trials, physician benchmarking programs, and patient-specific reporting - such surveys are our ADHD Impact Modules for adults and children - the
      <a href="/aim-a-adhd.php">
       AIM-A
      </a>
      and the
      <a href="/aim-c-adhd.php">
       AIM-C
      </a>
      , our
      <a href="/pro-sf1.php">
       Patient Respiratory Outcomes Survey (PRO-SF1)
      </a>
      and
      <a href="/fipre.php">
       Neonatal Parental Readiness Evaluation (FIPRE)
      </a>
      . HealthActCHQ has conducted the translation of more than 377 versions of the Company's PRO Surveys in over 98 languages.
     </p>
