            Hogging Up West Virginia Has Been Cancelled for a Variety of Reasons
           </a>
          </h3>
          <p class="date">
           <span class="upper">
            <span class="posted-date">
             Posted on
             <a href="https://hoggingupwv.wordpress.com/2014/07/15/hogging-up-west-virginia-has-been-cancelled-for-a-variety-of-reasons/" rel="bookmark" title="3:42 pm">

               July 15, 2014
              </time>
             </a>
            </span>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="https://hoggingupwv.files.wordpress.com/2014/07/cancelledwvflyer.jpg">

           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           We would like to thank the Journal, Cox Camping, and Governor for their support, but with heavy heart we must say that
           <a href="http://www.hoggingupwv.com">
            Hogging Up WV
           </a>
           has been cancelled.  It was our goal to keep a KCBS BBQ competition in West Virginia for 2014 just for our competitors, but also to give the public a glance at our “HOGGING UP BBQ TOUR” where they could learn tips and tricks from the pros  Like so many other barbecue competitions we have lack of monetary sponsors, and lack of volunteers. You can’t put on a good event without those two factors.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="https://hoggingupwv.files.wordpress.com/2014/07/fall-leaf.jpg">

           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Cox Camping will still have their Fall Leaf Festival on Saturday, October 25th.  We hope you will go out and support them. Take your BBQ have a picnic and see Vic grill up a pig!
          </p>
