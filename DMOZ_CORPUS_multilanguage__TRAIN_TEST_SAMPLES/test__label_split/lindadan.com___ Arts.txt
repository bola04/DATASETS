              <td valign="top" width="445">
               <p>
                <br/>
                Linda Thomas and Dan DeLancey are award-winning players who have performed together for over  twenty years, gaining the attention of fans and reviewers worldwide. The duo has recorded twelve albums together and facilitates workshops for beginner to advanced players. Linda has published several
                <a href="store/instructional.shtml">
                 instructional books
                </a>
                with arrangements for hammered dulcimer.
               </p>
               <p>
                Don't forget!  Linda and Dan are available for concerts, festivals, private parties and instructional workshops for hammered dulcimer and flatpicked guitar, too!
               </p>
               <p>
                In addition to their well-known duet appearances, the Thomas/DeLancey performance is available as a trio with Rick Desko (bass). THE FROSTY MORNING TRIO offers a variety of styles including Old-Time, Swing, Folk Titles and more.
               </p>
               <p>
                <img alt="Reviews" border="0" height="39" src="images/reviews.gif" width="117"/>
               </p>
               <p>
                "An absolute delight! Linda's hammered dulcimer and Dan's guitar blend together seamlessly on a great selection of contemporary and traditional tunes.  Linda is a tasteful dulcimer player and Dan is an excellent guitar player.  The combined sound is so very smooth that one tends to forget just how difficult some of these tunes are to play on any instrument."
                <br/>
               </p>
               <p align="right">
                <b>

                </b>

                <br/>
               </p>
               <p align="right">
                <b>

                </b>
               </p>

                <br/>
               </p>

Like Mr. Crary, Mr. DeLancey has that cleanly elegant, almost classical approach, full of warm, ringing notes and complex patterns that give his solos a solid, structured feel regardless of the tempo."
