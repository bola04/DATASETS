        <h1>
         Chicago Vegetarian Restaurant
        </h1>
        <h2>
         Serving Food as Medicine for 30+ Years
        </h2>
        <p>
         At Original Soul Vegetarian, we seek to offer a one-of-a-kind culinary
 	 experience for vegans and health aficionados throughout the Chicago area.
 	 For the past 30 years, we have offered food that is both healthy and delicious—winning
 	 the praise and consistent patronage of vegetarians and non-vegetarians alike.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          In fact, check out what critics have to say about our food:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <em>
           "One restaurant even non-vegetarians can appreciate"
          </em>
          – Chicago Sun Times
         </li>
         <li>
          <em>
           "Essential 'soulfulness' that helps define the African American
 			 experience…"
          </em>
          – Vegetarian Times
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         If you are looking for a place where you can go big on flavor without compromising
 	 on health, it is time that you visited Original Soul Vegetarian. In everything
 	 that we do, we promote the regeneration of our environment; we create
 	 food that is 100% plant based and completely free of animals and chemicals.
 	 Our food also uses no refined sugars, flours, or rice—allowing you
 	 to indulge in taste without the guilt.
        </p>
        <h3>
         Original Soul Vegetarian: We Know Your Health Is Your Wealth
        </h3>
        <p>
         From appetizers to sandwiches to platters to salads, we have everything
 	 you need to satisfy your appetite at Original Soul Vegetarian while walking
 	 away feeling light.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          More reasons that you should check us out:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          33 Years in Business
         </li>
         <li>
          We Prepare All the Food in Our Facility
         </li>
         <li>
          We Care About Our Customers' Health
         </li>
         <li>
          We Are Staying Green
         </li>
         <li>
          Family Owned &amp; Operated
         </li>
         <li>
          100% Plant Based, Animal &amp; Chemical Free
         </li>
         <li>
          No Refined Sugars, Flours, or Rice
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         If you are looking for a new restaurant to try in the Chicago area, then
 	 we encourage you to come visit us today! We have won the PETA Award for
 	 Catering, a Zagat Award for Food &amp; Service, and were even named as
 	 the City's Best Healthy Dining Restaurant by the AOL City Guide, Chicago.
 	 To learn more about our food, our services, or our mission, do not hesitate
 	 to visit our location or give us a call at 773-224-0104.
