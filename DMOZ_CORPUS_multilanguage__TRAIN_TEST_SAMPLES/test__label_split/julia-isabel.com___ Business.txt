         <p>
          Welcome to Joy in Daily Living a subsidiary of Maximize Your Publicity. Founded in December, 2015 to bring joy in daily living through inspiration and creative retreats.
         </p>
         <p>
          Clients receive the benefit of working with a Certified Meeting Professional and a Project Management Professional who understands the importance of schedule and budget.
         </p>
         <p>
          Since 2002 Maximize Your Publicity has served as a boutique consulting firm offering public relations, social media, event management and project management services.
         </p>
         <p>
          Here are some of the services provided by Joy in Daily Living:
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           VIP event planner creating strategic retreats or joyful events
          </li>
          <li>
           Retreat facilitator working with leaders to work
           <span style="color: #ff00ff;">
            <strong>
             on
            </strong>
           </span>
           their business not just
           <span style="color: #ff00ff;">
            <strong>
             in
            </strong>
            <span style="color: #000000;">
             the business
            </span>
           </span>
          </li>
          <li>
           Landscape photographer sharing inspirational messages via social media
          </li>
          <li>
           Travel writer for the over 50 crowd
          </li>
          <li>
           Speaker challenging audiences to know all the promises God has for them
          </li>
         </ul>
