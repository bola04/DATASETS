       <h1>
        Welcome to QMA, the only adult only, no party property in Queenstown.
       </h1>
       <p>
        We are conveniently located at 62 Frankton Road, 700 metres from the town centre on the main road into Queenstown. Only a one minute walk to both the Millennium Hotel and the Copthorne Lakeview, we are a popular alternative for conference attendees.
       </p>
       <p>
        We designed and built our 18 studio rooms especially to suit people (and their pockets) travelling alone, with a companion, or 2 or more couples travelling together who prefer their own bathroom, kitchenette and television remote control to use whenever and however they want to!
       </p>
       <p>
        We opened them in December 2004 as Queenstown Motel Apartments, (we were trading as Alpha Lodge prior to our new build)
       </p>
       <p>
        All our accommodation includes unlimited free WiFi, kitchenettes containing a microwave, toaster, jug, fridge, crockery and cutlery and in the 18 new rooms we also have something different... We designed our kitchen joinery to include recycling bins!
       </p>
       <h2>
        All 18 studio units feature:
       </h2>
       <ul>
        <li>
         Kitchenette with microwave, toaster, fridge, jug, coffee plunger,
         <br/>
         crockery, cutlery and recycling bins
        </li>
        <li>
         Sky TV with 50 plus channels including sport with great reception on all channels.
        </li>
        <li>
         Free Unlimited Wireless internet
        </li>
        Free off street parking
        <li>
         Great Beds
         <br/>
         <a href="/room-information">
          more &gt;&gt;
         </a>
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        'Reduce, re-use, re-cycle' has been one of our philosophies for many years and we have applied it through out the development. We are pleased with our end results and so are our guests. We try to be as environmentally friendly as possible.
       </p>
       <p>
        We are often complimented on the workmanship of our builders, the cleanliness of the rooms, how comfortable the beds are, the quality of the fittings and chattels, the clever room layout, how helpful our staff are and how great our unlimited free wifi is.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Please note:  ADULTS ONLY PROPERTY.  Not suitable for children under 18 including babies and toddlers
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Sadly at an assessment it was brought to our attention that neither our property nor our rooms were suitable for children.
       </p>
       <p>
        We agree with Qualmark, we have no suitable safe play area and a very busy driveway so for the safety of children and comfort of our guests we now have a strict adults only over 18 years of age "No children including babies and toddlers policy."
       </p>
       <p>
        We ask that you please respect our no children rule [ the decision was not made lightly]  because should you arrive with children we will have no alternative but to cancel the reservation with no refund.
       </p>
       <p>
        Please also note [added 28 April 2017]
       </p>
       <p>
        Our kitchenettes are suitable for reheating pre cooked food [via the microwave], toast and tea or coffee making only.
       </p>
       <p>
        For health and safety reasons we do not have any cooking facilities and do not allow the use of any cooking devices not provided by the Motel either indoors or outside anywhere on the property.
       </p>
       <p>
        We are only 700 metres from Queenstown. There are lots of great eating places to enjoy the scenery and ambience of this fantastic town and we are happy to recommend restaurants that will suit your dietary requirements as well as your budget.
       </p>
       <p>
        We thank you for your co operation and understanding.
       </p>
