          <h2 class="post-title entry-title">
           <a href="http://classicaltuesdays.blogspot.com/2018/07/clan-gordon-pipers-in-old-town-park.html">
            Clan Gordon Pipers in Old Town Park September 8, 4pm
           </a>
          </h2>
          <div class="post-header-line-1">
          </div>
          <div class="post-body entry-content">
           <p>
            <a href="https://scontent-sea1-1.xx.fbcdn.net/v/t1.0-9/35543414_2117091738507061_406279991823695872_n.jpg?_nc_cat=0&amp;oh=c35ea9529969c133226d85bcecc3c416&amp;oe=5BA230EE">
             Clan Gordon Pipe Band
            </a>
            will regale us with drums and Scottish pipes on Saturday September 8. We'll gather in Old Town Park at the end of
            <a href="https://www.facebook.com/pg/Job.Carr.Cabin.Museum/events/">
             Pioneer Day at Job Carr Cabin Museum
            </a>
            . The pipers start up at 4pm.
            <br/>
           </p>
           <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
            <a href="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-d3cwFCOfkuA/W0ZXLj_Jz7I/AAAAAAAABq4/Cdtekr9NhuUbFY4JeyMrRQDsiRzH6VZvwCLcBGAs/s1600/Clan%2BGordon%2BPipers.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
             <img border="0" data-original-height="720" data-original-width="960" height="240" src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-d3cwFCOfkuA/W0ZXLj_Jz7I/AAAAAAAABq4/Cdtekr9NhuUbFY4JeyMrRQDsiRzH6VZvwCLcBGAs/s320/Clan%2BGordon%2BPipers.jpg" width="320"/>
            </a>
           </div>
           <span style="font-size: small;">
            The Clan Gordon Pipe Band was formed In 1955 when a group
  of Scottish immigrants and Americans pooled their experience, and, with
  the sponsorship of Clan Gordon, formed the Clan Gordon Pipe Band in 
 Tacoma, Washington.
           </span>
           <span style="color: #222222;">
            ​
           </span>
           <span style="font-size: small;">
            The
  first Pipe Major was Johnnie Montgomery, father of longtime member, 
 Jack Montgomery. Even though Clan Gordon is no longer their sponsor, they
 retain the original name.
           </span>
           <br/>
           <span style="font-size: small;">
            The Clan Gordon Pipers play the music of the highland bagpipe throughout the Pacific Northwest.
           </span>
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Bring a blanket or beach chair and picnic makings, or purchase         food and beverage from
           <a href="http://www.oldtacomabusiness.com/DirectoryCat.php?busAddInfo=2">
            local           eateries
           </a>
           .
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <b>
            When &amp; where:
           </b>
           <br/>
           <b>
            Saturday, September 8, 2018 4:00pm
           </b>
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Old Town Park
           <br/>
           <a href="http://www.metroparkstacoma.org/old-town-park">
            2350 N.           30th St., Old Town Tacoma
           </a>
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Free/donations appreciated
           <br/>
           All ages welcome
           <br/>
           <br/>
           More information:
           <br/>
           (253) 752-2135
           <br/>
           <br/>

           <span class="_4n-j _3cht fsl" data-testid="event-permalink-details">
            <i>
             Classical Tuesdays in Old Town
            </i>
            looks forward to featuring the Camas Woodwind Quintet and saxophonist Erik Steighner on the closing concert of this, our 13th season, Tuesday February 13 in Slavonian Hall in Old Town Tacoma. All the performers teach on the music faculty at Pacific Lutheran University; clarinetist Craig Rine and saxophonist Erik Steighner have performed for
            <i>
             Classical Tuesdays
            </i>
            on previous concerts.
           </span>
           <br/>
           <span class="_4n-j _3cht fsl" data-testid="event-permalink-details">
           </span>
           <br/>
           <span class="_4n-j _3cht fsl" data-testid="event-permalink-details">
            The program includes Barber's
            <i>
             Summer Music
            </i>
            , Milhaud's
            <i>
             Chimney of King Rene
            </i>
            (charming, 
 Renaissance-inspired work), an arrangement of the Bartok's
            <i>
             Roumanian 
 Dances
            </i>
            , the
            <i>
             Sextet
            </i>
            by Dubois (with Erik Steighner on sax), and a reed 
 trio by Auric.

           </a>
          </h2>
          <div class="post-header-line-1">
          </div>
          <div class="post-body entry-content">
           <p>
            <br/>
            <br/>
           </p>
           <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
            <a href="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-zijk11ATCb8/WjV4iFEyK3I/AAAAAAAABjw/dlGZAD0PHKkjQIqLmZVi-0_JcCRXUr7twCLcBGAs/s1600/Catherine%2BCase.png" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">

            </a>
            <a href="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-igWdu9PCCMg/WjV4k3-qtYI/AAAAAAAABj0/pFNjj1KX9KgGDqKO5o1qvMwwYQx1NSMVwCLcBGAs/s1600/Maria05.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">

            </a>
           </div>
           <span style="font-size: large;">
            <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
             <b>
              <i>
               Classical Tuesdays in Old Town
              </i>
             </b>
             welcomes back violinists
             <a href="https://www.mariasampen.com/bio">
              Maria Sampen
             </a>
             and
             <a href="https://www.pugetsound.edu/faculty-pages/tchristie/">
              Tim Christie
             </a>
             for the January 9, 2018 performance in Slavonian Hall in Old Town Tacoma.
            </span>
           </span>
           <br/>
           <span style="font-size: large;">
            <br/>
           </span>
           <span style="font-size: large;">
            <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
             Following a recent visit to China, Maria and Tim, joined by harpist
             <a href="https://www.plu.edu/music/staff/catherine-case/">
              Catherine Case
             </a>
             , perform music of
             <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chen_Yi_(composer)">
              Chen-Yi
             </a>
             and
             <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bright_Sheng">
              Bright Sheng
             </a>
             , as well as Saint-Saens and other composers.
            </span>
           </span>

           <br/>

              Where &amp; When:
             </b>
            </span>
           </span>
           <br/>
           <span style='color: black; font-family: "calibri" , "sans-serif"; font-size: 16.0pt;'>
            <a href="http://www.slavhall.org/">
             <span style="font-size: small;">
              <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
               Slavonian Hall, 2306 N 30th St., Old Town Tacoma
              </span>
             </span>
            </a>
           </span>
           <br/>
           <span style='color: black; font-family: "calibri" , "sans-serif"; font-size: 16.0pt;'>
            <span style="font-size: small;">
             <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
              Tuesday January 9, 7:00pm
             </span>
            </span>
           </span>
           <br/>
           <span style='color: black; font-family: "calibri" , "sans-serif"; font-size: 16.0pt;'>
            <span style="font-size: small;">
             <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
              Free/donations appreciated
             </span>
            </span>
           </span>
           <br/>
           <span style='color: black; font-family: "calibri" , "sans-serif"; font-size: 16.0pt;'>

 18th Century rivalry between violinists Francesco Maria Veracini and Johann Georg 
 Pisendel at the Dresden Court Orchestra. The program will feature music 
 by Veracini and Pisendel as well as Bach and Telemann.

            </a>

            </i>

            <br/>
