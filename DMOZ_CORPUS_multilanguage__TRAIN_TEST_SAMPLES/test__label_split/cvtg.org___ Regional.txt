            <h1>
             Fun Home Auditions
            </h1>
            <p>
             <span>
              7pm August 13-14
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span>
              We are excited to present the area debut of
              <em>
               Fun Home
              </em>
              and the first community theatre production to perform at the JAMF Theatre in the new Pablo Confluence Center for the Arts. Please have a prepared Broadway or pop solo. There will be readings from the script as part of the audition. Choreography will not be done as part of the audition. Performances will be October 4-7, 2018. We have assembled a top-notch production team with considerable musical theatre experience as well as working with each other
              <br/>
              <br/>
              Keith Lorasch: Director and Musical Director
              <br/>
              Angie Alexander: Assistant Director
              <br/>
              Matt Kruszka: Pit Conductor
              <br/>
              Meg Gezella: Choreographer
              <br/>
              <br/>
              Rehearsals begin Monday, August 20. These rehearsals run Monday-Thursday from 6:30 PM- 9:00 PM until the Sunday prior to opening night.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              Synopsis:
              <br/>
              <br/>
              When her father dies unexpectedly, graphic novelist Alison dives deep into her past to tell the story of the volatile, brilliant, one-of-a-kind man whose temperament and secrets defined her family and her life. Moving between past and present, Alison relives her unique childhood playing at the family’s Bechdel Funeral Home, her growing understanding of her own sexuality, and the looming, unanswerable questions about her father’s hidden desires. Fun Home is a refreshingly honest, wholly original musical about seeing your parents through grown-up eyes.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              Casting:
              <br/>
              <br/>
              2m, 4f, 2 boys, 1girl
              <br/>
              ALISON – 43 years old, a cartoonist
              <br/>
              MEDIUM ALISON – 19 years old, a college freshman
              <br/>
              SMALL ALISON – around 11 years old
              <br/>
              BRUCE BECHDEL – Alison’s father, about 44
              <br/>
              HELEN BECHDEL – Alison’s mother, slightly older than Bruce
              <br/>
              CHRISTIAN BECHDEL – Alison’s brother, around 10 years old
              <br/>
              JOHN BECHDEL – Alison’s brother, around 6 years old or slightly older
              <br/>
              ROY – a young man Bruce hires to do yard work, PETE – a mourner, MARK – a high school junior; all played by one actor, around 18-20.
              <br/>
              JOAN – a college student, slightly older than medium Alison.
             </span>
