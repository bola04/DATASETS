          Neurology Group
         </h2>
         <p>
          At Hanczaryk Chiropractic Neurology Group we have both doctors of chiropractic and chiropractic neurologists. They both offer the same non-drug and non-invasive treatment that targets the underlying problem, not just the symptoms. Chiropractic neurologists also focus on postural muscles, nutrition, and life style changes that affect
  brain function. They also treat a wide range of neurological conditions
  just like a medical neurologist, with the important difference being 
 that the treatment and therapy do not include drugs or surgery. Typical 
 dysfunctions treated include movement disorders, vertigo, carpal tunnel 
 syndrome, and radiculopathies.
