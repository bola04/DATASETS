             <h1>
              Canaan Village Inn lodging in Canaan Valley
             </h1>
             <p>
              Canaan Village Motel has the best room rates in the Canaan Valley and Davis Area. Call us for rates for your reservation.
             </p>
             <p>
              Canaan Village Inn accommodates many groups. Boys and girls clubs, religious organizations, skiers, hunters, and fishermen, mountain bikers. Whether you want a relaxing get-away, or a week filled with activities,
             </p>
             <p>
              Canaan Village Inn has your accommodation. Located in the heart of Canaan Valley in Davis WV Canaan Village Inn sits on over 10 acres and offers an 18-unit motel, a 4-unit apartment building, a chalet cabin, an 18-hole miniature golf, and restaurants, shops and many activities.
             </p>

               Come stay at Canaan Village Inn

              </h3>
             </div>

Looking for vacation rental,   lodging in Davis or Canaan Valley  WV or a family friendly hotel or just   a vacation rental, Canaan Village Inn can accommodate your travel plans
              </p>

             </h4>

Canaan Village Inn sits on 10 acres of beautiful rolling land with views of the mountains and a nearby pond.  Canaan Village is located in the center of the Valley and was originally developed with a town center concept.  Come stay at Canaan Village Inn and enjoy the beauty and wonders of   Canaan Valley and nature's   splendor.
