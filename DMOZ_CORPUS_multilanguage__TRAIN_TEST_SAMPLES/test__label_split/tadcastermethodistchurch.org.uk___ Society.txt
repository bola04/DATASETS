           Home
          </h1>
         </header>
         <!-- .entry-header -->
         <div class="entry-content">
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <strong>
            Welcome
           </strong>
           to Tadcaster Methodist Church, celebrating over 250 years of methodism in Tadcaster, North Yorkshire, UK. Please feel free to look around our website and when you find our doors open pop in and join in with our community. The most obvious way to do this is at our weekly Sunday morning service which starts at 10.15am, which lasts for approximately an hour, with refreshments served afterwards. We also have a weekly ‘Coffee stop’ on Tuesday mornings between 10am and 11:30am, all are welcome.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           We have a variety of other things going in or around our community, all as part of our Christian faith: our
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           love of God, love for each other and love for our community; so please pop in and say hello.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.tadcastermethodistchurch.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/mugshot-mark.jpg">
            <img alt="A photo of Rev mark" class="alignnone wp-image-1419 " height="150" sizes="(max-width: 105px) 100vw, 105px" src="http://www.tadcastermethodistchurch.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/mugshot-mark-210x300.jpg" srcset="http://www.tadcastermethodistchurch.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/mugshot-mark-210x300.jpg 210w, http://www.tadcastermethodistchurch.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/mugshot-mark.jpg 360w" width="105"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Rev. Mark
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            You are invited to Our “Sunday Specials”
           </strong>
           <br/>
           I would also like to draw your attention to our latest ‘Sunday Specials’ which take place on the Last Sunday in the month starting at 10:15am with specific different styles from our normal church services, whether a church regular or it’s your first time, you are welcome to come along.
          </p>
         </div>
