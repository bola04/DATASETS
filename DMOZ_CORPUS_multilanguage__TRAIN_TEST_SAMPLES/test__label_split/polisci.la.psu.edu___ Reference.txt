          <h1 class="documentFirstHeading" id="parent-fieldname-title">
           Welcome to Political Science at Penn State
          </h1>
          <div class="row" id="portlets-below">
          </div>
          <div id="content-core">
           <div class="" id="parent-fieldname-text-b98aae581abb4f72906f6f663efd0011">
            <p>
             Whether studying election campaigns, government processes or court decisions, political science is a constantly changing, always exciting discipline. The Penn State Department of Political Science is a key player in those discussions because of its commitment to teaching, research, and service.
            </p>
            <p>
             Our faculty members are
             <a class="internal-link" href="http://www.polisci.la.psu.edu/research" target="_self" title="">
              leading scholars
             </a>
             in American politics, international politics, comparative politics, and methodology who frequently collaborate with peers from other disciplines throughout the University.
             <span>
              We strive to provide an array of experiences for our
             </span>
             <a class="internal-link" href="http://www.polisci.la.psu.edu/undergraduate" target="_self" title="">
              undergraduate
             </a>
             <span>
              and
             </span>
             <a class="internal-link" href="http://www.polisci.la.psu.edu/graduate" target="_self" title="">
              graduate
             </a>
             <span>
              students — and help them find jobs once they graduate.
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span>
              <span>
               The Department of Political Science, as part of the
              </span>
              <a class="external-link" href="http://la.psu.edu" target="_self" title="">
               College of the Liberal Arts
              </a>
              <span>
               is committed to maintaining a fearless and inclusive environment that supports an open and respectful exchange of ideas.
              </span>
             </span>
            </p>
            <p class="callout">
             <span>
              <strong>
               <a class="internal-link" href="http://www.polisci.la.psu.edu/graduate/graduate-students-on-the-job-market" target="_self" title="">
                Ph.D. candidates on the job market
               </a>
              </strong>
              <br/>
             </span>
            </p>
           </div>
