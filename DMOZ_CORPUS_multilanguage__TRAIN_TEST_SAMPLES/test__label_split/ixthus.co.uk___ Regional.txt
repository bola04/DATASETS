     <h1>
      Sensors and Transducers Manufacturer, Distributor and Supply Chain Partners
     </h1>
     <p style="font-family: Gotham, 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: 22px; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);">
      Ixthus Instrumentation supply sensors, transducers and complete systems to measure displacement, force, torque and vibration. Working with the market leading manufacturers from around the world, we can offer the latest technology solutions best suited to your application. Whether it be a single sensor or a complete measurement system, Ixthus have the answer.
     </p>
     <p style="font-family: Gotham, 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: 22px; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);">
      Non-contact measurement of displacement, length and position using one of the following technologies – inductive eddy current, capacitive, ultrasonic, fibre optic and laser techniques. With measurement ranges from 50µm to 500m, resolution to 0.01 nanometre, frequency response to 200 kHz and well proven in harsh environments.
     </p>
     <p style="font-family: Gotham, 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: 22px; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);">
      Force measurement with miniature and fatigue rated loadcells from 1N to 10MN, strain gauged bolts and clevis pins. Custom designed force transducers to meet your exacting requirements, including flight and automotive approvals.
     </p>
