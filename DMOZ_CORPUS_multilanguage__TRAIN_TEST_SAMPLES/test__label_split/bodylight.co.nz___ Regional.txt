          <p>
           <span style="color: #0099cc;">
            <em>
             <span style="font-family: book antiqua,palatino;">
              <span style="font-size: 14pt;">

             <sup>
              <span style="color: #0099cc;">

              </span>
             </sup>
            </span>
           </span>
           is a boutique Pilates studio situated in Mission Bay, Auckland. The studio has been in the Orakei-Mission Bay area for over 14 years and have a strong local clientele and feel.
          </p>
          <p>
           The studio is equipped with all the Pilates Equipment including: Pilates Reformers, Pilates Trapeze tables, Wunda-chairs, Ladder barrel and other props.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="https://bodylight.co.nz/about-edna-levy/" title="About Edna">
            Edna Levy
           </a>
           has been teaching pilates, yoga and dance in Auckland since 1998 and has developed the
           <span style="color: #149cba;">
            <em>
             Bodylight
            </em>
            <sup>
             ®
            </sup>
           </span>
           method, which incorporates Pilates Yoga and Qi-Gong, to enhance mind-body awareness and help create a centered, mindful body.
          </p>
          <p>
           The studio is currently open from 6:30am – 7:30pm during the week and 8:00am – 12:00 on Saturdays.
          </p>
