        <h2>
         <span>
          Coventry Amateur Radio Society
         </span>
        </h2>
        <h1 style="text-align: center;">
         The oldest affiliated club to the RSGB
        </h1>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         We provide training and exam facilities for all 3 levels.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         The Society has a full program of events and meets most Fridays at 8.00pm.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Visitors are welcome.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         (Check
         <a href="http://www.coventryradio.org.uk/programme.html">
          our calendar
         </a>
         for our current program)
        </p>
        <h3 style="text-align: center;">
        </h3>
        <h4 style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="about_us/how-to-find-us.html">
          <span style="font-weight: normal; text-decoration-line: underline; font-family: georgia, serif; font-size: 29.12px;">
           How to find us
          </span>
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         <span style="font-weight: normal; text-decoration-line: underline; font-family: georgia, serif; font-size: 29.12px;">
          <br/>
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3 style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="font-weight: normal; text-decoration-line: underline; font-family: georgia, serif; font-size: 29.12px; colour: #FF0000;">
          New membership Information
         </span>
        </h3>
        <h5 style="text-align: center;">
        </h5>
        <h3 style="text-align: center;">
         Please note that due to changes in Data Protection rules this year membership application has been updated.
        </h3>
        <h5 style="text-align: center;">
         <strong style="font-size: 11.2px;">
          Please read
          <a href="uploads/club_documents/CARS%20Privacy%20policyv2.docx">
           CARS PRIVACY POLICY
          </a>
          and the new
          <a href="uploads/club_documents/Membershio%20&amp;%20Consent%20Form%20May18.docx" target="_blank">
           MEMBERSHIP FORM
          </a>
         </strong>
        </h5>
        <h5>
         <span style="font-weight: normal; text-decoration-line: underline; font-family: georgia, serif; font-size: 29.12px;">
          <br/>
         </span>
        </h5>
        <div style="border: 3px solid black; padding: 4px;">
         <h3 style="text-align: center;">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           Where to find us on the air
          </span>
         </h3>
         <h3 style="text-align: center;">
          145.375Mhz. (2m FM Channel S15/ V30)
         </h3>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Used for outdoor events &amp; “club nights”.
          <br/>
          An Open-Net is held every Monday evening 20:00-20:45
          <br/>
          An Open-Net is also held on Friday evenings when no meeting is scheduled.
         </p>
         <h3 style="text-align: center;">
          7.175Mhz. (40m LSB)
         </h3>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Monday evenings when conditions prevail (+/-QRM)
         </p>
         <h3 style="text-align: center;">
          <strong>
           50.175Mhz.  (6m USB)
          </strong>
         </h3>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Thursday evenings  20:00-21:00
         </p>
        </div>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          If you're interested in finding out more,
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          contact our Secretary: John Beech
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Email:
         <a href="mailto:secretary@coventryradio.org.uk">
          secretary@coventryradio.org.uk
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Phone: 07958 777363
        </p>

        </h2>
        <div style="margin: .5em auto; text-align: center; width: 580px;">
