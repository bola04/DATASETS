            <h1>
             Health, Safety, Construction and Skills Training Courses UK
            </h1>
            <p>
             UTN Training, part of the AFI group of companies, are a specialist provider of Health, Safety and Skills training to industry, construction and maintenance with a long established history in Powered Access. We provide accredited
             <a href="/ipaftraining">
              IPAF training
             </a>
             and
             <a href="/pasmatraining">
              PASMA
             </a>
             Working at Height training from our 21 dedicated training centres throughout the UK or in some instances on suitable customer premises.
            </p>
            <h2 style="font-size:14px;color:#666666;font-weight:normal;line-height:22px;">
             Our experienced and fully qualified trainers deliver a wide range of courses from approved bodies such as
             <a href="/citbsitesafetyplus">
              CITB
             </a>
             ,
             <a href="/ipaftraining">
              IPAF
             </a>
             ,
             <a href="/pasmatraining">
              PASMA
             </a>
             ,
             <a href="/ioshtraining">
              IOSH
             </a>
             &amp;
             <a href="/neboshtraining">
              NEBOSH
             </a>
             . Some of the courses delivered include
             <a href="/courses/SSSTS_Site_Supervisor_Safety_Training_Scheme_CITB">
              SSSTS
             </a>
             ,
             <a href="/courses/SMSTS_Site_Management_Safety_Training_Scheme_CITB">
              SMSTS
             </a>
             ,
             <a href="/citbsitesafetyplus">
              CITB Site Safety Plus
             </a>
             ,
             <a href="/nrswatraining">
              New Road and Street Works
             </a>
             Cat &amp; Genny, NEBOSH General and
             <a href="/courses/ioshcourse">
              IOSH Managing Safely
             </a>
             .
            </h2>
            <p>
             <a href="/ipaftraining">
              IPAF courses
             </a>
             cover the main training subjects of 1a, 1b, 3a and 3b for the safe use of Mobile Elevating Work Platforms (MEWPs) whilst
             <a href="/pasmatraining">
              PASMA courses
             </a>
             cover the safe use of alloy towers and include PASMA Towers on Stairs.
            </p>
