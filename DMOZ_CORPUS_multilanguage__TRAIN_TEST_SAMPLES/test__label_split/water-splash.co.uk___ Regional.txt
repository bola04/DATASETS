            </li>
            <li>
             Most of our plumbed in water dispensers have an automatic cut off if any leaks are detected.
            </li>
            <li>
             Plumbed in coolers are not top heavy unlike bottled water versions. This makes a plumbed in water dispenser more stable if knocked by mistake.
