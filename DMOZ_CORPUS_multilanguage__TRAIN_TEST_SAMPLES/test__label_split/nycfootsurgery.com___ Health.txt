         <h2>
          <a href="newyorkpodiatrist/bunionsurgerynyc.htm">
           BUNION SURGERY
          </a>
         </h2>
         <p>
          <a href="newyorkpodiatrist/bunionsurgerynyc.htm">
           Bunion surgery
          </a>
          can correct painful  bunions to reduce patient discomfort and improve the appearance of your feet.  Bunions are bumps found on the side of your big toe or little toe which can get  worse over time if left untreated.
