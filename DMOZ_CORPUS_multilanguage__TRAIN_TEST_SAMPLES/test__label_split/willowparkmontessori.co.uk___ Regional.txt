             <h2>
              Mission Statement
             </h2>
             Willow Park Montessori Day Nursery is a setting which is guided by the philosophy of Dr Maria Montessori – aiming to inspire the development of individual children in a safe and supportive environment.
 
 Willow Park Montessori Day Nursery is committed to the development of the whole child by employing an integrated approach to learning and living.
 
 We implement a curriculum based on the Montessori philosophy of education and human development alongside the Revised Early Years Foundation Stage (2014).
