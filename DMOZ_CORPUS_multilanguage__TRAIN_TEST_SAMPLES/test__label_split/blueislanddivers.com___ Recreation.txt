        <div id="center-main-type" style="position: absolute; top: 658px; left: 150px; width: 362px; -adbe-c: c">
         Blue Island Divers is the premier center for St. Thomas Diving. We cater to all SCUBA divers and their families, from
         <a href="01_diving/CruiseShips.html">
          Cruise Ship SCUBA Divers
         </a>
         <span lang="en-gb">
          to
         </span>
         Mega-Yacht SCUBA Divers.  We are the leading dive center for
         <a href="03_training/padi/nitrox.html">
          Nitrox
         </a>
         on St. Thomas, both
         <a href="03_training/padi/nitrox.html">
          Nitrox Training
         </a>
         , and provision of Nitrox diving to those already certified.  Advanced open water, Nitrox, and rescue diver are also offered now as part of PADI's elearing program. We also offer a whole range of diving specialty courses, from wreck specialty to search and recovery
         <br/>
         <span lang="en-gb">
          Diving St. Thomas? then we are the diver's choice.
         </span>
         <p>
          You can also experience the underwater world for the first time, with our
          <a href="03_training/padi/padi.html">
           Discover Scuba
          </a>
          program. You will learn in a stress free environment at a beautiful tropical beach or on our spacious dive boat before taking the short ride to the
          <a href="02_sites/divesites.html">
           coral reef
          </a>
          for your first scuba dive under the close supervision of our professional instructors.
         </p>
         <p>
          We offer high quality, low volume SCUBA diving with the very best
          <a href="02_sites/divesites.html">
           Wreck Diving
          </a>
          and
          <a href="02_sites/divesites.html">
           Reef Diving
          </a>
          the Islands have to offer. Our western most location on the island make us the closest dive operator to all the most popular and requested wreck sites here on St Thomas. Wreck sites such as the
          <a href="02_sites/shoal.html">
           (LST 467) WIT Shoal II
          </a>
          , WIT Concrete, S.S. Grainton, WIT Service IV, WIT Power, Miss Opportunity, Kennedy barge, Mist, Navy Barges (5 total) to name but a few. Checkout our
          <a href="02_sites/divesites.html">
           Dive Sites
          </a>
          pages for more information. With over 35 years of local wreck diving experience, you'll see why we are the "St Thomas wreck diving specialists"
         </p>
         <p>
          Our
          <a href="02_sites/divesites.html">
           reefs
          </a>
          are outstanding and pristine. Reef sites such as Flat Cay, T-Table, Limestone, Sprat point, Castles, Dive Tank, Boat Hook, Fences and Gunsight provide a staggering array of fish and coral life, with southern stingrays, spotted eagle rays, hawksbill and green turtles a plenty. All the sites mentioned are within 30 minutes boat ride from the
          <a href="05_about/Location.html">
           dive store
          </a>
          . Day trips are available to Frenchman's Cap, Sail Rock and the relatively un-dived island of Savana.
         </p>
         <p>
          We welcome you to dive into our web pages for detailed information about our location, diver training, and you can even get to know our crew. We also have detailed information about many of our
          <a href="02_sites/divesites.html">
           Dive Sites
          </a>
          , with Actual Underwater Photographs. For our
          <a href="01_diving/CruiseShips.html">
           Cruise Ship Divers
          </a>
          we offer good maps of both cruise ship terminals here in St Thomas that show where we meet you from either location. You also have the opportunity to book your diving vacation with us using our
          <a href="https://ihscnet.commercebox.com/~blueisla/02_booking_ssl.htm" target="_blank">
           Booking Form
          </a>
          . All forms are hosted on a secure server for your security, privacy and piece of mind.
         </p>
         <p>
          Our
          <a href="05_about/Conservation.html">
           Conservation page
          </a>
          keeps you up to date with the conservation support and efforts of Blue Island Divers. We are active with PADI Project Aware, and the
          <a href="http://nolionfish.com/" target="_blank">
           CORE foundation
          </a>
          (Caribbean Oceanic Restoration and Education), Reef Conservation Program (RECON), the Ocean Conservancy and the
          <a href="http://www.cmc-ocean.org/" target="_blank">
           Center for Marine Conservation
          </a>
          (CMC).
         </p>
