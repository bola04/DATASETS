          </span>
         </h1>
         <h2 style="text-align: center;">
          <span style="color: #f8c563;">
           Welcome to our new, easier to use website! To view our catalog, click on the catalog links in the left panel.
           <br/>
          </span>
         </h2>
         <p>
          You no longer need to remember a password, and will no longer have to type in a long credit card number. Just call us at toll-free 800-628-0855, and we will do all the work for you!
          <span style="color: #ffcc00;">
           <strong>
            International customers
           </strong>
          </span>
          please e-mail us at
          <a href="mailto:gary@songlearning.com">
           gary@songlearning.com
          </a>
          with the composer’s name and name of the work you want, and your voice part (S1, S2, A1, A2, T1, T2, B1, B2), and we will send you the MP3 price, as well as simple instructions on how to place the order.
         </p>
         <p>
          <b>
           Also, be aware that we have more than 2500 octavos in our database, so please don’t hesitate to ask for what you need, if you don’t see it in our catalog!
          </b>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #fadeaa;">
           <em>
            FREE
           </em>
           <strong>
            LIFETIME REHEARSAL TRACKS for 375 CHORAL WORKS on one FLASH DRIVE!
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          This marks the 32nd year Hammond Music Service has been producing Songlearning Systems™ for choirs. During this extraordinary run, life-long director Gary Hammond has created and produced Rehearsal Tracks for more than 375 Significant Choral Works.
         </p>
         <p>
          To commemorate this milestone, we are offering our entire catalog on a 128GB flash drive. We call it the Legacy Library. This library includes a Lifetime License to make CD duplicates or distribute MP3s to every member of your choir. To own a Master License for every choral work in our HUGE library would normally cost your choir over $35,000!
         </p>
         <p>
          Originally priced at $1000, we are now offering it for FREE, as a BONUS for ordering Master sets of Songlearning MP3s or CDs. When the sum total of your orders over the next year or two equals the amount shown in the table below, according to the size of your choir, you will be sent the Legacy Library, at no extra cost to you.
         </p>
         <p>
          The FREE Legacy Library is now valued on a sliding scale based on the average number of singers in your choir:
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           Average Size of Choir
          </span>
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           The value of your FREE Legacy Library
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          20 – 50 singers = $250 (plus $10 S&amp;H + $30 cost of flash drive = $290)
         </p>
         <p>
          51 – 100 singers = $350 (plus $10 S&amp;H + $30 cost of flash drive = $390)
         </p>
         <p>
          101 or more singers = $450 (plus $10 S&amp;H + $30 cost of flash drive = $490)
         </p>
         <p>
          Examples of how to qualify for a FREE Legacy Library:
          <br/>
          Example #1 (Master CDs) — If the average size of your choir is between 20-50, and you were to purchase Master CDs of two major works priced at $149 each in the next year or two, you would qualify to receive a FREE Legacy Library, valued at $290.
         </p>
         <p>
          Example #2 (Master MP3s) — If the average size of your choir is between 20-50, and you were to purchase Master MP3s of three major works priced at $99 each in the next year or two, you would qualify to receive a FREE Legacy Library, valued at $290.
         </p>
         <p>
          For more information or to place an order please contact Gary Hammond at Toll-Free 800-628-0855. View the complete catalog on the panel to the left.
