    <h2>
     How Do You Say That?
    </h2>
    <h3>
     Pronunciation
    </h3>
    <p>
     Our last name is of germanic descent, and causes many a pronunciation problem despite it only being 5 letters long.  Actually, in German our surname is spelled 'Götz', and the normal translation of the
     <em>
      ö
     </em>
     is to change it to
     <em>
      oe
     </em>
     .  However, English has no words with an
     <em>
      ö
     </em>
     sound like you get in German, and thus the butchery of our name.  For our family, the pronunciation has evolved to 'Getz'.  It's not 100% correct, but that's what we say.
    </p>
