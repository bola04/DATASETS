         <h1 class="entry-title">
          Welcome to Wimborne Therapy Centre
         </h1>
        </header>
        <!-- .entry-header -->
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          <a href="https://wimbornetherapycentre.files.wordpress.com/2015/04/oc-255x170.jpg">
           <img alt="OC (255x170)" class=" size-full wp-image-84 aligncenter" data-attachment-id="84" data-comments-opened="1" data-image-description="" data-image-meta='{"aperture":"0","credit":"","camera":"","caption":"","created_timestamp":"1355417915","copyright":"","focal_length":"0","iso":"0","shutter_speed":"0","title":"","orientation":"0"}' data-image-title="OC (255×170)" data-large-file="https://wimbornetherapycentre.files.wordpress.com/2015/04/oc-255x170.jpg?w=660?w=255" data-medium-file="https://wimbornetherapycentre.files.wordpress.com/2015/04/oc-255x170.jpg?w=660?w=255" data-orig-file="https://wimbornetherapycentre.files.wordpress.com/2015/04/oc-255x170.jpg?w=660" data-orig-size="255,170" data-permalink="https://wimbornetherapycentre.com/2015/04/15/welcome-to-wimborne-therapy-centre/oc-255x170/" sizes="(max-width: 255px) 100vw, 255px" src="https://wimbornetherapycentre.files.wordpress.com/2015/04/oc-255x170.jpg?w=660" srcset="https://wimbornetherapycentre.files.wordpress.com/2015/04/oc-255x170.jpg 255w, https://wimbornetherapycentre.files.wordpress.com/2015/04/oc-255x170.jpg?w=150 150w"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <div style="word-wrap:normal;-webkit-hyphens:none;-moz-hyphens:none;hyphens:none;">
          <h4 style="text-align:center;">
           <span style="text-decoration:underline;">
            Welcome to Wimborne Therapy Centre
           </span>
          </h4>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           Wimborne Therapy Centre offers you an assurance that all the counsellors and psychotherapists who work here are fully qualified and work within a recognised ethical framework. There are many schools of counselling and psychotherapy and Wimborne Therapy Centre aims to offer counsellors and psychotherapists from as many of these different schools as possible.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           The counsellors and psychotherapists who work at Wimborne Therapy Centre have a dedicated page about them on this website. By working through these pages you will be able to find more details of each therapist’s experience, qualifications and membership of professional organistations along with specific details for you to contact them directly. We also offer the availability of sending a message to one of the therapists through the form on our ‘Contact Us’ page.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           Each therapist sets their own fee for counselling and psychotherapy so please see their individual page or contact them to ask for more details.
          </p>
          <h4 style="text-align:center;">
           <span style="text-decoration:underline;">
            About Wimborne Therapy Centre
           </span>
          </h4>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           Wimborne Therapy Centre has therapy rooms that offer a safe and confidential space for counselling and psychotherapy. The Therapy Rooms are located on the first floor and there is a flight of stairs to negotiate to get to them. Counsellors and Psychotherapists tend to liaise with each other to ensure clients are not arriving for or leaving their appointment at the same time as other clients so confidentiality is maintained.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           The therapy rooms are designed to be a comfortable space for you to explore feelings, emotions and experiences in a safe and confidential atmosphere.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           <span style="color:#0000ff;">
            We currently have therapy rooms available for rental by qualified therapists so if you are looking to set up or expand your practice into Wimborne please get in touch. We can accommodate a further two therapists at this time.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           The address and contact details for Wimborne Therapy Centre is as follows:
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           Wimborne Therapy Centre
           <br/>
           41c East Street
           <br/>
           Wimborne Minster
           <br/>
           Dorset
           <br/>
           BH21 1DX
          </p>
          <p>
           The Centre is open between 08:00 and 22:00 every day with different therapists working at different times.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:left;">
           Being based in Wimborne it is easily accessible to people living in
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li style="text-align:left;">
            Bournemouth
           </li>
           <li style="text-align:left;">
            Broadstone
           </li>
           <li style="text-align:left;">
            Corfe Mullen
           </li>
           <li style="text-align:left;">
            Ferndown
           </li>
           <li style="text-align:left;">
            Poole
           </li>
           <li style="text-align:left;">
            Sturminster Marshall
           </li>
           <li style="text-align:left;">
            Upton
           </li>
           <li style="text-align:left;">
            Verwood
           </li>
           <li style="text-align:left;">
            West Moors
           </li>
           <li style="text-align:left;">
            many of the other towns and villages in South and East Dorset.
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           Wimborne is also well situated for access from North Dorset with Shaftesbury only being 30 minutes away by car. Ringwood and other towns just over the Dorset border are equally near by car.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:center;">
           The Wimborne Therapy Centre administrator can be contacted through completing the form on our ‘Contact Us’ page.
          </p>
