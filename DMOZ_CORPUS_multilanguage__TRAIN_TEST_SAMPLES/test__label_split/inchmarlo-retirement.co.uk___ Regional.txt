         <h1>
          Inchmarlo
 Retirement Village
         </h1>
        </div>
        <div class="copy">
         Established in 1986, on Royal Deeside, Inchmarlo Retirement Village comprises nine neighbourhoods within magnificent parkland and gardens in a 100 acre estate. There are currently 200 homes for the over 55s ranging from one bedroom apartments to four bedroom detached houses with their own driveway and garage.
 
 At the heart of the estate is Inchmarlo House, a Georgian B-listed mansion house, a 52 single and double bed 5 star care home.
 
 Please take the time to explore our website and the many photographs which allow you to experience the wonderful haven that is Inchmarlo Retirement Village.
