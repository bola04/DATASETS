         <strong>

         <a href="accommodations.html">

         </a>

         <a href="location.html">
          location
         </a>

You can stay fit during your trip too with a fitness center full of modern equipment. Plus, there’s even an indoor pool and a whirlpool, so if the weather outside is too cold, you’ll feel just right making a splash indoors.
        </p>
        <p>
         Free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel means that you’ll easily be able to upload your vacation photos from the lobby to your room, or even in the fitness center or pool. But if you’re traveling for business, you’ll be happy to gain instant access to your important emails and websites so you can get your job done faster and hopefully, have some time to head to the indoor pool and whirlpool after the working day is done. For all your other business needs, there’s the 24-hours business center where you can even print your boarding pass.
        </p>
        <p>
         The onsite
         <a href="dining.html">
          restaurant
         </a>

         <a href="meeting-rooms.html">
          event space
         </a>
         that’s ideal for business meetings or small weddings, with a delicious menu of food options to keep your guests full and happy.
        </p>

         The fantastic location near the beach is surrounded by shopping,
         <a href="restaurants-near-courtyard-myrtle-beach-barefoot-landing.html">
          dining
         </a>
         and gobs of
         <a href="things-to-do-in-north-myrtle-beach.html">
          activities
         </a>
         too. Famed Barefoot Landing has something for everyone with the Marina, Alligator Adventure, House of Blues, Barefoot Princess Riverboat, and so much more. Dinner theater, family-friendly grand prix, an aquarium and plenty of golf courses are other things to keep you entertained during your stay.
        </p>
        <p>
         The Courtyard Myrtle Beach Barefoot Landing has truly thought of everything so all you need to do is relax, or get down to business. And that’s a nice thing knowing that all you have to do is be you when you’re traveling far from home.
