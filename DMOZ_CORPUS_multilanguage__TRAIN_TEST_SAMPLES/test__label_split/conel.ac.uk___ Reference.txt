           <p>
            The College of Haringey, Enfield and North East London (CONEL) offers a wide range of
            <a href="http://www.conel.ac.uk/for-learners/apprenticeship-vacancies.html">
             apprenticeships
            </a>
            ,
            <a href="/for-learners/2073-pre-apprenticeships.html">
             pre-apprenticeships
            </a>
            and
            <a href="http://www.conel.ac.uk/courses.html" title="Course explorer">
             courses
            </a>
            across many different subjects to give students the skills, knowledge and experience they need to succeed at work or university.
           </p>
           <p>
            We have two superb
            <a href="http://www.conel.ac.uk/about-us/258-our-centres.html">
             centres
            </a>
            in London, in Enfield and Tottenham, and are proud of the high quality of the facilities and services enjoyed by our students and the welcoming, lively environment here.
           </p>
           <p>
            If you have any questions while browsing our website, please
            <a data-reveal-id="cEnquire" href="#">
             send us a message
            </a>
            or call us. Or, come to visit the college on our
            <a href="http://www.conel.ac.uk/news-events/open-days.html">
             next Open Day
            </a>
            !
           </p>
