        <h2 class="hs-section-title">
         About Us
        </h2>
        <div class="hs-content">
         <p>
          <!--?php show_easylogo(); ?-->
          <br/>
          <a href="http://astutetraining.com.au/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/Astute-Logo.png">
           <img alt="Astute Logo" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-2824" height="107" src="http://astutetraining.com.au/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/Astute-Logo-300x107.png" width="300"/>
          </a>
          Astute Training Pty Ltd has been training and encouraging students for over 10 years and has an outstanding record in both domestic and international education.
         </p>
         <p>
          We believe that our commitment to delivering only the highest quality education and training, coupled with our dedication to maintaining all national legislative requirements, provides us with our leading competitive edge in the Vocational Education and Training Sector.
         </p>
         <p>
          Our team of highly trained professionals strive to deliver outstanding quality theoretical and practical experiences to all students. All trainers and assessors working for Astute Training Pty Ltd have undergone a rigorous interview and selection process to ensure that they are respected and accredited professionals in their field of expertise, possessing both industry experience and the necessary certification.
         </p>
         <p>
          In addition, our trainers are encouraged to utilise the most up-to-date adult learning techniques and student support methodologies in order to maximise student learning outcomes.
         </p>
         <p>
          Linked with several industries, employer groups and local communities, Astute Training Pty. Ltd. has an enviable reputation of excellence through participation in industry led consultations. This ensures our students are taught the most current and relevant practices.
         </p>
         <p>
          Students at Astute Training Pty Ltd become part of our wide reaching family and often find it hard to leave when their studies are completed. We believe that developing strong interpersonal relationships between trainers and students brings out the best in all concerned.
         </p>
         <p>
          We are confident that you will feel valued and respected in our modern well-equipped training rooms and that your experience at Astute Training will give you outstanding theoretical and practical experiences necessary for your new career.
         </p>
         <h2>
          <strong>
           WHY STUDY IN AUSTRALIA?
          </strong>
         </h2>
         <h5>
          <a href="http://astutetraining.com.au/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/ILoad515___source.png">
           <img alt="Why study in Australia?" class="alignleft wp-image-3082" height="499" src="http://astutetraining.com.au/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/ILoad515___source-74x300.png" width="123"/>
          </a>
          <em>
           <strong>
            World Class Education
           </strong>
          </em>
         </h5>
         <p>
          Australia is recognised as a great place to live – but did you know Australia also offers a world class education? The Australian education system has produced scientists, designers, educators, entrepreneurs, artists and humanitarians who have changed the world, winning awards from Oscars to Nobel prizes. Their global achievements include the “black box” now on every airplane, the Earth hour initiative, and the invention of Wi-Fi. Australia is proud of the individuals who have studied and worked in Australia (whether they were born here or another country) and gone on to achieve great things and contribute to making the world a better place.
         </p>
         <h5>
          <em>
           <strong>
            Global Recognition
           </strong>
          </em>
         </h5>
         <p>
          By studying in Australia, you will receive a qualification that’s recognised and sought after around the world. The Australian Qualifications Framework (AQF) allows students to easily move through the education system here and provides an easy way for countries around the world to recognise your qualification, and issue the comparable qualifications for local use. Visit the
          <a class="external" href="http://www.aqf.edu.au/" target="_blank">
           AQF
           <span class="accessible">
            (opens in a new window)
           </span>
          </a>
          website for more information.
         </p>
         <h5>
          <em>
           <strong>
            Your learning environment
           </strong>
          </em>
         </h5>
         <p>
          By choosing to study in Australia, you will join hundreds of thousands of students from Australian and all over the world – including many from your home country – who are discovering new friends and opportunities in this beautiful country. You will work closely with classmates, researchers, instructors, and other faculty – collaboration is a key part of our students’ successes. And in many cases you can gain practical and hands-on training in the industry in which you are studying. This combination of teamwork, shared learning, and industry focus will provide you with a leading edge for your further studies and career.
         </p>
         <p>
          For further information on why studying in Australia is great please visit the Austrade (Australian Trade and Investment Commission) website here: https://www.studyinaustralia.gov.au/english/why-australia/world-class-education
