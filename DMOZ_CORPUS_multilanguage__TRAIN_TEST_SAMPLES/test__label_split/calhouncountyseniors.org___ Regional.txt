      <h5>
       Assisting Florida Seniors, Since 1977
      </h5>
      <p>
       Calhoun County provides a vast array of services for local senior citizens, the elderly, and those that may be disadvantaged due to illness. Under the roof of the Senior Citizens Association, Inc., we act as a central location for senior services (CCSCA), transportation (Calhoun Transit) and as a specialized seniors travel agency (Calco Travel). Through the three divisions we provide case management, home delivered meals, outreach, hot lunches, companionship, recreation, health screenings, transportation and hundreds of other resources which benefit the residents of Calhoun County, Florida.
      </p>
