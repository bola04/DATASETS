            <p>
             "Mosaic Art has been around for many centuries. Using broken china, pottery shards, glass
 														and other found objects, I create what evokes a thought or an idea by recycling.
 														I have been creating pieces over 14 years, with no two pieces ever being
 														alike. Being a self taught non-directional creator of mosaic pieces, you
 														never know what you will see next. Most of my work is 2 and 3 dimensional
 														mixed media. When I am creating I like to use colors and folksy, fartsy pieces
 														in which I hope will provoke a happy feeling that will bring on a smile!"
