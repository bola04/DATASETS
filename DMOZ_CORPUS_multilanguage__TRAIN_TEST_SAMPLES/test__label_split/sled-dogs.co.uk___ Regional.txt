         <div class="sectionHeader">
          Cairngorm Sleddog Centre, Aviemore
         </div>
         Situated in Aviemore, at the foot of the Cairngorm Mountain Range,
         <strong>
          The Cairngorm Sleddog Centre
         </strong>
         offers a wide range of activities for the whole family. From Kennel &amp; Museum Tours to Sleddog Trips and Safaris.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Alan Stewart &amp; his family offer a unique opportunity to experience the awesome thrill and power of a top class sleddog team in training for European events &amp; Mountain expeditions. Meet our team and have as much hands-on with the dogs as you want, with spectacular views of the Cairngorm Mountains and the wildlife in the surrounding area.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         The Cairngorm Sleddog Centre is the only daily working sleddog centre in the UK and one of only 5 in Europe. For more information on the Cairngorm Sleddog Centre and the work that we do with our wonderful sleddogs click the link below.
        </div>

       <li>
        <div class="videoExplain">
         <div class="sectionHeader">
          Cairngorm Sleddog Safaris
         </div>
         Be a part of a
         <strong>
          real sleddog adventure
         </strong>
         by helping to prepare a 12 dog team, then be taken by dog team and guide into some of the very best scenic routes in the Cairngorms with breathtaking panoramic views of mountain ranges.Very often on this trip you will be 
 getting close to herds of wild red deer and other wildlife which live in the National Park. The remote forest in this area is alive with wildlife.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         You will 
 arrive at a forest bush camp where you will help secure the team, help water and snack them and enjoy a short break, brew up a cup of coffee/tea in one of our shelters.
         <br/>
         We then hook up the team again andmake our way back to the centre via some of the most hair raising and exiting trails in Europe for sleddogs.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         This really is a great way to get a true insight into the life of a working sleddog team.
        </div>
        <div class="videoHolder">
         <a href="http://templatica.com/preview/30">
          <img alt="Css Template Preview" height="224" src="_images/layout/experience.jpg" width="348"/>
         </a>
        </div>
       </li>
       <li>
        <div class="videoExplain">
         <div class="sectionHeader">
          CSC Kennel &amp; Museum Tours
         </div>
         Come and meet our wonderful team of professional sleddogs. this is a great way to get a brief insight into the history of the sport and the worldof working selddogs. The Carngorm Sleddog Centre is dedicated to Scotty Allan "King of the Arctic Trails" and you will be able to find out all about this great pioneer in the centres dedicated museum. There's a video shown in the small museum (1 hour)
         <br/>
         <br/>
         The way of life, The Stewart Family have with their K9 friends, living at the foot of the Cairngorm Mountain Range is not one that many people would  choose but this trip lets you see the wonderful relationship they share.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         You will also learn about the great sleddog races which many of the top mushers in Alaska, Canada, Europe and America still compete in today, including the Iditarod, The Last Great Race on Earth.
        </div>
