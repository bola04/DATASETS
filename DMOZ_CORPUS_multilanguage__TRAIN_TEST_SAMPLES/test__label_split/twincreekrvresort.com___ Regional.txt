              </h1>

             <div class="entry-content">
              <h4 class="home_left_img">
               <img alt="" class="home_wel" src="wp-content/uploads/2014/03/0742.jpg"/>
               Twin Creek RV Resort
              </h4>
              <p>
               <span class="tit_home_txt" style="color: #008000;">
                We will be opening for the season March 30, 2018!  Come enjoy Twin Creek, Gatlinburg, and the Great Smoky Mountains!
               </span>
              </p>
              <p>
               Join us at Twin Creek RV Resort! Family owned and operated since 1984, you’ll enjoy the peace and beauty of camping with all the comforts and conveniences of a full-service accommodation. Just imagine going to sleep with the sound of a rippling stream just outside your window. At Twin Creek we also have available
               <span style="color: #ff0000;">
                on-site car and Jeep rental
               </span>
               , camper supplies/grocery store,
               <span class="small_txt">
                playground, and free WI-FI. These are only a few of the many amenities offered. Sorry no tents, and there are some pet restrictions.
               </span>
              </p>
