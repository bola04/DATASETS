               <div>
                <span style="color: #ff6600; font-size: 23px;">
                 <strong>
                  Welcome to Brenner International Pty Ltd
                 </strong>
                </span>
                <div>
                </div>
                <div>
                 <div>
                  Brenner International is a privately owned Australian engineering company and we have been producing machinery components, parts &amp; products for more than 40 years.
                 </div>
                 <div>
                 </div>
                 <div>
                  We specialize in manufacturing Air Shafts/Core Holding Products, Idler &amp; Spreading Rollers, Safety Chucks, Chill/Heated Rollers, Printing Cylinders &amp; Mandrels, Dr Chamber Assemblies and the manufacturing of mechanical parts &amp; components for OEM industries. A growing part of our business is the manufacture and supply of parts and components for OEM industries.
                 </div>
                 <div>
                 </div>
                 <div>
                  Our head office and manufacturing opertaions are based in Melbourne Australia, at Brenner International.
                 </div>
                 <div>
                 </div>
                 <div>
                  Currently we manufacture and export to Australia, New Zealand, Japan, Germany and USA.
