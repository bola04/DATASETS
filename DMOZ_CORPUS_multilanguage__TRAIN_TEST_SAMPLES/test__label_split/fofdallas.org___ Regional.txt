                <h1>
                 Welcome to Fellowship of Freethought Dallas
                </h1>
                <p>
                 Welcome to the Fellowship of Freethought. We’re so glad you found us! We are an organization in the Dallas area committed to providing you with opportunities to connect with other Freethinkers, explore some of life’s big questions, reach out to the larger community in which we live, and basically have a lot of fun together.
                </p>
                <p>
                 We have plenty of room for you and your entire family, whether you just want to enjoy our many social activities or you are ready to take a leadership role in our next outreach project. Come to any
                 <a href="http://www.meetup.com/fofdallas/" target="_blank">
                  event
                 </a>
                 to get started. Contact us with any questions or suggestions. And be sure to join us for our monthly Gathering.
                </p>
                <p>
                 You can get more information on our Gatherings and other events on the Fellowship of Freethought
                 <a href="http://www.meetup.com/fofdallas/" target="_blank">
                  Meetup Calendar
                 </a>
                 .
                </p>
