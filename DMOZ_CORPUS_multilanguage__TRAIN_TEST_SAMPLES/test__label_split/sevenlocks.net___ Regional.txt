             </span>

            <h4>
             Home of the Sharks Swim and Dive Team
            </h4>
            <p>
             Every summer, Seven Locks Swim &amp; Tennis Club comes alive with the sights and sounds of summer. Conveniently located near Potomac and Bethesda, Seven Locks offers a refreshing pool, renovated tennis courts, shaded play areas and much more. Whether you’re looking to swim, play tennis, socialize with friends, or just relax by the pool, Seven Locks will be your “home away from home” all summer long!
