        <h2>
         <span style="color: #005dac">
          Welcome to Little Sur Inn
         </span>
        </h2>
        <p>
         In the heart of California’s central coast, Little Sur Inn, a family owned and operated 17 room boutique property, is ideally located on Moonstone Beach Drive in the resort village of Cambria.
        </p>
        <p>
         Our beachfront property provides a modern yet sophisticated design with a very comfortable and inviting ambiance. We offer a daily complimentary continental breakfast.
        </p>
        <p>
         Our rooms have a full or partial ocean view with a comfortable and inviting ambiance. All rooms have a fireplace, refrigerator, micro-wave, coffeemaker, and complimentary internet access. Relax in a Jacuzzi tub in the oceanfront rooms and enjoy the spectacular white water views and sunsets from your patio or balcony. All rooms are non-smoking and no pets allowed.
        </p>
        <p>
         Cambria is located on the Central Coast between Los Angeles and San Francisco, minutes away from Hearst Castle, Scenic Coast Highway 1, Piedras Blancas lighthouse, the elephant seals colony, and the wine country. The surrounding area offers many scenic walking trails and a boardwalk situated right in front of our property.
        </p>
