             <h2>
              Doing justice, loving mercy, and walking humbly with our God. We are a multi-ethnic, multi-generational church gathering in the heart of Oakland.
             </h2>
             <p>
              In a city bursting with cultural diversity, Regeneration is a collection of broken, eclectic people who come together to authentically love God and our community.
             </p>
             <p>
              We’d love for you to join us for one of our Sunday services, a mid-week home group, or serve with us as we care for the homeless, refugees, and youth in our community.
             </p>
             <p>
              To find out more about what’s going on at Regen join our
              <a href="http://regenerationweb.us6.list-manage1.com/subscribe/post?u=e118e24fec094c8e58eb45ff2&amp;id=6d666a0bdc">
               mailing list
              </a>
              or follow us on
              <a href="https://www.facebook.com/regenerationweb">
               Facebook
              </a>
              and
              <a href="https://twitter.com/ReGenerationOak">
               Twitter
              </a>
              .
             </p>
