       <div class="project-meta">
        <h2 class="project-title">
         Sculpture
        </h2>
        <div class="project-description">
         <p>
          Sculptures are made from reclaimed materials: Metals, glass, old jewelry, dirt and sand, discarded building materials, etc.
         </p>
         <p>
          The Plexiglas is painted in reverse to retain the clear brilliance of the material.
         </p>
         <p>
          Besides exploring and experimenting with the combination of materials I like to embed messages in each of the pieces. Many are actual puzzles that come with a sealed solution only to be to revealed to The Buyer.
         </p>
