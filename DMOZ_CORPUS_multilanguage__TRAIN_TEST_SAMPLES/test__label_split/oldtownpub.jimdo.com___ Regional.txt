           <h1 class="" id="cc-m-header-13269754934">
            Watercoolers and Stuff!
           </h1>
          </div>
          <div class="j-module n j-imageSubtitle " id="cc-m-13269755934">
           <figure class="cc-imagewrapper cc-m-image-align-1">
            <img alt="" class="" data-image-id="8279286434" data-src="https://image.jimcdn.com/app/cms/image/transf/none/path/s2967f47cdd3f35c5/image/i549ed8ccaa7f37bf/version/1464017565/image.jpg" data-src-height="625" data-src-width="768" id="cc-m-imagesubtitle-image-13269755934" sizes="(min-width: 768px) 768px, 100vw" src="https://image.jimcdn.com/app/cms/image/transf/none/path/s2967f47cdd3f35c5/image/i549ed8ccaa7f37bf/version/1464017565/image.jpg" srcset="https://image.jimcdn.com/app/cms/image/transf/dimension=320x10000:format=jpg/path/s2967f47cdd3f35c5/image/i549ed8ccaa7f37bf/version/1464017565/image.jpg 320w, https://image.jimcdn.com/app/cms/image/transf/dimension=640x10000:format=jpg/path/s2967f47cdd3f35c5/image/i549ed8ccaa7f37bf/version/1464017565/image.jpg 640w, https://image.jimcdn.com/app/cms/image/transf/none/path/s2967f47cdd3f35c5/image/i549ed8ccaa7f37bf/version/1464017565/image.jpg 768w"/>

           <script id="cc-m-reg-13269755934">

     window.regModule("module_imageSubtitle", {"data":{"imageExists":true,"hyperlink":"","hyperlink_target":"","hyperlinkAsString":"","pinterest":"0","id":13269755934,"widthEqualsContent":"0","resizeWidth":768,"resizeHeight":625},"id":13269755934});

          <div class="j-module n j-text " id="cc-m-13269755034">
           <p>
            Water coolers have advanced from merely relying upon a block of ice for cooling down water as used in old English Victorian houses to today's highly technical devices meeting strict health,
     sanitation and environmental requirements.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
            The water cooler emerged from it Victorian forbear and is now one of one of the most crucial pieces of equipment that have contributed to the health and well being of both children and adults
     alike.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
            In the last century, the water cooler has come a long way.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
            The water cooler as we now know it was first invented in the very early 1906 and its conception is credited to 2 guys - Mr Halsey Willard Taylor and also Mr Luther Haws. Luther Haws patented the
     very first drinking machine in 1911. It is believed that the main reason behind the innovation was to not just chill a beverage but also to provide purified water fit for human consumption.
     Infected drinking water resulted in the death of Luther Haws' father as result of the typhoid fever infection he caught. Consequently, Mr Haws decided to develop some apparatus which would
     provide safe drinking water.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
            Bottled
            <a href="http://www.waterboy.co.uk/" target="_blank" title="http://www.waterboy.co.uk/">
             office water coolers
            </a>
            initially appeared in the United States of America over one hundred years
     ago. However, it had not been till the 1980's that the U.S.A. saw a significant increase in demand, due to the introduction of plastic containers - rather than glass. This tiny modification
     revolutionised the mineral water cooler market which ended up becoming widely popular around the world.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
            By the mid to late 1980's, vending machine suppliers in the United Kingdom realised their chance to develop a market for water cooler machines in the UK.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
            Water coolers have actually developed in line with changing technology and fashion tastes as well. Modern water coolers are small, light and power efficient as well as can be found in a range of
     designs, colour and styles to match most areas of an office clock or manufacturing complex. Today, lots of water coolers or dispensers also incorporate a warm water facility along with cooled
     water. In addition, these dispensers have to comply with strict hygiene policies, cleanliness and environmental standards.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
            The most popular the water coolers at present are fed by a plastic container or bottle containing spring mineral water. This enabled clients to clearly view the quality of water through the
     bottle and examine the mineral content of the water by reading the label on the side of every bottle. Without a doubt, mineral water suppliers tapped into the growing demand from consumers for
     the health benefits provided by mineral water.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
            Throughout the 1990's, once more led by the United States, there was an adjustment in focus from bottled water coolers to water coolers fed by mains water supplies complete with integrated
     filters, removing the costs of the bottling process, cost of bottles and also the substantial expense of delivery.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p style="margin: 0cm; margin-bottom: .0001pt;">
            It is difficult to picture where the water cooler will go from now however. Is popularity together with its practical advantages and convenience factor will definitely see the humble water cooler
     continue to be in offices, factories and recreational facilities for many years to come.
           </p>
           <p>

           <p>
            Highway furniture describes all outside furniture consisting of outdoor artworks, seating, traffic signs, traffic lights, street lamps, phone booths, garbage receptacles and post boxes.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            The benefits afforded by street furniture to town residents includes directions to conveniences, personal, somewhere to site and rest as well as something nice to look at.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            An area's civic amenity culture and history is commonly determined by the number and kind of street furniture suppliers that are available and capable of of producing artful yet practical
     equipment that can be that is deployed around its civic areas.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            In our early times, horses and cattle were used as a means of transportation.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Among the very first items of street furniture that were presented on our streets, were water troughs made use of for the watering of horses.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            After water troughs, came road signs, seats as well as roadway lighting.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Lights were utilized in the evenings to make the roads more secure to walk on and seats were located in public locations and places like railway stations for the benefit of travellers.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            To guide people around their locality, basic road indications were set up to supply instructions and the identity of roads.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Litter bins, post boxes and phone boxes appeared over time as technology and civilisations progressed.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Mail boxes and phone booths included benefit for folks as they strolled down the streets and rubbish bins kept streets and outside areas clean.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            The needs of village dwellers have changed significantly over time. This was caused by development of innovation as well as civilisation with advances in the style of street furniture items put
     on the roads, the result.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Super top quality electrical street lights have replaced the old fashioned Victorian lights and LED signs are now seen around the urban landscape that show crucial news details and day-to-day
     weather condition.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Reflective street signs as well as traffic signals have taken over from antiquated basic metal signs and litter bins and post boxes have actually been upgraded to accommodate the exceptionally
     big influx in city dwellers that live in a single area.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Furthermore, the unbelievable cultural improvements of the mankind are reflected by using modern-day themes and contemporary creative styles in contemporary art statues which we now see in our
     city spaces.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Towns have become very worried about waste and ecological balance in the last few years.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            And, this can be seen outdoors with the emerging trend of using reclaimed street furniture components.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Recycled furniture makes it possible for
            <a href="http://www.rema.org.uk/standards-and-regulations/marker-posts-street-furniture/" target="_blank" title="http://www.rema.org.uk/standards-and-regulations/marker-posts-street-furniture/">
             civic authorities
            </a>
            to minimize their carbon footprint and upgrade their furniture at the very same time.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            For a smooth furniture look and feel, recycled street furniture is generally produced from plastic materials.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Using reclaimed thermoplastic products enables the production of elegant items consisting of litter receptacles, chairs, benches and picnic tables which can be deployed anywhere in the town.
           </p>
