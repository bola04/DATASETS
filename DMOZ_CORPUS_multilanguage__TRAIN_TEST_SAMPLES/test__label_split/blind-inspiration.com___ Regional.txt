        Window Blinds, Plantation Shutters &amp; Awnings All supplied and fitted
       </h1>
       <p>
        Blind Inspiration is a family run business based in Marlow with over 25 years' experience in window furnishings. We supply and fit window blinds, plantation shutters and awnings to the following areas:
       </p>
       <p>
        Marlow, Maidenhead, Beaconsfield, Gerrards Cross, Denham, Chalfont St Peter, Penn, High Wycombe, Princes Risborough, Bray, Farnham Common, Windsor, Wraysbury, Iver, Slough, Wokingham, Ascot, Virginia Water, Sunningdale, Bracknell, Twyford, Burnham, Cookham, Wooburn Green, Bourne End, Stokenchurch, Watlington, Henley, Henley on Thames, Thame, Hazlemere, Great Missenden, Amersham and Chorleywood.
       </p>
       <p>
        We can offer a complete and friendly service with experience and knowledge to advise you on the right product, colour and configuration. This website is geared towards our unique videos which all give away a wealth of product information. These videos assist in giving you an insight into all our products and going by our customer feedback they have been invaluable to them in making their final decision. For those of you who prefer the written word and images they are also available to peruse at your leisure.
       </p>
       <p>
        We have videos on this website on all the following products: Plantation Shutters, Conservatory roof and sides, Duette, Wood venetians, Snugfit, Metal venetians, Twist, Silhouette, Facette, Roller, Vertical, Velux Skylights, Awnings and Child Safety.
