       Studio D's digital recording studios are located in Wick, a small town situated in the far north of Scotland just 18 miles south of John O'Groats in the county of Caithness.  Wick is well connected to the rest of the UK and abroad having an airport, railway station and a number of daily coaches connecting to Inverness and the south.
      </p>
