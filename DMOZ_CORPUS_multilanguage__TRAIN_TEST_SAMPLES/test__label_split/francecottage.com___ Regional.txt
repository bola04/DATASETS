     <div class="prose">
      <h2>
       A secluded cottage with private pool
      </h2>
      Les Chauses is a secluded cottage set at the end of a small track with no passing traffic in the beautiful Dordogne countryside. Take a relaxing holiday in a villa with your own
      <strong>
       private pool
      </strong>
      in this delightful part of France.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      The cottage is totally private in a peaceful and relaxing setting, with 2 acres of beautiful garden and woods. This traditional stone farmhouse has been tastefully restored to retain its original charm yet provide modern comforts and amenities, with a 10m x 5m swimming pool.
     </div>
     <div class="wideprose">
      Despite the restful setting, you are only 3Km from the historic village of Bourdeilles with its 11th Century Chateau, and 15km from the picturesque town of Brant√¥me.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Relax in this beautiful setting in the rolling Dordogne countryside. Enjoy the top class food at local restaurants, canoe down the River Dronne, or visit the many interesting attractions and picturesque villages.
     </div>
     <div class="prose">
      <h4>
       An idyllic setting in a quiet hamlet
      </h4>
      The house is approximately 250 years old, and has been restored and equipped to a high standard with all modern conveniences, whilst retaining a rustic country farmhouse feeling with it's old beams, exposed stone walls, wooden floors and kitchen with local quarry tiles.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      New for 2014 is a modern en suite for the master bedroom and a completely refitted bathroom.
      <br/>
      <br/>
     </div>
     <div>
      <a class="vlightbox1" href="Graphics/DSCN1906L.jpg" title="The swimming pool is 2m deep">
       <img alt="Swimming pool" class="right-pic" src="Graphics/DSCN1906S.jpg"/>
      </a>
     </div>
     <div class="wideprose">
      There is a sunny patio to the side of the house, with a sun canopy to  provide some shade on hot summer days. The garden slopes down to attractive woods, and in the centre is the 10m x 5m swimming pool, built and maintained to a high standard. The pool is alarmed for the safety of children.
     </div>
     <div>

     <div class="prose">
      <h4>
       <em>
        Private garden
       </em>
      </h4>
      The garden is totally private, and birds and wildlife abound in the natural woodland. A variety of trees provide plenty of shade.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      An open sided barn to one side of the house is used to shelter the car and as a wood store, and there is ample garden furniture for barbeques or relaxing by the pool.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      The surrounding countryside is green and attractive, with rolling hills. and many delightful walks from the door. It is also wonderful cycling country and cycles are available on request.
