       <h1>
        Welcome to Caspe Fishing on the River Ebro
       </h1>
       <div style="width:320px; margin-left:20px; float:right; margin-right:0px;">
        <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="198" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/dgcR019DkU4" width="300">
        </iframe>
       </div>
       The apartments have been issued with the certificate for use for tourism by the local government
       <br/>
       We are based in the rural town of Caspe on the River Ebro in Spain. This is a picturesque area of Spain where the massive River Ebro has been dammed at the town of Mequinenza creating a mass of water 70 miles (100Km) long extending from a weir at the town of Escatron.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       We have no particular change-over day so you can arrive any day of the week.
       <br/>
       Due to change-overs we ask guests to arrive after 14:00 hrs on day of arrival and leave the apartments in a clean condition by 10:00 hrs on day of departure.
       <br/>
       If you wish to arrive or depart outside these times please ask us in advance, we can then advise if it is possible.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Our accomodation is situated on the Penninsular De Chacon which is 10 minutes drive from Caspe, Peninsular De Chacon is a small urbanisation on the banks of the River Ebro with the apartments only 50 metres from your boat and a fishing area.
       <br/>
       Caspe has an improving reputation as one of the premier areas for fishing in Europe and certainly in Spain.Catches of Catfish (Wels, Siluro,), Carp (mostly common) Zander (Lucia perca) are common with PBs being broken frequently by visiting anglers.
       <br/>
       We offer self catering accomodation including a boat for Do It Yourself fishermen who wish to run their own holiday in the privacy of our comfortable accomodation.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <em>
        Please advise us if you want to bring a dog so we can confirm if it is possible.
       </em>
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Other local atttractions include a close proximity to Motorland, Aragon's new motorsport venue. Also there are 3 sportwear factory shop including ADIDAS
