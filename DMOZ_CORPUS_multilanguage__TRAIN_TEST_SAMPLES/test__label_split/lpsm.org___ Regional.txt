           <h2 class="headline">
            About
           </h2>
           <div id="content">
            <div class="intro" id="intro">
             <div class="text-content">
              <blockquote>
               <p>
                The Libertarian Party of San Mateo County
                <span>
                </span>
                <em>
                 (LPSM)
                </em>
                <span>
                </span>
                is the local presence of the
                <span>
                </span>
                <a href="http://www.ca.lp.org/" target="_top">
                 Libertarian Party of California
                </a>
                <span>
                </span>
                <em>
                 (LPC)
                </em>
                . Our purpose is to assist local Libertarian candidates (in matters such as ballot access) and to engage in political and information activities within San Mateo County (promoting libertarian ideals such as self-determination, freedom, and individual responsibility).
               </p>
               <p>
                The
                <span>
                </span>
                <em>
                 LPSM
                </em>
                <span>
                </span>
                is best known for its active opposition to higher taxes in San Mateo County. Over the last 20 years we've used ballot arguments, newspaper interviews, and public appearances to help block more than two billion dollars in new taxes—saving over $8000 per household.
               </p>
               <p>
                <em>
                 When was the last time the establishment Republican or Democrat Parties stood up for the little guy instead of the special interest groups?!
                </em>
               </p>
               <p>
                January through October, we meet
                <span>
                </span>
                <a href="/meetup" target="_top">
                 almost monthly
                </a>
                <span>
                </span>
                in Belmont for dinner (
                <a href="/meetup" target="_top">
                 Visitors Welcome!
                </a>
                ) and a business meeting (members only) and confer more frequently via
                <span>
                </span>
                <a href="/discussion_groups">
                 email
                </a>
                .
               </p>
               <p>
                Questions? Write or call
                <span>
                </span>
                <a href="mailto:Harrison@LPSM.org">
                 Harland Harrison
                </a>
                , Chair, at (650) 591-5270.
               </p>
              </blockquote>
