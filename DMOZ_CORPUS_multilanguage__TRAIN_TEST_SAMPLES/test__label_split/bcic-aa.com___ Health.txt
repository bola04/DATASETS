            <font color="#000000" face="Arial">
             <b>
              "The
             </b>
            </font>
            <font color="#660000" face="Arial">
             <b>
              Broward
                      County Institutions
                      Committee
             </b>
            </font>
            <font color="#000000" face="Arial">
             <b>
              is a group of AA members who volunteer their
                      time for the purpose of carrying the message of
                      recovery from the disease of alcoholism into the
                      Corrections and Treatment facilities of Broward
                      County.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              The Broward County Institutions Committee
                      focuses on the process of "one drunk, talking to
                      another" as the most effective means of carrying
                      the AA message."
              <br/>
              <br/>
              BCIC takes A.A. meetings into 44 Treatment
                      facilities and 10 Correction facilities, at the
                      present time. 145 meetings per week requires 290
                      members to alternate from week to week so that
                      each person is doing a meeting twice a month.
                      The names and addresses of the 54 facilities are
                      published on a monthly basis.
              <br/>
             </b>
            </font>
            <font color="#000000" face="Arial" size="-1">

             </b>
