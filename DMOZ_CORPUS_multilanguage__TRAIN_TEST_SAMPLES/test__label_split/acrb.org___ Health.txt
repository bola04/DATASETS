      <p>
       Welcome to the Home Page of the American Chiropractic Rehabilitation Board (ACRB®). We are a professional organization dedicated to providing continuing education and training for Chiropractors who wish to offer their patients the latest treatments and evidence-based rehabilitation techniques. Helping patients attain their best level of health and mobility is our constant goal, and we do that with the specialized training and certifications offered through the ACRB®, with the highest level of certification being a Diplomate of the American Chiropractic Rehabilitation Board (DACRB). Combining the science of Chiropractic with Rehabilitation has been proven to help patients feel better and live more active lives!
      </p>
