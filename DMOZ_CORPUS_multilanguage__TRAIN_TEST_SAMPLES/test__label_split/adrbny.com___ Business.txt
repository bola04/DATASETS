         <h3>
          The BNY Mellon Depositary Receipt Market Review 2017
         </h3>
         <p>
          With the global economy finally enjoying a sustainable economic expansion, the global equity markets were optimistic and demand for cross-border equity investing in depositary receipts was strong throughout 2017.
         </p>
         <p>
          Global DR value held increased by $36.6 bn YoY as U.S. investment in non-U.S. equities increased 28%.  The BNY Mellon Composite Depositary Receipt Index surged 27.8% versus an increase of 21.8% in the S&amp;P 500.  BNY Mellon won Best Global Bank for Depositary Receipts and released thought leadership providing IR insights to its clients.
         </p>
