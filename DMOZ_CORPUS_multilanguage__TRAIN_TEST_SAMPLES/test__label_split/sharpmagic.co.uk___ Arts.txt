       <strong>
        Close up / Table magician:
       </strong>
       this type of  magician is perfect for adults and is usually booked for corporate functions,  dinners, private  parties. Frederic's  magic tricks and sleight of hand with cards, coins and other objects will certainly impress  your guests. Frederic also performs mindreading effects up close and surrounded by spectators. These demonstrations were made famous on TV by Derren Brown. Whilst guests are standing around during the drinks  reception, he performs his magic  in “Mix and Mingle” conditions, strolling amongst the guests. During dinner, he makes sure everyone is entertained by performing  magic around the tables in-between courses. As a
       <a href="http://www.sharpmagic.co.uk/table-magician.php">
        table  magician
       </a>
