           Welcome to our website, Longs have
           <a href="http://www.longsbridport.co.uk/locations/">
            two locations
           </a>

           <strong>
            eat in
           </strong>
           at our restaurant or outside tables or
           <strong>
            take away
           </strong>
           traditional fish and chip shop fare, we also make a varied specials menu that changes regularly.
