                WELCOME TO THE 2018 FREE EDINBURGH FRINGE 
 								FESTIVAL....

              <p>
               <b>
                <font color="#000066" face="Arial" size="4">
                 Welcome to our programme for 2018! We have new shows, 
 								new Venues and a new Logo!
                </font>
                <font color="#000066" face="Arial">
                 This year our 
 								shows will run July 29th to August 26th 
 								2018 at our venues across Edinburgh.
                </font>
                <font color="#0000FF" face="Arial">
                </font>
               </b>
               <font face="Arial">
                <b>
                 The first few 
 								days are our Kids festival dates, with the full 
 								programme starting August 2nd.
                </b>
               </font>
              </p>
              <p>
               <font face="Arial">
                This year we have a record-breaking 
 								9237 performances, of 402 shows at our 25 venues, 
 								more venues than ever, with more award nominated 
 								shows appearing, top name comedians, the best in 
 								theatre, cabaret and children's shows from all 
 								over the world - including the Fringe's smallest 
 								venue, and the only Fringe show entirely in 
 								Japanese.
               </font>
              </p>
              <p>
               <font color="#000066" face="Arial">
                <img align="left" border="2" height="415" src="images/2018.jpg" width="204"/>
               </font>
               <font face="Arial">
                <font color="#000066">
                 <b>
                  This 
 								year we have some exciting new venues for you to 
 								choose from, and several of our venues are 
 								benefiting from refurbishments to make your 
 								Fringe viewing all that more pleasurable!
                 </b>
                </font>
                <b>
                </b>
                There is the usual massive comedy selection, 
 								along with four music stages, some fantastic 
 								cabaret, theatre, children's shows, spoken word 
 								a walking tour and some busking, plus our free 
 								nightclubs to dance the night away after the 
 								shows.
               </font>
              </p>
              <p>
               <font face="Arial">
                As always there 
 								is so much to choose from - have a look through 
 								the programme now and make a note of what you 
 								want to see.
               </font>
              </p>
              <p>
               <font face="Arial">
                <font color="#000066">
                 <b>
                  It is also our 15th Anniversary year this year, 
 								so we're really happy to be presenting what we 
 								think is our best programme ever.
                 </b>
                </font>
                Can 
 								you believe that? 15 years of the very best free 
 								shows Edinburgh has to offer!
               </font>
              </p>
              <p>
               <b>
                <font color="#000066" face="Arial">
                 Last 
 								year
                </font>
               </b>
               <font face="Arial">
                <font color="#000066">
                 <b>
                  we 
 								had a hugely critically acclaimed Fringe
                 </b>
                </font>
                , 
 								with award winners and nominations, a massive bucket-load of five-star reviewed shows and even more four star reviewed shows 
 								- which proves that our free shows are a sign of 
 								top quality Fringe shows, while we allow 
 								performers and audiences experience the freedom 
 								of the Fringe, with a chance to experiment and 
 								enjoy, without the huge costs!
                <b>
                 Just what the Fringe 
 								should be!
                </b>
               </font>
              </p>

                 </b>

 								2018.
