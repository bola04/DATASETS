               <div style="display: none;">
                <h1>
                 Gelandesprung Ski Club of Green Bay, Wisconsin
                </h1>
                <div class="gel-custom-block floatright" id="gelandesprung-meaning">
                 <a href="http://dictionary.reference.com/browse/gelandesprung">
                  ge·län·de·sprung
                 </a>
                 <p>
                  a jump, usually over an obstacle, in which one plants both poles in the snow in advance of the skis, bends close to the ground, and propels oneself chiefly by the use of the poles.
                 </p>
                </div>
                <p>
                 The Gelandesprung Ski Club of Green Bay is a local non-profit organization of skiing enthusiasts who banded together over 40 years ago to enjoy downhill skiing and other year-round activities.
                </p>
                <p>
                 Gelandesprung Ski Club membership is open to adults of age 21 or over. The club's membership is made up of singles and married couples, ages 21 to over 60, with widely varying backgrounds and interests. Likewise, the skiing abilities of the membership range from pure beginners to all-out experts and all levels in between. The better skiers frequently assist those of us who are still in the learning process!
                </p>
                <p>
                 If you have any questions about our club, please send an
                 <a href="mailto:president@gelandesprung.org?subject=subject=Club%20Info%20Request">
                  email
                 </a>
                 or stop out for one of our
                 <a href="/meetings">
                  meetings
                 </a>
                 ; you are always welcome and the refreshments are always cold.
                </p>
                <p>
                 We hope to hear from you soon!
                </p>
                <p>
                 <a href="mailto:president@gelandesprung.org?subject=subject=Club%20Info%20Request">
                  Julie Steltz
                 </a>
                </p>
                <p>
                 President, Gelandesprung Ski Club
                </p>
