       <div class="item">
        <h3 class="h4 d2lOrange_txt">
         LMS
        </h3>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         A
         <a href="https://www.d2l.com/glossary/lms/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
          learning management system (LMS)
         </a>
         is web-based software for administering the delivery of online learning and training. Successful teaching and learning requires a flexible LMS that allows users to easily manage course work, assignments, and evaluations on campus, online, and around the world. Brightspace goes beyond the old “one-size-fits-all” LMS with an easier, more flexible, and smarter learning platform that delivers real results. Brightspace is created by D2L, a leading innovator in education technology.
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="item">
        <h3 class="h4 d2lOrange_txt">
         Learning Management Solutions
        </h3>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Learning solutions facilitate flexible teaching, learning, and evaluation formats such as blended learning, distance education, and flipped classrooms.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="/k-12/">
          <strong>
           LMS Solutions for K-12 Education
          </strong>
         </a>
         <strong>
          .

        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="/solutions/higher-education/">
          <strong>
           LMS Solutions for Higher Education
          </strong>
         </a>
         <strong>
          .
