      Windermere Lakeside Bed &amp; Breakfast is nestled in the tiny village of Windermere, British Columbia, a sleepy hamlet of Invermere and is just a few steps from Windermere Beach and the warm pristine waters of Lake Windermere. Our B&amp;B is located between Radium and Fairmont Hot springs and just twenty minutes from Panorama Resort. The golf resorts of Eagle Ranch, Fairmont Mountainside/Riverside Golf Resort, Copper Point and the Springs at Radium are just minutes away.
     </p>
