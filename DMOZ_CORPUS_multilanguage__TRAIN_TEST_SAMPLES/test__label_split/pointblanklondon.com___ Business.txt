        </span>

        </a>

       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Established in London in '94 and voted the world's
        <strong>
         'Best Music Production &amp; DJ School'
        </strong>
        by the readers of DJ Mag, Point Blank teaches the creative process of making music at the highest level. Aided by our long-term partnership with Middlesex University, expert instructors and state-of-the-art facilities, many graduates have enjoyed successful careers in music including Claude VonStroke, Goldie, Nicole Moudaber, Leona Lewis, Patrick Topping, Monki and Felix Jaehn.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        You can study Music Production, DJing, Radio Broadcasting, Singing &amp; Songwriting, Music Business and Live Sound Engineering with us. Using Logic, Ableton, Native Instruments and Pro Tools, every student has unlimited access to the studios including SSL Duality Delta, Audient 48 Channel, TLAudio and Oram mixing desks, fully equipped Mac workstations with Push, Maschine, Komplete Kontrol, the latest Pioneer and Roland kit and much more. Point Blank scored 100% for overall student satisfaction in the most recent National Students Survey (NSS). We are the only music production school to achieve this result.
