            <h2>
             Social Responsibility – Occupational Safety and The Environment
            </h2>
           </div>
           <!-- .et_pb_text -->
           <div class="et_pb_text et_pb_bg_layout_light et_pb_text_align_left">
            <p>
             <img alt="ScreenHunter_49 Jan. 16 10.46" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-924" height="300" src="http://www.acocorp.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/ScreenHunter_49-Jan.-16-10.46-207x300.jpg" width="207"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              Our Commitment
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             ACO Corp understands the fact that there is nothing so much important in the industrial world than protecting the global environment and respecting the rights of workers, communities and indigenous people around the world.
            </p>
            <p>
             In the Forestry &amp; Wood industries, it is more important to maintain sustainable forestry practices.
            </p>
            <p>
             ACO CORPORATION is not left behind in this direction and is committing to sustainable and responsible supply practices in its commercial operations. We wish to achieve this objective by providing wood products that our customers can verify their origins.  Clients will analyze their sourcing, then guaranteeing that the lumber has not been harvested –
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              <em>
               1) Illegally or without proper authorization;
               <br/>
              </em>
             </strong>
             <strong>
              <em>
               2) In violation of traditional and civil right;
               <br/>
              </em>
             </strong>
             <strong>
              <em>
               3) In areas where management activities threaten high conservation values;
               <br/>
              </em>
             </strong>
             <strong>
              <em>
               4) In areas being converted to plantation or non-forest use;
               <br/>
              </em>
             </strong>
             <strong>
              <em>
               5) In areas where genetically modified trees are planted.
              </em>
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             It is in this respect ACO Corp has secured an FSC© COC certification on November 2015 from BM TRADA (FSC-C127716).
            </p>
            <p>
             This commitment covers lumbers, and wood products originated from FSC© Certified Forestry Organizations and Wood Products Manufacturers.
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
