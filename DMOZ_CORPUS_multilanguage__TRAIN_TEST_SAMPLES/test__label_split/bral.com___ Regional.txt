       <h1>
        Blick Rothenberg is a long-standing specialist outsourced accounting provider with a proven track record of supporting multinational clients with subsidiaries throughout the world.
       </h1>
       <p>
        Our solutions are designed to support companies throughout their entire business lifecycle - from start-ups and overseas growth initiatives to global expansion and setting up shared service centres around the world. Services are provided by a UK-based multi-lingual team, collectively fluent in over 20 languages, working in a single, secure environment with an independent SOC1 accreditation. Our operating model is built on a customer-centric, client-first culture leveraging leading technology such as SAP Business One, Netsuite and Concur to provide seamless integrated accounting services as an extension of our clients' business.

       </p>
