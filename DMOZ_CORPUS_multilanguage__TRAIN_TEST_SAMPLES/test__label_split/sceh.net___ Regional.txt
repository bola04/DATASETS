Yet, it is just poverty that ensures that millions remain deprived of the beauty of sight and hearing that we take for granted. Each year, with the help of contributions from across the globe from individuals and organizations alike, we manage to make a difference in the lives of a few thousand, young and old . Numbers that could easily go up if we had access to more resources. We invite you to read the stories of some of the lives that we have had the honour of impacting.
