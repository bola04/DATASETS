        <h3>
         <span class="mw-headline" id="Managed_and_Administered_by_the_SA_Historical_.26_Philatelic_Association">
          <b>
           Managed and Administered by the SA Historical &amp; Philatelic Association
          </b>
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <b>
          Overview:
         </b>
        </p>
        <p align="justify">
         SAWiki is an on-line encyclopedia covering all aspects of The Salvation Army. Not only can you find a wealth of information relating to the Salvation Army but you can also contribute your own as well. Select a category from the index below to get started.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         If you wish to edit pages and/or make new pages you will need to sign up for a free account. You can do this by clicking the create account link in the top right of this page. You can find a list of wanted pages by clicking
         <b>
          <a href="/index.php/Special:WantedPages" title="Special:WantedPages">
           here
          </a>
         </b>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         If you have any problems using this site please visit our help pages. Remember, if you're at all nervous about editing the SAWiki you can test your submissions out in the
         <a href="/index.php/Sawiki:Sandbox" title="Sawiki:Sandbox">
          sandbox
         </a>
         first before editing or creating pages.
        </p>
        <hr/>

        </p>
