     <p>
      Ashfield House is an award winning B&amp;B - Fáilte Ireland
   Award and Family Homes of Ireland Award  -
   located just minutes from the picturesque village of Cong. Ashfield
   House is snuggled between two of the world's great trout fisheries,
   Lough Corrib and Lough Mask with the world-renowned Ashford Castle
   close by.
     </p>
     <p align="center">
      <img alt="Cong Bed &amp; Breakfast County Mayo Ireland" border="0" class="photo_border" height="338" src="http://www.congaccommodation.com/cong_county_mayo/cong_county_mayo.jpg" width="450"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      Cong is also known as "Quiet Man Country" after the making of the
 classic film "The Quiet Man" starring John Wayne and Maureen
 O'Hara.
     </p>
     <p>
      Enjoy beautiful scenic walks and traditional Irish Pubs where
 music and heritage is a way of life.
     </p>
