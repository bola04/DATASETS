                 <h3>
                  <i class="icon-tiny fa-check accent-color">
                  </i>
                  <a href="http://osdc.ca/construction-surveying/">
                   CONSTRUCTION SURVEYING
                  </a>
                 </h3>
                 <p style="text-align: justify;">
                  Construction Surveying includes the assessment of project sites in order to survey existing structures, topography and infrastructure. The project is plotted out in accordance with the survey and new buildings are marked out as a guide for construction crews.
                 </p>
                </div>
               </div>
               <div class="divider" style="height: 35px;">
               </div>
               <div class="wpb_text_column wpb_content_element ">
                <div class="wpb_wrapper">
                 <h3>
                  <i class="icon-tiny fa-check accent-color">
                  </i>
                  <a href="http://osdc.ca/as-built-construction-surveying/">
                   AS BUILT SURVEYING
                  </a>
                 </h3>
                 <p style="text-align: justify;">
                  These surveys are helpful in assessing the progress of a job to ensure that it is being completed as drafted, and are often required by governmental authorities to show conformity with local zoning and building requirements.
                 </p>
                </div>
               </div>
               <div class="divider" style="height: 35px;">
               </div>
               <div class="wpb_text_column wpb_content_element ">

                  <a href="http://osdc.ca/topographic-surveying/">
                   TOPOGRAPHIC SURVEYING
                  </a>
                 </h3>
                 <p style="text-align: justify;">
                  Topographic Surveying is conducted by measuring the contours of the ground and assessing the existing forestry, plant life, infrastructure and buildings for potential build sites. Elevations and horizontal distances are also measured.
                 </p>

                  </i>
                  <a href="http://osdc.ca/volumetric-surveying/">
                   VOLUMETRIC SURVEYING
                  </a>
                 </h3>
                 <p style="text-align: justify;">
                  Volumetric surveying is a type of topographical survey that is used for several applications. Measurements are taken at ground level from which two dimensional or three dimensional maps and models can be created.
                 </p>
                </div>
               </div>

                  </a>
                 </h3>
                 <p>
