       <h1>
        Howell's Greenhouse
       </h1>
       <div class=" afloatright">
        <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube-nocookie.com/embed/tu07_SU79Kc?rel=0&amp;showinfo=0?ecver=1" width="560">
        </iframe>
       </div>
       <p>
        Here at Howell's Greenhouse we take pride in growing quality annuals, perennials, succulents, and farm fresh vegetables in a variety of species and colors grown and nurtured from bare root, plugs and cuttings.
        <br/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Our talented staff specializes in planting combination hanging baskets and patio containers. Let them help you create your masterpiece or design one for you.
       </p>
       <h2>
        Howell's Gift Barn
        <br/>
       </h2>
       <p>
        Inside our 1910 barn is our eclectic gift shop filled with unique home décor, gift ideas, garden accents and our large selection of dried floral arrangements and wreaths. Weather you are sentimental or quirky, traditional or farmhouse chic-you'll find it here!
        <br/>
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         Milkhouse candles- Got them!
         <br/>
        </li>
        <li>
         Kinetic Spinners- Yep them too!
         <br/>
        </li>
        <li>
         Garden accessories &amp; tools- You bet ya!
         <br/>
        </li>
        <li>
         Colorful pottery &amp; containers- Check and check!
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        As the seasons change so does our shop. The gift barn is open year round 10am- 5pm 7 days a week- excluding major holidays.
       </p>
       <p>
        New things are arriving all the time so check back frequently!
       </p>
       <h1 class="clear">
        Howell's Dried Floral
        <br/>
       </h1>
       <p>
        <img class="afloatleft" height="500" src="_images/FlowerFields.jpg" width="500"/>
        A short drive from Des Moines, West Des Moines, Norwalk or Winterset brings you right here to Howell's Dried Floral. You will find 5 gently rolling acres of vibrantly colored flowers nurtured until there prime and ready to be harvested, hung and dried.
       </p>
       <p>
        Families return again and again to visit the upstairs of our 1910 barn to see the flowers hanging in 25 foot cascades from the rafters and to enjoy our scenic surroundings.
        <br/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Downstairs in the gift shop, you will be fascinated with the huge selection of our dried floral arrangements, wreaths, swags and nosegays.
        <br/>
       </p>
