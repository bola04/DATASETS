        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          With a varied portfolio  including conversions such as water-mills, barns, longeres, house and apartment  renovations and new build homes, we offer an individual, open and personal  design service and are always happy to chat informally about your ideas.
         </p>
         <p>
          Based in Dinan but covering all of Brittany and much  of Normandy we offer a range of services including:
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           Pre-purchase feasibility studies
          </li>
          <li>
           Development of project brief
          </li>
          <li>
           Design, concept, planning permit applications
          </li>
          <li>
           Schedules of works
          </li>
          <li>
           Liaising with local authorities, Architectes des bâtiment de France (listed buildings, historic sites)
          </li>
          <li>
           Arrangement of soil surveys
          </li>
         </ul>
         <blockquote style="width: 250px; float:right;">
          <p>
           Thank you once again for your assistance and your professional  expertise in making what we thought would be a very complicated process  straightforward for us.   P &amp; J Finch.
          </p>
         </blockquote>

          Andrew Allen Architects – Brittany
         </h2>
