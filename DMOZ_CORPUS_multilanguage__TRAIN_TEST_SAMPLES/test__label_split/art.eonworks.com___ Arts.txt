        Welcome to the official website of Dawid Michalczyk - a freelance illustrator and an artist.
 I specialize in creating artwork for video games, especially concept art, but I also do
 illustration for CD and book covers. I use digital and traditional art media.
        <p>
         Inside you will find examples of my professional work, illustration, and most of my personal artwork which is available as
 prints and for licensing purposes.

         In the
         <a href="/gallery/sci-fi/sci-fi_gallery_1.html">
          Sci-Fi art
         </a>
         gallery there are illustrations of future cities,
 spaceships, vehicles, and alien worlds. My
         <a href="/gallery/fantasy/fantasy_gallery_1.html">
          Fantasy art
         </a>
         gallery is at the moment populated by warriors, a skull, and a mystic illustration.
 The
         <a href="/gallery/surreal/surreal_gallery_1.html">
          Surreal art
         </a>
         gallery is full of
 colorful and thought provoking imagery with many enigmatic landscapes. You will also find quite a
 bit of
         <a href="/gallery/space/space_gallery_1.html">
          Space art
         </a>
         here - planets, nebulae,
 galaxies and other space art matter. I also do colorful
         <a href="/gallery/abstract/abstract_gallery_1.html">
          Abstract art
         </a>
         . Finally there are the
         <a href="/gallery/gallery_scifi_concept.html">
          SciFi concept art
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="/gallery/texture/texture_gallery_1.html">
          Textures
         </a>
         and
         <a href="/gallery/misc/misc_gallery_1.html">
          Misc
         </a>
         galleries which contain
 examples of my work done for video games and other projects.
