        <h1 class="spice">
         Welcome to
         <strong>
          Ludlow Food Centre
         </strong>
        </h1>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Ludlow Food Centre
         </strong>
         is a unique food shopping experience where farming, food production and retailing come together to create a very special environment. We are part of the Earl of Plymouth's
         <strong>
          Oakly Park Estate
         </strong>
         which extends to approximately 8,000 acres of Shropshire countryside. All our beef, lamb and Gloucester Old Spot pork comes from the estate along with a selection of game and vegetables.
        </p>
        <p>
         We are passionate about local food, with 80% of the food we sell coming from
         <strong>
          Shropshire
         </strong>
         and its surrounding counties. Ludlow Food Centre is designed to not only sell food but to produce it and
         <strong>
          half of our products are made on the premises
         </strong>
         in kitchen units that surround the food hall. These are visible through glass windows that allow you to see our artisan producers making your food by hand.
        </p>
        <p>
         Above all our food is fresh, local, seasonal and handmade.
        </p>
