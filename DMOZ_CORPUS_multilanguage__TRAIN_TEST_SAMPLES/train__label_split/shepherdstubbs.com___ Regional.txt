          </h2>
          <p>
           Whether you are a candidate seeking the right opportunity, or a client company seeking the ideal candidate, Shepherd Stubbs Recruitment Agency’s philosophy “it’s all about the right people” puts the right people in the right jobs and ensures it delivers a quality recruitment agency service.
          </p>
          <p>
           Shepherd Stubbs are the leading recruitment agency in Bedford and St Neots; we specialise in helping clients across a range of sectors to find the perfect people to join their businesses, and assist talented candidates in their job search. We take great pride in matching the right people with the right jobs, so whether your company needs a fantastic new team member to enhance the business, or you are looking for the next opportunity to progress your career, our recruitment specialists are here to help.
          </p>
          <p>
           If you want to choose from some of the best jobs in Bedford, St Neots, Milton Keynes, Luton, Huntingdon, Biggleswade and the surrounding areas, you have come to the right place.
