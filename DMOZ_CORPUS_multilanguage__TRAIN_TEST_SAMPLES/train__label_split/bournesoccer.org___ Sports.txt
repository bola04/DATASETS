          <h1 class="entry-title">
           Fall Soccer Signups are now open!
          </h1>
         </header>
         <div class="entry-content">
          <p>
           <strong>
            Fall Soccer
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           The fall soccer season is our primary program and provides instruction for coaches and players.
          </p>
          <p>
           The fall season is open to all Bourne residents aged 4 to 13. We also allow players from other towns who attend school in Bourne. This exception does not apply to Spring Soccer.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Age Groups:
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Pre-K/K (4 &amp; 5 years old; players must be 4 years of age as of August 1st)
           <br/>
           1
           <sup>
            st
           </sup>
           and 2
           <sup>
            nd
           </sup>
           Grade Girls and Boys (Co-ed teams)
           <br/>
           3
           <sup>
            rd
           </sup>
           and 4
           <sup>
            th
           </sup>
           Grade Girls
           <br/>
           3
           <sup>
            rd
           </sup>
           and 4
           <sup>
            th
           </sup>
           Grade Boys
           <br/>
           5
           <sup>
            th
           </sup>
           and 6
           <sup>
            th
           </sup>
           Grade Girls
           <br/>
           5
           <sup>
            th
           </sup>
           and 6
           <sup>
            th
           </sup>
           Grade Boys
           <br/>
           7
           <sup>
            th
           </sup>
           and 8
           <sup>
            th
           </sup>
           Grade Girls and Boys (Co-ed teams)
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Registration:
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Click on
           <a href="http://bournesoccer.org/?page_id=79">
            registration instructions
           </a>
           to get to the registration page.  Please register for the grade your child will be in Fall 2018.
          </p>
          <p>
           Notification of Team Assignments, Coaches, etc. is tentatively set to take place in late August. You will probably not hear from us before then about team assignments. Coaches will be contacted earlier in the summer as we put the teams together. Practices will start shortly after the coaches contact their teams, and the first game will probably take place the Saturday after Labor Day.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Cost:
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           The fee is:
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Player registration fees are; first child $60.00, $50.00 for each additional child.
            <br/>
            • Register before June 15 2018 and receive a $5.00 discount.
            <br/>
            • Registrations received after August 15 are subject to a $10.00 late fee, with the exception of the Pre-K/K league.  PLEASE SIGN UP BY AUGUST 15 TO HELP US DETERMINE COACH AND EQUIPMENT NEEDS!
            <br/>
            • Registration will close August 31st. Please contact our registrar at &lt;bournesoccer@gmail.com&gt; after this date. Note: Player may miss first practice or game.
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Schedule:
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Games are on Saturdays, starting after Labor Day.  We do not play Columbus Day Weekend
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Equipment Needed:
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Included in the registration fee are your player’s uniform; T-shirt and soccer socks.
           <br/>
           You will need to provide plain elastic- or drawstring-waist shorts, shin guards, and cleats (optional for the Under-6 age group). Please be sure to use soccer, not baseball, cleats.
          </p>
          <p>
           To Contact Registrar:
          </p>
          <p>
           Email: &lt;bournesoccer@gmail.com&gt;
          </p>
          <p>
           US Mail: PO Box 542 Cataumet, MA 02534
          </p>
