              <a href="http://theraftrestaurant.com/walvis-bay/walvis-bay-lagoon/" target="_blank">
               Walvis Bay lagoon
              </a>
              life happen in front of you, while enjoying the legendary service and hospitality of
              <span class="bold_text">
               The Raft Restaurant
              </span>
              .
             </p>
             <h2 class="" style="text-align: center;">
              A Great Location in Walvis Bay Lagoon
             </h2>
             <p>

              <span class="bold_text">
               Walvis Bay
              </span>
              jetty, lofty ceilings, panoramic views over the lagoon, cosy log,burning fires in winter, a deck with roll up up windows for the warm summer months, friendly efficient staff, excellent food and a very special ambience, it is considered by many to be the best restaurant on the coast of
              <span class="bold_text">
               Namibia
              </span>
              .
