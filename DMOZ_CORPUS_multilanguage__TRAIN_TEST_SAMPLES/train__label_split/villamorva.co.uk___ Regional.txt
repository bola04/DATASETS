      A Welcoming Cornish B&amp;B in St Ives
      <br/>
      <br/>
     </h1>
     <p>
      Villamorva is a charming, recently renovated Victorian house converted into a B&amp;B, situated very conveniently within a short walk to the several golden sandy beaches of St. Ives.  The sun shines on the front garden for most of the day, a lovely place to relax before returning to one of the luxury bedrooms, each with exceedingly comfortable king size beds and tranquil stylish décor.
     </p>
     <p>
      The St. Ives Theatre, Barbara Hepworth Museum and Tate Gallery are just a comfortable walk away.  Within minutes visit the stylish boutiques, art galleries, harbours and peaceful gardens of this most famous of Cornish towns. Villamorva bed &amp; breakfast is so close to all the amenities that to just pop back for a shower or change of clothes takes no time at all.
     </p>
     <p>
      Together with a  locally sourced hearty Cornish breakfast, Villamorva B&amp;B will make your stay in St Ives..perfect.
