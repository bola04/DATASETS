         You will never find a home away from home that is a as clean and charming as this Bed and Breakfast in Berlin, Ohio! Cleanliness and comfort are what we are about! In the heart of Amish Country on the East end of Berlin, nestled under big oak trees, you can park your car and let the hustle and bustle of every day living vanish. Enjoy lodging in Amish Country only a stones throw away from two wonderful shops that are owned by your hosts Steve and Ruth Schlabach.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          You can enjoy a special discount at both of these shops while staying at the Oaks Inn bed and breakfast.
         </strong>
         If not everyone is into shopping, the comfort and serenity of the Oaks Inn can be enjoyed. Husbands love this option! Relax on our swings, gazebo or if staying in the lofted suite, enjoy the comfort of the screened in porch!
        </p>
        <p>
         You can
         <strong>
          walk the whole town of Berlin, Ohio,
         </strong>
         to find many interesting shops, or eat at the famous hole-in-the-wall restaurant--Boyd and Wurthmans. So many people come from far away to enjoy the mouth-watering, down-home Amish cooking at unbelievable prices!
        </p>
        <p>
         From Thursday thru Saturday, visit the famous Holmes County Flea Market, which is 1/4 mile from the Oaks Inn bed and breakfast. Enjoy an Auntie Anne's famous pretzel while you are there. (A little secret that others have discovered is that if you stay on a Saturday night, you will
         <strong>
          awaken Sunday morning to the clip-clop of the horse and buggies going to Amish church
         </strong>
         .) If you wish to attend services on Sunday, you can walk to a Baptist or Mennonite church if you like.
        </p>
        <p>
         Your hosts, Steve &amp; Ruth Schlabach
