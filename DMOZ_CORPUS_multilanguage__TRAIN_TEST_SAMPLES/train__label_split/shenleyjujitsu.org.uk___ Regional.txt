           <h2>
            <a href="/home" title="Welcome to Shenley Jujitsu Club">
             Welcome to Shenley Jujitsu Club
            </a>
           </h2>
           <div class="content clear-block">
            <div class="img_container" style="text-align:center">
             <img src="/sites/default/files/goshinTraining2004b.jpg" title="goshinTraining2004b.jpg"/>
            </div>
            <p>
             We teach self-defence using the jujitsu martial art. This ancient system of unarmed combat was developed in Japan and used by the Samurai - it is a very effective means of defending yourself or others.
            </p>
            <p>
             We train at
             <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?f=d&amp;hl=en&amp;geocode=8917176743775989430,52.025750,-0.788470&amp;saddr=&amp;daddr=&amp;mra=pr&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;t=h&amp;ll=52.025987,-0.788398&amp;spn=0.007922,0.012875&amp;z=15&amp;source=embed" target="_blank">
              Shenley Leisure Centre,
             </a>
             Shenley Church End in Milton Keynes.
            </p>
            <p>
             Training sessions are on Fridays in the Gym
            </p>
            <ul>
             <li>
              Juniors (ages 8 - 16) -  19:00 to 20:30
             </li>
             <li>
              Seniors (over 16) -  20:30 to 22:00
             </li>
            </ul>
            <p>
             If you are interested in joining the club or want to find out more about jujitsu feel free to
             <a href="mailto:sensei@shenleyjujitsu.org.uk">
              e-mail us
             </a>
             or just come along to a session. You'll find photos and videos on the website that might help you decide if jujitsu is for you.
            </p>
            <!-- display:block to show, display:none to hide -->
            <!-- display:block to show, display:none to hide -->
            <div style="display:none; border: solid green 2px; padding: 1em; font-weight:bold">
             <h2>
              Important title
             </h2>
             <p>
              Important info
             </p>
            </div>
            <p>
             We teach defence against strikes, grabs and weapons such as sticks and knives. Jujitsu provides a wide range of techniques from pure escape, to controlling your attacker to the ground, to inflicting serious temporary or permanent harm when you are in fear of your life.
            </p>
            <p>
             Students start at red belt and move up through the belts by grading regularly. Getting to black belt is not the point of learning a martial art, it is more about the journey of learning control and technique.
            </p>
            <p>
             All the instructors have First Aid certificates and extended Criminal Records checks for working with children.
            </p>
