          <h2>
           June 3 @ 7:30 am – 4:30 pm
          </h2>
          <p>
           <img alt="" class="wp-image-429 alignright" height="133" src="http://sheltonjaycees.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/SJC_AnC_1-300x225.jpg" width="198"/>
           <br/>
           On behalf of the Shelton Jaycees alumni group, the Geese &amp; Bear club, we’re excited to invite you to our
           <strong>
            54th Annual Arts &amp; Crafts Fair on Sunday, June 3rd 2018
           </strong>
           .
           <br/>
           This year’s rain date is on June 10th.
          </p>
          <p>
           Always on the Huntington Green, this all-volunteer run event has grown into an established community tradition, kicking off the summer season in Fairfield County.
          </p>
          <p>
           We’re serious about diversity being the hallmark of our fair and we limit the number of exhibitors in each craft category to 3. Our fair is a juried event and cash prizes are awarded. Items must be your own hand crafted pieces or original fine artwork pieces.
          </p>

           </a>
          </h1>

          <p>
           <img alt="ArtsandCraftsFairImage" class="alignright" height="166" src="http://i387.photobucket.com/albums/oo318/sheltonjaycees/linked%20to%20web%20site/SN851386-1.jpg" title="SJC_ArtsandCraftsFair" width="221"/>
           On behalf of the Shelton Jaycees alumni group, the Geese &amp; Bear club, we’re excited to invite you to our
           <strong>
            53rd Annual Arts &amp; Crafts Fair on Sunday, June 4th 2017.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           This year’s rain date is on June 11th.
          </p>
          <p>
           Always on the Huntington Green, this all-volunteer run event has grown into an established community tradition kicking off the summer season in Fairfield County.
          </p>
          <p>
           We’re serious about diversity being the hallmark of our fair and we limit the number of exhibitors in each craft category to 3. Our fair is a juried event and cash prizes are awarded.  Items must be your own hand crafted pieces or original artwork pieces.
          </p>
          <p>
           Please
           <a href="http://sheltonjaycees.org/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/2017_ArtsandCraftsFair_ApplicationLetter.pdf" target="_blank" title="2017_artsandcraftsfair_applicationletter">
            <strong>
             download and print our application
            </strong>
           </a>
           and return it to us along with a description of your work and some photographs. We need to see your work, your work in progress and your work space.  If you have exhibited with us before, you don’t need to enclose the photographs.
          </p>
          <p>
           Our best source of new participants has always been word of mouth referrals.  We feel our tradition of having a high quality diverse group of exhibitors is so important we’re offering past exhibitors a $5 registration rebate if they refer a new (non-jewelry) exhibitor.  If the “New” referral is accepted, and is either brand new to our fair or has not exhibited at our fair in over three years, both the past exhibitor and the new referral will receive a $5 registration rebate on the morning of the event. Why must it be a new non-jewelry exhibitor, you ask? The answer is because we routinely have to deny dozens of jewelry exhibitors each year. Our jewelry categories just fill up too fast!
          </p>
          <p>
           When referring a new exhibitor you can either photocopy an application, or send them to this web site where they can download their own application .  Or you can just have them give us a phone call so we can mail them a paper application.  A referral section is on the application.
          </p>
          <p>
           This year we’re offering our
           <em>
            Arts &amp; Crafts Exhibitors
           </em>
           an
           <strong>
            “Early Bird Rate”
           </strong>
           of
           <strong>
            $59
           </strong>
           when your application is postmarked by
           <strong>
            April 15th, 2017
           </strong>
           .  To qualify for
           <strong>
            the regular pre-registered price
           </strong>
           of
           <strong>
            $74
           </strong>
           , your application must be postmarked no later than
           <strong>
            May 27th, 2017
           </strong>
           .  All
           <em>
            Food Vendors
           </em>
           must pre-register by May 27th and the fee remains constant at $79.  Sending your application in early will help ensure your acceptance.
          </p>
          <p>
           Please include your check or money order with your application.
          </p>
          <p>
           Any photos we receive will be returned to you within 10 days along with a confirmation letter.  Even if you are not able to participate in 2017, please complete the application and return it to us so we can keep you on our mailing list.
          </p>
          <p>
           Please send your application to:
           <br/>
           <strong>
            Arts and Crafts Fair to benefit the Shelton Jaycees
           </strong>
           <br/>
           <strong>
            P.O. Box 2334, Shelton, CT  06484
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           The application together with our welcome letter and additional details are available to download and print
           <strong>

             HERE

           </strong>
           If you have any questions, just give either of us a call before 9 pm. If you can’t reach us right away, you can also call our exhibitor coordinator, Jim Nissen, at (203) 521-2498.
          </p>
          <p>
           We’re looking forward to another beautiful and profitable day for everyone!

          <p>
           Once again, thanks to all of our exhibitors, all of the volunteers who run the event and the Shelton community, the 52nd Annual Arts &amp; Crafts Fair to benefit the Shelton Jaycees was an incredible success!
          </p>
          <p>
           Always the First Sunday AFTER the First Friday &amp; Saturday in June, and always on the Huntington Green. Our 2017 event will be on June 4th with a rain date on June 11th. Click on the Arts &amp; Crafts Fair tab on the Shelton Jaycees home page for all the details.
          </p>
          <p>
           The quality &amp; diversity of our 2016 exhibitors was second to none. The fair is a juried event with cash prizes for Best in Arts &amp; Best in Crafts. We limit exhibitors to a maximum of three in each category. In 2016, as in previous years, we had to reject several applications in order to create that perfect mix.
          </p>
          <p>
           Special thanks go out to the Shelton High School Robotics Club and their supporters. They were extremely helpful again this year aiding exhibitors with set up and take down. A portion of the proceeds was donated to them for all their heavy lifting!
          </p>
          <p>
           The 2016 Arts &amp; Crafts Fair was chaired by past member Wendy Holden. Additional volunteers included Kate Jager, Val Sabetta, Nikole Lindley, Michael Sheehan, Mark Holden and Jim Nissen.
          </p>

          <h1 class="entry-title">
           <a href="http://sheltonjaycees.org/2016/02/52nd-annual-arts-crafts-fair/" rel="bookmark">
            52nd Annual Arts &amp; Crafts Fair to benefit the Shelton Jaycees  POSTPONED TO RAIN DATE JUNE 12th!
           </a>
          </h1>
          <div class="entry-meta">
           <span class="entry-date">
            <a href="http://sheltonjaycees.org/2016/02/52nd-annual-arts-crafts-fair/" rel="bookmark">
             <time class="entry-date" datetime="2016-02-11T13:44:47+00:00">
              February 11, 2016
             </time>
            </a>
           </span>
           <span class="byline">
            <span class="author vcard">
             <a class="url fn n" href="http://sheltonjaycees.org/author/ed/" rel="author">
              Ed
             </a>
            </span>
           </span>
           <span class="comments-link">
            <a href="http://sheltonjaycees.org/2016/02/52nd-annual-arts-crafts-fair/#comments">
             1 Comment
            </a>
           </span>
          </div>
          <!-- .entry-meta -->
         </header>
         <!-- .entry-header -->
         <div class="entry-content">
          <p>
           <img alt="ArtsandCraftsFairImage" class="alignright" height="166" src="http://i387.photobucket.com/albums/oo318/sheltonjaycees/linked%20to%20web%20site/SN851386-1.jpg" title="SJC_ArtsandCraftsFair" width="221"/>
           <strong>
            6/4 UPDATE: JUNE 5TH ARTS &amp; CRAFT FAIR POSTPONED TO RAIN DATE JUNE 12TH. SAME TIME SAME LOCATION!
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           On behalf of the Shelton Jaycees alumni group, the Geese &amp; Bear club, I’m excited to invite you to our 52nd Annual Arts &amp; Crafts Fair on Sunday June 5th, 2016.
           <br/>
           This year’s rain date is on June 12th
          </p>
          <p>
           Always on the Huntington Green, this all-volunteer run event has grown into an established community tradition kicking off the summer season in Fairfield County.
          </p>
          <p>
           Last year’s co-chairs Kate Jager &amp; Jim Nissen are going to be traveling for work on our scheduled date. They’ve recruited me to take over for them and I couldn’t be more excited! 12 years ago I was the one who convinced Kate to chair this project her first time and she’s run it almost every year since! My friend Jim has volunteered at this event along with me for over twenty years. My very first time chairing this event was in 1992 when we set our all time record for most exhibitors. It’s been a long time since I’ve been the one in charge but Kate &amp; Jim are going to be very active helping our committee of Jaycee alumni do some of the advance work.
          </p>
          <p>
           This year’s online show advertising will increase dramatically. Print promotion will be in all of the major area newspapers as well as on radio and community event listings. Web promotion will include a dedicated Arts and Crafts Fair page on our main website in addition to our regular Arts &amp; Crafts Fair
           <a href="https://www.facebook.com/SheltonJayceesAnnualArtsandCraftsFair" target="_blank" title="Annual Arts and Crafts Fair Facebook Page">
            Facebook
           </a>
           page!
          </p>
          <p>
           Special note to new exhibitors: We’re serious about diversity being the hallmark of our fair and we limit the number of exhibitors in each craft category to 3. Our fair is a juried event and cash prizes are awarded. Items must be your own hand crafted pieces or original artwork pieces.
          </p>
          <p>
           Please
           <a href="http://sheltonjaycees.org/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/2016_ArtsandCraftsFair_ApplicationLetter.pdf" title="2016ApplicationLetter">
            <strong>
             download and print our application
            </strong>
           </a>
           and return it to us along with a description of your work and some photographs. We need to see your work, your work in progress and your work space. If you have exhibited with us before, you don’t need to enclose the photographs.
          </p>
          <p>
           Our best source of new participants has always been word of mouth referrals. We feel our tradition of having a high quality diverse group of exhibitors is so important we’re offering past exhibitors a $5 registration rebate if they refer a new (non-jewelry) exhibitor. If the “New” referral is accepted, and is either brand new to our fair or has not exhibited at our fair in over three years, both the past exhibitor and the new referral will receive a $5 registration rebate on the morning of the event. Why must it be a new non-jewelry exhibitor you ask? The answer is because we routinely have to deny dozens upon dozens of jewelry exhibitors each year. Our jewelry categories just fill up too fast!
          </p>
          <p>
           When referring a new exhibitor you can either photocopy an application, or send them to this web site where they can download their own application . Or you can just have them give us a phone call so we can mail them a paper application. A referral section is on the application.
          </p>
          <p>
           This year we’re offering our
           <em>
            Arts &amp; Crafts Exhibitors
           </em>
           an
           <strong>
            “Early Bird Rate”
           </strong>
           of
           <strong>
            $69
           </strong>
           when your application is postmarked by
           <strong>
            April 15th, 2015
           </strong>
           . To qualify for
           <strong>
            the regular pre-registered price
           </strong>
           of
           <strong>
            $79
           </strong>
           , your application must be postmarked no later than
           <strong>
            May 25th, 2016
           </strong>
           . All
           <em>
            Food Vendors
           </em>
           must pre-register by May 25th and the fee remains constant at $89. Sending your application in early will help ensure your acceptance.
          </p>
          <p>
           Please include your check or money order with your application.
          </p>
          <p>
           Any photos we receive will be returned to you within 10 days along with a confirmation letter. Even if you are not able to participate in 2016, please complete the application and return it to us so we can keep you on our mailing list.
          </p>
          <p>
           Please send your application to:
           <br/>
           <strong>
            Arts and Crafts Fair to benefit the Shelton Jaycees
           </strong>
           <br/>
           <strong>
            P.O. Box 2334, Shelton, CT 06484
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           The application together with our welcome letter and additional details are available to download and print
           <strong>
            <a href="http://sheltonjaycees.org/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/2016_ArtsandCraftsFair_ApplicationLetter.pdf" title="2016ApplicationLetter">
             HERE
            </a>
            .
           </strong>
           If you have any questions, just give me a call any time before 9 pm. If you can’t reach me right away, you can also call Kate at (203) 623-6698 or Jim at (203) 521-2498.
          </p>
          <p>
           We’re looking forward to another beautiful and profitable day for everyone!
          </p>
          <p>
           Sincerely,
          </p>
          <p>
           Wendy Holden,
           <br/>
           2016 Arts &amp; Crafts Chair
           <br/>
           (203) 913-3147
          </p>
          <p>
           ——————————————————————————————
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            <img alt="ArtsandCraftsFairImage2" class="alignleft" height="119" src="http://i387.photobucket.com/albums/oo318/sheltonjaycees/linked%20to%20web%20site/SN851390-1.jpg" title="SJC_ArtsandCraftsFair2" width="159"/>
            Directions to the Huntington Green
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            From Route 8 South Bound
           </strong>
           <br/>
           Get off exit 14.
           <br/>
           End of ramp, take left onto Howe Ave. (Route 110)
           <br/>
           Take first Right onto Kneen St.
           <br/>
           Next light take right onto Coram Ave.
           <br/>
           Follow Coram Ave. to end. (Plumb Library)
           <br/>
           Take left at stop Sign
           <br/>
           Follow Route 108, go 3.2 miles to Huntington Center. The Huntington Green will be on your left.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            From Route 8 North Bound
           </strong>
           <br/>
           Get off exit 11.
           <br/>
           End of ramp, take left.
           <br/>
           Go straight. (Huntington Rd. becomes Huntington St.) 3.2 miles.
           <br/>
           At Huntington Center, road will divide by a fire house.
           <br/>
           After the fire house, stay in the left lane and the Huntington Green will be on your left.
          </p>
          <p>
           If you prefer to use mapquest.com or a GPS device, look up the intersection of Huntington Street &amp; Shelton Avenue, Shelton CT 06484.
          </p>
          <p>
           ——————————————————————-
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            <img alt="ArtsandCraftsFairImage3" class="alignright" height="166" src="http://i387.photobucket.com/albums/oo318/sheltonjaycees/linked%20to%20web%20site/SN851395-1.jpg" title="SJC_ArtsandCraftsFair3" width="221"/>
            Other information:
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           First Prize for Arts is $100.
           <br/>
           First Prize for Crafts is $75.
          </p>
          <p>
           Once again we will have
           <strong>
            additional volunteers available to help
           </strong>
           exhibitors set up and take down, deliver lunch orders, and watch booths during bathroom breaks. We will have
           <strong>
            FREE coffee &amp; FREE pastries
           </strong>
           available beginning at 8:00am! Exhibitors are entitled to two free cups of coffee and two free pastries per space. If you have special needs please call us as soon as possible so we can help you with them. Call Kate at (203) 623-6698 or Jim at (203) 521-2498
          </p>
          <p>
           Volunteers will arrive at the fair starting at 7:00 am. The registration check in will be at the gazebo starting at 7:15 am. The fair runs from 10 am – 4 pm.
           <strong>
            Please do not arrive or start to set up before 7:00 am.
           </strong>
           The Shelton Police are pretty sensitive about this!
          </p>
          <p>
           Spaces are 12′ x 12′ square and are first come – first serve. We set the Green up in a ‘race track’ oval.
           <strong>
            Every space gets the same traffic.
           </strong>
           The food area &amp; kid’s craft areas will be marked off ahead of time. Please do not set up there. You will be required to move.
          </p>
          <p>
           The town will not permit any cars on the green itself but you may park along side the Green to unload. After unloading, you are required to immediately remove your vehicle from the perimeter of the green. Parking is available in the Shopping Plaza behind Common Bond supermarket.
          </p>
          <p>
           ###
          </p>
