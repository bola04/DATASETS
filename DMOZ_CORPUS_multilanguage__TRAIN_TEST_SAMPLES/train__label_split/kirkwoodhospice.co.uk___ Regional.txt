        <h1>
         Kirkwood Hospice
        </h1>
        <p class="introduction">
         We help to improve the lives of people with incurable conditions. Whether at the Hospice, in the home or out in the community, we make sure that those in our care have the opportunity to live the best quality of life possible and are able to make the most of the time they have left.
