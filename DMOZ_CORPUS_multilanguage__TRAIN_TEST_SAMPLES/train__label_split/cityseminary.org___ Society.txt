                  <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
                   <strong>
                    City Seminary of Sacramento is a school of theology
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                  at the graduate level which is committed to providing a biblical education to students in the Sacramento region. We provide a place where men are trained for the gospel ministry without having to relocate to another area. Seminary education should be accessible to those who are called by Jesus Christ to minister to his church. The seminary offers programs leading to the degrees of Associate of Theological Studies, Master of Theological Studies (2-year); Bachelor of Divinity and Master of Divinity (4-year); and, Certificates in Apologetics, Church History, New Testament, Old Testament, Systematic Theology, Greek, Hebrew and Pastoral Theology.
