       <p>

Our Group consists of seven brand leaders each experts in their niche field; Chemtech Waste, Chemtech Industrial, Blendcheck, Envirosol, Hazchem, Boxclever and Safeguard all here to share their specialist knowledge.

         the
        </strong>
        largest client support team in the hazardous waste industry with our experienced internal client services team operating from our state of the art Midlands Head Office and environmental facility. Backed by our field based Account Managers, we are perfectly positioned to discuss your waste needs either at your premises or over the phone.
       </p>

        With three fully permitted Environmental Management Facilities and probably the UK’s largest indoor treatment facility we can aggregate waste and pass on valuable cost savings to our clients.
       </p>

        Our extensive fleet of bespoke vehicles, means we are fully flexible to cater for the needs of all businesses from single drum through to large corporate multi sited more complex collections.
       </p>

        Our team all share our passion to transform waste back into the circular economy; driven to reduce materials away from landfill and providing our clients with a cost effective and environmentally compliant waste management solutions.
       </p>
