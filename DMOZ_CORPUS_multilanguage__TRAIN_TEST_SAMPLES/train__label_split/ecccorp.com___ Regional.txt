         <h1>
          Acquisitions
         </h1>
         <p>
          Epec is proud to have completed several acquisitions throughout North America, Europe, and Asia.
         </p>
         <p>
          The investments have enabled Epec to become a global leader, with the best people, facilities, technology, and culture of innovation.
         </p>
         <p>
          See our blog post on
          <a href="https://blog.epectec.com/how-to-minimize-risk-when-the-factory-manufacturing-your-pcbs-closes">
           how to minimize risk when the factory manufacturing your PCBs closes
          </a>
          for more information in regards to mergers and acquisitions in the circuit board industry.
         </p>
