    <section class="intro-panel">
     <h1 class="main-header">
      World Travel &amp; Tourism Council
     </h1>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
      The World Travel &amp; Tourism Council (WTTC) is the body which represents the Travel &amp; Tourism private sector globally. Our Members consist of the CEOs of the world's Travel &amp; Tourism companies and industry organisations engaging with the sector. Our priorities are:
      <strong>
       Security &amp; Travel Facilitation
      </strong>
      ,
      <strong>
       Crisis Preparedness &amp; Management
      </strong>
      , and
      <strong>
       Sustainable Growth
      </strong>
      . WTTC works to raise awareness of Travel &amp; Tourism as one of the world's largest economic sectors, supporting 313 million jobs and generating 10.4% of world GDP.
     </p>
    </section>
