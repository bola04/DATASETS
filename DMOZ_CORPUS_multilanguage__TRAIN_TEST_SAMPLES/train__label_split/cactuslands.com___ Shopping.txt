       <h1 class="style8">
        Welcome to Cactuslands...
       </h1>
       <p>
        Succulents are a very special group of plants that excel in colonizing 
 			arid and inhospitable environments. The cactus family is part of the 
 			succulent group and is unique to the Americas. Some people think of 
 			Cactus as "thorny" and look no further. Their beauty is in their robust 
 			nature, unusual branching, interesting spine structure and color, and 
 			the delicate vivid color of their flowers. Cacti have become the symbol 
 			of the "old west" and the southwest because of their rugged beauty and 
 			hardy nature.
       </p>
       <h2 class="style7">
        We offer plants and handcrafted decorative items 
 			that salute the wonders of this rugged spirit.
       </h2>
       <ul class="style2">
        <li>
         Our cactus and other succulent plants are grown from seed, offset 
 				or cutting. We do not sell field collected plants. Not all plants 
 				offered or pictured are from the cactus family; however, they are 
 				all from the succulent group. While native to various parts of the 
 				world, they exhibit similar qualities.
        </li>
        <li>
         Our leather magnets are handmade from quality leather and offer 
 				a selection of cactus, Native American, old west, and southwestern 
 				designs.
        </li>
        <li>
         Sandpaintings, Navajo Etchware, Horsehair Pottery, and hand 
 				carved Alabaster sculptures are great southwest accent pieces for 
 				your home.
        </li>
        <li>
         Our handcrafted Southwest picture frames, many with cactus rib inlay 
 				help tell the story of the North American West.
        </li>
        <li>
         Our wood sculptures have been hand carved from the wood of an 
 				"Ironwood" tree, it's name in Spanish is palo fierro (iron stick). 
 				It has a beautiful grain and as it's name implies, is very dense.
        </li>
       </ul>
