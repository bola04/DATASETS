       <h3 class="section-title">
        Key West Fine Art Photography &amp; Printing
       </h3>
       <p>
        Key West Fine Art Photography and Printing has been providing digital photographic services and large format prints for Keys artists and photographers since 2002. We are located in the new Stock Island Marina Village one mile from Key West. Our client list comprises over two hundred artists, photographers and galleries. Our experience, knowledge, and the fact that we are photographers and artists ourselves have allowed us to develop a unique relationship with the Florida Keys art community. We have made substantial investments in state-of-the-art equipment and software, specifically for photographing artwork.
       </p>
       <p>
        Our business philosophy is simple: to provide a high level of customer satisfaction. We do this by providing superior quality, personal attention and concern for every job that we take. We ask questions to ensure our customers get the best product that meets their needs and budget. We are happy to educate our client base so that they become a more knowledgeable consumer. This credo is what sets us apart from our competition.
       </p>
       <p>
        Whether you are an experienced professional looking for quality output at competitive prices or completely new to large format digital work, we look forward to working with you.
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         <i>
          Ready to print your image?

         <a href="http://www.keywestfineartprinting.com/contact-us/">
          Contact us now.
         </a>
        </b>
       </p>

       We do several types of photography, both in the studio and on site. A major part of the photography business is art work, both two dimensional paintings and three dimensional sculpture. We use a specialized camera and lighting from Betterlight .

       Two other camera formats, Nikon35mm and Hasselblad 4cmx6cm, are used for product, food, jewelry etc. These images can be used for large format prints or downsized for webwork. We have recently added aerial photography to our list of services.
