                 <strong>
                  Historic Racing South Africa
                 </strong>
                 (
                 <a class="st_tag internal_tag" href="https://www.historicracing.co.za/tag/hrsa/" rel="tag" title="Posts tagged with HRSA">
                  HRSA
                 </a>
                 ) (formerly The Historic Racing Car Register of South Africa) was formed in 1973 with the aim of preserving and racing old cars from yesteryear.
                </p>
                <p>
                 Many of the original members are still to be found participating in races and supporting the club, leading the Committee to believe that the current Formulae of racing offered by the
                 <a class="st_tag internal_tag" href="https://www.historicracing.co.za/tag/hrsa/" rel="tag" title="Posts tagged with HRSA">
                  HRSA
                 </a>
                 , is popular and also attracts a good following of spectators.
                </p>
                <p>
                 The Club offers a number of different categories of racing:
                </p>
                <ul>
                 <li>
                  <strong>
                   <a href="/sports-gt/" title="Sports &amp; GT">
                    Sports &amp; GT
                   </a>
                  </strong>
                 </li>
                 <li>
                  <strong>
                   <a href="/historic-saloon-cars/" title="Historic Saloon Cars">
                    Historic Saloon Cars
                   </a>
                  </strong>
                 </li>
                 <li>
                  <strong>
                   <a href="/historic-production-cars/" title="Historic Production Cars">
                    Historic Production Cars
                   </a>
                  </strong>
                 </li>
                 <li>
                  <strong>
                   <a href="/historic-pursuit-racing/" title="Historic Pursuit Racing">
                    Historic Pursuit Racing
                   </a>
                  </strong>
                 </li>
                 <li>
                  <strong>
                   <a href="/historic-endurance-series/" title="Historic Endurance Series">
                    Historic Endurance Series
                   </a>
                  </strong>
                 </li>
                </ul>
                <p>
                 Cars are homologated in terms of FIA guidelines for the Historical Technical Passport.
                </p>
                <p>
                 Current sponsor for the
                 <a class="st_tag internal_tag" href="https://www.historicracing.co.za/tag/historic-pursuit-racing/" rel="tag" title="Posts tagged with Historic Pursuit Racing">
                  Historic Pursuit Racing
                 </a>
                 Series is
                 <a class="st_tag internal_tag" href="https://www.historicracing.co.za/tag/charlies-superspar/" rel="tag" title="Posts tagged with Charlies SuperSpar">
                  Charlies SuperSpar
                 </a>
                 .
                </p>
                <p>
                 Historic Racing is one of the fastest growing categories of racing in South Africa. Come and join us and indulge yourself in an exciting, enjoyable and affordable form of racing.
                </p>
