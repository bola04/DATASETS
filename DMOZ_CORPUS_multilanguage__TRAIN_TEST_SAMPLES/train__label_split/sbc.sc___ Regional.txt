            <b>
             <span lang="EN-GB" style="font-family: Arial">
              PRESIDENT FAURE MEETS WITH RETIREES
             </span>
            </b>
           </font>
          </p>
          <p class="western" style="margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:
 justify;line-height:150%">
           <font color="#808080" face="Arial">
            <span lang="EN-GB" style="line-height: 150%">
             President Danny Faure personally thanked a group of retired public 
 		servants during a ceremony held at the State House on 19
             <sup>
              th
             </sup>
             October.
            </span>
           </font>
          </p>
          <p class="western" style="margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:
 justify;line-height:150%">
           <font color="#808080" face="Arial">
            <span lang="EN-GB" style="line-height: 150%">
             The 42 
 		retirees worked mostly in the Ministry of Education and Human Resource 
 		Development with others from several departments including social, 
 		economic, parastatal amongst others.
            </span>
           </font>
          </p>
          <p class="western" style="margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:
 justify;line-height:150%">
           <font color="#808080" face="Arial">
            <span lang="EN-GB" style="line-height: 150%">
             President Danny Faure thanked the workers for their years of service and 
 		said that the government acknowledged their devotion.
            </span>
           </font>
          </p>
          <p class="western" style="margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:
 justify;line-height:150%">
           <font color="#808080" face="Arial">
            <span lang="EN-GB" style="line-height: 150%">
             Most of 
 		the retirees had more than 30 years of service in their career.
            </span>
           </font>
          </p>
          <p class="western" style="margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;page-break-before:
 always">
           <font color="#FFFF00" size="4">
            <b>
             <span style="font-family:Arial">
              ADDITIONAL PUBLIC BUSES 
 		DURING FESTIVAL KREOL
             </span>
            </b>
           </font>
          </p>
          <p class="western" style="margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:
 justify;line-height:150%">
           <font color="#808080" face="Arial">
            <span style="line-height:150%">
             The Seychelles Public 
 		Transport Corporation has announced that there will be additional buses 
 		on all main routes throughout the duration of Festival Kreol.
            </span>
           </font>
          </p>
          <p class="western" style="margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:
 justify;line-height:150%">
           <font color="#808080" face="Arial">
            <span style="line-height:150%">
             The special arrangements 
 		will take effect on the official launching of Festival Kreol on 20
             <sup>
              th
             </sup>
             October and will go on until the 31
             <sup>
              st
             </sup>
             October. After the last 
 		buses leave the terminal at 8:30pm as per the normal schedule, the SPTC 
 		will determine whether to allocate additional buses to those routes.
            </span>
           </font>
          </p>
          <p class="western" style="margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:
 justify;line-height:150%">
           <font color="#808080" face="Arial">
            <span style="line-height:150%">
             There will be extra 
 		buses on Saturday the 28
             <sup>
              th
             </sup>
             October to ferry people from the 
 		districts to participate in the Laserenad activity set to start at 3pm 
 		in Victoria. The last bus service for that day will leave the bus 
 		terminal in Victoria at 8.30pm.
            </span>
           </font>
          </p>
          <p class="western" style="margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:
 justify;line-height:150%">
           <font color="#808080" face="Arial">
            <span style="line-height:150%">
             This year’s 'Dimans 
 		kreol bor lanmer' activity will be held at Beau Vallon on Sunday the 29
             <sup>
              th
             </sup>
             October and there will be additional north bound buses especially for 
 		that day. The last bus will leave Beau Vallon at 7pm to the bus terminal 
 		and other buses will depart from Victoria bus terminal at 8.30pm. Bus 
 		fares after 8 pm will cost 8 rupees.
            </span>
           </font>
          </p>

             <span style="font-family:Arial">
              MISS SEYCHELLES 
 				HAS LEFT FOR MISS WORLD PAGEANT
             </span>
            </b>
           </font>
          </p>
          <p class="western" style="margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:
 justify;line-height:150%">
           <font color="#808080" face="Arial">
            <span style="line-height:150%">
             Miss Seychelles 
 				another World 2017, Hillary Joubert left Seychelles for China on 
 				the 19
             <sup>
              th
             </sup>
             October to take part Miss World Pageant.
            </span>
           </font>
          </p>
          <p class="western" style="margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:
 justify;line-height:150%">
           <font color="#808080" face="Arial">
            <span style="line-height:150%">
             Miss Joubert’s 
 				preparations for the Miss World pageant began soon after she was 
 				crowned as Miss Seychelles on the 26
             <sup>
              th
             </sup>
             August. During 
 				an interview with the local press before her departure, she said 
 				that she feels ready for the challenge, even though she's had 
 				only 6 weeks to prepare.
            </span>
           </font>
          </p>
          <p class="western" style="margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:
 justify;line-height:150%">
           <font color="#808080" face="Arial">
            <span style="line-height:150%">
             The Miss World 
 				competition will last for about a month whereby the participants 
 				will part in different activities including visits, pre judging 
 				and the actual ceremony.
            </span>
           </font>
          </p>
          <p class="western" style="margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:
 justify;line-height:150%">
           <font color="#808080" face="Arial">
            <span style="line-height:150%">
             Miss World 2017 
 				pageant will be held in Sanya, China.
            </span>
           </font>
          </p>
          <p class="western" style="margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:.0001pt">
           <font color="#FFFF00" size="4">
            <b>
             <span style="font-family:
 Arial">
              PRESIDENT ASSENTS TO LEGISLATION
             </span>
            </b>
           </font>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:justify;line-height:150%">
           <font color="#808080" face="Arial">
            <span style="line-height:150%">
             President Danny Faure signed into law on 18
             <sup>
              th
             </sup>
             October 2017 three items of legislation all of which had been 
 				sent to him on 17
             <sup>
              th
             </sup>
             October for assent.
            </span>
           </font>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:justify;line-height:150%">
           <font color="#808080" face="Arial">
            <span style="line-height:150%">
             The President assented to an amendment to the Prevention of 
 				Drug Abuse &amp; Rehabilitation Agency Act, to provide for the 
 				Campaign for Awareness, Resilience and Education (CARE) to be 
 				represented on the Board of the Agency by its Chairperson, 
 				rather than at the level of Director.
            </span>
           </font>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:justify;line-height:150%">
           <font color="#808080" face="Arial">
            <span style="line-height:150%">
             President Faure also assented to an amendment to the 
 				Seychelles Media Commission Act to provide for the appointment 
 				of the Chairperson of the Commission to be made by the President 
 				upon the recommendation of the Constitutional Appointments 
 				Authority.
            </span>
           </font>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align:justify;line-height:150%">
           <font color="#808080" face="Arial">
            <span style="line-height:150%">
             The third law assented to by President Faure was the repeal of 
 				the National Drug Enforcement Agency Act with effect from 1
             <sup>
              st
             </sup>
             November 2017.
            </span>
           </font>
          </p>
          <p class="western" style="margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:.0001pt">
