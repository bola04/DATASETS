    <h4>
     <b>
      Have you ever dreamed of soaring like a bird?
     </b>
    </h4>
    <div class="w3-row-padding">
     <p>
      Airworks can make that dream a reality. Paragliding and paramotoring can be enjoyed by people of any age - you can learn to paramotor in as few as six days or learn to paraglide in as few as ten.
     </p>
     <p>
      With our expert tuition you will quickly develop the technique to become a competent pilot; strength is not a requirement. We have private sites covering all wind directions, ideal for the early days.
     </p>
     <p>
      Learn to fly with the Airworks Paragliding School on our premium sites at the heart of the beautiful South Downs National Park. We train more pilots to completion of their Club Pilot rating than any other southern school, despite keeping our group sizes small and having a small intake! We train many more pilots to the higher level 'Pilot' and 'Advanced Pilot' ratings than all other local schools put together. Our Club Pilot training includes the top landing and slope landing skills rarely taught abroad but required to fly safely in the UK.
     </p>
     <p>
      Airworks Paragliding Centre is based at Glynde train station, on the main Brighton and London to Eastbourne railway lines. By road, we are 400 metres north of the A27 in the village of Glynde just south of the National Opera at Glyndebourne. We can even accommodate arrival by air if you tell us in advance!
     </p>
     <p>
      Alongside our centre is
      <a href="http://www.winging-it.co.uk/index.php?option=com_content&amp;task=section&amp;id=5&amp;Itemid=35">
       Winging It's fully stocked shop
      </a>
      of paramotors, paragliders, hang gliders and associated equipment.
     </p>
     <p>
      We also have excellent classroom facilities equipped for all your needs and an on-site cafe.
     </p>
     <p>
      To discuss your needs please feel free to pop in for a coffee and a chat, call us on (01273)434002 or email info@airworks.co.uk.
     </p>
