                <h2>
                 Accommodation
                </h2>
                <p>
                 Spend your goan farmstay holiday in a cozy cottage with a lovely veranda. Live in the midst of a tropical palm-garden, with lots of exotic plants, birds and butterflies. Our cottages have spacious attached semi-open air bathroom and toilet. You can choose between the cozy cottage (sleeps 2 adults), the family cottage (sleeps 4 adults) and our plantation cottages (sleep 2-3 adults).
