         <h3>
          Wyoming County Intl. Speedway – The Bullring
         </h3>
         <h3>
          <span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 'times new roman', times, serif;">
           — UPDATE: Please read the information below —
          </span>
          <br/>
          <span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 'times new roman', times, serif;">
           With Mother Nature winning 3 of the last 4 weeks, the following adjustments have been made to our 58th Anniversary Celebration Racer Reunion and Senior Night:
          </span>
          <br/>
          <span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 'times new roman', times, serif;">
           – The addition of Scout Night and our Kids Bike Race and Bike Give-a-way (originally scheduled for June 23rd)… All boy/girl scout members and leaders are welcomed as guests to the speedway. We will also be hosting our annual kids bike race during intermission, so bring out your bikes and race this Saturday! We also have a variety of bikes courtesy David Heimes (#8 4 Cylinder), Greg &amp; Jacob Gustafson (#66 4 Cylinder), Tom Alloco (#6 SST Modified), and Parthemer Racing (#95 6 Cylinder) that will be raffled off during the event! All tickets dated June 23rd will be honored at this event.
          </span>
          <br/>
          <span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 'times new roman', times, serif;">
           – The addition of Wyoming County Business Night (originally scheduled for July 21st)… all tickets dated July 21st and related activities will be honored this Saturday!
          </span>
          <br/>
          <span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 'times new roman', times, serif;">
           – The 6 Cylinder division has been added as a result of the June 23rd rain out, and due to the July 21st rain out, will be a double points mid-season championship event.
          </span>
          <br/>
          <span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: 'times new roman', times, serif;">

This is the second of five scheduled qualifier events between Lancaster National Speedway, Hillside Buffalo (formerly Holland Speedway, and The Bullring WCIS, with Chuck Hossfeld locking himself into the field in the first qualifier event hosted by Lancaster. There will be an increased purse sitting at $850 to win/$225 to take the green, with additional position bonus money being finalized heading into the event. If interested in joining the event as a marketing partner, contact us at the info below.
