 But sometimes you must spend to save.
 When a major oil field operator came to Ohm Corp. for help, oil production was down. Oil prices were down too.
 He needed to reduce costs.
            <p>
             <b>
              Ohm's
             </b>
             solution was to engineer a switching station so our client could alternate between two utilities serving the oil field. This way he always captured the lower rate.
