        <p>
         Finding the right company to build your new custom home can be a very intimidating task. Maglich Homes, however, has always done its best to crush some of the well known myths about homebuilding. A very hands-on, one-on-one company, the Maglich brothers offer a personal approach that ensures the home you want is the home you get.  They take great pride in their product and combined with the Maglich integrity and experience, the value of your new custom home is unmatched. The quality is second to none.
        </p>
        <p>
         There is no home more important than
         <em>
          your
         </em>
         home; and
         <em>
          your
         </em>
         tastes are never going to exactly match anyone else’s. Home buyers appreciate the many ideas and suggestions the Maglichs have to offer and, in the end, are rewarded with a home that far exceeds anything that they could have imagined.
        </p>
        <p>
         When building a custom home, you need a builder with the knowledge, skill, and flexibility to transform thought into reality; the ordinary into the extraordinary. No matter what your idea of home includes – marble, granite, tile, solid surface, or stone, wood, columns or extensive use of windows – Maglich Homes can build something to match your needs.. .beautifully.
        </p>
        <p>
         Build your dream home on your site or one of their sites in Sarasota’s finest subdivisions. You don’t have to settle for cookie cutter floor plans that don’t suit your needs or tastes. Maglich Homes can design and build your custom dream home to your exact specifications….. your style….. your desires.
        </p>
        <p>
         Why spend tens of thousands of dollars for blueprints from an architect who will have nothing to do with the construction of your home? Maglich Homes will provide your custom plans, engineered to strict Florida building codes, as part of the price of your home. The Maglich brothers hands-on approach will take you from your first meeting all the way to your closing; they don’t hand you off to a superintendent just out of school, and then leave you with a punch-out guy at the end of the job. You will take all of your concerns directly to them ~ before, during, and after the sale. They will make it their priority to put your fears at ease and make sure you get exactly what you asked for.
        </p>
        <p>
         The Maglich team will work with you to avoid the frustration of finding out you owe a lot more money at closing than what you had expected. They will spend as much time as necessary with you to put together a list of specifications and ample allowances that address all of your needs and concerns. Their aim is to give you a price that you will be happy with, and to have no surprises at the end. There will inevitably be some changes that you will require during the course of construction. That is particularly where their personal touch helps you save money. They can make the changes quickly in-house and keep things moving forward smoothly. They don’t try to make a living off of a customer’s changes, as many builders do, and will treat you fairly when you do make a change.
        </p>
        <p>
         Come join the large community of satisfied home owners who have entrusted Maglich Homes with this very important decision. Let Rick and Dan Maglich work with you to create your most precious asset ~ the place you live, love, and grow with your family.
