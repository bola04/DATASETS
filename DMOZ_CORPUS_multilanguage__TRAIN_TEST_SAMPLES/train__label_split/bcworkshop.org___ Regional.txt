           <strong>

           <strong>
            [bc]
           </strong>
           recognizes that it must first understand the social, economic, and environmental issues facing a community before beginning work.
          </p>

           <strong>
            Texas
           </strong>
           ,
           <strong>
            [bc]
           </strong>
