                <p class="zfr3Q" id="h.p_ZqzlTgmnaAuM" style="text-align: justify; white-space: normal;">
                 The Mitsubishi 4WD Owners Club of Qld (Inc) was established in 1986.  The Club offers an opportunity for people with 4WD vehicles to be involved in recreational activities as a group.  Trips are conducted by the club into relatively inaccessible places where members can participate in activities such as camping, bushwalking, sight-seeing, fishing or photography.  There are many family groups within our club and our activities are designed to cater for all tastes, abilities and age groups.

Trips may vary in duration from one day to many weeks.

It is a good way for new members to learn from the experience and expertise of long-term members who are always willing to help.

Our club is affiliated with the Queensland Association of 4WD Clubs and through them with the federal body.  We participate in the association's C.A.R.E. programme which is a demonstration of our concern for the environment and the continuing enjoyment of four wheel drivers through conservation of tracks and parks.
                </p>
