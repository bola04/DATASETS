        <p>
         This website is designed to teach the Gospel of Jesus Christ.  It is filled with resources that will help in your Bible study.  I invite and encourage you to study for yourself the materials that are gathered here and not to take my word alone.  The Scriptures record, "And the brethren immediately sent away Paul and Silas by night unto Berea: who coming thither went into the synagogue of the Jews. These were more noble than those in Thessalonica, in that they received the word with all readiness of mind, and searched the scriptures daily, whether those things were so. Therefore many of them believed; also of honorable women which were Greeks, and of men, not a few." (Acts 17:10-12).  It was by noble minds and wonderful attitudes that these individuals diligently investigated the Word of God to determine what was being said was true.  As a result, many believed.  Thank you again for visiting and may you always study to show yourself approved by God (2Timothy 2:15).
