       <h2>
        Home
       </h2>
       <div class="entry">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.imdb.com/video/wab/vi856464665/" target="_blank">
          <img alt="Pour Me - KDM Productions" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-103" height="300" src="http://www.kdmproductions.com/wp-content/ndfeuhfuwbn38yr273y2ht3ufbn73/pour-me-kdm-productions1.png" title="pour-me-kdm-productions" width="213"/>
         </a>
         KDM Productions is a Video and Multi-Media Production Company based in Parker, Colorado just south of Denver. Please take the time to go through our website to find out how we can meet your production needs. KDM Productions is a full service studio capable of HD Production, Post-Production and Audio Post-Production including 5.1 Digital Surround Sound mixing as well as Visual FX and Compositing.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Our projects range from Corporate and Industrials to Documentaries and Entertainment Programming. KDM Productions provides a wide variety of services for clients on both, national and international levels.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Thanks for taking the time to visit us!
