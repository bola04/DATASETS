           <h3>
            Activities
           </h3>
           <h4>
            MAY - SEPTEMBER
           </h4>
           <ul>
            <li>
             Fly fish, cast, or troll for landlocked salmon, lake trout, brook and nearby small mouth bass.
            </li>
            <li>
             Photograph moose, deer, and a variety of other wildlife.
            </li>
            <li>
             Hike, mountain bike, and site-see.
            </li>
            <li>
             Go swimming, boating, water skiing, canoeing, and whitewater rafting.
            </li>
            <li>
             Take a wildlife cruise by boat or seaplane.
            </li>
            <li>
             Explore shops offering everything from unique local crafts, to antiques and fine collectibles.
            </li>
            <li>
             Dine in restaurants noted for their history and generous helpings, or elegant settings and fine cuisine. Or , toast marshmallows over an open fire while being serenaded by peep frogs, crickets and loons.
            </li>
           </ul>
           <h4>
            OCTOBER - NOVEMBER
           </h4>
           <p>
            Check our Hunting pages or call or write for a free brochure on our guided hunts for moose, deer, grouse and woodcock.
           </p>
           <h4>
            WINTER
           </h4>
           <ul>
            <li>
             Snowmobile hundreds of miles of groomed trails, including ITS 86 from your front door.
            </li>
            <li>
             Ice fish from your cabin, or let us arrange an ice shack rental for you.
            </li>
            <li>
             Ski Squaw Mountain, or tour miles of groomed cross-country trails.
            </li>
            <li>
             Snowshoe to otherwise inaccessible areas and enjoy the solitude.
            </li>
           </ul>
