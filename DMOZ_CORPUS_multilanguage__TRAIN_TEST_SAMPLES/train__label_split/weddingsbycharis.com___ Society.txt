          </h2>
          <p>
           You said, “
           <strong>
            YES
           </strong>
           ”!  This time in your life is exciting, exhilarating, beautiful and perhaps a bit overwhelming.  Now that you said, “YES”, the planning commences.  The date is set.  The search begins for a venue, a florist, a caterer, a photographer, an officiant and much more.  Throughout the planning process, do not lose track of the most important thing.  Your ceremony should communicate the expression of your love that is reflected in your journey together to this point.
          </p>
          <p>
           Love and Commitment are the greatest gifts to be both given and received. Marriage is the culmination of that gift. It is the beginning of a wondrous journey between two people as they are united as one. Your ceremony of love and commitment should be one of joy and a recognition of new beginnings.
          </p>
          <p>
           I am
           <strong>
            certified Life-Cycle Celebrant
           </strong>
           and an
           <strong>
            ordained Non-Denominational Minister
           </strong>
           who would be honored to work with you to create a meaningful customized ceremony that expresses your journey together as a couple.  I will create a ceremony that is a reflection of you and your love.  Be it traditional, humorous, or adventurous, held in a church, park, beach or backyard;  I am ready to collaborate with you to make your day memorable.
          </p>
          <p>
           I respect all religions, traditions, and customs.  In this exciting journey of love, I will facilitate the creation of your ceremony with integrity, open-mindedness and professionalism.
          </p>
