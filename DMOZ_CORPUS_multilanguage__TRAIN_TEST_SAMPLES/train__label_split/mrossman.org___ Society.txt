        <em>
         Michael Rossman died on May 12, 2008, after a yearlong battle with cancer.  He began assembling this website after he learned of his illness, and worked at it fervently until about a week before his death.  It was as if he was pouring his thoughts, his wisdom, as much of his lifes work as possible, and his very soul into a container that could hold it longer than could his failing body.  Although it was his intention that "This website is forever under construction " alas, his direct contribution to its content has ended. Further work will be put into residual content addition and correction.  Please direct web comments or questions to: Karen McLellan at karenmclellan@hotmail.com.  Be sure to put Michael Rossman in the subject line.  Questions and comments regarding Michael's AOUON poster collection should go to: lcushing "at" igc.org
