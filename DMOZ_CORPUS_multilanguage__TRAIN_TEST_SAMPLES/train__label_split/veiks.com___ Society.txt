       A few simple hikes in Rocky Mountain National Park several years ago sparked an interest in hiking that has grown progressively each year since.  The beauty of the Park is incredible and much of it can only be seen by hiking to it.  Like the mountains, the Grand Canyon allows the hiker to go well beyond the introduction that can be seen from roadside viewpoints; to immerse himself in the beauty, the geology and the challenge.
       <p>
        I have assembled some photographs from some of my recent hikes here.  I hope you enjoy them.
