       <h2>
        Mission
       </h2>
       <h5>
        The Foundation endeavors to strengthen, promote, and, where necessary, defend the contributions of the humanities and the arts to human flourishing and to the well-being of diverse and democratic societies.
       </h5>

        <h2>
         Our Core Programs
        </h2>
        <div class="program-item">
         <img class="program-image" src="/media/filer_public_thumbnails/filer_public/b3/01/b301fdc5-58a1-4e26-b8b3-4c922a588e9d/karen_shanton_matthew_staverjpg__2048x1365_q85_crop_upscale.jpg__615x390_q85_crop_upscale.jpg"/>
         <div class="text">
          <a class="program-title" href="/programs/higher-education-and-scholarship-humanities/">
           Higher Education and Scholarship in the Humanities
          </a>
          <p>
           Through this program, the Foundation assists select colleges, universities, and research institutes in training scholars and producing scholarship in the humanities that contributes to culture and society.
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="program-item">
         <img class="program-image" src="/media/filer_public_thumbnails/filer_public/ec/fb/ecfb2c13-6fb2-4e92-9115-df40f0699169/sfmoma_1jpg__1800x1120_q85.jpg__615x390_q85_crop_upscale.jpg"/>
         <div class="text">
          <a class="program-title" href="/programs/arts-and-cultural-heritage/">
           Arts and Cultural Heritage
          </a>
          <p>
           The Arts and Cultural Heritage program seeks to nurture exceptional creative accomplishment, scholarship, and conservation practices in the arts, while promoting a diverse and sustainable ecosystem for these disciplines.
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="program-item">
         <img class="program-image" src="/media/filer_public_thumbnails/filer_public/b1/23/b123568e-c163-4dab-a824-e3498feb99e6/diversity_collegehorizons_800.jpg__615x390_q85_crop_upscale.jpg"/>
         <div class="text">
          <a class="program-title" href="/programs/diversity/">
           Diversity
          </a>
          <p>
           The Diversity program seeks to help diversify the next generation of college and university faculty; it also aims to strengthen institutions that improve educational attainment of historically underrepresented groups.
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="program-item">
         <img class="program-image" src="/media/filer_public_thumbnails/filer_public/8b/86/8b86954e-faac-4c7c-9648-5cba1f912d16/scholarly-comm_highres_720.jpg__615x390_q85_crop_upscale.jpg"/>
         <div class="text">
          <a class="program-title" href="/programs/scholarly-communications/">
           Scholarly Communications
          </a>
          <p>
           The Scholarly Communications program assists research libraries, archives, museums, universities, presses, and arts organizations that seek to further our collective understanding of societies and cultures around the world.
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="program-item">
         <img class="program-image" src="/media/filer_public_thumbnails/filer_public/6f/ba/6fbaec87-218b-4488-9eb6-2d09643c9cc2/international_university_of_cape_town_2.jpg__615x390_q85_crop_upscale.jpg"/>
         <div class="text">
          <a class="program-title" href="/programs/international/">
           International Higher Education and Strategic Projects
          </a>
          <p>
           The Foundation seeks to strengthen the systems of higher education and cultural institutions essential to building and sustaining viable polities and societies in emerging as well as more established regions of the globe.
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
