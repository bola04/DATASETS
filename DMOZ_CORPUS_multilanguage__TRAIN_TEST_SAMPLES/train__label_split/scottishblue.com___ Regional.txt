       Dundee &amp; Angus Minibus Hire
      </h1>
      <p>
       Scottish Blue ® are a local and professional minibus hire company based in Dundee and Angus, providing ‘minibus hire with driver’ services across Scotland. We are a fully licensed PSV (Public Service Vehicle) Operator.
      </p>
      <p>
       Our minibus hire services can be used for a wide range of occasions, from airport and city transfers to golf tour shuttle services and business travel.
       <a href="airport_transfers/index.html">
        <u>
         You can find out more about our Airport Transfer services here!
        </u>
       </a>
      </p>

      </div>
      <p>
       We have a team of local, experienced and professional drivers, with full PCV (Passenger Carrying Vehicle) Licences. For further peace of mind, all our drivers hold PVG (Protecting Vulnerable Groups) Memberships.
      </p>
      <p>
       Our fleet of fully licensed and insured minibuses can transport up to 16 passengers, with large and secure storage trailers if required.
      </p>
      <p>
       Scottish Blue ® Minibus Hire also provide services to customers who require extra support on their journey. We have
       <a href="wheelchair_accessible_minibus/index.html">
        <u>
         wheelchair accessible minibuses
        </u>
       </a>
       that can carry up to 2 wheelchair users and 6 seated passengers at a time.
