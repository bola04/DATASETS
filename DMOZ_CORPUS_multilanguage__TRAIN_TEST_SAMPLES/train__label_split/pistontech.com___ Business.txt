          <h3 style="text-align: center;">
           Piston Technology Containers
          </h3>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <strong>
            Unique Piston System is virtually 100% efficient — piston &amp; seal produce air and moisture barrier
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <strong>
            Piston protects material &amp; reduces returns — product waste and contamination are eliminated
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <strong>
            Tough and durable bins withstand rough handling — labor and maintenance costs decrease
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
          </p>
          <h5 style="text-align: center;">
           <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
            <strong>
             River City’s unique proprietary piston system offers….
            </strong>
           </span>
          </h5>
          <p>
           <img alt="" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-30" height="123" src="https://www.pistontech.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/is71-1334337789-00025.gif" width="75"/>
           NEW solutions for the storage, handling and delivery of low, medium and high viscosity products. This sealed and closed-looped system also provides solutions for protecting high quality and expensive materials from air, moisture, dirt and other contamination. The piston bins are reusable and returnable containers that are virtually 100% efficient. They eliminate the high cost of drum waste, handling and disposal. They are rugged, durable and will last many years with minimum maintenance.  Viscous materials used include: Lubricating greases, silicon, polymers, fiber optic gels, plastisol sealers, and peanut butter.
          </p>
