         <h4>
          Our History
         </h4>
         <p>
          Paul Hogan and William Fox started Cymru Kitchens over 29 years ago. Their philosophy was to create rooms that suit your lifestyle, not force boxes into
     a given space. Hence their motto ‘We’ll fit ……….where others can’t’
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Together with their dedicated team of carpenters, installers, builders, electricians and plumbers, they have installed over 5,000 dream kitchens, bedrooms
     and studies.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Their work has been featured in several magazines including; 25 Beautiful Kitchens, Ideal Home, Wye Valley Life, Beautiful Kitchens and 25 Beautiful
     Homes.
         </p>

             <p>
              When it comes to design, our clients are at the heart of the project. We will visit your home to discuss the way you want to live, how you cook, the space
     you need, and the finish you have in mind. We will help you refine your specification by showing you solutions we have designed for other clients.
     We will design every aspect to suit your needs, space and wishes to create your dream kitchen.
             </p>
             <p>
              When you’re happy with the design, sit back and relax whilst our team of qualified fitters install it. If required we will organise and manage other tradesman
     including plumbers, tilers, plasterers, electricians, gas fitters and builders. We can also help you with planning and building regulations. We will
     take away your old cabinets and burn them in the bespoke incinerator that heats our building, so no landfill and no waste.

         <h4>
          What Makes Us Different
         </h4>
         <p>
          We work with our clients to design and build your dream kitchen. We make all our own furniture in our workshop based in Newport, so we can provide any
     style you require from modern, ultra-contemporary to traditional hand painted and framed.
         </p>
         <p>
          We guide our clients through the process from the initial design stage to the installation of their kitchen, bedroom or study. Almost every kitchen has
     a piece of furniture unique to the space, take a look at our Bespoke Features Images to view the individual items; angled doors under stairwells, wine
     racks built within a window seat, tree trunk support for worktops. We often design and create doors to our client’s specification and we hand paint
     our furniture using Farrow and Ball or Little Greene paint to give you an endless choice of colour.
