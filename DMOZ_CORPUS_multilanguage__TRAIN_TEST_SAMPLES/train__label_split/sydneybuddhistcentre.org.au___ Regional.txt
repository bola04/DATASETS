       <h2 class="block-title">
        About Us
       </h2>
       <p>
        The Sydney Buddhist Centre is part of the Triratna Buddhist Community, a worldwide movement founded in 1967.
       </p>
       <p>
        Our teachers are practising Buddhists who strive to incorporate the ideals and practice of Buddhism into daily life.
       </p>
       <p>
        Triratna is a Sanskrit word meaning "Three Jewels": the Buddha, Dharma (the Buddha's teaching) and the Sangha (the Buddhist community).
       </p>
      </section>
