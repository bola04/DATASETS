         <h3>
          Hi, we’re Ed &amp; Emma &amp; together we make up Bunch of Swines BBQ team from Swindon, UK.
         </h3>
         <hr/>
         <p class="text-left">
          <img class="no-image" src="/Websites/bunchofswines/images/HomePage/ed-emma-mobile.jpg"/>
         </p>
         <p class="text-left">
          Although we’ve been cooking BBQ for many years, these BBQ’s mainly involved grilling a few burgers. It was only whilst researching new recipe ideas that we discovered the American Style of BBQ. This is exactly the same stuff that you’ll see on 'Man v Food' or 'Diners, Drive Inns &amp; Dives', Large cuts of meat smoked over oak or hickory wood for up to 15+hrs till it’s tender!
         </p>
         <p class="text-left">
          So began our journey into this new style of BBQ; we started cooking for friends and family and after rave reviews decided to put our BBQ to the test and compete against other Pitmasters. Our first competition came in 2011 - it was the British BBQ Society’s Mayhem in May contest - and amazingly scooped the 'Best Newcomer' award along with a '3rd Place' in pork! From then on we were hooked.
         </p>
         <p class="text-left">
          Now fast-forward a few years to 2015 and we're competing all over the world at contests (gaining a cabinet full of trophies) and catering events up and down the country - spreading our passion for BBQ far and wide.
         </p>

         <h3>
          Bunch of Swines Competition BBQ Team
         </h3>
         <hr/>
         <p>
          When we talk to most people about competition BBQ, the first thing we get asked is do we cook a mean burger. And even though we like to think we do, that’s not what these competitions are about.
         </p>
         <p>
          The KCBS, IBQN &amp; Grillstock competitions that we do, mainly focus on our skills at cooking chicken, ribs, pork shoulder and brisket. We’ve spent years crafting, perfecting and practicing our recipes and have picked up more than our share of awards as well.
         </p>
         <p>
          This year alone, we have or will be traveling to countries such as Belgium, Holland, Germany, Italy, Ireland, Czech Republic, Austria, Switzerland, UK &amp; the USA to compete against the best BBQ teams in the world.
         </p>
         <p>
          If you'd like to see where we'll be next, check out our competition calendar and if you're near by, why not stop over and say hi.
         </p>
