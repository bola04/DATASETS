      <h1>
       Maine Canoe, Kayak and Tubing Trips
      </h1>
      <p>
       Registered Master Maine Guide Steve Toothaker offers guided
       <a href="/maine-canoe-trips">
        canoe trips
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/maine-kayak-trips">
        kayak trips
       </a>
       and
       <a href="/maine-tubing-trips">
        tubing trips
       </a>
       in Eastern, Central and Northwestern Maine. Day trips and rentals as well as overnight canoe camping adventures are available and can range from a calm paddle on a lake or pond to a wild whitewater run on a river or stream! We have many fun family adventures planned for this spring, summer and fall and hope you can join us! We also have a small eight site campground in the town of Caratunk, Maine and would love to have you stay with us while you explore this beautiful area in Northwestern Maine!
      </p>
      <aside class="content-bottom">
       <div class="message">
        <h2>
         Message From Steve
        </h2>
        <p>
         May 17, 2018
        </p>
        <p>
         Hi paddlers!
        </p>
        <p>
         It's time to dust off the tubes,canoes and kayaks for the upcoming 
 season! I am updating things here on the web site so check back often 
 for the latest plans!
        </p>
        <p>
         I would like to set up a few dates in early to mid June for 
 beginner whitewater canoe or kayaking classes. If you have wanted to try
  paddling on moving water this is your chance to learn the skills needed
  and have fun at the same time! I would like to have at least 3 or 4 for
  the classes and the cost would be about $50 per person for a group of 
 four. The classes will most likely be on the Kennebec near our office in
  Caratunk but may be available in other areas near to you if you get a 
 group together. This would also depend on the water levels. Give me a 
 call or send an email if you are interested!
        </p>
        <p>
         We have 37 new bright red tubes to add to the fleet! I will post some pictures as soon as I get a few inflated for the season!
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Thanks for checking out our website, we hope you can join us this 
 season! If you have any questions or would like to discuss your plans 
 please call or email us!
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         July 2, 2017
        </p>
        <p>
         The fireworks display last night over the Kennebec in West Forks was great! The heavy rain that we had earlier had stopped just in time for the 9pm event. The Kennebec River and all of the streams and small creeks in the area are flowing at springtime levels. We have been running tubing trips all weekend. Everything is inflated and ready to go, give us a call if you would like to make a reservation!
        </p>
        <p>
         Steve
        </p>
        <p>
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Hi paddlers!
        </p>
        <p>
         June 3, 2016
        </p>
        <p>
         Welcome to our website!!! We have been working to get the campground cleaned up and ready for the season, there are leaves and tree limbs everywhere! We will be starting to inflate the tube fleet this weekend and also working on the driveway and lawn. Steve will be guiding a group with kayaks on the Kennebec this Saturday.
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p>
         The water on the Kennebec is warming up early this year due to the abundant sun and lack of rain that we have had this spring. We anticipate the tubing trips to begin on June 21st and continuing through August. Our campground will also be opening for the season on this same day.
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="/message-from-steve">
          Read More
         </a>
        </p>
