     <h2>
      Welcome
     </h2>
     <p>
      Wakefield Grammar School Foundation is one of the country’s leading families of independent day schools.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Wakefield Girls' High School and Queen Elizabeth Grammar School, Junior and Senior Schools, offer a single-sex education from 4-18 years.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Mulberry House Nursery educates boys and girls from 3-4 years.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://www.wgsf.org.uk/article/about/overview">
       About the Foundation
