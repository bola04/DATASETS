                      <h4 style="margin-bottom: 8px;">
                       Bauhaus Tours, Shop, Gallery since 2000
                      </h4>

                      </p>

                       with UNESCO, the municipality of Tel Aviv-Yafo and institutions, museums and galleries world-wide.
