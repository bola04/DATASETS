      <h2>
       Office Partitions
      </h2>
      <p>
       Invotek office partitions are truly relocatable, meaning
 later changes of configuration can be achieved cost effectively, with minimum
 of disturbance to employees, utilising the basic elements, time and time again.
 Designed with simplicity in mind the one-piece glazed units (glass wall partitions)
 are easily relocatable and are interchangeable with solid or door units with
 a minimum of disruption.
       <br/>
       Invotek office partitions and glass wall partitions house a built-in tolerance
 within the head channel to allow for minor building deviations.
      </p>
