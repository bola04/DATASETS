         </p>
         <p>
          Whether you’re masterminding your next business venture, galvanizing your team or just celebrating, Middle Aston House has everything you need and more. An idyllic 17th century country manor house set in extensive, beautifully landscaped grounds, it’s an escape from the distractions of everyday life, a wonderful spot to plan, ponder or party. With spacious meeting rooms, plentiful accommodation, a wide choice of reception areas and a raft of outdoor activities, all your bases are covered. And let’s not forget the food… locally sourced, and cooked with a pinch of inspiration, we venture you’ll be tempted to come back for more.
         </p>
