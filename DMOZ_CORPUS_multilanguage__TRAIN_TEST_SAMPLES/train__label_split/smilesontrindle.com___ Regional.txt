           <h2>
            Drs. Jared Coble &amp; Jason Marks
           </h2>
           <p>
            Welcome! Our Mechanicsburg dental office, established over 45 years ago, prides itself on developing long-term relationships with our patients by providing friendly, affordable and comfortable dental treatment in a caring and gentle manner. The entire staff at Smiles on Trindle Dental Care is dedicated to your comfort and prompt attention as well. Our goal is to keep your teeth healthy and your smile bright. Services range from the cosmetic- Invisalign (wireless braces), BriteSmile (teeth whitening), porcelain veneers, and implants, ViziLite (screening for early oral cancer detection), to the more routine dentistry-fillings, both tooth colored or amalgam, crowns, bridges and root canal work. We want all of our patients to be informed decision makers and fully understand any dental issues they face. That is why we have developed a web site loaded with valuable information about dentistry and dental problems and treatments. We encourage you to visit this site whenever you have concerns about your teeth.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            We know how hectic life can be and are committed to making our practice convenient and accessible. We do our very best to make your treatment experience as enjoyable and affordable as possible. We participate or accept most major dental insurance plans and we have a dental financing specialist who will do everything possible, including flexible payment options, to fit your dental needs into your budget.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Please take a few moments to look through this site to get a better feel for Smiles on Trindle capabilities and services. We also invite you to email or call our office at any time to request an appointment or ask any questions. Thank you.
           </p>
