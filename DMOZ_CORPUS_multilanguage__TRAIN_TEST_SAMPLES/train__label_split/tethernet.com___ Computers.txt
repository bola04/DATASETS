              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              Tethernets TetherFilter offers a complete anti spam and anti-virus solution in an easy to deploy appliance. With accurate anti-spam 
 				technology, near-zero administration and simple deployment TetherFilter can effectively stop the deluge of spam and viruses into your email 
 				system.
             </p>
             <p>
              The right solution and price for your business, large or small, starting at $749. Tethernets new TetherFilter 50 breaks the price barrier
   				for anti-spam and anti-virus appliances.  For more information please visit our
              <a href="products.shtml">
               products
              </a>
              page or read our
              <a href="pr061604.shtml">
               press release
              </a>
              .
             </p>
             TetherFilter employs our
             <a href="techinfo.shtml">
              unique anti-spam technology
             </a>
             which focuses on email formatting and protocol violations, which means more spam
 				is caught and fewer false positives. Quick and easy to deploy, TetherFilter gives your users less spam and reliable email delivery.
             <p>
             </p>
             <p>
              <img height="46" src="picts/tnf100_large.jpg" width="497"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span class="subtitle">
               <br/>
              </span>
              TetherFilter is your solution to a more secure network. Our anti-spam appliance provides you with a simple, drop-in rack-mount server to provide
 				an
              <a href="emailfirewall.shtml">
               email firewall
              </a>
              for your email server, protecting it from malicious spam attacks. Providing an 
 				enterprise-class solution for business of all sizes, TetherFilter employs our custom, multi-tiered filtering approach to catch and quarantine 
 				over 96% of spam email traffic.
             </p>
             <span class="subtitle">
              Features
             </span>
             <p class="just">
              The TetherFilter anti-spam appliances are designed with one task: stop spam from hitting your network. Features include:
             </p>
             <ul>
              <li>
               15 minute installation into your existing infrastructure
              </li>
              <li>
               No modifications to your email server are required
              </li>
              <li>
               Ensures proper delivery of legitimate email
              </li>
              <li>
               Effectively blocks spam with a custom anti-spam engine
              </li>
              <li>
               Prevents dangerous and costly viruses from infiltrating your network
              </li>
              <li>
               Removes unwanted attachments
              </li>
              <li>
               Works alongside existing anti-spam technologies you may already have deployed
              </li>
              <li>
               Reduces load on your mail server by blocking junk email before it gets to your server
              </li>
              <li>
               Provides an easy to use
               <a href="screenshots.shtml">
                web interface
               </a>
               for management
              </li>
              <li>
               Allows end-users to access without administrator intervention
              </li>
              <li>
               Is affordable with no per-user licensing costs
              </li>
              <li>
               A solid email firewall at a reasonable price
              </li>
             </ul>
