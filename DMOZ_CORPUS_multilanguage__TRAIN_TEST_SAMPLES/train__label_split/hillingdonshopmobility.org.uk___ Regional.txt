          <h1>
           Home
          </h1>
         </header>
         <!-- end .post-title -->
         <div class="post-content clear-fix">
          <div class="post-entry">
           <p>
            Hillingdon Shopmobility  provides fantastic facilities for those with mobility challenges (even if they are not registered as disabled) and we have a wide range of mobility scooters and wheelchairs for all abilities.
           </p>
           <p>
            Our friendly and helpful team of staff and volunteers will help you choose exactly the right equipment to suit your needs and abilities and provide you with tuition on using it safely and confidently. Based in the car park of “intu Uxbridge” shopping centre, we have 22 electric scooters, 6 powerchairs, 20 manual wheelchairs and a 4 wheeled walker with a seat.
           </p>
           <p>
            But thats not all!
           </p>
           <p>
            You can also borrow blankets and folding walking sticks.
           </p>
           <p>
            There’s more! You can even use our services when we are closed! There are special wheelchairs and small electric scooters available for holiday hire at a very reasonable charge. So go ahead and enjoy planning your next trip, either to Uxbridge town or farther afield.
           </p>
           <p>
            We are open Monday to Saturday between the hours of 9.15am and 4.30pm (last hire 3.00pm)
           </p>
           <p>
            When you register with us, we collect and store some information about you such as your address and phone number. We have always handled this information carefully and now that the General Data Protection Regulations (GDPR) have come into effect we have taken steps to make sure that we comply fully. A copy of our policy can be found on this website under the ‘Company Documents’ heading.
           </p>
           <p>
            If you would like more information about the service you can leave a message at the bottom of this page.
