        <h1 id="u26816-2">
         How Can The Electricians Union Benefit You?
        </h1>
        <p id="u26816-3">
        </p>
        <h2 id="u26816-5">
         JOIN US TO TAKE ADVANTAGE OF GREAT PAY AND BENEFITS
        </h2>
        <p id="u26816-6">
        </p>
        <p id="u26816-8">
         If you’re an experienced electrician who wants a better quality of life, or an aspiring electrician who is looking for an apprenticeship, the International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers Local 405 is here to support you. We’ve been representing electricians in Cedar Rapids, Iowa City, and the surrounding areas since 1911. Being a part of the union comes with many benefits, including:
        </p>
        <p id="u26816-9">
        </p>
        <ul class="list0 nls-None" id="u26816-12">
         <li id="u26816-11">
          Great wage: Earn top wages based on your experience and job duties.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p id="u26816-13">
        </p>
        <ul class="list0 nls-None" id="u26816-16">
         <li id="u26816-15">
          Employer paid health insurance: Our members are eligible for health, vision, dental, disability, and life insurance.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p id="u26816-17">
        </p>
        <ul class="list0 nls-None" id="u26816-20">
         <li id="u26816-19">
          Employer provided pension plans: Start saving for your retirement with our employer funded pension plan.  You can add additional money with an optional 401k to accelerate your savings.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p id="u26816-21">
        </p>
        <ul class="list0 nls-None" id="u26816-24">
         <li id="u26816-23">
          Earn while you learn:  Apprentices work and earn a living in addition to getting their education.  Upon graduation you have no student loan debt as long as you continue to work for a contributing contractor for a period of 5 years.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p id="u26816-25">
        </p>
        <p id="u26816-30">
         Contact us today at 319-396-8241 to learn more, or submit your
         <a class="nonblock" data-href="page:U196" href="ibew-careers.html" title="IBEW Careers">
          resume on-line
         </a>
         <span title="IBEW Careers">
          .
         </span>
        </p>
        <p id="u26816-31">
        </p>
        <h3 id="u26816-33">
         IBEW LOCAL 405 MEMBERS HAVE ACCESS TO NUMEROUS JOB OPPORTUNITIES
        </h3>
        <p id="u26816-34">
        </p>
        <p id="u26816-36">
         You can rely on us to keep you updated on current job calls that you’ll see listed on our website, as well as other job opportunities outside of the Local.  With your IBEW membership you can work anywhere in the country if you choose and no one will ever question your qualifications.
        </p>
        <p id="u26816-37">
        </p>
        <h4 id="u26816-39">
         WE PROVIDE CONTRACTORS WITH QUALIFIED ELECTRICIANS
        </h4>
        <p id="u26816-40">
        </p>
        <p id="u26816-47">
         Do you need a qualified electrical contractor for your next project? IBEW Local 405 can provide you with a list of skilled and knowledgeable electricians who take pride in the work they do. When you contact us for an electrician, you can be sure that the job will get done right the first time. Contact us today to find the perfect fit for your next job in Cedar Rapids, IA and the surrounding areas.   You can also choose a
         <a class="nonblock" data-href="page:U9843" href="find-a-contractor.html" title="contractor listing">
          contractor
         </a>
         from the list on our website. If you are currently an electrical contractor and would like to hire one of our highly skilled electricians give us a call at
         <a class="nonblock" href="tel:+13193968241">
          319-396-8241
         </a>
        </p>
