             <h2>
              Valor Cornerstones
             </h2>
             <h3>
              Excellence
             </h3>
             <p>
              Valor Christian Academy pursues excellence in the classroom, on the field, and in every aspect of the Valor experience. We are accredited by WASC and a member of ACSI.
             </p>
             <h3>
              Leadership
             </h3>
             <p>
              Valor Christian Academy prepares each student for leadership. From Pre-K through Middle School, every student is given the tools and opportunities to develop the character, courage, and discipline required of effective leaders today.
             </p>
             <h3>
              Faith
             </h3>
             <p>
              Valor Christian Academy presents a Christ-centered worldview. Helping students understand the character of God and the power of His Word, Valor is committed to an uncompromising Christian education that emphasizes the importance of an active, living faith.
             </p>
             <h3>
              Community
             </h3>
             <p>
              Valor Christian Academy promotes community within our student body and abroad. We provide a setting where students learn to love God and others. This love extends to serving the South Bay and surrounding communities, as well as supporting our sister school in Uganda, Mercy’s Village.
             </p>
