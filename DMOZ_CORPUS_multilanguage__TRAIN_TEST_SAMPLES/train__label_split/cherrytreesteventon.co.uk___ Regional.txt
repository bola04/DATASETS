        </h2>
        <p>
         The Cherry Tree is a beautiful 18th Century building and is still run as a traditional village pub, serving the heart of its community. The low ceilings, oak beams and 3 open fireplaces are cosy in the winter whilst the large courtyard with giant umbrella and heaters are perfect for those warm summer evenings. We are well known in the village and surrounding areas for our diverse selection of real ales, featuring five full time Wadworth brews and further rotating guest ales.
        </p>
        <p>
         Come and join our regulars for a relaxing drink or bite to eat! Â Whether you are popping for a quiet drink, a great lunch or looking for somewhere to stay, The Cherry Tree pub offers a warm, homely, welcome. A superb place to spend your time whilst in the Medieval village of Steventon.
        </p>
