              <h3>
               About us
              </h3>
              <h2>
               <strong>
                Who We Are
               </strong>
              </h2>
              <p>
               The Allentown Youth Soccer Club, founded in 1998 is a
               <strong>
                501(c)(3)
               </strong>
               nonprofit organization whose purpose is to promote, foster and perpetuate the game of soccer for the children of the city of Allentown, Pennsylvania. The program is designed to introduce the Game to the children and develop their athletic skills, promote sportsmanship and foster teamwork. Additionally, we stress academic achievement and physical well being.
              </p>
              <h3>
               Mission
