               <h2>
                Isn’t it Best To Hire An Attorney Quickly?
               </h2>
               <p>
                <em>
                 What about urgency? Don’t I need to hire an attorney right away?
                </em>
               </p>
               <p>
                Eh, probably not. Another day or two is usually not going to make or break the defense of a criminal case.
                <strong>
                 High-pressure sales pitches have no place in criminal-defense lawyering.
                </strong>
                If a lawyer makes you feel that you have to hire him right now, consider whether he’s creating pressure — pressure that you don’t need, since you’re already dealing with the government trying to put you or a loved one in a box — just to get hired, or whether you can take a little more time to make sure you choose the lawyer who will do the best job.

               <h2>
                Who is the best criminal-defense trial lawyer in Houston?
               </h2>
               <p>
                I suspect that if you asked a hundred of my colleagues, a few would humbly name someone else, but the vast majority of them would identify themselves as the best Houston criminal defense lawyer. I hate to sound cynical, but still:
                <em>
                 Especially if they thought there was a buck in it for them.
                </em>
                And they might actually believe it.
               </p>
               <p>
                This is not necessarily unhealthy—trying criminal cases is not an occupation for those with weak egos—but the truth is, as always, more complicated. And we are here for no other reason than to give you the truth.
               </p>
               <p>
                The truth is that Houston is fortunate to have a large community of great criminal-defense lawyers; the top Houston lawyers compare favorably to lawyers anywhere else in the United States (or the known Universe, for that matter). But there is no one “best” criminal-defense lawyer in Houston or elsewhere. There is a best lawyer
                <em>
                 for your case
                </em>
                , but the best lawyer for that case wouldn’t be the best lawyer for another person, or even necessarily for another accusation against you.
               </p>
               <p>
                The trick is finding the lawyer who will be best
                <em>
                 for your case
                </em>
                . That is all that matters.
