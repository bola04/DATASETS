       Designata Studio sits at the foot of the Colorado Rockies near the banks of Lake Loveland in the charming town of Loveland, Colorado.  Sharon Shuster Anhorn works with her able and talented assistants to create copper ceilings, folding screens, large metal murals, sculptures, and wall pieces in an environment conducive to creativity.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       A wealth of commissions through the decades has produced a huge variety of works including public art.  As you peruse this site, know that you are viewing the work of an artist who has found her passion and thoroughly enjoys her days in the studio overlooking a lush garden lighted by Colorado sunshine.
