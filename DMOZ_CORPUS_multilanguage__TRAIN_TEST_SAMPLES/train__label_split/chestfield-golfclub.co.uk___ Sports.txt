         <h2 style="color: #d7b03c !important;">
          Home of the
         </h2>
         <h1>
          oldest building used as a clubhouse in the world
         </h1>
         <p>
          Welcome to one of the best golf courses in Kent where visitors are always welcome. There are many reasons which will attract members and visitors to Chestfield. Not least, it boasts a course that is an excellent test of golf with its undulating fairways and greens, extensive tree-planting programme coupled with magnificent views, all set in countryside worthy of a place in the Garden of England.
         </p>
         <p>
          Well known for its excellent cuisine and exceptional hospitality, Chestfield also boasts the ‘oldest building in the World being used as a Clubhouse’. It dates back to the 15th century and is the heart of the golf club.
         </p>
         <p>
          There are few clubs as warm and welcoming as that of Chestfield. Join us for the day, or longer, and enjoy a round of golf in an attractive part of the Kent countryside with a warm welcome in the clubhouse from our members and staff. Follow us on Facebook
         </p>
