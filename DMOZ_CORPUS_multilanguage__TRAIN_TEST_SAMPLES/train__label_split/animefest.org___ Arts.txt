        AnimeFest is a convention for fans of anime, manga, games and other forms of pop-culture.  We are a charity organization which relies on membership fees from members to host our annual event.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        AnimeFest 2018 will be held from Friday, August 17th through Monday, August 20th at the
        <a class="bbcode_url" href="/u/e/AF2018/Location">
         Sheraton Dallas Hotel
        </a>
        .  We are expecting around 11,000 fans in attendance.  Head to the
        <a class="bbcode_url" href="/u/e/AF2018/Programming">
         Programming page
        </a>
        to see all the activities going on.   The
        <a class="bbcode_url" href="/u/e/AF2018/Schedule">
         schedule of events
        </a>
        should be published about two to three weeks prior to the convention.
