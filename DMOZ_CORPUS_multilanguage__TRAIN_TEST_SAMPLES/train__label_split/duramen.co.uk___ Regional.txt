       <p>
        Trees can be the source of a range of disputes that get referred to solicitors to seek resolution. Expert opinion on arboricultural matters can be of high value in resolving such disputes.
       </p>
       <p>
        Our principal consultant,
        <b>
         Dr Jon Heuch
        </b>
        , has been involved as an Arboricultural Expert Witness in both civil and criminal cases dealing with :
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li value="0">
         <span style="line-height:1.3;font-size:12px;">
          Property damage as a result of tree root activity i.e. Subsidence &amp; Heave
         </span>
        </li>
        <li value="0">
         <span style="line-height:1.3;font-size:12px;">
          Compensation following damage to trees (civil and criminal)
         </span>
        </li>
        <li value="0">
         <span style="line-height:1.3;font-size:12px;">
          Valuation of amenity trees
         </span>
        </li>
        <li value="0">
         <span style="line-height:1.3;font-size:12px;">
          Personal and property injury from both tree and branch failure and disruption to patios, walls and footpaths
         </span>
        </li>
        <li value="0">
         <span style="line-height:1.3;font-size:12px;">
          Planning applications and appeals
         </span>
        </li>
        <li value="0">
         <span style="line-height:1.3;font-size:12px;">
          Tree Preservation Order applications and appeals
         </span>
        </li>

          Tree Preservation Order prosecutions &amp; defences (i.e. criminal court)

        </li>
        <li value="0">
         <span style="line-height:1.3;font-size:12px;">
