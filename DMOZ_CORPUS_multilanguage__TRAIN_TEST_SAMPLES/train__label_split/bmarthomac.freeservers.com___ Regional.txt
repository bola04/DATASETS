         <center>
          A Brief History of the Parish
         </center>
         <!--end heading_2-->
         <br/>
         <!--begin text_2-->
         <span>
          <span>
           <h5 align="justify">
            <br/>
            <font color="#008080">
             Two Prayer Groups ( DRDO &amp; ITI ) of the  Mar Thoma Syrian Church, Primrose Road, Bangalore formed a separate group and requested the Sabha authorities for approval as a separate parish.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             Approved as a Parish on March,1994.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             Foundation stone for Bethel Mar Thoma Church, Bangalore was laid by our late Valia Metropolitan, Dr. Alexander Mar Thoma on 25th January 1993
             <br/>
             <br/>
             Bethel Mar Thoma Church,Bangalore is one of the Parishes of the Chennai-Bangalore Diocese of the Mar Thoma Church, led by Rt.Rev.Dr. Geevarghese Mar Theodosius Episcopa. The HQ of our diocese is at Mar Thoma Bhavan, Chennai - 600031. Telephone (Ofice):044 2836 3165 and (residence): 044 2825 2105
             <br/>
             E-mail:mtcmartheo@yahoo.com.ph
             <br/>
             <br/>
             Rev. Dr. John Joseph was the first Vicar.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             Completed the chapel in 1993 and worship services were conducted there.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             www.bmarthomac.freeservers.com web site was launched on 01 January 2001 by our then Diocesian Thirumeni Rt. Rev. Thomas Mar Timotheos.
            </font>
           </h5>
          </span>

         <!--begin text_4-->
         <h5 align="justify">
          <br/>
          <font color="#008080">
           The prayer hall constructed in 1993 was found to be small for the expanding Mar Thoma Community around KR Puram,Bangalore.
           <br/>
           Extended the prayer hall and completed the construction of Church building in1995.
           <br/>
           Church was dedicated on 16th December 1995 by Rt.Rev.Thomas Mar Timotheos Episcopa, our then Diocesian Bishop.
           <br/>
           Parsonage construction started on 14th July 1998 and dedicated on 29th April 1999.
          </font>
         </h5>
