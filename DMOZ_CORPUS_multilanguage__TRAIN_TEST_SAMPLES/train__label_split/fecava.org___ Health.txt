     <h1>
      FECAVA, Europe’s community of companion animal veterinarians
     </h1>
     <p>
      Through its
      <a data-cke-saved-href="https://www.fecava.org/en/team/member-association" href="https://www.fecava.org/en/team/member-association" target="_blank">
       member associations
      </a>
      , the Federation of Companion Animal Veterinary Associations (FECAVA) represents more than 25,000 companion animal veterinarians in 40 European countries.
     </p>
     <p>
      FECAVA strives to improve the veterinary care of pets through professional development.
     </p>
     <p>
      It also provides a voice for companion animal issues at European level and works closely with other European veterinary organisations and stakeholders.
     </p>
     <p>
      FECAVA is strongly committed to topics such as One Health, animal welfare, the human-animal bond, continuing education, antimicrobial resistance and the availability of medicines.
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
      For more information, go to
      <a data-cke-saved-href="https://www.fecava.org/en/policies-actions/guidlines" href="https://www.fecava.org/en/policies-actions/guidlines">
       policies &amp; actions
      </a>
      .
     </p>
