        <h1 class="csc-firstHeader">
         SILVA
        </h1>
       </div>
       <h3>
        Welcome to the
        <strong>
         SILVA rRNA database project
        </strong>
       </h3>
       <p class="bodytext">
        <strong>
         A comprehensive on-line resource for quality checked and aligned ribosomal RNA sequence data.
         <br/>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p class="bodytext">
        <strong>
         SILVA
        </strong>
        provides comprehensive, quality checked and regularly updated datasets of aligned small (16S/18S, SSU) and large subunit (23S/28S, LSU) ribosomal RNA (rRNA) sequences for all three domains of life (
        <strong>
         <em>
          Bacteria
         </em>
         ,
         <em>
          Archaea
         </em>
         and
         <em>
          Eukarya
         </em>
        </strong>
        ).
       </p>
       <p class="bodytext">
        SILVA are the official databases of the software package
        <b>
         ARB
        </b>
        .
        <br/>
       </p>
       <p class="bodytext">
        <span class="internal-link">
         <span class="internal-link">

       <div class="csc-header csc-header-n5">
        <h1>
         ARB
        </h1>
       </div>
       <div class="csc-textpic csc-textpic-intext-right">
        <div class="csc-textpic-imagewrap">
         <div class="csc-textpic-image csc-textpic-last">
          <a href="http://www.arb-home.de">
           <img alt="" border="0" height="132" src="uploads/pics/ARB_tree_18.gif" width="203"/>
          </a>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="csc-textpic-text">
         <p class="bodytext">
          The
          <strong>
           software package ARB
          </strong>
          represents a graphically-oriented, fully-integrated package of cooperating software tools for handling and analysis of sequence information.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          The
          <strong>
           ARB project
          </strong>
          has been started more than 15 years ago by Wolfgang Ludwig at the Technical University in Munich, Germany, see
          <a class="external-link-new-window" href="http://www.arb-home.de" target="_blank" title="Opens external link in new window">
           <img alt="Opens external link in new window" src="typo3/sysext/rtehtmlarea/htmlarea/plugins/TYPO3Browsers/img/external_link_new_window.gif"/>
           www.arb-home.de.
          </a>
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>
      </div>
      <div class="csc-frame csc-frame-frame1" id="c3626">
       <div class="csc-header csc-header-n6">
        <h1>
         UniEuk
        </h1>
       </div>
       <div class="csc-textpic csc-textpic-intext-right">
        <div class="csc-textpic-imagewrap">
         <div class="csc-textpic-image csc-textpic-last">
          <a href="http://unieuk.org" target="_blank">
           <img alt="" border="0" height="39" src="uploads/pics/UniEuk_web.png" width="150"/>
          </a>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="csc-textpic-text">
         <p class="bodytext">
          SILVA is a member of the
          <a class="external-link-new-window" href="http://unieuk.org" title="Opens external link in new window">
           <img alt="Opens external link in new window" src="typo3/sysext/rtehtmlarea/res/accessibilityicons/img/external_link_new_window.gif"/>
           UniEuk
          </a>
          project, a community-based project to achieve a universal taxonomic framework for
          <i>
           Eukaryotes
          </i>
          , focused primarily on protists. UniEuk is integrating information from relevant genetic markers and classical morphology-based data, validated by a comprehensive network of taxonomy experts.
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>
