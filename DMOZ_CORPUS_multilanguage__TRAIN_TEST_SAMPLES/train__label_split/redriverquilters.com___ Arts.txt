       <font color="#ffffff">
        <font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">
         <font size="3">
          Red River Quilters is a non-profit organization formed in 1983.  Our purpose is to promote quilt appreciation, preservation &amp; education.
          <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
          </span>
          We provide a place for quilters to come together for fellowship, inspiration and a shared love of the craft as well as service projects to serve our community.
          <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
          </span>
         </font>
        </font>
       </font>

      <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0in 0in 0pt; line-height: normal;">
       <font color="#ffffff" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="3">
        We have monthly meetings which include a short business meeting, a quilting-related program and Show &amp; Tell of members’ quilts.
        <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
        </span>
        We also put on a quilt show in conjunction with the Red River Revel Arts Festival each fall.
        <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
        </span>
        <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
        </span>
        Periodically we offer quilting workshops with a nationally known quilter.
        <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
        </span>
        There are also numerous “satellite” groups (known as Tributaries) who meet more frequently to sew together.
        <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
        </span>
        Further information about all of these activities is available by clicking on the menus at left.
        <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
        </span>
        Please take a minute to learn about all we offer.
