        <p>
         <strong>
          Bradford Machine Company
         </strong>
         , located in Brattleboro, VT, in the heart of New England, manufactures precision components for the medical, optical, power generation, aerospace and other commercial industries. Capabilities include CNC 5 Axis Milling, CNC Turning (up to 6 axis), CNC Wire EDM and CNC High Speed EDM Hole Drilling. The company, though, specializes in 5-axis machining of complex parts.
        </p>
        <p>
         In addition to machined parts, the company offers superfinishing (bearing ratio), deburring, tumbling, and bead blasting, meeting demanding technical and visual finish requirements. The company can also meet a wide variety of process requirements in order to deliver parts complete to print. Activity on the production floor is supported by a full complement of inspection equipment, solid modeling and engineering and an integrated ERP/shop floor system.
        </p>
        <p>
         The company is committed to bringing advanced capabilities to meeting it's customers' needs.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Bradford now offers superfinishing to acheive exacting combinations of bearing ratio, Rmr, Ra, Rp and Rz parameters. We can manufacture your parts complete or provide superfinishing on parts provided by you.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Check out the Company's superfinishing and other recent investments under "News".
        </p>
        <p>
         It's small size and big capabilities makes the company an ideal partner for responsive machining of easy to complex parts.
        </p>
