         <p style="text-align: right;">
          Join us in exploring, preserving and promoting Olympia’s history. We are a non-profit membership-based organization presenting the stories from our past to enrich the present and inform the future of Olympia.
          <a href="https://olympiahistory.org/bigelow-house-museum-2/">
           The Bigelow House Museum
          </a>
          , owned and operated by the
          <em>
           Society and Museum
          </em>
          , is the oldest residence in Olympia, Washington, and one of the earliest still standing in the Pacific Northwest.
         </p>
