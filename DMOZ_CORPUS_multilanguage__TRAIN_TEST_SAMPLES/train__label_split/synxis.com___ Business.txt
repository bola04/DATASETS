      <h1>
       The unrivaled leader in hospitality distribution
      </h1>
      <p data-redactor-inserted-image="true">
       <img height="545" id="image-marker" src="/application/files/3614/8615/4219/CR_screen.png" style="display: block; margin: auto; width: 686px; height: 545px;" width="686"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       SynXis Central Reservations (CR) is the most robust, flexible, easy-to-use rate and inventory management SaaS application. These tools enable hoteliers to support a multitude of rate, inventory and distribution strategies to achieve their business goals.
       <br/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Expand Lifetime Share of Wallet and Grow ADR
        <br/>
       </strong>
       Leverage powerful revenue management controls to drive a significant increase in ADR.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Grow Core Contribution and Brand Loyalty
        <br/>
       </strong>
       Engage with common customers across brands with deep loyalty integration, and leverage distribution reach to drive adoption and engagement.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Unmatched Partner Connectivity and Ease of Integration
        <br/>
       </strong>
       Experience seamless connectivity to over 120 property management, 7 revenue management, 7 CRM and 18 content management solutions.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Rate Parity and Last Room Availability
        <br/>
       </strong>
       Capture more revenue with real-time rate and inventory information across all channels with integrated reporting tools.
      </p>
      <p>
       SynXis Central Reservations (CR) provides unlimited scalability connecting you to all channels (including GDS, OTAs and Call Center) with a simple user interface and tablet-ready features for managing hotel inventory from anywhere.
      </p>
      <p>
       A powerful pricing engine with sophisticated revenue management controls sets SynXis CR apart from the competition. Even the most complicated rate strategies can be implemented for a single property or across a large chain.
      </p>
      <p>
       Seamless integration between SynXis CR and property systems means cost savings, synchronized data and happy guests.
      </p>
      <p>
       <br/>
      </p>
