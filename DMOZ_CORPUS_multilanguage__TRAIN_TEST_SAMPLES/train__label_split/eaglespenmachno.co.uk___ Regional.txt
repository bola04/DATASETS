                  <h3 class="builder-item-title">
                   In The Heart of Snowdonia, a Bunkhouse above a Real Ale Pub
                  </h3>
                 </div>
                 <span class="img left">
                 </span>
                 <div class="content-txt">
                  <p>
                   <b>
                    T
                   </b>
                   he Eagles Bunkhouse is set in the peaceful village of Penmachno, lying at the heart of Snowdonia in a secluded valley, four miles from Betws-y-coed.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   For walkers and climbers, Snowdon, the Carneddau, the Glyders, in fact all of the Welsh 3000ft peaks are within 15 miles.
                   <br/>
                   For kayakers, the Conwy, Llugwy, Lledr and Ogwen are only a short drive away.
                   <br/>
                   For mountain-bikers, the world class Penmachno track is less than half a mile  away.  The Marin track at Llanrwst and Coed-y-Brenin are also easily accessible.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   The Eagles' centrally heated bunkhouse sleeps up to 30, mainly in 4-bunk rooms; all rooms are private (you won't be sharing with strangers) and all bed-linen is provided.
                   <br/>
                   Facilities include a secure bike lock-up, drying room and free WiFi.
                  </p>
