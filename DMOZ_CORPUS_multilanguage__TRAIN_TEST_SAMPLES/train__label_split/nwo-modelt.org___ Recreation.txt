         <p>
          <font size="+1">
           The Northwest Ohio Model T Ford Club was formed to preserve and maintain Model T Fords of ancient age and historical value and to serve as an accurate and technical source of information concerning Model T Fords for the benefit of its members as well as the general public.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           The club members support one another and help complete projects with combined knowledge and expertice.
           <br/>
          </font>
          <br/>
          <img border="0" height="6" src="http://www.geocities.com/clipart/pbi/hrules/Generic/marble_gold.gif" width="588"/>
          <font size="+1">
           <br/>
           <p>
            Meetings are usually held the first Wednesday of every month, 7:30pm, at Jack Putman's Garage. For directions, contact Jack with the e-mail link at the bottom of this page.
            <br/>
            <br/>
           </p>
