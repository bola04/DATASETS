             <h3 class="post-title">
              <a href="http://ostrogoto.blogspot.com/2010/05/ostra-liriche-lune-e-lacune.html">
               OSTRA LIRICHE:  "LUNE E LACUNE"
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header-line-1">
             </div>
             <div class="post-body">
              <p>
              </p>
              <div align="center">
               <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_kcCA4KGduUQ/S-rJ-VmSqSI/AAAAAAAABHw/2X2cakzmOQU/s1600/luna.jpg">
                <img alt="ostra,liriche,lune e lacune" border="0" id="BLOGGER_PHOTO_ID_5470406770181712162" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_kcCA4KGduUQ/S-rJ-VmSqSI/AAAAAAAABHw/2X2cakzmOQU/s320/luna.jpg" style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN: 0px auto 10px; WIDTH: 238px; CURSOR: hand; HEIGHT: 320px; TEXT-ALIGN: center"/>
               </a>
               <span style="font-size:78%;color:#ff0000;">
                <strong>
                 Ducunt volentem fata, nolentem trahunt
                </strong>
               </span>
              </div>
              <p align="center">
               <span style="color:#cc9933;">
               </span>
              </p>
              <span style="color:#cc9933;">
               <p align="center">
                <br/>
                <span style="font-family:times new roman;font-size:130%;">
                 <strong>
                  Per ogni ascesa d'anima
                 </strong>
                </span>
               </p>
               <p align="center">
                <span style="font-family:times new roman;font-size:130%;">
                 <strong>
                  che corona
                 </strong>
                </span>
               </p>
               <p align="center">
                <span style="font-family:times new roman;font-size:130%;">
                 <strong>
                  in uno sventolar di vetta,
                 </strong>
                </span>
               </p>
               <p align="center">
                <span style="font-family:times new roman;font-size:130%;">
                 <strong>
                  ne àbdicano schiere
                 </strong>
                </span>
               </p>
               <p align="center">
                <span style="font-family:times new roman;font-size:130%;">
                 <strong>
                  che in picchiata
                 </strong>
                </span>
               </p>
               <p align="center">
                <span style="font-family:times new roman;font-size:130%;">
                 <strong>
                  s'ammàinano in fretta.
                 </strong>
                </span>
               </p>
              </span>

               OSTRA LIRICHE ED AFORISMI: "Opera Omnia"
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header-line-1">
             </div>
             <div class="post-body">
              <p>
              </p>
              <div align="center">
               <a href="http://www.kuthumadierks.com/articoli/isis/immagini/dio%20creatore.jpg">
                <img alt="Ostra liriche,Opera Omnia" border="0" src="http://www.kuthumadierks.com/articoli/isis/immagini/dio%20creatore.jpg" style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN: 0px auto 10px; WIDTH: 196px; CURSOR: hand; HEIGHT: 263px; TEXT-ALIGN: center"/>

               OSTRA LIRICHE: "IL Re Sùddito"
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header-line-1">
             </div>
             <div class="post-body">
              <p>
              </p>
              <div align="center">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_kcCA4KGduUQ/SoVC28rn_EI/AAAAAAAABG4/NI1xFw0EeXM/s1600-h/re_regina.jpg">
                <img alt="Ostra Liriche,Il RE sùddito" border="0" id="BLOGGER_PHOTO_ID_5369771642478656578" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_kcCA4KGduUQ/SoVC28rn_EI/AAAAAAAABG4/NI1xFw0EeXM/s320/re_regina.jpg" style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN: 0px auto 10px; WIDTH: 217px; CURSOR: hand; HEIGHT: 258px; TEXT-ALIGN: center"/>

                il buonsenso vassallo...

             <h3 class="post-title">
              <a href="http://ostrogoto.blogspot.com/2009/07/ostra-liriche-la-stirpe-di-giona.html">
               OSTRA LIRICHE: "La Stirpe di Giona"
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header-line-1">
             </div>
             <div class="post-body">
              <p>
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_kcCA4KGduUQ/Sktcx2C-48I/AAAAAAAABGo/ECbUd2U3GG8/s1600-h/la+stirpe+di+giona.bmp">
                <img alt="Ostra,Liriche,Staccioli,Giona" border="0" id="BLOGGER_PHOTO_ID_5353474593451336642" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_kcCA4KGduUQ/Sktcx2C-48I/AAAAAAAABGo/ECbUd2U3GG8/s320/la+stirpe+di+giona.bmp" style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN: 0px auto 10px; WIDTH: 230px; CURSOR: hand; HEIGHT: 320px; TEXT-ALIGN: center"/>
               </a>
               <br/>
              </p>
              <div align="center">
               <span style="font-family:times new roman;font-size:130%;color:#ff9900;">
                Da un pescator sadico e senza esche
                <br/>
                in acque torbide concepìti,
                <br/>
                benchè fidenti
                <br/>
                e a fortunale cheto,
                <br/>
                d'april ci scoprirem brumeggio
                <br/>
                e rigùrgito di ventre,
                <br/>
                facezia non di branco
                <br/>
                d’un colossàl pesce
               </span>
              </div>
              <div align="center">
               <span style="font-family:times new roman;font-size:130%;color:#ff9900;">
                d’occhio cèreo
               </span>
               <span style="font-family:times new roman;font-size:130%;color:#ff9900;">
                e di spada monco.
               </span>
              </div>

             <h3 class="post-title">
              <a href="http://ostrogoto.blogspot.com/2009/06/ostra-liriche-panem-et-circenses.html">
               OSTRA LIRICHE: "Panem et Circenses"
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header-line-1">
             </div>
             <div class="post-body">
              <p>
              </p>
              <div align="center">
               <a href="http://www.brunoprota.it/media/2007/tb/Circo.jpg">
                <img alt="" border="0" src="http://www.brunoprota.it/media/2007/tb/Circo.jpg" style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN: 0px auto 10px; WIDTH: 186px; CURSOR: hand; HEIGHT: 317px; TEXT-ALIGN: center"/>
               </a>
               <span style="font-size:78%;color:#ff0000;">
                Bruno Prota "Circo" 1999
               </span>
              </div>
              <div align="center">
               <span style="font-size:78%;color:#ff0000;">
                (olio su tela)
               </span>
              </div>
              <div align="center">
               <span style="font-size:78%;color:#ff0000;">
                <br/>
               </span>
              </div>
              <div align="center">
               <span style="color:#ff9900;">
                Foss’anche questo incedere,
                <br/>
                beccheggio
               </span>
              </div>
              <div align="center">
               <span style="color:#ff9900;">
                su trapezio ciondolante,
                <br/>
                bìlico
               </span>
              </div>
              <div align="center">
               <span style="color:#ff9900;">
                su fune inamidata,
               </span>
              </div>
              <div align="center">
               <span style="color:#ff9900;">
                o capriola
               </span>
              </div>
              <div align="center">
               <span style="color:#ff9900;">
                su palco di rena,
                <br/>
                è il calibro dello squarcio
                <br/>
                nell’ordìto del tramaglio
                <br/>
                che culla o maciulla
                <br/>
                il cuore del saltimbanco.
               </span>
              </div>

             <h3 class="post-title">
              <a href="http://ostrogoto.blogspot.com/2009/05/ostra-liriche-terra-nullius.html">
               OSTRA LIRICHE: "Terra Nullius"
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header-line-1">
             </div>
             <div class="post-body">
              <p>
               <a href="http://www.arsvalue.com/webapp/upl/immagini/853ef23b-8bd6-46c1-bc99-d6766d83aa83%5C200705%5C12890512-codice-delta-gamma,-laquilone_big.jpg">
                <img alt="Camillo Francia,L'aquilone -olio su tela-,Ostra Liriche" border="0" src="http://www.arsvalue.com/webapp/upl/immagini/853ef23b-8bd6-46c1-bc99-d6766d83aa83%5C200705%5C12890512-codice-delta-gamma,-laquilone_big.jpg" style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN: 0px auto 10px; WIDTH: 323px; CURSOR: hand; HEIGHT: 215px; TEXT-ALIGN: center"/>
               </a>
               <br/>
              </p>
              <div align="center">
               <span style="color:#ff9900;">
                Non còlsi allora
                <br/>
                lo schianto d’aquiloni sbrigliati
                <br/>
                imbizzarriti dall’incoerenza delle correnti...
                <br/>
                ed il presente or m’arguisce:
                <br/>
                nel camposanto dei draghi volanti
                <br/>
                s’è adagiato
                <br/>
                mansueto
                <br/>
                anche il filo aggrovigliato
                <br/>
                dell’acrobata.
               </span>
              </div>
