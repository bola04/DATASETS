      <p>
       My name is Tina Griffiths, and my counselling and coaching practice is conveniently based within the Natural Health Clinic at the Health Hydro Milton Road Swindon. The website shows information on me and the counselling and coaching service that I offer to individuals and couples in Swindon and the surrounding area.
      </p>
      <p>
       I am an experienced and qualified counsellor. As an Accredited Member of the British Association of Counsellors and Psychotherapists (BACP), my counselling work is overseen by this regulatory organisations code of counselling ethics and standards.
      </p>
      <p>
       I personally believe  it is very important to feel comfortable working with a counsellor so I offer an initial free phone consultation, where we can discuss any questions you may have around counselling or you can find out more about how counselling may help you either as an individual or as a couple for relationship counselling. My aim is to offer a safe and supportive environment where you will feel able to freely talk about the issues that brought you to counselling.
      </p>
      <p>
       As an accredited member of the BACP my practice has been judged competent and ethical by the national association of UK therapists.
      </p>
      <p>
       I can offer flexible counselling sessions to fit in with you, around such things as work commitments, shift patterns, child care or finances.
      </p>
      <p>
       For an initial free phone consultation to find out how counselling can help you or you and your partner for couples counselling please call:
       <b>
        07586 315720
       </b>
      </p>
      <p>
       I welcome trainee/student Counsellors and I can offer concessions while you are undertaking your own Counselling Training.
