      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       To All Friends of the Uptown:
       <br/>
       <br/>
       For the 16 years that I’ve lived in Uptown, I’ve been taken with the Uptown Theatre. I know I’m 
 not alone when I say that I sense how undeniably special this building is. It holds a singular place in the history of Chicago entertainment, and exemplifies architecture we may never see again.
      </p>
      <p>
       I believe that next chapter of the story of this great theater will be written very soon, and there has never been a better time for this project to move forward. With support from Mayor Emanuel, this venue will be the centerpiece of the revitalized Uptown Entertainment District.
      </p>
      <p>
       This building embodies the spirit of Uptown, full of rich history and a future bright with possibility. I’m honored to have the opportunity to be part of the future of the Uptown Theatre.
      </p>
      <p>
       Updates will be available at
       <a href="http://www.james46.org">
        www.james46.org
       </a>
       as the 
 project moves forward.
      </p>
      <p>
       Best,
      </p>
      <p>
       James
      </p>
      <p>
       [Ald. James Cappleman, 46th Ward]
      </p>
