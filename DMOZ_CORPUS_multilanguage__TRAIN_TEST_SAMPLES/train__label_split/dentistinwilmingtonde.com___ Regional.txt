           <h2>
            Welcome to the Wilmington dental practice of Karen C. Conlin, D.D.S.
           </h2>
           <h1>
            Wilmington Dentist
           </h1>
           <p>
            While visiting our dental office, you can expect high quality care from a knowledgeable and kind
            <a href="http://dentistinwilmingtonde.com/meet-the-team/" title="Meet the Team">
             dental team
            </a>
            in a welcoming, modern environment. We take the time to learn about your individual needs. Whether you are concerned with the appearance of your smile, your oral health or your overall health, we can help.
           </p>
           <h3>
            Patient Care and Comfort Focused
           </h3>
           <p>
            We are dedicated to offering the most comfortable care for you and your family. Our office is beautifully designed to help create a warm and stress-free environment where you can feel at ease and relaxed. Help yourself to a cup of coffee, tea or hot chocolate, and feel at home with our Wi-Fi and television in the reception area.
           </p>
           <p>
            During your appointment, you can relax and enjoy the massage, dental chairs while
            <a href="http://dentistinwilmingtonde.com/meet-the-doctor/">
             Dr. Conlin
            </a>
            and our friendly hygienists work on keeping your teeth healthy and beautiful. We encourage you to ask questions about your dental health and promise to listen carefully to your concerns and goals. Our team will take the time to answer your questions and address your concerns. We will also consider your individual needs when recommending treatment options.
           </p>
           <p>
            Establishing a personal and trustworthy relationship with you allows us to provide the highest quality dental care that lasts.
           </p>
           <h3>
            Welcoming New Patients
           </h3>
           <p>
            We always welcome new patients. We offer a comprehensive list of dental
            <a href="http://dentistinwilmingtonde.com/services/">
             services
            </a>
            for adults and children of all ages. Our team aims to provide you with the most positive dental experience at your first visit, as well as every subsequent visit to our office.
           </p>
           <p>
            Reference our
            <a href="http://dentistinwilmingtonde.com/patient-resources/" title="Patient Resources">
             Patient Resources
            </a>
            page to learn more about what to expect during your first visit at our dental office.
           </p>
           <h3>
            Comprehensive Dental Solutions
           </h3>
           <p>
            We provide a wide variety of general, restorative and cosmetic dental solutions. We utilize modern techniques and state of the art equipment in order to provide you with high-quality dental
            <a href="http://dentistinwilmingtonde.com/services/">
             services
            </a>
            , including teeth whitening, dentures, veneers and Invisalign®.
           </p>
           <h3>
            Schedule an Appointment
           </h3>
           <p>
            Please visit our
            <a href="http://dentistinwilmingtonde.com/contact-us/" title="Contact Us">
             Contact Us
            </a>
            page for location, office hours and to request an appointment at our Wilmington dental office. We look forward to seeing you!
           </p>
