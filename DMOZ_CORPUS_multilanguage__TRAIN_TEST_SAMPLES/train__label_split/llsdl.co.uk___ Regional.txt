             How to complete and submit match cards
            </a>
           </h2>
           <div class="entry">
            <p style="text-align: left;">
             We have received several match cards that been completed incorrectly.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: left;">
             As per the rules “A draw will be made by the captains to decide the order of individual matches”. Players names should be written down on the card in the order they are drawn.  Names should not be written on the card first and then numbered as this causes confusion and cards may be processed incorrectly.
            </p>
            <p>
             Please ensure that match cards are posted to the address on the card and not Hardy Hall.
            </p>
            <p>
             Thank you for your assistance in this matter.

              Rules
             </b>
            </p>
            <p>
             All Little Lever Summer Darts League rules apply unless stated.  All matches to be played at Hardy Hall.  All matches will be played on the usual night of play (Tuesday for Ladies, Thursday for Men.)  Ladies matches best of 3 games 801 except final which is best of 5.  Mens matches best of 3 games 1001 except final which is best of 5.  Section 1 teams  must give Section 2 teams a start of 75 for ladies and a minimum of 100 or the value of the teams seeds/handicaps whichever is the greater, for men.  All seeds count.  See seeding list on llsdl.co.uk
