                <p class="bdy">
                 For forty years, Milton Residences for the Elderly, (MRE) has held a prominent place in the Town of Milton. Started by the Milton-Mattapan Clergy Association MRE has provided homes for moderate income persons from all walks of life. Holding true to its mission, MRE continues to seek ways in which to provide housing and services for all who take residence in our communities.
                </p>
                <p class="bdy">
                 We welcome you to our website and hope you find it informative and useful as you explore housing options for you or your loved ones.
                </p>
                <blockquote>
                 William J. Sullivan,
                 <br/>
                 President of the Board of Directors
                </blockquote>
                <div align="center">
                 <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="images/bluepixel.gif" width="300"/>
                </div>
                <br/>
                <div align="center" class="homeheader">
                 Experience and expertise in:
                </div>
                <p class="homeheader">
                 - Development of affordable and market-rate housing for seniors
                 <br/>
                 - Property management of elderly housing
                 <br/>
                 - The development, organization, management and delivery of support
    services to residents and off site, aging-in-place, households
                 <br/>
                 - Consulting to housing providers, developers and community groups
                </p>
                <div align="center">
                 <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="images/bluepixel.gif" width="300"/>
                </div>
                <p class="bdy">
                 Milton Residences for the Elderly was created in 1968 by the Milton-Mattapan Clergy Association to provide affordable
                 <strong>
                  senior housing and elderly care in the Boston, Quincy, Newton, Braintree and Canton region
                 </strong>
                 . Since
 completing its first apartment community,
                 <a href="unquity.html">
                  Unquity House
                 </a>
                 , in 1972, MRE has
 continued to fulfill its original mission:
                 <em>
                  "to meet the needs of the seniors - in Milton and beyond - by providing housing and services, with sensitivity and care, as a means of assuring their well-being and preserving their dignity."
                 </em>
                </p>
                <p class="bdy">
                 In recent years, MRE has branched out to provide both subsidized and market rate housing, as a developer, property management organization and 
 provider of support services, including assisted living at our
                 <a href="winter.html">
                  Winter Valley
                 </a>
                 property.
                </p>
                <p class="bdy">
                 MRE is recognized throughout Greater Boston for its professionalism and its commitment to a caring approach in providing housing for seniors. With the  
 recent acquisition of an affordable elderly development in
                 <a href="sbostonprop.html">
                  South Boston
                 </a>
                 , MRE has expanded its geographic base.
                </p>
                <p class="bdy">
                 MRE has built its reputation over the past 40 years by meeting the challenges of providing elderly housing with care, professionalism and integrity.
                </p>
