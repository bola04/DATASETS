         </h2>
         <p>
          <a class="imgLarge zoom iconZoom iconZoom" href="http://twelvebasketscatering.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/ColumbiaWinery.jpg" title="Columbia Winery">
           <img alt="Columbia Winery" height="199" src="http://twelvebasketscatering.com/wp-content/themes/parallelus-traject/includes/timthumb.php?src=/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/ColumbiaWinery.jpg&amp;w=593&amp;h=199&amp;zc=1" width="593"/>

         </p>
         <p>
          Recently renovated, Columbia Winery is nestled in the heart of Woodinville wine country. They offer a selection of indoor and outdoor settings for small and large groups, including the signature wine country ambiance of a Barrel Room. Picturesque rental options throughout the property help you create an unforgettable occasion with key components such as fireplaces, gorgeous chandeliers, and hard wood floors.

         <h2>
          A Little Bit About Us…
         </h2>
         <p>
          <a class="imgLarge zoom iconZoom" href="#" title=" David Lai Photography">
           <img alt=" David Lai Photography" height="199" src="http://twelvebasketscatering.com/wp-content/themes/parallelus-traject/includes/timthumb.php?src=/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/daniellerob_wedding-5044-e1485914914444.jpg&amp;w=593&amp;h=199&amp;zc=1" width="593"/>
           <span class="imgFrame">
           </span>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          With over 40 years of experience and surpassing 30,000 events, Twelve Baskets Catering has set a standard for food service excellence in the Seattle area. Our longtime partnership with nearby vendors ensures the freshest options in locally harvested food. And while our experience guarantees our professionalism, we want to work to infuse your personality into our offerings. Whether it’s integrating your favorite ingredient into the menu or crafting a delicious vegetarian dish, our personal touches have made Seattle Magazine readers vote us Seattle’s Best Caterer.
         </p>

          Twelve Baskets Catering provides fresh meals and food service to weddings, corporate functions and other special events. Regardless of size or theme, we work to provide a tasteful and memorable gathering.
