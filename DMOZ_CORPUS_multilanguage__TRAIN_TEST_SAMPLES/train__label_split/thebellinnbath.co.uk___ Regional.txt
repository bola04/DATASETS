          <h1 class="tm-heading-main">
           The Bell Inn Pub
          </h1>
          <p>
           <span style="color: #ffffff;">
            The Bell is a historic free house and music pub on the edge of Bath city centre: it is
            <a href="http://www.bellcommunity.org.uk/" rel="noopener noreferrer" style="color: #ffffff;" target="_blank">
             owned by 518 of its customers, fans and workers
            </a>
            under IPS CoOperative rules. We finally bought the pub on July 2nd 2013. We have live music three times a week ranging from jazz to blues to folk to other things- check out the free
            <a href="http://www.thebellinnbath.co.uk/live-music" style="color: #ffffff;">
             band listings
            </a>
            and
            <a href="/what-s-on" style="color: #ffffff;">
             What's On
            </a>
            page,
            <a href="http://www.thebellinnbath.co.uk/the-love-lounge" style="color: #ffffff;">
             open mic nights
            </a>
            on thursday, plus
            <a href="http://www.thebellinnbath.co.uk/vinyl-dj-sets" style="color: #ffffff;">
             DJ nights
            </a>
            at the weekend - always free!
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="color: #ffffff;">
            We stock
            <a href="http://www.thebellinnbath.co.uk/our-drinks" style="color: #ffffff;">
             seven real ales
            </a>
            plus two guest ales. We are in the Guardian and Observer guides to summer pubs as well as the Lonely Planet, Rough Guide, the Good Beer Guide and Le Guide du Routard. For your amusement we have table football, bar billiards (Mon - Weds), Pool,  chess, backgammon, free wi-fi, a real fire and even a launderette! Every Wednesday and Friday from 5pm, and Sunday from noon we have The Pizza Bike preparing and serving freshly made, wood-fired pizza in the garden.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <span style="color: #ffffff;">
            <span style="font-family: Arial; font-size: 13.3333px;">
            </span>
            <strong>
             The Bell Inn is a Living Wage employer
            </strong>
            accredited by
            <a href="https://www.livingwage.org.uk/" rel="noopener" style="color: #ffffff;" target="_blank">
             The Living Wage Foundation
            </a>
            . The real Living Wage is based on the cost of living and is voluntarily paid by 3,500 UK employers who believe a fair day's work deserves a fair day's pay.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <span style="color: #ffffff;">
            <strong>
             The Bell Inn is part of
             <a href="https://www.musiciansunion.org.uk/Home/News/2015/Nov/Fair-Play-Venues" rel="noopener" style="color: #ffffff;" target="_blank">
              The MU’s Fair Play Venue Scheme
             </a>
            </strong>
            which aims to support grassroots venues, recognise good practice and stamp out the unfair treatment of musicians.
           </span>
          </p>
