        Armenian Freenet (Freenet.am) provides FREE Internet services to promote the development of the information technologies and national information infrastructure in Armenia. Freenet.am provides: FREE E-mail service, FREE Webhosting service &amp; FREE Dial-up access service with national WWW resources availability via proxy service.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Following the best traditions of the Internet, Freenet.am provides FREE services to individuals,
        <br/>
        as well as non-profit, education and research organisations, State and other institutions.
        <br/>
        If you decided to join the Freenet.am Community, please complete the
        <a class="index_link2" href="registration.php?langid=1">

        </a>

        <br/>
        <br/>
        Freenet.am was created in the framework of
        <a class="index_link2" href="http://www.undp.am/" target="_blank">
         United Nations Development Programme (UNDP) Armenia
        </a>
        Internet Project in 1997. The Internet Project aimed to support the development of Internet and information technologies in Armenia.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        At the end of the initial project in 2006, UNDP requested that
        <a class="index_link2" href="http://www.isoc.am/" target="_blank">
         Internet Society of Armenia (ISOC-AM)
        </a>
        take over the management of Freenet.am.
        <a class="index_link2" href="http://www.isoc.am/" target="_blank">
         ISOC-AM
        </a>
        is current manager of Freenet.am. Hosting and technical support for the FreeNet.am is provided by the
        <a class="index_link2" href="http://www.asnet.am/" target="_blank">
         ASNET-AM
        </a>
        , which is the joint structure of laboratories of
        <a class="index_link2" href="http://iiap.sci.am/" target="_blank">
         Institute for Informatics and Automation Problems (IIAP)
        </a>
