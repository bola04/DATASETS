            <h1>
             Mater Health Services North Queensland
            </h1>
            <p>
             <strong>
              Welcome to the Mater community
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             Mater Health Services North Queensland provides private hospital and medical services to Townsville and the North Queensland region with a strong commitment to offering excellence in patient care.
            </p>
            <p>
             Since 1945 the Mater has continuously met and exceeded patient standards through employing dedicated and skilled staff and utilising the latest in medical equipment and technology.
            </p>
            <p>
             We firmly stand by our Sisters of Mercy values of spirituality, compassion, excellence in care, respect and justice.
            </p>
            <p>
             Our staff are treated with the same respect as our patients, making the Mater a popular workplace for health professionals from all over the world.
            </p>
            <p>
             With two campuses - the main site in Pimlico and another at Hyde Park that specialises in women’s care - Mater Health Services North Queensland is ideally placed to provide the ultimate in modern health services.
            </p>
            <p>
             Whether you’re an existing patient, prospective patient, staff member or you’re looking for rewarding work, we hope your experience with the Mater is enjoyable and fulfilling.
