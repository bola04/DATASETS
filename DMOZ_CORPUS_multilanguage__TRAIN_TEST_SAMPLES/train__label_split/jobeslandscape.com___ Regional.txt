              </h2>
              <span class="rdf-meta element-hidden" content="Jobe's - Exquisite Landscapes For Your Senses - Sussex Co, Delaware" property="dc:title">

                   <div class="pad">
                    <h2>
                     Exquisite Landscapes For Your Senses
                     <img alt="Jobe's Landscape - Sussex County, Delaware" class="imgright" height="139" src="/sites/jobeslandscape.com/files/images/tulips.jpg" width="205"/>
                    </h2>
                    <p>
                     Call Jobe's to assist you in creating the landscape that appeals to your senses. Here in Sussex County, Delaware, we are considered the design and installation specialists of choice.
                    </p>
                    <p>
                     We'll help you plan, plant and enjoy your beautification projects. Our full range of services are available year round for both residential and commercial clients. Our love of nature combines with our dedication to quality to provide you with exactly the 'look' and service you desire.
                    </p>
                    <p>
                     Browse our site to see the wide variety of home improvements projects we can assist with. You are invited to come and visit our Resource Library and browse our gardening and landscape books and magazine to help you with ideas, designs, colors, plants and garden styles. There is never an obligation when you utilize our reference resources. Borrow a few books to enjoy in your spare time.
                    </p>
                    <p>
                     We'll also provide a free landscape design consultation at your home with our expert knowledge of the plants, grasses and other ornamental landscaping that work best in our climate zone.
                     <img alt="Jobe's Landscape - Sussex County, Delaware" class="imgright" height="155" src="/sites/jobeslandscape.com/files/images/3-2.jpg" width="206"/>
                    </p>
                    <p>
                     Serving Sussex County for more than 15 years, Jobe's is owned and operated by Joy and Jobe Tomer, who offer courteous, knowledgeable, and reliable services.
                    </p>
                    <p>
                     Landscaping not only beautifies your home, it also greatly increases the "curb" appeal and value of your home as well.
                    </p>
                    <p>
                     We are here to assist our clients achieve their outdoor enhancement goals and maintain the beauty and quality of the environment for years to come.
                    </p>
                    <p>
                     Call now for a free at home consultation. We service the eastern side of Sussex County, Delaware, from Milford to Lewes, Rehoboth Beach, Dewey Beach, Millsboro, Bethany Beach, Ocean View and Millville. We are here to assist both our residential and commercial clients achieve their outdoor enhancement goals and maintain the beauty and quality of the environment for years to come.
                    </p>
