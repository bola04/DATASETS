All of our activities are  performed by or under the direct supervision of NACE International Corrosion / Cathodic Protection Specialists and / or Professional Engineers.

      <h2>
       Accurate Corrosion Project Highlights
       <span>
       </span>
      </h2>
      <h4>
       Accurate Corrosion Control, Inc (ACCI) has performed thousands of projects since 1984.
      </h4>
      <ul class="list">
       <li>
        <strong>
         Close Interval Survey (CIS)
        </strong>
        : ACCI has performed thousands of miles of on - off, and depolarized CIS.
       </li>
       <li>
        <strong>
         Short Location
        </strong>
        : ACCI has located and cleared hundreds of "shorts" involving complex gas distribution systems along many miles of piping.
       </li>
       <li>
        <strong>
         Deep Anode Groundbed Installation
        </strong>
        : ACCI has hundreds of successful deep groundbed installations of various design.
       </li>
       <li>
        <strong>
         Rectifier Influence Studies
        </strong>
        :  ACCI has performed multiple rectifier influence studies to identify  client and foreign CP system influences, determine CIS interruption  plans and to follow up areas of concern identified in CIS involving  multiple pipeline operators.
       </li>
       <li>
        <strong>
         CP Remote Monitoring Units (RMU's)
        </strong>
        :  ACCI has installed, commissioned, and maintained hundreds of remote  monitoring units involving many types of units and applications.
       </li>
       <li>
        <strong>
         AC Mitigation
        </strong>
        : ACCI has experience in AC mitigation design studies and remedial activity installations.
       </li>
      </ul>
     </article>
     <aside>
      <h2>
       The ROTA-CELL
       <span>
       </span>
      </h2>
      <p>
       <img alt="Rota-Cell" src="images/page-images/rota-cell-logo.png"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       ROTA-CELL is a Constant Contact Reference Electrode (U.S. Patent 7446522). ROTA-CELL is the reference electrode "accessory" for constant soil contact during Close Interval Survey.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Rota-Cell" src="images/page-images/rota-cell.jpg"/>
      </p>
      <h2>
       ROTA-CELL System Features:
       <span>
       </span>
      </h2>
      <p>
       Used for performing over the line close interval surveys:
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        The reference electrode remains in continuous contact with soil through a water pathway during the survey. Permitting the surveyor the option of triggering data on a timed cycle, by footage or to view a constant profile.
       </li>
       <li>
        Easily mounts to the hitch of an ATV or truck.
       </li>
       <li>
        Use with any standard “plastic” water tank.
       </li>
       <li>
        Use your existing data logger or voltmeter, standard reference electrode and backpack.
       </li>
       <li>
        Survey foreign pipeline crossings or any other area where stray current interference is suspected.
       </li>
      </ul>
