     <h1 class="em17 italic">
      Welcome to Bruce A. Hollett &amp; Associates
     </h1>
     <p>
      Are you looking for an employee group insurance plan? Have  you been to multiple websites trying to figure out what broker you should use  based on information provided? Have you noticed they all say the same thing?  Welcome to something different!
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
      Our approach to employee benefits is to buy wholesale from  major insurance companies and to use benefit pools. This has enabled us to provide  premium stability for our clients in this volatile market. Our company has been  providing employee benefit plans since 1978, and has a 99% client retention  rate.
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
      Our clients have done their due diligence and shopped the  market through us, or with other advisors. In “apples to apples” marketing, our  programs have continued to prove our pricing model is the most  competitive.  As this model is only available to a select group of  employee benefit specialists; your advisor will not have this advantage.
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
      To sum it up other advisors are selling you the higher  retail priced benefit plans, plus you do not have the protection of a benefit  pool.
     </p>
