         <div style="text-align: center;">
          Bison Bushcraft is one of the earliest established wilderness skills training and outfitting businesses in The UK. Roger either ended up either making it himself (the knives) or designing various items and having them made to his own exacting specifications. Roger has advised on film and television work, taught at schools and other organisations such as The Forestry Commission and local councils.He teaches at local scout groups and is a training advisor to The UK Scout movement.
