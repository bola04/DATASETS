   <section class="front-page-section" id="about" style="">
    <div class="section-header">
     <div class="container">
      <div class="row">
       <div class="col-sm-10 col-sm-offset-1">
        <div class="section-description">
         The Central Saanich Police Service, through its governing Municipal Police Board, consists of 25 sworn members, supported by four civilian staff, three volunteer Reserve Constable Officers, and several volunteers. They provide a professional level of police service with a strong commitment to community policing, integrated policing, and multi -agency cooperation.
 
 Values
 
 Care About People
 Respect The Rights Of Individuals
 Respond To Our Community
 Work In Partnership With Others
 The Police Service is organized into the following areas: uniformed investigation and patrol, general investigative services, community relations, traffic safety and administration. The Service supports two full time secondments with one police officer participating in the Combined Forces Special Enforcement Unit and the other in the Integrated Road Safety Unit.
        </div>
