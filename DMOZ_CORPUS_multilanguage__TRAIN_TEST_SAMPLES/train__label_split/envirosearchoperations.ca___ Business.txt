       <p>
        The sourcing, treatment and supply of safe drinking water to the public is
   no longer optional in Ontario.  As a result of events where public
   health and safety may have been jeopardized, such as in Walkerton, Ontario,
   all aspects of the supply of drinking water to the public are now governed by
   Ontario statutes such as the Safe Drinking Water Act 2002, the Health Protection &amp;amp Promotion Act along with associated
   regulations including, in particular, Ontario Regulations 170/03 and
   319/08.
