         <h3>
          <a href="http://www.fourplay.com.au/">
           FourPlay String Quartet
          </a>
         </h3>
         <div class="text">
          <p class="P">
           Welcome to the official website of the electric string quartet FourPlay, based in Sydney, Australia.
          </p>
          <h2>
           New single –
           <em>
            Bound
           </em>
           – and tour
          </h2>
          <p>
           FourPlay return with a rocking new single and a stack of national tour dates to celebrate. Featuring original track ‘Bound’, the digital-only single also includes three archival remixes of FourPlay songs.
           <br/>
           BUT FIRST, the video!
           <br/>
           <iframe allow="autoplay; encrypted-media" allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/55I8zhfsXBQ?rel=0" width="560">
           </iframe>
           <br/>
           It’s directed by Emilie Morgane B. See full credits at the
           <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=55I8zhfsXBQ">
            YouTube page
           </a>
           . It also seems to feature some weird rock band?
          </p>
          <p>
           So: ‘Bound’ is classic FourPlay, a cut that’s got it all: a big dirty riff that pushes and pulls, urgent and soaring vocals, a deeply melodic chorus, and a crazed viola solo to push everything over the edge.
          </p>
          <p>
           ‘Bound’ began as a riff inspired by Jack White and Beyoncé, written out of a jam while the band was a guest of the Bundanon Trust’s Artist-In-Residency program in the Southern Highlands of NSW. Vocals were later added by Lara Goodridge with lyrics about staring life’s challenges down while taking responsibility for who we are, and who we love. ‘Bound’ reminds us that our decisions have repercussions, and that ultimately we’re all bound together. It’s big and bold and sure to be a live favourite on this upcoming tour!
           <br/>
           <iframe seamless="" src="http://bandcamp.com/EmbeddedPlayer/album=3248667801/size=large/bgcol=ffffff/linkcol=0687f5/artwork=small/transparent=true/" style="border: 0; width: 400px; height: 274px;">
            <a href="http://shop.fourplay.com.au/album/bound">
             Bound by FourPlay String Quartet
            </a>
           </iframe>
           <br/>
           (Album artwork by Kerri Ambler)
          </p>
          <p>
           Don’t miss FourPlay as they showcase ‘Bound’ along with tracks from their entire career at a venue near you. Every concert ticket includes a free download of ‘Bound’.
          </p>
          <p>
