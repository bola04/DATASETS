          <h2 class="entry-title">
           <a href="http://www.semanasantabaena.com/advantages-using-steel-doors-windows/" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to Advantages of Using Steel Doors and Windows">
            Advantages of Using Steel Doors and Windows
           </a>
           <div class="entry-meta">
            <span class="meta-date">
             <a href="http://www.semanasantabaena.com/advantages-using-steel-doors-windows/" rel="bookmark" title="5:33 am">
              <time class="entry-date updated" datetime="2018-07-13T05:33:22+00:00">
               13/07/2018
              </time>
             </a>
            </span>
            <span class="author vcard">
             <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.semanasantabaena.com/author/conni3c6b7v/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Connie Souder">
              Connie Souder
             </a>
            </span>
            <span class="cat-links">
             <a href="http://www.semanasantabaena.com/category/construction/" rel="category tag">
              Construction
             </a>
            </span>
           </div>
           <!-- .entry-meta -->
          </h2>
         </header>
         <!-- .entry-header -->
         <div class="entry-content">
          <div class="featured-image">
           <a href="http://www.semanasantabaena.com/advantages-using-steel-doors-windows/" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to Advantages of Using Steel Doors and Windows">
            <img alt="steel door" class="attachment-blog-page size-blog-page wp-post-image" height="200" src="http://www.semanasantabaena.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/yr1r123r13iit8t9-960x200.jpg" width="960"/>
           </a>
          </div>
          <p>
           Steel windows and doors are mainly used for commercial establishments, schools, and stores. You can also install them in your residential properties. They are versatile door options. All the
           <a href="https://www.eurolinesteelwindows.com/">
            custom steel doors &amp; windows
           </a>
           are capable of withstanding heavy exterior elements and traffic. They are not made of pure steel. They are comprised of polyurethane and polystyrene cores which are encased in steel covers. The recommended gauge for steel doors ranges from 16-12 mm. It would be very expensive to install them throughout a home because they are expensive. However, they are less expensive as compared to the solid doors.             What You Need To Know About double glazed windows
           </a>
           <div class="entry-meta">
            <span class="meta-date">
             <a href="http://www.semanasantabaena.com/what-you-need-to-know-about-double-glazed-windows/" rel="bookmark" title="10:27 am">
              <time class="entry-date updated" datetime="2017-11-26T10:27:57+00:00">
               26/11/2017
              </time>
             </a>
            </span>
            <span class="author vcard">
             <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.semanasantabaena.com/author/maryujsg123e/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Mary Lamm">
              Mary Lamm
             </a>
            </span>
            <span class="cat-links">
             <a href="http://www.semanasantabaena.com/category/windows/" rel="category tag">

             </a>

           <!-- .entry-meta -->
          </h2>
         </header>
         <!-- .entry-header -->
         <div class="entry-content">
          <div class="featured-image">
           <a href="http://www.semanasantabaena.com/what-you-need-to-know-about-double-glazed-windows/" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to What You Need To Know About double glazed windows">
            <img alt="double glazed windows" class="attachment-blog-page size-blog-page wp-post-image" height="200" src="http://www.semanasantabaena.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/098765retyuiop-960x200.jpg" width="960"/>
           </a>
          </div>
          <p>
           Insulated glass units or double glazed multiple planes are made by combining multiple glass planes. They are either made using two (double glazing) or three (triple glazing) panes. The different panes are usually separated by a layer of gas and a spacer. They are increasingly becoming more popular in the modern days due to the rising cost of energy. They are fitted with wider windows. The space between glass panes is meant for increasing the insulation to prevent heat loss.
          </p>
          <p>
           Preventing heat loss is an effective method of keeping the house warm in winter. They are also used in preventing unwanted heat that would increase the indoor temperatures thereby eliminating the need for air conditioners and artificial heaters. In addition to this, they are also effective in reducing the noise levels. Increasing the gap between the two panes is also useful in improving sound reduction.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Insulation
          </h2>
          <p>
           <img alt="double glazed window-providing insualtion" class=" wp-image-153 alignright" height="154" sizes="(max-width: 231px) 100vw, 231px" src="http://www.semanasantabaena.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/098765tryuiop-300x200.jpg" srcset="http://www.semanasantabaena.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/098765tryuiop-300x200.jpg 300w, http://www.semanasantabaena.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/098765tryuiop.jpg 510w" width="231"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           Typically, the space between the glass ranges from 6-20 mm. The optimum space for improving the thermal performance is 12mm. A 15 mm space is recommended for reducing low-frequency noise. Large gaps are known for increasing the rate of convection. This, in turn, reduces the insulating performance. To improve the energy efficiency, you are advised to use a double glazing having a space of 10-20 mm.
          </p>
          <h2>
           What is in between the gall planes?
          </h2>
          <p>
           The space between the glass planes should be fully sealed. This space is meant to increase the level insulation thereby preventing heat loss from the building. The gas filling this space is also useful in increasing acoustic and thermal performance. The gas used should have conductivity properties. The commonly used gas is argon. A spacer is also included for removing any moisture that might be trapped within the airspace.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Glass used
          </h2>
          <p>
           <img alt="glazed glass window" class=" wp-image-154 alignleft" height="133" sizes="(max-width: 200px) 100vw, 200px" src="http://www.semanasantabaena.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/098765tryuiop-1-300x200.jpg" srcset="http://www.semanasantabaena.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/098765tryuiop-1-300x200.jpg 300w, http://www.semanasantabaena.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/098765tryuiop-1.jpg 510w" width="200"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           Laminate and low-e glasses are the main types of glass used in making the double glazing units. They are known for being effective in controlling noise and increasing energy efficiency. Additionally, low-e glass is helpful in reducing heat loss and disrupting sound waves. This has made it ideal for improving the acoustic performance.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h2>
           Pros
          </h2>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Improved home security: manual locks and security systems can be incorporated into these windows and
           </li>
           <li>
            doors to provide extra security.
           </li>
           <li>
            Maintenance: Little maintenance is required.
           </li>
           <li>
            Saving money: They are effective in lowering energy consumption.
           </li>
           <li>
            Reducing noise pollution
           </li>
          </ul>
          <h2>
           Cons
          </h2>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Heat cannot flow easily across the window
           </li>
           <li>
            Costly: The acquisition cost is very high, but they will help you in saving cost in the long run
           </li>
          </ul>

It is important to note that the durability of the mattress will largely depend on the quality of the materials used to make the mattress. One can get a high-quality mattress by buying from a brand that is known to produce high-quality mattress. The way it is made also affects the mattress’ durability. For this reason, one can opt for hand crafted mattresses.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h2>
           3. Motion disturbance
          </h2>
          <p>
           Sharing a bed with someone needs to be considered. Some people toss and turn before they sleep which makes it easy to disturb the one they are sharing the bed with. This is what is referred to as motion disturbance. This can be prevented by going for a mattress which molds to one’s movement and absorbs the movement shocks which will not disturb the other person’s sleep. One should go for the foam mattress to cater for this.
          </p>
          <h2>
           4. The firmness of the mattress
          </h2>
          <p>
           Hard or soft mattresses are chosen depending on one’s preferences. Previous researches done have shown that for people having lower back problems should go for a mattress that is medium firm. If a mattress is very firm one may need a pillow for further support. If one is petite, they should go for one a medium thick as well since their size may not allow them to touch the support system which could have negative effects on their spine.

          </p>
          <h2>
           5. The warranty
          </h2>
          <p>
           This should be enquired first before deciding to purchase. This will make sure that you are protected since you can be allowed to replace the mattress in case it is faulty. This is done at no extra financial cost so long as the mattress will be returned within its warranty period.
          </p>
