              Welcome to Bri-Ski Propellers
             </h3>
             <img alt="Bri-Ski Propellers" class="right_img img_border" height="276" id="img1" src="images/yacht.jpg" width="200"/>
             <p id="p2">
              Bri-Ski Ltd has been designing and manufacturing all types of marine propellers for 42 years.
             </p>
             <p id="p3">
              We manufacture 2, 3, 4 and 5 Blade propellers ranging from 12" - 40" dia, from Manganese bronze and Nickel Aluminium bronze. Our propellers are produced to a high quality standard for both pleasure and commercial boats.  With all propellers being measured by computerised pitching system.
             </p>
