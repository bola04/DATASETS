        <h2 class="featured-title">
         <a href="http://www.calwaterassn.com/news-release-californias-regulated-water-utilities-invested-more-than-645-million-on-needed-infrastructure-in-2017/">
          News Release: California’s Regulated Water Utilities Invested more than $645 Million on Needed Infrastructure in 2017
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
