          </h1>
          <h1 style="text-align: center;">
           Welcome to Avon Riding Centre
          </h1>
          <h1>
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.avonridingcentre.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/IMG_2401.jpg">
             <img alt="IMG_2401" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3372" height="3456" sizes="(max-width: 5184px) 100vw, 5184px" src="http://www.avonridingcentre.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/IMG_2401.jpg" srcset="http://www.avonridingcentre.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/IMG_2401.jpg 5184w, http://www.avonridingcentre.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/IMG_2401-300x200.jpg 300w, http://www.avonridingcentre.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/IMG_2401-1024x683.jpg 1024w, http://www.avonridingcentre.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/IMG_2401-624x416.jpg 624w" width="5184"/>
            </a>
           </strong>

          <p>
           Here at Avon Riding Centre we strongly believe that riding is for everyone.  We currently cater to almost two hundred riders with varying levels of disability, from people who just want to enjoy a ride around the woods, to those who want to challenge themselves to enter RDA competitions, such as dressage and show jumping.  If you would like to join us as one of our RDA riders,
           <a href="http://www.avonridingcentre.org.uk/disabled-riding/">
            then click here to find out more…
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.avonridingcentre.org.uk/disabled-riding/">
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-3363" height="325" sizes="(max-width: 488px) 100vw, 488px" src="http://www.avonridingcentre.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/IMG_2264.jpg" srcset="http://www.avonridingcentre.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/IMG_2264.jpg 5184w, http://www.avonridingcentre.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/IMG_2264-300x200.jpg 300w, http://www.avonridingcentre.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/IMG_2264-1024x683.jpg 1024w, http://www.avonridingcentre.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/IMG_2264-624x416.jpg 624w" width="488"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           We also cater to non-disabled riders.  As well as helping to subsidise our important charity work, our non-disabled riders benefit from the use of our excellent facilities, including an Olympic sized indoor arena, and over 100 acres of land to hack on.  We are a BHS approved Centre, and all of our instructors are BHS qualified.
           <a href="http://www.avonridingcentre.org.uk/non-disabled-riding/">
            To find out more about becoming a non-disabled rider, click here…
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.avonridingcentre.org.uk/non-disabled-riding/">
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-2240" height="414" sizes="(max-width: 485px) 100vw, 485px" src="http://www.avonridingcentre.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/P3140088.jpg" srcset="http://www.avonridingcentre.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/P3140088.jpg 480w, http://www.avonridingcentre.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/P3140088-300x256.jpg 300w" width="485"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           We could not continue our vital work without our brilliant volunteers.  Everyone involved in our RDA Riding programme, including many of the coaches are volunteers.  We rely on volunteers to help run lessons, either by sidewalking, leading, or coaching, to help support the yard team with grooming and mucking out, or to open the cafe, creating a friendly and welcoming environment for parents and carers to wait during the lessons.
           <a href="http://www.avonridingcentre.org.uk/volunteer/">
