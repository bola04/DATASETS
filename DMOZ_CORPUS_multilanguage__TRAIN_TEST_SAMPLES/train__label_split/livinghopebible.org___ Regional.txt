           <p>
            <br/>
            Sundays at Living Hope
           </p>
          </div>
         </header>
         <div class="text-content text-1 editable" data-category="text" data-id="9729422">
          <div>
           <p style="font-weight: 700; font-size: 1.1068em;">
            9:15 - Sunday Schools (various classes, 6th grade through adult) and Jr. Worship (5th grade and below)
           </p>
           <p style="font-weight: 700; font-size: 1.1068em;">
            10:30 - Worship Service and Children's Sunday School (5th grade and below)
           </p>
           <p style="font-weight: 700; font-size: 1.1068em;">
            12:00 - Fellowship Gathering
           </p>
           <p style="font-size: 1.1068em;">
            <br/>
           </p>
           <p style="font-size: 1.1068em;">
           </p>
           <p style="font-size: 1.1068em;">
            Our Sunday mornings begin with a study of the Word to engage our minds with biblical truth and to prepare our hearts for worship. We have classes at 9:15 am for adults as well as middle school and high school students. The children have a joint children's worship (Pre-K through 5th grade in Room 206) and a volunteer-staffed Nursery for infants and toddlers.
           </p>
           <p style="font-size: 1.1068em;">
           </p>
           <p style="font-size: 1.1068em;">
            Take a look at our
            <a class="cloverlinks" data-category="link" data-detail="344504" data-location="existing" href="/sunday-ministries/adult-sunday-school" style="color: rgb(0, 56, 116);" target="_self">
             adult Sunday School classes
            </a>
            ,
            <a class="cloverlinks" data-category="link" data-detail="344508" data-location="existing" href="/sunday-ministries/youth-sunday-school" style="color: rgb(0, 56, 116);" target="_self">
             middle school and high school classes
            </a>
            , or
            <a class="cloverlinks" data-category="link" data-detail="344512" data-location="existing" href="/sunday-ministries/childrens-ministry" style="color: rgb(0, 56, 116);" target="_self">
             Children's Ministry
            </a>
            for more details.
           </p>
           <p style="font-size: 1.1068em;">
            <br/>
           </p>
           <p style="font-size: 1.1068em;">
           </p>
           <p style="font-size: 1.1068em;">
            At 10:30 am, we begin our worship service with praise and singing before we hear the message of God's word preached. At this time, the children also have their Sunday school classes. For children 3 and under, nursery care is provided. We also encourage you to stay after service for some fellowship and refreshments downstairs in the Fellowship Hall.
           </p>
