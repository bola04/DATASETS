            Based in College Point, New York, Independent Temperature Control
 Services, Inc. (ITC) specializes in providing and installing automatic
 temperature control systems in commercial and industrial buildings. The
 company’s projects range from buildings as small as 10,000 square feet
 to more than a million square feet of high rise construction. As a
 turnkey temperature control contractor, ITC is active in the retrofit,
 energy management and service markets. In 1984, ITC became the
 exclusive authorized licensee for Robertshaw Controls in the New York
 metropolitan area. Because of ITC’s high level of excellence, it was
 consistently rated as one of the top 10 controls contractors in the
 nation.

            In 1994, ITC signed an agreement with Johnson Controls, making the
 company an exclusive Authorized Building Control Specialist (ABCS).
 Today, ITC continues to rank as one of Johnson Controls’ top ten
 ABCS's. Designing control systems with extreme care and expertise, ITC
 has developed an unparalleled reputation for completing projects on
 time and on budget.
            <h2>
             <strong>
              The Value of Value Engineering
             </strong>
            </h2>
            One
 reason so many well-known clients turn to ITC time and again is that
 the company offers first-rate services customized to meet each
 customer’s financial needs. For example, ITC saves clients untold
 amounts of money by value engineering their projects, factoring in the
 cost of equipment, installation and energy consumption. By making these
 calculations during the design phase of the project, ITC often lowers
 both the cost of installation and energy consumption.

 built and operated and to create environmentally responsible,
 profitable and healthy places to live and work in the greater New York
