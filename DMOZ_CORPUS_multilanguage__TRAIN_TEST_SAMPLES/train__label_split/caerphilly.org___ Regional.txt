          Caerphilly Woodlands Trust
         </h1>
        </header>
        <!-- .entry-header -->
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.caerphilly.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/11/Purple-flower.jpg">
           <img alt="Purple flower" class="size-medium wp-image-53 alignright" height="271" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="http://www.caerphilly.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/11/Purple-flower-300x271.jpg" srcset="http://www.caerphilly.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/11/Purple-flower-300x271.jpg 300w, http://www.caerphilly.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/11/Purple-flower.jpg 449w" width="300"/>
          </a>
          Welcome to the Caerphilly Woodlands Trust website!
         </p>
         <p>
          The objective of this site is to promote the wealth of nature and beauty found at Coed-y-Werin.  We also hope to educate people, not only about the sites impressive history and geology, but also on our ongoing efforts in protecting and maintaining the area for both wildlife, and people for many generations to come.
         </p>
         <p>
          Whether you are lucky enough to have this piece of paradise on your doorstep, have experienced the woods before, or you are planning a trip here, we would love to hear from you!
         </p>
         <p>
          Note: If you are a member but are not receiving our newsletters please let us know through the
          <a href="http://www.caerphilly.org/contact-us/">
           Contact
          </a>
          page.
         </p>
