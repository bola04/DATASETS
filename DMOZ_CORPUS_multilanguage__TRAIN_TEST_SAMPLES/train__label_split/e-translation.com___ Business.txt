       <b>
        Global Languages &amp; Cultures, Inc. (GLC)
       </b>
       is a multi-service translation service company, headquartered in Chicago, specializing in technical, pharmaceutical, patent and legal translations since 1993. We are committed to providing
       <a href="compedge.html">
        top quality
       </a>
       translation, interpretation, voice-over, localization and desktop publishing services in all languages at competitive rates.
       <p>
        With a myriad of translators located all over the world, you will receive native-speaking expert
        <a href="services.html">
         language services
        </a>
        relevant to the country of your client's choice. Not only will this ensure accurate dialect and regional considerations, but also cultural sensitivity.
       </p>
       <p>
        Whether it is technical translation, interpretation, software or web site localization, patent translation, voice-over, or another language-related service that you or your company is looking for, we can help! To find out more about some of our services, please click on the
