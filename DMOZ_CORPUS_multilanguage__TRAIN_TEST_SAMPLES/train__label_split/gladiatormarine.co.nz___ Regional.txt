        <div class="section-boxed">
         DONNA HAS WON THE INTERNATIONAL GAMEFISHING ASSOCIATION BEST WORLD RECORD OF 2014 WITH HER PACIFIC BLUEFIN TUNA.  SHE WAS PRESENTED WITH HER TROPHY AT THE PRIZEGIVING HELD IN FLORIDA IN APRIL 2015.
         <p>
          The IGFA also confirmed that Donna Pascoe now holds the All Tackle World Record and the Women’s 60kg lineweight Womens World Record for her Pacific Bluefin Tuna that she caught on 19 February 2014 onboard charter boat Gladiator during the National Tournament. The tuna weighed 411.6kg. The crew onboard Gladiator consisted of Skipper Scott Sutherland, Ken Pascoe, Paddy Bohane and Nathan Carr-Clarke. More on the catch under the ‘News’ section.
         </p>

         <p>
          <br class="clear"/>
          Enjoy Unrivalled Luxury Onboard Gladiator
         </p>
         <p>
          Available for corporate events, harbour cruising and fishing charters
          <br/>
          Conveniently located in Auckland’s Viaduct Harbour
          <br/>
          We serve Auckland and the Hauraki Gulf – contact Ken on 027 2727350 or via email at ken@gladiatormarine.co.nz
         </p>
         <p>
          Owned and skippered by Ken Pascoe and operating out of the Viaduct Basin, Gladiator is one of Auckland’s leading charter boats. Gladiator is available for hire for any period of time from 3 hours to 3 weeks and offers the most competitive charter boat rates in Auckland.
         </p>
         <p>
          Gladiator is a 55 foot luxury sports fisher equipped with Sky TV and a quality Bose surround sound music system, making it the ideal charter boat for your next corporate event, fishing trip, or simply cruising on Auckland Harbour and the Hauraki Gulf.
         </p>
         <p>
          As a fully equipped fishing charter boat we provide top quality fishing equipment for hire at very reasonable rates, along with full food and beverage catering or BYO as required.
         </p>
         <p>
          Outside the Auckland region some of our most popular charter destinations include Waiheke Island, Kawau Island and Great Barrier Island, however we are also able to take private fishing charters (including big game fishing) just about anywhere in New Zealand waters. Regardless of your chosen destination, our friendly and vastly experienced crew will ensure your fishing trip is a memorable one.
         </p>
         <p>
          To enquire about chartering Gladiator for your next corporate event or to find out more about hiring Gladiator for a fishing trip, get in touch with Ken on 027 2727350 or via email at ken@gladiatormarine.co.nz
         </p>

          We look forward to giving you the experience of a lifetime!
