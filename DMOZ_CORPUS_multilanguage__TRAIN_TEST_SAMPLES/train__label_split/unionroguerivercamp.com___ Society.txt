             <h4 style="text-align: center;">
              Our Annual Union Rogue Baptist Camp (Youth and Family)  is held in July
              <a href="http://www.unionroguerivercamp.com/union-rogue-baptist-camp">
               Click Here for more info
              </a>
             </h4>
             <p>
              A beautiful, rustic, Rogue River Oregon retreat. Specializing in offering you a rustic Rogue River camp experience while providing some of the comforts of home. Our cabins and lodge are perfect for large or small group camping such as a retreat, conference, youth group camp, wedding, family reunion, outdoor recreation group or for those who want to just get away from the hussel  of modern city life.
              <br/>
              <a href="http://www.unionroguerivercamp.com/location.html">
               Centrally located
              </a>
              to
              <a href="http://www.unionroguerivercamp.com/season.html">
               year round
              </a>
              <a href="http://www.unionroguerivercamp.com/local.html">
               recreational
              </a>
              ,
              <a href="http://www.unionroguerivercamp.com/nature.html">
               educational
              </a>
              ,
              <a href="http://www.unionroguerivercamp.com/history.html">
               historical
              </a>
              , and
              <a href="http://www.unionroguerivercamp.com/around.html">
               cultural interests
              </a>
              and
              <a href="http://www.unionroguerivercamp.com/local.html">
               events
              </a>
              .
             </p>
             <p>
              We welcome you to enter into the pages of our preserved slice of heaven located among a hidden canopy of old growth trees right next to the legendary wild and scenic Upper Rogue River.
             </p>
             <p>
              We hope you’ll consider this spectacular Oregon retreat as you search for group camping rentals for your small or large group. You’ll find very reasonable rates and a wonderful camping experience in any season!
             </p>
