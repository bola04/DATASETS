           Welcome to Heidel Hollow Farm!
          </h1>

         <!-- .entry-header -->
         <div class="entry-content">
          <div>
           <a href="http://heidelhollowfarm.com/deer-blocks/">
            <img alt="" class="wp-image-162 aligncenter" height="456" sizes="(max-width: 519px) 100vw, 519px" src="http://heidelhollowfarm.com/wp/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/Deercharacter-300x264.jpg" srcset="http://heidelhollowfarm.com/wp/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/Deercharacter-300x264.jpg 300w, http://heidelhollowfarm.com/wp/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/Deercharacter-768x676.jpg 768w, http://heidelhollowfarm.com/wp/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/Deercharacter-1024x901.jpg 1024w, http://heidelhollowfarm.com/wp/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/Deercharacter.jpg 1275w" width="519"/>

          <div>
          </div>
          <div id="NewsList0">
           <strong>
            The Egg Room
           </strong>
           <span style="color: #808080; font-family: Arial;">
           </span>
          </div>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="color: #808080; font-family: Arial;">
            Customers and friends still know our farm store as the “Egg Room.” For many years, this is where we did our egg packing from laying hens and where you could come and watch us pack eggs directly from the hen house and also purchase eggs and potatoes. Though the chickens are at another farm, you can still buy eggs as well as a variety of vegetables and fruits grown by son Michael during the summer season.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="color: #808080; font-family: Arial;">
            Starting In May, we will have a full selection of Bedding and Vegetable plants, hanging baskets, herbs, and mixed planters for sale at the farm.? All plants are grown here in our Greenhouse facilities.? Stop in to stock your gardens for the season!
            <br/>
            In June and July, we start with seasonal produce such as strawberries, Mike Fink’s Sweet Corn, tomatoes, peppers, local peaches, watermelon, and much, much more! Keep stopping by all summer for a great variety of fresh, local produce.
            <br/>
            In the fall, we switch gears to pumpkins grown right here on the farm, mums, local apples and cider, and fall decorations, as well as home-grown spaghetti, butternut, and acorn squash.
            <br/>
            All winter long, we can supply your home with fresh eggs and local potatoes.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="color: #808080; font-family: Arial;">
            The “Egg Room Store” is open Monday-Friday from 8am-4pm, and Saturdays from 9am-1pm.
           </span>
          </p>

             </a>
             .
            </span>
           </p>

          <p>
           <span style="color: #808080;">
            Mike Fink is a fifth generation of the Fink family to continue farming. Mike Fink started working with vegetables for 4-H projects at an early age.
            <br/>
            By the age of 18, he had over 25 acres of vegetables in production for wholesale and retail sales. Visit the Mike Fink’s Produce website at?

Though the chickens are at another farm, you can still buy eggs as well as a variety of vegetables and fruits grown by son Michael during the summer season.

            <br/>
            In the fall, we switch gears to pumpkins grown right here on the farm, mums, local apples and cider, and fall decorations, as well as home-grown spaghetti, butternut, and acorn squash.
            <br/>
            All winter long, we can supply your home with fresh eggs and local potatoes.
