          <h2>
           Philosophy
          </h2>
          <p>
           <br/>
           Our aim at Tip Top Hair Design is to offer our clientele a warm and relaxing visit to the salon they'd like to repeat again and again. We have a beautiful salon, with a team of professional and dedicated stylists trained to the highest of standards, ensuring you leave Tip Top looking and feeling sensational.
           <br/>
           Whether customers are wanting the tiniest of trims for the health of the hair, or a dramatic restyle, they are given the same high level of service.
           <br/>
           We guarantee a warm welcome and friendly smile from the moment our clients first connect with us; we want them to feel we are always there and that nothing is too much trouble.
           <br/>
           From online bookings to our online shop, it couldn't be easier to tailor their experience with us. Connecting on Facebook and Twitter means we are there for our customers whenever they need us giving Tip Top that personal touch.
           <br/>
           We pride ourselves in looking after the whole client by creating a space for children to relax and play while their parents enjoy a stress free visit.
           <br/>
           From free parking to cottage craft industries and tearooms all set in the grounds of a stately home, perfection!
           <br/>
          </p>
          <h2>
           About
          </h2>
          <p>
           Tip Top Hair Design, are a well established hairdressers with a team of dedicated hairstylists to ensure you receive an excellent service, in and out of the hair salon.
          </p>
          <p>
           Conveniently situated in The Ferrers Centre, Staunton Harold, nr Ashby de la Zouch, Leicestershire. There is a great selection of craft and gift shops, tea rooms and a garden centre which makes it an ideal place to visit if you fancy incorporating your appointment with a relaxing trip out. The beautiful park setting means there's no stressful crowds, or noisy traffic to worry about - and no shortage of parking either.
          </p>
          <p>
           Tip Top Hair Design offer a friendly and warm service, a range of colouring and styling services, Great Lengths hair extensions and stocks a fantastic range of hair products Offical Stockist of
           <a href="http://www.tiptophairdesign.co.uk/shop/ghd-hair-straighteners">
            GHD
           </a>
           , Matrix Biolarge
           <a href="http://www.tiptophairdesign.co.uk/shop/color-wow">
            Color Wow
           </a>
           and Great Lengths, with Matrix SoColour Beauty, Sync and ColourInsider for those technical colour services, We are also the Midlands stockist for hr Fabulous Eye of Horus eye and Brow makeup.
           <a href="http://www.tiptophairdesign.co.uk/shop/">
            new online shop please feel free to visit.
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Tip Top specialises in Special Occasions, Bridal Hair and Hair Extensions and stresses the importance of a personal service.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Catering for all the needs of ladies, gents and children.
          </h2>
          <p>
           Tip Top Hair Design boasts a fantastic child friendly area designed especially to provide a range of safe fun activities for little ones alongside state of the art salon facilities. Children remain the parents responsibility, but have their own area within the room including a great range of toys and books, There's a sofa to snuggle up and have a rest on, and a choice of children's DVDs.
          </p>
          <p>
           Are you ready to change your look, impress and turn heads. Book a free consultation with one of our talented stylists to discuss the styles you like and how we can make you look and feel sensational.
          </p>

            offer online bookings
           </a>
           making it easier for clients to book appointments, we are also on Facebook and Twitter.
