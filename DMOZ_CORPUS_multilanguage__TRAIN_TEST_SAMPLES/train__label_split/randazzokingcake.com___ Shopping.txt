       <h2 style="color:#F9E48B;">
        Manny Randazzo King Cakes, New Orleans
       </h2>
       Manny Randazzo King Cakes is New Orleans' choice for delicious king cakes and unparalleled service. This Randazzo family recipe has become a New Orleans Mardi Gras tradition for decades. Customers throughout the New Orleans area and across the nation agree that these freshly-baked and one-of-a-kind king cakes are the tastiest in the business. Manny Randazzo himself sees to the perfection of these unique cakes and allows his family recipe to express his passion for king cakes, New Orleans, and Mardi Gras itself.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       While Randazzo proudly services New Orleanians and Louisianians alike, the local, New Orleans flavor of Manny Randazzo King Cakes extends beyond the Crescent City. Seasonal delivery to any location across the country ensures customers everywhere get a taste of king cake and a chance to find KC, the famous Randazzo king cake baby. With such a passion for king cakes, Randazzo bakes these specialty cakes all year long and for every season. Why only eat your king cakes during Mardi Gras? Enjoy a cake for Valentine's Day, Easter, Thanksgiving, and Christmas. No tailgate is complete without this New Orleans tradition. Cheer on the Tigers and the Saints with uniquely designed cakes to support your team!
       <br/>
       <br/>
       In Season, come visit us at the shop and pick up a taste of New Orleans Mardi Gras tradition. Manny Randazzo King Cakes are the perfect gift or dessert for your Christmas party. Celebrate the festivities of Mardi Gras with KC and our beautifully decorated and homemade king cakes. You haven't tasted Mardi Gras until you've tasted Manny Randazzo King Cakes!
