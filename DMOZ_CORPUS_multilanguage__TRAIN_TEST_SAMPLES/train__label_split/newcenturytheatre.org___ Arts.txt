                    <p class="style17" style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0;">

                    <p class="style17" style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0;">
                     Dear Friends of NCT,
                    </p>
                    <p class="style17" style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0;">
                    </p>
                    <p class="style17" style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0;">
                     On March 4, I announced that New Century Theatre would not be presenting a summer season and would instead be on hiatus for the year. Our small but enthusiastic board will use this time to begin raising money to cover our modest expenses this year and make plans to mount a season in 2019. Part of the plan is to present a few fund-raising events before year’s end. We will send out information once things are finalized.
                    </p>
                    <p class="style17">
                     I wish to thank all of you who have reached out to me to share how much the company has meant to you over the years and to express your unwavering
                     <br/>
                     support.
                    </p>
                    <p class="style17">
                     New Century Theatre isn’t participating in this year’s VALLEY GIVES DAY on May 1st  but that doesn’t mean we don’t welcome your donations given at any point during the year. When New Century Theatre began in 1991 we began by asking for support from the community before any show was every mounted – we asked for an investment in what could be. The resulting  “Century Club” was instrumental in the success of our first decade of operation.
                    </p>
                    <p class="style17">
                     Your gift now to NCT would be an investment in productions yet to be. You can send any contributions to: New Century Theatre, P.O. Box 186, Northampton, MA 01061.
                    </p>
                    <p class="style17">
                     Gratefully,
                    </p>
                    <p class="style17">
                     Sam Rush,
                     <br/>
                     Producing Director
                    </p>
