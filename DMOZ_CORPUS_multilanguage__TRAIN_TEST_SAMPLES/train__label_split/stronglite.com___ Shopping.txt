          Massage Table and Chair Packages
         </h1>
         <div style="float: left; padding: 0 66px 0 58px; width: 372px;">
          <h2>
           Massage Tables That Are Built to Last
          </h2>
          <p>
           Stronglite products are designed with durability and safety in mind. Throughout our entire manufacturing process we take every measure to ensure your
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.stronglite.com/massage-table-packages.html">
             massage table
            </a>
           </strong>
           or
           <a href="http://www.stronglite.com/massage-chair-packages.html">
            massage chair
           </a>
           is built with attention and care. Safety is our number one priority, which is why all of our equipment is tested with a computerized stress analysis. By doing this we can make sure there are no weak points and that all of our equipment will hold up even in the most strenuous conditions. Our massage equipment holds up under conditions where most would not. Since our chairs are built with such strength and safety in mind, you and your clients will feel secure knowing your chair will not fail.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div style="float: left; width: 406px;">
          <h2>
           The Stronglite Massage Table Difference
          </h2>
          <p>
           At Stronglite we believe in function over form. While our
           <strong>
            massage tables
           </strong>
           and chairs are beautiful they are built for functionality. This is why you may not see a laser-cut logo on our equipment because we would prefer to put two useful knobs there instead. While safety is our top priority; durability, functionality, value, and aesthetics are all important. We work to find a balance between all of these traits, so that your chair will be a long lasting, reliable piece of equipment that you will love. We are always looking to improve our business, so please let us know if you have any suggestions or comments about our products. If we work together, we can build a stronger bodywork community and in turn a healthier world.
          </p>
