           <p>
            This is the site for the churches of Walsden, St Peter, Cornholme, St Michael, and Todmorden, St Mary which make up the Todmorden United Benefice.
           </p>
           <p>
            We are a part of the Diocese of Leeds,  in the Huddersfield Episcopal Area
           </p>
           <p>
            We are the Church of England in our three valleys,  we love God and want to share that love in all we do.
           </p>

            Click on  the  links above to find out a bit more about us
