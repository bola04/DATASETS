      <h2>
       <div class="a-area a-normal a-area-about_header singleton aText clearfix" id="a-area-1-about_header">
        <div class="a-slots clearfix" id="a-slots-1-about_header">

           We have a wide range of stone &amp; granite worktops suitable for the kitchen and bathroom.
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>

      </h2>
      <div class="a-area a-normal a-area-about-summary singleton aRichText clearfix" id="a-area-1-about-summary">
       <div class="a-slots clearfix" id="a-slots-1-about-summary">
        <div class="a-slot a-normal aRichText clearfix" data-name="about-summary" data-pageid="1" data-permid="1" id="a-slot-1-about-summary-1">
         <div class="a-slot-content clearfix" id="a-slot-content-1-about-summary-1">
          <p>
           Welcome to the fine world of Granite &amp; Quartz Stone worktops from County Stone Granite Ltd West Sussex.
          </p>
          <p>
           An established family business with over 20 years experience, where we pride ourselves in fine workmanship and excellence of quality. Our reputation has been built, both locally and nationally.
          </p>
          <p>
           Here at County Stone Granite, based in the South of England, we specialise in the manufacturing of granite kitchen worktops and granite bathroom worktops for both commercial and private markets, plus the finest handcrafted fire surrounds and limestone fireplaces.
          </p>
          <p>
           Please feel free to come and see us for inspiration, advice, design ideas and product choice. We look forward to welcoming you to County Stone Granite - after all, it's our product...and our passion!
          </p>
