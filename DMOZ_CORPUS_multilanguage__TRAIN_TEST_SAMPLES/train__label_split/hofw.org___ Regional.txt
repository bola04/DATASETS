         </font>
        </font>
       </b>
      </h2>
      <ul>
       <font face="TAHOMA">
        <p>
         Welcome to the homepage of the Humanists of Ft. Worth, an organization for non-theists, freethinkers, and atheists in Cowtown. Please take the time to navigate through our site. It should answer some of your questions about humanism and 
 our organization.
        </p>
        <p>
         We offer thought-provoking programs, social activities, and a humanist voice to the community of Ft. Worth. We also offer the services of a
         <a href="minister.htm">
          Humanist Minister
         </a>
         for those who desire weddings, namings, and memorials without religious overtones.
        </p>
        <p>
         Please do not hesitate to
         <a href="cont.htm">
          contact
         </a>
         us with any further questions. You are welcome
 to visit our meetings and take advantage of our programs.
        </p>
