        <h1>
         Orange County Permanent Makeup Specialist
        </h1>
        <br/>
        <p>
         We all know that true beauty isn’t skin deep, but it is also  true that having wonderful looking skin and facial features makes us feel more  confident and look healthier.  With
         <a href="http://www.beautymarksbycandace.com/about.html">
          permanent makeup in  Orange County CA
         </a>
         you can have your skin looking beautiful all the time with  the wide variety of makeup options that can be permanently introduced onto your  skin.
        </p>
        <p>
         Orange  County permanent makeup is considered to be a fairly simple yet innovative  form of the art of beautification. You may have even heard it called "facial tattooing".  It provides women with the opportunity to  spend less time in front of the mirror everyday worrying about how they look or  whether their makeup has smudged, and more time living life to its fullest  while knowing they look their best.
        </p>
        <p>
         In the business of permanently applying beauty since 2000,  Candace Nichols of Beauty Marks by Candace has the artistic expertise and  in-depth training necessary to fittingly apply proper shapes and shades of  color to suit each client’s face, producing a very natural looking application  of permanent cosmetics.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Orange County Permanent Makeup Services
        </h2>
        <p>
         Candace Nichols works on a wide variety of
         <a href="http://www.beautymarksbycandace.com/faqs.html">
          permanent makeup in Orange  County
         </a>
         , including mobile, in-house and in-store Orange County airbrush  tanning which can be scheduled as a single tanning event or as multiple airbrush  tanning visits.  Anyone can attain that  perfect glow to their skin with these
         <a href="http://www.beautymarksbycandace.com/pricing.html">
          Orange County airbrush  tanning
         </a>
         services.
        </p>
        <p>
         For
         <a href="http://www.beautymarksbycandace.com/pricing.html">
          permanent eye makeup
         </a>
         in Orange County,  Candace offers
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          California permanent eyebrows
         </li>
         <li>
          eyebrows tattoos and eyebrow  tinting
         </li>
         <li>
          enhancement to eyeliner, and permanent eyeliner
         </li>
         <li>
          eyelash tinting, and  eyelash extensions
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         For
         <a href="http://www.beautymarksbycandace.com/pricing.html">
          lip tattoos
         </a>
         and  permanent makeup for lips, she offers:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          permanent lip coloring
         </li>
         <li>
          full  lip color, and permanent lip liner
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Candace also handles
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.beautymarksbycandace.com/gallery.html">
           Orange County permanent  makeup
          </a>
          touch-up work
         </strong>
         , which is generally performed within one month after  an initial permanent cosmetics application in order to let the new pigment  settle into the skin, thus showing its true color.  Additionally she handles permanent cosmetics  correction work on any permanent makeup which may have been unsuitably applied  elsewhere.
        </p>
