               <p>

               <h2 style="text-align: center;">
                It really is a love story.
               </h2>
               <p style="text-align: center;">
                A lovely girl meets an awesome boy, they hang about together for a bit and become a couple. Soon they meet each other’s lovely friends, weird friends and crazy friends and they meet their wonderful families.
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: center;">
                They fall madly in love, so much in love that they cannot imagine being apart. Then one day this awesome boy asks the lovely girl to marry him and she says YES!
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: center;">
                So now they are planning a wedding……it’s going to be the day of their dreams……it could be in a Scottish castle or by the beach or in a country hotel……it might be an elopement with just the two of them or they may want all their families and friends around them.
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: center;">
                They want some great food, a few beers to get the party started, a band to keep the dance floor full until the wee,small hours. The bride will look stunning in “the dress” and the bridesmaids will want to look fabulous too. Kilts will be bought and fresh flowers ordered.
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: center;">
                They will need a really good photographer to document their whole day. Someone who is going to be unobtrusive and who will let them get on with enjoying themselves but who will capture all the wonderful moments and the love between these two very special people.
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: center;">
                That’s where I come in : and so if you are looking for a photographer and you like my pictures then please get in touch with me. I’d love to have a chat about your very special wedding day.
               </p>
               <div>
                <p style="text-align: center;">
                 I specialise in photographing weddings in St Andrews and Dundee and love working at my favourite Scottish venues :

                </p>

                 Please feel free to call on 07766758085 or email me ally@allystuartphotography.co.uk
                </p>
