     <span class="introtext">
      <p style="padding-left:10px;">
       Welcome to
       <a href="http://www.HebrewArt.com">
        HebrewArt.com
       </a>
       , the online art gallery of Judaica artist Adam Rhine. Since 1999, I have created ornate, highly detailed Judaic paintings in acrylic, watercolor, colored pencil, and ink. Each work is crafted to glorify the infinite nobility of the Lord and visually bring forth His energy within different combinations and intricate patterns.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       My works have appeared in  the full-color
       <a href="https://www.amazon.com/Hebrew-Illuminations-2019-Wall-Calendar/dp/163136409X/" target="_blank">
        Hebrew Illuminations Wall Calendar
       </a>
       for the past thirteen years.
       <a href="https://www.amazon.com/Hebrew-Illuminations-2018-Wall-Calendar/dp/1631362755/" target="_blank">
        Last year's edition
       </a>
       was the #1 Jewish wall calendar on Amazon.
      </p>
      <p style="padding-left:10px;">
       For those who enjoy adult coloring books, the
       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Hebrew-Illuminations-Coloring-Book-Journey/dp/1631362399/" target="_blank">
        Coloring Journey through the Jewish Holy Days -A Coloring Book for Adults
       </a>
       and the
       <a href="https://www.amazon.com/Coloring-Your-Jewish-Year-Calendar/dp/163136264X/" target="_blank">
        Coloring Your Jewish Year Wall Calendar
       </a>
       are both on Amazon.com today.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Forty-four of my paintings are collected together in the
       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Hebrew-Illuminations-Adam-Rhine/dp/B002QGSYXQ/" target="_blank">
        Hebrew Illuminations hardcover art book
       </a>
       . It's also available as a
       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Hebrew-Illuminations-Adam-Rhine-ebook/dp/B001HBI6PC/" target="_blank">
        Kindle e-book
       </a>
       on Amazon.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       I am also a featured artist on
       <a href="http://www.ketubah.com/artist/adam-rhine" target="_blank">
        Ketubah.com
       </a>
       , the largest manufacturer of Jewish wedding contracts in North America. Over 20 of my designs are available as Ketubot for newlywed couples.
       <br/>
       <br/>
      </p>
