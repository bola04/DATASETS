                <p>
                 In the week 3 race on July 3, Tom Petrykiewicz was the overall winner, running the 5k course in 21:19. Joe Michelini (22:34) and Ron Burkey (22:37) finished in second and third.
                </p>
                <p>
                 Taylor Guthrie was the first female finisher, with a winning time of  21:44.  Lillian Sarno (22:02) and Brittany Wissel (22:28) finished second and third.

               <font class="content">
                The Ocean Running Club was well represented at the 19th Annual Pine Beach 5K on Sunday June 24th. Over 400 runners cruised the quiet streets of Pine Beach, finishing along the Toms River. It was nice to see so many ORC racing singlets lining up for the race and walking around the field after finishing.  Kyle Kroon was the overall winner with a time of 16:22. Shlomo Schreiber (16:58) and Marquis Cox (17:32) finished in second and third place.  Molly Applegate was the first female to cross the line with a winning time of 18:00. Jennifer Malavlta (19:11) and Dianne Deoliveira (19:24) finished in second and third place.
                <p>
                 Congratulations to race director, Bethanie Adams, and the Ocean Running Club for putting on another successful event. Thank you to all the volunteers!
