        <h2 class="entry-title">
         Welcome
        </h2>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          <a href="http://saintc.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/12/Picture-009.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="alignleft wp-image-495" height="270" sizes="(max-width: 179px) 100vw, 179px" src="http://saintc.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/12/Picture-009-199x300.jpg" srcset="http://saintc.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/12/Picture-009-199x300.jpg 199w, http://saintc.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/12/Picture-009-679x1024.jpg 679w, http://saintc.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/12/Picture-009.jpg 1168w" title="Sanctuary Light" width="179"/>
          </a>
          Saint Columba Church is a parish of the Anglican Church in North America, located in Fresno, California.
         </p>
         <p>
          We are an outpost of God’s Kingdom, proclaiming good news: God is rescuing the world through the death and resurrection of Jesus Christ, and all who receive this gift and follow the Lord Jesus are transformed by His victory and share in His love.
         </p>
         <p>
          We are a pilgrim people, of all sorts and conditions, drawn together in one family as we “
          <em>
           Hear God’s Call… Answer with Faith
          </em>
          .”
         </p>
         <p>
          Look around this site to get to know us… or drop by on Sunday morning, or visit one of our Bible studies or other events.  We would love to share God’s love with you.
         </p>
         <p>
          May the grace of our Lord Jesus Christ, and the Love of God, and the fellowship of the Holy Spirit be with you.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
