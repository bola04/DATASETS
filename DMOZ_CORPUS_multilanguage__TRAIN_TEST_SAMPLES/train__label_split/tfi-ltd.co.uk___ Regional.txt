         <p class="title">
          Creating 
               Wealth From Emerging &amp; Disruptive Technologies
         </p>
         <p class="title">
         </p>
         <p class="background">
          Technology for Industry is transfering technology 
               from the research base into volume manufacturing.
         </p>
         <p class="background">
         </p>
         <p class="background">
          On behalf of industrial clients, we search for 
               technologies that can match a real unfulfilled market need.
         </p>
         <p class="background">
         </p>
         <p class="background">
          We work with Universities to maximise the value 
               in their IPR pipeline by selection of the most appropriate route 
               forward, whether that be by licensing technology, creating joint 
               ventures with supply chain partners or by spin out company formation.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          A search for new technology begins with a thorough assessment of 
               the market and product needs so that the most appropriate technology 
               can be selected.
         </p>
         <p class="background">
         </p>
         <p class="background">
          Our technology focus is on Materials Science, 
               Chemistry, Microelectronics, and Photonics. These are the underpinning 
               foundation for the recent growth in micro and nanotechnology.
         </p>
         <p class="background">
         </p>
         <p class="background">
          We cover most market sectors for the above technologies. 
               Our particular focus is on Healthcare, Pharmaceuticals, Specialty 
               Chemicals, Food &amp; Nutraceuticals.
         </p>
         <p class="background">
         </p>
         <p class="background">
          Whether your team is strong technically or business 
               orientated, we can provide you with the matching skills, knowledge 
               and experience to use technology to grow your business.
         </p>
