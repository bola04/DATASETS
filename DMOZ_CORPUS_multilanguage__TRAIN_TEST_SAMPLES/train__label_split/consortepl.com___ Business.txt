               </a>
              </h3>

               </a>
              </h3>
             </header>
             <div class="iconbox_content_container " itemprop="text">

With SLREC installed, an SL controller expands the heating control options to include temperature and time control. The SLREC can control a load up to 3kW and has a maximum switching current of 13A/250V. Users have a choice of four different SL controllers to control heating:- the SLPB, SLVT, SLVTB or SLTI. Multiple heaters can be controlled by an SL controller.
