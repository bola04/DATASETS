        <h3>
         Since 1980, SRC Refrigeration has earned a reputation as THE commercial refrigeration specialists having sold thousands of best-in-business walk-in coolers, walk-in freezers, and cold rooms.
        </h3>
        <p>
         <strong>
          SRC Refrigeration designs and builds the highest quality beer caves, restaurant coolers, food service coolers, floral coolers, funeral coolers, combo units, and industrial coolers of all shapes and sizes. We are the preferred choice for contractors and re-sellers.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Located in Sterling Heights, Michigan, SRC has one of the industry's top-rated commercial cooler and commercial freezer-manufacturing facilities. With our highly advanced manufacturing equipment and innovative processes, we can quote, design, build, and ship special orders with custom designed refrigerators, display cases, and freezers faster than anyone in the industry.
        </p>
        <p>
         Our premium commercial walk-in refrigerators, display cases, and walk-in freezers are dependable, energy-efficient, and come with our exclusive 50-year thermal warranty. We use only American-made parts and guarantee our work 100%. With SRC building your cold room, you know it will be built to spec and built to last.
