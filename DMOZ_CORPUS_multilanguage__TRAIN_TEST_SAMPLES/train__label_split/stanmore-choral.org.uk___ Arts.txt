         <h1 style="text-align: left;">
          <strong>
           <em>
            Our Summer Concert for 2018 will be “SUMMER SPIRIT” to be performed on Saturday 7th July at 7.30pm.
           </em>
          </strong>
         </h1>
         <p>
          <a href="http://stanmore-choral.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Summer_spirit_poster.png">
           <img alt="" class=" wp-image-1335 alignleft" height="419" sizes="(max-width: 299px) 100vw, 299px" src="http://stanmore-choral.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Summer_spirit_poster-214x300.png" srcset="http://stanmore-choral.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Summer_spirit_poster-214x300.png 214w, http://stanmore-choral.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Summer_spirit_poster-107x150.png 107w, http://stanmore-choral.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Summer_spirit_poster.png 457w" width="299"/>
          </a>
          “Summer Spirit” will include  “Feel the Spirit” by Rutter, and songs by Chilcott and others.
         </p>
         <p>
          The venue will be St Mary-the-Virgin Church, 3 St. Leonard’s Avenue, Kenton,  HA3 8EJ.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://stanmore-choral.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Summer-2018-SCS-Poster.pdf">
           Click here to download .pdf version of poster.
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <strong>
           <em>
            NEW SINGERS WANTED: we invite you to join us, or inform friends about our search for new singers, particularly tenors and basses to join us on 8th January 2018  when rehearsals for the next concert begin.
           </em>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <strong>
           Enquiries:
          </strong>
          <a href="javascript:DeCryptX('1f1o1r0u1j1s2k2g3v1A0s1u1b2p0m0o2t2g0-3f2j0o2t3d0l0.3r1s3j312w0k')">
           <img alt="enquiries@stanmore-choral.org.uk" class="cryptxImage" src="http://stanmore-choral.org.uk/268e6f551c07c23d09389c1362588e28/enquiries@stanmore-choral.org.uk" title="enquiries@stanmore-choral.org.uk"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          This versatile and friendly non-auditioned choir is currently recruiting Tenors and Basses, although singers for all sections are always welcome.
         </p>
         <p>
          The choir is about 50-strong and draws singers from Stanmore, Harrow, Kenton, Pinner, Ruislip, Watford, Bushey, Barnet, Edgware, Burnt Oak, Colindale, Wembley and Mill Hill. The  conductor is
          <a href="http://stanmore-choral.org.uk/musical-director-2/" title="Musical Director">
           Nick Austin
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          The choir produces three main concerts and a carol concert each year.
          <a href="http://stanmore-choral.org.uk/future-programme-2/" title="Concert programme">
           Click here
          </a>
          for details of this season’s concerts.
         </p>
         <p>
          If you enjoy singing, you are very welcome to come along and
          <a href="http://stanmore-choral.org.uk/join-us-2/" title="Join us">
           join us
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          Rehearsals are on Mondays at 7.45pm at the hall of St William of York Church, 1 Du Cros Drive, Stanmore
          <a href="https://www.google.co.uk/maps/place/Du+Cros+Dr,+Stanmore+HA7+4TJ/@51.6139843,-0.3054197,17z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m5!3m4!1s0x487614138e0d1057:0xa7d0a67857d7bfe1!8m2!3d51.6140338!4d-0.3030207" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
           <b>
            HA7 4TJ
           </b>
          </a>
          and restarted on Monday September 4th 2017.
         </p>
         <p>
          Nearest underground stations are Canons Park or Stanmore; there is also off-street parking.
         </p>
