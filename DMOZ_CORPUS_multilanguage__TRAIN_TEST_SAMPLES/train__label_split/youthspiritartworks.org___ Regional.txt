               <img alt="cropped-YSA_Logo_Small.jpg" class="alignleft wp-image-986 size-medium" height="195" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="http://youthspiritartworks.org/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2016/08/cropped-YSA_Logo_Small-300x195.jpg" srcset="http://youthspiritartworks.org/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2016/08/cropped-YSA_Logo_Small-300x195.jpg 300w, http://youthspiritartworks.org/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2016/08/cropped-YSA_Logo_Small-768x500.jpg 768w, http://youthspiritartworks.org/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2016/08/cropped-YSA_Logo_Small-148x96.jpg 148w, http://youthspiritartworks.org/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2016/08/cropped-YSA_Logo_Small-31x20.jpg 31w, http://youthspiritartworks.org/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2016/08/cropped-YSA_Logo_Small-38x25.jpg 38w, http://youthspiritartworks.org/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2016/08/cropped-YSA_Logo_Small-330x215.jpg 330w, http://youthspiritartworks.org/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2016/08/cropped-YSA_Logo_Small.jpg 920w" width="300"/>
              </a>
              Youth Spirit Artworks (YSA) is an interfaith “green” art jobs and job training program located in Berkeley, California which is committed to empowering homeless and low-income San Francisco Bay Area young people, ages 16-25.
             </p>
             <p>
              YSA was founded in 2007, as a response to the enormous employment challenges of older homeless and low-income youth.
             </p>
             <p>
              The mission of Youth Spirit Artworks is to use art jobs and jobs training to empower and transform the lives of youth, giving young people the skills, experience, and self-confidence needed to meet their full potential.
             </p>
