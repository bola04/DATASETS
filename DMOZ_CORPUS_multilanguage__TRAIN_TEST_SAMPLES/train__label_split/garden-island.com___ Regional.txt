              <font color="#DCDCDC" face="verdana, arial" size="2">
               <b>
                Welcome to Garden Island Lodge on Lady Evelyn Lake
               </b>
               <p>
                In the heart of Ontario's vast northern wilderness is a very special lake, which we are proud to introduce to you. Lady Evelyn Lake is beautiful with pristine shoreline, towering trees, photographic outcroppings of Canadian Shield rock and big water, which is dotted with many islands. It's also famous for unusually large
                <b>
                 Trophy Walleye
                </b>
                , aggressive
                <b>
                 Northern Pike
                </b>
                and feisty
                <b>
                 Smallmouth Bass
                </b>
                . On an island in the most remote area of the lake is Garden Island Lodge.
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <p>
                <b>
                 Fishing
                </b>
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <p>
                Lady Evelyn Lake is stuffed with hard fighting Northern Pike and acrobatic Smallmouth Bass. There are also well-cleared portages where you can easily reach lakes with
                <b>
                 Lake Trout
                </b>
                as well as streams with
                <b>
                 Brook Trout
                </b>
                .
               </p>
               <p>
                Garden Island Lodge's true claim-to-fame is the unusually high population of Giant Trophy Walleye. You can catch lots of good eating size Walleyes for
                <b>
                 shore lunch
                </b>
                or breakfast but your focus should be set on size. There is a good chance that you may catch the biggest Walleye of your life. Lady Evelyn Lake is one of the top Walleye Lakes in Canada for a mix of numbers and trophy size Walleye. No other lake in Ontario consistently produces Walleyes over 30 inches. We even had a guest catch a 36-inch Walleye that weighed an astonishing 18.5 pounds. Lady Evely Lake is a Walleye Fisherperson's dream come true.
               </p>
