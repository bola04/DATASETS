      <h2>
       Anchorage Holiday Units​
      </h2>
      <p>
       Anchorage Holiday Units are fully self contained ground floor 
 			units within walking distance to supermarkets, the boardwalk to Main 
 			Beach, restaurants and shops. The units have two bedrooms, sleeping 
 			up to six people, all linen is provided. Anchorage Holiday units has 
 			been renovated and upgraded. For the comfort of our guests we have 
 			installed heat lamps in the bathrooms and reverse cycle 
 			air-conditioning in each unit. There is undercover car park for each 
 			unit.
      </p>
      本店现由华人全面管理，随时为您提供热情周到的服务。如需了解更多信息欢迎致电我们，在此谨祝您在本镇住宿游玩愉快。
      <h4>
       CLOSE TO EVERYTHING
      </h4>
      <p>
       As there are only 8 units in the complex, which is built in such 
 			a way as to face away from the road, you will be able to enjoy a 
 			quiet peaceful time away from home.  The units are situated 
 			only 50 metres from the centre of town.  As Lakes Entrance is a 
 			small coastal town you will be able to leave your car at the unit 
 			and walk to most attractions.
      </p>
