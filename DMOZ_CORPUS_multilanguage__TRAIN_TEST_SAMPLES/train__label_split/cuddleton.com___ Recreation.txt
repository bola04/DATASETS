      <h2>
       Breeding Gorgeous British Shorthair Cats &amp; Kittens in Perth, Western Australia
      </h2>
      <p>
       If there is just one word to describe how we feel about our cats and kittens it's devoted.  We just adore them and it shows in the quality and temperament of our kittens.  As a small hobby breeder of British Shorthair cats &amp; kitten we breed for ourselves first in the hope that we can produce show stopping British Shorthairs.
      </p>
      <p>
       As dedicated show enthusiasts we try to breed loving British Shorthair cats and kittens that are healthy, friendly and conform closely to the breed standard.  The end result from our careful breeding is beautiful cats that make wonderful pets.
      </p>
      <p>
       We have a full range of colours including traditional British blue right through to our exciting cinnamon and fawn British Shorthairs, the first in Australia!  Our full list of colours includes blue, black, chocolate, lilac, fawn and cinnamon all in solid and bicolour.
       <a href="breed/colours/">
        Click here
       </a>
       for more details on the range of colours we breed.
      </p>
      <p>
       Our British Shorthair breeding cats are tested for known genetic problems such as PKD, blood typed and fully vaccinated.  They all receive the very best of care because in reality they are not breeding stock, they are our much loved pets who just happen to have babies and go to shows!
      </p>
      <p>
       British Shorthairs are seasonal breeders which means kittens are normally available during the warmer months of the year. We breed for quality not quantity so this means we only have a limited number of British Shorthair kittens available every year and we often have a lot of people waiting to adopt kittens.
      </p>
      <p>
       The breed is very popular so there is always a lot of new owners ready to adopt kittens.  This may mean there is a wait to adopt especially f we have smaller litters.  If you are interested in a Cuddleton British Shorthair please visit our enquiries information page then complete our kitten enquiry form and we will be in touch.
      </p>
      <p>
       <b>
        Have you visited our Cuddleton British Shorthair Facebook page?
       </b>
       We post regularly with photos of our cats as well as lots and lots of updates from owners of the cats we have bred.
