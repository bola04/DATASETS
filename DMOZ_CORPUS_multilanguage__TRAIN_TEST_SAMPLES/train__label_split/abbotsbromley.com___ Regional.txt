      <h2>
       Best Kept Village
      </h2>
      <p>
       Situated in the heart of England, Abbots Bromley is a village of character and
       <a href="history" title="A brief history of Abbots Bromley">
        history
       </a>
       . Although best known for the annual
       <a href="horn_dance" title="About the Abbots Bromley Horn Dance">
        Horn Dance
       </a>
       held each September, the village is also a thriving community that combines a respect for fine heritage and traditions with the practicalities of life in the 21st century.
      </p>
      <p>
       A regular entrant, and frequent winner, in the Staffordshire
       <a href="best-kept-village" title="Abbots Bromley entry to the BKV competition">
        Best Kept Village competition
       </a>
       , Abbots Bromley is a community that takes pride in the way the village looks.
      </p>
      <a class="btn btn-large btn-success pull-center" href="best-kept-village">
       Abbots Bromley's Winning entry of Best Kept Village competition
      </a>

       The Horn Dance

      <p>
       <a href="horn_dance">
        <img alt="Horn Dance" src="images/horndance.jpg"/>
       </a>
       Abbots Bromley is the home of a unique traditional custom. Read about the
       <a href="horn_dance" title="More about the Horn Dance">
        Horn Dance
       </a>
       and plan your visit to this spectacle.
      </p>
