       <article id="introduction">
        <h2>
         Welcome to UnrealSP.org
        </h2>
        We are a community dedicated to supporting the single player adventures available for
        <a href="./gameguide">
         <strong>
          Unreal
         </strong>
        </a>
        , Epic Games' blockbusting 1998 first-person shooter. UnrealSP.org is home to players and modders who strive to maintain, improve and build upon Unreal's unique game design as well as the numerous
        <a href="./reviews">
         custom-made single player campaigns
        </a>
        created for it and its sequel games.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Start browsing by reading our
        <a href="./articles">
         articles
        </a>
        , find interesting custom maps on our
        <a href="./reviews">
         review listing
        </a>
        , check out
        <a href="./upcoming">
        </a>
        upcoming modding projects and say hello in our
        <a href="#forums">
         discussion forums
        </a>
        .
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <ul class="linklist">
         <li class="rightside">
          <a>

          or
          <a>
           log-in

          to remove this introduction
