       <h3 style="text-align: center;">
        Cape Neddick Lobster Pound-Harborside Restaurant
       </h3>
       <h2 style="text-align: center;">
        <span style="font-size: medium;">
         You Can Get Here from There!
        </span>
       </h2>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: medium;">
         The Cape Neddick Lobster Pound-Harborside Restaurant, known
        </span>
        <span style="font-size: medium;">
         locally as "The Pound", has a long standing tradition of serving
        </span>
        <span style="font-size: medium;">
         exceptional food and drink in a setting unmatched throughout the local areas.
        </span>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        <span style="font-size: medium;">
         We are off the beaten path, overlooking the
        </span>
        <span style="font-size: medium;">
         Cape Neddick Harbor and Tidal River.  A place where locals gather for sunsets, paddlers and kayakers float by
        </span>
        <span style="font-size: medium;">
         , children skip rocks, seagulls soar and lobstermen bring in the daily catch.
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: medium;">
         Join us… Belly-
        </span>
        <span style="font-size: medium;">
         up to the bar, take in the view while enjoying your meal in our dining room,
        </span>
        <span style="font-size: medium;">
         relax on one of our decks, settle in by the fireplace, or celebrate with family
        </span>
        <span style="font-size: medium;">
         and friends on your wedding day.   Just a
        </span>
        <span style="font-size: medium;">
         few of the reasons guests love visiting “The Pound”.
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: medium;">
         We’re so glad
        </span>
        <span style="font-size: medium;">
         you found us!  The Young Family
        </span>
       </p>
