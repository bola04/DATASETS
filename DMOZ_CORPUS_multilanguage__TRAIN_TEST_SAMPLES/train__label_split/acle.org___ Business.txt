        ACLE (Associazione Culturale Linguistica Educational) is a non-profit organisation accredited by the Italian Ministry of Education and the World TEFL Accrediting Committee. ACLE was founded over 35 years ago, and continues to grow and develop successfully. The company was founded by Arrigo Speziali and is managed by a team of collaborators located in Sanremo, Belluno, Rome and Milan area.
