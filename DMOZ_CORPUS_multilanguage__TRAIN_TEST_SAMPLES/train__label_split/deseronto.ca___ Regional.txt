             <p>
              Deseronto offers a welcome reprieve from mainstream sights and activities, while providing ease of access to both rural and urban amenities. Characterized by its beautiful open waterfront on the Bay of Quinte; historic downtown features specialty shops, antique &amp; collectible dealers, artisans, dining and services; modern accommodations, cultural and recreation experiences.
             </p>

              Come explore all that Deseronto has to offer.
