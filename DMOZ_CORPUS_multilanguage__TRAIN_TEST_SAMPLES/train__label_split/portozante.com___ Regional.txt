        <h1>
         BEST LUXURY HOTELS &amp; VILLAS IN GREECE
        </h1>
        <h3>
         MEMBER OF SMALL LUXURY HOTELS OF THE WORLD
        </h3>
        <p>
         Standing majestically on a private sandy beach of a secluded bay, on the island of Zakynthos, just west of Greece’s mainland and famous for its turquoise waters and Venetian villages, Porto Zante Villas &amp; Spa is a
         <strong>
          boutique luxury hotel
         </strong>
         comprising world class
         <strong>
          luxury villas
         </strong>
         on the beach, all with private pools, exquisite Greek &amp; Mediterranean cuisine at the
         <a href="http://portozante.com/luxury-resort/dining/club_house_greek_and_mediterranean_restaurant_en">
          Club House Restaurant
         </a>
         , a luxury
         <a href="http://portozante.com/luxury-resort/dining/maya_asian_luxury_asian_fusion_restaurant_en">
          Asian Fusion Restaurant
         </a>
         offering delicious fresh Sushi, Sashimi and Thai options, a fabulous waterfront
         <a href="https://portozante.com/luxury-resort/spa">
          Spa
         </a>
         , 24-hour five-star hotel services, and an array of unique activities &amp; experiences for families and couples alike.
        </p>
        <p>
         The
         <a href="http://portozante.com/en/luxury-resort/villas">
          luxury villas in Greece
         </a>
         at Porto Zante are decorated and furnished with selected pieces of Armani Casa &amp; Gervasoni, paintings of prominent Greek artists and are equipped with some of the best exclusive amenities including Bang &amp; Olufsen Entertainment systems, Christofle Paris cutlery &amp; glasses, Bernardaud design porcelain &amp; BVLGARI guest amenities.
        </p>
        <p>
         One of the
         <strong>
          25 World’s Best Family Hotels
         </strong>
         by Cond
         <em>
          é
         </em>
         Nast Traveller, Top 100 Hotels of the World by Hideaways and Europe's
         <strong>
          Leading Villa Resort
         </strong>
         by the World Travel Awards for years now,
         <strong>
          this 5 star hotel in Greece
         </strong>
         has been the choice of famous clientele, including political leaders, important businessmen and artists.
        </p>
