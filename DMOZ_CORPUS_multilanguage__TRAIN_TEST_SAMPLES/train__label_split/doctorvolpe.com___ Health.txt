            <h2>
             Dr. Arturo M. Volpe
            </h2>
            <p>
             Arturo Volpe graduated as valedictorian of his Texas Chiropractic College class on August 22, 1998 and made a peaceful transition to the next life on August 22, 2013.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: right;">
             <img alt="Dr. Arturo M. Volpe" class="alignright wp-image-294" height="192" sizes="(max-width: 144px) 100vw, 144px" src="http://doctorvolpe.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/Doctor-Volpe-225x300.jpg" srcset="http://doctorvolpe.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/Doctor-Volpe-225x300.jpg 225w, http://doctorvolpe.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/Doctor-Volpe.jpg 240w" width="144"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             After completing a residency at the Natural Medicine Center in Clear Lake, in January 1999 Dr. Volpe opened Natural Health Solutions in Houston’s Rice Village as a sole practitioner. He expanded to larger quarters at 1714 Sunset Boulevard in July of 2003, and there he had his last patient appointments on July 17, 2013.
            </p>
            <p>
             This website will remain online as a tribute to the 15 years Arturo dedicated to helping those who needed and valued his advice, treatment and counsel. Articles from the e-newsletters that he provided as a free educational service beginning in 2000, and that he so enjoyed researching and writing to share with his subscribers, will continue to be available as a resource for all, searchable by keyword, on the site.
            </p>
            <p>
             Below is a link to the notice that ran in the Houston Chronicle. We invite you to sign the guestbook if you are so inclined, and help us remember Dr. Volpe with joy, admiration, gratitude, appreciation, and respect for a meaningful life well-lived.
            </p>
