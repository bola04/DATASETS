      <h2>
       An Accredited Dentist of the AACD
      </h2>
      <p>
       Dr.&amp;nbspHoward was the first dentist in all of North San Diego County and is one of 370+ dentists in the world to be an Accredited Member of the American Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry (AACD), the largest association of dentists specializing in cosmetic dentistry in the world. This award was earned by Dr.&amp;nbspHoward for his clinical excellence, exceptional skill, and deep commitment in the Art and Science of Cosmetic Dentistry.

      Committed to High-Quality Dentistry
     </h2>
     <p>

We invite you to experience a new kind of dentistry where total quality care is provided with exceptional customer service, caring team members whose goal is your absolute well-being, state-of-the-art techniques designed for your comfort, and in a fun and relaxing environment. We know that your visit with us will be the most rewarding of any dental experience you have ever had. My team and I believe that people should keep their teeth for a lifetime; with a common effort between us, that goal can be achieved.
