       <h2>
        Welcome to the Kapitalist Homepage
       </h2>
       <p>
        Kapitalist is a Monopoly
        <sup>
         Â®
        </sup>
        -like board
 game for 2-8 players. Walk around the board, buy properties, receive
 rent from your competitors, try to get monopolies to build houses and
 hotels on them and finally be the richest on the board.
       </p>
       <p>
        Kapitalist is server-based (the server called capitalist) and is
 written for
        <a href="http://www.kde.org">
         KDE 3.x
        </a>
        . Nevertheless it
 should also work fine under other desktop environments provided you
 have the KDE 3.x/Qt 3.x libraries installed.
       </p>
       <p>
        Have a look at the
        <a href="screenshots.html">
         screenshots
        </a>
        and
        <a href="download.html">
         download
        </a>
        the latest sources or
 RPMs. Furthermore, you can read the
        <a href="doc/en/index.html">
         documentation
        </a>
        for the latest version
 online.
       </p>
