         <div class="content-snippet-detail">
          <p>
           The Sudwala Caves are a popular tourist attraction in the Sudwala Valley, Mpumalanga, and are next to the P R Owen Dinosaur Park. One-hour tours of the cave are run during the day, and a monthly five-hour-long "Crystal Tour" takes adventurous visitors 2000 metres through the cave, with the tour culminating at a crystal chamber that bears aragonite crystals.
          </p>
          <p>
           A truly spectacular natural tourist attraction situated in the lush Crocodile River Valley in Mpumalanga, South Africa, the Sudwala Caves near Nelspruit are an absolute must see for any tourist visiting the area.
          </p>
