           Foraging for wild food is an ancient art. A slow and graceful way to feed and sustain ourselves.
          </p>

           <a href="https://www.eatweeds.co.uk/a-foragers-healing-journey">
            healing and transformative
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           Its benefits extend far beyond simply putting food on the table. It opens you up to the mystery and wonder of Life.
          </p>
          <p>
           There is nothing more fulfilling than wandering around picking a bit here, a bit there. Crushing. Smelling. Nibbling.
          </p>
          <p>
           Only to return home and prepare scrumptious food for loved ones and friends.
          </p>

          <h2 style="text-align: center;">
           About The Forager
          </h2>
          <p>
           <img alt="Robin Harford" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-7508" height="425" sizes="(max-width: 750px) 100vw, 750px" src="https://eatweeds2-0q5s3hnrbpjqfztuf.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/robin-harford.png" srcset="https://eatweeds2-0q5s3hnrbpjqfztuf.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/robin-harford.png 750w, https://eatweeds2-0q5s3hnrbpjqfztuf.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/robin-harford-300x170.png 300w, https://eatweeds2-0q5s3hnrbpjqfztuf.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/robin-harford-741x420.png 741w" width="750"/>
           <br/>

          </p>

           <a href="https://www.foragingcourses.com">
            foraging courses
           </a>
           were voted #1 by BBC Countryfile in 2017.
          </p>

          </p>
          <p>
           He is a co-director of Plants &amp; Healers International, a non-profit that connects people, plants and healers around the world.
