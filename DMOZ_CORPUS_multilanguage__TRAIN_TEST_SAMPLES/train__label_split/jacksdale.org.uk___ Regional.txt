      <h2>
       Welcome to the Jacksdale &amp; Westwood Community &amp; Heritage Web Site
      </h2>
      <p>
       Thank you for visiting our  village community &amp; heritage website, made possible by the generosity of the Heritage Lottery Fund. Here you will find information, articles and images from both the present and the past, relating to Jacksdale and Westwood, Nottinghamshire. Our first site was launched back in 2001 and over the years we have been contacted by many existing and past residents from all over the world. If you have stories or images to share or have any questions regarding the area, please get in touch as we would love to hear from you. The new style site is still building and we still have much to add, so please bear with us.
