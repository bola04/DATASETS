      <h2>
       Learn where to shop online in Canada
      </h2>
      Just browse the above 
 	categories to find out! In fact, OnlineShoppers.ca is a categorized and easy 
 	to use directory of best online shopping sites in Canada. The online 
 	stores listed here, have been handpicked, reviewed and placed in the proper 
 	categories to make it easier for you to find, shop and buy online at your 
 	convenient time. In addition, for your convenience and in order to have more 
 	choices when shopping online, we have also added some great international 
 	e-commerce sites that are mostly American retailers that ship to Canada.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      The retailers listed here are mostly Canadian-based and their local
 	retail stores located in big cities like Toronto, Vancouver, Ottawa,
 Montreal, and Calgary. These online retailers charge in Canadian dollars and most of them
 offer free shipping to almost anywhere in Canada.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      In order to help Canadian families, we've also listed some good outlet and department stores that offer
 cheap prices plus free shipping. We have worked hard to make this site an 
 	informative and useful source for those who want to buy their needs and 
 	gifts on the internet. Our goal is to provide the best online shopping 
 	experience for our visitors. We would be more than happy to hear from our 
 	visitors, so if you have any suggestions on how to improve this website, 
 	please do not hesitate to contact us at your convenient time.
