             <div class="columns four ">
              <div>
               <h3 style="color: #2f8ec9;">
                Preventive
               </h3>
               <div style="height: 265px;">
                Preventive care is the branch of dentistry that focuses on preserving healthy teeth, gums and mouth through the practice of good hygiene. Brushing and flossing your teeth are important to fighting off plaque and tartar, but coming in for a checkup and cleaning twice a year is highly recommended in order to detect early signs of dental health issues.
               </div>
               <p>
                <a href="http://www.blueskydental.com/services/preventive-dentistry">
                 <img alt="Learn More" src="http://www.blueskydental.com/wp-content/themes/BSD/tooth_button.png" title="Tooth Button"/>
                </a>
               </p>
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="columns four ">
              <div>
               <h3 style="color: #2f8ec9;">
                Restorative
               </h3>
               <div style="height: 265px;">
                This branch of dentistry deals with the restoration of abnormal teeth to their normal state and function. Restorative dentistry not only benefits a patient’s physical appearance, it is also important in maintaining healthy teeth. If problems such as gaps or other damages occur to the teeth or gums, they should be examined and treated in a timely manner in order to prevent further deterioration that could become hazardous.
               </div>
               <p>
                <a href="http://www.blueskydental.com/services/restorative-dentistry">
                 <img alt="Learn More" src="http://www.blueskydental.com/wp-content/themes/BSD/tooth_button.png" title="Tooth Button"/>
                </a>
               </p>
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="columns four ">
              <div>
               <h3 style="color: #2f8ec9;">
                Cosmetic
               </h3>
               <div style="height: 265px;">
                Innovative advances in dentistry have expanded to create an entirely new branch called Cosmetic Dentistry. While traditional dentistry focuses on keeping your teeth healthy to prevent disease and decay, cosmetic dentistry concentrates on brightening, constructing, and perfecting your smile.
               </div>
               <p>
                <a href="http://www.blueskydental.com/services/cosmetic-dentistry">
                 <img alt="Learn More" src="http://www.blueskydental.com/wp-content/themes/BSD/tooth_button.png" title="Tooth Button"/>
                </a>
               </p>
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="columns four last clearfix ">
              <div>
               <h3 style="color: #2f8ec9;">
                Orthodontics
               </h3>
               <div style="height: 265px;">
                Orthodontics is the practice of preventing and correcting irregular teeth by using braces. Teeth that grow in crooked or out of line can be embarrassing as well as potentially hazardous to your dental health. Therefore, it is important to get them straightened out.
               </div>
               <p>
                <a href="http://www.blueskydental.com/services/orthodontics">
                 <img alt="Learn More" src="http://www.blueskydental.com/wp-content/themes/BSD/tooth_button.png" title="Tooth Button"/>
