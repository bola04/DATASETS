         <h1>
          <i>
           The American Flag.
          </i>
         </h1>
         <p>
          <i>
           One nation, under God, with Liberty and Justice for all.
          </i>
          <br/>
          <img alt="line-stars" class="wp-image-44" height="13" sizes="(max-width: 433px) 100vw, 433px" src="http://www.usa-flag-site.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/line-stars-300x9.jpg" srcset="http://www.usa-flag-site.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/line-stars-300x9.jpg 300w, http://www.usa-flag-site.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/line-stars.jpg 501w" width="433"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          USA Flag Site is the home of all things patriotic. With every anniversary of 9/11, our hearts and prayers are with the September 11 victims, families and the entire nation.
         </p>
         <p>
          Just as the American flag represents much more than a simple banner, USA Flag Site represents the national symbols, songs and the culture that celebrate the greatest nation on earth. While our name highlights the importance of the American flag, we think you’ll find us to be a useful source for information on other national symbols, songs and icons.
         </p>
