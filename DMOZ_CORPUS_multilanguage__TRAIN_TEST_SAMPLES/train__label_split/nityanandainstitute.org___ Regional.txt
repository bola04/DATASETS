     <h1>
      Nityananda Institute - Founded By Swami Chetanananda
     </h1>
     <p class="textbody">
      Nityananda Institute, a center for spiritual practice in the tantric
 tradition, was founded by Swami Chetanananda in 1971. In August 2008,
 the Institute changed its name to The Movement Center. The Movement
 Center is currently based in Portland, Oregon, and offers programs and
 instruction in kundalini meditation, hatha yoga, Vajrayana practices,
 health and well-being, and leadership.
     </p>
     <h2>
      Spiritual Practice At The Movement Center
     </h2>
     <p class="textbody">
      The spiritual practice at The Movement Center offers you the
 opportunity to tap into the highest potential that exists for you as a
 human being. We support your growth as you awaken to the power of life
 within you, are enlightened by discovering the dimensions of that
 power, and become empowered to live a life of your own design.
     </p>
     <h2>
      Resemblance
     </h2>
     <p class="textbody">
      The name “The Movement Center” was chosen to create a new identity, one
 that reflects our unique Western cultural context for Eastern
 spirituality. Center refers to our core, which is total well-being, and
 movement refers to flow, so the name reflects the circulation of energy
 that is the essence of our spiritual practice. The name also evokes
 dynamic stillness, the phrase Swami Chetanananda has often used to
 describe the ultimate reality.
     </p>
