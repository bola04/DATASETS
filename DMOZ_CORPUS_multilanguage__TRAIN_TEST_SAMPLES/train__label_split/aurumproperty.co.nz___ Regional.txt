          <h3>
           How does Aurum work?
          </h3>
          <p>
           Building a home is the single biggest project, and financial investment that most people will undertake. Aurum’s role is to make that experience as simple, and as easy as possible.
          </p>
          <p>
           For some of our Canterbury clients the choice to build has been made for them. Aurum are here to assist with the process to ensure you get back into your home as quickly as possible.  Aurum’s greatest asset is its associate company KSL Audit Services Limited (KSL). KSL assists Aurum clients with their insurance negotiation, and claim settlement process.
          </p>
          <p>
           Aurum is a completely transparent fee based business, so you know exactly what you’re getting, with nothing hidden and no surprises.
          </p>
