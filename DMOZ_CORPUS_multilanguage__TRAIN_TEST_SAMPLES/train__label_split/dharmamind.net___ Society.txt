      <h4 style="text-align: center;">
       Welcome to the DharmaMind Buddhist Group Website
      </h4>
      <p>
       <a href="http://dharmamind.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/Untitled-2.jpg" rel="lightbox[5]">
        <img alt="Untitled-2" class="alignright size-full wp-image-161" height="189" src="http://dharmamind.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/Untitled-2.jpg" width="160"/>

       We are a non-sectarian western Mahayana Buddhist group and could be described as having a close resemblance to the Chan/Zen style of practice. Our students come from all over the UK and Ireland, and where possible come together to practice on a weekly basis with their local group. Beyond the local meetings we also meet regularly to hold retreats in various centres throughout the year. Our main central meetings are held monthly in Birmingham.
      </p>
      <p>
       We are probably a unique independent western Buddhist group in that our training form and meditation follow accurately the traditional structure of far eastern Buddhism. It is common for western groups who have no affiliation or obligations to eastern schools or traditions to add or subtract elements of established training in order to create their own identity, or in the conviction that westerners somehow need to train differently.
