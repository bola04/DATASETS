       CFA’s CFAWin software is a fleet maintenance management solution that provides
                                             a clean and direct approach to managing your fleet maintenance operations.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       At CFA, we realize your need to provide fleet cost controls and cost savings --
                                             no matter the size of your fleet. Our CFAWin solution works for small and
                                             large fleets alike. CFAWin can be operational in just days, and you can access
                                             CFAWin from anywhere in the world.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       CFAWin products are designed around the adage of what gets measured is what
                                             gets done. CFAWin offers enterprise-level features and functionality that
                                             provide you the ability to identify, reduce and control fleet costs without enterprise
                                             pricing.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Since 1969, CFA has been providing software to public and private equipment fleets
                                             to manage their fleet maintenance operations. Our products are designed around these
                                             40 years of servicing a large variety of equipment fleets.
