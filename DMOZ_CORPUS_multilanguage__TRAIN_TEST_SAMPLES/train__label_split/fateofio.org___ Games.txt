       <p>
        Fate of Io is a project aimed at creating a computer role-playing game.  Anyone is allowed to join the project and contribute ideas.
       </p>
       <p>
        If you simply want to browse through our ideas so far,
        <a href="/game">
         start here
        </a>
        and use the sidebar to navigate.  Also check out the art and music submissions found in the
        <a href="/media">
         media section
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         If you are interested in contributing to the project, start by reading the
         <a href="/project/intro/">
          introduction
         </a>
         .  Please at least look through it before posting.
        </b>
       </p>
       <p class="noMargin">
        If you are looking for a finished game that you can play, you won't find one here.  Not yet, anyway.  This is a work in progress, and we have a long way to go before the game is ready to be played.
