        <div class="widget-wrap">
         <h3 class="widgettitle widget-title">
          When we ask people how they are, they usually give an answer such as, “Fine.”
         </h3>
         <div class="textwidget">
          <div class="one-half first align-left letter-spacing-1">
           The word alone doesn't tell us much, but from the tone of voice we can hear if they are
           <span class="emphasis">
            tired
           </span>
           ,
           <span class="emphasis">
            frustrated
           </span>
           ,
           <span class="emphasis">
            distracted
           </span>
           , or having a
           <span class="emphasis">
            decent day
           </span>
           .
           <br/>
           <div class="margin-top-13">
            Meant to Be Heard maps a sophisticated communications network that explains how we hear the countless shades of meaning communicated by a single tone, and tracks its impact on self and listeners.
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="one-half align-left letter-spacing-1" id="front-page-second-text">
           Meant to Be Heard is a
           <span class="emphasis">
            holistic approach
           </span>
           that works with speaking as well. It applies techniques in relation to the way vocal frequencies energize different organs and regions of the body, influence emotional balance, and activate memories, beliefs and imagination.
           <br/>
           <div class="margin-top-13">
            You can learn more about specific issues at
            <a href="http://meanttobeheard.com/blog" style="border-bottom: 0 !important;" target="_blank">
             <span class="emphasis gold">
              Jocelyn's blog
             </span>
            </a>
            . You will find answers to questions other readers have posted and you can post your own.
           </div>
          </div>
          <div id="front-page-third-text">
           <div style="font-size: 2.5em; color: #bf9f0b !important;">
            <a href="https://www.voices.com/podcasts/soundstories/podcast/sound-stories-001-authentic-voice/" style="color: #bf9f0b !important;" target="_blank">
             Recent Podcast
            </a>
           </div>
           <br/>
           <br/>
