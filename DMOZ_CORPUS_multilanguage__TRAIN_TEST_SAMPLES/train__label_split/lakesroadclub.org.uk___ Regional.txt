          <h1 class="entry-title">
           Home
          </h1>
         </header>
         <!-- .entry-header -->
         <div class="entry-content cf">
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           The ‘Lakes’, as it’s often known, is a friendly cycling club of keen cyclists who enjoy getting out and doing all sorts of riding. We have four regular
           <a href="http://lakesroadclub.org.uk/day-rides/" title="Day Rides">
            club rides
           </a>
           throughout the week and a number of members who compete in all formats of racing.  The club is involved in organising the
           <a href="http://lakesroadclub.org.uk/the-fred-whitton/" title="The Fred Whitton">
            Fred Whitton Challenge
           </a>
           . This epic sportive is organised in honour of Fred, a former member of the club, to raise money for cancer charities. Please
           <a href="http://lakesroadclub.org.uk/contact/" title="Contact">
            contact us
           </a>
           if you are looking to join the club.
          </p>
          <hr/>

            L A T E S T   N E W S
           </span>
          </h2>
