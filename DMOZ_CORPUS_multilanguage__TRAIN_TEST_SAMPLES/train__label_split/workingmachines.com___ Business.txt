       accuracy in your warehousing and distribution center.
     </p>

      By replacing paper based picking with
      <a class="dlink" href="p/pick-to-light.html" onmouseout="this.className='dlink'" onmouseover="this.className='dlinkover'">
       pick to light
      </a>
      technology, clients can expect their picking productivity to double in
 flow/shelf rack picking, mobile cart picking and assembly/kitting
 workstations.
     </p>

      <a class="dlink" onclick="window.location='o/order-picking-p.html'" onmouseout="this.className='dlink'" onmouseover="this.className='dlinkover'">
       order picking
      </a>
      zones to increase outbound productivity and throughput.

     </p>

      Warehouse Automation
     </h2>
     <p>
      <b>

      </b>
      ,our

       material handling software
      </a>
