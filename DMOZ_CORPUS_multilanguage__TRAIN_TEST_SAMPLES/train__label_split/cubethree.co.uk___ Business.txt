        <h1>
         <br/>
         What We Do
         <br/>
        </h1>
        <p>
         We aim to design products that are successful and help increase our client's profit.  We love to work with SME's on new product ideas and get involved as early in the process as possible to help steer the design to a successful conclusion.  We are equally happy to help assist your in-house design team in any aspect of the design process.  We offer the full range of design services from research, concepts, CAD design, prototyping, design for manufacture and fulfilment of production tooling if required.
         <br/>
