     <h1>
      Welcome to Thomas Potter
     </h1>
     <p>
      Welcome to our new website, we hope you will find it both informative and a useful guide to the products we stock.
     </p>
     <p>
      We have recently moved into much larger premises to accommodate our ever increasing stock range.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      As well as our comprehensive range of industrial fasteners and fixings which we have been renowned for since 1923, we now have probably the widest stock range of pipe and fittings in the North East.
     </p>
     <p>
      While some of our competitors will tell you they can get what you need for tomorrow, we know from experience that tomorrow isn't always good enough.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      With this in mind we always strive to keep decent stock levels of all the products in our stock range.
     </p>
     <p>
      We are well known throughout the trade as a stockist of non-preferred items, so if you are looking for something that no one else keeps, there is a very good chance we will have it in stock.
     </p>
     <p>
      The company is a family owned business and we have built our reputation for great customer service over many years by being big enough to cope but small enough to care.
     </p>
     <p>
      We are proud to have been associated with many large scale projects throughout the country and have supplied to most of the well known major contractors as well as smaller privately owned companies.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Please spend a while having a look at what we offer, if you need any advice we have an
      <span>
       extensive knowledge of components used in all industry sectors and will be happy to help.
      </span>
     </p>
