 Tell The Truth, releases 01 August 2018
 
 
 Get ready for another dose of my personal music and musings!  Our goal is to get in the studio THIS month and record a new solo EP with a live band and if we can raise enough money: live strings!!  The EP will be produced by the lovely and brilliant Travis Frith Warner.
 
 Anyone that pre purchases the EP, they will also get 2 unreleased tracks as a Bonus &amp;quot;I Love and Thank you forever for supporting my music&amp;quot; gift!! ;)
 
 And thank you to everyone who has been nudging me to make a new acoustic album.  There are some songs that have been neglectfully unrecorded, like “Born To Die” and “Kissing Ghosts” - which many of you have heard me play live, along with some other old school country heartbreak songs. They will be in your ears very soon with the making of this EP!
 
 Thanks in advance for choosing to support my music.  With our wonderfully digital world, Music is free to listen to with Spotify etc (and I hope you have Tiff Randol and IAMEVE added to your playlists :)) But it is certainly not free to make, so your support in my music making endeavors is deeply appreciated!

          Get ready for another dose of my personal music and musings!  Our goal is to get in the studio THIS month and record a new solo EP with a live band and if we can raise enough money: live strings!!  The EP will be produced by the lovely and brilliant Travis Frith Warner.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Anyone that pre purchases the EP, they will also get 2 unreleased tracks as a Bonus "I Love and Thank you forever for supporting my music" gift!! ;)
          <br/>
          <br/>
          And thank you to everyone who has been nudging me to make a new acoustic album.  There are some songs that have been neglectfully unrecorded, like “Born To Die” and “Kissing Ghosts” - which many of you have heard me play live, along with some other old school country heartbreak songs. They will be in your ears very soon with the making of this EP!
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Thanks in advance for choosing to support my music.  With our wonderfully digital world, Music is free to listen to with Spotify etc (and I hope you have Tiff Randol and IAMEVE added to your playlists :)) But it is certainly not free to make, so your support in my music making endeavors is deeply appreciated!
