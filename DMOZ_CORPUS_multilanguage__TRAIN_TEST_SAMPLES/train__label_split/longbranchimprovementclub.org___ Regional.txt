      <p>
       The Longbranch Improvement Club (LIC) was formed in 1921 "
       <i>
        to encourage any activity for the betterment of Schools, Home making, Roads, Marketing, Dairy, Poultry and all its branches
       </i>
       ." Today, the LIC continues to serve the community with thousands of hours of volunteer enthusiasm.
      </p>

       The club depends on its membership for continuing the mission of community betterment. Resident and associate memberships encourage participation by all.
