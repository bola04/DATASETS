          At Storehouse, everything we do revolves around our mission statement: Know, Grow, Go. We believe these are three key experiences that give you the optimal journey toward a growing relationship with Jesus.
          <h1>
           Know
          </h1>
          <p>
           Invite you to develop a personal relationship with Jesus. We believe God wants a close and personal relationship with you that will impact every aspect of your life. He can be known personally, and we want you to know Him. You will get to know God as you read the Bible daily, reflect on it, and pray asking God to direct your every move. Your relationship with Him will be strengthened as you gather with others each and every week at our Sunday Celebration Service. A personal relationship with Jesus is the key that unlocks the power of God in your life.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="tab-pane clearfix" id="t3Tab">
          At Storehouse, everything we do revolves around our mission statement: Know, Grow, Go. We believe these are three key experiences that give you the optimal journey toward a growing relationship with Jesus.
          <h1>
           Grow
          </h1>
          <p>
           Join with others for on-going growth and encouragement The goal as you travel on your spiritual journey is to grow closer to God and other Christ followers through biblical community. At Storehouse, we connect together weekly in small groups to grow spiritually, emotionally, and relationally. This happens as each group explores the truths of Scripture, prays for one another, encourages each other, and applies God’s Word to their lives.
          </p>

We help you discover your God-given talents and provide opportunities for you to contribute in the church, in our local communities, and in the world.
