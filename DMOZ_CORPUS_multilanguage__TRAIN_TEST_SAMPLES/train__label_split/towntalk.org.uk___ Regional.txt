      Welcome to Towntalk.org.uk

     <p>
      TownTalk are the leading experts in the development and hosting of web sites for town centres, shopping centres, communities and independent organisations and businesses. With over 800 web sites created, we have the experience and software to design everything from a bespoke micro-site to a complete, interactive city centre gateway.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      TownTalk also specialise in town centre consultancy providing a wide range of essential services for the busy urban regeneration team; from base-line assessments and auditing to comprehensive marketing and regeneration proposals.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Of course, it´s the multi award-winning TownTalk network for which the TownTalk name is best known. More than 100 towns and cities working together to promote the attractions and unique appeal of their urban heartlands helping to restore the status of the traditional ‘town centre‚ brand.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      TownTalk has worked with Government Departments, Ministers, Regional Development Agencies, Local Authorities and Local Strategic Partnerships to help re-establish town centres at the heart of our communities.
