       <h2>
        About Us
       </h2>
       <div class="entry">
        <p>
         Herne Bay Tennis Club is a small, friendly but competitive club housed close to the sea. The club was originally based in the Memorial Park in the town, but moved to its new home at Kent Close in the 1970â€™s with a purpose built clubhouse and 3 shale courts. Over the years the club changed to astroturf courts shared with the hockey club and most recently to new cushioned acrylic, floodlit courts completed in 2003.
        </p>
        <div class="ngg-gallery-singlepic-image ngg-center" style="max-width: 320px">
         <a class="ngg-fancybox" data-description="A view of our cushioned acrylic courts" data-image-id="1" data-src="http://www.hernebaytennis.co.uk/wp/gallery/about-us/112_1286.jpg" data-thumbnail="http://www.hernebaytennis.co.uk/wp/gallery/about-us/thumbs/thumbs_112_1286.jpg" data-title="A view of our cushioned acrylic courts" href="http://www.hernebaytennis.co.uk/wp/gallery/about-us/112_1286.jpg" rel="be936bd15fcfd52f147835a5509602aa" target="_self" title="A view of our cushioned acrylic courts">
          <img alt="A view of our cushioned acrylic courts" class="ngg-singlepic" src="http://www.hernebaytennis.co.uk/wp/gallery/about-us/dynamic/112_1286.jpg-nggid011-ngg0dyn-320x240x100-00f0w010c010r110f110r010t010.jpg" title="A view of our cushioned acrylic courts" width="320"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         Our facilities include:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          4 Cushioned Acrylic Courts (2 floodlit)
         </li>
         <li>
          Purpose built clubhouse (incl. bar, changing rooms, kitchen etc.)
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Our new courts were completed in 2003 and were the result of a jointly funded project by Herne Bay Tennis Club and the LTA. They were built on the site of the clubâ€™s old shale courts. The club was awarded its Tennismark accreditation in July 2016.
        </p>
        <p>
         Herne Bay Tennis Club is keen to show its commitment to the
         <a href="https://www.lta.org.uk/globalassets/about-lta/equality--diversity/British-Tennis-Diversity-and-Inclusion-Policy.pdf">
          British Tennis Diversity Policy
         </a>
         of tolerance and inclusion, so please read this document carefully and ensure that you adhere to all its tenets and advice.
        </p>
        <p>
         Our Coaches, Nicola Mileham and Dan Walker runs courses for both adults and juniors. More information about junior coaching can be found on the junior page and in the Junior Newsletter. Details of the adult courses can be found in the Newsletter or via this link
         <a href="http://clubspark.lta.org.uk/NicolaMilehamCoaching/Coaching/Adult">
          Nicola Mileham Adult Coaching
         </a>
         . They include 2 Rusty Rackets sessions, technical coaching, social tennis coaching and a Ladies tennis Xpress course run by Nicola and an evening Adult session run by Dan.
        </p>
