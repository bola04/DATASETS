      <h1>
       Welcome to Grand River Troutfitters Ltd.
      </h1>
      <p>
       Troutfitters is still Canada's #1 store for
       <a href="http://www.grandrivertroutfitters.com/learn/learn-to-fly-fish">
        Fly-fishing education.
       </a>
       We take pride in our abilities to provide personal outdoor education experiences. Our small class of 1,2, or 3 students per instructor is our secret. Our classroom is the river and the entire course is taught there. This guaranteed individual attention from our seasoned professionals ensures your new
       <a href="http://www.grandrivertroutfitters.com/about/testimonials">
        fly fishing skills develop properly
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       Now that you have some skills, or want to improve them or just one of our regular clients that wants to experience Ontario fly fishing on the next level. We call it
       <em>
        tight line guiding
       </em>
       because the ultimate goal of all our veteran guides is to hook you up. Fly fishing fish fights are top notch experiences and we are well known for providing clients with
       <a href="http://www.grandrivertroutfitters.com/gallery">
        numerous world class memories
       </a>
       each trip.
      </p>
      <p>
       So whether
       <a href="http://www.grandrivertroutfitters.com/guiding/brown-trout">
        walk n wading
       </a>
       Ontario s famous Grand River Tailwater or
       <a href="http://www.grandrivertroutfitters.com/guiding">
        Drift boating
       </a>
       other world class Ontario rivers -  Fly Fishing for
       <a href="http://www.grandrivertroutfitters.com/guiding/bass">
        smallmouth bass
       </a>
       <strong>
        ,
       </strong>
       <a href="http://www.grandrivertroutfitters.com/guiding/steelhead">
        steelhead
       </a>
       , or
       <a href="http://www.grandrivertroutfitters.com/guiding/muskie">
        muskie
       </a>
       book your day or two with us and understand
       <a href="http://www.grandrivertroutfitters.com/about/testimonials">
        why our clients keep on re booking
       </a>
       <strong>
        .
       </strong>
      </p>
