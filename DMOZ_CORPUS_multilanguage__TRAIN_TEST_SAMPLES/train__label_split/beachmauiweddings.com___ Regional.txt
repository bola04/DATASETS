         <h3 align="center" style="text-align: left;">
          Tropical, Unique and Exquisite Weddings from Hawaii’s Premiere Wedding and Reception Planners
         </h3>
         <p>
          At Aloha Beach Weddings of Maui we take all of our weddings personally from start to finish. All of our team members are extremely personable, professional, attentive and knowledgeable. Although you will have your own dedicated wedding planner from start to finish, we have quite a large office staff to ensure that there will be someone there for you at all times. This is the reason why a large percentage of our business is through referrals from past wedding couples.
         </p>
         <p align="center" style="text-align: left;">
          We can arrange every detail of your event after you have had time to review all of your options. And although our main focus will always be your wedding we are always more than happy to assist you with your travel, accommodations, luau and activity arrangements. Consider us to be your personal wedding and reception concierge.
         </p>
         <p>
          Whether you are planning a beach wedding, vow renewal, commitment ceremony or an elaborate wedding with reception, we are here to make sure you enjoy the entire planning process. After many years in the wedding business we are able to offer the most elite professional service providers at special reduced rates. As our vendors will tell you, they prefer to have our coordinators take care of all the details so they are free to concentrate on your special day only
         </p>
         <div style="background:#006;text-align:center;color:#fff;padding:10px;">
          Aloha Beach Weddings of Maui | 3530 Lower Honoapiilani Road | Lahaina, Maui, Hawaii, 96761 | 1-808-276-0572
         </div>
         <p>
          All of the photographs on our website are actual weddings that we have planned and coordinated over the years. We try and keep then as current as possible in order to showcase the latest trends. Please note that none of the pictures are changed in any way except for the resolution (size). The image you see is exactly the same as it was when the shot was taken. This is very important to us as we work very hard to ensure the look and feel of your event is stunning without adding digital enhancements. We also know without a doubt that the professional photographers we use can capture the brilliant colors of the ocean, sunlight and tropical setting.
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           “My wedding and reception could not have been any more perfect. The entire Aloha Beach Weddings of Maui staff was at my side during the entire year of the planning stage. Not only did they make themselves available for each and every question or idea I had, they also attended to my guests as if they were their own family. I highly recommend Aloha Beach Weddings of Maui to anyone considering getting married in Hawaii. Sincerely, Jeff and Stacy Johnson”
