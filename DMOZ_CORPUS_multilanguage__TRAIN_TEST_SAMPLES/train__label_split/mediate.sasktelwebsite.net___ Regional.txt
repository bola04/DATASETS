       <span class="fd">
        Our goal in offering services
       </span>
       <span class="size10">
        is to reduce conflict and  violence in community and institutional settings by strengthening people's  ability to respond to conflicts before they escalate and also to provide a  restorative approach when conflict does happen.Â          One of our strategies is  to establish alliances with like minded community groups and individuals to  make these changes together
