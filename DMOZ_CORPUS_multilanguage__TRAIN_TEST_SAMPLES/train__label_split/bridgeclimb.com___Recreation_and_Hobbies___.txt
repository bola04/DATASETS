       <section class="checklist">
        <h2>
         Pre-climb Checklist
        </h2>
        <div aria-multiselectable="true" class="checklist-list" id="accordion-checklist" role="tablist">
         <div class="half left-col">
          <div class="panel panel-default">
           <div class="panel-heading" role="tab">
            <h4 class="panel-title">
             <a aria-controls="item-1" aria-expanded="true" class="accordion-toggle collapsed" data-parent="#accordion-checklist" data-toggle="collapse" href="#item-1">
              5 THINGS YOU NEED TO KNOW BEFORE YOU CLIMB:
             </a>
            </h4>
           </div>
           <div class="accordion-body panel-collapse collapse" id="item-1" role="tabpanel">
            <ol>
             <li>
              <strong>
               Your health and safety is important:
              </strong>
              If you are pregnant, over 75 years of age or have any pre-existing health conditions please refer to the
              <a href="#healthsafetyessentials">
               Health and Safety Essentials
              </a>
              section below as you may need to provide a doctor’s
              <a href="http://www.bridgeclimb.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/2017_BridgeClimb_FORM_Certificate-of-Fitness.pdf">
               Certificate of Fitness
              </a>
              note before you climb. You must have a blood-alcohol reading below 0.05 to climb (the same as if you are driving). We will ask you to complete a BridgeClimb Declaration Form before your Climb. Please be honest about your health so we can ensure your wellbeing is not at risk. All discussions will be confidential.
              <a href="#healthsafetyessentials">
               Learn more
              </a>
              .
             </li>
            </ol>
            <ol start="2">
             <li>
              <strong>
               How should you prepare for your Climb:
              </strong>
              Ensure that you have eaten before your Climb and you’re well-hydrated – the experience can last up to 3 ½ hours. Wear comfortable, enclosed rubber-soled shoes such as running, sport or hiking shoes and don’t forget your sunglasses. We recommend wearing comfortable clothing. We’ll equip you with everything else you need for your Climb! Ensure you arrive 15 minutes before your scheduled Climb time to allow enough time to check in.
             </li>
            </ol>
            <ol start="3">
             <li>
              <strong>
               All your personal belongings can be stored in lockers, including cameras
              </strong>
              <strong>
               :
              </strong>
              We provide FREE secure lockers for you to keep your personal belongings in, including phones, cameras and GoPros. Our Climb Leaders will capture photos of you during your Climb. For safety and security reasons, cameras, video technology and mobile devices are prohibited on the Climb.
             </li>
            </ol>
            <ol start="4">
             <li>
              <strong>
               You must be 8 years old and 1.2 metres in height, or taller
              </strong>
              : Children aged 8 to 15 must be accompanied by and are the responsibility of an adult, with a maximum of three children per adult. Learn more about our
              <a href="#guideforparents">
               guide for parents climbing with children
              </a>
              .
             </li>
            </ol>
            <ol start="5">
             <li>
              <strong>
               Climbs operate in all weather conditions
              </strong>
              : We will equip you with all the gear you need to keep you dry, but remember to bring your own shoes, which should be enclosed, like running shoes. In case of extreme weather conditions like electrical storms or during times of extremely high wind, Climbs may be postponed. If you have any concerns, please contact our team on +61 2 8274 7777 or admin@bridgeclimb.com.
              <a href="http://www.bridgeclimb.com/things-you-need-to-know-to-climb/weather-info/">
               Learn more
              </a>
              .
             </li>
            </ol>
           </div>
