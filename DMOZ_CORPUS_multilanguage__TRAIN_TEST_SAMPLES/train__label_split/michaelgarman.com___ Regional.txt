               MAGIC TOWN
              </a>
             </h2>
             <span class="fusion-imageframe imageframe-none imageframe-3 hover-type-none">
              <a class="fusion-no-lightbox" href="https://www.michaelgarman.com/magic-town/" target="_self">
               <img alt="" class="img-responsive wp-image-7488" height="330" src="https://www.michaelgarman.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/MT-crown-theater2.jpg" width="390"/>
              </a>
             </span>
             <p>
              Located in the Old Colorado City historic district in Colorado Springs, Magic Town is a 3,000 square/foot, 1/6 scale miniature city, complete with dozens of different buildings, hundreds of characters, and magical elements – like holograms, rooms that transform right before your eyes, and much more.
             </p>
