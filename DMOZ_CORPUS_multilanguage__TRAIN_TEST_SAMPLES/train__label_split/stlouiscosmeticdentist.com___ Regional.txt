      <h1>
       About St. Louis County Dental Partnership
      </h1>
      <p>
       Our state-of-the-art office has been  providing world class dental care to the community of St. Louis for over 20  years.  Dr. Grayem and Dr. Brace are well recognized as premier
       <a href="/services/cosmetic-dentistry">
        <strong>
         St Louis cosmetic dentists
        </strong>
       </a>
       providing
       <strong>
        <a href="/services/dental-implants">
         implant
        </a>
       </strong>
       and
       <strong>
        <a href="/services/sedation-dentistry">
         sedation dentistry
        </a>
       </strong>
       in addition to  providing excellent family dentistry care.   Through their expertise in these areas they can bring back your beautiful,  healthy smile and restore your self-confidence.
      </p>
      <p>
       Our dental team is committed to providing the very best in dental care available in the St. Louis area. St. Louis County Dental utilizes the latest dental technology. Our practice emphasizes comprehensive General Dentistry, which is made up of many types of dental care: preventative dentistry,
       <a href="/services/cosmetic-dentistry">
        <strong>
         cosmetic  dentistry
        </strong>
       </a>
       <strong>
        ,
        <a href="/services/dental-implants">
         implant dentistry
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="/services/cosmetic-dentistry">
         restorative dentistry
        </a>
       </strong>
       , the treatment of
       <strong>
        <a href="/services/sleep-apnea">
         snoring  and sleep apnea
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="/services/dentures">
         periodontal  disease
        </a>
       </strong>
       , and
       <strong>
        <a href="/services/dental-implants">
         endodontic  treatments
        </a>
        .
       </strong>
      </p>
      <a href="/schedule-online-appointment">
       <img alt="St. Louis County Dental Partnership - Schedule an Appointment" class="aligntop" height="244" src="images/content/home-text-body.jpg" width="800"/>
      </a>
      <p>
       Dr. Grayem and Dr. Brace are preventive dentists who emphasize frequent cleanings, tooth brushing, and flossing. They believe in timely treatment and intervention, since this is the best way to eliminate many of the problems patients experience. The scope of preventive dentistry may extend beyond the mouth. There is mounting evidence that having a clean and disease-free mouth can help prevent other medical problems. Early recognition and counseling regarding dental habits that might be negatively influencing your dental health, such as poor diet and smoking, can help create more successful treatment outcomes.
      </p>
      <p>
       As a
       <a href="/services/cosmetic-dentistry">
        <strong>
         cosmetic  dentist
        </strong>
       </a>
       in St. Louis, Dr. Grayem has a reputation for providing natural looking, life-like smiles that appear not to be altered or filled.  We are fortunate to be able  to utilize some of the best cosmetic dental technology at our
       <a href="/">
        <strong>
         St Louis dentist
        </strong>
       </a>
       office.  Our crowns and porcelain laminate veneers  look great.
      </p>
      <p>
       Our Saint Louis dentist has been whitening the smiles of St. Louis dental patients for over 20 years. Dr. Grayem utilizes either tray bleaching at home, or in-office
       <a href="/services/cosmetic-dentistry">
        <strong>
         teeth whitening
        </strong>
       </a>
       systems.
      </p>
      <p>
       Dr. Grayem is also an experienced cosmetic dentist. Using innovative technologies, he replaces lost teeth, and eliminates the need for denture adhesive to stabilize
       <a href="/services/dentures">
        <strong>
         dentures
        </strong>
       </a>
       . His dental philosophy is to create cosmetic smiles that are both aesthetically pleasing and long lasting. He also utilizes the latest technology when restoring both implants and natural teeth.
      </p>
      <blockquote class="quote-left">
       Looking For a Dentist That Restores Your Trust?
       <span>
        Science * Art * Caring * Compassion
       </span>
      </blockquote>
      <p>
       As expert St. Louis restorative dentists, Dr. Grayem and Dr. Brace restore teeth with cavities, or that are cracked or broken, in a highly natural and cosmetic way by using white, mercury-free
       <a href="/services/dentures">
        <strong>
         fillings
        </strong>
       </a>
       . Most patients desire these and think they look best. Not only are these restored teeth attractive, but they are also affordable and  fully functional.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="/services/cosmetic-dentistry">
        <strong>
         Dental  emergencies
        </strong>
       </a>
       are welcome and in most  cases are treated the same day as the incident at our St. Louis dentist office.
      </p>
      <p>
       If you are not yet a patient then it might be  best to look at our
       <a href="/patient-reviews">
        <strong>
         testimonial  page
        </strong>
       </a>
       or our
       <a class="l-gallery" href="/lightboxes/gallery?type=rslides">
        <strong>
         cosmetic photo  gallery
        </strong>
       </a>
       to view cosmetic dentistry before and after photos.
      </p>
      <p class="legal-notice">
       Legal  Notice: Our success is built on long term relationships with our patients  though genuine caring and unrivaled excellence in value, comfort, service and  the relentless quest of improvement. However, nothing stated herein should be  construed as a claim of secret, superior or exclusive techniques, ability or  equipment. All are well documented in the literature and training is widely  available to any dentist who cares to pursue these goals. Our doctors are  General Dentists. Cosmetic, Implant and Sedation Dentistry and Dental Sleep  Medicine are non-specialty interest areas not recognized by the ADA that  require no specific educational training to advertise these services. Dr.  Daniel Bialecki and Dr. Timothy Grayem currently provide sedation services in  our practice.
      </p>

      <h1>
       About St. Louis County Dental Partnership
      </h1>
      <p>
       Our state-of-the-art office has been  providing world class dental care to the community of St. Louis for over 20  years.  Dr. Grayem and Dr. Brace are well recognized as premier
       <a href="/services/cosmetic-dentistry">
        <strong>
         St Louis cosmetic dentists
        </strong>
       </a>
       providing
       <strong>
        <a href="/services/dental-implants">
         implant
        </a>
       </strong>
       and
       <strong>
        <a href="/services/sedation-dentistry">
         sedation dentistry
        </a>
       </strong>
       in addition to  providing excellent family dentistry care.   Through their expertise in these areas they can bring back your beautiful,  healthy smile and restore your self-confidence.
      </p>
      <p>
       Our dental team is committed to providing the very best in dental care available in the St. Louis area. St. Louis County Dental utilizes the latest dental technology. Our practice emphasizes comprehensive General Dentistry, which is made up of many types of dental care: preventative dentistry,
       <a href="/services/cosmetic-dentistry">
        <strong>
         cosmetic  dentistry
        </strong>
       </a>
       <strong>
        ,
        <a href="/services/dental-implants">
         implant dentistry
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="/services/cosmetic-dentistry">
         restorative dentistry
        </a>
       </strong>
       , the treatment of
       <strong>
        <a href="/services/sleep-apnea">
         snoring  and sleep apnea
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="/services/dentures">
         periodontal  disease
        </a>
       </strong>
       , and
       <strong>
        <a href="/services/dental-implants">
         endodontic  treatments
        </a>
        .
       </strong>
      </p>
      <a href="/schedule-online-appointment">
       <img alt="St. Louis County Dental Partnership - Schedule an Appointment" class="aligntop" height="244" src="images/content/home-text-body.jpg" width="800"/>
      </a>
      <p>
       Dr. Grayem and Dr. Brace are preventive dentists who emphasize frequent cleanings, tooth brushing, and flossing. They believe in timely treatment and intervention, since this is the best way to eliminate many of the problems patients experience. The scope of preventive dentistry may extend beyond the mouth. There is mounting evidence that having a clean and disease-free mouth can help prevent other medical problems. Early recognition and counseling regarding dental habits that might be negatively influencing your dental health, such as poor diet and smoking, can help create more successful treatment outcomes.
      </p>
      <p>
       As a
       <a href="/services/cosmetic-dentistry">
        <strong>
         cosmetic  dentist
        </strong>
       </a>
       in St. Louis, Dr. Grayem has a reputation for providing natural looking, life-like smiles that appear not to be altered or filled.  We are fortunate to be able  to utilize some of the best cosmetic dental technology at our
       <a href="/">
        <strong>
         St Louis dentist
        </strong>
       </a>
       office.  Our crowns and porcelain laminate veneers  look great.
      </p>
      <p>
       Our Saint Louis dentist has been whitening the smiles of St. Louis dental patients for over 20 years. Dr. Grayem utilizes either tray bleaching at home, or in-office
       <a href="/services/cosmetic-dentistry">
        <strong>
         teeth whitening
        </strong>
       </a>
       systems.
      </p>
      <p>
       Dr. Grayem is also an experienced cosmetic dentist. Using innovative technologies, he replaces lost teeth, and eliminates the need for denture adhesive to stabilize
       <a href="/services/dentures">
        <strong>
         dentures
        </strong>
       </a>
       . His dental philosophy is to create cosmetic smiles that are both aesthetically pleasing and long lasting. He also utilizes the latest technology when restoring both implants and natural teeth.
      </p>
      <blockquote class="quote-left">
       Looking For a Dentist That Restores Your Trust?
       <span>
        Science * Art * Caring * Compassion
       </span>
      </blockquote>
      <p>
       As expert St. Louis restorative dentists, Dr. Grayem and Dr. Brace restore teeth with cavities, or that are cracked or broken, in a highly natural and cosmetic way by using white, mercury-free
       <a href="/services/dentures">
        <strong>
         fillings
        </strong>
       </a>
       . Most patients desire these and think they look best. Not only are these restored teeth attractive, but they are also affordable and  fully functional.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="/services/cosmetic-dentistry">
        <strong>
         Dental  emergencies
        </strong>
       </a>
       are welcome and in most  cases are treated the same day as the incident at our St. Louis dentist office.
      </p>
      <p>
       If you are not yet a patient then it might be  best to look at our
       <a href="/patient-reviews">
        <strong>
         testimonial  page
        </strong>
       </a>
       or our
       <a class="l-gallery" href="/lightboxes/gallery?type=rslides">
        <strong>
         cosmetic photo  gallery
        </strong>
       </a>
       to view cosmetic dentistry before and after photos.
      </p>

Our practice emphasizes comprehensive General Dentistry, which is made up of many types of dental care: preventative dentistry,

       Dr. Grayem and Dr. Brace are preventive dentists who emphasize frequent cleanings, tooth brushing, and flossing. They believe in timely treatment and intervention, since this is the best way to eliminate many of the problems patients experience. The scope of preventive dentistry may extend beyond the mouth. There is mounting evidence that having a clean and disease-free mouth can help prevent other medical problems. Early recognition and counseling regarding dental habits that might be negatively influencing your dental health, such as poor diet and smoking, can help create more successful treatment outcomes.

         teeth whitening
        </strong>
       </a>
       systems.

       <a href="/services/dentures">
        <strong>
         dentures
        </strong>
       </a>
       . His dental philosophy is to create cosmetic smiles that are both aesthetically pleasing and long lasting. He also utilizes the latest technology when restoring both implants and natural teeth.
