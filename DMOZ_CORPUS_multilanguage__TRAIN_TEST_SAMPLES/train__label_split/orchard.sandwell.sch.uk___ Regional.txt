        <h2 class="featured-subtitle">
         Hello and welcome to The Orchard School website
        </h2>
        <p class="featured-text">
        </p>
        <p>
         Together with home and the community, The Orchard School aims to provide a nurturing, safe and happy environment.  We strive to provide a wealth of opportunities and experiences for all our children, reflecting their own individual learning styles and needs. All children receive high quality teaching and learning through a personalised curriculum supported by our multi agency team and our close links with families within a well-resourced and stimulating environment.
        </p>
        <p>
         We hope you are able to find the information you require on our website, but if not please contact us to find out more information or arrange to visit us.
        </p>
        <p>
         Mrs Diane Ellingham - Headteacher
        </p>
