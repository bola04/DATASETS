             Center for Reproductive Wellness is the first Traditional Chinese Medicine Clinic in Utah entirely dedicated to treating infertility and men's and women's reproductive health.
            </p>
            <p>
             Many reproductive disorders, in both men and women, are the result of an imbalance. At Center for Reproductive Wellness, we use Traditional Chinese Medicine to treat the root causes of these imbalances, nourishing and balancing your body, mind, and spirit to restore reproductive health. Acupuncture works as an effective alternative and adjunct treatment modality. It is a safe, effective, and natural approach to help regain and maintain health and well being.
            </p>
            <p>
             For those requiring Assisted Reproductive Therapy we work closely with a number of local reproductive specialists to provide support for treatments such as In Vitro Fertilization (IVF) and Intra Uterine Insemination (IUI).
