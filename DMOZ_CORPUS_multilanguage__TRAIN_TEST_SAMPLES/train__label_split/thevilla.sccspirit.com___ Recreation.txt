       <b>
        Welcome to La Villa delle Sorelle, the Sisters Villa
       </b>
       <br/>
       H.E. Mistress Gwenhwyfar Dinas Emrys and her sister, H.E. Mistress Zaneta Gavlinne Angiolieri have left the harsh cold climates of Northern England and ventured to the more pleasant atmosphere of Southern Italy.
       <p>
        The pair have invested in a modest, yet comfortable Villa, which they use as a stopping point on their many travels.  Large enough to accommodate their two separate households, the Villa and it's beautiful surroundings also attracts a number of visitors annually.  Gwen and Gav greet them all with their traditional hospitality.  These guests bring with them many stories, experiences, and treasures from afar that we want to share with you.
       </p>
       <p>
        Please feel free to wander throughout the Villa and explore the wonders that inspire us the most, from poetry to pictures, artisans to upcoming events.  And remember, the site will be updated regularly, so check back often.
       </p>
