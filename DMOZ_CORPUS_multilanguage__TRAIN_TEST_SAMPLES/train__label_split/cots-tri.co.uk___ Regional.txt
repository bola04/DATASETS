        <h1>
         Welcome to The Cotswold Trichology Centre
        </h1>
        <p>
         Qualified Care For Hair Loss and Scalp Problems By Trichologist Sally-Ann Tarver MIT.FTTS
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="col col-12 tablet-col-12 mobile-full subheader">
        <p>
         I'm Here To Help You With Hair Loss
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="col col-2 tablet-col-4 mobile-full roundimg">
        <img alt="" src="https://www.cots-tri.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/xpic1.png.pagespeed.ic.7qy3xzcZ4J.png"/>
       </div>
       <div class="col col-5 tablet-col-7 mobile-full sec1p">
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Losing hair is stressful and can affect your self confidence and self esteem. Loss of hair is traditionally considered a male problem however, as hair is a symbol of a woman’s femininity and sexuality, hair loss for women can be psychologically damaging. Trichologists are trained to diagnose and advise on all aspects of hair loss and scalp conditions. Since I set up my practice in 1998, I have seen and helped many patients with both common and rare or unusual scalp and hair loss conditions. If you have a hair loss problem or are concerned about any aspect of your hair, I recommend you act sooner rather than later to seek advice from a qualified reputable Trichologist. I am also a wig consultant and all too frequently I see clients who could have been treated for a problem had they got the right advice when it first started.
        </p>
