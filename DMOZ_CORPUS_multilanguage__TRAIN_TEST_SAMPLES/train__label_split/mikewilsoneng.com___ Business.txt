         <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
          <img alt="fontpageimage2-300x170" class="alignright size-full wp-image-36" height="170" src="http://mikewilsoneng.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/fontpageimage2-300x170.jpg" width="300"/>
          <span style="font-size: large;">
           Wilson Engineering
          </span>
          is a Toronto, Canada based design mechanical design engineering firm specializing in product development of innovative new products for the consumer, commercial and medical appliance markets.
         </p>
         <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
          Developing a successful new product requires a multi-disciplinary team approach. The primary goal of a new product development  is to satisfy the customer needs in a way that optimizes the benefit to the company. This requires an understanding of the diverse factors that influence how the product is designed, manufactured, distributed, sold and used by the end customer.
         </p>
         <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
          With over 25 years of product design and development experience we can work as part of your in-house development team or provide a complete turn-key product development solution or all points in between.
         </p>
         <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
          Take a few minutes to explore our site, and if our experience and capabilities match your needs please contact us to discuss how we can help you develop your next winning product.
