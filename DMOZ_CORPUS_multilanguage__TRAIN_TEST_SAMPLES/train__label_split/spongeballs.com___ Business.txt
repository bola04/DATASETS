     <h1>
      Spongeballs
     </h1>
     <p>
      <span class="Luxx">
       Luxx Ultra-Tech, Inc.
      </span>
      is one of the world suppliers of condenser cleaning rubber spongeballs.  
 Our spongeballs are used for Ultrafiltration Membrane Module and Condenser Tube Cleaning while providing the best performance for U/F and
      <u>
       Condenser Tube Cleaning Systems
      </u>
      (CTCS).
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
      A number of balls have been developed to cope with various membrane and condenser tube fouling, as well as different tube materials and pressure drops of membranes and condensers.
      <span class="Luxx">
       Luxx Ultra-Tech’s
      </span>
      spongeballs are quality assured.
     </p>
     <img class="align-right noborder" src="images/system/balls_5.gif" style="padding-top:20px; padding-right:80px;"/>
     <ul class="bold">
      <li>
       Improved Cleaning Efficiency
      </li>
      <li>
       Preserved Integrity of Film for Condenser Tubes
      </li>
      <li>
       Maximized Ball Life
      </li>
      <li>
       Membrane Quality Assurance
      </li>
     </ul>
     <p>
      Balls are made from the highest quality of raw materials.  
 First grade natural rubber is used and blended with various additives giving them their fine quality.
     </p>
