                   <div class="summary-excerpt">
                    <p>
                     Five decades ago Title VII of the Civil Rights Act was signed into law.  This final piece of civil rights legislation prohibits the discrimination in the sale and rental of residential housing.  The goal of this act was to end the decades-long problem of discrimination based on race, sex, age, disability, and several other personal characteristics when it comes to access to housing.

              <h2>
               Why Use A Realtor®?
              </h2>
              <p>
               REALTORS® hold exceptional value for buyers and sellers alike. A licensed REALTOR® who adheres to the strict industry-regulated code of ethics can facilitate one of the most important processes of your life, the purchasing or selling of a home. REALTORS® advocate on your behalf; they are market experts with honed negotiating skills and are dedicated to continuing their education of the real estate sector.  Licensed REALTORS® belong to an expansive network of professionals across the country and have access to a wide variety of industry resources.
              </p>
              <p>
               Your REALTOR® can help you save money, meet with important contacts in the industry, be an unbiased voice of reason during your transaction, defend your homeownership rights in the political arena, and much more.
               <a href="http://www.realtor.com/advice/why-you-should-use-realtor/">
                Visit realtor.com
               </a>
               for a more in depth look at why you should use a REALTOR®.
              </p>
