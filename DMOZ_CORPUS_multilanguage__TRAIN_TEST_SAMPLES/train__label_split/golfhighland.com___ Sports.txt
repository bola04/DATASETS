                   </h2>
                   <p>
                    Welcome to the new Golf Highland website which contains details of our member courses throughout the area. Traditional links golf and superb inland experiences that offer you tranquillity, solitude and outstanding natural beauty - the Highlands of Scotland provides some of the best golf courses in the country, to suit every budget and every ability.
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    Superb links courses, many the work of Old Tom Morris and James Braid, attract the major winners of today, who visit the Highlands of Scotland for the pure personal pleasure of walking the natural seaside turf.
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    Our inland courses some of whom were also from the drawing board of the great architects provide rolling fairways, mature trees and blooming heather which typify the splendour of the hills and mountains of the Highlands.
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    Explore the Highlands using our interactive regional image links, and Play the Courses by clicking your preferred region then clicking on any of the golf courses in the image box.
                   </p>
