      <h3 align="left">
       Mathematical graph and charting software for geometry and statistics
      </h3>
      <p align="left">
       The calculator can do statistics, best fits, function plotting, integration. 
 
   It handles
       <a href="./tour/etour7.html">
        vectors
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="./tour/etour8.html">
        matrices
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="./tour/etour7.html">
        complex numbers
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="./tour/etour14.html">
        quaternions
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="./tour/etour12.html">
        coordinates
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="./tour/etour15.html">
        regular polygons
       </a>
       and
       <a href="./tour/etour9.html">
        intersections
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p align="left">
       For
       <a href="./tour/etour9.html">
        point
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="./tour/etour11.html">
        line, plane
       </a>
       , sphere,
       <a href="./tour/etour10.html">
        circle
       </a>
       Calc 3D calculates distances, 
 
   intersections, and some additional information like volume and area.
      </p>
      <p align="left">
       <a href="./tour/etour12.html">
        Cartesian, spherical and cylindrical coordinates
       </a>
       can be transformed into each other.
      </p>
      <p align="left">
       Carthesian plot,
       <a href="./tour/etour3.html">
        polar plot
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="./tour/etour5.html">
        parametric plot
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="./tour/etour4.html">
        best fit
       </a>
       , 
 
 fast fourier transformation, 
 
 histogram, 
 
 smooth,...
      </p>
