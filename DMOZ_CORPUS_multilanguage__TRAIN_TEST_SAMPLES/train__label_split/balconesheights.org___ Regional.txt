                 <p>
                  Balcones Heights is an incorporated city at the center of San Antonio. Located mid-way between the South Texas Medical Center and downtown San Antonio, at the intersection of I-10 and Loop 410, Balcones Heights is home to the annual Balcones Heights Jazz Festival, Dave &amp; Buster’s, Wonderland of the Americas, the Bijou Cinema Cafe, and Methodist TexSan Hospital. The City is governed by its own City Council, operates its own Police and Fire Departments, and is at the center of San Antonio’s pilot Bus Rapid Transit System.
