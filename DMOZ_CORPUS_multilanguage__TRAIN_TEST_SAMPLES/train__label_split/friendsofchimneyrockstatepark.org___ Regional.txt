        <div class="nivo-html-caption" id="caption0">
         <h2>
          Friends of Chimney Rock State Park
         </h2>
         <div class="slider-text-separator">
         </div>
         <div class="slide-text">
          <div class="inline-slide-text">
           The “Friends of Chimney Rock State Park” is a 501-C-3 non-profit organization that supports Chimney Rock State Park through volunteering and fundraising with a focus on recreational access, education, and protection of the ecological resources within Hickory Nut Gorge.
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="nivo-html-caption" id="caption1">
         <h2>
          NURTURE
         </h2>
         <div class="slider-text-separator">
         </div>
         <div class="slide-text">
          <div class="inline-slide-text">
           Our Vision is to Nurture the 'Wild Life'. We hope to do this by creating ample opportunity for everyone to be able to enjoy the stunning beauty &amp; biodiversity of Chimney Rock State Park, and the surrounding Hickory Nut Gorge region.
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="nivo-html-caption" id="caption2">
         <h2>
          TRAILS
         </h2>
         <div class="slider-text-separator">
         </div>
         <div class="slide-text">
          <div class="inline-slide-text">
           By rebuilding existing trails, and expanding current trails, FCRSP hopes to aid the NC State Park system create more opportunities for nature lovers of all ages to appreciate the beauty of Chimney Rock State Park.
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="nivo-html-caption" id="caption3">
         <h2>
          EDUCATE
         </h2>
         <div class="slider-text-separator">
         </div>
         <div class="slide-text">
          <div class="inline-slide-text">
           Much of our funding goes to create wonderful &amp; awe-inspiring interactive educational opportunities for nature lovers of all generations.
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="nivo-html-caption" id="caption4">
         <h2>
          VOLUNTEER
         </h2>
         <div class="slider-text-separator">
         </div>
         <div class="slide-text">
          <div class="inline-slide-text">
           Along with our family fun-raising events, our group raises awareness that volunteers are the cornerstone of an effective hands on stewardship organization.
          </div>
