          <blockquote>
           Mac Kinnons Campground is an open and wooded family campground on Lake Ainslie, the largest natural fresh water lake in Nova Scotia.
           <br/>
           We are 45 minutes from the
           <a href="http://www.cansocauseway.ca/" target="_blank">
            Canso Causeway
           </a>
           ,
           <a href="http://www.cheticampns.com/" target="_blank">
            Cheticamp
           </a>
           and
           <a href="http://www.baddeck.org/" target="_blank">
            Baddeck
           </a>
           , 20 minutes from the
           <a href="http://www.destination-ns.com/common/trails/AreaID.asp?AreaID=7B" target="_blank">
            Cabot Trail
           </a>
           , the
           <a href="http://www.margareens.com/" target="_blank">
            Margarees
           </a>
           and
           <a href="http://www.destination-ns.com/common/places.asp?PlaceID=1612" target="_blank">
            Inverness
           </a>
           , 90 minutes to
           <a href="http://sydney.capebretonisland.com/" target="_blank">
            Sydney
           </a>
           and 120 minutes to
           <a href="http://louisbourg.com/" target="_blank">
            Louisbourg
           </a>
           . An ideal location to park your RV while you do day trips.
