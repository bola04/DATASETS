         <div class="homepageintro">
          <p>
           Cheveley Park Stud is renowned as one of the most successful thoroughbred Stud Farms in Europe.
           <br/>
           Situated in Newmarket, England – the ancestral home of horse racing – the Stud extends to just under 1,000 acres.
           <br/>
           Cheveley Park Stud itself is home to eight top class stallions, while the nearby Sandwich, Strawberry Hill, Ashley Heath and Warren Hill divisions accommodate the Company’s other bloodstock interests.
          </p>
