       <p align="left">
        <font color="#3300CC" size="+1">
         <strong>
          Welcome to author Jane
        Hatton's home page
         </strong>
        </font>
       </p>
       <p align="left">
        <strong>
         <em>
          General fiction novelist from Cornwall, UK.
         </em>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p align="left">
        Jane Hatton was a child during World War II, and grew 
           up in the unpermissive fifties, when career options for women were confined 
           to Secretary/Nurse/Teacher/Physiotherapist. She opted for the first, 
           thinking the skills required would be useful in her preferred career 
           as a writer, but has since worked in hotels, as a sailing instructor, 
           in a craft workshop and as a cookery demonstrator - a remarkably unstructured 
           career - while continuing to write whenever there was a spare moment 
           - sometimes there were not many!
       </p>
       <p align="left">
        She has had two children’s books published (a long time ago), 
           and three novels, plus the one too awful to mention. Apart from writing, 
           her interests include boats, painting - including scenery for local 
           pantomimes - and photography. She lives in Cornwall with her husband 
           and a small black cat called Kitty-Kitty.
       </p>
