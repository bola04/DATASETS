     Back 
     in 1993 Privateer was released as part of the Wing Commander series and became a great success in the gaming 
     industry. Over 10 years later a small group of privateer enthusiasts reintroduced the game with a new graphic 
     engine and real 3D spaceflight.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     As Grayson Burrows you inherited a small old scout class ship 
     from your grandfather, to start over as a Privateer in a new and sparsely populated sector of the Confederation. 
     Take your chance and become a pirate, merchant or bounty hunter upgrade your ship and experience the hard truth 
     in a border sector where no one has anything to give away ...
