                 <a class="contentpagetitle" href="/about-masm">
                  Who we are
                 </a>
                </h2>
                <div class="article-content">
                 <p>
                  <img align="left" alt="RC flying at it's best" border="0" hspace="5" src="/images/stories/2008/story_who_we_are/who_we_are_image01.png" vspace="0"/>
                  Our Club is located in Sacramento, California at the Mather Regional park (formerly known as Mather AFB). We have over 100 members flying a wide variety of planes from electric foam planes to gasoline 40% giant scale planes. We welcome beginners and provide training for new members.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  Mather Aerospace Modelers, Inc is a nonprofit club that has over 100 members that fly radio controlled models at Mather Field. This organization is a member of the Academy of Model Aeronautics (AMA), a national group with over 2500 Chapters.
                 </p>
