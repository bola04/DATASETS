     <h2>
      Profile of company:
     </h2>
     <p align="justify">
      <b>
       KAMEA
      </b>
      was founded in 1991. In beginning we focused on
 	  providing services for special making semiconductors equipment. The
 	  movement of our firm was supported by high qualification of our
 	  workers, who graduated many courses abroad, directly at producers of
 	  such machines. In the first period of approximately 2 years we were
 	  providing these services for the last concern TESLA...
     </p>
     <p align="justify">
      The semiconductor production in former Czechoslovakia was on
 	  decline, therefore we must have spread our offers to the
 	  neighbourhood states. To attract new customers we had to proove high
 	  standard of our services. In these years we deserved the support of
 	  semiconductor equipment makers, for example
      <a href="http://www.esec.com" target="_blank">
       ESEC
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="http://www.asm.com" target="_blank">
       ASM
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="http://www.fkdelvotecusa.com" target="_blank">
       DELVOTEC
      </a>
      ,
 	  etc. As we were a small "East" European company, difficult to
 	  identify by our potential clients, this support was very
 	  helpful.
     </p>
     <p align="justify">
      After getting over the early stage problems, we extended our field
 	  of development and construction of new machines. We also started
 	  refurbishing and upgrading used equipment. Gradualy we built up our
 	  construction part from
      <i>
       back end
      </i>
      , through optical equipment
 	  (
      <i>
       microscopes
      </i>
      ) to wafers (
      <i>
       front end
      </i>
      ). Throughout this
 	  period we started providing deliveries of spare and newly developed
 	  parts for semiconductor companies too.
     </p>
     <p align="justify">
      Slovakia accessing the European Union brings new opportunities.
      <b>
       KAMEA
      </b>
      is trying to be prepared for this. Therefore we pay
 	  even more attention to keeping respectively reaching highter quality
 	  in tasks completion. We also put more effort to opening of new
 	  production.
     </p>
