       <h2>
        Welcome to Congregation Agudath Achim
       </h2>
       <div class="at-above-post-homepage addthis_tool" data-url="http://www.agudath-achim.com/">
       </div>
       <p>
        Congregation Agudath Achim is the Conservative congregation of Savannah, GA. Agudath Achim is an egalitarian congregation that provides a spiritual, educational and social community blending traditional ritual and practices with contemporary realities. At Agudath Achim we come “to praise, to labor and to love.” Every day is an opportunity for learning,
        <em>
         davening
        </em>
        and
        <em>
         tikkun olam
        </em>
        . We are over 100 years old and growing younger every year. AA believes that our best fundraising is “family raising” and that Judaism of the heart can also be Judaism of the mind. We try to intensify our Jewish involvement every day and we accept upon ourselves Jewish Law while living in the 21st century. AA is not only a shul it’s a
        <em>
         mishpacha
        </em>
        .
       </p>
