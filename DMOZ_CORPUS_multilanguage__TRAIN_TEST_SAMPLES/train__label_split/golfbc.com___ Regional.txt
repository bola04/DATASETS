From the renowned Nicklaus North in Whistler, to the three-course paradise of Wailea on Maui, to the sunny climes of the Okanagan and Vancouver Island, GolfBC offers the most naturally beautiful and challenging golf found north of the equator. Indulge yourself in the best golf experiences nature has to offer.

          <h1>
           GolfBC Championship is back for 2018!
          </h1>
          <div class="text">
           <img align="right" alt="Robby Shelton" border="0" class="image-right " height="138" src="/images/newsletter/Headers/enews_banner_GolfBCChampionship-winner.png?action=scale&amp;width=300&amp;height=138" style="float:right; height:138px; width:300px" title="Robby Shelton" width="300"/>
           The GolfBC Championship returns to Gallagher’s Canyon Golf and Country Club June 11-17th! This Mackenzie Tour – PGA TOUR Canada event has made a significant positive charitable impact in the Kelowna area with $300,000 raised in its first two years for its designated charity, the BC Cancer Foundation (and last year also the Canadian Red Cross for Kelowna flood relief). Visit the event website for stories, updates and photos!

          <div class="subheader">
           GolfBC Announces Change of Ownership of Furry Creek
          </div>
          <div class="text">
           <img align="right" alt="" border="0" class="image-right " height="26" src="/images/photos/logos/fc_logo.gif?action=scale&amp;width=125&amp;height=26" style="float:right; height:26px; width:125px" title="" width="125"/>
           GolfBC Group announced on October 10, 2017 that it has closed on the sale of Furry Creek Golf and Country Club, including development land surrounding the golf course. Future plans for Furry Creek includes further development of the residential community as well as building on the vision set for “BC’s Most Scenic Golf Course”. To ensure the smooth continuation of operations, the current team of managers and staff will be retained under the new management, who have also expressed desire to continue to grow the employment base in the region in their long-term vision for the Sea-to-Sky corridor.
