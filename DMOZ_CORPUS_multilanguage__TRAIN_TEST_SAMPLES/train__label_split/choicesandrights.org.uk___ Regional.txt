            <h2>
            </h2>
            <h2 style="text-align: center;">
             THE CENTRE FOR INDEPENDENT LIVING
            </h2>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: left;">
             <span style="margin: 0px; font-family: 'Arial','sans-serif'; font-size: 14pt;">
              <span style="color: #000000;">
               The Centre for Independent Living, covering Hull and the East Riding of Yorkshire managed by Choices and Rights Disability Coalition.
              </span>
             </span>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: left;">
             <span style="margin: 0px; font-family: 'Arial','sans-serif'; font-size: 14pt;">
              <span style="color: #000000;">
               The Centre for independent Living (CIL) is a collection of key services for disabled people..
              </span>
             </span>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: left;">
             <span style="margin: 0px; font-family: 'Arial','sans-serif'; font-size: 14pt;">
              <span style="color: #000000;">
               Choices and Rights staff members manage the Information Service,
               <span lang="X-NONE">
                Direct Payments Support Schemes
               </span>
               ,
               <span lang="X-NONE">
                Database of Potential Personal Assistants
               </span>
               ,
               <span lang="X-NONE">
                Disability Information Service
               </span>
               ,
               <span lang="X-NONE">
                Disability Equality Training
               </span>
               ,Â
               <span lang="X-NONE">
                Consultancy
               </span>
               ,
               <span lang="X-NONE">
                Equality Act Advice (Disability) and
               </span>
               <span lang="X-NONE">
                Personal Health Budgets Support Scheme.
               </span>
              </span>
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: left;">
             <span style="margin: 0px; font-family: 'Arial','sans-serif'; font-size: 14pt;">
              <span style="color: #000000;">
               Choices and Rights is an organisation for disabled people in the Kingston upon Hull and East Riding of Yorkshire area. We are 100% run and controlled by disabled people. We believe that the best experts on the needs of disabled people are disabled people themselves and as a result, Choices and Rights membership and reputation has grown because agencies and organisations know that when they seek consultation with us they will have dialogue with a delegation
               <em>
                of
               </em>
               disabled people.Â
              </span>
             </span>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: left;">
             <span style="margin: 0px; font-family: 'Arial','sans-serif'; font-size: 14pt;">
              <span style="color: #000000;">
               Our main aims are:.
              </span>
             </span>
            </p>
            <ul>
             <li style="list-style-type: none;">
              <ul>
               <li>
                To promote the independence and inclusion of all disabled people in society
               </li>
              </ul>
             </li>
            </ul>
            <ul>
             <li style="list-style-type: none;">
              <ul>
               <li>
                To Identify and pro-actively challenge the discrimination faced by disabled people
               </li>
              </ul>
             </li>
            </ul>
            <ul>
             <li style="list-style-type: none;">
              <ul>
               <li>
                To encourage disabled people to work together to achieve change locally
               </li>
              </ul>
             </li>
            </ul>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.choicesandrights.org.uk/admin/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/DC-Leader.jpg">
              <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-740" height="141" src="http://www.choicesandrights.org.uk/admin/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/DC-Leader.jpg" width="286"/>
