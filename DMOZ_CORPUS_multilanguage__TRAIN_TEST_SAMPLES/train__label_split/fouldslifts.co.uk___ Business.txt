      <h2>
       <span>
        Welcome to Foulds Lifts
       </span>
      </h2>
      <div class="content-padding">
       <p class="p1">
        Established in 1907, Foulds Lifts is one of the North of England's leading independent lift companies. The company has been continuously owned and managed by four generations of the Foulds family through to the present day - a proud heritage.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong class="red-txt">
         With our wealth of experience and knowledge of all aspects of passenger and goods lift engineering, Foulds Lifts can provide your total lift solution:
        </strong>
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         24 hour emergency call out service 365 days a year
        </li>
        <li>
         maintenance and repair of all makes of lifts
        </li>
        <li>
         safety examinations and testing
        </li>
        <li>
         design and installation of new lifts
        </li>
        <li>
         tailor-made refurbishment or modernisation packages for existing lifts
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        Our extensive customer base is centred in the North of England, with a large proportion of work coming from NHS Trusts, facilities management companies, hotels, construction companies, architects and consultants in this region. Foulds Lifts also specialises in installing and maintaining lifts in a number of heavy industry sectors including the steel, pharmaceutical and chemical industries.
       </p>
       <p>
        At Foulds Lifts, our focus is on providing unrivalled customer service. With our professional, friendly approach and our total commitment to customer care, we provide a quality service you can rely on. We are full members of the
        <a href="http://www.leia.co.uk/" target="_blank" title="Visit the Lift and Escalator Industry Association website">
         Lift and Escalator Industry Association (LEIA)
        </a>
        and have achieved accreditation to BS EN ISO 9001:2000.
       </p>
