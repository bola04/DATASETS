        <h1 style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          Welcome to Meadville KOA Campground &amp; RV Park
         </span>
        </h1>
        <h2 style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          Northwestern PA’s Fun Family Camping and Cabins Zone!
         </span>
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          Meadville KOA Campground, located in Meadville PA features some of the best in Western Pennsylvania family camping and cabin rentals!  Whether you seek RV camping, tent camping, or cabin rentals for a weekend getaway or longer vacation stay, Meadville KOA Campground has a cabin or campsite to meet your family’s camping needs. In addition, Meadville KOA also offers extended stay RV camping where you can park your RV for a month or the entire summer!  Check out our
          <a href="https://meadvillekoa.com/seasonal-camping/" rel="nofollow" style="color: #333333;" target="_blank">
           Seasonal Camping
          </a>
          !
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          Meadville KOA Campground is a full service camping facility and RV Park where modern conveniences meet the beauty and tranquility of nature!  We are located in the heart of the beautiful Great Lakes Region of Western PA! Therefore, the region is known for it’s many “great lakes”, the most famous being Lake Erie!  But, there are many other “great” lakes in the area like nearby Pymatuning Lake and Spillway, Conneaut Lake, Sugar Lake, Woodcock Lake, Canadohta Lake, and more!  We’re conveniently located in the center of Crawford county, just a few miles East of Meadville, Pennsylvania!
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3>
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          Our Camping
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          Our full hookup
          <a href="https://meadvillekoa.com/campsites/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           campsites
          </a>
          includes water, electric, sewer, cable TV, and Wi-Fi.  These spacious campsites are open or wooded with maples, oaks, and evergreens.  Additionally, the Campground features 5 fishing ponds and one 2 acre lake and one 1 acre lake, a creek, and a hiking trail.  So you can connect to family, friends, or nature, as you choose.
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3>
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          Our Cabins
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          If you are looking for cabin rentals in Meadville PA, Meadville KOA
         </span>
         <a href="https://meadvillekoa.com/cabins/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          cabins
         </a>
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          range from primitive
         </span>
         <a href="https://meadvillekoa.com/camping-cabin/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          camping cabins
         </a>
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          to “glamping” in
         </span>
         <a href="https://meadvillekoa.com/deluxe-cabins/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          deluxe cabins
         </a>
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          which are lake-front, full service cabins! “Glamping” is a mixture of the words glamorous and camping! So whether you just want out of the weather and off the ground, or you want a “glamping” experience, we have a cabin to fit your family’s needs.  Most noteworthy is that all
         </span>
         <a href="https://meadvillekoa.com/cabin-rental-rates/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          cabin rentals
         </a>
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          include 2 adults &amp; 2 children, our
         </span>
         <a href="https://meadvillekoa.com/themed-weekends/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          themed weekend events
         </a>
         with planned activities, and outdoor
         <a href="https://meadvillekoa.com/recreation/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          recreation
         </a>
         .
        </p>

        <h3>
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          Themed Weekends &amp; Outdoor Recreation
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          Meadville KOA is a top-rated national and Pennsylvania family campground and RV park. In addition, this Founder’s and President’s Award Winning KOA features themed weekends with planned activities, outdoor recreation, and rental amenities!  Consequently, you can be as busy as you’d like while enjoying our outdoor
          <a href="https://meadvillekoa.com/recreation/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           recreation
          </a>
          , Fun Band Program Rental Amenities Program, or
          <a href="https://meadvillekoa.com/themed-weekends/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           themed weekends
          </a>
          !  Or rather, you can fill your days with the natural beauty of the campground and just relax.  Enjoy catch and release fishing, wade the creek, walk our hiking trail, or catch some rays by the swimming pool.  Enjoy the tranquility of the campground and relax by a campfire where your family can share a laugh, cook dinner, and make s’mores.  Most noteworthy, our goal is to bring you a great camping experience where your family can create great memories!
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3>
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          Area Festivals &amp; Events
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          Come explore
          <a href="https://meadvillekoa.com/directions/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Northwestern PA
          </a>
          !  Please check out our links to
          <a href="https://meadvillekoa.com/local-attractions/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Local Attractions
          </a>
          and festivals.  In addition, don’t miss the unique local festivals that our area offers!
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          Local events include: Pioneer Steam and Gas Engine Shows, Thurston Classic Hot Air Balloon Event, The Crawford County Fair features top rated Music Entertainment, Local Amish Benefit Auction (auction proceeds benefit the local Amish School), Meadville Thunder in the City (motorcycle &amp; car show), Erie Roar on the Shore (motorcycle charity fundraiser), Franklin Applefest, Conneaut Lake Pumpkinfest, and more!
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3>
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          The Local Area
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          Meadville KOA is centrally located near some of Pennsylvania’s most visited state parks!  We’re less than an hour’s drive from Presque Isle State Park, Pymatuning Spillway, and Oil Creek State Park. We’re also near Conneaut Lake, Oil City, and Franklin.  In addition, we are just a 45 minute drive to the Erie Mall or South to Grove City Outlets.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          We hope you’ll come see why campers love Meadville KOA Campground and why people come back here year, after year, and generation after generation.
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          FAQ You Might Have…
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
        </p>
        <div class="accordian">
         <div class="toggle">
          <a class="question" href="#">
           <i class="q_a">
           </i>
           <strong>
            Store/Office Hours
           </strong>
          </a>
          <div class="answer" style="display: none;">
           <span style="color: #333333;">
            Spring Office Hours are 9-8 Friday &amp; Saturday, 9-6 Sundays, and 9-7 Monday – Friday.  The best time and day to make reservations in Sunday – Thursday.  If you call and don’t get an answer, please leave us a message and we’ll call you back as soon as we can.
           </span>
           However, if you want to book a site right now, you can make an online reservation and ask a specific site (or next to a friend) in the comments section. Finally, we will grant you your request if available, and if not, we’ll contact you back to confirm your campsite. Thank you!
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="toggle">
         <a class="question" href="#">
          <i class="q_a">
          </i>
          <strong>
           Check-in/Check-Out Times
          </strong>
         </a>
         <div class="answer" style="display: none;">
          Campsites check-In/Check-Out is 2:00 daily. There are early checkin/early check out fees on campsites, pending availability.  Cabins Check-In is 3:00pm, Check-Out is 1:00pm.
          <em>
           Sorry, but at this time, all of our cabins are pet and smoke free.
          </em>
          Check-Ins are expected during normal Store/Office Hours due to our Security Gate. After Hour Check-Ins require advance arrangements.
         </div>
        </div>
        [/accordian]
        <div class="toggle">
         <a class="question" href="#">
          <i class="q_a">
          </i>
          <strong>
           Important Policies
          </strong>
         </a>
         <div class="answer" style="display: none;">
          <ul>
           <li>
            This is a Family Campground.
           </li>
           <li>
            Quiet Hours are 11:00pm – 8:00am.
           </li>
           <li>
            All Visitors must register at the Store/Office and pay the prevailing fee.
           </li>
           <li>
            Alcohol permitted at your campsite, but not in open display (Koozie required).
           </li>
           <li>
            All KOA Cabins and Buildings are Pet and Smoke Free.  Sorry, but we do not offer pet-friendly cabins at this time.
           </li>
           <li>
            We are a pet-friendly campground with the following restrictions: No Aggressive pets permitted. All pets controlled by leash 100% of the time! Pets cleaned up after 100% of the time. Pets barking minimized.
           </li>
           <li>
            Firewood from within Crawford County ONLY, please!  Unfortunately, invasive species are damaging PA forests and hitch a ride on firewood. Please buy it where you burn it and help protect PA forests!
           </li>
           <li>
            <em>
             All children under age 12 require helmets per Penna State Law to ride bicycles on our campground roadways.
            </em>
            However, if you forget yours, we sell generic helmets at the campstore. See our
            <a href="https://meadvillekoa.com/campground-policies/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             Campground Policies
            </a>
            for a complete list.
           </li>
          </ul>

As a result, all equipment may be signed out/in at the store/office at no charge by name and campsite. See our
          <a href="https://meadvillekoa.com/recreation/" rel="nofollow" style="text-align: justify;" target="_blank">

        <div class="toggle">
         <a class="question" href="#">
          <i class="q_a">
          </i>
          <strong>
           Rental Amenities &amp; Fun Bands
          </strong>
         </a>
         <div class="answer" style="display: none;">
          We offer rental amenities($), weather permitting, as follows: A Jumping Pillow (on weekends only – On Saturdays twice a day, for one hour each, and for one hour on Sunday morning), Pedal Karts, Paddle Boats, and Mini Golf for rent. All rentals (except minigolf) is for 30 minutes ONLY for $4-$5 per person per half hour. In addition, Fun Bands are an unlimited use band (half hour on and half hour off) for your stay up to one week for $12 per person per stay, or $7 per person per day. Children 4 and under are just $1.00. Furthermore, the program is for children 12 – adult. Children under 12 require adult supervision. Program requires policy agreement and safety waiver for everyone participating. We recommend helmets for all riders.
          <span style="color: #800000;">
           <em>
            Helmets are required for a
           </em>
          </span>
          <span style="color: #800000;">
           <em>
            ll children under 12 to ride pedal karts.
           </em>
          </span>
          <span style="text-align: justify;">
           Hence, you can bring your own bicycle helmet, borrow one our ours with each rental, or buy a generic helmet at our camp store. In addition, we have paddle boats rentals and we provide life jackets with each rental. See
          </span>
          <a href="https://meadvillekoa.com/recreation/" rel="nofollow" style="text-align: justify;" target="_blank">
           Rental Amenities
          </a>
