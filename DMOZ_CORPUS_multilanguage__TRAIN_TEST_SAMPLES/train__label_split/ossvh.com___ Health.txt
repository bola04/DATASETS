      <div class="maintext">
       <p>
        At Olney-Sandy Spring Veterinary Hospital (OSSVH), we treat your pets as if they are our own. Our lives are truly enriched by our daily interactions with our pets. As an American Animal Hospital Association (AAHA)-accredited, full-service veterinary hospital, we offer only the highest quality, professional pet care services provided by a compassionate and expert team.
       </p>
       <p>
        Our mission:
       </p>
       <p style="padding-left:20px;  padding-right:20px;">
        <em>
         The Olney-Sandy Spring Veterinary Hospital exists to promote animal wellness, facilitate the human-animal bond and alleviate pain and suffering. While taking care of our patients and their people, we will also take care of ourselves by providing a working environment that our employees can take pride in and a business that provides a valued service to the community.
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        We strive to achieve our mission by offering you and your pet access to an exceptional professional staff of doctors and technicians operating in a state-of-the-art veterinary hospital. By focusing on continuing education, our veterinarians learn and practice the most current and advanced techniques in the areas of
        <a href="/veterinary-services/healthy-pet-plans.html">
         small animal medicine
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="/veterinary-services/pet-surgery.html">
         surgery
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="/veterinary-services/pet-dental-services.html">
         dentistry
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="/veterinary-services/pet-oncology.html">
         oncology
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="/veterinary-services/pet-reproductive-services.html">
         reproductive services
        </a>
        , and integrative medicine.
       </p>
       <p>
        We believe that wellness and preventive care are strongly coupled. Our commitment to preventive medicine extends and improves the quality of your pet's life by keeping them in optimum health. At OSSVH, we take a holistic approach to the wellness of all our patients by treating each of them as an individual with unique needs. We stress compassion and respect for all patients and pet owners, and we will make every effort to closely communicate with you and educate you to promote the best possible health for your pet.
       </p>
