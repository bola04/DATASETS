                              magicJack Vocaltec invented Voice over IP (VOIP), having sold over 10 million magicJacks since product launch.                              </div>
                             <div>
                             </div>

                             </div>
                             <div>
                             </div>

                              This unique combination of proprietary assets allows us to offer low prices and high quality to Billions of users across the World, eliminating any dependence on third-party products and service licensing.                              </div>
