        <h1 class="title">
         Marina
        </h1>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.emeraldisleresort.com/wp/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/emerald.jpg" rel="lightbox[32]">
          <img alt="emerald" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-330" height="344" sizes="(max-width: 613px) 100vw, 613px" src="http://www.emeraldisleresort.com/wp/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/emerald-1024x576.jpg" srcset="http://www.emeraldisleresort.com/wp/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/emerald-1024x576.jpg 1024w, http://www.emeraldisleresort.com/wp/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/emerald-600x337.jpg 600w" width="613"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Emerald Isle Marina offers all the amenities you’d expect, fuel, food, supplies, boat rentals, slips, and much more!
        </p>
        <p>
         Our full service marina has both covered and uncovered rental slips and a ship store complete with a variety of items for your convenience. The grill cooks up a menu of snacks and sandwiches that will satisfy any appetite.
        </p>
        <p>
         Stop in from an exciting day of recreation and enjoy a delicious meal from our Restaurant, fuel up your boat, pick up supplies or browse our gift shop.
        </p>
        <p>
         Relax and enjoy the view of Green River Lake from your boat in one of our many scenic wooden deck slips.
        </p>
