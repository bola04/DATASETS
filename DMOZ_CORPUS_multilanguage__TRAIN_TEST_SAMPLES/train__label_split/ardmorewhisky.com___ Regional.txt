      <p>
       Guided by the majestic golden eagle that glides high above the distillery as protector and protagonist, The Ardmore® is a peated single malt whisky shaped by its deep rooted connection to the land, people and wildlife of its Scottish Highland home.
      </p>
      <p>
       Take a journey to the true heart of whisky with The Ardmore® and reach a new understanding of the heights of flavour encapsulated in its rich, amber liquid.
      </p>
      <p>
       Let your spirits soar with The Ardmore®.
      </p>

    <!-- The Ardmore / Distillery -->
    <div class="page distillery" id="distillery">
     <div class="container">
      <div class="page-title">
       <img alt="The Ardmore" class="img-responsive" src="img/theardmore.png"/>
      </div>
      <p>
       The Ardmore distillery sits at the highest point of the Northern railway line, 600 feet above sea level. Close to the small village of Kennethmont, the proximity to the railway line allowed founder Adam Teacher to transport materials from Glasgow to this remote corner of Aberdeenshire, helping the distillery flourish.
      </p>
      <p>
       Almost unchanged from the moment he made his dream a reality, the water is still drawn from the naturally rising springs that sit on the 1,500 foot tall Knockandy Hill.
      </p>
      <p>
       Those who live on the rugged fringes of the Highlands embrace the freedom of the great outdoors and The Ardmore® brings together the power of nature in every drop that travels from our distillery.
      </p>

    <div class="page whiskys" id="our-whiskys">
     <div class="container">
      <div class="page-title">
       <img alt="Our Whiskys" class="img-responsive" src="img/ourwhiskys.jpg"/>
      </div>
      <div class="whisky-list">
       <!-- The Ardmore Legacy -->
       <div class="row">
        <div class="col-md-5 whisky-img">
         <img alt="The Ardmore Legacy" class="img-responsive" src="img/whiskylegacy.jpg"/>
        </div>
        <div class="col-md-7">
         <h2>
          Legacy
         </h2>
         <p>
          The Ardmore® Legacy celebrates the distillery’s unique location on the fringes of the Highlands, offering a lightly peated liquid that is sweet and uplifting. The gentle flavour fully captures the essence of this spirit’s stunning Highland home. The Ardmore® Legacy will appeal to experienced peated malt drinkers and newcomers, alike.
         </p>
         <div class="whisky-description">
          <div class="item">
           <strong>
            Colour
           </strong>
           – Light golden.
          </div>
          <div class="item">
           <strong>
            Nose
           </strong>
           - Heather Honey with hints of cinnamon and toffee bonbon.
           <br/>
           Very subtle hints of peat smoke.
          </div>
          <div class="item">
           <strong>
            Palate
           </strong>
           - Creamy vanilla spice gives way to more overt smoky charcoal notes, especially with the addition of water. Smoke doesn’t dominate and is balanced by the sweet honey and spice flavours.
          </div>
          <div class="item">
           <strong>
            Finish
           </strong>
           - Full bodied, silky mouth feel with a dry, tangy and lingering aftertaste, coupled with delicious spice notes.
          </div>
          <div class="item">
           <strong>
            ABV
           </strong>
           – 40%
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
       </div>
       <!-- The Ardmore Tradition -->
       <div class="row">
        <div class="col-md-5 whisky-img">
         <img alt="The Ardmore Tradition" class="img-responsive" src="img/whiskytradition.jpg"/>
        </div>
        <div class="col-md-7">
         <h2>
          Tradition
         </h2>
         <p>
          A Travel Retail exclusive, The Ardmore® Tradition is a taste of history itself, journeying back to a time when most Highland distilleries dried their barley with the sweet smoke of local Highland peat. Double matured in oak barrels then smaller Quarter Casks, The Ardmore® Tradition is a full and rich liquid, with unique Highland peat smoke ﬂavours.
         </p>
         <div class="whisky-description">
          <div class="item">
           <strong>
            Colour
           </strong>
           - Burnished Gold
          </div>
          <div class="item">
           <strong>
            Nose
           </strong>
           - At bottle strength (46%), the influence of both maturations compliment each other producing a full bodied smooth aroma. The maturity comes from the American barrels and the spicy, creamy nose from the old style smaller casks.
          </div>
          <div class="item">
           <strong>
            Palate
           </strong>
           - The full rich flavours burst and fill the mouth with a creamy peat tang and a touch of vanilla’s soft gentleness.
           <br/>
           If a touch of water is added, then the flavours increase with the addition of a sweet ripe fruit start followed by a tang of peat.
          </div>
          <div class="item">
           <strong>
            Finish
           </strong>
           - Full, succulent and quite long.
          </div>
          <div class="item">
           <strong>
            ABV
           </strong>
           - 46%
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
       </div>
       <!-- The Ardmore Portwood -->
       <div class="row">
        <div class="col-md-5 whisky-img">
         <img alt="The Ardmore Portwood" class="img-responsive" src="img/whiskyportwood.jpg"/>
        </div>
        <div class="col-md-7">
         <h2>
          Port Wood Finish
         </h2>
         <p>
          A unique and premium expression of The Ardmore®. Double cask matured in American white oak bourbon barrels and European half port pipes, resulting in a liquid that is light and sweet with a balance of fruity, smoky notes.
          <br/>
          Non-chill filtered and aged for 12 years, this ruby gold single malt has been made using the same traditional distilling methods for 100 years.
         </p>
         <div class="whisky-description">
          <div class="item">
           <strong>
            Colour
           </strong>
           - Ruby gold.
          </div>
          <div class="item">
           <strong>
            Nose
           </strong>
           - Initial nose of strawberries and summer fruits with a hint of pepper. Notes of burnt orange and cinnamon spice underlie. With water, the rich fruit aromas of red apple and cranberry intensify, accompanied by sweet honey and subtle spiced wood notes.
          </div>
          <div class="item">
           <strong>
            Palate
           </strong>
           - Sweet red apple and honey with the subtlest hint of charcoal smoke.
          </div>
          <div class="item">
           <strong>
            Finish
           </strong>
           - Initially smooth with a long, lingering finish culminating in the trademark Ardmore dryness.
          </div>
          <div class="item">
           <strong>
            ABV
           </strong>
           - 46%
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
       </div>
       <!-- The Ardmore Triple Wood -->
       <div class="row">
        <div class="col-md-5 whisky-img">
         <img alt="The Ardmore Triple Wood" class="img-responsive" src="img/7587-AR-TripleWood-BottleandTube-L04-V03_LR.png"/>
        </div>
        <div class="col-md-7">
         <h2>
          Triple Wood
         </h2>
         <p>
          The Ardmore® Triple Wood is a complex and rewarding travel retail exclusive Highland malt. Triple matured in American Oak barrels, quarter casks and puncheons, this non-chill filtered expression is rich in traditional Highland peat smoke notes.
         </p>
         <div class="whisky-description">
          <div class="item">
           <strong>
            Colour
           </strong>
           - Golden straw, natural honey.
          </div>
          <div class="item">
           <strong>
            Nose
           </strong>
           - Biscuity cereal notes and the scent of banana underlie the initial nose of ginger, burnt sugar, cherries and honey. A drop of water intensifies the ginger snap biscuit notes with a hint of cinnamon, and soft highland peat smoke.
          </div>
          <div class="item">
           <strong>
            Palate
           </strong>
           - Light caramelised sugar, toasted barley, and warming, light peat smoke are followed by sweet vanilla custard. Water releases notes of pink peppercorn, and dried fruit flavours (raisin and candid orange peel).
          </div>
          <div class="item">
           <strong>
            Finish
           </strong>
           - Light with soft peat smoke, lingering pepper and toasted almonds with a well-balanced dry mouth feel.
          </div>
          <div class="item">
           <strong>
            ABV
           </strong>
           - 46%
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
       </div>
      </div>
