          <h1 class="art-postheader">
           Welcome
          </h1>
          <div class="art-postcontent clearfix">
           <div class="fcbk_share">
            <div class="fcbk_button">
             <a href="http://www.facebook.com/pages/St-Johns-Cathedral-Sligo/222629951121067" target="_blank">
              <img alt="Fb-Button" src="http://sligocathedral.elphin.anglican.org/wp-content/uploads/facebook-image/facebook-ico1.png"/>
             </a>
            </div>
           </div>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            We welcome you to Sligo Cathedral Group of
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            St. John’s Cathedral, John Street, Sligo Town,
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            Rosses Point Church and St. Anne’s Church, Knocknarea, Strandhill, Co. Sligo.
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: left;">
            <a href="http://sligocathedral.elphin.anglican.org/wp-content/uploads/2017/09/St-Johns-Christmas-Tree-Festival-Poster-01.jpg">
             <img alt="St._Johns_Cathedral-Ireland" class="wp-image-743 aligncenter" height="231" src="http://sligocathedral.elphin.anglican.org/wp-content/uploads/2009/12/St._Johns_Cathedral-Ireland-300x196.jpg" width="354"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            Sligo is situated in the North West of Ireland on the coast in the midst of a beautiful area.
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            It is our endeavour to present the church as a vibrant body in the Sligo community, seeking  to extend the family of God through social concerns and Christian teaching.
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
             <em>
              <strong>
               Important Dates for your Diary
              </strong>
             </em>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            Sunday 17th June – Messy Church in St John’s Hall from 4pm to 6pm
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            Monday 18th June – St John’s Select Vestry, Parish Room at 8pm
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            Thursday 21st June – St Anne’s Select Vestry, in the church at 8.30pm
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            Diocesan Council Meeting changed to Thursday 28th June at 7.45pm in the Bush Hotel, Carrick-on-Shannon
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            EVERY Tuesday – Bowls Club meeting in the Hall at 8.00pm – all welcome.
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            Also EVERY Tuesday – Whist Club meets in the Parish Room (Magee House) at 8.00pm – all welcome.
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            ********
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            <span style="color: #3366ff;">
             MOTHERS’ UNION
            </span>
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            Tuesday 29th May – Festival Service in Lissadell Church at 8pm – preacher Bishop Ken Clarke
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            Saturday 21st July – Outing to Letterkenny and Raphoe, booking required by end of June, call Trudi 086 8181 110
           </p>
