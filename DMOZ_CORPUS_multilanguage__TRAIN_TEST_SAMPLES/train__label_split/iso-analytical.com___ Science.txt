            <p align="left">
             Iso-Analytical
             <font face="Arial" size="2">
              is a modern 
             laboratory specializing in Stable Isotope Ratio Analysis (SIRA). We 
             provide a high quality analytical service for the light stable 
             isotopes: hydrogen-2, carbon-13, nitrogen-15, oxygen-18 and 
             sulfur-34. Established in 2000, we have analysed in excess of 300,000 samples from 5,000 individual studies in 
 	    agriculture, archaeology, ecology, environmental science, food 
             authenticity, forensic chemistry, geochemistry and medical research. 
             We pride ourselves in providing a quality assured service, in good time and 
             at a competitive price.

              </a>

             price.
