             </h3>

            </header>

            <div class="entry-summary">
             <p>

               <img alt="" class="size-medium wp-image-3403 alignleft" height="237" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="http://ngwte.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/North-Georgia-Walk-to-Emmaus-Picture-300x237.png" srcset="http://ngwte.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/North-Georgia-Walk-to-Emmaus-Picture-300x237.png 300w, http://ngwte.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/North-Georgia-Walk-to-Emmaus-Picture.png 320w" width="300"/>

We will also unveil the next set of engraved Bricks. Make your plans to attend now!
