          Welcome to PUUF [Prescott UU Fellowship]
         </h1>
        </header>
        <!-- .page-header -->
        <div class="entry-content clearfix">
         <link href="https://puuf.net/" itemprop="mainEntityOfPage"/>
         <p>
          <!-----

         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            <span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif;">
             <span style="color: #800000;">
              <span style="color: #800000; font-size: medium;">
               <strong>
                <img alt="" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-11" height="103" src="https://puuf.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/puufwindow2.jpg" title="puufwindow" width="103"/>
                Prescott UU Fellowship or PUUF [“puff”] is a lay-led, welcoming congregation with Sunday services that are religious only in the broadest sense.
               </strong>
              </span>
             </span>
            </span>
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">
           We meet at 945 Rancho Vista Drive in Prescott, Arizona each Sunday at 11:00am.
          </span>
         </p>
         <a class="pdfemb-viewer" data-height="max" data-toolbar="bottom" data-toolbar-fixed="off" data-width="max" href="/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/Coming-up-at-PUUF-2018-07.pdf" style="">
          Coming-up-at-PUUF-2018-07
          <br/>
         </a>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium; font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif;">
           Our services celebrate the merged stream of ethical and moral concepts which characterize our increasingly multicultural society, provide learning opportunities and discuss local environmental and community issues.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">
            Our programs encourage time for congregational response and feed-back and an atmosphere of informality prevails.  We hear from invited guest speakers who provide programs relating to our seven U U principles and clergy once a month.
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium; font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; color: #000000;">
           Other activities include social evenings for a movie and potluck supper or circle suppers, book discussion groups and other adult educational events.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: medium; font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; color: #000000;">
           We meet in the Fellowship Hall in the pines at the southwest edge of Prescott, AZ.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">
           <strong>
            <span style="font-size: small;">
             <em>
              From the Square proceed south on Montezuma one mile to the stop light of Copper Basin Rd.  Turning right, proceed 2.4 miles to the first stop sign, Rancho Vista and turn right to 945.
             </em>
            </span>
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: right;">
          <a href="https://puuf.net/map/">
           <span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: medium; color: #800000;">
            Click Here to See Map
           </span>
          </a>
         </p>
         <div class="belcheryellow roundfifteen aligncenter" style="width: 625px; border-width: 3px; border-style: solid;">

              <p style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="color: #800000; font-size: large; font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif;">
                <strong>
                 Vision Statement:
                </strong>
               </span>
              </p>
              <p>
               <span style="font-size: medium; font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif;">
                <span style="font-size: medium;">
                 We will seek growth in our Fellowship to include members and friends.
                </span>
               </span>
              </p>
              <p>
               <span style="font-size: medium; font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif;">
                We will build a better community
               </span>
               <br/>
               <span style="font-size: medium; font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif;">
                <span style="font-size: medium;">
                 through social justice.
                </span>
               </span>
              </p>
              <p>
               <span style="font-size: medium; font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif;">
                We will strive to be progressive leaders of the community.
               </span>
              </p>
             </td>
             <td valign="top" width="50%">
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: large; color: #800000; font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif;">
                <strong>
                 Mission Statement:
                </strong>
               </span>
              </p>
              <p>
               <span style="font-size: medium; font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; color: #000000;">
                As a Fellowship we will:
                <br/>
                Work with outreach organizations
               </span>
               <span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: medium;">
                in the community to achieve social justice.
               </span>
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: left;">
               <span style="font-size: medium; font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif;">
                Develop programs which will appeal to progressively inclined people, and
               </span>
              </p>
              <p>
               <span style="font-size: medium; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">
                Serve as an extended family to each other.
               </span>
              </p>
             </td>
            </tr>
           </tbody>
          </table>
         </div>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">
           <strong>
            <span style="font-size: medium;">
             The facilities of PUUF are available to the public for meetings, weddings and other occasions.
            </span>
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
