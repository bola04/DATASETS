       <div class="block block--block block--block-7" id="block-block-7">
        <div class="block__content">
         <h2>
          Combined services/Ultimate PCB and Interconnection Packages
         </h2>
         <p>
          Connect-2 Technology and Cleveland Circuits often combine their services to offer manufactures what we refer to as Ultimate Packages.  Due to the significant synergy between Electronic Interconnection and Printed Circuit Boards many customers choose to save time, overhead and increase their quality control by combining their electronic sub-assembly requirements and sourcing both their printed circuit boards and wiring interconnection from one source.  If required we can also combine the above with electro/mechanical assembly and build your complete sub-assembly ready for market.  With Cleveland Technologies the choice is yours, whether it be a stripped and terminated wire or a complete box build.
         </p>
