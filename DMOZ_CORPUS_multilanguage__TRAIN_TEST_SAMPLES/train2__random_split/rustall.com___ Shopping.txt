     <br/>
     Rustall is an easy to use rust &amp; weathering product.  First patented in 1991,
 It is an easy to use four step weathering wash process designed to simulate a random,
 natural rust effect on scale model tanks, model trains, trackside junk,
 scale autos or trucks and one inch scale miniatures.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     Rustall will simulate rust on styrene, metal, cast resin and even paper.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     The Rustall rust &amp; weathering system is non corrosive, non flammable,
 dries fast and won't craze plastic models. It requires no special equipment 
 to produce professional results and cleans up with soap and water.
     <br/>
