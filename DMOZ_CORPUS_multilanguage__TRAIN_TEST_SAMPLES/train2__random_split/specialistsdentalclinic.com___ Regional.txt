A crown - sometimes called a "cap" - is a tooth-like covering placed over a carefully prepared existing tooth. It is used to strengthen, restore or improve the appearance of your natural tooth

         </h4>

          Removal of impacted third molar
         </h4>
         <p>
          The last tooth in the dental arch erupts late into the oral cavity. Often there will be lack of space for these teeth to move into the mouth. These teeth get impacted onto the adjacent teeth / bone which can lead to decay and food impaction. These conditions require removal of the impacted teeth by a minor surgical procedure under local anesthesia. We have an expert surgical team for doing this procedure.
