    <h3>
     Welcome
    </h3>
    to the official WEB site of the Narrow-bandwidth Television Association devoted 
 to all aspects of early television. Members' interests include:
    <p align="left">
     - Historical interest in the origins of early television.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     - Restoration of original equipment.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     - Construction of replica mechanical television apparatus
     <br/>
     <br/>
     - Conventional electronic circuit design and construction
     <br/>
     <br/>
     - Personal Computers and software associated with the display and production 
 of low definition television.
    </p>
    <br/>
    Regards, Chris Lewis, NBTVA Webmaster.
