           <h1 style="text-align: center;margin-top: 10px;margin-bottom: 10px">
            Welcome to the Museum of the History of Science
           </h1>
           <p style="text-align: left;line-height: 1.5em">
            MHS houses an unrivalled collection of early scientific instruments in the
            <a href="/about/history/">
             world’s oldest surviving purpose-built museum building
            </a>
            , the Old Ashmolean on Broad Street, Oxford. We are both a department of the University of Oxford and an active public museum, offering a
            <a href="http://www.mhs.ox.ac.uk/events/">
             programme
            </a>
            of family-friendly events, gallery tours, talks and much more, along with
            <a href="/education/">
             taught sessions for schools
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: left;line-height: 1.5em">
            Visits to the Museum by groups of more than 15 should be booked in advance by using the
            <a href="/visit/groups/group-booking-form/">
             group visits booking form
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <p>
            For an introduction to the Museum and its collections, come along to one of our regular tours. Led by our award-winning volunteer guides, they usually take place every Thursday at 2.30pm and 3.15pm and every Saturday at 12.30pm and 1.15pm.
           </p>
           <p>
            For those unable to come to Oxford we produce
            <a href="/exhibits/">
             online versions of our exhibitions
            </a>
            , along with standalone online resources and
            <a href="http://www.mhs.ox.ac.uk/collections/imu-search-page/">
             a web version of our collections database
            </a>
            . For the latest additions to this site you can
            <a href="/feed/rss">
             subscribe to our newsfeed
            </a>
            .
            <a href="http://www.mhs.ox.ac.uk/feed/rss/">
             <img alt="General Newsfeed for the Museum of the History of Science" border="0" src="/wp-content/uploads/feed-icon-28x28.png" style="float: right" title="General Newsfeed for the Museum of the History of Science"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            To support the Museum’s broad range of work, please consider
            <a href="http://www.mhs.ox.ac.uk/support-us/">
             making a donation
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
