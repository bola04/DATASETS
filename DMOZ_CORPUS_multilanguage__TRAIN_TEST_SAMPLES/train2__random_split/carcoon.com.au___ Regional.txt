           <h2>
            Welcome to
            <strong>
             Carcoon Australasia
            </strong>
           </h2>
           <p>
            <img alt="bean-car-carcoon" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-1348" height="127" src="https://www.carcoon.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/bean-car-carcoon.jpg" width="250"/>

           </p>
           <p style="clear: both;">
            <a href="https://www.carcoon.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/mephisto-tank-carcoon.jpg">
             <img alt="Mephisto Tank Carcoon" class="size-full wp-image-1366 alignright" height="150" src="https://www.carcoon.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/mephisto-thumb.jpg" width="200"/>
            </a>
            <strong>
