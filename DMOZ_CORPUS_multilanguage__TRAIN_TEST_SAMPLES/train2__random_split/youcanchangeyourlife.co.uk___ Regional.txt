           Too many people are living a life that is not at all like the dreams they had. Perhaps you too wonder what happened to the dreams you had. Are you resigned to the way things are? Do you believe that you have to put up with it all and that there is nothing you can do to make a difference?
          </p>
          <p>
           Perhaps you are on a path which you believe will bring you at some stage your just rewards.
           <br/>
           Perhaps you believe that your life, especially your personal life has to be left ‘on hold’ until such time.
          </p>
          <p>
           Has it ever occurred to you that life moves on and there is never that mythical time when everything is ‘sorted’?
          </p>
          <p>
           You may find it difficult to believe right now, but it’s true. There is always change. As some situations improve, other challenges arise.
          </p>

           The most important thing is to start to live the life you want now.
          </p>

           You can begin to introduce changes into your life.
          </p>
          <p>
           When you do that, not only will your life change for the better but other things will change too.
          </p>
          <p>
           A basic principle of change is that you can only change yourself, you cannot change other people. However by changing yourself other people will change and your world will change too.
          </p>
