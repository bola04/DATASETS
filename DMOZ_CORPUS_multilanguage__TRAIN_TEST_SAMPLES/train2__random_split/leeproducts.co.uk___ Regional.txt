         <h1>
          Welcome to Lee Products
          <br/>
          <span>
           Innovation in Miniature
          </span>
         </h1>
         <p>
          Since 1948, The Lee Company has pioneered the 
 						development of miniature, precision fluid control 
 						components for use throughout industry.
         </p>
         <p>
          Markets served include aerospace, down-hole oil tools, machine 
 						tools, medical / scientific instrumentation and ink-jet 
 						printing.
         </p>
         <p>
          Lee's unique 
 														capabilities in 
 														miniaturisation and 
 														engineering expertise 
 														keep the company at the 
 														forefront of fluid flow 
 														technology.
         </p>
         <p>
          Lee Products 
 														maintains a sales force 
 														of graduate engineers 
 														who are ready to assist 
 														customers in solving 
 														fluid flow problems, and 
 														a significant amount of 
 														stock to ensure on-time 
 														deliveries.
         </p>
         <p>
          Quality Assurance at 
 														Lee is a management 
 														commitment of the 
 														highest order. Lee 
 														Products Ltd. achieved 
 														accreditation to ISO9002 
 														in 1999, ISO9001:2000 in 
 														2003 and ISO9001:2008 in 
 														2010 whilst The Lee 
 														Company was one of the 
 														first aerospace 
 														companies in USA to 
 														achieve accreditation to 
 														AS9000.
         </p>
         <p>
          The Lee Company has 
 														subsidiary companies in 
 														France, Germany, Italy 
 														and Scandinavia, and is 
 														represented by agents 
 														and distributors 
 														throughout the world.
         </p>
         <p>
          Visit our worldwide 
 														sites through our
          <a href="sales_offices.htm">
           International Sales 
 														Offices
          </a>
          page..
         </p>

          <span>
           The Lee Company - Lee Plugs
          </span>
         </h5>
         <p>
          Lee Plug® expansion plugs seal fluid passages 
 						leak-tight, without the use of O-rings, threads, or 
 						sealants. It is simply the most successful method of 
 						sealing drilled holes in critical applications. Well 
 						over 100 million Lee Plugs have been used on aircraft, 
 						missiles, spacecraft, and other sophisticated hydraulic 
 						systems that require a permanent leak-tight seal.
