                         Are you looking for practical methods and techniques for overcoming obstacles to creativity? In this course we will write from prompts and discuss ways to leap and go deep on the page whether you write poetry, nonfiction, memoir or fiction.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                         Together we’ll examine the kinds of blocks all of us encounter in our work, and we will explore ways to leap over them using exercises to develop action, depth, perspective, lyricism, and voice to renew our awareness and confidence as a writer.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                         Laurie McMillan, M.Ed., writer and Therapeutic Writing Specialist, has led workshops in several states for over 20 years focusing on the power of creativity and community in her writing classes.
