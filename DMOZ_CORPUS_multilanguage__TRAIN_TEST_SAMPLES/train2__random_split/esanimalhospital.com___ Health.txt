   4. Call appendAround() on that element when the DOM is ready

   (function( $ ){
     $.fn.appendAround = function(){
       return this.each(function(){
         
         var $self = $( this ),
             att = "data-set",
             $parent = $self.parent(),

             attval = $parent.attr( att ),
             $set = $( "["+ att +"='" + attval + "']" );

           var found = 0;
           $set.each(function(){

               $self.appendTo( this );
               found++;
               parent = this;
             }
           });

         Eastern Shore Animal Hospital
        </h1>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Eastern Shore Animal Hospital is now utilizing state of the art dental  equipment including digital x-rays! Learn more about our comprehensive  approach to dental care including dental health assessment, treatment  and prevention
          <a href="http://www.esanimalhospital.com/p/veterinarian/dental_care_4427/painter-va-23420/eastern-shore-animal-hospital-4427">

        <p>
         Congratulations to Dr. Paula Cameron, Dr. Emily Humphries, Dr. Drew   Humphries, and Melanie Parker, LVT, who have recently undergone advanced   dental training with Dr. Luskin at the Animal Dental Training Center!
        </p>
        <p>
         Eastern Shore Animal Hospital in Painter, VA is a full service companion animal pet and large animal hospital offering a full veterinary laboratory, pet surgery, a pet pharmacy with tick and flea control. Veterinary Doctor Charles E. Cameron’s experienced team of skilled vets welcomes you to our veterinary practice serving Painter.
        </p>
        <p>
         We provide a broad spectrum of diagnostic procedures through in-house testing and the use of external laboratories. We also work closely with local practices when special diagnostic procedures are required. The facility includes a well-stocked pet pharmacy, in-hospital surgery suite, in-house x-ray capabilities, diagnostic ultrasound exams,
         <a href="http://www.esanimalhospital.com/p/veterinarian/spay_neuter_4427/painter-va-23420/eastern-shore-animal-hospital-4427" target="_self">
          spays &amp; neuters
         </a>
         , a closely supervised hospitalization area, and
         <a href="http://www.esanimalhospital.com/p/veterinarian/boarding_4427/painter-va-23420/eastern-shore-animal-hospital-4427" target="_self">
          indoor boarding kennels
         </a>
         with outdoor walking areas.
        </p>
        <p>
         It is our daily privilege to provide quality veterinary care to your dog, cat or other small animal pet with both compassion and competence, even exotic pets like rabbits, rats, turtles and snakes. We hope to ensure a positive impact on our patients, clients, and the local community. Our services and facilities are designed to assist in routine preventive care for young, healthy pets, early detection and treatment of disease as your pet ages, and complete medical and surgical care as necessary during his or her lifetime.
        </p>
        <p>
         At this site, you will find information about our practice philosophy, our services, and helpful forms to assist you.
        </p>
