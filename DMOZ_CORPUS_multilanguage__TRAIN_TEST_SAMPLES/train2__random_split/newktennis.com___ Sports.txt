            <h1>
             World Champion John Newcombe is very proud to celebrate 50 years of service to tennis-loving enthusiasts of all ages and abilities the world over.
            </h1>
            <p>
             Over the last 50 years, the Ranch has evolved from a run-down dude ranch, to a junior camp,  to a full-service destination resort offering tennis programs, conference and meeting facilities, adventure camps, experiential learning, and much more! The Ranch is a very special and beautiful place – join us to experience our unique “team-family-team” philosophy.
            </p>
            <p>
             <iframe allow="autoplay; encrypted-media" allowfullscreen="" class="hide_this_mobile" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/-t5eGb5OyJI?rel=0" width="560">
             </iframe>
            </p>
            <p>
             Established in 1968 by tennis legend John Newcombe, the Tennis Ranch has evolved into one of the premier tennis destinations in the world. Nestled in the Hill Country of Texas, the Ranch offers year-round junior tennis camps, adult tennis vacations, a world-class tennis academy, and fantasy camps with tennis legends. The Ranch is a totally unique tennis environment that can be enjoyed by everyone, at all levels. Whether you’re a beginner or an expert, there’s a program here for you! In addition to tennis, the Ranch offers corporate team building, adventure camps, meetings, and special events.
            </p>
