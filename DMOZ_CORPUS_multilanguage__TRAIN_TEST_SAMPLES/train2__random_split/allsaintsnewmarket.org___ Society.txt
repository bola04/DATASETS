        <h1 style="padding-left: 80px;">
         Welcome
        </h1>
        <p>
         Welcome to the parish church of All Saints, Newmarket. Many of our church family members are long time Christians. Others are recent converts to Christianity and a few people among us are simply enquiring and exploring Christian faith and understanding. We greet everyone without judgment or prejudice and commit to seeking God’s Heart and serving God’s World. Our aim is to live faithfully and generously amidst our community in worship and with servant hearts. Come and join us!.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Reverend Max Osborne (Vicar)
         </strong>
        </p>

          <h2>
           Prayer for Growth
          </h2>
          <p>
           God who brings life to creation and to people, help us to see you at work in Suffolk and to work with you so your kingdom grows here. By the grace of your Spirit, be the source of our love, our energy and our songs so that, separately and together, we may draw closer to you. May we speak of you and share your life with people who live, work and visit here, bringing glory to your name through Jesus Christ our Lord and in the power of the Spirit. Amen.
          </p>
