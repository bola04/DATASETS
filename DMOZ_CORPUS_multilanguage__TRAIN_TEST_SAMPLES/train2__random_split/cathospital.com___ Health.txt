            The Cat Hospital of Fairfax was founded in 1991. We have provided exemplary care for over twenty-five years to thousands of feline family members. Our practice is extremely cat-friendly, with every part of our hospital designed to ensure that your loved one is as comfortable as possible. Every examination that we perform is comprehensive to ensure that any health problems are identified early. We offer numerous cutting-edge procedures, technology, and diagnostic tests to direct clients to the medical services appropriate for screening and treatment for their cats. We make sure that your feline gets the most personalized care possible.
