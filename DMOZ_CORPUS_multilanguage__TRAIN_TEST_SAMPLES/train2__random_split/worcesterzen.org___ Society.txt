          <header class="entry-header">
           <h1 class="entry-title">
            Welcome Home!
           </h1>
          </header>
          <div class="entry-content">
           <p>
            <img alt="" data-recalc-dims="1" src="https://i2.wp.com/worcesterzen.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/wzc2.png?w=770&amp;ssl=1"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            We warmly welcome you to Boundless Way Temple of Worcester MA, a place of Zen Buddhist training and practice. We invite you to join us as we come together in stillness and silence to awaken to the possibility of being fully human.
           </p>
           <p>
            The Temple teachers offer guidance in traditional Zen practices such as breath awareness, mindfulness practices, inquiry into the moment, shikantaza (just sitting), and koan practice. We are dedicated to helping every student find a meditation practice that fits their unique personality and deepest intention for awakening. We are committed to the continuing work of integrating our practice into daily life.
           </p>
           <p>
            Visitors, whether new to meditation or experienced, are always welcome to join in Morning or Evening Practice, which usually happens eight times a week. You do not need to be member to practice at Boundless Way Temple.
           </p>
           <p>
            Be sure to check out our
            <a href="/calendar/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">
             calendar
            </a>
            for more information on daily meditation practice, special events, retreats, and workshops. Our regular practice schedule is as follows:
           </p>
           <div class="row-fluid">
            <div class="span12">
             <ul>
              <li>
               <strong>
                Morning Practice
               </strong>
               is held Monday through Friday from 7 AM to 8 AM
              </li>
              <li>
               <strong>
                Evening Practice
               </strong>
               is held Tuesdays &amp; Thursdays from 7 PM to 8 PM and Sundays from 7 PM to 8:30 PM.
              </li>
             </ul>
            </div>
           </div>
           <p>
            We are located on the west side of Worcester on Pleasant Street near Tatnuck Square near a bus route (link to directions) and with free parking. Our lovingly-tended meditation garden is open during daylight hours in good weather for quiet enjoyment and contemplation.
           </p>
           <div class="row-fluid">
            <div class="span12">
             <p>
              If you are new to our practice we recommend you visit our page about
              <a href="https://worcesterzen.org/first-visit-to-boundless-way-zen-temple/">
               visiting for the first time
              </a>
              .
             </p>
             <p>
              <!--
 
 
 <div class="row-fluid">
 
 
 <div class="span12">
 We warmly welcome you to Boundless Way Temple of Worcester MA, a place of Zen Buddhist training and practice. Our regular practice schedule is as follows:
 
 
 <ul>
     
 
 <li><strong>Morning Practice</strong> is held Monday through Friday from 7 AM to 8 AM</li>
 
 
     
 
 <li><strong>Evening Practice</strong> is held Tuesdays &amp; Thursdays from 7 PM to 8 PM and Sundays from 7 PM to 8:30 PM.</li>
 
 
 </ul>
 
 
 Be sure to check out our <a href="/calendar/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">calendar</a> for our talks &amp; dokusan schedule, and for more information on daily meditation practice, talks, retreats, and workshops.
 
 People of all experience levels and walks of life are welcome to come to the Temple for any of our practice periods. You do not have to be a member to participate in our programs.
 
 <b>What to wear</b>
 <span style="font-weight: 400;">It’s recommended to wear loose, comfortable clothing.  Anything that’s too restrictive could become uncomfortable or distracting to you.  Please also keep in mind the other people who will be sitting with you, and avoid wearing anything that could be overly distracting to them, as well (including strong fragrances).  In cooler weather, a light sweater or similar garment could be helpful if the zendo is chilly, but there are also blankets available to borrow.</span>
 
 <b>When you arrive</b>
 <span style="font-weight: 400;">Please try to arrive early (around 15 minutes) before the start of the meditation period, so that someone can greet you and give you a personal introduction to meditation practice.  Remove your shoes in the vestibule and place on the shelves there, then enter through the main door.  The first doorway on the left is the parlor, where you may hang your coat in the closet.  If someone hasn’t already greeted you, they will likely be along shortly.</span>
 
 <b>The restroom</b>
 <span style="font-weight: 400;">If you walk past the parlor towards the mirror, on your left just beyond the stairs, you’ll see a door.  That’s the first floor restroom.  If the door is closed and your needs are urgent, someone can direct you to other available facilities in the building.</span>
 
 If you are new to Zen, we recommend you arrive 15 minutes prior to your first practice so that one of our <a href="https://worcesterzen.org/teachers/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">resident teachers</a> or <a href="https://worcesterzen.org/teachers/#practiceleaders">practice leaders</a> can explain <a href="https://worcesterzen.org/practice-forms/">basic forms</a> before we go into silence, and answer any questions you may have.
