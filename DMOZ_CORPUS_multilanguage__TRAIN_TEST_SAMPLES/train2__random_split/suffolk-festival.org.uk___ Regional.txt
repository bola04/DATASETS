       <h2>
        Good news!
       </h2>
       <ul type="disc">
        <li>
         <strong>
          Prices       held at 2015 levels for THIRD year running
         </strong>
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Discount       for early entries
         </strong>
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Prizes       totalling over £2,000
         </strong>
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          FREE       introductory offer
         </strong>
         for Grade 1 and under classes in Organ,       Piano, Strings and Wind, plus novice Junior Vocal classes and 7 yrs       and under Verse Speaking. These classes will be
         <strong>
          FREE
         </strong>
         !
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        Thank you to all performers, supporters and helpers who made last  year’s Festival such a success. It was good to see Organ classes back, with 12  entrants. Why enter the Suffolk Festival? A recent survey revealed that the  majority of those who enter our classes do so for
        <u>
         enjoyment
        </u>
        . Why not  join them!
        <br/>
        <strong>
         Andrew Leach,
        </strong>
        <br/>
        <strong>
         Chairman
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Suffolk Festival is for people of all ages and a  wide range of ability. Amateur  musicians, dancers and actors have the chance to  perform in a friendly and supportive environment, with the constructive advice  of a professional adjudicator. Most  classes are competitive, but we encourage a friendly atmosphere in which the  emphasis is not on winning, but on celebrating a wealth of talent, dedication  and achievement.
       </p>
       <p>
        The Festival takes place throughout October,  followed by the Gala Concert in November and the Spring Concert the following  April. Performers also appear in other events around the county.
       </p>
       <h3>
        Lots of opportunities to participate, including:
       </h3>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Dance
        </strong>
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         Classical Ballet, Jazz, Contemporary
        </li>
        <li>
         Modern, Tap, Song &amp; Dance
        </li>
        <li>
         Acrobatic, Improvisation
        </li>
       </ul>
       <ul type="disc">
        <li>
         All       England Dance qualification year
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Music
        </strong>
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         Choral, Orchestral, Vocal
        </li>
        <li>
         Organ, Piano, Strings, Wind
        </li>
        <li>
         Concerto and Mixed Ensemble classes
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Speech and  Drama
        </strong>
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         Solo Verse, Prose Reading and Speaking, Solo  Shakespeare
        </li>
        <li>
         Bible Reading, Choral Verse, Duologues
        </li>
        <li>
         Solo Acting, Modern Drama, Group Drama
        </li>
       </ul>
