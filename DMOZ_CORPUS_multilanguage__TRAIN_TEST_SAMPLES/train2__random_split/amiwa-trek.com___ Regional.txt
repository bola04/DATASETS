          <em>
           Adayeen!!
          </em>
         </strong>
         Welcome to the website of Amiwa,  a trekking agency located in the Yunnan mountains in southern China. We  appreciate the few moments you will spend discovering this beautiful region and  our agency.
         <br/>
         We are a small organisation, both local and friendly.  Here, you won’t find endless catalogues of innumerable  countries spanning the globe: we talk about what we know and what we live  day-to-day.  Yunnan Province is extensive,  abounding in new vistas and new cultures for the adventurer. With a land area  greater than Germany and numerous indigenous ethnic groups (several dozen), you  are not apt to find yourself bored in this ‘land south of the clouds’…
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Come with us and find a change of pace! Amiwa is at your service to help you  discover Yunnan’s peoples and their cultures. We will lead you through  magnificent mountains where you can take the time to contemplate grand  landscapes, to relax and enjoy a good meal...without forgetting that it is the trek  and the mountain that make the journey.
         <br/>
         You enjoy certain surroundings or you’ve heard about one mountain or another:  map in hand, Amiwa’s expertise at your side, you will travel across monoliths  off the beaten track.  The Amiwa team is  constantly exploring and re-discovering the trails that crisscross Yunnan Province.   We delight in sharing these paths with  other hikers of any level of experience.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Amiwa offers  custom-designed treks for individuals or small groups who want a Chinese  vacation that has been planned down to the last detail and designed to fit your  preferences and abilities. Amiwa will handle all the organisation; all you need  to do is enjoy yourself while climbing mountains and discovering new cultures.
         <a href="htm/custom_designed_treks.htm">
          Learn more about our custom-designed treks
         </a>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Amiwa also welcomes those  with a passion for the hazards and joys of discovery and exploration to  accompany our team on scouting expeditions. If you are not daunted by chaotic  bus schedules, camping in all kinds of weather, and the unpredictability of a  new route or forgotten trail, come join us in our exploratory wanderings.
         <a href="htm/scouting_around.htm">
          Learn more about our scouting treks
         </a>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Depending upon the amount  of time you plan to spend in China, we can also help you organise the rest of  your trip, beyond Yunnan, both in the countryside and on the coast and we can  share with you our knowledge of cities like Shanghai and Beijing.
         <br/>
         Feel free to click through to learn more about
         <a href="htm/history.htm">
          our history
         </a>
         ,  the
         <a href="htm/index_nature.htm">
          wildlife
         </a>
         and
         <a href="htm/index_people.htm">
          people
         </a>
         who inhabit Yunnan  Province, or to flip through the
         <a href="htm/index_photos.php">
          photo galleries
         </a>
         of our most recent trips...
