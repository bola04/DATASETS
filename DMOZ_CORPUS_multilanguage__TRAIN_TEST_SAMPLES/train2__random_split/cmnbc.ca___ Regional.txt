          <h1>
           <span style="font-size:28px;">
            Welcome to the Community Mapping Network!
           </span>
          </h1>
          <p>
           <span style="font-size: 16px;">
            The Community Mapping Network (CMN) helps communities map sensitive habitats and species distributions
           </span>
           <span style="font-size: 16px;">
            in British Columbia and Canada.  Since 2000, the CMN has created community based atlases with
           </span>
           <span style="font-size: 16px;">
            customized data entry and reporting tools. The atlases integrate many different data sources including local and remote data sets, geo-referenced videos and sensitive habitat mapping projects. The Atlas Gallery has over sixty user friendly atlases that use Mapguide Open Source. By providing accurate and up to date information, the CMN and its many partners help plan sustainable communities.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 19px; color: rgb(48, 48, 48); font-family: Arial, Helvetica, 'Nimbus Sans L', sans-serif;">
            <span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;">
             <strong>
              <span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0);">
               Please consider making a donation to help CMN
              </span>
              <span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0);">
               fund new projects and continue providing natural resource information services. The CMN is
              </span>
              <span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0);">
              </span>
              <span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0);">
               dependant on user donations and partners' funding new projects.
              </span>
             </strong>
            </span>
            This
            <strong>
             <span style="font-size:20px;">
              <span style="font-size:22px;">
               <u>
                <a href="https://www.canadahelps.org/dn/1328">
                 donate link
                </a>
               </u>
              </span>
             </span>
            </strong>
            connects you to the BC Conservation Foundation (BCCF) donation page on Canada Helps.
            <strong>
             Select CMN
            </strong>
            from the list of funds BCCF supports.
           </span>
           <span style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 19px; color: rgb(48, 48, 48); font-family: Arial, Helvetica, 'Nimbus Sans L', sans-serif;">
            To focus your donation for a paticular atlas on CMN please leave a message in the area below the list of funds that BCCF supports.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p class="rtecenter">
           <span style="font-size:16px;">
            <a href="/about-cmn" style="font-size: 18px;">
             Continue reading about the Community Mapping Network
            </a>

           </span>
