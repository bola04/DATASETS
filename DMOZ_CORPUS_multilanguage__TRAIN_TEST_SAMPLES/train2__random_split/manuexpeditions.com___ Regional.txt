               <h2>
                <span class="field-content">
                 The Complete Manu Biosphere Reserve Experience
                </span>
               </h2>
               <div class="field-content">
                <a href="/complete-manu-biosphere-reserve-peru.html">
                 <img alt="" height="317" src="https://www.manuexpeditions.com/sites/default/files/styles/661/public/Andes-to-Amazon-Manu-Wildlife-Centre_0.jpg?itok=oQdBv5pV" typeof="foaf:Image" width="649"/>
                </a>
               </div>
               <div class="duration">
                <span>
                 Duration:
                </span>
                <div class="field-content">
                 7 Days (6 nights tour)
                </div>
               </div>
               <div class="read_more">
                <span class="field-content">
                 <a href="/complete-manu-biosphere-reserve-peru.html">
                  read more
                 </a>
                </span>
               </div>
               <div class="tour-content">
                <div class="field-content">
                 This Trip is the Complete Manu Experience because we visit all points of interest in the Manu National Park. From Cocha’s Salvador and Otorongo, the pristine Manu River, the Blanquillo Macaw Lick, Mammal and Tapir Licks and walk-up Canopy Towers. A fantastic combination of comfortable lodges and wildlife attractions. Some of the time will be spent canoeing the lake on a floating catamarans observing ox-bow lake animal life from the water. We may encounter an Agami Heron or a Sungrebe and Shock-headed &amp; Large-headed Capuchin Monkeys are usually feeding on fruits nearby. We look for a family of Giant Otters that live here. These, the world’s largest freshwater carnivores, remain common only in Manu.
                </div>
               </div>
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="views-row views-row-2 views-row-even">
              <div class="tour_inner">
               <h2>
                <span class="field-content">
                 Manu Wildlife Center - Andes to the Amazon
                </span>
               </h2>
               <div class="field-content">
                <a href="/manu-wildlife-center-andes-to-amazon-peru.html">
                 <img alt="" height="317" src="https://www.manuexpeditions.com/sites/default/files/styles/661/public/homepage-2nd-main-tour1.jpg?itok=s7Vdm4KD" typeof="foaf:Image" width="649"/>
                </a>
               </div>
               <div class="duration">
                <span>
                 Duration:
                </span>
                <div class="field-content">
                 5 Days  (4 nights lodge)
                </div>
               </div>
               <div class="read_more">
                <span class="field-content">
                 <a href="/manu-wildlife-center-andes-to-amazon-peru.html">
                  read more
                 </a>
                </span>
               </div>
               <div class="tour-content">
                <div class="field-content">
                 A short intense  trip to Manu staying 3 nights at the comfortable Manu Wildlife Centre This trip has several major wildlife attractions – the Blanquillo Macaw and Parrot lick, a Tapir lick where in the comfort of mattresses and mosquito nets you may watch the largest of all South American Land Mammals visit, plus Oxbow Lakes with floating Catamarans for observing Giant Otters and other lake-side wildlife and two walk-up canopy towers to get you into the rainforest canopy.
                </div>
               </div>
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="views-row views-row-3 views-row-odd">
              <div class="tour_inner">
               <h2>
                <span class="field-content">
                 The Inca Trail Ride
                </span>
               </h2>
               <div class="field-content">
                <a href="/inca-trail-ride-peru.html">
                 <img alt="" height="317" src="https://www.manuexpeditions.com/sites/default/files/styles/661/public/Inca-Trail-Ride.jpg?itok=pid3q6Wt" typeof="foaf:Image" width="649"/>
                </a>
               </div>
               <div class="duration">
                <span>
                 Duration:
                </span>
                <div class="field-content">
                 4 Days
                </div>
               </div>
               <div class="read_more">
                <span class="field-content">
                 <a href="/inca-trail-ride-peru.html">
                  read more
                 </a>
                </span>
               </div>
               <div class="tour-content">
                <div class="field-content">
                 <p>
                  Exploration of The Sacred Valley of The Incas And Inca Trails on Horseback. A comfortable bus-supported multi-day horseback trip following ancient Inca and colonial pathways through Peru's Sacred Valley and Inca Heartland with an optional narrated visit to Machu Picchu… We stay each night in a t comfortable inn or hotel. The focus is learning as much as possible about Andean culture, traditions, history of the Incas and their ancestors while enjoying an unusual, memorable mountain excursion on quality, dependable horses and tack.
                 </p>
                </div>
               </div>
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="views-row views-row-4 views-row-even views-row-last">
              <div class="tour_inner">
               <h2>
                <span class="field-content">
                 Choquequirao - The Lost City Trek
                </span>
               </h2>
               <div class="field-content">
                <a href="/choquequirao-the-lost-city-trek-peru.html">
                 <img alt="" height="317" src="https://www.manuexpeditions.com/sites/default/files/styles/661/public/condors.jpg?itok=Fl6WOOLB" typeof="foaf:Image" width="649"/>
                </a>
               </div>
               <div class="duration">
                <span>
                 Duration:
                </span>
                <div class="field-content">
                 11 Days
                </div>
               </div>
               <div class="read_more">
                <span class="field-content">
                 <a href="/choquequirao-the-lost-city-trek-peru.html">
                  read more
                 </a>
                </span>
               </div>
               <div class="tour-content">
                <div class="field-content">
                 <p>
                  An Andean Adventure by mule and foot to Inca lost cities in the remote Vilcabamba cloud forests of the Peruvian Andes. Beyond Machu Picchu lies a remote, wild region of cold high uplands and hot tropical valleys known as the Vilcabamba. The is a steep mountainous region of and last refuge for the Inca resisting the Spanish colonization of Peru. Our ultimate goal is to visit the large Inca ceremonial city of Choquequirao, a distant replication of its more famous sister site Machu Picchu
                 </p>
