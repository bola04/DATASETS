       <h2>
        Welcome to the New Mexico
       </h2>
       <h3>
        Department of Information Technology (DoIT)
       </h3>
       <p>
        DoIT was established as a single, unified cabinet level department that consolidates enterprise information technology services previously duplicated within state agencies.  DoIT also provides IT services and functionality that improve and streamline state agencies' IT systems.
       </p>
       <p>
        The mission of the Department of Information Technology is to provide cost-effective and efficient enterprise products, services and solutions within a secure and reliable environment for our customers through leadership, strategic planning, standards and policy, architecture and oversight.
       </p>
       <p>
        The vision for DoIT is to improve efficiency and effectiveness in the delivery of state services, and to be the trusted leader in delivering enterprise information technology services and solutions that enable state government to better service New Mexico's residents.
       </p>
