        <h2>
         Your Rockland County, NY Real Estate Source!
        </h2>
        <div class="editor">
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif;">
           <span style="font-size: 10pt;">
            Sheryl Vogel &amp; Team Rockland offer unparalleled service to ALL clients in the Rockland County New York real estate market. Your complete satisfaction with our service and representation is our number one priority.
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif;">
           <span style="font-size: 10pt;">
            If you are interested in buying or selling a home in the Rockland County area, Sheryl Vogel and her Team of Experts will provide you with seamless customer service throughout the home buying and selling process.  Sheryl Vogel and her Team are highly trained and have expert knowledge of
            <strong>
             Rockland County
            </strong>
            . As one of the top Realtors in the county, and over 25 years experience - Sheryl gets RESULTS!
            <strong>
             Now more than ever, experience counts.
            </strong>
            Working together we will find your Rockland County dream home. Applying our knowledge, use ot todays top technology and expert skills in negotiation, we will sell your home fast and for top dollar.
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif;">
           <span style="font-size: 10pt;">
            <span style="font-family: Verdana;">
             <a href="http://www.co.rockland.ny.us/Towns/Towns&amp;Villages.htm" target="_blank">
              Rockland's communities
             </a>
             have a small-town ambience with tree-lined streets, boutiques and antique shops, well-laid out residential developments of single-family homes and turn-of-the-century Victorian homes along the river. The five towns of
             <a href="http://www.orangetown.com/index.jsp" target="_blank">
              Orangetown
             </a>
             ,
             <a href="http://www.town.clarkstown.ny.us/" target="_blank">
              Clarkstown,
             </a>
             <a href="http://www.ramapo.org/" target="_blank">
              Ramapo
             </a>
             ,
             <a href="http://www.townofhaverstraw.us/" target="_blank">
              Haverstraw
             </a>
             and
             <a href="http://www.stonypointny.org/">
              Stony Point
             </a>
             are broken up into numerous hamlets and villages each with its own distinctive flair - from
             <a href="http://www.rocklandhomesearch.com/" target="_blank" title="Search New City Homes">
              New City
             </a>
             , the county seat; to the river towns of Nyack, Piermont, Grandview and Haverstraw; and a bit more rural areas of Suffern, Pomona and Stony Point. Once you visit, you will want to make Rockland home.
            </span>
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>

No obligation.
