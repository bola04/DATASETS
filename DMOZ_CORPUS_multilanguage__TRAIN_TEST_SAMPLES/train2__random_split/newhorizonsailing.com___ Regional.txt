             <p class="firstpara">
              A skippered, hands-on, sailing holiday or RYA Training Course
                 on the West Coast of Scotland. Sailing amongst the islands of 
                 the Hebrides, Orkney, Shetland, Ireland and the Isle of Man in comfort aboard a quality Oyster yacht. Charter a berth or the whole boat.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              Or you can take a RYA Practical Training Course at this accredited sailing school. You can combine a sailing holiday with RYA approved training from Start Yachting, to Competent Crew, Day Skipper, Coastal Skipper and Yachtmaster preparation.
             </p>
             <p>
              <img height="1" src="images/white.gif" width="640"/>
             </p>

            <td width="640">
             <div align="justify">
              <p>
               <br/>
               <a href="images/big/home1b.jpg" target="_blank">
                <img align="right" alt="Towards Scarba (click on the image for a larger version - please be patient while the image loads)" border="0" height="161" hspace="10" src="images/home1.jpg" vspace="5" width="250"/>
               </a>
               The Scottish West Coast and the islands of the Hebrides are 
                   as spectacular as the wildlife that inhabits them. There is 
                   no better way to visit than from the sea.
              </p>
              <p>
               This
               <a href="https://youtu.be/1buQB_hbfLc" target="_blank">
                short video
               </a>
               shows us sailing in the area of Skye, Canna, Rum, Muck and Eigg.
              </p>
              <p>
               Join us for exhilarating Scottish sailing as we explore aboard 
                   our pedigree Oyster
               <a href="yacht.htm">
                yacht
               </a>
               which combines comfort with proven sailing ability.
              </p>
              <p>
               Discover deep sea lochs, dramatic mountains, puffins, dolphins, 
                   whales or eagles and, at the end of the day, good food and like-minded 
                   company in one of the many secluded anchorages.
              </p>
              <p>
               This
               <a href="https://youtu.be/vLOqkJcjcNY" target="_blank">
                short video
               </a>
               was shot on passage from Orkney to Tobermory, Isle of Mull.
              </p>
             </div>
             <p align="justify">
              <a href="images/big/home2b.jpg" target="_blank">
               <img align="left" alt="Anchored at Treshnish (click on the image for a larger version - please be patient while the image loads)" border="0" height="189" hspace="10" src="images/home2.jpg" vspace="5" width="250"/>
              </a>
              Richard 
                 is your
              <a href="skip.htm">
               skipper
              </a>
              and host, He is equally 
                 happy to handle the boat or leave you to sail while he keeps the 
                 mugs of tea and coffee topped up, prepares the meals or uncorks 
                 the wine.
             </p>
             <p align="justify">
              He is a qualified RYA Yachtmaster Instructor 
                 and those who want to learn more will find him a calm and patient 
                 coach.
             </p>
             <p>
              Experienced sailors will enjoy improving their navigation and 
                 boat handling skills in an area with interesting tides and snug 
                 anchorages.
              <br/>
              Beginners will soon find themselves spending time at the wheel, 
                 trimming the sails or launching the dinghy.
             </p>
             <p>
              Wind, Tide, Weather and the mood of the group dictate that no 
                 two cruises will be the same.
             </p>
             <p>
              <a href="images/big/home3b.jpg" target="_blank">
               <img align="right" alt="The Summer Isles (click on the image for a larger version - please be patient while the image loads)" border="0" height="188" hspace="10" src="images/home3.jpg" vspace="5" width="250"/>
              </a>
              Favourite 
                 memories include;
              <br/>
              reefed-down sailing in magnificent winds, barrelling along with 
                 the tide in narrow channels, dry-mouthed moments creeping into 
                 tight anchorages or rather wetter ones landing on rocky shores 
                 in the tender.
             </p>
             <p>
              And;
              <br/>
              a stroll on an empty beach before dinner, local hospitality, laughing at the antics of puffins around their burrows, the excitement of sighting the first dolphins or dining on fish caught that afternoon.
             </p>
             <p>
              Whatever your age or experience, whether looking for a relaxing 
                 sailing holiday in Scotland cruising between islands, an
              <a href="learn.htm">
               approved 
                 RYA sailing course
              </a>
              , a concentrated week of mile building in 
                 the Outer Hebrides, a short break, or more adventurous sailing 
                 to
              <a href="sail4.htm#stk">
               St. Kilda
              </a>
              ,
              <a href="sail4.htm#ork">
               Orkney
              </a>
              , 
                 Fair Isle,
              <a href="sail4.htm#shet">
               Shetland
              </a>
              or South West Ireland, you will find something 
                 to suit you.
             </p>
             <p>
              The
              <a href="prog.htm">
               2019 Programme
              </a>
              is now available.
             </p>
             <p>
              <img align="left" border="0" height="333" hspace="10" src="images/lochscavaigevening.jpg" vspace="5" width="250"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              In keeping with the New Horizon sailing concept of choice  of top yacht, great location, guaranteed warm welcome and quality of experience, we offer:
             </p>
             <p>
              Sailing Holidays, Short Breaks, Milebuilding and RYA Training in Scotland including the Clyde, West Coast, Mull, Skye, the Outer Hebrides, St Kilda, Orkney, Fair Isle &amp; Shetland and Ireland.
             </p>
             <p>
              We look forward to welcoming you aboard.
             </p>
