           <header class="entry-header">
            <h2 class="entry-title">
             Home
            </h2>
           </header>
           <div class="entry-content clearfix">
            <table border="0" cellpadding="10" cellspacing="0" width="100%">
             <tbody>
              <tr>
               <td bgcolor="#ffffff">
                <p align="center" class="style12 style27">
                 <strong>
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   THE TRUTH ABOUT THE YORKSHIRE RIPPER
                  </span>
                 </strong>
                </p>
                <p>
                </p>
               </td>
              </tr>
              <tr>
               <td bgcolor="#ffffff">
                <figure class="wp-caption alignleft" id="attachment_1228" style="width: 150px">
                 <img alt="Peter Sutcliffe The Yorkshire Ripper Story" class="wp-image-1228 size-thumbnail" height="150" sizes="(max-width: 150px) 100vw, 150px" src="http://yorkshireripper.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Peter_Sutcliffe_The_Yorkshire_Ripper_Story-150x150.jpg" srcset="http://yorkshireripper.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Peter_Sutcliffe_The_Yorkshire_Ripper_Story-150x150.jpg 150w, http://yorkshireripper.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/Peter_Sutcliffe_The_Yorkshire_Ripper_Story-200x200.jpg 200w" width="150"/>
                 <figcaption class="wp-caption-text">
                  Peter Sutcliffe
                 </figcaption>
                </figure>
                <div>
                 <p align="justify" class="style28">
                  <span style="font-size: small; color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    Peter Sutcliffe
                   </strong>
                   was
                   <strong>
                    the
                    <span class="style29">
                     Copy-Cat Yorkshire Ripper
                    </span>
                   </strong>
                   , responsible for only
                   <span class="style29">
                    four
                   </span>
                   of the thirteen murders which he ‘confessed’ to. He had been eliminated twelve times by the Yorkshire police because he was blood group O… the Ripper was known to be blood group B.
                  </span>
                 </p>
                 <p align="justify" class="style4">
                  <span style="color: #000000;">
                   <strong>
                    <span style="font-size: small;">
                     The police knew that two men were involved in the
                     <a href="http://yorkshireripper.co.uk/the-victims/" title="The Yorkshire Ripper Murder Victims">
                      bizarre series of murders
                     </a>
                     , but tactical blunders made it expedient to make a deal with the Copy-Cat killer, who was offered ten years in a luxury mental home for his ‘confessions’ to everything, by a police force desperate to close the file.
                    </span>
                   </strong>
                  </span>
                 </p>
                </div>
                <p align="justify" class="style4">
                 <span style="color: #000000;">
                  <strong>
                   <span style="font-size: small;">
                    After all these years, the Real Ripper, far from being a hoaxer who wrote mockingly to the police in order to implicate the Copy-Cat, is still free in the U.K.
                   </span>
                  </strong>
                 </span>
                </p>
                <p align="center" class="style4">
                 <span style="color: #000000;">
                  <strong>
                   <span class="style4">
                    <object classid="clsid:CFCDAA03-8BE4-11CF-B84B-0020AFBBCCFA" data-mce-fragment="1" height="36" width="350">
                     <embed autostart="0" backgroundcolor="#000000" center="0" controls="controls" height="36" loop="loop" maintainaspect="0" nolabels="0" numloop="0" prefetch="0" shuffle="0" src="http://yorkshireripper.co.uk/audio/snippet.rm" title="undefined" type="audio/x-pn-RealAudio-plugin" width="350">
                     </embed>
                    </object>
                   </span>
                  </strong>
                 </span>
                </p>
                <p align="center" class="style10">
                 <span style="color: #000000;">
                  <strong>
                   <span style="font-size: small;">
                    Sample of RTE Interview with Noel O’Gara and John Sutcliffe
                   </span>
                  </strong>
                 </span>
                </p>
                <p align="justify" class="style4">
                 <span style="color: #000000;">
                  <strong>
                   <span style="font-size: small;">
                    <a href="http://yorkshireripper.co.uk/the-author/">
                     <span class="style29">
                      Noel O’Gara
                     </span>
                    </a>
                    <a href="http://yorkshireripper.co.uk/the-author/">
                     ,
                    </a>
                    the author of this website, had an insight into the Ripper investigation from none other than the Yorkshire Ripper himself.
                   </span>
                  </strong>
                 </span>
                </p>
                <p align="justify" class="style4">
                 <span style="color: #000000;">
                  <strong>
                   <span style="font-size: small;">
                    This insight has enabled him to piece together
                    <a href="http://yorkshireripper.co.uk/police-officers/">
                     the police handling
                    </a>
                    of the investigation and their framing of the mentally-disturbed Peter Sutcliffe.
                   </span>
                  </strong>
                 </span>
                </p>
                <p align="justify" class="style4">
                 <span style="color: #000000;">
                  <strong>
                   <span style="font-size: small;">
                    Police reluctance to look at their mistakes left the author no avenue other than to record the story of
                    <em>
                     the Yorkshire Ripper
                    </em>
                    and name the real killer, who is
                    <span class="style29">
                     still at large
                    </span>
                    .
                   </span>
                  </strong>
                 </span>
                </p>
                <p align="center">
                 <span style="color: #000000;">
                  <object classid="clsid:D27CDB6E-AE6D-11cf-96B8-444553540000" codebase="http://active.macromedia.com/flash4/cabs/swflash.cab#version=4,0,0,0" data-mce-fragment="1" height="100" width="650">
                   <embed height="100" loop="loop" play="true" pluginspage="http://www.macromedia.com/shockwave/download/index.cgi?P1_Prod_Version=ShockwaveFlash" quality="high" src="http://yorkshireripper.co.uk/imadjz/anims/ban650-large.swf" width="650">
                   </embed>
                  </object>
                 </span>
                </p>
               </td>
