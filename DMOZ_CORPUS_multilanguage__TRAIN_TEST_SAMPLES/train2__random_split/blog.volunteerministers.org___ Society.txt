          <h2 class="title">
           <a href="/Amazon6">
            Scientology Volunteer Ministers Bring Help to Indigenous Peoples of South America’s Amazon Region
           </a>
          </h2>
          <span class="submitted">
           Submitted by vmupdate on Sun, 2015-06-07 16:27
          </span>
          <div class="taxonomy">
           <ul class="links inline">
            <li class="taxonomy_term_126 first">
             <a href="/category/categories/amazon-region" rel="tag" title="">
              Amazon Region
             </a>
            </li>
            <li class="taxonomy_term_127">
             <a href="/category/categories/brazil" rel="tag" title="">
              Brazil
             </a>
            </li>
            <li class="taxonomy_term_103 last">
             <a href="/category/categories/colombia" rel="tag" title="">

             </a>
            </li>
           </ul>
          </div>
          <div class="content">
           <div class="article-body-image-default">
            <a href="/Amazon6" title="The Scientology Volunteers Ministers Amazon Goodwill Tour riverboat carried tour members down the river from Leticia, Colombia, this month across the border of Brazil to the town of Iranduba, the new base of operations of the tour.">
             <img src="http://blog.volunteerministers.org/sites/default/files/imagecache/blog_body_default/the-VM-River-boat_0.jpg"/>
            </a>
            <div class="caption">
             The Scientology Volunteers Ministers Amazon Goodwill Tour riverboat carried tour members down the river from Leticia, Colombia, this month across the border of Brazil to the town of Iranduba, the new base of operations of the tour.
            </div>
           </div>
           <p class="p1">
            The Scientology Volunteer Ministers Goodwill Tour bid farewell to their friends in Leticia, Colombia, and crossed over into Brazil in May to continue bringing help to the indigenous people who live along the Amazon.
