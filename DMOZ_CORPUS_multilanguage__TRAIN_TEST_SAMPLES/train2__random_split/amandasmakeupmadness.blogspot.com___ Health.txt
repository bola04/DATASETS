                     <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                      <a href="http://amandasmakeupmadness.blogspot.com/2014/05/wen-by-chaz-dean-spring-orange-blossom.html">
                       WEN by Chaz Dean Spring Orange Blossom Cleansing Conditioner
                      </a>
                     </h3>
                     <div class="post-header">
                      <div class="post-header-line-1">
                      </div>
                     </div>
                     <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-7580300430613826940" itemprop="description articleBody">
                      WEN Spring Orange Blossom Cleansing Conditioner is a limited edition seasonal scent inspired by Chaz Dean's childhood.  Of walking past orange groves in Simi Valley, California and visiting his grandparents in Florida.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-pBaxvFu0IoI/U2gpDpf-NEI/AAAAAAAABwE/jX3XZk5Pwkc/s1600/DSC08760+(2).JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="320" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-pBaxvFu0IoI/U2gpDpf-NEI/AAAAAAAABwE/jX3XZk5Pwkc/s1600/DSC08760+(2).JPG" width="166"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      Spring Orange Blossom Cleansing Conditioner is sodium lauryl sulfate-free. Eliminating the use of stripping agents, detergents, and harsh chemicals, this Cleansing Conditioner will keep your hair bouncy, shiny, hydrated, and healthy-looking.  This hydrating Cleansing Conditioner is universal for all hair types, textures, and lengths.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      I will have a video and blog soon on how I apply my WEN products in detail.  I apply the WEN Cleansing Conditioner to four sections of my hair, the crown, nape, the ends of my hair on the left, and the ends of my hair on the right.  Since my hair is past my shoulders, I use a minimum of 24 pumps.
