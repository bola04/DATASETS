         Our Annual Report
        </font>
       </p>
       <p align="justify">
        A copy of our latest Form CHAR 500 annual report (which includes our Form 990EZ for the calendar year) may be obtained upon request from Cairn Rescue USA by emailing us at webmaster@CairnRescueUSA and putting "Annual Report" in the subject, or from the New York State Attorney General's Charities Bureau, Attn: FOIL Officer, 120 Broadway, New York, NY 10271. Copies can also be viewed and dowloaded by going to  the
        <a href="http://www.charitiesnys.com/RegistrySearch/search_charities.jsp" target="_blank" title="NY Charities Bureau">
         NY Charities Bureau search engine
        </a>
        and typing
        <strong>
         Cairn Rescue USA
        </strong>
        in the box titled Organization Name.  The NY Charities Bureau provides free access to online copies (in PDF format) of all CHAR 500 Annual Reports (which include the organization's Form 990, 990EZ or 990PF) filed since 2006 by NY registered organizations like CRUSA. CRUSA files a Form 990EZ with the IRS and includes a copy of it with its NY CHAR 500 filings.
