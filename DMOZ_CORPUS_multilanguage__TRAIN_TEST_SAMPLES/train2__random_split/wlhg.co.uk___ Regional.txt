          <font face="Verdana">
           <font size="+1">
            Welcome to the
           </font>
          </font>
         </p>
         <p align="center">
          <font face="Verdana">
           <font size="+3">
            Whitwell
           </font>
           <br/>
           <font size="+3">
            Local History
           </font>
           <br/>
           <font size="+3">
            Group
           </font>
          </font>
         </p>
         <p align="center">
          <font face="Verdana">
           <font size="+1">
            Homepage.
           </font>
          </font>
         </p>
         <p align="center">
          <b>
           <font color="#FF0000" face="Verdana" size="4">
            <a href="http://www.wlhg.co.uk">
             www.wlhg.co.uk
            </a>
           </font>
          </b>
         </p>
         <p align="center">
         </p>
         <hr/>
         <p align="center">
         </p>
         <p align="center">
          <font face="Verdana" size="4">
           Much interest has been shown in the more recent 
 'living history' of the village in a new Facebook group called
          </font>
         </p>
         <p align="center">
          <font color="#0000FF" face="Verdana" size="4">
           <a href="https://www.facebook.com/groups/532005340204800/">
            Whitwell Photos and 
 Memories
           </a>
          </font>
         </p>
         <p align="center">
          <font face="Verdana" size="4">
           Please come and take a look, some great pictures 
 and memories on there.
          </font>
         </p>
         <p align="center">
          <font face="Verdana" size="4">
           You will need to join the group to see the 
 information.
          </font>
         </p>
         <p align="center">
         </p>
         <hr/>
         <p>
          <font face="Verdana">
           Whitwell is a small village situated in the North East corner of Derbyshire,
 England.  It sits in a unique position where the great counties of
 Derbyshire, Nottinghamshire and South Yorkshire meet.
          </font>
         </p>
         <p>
          <font face="Verdana">
           Our group meets weekly at the Whitwell Community Centre at 7.00pm on
 Tuesday evenings.
           <br/>
          </font>
         </p>
         <hr/>
         <p>
          <font face="Verdana">
           My name is Peter Hopkins.  I am a member of the group with
 an interest in computers and the internet.  I originally offered
 to present some of the work completed on the Parish Survey, possibly in
 book form.  I was then asked to reproduce the book, 'Whitwell a Parish
 History'.  I thought this presented a great opportunity to publish
 the work on the Internet for all to see.
          </font>
         </p>
         <p>
          <font face="Verdana">
           The book in it's slightly expanded form is now available to buy, (the millennium
 copy!)
          </font>
         </p>
         <p>
          <font face="Verdana">
           Sadly, some of the people responsible for 'Whitwell, A Parish History', have 
 now become part of the history they cherished so much.
          </font>
         </p>
         <p>
          <font face="Verdana">
           The book cost £5 plus P&amp;P.   These books have now gone all over the world!
          </font>
         </p>
         <p>
          <font face="Verdana">
           Please see the
           <a href="publications_for_sale.htm" target="_blank">
            Publications for sale
           </a>
           page
          </font>
         </p>
         <p align="center">
          <font face="Verdana">
           <span lang="en-gb">

          </font>
         </p>
         <hr/>
         <p align="center">
          <font face="Verdana">
           <u>
            <b>
             Photograph collection
            </b>
           </u>
          </font>
         </p>
         <p align="center">
          <font face="Verdana">
           We are pleased to announce, that with the help of Lottery 
 funding, that our extensive collection is in the process of being digitised and 
 made available for anyone to view.
          </font>
         </p>
         <p align="center">
          The archive is available
          <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/127663472@N05/sets/">
           here
          </a>
         </p>
         <p align="center">
          We welcome personal photos or memories to add to our archives
         </p>
         <p align="center">
          <font face="Verdana">
           Previous photos are 
 accessible on the
           <a href="monthly_photos.htm">
            'Photo Page'
           </a>
           here
          </font>
         </p>
         <center>
          <hr/>
          <p>
           <font face="Verdana">
            Perhaps you are an old Whitwellian.  We would love to hear from
 you, and please feel free to add any comments/information on our site. 
 We are always grateful for copies of old photographs, or snippets of information
 to fill in a few of the gaps....
           </font>
          </p>
          <p>
           <font face="Verdana">
            After receiving many requests for genealogy research, I decided to publish
 the results so that the information is available to any interested parties.
 These are available on the enquiries page.
           </font>
          </p>
