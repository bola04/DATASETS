          <p>
           <img alt="arms_grandlodge" class="wp-image-20 size-medium alignleft" height="300" sizes="(max-width: 225px) 100vw, 225px" src="http://www.glnl.ca/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/iv008_19970064_arms_grandlo-225x300.jpg" srcset="http://www.glnl.ca/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/iv008_19970064_arms_grandlo-225x300.jpg 225w, http://www.glnl.ca/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/iv008_19970064_arms_grandlo.jpg 490w" width="225"/>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            <strong>
             Freemasonry is
            </strong>
           </span>
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            <strong>
             the world’s oldest, largest, and most philanthropic fraternal organization.  Freemasons live by an ethical and moral code that distinguishes them and creates an atmospher
            </strong>
           </span>
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            <strong>
             e where friendship and understanding can grow among men regardless of religion, race and culture.
            </strong>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            <strong>
             This is the public website of the Grand Lodge of Newfoundland &amp; Labrador.
            </strong>
            <strong>
             If you’re interested in our lodges, and maybe considering becoming a member, view this site and click and visit our
             <a href="http://www.glnl.ca/district-grand-lodges-of-newfoundland-and-labrador/" style="color: #000000;">
              Lodges Page.
             </a>
            </strong>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <h3>
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            EVEN BETTER…
           </span>
          </h3>
          <h2>
           <span style="color: #ff0000;">
            <a href="https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCIp-b7bUjj92IBb5wZSHl3g" style="color: #ff0000;">
             Not just a man. A Mason.
            </a>
           </span>
          </h2>
          <h4>
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            <strong>
             Even Better.
            </strong>
            How does a good man become even better? By working out? By working his way up the corporate ladder? Travelling the world? Or is it found in the power of integrity, service, and brotherly love?
           </span>
          </h4>
          <h4>
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            It’s why in the craft we can say, “Not just a man. A Mason.”
           </span>
          </h4>
          <p>
          </p>
