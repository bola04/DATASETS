       Welcome to Dinette.
      </h2>
      <br/>
      <p>
       We hope you've brought your appetite. Our homey Capitol Hill restaurant serves dinner and five nights a week—mostly rustic European fare thats great for sharing. Stop by early for happy hour and sample our specialty cocktails.
      </p>
      <p>
       We are also offering a
       <a href="Lunch.html" title="Sandwich Bar">
        summertime sandwich bar
       </a>
       . Have a seat on our covered patio and enjoy lunch al fresco.
      </p>
      <p>
