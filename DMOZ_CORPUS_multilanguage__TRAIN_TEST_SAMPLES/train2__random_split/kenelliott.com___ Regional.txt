                  <p style="word-spacing: 3px; margin: 3px">
                   <font color="#666699" size="2">
                    <b>
                     Southwest Art Magazine:
                     <br/>
                    </b>
                    Although his paintings loosely reference photographs and 
 							plein-air sketches, they refuse to exist within the 
 							confines of already-prescribed images. Rather, they 
 							stand independent of any specific place or time, the 
 							fulfilled pursuits of the artist’s visual and 
 							intellectual explorations. Elliott wields color 
 							boldly, creating sizzling, vibrant shapes that are 
 							at once kinetic and placid, grounded in unifying 
 							illumination, and focused compositions. He has a 
 							fascination with line structure as well, believing 
 							that “edges empower colors.”
                   </font>
                  </p>
