When we start working with a new client, we assess their needs before assigning them a project manager. We are not committed to bring a project manager to the task is one is not required to avoid charging the clients with unnecessary costs. Other firms will always assign clients project managers—regardless if one is needed or not—because they have to keep their in house staff busy.
