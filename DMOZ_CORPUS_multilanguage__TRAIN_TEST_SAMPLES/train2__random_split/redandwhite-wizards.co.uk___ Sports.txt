          Welcome to the Red and White Wizards a fan site dedicated to the Sheffield United FC. The site has been a place where I was able to post my recollection of each match I have attended between 1999-2013. I say ‘was‘ because it is not longer being updated as of 31 Dec 2013. I ran the website much like a blog with my own match reports, player ratings and personal man of the match choice. Unfortunately, due to a change of circumstances I don’t have the time to maintain it any longer. I will leave it here as an archive of the matches I’ve attended, feel free to browse around. There will still be periodic updates, and I of course still follow the Blades and rarely miss attending matches. Up The Blades!
