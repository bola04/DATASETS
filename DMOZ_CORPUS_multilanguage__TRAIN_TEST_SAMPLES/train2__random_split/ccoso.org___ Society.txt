           <p>
            The Coalition was founded in 1986 in response to California’s growing need for a formally structured resource for professionals working with persons involved in the commission of rape, child sexual abuse, incest, and other forms of sexual assault.
           </p>
           <p>
            Coalition members represent law enforcement, criminal justice, mental health, probation, parole, and other community services. Members are dedicated to addressing complex issues related to sex crimes and sexual deviance.
           </p>
           LIKE us on
           <a href="https://www.facebook.com/TheCCOSO" target="_blank">
            Facebook
           </a>
           <p>
            FOLLOW us on

             LinkedIn
            </a>
           </p>
