                  <h1 class="title">
                   Welcome to Boyes, James, McKay
                  </h1>
                  <div class="content-content" id="content-content">
                   <div class="node odd full-node node-type-page" id="node-4">
                    <div class="inner">
                     <div class="content clearfix">
                      <p>
                       Boyes James McKay is a three Valuer, Whakatane-based practice offering our clients a comprehensive property valuation and advisory service including Kawerau, Edgecumbe, Opotiki, Taneatua and the surrounding rural areas.
                      </p>
                      <p>
                       We are the first valuation firm in the Eastern Bay of Plenty to gain Quality Assurance Accreditation from the Property Institute of New Zealand.
                      </p>
                      <p>
                       Whether you're looking to buy, sell, finance, insure, convert, expand, renovate, rent, separate or subdivide, we can help.
                      </p>
                      <p>
                       We cover the breadth of the Eastern Bay of Plenty, from Otamarakau in the west to Hicks Bay in the east, servicing the three main townships of Whakatane, Kawerau and Opotiki alongside smaller settlements at Coastlands, Ohope, Edgecumbe, Matata, Taneatua and Te Teko.
                      </p>
                      <p>
