       <h2>
        Surrounded by the lush, clean, green, fertile plains of the Manawatu, the City of Palmerston North in New Zealand is home to the business of MorganLaurenson Ltd, specialists in prepared and pre-packed fresh vegetables.
       </h2>
       <p>
        This long-established, privately-owned company is operated by an experienced and forward-thinking group of Directors, each one passionate about providing excellence.
       </p>
       <p>
        Specialists in their field, they source, grow, process, pre-pack and distribute fresh
        <br/>
        New Zealand grown vegetables to the food supply and catering industry throughout New Zealand and the world.
       </p>
       <p>
        With equal fervour and a long history of seed potato production they research, grow and supply traditional and new varieties of seed potatoes.
       </p>
       <p>
        Renowned for their ability to provide innovative solutions, nimbly and efficiently, MorganLaurenson are professional and personable in their dealings with clients throughout Europe, Asia and New Zealand.
