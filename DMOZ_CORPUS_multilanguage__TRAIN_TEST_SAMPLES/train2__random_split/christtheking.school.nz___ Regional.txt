         <h1>
          Welcome To Christ The King School
         </h1>
         <p class="postmetadata">
         </p>
         <p>
          C
          <span style="font-family: Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: small;">
           hrist the King School offers a Catholic primary education for girls and boys from Year 1 to Year 8. Our aim is to treat every child as an individual and encourage them spiritually, academically, socially, physically and emotionally to develop according to their needs.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          At Christ the King we regard education as a life time journey. Within the caring and nurturing atmosphere which characterises our school, values are taught and lived, high standards of personal integrity are encouraged, and a strong cultural, social awareness and concern for others is expected.
         </p>
         <p>
          We welcome your interest and warmly invite you to call or visit us for further information.
         </p>
         <p>
          Christ the King welcomes enrolments from International Students. For further information please visit our International Student page.
         </p>
         <p>
          Mike Bonisch
          <br/>
          <span style="font-family: Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: small;">
           Principal
          </span>
         </p>
