              <h3 class="gdl-header-title">
               Guided Whitewater Rafting and Kayaking Trips
              </h3>
             </div>
             <p>
              Wet Planet Whitewater is the Center for River Rafting and Kayaking in the Columbia River Gorge – Washington and Oregon. Wet Planet is a group of motivated, energetic, whitewater professionals who have come together to share a passion for whitewater rafting and kayaking. We offer guided
              <a href="https://wetplanetwhitewater.com/rafting/day-trips/">
               river rafting trips
              </a>
              on the Northwest’s classic “wild and scenic” rivers,
              <a href="https://wetplanetwhitewater.com/kayaking/essential-kayak-classes/">
               whitewater kayak lessons and instruction
              </a>
              , and
              <a href="https://wetplanetwhitewater.com/package/river-rescue-rrc-pro/">
               river rescue training
              </a>
              for whitewater professionals.
             </p>
             <div class="sh-link post-link sh-hide" id="post-link-3896">
              <a aria-expanded="false" href="#" onclick="showhide_toggle('post', 3896, 'READ MORE »', ''); return false;">
               <span id="post-toggle-3896">
                READ MORE »
               </span>
              </a>
             </div>
             <div class="sh-content post-content sh-hide" id="post-content-3896" style="display: none;">
              Whether you are looking for white water rafting or kayaking in Oregon or Washington, with Wet Planet, you can experience the true excitement and passion of white water adventures.
              <p>
               <strong>
                Discover the Adventure of Whitewater Rafting &amp; Kayaking in Oregon &amp; Washington
               </strong>
              </p>
              <p>
               Wet Planet takes advantage of the crystal clear water and stunning scenery of the Pacific Northwest to offer whitewater rafting trips for all skill levels of rafters in the Columbia River Gorge in Oregon and Washington. From the remote wilderness stretches of the class III
               <a href="https://wetplanetwhitewater.com/package/klickitat-river/">
                Klickitat River
               </a>
               and
               <a href="https://wetplanetwhitewater.com/package/tieton-river/">
                Tieton River
               </a>
               , to the classic glacier waters and basalt canyons of the
               <a href="https://wetplanetwhitewater.com/package/white-salmon-river/">
                White Salmon River
               </a>
               , to the challenges of class V whitewater rapids on the
               <a href="https://wetplanetwhitewater.com/package/wind-river/">
                Wind River
               </a>
               and
               <a href="https://wetplanetwhitewater.com/package/the-farmlands/">
                Farmlands
               </a>
               , we have designed a variety of day-trips that are certain to meet the expectations of any whitewater rafter. If you are looking for the phenomenal wilderness experience of a
               <a href="https://wetplanetwhitewater.com/rafting/multi-day-trips/">
                multi-day rafting trip
               </a>
               , you can immerse yourself in Oregon and Idaho’s incredible canyon scenery on our
               <a href="https://wetplanetwhitewater.com/package/main-salmon-river-idaho-rafting-trip/">
                Main Salmon River 6-Day Idaho Rafting Trip
               </a>
               and
               <a href="https://wetplanetwhitewater.com/package/owyhee-river/">
                Owyhee River 5-Day Rafting Trip
               </a>
               .  We also have great multi-day adventures during the spring with our
               <a href="https://wetplanetwhitewater.com/package/multi-day-rafting-combos/">
                multi-river package trips
               </a>
               .
              </p>
              <p>
               For those looking to develop the skills necessary to run rivers on their own, or experienced paddlers looking to improve their technique, the Wet Planet Kayak School offers kayak instruction for beginning to advanced paddlers. Taught by our ACA and BCU certified kayak instructors, our
               <a href="https://wetplanetwhitewater.com/package/beginner-kayak-instruction/">
                two-day and three-day beginner kayak lessons
               </a>
               , intermediate and advanced clinics, and certified
               <a href="https://wetplanetwhitewater.com/package/river-rescue-rescue-3-srt-and-wrt-cert/">
                Rescue3 River Rescue Courses
               </a>
               are perfect for developing skills or helping you reach the next level of paddling.
              </p>
              <p>
               The Wet Planet Rafting and Kayaking Center is located in the Columbia River Gorge National Scenic Area, on the Washington state side, along the banks of the White Salmon River. We are just 15 minutes from Hood River, Oregon, and only 1 hour and 15 minutes from Portland, OR (
               <a href="https://wetplanetwhitewater.com/info/directions/">
                directions
               </a>
               ).
              </p>
