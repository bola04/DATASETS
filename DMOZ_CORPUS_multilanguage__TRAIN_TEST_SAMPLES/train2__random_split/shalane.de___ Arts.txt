            </a>
           </h3>
           <div class="submitted">

           </div>
           <div class="content">
            <p>
             While updating some older modules, I discovered that hundreds of spam comments have been made since I revamped this page. Fortunately, all of them went into the approval queue, so none of it was visible.
            </p>
            <p>
             But, I also found one real comment (sorry to the poster that this comment never got visible until now), so I decided to install a CAPTCHA module (Mollom) to prevent spam from passing thru but allowing real comments to be visible. I also set up an email rule so I get notified of every new submitted comment that made it thru the CAPTCHA check.

           <h3 class="title">
            <a href="/node/6" title="Page currently being revamped!">
             Page currently being revamped!
            </a>
           </h3>
           <div class="submitted">
            Submitted by Joerg on 16. December 2009 - 20:27
           </div>
           <div class="content">
            <p>
             Finally, finally, finally I initially installed
             <a class="elf-external elf-icon" href="http://drupal.org/">
              Drupal
             </a>
             to revamp the
             <strong>
              Shalane McCall Appreciation Page
             </strong>
             .
            </p>
            <p>
             Originally I wanted to leave the page as it existed now for over a decade as a monument of Web 0.x technology, but on the other hand, the page was really ugly and it just had to be improved. I will copy/merge all previous content into the new page within the next couple of days. You may still reach the old page at
             <a href="../../index2.php" title="www.shalane.de/index2.php">
              www.shalane.de/index2.php
             </a>
             but note that the links back to the index page do not work as they point directly to
             <a href="../../" title="www.shalane.de">
              www.shalane.de
             </a>
             . Use your browser's back button instead of the "Index" link.
            </p>
            <p>
             (I wanted to keep the revamped page unpublished until I finished copying/merging of most of the content, unfortunately, there is either a bug in Drupal or the CKEditor that I am using which creates strange side effects if the new page is not reachable under
             <a href="../../" title="www.shalane.de">
              www.shalane.de
             </a>
             . This is the reason why I had to publish the new version already as I need to make the necessary additions without having to hassle around with this bug.)
            </p>
            <p>
             Commenting and user registration (not quite sure whether I will really switch on user registration at all or just allow guest comments) has not been configured yet but will be enabled once the new page is ready.
            </p>
            <p>
             If you would like to send me a message meanwhile, please use the
             <a href="../../contact">
              contact
             </a>
             form.
            </p>
            <p>
