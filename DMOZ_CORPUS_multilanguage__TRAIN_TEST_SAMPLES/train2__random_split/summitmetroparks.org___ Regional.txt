      <h1>
       Summit Metro Parks
      </h1>
      <article class="flexbox-content" id="hp-intro-stats">
       <div>
        <img alt="Summit County" src="/App_Themes/images/universal/icon-summit-county.png" width="30"/>
        <strong>
         14,300
        </strong>
        <span>
         Acres Managed
        </span>
       </div>
       <div>
        <img alt="Tree" src="/App_Themes/images/universal/icon-tree.png" width="60"/>
        <strong>
         16
        </strong>
        <span>

       <div>
        <img alt="Map" src="/App_Themes/images/universal/icon-map.png" width="56"/>

      </article>
      <article class="flexbox-content" id="hp-intro-visits">
       <div class="hp-visits-icons">

        </strong>
        <span>
         Park Visits Annually
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="hp-visits-icons">
        <img alt="" role="presentation" src="/App_Themes/images/universal/icon-activity-group-right.png"/>
       </div>

      <p>
       Headquartered in Akron, Ohio, Summit Metro Parks manages 14,300 acres, including 16 parks, several conservation areas and more than 125 miles of trails, with 22.4 miles of the Ohio &amp; Erie Canal Towpath Trail. Annual attendance averages 5.2 million visits.
      </p>
