          <div about="/box/honored-excellence" class="node node-box view-mode-full node-653" typeof="sioc:Item foaf:Document">
           <div class="u-padding--top-bottom-50 u-bg-image u-bg-color--white">
            <div class="row">
             <div class="large-12 medium-12 small-12 columns">
              <h2 class="text-center node-box__title">
               Honored for Excellence
              </h2>
             </div>
             <div class="large-12 medium-12 small-12 columns">
              <h3 class="text-center u-padding--bottom-30 node-box__sub-title">
               Professor Sharon Donovan Elected to National Academy of Medicine
              </h3>
             </div>
            </div>
            <div class="row node-box__row node-box__row--one-image">
             <div class="large-4 medium-6 small-12 columns">
              <div class="node-box__box">
               <figure class="e-figure--box e-figure--box__figure--no-body" style="background-image: url('https://fshn.illinois.edu/sites/fshn.illinois.edu/files/box/donovan-sharon-honor-excellence--ws.jpg');">
                <div class="e-figure--box__a">
                </div>
                <div class="e-figure--box__figcaption--no-title">
                 <figcaption>
                  <span>
                  </span>
                 </figcaption>
                 <div class="e-figure--box__content">
                 </div>
                 <div class="e-figure--box--blur" style="background: url('https://fshn.illinois.edu/sites/fshn.illinois.edu/files/box/donovan-sharon-honor-excellence--ws.jpg');">
                 </div>
                </div>
               </figure>
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="large-8 medium-6 small-12 columns">
              <div class="node-box__content large-12 columns">
               <p>
                Sharon M. Donovan, professor of nutrition and Melissa M. Noel Endowed Chair in Nutrition and Health at the University of Illinois, has been elected to the National Academy of Medicine (NAM). Being elected to NAM is considered one of the highest honors in the fields of health and medicine, used to recognize those who have demonstrated outstanding professional achievement and commitment to service.
               </p>
               <p>
                Donovan’s work in pediatric nutrition looks into dietary approaches for improving the development of the intestines, brain, and gut microbiome. She is also working with researchers in other fields to discover ways of reducing or preventing childhood obesity, picky eating habits, and symptoms of autism.
               </p>
              </div>
             </div>

               <a class="e-button e-button--action expand" href="https://news.illinois.edu/blog/view/6367/566505">
                Learn more from the Illinois News Bureau
               </a>
              </div>
