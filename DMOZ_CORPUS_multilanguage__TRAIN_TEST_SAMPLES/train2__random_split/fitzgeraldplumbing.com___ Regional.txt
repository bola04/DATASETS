       <h3>
        About FitzGerald &amp; Sons
       </h3>
       <p>
        Welcome to Quality Through Craftsmanship! We are FitzGerald and Sons Plumbing Company. As a family owned company with three generations of FitzGeralds represented, we are committed to continuing our long-lasting legacy of innovation and integrity. Whether the job is one toilet in a customer’s home or an entire stadium’s plumbing concerns, our team is equally dedicated to serving our customers like family. FitzGerald and Sons is a truly versatile plumbing company handling all plumbing related needs in commercial buildings, such as stadiums, malls, hospitals, and schools, as well as residential concerns, like a backed-up sink in a kitchen. We also specialize in new additions or buildouts, in which we can lay the groundwork to ensure all plumbing needs will be appropriately planned for in a new space. Regardless of the size of the need, the team at FitzGerald and Sons is dedicated to quality work and satisfied customers.
