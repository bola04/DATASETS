            <span style="color: #ff0000;">
             05/19/2018 – Roughriders versus Corpus Christi Hooks
            </span>
           </h3>
           <h5 style="text-align: left; padding-left: 30px;">
            <span style="color: #ff0000;">
             <a href="https://www.holynativity.org/wp-content/uploads/Roughriders-1.jpg" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackPageview', '/download/wp-content/uploads/Roughriders-1.jpg']);">
              <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-3717 alignleft" height="105" src="https://www.holynativity.org/wp-content/uploads/Roughriders-1.jpg" width="164"/>
             </a>
             <span style="color: #001000;">
              F
             </span>
            </span>
            or 10 members of Holy Nativity, it was an extraordinary evening at Dr Pepper Ballpark last night. Well, maybe for 8 out of 10 of us. Two of us left early and essentially missed the whole game. The Riders were losing 5-0 in the 5th inning when we finally got on the board with a run. Then in the 6th we scored again with a 3-run home run making it 5-4. Started to get interesting. Then in the 8th inning we added 6 hits, scoring 6 runners making the score 10-5. A Hooks runner got to first on a single in the 9th but then a double play ended the game for a Roughriders win. Last year the game we attended ended with a walk-off home run to break the tie, winning in the 11th inning. Two years in a row the Riders have come back from behind to win the game. Holy Nativity must be a good omen for the Roughriders!!!
