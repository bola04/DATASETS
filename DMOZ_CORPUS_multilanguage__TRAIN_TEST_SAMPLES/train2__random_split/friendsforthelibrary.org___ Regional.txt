     <div class="pure-u-1 pure-u-md-1-2" data-hook="hook-column" id="column-main">
      <p class="lead">
       Founded in 1969, the Friends for the Public Library is a nonprofit, 501(c)3 organization that supports the Albuquerque/Bernalillo County (ABC) Library System.
      </p>
      <p>
       Through the sale of donated books, DVDs and CDs, some 55 volunteers and one part-time coordinator raise over $200,000 annually to support:
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        Annual citywide Summer Reading Program
       </li>
       <li>
        AMP Concert Series at the branch libraries
       </li>
       <li>
        Non-partisan advocacy for library bonds
       </li>
       <li>
        Speaker Series at Special Collections Library
       </li>
       <li>
        Staff development &amp; training
       </li>
       <li>
        El Día de los Niños &amp; other community outreach programs
       </li>
       <li>
        Adopt-a-Branch stipend for branch libraries
       </li>
       <li>
        Other needs, such as the purchase of a Book Van
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       In addition to the satisfaction of supporting the library and promoting literacy, members receive
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        Free admission to the first three hours of the monthly book sale
       </li>
       <li>
        10% discount at the Friends Bookshop in the Main Library
       </li>
      </ul>
