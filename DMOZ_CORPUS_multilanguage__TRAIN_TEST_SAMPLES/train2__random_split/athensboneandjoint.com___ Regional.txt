          Athens Bone and Joint announces merger with Athens Orthopedic Clinic
         </a>
        </h3>
        <p>
         By
         <span class="author">
          admin
         </span>

       <div class="content">
        <p class="image">
         <a href="http://www.athensboneandjoint.com/athens-bone-and-joint-announces-merger-with-athens-orthopedic-clinic/">
          <img alt="" src="http://www.athensboneandjoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/Social-Media-Image-of-merger.jpg"/>
         </a>

        <div class="col span_3_of_4" id="content">
         <div class="post-2944 post type-post status-publish format-standard hentry category-announcements category-news" id="post-2944">
          <p class="page-title">
           We are pleased to announce the upcoming merger with Athens Orthopedic effective July 1, 2018.
          </p>
          <div class="entry">
           <p>
            Beginning Monday, July 2, 2018 all Athens Bone &amp; Joint appointments will be at Athens Orthopedic Clinic, 1765 Old West Broad Street, Building 2, Suite 200, Athens, Ga 30606. All satellite locations of both AOC and AB&amp; J will remain open and in their same locations.
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: left;">
            When this transition occurs on July 1, Dr. David Bacastow has decided that he will be retiring from orthopedic surgery.  Dr. Bacastow has touched many lives across our community and will be deeply missed; however, he will now be able to dedicate this time to his family.  Dr. Bacastow will be finishing care with his patients until June 30, 2018.
            <a href="http://www.athensboneandjoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/Social-Media-Image-of-merger.jpg">
             <img alt="Social Media Image of merger" class="size-medium wp-image-1308 alignright" height="300" sizes="(max-width: 207px) 100vw, 207px" src="http://www.athensboneandjoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/Social-Media-Image-of-merger-207x300.jpg" srcset="http://www.athensboneandjoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/Social-Media-Image-of-merger-207x300.jpg 207w, http://www.athensboneandjoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/Social-Media-Image-of-merger.jpg 662w" width="207"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Please feel free to contact our office for any assistance during this time of transition (706) 549-1663 or by email atinfo@AthensOrthopedicClinic.com.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.onlineathens.com/news/20180509/athens-orthopedic-clinic-athens-bone-amp-joint-announce-merger">
             http://www.onlineathens.com/news/20180509/athens-orthopedic-clinic-athens-bone-amp-joint-announce-merger
            </a>
           </p>
