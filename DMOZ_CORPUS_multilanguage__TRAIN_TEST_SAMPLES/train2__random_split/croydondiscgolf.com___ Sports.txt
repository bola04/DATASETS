          <h3>
           <span class="welcome">
            Welcome to
           </span>
           London's premier disc golf course
          </h3>
          <p class="p1">
           Croydon Disc Golf Club is set in Lloyd Park in Croydon, South London. The course is laid out in the beautiful surroundings of the park. We have 18 permanent baskets with fixed tee mats providing a truly first class experience.
 
                                 We welcome beginners and have an amazing short 9-hole beginner course designed to help those new to the game. Disc hire is available at the park cafe.
          </p>
