       <h2 class="entry-title" id="featured-heading">
        Where Illinois History Began
       </h2>
       <p>
        The LaSalle County Historical Society campus is located on the banks of the historic Illinois &amp; Michigan Canal and is situated in the  quaint village of Utica, Illinois. Bordering beautiful Starved Rock State Park with an annual attendance of 2.2 million, it sits at the heart  of one of Illinois’ top tourism destinations. Utica is easily accessible and conveniently located near where Interstates 80 and 39 meet. The Society’s exhibits and activities include: Canal Warehouse, Heritage Center, Canal Market, Blacksmiths Shop, One-Room School, and much more.
       </p>
