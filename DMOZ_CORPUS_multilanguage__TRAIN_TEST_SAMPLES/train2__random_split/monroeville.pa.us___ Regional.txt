                <br/>
                100 Years of Heroes Project...From the Office of the Lt. Governor, 
                     Mike Stack...
               </font>
              </strong>
              Mr. Salim Makhlouf, Director 
                     of Penn Studios, has reached out to the LT. Governor's Veterans 
                     Task Force for assistance on locating veterans for personal 
                     interviews on their service in the military. These interviews 
                     will be used for an upcoming project that he is developing 
                     to celebrate the 100th Anniversary of Armistice Day.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              If anyone can recommend or nominate any veterans in Monroeville 
                     for this project, feel free to contact Mr. Makhlouf directly 
                     at 717-255-8470 or email:
              <a href="mailto:smakhlouf@pennlive.com" target="_blank">
               smakhlouf@pennlive.com
              </a>
              . 
                     It should be noted that Mr. Makhlouf currently has a high 
                     number of WW2 veterans and is looking for minority servicemen 
                     and women that have served in recent conflicts such as Afghanistan, 
                     Bosnia, Somalia, Iraq, etc.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              Further questions can be directed to the LT. Governor's Office: 
                     Beth Ann Alboucq - 717-787-3300.
