          <p>
           Solar panel produces electricity by accumulating sunlight and then converting it into electricity. A solar panel is a set of solar cells, which are also called PV cells. Development of solar cells started over 100 years ago. Solar cells were earlier on used to produce steam, which helps in operating machinery.
          </p>
          <p>
           Solar panels in Melbourne are used today for various applications such as, telecommunication devices, remote sensing, remote power systems and generating electricity in commercial and residential solar electric systems.
          </p>
          <p>
           Solar panels collect clean sustainable energy as sunlight and transform the light into electric cells. The electricity is then used to power electrical equipment. It is made of several individual solar cells; they contain layers of silicon, phosphorous and boron. Solar panels gets into the photons and by doing that activate the electric power.
          </p>
          <p>
           Every home or commercial premises have rooftops where the solar panels are laid to produce enough sun-based electricity to supply enough electric energy for its needs. The benefit of using sun-oriented power is that it is both cleaner and sustainable.
          </p>
