       <h3 class="post-title">
        The End /The Beginning
       </h3>
       <div class="post-body">
        <div>
         <div style="clear:both;">
         </div>
         <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
          Time goes by in the blink of an eye. Things happen, then pass. Much like our time in Tassie it all went so quickly.
          <br/>
          We watched our children grow up in Tasmania and they will remember it  as being their childhood home with familiarity and fondness.
          <br/>
          We became Australian's on Australia day 2010.   Shortly after we sold our Tasmanian home packed a container for storage, and purchased a caravan.
          <br/>
          I have to end this blog here as We left Tasmania on February 3, 2011 and with our departure a hole in our hearts. However with another adventure we continue on another blog: One year, a caravan, a family of four, and a sausage dog.  The saga continues at www.shareaustralia.blogspot.com  This is a sequel called Allover Downunder as that is what we intend to do!
          <br/>
          <br/>
          PS. Keep Tasmania a  secret as it is a very, very, special place.
