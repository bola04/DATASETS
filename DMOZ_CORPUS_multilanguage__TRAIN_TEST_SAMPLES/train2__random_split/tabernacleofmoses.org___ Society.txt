 INSIDE THE TABERNACLE
    </font>
   </p>
   <p>
    <font size="4">
     These publications are grounded in
 and around the
 Tabernacle of Moses. The biblical types issuing forth from the
 Tabernacle of Moses are an exact typical prefiguration of the body of
 Christ; the eternal bones of the body of Christ born in the First
 Covenant. The types of the Tabernacle of Moses and the types of the
 seven Feasts of God are the "reed like unto a rod" that we
 may use to "measure the temple of God, and the altar, and them
 that worship therein;" they are the rule and sounding board, the
 line and plummet against which all doctrine may be tested for
 veracity. In this present dispensation of forgiveness, these types
 are a reed shaken with the wind; soon a rod of iron in the ensuing
 dispensation of judgment. (Rev 2:27). Picture a parallel: the wooden
 cross of forgiveness is being transformed (Rom 12:2) into the iron
 sword of judgment (Rev 19:15). Both pierce the heart of the earth.
    </font>
