     <h1>
      Quality New Zealand Pine Timber Product Manufacturers and Exporters
     </h1>
     <p>
      South Pine Nelson Ltd. is a major New Zealand producer of quality solid timber products for both the domestic and international markets. Since 1948, the South Pine name has been synonymous with quality, service and reliability in the timber industry.
     </p>
     <h2>
      Our Product Standards
     </h2>
     <ul>
      <li>
       Logs sourced from higher density,
       <strong>
        sustainably managed
       </strong>
       pine forrests.
      </li>
      <li>
       South Pine is FSC
       <strong>
        ®
       </strong>
       certified for Chain of Custody, from purchase of logs through to processing and sales of green and kiln dried, rough sawn and dressed, treated and untreated lumber using the transfer system
      </li>
      <li>
       All structural timber products are graded using modern
       <strong>
        acoustic “A” grader
       </strong>
      </li>
      <li>
       Licensed producer under the
       <strong>
        Verified Timber
       </strong>
       programme
      </li>
      <li>
       NZ Preservation Council “
       <strong>
        Woodmark
       </strong>
       ” guarantee on treated timber
      </li>
     </ul>
