       <h2 class="page-title">
        About
       </h2>
       <div class="entry clearfix">
        <h3>
         <strong>
          Welcome to the website of Palmerston Place Church.
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         Palmerston Place is a city centre Church and is a gathered congregation drawing in people from all across Edinburgh. It has a real family feel. We would love to welcome your here in 2018.
        </p>
        <p>
         Palmerston Place is a forward thinking church which is always looking at how it serves those around it – this is reflected in its aim of
         <em>
          <strong>
           “Bringing Christian Worship, Fellowship and Service to the heart of everyday life”
          </strong>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         Sunday Worship is at 11am and 6.30pm
         <em>
          (there are no 6.30pm services in July and August)
         </em>
         . On a typical Sunday we have close to 200 people at the morning service and around 40 people at the evening service. On a Sunday morning we have a Crèche and a number of groups for children and young people.
        </p>
        <p>
         PPC has a reputation of biblical preaching and music is a mixture of traditional and new. Our Minister is Rev Colin Sinclair who has been with us since 1996.
        </p>
        <p>
         You can listen to the sermons by going to the ‘Sermons’ page. Our sermon archive is also available.
        </p>
        <p>
         During the week we have a wide range of groups and activities from a Baby &amp; Toddler Group (meets during School term-time), to a Friendship Club for Over 60s to the monthly Sunflower Café for those struggling with the issue of memory loss and their carers. We also have a number of stand-alone events. Please look at the ‘Activities’ section of the website and ‘This Month @PPC’.
        </p>
        <p>
         We are located at 12 Palmerston Place, Edinburgh, EH12 5AA. Palmerston Place is located between the West End of the city and the Haymarket area.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
