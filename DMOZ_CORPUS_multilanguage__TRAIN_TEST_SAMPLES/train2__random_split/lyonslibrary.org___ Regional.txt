            </h3>
            <p>
             Need to send an e-mail or finish a resume or school assignment? Drop in with your laptop or other device and connect to our wireless network to get the job done!
            </p>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="twocol-one last">
           <div class="homepage-item fix">

            <h3>
             Forgotten Treasures
            </h3>
            <p>
             Remember those classic book titles that you couldn’t put down? Lyons Public Library has several of these forgotten treasures! Come check them out or offer some recommendations to add to our list!
            </p>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="clear">
          </div>
          <div class="twocol-one">
           <div class="homepage-item fix">

            <h3>
             <a href="https://www.printeron.net/system/printspot/interface/select_file.jsp?Prod_81_PrinterOn=PGPMVyQ9mLfxXW2W8zhJxThZv5Q2nZL3WnkvPprn42TdwS2x6fhn!-706698880!-461247356&amp;url=lyonspl/webprint&amp;protocolSwitched=true">
              Direct Printing
              <i class="fa fa-arrow-right">
              </i>
             </a>
            </h3>
            <p>
             Users must have their own equipment, a compatible wireless card or device, headphones, and software. Applications requiring excessive bandwidth may not be supported. You can also print from home and come in to pick up your print job. The library can only hold the print job for 2 hours.
            </p>
           </div>
           <div class="homepage-item fix">

              Media on Demand
              <i class="fa fa-arrow-right">
              </i>
             </a>
            </h3>
            <p>
             Use your library card to download hundreds of free eBooks, audiobooks and music to your mobile device, computer or any eReader, including the Sony Reader, Barnes &amp; Noble’s Nook and Amazon’s Kindle. Many current and popular titles are available. Call 708.447.3577 or visit the library for assistance.
            </p>
           </div>
