       <h1>
        Getting Braces - there are many different types
       </h1>
       <p style="text-align:left;">
        The headings below outline some of the popular types of braces used to correct the position of teeth.  Different types are used depending on the extent to which teeth are mis-aligned.  Our Dentist-in-Town practice has a very wide range of orthodontic braces and we'll be happy to discuss which type of brace would be the most suitable for you.  To see some of the cases we've treated, click on the 'Gallery' button in the navigation menu and you'll see a number of 'before' and 'after' illustrations.
       </p>

          </a>
          <div class="items-hdr-txt">
           <a href="getting-braces.shtml#invisalign" style="color:#FFF;">
            Invisalign
           </a>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="items-text">
          <b>
           Invisalign
          </b>
          is the leading brand of clear, removable braces that are custom moulded to fit exactly over your teeth.  For a course of treatment several sets of braces are required, each one being used along a journey to realigned teeth.
          <div class="item_footer clearfix">
           <a class="more" href="getting-braces.shtml#invisalign" title="Read more">
            Read more →
           </a>

          </a>
          <div class="items-hdr-txt2">
           <a href="getting-braces.shtml#invisalign" style="color:#FFF;">
            6 Month
            <br/>
            Smile
           </a>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="items-text">
          <b>
           6 Month Smile Braces
          </b>
          are similar to Invisalign in that the treatment is a set of clear braces which, on average, take six months to realign teeth.  However, suitability depends upon the extent to which teeth need to be moved.
          <div class="item_footer clearfix">
           <a class="more" href="getting-braces.shtml#six-month-smile" title="Read more">
            Read more →
           </a>
          </div>

        </li>
        <li class="item_content clearfix">

          </a>
          <div class="items-hdr-txt2">
           <a href="getting-braces.shtml#self-ligating-braces" style="color:#FFF;">
            Self
            <br/>
            Ligating
           </a>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="items-text">
          <b>
           Self Ligating Braces
          </b>
          are also known as "speed braces" because they can produce the quickest results.  With a very low friction design they allow the teeth to move more freely with less applied force.
          <div class="item_footer clearfix">
           <a class="more" href="getting-braces.shtml#self-ligating-braces" title="Read more">
            Read more →
           </a>

         <div class="items-text">
          <b>
           Traditional Fixed Braces
          </b>
          are non-removable by the patient and they are usually the lowest cost.  They consist of small bonded brackets fixed to teeth that  are then linked with  a thin arch wire.
          <div class="item_footer clearfix">
           <a class="more" href="getting-braces.shtml#fixed-braces" title="Read more">

          </a>
          <div class="items-hdr-txt">
           <a href="getting-braces.shtml#inman-aligner-brace" style="color:#FFF;">
            Inman
           </a>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="items-text">
          <b>
           Inman Aligner Brace
          </b>
          is a visible removable brace which can achieve quick results in minor cases. In certain cases, treatments can be as short as 8 weeks and it is necessary to wear the brace for 16-20 hours per day.
          <div class="item_footer clearfix">
           <a class="more" href="getting-braces.shtml#inman-aligner-brace" title="Read more">
            Read more →
           </a>

          </a>
          <div class="items-hdr-txt">
           <a href="getting-braces.shtml#lingual-braces" style="color:#FFF;">
            Lingual
           </a>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="items-text">
          <b>
           Lingual Braces
          </b>
          are fitted to the rear of your teeth and as such they are totally invisible.  However, they take longer to fit and are the most expensive option and not suitable for complex cases.
          <div class="item_footer clearfix">
           <a class="more" href="getting-braces.shtml#lingual-braces" title="Read more">
