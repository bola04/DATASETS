       <p>
        <b>
         WELCOME,
        </b>
        <br clear="none"/>
        I am an artist, educator, and photographer living in Cleveland, Ohio. Through my artwork, I seek to elicit ideas addressing experiences of everyday life and the constructs of subjective knowledge by utilizing the means of making, the formal display, and the contemporary discourse regarding photographs. I began producing commercial photography in 1993, teaching college level photography courses in 1998, and have always made artwork. My passion for photography is matched only by my passion for rigorous thought translated into art.
