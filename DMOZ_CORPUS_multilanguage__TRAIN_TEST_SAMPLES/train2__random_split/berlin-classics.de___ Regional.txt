       <h2>
        Hello &amp; Welcome
       </h2>
       <div>
        <img alt="We are the V8 MG B specialists." border="1" height="373" src="images/cwb-mgb-v8-sebring-homepage-jan13.jpg" width="560"/>
        <span>
         <bold>
          We build unique and characterful vehicles with plenty of pace, such as this Sebring MGB V8.
         </bold>
        </span>
       </div>
       <br/>
       <div class="teaserimg">
        <a href="restoration.html">
         <img alt="" border="0" height="164" src="images/widearchmgbv8.jpg" width="219"/>
        </a>
        <span>
         This subtly modified MGB V8 was restored by us.
        </span>
       </div>
       <p>
        Welcome to the online garage of Classic Wheels Berlin. We are the foremost address in Berlin specialising in the repair and restoration of British classic  cars. We have many years experience working on all the great marques, with particular emphasis on Jaguar and MG. We maintain as well as restore a growing number of historic vehicles entrusted to us by owners from ever further afield.
       </p>
       <p>
        After what seemed a particularly long winter the first tentative shoots of spring have arrived. Keen motorists thoughts now turn to waking their beloved classic vehicles from the winter slumber. Now is the time to repair any problems which need to be rectified before the classic motoring season begins. We can assist with whatever restoration or repair work is necessary to ensure your vehicle is in top form, utterly roadworthy and simply a pleasure to drive. However, we highly recommend that you call first and make an appointment with us, as demand is high.
       </p>
       <br/>
       <h2>
        What we do
       </h2>
       <div class="teaserimg">
        <a href="restoration.html">
         <img alt="" border="0" height="164" src="images/jagxk140dhc-bodyshell.jpg" width="219"/>
        </a>
        <span>
         A Jaguar XK140 which was fully refurbished at CWB.
        </span>
       </div>
       <p>
        In our  workshop we specialise in the
        <a class="or" href="service.html">
         day-to-day maintenance and repair
        </a>
        or even
        <a class="or" href="restoration.html">
         thorough restoration
        </a>
        of classic vehicles. In addition, we carry out
        <a class="or" href="design.html">
         bespoke design and coachwork
        </a>
        for clients who wish to enhance their vehicles. We have a passion for historic motorsport. We also offer a
        <a class="or" href="racing.html">
         complete fully-supported historic motor racing experience
        </a>
        behind the wheel of one of our trio of race-ready MG Bs. Our skillset includes virtually every aspect of the motor trade, though when needed we  call upon the services of a carefully vetted talent pool of experienced master trades- and craftsmen.
       </p>
       <h2>
        <a href="sales.html">
         Vehicles For Sale
        </a>
       </h2>
       <div class="teaserimg">
        <a href="sales.html">
         <img alt="" border="0" height="164" src="images/2tvrs_aston_jag.jpg" width="219"/>
        </a>
        <span>
         We always offer a selection of prestige vehicles.
        </span>
       </div>
       <p>
        We always have a fast moving selection of
        <a class="or" href="sales.html">
         delightful classic vehicles for sale
        </a>
        . We would be delighted to handle the sale of your classic car on your behalf. We are experienced in trading vehicles across international borders, and can ably assist with the necessary paperwork.
       </p>
       <p>
        We also provide a scouting service to locate the special or rare classic you seek. Furthermore we offer a secure storage service for prestige vehicles, subject to limited availability. Please enquire in person for further information. Our secure storage capacity is increasing.
       </p>
       <h2>
        <a href="racing.html">
         Historic Motorsport
        </a>
       </h2>
       <div class="teaserimg">
        <a href="racing.html">
         <img alt="" border="0" height="160" src="images/v8mgb-battle-spreewaldring09.jpg" width="219"/>
        </a>
        <span>
         Two of our recent MGB V8 conversions do battle.
        </span>
       </div>
       <p>
        When we're not wrenching under classic cars we can be found racing them.
        <a class="or" href="racing.html">
         The Classic Wheels Berlin Racing Team
        </a>
        compete in the Yokohama FHR Paragon  Cup.
 In addition to supported driver places in our own trio of MG B racecars, we offer a comprehensive motorsport service to prepare your car for competition in accordance with  FIA and DEUVET regulations. We can tune your vehicle for motorsport, including modifiying the drivetrain, suspension and bodywork.
       </p>
       <h2>
        <a href="design.html">
         Bespoke Design
        </a>
       </h2>
       <div class="teaserimg">
        <a href="design.html">
         <img alt="" border="0" height="150" src="images/home-bespokeMGB.jpg" width="219"/>
        </a>
        <span>
         Bespoke all-metal flared-arch MGB V8
        </span>
       </div>
       <p>
        We can modify your vehicle to suit your every whim, whether your tastes are subtle, or tend toward the extravagant. From initial consultation to the finished vehicle, all work is carried out in house by our highly experienced mechanics and bodywork professionals. Stay tuned for the development of our own unique custom fabricated racecar transporter, a large-scale rolling advertisement for our skill that should soon be hauling our racecars to and from the racetrack.
       </p>
