        <h1>

        </h1>
        <p>
         The Kansas Legislature consists of a 125-member
         <a href="http://www.kslegislature.org/li/b2015_16/chamber/house/">
          House of Representatives
         </a>

         <a href="http://www.kslegislature.org/li/b2015_16/chamber/senate/">
          Senate
         </a>
         .  The Legislature convenes on the second Monday in January for an annual session and generally adjourns in early May.  For more information about the Kansas Legislature go to

          About the Legislature
         </a>
