             <p align="left">
              <em>
               Holiday Cottage Northumberland near the beach- Available for Short Lets
               <br/>
              </em>
              The seaside towns of Bamburgh, Newton-by-the-Sea, Alnmouth and Warkworth are a short drive away along the coast.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              UK beach holidays are back in vogue- Devon and Cornwall are beautiful too, but the smart people head to Northumberland where you can have this beach almost to yourself in August!
              <br/>
             </p>
             Boulmer is a traditional Northumbrian fishing village in the heart of the Heritage Coast. Previously infamous as a haven for smugglers, Boulmer now attracts a friendly mix of locals and holidaymakers searching for fresh sea air. Close to Craster which is famous for its oak smoked kippers and fishing harbour, as well as the castles at Warkworth, Dunstanburgh, Bamburgh and Holy Island and just down the road is Alnmouth with its shops, pubs, restaurants and golf courses. There is plenty of fishing to be had on the Coquet and Aln as well as sea fishing from Craster and Amble. The coastal path is a 2 minute walk away and the "FBI" or Fisherman´s Boat Inn, is a short stroll away- this is a popular pub thanks to its excellent pub grub.
             <br/>
             <p align="left">
              Don´t mispronounce Alnwick when you are visiting! It´s equivalent to saying "Edin-Berg" for Edinburgh. The locals pronounce "Alnwick" anick, alnick or annick. The l and w are silent.
