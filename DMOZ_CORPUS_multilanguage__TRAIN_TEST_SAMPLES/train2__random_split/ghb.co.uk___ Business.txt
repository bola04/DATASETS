       <h1>
        Gover Horowitz &amp; Blunt Ltd
       </h1>
       <h5>
        <font size="2">
         <strong>
          Gover Horowitz &amp; Blunt Ltd
         </strong>
         is a family company, which was established 50 years ago by our present Chairman, Mr Lucien Horowitz. We trade primarily in coir fibre, coir yarn, brush and other natural fibres. In recent years the product range has diversified to include synthetic fibres, steel wire and mattress spring units.
        </font>
       </h5>
       <h5>
        <font size="2">
        </font>
       </h5>
       <h5>
        <font size="2">
         The Company enjoys an unrivalled reputation in the market-place for sourcing and shipping products in bulk from all over the world. We employ a team of multi-lingual traders with extensive knowledge and experience of global trading, plus expertise in their chosen business specialities. We provide an efficient and professional service to suit our customers' requirements whether buying and selling on tailor-made trading terms or dealing in any currency to reduce foreign exchange exposure.
        </font>
       </h5>
       <h5>
        <font size="2">
         Based in central London, Gover Horowitz &amp; Blunt Ltd is easily accessible from major airports or via the road and rail networks. We welcome trade enquiries at our London office and are very happy to see customers in person.
        </font>
       </h5>
