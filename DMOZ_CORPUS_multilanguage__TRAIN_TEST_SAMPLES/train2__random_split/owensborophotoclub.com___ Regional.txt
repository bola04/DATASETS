       <h1>
        Welcome to the Owensboro Photography Club
       </h1>
       <p>
        We meet on the 4th Monday of the month at the Ralph Center on Kentucky Wesleyan Campus (Corner of South Griffith and College Drive) at 7 p.m. Be sure to check out the monthly newsletter for information about current events at OPC.
       </p>
       <h3>
        <strong>
         OPC History
        </strong>
       </h3>
       <p>
        The Owensboro Photography club is a group of people drawn together by an interest in photography. We have members who are just getting started and want to learn to improve their photos. Some members are seasoned phtographers looking to learn about new techniques. We even have professional photographers who earn a living through photography.
       </p>
       <h3>
        <strong>
         Message from the President of the club
        </strong>
        - Eddie Atherton
       </h3>
       <p>
        By looking at our website it demonstrates that you have an  interest in photography.  The Owensboro  Photography Club has been in existence for many years.  Some of those earliest members still  participate or stay in touch.  Whether  you use a point and shoot or the best of whatever brand around, you will enjoy  attending our meetings and being active with other people with the same  interest.  Come join us for a meeting and  check it out and if you would like, join when you are ready.  Thanks for checking us out.
       </p>

       <p>
        The Owensboro Photographic Club is a group of people drawn together by an interest in phtography. We have members who are just getting started and want to learn to improve their photos. Some members are seasoned phtographers looking to learn about new techniques. We even have professional phtographers who earn a living through phtography.
       </p>
       <p>
        The OPC was formed in 1948 by Mr. Arnett Husk. In the beginning; shutter snappers met in a basement to talk about photography. The club still meets to this day; with Frank Abrams as the current longest active member. Meetings are held the 4th Monday of every month at the Owensboro Area Museum of Science and History. The museum is located at 220 Daviess St. Meeting are from 7:00 pm to 9:00 pm.
       </p>
       <p>
        If you have an interest in photography this may be the club for you! Ask an OPC member about club benefits, or join as at a meeting. Guests are always welcome. To find out more see Contact OPC.
