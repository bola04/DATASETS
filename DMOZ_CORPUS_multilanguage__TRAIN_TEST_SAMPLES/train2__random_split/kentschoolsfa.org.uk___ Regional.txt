            <h1 class="title">
             Kicking The Ball Into The Goal Of Football Success
            </h1>
           </header>
           <div class="post-content box mark-links">
            <p>
             <img alt="" class="size-medium wp-image-17 alignleft" height="170" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="http://kentschoolsfa.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/sites/98/2018/05/carousel-300x170.jpg" srcset="http://kentschoolsfa.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/sites/98/2018/05/carousel-300x170.jpg 300w, http://kentschoolsfa.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/sites/98/2018/05/carousel.jpg 620w" width="300"/>
             Football is not just any ball game, it is one of the most competitive. This is reflected by the use of various social influences, from memorabilia to video games, and even collectors items. This community enhancing game also transcends genders and is embraced by the global community in general. Countries pride rely on having strong players that will help the team climb the leaderboard of success into the desired premier league. More than an extracurricular activity, football is also a sport of camaraderie.
            </p>
            <h3>
             Local Roots To International Recognition
            </h3>
            <p>
             Ensuring its longevity starts from the local communities and Kent is no different. Strong teams are built from the ground up, and famous players started in some form of football school, thus leading to the exciting game that we know today.
            </p>
            <p>
             Starting them young with the kids football in their local community is important. With so much hanging on the line in terms of grants and future possibilities, parents needs to know where to go if they want to enrol their children in a football school.
            </p>
            <p>
             Once a school has been selected, it becomes important to acquire the right football equipment. With so many brands now on the market, durability and performance enhancement are the key essentials.
            </p>
            <p>
             Scouts in this field are always on the look-out for the next big star. This is reflected by the increased number of people interested in taking part in this sport. Talent and discipline is also part and parcel of the sport, because success and failure are determined by individual application and determination.
            </p>
