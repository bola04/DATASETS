       <h2>
        <span style="color: #000000;">
         What Does Taylor Montgomery Facility Do?
        </span>
       </h2>
       <h1 id="title" style="font-weight: 400; color: #e31937;">
        DUMPSTER RENTALS, DEMOLITION, MATERIALS PROCESSING &amp; RECYCLING
       </h1>
       <p>
        Taylor-Montgomery, LLC rents out roll-off dumpsters, performs expert demolitions and recycles construction and demolition waste materials into new and useful products available for purchase at our Montgomery, NY facility. Taylor’s mission is to reduce landfill waste and greenhouse gas emissions by generating useful products from
        <strong>
         95%
        </strong>
        , or more, of the debris brought to our site, which was once considered useless. We envision a cleaner tomorrow, less dependent on landfills or incineration.
       </p>
       <p>
        Taylor-Montgomery, LLC has received many inquiries into this decade long project, the
        <strong>
         Taylor Biomass
        </strong>
        . Take some time to read about this incredibly exciting project in its final stages and how this Biomass will change the was you see garbage, and the way you see Taylor!
        <span style="color: #0000ff;">
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.taylor-montgomery.com/wp-content/uploads/CLICK-HERE-TO-LEARN-MORE-ABOUT-THE-TAYLOR-SOLUTION1.pdf" style="color: #0000ff;">
           CLICK HERE TO LEARN MORE ABOUT THE TAYLOR SOLUTION
          </a>
         </strong>
        </span>
       </p>

        <span style="color: #0000ff;">
         <a href="http://www.taylor-montgomery.com/wp-content/uploads/Cover-Letter-and-Petition-Combined2.pdf" style="color: #0000ff;">
          Cover Letter and Petition Combined

         <a href="http://www.taylor-montgomery.com/wp-content/uploads/Taylor-Biomass-Energy-LLC-public-comments-regarding-NYSDEC-6NYCRR-Part-360-Drated-Proposed-rules-and-regulations-dated-9-13-2016.pdf" style="color: #0000ff;">
          Taylor Biomass Energy, LLC- NYSDEC 6NYCRR Part 360 Drated Proposed Rules &amp; Regulations

        <strong style="font-size: 16px; color: #e31937; font-weight: 400;">
         Hours of Operation

        Our 350 Neelytown Road facility, Montgomery, NY accepts construction and demolition debris from any hauler, contractor or homeowner, in any sized vehicle, during the following days and hours:
