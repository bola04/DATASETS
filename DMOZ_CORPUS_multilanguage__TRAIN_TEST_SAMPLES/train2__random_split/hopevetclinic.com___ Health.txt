       <h1>
        The Highest Standards of Care
        <br/>
        Three Area Locations
       </h1>
      </header>
      <div class="bi-text-envelope">
       <!-- start home page content -->
       <figure class="bi-content-img right">
        <img alt="dog in suitcase" src="https://www.hopevetclinic.com/images/home/happy-white-dog.jpg" title="dog in suitcase"/>
       </figure>
       <p class="bi-p-highlight">
        Your pet deserves the highest quality care, and Hope Veterinary Clinic provides a full range of advanced diagnostic services and treatment options for cats and dogs in Columbus, Indiana and the surrounding communities.
       </p>
       <p>
        We have
        <span class="bi-contact-controller bi-fake-anchor">
         three area locations
        </span>
        — Hope Veterinary Clinic East, Hope Veterinary Clinic North, and Hope Veterinary Clinic West. We take great pride in the fact that all of our facilities are certified by the
        <a href="https://www.aaha.org/pet_owner/about_aaha/default.aspx" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
         American Animal Hospital Association (AAHA)
        </a>
        — the only accrediting body for small animal practices in the U.S. and Canada. Our AAHA certification means your pet is being treated at a practice that holds itself to the highest standards of veterinary care.
       </p>
       <p>
        Our six doctors have a combined 120+ years of experience. Together, our medical team and office staff strive every day to provide the highest quality medical and surgical care for our patients. We place a high value on communication and education because we want every pet to live the longest, healthiest life possible.
       </p>
       <p>
        Please take a few moments to review our full range of
        <a href="https://www.hopevetclinic.com/services/index.html">
         services
        </a>
        and to
        <a href="https://www.hopevetclinic.com/staff/index.html">
         meet our team
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        Whether it’s time for an annual checkup or your pet is showing signs of illness, please
        <span class="bi-contact-controller bi-fake-anchor">
         contact us
        </span>
        at the Hope Veterinary Clinic location that’s most convenient for you. We look forward to meeting you and providing your pet with the quality, experienced care they deserve.
       </p>
