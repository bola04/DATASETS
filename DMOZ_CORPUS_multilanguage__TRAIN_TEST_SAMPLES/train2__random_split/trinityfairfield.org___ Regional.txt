           <p align="left" style='text-align: center; font-family: Lato, "Lato Regular", sans-serif; font-size: 1.44em; font-style: italic; font-weight: 400;'>
            "even as the Son of Man came not to be served but to serve,
           </p>
           <p align="left" style='text-align: center; font-family: Lato, "Lato Regular", sans-serif; font-size: 1.44em; font-style: italic; font-weight: 400;'>
            and to give His life as a ranson for many."
           </p>
           <p align="left" style='text-align: center; font-family: Lato, "Lato Regular", sans-serif; font-size: 1.31em; font-style: normal; font-weight: 400;'>
            <span class="clovercustom" style="letter-spacing: -0.01em;">
             Matthew 20:28
             <span class="clovercustom" style="letter-spacing: -0.01em;">
              <br/>
             </span>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p align="left" style='text-align: center; font-family: Lato, "Lato Regular", sans-serif; font-size: 1.12em; font-weight: 400;'>
            <br/>
           </p>

            <br/>

           <p align="left" style='text-align: center; color: rgb(74, 128, 19); font-family: Lato, "Lato Regular", sans-serif; font-weight: 700; font-size: 1.1348em;'>
            Trinity exists to lead people back to God through Jesus Christ and to help us become God-first believers.
           </p>
           <p align="left" style='text-align: center; font-family: Lato, "Lato Regular", sans-serif; font-size: 1.12em; font-weight: 400;'>
            We want to know more about God, and we want that knowledge to impact every corner of our lives. We believe Christ is our model for life and living. He is the source of lasting love, joy, peace, and hope; and through Him comes genuine reconciliation and restoration. Putting God first isn't easy. It means serving as He served and putting others before ourselves. But that changes us from the inside out. It not only affects us for the better; it affects our families, it affects our communities, and it affects our world.
           </p>
