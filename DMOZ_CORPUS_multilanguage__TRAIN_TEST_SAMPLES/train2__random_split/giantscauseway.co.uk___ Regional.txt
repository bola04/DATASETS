          <p align="justify">
           <span style="font-family: Arial;">
            Welcome to Ballylinny Cottages – nine luxury self contained cottages and two barn lofts which are indeed something rather special.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p align="justify">
           <span style="font-family: Arial;">
            <img alt="Ballylinny Cottages" class="ngg-singlepic ngg-none" height="150" src="http://www.giantscauseway.co.uk/wp-content/gallery/main/ballylinny-cottages-6.jpg" width="200"/>

          </p>
          <p align="justify">
           <span style="font-family: Arial;">
            Experience unrivalled views over the Giants Causeway, the sleepy seaside village of Portballintrae and the sweeping Donegal Coastline beyond.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p align="justify">
           <span style="font-family: Arial;">
            Situated only 1 mile from the village of Bushmills, home to the world’s oldest licensed whiskey distillery, half a mile from the Giants Causeway and 7 miles from the resorts of Portrush and Portstewart, there is so much to do and see.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p align="justify">
           <span style="font-family: Arial;">
            At Ballylinny Cottages, guests can enjoy the ultimate in luxury living set amongst some of the most breathtaking scenery Ireland has to offer. It really is something rather special!
