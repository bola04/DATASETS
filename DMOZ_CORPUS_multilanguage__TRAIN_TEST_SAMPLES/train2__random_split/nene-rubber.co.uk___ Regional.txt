     <h1 title="Welcome to Nene  Rubber &amp; Plastics Ltd">
      Welcome to Nene  Rubber &amp; Plastics Ltd
     </h1>
     <p>
      Nene Rubber &amp; Plastics Ltd, based in Peterborough, Cambridgeshire
         manufacture anti-vibration mounts, rubber moulding's, seal &amp; gaskets
         and machining services as well as supplying rubber &amp; plastic products
         such as rubber matting, door curtains and engineering plastics to uk
         industry.
     </p>
     <h2>
      Overview
     </h2>
     Nene Rubber &amp; Plastics Ltd have over 25 years experience in the rubber &amp; plastics
         industry. Operating from their 5,000ft
     <sup>
      2
     </sup>
     facility Nene Rubber &amp; Plastics
         Ltd employ 10 staff and serve a wide range of industries including the
         automotive, pharmaceutical, electronics and power sectors. The business
         consists of two division, rubber converting and the sale of standard
         rubber and plastic products such as matting, hose and engineering plastics.
     <h2>
      <img align="right" alt="Rubber Sheeting" height="267" src="images/image004.jpg" width="200"/>

       25 years and offer a range of standard off the shelf parts as well as

     <h2>
      Rubber Moulding's
     </h2>
     We offer both compression moulding and transfer moulding of most thermoset
       rubber compounds including natural rubber, neoprene, EPDM, Silicone (with
       silver/nickel), nitrile, Viton and SBR. We operate 14 heated hydraulic
       moulding presses with Platen sizes from 200mm
     <sup>
      2
     </sup>
     up to 500mm
     <sup>
      2
     </sup>
     and 25 tons
         up to 175 tons capacity. One of our key strengths is our ability to
         offer a fast turn around on low to medium volume work, as well as accepting
         existing customer tooling.
     <h2>
      Seals &amp; Gaskets
     </h2>
     We offer specialist low volume production (1 to 10 off) of seals and
       gaskets based on customer drawings or existing components and seals.
       Materials handled include natural rubber, neoprene, nitrile and cork.
