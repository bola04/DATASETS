       Welcome to Minnetonka Animal Hospital
      </p>
      <h1>
       Exceptional Integrative Veterinary Care
      </h1>
     </div>
     <div class="s-grid-12 m-grid-6 l-grid-8 padded-sides">
      <!-- start home page content -->
      <p>
       <img alt="Cat Drinking From Coi Pond" class="in-text-img align-left" src="/images/home/thirsty-cat.jpg"/>
       Minnetonka Animal Hospital provides high-quality, comprehensive care to cats, dogs, and small mammals. To meet the diverse needs of each client, we offer the best of Eastern and Western medicine. Our team of experienced professionals makes recommendations that are tailored to your pet and can help you explore how he or she may benefit from an integrated, holistic approach.
      </p>
      <p>
       As part of our commitment to excellence, our practice is accredited by the
       <a href="https://www.aaha.org/pet_owner/default.aspx" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
        American Animal Hospital Association
       </a>
       (AAHA), which sets the highest standards of veterinary care. Accreditation is voluntary, and we are regularly evaluated by AAHA to maintain our standing. Fewer than 15 percent of hospitals nationwide achieve this level of distinction.
      </p>
      <p>
       Minnetonka Animal Hospital has also been certified as a Gold Standard
       <a href="http://www.catvets.com/cfp/cat-owners" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
        Cat-Friendly Practice
       </a>
       by the American Association of Feline Practitioners. Our team understands the unique needs of cats and their owners, and we integrate the optimum level of
       <a href="http://www.catvets.com/public/PDFs/CatFriendlyPractice/CFP-OwnerBrochure.pdf" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
        feline-friendly considerations
       </a>
       during each visit.
      </p>
