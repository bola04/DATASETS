              <h3 class="text-align-center">
               <strong>
                We Would Like To Welcome You to the
               </strong>
               <strong>
                Maryland Bowhunters Society
               </strong>
              </h3>
              <p>
               <strong>
                <span style="font-size:14px">
                 The Maryland Bowhunters Society is dedicated to promoting and preserving  bowhunting; educating the non-hunting public about the role of the bowhunter in  wildlife management and conservation; and educating bowhunters in safety, shooting  skills, hunting techniques and landowner relations. The MBS strongly advocates ethical bowhunting within the laws of the jurisdiction hunted and in a sportsmanlike  manner under the Rules of Fair Chase established by the
                </span>
                <a href="http://www.pope-young.org/" target="_blank">
                 Pope and Young Club
                </a>
                <a href="http://www.pope-young.org/" target="_blank">
                 .
                </a>
               </strong>
               <br/>
              </p>
              <p>
               <strong>
                <span style="font-size:14px">
                 MBS led the charge to bring the
                </span>
                <a href="http://www.nasparchery.com/activea.asp" target="_blank">
                 National Archery in the Schools Program
                </a>
                <span style="font-size:14px">
                 to MD in 2005 and we continue to support NASP through a partnership with MD DNR. We have raised and donated over $50,000 to schools across MD and have impacted thousands of MD in the last 10 years.
                </span>
                <span style="font-size:14px">
                 We actively fight the anti-hunting movement and have been recognized by DNR and the Maryland Wildlife Advisory Commission for our efforts to protect and promote hunting.
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 If you wish to join the MBS, click
                 <a href="/join-the-mbs">
                  HERE
                 </a>
                 .
                 <br/>
                 If we can answer any question or concerns, please send us an email via our
                 <a href="/contact">
                  CONTACT US
                 </a>
                </span>
               </strong>
               <strong>
                <span style="font-size:14px">
                 <a href="/contact">
                 </a>
                 page
                </span>
                <span style="font-size:14px">
                 .
                 <br/>
                 You can also sign up for the MBS News &amp; E-Alerts to stay informed on MBS News, Events and any Calls to Action affecting hunting issues. Click
                </span>
               </strong>
               <strong>
                <span style="font-size:14px">
                 <a href="http://visitor.r20.constantcontact.com/d.jsp?llr=xjwte6cab&amp;p=oi&amp;m=1102637029687&amp;sit=o8j4h8jeb&amp;f=7f577aa3-9f16-4348-ba11-a67f0756f6de" target="_blank">
                  HERE
                 </a>
                 to sign up.
                </span>
               </strong>
              </p>
