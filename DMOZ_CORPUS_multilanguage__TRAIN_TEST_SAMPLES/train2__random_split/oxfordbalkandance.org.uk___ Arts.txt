     Welcome, dancers present and future!  Balkan dancing is great fun - invigorating, social and joyful.  Its good exercise and the music is addictive!  You can help preserve the folk dances and music of the Balkan regions right here in Oxford, by coming along to our regular weekly sessions.
    </p>

     Our small friendly group meets every Thursday evening (except for election days, and short breaks over Christmas and summer) between 8.15 and 10.15 p.m.
