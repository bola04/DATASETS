            <p>
             Stroud International Textiles now known as SIT select is an arts organisation supporting the visual arts and applied arts. Based in Stroud, Gloucestershire we support local and national artists in curated and artist-led exhibitions, events, talks and educational opportunities with a successful annual Select Trail Open Studios and Select Festival every May. We work to raise artist’s profiles and support their creative journey.
                     SIT supports three artist studios in the centre of Stroud.
            </p>
