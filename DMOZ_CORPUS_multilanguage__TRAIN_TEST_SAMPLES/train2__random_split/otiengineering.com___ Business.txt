                 <p class="BasicParagraph" style="text-align: justify;">
                  <span style="font-size: 14pt; line-height: 120%;">
                   is a professional engineering corporation with offices in Louisiana and Mississippi specializing in advanced technology solutions to real world problems. Centered in sophisticated engineering technology, “Omni Tech” was founded in 1994 and has the mission to provide a variety of high tech services.....
                  </span>
                 </p>
                 <p class="BasicParagraph" style="text-align: justify;">
                  <strong>
                   <span style="font-size: 14pt; line-height: 120%;">
                    Research, Development, and Applications Engineering Services
                   </span>
                  </strong>
                  <span style="font-size: 14pt;  line-height: 120%;">
                   to government, academic, medical, and private sectors. Omni Tech’s core competencies are in electrical and electronic design, telecommunications, power engineering, data acquisition, sensor design, fiber optics, optical systems, application and embedded programming, communications, sensor systems, power generation and distribution, digital signal processing, acoustics and oceanographic instrumentation. Omni Tech has considerable experience with marine electronics, sensors and systems for the military and oil field markets. Omni Tech strives to work on new and exciting technology and provides research and development services to our clients as well as performing our own internal R&amp;D.
                  </span>
                 </p>
                 <p class="BasicParagraph" style="text-align: justify;">
                  <strong>
                   <span style="font-size: 14pt; line-height: 120%;">
                    Product Development/Manufacturing
                   </span>
                  </strong>
                  <span style="font-size: 14pt; line-height: 120%;">
                   for commercial and government clients. As a result of our design and development work, Omni Tech has developed an expertise in product development and manufacturing. Omni Tech can manufacture small production runs (tens to hundreds of units) on a cost effective basis and has demonstrated an ability to maintain high yields. Our design expertise provides value added capabilities for our clients to improve their products.
                  </span>
                 </p>
                 <p class="BasicParagraph" style="text-align: justify;">
                  <strong>
                   <span style="font-size: 14pt; line-height: 120%;">
                    System Integration
                   </span>
                  </strong>
                  <span style="font-size: 14pt; line-height: 120%;">
                   of technology solutions for real world problems. OTI is not just a desktop engineering firm. Omni Tech is involved in installation and maintenance of systems we design and manufacture. This runs the gamut from CCTV systems to offshore fiber optic networks. Omni Tech has traveled the world testing, installing, supporting and maintaining technology systems.
                  </span>
                 </p>
                 <p class="BasicParagraph" style="text-align: justify;">
                  <strong>
                   <span style="font-size: 14pt; line-height: 120%;">
                    Design and Consulting Services
                   </span>
                  </strong>
                  <span style="font-size: 14pt;  line-height: 120%;">
                   for clients who need technical expertise in specific areas and not end-to-end solutions. OTI offers our technical expertise to clients through design and consulting services. This might include development of electronic components for a larger system or acting as an expert witness in a trial.
                  </span>
                 </p>
                 <p class="BasicParagraph" style="text-align: justify;">
                  <strong>
                   <span style="font-size: 14pt; line-height: 120%;">
                    Expert Technology Transfer and Laboratory Transition Assistance
                   </span>
                  </strong>
                  <span style="font-size: 14pt;   line-height: 120%;">
                   between small businesses and the government or university sector, including those activities regarding the introduction of a new technology within a corporation and the problems associated with the process.
                  </span>
                 </p>
                 <p class="BasicParagraph" style="text-align: justify;">
                  <strong>
                   <span style="font-size: 14pt; line-height: 120%;">
                    Advanced Training Services
                   </span>
                  </strong>
                  <span style="font-size: 14pt;  line-height: 120%;">
                   in the areas of fiber optics, telecommunications, acoustics and networking using seminar forums and classroom situations.
                  </span>
                 </p>
                 <p class="BasicParagraph" style="text-align: justify;">
                  <span style="font-size: 14pt; line-height: 120%;">
                   Furthermore, the company’s association with several prominent academic institutions and universities as an industrial partner for concerted research work and occasional in-kind services on projects, provides the staff with unique technical experiences and challenges beyond the routine. This requires a multi-disciplinary staff of “omni technologists” that include physicists, mechanical engineers, electrical engineers, telecommunications engineers, signal processors and computer scientists.
                  </span>
                 </p>
