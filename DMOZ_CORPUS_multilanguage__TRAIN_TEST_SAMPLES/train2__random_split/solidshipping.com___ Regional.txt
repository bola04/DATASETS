        <h3>
         Welcome to Solid Shipping Lines Corporation
        </h3>
        <p>
         Active in the domestic shipping industry for the past forty years, Solid Shipping Lines Corporation operates nine vessels with a total capacity of 57,529 DWT that promptly serves the following ports on a "Direct Cargo Liner Service":
        </p>
        <p style="margin-left: 20px;">
         ●   Manila - Davao - Manila
         <br/>
         ●   Manila - Gen. Santos - Manila
         <br/>
         ●   Manila - Cagayan de Oro - Manila
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p>
         The Company constantly strives to remain among the top performers in the industry by continually upgrading its fleet through the acquisition of brand new, bigger-capacity vessels from Japan. At present four of these new vessels are already delivered and active, while the fifth has been commissioned for construction.
        </p>
        <p>
         The Company is also steadfast in its belief that customer satisfaction is the ultimate reward for a service well done. Hence, it has committed to the following:
        </p>
        <p style="margin-left: 20px;">
         ●   Invest in new software to further reduce customer stay-time
         <br/>
         ●   Ensure that all its containers are free from damages
         <br/>
         ●   Train all staff to better serve our customers
         <br/>
         ●   Be more efficient and effective, making sure that all cargo are delivered safely and on time
         <br/>
        </p>
        <p>
         For you, we work to remain as "The Most Reliable Shipping Company".
        </p>
