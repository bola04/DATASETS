           <p>
            <strong>
             You too can accomplish the tree shaping projects of your desires and support the growth of this art by purchasing Richard's book.
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            The book includes the history of the art, how to do it yourself and interviews with nine tree trunk artist from six different countries.
           </p>
           <p style="margin-left:40px; margin-right:0px">
            The book decribes the rich history of the art of arborsculpture, stretching as far back as medieval times. Interviews with the people around the world who practice the art along with step by step how to start your own live arborsculpture projects.
           </p>
