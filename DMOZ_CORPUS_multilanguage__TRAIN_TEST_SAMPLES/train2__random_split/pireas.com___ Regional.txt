                    <h1 class="title">
                     About us
                    </h1>
                   </div>
                   <p>
                    Behind the "pireas.com" is not an enterprise, a tourist office or an internet service provider. It is created with love in purpose to build a communication bridge between people of Piraeus  and of all the world.  A a result of the dedication the pireas.com is a respectable and complete informative guide for those who are interested in city Piraeus and wish to visit it. Aside the historical, geographical and folkloric elements, you will find also registrations, selected on the bases of the high quality in offered services.
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    With the help of "pireas.com" one can plan trips to Piraeus. It also gives guidelines for travels to the picturesque islands around Piraeus and the beautiful, sun -drenched coastlines.
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    On the following pages we recommend you carefully selected tourist services and make some proposals for:
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    The supreme places to visit (Sights, museums, monasteries, Monuments, Saronic Island)
                    <br/>
                    The best and the most comfortable accommodation (hotels, apartments, rooms)
                    <br/>
                    The best and the fastest ways to travel and ways how to explore each corner of Piraeus (renting of cars, motorbikes, bicycles)
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    Piraeus has  many interesting places to visit: historical places, museums and the beautiful coastline mixed with the modern life a of a big metropolis, with the Nightlife, Cafe &amp; Bars. There is something for everybody, it is up to you what to choose. For you, who like sports; you can join sailing, windsurfing or any other kind of sport.  In the evenings you can have a good time in nightclubs, restaurants and cafeterias around the harbors, enjoying your drink with music and good company.
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    All the following pages are made with love and enthusiasm by a team of young people from all over the world who are residents of Piraeus. Also by others, who have visited Piraeus  and love the city that has so many virtues.  Together we have worked hard to offer you, who you are interested in Piraeus, a complete informative guide about this city and a very useful tool for the visitors of Piraeus.
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    You are welcome! We wish you a good time and give the best to insure you the good memories about the visit to Piraeus.
                   </p>
