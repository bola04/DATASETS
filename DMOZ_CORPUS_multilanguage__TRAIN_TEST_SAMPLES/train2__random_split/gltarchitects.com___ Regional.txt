             <h1>
              Kaleidoscope Charter School
             </h1>
             <p>
              With successful elementary and middle school programs, Kaleidoscope Charter School worked with GLTArchitects to expand their facilities to meet their growing space needs and offer high school grade levels.  Through selective remodeling and a significant addition, features include a new media center, cafeteria with adjacent stage and classrooms which support their Science, Technology, Engineering, Art and Math (S.T.E.A.M.) programming.

             <h1>
              Country Manor - St. Joseph
             </h1>
             <p>
              Building on the success of the Waterford at Country Manor, Country Manor Campus partnered with GLTArchitects to pursue a new senior living campus located on an expansive natural site in the City of St. Joseph.  Phase I includes 60 independent living and 24 memory care apartment homes.  The facility will offer wellness / therapy, chapel, community room, general store, coffee shop and beauty shop.  Teaming with Alliance Building Corporation the overall master plan includes a significant facility expansion as well as a large number of patio home sites.
              <br/>
             </p>
