          <h4>
           <a href="http://bellairepublicschools.com/new-open-enrollmentschool-of-choice-requests/" title="">
            Open Enrollment/ School Of Choice Request 2018-19
           </a>
          </h4>
          <p>
           July 17, 2018
          </p>
          <div style="clear: both;">
          </div>
          <p>
           The priority at Bellaire Public Schools has always been to provide a quality education for our students. One component that truly aids in that goal is having a low student-to-teacher ratio as we believe that class size is a critical part of our children’s education. Therefore, during the 2018-19 school year, Bellaire Public Schools will not be participating in the school-of-choice program and will not be accepting any new open enrollment students.
          </p>
