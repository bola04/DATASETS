          Welcome to the new website for Derbyrun~Folklore Cavaliers, home of our beautiful Cavalier King Charles Spaniels. We are hobby breeders of Cavaliers and enjoy showing our dogs in the AKC. Our dogs are tested by veterinary specialists for health issues affecting the breed before being considered for breeding. Our Cavaliers are first and foremost beloved members of our families. Puppies are whelped in our homes and raised underfoot. Our goal is to make a contribution to the breed, striving for healthy, typey Cavaliers with the happy loving temperament that is the Hallmark of this little dog we love so much.
