He is the person other dealers contact for an opinion or to whom they will sell coins. Because of the growth of his business, Mike is expanding from his current status of acting primarily as an intermediary between dealers to establishing a direct method for the general public to market their coins. By dealing directly with Mike, you will be able to benefit not only from his experience but you will very possibly realize higher prices for your coins than would be the case with other dealers.
