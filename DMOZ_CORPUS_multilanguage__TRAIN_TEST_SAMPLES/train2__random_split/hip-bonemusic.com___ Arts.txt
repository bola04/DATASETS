          The History of Hip-BoneMusic Inc.
         </h1>
         <p class="simple_box_txt">
          Founded and created by Michael Davis and Shari Feder in 1994, Hip-BoneMusic has established itself as the preeminent source of high quality publications and recordings for brass players of every level. Dedicated to contemporizing and broadening the repertoire for brass ensembles and soloists, Hip-BoneMusic has developed a signature sound and style through the release of its CDs, books and compositions. Works from the Hip-Bone Music catalog are performed regularly throughout the world, including Australia, Japan, China, Taiwan, Korea, Russia, Norway, Finland, Sweden, Denmark, Holland, Italy, Spain, Portugal, France, Great Britain, Mexico, Peru, Argentina, Brazil, Chile, South Africa, Israel, the United States and Canada.
