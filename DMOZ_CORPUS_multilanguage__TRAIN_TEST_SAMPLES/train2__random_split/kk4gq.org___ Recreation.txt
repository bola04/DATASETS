         <h2 class="block-title">
          Monthly meetings
         </h2>
         <div class="content">
          <p>
           Meetings will now be at the First Methodist Church in Fayetteville on the LASTThursday of the month at 7:00PM beginning in February 2014. NOTE: this is a change for 2018. The church is in downtown Fayetteville adjacent to the clock tower. See
           <a href="http://kk4gq.org/node/13">
            Meetings
           </a>
           for more information.
          </p>
          <p>
           The monthy meetings of the FCARC are open to all who are interested in amateur radio.  You do not have to be licensed nor do you have to be a member of the FCARC.
          </p>
         </div>

        </div>

          Net
         </h2>
         <div class="content">
          <p>
           You are invited to check into the Tuesday night net on 145.210 (-) (131.8 hz tone) at 2100 local time. There are a number of other vhf and uhf repeaters that will be linked in.
          </p>

               <p>
                <img align="left" src="/images/arrl.gif" style="margin: 0px 20px"/>
                <br/>
                This is the home page for the Fayette County (GA) Amateur Radio Club  an active affiliate club of the American Radio Relay League (ARRL).   Our annual meeting takes place in January and is held at an area restaurant  to encourage families to attend.  Members are active in a number of specialized areas including Field Day, Contesting, Digital Modes, and EMCOMM to name a few.  Most of the activity in the Fayetteville area is on the 145.210 repeater (131.8 hz tone) so come join us. Join us on our mailing list, instructions

                or Read more
