          <h2 style="text-align: center;">
           Welcome to the Sea Breeze Inn at the
           <br/>

          </h2>
          <p>
           Sea Breeze Inn is a wonderfully modern and recently remodeled hotel in San Simeon, California. The Sea Breeze Inn offers affordable accommodations near the Hearst Castle in San Simeon, California. Each of our 26 rooms is uniquely decorated and we offer King size rooms with ocean views and rooms with two Queen beds with ocean views for your convenience.
          </p>
          <p align="left" class="bodyTextnewer">
           <a href="http://www.seabreezeinnsansimeon.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Room127_view2.jpg">
            <img alt="Sesbreeze Inn is located on the coast in San Simeon California near Cambria, Big Sur and Hearst Castle" class="alignnone wp-image-156 size-large" height="780" sizes="(max-width: 1024px) 100vw, 1024px" src="http://www.seabreezeinnsansimeon.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Room127_view2-1024x780.jpg" srcset="http://www.seabreezeinnsansimeon.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Room127_view2-1024x780.jpg 1024w, http://www.seabreezeinnsansimeon.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Room127_view2-300x228.jpg 300w, http://www.seabreezeinnsansimeon.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Room127_view2-393x300.jpg 393w" title="Sesbreeze Inn is located on the coast in San Simeon California near Cambria, Big Sur and Hearst Castle" width="1024"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p align="left" class="bodyTextnewer">
           Sea Breeze Inn is walking distance to the beach and has a swimming pool. San Simeon is also known as the southern gateway to Big Sur which offers some of the most spectacular scenery in the world and is rated one of the “Top 50 Places” to visit by National Geographic.
          </p>
          <p align="left" class="bodyTextnewer">
           <a href="http://www.seabreezeinnsansimeon.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Screen-shot-2014-10-14-at-4.14.48-PM.png">

           </a>
           <br/>
           Sea Breeze Inn is located on Highway One near several restaurants and just down the street from the San Simeon Chamber of Commerce visitors center. At the visitors center you will be able to get more information on what to do while visiting San Simeon.
          </p>

           </a>
           VISIT – Hearst Castle and the National Geographic Theater, Local Wineries, State Parks, Art Galleries in nearby Cambria. San Simeon is often called the Southern Gateway to the world famous Big Sur coastline.
          </p>
          <p>
           RELAX in the sun, on the shore, by the pool. Beach barbecue, picnic… Bring your sketch pads and paint!
          </p>
          <p>
           WATER SPORTS – Ocean Kayaking, Ocean Fishing, Deep Sea Fishing, Fishing from San Simeon Pier, Surfing, Wind Surfing, Beach Combing, Elephant Seal Viewing, Whale Watching.
          </p>
