           Welcome
          </h1>
         </header>

         <div class="entry-content">
          <p>
           <b>
            Who are Wasp Flight Systems ?
           </b>
           <a href="http://waspsystems.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Kent-estuary.jpg">
            <img alt="Kent estuary" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-132" height="354" src="http://waspsystems.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Kent-estuary-1024x766.jpg" width="474"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Wasp Flight Systems is a Lake District (UK) based company which has been designing, manufacturing and supporting the growth of foot launched powered hang gliding since 1998. We have a wealth of hang gliding experience dating back to the late seventies and understand the needs of the pilots we serve.
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            <i>
             Our underpinning philosophy is one of quality, reliability, complete after sales support and continued product development.
            </i>
           </b>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://waspsystems.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/harness.jpg">
            <img alt="harness" class="alignnone wp-image-80" height="99" src="http://waspsystems.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/harness-300x99.jpg" width="546§1"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            NEW   Try before you buy!
           </b>
           <br/>
           <i>
            Yes try before you buy, come and see what you’re missing!
           </i>
           . Have a day getting converted onto the wasp flying in the stunning lake district. Ground and air to air radio contact at all times ensures a safe way of converting to powered hang gliding. Get all that flying kit and yourself where it belongs get airborne!!
           <a href="http://waspsystems.co.uk/contact/" title="Contact">
            Call for more details
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            <i>
             WASP- SP MK4 Powered Hang Gliding Harness
            </i>
           </b>
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            <i>
             now powered by Radne Racket 120cc or for the heavy pilot and those who want to get higher faster Vittorazi EVO 100cc
            </i>
           </b>
          </p>
          <p>
           It is as a result of constantly striving to achieve the best that we now offer the
           <b>
            Wasp Venom
           </b>
           powered harness fitted with Italian
           <b>
           </b>
           <a href="http://waspsystems.co.uk/spec.html">
            <b>
             Vittorazi fly 100 Evo engine
            </b>
           </a>
           <br/>
           (18 HP  58kgs thrust)  aimed at the larger pilot but with two frame sizes so can be used by the lighter pilot who just wants the extra performance
           <a href="http://waspsystems.co.uk/technical-specifications/" title="Technical Specifications">
            <i>
             see specifications
            </i>
           </a>
           for full info.
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            Tel: 01539 822 848
