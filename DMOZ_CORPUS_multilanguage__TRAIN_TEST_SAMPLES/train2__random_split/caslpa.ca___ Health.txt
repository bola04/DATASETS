             <h2 class="pane-title">
              Speech-Language &amp; Audiology Canada
             </h2>
             <div class="pane-content">
              <article class="node-161 node node-page view-mode-full clearfix">
               <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
                <div class="field-items">
                 <div class="field-item even">
                  <p>
                   <!--StartFragment-->
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   We are a membership-driven organization that
                   <em>
                    <strong>
                     supports
                    </strong>
                   </em>
                   ,
                   <em>
                    <strong>
                     promotes
                    </strong>
                   </em>
                   and
                   <em>
                    <strong>
                     elevates
                    </strong>
                   </em>
                   the professions of our members and associates. We are the only national organization passionately supporting and representing speech-language pathologists, audiologists and communication health assistants inclusively. Through this support, we champion the needs of people with communication disorders.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   <!--StartFragment-->
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   Our members and associates are why we exist. When you’re an SAC member or associate, you can focus on your work knowing that we are supporting and representing you, every day.
                   <br/>
                   <strong>
                    <a href="/about-sac">
                     Learn more.
                    </a>
                   </strong>
                  </p>
