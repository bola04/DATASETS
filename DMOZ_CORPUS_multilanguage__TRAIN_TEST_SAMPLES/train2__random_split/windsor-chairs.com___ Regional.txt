 			  accurate reproduction of documented pieces from the Golden Age of Windsor
 			  Furniture 1740-1800
                </big>
                <br/>
                <p>
                 <big>
                  * Each Windsor chair, settee, bench, table, fire screen or candle stand
 			  , as well as any other piece that comes out of his shop, is handmade one
 			  at a time by this critically acclaimed furniture maker using only historically
 			  accurate construction methods.
                 </big>
                 <br/>
                </p>
                <p>
                 <big>
                  * With today's interest in using resources wisely and selecting products
 			  that will last rather than be disposable, the Windsor is more than ever the
 			  ideal choice. It is made from sustainable domestic woods using mainly
 			  people-powered hand tools and promises to remain in style as it already has
 			  for more than 200 years.
                 </big>
                 <br/>
                </p>
                <p>
                 <big>
                  * Each comb-back , continuous-arm , sack-back, settee, writing armchair,
