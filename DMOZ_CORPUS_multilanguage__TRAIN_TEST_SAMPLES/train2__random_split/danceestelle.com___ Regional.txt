     <p>

      <strong>
       <a href="estelle-clifton.html">
        Estelle Clifton
       </a>
      </strong>
      is East Anglia’s most successful choreographer and teacher, personally teaching the exciting and dynamic classes. Estelle’s aim is to mentally and physically prepare a student from a young age ready to further their training and achieve their goal in show business.
      <br/>
      <strong>
       DANCE ESTELLE
      </strong>
      has been running since September 2003. Since opening, Dance Estelle has had many students that have gone on to professional Dance Colleges to further their career in Show business.
      <br/>
      Numerous students have appeared alongside international professional artistes in Peter Jay’s Christmas and Water Spectacular and because of the high standard they achieve remain to be invited back each year.
      <br/>
      Our Dance Estelle end of term show is performed every July at the Hippodrome Circus. They show many different styles of dance from tap, ballet, gym, modern, lyrical, street and commercial jazz. The end of term performance shows the improved ability of the students to their fullest potential.

     Privacy Policy : Data Protection 
 As your child will be involved in many shows it is regulation that parents 
 consent is needed for photographs and video images to be taken of your child. 
 This consent will enable them to be in promotional photographs and videos 
 used for the press, displays &amp; social media advertising.  
 As part of the new GDPR Regulations &amp; Mutural Sharing Data Agreement I am required to have your permission to control your personal data. Each one of the students in the school have a record with their contact &amp; medical details. Therefore, if I’m unable to run a class for any unforeseen circumstances (e.g. weather conditions) or if something happens to your child, I am able to contact parents &amp; guardians.
  To comply with data protection, we must ask your permission to store &amp; process your personal data. All data is locked away &amp; only staff have access to these files (Teachers, Assistants, Matrons/Chaperones, Costume Suppliers, Costumer Makers, Costume Designer, Office staff at the Hippodrome Circus &amp; Dance Organisations for any outside competitions or events).
