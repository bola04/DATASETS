            </h2>
            <div class="pane-content">
             <p>
              The
              <a href="/content/ceb">
               CEB
              </a>
              brings together the
              <a href="content/executive-heads">
               executive heads
              </a>
              of
              <a href="members">
               31 specialized organizations
              </a>
              to deliver as one at the global, regional and country levels. It is supported by two pillars:
             </p>
             <ul>
              <li>
               <a href="content/hlcp">
                High Level Committee on Programmes (HLCP)
               </a>
              </li>
              <li>
               <a href="content/hlcm">
                High Level Committee on Management (HLCM)
               </a>
              </li>
             </ul>
             <p>
              It is the prime instrument for strengthening the coordination role of UN inter-governmental bodies on
              <a href="/content/agenda">
               social, economic and related matters
              </a>
              .
             </p>
