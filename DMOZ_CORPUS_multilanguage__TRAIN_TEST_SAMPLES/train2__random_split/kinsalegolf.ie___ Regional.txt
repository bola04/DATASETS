           <img alt="U14 Interpros" class="alignright wp-image-1013 size-thumbnail" height="150" src="http://d2tbfnbweol72x.cloudfront.net/wp-content/blogs.dir/5735/files/2016/04/U14-Interpros-150x150.jpg" width="150"/>

         <div>
          <br/>
          <span>
           Eoghan has been in great form, as he backed up his win in the Connacht U14 Championship with a third place finish at the Leinster U14 Championship this week.
          </span>
         </div>
         <div>
          <hr/>
          <a href="http://d2tbfnbweol72x.cloudfront.net/wp-content/blogs.dir/5735/files/2016/04/Kinsale-AIG-Jimmy-Bruen-Shield-2018.jpg" target="_blank">
           <img alt="Kinsale AIG Jimmy Bruen Shield 2018" class="alignleft wp-image-1016 size-thumbnail" height="150" src="http://d2tbfnbweol72x.cloudfront.net/wp-content/blogs.dir/5735/files/2016/04/Kinsale-AIG-Jimmy-Bruen-Shield-2018-150x150.jpg" width="150"/>
          </a>
          It was an incredible day at Dundrum House as our Jimmy Bruen Shield team bought home our first national pennant.
          <br/>
          <br/>
         </div>
         <div>
          Kinsale beat West Waterford in the morning and then defeated Nenagh in the afternoon. Both matches went to the 18th hole with Kinsale 1 up, and the match was closed out to secure a historic win. The All Ireland Finals will be held in Thurles 4th to 7th October.
         </div>
         <div>
          <hr/>
         </div>
         <div>
          <div>
           <span>

           </span>
           <a href="https://www.aidanstaffordphotography.com/Pro-Shop-Challenge-111/n-XBZHPG/" target="_blank">

           </a>
           <span>
           </span>

           <br/>
           <span>
            It was a fantastic day, with incredible golf seen. The Amateurs won on the day, but the real winners were the deserving causes. With a little push the amount raised could break the €5,000 – and that would be an incredible achievement. On behalf of Ger, Ian, Cathal and John I would like to thank all those who donated, the sponsors, the caddies and spectators for making the Challenge such a massive success.
           </span>

           <hr/>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Course Improvement Plan
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            Work on the course, as detailed in the development plan, continues as the weather permits. New bunkering on the 6th, 7th and 16th have made a terrific improvement, and will be well tested this coming season.
           </p>
           <p>
            The comprehensive improvement plan has been submitted by Ken Kearney Golf Design. The plan is located on the
            <a href="https://www.kinsalegolf.ie/club-docs/">
             Club Documents
            </a>
            page.
           </p>
           <hr/>
           <p>
            Kinsale Golf Club has a long and distinguished history and can trace its origins back to 1880.
           </p>
           <div>
            <p>
             It is situated 5 kilometers from the Historic town of Kinsale and is just over 20 kilometers from Cork City. The course is in a rural setting and has stunning views of the surrounding countryside. It measures 6,935 yards and consists of two nine hole loops finishing at the Clubhouse. The Club has played host to major tournaments including the All Ireland Finals of the Cups &amp; Shields in its Centenary year 2012. The Club has a full range of practice facilities including , 6 bay driving range, putting and chipping greens, Pro Shop and Catering facilities. Attractive Green Fee rates are available,
             <a href="https://www.kinsalegolf.ie/club/">
              click here for details
             </a>
             .
            </p>
            <p>
             Kinsale is the southernmost town on the Wild Atlantic Way. The town is known as the
             <a href="http://www.kinsale.ie" target="_blank">
              Gourmet Capital Of Ireland
             </a>
             with a large selection of restaurants, cafes, bars and hotels to suit every budget. There are many sandy beaches nearby with sailing and fishing well catered for. It is an ideal location for those planning a golfing holiday.
            </p>
