       <strong>
        ANCHOR INN
       </strong>
       on Nantucket is an historic inn located in the quaint cobblestoned whaling town of Nantucket on an island located off Cape Cod, Massachusetts.  We are located a few minutes walk  to  Nantucket's Main Street and also just a short walk to the beach at Brant Point Lighthouse and ferries. Situated in the Historic residential neighborhood, ANCHOR INN offers eleven guest rooms, all with private bathroom. There is a quaint breakfast porch, a garden patio and a common room.
      </p>
      <p class="pmain">
       Situated next to the Old North Church on Centre Street, the ANCHOR INN is the perfect location for your Nantucket vacation. Unlike larger Nantucket hotels, our charming inn retains a special character and scale true to the period of our historic Nantucket inn, that is now operated as a Bed &amp; Breakfast.
      </p>
      <p class="pmain">
       Our Nantucket community offers a wide range of experiences for everyone throughout the year. With conservation and historical presevation in the forefront, Nantucket is truly both an architectural and natural treasure mantaining a fragile balance 30 miles at sea.
