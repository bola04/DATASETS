      <h1>
       Welcome to the Brewery!
      </h1>
      <p>
       Welcome to the Brewery: a world-wide web server dedicated to providing
       a comprehensive, high quality, collection of information
       a bout the art and science of brewing beer.
      </p>
      <dl>
       <p>
       </p>
       <dt>
        <a href="cm3/CatsMeow3.html">
         The Cat's Meow 3
        </a>
       </dt>
       <dd>
        A collection of beer recipes, gleaned from past
           issues of Homebrew Digest, rec.crafts.brewing, the Mead Lovers Digest,
           the Cider Digest, and other Internet sources.
       </dd>
       <dt>
        <a href="Library.html">
         Library
        </a>
       </dt>
       <dd>
        An index of online technical articles about various aspects of
           brewing, ingredients, and beer styles.
       </dd>
       <dt>
        <a href="taproom/DBindex.html">
         Dave Brockington's Notes
        </a>
       </dt>
       <dd>
        David Brockington's notes on U.S. microbrewery beers. Excellent
           examples of the art of beer criticism.
       </dd>
       <dt>
        <a href="Software.html">
         Software
        </a>
       </dt>
       <dd>
        Large collection of homebrew-related software: shareware, public-domain,
           and commercial demo software.
       </dd>
       <dt>
        <a href="gambmug/gambmug.shtml">
         Gambrinus Mug
        </a>
       </dt>
       <dd>
        An interactive recipe database. Browse for recipes, or enter
           your personal favorite to share with other homebrewers.
       </dd>
       <dt>
        <a href="Links.html">
         Links
        </a>
       </dt>
       <dd>
        Pointers to related web sites, FTP archives, newsgroups,
           FAQs, and related resources.
       </dd>
       <dt>
        <a href="TastingCont.html">
         Tap Room
        </a>
       </dt>
       <dd>
        Information about tasting, evaluating, and judging beers. Includes
 	  style information and study materials for BJCP judges.
       </dd>
       <dt>
        <a href="MHall.html">
         Mead Hall
        </a>
       </dt>
       <dd>
        Information, recipes, software, and other resources for mead makers.
       </dd>
       <dt>
        <a href="http://hbd.org/hbd/HBD.html">
         Homebrew Digest
        </a>
       </dt>
       <dd>
        Interactive version of current and past Homebrew
 	  Digest issues---includes search engine and hypertext links.
       </dd>
       <dt>
        <a href="wwwboard">
         Brews and Views; the Discussion Board
        </a>
       </dt>
       <dd>
        Post questions and answers to homebrew-related topics. Bulletin
 	  board.
