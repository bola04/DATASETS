     <p>
      <img align="left" height="220" src="Images/oscarblue.gif" width="183"/>
      Little Rock Dog Training Club has been helping dog owners to have a better “best friend” since 1950.  An American Kennel Club-licensed club, we have experienced instructors, a friendly atmosphere, and quality facilities.  We train you to train your dog.
     </p>
     <p>
      All dogs are welcome, purebred or mixed breed.  Classes are offered in puppy kindergarten, obedience, agility, Rally, and conformation, all using positive reinforcement techniques.
      <img align="right" alt="dog jumping" height="199" src="Images/jump.gif" width="184"/>
      From home manners to advanced competition, our volunteer instructors share knowledge that makes training enjoyable for both humans and canines.
     </p>
     <p>
      LRDTC hosts annual AKC-licensed obedience and Rally trials in March and two annual agility trial weekends in April and November.  The club is active in community events, supports dog-related charities, and hosts matches and “fun days.”
      <img align="left" height="204" src="Images/willie.gif" width="210"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      Our goal is to help you to make your dog a more valued member of your family and a well-mannered canine member of the community.  Training will bring more fun into your relationship.  Come join us!
     </p>
