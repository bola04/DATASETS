              <p style="text-align: justify;">
               Archetype Central offers an architectural design and planning service. Specialising predominantly in residential design, the practice’s portfolio spans a wide range of development:
              </p>
              <ul>
               <li style="text-align: justify;">

               </li>

               </li>

               The practice is able to offer design solutions from the traditional to the contemporary. Ultimately, you are in control of the process and we will listen to you, taking on board your preferences. The services that we offer are divided into clear stages, enabling you – the client – the opportunity to pick and choose the level of service appropriate to your needs and budget.
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: justify;">
               The amount of involvement that you have in the project is entirely down to you. We will listen carefully to your needs and translate them into a design that fully meets your requirements.

               The practice offers services on a sub-contract basis to:
