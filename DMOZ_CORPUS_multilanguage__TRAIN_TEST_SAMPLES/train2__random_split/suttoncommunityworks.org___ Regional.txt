         </div>
         <p>
          Community Works started as an informal gathering of Sutton &amp; Cheam churches as part of Soul in the City in 2004. In 2010 Sutton Community Works was established as a charity to oversee the Foodbank which started in 2009 and Sutton Street Pastors which was initiated independently of Community Works in 2005. Sutton Community Works has grown into a charity which operates six projects.
         </p>
         <p>
          Our aim is to encourage churches in the borough to put their faith into action through larger projects that respond to local and borough wide needs.
