         <h3 class="section-title section-title--white">
          Overview
         </h3>
         <div class="section-subheading">
         </div>
         <div class="section-text">
          <p>
           Do you run or dream of running your own small business, or managing an existing business?
          </p>
          <p>
           Our
           <strong>
            New Zealand Certificates in Business (Level 3 and Level 4) and New Zealand Diploma in Business (Management and Leadership) (Level 5)
           </strong>
           give you the freedom to study from anywhere in New Zealand.
          </p>
          <p>
           Our programmes are New Zealand Qualifications Authority (NZQA) approved.
          </p>
          <p>
           The Business Management Programmes have been designed to be comprehensive introductions to small business management - for those who are either wanting to start/run their own business, move into a supervisory or entry-level management position, or for those in a management position wanting to formalise their experience.
          </p>
          <p>
           The Level 3 is primarily designed for those with either limited or no prior workplace experience; and is focused around developing and understanding feasibility for a new business.
          </p>
          <p>
           Our Level 4 Small Business Qualification is aimed at those with previous work experience, or having completed Level 3; and is focused on the management of a small business which is already established. It is suitable for those wanting to manage their own business, manage one for someone else, or to formalise experience.
          </p>
          <p>
           Level 5 builds on prior learning, or experience, for those wanting to move into management or leadership positions. This programme can also lead onto degree level education in management.
           <br/>
          </p>
          <p>
           For further information about the content of each qualification, please read the prospectus below.
          </p>
