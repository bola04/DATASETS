                   </a>
                  </span>
                 </div>
                 <div class="views-field views-field-body">
                  <div class="field-content">
                   <p>
                    The 2018 ICSA Applied Statistical Symposium was held from June 14 to 17, 2018 at Hyatt Regency New Brunswick, NJ. It had more than 600 attendees, 115 sessions (including keynote, invited and contributed sessions). We would like to thank Dr. Min-ge Xie, all the organizers and members of the program committee, local arrangement committee, the volunteers, and sponsors for their time, efforts and contributions. Everyone enjoyed the Symposium very much. The next ICSA Applied Statistics Symposium will be held June 9-12, 2019 in Raleigh Convention Center in Raleigh, NC.
