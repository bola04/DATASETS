            <br/>
            <font face="Arial Unicode MS">
             <font size="4">
              W
             </font>
             <b>
              e are a Professional
                     Membership Association
             </b>
             of commissioned civil law notary
                     publics, who've joined together to promote continuing
                     education and ethics in all acts where a notary public is
                     involved.   Louisiana is unique in that our courts
                     are based upon Civil Law, rather than the Common Law
                     that can be found in every other state in the union.
             <span class="solutionsbody">
              The Louisiana Civil Law Notary
                     can draft, prepare and execute affidavits, acknowledgements
                     and authentic acts.  Louisiana Civil Law provides for much
                     of our legal documentation to be passed by  a notary, a
                     public official who has qualified for a commission
                     authorizing those powers.
             </span>
            </font>
           </p>
           <p align="justify">
            <font face="Arial Unicode MS">
             <span class="solutionsbody">
              Using an experienced Notary can
                     be an extremely cost effective and convenient alternative
                     for legal document preparation.  The Louisiana Civil Law
                     Notary has broad powers usually reserved for attorneys in
                     other states.  If you need a NOTARY PUBLIC, you've come to
                     the right place.  Our member's directory and the search
                     features are unique tools to help you locate a Notary near
                     you.
             </span>
            </font>
           </p>

                  <p align="justify">
                   Welcome to the Professional Civil Law Notaries Association (PCLNA) web site.  I am honored to have the opportunity to serve the Association this year and look forward to serving both the membership and whatever your need is for visiting our site.
                  </p>
                  <p align="justify">
                   The goal, at the formation of PCLNA, was and continues to be that of promoting and maintaining the highest degree of knowledge and ethical standards in the practice of Notary Public.  We strive to promote honesty, professionalism, and ethics within our industry and membership.
                  </p>
                  <p align="justify">
                   When you choose a member of the PCLNA as your notary, you are choosing a professional who has made the effort to maintain their education and stay up-to-date with the changing laws; and, has agreed to adhere to a code of ethics that sets them apart as a professional.
                  </p>
                  <p align="justify">
                   If you are a notary public, I invite you to join the PCLNA. The network of practicing notaries and the continuing education programs are only two of the many benefits of membership.
                  </p>
