       Rockford Separators
      </h1>
      <div class="region region-content">
       <section class="block block-system clearfix" id="block-system-main">
        <article about="/content/rockford-separators" class="node node-home-page clearfix" id="node-556" typeof="sioc:Item foaf:Document">
         <div class="home-content">
          <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
           <div class="field-items">
            <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
             <p class="body-subheader">
              Architects, engineers, building officials, health agencies, plumbing contractors, and other specifiers have come to rely on Rockford for the utmost in quality, precision engineering and quick turnaround time.
             </p>
             <p class="normal">
             </p>
             <p class="normal">
              Rockford Separators are used in separating and retaining numerous wastes: grease, oil, fuel, fats, lint, hair, soap residue, food solids, chemicals, sediment, solid waste, sand, gravel, sludge, plaster, precious metals, glass chips and grindings, fibrous materials, metal chips, and waxes.
             </p>
             <p class="normal">
              With separation as its sole business, Rockford Separators has supplied quality separation systems to the plumbing industry for over 40 years. More than 35 varieties in over 600 available sizes are engineered and custom-built in the USA for job-site specific applications and installed wherever separation systems are required.
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="color:#000000">
               <strong>
                THESE INCLUDE:
               </strong>
              </span>
             </p>
             <ul>
              <li>
               Industrial Plants
              </li>
              <li>
               Office Buildings
              </li>
              <li>
               Multi-Unit Apartments
              </li>
              <li>
               Restaurants
              </li>
              <li>
               Food-Processing Plants
              </li>
              <li>
               Laundromats
              </li>
              <li>
               Health Centers
              </li>
              <li>
               Retail Service Centers
              </li>
             </ul>
