                  <h2 class="entry-title">
                   <a href="http://townofnarrows.org/sanitary-sewer-smoke-testing/">
                    Sanitary Sewer Smoke Testing
                   </a>
                  </h2>
                  <div class="post-content">
                   <p>
                    PUBLIC NOTICE
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    <strong>
                     THE TOWN OF NARROWS
                    </strong>
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    <strong>
                     SANITARY SEWER SMOKE TESTING
                    </strong>
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    July 13, 2018
                   </p>
                   <p>
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    Please be advised that the Town of Narrows will be conducting a physical survey of the sanitary sewer system beginning on July 23
                    <sup>
                     rd
                    </sup>
                    2018 and continuing for the next six (6) to twelve (12) months. This study will involve the opening of manholes in the streets and easements and SMOKE TESTING of sewer lines to locate breaks and defects in the sewer system.
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    The smoke that you may see coming from vent pipes on houses or holes in the ground is
                    <strong>
                     NONTOXIC, HARMLESS TO PLANTS AND ANIMALS, IS NONSTAINING, HAS NO ODOR, AND CREATES NO FIRE HAZARD.
                    </strong>
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    The smoke should not enter your home unless you have defective plumbing or dried up drain traps. Correction of any defects on private property is the responsibility of the property owner.
                    <strong>
                     Please pour about a quart of water into any seldom used floor drains or sinks to fill the trap to prevent sewer gas or odor from entering the building.
                    </strong>
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    The information gained from this study will be used to improve your sewer service and may reduce the eventual cost to users.
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    If you need additional information, you may contact the Narrows Town Office at 540-726-2423.
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    Sincerely,
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    James “Buddy” Kast
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    Public Works Director
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    Town of Narrows
                   </p>
                  </div>
                 </article>
