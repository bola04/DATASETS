      <h1>
       WELCOME!
      </h1>
      <p align="left" padding-left:="padding-left:">
       Medical Center Laboratory has made a commitment to offer the local medical community the best possible laboratory service for physicians.  To achieve this, we are setting the highest standards in reliability, service and quality.  Compare our standards with your current referral laboratory and we think you will agree that Medical Center Laboratory has a commanding advantage.
      </p>
      <p align="left">
       <span class="style13">
        QUALITY FIRST
       </span>
       - This commitment to quality stems from its founding and direction by our pathologists.  These physicians are inherently aware of the need for precision and accuracy in laboratory testing.  Stringent guidelines have been developed for all laboratory procedures.
      </p>
      <p align="left">
       <span class="style13">
        HIGH CALIBER PERSONNEL
       </span>
       - Strict criteria are used in the hiring of all 
         
       personnel.  Each section has a pathologist assigned   and 
         
       technologists are ASCP registered.  Laboratory procedures and results are continuously monitored by our pathologists.
      </p>
