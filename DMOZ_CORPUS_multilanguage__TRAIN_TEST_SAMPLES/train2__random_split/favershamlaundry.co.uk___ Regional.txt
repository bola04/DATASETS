         <h1>
          Faversham Linen Services
         </h1>
         <p class="lead">
          Founded in 1897 as Faversham Sanitary Steam Laundry Company Limited Faversham Linen Services has provided a premium laundry service for well over 100 years to our customers in the Kent, Sussex Surrey and South London areas.
         </p>
         <div class="contentArea1">
          <div class="row">
           <div class="col-sm-6">
            <p>
             Operating from our new 28,000 ft² state of the art factory in Faversham we are proud to remain an independant  family owned company offering the very best in commercial linen and workwear services to local businesses as well as to some of the biggest names in the leisure, hospitality, manufacturing and retail sectors.
            </p>
           </div>
           <div class="col-sm-6">
            <p>
             <img alt="Faversham Linen Services" src="uploads/content/images/IMG_2531.JPG"/>
            </p>
           </div>
          </div>
         </div>
         <h2>
          Our simple and straightforward mission and guiding principles:
         </h2>
         <ul>
          <li>
           To be recognised as the premier Commercial Laundry Service provider in the South East
