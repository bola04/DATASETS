                <h2>
                 <strong>
                  Magnolia Paper
                 </strong>
                 is a handmade paper studio and research laboratory founded 1981 in Oakland, CA.
                </h2>
                <p>
                 From traditional Renaissance and Japanese papermaking methods to contemporary discoveries, Magnolia Paper continues to develop new possibilities for papermakers and conservators.
                </p>

              </div>
