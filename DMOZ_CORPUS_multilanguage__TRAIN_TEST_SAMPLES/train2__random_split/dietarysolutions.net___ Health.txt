          </a>
         </h1>
         <div class="post-excerpt">
          <p>
           Dietary Solutions is the premier dietary consulting company in Ohio, Indiana, and Michigan. Founded in 1994, Dietary Solutions has over 25 years of proven success, providing services in long-term care and assisted living facilities as well as group homes. We also partner with acute care hospitals, home health agencies, schools, and mental health and addiction facilities.
          </p>

           Our team of registered dietitians and dietitian technicians provides unparalleled care and expertise, with the majority of our staff holding advanced degrees and specialized credentials in gerontology, nutrition support, and school nutrition.
