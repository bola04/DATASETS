       <div class="modal-body">
        <b>
         Istanbul Vip Online Booking System
        </b>
        <br/>
        For the agencies, the process of booking accommodation for a holiday or business trip might be a complicated one, which takes up a great deal of time. Every destination has numerous hotel options, each of which offers varying quality, pricing, facilities and availability. And it’s this very diversity which makes it so tough to find just what you’re looking for quickly and efficiently.
        <br/>
        The Istanbul Vip APIs has been created bearing in mind the challenges that small, medium and big sized travel agents face when selecting hotels for their customers. Not only should the interface be a user friendly one, but all the information including pricing and availability, must be presented in real time.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <b>
         Istanbul Vip Tourism – XML/API
        </b>
        <br/>
        The Istanbul Vip XML API enables online travel agencies and travel suppliers access to Istanbul Vip vast global travel content via XML interface for integration into their reservation system or website. The Istanbul Vip XML/API technology is exceptional, offering the very best speed, performance and hotel choice for agents.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <b>
         Preferred Currency
        </b>
        <br/>
        You can view results in your preferred currency. Also mitigate your currency exchange risk and reduce your accounting workload by paying in currency of your choice for worldwide hotel bookings.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <b>
         Instant confirmation without waiting!
        </b>
        <br/>
        With Istanbul Vip there's no waiting because we display only the available hotels. The booking is confirmed instantly.
        <br/>
        <br/>

         24/7 support
