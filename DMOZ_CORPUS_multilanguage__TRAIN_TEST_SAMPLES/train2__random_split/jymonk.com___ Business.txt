         <article class="navigation-block">
          <h3 class="">
           Broker Prelicensing Courses
          </h3>
          <p>
           The
           <em>
            Broker Prelicensing Course (75 hours)
           </em>
           is your first step to obtaining a real estate license in North Carolina. Successful completion of this course will qualify and prepare you to sit for the State Licensing Exam, and to work for any real estate firm in North Carolina. This course includes complimentary enrollment in our Live
           <em>
            Exam Prep Course
           </em>
           , designed to fully prepare you for success on the N.C. Real Estate Licensing Exam.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a class=" btn btn-pms-188 btn-block" href="https://www.jymonk.com/broker-prelicensing/courses/">

          <h3 class="">
           Broker Postlicensing Courses
          </h3>
          <p>
           North Carolina real estate school students must complete the Broker Prelicensing Course successfully, pass the State Licensing Exam and actually hold a Broker License on “Provisional Status” before they are eligible to take the three 30 Hour Broker Postlicensing Courses. You must complete at least one of your 30-hour courses within the first year of licensure.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a class=" btn btn-pms-188 btn-block" href="https://www.jymonk.com/broker-postlicensing/courses/">

Last year we received more than 12,000 registrations for our GENUP, BICUP, and CE Elective Courses from the mountains to the coast of North Carolina. We offer live classroom and online electives.
