         </h1>

           <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
            <div class="field-items">
             <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
              <p>
               We are an internationally renowned equine veterinary practice servicing the greater Sydney region. We provide a 24 hour, 7 days a week emergency service for our clients and referring veterinarians. As well as the many experienced, purely equine veterinarians “on the road”, our state of the art hospital at Randwick is furnished with sophisticated diagnostic, medical and surgical equipment and our team includes multiple registered specialists in the fields of surgery, medicine and anaesthesia.
              </p>
              <p>
               For over 60 years we have been leading the field in equine veterinary medicine and surgery and our reputation is built on experience, expertise and providing excellent, committed and innovative care to all types of horses
