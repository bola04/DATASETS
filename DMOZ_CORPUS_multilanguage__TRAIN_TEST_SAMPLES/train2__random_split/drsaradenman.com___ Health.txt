        <h1>
         Welcome
        </h1>
        <p>
         As a licensed Psychologist, I provide
         <span class="boldwhite">
          compassionate therapy for change,
         </span>
         transitioning clients from merely surviving to thriving by helping them identify and remove obstacles that prevent them from reaching their full potential. This is achieved by looking for people's strengths and building upon those to overcome challenges.
        </p>
        <p>
         I work with adult clients (high school age and beyond) on a variety of issues that include, but are not limited to;
         <span class="boldwhite">
          divorce survival, life changes, trauma, anxiety, depression, addictions, eating disorders and self-esteem challenges.
         </span>
         I make every effort to exceed client's expectations for quality of care.
        </p>
        <p>
         Often
         <span class="boldwhite">
          people wait until they are in
          <span class="boldwhite">
           crisis
          </span>
          to look for a therapist.
         </span>
         I understand that and will work with you to get you started as soon as possible to help relieve the issues in the crisis. Entering into therapy can be intimidating and not high on the priority list unless there are no other alternatives. There are
         <span class="boldwhite">
          no issues too large or too small
         </span>
         to bring into the safe environment of my office.
        </p>
        <p>
         Each client is unique and special and it is an
         <span class="boldwhite">
          honor to watch a person reach his or her goals.
         </span>
         Although making the decision to seek therapy can be overwhelming, my technique, training and experience puts clients at ease and allows the process of therapy to feel more comfortable which enables goals to be met.
        </p>
