          </div>

          </div>

The music starts on June 3rd at Bold Rock cider in Nellysford, Virginia for an afternoon show from 1 to 4 on Sunday.  On Saturday, June 9th we will be up the road and at the top of the mountain at The Edge in Wintergreen from 8:30 until 12:30.  Next we are in downtown Charlottesville at The Whiskey Jar for a mid week performance on Wednesday, June 13th, from 6:30 to 9:30.  On Saturday, June 16th, join us at Brent Manor Vineyards for Wine Till Nine "Date Night" with Marshall Artz from 6 until 9.  Bring a blanket and lawn chairs and enjoy the excellent Brent Manor wines selection.  The last day of June (the 30th) Kevin Artz will be performing solo at Third Base in Occoquan, Virginia from 6 until 10 for an outdoor show. Get directions and details from our Shows tab.          <div class="entry floatwrapper odd">
          <div class="title heading">
           Mad March Winds Blowing Wild
          </div>
          <div class="timestamp">
           Mar  6, 2018
          </div>
          <div class="text">
           March winds blew strong and wild to bring in the month. Downed trees, flying debris, and extensive power outages challenged the day to day routines over the first weekend.  Good thing Marshall Artz plays Martin acoustic guitars.  The first thing to go to in a power outage is the guitar.  Next is to head to the wood pile and get a warm fire started to keep the cold that is blowing around and into the house at a manageable minimum.  Music in the warm fire glow with a libation in hand to assuage the powers that be is the magic combination for survival. Cheers to those that weathered the storm and came through with a smile on their weary faces! Nothing can conquer the indomitable will of a lover with a song in their heart to successfully chase adversity away.Come celebrate the calm after the storm with us at Wild Wolf Brewing in Nellysford, Virginia on Friday, March 9th for an evening of energetic acoustic music.  Two days before Daylight Savings time and a splendid time is guaranteed for all.!
           <br/>
           On Saturday night, March 10th, in Northern Virginia The Sometimes are debuting their outstanding new CD Release, Cicada Nation, at Fair Winds Brewing in Newington, Virginia.  Kevin Artz (the Artz of Marshall Artz) will be opening the show with a solo performance.  Join us for an evening of original music, good company, and great beer. No better way to ease into the new time change and Spring forward into the great unknown than with great music to inspire the journey.  See you there!
