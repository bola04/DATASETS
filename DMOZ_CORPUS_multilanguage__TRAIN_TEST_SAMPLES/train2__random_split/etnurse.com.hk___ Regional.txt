         </h1>

         <p>
          Welcome to the official website of Hong Kong Enterostomal Therapists Association. The Hong Kong Enterostomal Therapists Association Limited (香港造瘻治療師學會有限公司) , formerly the Hong Kong Council of Enterostomal Therapists, was formed in 2000. The association is made up of specially trained nurses and professional who have interests in stoma , wound and continence care.
