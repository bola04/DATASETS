 								  your income tax return. However,

                 </b>

 								  sightseeing to provide a well rounded 
 								  itinerary.

               <p>
                <span class="style4">
                 An Australian Farmers Tour offers more than 
 								    just travel as each farming tour is uniquely planned to 
 								    provide something special. A careful blend of agricultural 
 								    visits, sightseeing and free time is designed to 
 								    inform, fascinate and entertain. Our worldwide programme 
 								    of rural tours offers the chance to see how farming is 
 								    practised in every sort of climate and condition.
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 In addition all our tours provide a sense of fellowship 
 								    and a chance to meet other farm families from all 
 								    over Australia. Friendships formed on an Australian 
 								    Farmers Tour often last a lifetime.

                 <br/>
                 We value highly the loyalty of our tour members 
 								    and appreciate the fact that many of our members 
 								    continue to book with us year after year. We feel 
 								    this is a mark of the quality of our tours and we 
 								    will continue to strive to attain the high standards 
 								    that we set ourselves.
