         Why Not Integrate?
        </span>
        <p>
         Why not integrate your vacuum along with all your other major appliances? A built-in central vacuum eliminates the noise, smell, and inconvenience of portables. It can be used to clean any surface, any spill, anywhere using its powerful remote motors. Plus, central vacuums are proven to reduce allergies, and 99% of people that install one, say they will never use any other kind of vacuum. And with MD Central Vacuum, you can trust 55 years of made-in-the-USA durability, along with professional, independent dealers.
        </p>

        <span class="subheading4-page font-bold futura">
         Ditch Your Portable!
        </span>
        <p>
         We have all experienced it—hauling a vacuum cleaner around the house, kicking a canister vacuum across the room, and walking all the way back to untangle a cord. But even worse, finding ourselves back at the stores every couple years to purchase yet another vacuum. More and more homeowners are ditching costly portable vacuums in favor of central vacuums. Why? Central vacs cost as much as a high end portable vacuum, but last longer, provide more powerful, deep cleaning, and are significantly quieter. Central vacuum systems are easily installed in both new construction homes and existing homes, and with new technology and innovations, they are better than ever.
