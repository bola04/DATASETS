         <h2>
          <em>
           The Free and Open Source software industry is now a critical supplier to your organisation; there are no proprietary alternatives.
          </em>
         </h2>
         <p>
          We are a UK membership organisation with the purpose of supporting the growth of the Free (libre) &amp; Open Source supply chain.
         </p>
         <h3>
          Are you a Free &amp; Open Source service provider?
         </h3>
         <p>
          <a href="https://openuk.uk/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/compressed-20160131-180226.jpg">
           <img alt="FSFE hoodie" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-1449" height="168" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="https://openuk.uk/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/compressed-20160131-180226-300x168.jpg" srcset="https://openuk.uk/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/compressed-20160131-180226-300x168.jpg 300w, https://openuk.uk/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/compressed-20160131-180226-768x431.jpg 768w, https://openuk.uk/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/compressed-20160131-180226-1024x574.jpg 1024w" width="300"/>
          </a>
          Our members are professional free &amp; open source technology specialists who benefit from working together and using our organisation as a platform to communicate with organisations across both public and private sectors.
         </p>
         <p>
          If you are an organisation or business working with open source principles, OpenUK may be able to help. We can assist with business development opportunities, peer support and networking, and other affiliate benefits.
          <a href="https://openuk.uk/membership-information/">
           Why not join us?
          </a>
         </p>
         <h3>
          <strong>
           Looking to engage?
          </strong>
         </h3>
         <p>
          If you are interested in locating suppliers or services, you can locate a service provider in our
          <a href="https://openuk.uk/membership-information/members-directory/">
           member directory
          </a>
          or
          <a href="https://openuk.uk/contact-us/">
           get in touch with OpenUK
          </a>
          for anything else.
         </p>
         <h3>
          <strong>
           Just want to find out more about what we do?
          </strong>
         </h3>
         <p>
          Take a look at our
          <a href="https://openuk.uk/blog/">
           blog
          </a>
          which covers subject matter important to us.
         </p>
