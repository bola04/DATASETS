          <a href="http://www.canoeimports.com/benefits-renting-luxury-yacht/">
           Benefits of Renting a Luxury Yacht
          </a>
         </h2>
         <div class="m-f">
          <a class="fwit" href="http://www.canoeimports.com/benefits-renting-luxury-yacht/" style="background-image: url('http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/bg3.jpg')">

         <p>
          If you are thinking of going on a vacation to someplace where you can relax and enjoy the silence, then we strongly recommend that you try out renting a luxury yacht and going out on a trip. If you really want to enjoy the silence and the sun, then there is no better option than renting out a yacht and going anywhere you want. If you never experienced this type of vacation, then this is the right time to do it.
         </p>
         <p>
          You don’t have to be scared by a yacht, they are really easy to drive, and you will have no issues with it what so ever. It is like having a hotel room in the middle of the sea and you can jump in and take a swim at any point. If are still not convinced that this is the perfect vacation option, then continue reading because we want to make sure that you completely understand all of the benefits that you can and will have if you rent a luxury yacht.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Comfort
         </h2>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-61" height="228" sizes="(max-width: 458px) 100vw, 458px" src="http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/luxury-yacht-charter-master-1-458-228.jpg" srcset="http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/luxury-yacht-charter-master-1-458-228.jpg 458w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/luxury-yacht-charter-master-1-458-228-150x75.jpg 150w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/luxury-yacht-charter-master-1-458-228-300x149.jpg 300w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/luxury-yacht-charter-master-1-458-228-65x32.jpg 65w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/luxury-yacht-charter-master-1-458-228-320x159.jpg 320w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/luxury-yacht-charter-master-1-458-228-201x100.jpg 201w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/luxury-yacht-charter-master-1-458-228-241x120.jpg 241w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/luxury-yacht-charter-master-1-458-228-390x194.jpg 390w" width="458"/>
          If you want to spend your vacation by relaxing the entire time in a comfortable place, then a luxury yacht is a really good option for you. You just have to find the right
          <a href="http://LUXURYMIAMIYACHTRENTALS.COM">
           <strong>
            premium boat rental service company
           </strong>
          </a>
          that will give you the best service. There are a lot of rental places that offer yachts, but they are really not maintained well, and they cannot be considered as a luxury boat, you need to do some research and find the perfect one. If you find a really good rental company, then you will be able to enjoy fully in the comfort level of a luxury yacht.
         </p>
         <p>
          That type of comfort is really unique, manufacturers put a lot of focus on creating the perfect boat that can provide people anything they want. One of the most comfortable rides that you can ever get will be a luxury yacht. When you rent one, just remember that you don’t need to go fast in order to enjoy, yachts are made for quality time.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Unique Experience
         </h2>
         <p>
          Believe it or not, one of the major benefits that you can have from renting and going on a vacation with a luxury yacht is the unique experience that you will get. This type of experience cannot be compared with anything else in the world, just like flying with an airplane for the first time, it will be a unique experience that you will definitely never forget.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-63" height="515" sizes="(max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" src="http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/stern.jpg" srcset="http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/stern.jpg 1000w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/stern-150x77.jpg 150w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/stern-300x155.jpg 300w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/stern-768x396.jpg 768w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/stern-65x33.jpg 65w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/stern-320x165.jpg 320w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/stern-194x100.jpg 194w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/stern-233x120.jpg 233w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/stern-390x201.jpg 390w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/stern-777x400.jpg 777w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/stern-760x391.jpg 760w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/stern-640x330.jpg 640w" width="1000"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Many people who had previously rented luxury yachts are doing it again because they have discovered something truly unique. Of course, you need to keep your vacations different all the time in order to not get bored with one thing, but with luxury yacht we actually recommend renting them at least two or three times in a row because it is an experience that is always filled with new adventures and you never know what is coming for you next or what will you get to see.
         </p>

         </h2>
         <div class="m-f">

Renting boats is relatively straightforward, but that doesn’t mean that the number of options, when it comes to renting, is small. How this works is the subject of this post.

         <h2>
          Bareboat charter – How does it work?
         </h2>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignright size-full wp-image-30" height="188" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/1.jpg" srcset="http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/1.jpg 300w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/1-150x94.jpg 150w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/1-65x41.jpg 65w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/1-160x100.jpg 160w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/1-191x120.jpg 191w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/1-271x170.jpg 271w" width="300"/>
          A bareboat charter is a rental of a yacht where you take the full responsibility for the vessel. This means that there is no crew to pilot the ship. You or someone from your group must have the experience as well as papers that show that you know how to run it. If you rent a boat with sails, then the whole group has to pitch in and help. The other option is to rent a vessel without sails, and that will allow you to pilot it alone, without any help from other people.
         </p>
         <p>
          There is an option to hire a skipper if you don’t want to pilot the boat or no one from your group has any knowledge to steer it. The skipper will take over the responsibility and navigate it according to your desire. If the boat in question is the yacht with sails, then you will have to pitch in and help. If the ship doesn’t have sails, then you can lay back and relax while the skipper does everything.
         </p>
         <h2>
          The luxury of crewed yachts
         </h2>
         <p>
          If you want to sail the seas and still enjoy the luxury of a high-quality hotel, then you should hire a crewed yacht. The level of luxury you want will depend on the amount of money you are willing to spend on a cruise. We aren’t talking about large yachts that can accommodate hundreds, but small boats you hire that will accommodate you and a selected few.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-29" height="521" sizes="(max-width: 927px) 100vw, 927px" src="http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/2.jpg" srcset="http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/2.jpg 927w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/2-150x84.jpg 150w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/2-300x170.jpg 300w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/2-768x432.jpg 768w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/2-65x37.jpg 65w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/2-320x180.jpg 320w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/2-178x100.jpg 178w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/2-214x120.jpg 214w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/2-390x219.jpg 390w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/2-712x400.jpg 712w" width="927"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          If you want a real ship experience, then you can hire a boat with sails and sit in the back while the crew works on it. This allows you to see how people run boats hundreds of years ago. You can pitch in and help if you desire to experience it firsthand.
         </p>
         <p>
          The best way to enjoy the sea and the luxury is to hire motor yachts with a full crew. In this case, you can just lay back and enjoy while team runs the ship and prepares everything and that includes drinks and food. The crew on this type of boat is smaller than the crew on yachts with sails, but you will still have to pay a lot of money for this kind of experience, and it will be worth every dollar you spend.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>

         <h2 class="entry-title">

         <p>
          Yes, you can take a tour on the sea in a standard boat and have an excellent time, but that won’t be as fun or exciting as renting a yacht. There is something special about yachts, and it’s not all about the luxury.
         </p>
         <p>
          Yachts are fast and maneuverable, and that means you will arrive at the goal in a short time frame. They are also comfortable, and you won’t have to endure the elements while you travel on the sea. Some bigger yachts can also have smaller motor boats that you can take out to explore smaller islands and all secrets they hide.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Enjoy a tour with a hand-selected group
         </h2>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-19 alignleft" height="225" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/Searay-Sundancer.jpg" srcset="http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/Searay-Sundancer.jpg 300w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/Searay-Sundancer-150x113.jpg 150w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/Searay-Sundancer-65x49.jpg 65w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/Searay-Sundancer-133x100.jpg 133w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/Searay-Sundancer-160x120.jpg 160w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/Searay-Sundancer-227x170.jpg 227w" width="300"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          If you want to go on a vacation alone or with a small group, then you should hire a small yacht and save some money. Our fleet is enormous, and we have quite a variety when it comes to smaller ships (from 48 to 79-foot vessels).
         </p>
         <p>
          The smallest ship in our fleet is the Searay Sundancer which is a 48-foot long yacht that can accommodate up to four people. Even though it is small, this yacht has a jetski and other stuff for water sports. You can rent it for a pocket change for one day or day and night.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignright size-full wp-image-18" height="200" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/Sunseeker-Predator.jpg" srcset="http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/Sunseeker-Predator.jpg 300w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/Sunseeker-Predator-150x100.jpg 150w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/Sunseeker-Predator-65x43.jpg 65w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/Sunseeker-Predator-180x120.jpg 180w, http://www.canoeimports.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/Sunseeker-Predator-255x170.jpg 255w" width="300"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Princess Flybridge is one of several 65 feet yachts we rent. This one is packed with three heads as well as three staterooms and a full galley. You can relax in a massive air conditioned salon or enjoy in the sea on Jet Ski. This ship can accommodate up to six people for a cruise that can last up to three days.
         </p>
         <p>
          Sunseeker Predator is a 75-foot long yacht that is perfect for large groups of individuals. Only six people can spend the night on the boat, but it can accommodate up to twelve people during the cruise. It has a large deck where you can soak up sun rays, and it comes with several items for water sports that include Jet Ski. Gather your friends and embark on an adventure with Sunseeker Predator.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Large yachts you can rent from us
         </h2>
         <p>
          If you want to rent a boat for a greater number of people, then we have something for you. At
          <strong>
           <a href="http://CharterMiamiBeach.com">
            Miami Beach private yacht rental
           </a>
          </strong>
          You can rent a yacht or go on our official web site that will be linked on the main page. This, of course, includes the rental of Azimuth Open, which is a medium sized yacht with a length of 86 feet. It can accommodate up to eight people (overnight stay) and twelve people for a day-long party and enjoyment.

          But if you want a big yacht then there are, but a handful of larger boats than a Ferretti Flybridge, a 94-foot long boat that is quite expensive to rent but it contains features that smaller yachts can’t have due to the size. This monster of a ship has four staterooms and the same number of enclosed heads. It sports audio video integration on all decks, ice maker, grill and many more things that will ensure a satisfactory vacation.
         </p>
