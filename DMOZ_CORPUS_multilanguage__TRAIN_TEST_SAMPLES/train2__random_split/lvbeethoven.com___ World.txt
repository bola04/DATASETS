    <strong>
     Beethoven, Ludwig van 1770-1827
    </strong>
   </p>
   <p>
    Ludwig van Beethoven started his musical career at an early age and although he was deaf he became known as the most famous and influential composers in the world of classical music. LvBeethoven.com provides a look into the music and life of the musical composer, Ludwig van Beethoven. At the Ludwig van Beethoven website, you can read Beethoven’s biography, find books on Beethoven, view the family trees of Ludwig Beethoven, listen to his music and much more. Fans of Ludvig van Beethoven can talk to other fan and learn more by participating in the discussion forums as well.
   </p>

     Music is a higher revelation than all wisdom and philosophy ~
