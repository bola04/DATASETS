                 <strong>
                  ISO 14001 Environmental compliant
                 </strong>
                </h3>
                <p class="text-align-center">
                 In addition to our environmental compliance, we are voluntary members of the Woodland Trust’s carbon capture scheme – Woodland Carbon. When we purchase FSC papers from our suppliers, a proportion of the cost is calculated in relation to the amount of CO2 generated in the production and delivery of the paper. These additional costs are charged to us, collected by the supplier and paid directly to the Woodland Trust, enabling them to plant native woodlands and restore woodland habitats right here in the UK.
                </p>

                <p class="text-align-center">
