      <br/>
      <b>
       Jody Marshall
      </b>
      , a native of the Washington, D.C. area, grew up in a musical
 family. Enchanted by the sound and visual appeal of the hammered dulcimer, she
 developed a passion for the instrument.
      <br/>
      She has been performing and teaching dulcimer since 1988
 and was a founding member of the popular folk trios,
      <b>
       Ironweed
      </b>
      and
      <b>
       MoonFire
      </b>
      . She also performed extensively with
      <b>
       Connemara
      </b>
      . Her debut solo album, "Cottage in the Glen," was
 released by the Maggie's Music label in Spring 2005.

       credits include the Corcoran Gallery of Art, the Maryland Renaissance Festival,
       <br/>

       <br/>
