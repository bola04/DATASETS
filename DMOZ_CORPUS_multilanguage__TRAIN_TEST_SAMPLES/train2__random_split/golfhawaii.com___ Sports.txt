          <h1>
           Play your BEST golf in HAWAII
          </h1>
          <p>
           Unlike anywhere else on earth Hawaii showcases a wonderful environment for golfers of all skill levels.      You can play your BEST golf in Hawaii here’s why:
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            <span style="font-size: small;">
             The ground is firm and you will get a lot of roll.  You’ll hit some of your longest drives ever.
             <br/>
            </span>
           </li>
           <li>
            <span style="font-size: small;">
             Most of the resort courses are very user friendly and don’t require too many forced carries.
             <br/>
            </span>
           </li>
           <li>
            <span style="font-size: small;">
             The quality of the turf is excellent. You will be surprised how consistently your ball sits up in the fairway.
             <br/>
            </span>
           </li>
           <li>
            <span style="font-size: small;">
             Hawaii’s climate is perfect for good course conditioning which will help lower your scores.
            </span>
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
           <strong>
            So come and enjoy
           </strong>
           Hawaii’s premiere
           <a href="/hawaii-golf-resorts/favorite-resorts.asp">
            golf resorts
           </a>
