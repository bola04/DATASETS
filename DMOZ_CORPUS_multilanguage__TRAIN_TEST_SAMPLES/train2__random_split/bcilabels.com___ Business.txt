        <h1>

        <div class="BlockContent Home">
         <p>
          <strong>
           High-Quality Clothing Labels for the Apparel Industry
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          BCI Label Corporation is a premier clothing label manufacturer that produces all types of woven and printed labels for the apparel industry. BCI has manufactured quality labels for over 20 years and is recognized as a world leader in fashion labeling with over 250 label styles, numerous weaving looms, rotary print systems, off-set, Flexo print and
          <a href="http://www.bcilabels.com/tag/">
           sublimation process platform
          </a>
          s. BCI specializes in woven labels, utilizing high-performance Swiss and Italian weaving looms that create products designed for exquisite quality and softness. Products such as the advanced
          <a href="http://www.bcilabels.com/woven-labels/">
           damask air-jet woven labels
          </a>
          , satin needle loom labels, high definition satin woven with 50 denier yarns, can’t be found, in product or quality, anywhere else. We offer a line of
          <a href="http://www.bcilabels.com/custom-tapes/">
           vintage labels
          </a>
          produced on old shuttle looms which are a rarity anymore in the apparel industry.
