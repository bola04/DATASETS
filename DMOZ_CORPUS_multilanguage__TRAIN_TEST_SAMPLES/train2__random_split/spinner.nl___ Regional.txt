       </font>

      <p class="cc">

      <p>
       Here you can find information about me and my company, Spinner Software B.V.
      </p>
      <p>
       <font style="font-weight:bold;">
        Who am I ?
       </font>
      </p>
      <p>
       My name is Nicolai Kjaer, and I'm a developer with years of experience in software research and development. 
 	I develop and sell software products, or work as an external consultant/contractor. 
 	I'm good at prototyping, proof of concepts, tooling and automation.
      </p>
      <p>
       For the last 10+ years I've been focusing on packaging/scripting and virtualization of software, and regularly 
 	do contracting for Dutch customers. I also write tools, guides and helpful scripts - you can find more in the Kb.
      </p>
      <p>
       I work with installation and virtualization technologies for large scale software deployments and OS migrations.
 	Software is either virtualized (AppV, ThinApp, Citrix/XenApp), or packaged (Windows Installer Packages/Transforms),
 	and finally deployed to the end users. The PCs are typically managed via GPOs, Microsoft SCCM, Microsoft App-V, RES, and/or AppSense.
      </p>
      <p>
       I'm currently working at
       <a class="extlink" href="http://www.helionit.nl" target="_blank" title="Helion IT">
        -&gt;Helion IT
       </a>
       , and therefore not available for contracting.
      </p>
      <p>
       <font style="font-weight:bold;">
        Packaging services:
       </font>
      </p>
      <p>
       I create customized Windows Installer packages (or transforms) according to specifications/guidelines.
 	Common changes include:
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        Conform to a company-wide package standard
       </li>
       <li>
        Installation package with specific features and options
       </li>
       <li>
        Disabling updates
       </li>
       <li>
        Adding files or registry keys to a package
       </li>
       <li>
        Deployment files (RES Building Blocks / SMS files)
       </li>
       <li>
        Patches and updates
       </li>
       <li>
        Documentation
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       I can also assist as an external consultant on problematic packages, or with troubleshooting.
      </p>
