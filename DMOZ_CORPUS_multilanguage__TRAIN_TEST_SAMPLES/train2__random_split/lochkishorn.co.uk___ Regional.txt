     <h1 class="mainbody">
      <span class="heading-text-1">
       Welcome to Achintraid, Loch Kishorn. Get details of our holiday cottages in the picturesque North west coast of Scotland. Learn more about our superb West Coast locality.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Our two holiday homes, Woodside lodge and Pine lodge are situated in the small crofting township of Kishorn which is set in one of the most dramatic and beautiful, tranquill corners of the West Highlands of Scotland. Although Kishorn itself is extremely quiet and unspoilt, it is within easy reach of many places of interest along the West Coast and of course, the Highland capital, Inverness. The lodges provide breathtaking views of Loch Kishorn and the Applecross mountains. The village is a very quiet crofting township with traditional houses scattered along the shore and neighbouring hillsides. It has a very friendly, local shop called Patterns of Light (approximately one mile from the lodges - easy walking distance) which sells a good selection of artwork, crafts, books and gifts. The award winning Kishorn Seafood Bar is open during the summer months and very popular with tourists and locals alike (again approximately a mile from the holiday lodges). There is a wealth of art/craft studios in the local area including two well- known artists' studios in Kishorn itself.
