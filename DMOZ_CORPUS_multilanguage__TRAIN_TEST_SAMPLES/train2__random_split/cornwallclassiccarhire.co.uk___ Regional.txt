       <h1>
        Vintage Classics Classic Car Hire
       </h1>
       <h2>
        Wonderful Memories - Available for Hire!
       </h2>
       <p>
        Picture the scene; a classic British sportscar touring unspoilt coast and countryside. No, this isn't a flashback to the sixties but a great idea for a nostalgic classic car break.
       </p>
       <p>
        Classic Car Hire is an ideal holiday idea for the discerning visitor to the Westcountry. 
 	Be it for a special break,
        <a href="/site/birthday/">
         birthday
        </a>
        or
        <a href="/site/anniversary/">
         anniversary gift
        </a>
        taking to the wheel of a classic British sportscar is just the ticket for a 
 	special occasion or short break. Ideal for classic car
        <a href="/site/honeymoon">
         honeymoons
        </a>
        or just simply to indulge a 
 	long-held ambition to drive a certain classic car, we would be delighted to make arrangements for you or your loved one... 
 	As such we offer the perfect
        <a href="/site/birthday/">
         birthday gift idea
        </a>
        for
        <a href="/site/birthday/30th-birthday-gift-idea.asp">
         30th
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="/site/birthday/40th-birthday-gift-idea.asp">
         40th
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="/site/birthday/50th-birthday-gift-idea.asp">
         50th
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="/site/birthday/60th-birthday-gift-idea.asp">
         60th
        </a>
        or
        <a href="/site/birthday/70th-birthday-gift-idea.asp">
         70th
        </a>
        celebrations as well as
        <a href="/site/honeymoon">
         honeymoons
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="/site/anniversary/">
         anniversaries
        </a>
        and other special breaks in the Westcountry from our base in Wiltshire – Gateway to the West.
       </p>
       <p>
        Visit gardens, historic houses and beautiful sandy beaches by classic car between driving some of the best touring roads in the UK. If this sounds like your idea of motoring heaven (or the classic car dream of someone close to you) then read on...
       </p>
       <p>
        Vintage Classics has an exemplary track-record in the UK classic car hire market since our business launch in 2003.  Since that time we have established a reputation for providing high levels of personal service, offering desirable classic cars and for being a pro-active partner with other quality minded West Country based tourism organisations.
       </p>
       <p>
        The owners are also Directors of Historic &amp; Classic-car Hirer's Guild - the only quality focused association of classic car hire companies worldwide. Based in Wiltshire - but covering the West country - we are the longest established multi-make provider of classic cars in the south west offering you a great opportunity to enjoy the 'right' cars in the 'right' touring locations... Wonderful memories - available for hire!
       </p>
       <p>
        Enjoy looking through our classic car hire website and if you would like to enquire further about our fleet of classic cars and other related services please use the Vintage Classics Classic Car Hire
        <a href="/site/contact">
         Contact Us
        </a>
        form.
       </p>
       <h2>
        The Classic Car Hire Experience...
       </h2>
       <p>
        Perhaps you long for the thrill of driving clear roads or would like to relive the golden era of motoring when cars were less like appliances and rather more fun and involving than today's average daily run around? Take a classic car hire for a few days and experience a proper driving experience; fun, involving, challenging and ultimately very rewarding. Unlike museum pieces, our classic hire cars are alive – ready to go – so touch, drive and experience our motoring heritage - smell the leather, hear the burble of the exhaust and step back in time... And of course where better to create your very own ‘Black &amp; White’ movie set than against the backdrop of the West Country's beautiful coast and countryside? All the props are here – come and enjoy the chance to create a few memories.
       </p>
