            <img align="right" alt="Central Synagogue of Chicago" src="images/css/torah2.gif"/>
            Welcome To Central Synagogue of Chicago
           </h1>
           <p>
            <b>
             We’re glad you found us!
            </b>
           </p>
           <p>
            Join us for spiritually inspiring services, Torah study
                   and social holiday events. Central Synagogue of Chicago is a
                   small, friendly, welcoming urban Conservative Chicago Synagogue  where
                   everyone is important and everyone counts. We provide a traditional
                   approach to Judaism with contemporary insights. Our services are
                   traditional and egalitarian with spirited davening, inspiring hazzanut
                   and congregational participation. Sign up on our mailing list to
                   be informed of upcoming events.
           </p>
           <p>
            Among Chicago Synagogues we are the only centrally located Conservative 
                     downtown loop Synagogue, however our congregants
                   come from all over Chicagoland.
           </p>
           <p>
            So if you …
            <br/>
            - are new to Chicago
            <br/>
            - just moved back to the city from the suburbs
            <br/>
            - were here all the time but just found us...
           </p>
           <p>
            …if you are looking for a place to call spiritual
                   "home", know that everyone is truly welcome here.
           </p>

                   Chicago, enjoying our services and hospitality. So, be our guest! Come
                   daven with us any time you are in Chicago on Shabbat or Yom Tov, and
                   stay for Kiddush lunch.
