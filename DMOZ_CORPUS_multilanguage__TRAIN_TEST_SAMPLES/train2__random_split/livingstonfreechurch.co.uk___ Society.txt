       <p>
        Livingston Free Church of Scotland is located in Eliburn, Livingston. We are situated close to St. John's Hospital. Our emphasis is on simplicity, spirituality and sound biblical teaching. We are a congregation belonging to the Free Church of Scotland. We are an evangelical, reformed, presbyterian church. Livingston Free Church of Scotland is a Designated Religious Charity.
