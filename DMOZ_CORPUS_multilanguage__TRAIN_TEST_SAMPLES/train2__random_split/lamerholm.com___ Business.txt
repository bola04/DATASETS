         <h3>
          Measurements available: Shock, vibration, tilt, roll, GPS and RH/T
          <img alt="Hanwell-Solutions-British-Made" class="wp-image-6992 size-full alignright" height="47" src="http://www.shockwatchuk.com/app/uploads/made-in-britain.png" width="186"/>

         <p>
          Manufactured in the UK by The IMC Group, these flexible, reliable and extremely accurate ShockLogs are the bestselling shock data logger &amp; recorders on the market and can monitor valuable components in operation, transit, or storage. The world-leading shock data logger range comprises 3 battery-operated, self-contained logger devices featuring advanced tri-axial piezo electric accelerometer technology.
         </p>
         <p>
          From the entry-level ShockLog 208, to the mid-range ShockLog 248, and the top of the range ShockLog 298, each powerful, compact, shock data logger offers up to 18 months of continuous monitoring for unbeatable, low-cost performance.
         </p>
         <p>
          In addition to accurately recording shock and vibration, users can opt to monitor levels of relative humidity, temperature and tilt &amp; roll, while connectivity is provided by internal radio, internal GPS tracking, and the accessory eTrak GPS tracking module where required.
         </p>
         <p>
          Flexible and functional, and supplied with sophisticated electronics and easy-to-use software, these innovative shock and vibration monitoring instruments have applications in virtually every industry.
