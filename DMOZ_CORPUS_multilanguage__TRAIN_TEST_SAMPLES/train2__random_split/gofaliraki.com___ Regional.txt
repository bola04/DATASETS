              <h3>
               Faliraki is situated  
 
  alongside a 3mile long sandy beach boasting crystal clear waters  
 
  and numerous daytime activities and sports.  
 
  Every summer the popular
               <a href="http://www.gofaliraki.com/FaliResort.html" style="COLOR: blue">
                resort
               </a>
               attracts thousands of families, couples and singles  
 
  from all over Europe.
               <br/>
               Most of the
               <a href="http://www.gofaliraki.com/FaliHotels.html" style="COLOR: blue">
                hotels
               </a>
               have their own swimming pool, snack bar and probably a  
 
  restaurant as well.
               <br/>
               Of course, there are many
               <a href="http://www.gofaliraki.com/FaliHotels.html" style="COLOR: blue">
                bars/clubs
               </a>
               and restaurants  
 
  all over Faliraki that offer drinks, entertainment and tasty food at very  
 
  reasonable prices. Faliraki night life is definitely unbeatable.
               <br/>
               There are many
               <a href="http://www.gofaliraki.com/FaliHotels.html" style="COLOR: blue">
                places to visit
               </a>
               ,  
 
  we would hightly recommend Rhodes old town and the picturesque  
 
  village of Lindos. Even Turkey is only 40 minutes away.
               <br/>
               <a href="http://www.gofaliraki.com/FaliHotels.html" style="COLOR: blue">
                Working in
               </a>
               Faliraki can be fun! Most of  
 
  the jobs are related to bars, hotels, restaurants, clubs, etc.
               <br/>
               All in all Faliraki  
 
  offers all the essential ingredients for an unforgettable holiday.
