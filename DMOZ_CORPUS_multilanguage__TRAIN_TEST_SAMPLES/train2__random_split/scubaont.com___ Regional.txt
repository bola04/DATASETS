          <br/>
          Wow, can you say long time between updates? Alright so its been a while but I have good reasons for this. Just a quick notice 
           on this site to let people know we are looking for scuba divers who would like to take their
          <a href="http://www.aquaspeleo.com">
           Cave Diving Certification
          </a>
          or
          <a href="http://www.aquaspeleo.com">
           Introduction To Cave Diving Certification
          </a>
          this winter. We are planning multiple trips to Northern Florida, some for 
           instructional purposes and a couple for those of us that are already certified in intro to cave diving and full cave diving. If you are interested 
           in cave diving contact me at 888-909-9004 or
          <a href="cavediving@statustechnologies.com">
           cavediving@statustechnologies.com
          </a>
          and I can send you some information on 
           the timing, who we are
          <a href="http://www.aquaspeleo.com">
           hiring for courses
          </a>
          , cost etc. Look forward to hearing from some of you.

          There are some excellent events coming up in the next couple of weeks. The first is the 10th Annual SCUBA Swap and Sale, Saturday April 21, 2001.
           Doors open 9:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m. and is held at the Olympium Gymnasium in Etobicoke Ontario (590 Rathburn Road, Etobicoke, ON). For more information you can 
           visit
          <a href="http://www.ontario-underwater-explorers.org" target="_new">
           http://www.ontario-underwater-explorers.org
          </a>
          .
          <br/>
          <br/>
          The second is
          <a href="http://www.divesource.com/index.html?media=divesourceclick" target="_new">
           Dive Source Scuba Supply's
          </a>
          Fifth Anniversery Sale. When: Saturday May 12, 2001 from 9 am to 8 pm at
          <a href="http://www.divesource.com/index.html?media=divesourceclick" target="_new">
           Dive Source Scuba Supply
          </a>
          , 
           3015 Brock St. N. Whitby, ON Canada -- 905-668-8566 -- and you can also visit them on-line. They will have more information posted soon. 
           "This day will feature product specials, food, refreshments, door prizes and factory sales reps to answer technical questions. We are very excited about this event and hope you will come on by to help us celebrate!"
