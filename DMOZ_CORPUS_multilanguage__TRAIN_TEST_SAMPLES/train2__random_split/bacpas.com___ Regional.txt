          <h2 style="text-align: left;">
           Brown Armstrong History
          </h2>
          <p>
           In 1974, our founder Peter C. Brown, opened the doors of his accounting firm in Bakersfield, California. In 1985, Pete merged his accounting and entrepreneurial skills with Burton H. Armstrong.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Brown Armstrong Today
          </h2>
          <p>
           Over 40 years later, Brown Armstrong Accountancy Corporation has grown to a team of
           <a href="https://bacpas.com/about-us/partners/" title="Partners">
            <strong>
             <em>
              nine partners
             </em>
            </strong>
           </a>
           supported by a group of employees who are highly skilled certified public accountants and financial professionals. Our team is focused on a single mission:
           <em>
            “Exceed client expectations and provide opportunities.”
           </em>
           Through relationship building and a creative, tailored approach, we can find the perfect financial solution for you or your business.
