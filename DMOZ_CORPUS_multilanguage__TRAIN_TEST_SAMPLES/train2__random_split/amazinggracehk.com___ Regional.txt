           Asia has long been a place associated with mysticism and opulence. Throughout history, people have been enthralled by the mystical traditions, fabulous imperial wealth, and stunning art which characterize this continent. At the Amazing Grace Elephant Company, we have fostered a passion for all things Eastern for over 45 years and founded our store to share some of Asia's incredible craftsmanship and design with the world. Our diverse range of goods available to buy online encompass some of the most iconic designs from Asian countries including China, India, Thailand, Vietnam and Indonesia.
          </p>
          <h3>
           From the Chinese Qipao / Cheongsam Dress for Women to Dragon Ties, we sell a range of Iconic Asian Clothing
          </h3>
          <p>
           When you think of traditional Asian garments, what likely comes to mind is richly colored silks embroidered with swirling dragons, phoenixes, or cherry blossoms and indeed our range of clothing reflects this classic Eastern aesthetic. Traditional Chinese dress features strongly in our range of clothing for sale and includes women's qipao / cheongsam dresses, shawls, and mandarin collar tops. We also have a range of modern designs which take traditional Asian motifs and reimagine them in western clothing such as t-shirts, ties, and tote bags. So whether you wish to evoke the East of days gone by in a qipao for women or embrace the modern with a calligraphy tie or t-shirt, we have something to suit your needs.
          </p>
          <h3>
           Buy Stunning Eastern Decor to Adorn your Living Space
          </h3>
          <p>
           Asian interior design and sense of spatial balance has had an enormous influence on living space trends in the West and around the world for more than a century now and for good reason; Asian design strikes the perfect equilibrium between refinement and opulence. In addition to our qipao dresses, dragon ties and shawls, Amazing Grace has an expansive range of decor and collectibles representing the very pinnacle of Eastern craftsmanship and design. Our range includes Chinese ornamental chopstick sets, snuff bottles, Thai Buddha sculptures, and reproduction candle holders.
