                   <br/>
                   The 14th Annual Poker Run is slated for July 27-28.  A big thank you goes to Larry Rubis, long time Club member, who organized and managed the Poker Run for the past 13 years. Larry has decided to step away from managing the event this year. He always made the Poker Run fun, and generated great contributions for the
                   <a href="https://www.jasonsfriends.org/" target="_blank">
                    Jason's Friends Foundation
                   </a>
                   . If you see Larry, at the Club, please take a moment to thank him for the success of the Poker Run these past years.
                   <br/>
                   This year the theme for the Poker Run is
                   <strong>
                    <font color="#5040ae">
                     Mardi Gras
                    </font>
                   </strong>
                   ! Get your costumes ready and your boats decorated. There will be prizes for both! As always, the goal of the Poker Run is to generate contributions for local charities. This year CBC is working with two great charities:
                   <a href="http://mealswheels.com/" target="_blank">
                    Meals on Wheels
                   </a>
                   , and
                   <a href="http://huntingwithheroes.org/" target="_blank">
                    Hunting with Heroes
                   </a>
                   .
                   <br/>
                   An information/registration packet is now available
                   <a href="/uploads/3/6/6/8/3668840/poker_run_pkt18.pdf" target="_blank">
                    online
                   </a>
                   and at the Club. Pick one up today and get ready for...Mardi Gras!

                    UPDATE

                    map
