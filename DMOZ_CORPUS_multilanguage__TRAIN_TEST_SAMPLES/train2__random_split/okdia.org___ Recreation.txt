       <p>
        The OK Dinghy International Association (OKDIA) is the world organization for the OK-Dinghy class.
        <br/>
        The OK Dinghy is a 4m long single-handed sailing racing dinghy.
        <br/>
       </p>
       <p>
        The design of the OK dinghy celebrated it's 60th anniversary in 2017.
       </p>
