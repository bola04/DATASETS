             Check out and show some support for the work of one of our Interns!

             "As the project's manager, I've decided on collecting toiletries and makeup items for female survivors of Domestic Violence. At our center, we are counting with donation boxes where people can come by and drop off any product as a donation for us to collect and after to donate to local shelters in the Seattle area. We believe these items can help some women feel better about their appearance after they get to experience patterns of abusive behaviors by their partners in an intimate relationship such as marriage, dating, family, friends or cohabitation. We hope to change women’s stories through this amazing project by bringing a new resource of help for them to feel supported."
