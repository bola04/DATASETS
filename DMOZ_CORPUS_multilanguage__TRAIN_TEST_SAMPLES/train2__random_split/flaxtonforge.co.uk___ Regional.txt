       <span style="font-size:14pt; font-family:'Times New Roman', Times, serif; color:#773E1B; ">
        Tom Heys is an Artist blacksmith who studied at York College, gaining an HND in design crafts, before working for a well respected blacksmith in the North of England.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        In 2005, he set up Flaxton Forge with a fellow blacksmith, undertaking all aspects of ironwork from small individual pieces, sculptures, ornate gates and railings to much larger projects including plenty of renovation work under the supervision of architects.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Tom  moved Flaxton Forge to Beechlea, Cross Lane, Flaxton.  From there he is carrying on the same business, combining traditional forging skills with fabrication work to produce a wide variety of ironwork at prices to suit all pockets.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Tom has a natural artistic ability which he puts to good use in his designs and forge work, producing work of a high standard .
        <br/>
        <br/>
        He can also work to a commission and offer suggestions at the design stage to ensure that customers are entirely satisfied with the results.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Tom is happy to quote for any job, large or small, to create a unique hand-crafted piece of ironwork.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Additional services
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Sandblasting, galvanising, spray painting, powder coating, gilding, mobile welding.
