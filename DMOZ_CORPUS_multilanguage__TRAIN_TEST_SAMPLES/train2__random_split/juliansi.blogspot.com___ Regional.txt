                       <br/>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-SsgtgUe3P08/UjNvuihipwI/AAAAAAABJ90/wmpkT2508bU/s1600/DSCF8470.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                         <img border="0" height="266" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-SsgtgUe3P08/UjNvuihipwI/AAAAAAABJ90/wmpkT2508bU/s400/DSCF8470.jpg" width="400"/>
                        </a>
                       </div>
                       <br/>
                       Here's what I came for (In fact I actually wanted their wings, but they had sold-out) ... and so, I chose the Spicy Chicken Strips. I was having flashbacks to the Mini Fillet meal disaster from KFC last week!
                       <br/>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-4Jhm_fIa7O0/UjNvvtV2z6I/AAAAAAABJ-I/_Oo_c7j_aEo/s1600/DSCF8471.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                         <img border="0" height="213" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-4Jhm_fIa7O0/UjNvvtV2z6I/AAAAAAABJ-I/_Oo_c7j_aEo/s320/DSCF8471.jpg" width="320"/>
                        </a>
                       </div>
                       <br/>
                       Thank goodness the McDs staff were quick, courteous and ever so friendly - Even whilst I was in the queue, someone came up to me and said hello, and asked what I wanted to eat, and quickly ushered me to an open counter. Now, that's service for you!
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       As for the Chicken Strips ... Nothing much to shout about, I think it was breast-meat, tasted kinda dry, but hey ... McDs are forgiven, when their service is so darn good! KFC ... Tsk tsk.

                     <h3 class="post-title entry-title">
                      <a href="http://juliansi.blogspot.com/2013/09/kfc-khalidiya-abu-dhabi-bad-customer.html">
                       KFC Khalidiya, Abu Dhabi - Bad customer service
                      </a>
                     </h3>
                     <div class="post-header">
                      <div class="post-header-line-1">
                      </div>
                     </div>
                     <div class="post-body entry-content">
                      <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        I don't think I can ever convince myself to go to my local
                        <span class="Apple-style-span" style="color: red;">
                         KFC
                        </span>
                        again ... That's the one located at Zayed the 1st Street, here in Abu Dhabi's Khalidiyah area.
                       </div>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-spdC9OBRipU/Ui1wupYTWsI/AAAAAAABJ9E/gBncTTU7p5I/s1600/KFC+complaint+0.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                         <img border="0" closure_lm_99135="null" height="300" isa="true" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-spdC9OBRipU/Ui1wupYTWsI/AAAAAAABJ9E/gBncTTU7p5I/s400/KFC+complaint+0.jpg" width="400"/>
                        </a>
                       </div>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        Just last week - At 6:23pm in the evening, with hardly a person in the restaurant ... I ordered my
                        <strong>
                         Mini Fillet meal
                        </strong>
                        . I waited ... and I waited ...
                       </div>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-v2UlYfu_X_I/Ui1wvTyj2AI/AAAAAAABJ9M/F81MTPIq22I/s1600/KFC+complaint+1.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                         <img border="0" closure_lm_99135="null" height="320" isa="true" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-v2UlYfu_X_I/Ui1wvTyj2AI/AAAAAAABJ9M/F81MTPIq22I/s320/KFC+complaint+1.jpg" width="240"/>
                        </a>
                       </div>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        After 2 other patrons who came after me received their meals prior to myself .. I complained to the Manager, who then tried to expedite my order, which was when I realised he
                        <span style="color: red;">
                         wasn't wearing any gloves
                        </span>
                        when he used a temperature probe to measure the temperature of the chicken at the counter. I would be surprised if this was part of KFC's HACCP management system!
                       </div>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-jVGqrEJ84HM/Ui1wxpXNmxI/AAAAAAABJ9U/MKy5cu18UnE/s1600/KFC+complaint+2.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                         <img border="0" closure_lm_99135="null" height="240" isa="true" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-jVGqrEJ84HM/Ui1wxpXNmxI/AAAAAAABJ9U/MKy5cu18UnE/s320/KFC+complaint+2.jpg" width="320"/>
                        </a>
                       </div>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        <span style="color: black;">
                         After waiting
                         <strong>
                          15 minutes
                         </strong>
                         , I decided to remind the staff that I have been waiting an extraordinarily length of time for what is considered "fast" food, and what surprised me was - There was only one or two other persons waiting around the counter ... Why did it take so long to prepare a couple of fries and my "mini fillets" and why could I not have been kept informed about the reasons for the delay?!
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        <strong>
                         <span style="color: red;">
                          22 minutes later
                         </span>
                        </strong>
                        ... I got my Mini Fillet meal. Am I going to take the opportunity to write about my experience eating this take-away meal? I think not - This experience has left me genuinely dissapointed and surprised with KFC.
                       </div>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-_68lLKqVfAI/Ui1wxjntrGI/AAAAAAABJ9Y/1-lwuhYchD0/s1600/KFC+complaint+3.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                         <img border="0" closure_lm_99135="null" height="400" isa="true" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-_68lLKqVfAI/Ui1wxjntrGI/AAAAAAABJ9Y/1-lwuhYchD0/s400/KFC+complaint+3.jpg" width="300"/>
                        </a>
                       </div>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        <strong>
                         <span style="color: blue;">
                          Update 10th Sept
                         </span>
                        </strong>
                        - I provided the above feedback to Americana-Food and Yum group who operates KFC in the MENA region, and to their credit, they seemed to take my concerns seriously.
                        <strong>
                         Pity the staff in this store didn't!
                        </strong>
                       </div>

                      </a>
                     </h3>
                     <div class="post-header">
                      <div class="post-header-line-1">
                      </div>
                     </div>
                     <div class="post-body entry-content">
                      <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                        Ah ha ... we now have the precise GPS coordinates for one of my favourite Indian restaurants in town ...
                        <b>
                         Tandoori Corner
                        </b>
                        of Abu Dhabi:
                       </div>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                        <b>
                         <span class="Apple-style-span" style="color: red;">
                          N 24.46294
                         </span>
                        </b>
                       </div>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                        <b>
                         <span class="Apple-style-span" style="color: red;">
                          E 54.37854
                         </span>
                        </b>
                       </div>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">

                        </a>
                       </div>
                       <br/>
                       I felt like an Indian meal tonight ... so I went for a drive :-) Free fresh poppadoms for starters...
                       <br/>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-IGY6ASV_3L4/UitTiyaiMZI/AAAAAAABJ74/GZeEjKHqXzs/s1600/DSCF8459.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">

                        </a>
                        <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-JhFLA5n1S8k/UitTiW_WwZI/AAAAAAABJ7w/WLi5HgXwT_0/s1600/DSCF8460.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                         <img border="0" height="133" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-JhFLA5n1S8k/UitTiW_WwZI/AAAAAAABJ7w/WLi5HgXwT_0/s200/DSCF8460.jpg" width="200"/>
                        </a>
                       </div>
                       <br/>
                       My mushroom briyani, pretty good - And great value at 16 AED (RM$13), a really large portion that is easily enough for 2 persons.
                       <br/>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-JhFLA5n1S8k/UitTiW_WwZI/AAAAAAABJ7w/WLi5HgXwT_0/s1600/DSCF8460.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                         <br/>
                        </a>
                        <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-ntc9bxmyN8I/UitTjMbmOkI/AAAAAAABJ8I/3qjahaKNa-g/s1600/DSCF8461.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">

                       <b>
                        <u>
                         Tandoori Chicken
                        </u>
                       </b>
                       . Half portion at 16 AED.

                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">

                        </a>
                       </div>
                       <br/>
                       The rest of my meal :-) Dipping sauce and Naan Bread which is served with the above. Yummy!

                        </a>

                         <img border="0" height="213" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-9R-i4KsDHcY/UitTl8R6SPI/AAAAAAABJ8s/A4yD7_P24p0/s320/DSCF8464.jpg" width="320"/>
                        </a>
                       </div>
                       <br/>
                       Fennel, Rock Sugar and coated coriander seeds to freshen your mouth ;-) BURP!
                       <br/>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-rj4zvi8MfM0/UitTlcyR_nI/AAAAAAABJ8g/ArqQX_JVB64/s1600/DSCF8465.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">

                        </a>
                       </div>
                       <br/>
                       Located along 2nd Road or Airport Road, next to Fathima Supermarket, and not far from Mashraq Bank (A few blocks from Al Wahda Mall), this place is truly an Abu Dhabi institution ... We usually do take-aways as do many people (Call
                       <b>
                        <span class="Apple-style-span" style="color: red;">
                         +971 2 445 8686
                        </span>
                       </b>
                       ), but try dining in - Its worth a visit!
