      <h2 class="wsite-content-title" style="text-align:center;">
       WELCOME TO TREASURE COAST MARINA!
       <br/>
      </h2>
      <div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;display:block;">
       <font size="4">
        Treasure Coast Marina is a full service facility in Grant, Florida, at Marker #35 on the ICW and just 4.5 miles north of Sebastian Inlet. By car it is conveniently situated on US Hwy #1, twelve miles south of Melbourne, located between the two county boat ramps of South Brevard County.
        <br/>
        There are thirty five wetslips available, and thirteen lifts with water and electric at each slip.  Boat/trailer storage is available at the Boat Park across the street.  Offering Non-ethanol 93 octane fuel, the best there is, to protect your fuel system. The docks, rest rooms, and shower facilities are handicapped accessible.  Ship's Store, Bait &amp; Tackle, and Camping supplies.  Rod and Reel Repair on site.  Line Spooling.  We offer boat and kayak rentals to explore the Indian River Lagoon.
        <br/>
        We also have a full liquor bar and restaurant on site, with al fresco dining and entertainment daily.  Our menu is "Floribbean" - a combination of Florida style and a Caribbean twist.  We serve only FRESH, never frozen local seafood and steaks.
        <br/>
        See the rest of the site for more details!  We look forward to meeting you...
