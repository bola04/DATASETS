               <p>
                Please visit us at
                <a href="http://www.nonpareilscake.com">
                 www.nonpareilscake.com
                </a>
                for all cake info.
                <br/>
                <br/>
                If you're here, you're probably looking for cake info and blog posts...She Takes The Cake, owned by Jennifer Comfort in Central New York, has changed to Nonpareils, Ltd.  Only the company structure and name have changed as far as the cakes are concerned.  Jen still does every aspect of running the company and is lead designer/decorator on all cakes that pass through the doors.  We'll be adding some exciting new things in 2011 but this blog is no longer going to be updated.  Old posts are archived for your reading/viewing pleasure, but please go to the new site for current content.
                <br/>
                <br/>
                Thanks, and I hope you'll continue to follow our
                <a href="http://www.nonpareilscake.com/blog/">
                 blog
                </a>
                there.
