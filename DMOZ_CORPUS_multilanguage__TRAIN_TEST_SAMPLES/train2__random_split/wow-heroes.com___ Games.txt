            </h3>
            <span class="section-date">
             14.October.2014.

           </div>
           <div class="section-body2">
            <div class="section-text">

             <br/>
             <br/>
             - Items are squished, and it will take a while until all character stats are updated to new values

             - Hit and expertise caps are gone

             <br/>
