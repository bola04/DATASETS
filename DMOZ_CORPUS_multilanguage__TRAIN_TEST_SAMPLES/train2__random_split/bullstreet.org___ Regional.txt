           <p style="text-align: left;">
            On behalf of the members of Bull Street Baptist Church, welcome to our site. We hope that you will find the information helpful and that you will get a better feel for what God is doing in and through his people here at Bull Street. Bull Street Baptist Church has been impacting the city of Savannah with the life-changing message of the Gospel for generations. In 2016 we celebrated our 125th year of gospel ministry.
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: left;">
            <br/>
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: left;">
            Our campus is located just south of Forysth Park along Bull Street in the heart of Savannah. While our membership comes from all over the Low Country, we remain committed to being a faithful gospel witness in downtown Savannah. It is our desire to equip people to know Jesus Christ and to tell of his amazing grace. We seek to accomplish this through biblical preaching and teaching, Christ-centered worship and fellowship, intentional evangelism and discipleship, worldwide missions and God-honoring service. We hope that you will make plans to visit with us soon. We would enjoy the privilege of welcoming you to our church in person.
