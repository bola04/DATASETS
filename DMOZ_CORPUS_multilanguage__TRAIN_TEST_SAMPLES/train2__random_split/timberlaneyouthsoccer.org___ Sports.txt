        <h1>
         Welcome to TYSL!
        </h1>
        <p>
         Timberlane Youth Soccer League proudly serves the children from Sandown, Plaistow, Atkinson, Danville, and surrounding areas.
        </p>
        <p>
         Please like and share our
         <a href="https://www.facebook.com/pages/Timberlane-Youth-Soccer/115817368459502" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">
          facebook page
         </a>
         to keep up to date with notifications and events.  For complete information about games, practices, equipment, and eligibility, please see
         <a href="http://www.timberlaneyouthsoccer.org/faq/">
          FAQ page
         </a>
         <strong>
          .
         </strong>
         Subscribe to our
         <a href="https://www.timberlaneyouthsoccer.org/event-calendar/">
          Google Calendar
         </a>
         to stay informed of upcoming events (not games).
        </p>

         2018 Fall Season Registration

        <p>
         Registration is now open for the 2018 Fall season.
         <br/>

         <br/>
         Registration closes August 19th.
         <br/>
         See the
         <a href="https://www.timberlaneyouthsoccer.org/register/">
          Registration Page
         </a>
         for more information.

         2018 Fall Season
        </h1>
        <p>
         Games are on Saturdays Sep 8 – Nov 3 2018, except Oct 6, 2018.
