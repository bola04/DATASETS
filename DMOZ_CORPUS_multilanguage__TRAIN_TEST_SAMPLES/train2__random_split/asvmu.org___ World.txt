             Fusionex CEO receives Technopreneur of Excellence Title
            </a>
           </h3>
          </header>
          <!-- .entry-header -->
          <div class="entry-content">
           <p>
            Fusionex Managing Director and CEO Dato’ Seri Ivan Teh was awarded the Technopreneur of Excellence 2017 Award at the PIKOM ICT Awards ceremony over the weekend. The coveted award was presented to Teh, in recognition of Fusionex’s achievements and contributions towards growing and supporting the ICT sector in the region.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="https://www.fusionex-international.com/Latest-News-Announcements/Fusionex-CEO-receives-Technopreneur-of-Excellence-Title">
             Fusionex Ivan Teh
            </a>
            (center) on stage to receive the Technopreneur of Excellence award
           </p>
           <p>
            The star-studded event was marked by an attendance of approximately a thousand attendees, consisting of industry leaders, talents, and budding stars in the ICT arena. The Technopreneur of Excellence award is reserved for individuals demonstrating ICT best practices; as well as to commend their diligence, commitment, and continuous innovation.
           </p>
           <p>
            This award marks the continuous excellence exhibited by Fusionex, helmed by Teh, and follows Fusionex’s earlier win of the Company Excellence Award in 2016.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="https://fskm.uitm.edu.my/scds2016/abstractivanteh.html">
             <img alt="fusionex ivan teh" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-101" height="149" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="http://www.asvmu.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/fusionex-ivan-teh-300x149.png" srcset="http://www.asvmu.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/fusionex-ivan-teh-300x149.png 300w, http://www.asvmu.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/fusionex-ivan-teh-768x382.png 768w, http://www.asvmu.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/fusionex-ivan-teh.png 820w" width="300"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Teh carried the Malaysian and ASEAN flags up high when he represented the country and region to speak at the 2017 Belt and Road Summit. Held at the Hong Kong Convention and Exhibition Center (HKCEC), Teh was one of the most prominent speakers, gathered to share their perspective and vision with thousands of business leaders and key government officials from almost 50 countries.
           </p>
           <p>
            With Fusionex as a premier data technology player, Dato’ Seri Ivan Teh has also led Fusionex towards striking a strategic alliance with Alibaba Cloud, one of the fastest growing cloud providers in the world. Fusionex technology is also incorporated into the syllabus of certain local universities, to encourage homegrown ICT talent. Teh is a strong advocate for the nurturing of young talent, frequently encouraging students to consider ICT and data technology as a viable interest and career. He was also a judge for the Sunway University Dolphin Tank tech entrepreneur competition (by the Young Outliers) as well as Battle of the Digital Ninja event by MDEC held at KLCC.

           </p>
           <p>
            I would like to congratulate and thank everyone from Team Fusionex who made this possible. We will continue to move upwards and onwards in our journey and mission to help shape this country and region in a positive manner with technology.”

             Color Contact Lenses For The Win
            </a>
           </h3>
          </header>
          <!-- .entry-header -->
          <div class="entry-content">
           <p>
            Want to change something about your appearance? How about your eye color? You would be amazed how eye color affects your image. Find out what color contacts can do for your appearance and see how to get the best out of them
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Contact_lens">
             Color contact lenses
            </a>
            choices
           </p>
           <p>
            Enhancement contact lenses are designed to enhance the color of light eyes. They don’t alter your color, but offer your eyes a light blue, aqua or green tint. Enhancement color lenses make your eye color better and more defined.
           </p>
           <p>
            The most popular enhancement
            <a href="https://www.lensvillage.com/category/sclera-contacts">
             <span data-sheets-userformat='{"2":513,"3":[null,0],"12":0}' data-sheets-value='{"1":2,"2":"green contacts"}'>
              sclera contacts
             </span>
            </a>
            are Acuvue 2 Colors Enhancers by Johnson &amp; Johnson and Freshlook Dimensions by Ciba Vision. Freshlook Radiance is likewise a really interesting style; these lenses create an illuminating impact. Improvement color contacts just deal with light eyes; on dark eyes they will not have any result at all.
           </p>
           <p>
            Nontransparent color contacts completely alter the color of your eyes. They work similarly well for light and dark eyes. Even if you have an extremely dark brown eyes, opaque color contacts like Freshlook Colorblends can transform your color into sapphire blue or jade green.
           </p>
           <p>
            The color of a nontransparent color lens isn’t really solid – it has an imprinted pattern that imitates the natural pattern of a human eye. High quality nontransparent color contacts like Acuvue 2 Color opaque or Freshlook Colorblends look extremely reasonable. People would be surprised by what beautiful blue, green or amethyst eyes you have.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="https://www.lensvillage.com/category/cosplayer-contacts">
             <img alt="cosplay contacts" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-96" height="253" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="http://www.asvmu.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/colored-lens-300x253.jpg" srcset="http://www.asvmu.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/colored-lens-300x253.jpg 300w, http://www.asvmu.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/colored-lens.jpg 365w" width="300"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Outfit color contacts are constantly Halloween favorites. While the contacts mentioned above are developed to offer your eyes a different however realistic looking color, costume lenses can provide you feline’s eyes or werewolf eyes, while complete whiteouts are perfect for the effect of blind eyes. And who said that you can only use outfit color contacts for a costume party? It can be a real enjoyable to wear them when going to the club, or just at school or deal with otherwise common clothes. With a pair of cat’s eyes, you are ensured to be seen.
           </p>
           <p>
            Many costume contacts just cover the color area of your eye. However, there are also scleral color contacts that cover the visible portion of your eye. Scleral lenses can produce incredible results. The most popular outfit contacts are Crazy Lenses by Cooper Vision and Wild Eyes by Ciba Vision.
           </p>
           <p>
            All brand-name color and outfit contact lenses are available with corrective powers in addition to in plano (no correction) This implies that if your eyesight isn’t best and you wear restorative contact lenses anyway, you can get color lenses with corrective powers and eliminate two birds with one stone.
            <br/>
            <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/M9XPvr2HlO4" width="560">
            </iframe>
           </p>
           <p>
            Color contact lenses using guidance
           </p>
           <p>
            You must follow the same guidelines as for regular contact lenses – wash your hands before putting color lenses in, clean the lenses each time you use them and keep them in a contact lens case in an unique service.
            <br/>
            Color lenses resemble your tooth or hair brash, and ought to not be shown other people
            <br/>
            You ought to put your make-up on after the lenses remain in. If you utilize hairspray, do it before you put the lenses in, or shut your eyes extremely tightly while spraying.
            <br/>
            Usage just a mild multipurpose option for cleaning your color contacts; never ever use
            <br/>
            peroxide based solutions. And don’t rub the lenses – it can damage the color
            <br/>
            If you do not use your lenses frequently, alter the option they are stored in at least when a week
            <br/>
            Do not use your color contacts for longer than 8 hours at a time
            <br/>
            Your eyes and your hair are probably the two things most people see initially. And with color contacts you can have any eye color you desire. Try them, and I am sure you will see many men turning their heads when you pass.

            </a>
            takes place due to the compression at the base of the spine. This compression results in discomfort in the lower back and gluteal area of the body. This discomfort travels to ankle, calf, thighs and knee. Genuine Sciatica takes place when the discomfort reaches the knees and the ankles. Sciatica actually is the pain that moves through the sciatic nerve – the longest nerve in your body. This nerve begins with your spinal cord to your hips and down to the leg. The discomfort therefore moves along this nerve and the parts of body it connects. Sciatica really might not be issue in itself. It may have other intrinsic causes which are connected to the sciatic nerve like herniated disk.
           </p>
           <p>
            Sciatica can reduce your mobility and regular day-to-day activities substantially. The period of the pain differs according to the fundamental cause and may go away in 4-8 weeks time. However, there is no have to loose hope. There are many sciatica treatments both scientific and home based which can assist treat and manage sciatica and lower pain.

            Common Sciatica Treatments

            Bed Rest
           </p>

            The most typical sciatica treatment suggested by physicians is bed rest. It helps in alleviating pain. However, only taking bed rest is not going to resolve the problem fully. You should combine rest with exercise that suffices to keep you active without intensifying the pain.
           </p>
           <p>
            Exercise
           </p>
           <p>

           </p>

           <h3 class="entry-title">
            <a href="http://www.asvmu.org/5-things-to-consider-when-youre-hiring-a-software-outsourcing-partner/" rel="bookmark">
             5 Things to Consider When You’re Hiring A Software Outsourcing Partner
            </a>
           </h3>
          </header>
          <!-- .entry-header -->
          <div class="entry-content">
           <p>
            Need a software developer? It’s easy to outsource them globally, but finding the right
            <a href="https://www.pslcorp.com/it-outsourcing-services-companies/">
             IT outsourcing
            </a>
            partner isn’t nearly as simple — though there are some strategies out there that will make your task a little bit easier.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="https://www.pslcorp.com/it-outsourcing-services-companies/">
             <img alt="it outsourcing" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-74" height="151" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="http://www.asvmu.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/it-outsourcing-300x151.jpeg" srcset="http://www.asvmu.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/it-outsourcing-300x151.jpeg 300w, http://www.asvmu.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/it-outsourcing.jpeg 316w" width="300"/>
            </a>
            Say, for example, that you want to outsource your software development. Maybe you’re convinced by success stories like that of Skype, which built its beta version with the help of three Estonian developers. Or a story like that of Slack, which originally outsourced the development of its app, website and even logo. Or maybe you just don’t have the cash — or the need or the time — to hire an in-house development team.
           </p>
           <p>
            Whatever your need, to be successful at your next
            <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Information_technology_outsourcing">
             outsourcing
            </a>
            endeavor, here are the top five things to consider before hiring a software outsourcing partner:
           </p>
           <p>
            <iframe allow="autoplay; encrypted-media" allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/ULfmkgkyEp8" width="560">
            </iframe>
           </p>
           <p>
            1. First, consider geography.
           </p>
           <p>
            Before starting to look for an outsourcing partner, make it easy on yourself by carefully defining the geography in which you would like to search. Today there are tens of thousands of software outsourcing partners available across the world; Latin America, Ukraine, India, and China are just a few areas full of vendors that are ripe for the taking.
           </p>
           <p>
            Because communication is key for successful outsourcing relationships, regions with similar time zones are often a good starting point; however, other factors such as nearshore versus offshore, cultural compatibility, political stability, low inflation rates and geographical proximity, are also critical.
           </p>
           <p>
            “Even software-outsourcing firms themselves have begun to take note of these elements,” Ludovic Gaudé, CEO of intive, noted.
           </p>
           <p>
            Referencing his company’s recent acquisition of a Latin American software development firm, Gaudé highlighted the importance of geography. “Apart from a talented pool of engineers, a business culture similar to the U.S. and Europe, and being in a similar time zone to our clients, the [acquisition] also means that our U.S. customers benefit from bilingual developers,” he said.
           </p>
           <p>
            2. Next, decide on price or quality.
           </p>
           <p>
            As the saying goes, you can’t have your cake and eat it, too. The same goes for software development,even more so when you are outsourcing it. Although price and quality always have a spectrum, potential software-outsourcing partners generally fall within one of two categories: price-first vendors or quality-first vendors. So, you must choose which you prefer.
           </p>
           <p>
            Price-first vendors will often provide a fixed-bid quote for your project, and tend to be more transactionally focused than focused on a long-term relationship. Their expertise lies in finding resources and quickly deploying them for clients with little supervision.
           </p>
           <p>
            According to a Medium post by Mike Svystun, VP of business development at Vertalab, a price-first strategy “could work well for developing minimum viable products or isolated products limited in scope.” That said, Svystun added, “We think it’s rarely a good choice for well-established startups.”
           </p>
           <p>
            Quality-first vendors, on the other hand, are more expensive — as well as significantly more selective about the types of projects they take. Still, they’re a better choice for all complex or mission-critical projects. That’s because they will most often work under a time-and-materials model, look for a longer term relationship and reject fixed-price bids, on principle.
           </p>
           <p>
            Similarly, quality-first vendors will spend significant effort and resources training their teams, and often work with the partner’s senior personnel to ensure proper delivery and execution.
           </p>
           <p>
            3. Put your candidates to the test.
           </p>
           <p>
            Software-outsourcing vendors will each have their unique strengths and quirks. Nevertheless, there are certain core questions you can ask during an initial phone conversation to make sure a potential partner is a good cultural, philosophical and methodological fit for your company and project. Those questions:
           </p>
           <p>
            1. “What is your approach to software development?” Listen for words like: agile, SCRUM, MVP, short sprints, quick iteration, constant communication and any others you find most important.
           </p>
           <p>
            2. “Tell me about your previous experience with software outsourcing projects with other U.S./foreign clients similar to us.” Try to understand the type of clients the vendor has worked with in the past and how his/her services were valuable and effective. Request at least two or three references, and never risk being the vendor’s first foreign client.
           </p>
           <p>
            3. “What are the most critical risks to a software-outsourcing relationship, and how do you manage/mitigate them?” Get an idea of the outsourcing vendor’s real experience, as well as what he or she cares most about in a project.
           </p>
           <p>
            4. “What are your strengths as a company versus those of your other competitors?” Gauge what your software-outsourcing vendor most underscores in his/her own organization, in addition to any particular strategies and strengths the company is investing in.
           </p>
           <p>
            If possible, ask a business development representative to be present for the call to help walk you through the high-level perspective of a relationship. Don’t get too technical until you delve into details that check for a potential partner’s overall fit, as well as cultural and methodological compatibility, which are paramount.
           </p>
           <p>
            4. Visit the partner in person, if possible.
           </p>
           <p>
            Depending on the location you select for your search, visiting may or may not be possible. However, if it is, a personal visit can be a great way to see behind the veil and accurately determine your prospective partner’s true features.
           </p>
           <p>
            Business development routines are well orchestrated within any professional services organization, so during a remote exploratory process, you will likely see exactly what the prospective partner wants you to see. An in-person visit, however, is much more difficult to stage, and will communicate the real state of your prospective outsourcing vendor, as Mark Kobayashi-Hillary explained in a post for Computer Weekly.
           </p>
           <p>
            “If you have already received a dozen requests for information from your potential suppliers, each one summarising how they can meet your requirements, along with a visit from each sales team — all with a similar pitch — the site visit can highlight a supplier’s real strengths and weaknesses,” Kobayashi-Hillary wrote.
           </p>
           <p>
            In addition, if you make the trek to visit a potential partner, keep in mind that the trip need not be too long; a day or two will give you a good sense of the location, the overall motivation level of the team, the commitment of the leadership team, the state of the physical infrastructure and the overall professionalism that the location reflects.
           </p>
           <p>
            5. Make a decision and then communicate.
           </p>
           <p>
            Just because the MSA and SOWs are signed and you are ready to get started with your chosen software outsourcing partner, doesn’t mean your job is over. Your vendor-partner faces the same pressures remotely that you face locally: Good talent is hard to find and hire. Understand that it could take some time for your partner to put together a team.
           </p>
           <p>
            Once the team is finally set up, you can expect about two additional months of ramp-up, accompanied by supplementary investment for the new team to learn the ropes and adequately understand your business. Short sprints can help accelerate that training and give you a better idea of what it takes to put code into production. As the team is learning, take the time to iron out the best communication structures and mechanisms to raise red flags with your partner.
           </p>
           <p>
            Communication can be difficult when you’re working with remote teams, but Mike Galarza, CEO of the cloud-banking and finance platform Entryless, suggested using tools like Asana, Skype and Github to facilitate communication for software outsourcing projects. “It is crucial to be super-proactive on the communication side, since we are far away and not physically present,” Galarza said.
           </p>
           <p>
            Shamim Mohammad, CIO at used car retailer CarMax, pointed out an additional detail to consider. “When managing deadlines and projects,” he wrote in CIO.com, consider potential resource and technology challenges that might emerge.”
           </p>
           <p>
            He continued, “Make sure there is time allocated for undiscovered work, and develop a contingency plan for it. Lots of projects assume a happy path, but do not plan for unanticipated and undiscovered work, which will inevitably happen.”
           </p>
           <p>
            With the wide availability of software developers across the world, it’s easy to be overly eager in rushing to choose a partner for your project. However, the decision process must be carefully executed in order to find best fit for the task at hand. With the right decision and a little bit of luck, you too can be on your way to building the next software unicorn with the help of the right offshore IT partner.
           </p>

            <a href="https://www.pslcorp.com/it-outsourcing-services-companies/">
             https://www.pslcorp.com/it-outsourcing-services-companies/
            </a>
