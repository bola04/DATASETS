       </p>
       <h3>
        How Domain Concierge Service Works
       </h3>
       <ul>
        <li>
         Buyers, Sellers and Brokers can request the Domain Concierge Service when setting up a domain transaction on Escrow.com.
        </li>

        </li>
        <li>
         When the transaction has been agreed by all parties, we request the buyer to send funds and the seller to transfer the domain(s) to Escrow.com's secure holding account.
        </li>
        <li>
