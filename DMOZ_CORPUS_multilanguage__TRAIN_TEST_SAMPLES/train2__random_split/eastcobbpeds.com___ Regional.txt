               <h3 style="text-align: center;">
                We’re the best pediatricians in your neighborhood!
               </h3>
               <p style="text-align: left;">
                East Cobb Pediatrics was established in the the 1970’s by Dr. Eduardo Montana Sr.  Our  growth over the years has been tremendous. To meet the growing demand of the prevention and treatment of childhood illnesses, disorders and disease, the addition of several physicians and nurse practitioners was required.  We have two convenient locations to serve your child’s healthcare needs, one office in Marietta off of Johnson Ferry Road and one office in Kennesaw off of Cobb Parkway and Pine Mountain Road.
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: left;">
                We are dedicated
                <a href="http://www.eastcobbpeds.com/our-providers/">
                 pediatric doctors
                </a>
                and
                <a href="http://www.eastcobbpeds.com/our-providers/">
                 nurse practitioners
                </a>
                determined to provide high quality healthcare for children in the Metro Atlanta area. It is a privilege to be entrusted with restoring and promoting your child’s health. We look forward to taking care of your child!
               </p>
               <h3 style="text-align: left;">
                Our Goals
               </h3>
               <ul style="text-align: center;">
                <li style="text-align: left;">
                 Creating and sustaining relationships with our patients and their families based on mutual respect and honesty.
                 <a href="http://www.eastcobbpeds.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/darklogo1.jpg">
                  <img alt="East Cobb Pediatrics Logo" class="alignright wp-image-721 size-medium" height="186" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="http://www.eastcobbpeds.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/darklogo1-300x186.jpg" srcset="http://www.eastcobbpeds.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/darklogo1-300x186.jpg 300w, http://www.eastcobbpeds.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/darklogo1.jpg 635w" width="300"/>
                 </a>
                </li>
                <li style="text-align: left;">
                 Providing clean, comfortable and well-equipped facilities for our patients and staff.
                </li>
                <li style="text-align: left;">
                 Maintaining an environment that is friendly, professional and responsive to our patient’s needs.
                </li>
               </ul>

                Thank you for choosing East Cobb Pediatrics!
