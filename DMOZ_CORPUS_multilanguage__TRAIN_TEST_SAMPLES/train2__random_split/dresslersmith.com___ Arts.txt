            <h1 style="text-align: center;">
             <span style="font-size: 36pt; font-family: book antiqua, palatino, serif;">
              <strong>
               Dressler Smith, She’s An Artist
              </strong>
             </span>
            </h1>
            <p>
            </p>
           </div>
           <p>
            <img alt="" class="wp-image-404 size-large aligncenter" data-recalc-dims="1" height="506" sizes="(max-width: 900px) 100vw, 900px" src="https://i0.wp.com/dresslersmith.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/DresslerSmithArtist.png?resize=900%2C506" srcset="https://i0.wp.com/dresslersmith.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/DresslerSmithArtist.png?resize=1024%2C576 1024w, https://i0.wp.com/dresslersmith.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/DresslerSmithArtist.png?resize=300%2C169 300w, https://i0.wp.com/dresslersmith.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/DresslerSmithArtist.png?resize=768%2C432 768w, https://i0.wp.com/dresslersmith.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/DresslerSmithArtist.png?w=1800 1800w" width="900"/>
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.camdenfireworks.org/exhibitions/">
           http://www.camdenfireworks.org/exhibitions/
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <hr/>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
           <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
            Introducing Dresscapes.  Dressler Smith is a painter, author and pastel colorist who favors tranquil in a way which invokes both awe and reverence, and is designed to touch the human spirit. Her book the Inspirational Journey of Dressler Smith was released in late 2014.
           </span>
           <br/>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-25 aligncenter" data-recalc-dims="1" height="437" sizes="(max-width: 650px) 100vw, 650px" src="https://i2.wp.com/dresslersmith.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/ph-cp021008-1.jpg?resize=650%2C437" srcset="https://i2.wp.com/dresslersmith.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/ph-cp021008-1.jpg?w=650 650w, https://i2.wp.com/dresslersmith.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/ph-cp021008-1.jpg?resize=300%2C202 300w" width="650"/>
         </p>
         <hr/>
         <h2 style="text-align: center;">
          <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
           <span style="font-size: 36pt;">
            <strong>
             Tranquil Landscapes
            </strong>
           </span>
          </span>
         </h2>
         <p>
          Many of her works focus on landscapes of sunrises, morning light and morning skies which, for her, symbolize new beginnings. She has chosen pastel as her primary medium because of its vibrancy. The clean, rich colors of pure pigment, combined with her affinity for nature and all things spiritual, result in landscapes filled with peace and tranquility, and a place to start anew. Dressler’s art has been received by Oprah Winfrey, former NFL quarterback, Randall Cunningham and the late Coretta Scott King.
         </p>

         <h2 style="text-align: center;">
          <span style="font-size: 36pt;">
           <strong>
            Vibrant and Beautiful Stained Glass
           </strong>
          </span>
         </h2>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
           In addition to her work as a painter, Dressler is developing recognition as an artist in designing stained glass windows. A sample of her stained glass designs, Sunday Morning, created for St. John Baptist Church in East Camden, NJ, is available on this site. She has designed stained glass windows for the First Nazarene Baptist Church in Camden, NJ, and for the
          </span>

         <h2 style="text-align: center;">

           <span style="font-size: 36pt;">
            Beauty on Canvas, Dressler Smith
           </span>
          </strong>
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-289" data-recalc-dims="1" height="545" sizes="(max-width: 420px) 100vw, 420px" src="https://i2.wp.com/dresslersmith.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/Dresslerme-too_logo.jpg?resize=420%2C545" srcset="https://i2.wp.com/dresslersmith.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/Dresslerme-too_logo.jpg?w=499 499w, https://i2.wp.com/dresslersmith.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/Dresslerme-too_logo.jpg?resize=231%2C300 231w" width="420"/>

         <hr/>
         <p>
          Dressler dreamed of becoming a professional artist from the time she was a
          <br/>
          small child. After attending art classes in the Philadelphia Art Museum,
          <br/>
          Dressler received a B.F.A. from Moore College of Art in 1980. Currently,
          <br/>
          this remarkable painter continues to pursue her passion for artistic expression while
          <br/>
          sharing lessons learned with others as an adjunct faculty member at Camden
          <br/>
          County College (since 1994). Dressler was Coordinator of Adult Programs at
          <br/>
