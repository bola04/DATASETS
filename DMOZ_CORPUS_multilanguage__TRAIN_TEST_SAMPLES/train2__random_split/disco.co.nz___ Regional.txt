              <div class="et_pb_text_inner">
               <h1>
                Richard Mills, MC (Master of Ceremonies), Wedding Celebrant &amp; DJ
               </h1>
               <blockquote>
                <h3>
                 <em>
                  <strong>
                   Your celebration deserves a GREAT soundtrack!
                  </strong>
                 </em>
                </h3>
               </blockquote>
               <p>
                You and your guests will be able to truly relax and enjoy your celebration with ShowPro Ltd. With DJ Richard, every event is different, fun and delightfully memorable, and one that you’ll remember fondly for years to come.
               </p>
              </div>
             </div>
             <!-- .et_pb_text -->
            </div>
            <!-- .et_pb_column -->
           </div>
           <!-- .et_pb_row -->
           <div class="et_pb_row et_pb_row_1">
            <div class="et_pb_column et_pb_column_2_3 et_pb_column_1 et_pb_css_mix_blend_mode_passthrough">
             <div class="et_pb_module et_pb_text et_pb_text_1 et_pb_bg_layout_light et_pb_text_align_left">
              <div class="et_pb_text_inner">
               <p class="style5">
                Based in Wellington, but travelling throughout New Zealand and occasionally overseas to entertain, Richard Mills specialises in energetic, tasteful wedding and corporate entertainment. Richard is also available to act as your
                <a href="https://www.djrichard.co.nz/tips-and-tools/wedding-mc-guide/" title="Master of Ceremonies">
                 qualified MC (Master of Ceremonies)
                </a>
                and to assist with the planning, organising and directing of your event including prize-givings, announcements, personalised wedding reception Grand Entrances or a memorable last dance and farewell. He is also an official marriage celebrant, and can formally officiate over your wedding ceremony.
               </p>
               <p class="style5">
                The correct selection of wedding music played at the right time, in the right sequence
                <span id="more-2">
                </span>
                and at an appropriate volume can really help make a magical day complete. For many years, DJ Richard would offer a link to his song list for people to download. Unfortunately he now carries around 40,000 tracks (including many on video) and is a monthly subscriber to a DJ-only professional music video subscription service, so the list grows every week. Needless to say, in the unlikely occurrence that a song you want isn’t already in the collection, Richard will do everything that he can to get it for you!
               </p>
               <p>
                Click
                <a href="http://www.3news.co.nz/The-big-MC/tabid/1807/articleID/104114/Default.aspx" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="How to be a great wedding MC, TV3">
                 HERE
                </a>
                to read the guide to being a great wedding MC, written by Richard Mills for TV3, M2 Magazine and Engaged Magazine. As well as the MC guide, you can also look through Richard’s
                <a href="https://www.djrichard.co.nz/tips-and-tools/free-wedding-reception-planning-guide/">
                 Wedding Planning Guide
                </a>
                , which offers prompts for things you’ve probably never thought of. This will help ensure that your day is perfect and that nothing is missed.
               </p>
              </div>
             </div>

              <div class="et_pb_text_inner">
               <h2>
                What does DJ Richard do?
               </h2>
               <p>
                It isn’t just traditional weddings that are performed at either. Currently DJ Richard is driving over 35,000km per year all over the country to entertain and present to couples, companies, groups and organisations. A regular entertainer in Auckland, Hawkes Bay, Central Otago and Wairarapa, there aren’t many cities in New Zealand that Richard hasn’t yet worked in. Richard officiates and entertains at
                <a href="https://www.djrichard.co.nz/weddings/dj-mc-for-same-sex-weddings/">
                 same sex weddings
                </a>
                also, so please contact him about yours.
               </p>
               <p>
                Contact DJ Richard to discuss your next event or celebration, or click here to download the
                <a href="https://www.djrichard.co.nz/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/Entertainment-Agreement.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">
                 DJ Richard Entertainment Agreement
                </a>
                .
               </p>
