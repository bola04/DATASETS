       The Group
       <span class="style22">
        <p>
         We are a very active and friendly group of people, from all walks of life,
       who all have one thing in common – a love of walking. Our base is the Crewe
 		and Nantwich area although anyone is welcome to choose us as their 
 		group wherever they live.
         <br/>
         You don't have to be a member to come on a few walks with us. You can try up to as 
 		many as three walks before deciding if you like us enough to join the Ramblers.
         <a href="https://www.ramblers.org.uk/join">
          <span target="_blank">
           <strong>
            Join.
           </strong>
          </span>
         </a>
         This link takes you to the
 		Ramblers web site.
        </p>
        <span class="style21">
         If you would like to walk with us
 		but not sure how to start try a SHORT WALK. Information in our walk programme.
         <br/>
         <p>
          <span class="style21">
           <strong>
            Check details for
            <a href="walks/index.htm" target="_blank">
             Next Walk.
            </a>
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <span class="style21">
          <strong>
           What do you need ?
          </strong>
         </span>
         <span class="style22">
          <p>
           Suitable walking boots/shoes, warm and waterproof clothing, a rucksack, picnic 
        lunch and drinks. If you don't have your own transport, you can be sure of a lift if 
        you can get to the initial meeting point. Things you might want to know about when joining 
        us for a walk, grades, meeting places, what to bring, fuel share arrangements etc.
           <a href="walks/InfoNotes1.htm" target="_blank">
            <strong>
             More.
