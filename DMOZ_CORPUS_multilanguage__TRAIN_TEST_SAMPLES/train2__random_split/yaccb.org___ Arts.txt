         <h1>
          <span style="font-size:36px;">
           <span style="font-family: allura;">
            Our 2018 Summer Concert Theme is:
           </span>
          </span>
          <br/>
         </h1>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="wsb-element-text" data-type="element" id="wsb-element-9dbcd9e5-0ea3-49b3-ade0-2a986327435b">
        <div class="txt ">
         <p>
          Friends and followers of the YACCB,
          <br/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <br/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Thank you for your continued support of the YACCB, and for following us on our website.  I want to give you a little preview of what music you will hear on our summer schedule for 2018.  Our theme this year is simply
          <em>
           "Water"
          </em>
          .  Many of my band members wondered how we could put together concerts with that kind of theme.  Well, as you will see, we've got a lot of material and quite a variety of music for your listening pleasure this year.
          <br/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <br/>
         </p>
         <p>
          We will be performing music about rivers, such as Shenandoah and the Platt River.  Other river-like music is not about a specific river, but about what a moving body of water can provide in the way of inspiration and beauty, such as a wonderful piece called River Trail Expedition.
          <br/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <br/>
         </p>
         <p>
          One of my favorites is a "Count Basie-like" feel of a spiritual called Deep River.  Others include music that is just associated with water, such as a medley of Beach Boys Hits, Sousa's march Manhattan Beach and a John Williams piece from Steven Spielberg's comedy "1941", called the March from 1941.  A Broadway hit called "Showboat" features the popular song "Old Man River".  Another movie soundtrack that will be performed is "Pearl Harbor".  And, a very entertaining piece about a steamboat race (true story) that took place on the Mississippi River, titled The Great Steamboat Race.
          <br/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <br/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Several soloists will be featured, both vocal and instrumental, in the following; Moon River, My Heart Will Go On from "Titanic", Beautiful Colorado and a piece featuring a narration to commemorate the life of Martin Luther King, Jr. called Until Justice Rolls Down Like Waters.   A few of our concerts that take place around the 4th of July will also feature patriotic music, highlighting World War II and our Armed Forces.
          <br/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <br/>
         </p>
         <p>
          We hope to see you and many of your friends at one or many of our concerts around the Mahoning Valley this summer.
          <br/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <br/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Thank you for reading and listening!
          <br/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Joseph Pellegrini, Conductor
          <br/>
         </p>
         <br/>
