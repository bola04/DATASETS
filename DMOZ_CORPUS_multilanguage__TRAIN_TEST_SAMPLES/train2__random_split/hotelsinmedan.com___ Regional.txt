It is the center of trade and industry on North West of Indonesia since the era of Dutch known as the source of coffee, cacao, tobacco, and rubber. After the independence Medan city has also been developing as one of the center of Sumatra's tourism business. As the capital city of North Sumatra Medan is the center tourism business organizer offering tour packages, transport service, tour guide service, hotel booking and other related travel sevices
