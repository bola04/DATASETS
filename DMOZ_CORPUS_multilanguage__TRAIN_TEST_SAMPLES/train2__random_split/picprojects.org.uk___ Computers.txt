         <h1 class="entry-title">
          Welcome to Picprojects
         </h1>
        </header>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          The PIC Projects website features projects that I’ve created since 2004.  They use Microchip PIC micro-controllers from the mid-range 12F and 16F ranges such as the 16F628A and 12F629, as well as the newer enhanced mid-range devices like the 16F1823.
         </p>
         <p>
          All the projects have been built and tested and there is lots of information on the pages if you want to have a go at making them.  The programmer ready HEX files are all free to use and in most cases the source code is available too.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
