              <h2>
               About the PPWC
              </h2>
             </td>
            </tr>
            <tr>
             <td align="left" class="producttextnopicDIV" valign="top">
              <p>
               The PPWC constitution ensures that our union will be controlled by rank-and-file workers. We believe in full voice for our membership. Elections for PPWC officers are by referendum secret ballot vote at the plant level.
              </p>
              <p>
               The PPWC places emphasis on the membership not on top officials. We keep new people moving into office to prevent stagnation.
              </p>
              <p>
               There are no trusteeship or administration provisions in our constitution enabling national union officers to come into a local and throw the local officers out of office or interfere with any decisions made by the local union.
              </p>
              <p>
               Each local union elects a member to sit on the National Executive Board (NEB) ensuring that the local unions have direct involvement in the operation of the national union. The NEB is charged with the administration and management of the national union between conventions.
              </p>
             </td>
