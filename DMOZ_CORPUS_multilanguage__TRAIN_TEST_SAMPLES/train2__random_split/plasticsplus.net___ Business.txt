          Advertising, marketing and promotion is essential for any business. These essential aspects are often achieved through the proper placement and use of effective signage. These signs may highlight specials, present information about the business, or list future events. Whatever the case, these signs and displays become an important part of relaying information that is essential to the maintenance and growth of your business. When choosing the right custom made acrylic displays for your business, trust in the quality products you will find at Plastics Plus Inc. We give our customers exactly what our name implies: plastics, such as sign holders and displays, plus great customer service. All of our quality products are made in the USA at our fabrication facility in Bismarck North Dakota.

          Since 1998, we have specialized in standard and custom plastic fabrication, along with offering the largest variety of acrylic sign holders. If you do not find a standard style that will satisfy your needs, we would be happy to work with you to create a custom design that will work for you. Custom design options are available to you on all of our products, this includs, but is not limited to, ballot boxes, donation boxes, business card holders. These items are extremely versatile and hold a variety of uses throughout any business. Custom designs do not have to be expensive. We will work to find the most cost effective way to meet your display needs and we also offer free estimates.

Whether you require a large number of items or only a few, we allow you to order the number you need. We will not restrict you to ordering by th bundle or carton quantity. Our ability to get the job done efficiently with the quality and attention you deserve, is not dependent on the number of items you order. If you have any questions about a specific product or are looking for something you don’t see on our website – feel free to give us a call at 1-877-858-5751 and we will be more than happy to assist you.
