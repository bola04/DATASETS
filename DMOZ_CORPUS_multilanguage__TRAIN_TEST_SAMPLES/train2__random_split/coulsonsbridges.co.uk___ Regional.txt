       <h1>
        Wooden bridges for lakes, streams and large ponds
       </h1>
       <p>
        <br/>
        A range of pedestrian footbridges that can be placed over any stream or pond in a garden to make a distinctive eye catching feature.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Designed and made by Coulson's Bridges, the bridges are of a laminated construction which gives them great strength and elegance. Constructed from selected joinery timber, they are available in six styles - Monet, Chinese, Japanese, Edinburgh, Monet plus 1 and Nikko.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Bridges can be produced to the customers own design, in any arch form. They can be made to lengths of up to 40 ft (12m). Widths of up to 4 ft (1.2m) are available.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        They are finished in high quality wood preservatives in almost any colour to suit the customer's requirements. This ensures very good protection for the wood and allows easy maintenance.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Bridges are delivered as a complete kit, in the form of two matching wooden girders, and come with full simple to follow construction notes. A handyman or builder can be employed by the customer to carry out the construction.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        We are always willing to discuss designs and details, as a personal service is a mark of our company.
       </p>
