      <div class="sec-title">
       <h2>
        Your Fishing Vacation Awaits
       </h2>
       <h3>
        There’s nothing like the sound of casting your line into a private lake.
       </h3>
       <h4>
        Or being on the open water, experiencing the world, one bite at a time.
       </h4>
       <h4>
        Disconnect from busy life, and restore your soul.
       </h4>
       <br/>
       <p>
        Between the piles of paperwork, your rising blood pressure, and the stress that comes from being incredibly busy, you know it’s time to get away, back to the kind of lifestyle that allows you to breathe.
       </p>
       <p>
        Oak Lake Lodge provides you with a secluded, safe, nature-filled environment, while offering lodging comforts you are used to at a hotel. Housekeeping service, delivering your coffee in the morning, and the supply of fresh linens and towels. (So you don’t have to travel with a tent, food, water, or sheets.)
       </p>
       <p>
        As part of the English River System, we are a fly-in only fishing lodge. Because of the waterfalls, the lake, and the terrain, most boats (and fishermen) cannot access our area.
       </p>
       <p>
        The oxygen and nutrient-rich protected habitat is amazing for smallmouth bass and walleye. It also means that our lake is private — and the only people fishing in it are our guests.
       </p>
       <p>
        You don’t pay extra for your meals, your excursions, or your turn-down service. (Your housekeeping, the roaring fireplace on colder mornings, coffee delivery, hospitable staff, and flight over hundreds of miles of lush, Canadian forest from Vermilion Bay, Ontario, to Oak Lake Lodge is included as well.)
        <br/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Your vacation will be filled with fish, fun, and familiar comforts you love.
       </p>
       <p>
        Let us take care of everything while you get back to what makes you happy.
       </p>
       <p>
        Oak Lake customers rave about three things: the fishing, the food, and the hospitality.
       </p>
       <p>
        Start dreaming today about your trip to Oak Lake Lodge.
       </p>
