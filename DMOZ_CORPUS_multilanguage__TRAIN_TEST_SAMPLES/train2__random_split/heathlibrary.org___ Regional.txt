                     <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                      <a href="http://www.heathlibrary.org/2018/07/overdrive-magazines-are-here.html">
                       Overdrive Magazines are Here!
                      </a>
                     </h3>
                     <div class="post-header">
                      <div class="post-header-line-1">
                      </div>
                     </div>
                     <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-3881775861651490215" itemprop="articleBody">
                      <table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;">
                       <tbody>
                        <tr>
                         <td style="text-align: center;">
                          <a href="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-1pXyfuPtMbo/W0TbQI2gRdI/AAAAAAAABLk/bFq-06yT_ukec6zH_BVE0sz38FLk1cbvACLcBGAs/s1600/overdrive%2Bmagazines.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;">
                           <img border="0" data-original-height="966" data-original-width="1600" height="193" src="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-1pXyfuPtMbo/W0TbQI2gRdI/AAAAAAAABLk/bFq-06yT_ukec6zH_BVE0sz38FLk1cbvACLcBGAs/s320/overdrive%2Bmagazines.JPG" width="320"/>
                          </a>
                         </td>
                        </tr>
                        <tr>
                         <td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;">
                          Overdrive magazines
                         </td>
                        </tr>
                       </tbody>
                      </table>
                      The first wave of 50 magazines is available in the CW MARS OverDrive catalog. Take a look at the "Magazine Room" for a list of the available titles:
                      <a href="https://cwmars.overdrive.com/collection/184768">
                       https://cwmars.overdrive.com/collection/184768
                      </a>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      Patrons can access them either through the
                      <a href="https://meet.libbyapp.com/">
                       Libby app
                      </a>
                      or through the
                      <a href="https://app.overdrive.com/">
                       OverDrive app
                      </a>
                      . They can be read online or downloaded for offline reading.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      Patrons can read a sample from any magazine before borrowing. To borrow, they have to be signed in normally.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      The magazines are all purchased under a "simultaneous use" agreement. This means every magazine is always available. There's no waiting. If someone wants to read a magazine, they can.

                     <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                      <a href="http://www.heathlibrary.org/2018/06/summer-reading-fun.html">
                       Summer Reading Fun
                      </a>
                     </h3>
                     <div class="post-header">
                      <div class="post-header-line-1">
                      </div>
                     </div>
                     <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-8305659362987984937" itemprop="articleBody">
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      </div>
                      Join us for rock-related crafts, stories, a geology hike, and some good old fashioned rock &amp; roll music! Sign up at the library! All ages welcome.
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-EBPXyMFDuxc/WxguQ9WwO5I/AAAAAAAABJ0/uM27jB4DccwjGPJdQJeZxRh2gR5jN1dTgCEwYBhgL/s1600/Libraries%2BRock%2B1.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" data-original-height="793" data-original-width="750" height="320" src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-EBPXyMFDuxc/WxguQ9WwO5I/AAAAAAAABJ0/uM27jB4DccwjGPJdQJeZxRh2gR5jN1dTgCEwYBhgL/s320/Libraries%2BRock%2B1.jpg" width="302"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-size: small;">
                       <b>
                        Friday, July 6th, 10:30:
                       </b>
                      </span>
                      Children's music with local musician Karen Brooks. We'll make musical instruments, read a story, sing, and dance! At the Heath Free Public Library, outside if weather is clement.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       Friday, July 13th, 11:00:
                      </b>
                      Hike to
                      <a href="http://www.newenglandwaterfalls.com/ma-tanneryfalls.html">
                       Tannery Falls in Savoy
                      </a>
                      with local naturalist Bob Richardson. Meet at the Heath Library at 10:30 to caravan.
                      <i>
                       Children absolutely must be accompanied by a responsible adult.
                      </i>
                      Families and all ages welcome! Find out why this beautiful area is so special geologically.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       Friday, July 20, 10:30:
                      </b>
                      Musical Story Hour at the Heath Library. We'll make musical instruments, read stories, sing, and have a snack.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       Friday, July 27, 10:30:
                      </b>
                      Beatles sing-along with 
 Ed Fuller (Lyra's husband) at the Music Garden at Heath Elementary School (in the School Library if rainy.) We'll have stories and children's songs, including kid-friendly Beatles favorites!

                      <br/>
                      <div style="text-align: center;">
                       <b>
                        Sign up for the Summer Reading Program!
                       </b>
                      </div>
                      <blockquote class="tr_bq">
                       Children ages 12 and under:
 pick up a reading log from Don or Lyra. 
 Keep track of the books you read with your family this summer. 
 At the end of the summer, 
 bring back your log to the library to receive prizes and enter a free raffle!
                      </blockquote>
                      <br/>
                      ~Libraries Rock! is sponsored by the Friends of the Heath Library, the Massachusetts Library System, the Boston Bruins, and the Massachusetts Board of Library Commissioners.~
