         <h1 class="main_title">
          Welcome
         </h1>
         <div class="post-content clearfix">
          <div class="entry_content">
           <p>
            <a href="http://northshorescouts.ca/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/c-header1.png">
             <img alt="" class="alignnone wp-image-262" height="81" src="http://northshorescouts.ca/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/c-header1.png" title="c-header" width="605"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="color: #000000;">
             <strong>
              Welcome
             </strong>
             to the North Shore Scouts website! Here you will find information on the various groups who meet in North and West Vancouver as well as general Scouting information and registration details.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="color: #000000;">
             Please call or email Dawn Martin-Smith to register for Scouts!
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="color: #000000;">
             Email:
             <span style="color: #0000ff;">
              <a href="mailto:hawenc@yahoo.ca">
               <span style="color: #0000ff;">
                scouter.dawn.nsa@gmail.com
               </span>
              </a>
             </span>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="color: #000000;">
             Phone: 604.250.1756
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://northshorescouts.ca/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/c-bar.png">
             <img alt="" class="alignnone wp-image-268" height="8" src="http://northshorescouts.ca/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/c-bar.png" title="c-bar" width="604"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="color: #000000;">
             <strong>
              Scouts Canada
             </strong>
             is the country’s leading youth organization. For more than 100 years, we have brought a world of adventure, outdoor experience and friendship to 17 million Canadian youth.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="color: #000000;">
             Scouts have a lot of fun discovering new things and experiences they wouldn’t have elsewhere. Along the way they develop into capable, confident and well-rounded individuals, better prepared for success in the world.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="color: #000000;">
             Scouting offers a world where you can discover the best in yourself and the best in others. Dollar for dollar, our programs provide significant value. They run all year round and offer adventures that youth will remember for a lifetime!
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://northshorescouts.ca/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/word-mark-4.png">
             <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-272" height="148" src="http://northshorescouts.ca/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/word-mark-4.png" title="word-mark 4" width="144"/>
            </a>
           </p>
