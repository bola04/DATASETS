      <h3>
       About The Site
      </h3>
      <p>
       This site has been developed to help Councillors and young people in Norfolk understand more about each other's lives.
      </p>
      <p>
       Every hour on Tuesday 18th October 5 county councillors and 5 young people from Norfolk will be taking a photo using a Nokia 6230i mobile phone supplied by O2. They will add a caption and MMS it to this website.
      </p>
      <p>
       On the home page you will see the most recent pictures from each person. Clicking on a picture will take you to a page containing all the pictures from that person.
      </p>
      <p>
       The Archive page will show you all the pictures taken so far in the event.
