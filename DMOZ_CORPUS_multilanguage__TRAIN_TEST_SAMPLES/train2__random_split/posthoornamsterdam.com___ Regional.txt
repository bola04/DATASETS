                 <h3>
                  Historical 17th Century Prinsengracht canal house
                 </h3>
                 <p>
                  On one of the most beautiful Amsterdam canals, the PRINSENGRACHT, you will find
                  <em>
                   THE POSTHOORN
                  </em>
                  , an Amsterdam Canal house and B&amp;B with 3 Luxurious rooms.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  Imagine having an unforgettable stay in a historical 17th century canal house, right opposite of the most popular area of Amsterdam, THE JORDAAN. You will find most points of interest within walking distance of the house and there is an abundance of small pubs, cozy restaurants and quant shops all around you. To give you a few examples, the famous Noordermarkt, with its twice weekly market is just opposite, the Anne Frank House just a 5 minutes walk and ‘THE NINE STREETS’ a mere 10 minutes walk. In a way, Amsterdam is just a village and you will be staying right in the middle of it!
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  If you are looking for a Amsterdam Canal Boutique B&amp;B with fully equiped
                  <a href="http://www.posthoornamsterdam.com/rooms/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
                   luxurious rooms
                  </a>
                  , you will not find a better place. Located in the City Center Canal District and with the Jordaan situated just across the canal,
                  <em>
                   THE POSTHOORN
                  </em>
                  is the perfect place to enjoy the best Amsterdam has to offer.
                 </p>
