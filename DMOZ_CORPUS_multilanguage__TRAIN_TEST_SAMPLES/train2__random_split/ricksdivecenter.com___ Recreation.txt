     <h2>
      Learn to dive at Rick's Dive 'N Travel Center
     </h2>
     <p class="center">
     </p>
     <!--<h2>Want to buy this special online?<br>Click <a class="bluefont" href="https://www.facebook.com/ricksdivecenter/app_251458316228">here</a> to purchase from our Facebook page.</h2>-->
     <!--<p class="center"><a href="ruth.html"><img border="0" src="images/RUTH.jpg" width="779" height="552"></a></p>-->
     <p>
      Scuba Diving is an ageless recreation that offers something for everyone. It can be enjoyed by your entire family; as young as ten years old, and as old, as well, that's hard to say because Rick's has divers in their seventies and even eighties who are still enjoying this sport. It can be relaxing or adrenaline pumping. It's easy diving or you can make an exotic travel adventure out of it. Very few activities have such versatility. Come "DIVE IN" with the Pro's at Rick's Dive 'N Travel Center and let our staff of instructors introduce you to the wonderful underwater world.
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <h3 class="orangefont center">
      At Rick's we are
      <br/>
      <em>
       "THE STANDARD OF EXCELLENCE"
      </em>
     </h3>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
      There is more to learning to scuba dive than just "enrolling in a scuba class" and all dive courses are not created equal. At Rick's, SCUBA diving is a lifestyle that can offer you many years of excitement and enjoyment.
     </p>
     <p>
      We have designed our diving programs to be educational and informative, yet the emphasis is on fun. Our onsite deep pool also allows our students not only the opportunity to learn SCUBA, but to practice dive skills and try new dive equipment. Having our classrooms and pool on premises makes learning to dive more convenient and enjoyable.
     </p>
     <p>
      Rick's is Arkansas' foremost PADI 5-Star Instructor Development Center so our PADI Instructors and Divemasters are the cornerstones to a great underwater experience. They have more training, certifications, and experience training divers than almost any other group of instructors, anywhere.
     </p>
