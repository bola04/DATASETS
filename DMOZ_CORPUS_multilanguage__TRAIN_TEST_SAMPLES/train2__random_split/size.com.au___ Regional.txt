        <div class="row">
         <div class="col-sm-6">
          <blockquote>
           “If one should desire to know whether a kingdom is well governed, if its morals are good or bad, the quality of its music will furnish the answer.” Confucius
          </blockquote>
         </div>
         <!-- / column -->
         <div class="col-sm-6">
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           Size Music specialize in new orchestral musical instrument sales, repairs and hire, and take great pride in providing high quality student instruments for hire or rental to schools and students throughout Australia.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           Instruments include violin, viola, cello, double bass, flute, clarinet, oboe, saxophone, trumpet, French horn and trombone to name a few. Quality brands stocked include Yamaha, Bach, Pearl, Buffet, Gliga, Hagen Weise, Heinrich Gill, Otto Jos Klier, Schroeder and more.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           We also stock a large range of accessories, print and sheet music which are available to search and purchase on this website. Accessories include strings, rosin, shoulder rests, cases, straps, reeds, music stands, instrument stands, valve oil, mouthpieces, cleaning cloths and swabs. If you can’t find what you’re looking for, give us a call and our expert staff can help.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           We can provide expert advice on general maintenance and repairs, so feel free to drop in with your instrument for a free quote.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           Our professional approach and high level of service is second to none. Take a look around our website for prices, advice and lots of other information.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           Size Music is an Australian family owned company, operating successfully for over 40 years in the music industry in Parkside, Adelaide, South Australia.
          </p>
