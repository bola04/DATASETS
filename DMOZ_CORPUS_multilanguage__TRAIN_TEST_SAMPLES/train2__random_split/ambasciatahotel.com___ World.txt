            <div class="testo">
             The newly constructed Hotel Ambasciata is conveniently located near the Venezia Mestre railway station in a quiet, residential quarter of the city.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             Public transport to and from the splendid city of Venice is guaranteed 24 hours per day and the Hotel offers night-time reception services so our guests might also enjoy Venice by moonlight.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             Thanks to our strategic position in the heart of Mestre, the Hotel Ambasciata is the perfect base for visiting not only Venice, but also other famous Triveneto cities, like Padua, Treviso, Vicenza, and Verona. Our proximity to the railway station and important Italian motorways (the A4 that crosses the entire Po river plain), allows tourists to easily embark for other interesting destinations: Grado, Aquileia, and the Veneto Dolomite district, even as far as Florence and Rome! The Hotel Ambasciata offers plenty of free parking, even for your entire departure date, while a delicious buffet breakfast is served daily in our cosy dining room.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             The Hotel Ambasciata also offers free WiFi internet, shuttle service upon request, on-site car rental, and facilities for otherwise-abled guests, while the furnishing, window treatments, fabrics and colours have been tastefully selected to welcome our guests with a sensation of serene well-being and comfort that makes them feel right at home.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             Cordiality and personalised services based on careful attention paid to your every need are the fruit of years of experience as hoteliers: you are above all our Guest, and for this reason your satisfaction and comfort always comes first...

              <strong>
               By train:
              </strong>
              <br/>
              Get off at Venezia-Mestre and just leave the station turn left. The third street on your right is Via Fagarè and after 100 meters you will find the Hotel Ambasciata.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <strong>
               By car:
              </strong>
              <br/>
              From the exit of the A4 motorway take the Mestre ring road until exit Via Miranese. Follow the signs to the station, with the station in front of you turn right and then the third street right is Via Fagarè otherwise pass leaving station on your left and turn the third street right is Via Fagarè.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <strong>
               By plane from Venice:
              </strong>
              <br/>
              The Marco Polo International Airport is only 8 km far from the Ambasciata Hotel and is served by both the ACTV public transport (bus nr.15 stopping at the railway station of Mestre) and by private taxi (cost about € 30.00).
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <strong>
               By plane from Treviso:
              </strong>
              <br/>
              The San Giuseppe International airport is about 20km far from Venice Mestre. By taxi, the approximate cost is about 60 euros.
