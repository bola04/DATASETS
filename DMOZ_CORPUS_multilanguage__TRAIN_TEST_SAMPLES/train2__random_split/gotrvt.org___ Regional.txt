         <h2>
          <strong>
           Changing the World, One Girl at a Time…
          </strong>
         </h2>
         <p>
          At
          <strong>
           Girls on the Run Vermont
          </strong>
          , we believe that every girl deserves the opportunity to celebrate what makes her unique, to know that she matters, and to believe that her dreams are always within reach.
         </p>
         <p>
          Girls on the Run is a non-profit with a mission to inspire girls to be joyful, healthy, and confident. By using an interactive curriculum that creatively integrates running, we help to positively impact girls in 3rd to 8th grade. In addition to the life-long lessons learned, the 10-week program culminates with the girls being physically and emotionally prepared to complete a celebratory 5k. The goal of the program is to unleash confidence through accomplishment while establishing a lifetime appreciation of health, compassion, and community.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          Girls on the Run is an Independent Council of
          <a href="www.girlsontherun.org" target="_blank">
           Girls on the Run International
          </a>
          , which has a network of over 225 councils across the United States and Canada.
         </p>
