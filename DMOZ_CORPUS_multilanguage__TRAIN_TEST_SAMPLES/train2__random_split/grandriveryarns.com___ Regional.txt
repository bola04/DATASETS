        <p align="center">
         <b>
          How to move around on this web site.
          <br/>
         </b>
         You are on Home page which has an list of Kits, Books, Patterns, Yarn and other 
 	stuff
         <br/>
         When you open any of these headings you get details such as what kind of 
 	kits.
         <br/>
         When you open the kind of kit you are interested in you will see what 
 	choices we have in  that kind of kit for example women's sweaters kits.
 	When you click on one of the sweaters you will get full details and a chance 
 	to order whatever it is. Also
         <b>
          note
         </b>
         that at the top of each page there 
 	are links to take you back a step or two. If you are hopping around the web 
 	site and see things you like I suggest that you write the name of the page 
 	(located in the tool bar at the top of your page) down because you may have 
 	trouble remember where you were. You can always pop the item into the order 
 	page and when you are finished you can delete anything you don't want.
