   <blockquote>
    <center>
     <b>
      Black Creek Consulting, Ltd. is an asset management and liquidation
 consulting firm specializing in receivership management. The in-depth,
 practical, professional experience of Black Creek includes mortgage and
 statutory receivership management, bankruptcy proceedings, loan workouts,
 financial analysis, construction and development management, problem real
 estate marketing, and tailored reporting.
     </b>
     <p>
      <b>
       Through seasoned experience and complementary talents, Black Creek
 offers cost effective, responsible, independent management of receiverships,
 trustee estates, problem assets and portfolios. Black Creek also provides
 portfolio management and real estate problem solving consultation.
      </b>
     </p>
     <p>
      <b>
       Black Creek is headquartered near Annapolis, Maryland, a location
 that allows the company to easily access and address the MidAtlantic region
 from Pennsylvania to the Carolinas.
      </b>
