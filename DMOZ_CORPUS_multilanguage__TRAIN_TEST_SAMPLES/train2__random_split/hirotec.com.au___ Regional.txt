         <h2 style="margin-bottom: 25px;">
          Welcome to Hirotec
         </h2>
         <p>
          Hirotec is a privately owned national company specialising in the
 maintenance and engineering of integrated technical solutions comprising
 Mechanical, Electrical, Fire and Energy services. We have a highly
 skilled building maintenance workforce supported by an
 experienced and successful management team enabling the formation of strong,
 loyal and mutually beneficial relationships.
         </p>
         <p>
          We offer a
 diversified service offering across multiple market sectors including
 education, health, utilities, transport, arts and recreation. This includes a full range of
 facilities services incorporating asset life cycle and
 energy audits, systems design, installation and refurbishment, project
 engineering and management, building maintenance management and survey,
 24/7 breakdown response and technical support, commissioning and
 re-tunning, CMMS System and data integration and remote facility and
 asset monitoring.
         </p>
         <p>
          Employing over 250 highly trained and experienced staff throughout
 Australia, our customer base includes  property and facility managers, property owners, industrial
 corporations and government departments
