           <h4 class="banner">
            Section 5 of the Village of Chevy Chase
           </h4>
          </h4>
          <p>
           Established in 1922, Section 5 of the Village of Chevy Chase became an
   incorporated municipality on June 25, 1982. Section 5 is a small, quiet
   community of 227 houses and one
           <a href="http://www.lafermerestaurant.com/">
            restaurant
           </a>
           .
   It is a town known for its tree-lined streets, porches, and friendly
   neighborhood atmosphere.
          </p>
          <p>
           The section is administered by an elected five-member Town Council, members
   of which are elected for two (2) year terms of office.  Elections take place
   in the spring at our annual meeting.  There is one paid part-time
   employee, the Town Manager, who acts as a liaison between the Council and
   residents.
