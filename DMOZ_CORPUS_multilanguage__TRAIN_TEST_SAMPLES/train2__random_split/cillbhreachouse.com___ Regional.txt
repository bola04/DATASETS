    <h4>
     <div class="fLetter">
      L
     </div>
     ocated in one of Dingle's most desirable areas due to the expansive views that it boasts over Dingle Bay and of the mountains that encase Dingle Town, Cill Bhreac House is a charming and friendly, family-run bed and breakfast that is a popular destination amongst visitors to Dingle. Located on the Slea Head Drive and at the start of the Dingle Way walking trail, Cill Bhreac House offers guests a scenic and tranquil place to stay that is a mere 1km (10-15 minute scenic waterfront walk) from the hustle and bustle of Dingle town centre.
    </h4>
    <h4>
     <div class="fLetter">
      B
     </div>
     reakfast is a highlight of many visitor's stay. Home-baked items along with locally sourced and free-range produce dominate a menu that contains delights such as Dingle Bay Atlantic smoked salmon and scrambled eggs, homemade pancakes filled with fresh fruit and drizzled with maple syrup, porridge infused with Bailey's liquor, as well as more traditional offerings such as the Irish breakfast and cheese and fruit platters.
    </h4>
    <h4>
     <div class="fLetter">
      A
     </div>
     distinguishing feature of our bed and breakfast is the picturesque relaxation areas that are dotted throughout the house that are available for guest use. Each of these areas also commands breathtaking views over Dingle Bay and vibrant Dingle town.
    </h4>
    <h4>
     <div class="fLetter">
      A
     </div>
     t no additional cost we also provide guests with a number of
     <strong>
      complimentary / free services
     </strong>
     such as wireless internet access, off-street private car parking, pick-up/drop-off service from local bus stop, and storage garage (e.g. for bicycles, fishing gear).
