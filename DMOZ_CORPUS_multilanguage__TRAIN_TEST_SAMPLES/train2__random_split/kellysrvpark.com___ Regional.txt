                   <h1 style="text-align: left;">
                    <strong>
                     Pet-Friendly, Year-Round RV Park for those Who Enjoy the Outdoors
                    </strong>
                   </h1>
                   <p style="text-align: left;">
                    Bring your RV or motor home to Kelly's RV/MH Park for an overnight stay or become a resident of our friendly community with
                    <a href="events.html" target="_self">
                     fun events
                    </a>
                    . Open for more than two decades and easily accessible from both I-10 and I-75, our family-run RV park in White Springs, Florida features 20 acres of oversized sites that can accommodate up to a 55-foot trailer or motor home.
                   </p>
                   <p style="text-align: left;">
                    Many of the RV site are paved and all sites contain electric, water and sewer. There are also ample tent sites located close to restrooms with showers. We offer daily, weekly, or monthly
                    <a href="amenities-and-rates.html" target="_self">
                     rates
                    </a>
                    (lowest in the area) and cabin rentals. Pets are welcome under our pet
                    <a href="pet-policies.html" target="_self">
                     guidelines.
                    </a>
                    Feel free to invite your children or grandchildren for a visit.

                            <a href="attractions.html" target="_self">
