             <ul class="captions">
              <li>
               Fairfax Village Neighborhood Center, Fort Belvoir, VA: LEED-NC process management for this new 3,770 sf community center designed by Torti Gallas as part of the U.S. Army's Residential Communities Initiative. This project earned LEED Platinum certification in 2009, becoming the second LEED Platinum project in Virginia, and the first building on a U.S. Military installation to be certified as LEED Platinum.
              </li>
              <li>
               George Howard Building Renovation, Ellicott City, MD: SDC provided LEED process management for a building renovation and interior fit-out by CMSS Architects for a three-story, 89,043 sf office building for Howard County Government. This project achieved LEED-NC Silver certification in November 2011. SDC also provided LEED O+M Consulting as a service directly to Howard County.
              </li>
              <li>
               Overlook at Monarch Mills, Columbia, MD: The new development by Grimm + Parker rejuvenated this affordable housing community. SDC provided Green Communities process management for the residences as well as LEED-NC Gold-level process management for the clubhouse. It also won Best Large Affordable Housing Project from the 2013 Housing Association of Nonprofit Developers Housing.
              </li>
              <li>
               Washington Nationals Youth Baseball Academy, Washington, DC: This 17,375 sf training center was designed to provide a safe environment for children in grades 3-12 east of the Anacostia River to learn and play baseball. SDC provided LEED process management services directly to the Owner, and the project achieved LEED-NC Silver-level certification in January 2015.
              </li>
              <li>
               National Rural Utilities Cooperative Finance Corporation (NRUCFC) Headquarters, Dulles, VA: SDC provided LEED-NC Gold-level process management for this 119,851 sf office building designed by KGD Architecture, and it achieved certification in 2013. SDC also provided ENERGY STAR Consulting directly to the Owner for this same building.
              </li>
              <li>
               American Legacy Foundation, Washington, DC: This 45,000 sf office building renovation designed by OTJ Architects also includes an auditorium and an outdoor garden with seating. SDC provided LEED-NC process management for this project and it achieved Gold-level certification in 2009. It also received the Washington Chapter AIA's Presidential Citation for Sustainable Design in 2008.
              </li>
              <li>
               Pentagon Library and Conference Center, Arlington, VA: This 115,000 sf renovation by BBG-BBGM converted this historic underground facility into a library and conference center. The project achieved LEED-NC Silver certification in 2008 and the Closing the Circle Award from the Office of the Federal Environmental Executive. Sustainable strategies included skylights, green roof, and user education.
              </li>
             </ul>
