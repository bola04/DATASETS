          <p class="mainText">
           The No 1 Live Music Bar in the Scottish Borders 
                 offers a warm welcome to the Tavern Bar. The bar is set on the 
                 beach front in the small fishing town of Eyemouth on the East 
                 Coast of Scotland.  The Tavern Bar is a family-run bar and 
                 enjoys a large Beer Courtyard and access to a children's play-park 
                 overlooking Eyemouth Bay and so is the ideal The Tavern is also host to some of the best live music 
                 ever heard in the Scottish Borders from solo acts, duos, rock 
                 &amp; tribute bands and discos.

         away, seafood takes pride of place on our menu.  The superb 'Poo Tae 
         Salad' and a 'Muckle Heighmooth' Haddie &amp; Chips deep fried in our 
         special Belhaven 
         Beer Batter as specialities.

           The Tavern is able to cater for private functions such as wedding 
         receptions, funeral,  christenings and birthday parties. Our 
         function room has a capacity of 100 people, contains a private lounge bar and 
         boasts picturesque views of Eyemouth bay.  We also have our own
