       Your Joliet, IL Heating and Air Conditioning Company

      <p>
       Since 1946, Johansen &amp; Anderson Inc has provided quality HVAC services to Joliet, IL and the Will County area. Our services cover installation, repair and maintenance on heating and air conditioning systems including, boilers, furnaces, heat pumps, ductless mini split systems, refrigeration equipment and more. Our founders were helping families stay comfortable throughout the year around the time the soldiers were returning from World War II. Our current staff remains dedicated to the integrity and skill of the original owners, and we stay up to date with advancing technology.
      </p>
      <p>
       Our HVAC specialists rank among the top 5% in the state certified by the NATE (North American Technical Excellence) committee. Whether you’re looking for a way to keep your family cooled down during a hot summer day, or you own a business and need the right heating system—or perhaps the right refrigeration equipment—you can trust us. Call Johansen &amp; Anderson Inc today, sleep tight tonight.

         <p>
          Low indoor air quality has turned into something of an epidemic in the U.S. Often, the air inside a building is three to four times worse than the air outside! The reason for this is that airborne contaminants from indoor sources are easily trapped within buildings because they have efficient heat sealing on them that also prevent the movement of fresh air.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          But there are solutions to this dilemma, and our specialists are ready to help you with them. We install air purifiers, air filters, humidifiers, dehumidifiers, energy recovery ventilators, and more. Contact us today to learn how we can boost your residential air quality.
