         <h2 class="mceTemp">
          <span style="color: #15557e;">
           About David
          </span>
         </h2>
         <p class="mceTemp">
          With over 40 years hands-on experience gained by treating leading national and international Martial artists, and having practiced in a number of leading clinics across the country, you can be assured that you are in the hands of a very capable multi-disciplined therapist.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          He has been taught his techniques to numerous other qualified therapists on national courses and was a guest instructor at The Complimentary Therapies College of Wales in Cardiff.
         </p>
         <p>
          Due to popular demand, the highly successful MyOsteo clinic opened in 1999.
         </p>
         <p>
          David specialises in musculoskeletal and nerve related problems, using Remedial massage, Manipulation, Sports Injury Treatments and Orthotics
         </p>
         <h2>
          <span style="color: #15557e;">
           Restoring Comfortable Movement
          </span>
         </h2>
         <p>
          In order to restore comfortable movement that is sustainable, you need to be treated by a range of safe and effective therapies that treat the source of the problem rather than merely the symptoms felt.
         </p>
         <p>
          Although the website is segregated into different areas of expertise, it is important to realise that most injuries will involve more than one aspect as the body components are interconnected and automatically try to compensate for each others problem.
         </p>
         <p>
          David will carry out an assessment of the whole body structure and postural implications, as well as giving post-treatment advice on stretching &amp; exercises, in order to keep you capable and enjoying life through well-being.
         </p>
         <p>
          The myOsteo name is derived from the medical terms for muscle (myo) and bone (osteo).
         </p>
