           </a>
          </h2>

           by
           <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="" itemtype="https://schema.org/Person">

            <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-3798 alignright" data-attachment-id="3798" data-comments-opened="0" data-image-description="" data-image-meta='{"aperture":"0","credit":"","camera":"","caption":"","created_timestamp":"0","copyright":"","focal_length":"0","iso":"0","shutter_speed":"0","title":"","orientation":"0"}' data-image-title="Unstuck_updated" data-large-file="https://mattheerema.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/Unstuck_updated.jpg" data-medium-file="https://mattheerema.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/Unstuck_updated-210x300.jpg" data-orig-file="https://mattheerema.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/Unstuck_updated.jpg" data-orig-size="350,500" data-permalink="https://mattheerema.com/productivity-business-management/2018/05/the-second-most-important-book-gets-a-follow-up/attachment/unstuck_updated/" height="500" sizes="(max-width: 350px) 100vw, 350px" src="https://mattheerema.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/Unstuck_updated.jpg" srcset="https://mattheerema.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/Unstuck_updated.jpg 350w, https://mattheerema.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/Unstuck_updated-210x300.jpg 210w" width="350"/>

           When people ask me for the number 1 book I recommend (apart from the Bible), in any context: business, leadership, productivity, ministry, etc…, immediately my answer is
           <a href="https://mattheerema.com/productivity-business-management/2014/03/one-book-read-year-whats-best-next-matt-perman/">
            What’s Best Next by Matt Perman
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           This is not simply because Matt is a friend and I’m a fan. This book is absolutely crucial in our current knowledge-work economy. Skill in dealing with information overload is a basic survival life skill in the age of the smartphone, and What’s Best Next compiles and synthesizes all the best material  on the subject.
           <a href="https://mattheerema.com/productivity-business-management/2014/03/one-book-read-year-whats-best-next-matt-perman/">
            Read more about my thoughts on the book here
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Today, the follow-up to WBN has dropped.
            <a href="https://amzn.to/2JEKC7i">
             “How to Get Unstuck” by Matt Perman
            </a>
            is a necessary next step and answers many of the questions that have come up over the past few years as I’ve consulted, advised, and mentored on the principles that WBN espouses.
           </strong>

           </a>
          </p>

          <p>
