         <p>
          <span style="font-size: 20px;">
           <span style="font-size: 24px;">
            About our Club
           </span>
           <br/>
           We are a local bass club in Tallahassee, FL.  We are affiliated with
           <a href="http://www.floridafederationnation.com/" target="">
            Florida B.A.S.S Federation Nation
           </a>
           and
           <a href="http://www.floridabassfederation.com/" target="">
            Florida Bass Federation
           </a>
           .  Our club meets once per month on the second Wednesday of the month at Beef 'O' Brady's (across from Whole Foods). At this meeting we discuss any club business and draw partners for the upcoming tournaments which are held on various lakes and rivers in the North Florida-South Georgia area. Our tournaments are generally held on the second weekend following the meeting. We fish ten tournaments, one per month January through October with each angler accruing points for the year. The leader in points after these nine tournaments is awarded the title of Bass Man of the Year and is awarded a cash prize. At the end of the year in November we fish a "Classic" tournament at which the top six anglers from the previous ten tournaments fish for prize money that is set aside from the fees collected to fish the monthly tournaments. The site for the classic is determined by the tournament committee and is a neutral site out of the area. For the non top five anglers there is also a cash prize awarded to the angler finishing highest in the classic.
          </span>

         </p>
