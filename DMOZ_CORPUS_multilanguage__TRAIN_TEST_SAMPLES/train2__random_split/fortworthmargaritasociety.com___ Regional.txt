     <h2>
      Children's Charities of Fort worth
     </h2>
     <div class="contain">
      <h3>
       Powered by Fort Worth Margarita Society
      </h3>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       This year Children’s Charities of Fort Worth is celebrating 30 years of service in Tarrant County. What began as heartfelt mission to provide toys at Christmas to underprivileged children has evolved in recent years to now giving back to more organizations, self-development programs and providing hope for some of Tarrant County’s under-served children.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       Our signature event, the Fort Worth Margarita Ball is set for November 17th
       <sup>
       </sup>
       at the Worthington Hotel. At the Margarita Ball, we raise funds to support 13 local children’s charities. Guests dress up for an evening of fundraising with dancing, entertainment and of course margaritas; all to benefit a worthy cause. More than 2,000 attendees are expected at the event, which offers ample opportunity for community partners to get involved.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       Children’s Charities of Fort Worth formed in 1989 as a 501(c)(3) charitable organization. The charity is supported through directors, members, sponsorships, donations and volunteers.
      </p>
