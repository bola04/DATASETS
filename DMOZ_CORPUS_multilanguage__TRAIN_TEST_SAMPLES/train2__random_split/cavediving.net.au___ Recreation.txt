               <td colspan="2" valign="top">
                <p>
                 Cave Diving Down Under is an online resource for Australian cave divers.
                </p>
                <p>
                 A comprehensive listing of
                 <a href="/index.php/caves">
                  cave diving sites across the country
                 </a>
                 is augmented by a great collection of
                 <a href="/index.php/vision">
                  underwater videos and still images
                 </a>
                 of Australia's caves and sinkholes.
                </p>
                <p>
                 Read of some of the
                 <a href="/index.php/history">
                  history of the sport
                 </a>
                 in the last 60 years or visit the
                 <a href="/index.php/shop">
                  online shop
                 </a>
                 to purchase cave diving books and DVDs. For those divers considering moving to the dark side recognised course options are outlined on the
                 <a href="/index.php/training">
                  training page
                 </a>
                 .
                </p>
                <p>
                 Join the
                 <a href="https://twitter.com/cavediveaus" target="_blank">
                  Twitter feed
                 </a>
                 to keep up to date with cave diving developments from around the globe.
                </p>
