                <div class="widget-content">
                 <h1>
                  <span style="line-height: 1.42;">
                   WELCOME
                  </span>
                 </h1>
                 <p>
                  <span style="font-size: 14px;">
                   The Australian-German Welfare Society (AGWS) is a not-for-profit member organisation dedicated to supporting community members with German cultural links in Australia.
                  </span>
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <span style="font-size: 14px;">
                   Main objectives of the organisation are to:
                  </span>
                 </p>
                 <ul>
                  <li>
                   <span style="font-size: 14px;">
                    <span style="font-weight: bold;">
                     help members
                    </span>
                    of the German community with financial or material problems
                   </span>
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   <span style="font-size: 14px;">
                    <span style="font-weight: bold;">
                     provide opportunities
                    </span>
                    for members to stay in touch with their 'old' culture, talk  in their mother tongue and take part in German traditions (eg. Mother's Day, Christmas)
                   </span>
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   <span style="font-size: 14px;">
                    <span style="font-weight: bold;">
                     support the elderly
                    </span>
                    (
                   </span>
                   <a class=" link" href="services/ttha/" style="font-size: 16px;">
                    nursing homes
                   </a>
                   ,
                   <a class=" link" href="services/friendly-visiting-service/" style="font-size: 16px;">
                    friendly visiting service
                   </a>
                   <span style="font-size: 14px;">
                    , computer courses)
                   </span>
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   <span style="font-size: 14px;">
                    <span style="font-weight: bold;">
                     promote German culture
                    </span>
                    by offering book and film clubs  in German language
                   </span>
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   <span style="font-size: 14px;">
                    <span style="font-weight: bold;">
                     assist young families
                    </span>
                    by offering a German playgroup  for pre-school children.
                   </span>
                  </li>
                 </ul>
                 <p>
                  <span style="font-size: 14px;">
                   We encourage you to become a member of the AGWS, participate in the many activities and/or get involved as a volunteer. Donations and/or a legacy in your will  are welcome.
                  </span>
                 </p>
