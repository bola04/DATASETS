         <p>
          <br/>
          <font size="2">
           The Stauer House is a place of Victorian charm filled 
             with unique bedrooms of luxury and comfort.  Centrally located 
             in the city of McGregor, Iowa, along the Mississippi river, you will 
             be able to visit surrounding attractions that include numerous 
             parks, antique, gift shops and book stores with many other fun 
             activities McGregor has to offer.
          </font>
         </p>
         <p>
          <font size="2">
           Enjoy relaxing accommodations in a recently renovated Victorian 
               home that was built in 1882 by local lumber baron Peter Stauer. 
               The property is listed on the National Register of Historic Places 
               and is situated just a short walk from the downtown area of 
               McGregor.
          </font>
         </p>
         <p>
          With numerous parks to enjoy, Pikes Peak State Park, Effigy 
               Mounds National Monument, Yellow River State Forest and the Upper 
               Mississippi River Wildlife Refuge. The area offers something for 
               everyone, beautiful scenery and picnicking, cross country skiing, 
               hiking, boating, antiquing, visiting historic sites. Spend time on 
               the mighty Mississippi or on one of the dozen trout streams close 
               by. You can fish, boat, watch for wildlife, we have numerous deer 
               and bald eagles year round. Maybe just relaxing and being close to 
               nature the area has something for everyone.
         </p>
         <p>
          <font size="2">
           Let the Stauer House staff provide you with 
               outstanding and comfortable lodging for your next trip to McGregor 
               or Marquette, Iowa, or your next getaway to Prairie du Chien, 
               Wisconsin.
          </font>
         </p>
