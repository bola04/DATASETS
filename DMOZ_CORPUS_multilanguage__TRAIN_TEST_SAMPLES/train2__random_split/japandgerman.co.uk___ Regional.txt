              <h1 style="font-size:16px;">
               Independent BMW motorbike servicing and motorcycle repair in London
              </h1>
              <p>
               Jap and German are one of London's longest running independent BMW motorbike specialists who provide BMW bike servicing and motorcycle repairs a mere 2 minute ride from the City of London. For motorcycle servicing we can arrange collection/delivery from your workplace within the city.
              </p>
              <p>
               We offer same day BMW bike servicing (or any other marque for that matter), stocking a comprehensive range of consumable items for your BMW motorbike - drop by and see for yourself! You can also
               <a href="/contact">
                book
               </a>
               your motorbike service online.
              </p>
              <p>
               Our workshop is equipped to the highest standard to accommodate routine servicing and motorcycle repair to major overhaul of all marques to include:
              </p>
              <ul>
               <li>
                Electronic carb balancing
               </li>
               <li>
                BMW diagnostic equipment inc coding/programming
               </li>
               <li>
                BMW workshop tools
               </li>
               <li>
                Electronic wheel balancing
               </li>
               <li>
                Welding
               </li>
               <li>
                All major tyre brands stocked
               </li>
              </ul>
              <p>
               We always have a selection of clean, pre-owned machines on display, you are also welcome to display your vehicle at our premises and we will sell it on your behalf for a modest fee. We also purchase bikes for cash.
              </p>
