        <p class="center light">

         <span class="cmidred2">

Typing can be effected directly in any Windows application (MS Word, Facebook, WhatsApp, SMS, Gmail, Twitter, PhotoShop, PageMaker, InDesign, CorelDraw, Skype, Excel, PowerPoint, etc.) by the simple press of an hotkey.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Azhagi+ is
         <a href="pics/azp-azplus-appwin-1.png" target="_blank" title="Sleek and Compact">
          sleek
         </a>
         and
         <a href="steps-portable.html" title="">
          portable
         </a>
         . It is very small (less than
         <span class="cmidred2">
          1 MB
         </span>
         ) yet power-packed with
         <a href="uu.html" target="_blank" title="">
          innovative
         </a>
         features.
         <!-- And, it is 100% free for both personal and commercial use.-->
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Azhagi+ has possessed the
         <a href="http://www.mazhalaigal.com/tamil/software/0806akr_azhagi.php#easiest-fastest" target="_blank" title="Superfast">
          fastest
         </a>
         of all Tamil transliteration schemes, ever since its inception in 2000. You can quickly extend Azhagi+ to transliterate in
         <span class="cmidred2">
          any Tamil font
         </span>
         by using a
         <a href="anytamilfont.html" target="_blank" title="Transliterate in ANY Tamil font">
          never-before-seen
         </a>
         feature. You can easily extend Azhagi+ to type in
         <span class="cmidred2">
          any language+font+keyboard
         </span>
         combination by availing a
         <a href="changecreate.html#create" target="_blank" title="">
          very special
         </a>
         feature. You can very well
         <a href="changecreate.html#change" target="_blank" title="">
          change
         </a>
         the existing key mappings, have custom hotkeys to toggle between languages, utilise an in-built font viewer, avail a
         <a href="plus.html#feats-converter" target="_blank" title="Tamil fonts converter">
          first of its kind
         </a>
         Tamil font converter which is extendable by the user himself/herself and do much
         <a href="#keys" title="">
          more...
         </a>
