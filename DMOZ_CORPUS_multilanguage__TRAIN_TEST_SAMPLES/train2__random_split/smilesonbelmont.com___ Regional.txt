       <img alt="Clifford L. Brock, DMD, PC" class="img-responsive pull-left bio-image" data-class-lg="pull-left" data-class-md="pull-left" data-class-sm="pull-left" data-class-xs="" data-class-xxs="" src="images/bio.jpg"/>
       <div class="text-wrap">
        <h3>
         Clifford L. Brock, DMD, PC
        </h3>
        <p>
         General Dentist
        </p>
        <p>
         Dr. Brock graduated from Oregon Health Sciences University's School of Dentistry. He began his family practice at its current location on Belmont Street over 20 years ago. He has taken extensive post-graduate training in cosmetic dentistry and especially enjoys providing his patients with more beautiful smiles. He is a graduate of the Las Vegas Institute (LVI) where he completed both the Advanced Posterior Aesthetics and Master Aesthetic Dentistry programs.
        </p>
        <p>
         Born in Indiana, Dr. Brock has lived in Oregon since 1959. He completed his Bachelor's Degree at the University of Oregon before attending dental school. His wife, Joan and he have 3 sons. Dr. Brock's interests include University of Oregon football (Go Ducks!), traveling, and spending time with family and friends.
        </p>

        </p>

      <div class="col-xs-10 col-xs-offset-1 col-sm-4 col-sm-offset-0">
       <article>
        <img alt="" class="img-responsive" src="images/icons/smile-whitening.png"/>
        <h3 class="text-center">
         Smile Whitening
        </h3>
        <p>
         Many everyday things like coffee, tea and wine can significantly dull teeth. Smile-whitening enables us to significantly brighten your smile for that dazzling white smile you deserve.
        </p>
       </article>
      </div>

      <div class="col-xs-10 col-xs-offset-1 col-sm-4 col-sm-offset-0">
       <article>
        <img alt="" class="img-responsive" src="images/icons/crowns-bridges.png"/>
        <h3 class="text-center">
         Crowns &amp; Bridges
        </h3>
        <p>
         Protect and keep badly decayed or fractured teeth while maintaining a beautiful smile. Made to perfectly match the shape and shade of your other teeth, they’re durable, strong, and undetectable.
        </p>
       </article>
      </div>
      <!-- Dental Implants -->
      <div class="col-xs-10 col-xs-offset-1 col-sm-4 col-sm-offset-0">
       <article>
        <img alt="" class="img-responsive" src="images/icons/dental-implants.png"/>
        <h3 class="text-center">
         Dental Implants
        </h3>
        <p>
         Dental implants are a great option for replacing missing teeth, improving chewing ability, eliminating bridges, and replacing dentures. Several implants may provide a base for fixed bridges or can be used to secure dentures.
        </p>
       </article>
      </div>

        <h3 class="text-center">
         Restorations
        </h3>
        <p>
         The common silver filling contains mercury. We use the highest-quality biocompatible, tooth-colored restorations to create healthy, beautiful smiles without all of the prep-work associated with traditional fillings.
        </p>
