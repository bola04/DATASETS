          <h2>
           CRATEX – Leading US Abrasive Manufacturer &amp; Supplier
          </h2>
          <p>
           Since 1927,
           <strong>
            CRATEX Manufacturing Co.
           </strong>
           has been supplying the United States market with high-quality industrial abrasive wheels, wheel mandrels, cones, points, blocks &amp; sticks, and other abrasive tools and accessories, which have found their purpose and application in numerous industries.
          </p>
          <p>
