          <h1>
           Our Services
          </h1>
          <h2>
           <a href="https://www.withoutatrace.com/reweaving/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.withoutatrace.com/reweaving/', 'Reweaving');" title="Reweaving">
            Reweaving
           </a>
          </h2>
          <p>
           Using the lost arts of
           <a href="https://www.withoutatrace.com/reweaving/french-weaving/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.withoutatrace.com/reweaving/french-weaving/', 'French weaving');" title="French Weaving">
            French weaving
           </a>
           ,
           <a href="https://www.withoutatrace.com/reweaving/inweaving/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.withoutatrace.com/reweaving/inweaving/', 'inweaving');" title="Inweaving">
            inweaving
           </a>
           and
           <a href="https://www.withoutatrace.com/reweaving/reknitting/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.withoutatrace.com/reweaving/reknitting/', 'reknitting');" title="Reknitting">
            reknitting
           </a>
           , we return damaged clothing to its original condition. Any garment can be ruined by even the smallest of holes or tears. Patching or sewing over the damaged area has never been a subtle repair, leaving the garment ruined. Consider the only true repair alternative for your valuable clothing: reweaving.
          </p>
          <p>
           Reweaving, also known as invisible mending, is an old-world skill of intricate hand weaving, practically becoming extinct as more businesses turn toward today’s technology. As one of only a handful of reweaving establishments in the entire United States, Without A Trace leads the reweaving field in quality and service with years of expertise.
          </p>
          <h2>
           <a href="https://www.withoutatrace.com/leather-suede-shearling/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.withoutatrace.com/leather-suede-shearling/', 'Leather, Suede &amp; Shearling');" title="Leather, Suede &amp; Shearling">
            Leather, Suede &amp; Shearling
           </a>
          </h2>
          <p>
           Without A Trace knows the proper approach for
           <a href="https://www.withoutatrace.com/leather-suede-shearling/cleaning-conditioning-refinishing/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.withoutatrace.com/leather-suede-shearling/cleaning-conditioning-refinishing/', 'cleaning');" title="Cleaning, Conditioning &amp; Refinishing">
            cleaning
           </a>
           your
           <a href="https://www.withoutatrace.com/leather-suede-shearling/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.withoutatrace.com/leather-suede-shearling/', 'leather, suede and shearling');" title="Leather, Suede &amp; Shearling">
            leather, suede and shearling
           </a>
           garments. It is not unusual for us to spend several hours per piece. Our expert tailors take great care in
           <a href="https://www.withoutatrace.com/leather-suede-shearling/repairs-alterations/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.withoutatrace.com/leather-suede-shearling/repairs-alterations/', 'repairs and alterations');" title="Repairs &amp; Alterations">
            repairs and alterations
           </a>
           . We can also protect your leather, suede and shearling garments with our
           <a href="https://www.withoutatrace.com/leather-suede-shearling/stainguard/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.withoutatrace.com/leather-suede-shearling/stainguard/', 'stainguard');" title="Stainguard">
            stainguard
           </a>
           treatment. We pride ourselves on many years of experience on the most expensive garments in the fashion industry. Just listen to what
           <a href="https://www.withoutatrace.com/leather-suede-shearling/penn-jillette-leather-jacket-repair/">
            <b>
             Penn Jillette
            </b>
           </a>
           has to say about our service.  We had no idea it was his leather jacket from the 70’s.
          </p>
          <h2>
           <a href="https://www.withoutatrace.com/fur/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.withoutatrace.com/fur/', 'Fur');" title="Fur">
            Fur
           </a>
          </h2>
          <p>
           We are experts in fur
           <a href="https://www.withoutatrace.com/fur/cleaning-glazing/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.withoutatrace.com/fur/cleaning-glazing/', 'cleaning and glazing');" title="Cleaning &amp; Glazing">
            cleaning and glazing
           </a>
           , as well as
           <a href="https://www.withoutatrace.com/fur/repairs-alterations-restyling/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.withoutatrace.com/fur/repairs-alterations-restyling/', 'repairs, alterations and restyling');" title="Repairs, Alterations &amp; Restyling">
            repairs, alterations and restyling
           </a>
           . Furs are cleaned delicately in powders and glazed to restore their luster. We replace linings, hooks and eyes and European hooks. Extend the life of your fur by
           <a href="https://www.withoutatrace.com/fur/storage/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.withoutatrace.com/fur/storage/', 'storing');" title="Storage">
            storing
           </a>
           it in our climate-controlled vault. We offer free pick up and delivery for fur storage in the Chicago area, or you can ship to us from anywhere in the country.
          </p>
          <h2>
           <a href="https://www.withoutatrace.com/leather-suede-shearling/purse-cleaning-repair/">
            Purses &amp; Handbags
           </a>
          </h2>
          <p>
           Keep your favorite purse in service with
           <a href="https://www.withoutatrace.com/leather-suede-shearling/purse-cleaning-repair/">
            cleaning and repair
           </a>
           . Purses are cleaned delicately by hand to maintain finish, shape and hardware. Leather purses can be refinished to cover light wear. Replace worn lining, fix broken straps, replace zippers and repair damage to add years to your purse’s life. Consider
           <a href="https://www.withoutatrace.com/leather-suede-shearling/stainguard/">
            stainguarding
           </a>
           your new purse to protect against water spotting and stains.
          </p>
          <h2>
           <a href="https://www.withoutatrace.com/leather-suede-shearling/ugg-boots-cleaning-repair/">
            Ugg Boots
           </a>
          </h2>
          <p>
           Nothing takes a beating during an average winter like your favorite boots. We specialize in
           <a href="https://www.withoutatrace.com/leather-suede-shearling/ugg-boots-cleaning-repair/">
            cleaning
           </a>
           Ugg-style shearling boots, removing months of dirt and salt buildup, and conditioning the skins to help maintain their softness. After cleaning, boots are
           <a href="https://www.withoutatrace.com/leather-suede-shearling/stainguard/">
            stainguarded
           </a>
           free of charge.
          </p>
          <h2>
           <a href="https://www.withoutatrace.com/custom-fur-headbands/">
            Custom Fur Headbands
           </a>
          </h2>
          <p>
           Add a warm and stylish
           <a href="https://www.withoutatrace.com/custom-fur-headbands/">
            fur headband
           </a>
           to your winter ensemble. A variety of styles and sizes are available.
          </p>
