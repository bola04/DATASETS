          <h3 style="font-size: 24px;">
           Escape to the magic of Kaya Vadi
          </h3>
          <p>
           You will find us in the picturesque Kaya valley which is between the harbour town of Fethiye and the famous beach resort of Olu Deniz.
          </p>
          <p>
           Holidays at Kaya Vadi are for those who want to get away from the hustle and bustle of busy holiday resorts! Our little spot of paradise nestles between fields and mountains but is within easy walking distance from the local village with its shops and restaurants.
          </p>
          <p>
           All the beaches, bars and nightlife are just a 10 or 15 minute taxi ride away.
          </p>
          <h3>
           Stress Free Holidays
          </h3>
          <p>
           We try to do everything to help you relax into your holiday straight away.
          </p>
          <p>
           One of the unique benefits of a holiday at Kaya Vadi is that the friendly owner and manager of the villas, Cem, is also licenced to offer airport transfers and car hire services. So, through Cem we will organise a private transfer for you from Dalaman airport to your villa. Whatever time you arrive Cem or one of his staff will be there to show you in to your villa which will be stocked with all the essentials to get your holiday off to a good start.
          </p>
          <p>
           For those that like to hire a car, Cem will arrange to have one delivered to your villa. If you prefer not to hire a car he will offer to run you to a local supermarket, and will wait to bring you back with your shopping. If you would like a private chauffeured day out to visit, for example, the famous ruins at Tlos, followed by lunch over the cool river at Saklikent, and maybe a visit to a carpet factory or a beach â then he will arrange it for you. Also, he is on hand every day to provide you with any help you may need to book other activities such as water sports, boat trips, horse riding, jeep safaris and so on.
          </p>
