       </p>
       <p>
        In 2016 we merged with Aberdeen firm
        <strong>
         Duthie Ward
        </strong>
        which has strengthened our hugely experienced family law team and enabled us to offer private client and property services to our clients.

        We understand that no two cases are the same and offer a personal service tailored to individual clients during what are undoubtedly difficult and traumatic times in their lives.
