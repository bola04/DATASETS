      <h1>
       About us
      </h1>
      <p class="quote">
       At Dugardyn &amp; Partners we strive to translate legal skills into business-oriented solutions
      </p>
      <p>
       Dugardyn &amp; Partners is an independent business law firm
       <span style="FONT-WEIGHT: bold">
        based in Brussels, Belgium
       </span>
       , the capital of the European Union, within walking distance of the European Commission and with a local office in Leuven. Our lawyers are specialized
       <span style="FONT-WEIGHT: bold">
        in a broad range of practice areas
       </span>
       and are fluent in many languages including English, French, Dutch, German, Italian and Chinese. The firm offers legal advice and services in business law and all related areas of law on the national, European and international level.
      </p>
      <p>
       Our main concern is
       <span style="FONT-WEIGHT: bold">
        our clients.
       </span>
       We are committed to nurturing and maintaining long-lasting relationships, as witnessed by the fact that most of our clients have been with us for years.
       <span style="font-weight: bold;">
        We are loyal to our clients and in return they remain loyal to us.
       </span>
       <br/>
      </p>
      <p>
       In our practice, we stress a high degree of specialisation combined with a strong industry knowledge and economic realism, direct follow-up of cases, timely feed-back, transparent invoicing, respect of our agreements, and contained billable hours and expenses. Understanding our clients' business, vision and culture in order to provide tailor-made legal solutions is our top priority.  Our practice is characterised by a
       <span style="font-weight: bold;">
        dedicated, personal and flexible approach
       </span>
       tailored to fit the clients' needs.
      </p>
      <p>
       In a "
       <strong>
        glocalizing world"
       </strong>
       , our firm has developed a strong network of lawyers and legal experts from across the globe, making it possible to assist clients not only in our home market but also in almost every foreign market where they have dealings.
      </p>
