                      <td style="text-align: justify;">
                       CabNet Group is principally involved in the provision of building management solutions, where our core business segments comprise structured cabling works and ELV systems for building and other facilities. We also provide IT services which can be offered as complementary IT services to our building management solutions or offered separately on a standalone basis as an additional product offering.
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       Our history can be traced back to 1995 when CabNet Systems (M) Sdn Bhd was first incorporated to carry out the design and build of structured cabling works for ICT segment (Information and Communication Technology) for multinational companies in the commercial and industrial sector in the Southern region of Peninsular Malaysia. As a result of their extensive experience, in ICT related industry, coupled with foresight, the company saw the potential of ELV systems as a business avenue following the proliferation of ICT trends in Malaysia.
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       As we grew, we diversified our business to include more complex and sophisticated services. Year 2007 was milestone year for us as we acquired a major stake in ITWin Technology Sdn Bhd, an IT solution provider and system integration company. With the advancement of IT trends in the market, this was a strategic acquisition it allowed our Group to broaden our range of services, to include IT services to further strengthen our portfolio.
                       <br/>
                       <br/>
                       Over the past 20 years, our Group has transformed itself from a local provider of structured cabling works for small to mid-sized projects to a solution provider of building management for large scale structured cabling, ELV systems, and IT services projects. Through the years, our Company has established a strong project track record in commercial and industrial sector.
