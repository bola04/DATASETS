           <h3>
            IMPORTANT SALT SPRING PHOTOS NEWS
           </h3>
           <p>
            After nine years—and as of December 2nd, 2016, this website (saltspringphotos.com) will function as an archive only. It will no longer be updated.
           </p>
           <p>
            Salt Spring Photos is now located at
            <a href="http://www.johncameron.ca/blog/">
             johncameron.ca/blog
            </a>
            . There you’ll find the current ‘photo stream’ of images.
           </p>
           <p>
            Should you want to see only Salt Spring images, there’s a pull-down menu for that:
            <br/>
            <a href="http://johncameron.ca">

            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            The frequency of posts is unknown. However, I intend to continue with email notification for each new post.
           </p>
