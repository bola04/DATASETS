         <h3>
          CIL works with you and your organisation to boost your resilience and responsiveness in these times of turbulent disruptive change.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          CIL helps organisations, teams, and communities to continually and sustainably raise their levels of performance while honouring the needs of the individual.
         </h3>
         <br/>
         Since 1989 CIL has been building robust organisations for the future economy.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Our clients choose us because we build capacity in their organisations to sustain the ability to manage continuous change in an increasingly complex world.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Our global portfolio of clients' work with us because:
         <ul>
          <li>
           We help them understand the plausible futures they face.
          </li>
          <li>
           We help them craft a strategy which is robust in the face of these futures.
          </li>
          <li>
           We develop their leadership capability at every level so that they may take the organisation with them with an economy of means.
          </li>
          <li>
           We regularly work at scale with organisations of 20,000-60,000 employee range.
          </li>
          <li>
           Our whole systems approach also integrates scenario-based strategy, leadership development and organisation change management.
          </li>
         </ul>
