    <p>
     Little Green Consulting Ltd is a UK based consultancy specialising in the Environment and Business Continuity. The Company was founded in 2008 in response to the growing demand for services in each of these sectors. To date, the Company has worked with manufacturers in the Food and Drink sector, carried out Carbon Footprint assessments of haulage fleets, assisted with Ecological and Environmental Impact Assessments, and developed and implemented Environment and Business Continuity Systems to meet the requirements of
     <a href="em_ISO14001.htm">
      ISO14001:2004
     </a>
     and
     <a href="bcm_ISO25999.htm">
      BS25999:2007
     </a>
     .
    </p>
    <p>
     The Company's consultants are Lead Assessors in both fields, and can provide Legal Compliance and internal audit services, as well as assist with the application and management of Environmental Permits.
    </p>
    <p>
     Please use this website to find out more about our range of services and discover the benefits of using Little Green Consulting Ltd.
