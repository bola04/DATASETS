       <br/>
       <h2>
        We're Your Independent Living Resource Centre for Thunder Bay and the Northwestern Ontario Region
       </h2>
       <p>
        Your ILRC Thunder Bay has been proudly providing a wide range of programs and services to all persons with disabilities and our regional community for more than 25 Years. To celebrate this anniversary milestone, we've held events that remind us of our past and the amazing CHOICE, FLEXIBILITY and CONTROL that Independent Living brings to all persons with disabilities everywhere.
       </p>
       <p>
        If you already know about your ILRC, you know we are working hard to make sure our programs and services reflect our commitment to you; our participants and friends! We continue to hone and change ourselves to meet the individual and unique needs of our participants.
       </p>
       <p>
        Visiting us here on our website or joining us on Facebook, will always provide you with the most up-to-date information about your Centre; but what we'd really like is for you to visit us in person anytime for real interaction and involvement with everything your ILRC has to offer.
       </p>
       <hr/>
       <p>
        <a href="/article/fast-for-employment-241.asp" title="/article/fast-for-employment-241.asp">

        </a>
       </p>
       <h2>
        The NEW FAST for Employment Has Begun...
       </h2>
       <p>
        If you are a person with a disability in Thunder Bay or Northwestern Ontario looking for employment - Contact Us Today!
       </p>
       <p>
        If you are an employer in Thunder Bay or Northwestern Ontario looking to hire - Contact Us Today!
       </p>
       <hr/>
       <p style="text-align: left;">
        <a href="/article/employment-supports-service-provider-210.asp" title="/article/employment-supports-service-provider-210.asp">
         <img alt="Work ES Logo" height="167" src="/upload/images/logo_work-es.jpg" title="Employment Supports Service Provider" width="446"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: left;">
        We are an Employment Supports Service Provider for the Ontario Disability Supports Program.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: left;">
        Even if you are not in reciept of regular ODSP benefits, but would like to become employed - We Can Help!
       </p>
