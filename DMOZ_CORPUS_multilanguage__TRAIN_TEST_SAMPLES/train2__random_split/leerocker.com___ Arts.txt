                     <h3 class="text-main alt-font heading-secondary heading-blog">
                      <a href="/home/blog/first-north-american-performance-in-10-years-at-upcoming-viva-las-vegas-rockabilly-weekend">
                       FIRST NORTH AMERICAN PERFORMANCE IN 10 YEARS AT UPCOMING   “VIVA LAS VEGAS ROCKABILLY WEEKEND"
                      </a>
                     </h3>
                     <div class="post">
                      <div class="message">
                       <p>
                        <img class="size_l justify_center border_" src="//d3jjg4nf4bbybe.cloudfront.net/u/289370/6d9d0fae035d0d93f6c7a0b55251e99815d24b10/original/image-1-732x488.png"/>
                        STRAY CATS BRIAN SETZER, LEE ROCKER &amp; SLIM JIM PHANTOM REUNITE FOR FIRST NORTH AMERICAN PERFORMANCE IN 10 YEARS AT UPCOMING “VIVA LAS VEGAS ROCKABILLY WEEKEND”  APRIL 21, 2018
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        Brian Setzer, Lee Rocker and Slim Jim Phantom–original founding members of the acclaimed American Rockabilly trio, the STRAY CATS–will reunite for their first North American performance in 10 years on Saturday, April 21 in Las Vegas at The Orleans when they headline “Viva Las Vegas Rockabilly Weekend.” The iconic group, known for their high-energy performances with hits such as“Stray Cat Strut,” “(She’s) Sexy + 17,” and “Rock This Town,” are honored to share the stage with such musical legends as Jerry Lee Lewis and Duane Eddy.
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        “There’s something magic about a Stray Cats show, Viva Las Vegas is the perfect event to experience that. C’mon down and Rock This Town!”  –Brian Setzer
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        “Just a string bass, a guitar and a drum, Stray Cats are back to show em how it’s done. 39 years after our first gigs, we’re back!  Can’t wait to Rock again with my brothers Brian and Slim.” –Lee Rocker
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        “Happy and thrilled to be hitting the stage with the Cats at Viva Las Vegas. We’re The Rockabilly Kings back to reclaim our throne!” –Slim Jim
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        Beginning April 19, “Viva Las Vegas Rockabilly Weekend” will showcase over 100 live bands across four days, eight burlesque shows, a massive car show (all cars built before 1964), vintage fashion show, a pin-up contest and jiving lessons and jiving contest. On hand will be over 120 vendors, pool parties, bands, music all night,  DJ’s &amp; record hops plus much more. For tickets and more information, visit: www.vivalasvegas.net
                       </p>
