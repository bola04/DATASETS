          <h1>
           Traralgon Buds in Bloom.
          </h1>
         </header>
         <p class="lead">
          <strong>
           We are open
          </strong>
          <br/>
          Monday to Friday 9am – 5.30pm
          <br/>
          Saturdays 9am – 1pm
         </p>
         <p>
          Please note we are
          <strong>
           CLOSED on Sundays and Public Holidays
          </strong>
          and no deliveries are made on these days
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           The Story of Buds In Bloom
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Buds in Bloom opened in Seymour Street in Traralgon in 1995, and has been run by local, dedicated and passionate florists since that time.
          <br/>
          We pride ourselves on the design, presentation and longevity of our arrangements.
          <br/>
          At Buds In Bloom we travel regularly to the Melbourne Wholesale Flower Market in Epping to bring back the best and freshest blooms as well as inspiration and ideas to share.
          <br/>
          Where possible we also source flowers locally from growers in Gippsland for maximum freshness and to support our region.
          <br/>
          We are proud supporters of our industry and enjoy supporting young florists with employment and training.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Same day delivery is available
          </strong>
          <br/>
          Online order cut off is
          <strong>
           1pm
          </strong>
          for same day delivery
          <br/>
          Phone order cut off is
          <strong>
           3pm
          </strong>
          for same day delivery
         </p>
         <p>
          Thanks for visiting our website,
          <br/>
          Why should you choose us to surprise your loved one this year?
          <br/>
          *We are locals – Our store is in Traralgon and our Florists and live locally, we support our local community and schools
          <br/>
          *We are experienced – Buds in Bloom has been open over 20 years, our staff are qualified and love what they do!
          <br/>
          *Our Flowers – We pride ourselves in sourcing the best quality locally grown flowers, you wont find any imported roses here 🙂
          <br/>
          *Value – You are ordering from the florist who makes your arrangement, we are not an online relay company with a call centre, so you wont pay commission
         </p>
         <p>
          Our team of designers are qualified and experienced in all facets of floristry and enjoy creating innovative and classic designs for every occasion.
          <br/>
          Our online store offers the opportunity for clients to shop from home or the office. We are excited to introduce this new and updated website in June 2016
         </p>
         <p>
          We look forward to your order,
          <br/>
          Thank you, The team at Buds In Bloom
         </p>
