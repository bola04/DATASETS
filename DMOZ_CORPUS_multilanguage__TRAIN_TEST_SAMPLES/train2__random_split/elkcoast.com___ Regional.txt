             <strong>
              WELCOME TO THE ELK COAST
             </strong>
             and the hamlets of North Greenwood, Elk, Bridgeport and Manchester.
            </p>
            <p class="text">
             <strong>
              » An excellent read,
             </strong>
             by author Keith Skinner!
             <a href="http://keith-skinner.com/uncovering-1866-greenwood-mendocino-county-frontier-town/" target="_blank">
              Uncovering 1866 Greenwood          — Mendocino County Frontier Town
             </a>
             .
            </p>
            <p class="text">
             Enjoy luxurious bed and breakfast inns, fine restaurants, pubs and delis, vacation rentals and campgrounds ... parks and beaches. 
                   Go wine tasting, kayaking, horseback riding, whale watching, beach combing; or wander into the Elk Coast unique shops and galleries featuring antiques, clothing, books and local artists' creations. Then relax and have an expert spa therapist  give you a perfect    massage.
            </p>
            <p class="text">
             Have your Elk Coast wedding or renew your vows in one of two churches. Visit the famous lighthouse, a century old working garage, the community center, or the historical museum. The stunning scenery along the Elk Coast of Highway One plus the warm hospitality and wealth of interesting experiences await your visit!
