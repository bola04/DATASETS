       <h1>
        Audio Visual Furniture Collection
       </h1>
       <p>
        At Off The Wall our unique range of audio visual furniture is strikingly different, simply stylish and extremely versatile. Not only does our furniture cleverly hide all the clutter of cables and wires but is also designed to suit every make and model of flat panel TV. Our furniture is packed full of practical features from adjustable shelves, soft open/easy close doors to enclosed power sockets. Designed and manufactured by us here in the UK our hand crafted finishes ensure exceptional quality and attention to detail. With so many designs, painted colours and real wood veneer options, make the statement you want with furniture that can compliment, integrate or stand out in any room setting.
       </p>
       <h2>
        Watch the film… not the wires
       </h2>
       <p>
        No matter what your AV set up, interior space or style, we have designed furniture that is just right for you. Have a look at the full Off The Wall furniture range and find the perfect choice for your TV and home.
