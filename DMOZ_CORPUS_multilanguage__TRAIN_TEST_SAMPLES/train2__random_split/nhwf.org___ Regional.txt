             <strong>
              Education
             </strong>
             – Support for scholarships, NH BOW and Barry Conservation Camp youth programs
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              Legislation
             </strong>
             – Voice of the Sportsmen in Concord
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              Conservation
             </strong>
             – Partner with programs to improve habitat
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              Affiliates
             </strong>
             – Power in numbers through many clubs in New Hampshire
            </p>
            <p>
