          Welcome, Skipton Badminton Club is a friendly club with members of all ages. The senior club competes in the Craven League entering Mens, Womens, Mixed and Composite teams into various league divisions. We also enter pairs into knockout tournaments run by the league.
         </p>
         <p>
          Our senior club meets at Sandylands Sports Centre in Skipton, North Yorkshire, on a Tuesday evening from 8pm until 10pm. We are always looking for new players, so if you have talent or potential, please get in touch.
         </p>
         <p>
          We run a junior club, for players aged seven and upwards, on a Friday evening from 7pm until 8pm. Coaching is also available from 8pm until 9pm so if you are the next rising star, please contact us.
         </p>
