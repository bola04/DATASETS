          <strong>
           Brocklebank Ground Holiday Cottages are three 
               luxury self catering holiday cottages in Torver village on the outskirts 
               of Coniston in the Lake District, only half a mile from the lake 
               of Coniston Water and close to the foot of the Old Man of Coniston.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p class="body">
          The holiday Cottages are 4 star E.T.C. rated, they have been 
               converted from an original Lakeland â€˜Bank Barn. All retaining
               many original features including beamed ceilings, cobbles and exposed
               stone walls, situated along a tarmaced private driveway 200yds
               from the road with a enclosed gardens and private parking area
               with woodland and fells to the rear, excellent views to the front
               across Torver common which is an ideal place to exercise well behaved
             dogs which are welcome in the Holiday Cottages.
         </p>
         <p class="body">
          The Holiday Cottages are ideally placed for
               walkers wanting to explore the Lake district countryside on foot,
               with footpaths from the door
               both onto Coniston Old Man or across Torver common to Coniston
             Water itself, as well as being an excellent base for touring the
             Lake District
               by car with Ambleside and Lake Windermere being only 15 minutes
             drive away.
         </p>
         <p class="body">
          The difficult decision for guests being whether to
             eat in at the cosy fireside in the Holiday Cottages or to sample
             the local atmosphere
                 in one of the 2 excellent pubs which are only a 600yds walk away.
          <br/>
          Since the properties are managed by the owners who live on site,
                 we are able to offer the special personal touch and try to help
                 with any
                 special requests in advance of arrival such as deliveries from
               Asda and Tesco.
         </p>
         <p class="body">
          The enclosed garden provides
                 a perfect place for sitting out with a relaxing drink, having
             a barbecue, (these are provided)
                   whilst
                   allowing
                   a safe area for children to play.
         </p>
