           <img alt="VladimirParizher" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-195" height="300" sizes="(max-width: 172px) 100vw, 172px" src="http://www.PARIZHERLAW.COM/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/VladimirParizher-172x300.jpeg" srcset="http://www.parizherlaw.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/VladimirParizher-172x300.jpeg 172w, http://www.parizherlaw.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/VladimirParizher.jpeg 280w" width="172"/>
          </a>
          West Hollywood Immigration Lawyer
         </h1>
         <p>
          Do you need help applying for a work visa? Do you wish to bring family members to the United States? Are you seeking asylum in the U.S.?
         </p>
         <p>
          If you answered “yes” to any of these questions, then you have come to the right place. My name is Vladimir Parizher, and I am a licensed attorney in West Hollywood who focuses on helping individuals throughout Southern California and overseas with U.S.
          <a href="http://www.PARIZHERLAW.COM/immigration/" title="Immigration">
           immigration law
          </a>
          . My experience includes everything from filing work- or family-based petitions to helping those seeking asylum to escape difficult situations in their home countries.
         </p>
         <h2>
          What Makes My Immigration Law Firm Different?
         </h2>
         <p>
          <strong>
           I strongly believe in honesty.
          </strong>
          If there is even a small chance of success, I will do all that I can to help you. If there is nothing I can do, I will tell you that upfront. Since opening
          <a href="http://www.parizherlaw.com/About/">
           my practice
          </a>
          , I have successfully helped many clients get their petitions approved by U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           When you work with my firm, you work with me.
          </strong>
          I personally handle all aspects of your case. I will not give the work to another lawyer or paralegal. If I cannot respond to your call or email right away, I make every effort to respond the same day. I will also keep you informed about the status of your case so you will know what I am doing on your behalf.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           I charge reasonable fees,
          </strong>
          which we will discuss in detail before any work begins. We will use your first consultation to briefly talk through your particular immigration concerns and to negotiate a suitable payment structure.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Contact The Law Offices Of Vladimir Parizher
         </h2>
         <p>
          To get started on your case, please
          <a href="http://www.PARIZHERLAW.COM/contact-us/" title="Contact">
           contact my firm online
          </a>
          or call 310-595-4963 to schedule an appointment. I speak Russian fluently (Я говорю по-русски) and many of my clients come from Russian-speaking communities within the U.S. and nations of the former Soviet Union. That said, I welcome inquiries from people of all nationalities seeking a new life in the United States.
         </p>

 				if($.trim($(this).val()) ==  'notchecked') {
                                     $(this).addClass('inputError');
                                     $(this).next('.disclaimer_confirm').addClass('inputError');
                                     hasError = true;
 				}
                                 
                         } else if($(this).hasClass('txt requiredField')) {
 				if($.trim($(this).val()) == defaults[this.name])  {
 					$(this).addClass('inputError');
 					hasError = true;
 				}
 				
 			}
 			
 		});
 		
 		if(!hasError) {
 			var formInput = $(this).serialize();
 			$.post($(this).attr('action'),formInput, function(response){
 				//If sending was successful
 				if (response == '1') {
                                     $('form#contactForm').slideUp("fast", function() {				   
                                             $(this).before('<p class="tick">' + defaults['success'] + '</p>');
                                     });
 				} else if (response.indexOf('error-captcha') > -1){
 					$(".captcha").addClass('inputError');
 				} else {
                                     $('form#contactForm').slideUp("fast", function() {				   
                                                 $(this).before('<p class="alert">' + response + '</p>');
                                         });
                                 }
 				
 			});
 		}
 		
 		return false;
 		
 	});
 });
