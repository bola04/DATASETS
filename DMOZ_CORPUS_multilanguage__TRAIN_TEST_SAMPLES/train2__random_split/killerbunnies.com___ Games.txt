                               <p>
                                Hello Killer Bunnies fans … Jeff Bellinger here!  On behalf of myself, and the very talented Jonathan Young, I am quite happy to announce that we have several new 55-card Killer Bunnies Booster Decks now available and a few more coming soon.
                               </p>
                               <p>
                                Killer Bunnies Booster decks are now universal and may be added to your existing set at any time.  Also, Quest, Conquest and Kinder (which have always been compatible) are all officially smelted together under the Quest umbrella.  Our new booster decks will either be themed or flavored.
                               </p>

                                </li>

                                 </strong>
                                </li>
                               </ul>
                               <p>
                               </p>

                                <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-5709" height="267" src="http://killerbunnies.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/07/JeffJonathan2-300x267.jpg" title="JeffJonathan2-300x267" width="300"/>
                                <p class="wp-caption-text">
                                 Jeff (left) and Jonathan (right).
                                </p>

                               <p>
                                In my travels all over the world, I have met quite a few folks who have been fans of the game for a very long time (and many that have recently discovered the game), and so it is with great sincerity that I thank you all for the accolades.
                               </p>
                               <p>
                                But I also have this proposal… I want to hear from you!  We intend to work directly with fans to create new cards, themes for decks and possibly Killer Bunnies spin-off games.  Were you a Bunny Patrol agent?  Did you write a card for the 10th Anniversary deck?  Did you post on any of our fan forums?  Killer Bunnies has a history to be re-discovered, and if you’d like to get involved and be part of that history, we’d love to hear from you!
                               </p>

                                <li>

                                And we’re off!
