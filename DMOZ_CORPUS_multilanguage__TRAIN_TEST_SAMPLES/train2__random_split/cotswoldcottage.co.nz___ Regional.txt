      <h2 align="center">
       Cotswold Cottage
      </h2>
      <p align="justify">
       Our home  is a charmingly restored 100 year old villa.  Set in the hills above a  bend in the Kauaeranga River, this is a stunning place to relax and  unwind.  The property gets all day sun, and has fantastic views from every  room.  It is ideally situated for your first night’s accommodation in NZ or the last  night of your journey, as we are only an hour’s drive from Auckland  International Airport.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       When you arrive at Cotswold, you will be welcomed with afternoon tea.  We  have information on local restaurants for your evening meal, and if you give us  plenty of advance notice we can prepare a meal for you.  In summer months  you may fancy barbequeing your own food, in which case we can prepare for you a  BBQ tray.  This will allow you to sit in  our lovely garden and enjoy one of our marvellous sunsets.
       <br/>
      </p>
      <p align="justify">
      </p>
      <p align="justify">
       In the  morning we offer a full continental buffet, followed by a cooked breakfast.
       <br/>
      </p>
      <p align="justify">
      </p>
      <p align="justify">
       If you  need to ease your tired traveller’s or tramper’s body, take a spa in our  secluded spa pool in our private rear garden.  Extra towels are provided.
       <br/>
      </p>
