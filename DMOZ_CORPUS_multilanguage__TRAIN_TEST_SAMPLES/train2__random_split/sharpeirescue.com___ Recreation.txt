             Welcome to North American Shar-Pei Rescue - Shar-Pei Adoption &amp; Fostering
            </h1>
           </header>
           <div class="content clearfix">
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 10pt;">
              <img alt="Kermits Painting 001" height="225" src="/images/Kermits%20Painting%20001.jpg" style="margin: 10px; float: right;" width="300"/>
             </span>
             <span style="font-size: 10pt;">
              NASPR (North American Shar-Pei Rescue) is a 501(c) 3 nonprofit rescue dedicated to those "last chance" purebred Shar-Pei in need.  Our members are all volunteers that work diligently to rehabilitate and care for each rescue with love and commitment.  Our Shar-Pei are respected as the individual personalities that they are and we invest the time to work with our adoptive families to introduce them to the Shar-Pei that will be the best addition to their family.  NASPR cannot save all those in need, but for those that we can, we make a lifetime commitment to not only the dog but the adoptive families so that our Shar Pei will never find themselves alone, scared and uncared for again.
             </span>
            </p>
