     <h2>
      Why HiSpeed is Better
      <br/>
      <span>
       Technology. Integrity. Committment.
      </span>
     </h2>
     <p class="largegraytext">
      Local Ownership
     </p>
     <p>
      HiSpeed is not a branch office at   the end of a corporate food chain. Local decision making and local advocacy for   our clients are just a few of the advantages of working with a company whose   ownership is local. HiSpeed is the only Alaskan owned, full service,   manufacturer certified copier dealership on the Kenai Peninsula today.
     </p>
     <p class="largegraytext">
      Local Service
     </p>
     <p>
      It is common practice in the   document imaging business to provide only first line   support in the outlying markets. Anything more technical than simple break fix   requires a technician to be brought in from a larger market, usually Anchorage.    HiSpeed trains its technicians in both xerographic (copier &amp; printers) and   connectivity (computers, servers, networks) technology. What this means to our   clients is less down time, quicker responses, and less hassle for end   users.
     </p>
