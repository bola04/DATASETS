           <h4 class="widget-title widgettitle">
            Learn about the Horizon’s East Experience
           </h4>
           <article class="post-4 page type-page status-publish has-post-thumbnail entry">
            <div class="entry-content">
             <p>
              <a href="https://www.showstables.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/Brookside-2016-Lauren-Winner-Circle-.jpg">
               <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1014" height="475" sizes="(max-width: 633px) 100vw, 633px" src="https://www.showstables.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/Brookside-2016-Lauren-Winner-Circle-.jpg" srcset="https://www.showstables.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/Brookside-2016-Lauren-Winner-Circle-.jpg 633w, https://www.showstables.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/Brookside-2016-Lauren-Winner-Circle--300x225.jpg 300w" width="633"/>
              </a>
             </p>
             <h3>
              <strong>
               Horizons East Equestrian Center is a family owned and operated stable that has trained the Tri-Valley’s top riders for over 25 years.
              </strong>
             </h3>
             <p>
              <a href="https://www.showstables.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/Carrie-Wins.jpg">
               <img alt="" class=" wp-image-1017 alignright" height="421" sizes="(max-width: 244px) 100vw, 244px" src="https://www.showstables.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/Carrie-Wins.jpg" srcset="https://www.showstables.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/Carrie-Wins.jpg 371w, https://www.showstables.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/Carrie-Wins-174x300.jpg 174w" width="244"/>
              </a>
              This state of the art stable, spotlighted on NBC, is
              <a href="https://www.showstables.com/about-us/directions-and-contact/" title="Directions">
               located on the border of Pleasanton and Livermore California
              </a>
              .  It offers the finest in care for Hunter / Jumper performance horses and their riders.
             </p>
             <p>
              From your first lesson to competing at national competitions, Horizons East is the place to learn and grow.
             </p>
             <p>
              <a href="https://www.showstables.com/events-camps/summer-riding-camps-2015/" title="Summer Riding Camps 2015">
               We offer week long summer camps in June, July and August.
              </a>
             </p>
             <p>
              For the youngest of horse enthusiasts
              <a href="https://www.showstables.com/classes-training/toddler-riding-lessons/" title="Toddler Riding Lessons">
               we offer toddler classes for ages 3, 4, and 5 year olds
              </a>
              . Regular lessons should start at 5 years old.
             </p>
             <p>
              Our full service facility offers lesson, lease, and sale horses for all riders. Horses are exercised seven days per week, and stalls are cleaned every day.
             </p>
             <p>
              Please explore our web site or
              <a href="https://www.showstables.com/about-us/contact-us/" title="Contact Us">
               call us
              </a>
              to learn more about getting involved in equestrian sports or how we can help riders reach their goals.  Check out the
              <a href="https://www.showstables.com/special-deals-2/" title="Special Deals!">
               special deals and discounts that we are currently offering.
              </a>
             </p>

              The Horizons East Training Facility
