         <p>
          The Content Type Hub is available on premise, and online.
         </p>
         <p>
          In SharePoint Server, you enable the feature on the Site Collection you wish to use as the hub.
         </p>
         <p>
          You then enable it in Central Admin, on the Service Application Proxy for your Managed Metadata Service in the farm, pasting in the URL to the Site Collection you nominated.
         </p>
         <p>
          Published Content Types can reach all Site Collections in every Web Application that consumes that Proxy. Even other Farms.
         </p>
         <p>
          In SharePoint Online, it is already set up for you at /sites/contenttypehub, and extends to all the Site Collections in your tenancy.
         </p>
         <p>
          Once you have made Site Columns and Content Types in your Content Type Hub, you have publication options on the Advanced menu of the Content Type.
         </p>
         <p>
          You can Publish, Republish or Unpublish.
         </p>
         <p>
          Changes you make are propagated by an hourly Timer Job.
         </p>
         <p>
          When the Content Types land in the target Site Collections, they are marked Read Only to discourage editing by users.
