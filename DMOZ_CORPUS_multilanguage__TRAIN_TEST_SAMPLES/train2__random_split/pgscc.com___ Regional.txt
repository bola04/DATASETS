        <h3 style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          “A classic New England golf experience since 1900”
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Pine Grove Springs Golf Course, situated within view of Lake Spofford, presents 9 holes of golf on a majestically tree-lined canvas of dramatic elevation changes, water features, rock outcroppings, and wildlife habitat. The layout is challenging for all skill levels from beginner to pro! Pine Grove Springs Golf Course strives to be a welcoming facility to all who enjoy the sport of golf, from players who are just looking to squeeze in a quick nine holes, to those who enjoy coming out to meet up with friends, grab a cold beverage, and play the day away. We are proud of our history and longevity in the region and would love for you to make yourself at home at our facility. So please peruse our site, learn about who we are and what we offer, and then come on out and get your game on!
        </p>

          DIRECTIONS

        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <strong>
          FROM the WEST (Interstate 91)
         </strong>
         – Take Route 9 East toward Keene, NH. Cross over the Connecticut River and after about 7 miles, turn LEFT onto Route 9A. The clubhouse – a large red barn – will be on the right after 1.5 miles.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <strong>
          FROM the EAST (Keene)
         </strong>
         – Take Route 9 West toward Brattleboro, VT. We are approximately 7 Miles from Keene. After climbing Chesterfield Hill, passing J&amp;J Discount and Manny’s Appliances, take a RIGHT onto Route 9A. After about one mile you will see the Tower Light Inn and our driving range on the right. Our clubhouse will be just past them on the left
