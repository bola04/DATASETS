        <h1>
         The future of your enterprise is in Cherwell
        </h1>
        <p>
         Whether you own or manage a business already in the Cherwell district, or are thinking of starting an enterprise or expanding into north Oxfordshire, this website offers you information and guidance to facts, figures and contacts to assist your business decisions.
        </p>
        <p>
         Cherwell is a thriving area with a mix of bustling market towns and productive rural areas, so you are welcome to explore through these pages how your enterprise can develop locally with useful contacts to help you along the way. Considerable support is also offered directly through the local authority at:
         <a href="http://www.cherwell.gov.uk" target="_blank">
          Cherwell District Council
         </a>
        </p>
