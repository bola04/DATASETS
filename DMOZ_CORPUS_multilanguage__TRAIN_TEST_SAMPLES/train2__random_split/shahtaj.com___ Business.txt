               Shahtaj Textile Limited is a Shahnawaz Group Company. It was incorporated as a public limited company in January 1990, with the sole purpose of manufacturing grey fabric and its marketing. The company is listed at Pakistan Stock Exchange Limited. The mills went into commercial production in January 1992.
              </p>
              <p>
               Its production facilities are situated in the hub of Textile Industries near Lahore (Pakistan) and is equipped with the most advanced high speed Toyoda and Picanol Air Jet Looms of Japanese and Belgium origin. Starting with 72 looms now the number of installed looms is 178 with complete back up process and stand-by self power generation.
              </p>
              <p>
               Its business philosophy has been to be the supplier of choice for leading processing and export houses in Pakistan. Over the last 20 years of operations Shahtaj Textile Limited, has established its reputation in Pakistan as a premier supplier of highest quality greige fabric to key exporters besides exports to many foreign buyers.
              </p>
