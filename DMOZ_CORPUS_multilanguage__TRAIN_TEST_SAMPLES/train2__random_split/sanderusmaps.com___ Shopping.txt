     <h2>
      SANDERUS ANTIQUARIAAT - ANTIQUE MAPS AND BOOKS
     </h2>
     <p>
      Antiquariaat Sanderus specialises in rare old maps, atlases and precious books. We offer thousands of maps from the 15th to the 18th centuries, of all parts of the world, illustrated and described. All antique maps and prints are guaranteed authentic. We exhibit every year at a number of important antique map and book fairs.
     </p>

     <h2>
      Book in the spotlight
     </h2>
     <a class="featuredmap" href="/en/our-catalogue/detail/165454/rare-book-atlas-by-magini-1620/">
      <div class="mapinfo">
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: 14px;">
         <strong>
          <span style="color: #008080;">
           <em>
            First edition of the first printed atlas of Italy.
           </em>
          </span>
         </strong>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: 14px;">
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: 14px;">
         <strong>
         </strong>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: 14px;">
         <strong>
          MAGINI G.A. Italia.
         </strong>
         Bologna: Sebastiano Bonomi, 1620.
         <br/>
        </span>
        Folio, engraved allegorical title by Olivero Gatto printed within typographical border, 61 engraved maps as called for in index (all but 3 double-page), in fine dark impression, engraved portrait of Magini, text on 15 leaves. Seventeenth-century red morocco, gilt, sides panelled in gilt and blind, gilt edges. A few marginal repairs, some occasional marginal soiling.
       </p>
