        <h1>
         Kitchens Continental is an independent supplier of bespoke kitchens in London with over 20 years of experience in the industry.
        </h1>
        <p>
         We specialise in design, supply and installation of contemporary and traditional kitchens from the UK and European continent, including high end designer ranges of English kitchens, Italian kitchens and German kitchens.
        </p>
        <p>
         All our kitchens are bespoke, which means they are designed completely to our clients requirements following extensive design consultations. We put an extra effort to ensure every detail and request is taken care of. Most importantly, our bespoke kitchens are affordable to everyone as we cater for clients ranging from new homeowners to architects, interior designers and property developers.
        </p>
        <p>
         We display a number of fully functional kitchens along with an extensive range of accessories, glass and worktop samples and special features in our combined kitchen showrooms in North West London.
        </p>

        <h2>
         Supplying bespoke kitchens in London
        </h2>
        <p>
         Kitchens Continental has built strong and long standing relationships with leading European kitchen manufacturers, including the high end designer brands. Some of our ranges are designed exclusively for us and our clients can benefit from completely unique and individual kitchen designs.
        </p>
        <p>
         Our design team has a vast experience in designing bespoke kitchens and is also up to date with the latest trends in the industry, offering our clients a variety of concepts and an expert advice on how to create a dream kitchen. Features such as colour coordinated carcasses, ceiling high wall units, islands or curved kitchen units are only a few ideas that can be incorporated into our kitchens.
