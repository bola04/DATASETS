     <p>
      Glazed &amp; Amazed is a “Paint Your Own Pottery”, Canvas Painting and Glass Fusing Studio, located in beautiful downtown Edmonds. We are open 7 days a week with no studio fee’s no appointments needed-just come on in!
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       How it works:
      </strong>
     </p>
     <ol>
      <li>
       Pick a piece of pottery or a glass base from our large selection.
      </li>
      <li>
       Design and decorate pottery using our stencils, stamps, patterns and silk screens or design glass using our large selection of fused glass pieces.
      </li>
      <li>
       Leave it with us to be fired in our kilns.
      </li>
      <li>
       Pieces will be ready in 5-7 days.
      </li>
     </ol>
     <p>
      Canvas Painting classes available two nights a month or book a private canvas class at Glazed &amp; Amazed. Call to book today.  425.673.5474
