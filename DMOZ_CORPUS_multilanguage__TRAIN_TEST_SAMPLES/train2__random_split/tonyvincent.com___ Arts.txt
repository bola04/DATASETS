                         Christmas Gift List and A New Arrival...
                        </a>
                       </div>
                       <!-- Metadata (Below Title) -->
                       <div class="summary-metadata-container summary-metadata-container--below-title">
                        <div class="summary-metadata summary-metadata--primary">

                       <div class="summary-excerpt">
                        <p>
                         It’s hard to believe that 2017 is coming to a close. I know that for many people, myself included, it has gone by incredibly fast. That being said, I'm really grateful for a wonderful year. I was a part of bringing
                         <a href="https://rocktopia.com" target="_blank">
                          <em>
                           Rocktopia
                          </em>
                         </a>
                         to the East Coast... I loved my time touring with The Music of David Bowie (seriously one of the most enjoyable projects I've ever worked on)... My role as an artist has expanded into that of an in-demand music
                         <a href="https://www.thumbtack.com/ny/new-york/recording-studio/recording-mixing" target="_blank">
                          producer and mixer here in NYC
                         </a>
                         . But more than anything, I’m feeling incredibly overjoyed because Aspen and I will soon be adding a new member to our family— a son, due late January 2018.
                        </p>
                        <p>
                        </p>
                        <p>
                         In light of these things and more, I’m grateful to the many friends and fans who have been a massive source of encouragement this year. To all those I met on the road: Thank you, each and every one of you. THANK YOU! Your words and prayers inspire me to continue pursuing my passion. You all are the best.
                        </p>

                       <div class="summary-excerpt">
                        <p>
                         The snow is falling on a blustery mid-March morning, and I’m gearing up to begin rehearsals with the talented
                         <a href="https://www.rocktopia.com" target="_blank">
                          <em>
                           ROCKTOPIA
                          </em>
                         </a>
                         team for an East Coast tour that begins later this March. If you are in a PBS market that aired the
                         <em>
                          <a href="http://www.pbs.org/video/2365885378/" target="_blank">
                           Rocktopia- Live From Budapest
                          </a>
                         </em>
                         concert over the holiday season, then you have a pretty good idea of how unique this epic show is. I’m incredibly excited to be part of this whirlwind of a tour, which will visit
                         <a href="/s/ROCKTOPIA_tourdates_550.jpg" target="_blank">
                          23 US cities
                         </a>
                         . (View tour schedule
                         <a href="https://www.facebook.com/TonyVincentOfficial/photos/a.306341432719750.71182.142059359147959/1337205666299983/?type=3&amp;theater" target="_blank">

                         </a>
                         .) For those who aren’t able to attend any of these dates, make sure you follow me on
                         <a href="https://twitter.com/TonyTheVincent" target="_blank">
                          Twitter
                         </a>
                         and
                         <a href="https://www.facebook.com/TonyVincentOfficial/" target="_blank">
                          Facebook
                         </a>
                         — as more domestic and European dates are being added!

           <div class="sqs-layout sqs-grid-12 columns-12" data-type="page" data-updated-on="1487252701140" id="page-58297c6b2994ca2c676df610">
            <div class="row sqs-row">

              <div class="sqs-block html-block sqs-block-html" data-block-type="2" id="block-92eb1f38852f888333c5">
               <div class="sqs-block-content">
                <h2 class="text-align-center">
                 ABOUT
                </h2>
               </div>
              </div>
              <div class="row sqs-row">
               <div class="col sqs-col-2 span-2">
                <div class="sqs-block spacer-block sqs-block-spacer sized vsize-1" data-block-type="21" id="block-yui_3_17_2_1_1409943258044_35207">
                 <div class="sqs-block-content">
                 </div>
                </div>
               </div>
               <div class="col sqs-col-8 span-8">
                <div class="sqs-block html-block sqs-block-html" data-block-type="2" id="block-yui_3_17_2_1_1409943258044_35577">
                 <div class="sqs-block-content">
                  <p>
                   Best known for his appearance on NBC's
                   <em>
                    The Voice
                   </em>
                   , recording artist, actor and producer Tony Vincent is from Albuquerque, NM, and lives in New York City. In 1993 Vincent started his own record company, Adobe Flats, writing and producing an EP (
                   <a href="http://www.cdbaby.com/cd/tonyvincent4" target="_blank">
                    <em>
                     Love Falling Down
                    </em>
                   </a>
                   ) that led to a recording contract with EMI records. The two solo albums that followed
                   <em>
                    , (
                    <a href="http://www.cdbaby.com/cd/tonyvincent3" target="_blank">
                     Tony Vincent
                    </a>
                   </em>
                   ,
                   <em>
                    <a href="http://www.cdbaby.com/cd/tonyvincent2" target="_blank">
                     One Deed
                    </a>
                   </em>
                   ) produced six #1 Billboard radio singles.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   Shortly after his 1997 move to NYC, he took a detour into rock-based theater, starring on Broadway in
                   <em>
                    RENT
                   </em>
                   (Mark, Roger),
                   <em>
                    Jesus Christ Superstar
                   </em>
                   (Judas Iscariot) and Green Day's
                   <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/american-idiot-original-broadway/id366916135" target="_blank">
                    <em>
                     American Idiot
                    </em>
                   </a>
                   (St. Jimmy). He played Simon Zealotes in Andrew Lloyd Webber's film remake of
                   <a href="https://www.amazon.com/Jesus-Christ-Superstar-Gale-Edwards/dp/B000056NX5/ref=sr_1_4?s=movies-tv&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1484064260&amp;sr=1-4&amp;keywords=jesus+christ+superstar" target="_blank">
                    <em>
                     Jesus Christ
                    </em>
                   </a>
                   <em>
                    <a href="https://www.amazon.com/Jesus-Christ-Superstar-Gale-Edwards/dp/B000056NX5/ref=sr_1_4?s=movies-tv&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1484064260&amp;sr=1-4&amp;keywords=jesus+christ+superstar" target="_blank">
                     Superstar
                    </a>
                   </em>
                   , and is also featured in the film
                   <em>
                    <a href="https://www.amazon.com/Andrew-Lloyd-Webber-Masterpiece-Collectors/dp/B0006U3U8S/ref=pd_sbs_74_1?_encoding=UTF8&amp;psc=1&amp;refRID=WFCBR0335Q2QBQTRYJ44" target="_blank">
                     Andrew Lloyd Webber: Masterpiece
                    </a>
                    .
                   </em>
                   Vincent originated the role of Galileo Figaro in the rock band Queen's
                   <a href="https://www.amazon.co.uk/d/ss5/We-Will-Rock-You-Album-Original-London/B00009ILC4" target="_blank">
                    <em>
                     We Will Rock You
                    </em>
                   </a>
                   in London's West End (2002), and opened the U.S. production in 2004. He also fronted the band itself, performing "
                   <a href="https://www.facebook.com/TonyVincentOfficial/videos/10153221695210521/" target="_blank">
                    Bohemian Rhapsody
                   </a>
                   " for Queen Elizabeth II, with a live audience of over 1 million people surrounding Buckingham Palace.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   Vincent independently released two more albums,
                   <a href="http://www.cdbaby.com/cd/tonyvincent5" target="_blank">
                    <em>
                     A Better Way
                    </em>
                   </a>
                   , produced by Adam Anders (
                   <em>
                    Glee
                   </em>
                   ) in 2008, and the self-produced
                   <a href="http://www.cdbaby.com/cd/tonyvincent6" target="_blank">
                    <em>
                     In My Head
                    </em>
                   </a>
                   in 2012, following his appearance on NBC's
                   <a href="http://video.search.yahoo.com/yhs/search?fr=yhs-iry-fullyhosted_011&amp;hsimp=yhs-fullyhosted_011&amp;hspart=iry&amp;p=tony+vincent+the+voice#id=1&amp;vid=2c5d4411350d59c3de1dfb65c19d9edc&amp;action=click" target="_blank">
                    <em>
                     The Voice
                    </em>
                   </a>
                   .
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   Tony Vincent is currently headlining the North American symphony tour
                   <em>
                   </em>
                   of
                   <em>
                    The Music of David Bowie,
                   </em>
                   and working as a record producer at his New York recording studio,
                   <a href="mailto:SoundShop370@tonyvincent.com" target="_blank">
                    Soundshop 370
                   </a>
                   .
                  </p>
                 </div>
