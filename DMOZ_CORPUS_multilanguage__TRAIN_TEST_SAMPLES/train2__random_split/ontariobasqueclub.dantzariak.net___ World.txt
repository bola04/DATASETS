     <h2>
      Ongi Etorri! Welcome
     </h2>
     <p>
      <a href="mus_tournament.html" target="_blank">
       <br/>
       <img align="right" alt="Mus 2018" height="186" src="images/mus_poster_2018_sm.jpg" width="144"/>
      </a>
      Ontario, Oregon Basque Club was founded in 1947.
     </p>
     <p>
      Thirteen years ago Ontario Basque Club held its first mus tournament. It was a small affair and most of the players were new to the game. Over the years the tournament, while still relatively small, has grown and attracted players from other Basque clubs from the Treasure Valley and even Salt Lake City. This year's tournament will be held on May 5, 2018. Players must be mebers of the club and the winners will go on to the NABO Championship to be held in Homedale, Idaho on June 2.
     </p>
