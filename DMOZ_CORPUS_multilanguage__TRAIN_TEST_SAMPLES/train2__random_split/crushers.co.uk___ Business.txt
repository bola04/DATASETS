          <h2>
           CMS Cepcor® | The difference since 1960
          </h2>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.cmscepcor.com/">
            <strong>
             CMS Cepcor®
            </strong>
           </a>
           have supplied hundreds of new, factory reconditioned, refurbished and used jaw crushers, cone crushers, impactors,  screens and feeders to mining and aggregate processing; establishing a worldwide reputation for the supply of quality crushing and screening plant.
          </p>
          <p>
           We are experts in the re-building of crushers, screens and feeders originally manufactured by Allis-Chalmers, Goodwin Barsby, Kue-Ken, Metso®, Nordberg, Parker, Pegson, Svedala, Sandvik®, Symons, Telsmith®, Terex® + more. All crushers that are reconditioned are re-built by our own OEM factory trained crusher technicians in Coalville, United Kingdom observing original factory tolerances and fitting new
           <a href="http://www.cmscepcor.com/crusher-spares">
            <strong>
             CMS Cepcor®
            </strong>
           </a>
           compatible
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.cmscepcor.com/crusher-spares" target="_blank">
             crusher spare parts
            </a>
           </strong>
           and premium manganese
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.cmscepcor.com/manganese-wear-parts" target="_blank">
             crusher liners
            </a>
           </strong>
           where required.
          </p>
          <p>
           Unlike normal machinery dealers; our approved used crushers, screens and feeders that undergo factory reconditioning can be seen test run in our crusher rebuild facility prior to despatch, giving you complete peace of mind.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.cmscepcor.com/services/goodwin-barsby">
            <strong>
             CMS Cepcor®
            </strong>
           </a>
           also manufacture the full range of
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.goodwinbarsby.com/" target="_blank">
             Goodwin Barsby
            </a>
           </strong>
           series 5 jaw crushers, granulators and vibrating grizzly feeders in the United Kingdom, supplying them worldwide.
          </p>
