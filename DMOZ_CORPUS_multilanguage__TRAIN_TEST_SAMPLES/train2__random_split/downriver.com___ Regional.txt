               <h1 style="text-align: center;">
                <strong>
                 Downriver Canoe Company – Your Next Great River Adventure!
                </strong>
               </h1>
               <h1>
               </h1>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                <strong>
                 Located in the stunning Shenandoah Valley, a short distance from Washington DC
                </strong>
                , the South Fork of the Shenandoah is a flowing mountain river long recognized as one of the premier canoeing rivers in the East.
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                The river flows north through the
                <strong>
                 Shenandoah Valley
                </strong>
                with Shenandoah National Park to the East and George Washington National Forest to the West.
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                The normally crystal clear water winds its way through surprisingly remote terrain while passing over a solid rock bottom creating many interesting riffles and pools, with some beginning white water (class II), making it
                <em>
                 an ideal river for novice and experienced canoeists
                </em>
                alike. Fishing is superb.
               </p>
