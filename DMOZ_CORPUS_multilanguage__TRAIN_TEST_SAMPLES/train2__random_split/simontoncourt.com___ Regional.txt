       </h2>
       <p class="p1">
        Simonton Court Hotel is a tropical boutique bed and breakfast located in the heart of Old Town Key West. Whether you’re relaxing by one of our four pools, enjoying the lush secluded beauty of our gardens and restored Victorian and Conch architecture, or exploring the world famous historic attractions of Key West just steps from our property, how you enjoy Key West is entirely up to you. You’ll be conveniently located just off the beaten path.
       </p>
       <p class="p1">
        Simonton Court Hotel, a Moloney Smith Project, is a living design property. With two acres to work with and an eclectic historical platform, nearly every inch has been meticulously crafted to give our guests the most unique Key West experience possible. World renowned artists and designers have poured themselves into Simonton Court Hotel and the lead designer, Susan Moloney, has made every second of that work her personal mission to oversee.
       </p>
