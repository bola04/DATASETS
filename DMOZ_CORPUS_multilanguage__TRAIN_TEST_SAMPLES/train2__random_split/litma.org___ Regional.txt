    <p>
     The Long Island Traditional Music Association is an all volunteer, not
     for profit organization.
    </p>
    <hr/>
    <p>
     LITMA T-shirts! LITMA Mugs!
     <a href="https://www.cafepress.com/litmalogoshop" target="_blank">
      See the LITMA store on Cafe Press
     </a>
    </p>
    <hr/>
    <p>
     We present many Music and Dance events throughout the year, and also sponsor programs
         to give musicians a chance to learn to play and sing traditional music in many flavors.
    </p>
    <hr/>
    <p>
     We have a modest presence over on Facebook. Look for the
     <a href="https://www.facebook.com/groups/litma/" target="_blank">
      Group 'LITMA'
     </a>
    </p>
    <hr/>
    <p>
     Inclement weather announcements will be made here, on the LITMA mailing list and on Facebook
    </p>
    <hr/>
    <p>
     Schedule page updates are in progress for 2018 Spring, Summer &amp; Fall events
    </p>
    <hr/>
    <p>
     Site updates include a revised LITMA logo [Thanks Barbara Jo!]
    </p>
