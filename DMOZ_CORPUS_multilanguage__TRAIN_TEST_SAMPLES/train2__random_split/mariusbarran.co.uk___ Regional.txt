         <p align="justify">
          Marius Barran offers 
               a full range of architectural services in both rural and urban environments, 
               for residential and smaller specialised commercial buildings.  
               The practice was established in 1982 following eight years working 
               with the Greater London Council Architects Department and three 
               years with Green, Lloyd and Adams.
         </p>
         <p align="justify">
          <font color="#CCCC99">
           During the past ten years, 
               two specialist strands have emerged: adaptations of residential 
               property for disabled use and the restoration, alteration and extension 
               of listed buildings.
          </font>
         </p>
         <p align="justify">
          <font color="#CCCC99">
           Our aim is to assess the 
               particular needs of each client, consider them carefully and provide
