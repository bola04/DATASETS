                     <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                      <a href="http://www.kaliptusotelkemer.com/2012/11/kaliptus-otel-kemer.html">
                       Kaliptus Otel Kemer
                      </a>
                     </h3>
                     <div class="post-header">
                      <div class="post-header-line-1">
                      </div>
                     </div>
                     <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-2801681643506218363" itemprop="description articleBody">
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <span style="color: #444444; font-family: inherit;">
                        <b>
                        </b>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-yp4PqRhEw48/VgqwEBh5v3I/AAAAAAAAJkI/rgylua1TeiM/s1600/kaliptusotel1.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="115" src="https://4.bp.blogspot.com/-yp4PqRhEw48/VgqwEBh5v3I/AAAAAAAAJkI/rgylua1TeiM/s400/kaliptusotel1.jpg" width="400"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="color: #444444; font-family: inherit;">
                       <b>
                        Tatil günlerinizi eşsiz güzelliklere sahip Akdenizin mavi bayraklı sahillerindegeçirmek istiyorsanız.
                       </b>
                      </span>
                      <span style="color: #444444; font-family: inherit;">
                       <b>
                        Bulunduğunuz konum itibariyle sizlere huzurlu, keyifli ve rahat bir tatil sunan Kaliptus Otel Kemerde keşfedilmeye değer bir tatil yeridir.
                       </b>
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="color: #444444; font-family: inherit;">
                       <b>
                        <br/>
                       </b>
                      </span>
                      <span style="color: #444444; font-family: inherit;">
                       <b>
                        Otelimiz Kemerin tamda merkezinde olup Denize 250 mt. Plajlara 600 mt. ve Gece Clublerine 300 mt. mesafededir.
                       </b>
                      </span>
                      <b style="color: #444444; font-family: inherit;">
                       Ulaşım ise Antalya havalalanına 65 km. mesefede bulunmaktadır.1994 yılında inşa edilen otelimiz 1000 mt. toplam alanda 2 adet 3 katlı bina yapısana sahiptir.
                      </b>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-family: inherit;">
                       <span style="color: #444444;">
                        <br/>
                       </span>
                       <span style="color: #444444;">
                        <br/>
                       </span>
                       <span style="color: #444444;">
                        <br/>
                       </span>
                       <span style="color: #444444;">
                        <br/>
                       </span>
                       <b>
                        <span style="color: #a64d79;">
                         The hotel enjoys a very central location, being just 50 meters behind the main shopping street in the resort centre. The public beach is 250 meters and
                        </span>
                       </b>
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       <span style="color: #a64d79; font-family: inherit;">
                        a short walk and other local amenities, such as restaurants and bars can be found within a close proximity of the hotel.
                       </span>
                      </b>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="color: #a64d79; font-family: inherit;">
                       <b>
                        <br/>
                       </b>
                       <b>
                        Das Hotel befindet sich im Zentrum von Kemer,50 meter hinter der Haupteinkaufsstraße wo alle Restaurants und Bars in Gehweite liegen. Das Hotel liegt 250 m vom Kies/ Sandstrand entfernt.
                       </b>
                      </span>
                      <br/>
