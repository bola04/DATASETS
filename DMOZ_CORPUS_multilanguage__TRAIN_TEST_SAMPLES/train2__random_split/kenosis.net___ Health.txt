      </h3>
      <p>
       WHAT IS KENOSIS? The word kenosis comes from Greek and means to empty. In Western culture there’s the ever-increasing emphasis on faster and more at the expense of quality in our lives. In today's confusing world there’s a need for ways to discern what is real and what truly matters. When the mind stills, peace comes and shapes who we are in the world. Then we naturally reach out to touch others by role modeling genuine peacemaking — and a ripple effect is created. We owe it to future generations. We owe it to the planet.
      </p>
