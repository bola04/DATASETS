       <i>
        Spandan
       </i>
       is a sanskrit word that signifies vitality.
             Often loosely translated to 'pulse' or 'alive,' it has more to do
             with the gift of life than living. Spandan.com gets its name from
             the Maulana Azad Medical College (MAMC) magazine, Spandan.
      </p>

       Spandan.com is an unofficial MAMC website, created

             explore most of the website. If you can't find what you need, direct
