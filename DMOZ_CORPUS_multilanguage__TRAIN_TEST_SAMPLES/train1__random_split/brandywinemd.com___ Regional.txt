         <header class="entry-header">
          <h1 class="entry-title">
           Welcome to Brandywine, Maryland!
          </h1>
         </header>
         <!-- .entry-header -->
         <div class="entry-content cf">
          <p>
           <a href="http://brandywinemd.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/BrandywineMD-map3.gif">
            <img alt="Brandywine MD 20613 map - south and southeast of Clinton, south and southwest of Croom in Upper Marlboro and north of Waldorf" class=" wp-image-650 alignright" height="224" src="http://brandywinemd.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/BrandywineMD-map3.gif" title="Click to see larger view" width="264"/>
           </a>
           This site encompasses all that pertains to the southeast corner of Prince George’s County, extending slightly over into Charles County. See the
           <a href="http://brandywinemd.com/statistics/">
            statistics page
           </a>
           for more information about Brandywine, Aquasco, and Eagle Harbor demographics.
          </p>
          <p>
           There are new and exciting things happening in Brandywine. There’s a
           <a href="http://www.pgparks.com/602/Brandywine-Revitalization-Preservation-S" target="_blank" title="Parks and Planning Study will open in a new window">
            Brandywine Revitalization Study
           </a>
           that could help make the community walkable and bikeable and the
           <a href="http://www.mncppc.org/268/Southern-Area-Aquatic-Recreation-Complex" target="_blank" title="Parks and Recreation will open in a new window">
            Southern Area Aquatic and Recreation Complex
           </a>
           is also being developed in Brandywine which will have pools and community center activities for all ages. These projects are for the center of the town, whereas the county is protecting the scenic and natural area of the rural tier to the east.
          </p>
          <p>
           If you’re a new resident,
           <a href="http://brandywinemd.com/organizations-2/">
            find your local civic association
           </a>
           to meet and get updates from your local representatives and community safety officers.
          </p>
          <p>
           Find local
           <a href="http://brandywinemd.com/organizations-2/storesproduct-oriented-businesses/" title="Stores/Product-Oriented Businesses">
            businesses
           </a>
           and
           <a href="http://brandywinemd.com/organizations-2/" title="Organizations">
            organizations
           </a>
           and learn about area
           <a href="http://brandywinemd.com/history/" title="Brandywine History">
            history
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://brandywinemd.com/faq/entertainment-in-the-brandywine-area/" title="Entertainment In The Brandywine Area">
            Find events and entertainment in the area check out this page.
           </a>
           You can also like our
           <a href="https://www.facebook.com/BrandywineMaryland" target="_blank" title="Facebook will open in a new window">
            Facebook page
           </a>
           and post events there. You can also buy things
           <a href="http://washingtondc.craigslist.org/search/sso?sort=rel&amp;query=brandywine" target="_blank">
            for sale by Brandywine residents here
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="https://www.facebook.com/BrandywineMaryland" target="_TOP" title="Brandywine, Maryland">
            <img alt="" class="alignright" src="https://badge.facebook.com/badge/409360182491770.1802.1147656536.png" style="border: 0px;"/>
           </a>
           <!-- Facebook Badge START -->
           The photograph in the top banner is an example of our beautiful rural tier. These lily pads are in a wetlands which can be seen from Brandywine Road about 2 miles east of Rt. 301 across the railroad tracks on the right (south side of the street).
          </p>
          <p>
           <!-- Facebook Badge END -->
           Please note that Brandywine is
           <strong>
            not
           </strong>
           an incorporated town and this web site was created by a resident as a community service. Clicking on sponsor links will help support this site.
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
            For more information about this site, to report problems or suggestions, or how you can add your listing or sponsor this site, contact
           </span>
           <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
            Joyce through this email here (click if you have email software or for web mail, copy the email in the image):
            <br/>
            <a href="http://tinyurl.com/brandywinemd">
             <img alt="email" class="alignleft wp-image-815 size-full" height="16" src="http://brandywinemd.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/email.png" width="166"/>
            </a>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Sponsors:
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.spinemd.com/" target="_blank">
            Virginia Spine Institute
           </a>
           is dedicated to improving people’s lives through treatment plans designed to heal the spine and help with pain management. They offer operative care, non operative treatments and therapy options to find the best treatment plan to get you back on your feet.
          </p>
