It's been trading for over 30 years so we know what we're talking about. Piano wire, aluminium and brass profiles and sheet of many lengths and sizes are also available as well as plastic profiles by Plastruct.

           "I want some glue" is what many customer ask but glues aren't simple.  We always try to establish what the customer is sticking before we try to find a glue suitable. There are superglues (cyanoacrylates) of varying viscosities, as well as Epoxy glues with different setting times, odourless glues, canopy glues, wood glues, waterproof glues,  aliphatic resin  glues. So, what did you say you were sticking?  We sell Zap, Vitalbond, Humbrol, BSI, PVA,  to name but a few.
          </p>

We have whole walls covered in spares and accessories for planes and helicopters  including,  Solarfilm, Solartex, Solartim,  and Trimline in a wide variety of colours,  Deluxe Materials, J Perkins, Dubro, Etronix, Flightline,  E-Flite, Blade,  Ripmax, Radio Active and SLEC with fuels by Model Tecnics and Tornado. Car spares by Thunder Tiger, Ansmann, HPI,  Kyosho, Mardave, and Schumacher.

If the train is too far gone, we will tell you before spending money on a lost cause.  We are a Hornby authorized Service agent. We also repair model cars of certain brands.
