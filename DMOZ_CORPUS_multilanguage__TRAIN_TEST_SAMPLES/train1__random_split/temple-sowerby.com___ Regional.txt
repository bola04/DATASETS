           <h2>
            <a href="http://temple-sowerby.com/business/how-to-get-more-youtube-channel-views.html" target="_self">
             How To Get More YouTube Channel Views
            </a>
           </h2>
          </div>
          <!-- End .blog-post-title -->
          <p>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Success on YouTube is both art and science. Many keep asking the question of
           <a href="http://www.affiliateignition.com/promote-youtube-channel-learn-tips/">
            <strong>
             how to promote my youtube channel
            </strong>
           </a>
           . If you’re making videos for YouTube and still not quite satisfied with the number of channel views you’re getting, you must be doing this wrong. Many people fail to increase their YouTube channel views and blame it on luck.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Although many consider YouTube is a great place to upload videos of cats chasing dogs or kids opening their Christmas presents, you should never overlook the fact that this video sharing website offers you as a business potential exposure to hundreds of millions of people. Keep these basic steps in mind while uploading your next video:
          </p>
          <ol style="text-align: justify;">
           <li>
            <strong>
             Quality, Not Quantity
            </strong>
           </li>
          </ol>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           With millions of videos on YouTube, you have to make sure your channel stands out above the rest, and you can achieve this by releasing excellent quality videos and not just flooding the channel with short clips that do not mean anything. Remember, YouTube viewers love “how to” videos and general advice videos.
          </p>
          <p>
           <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="641" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/eStdHFOG3tA?feature=oembed" width="1140">
           </iframe>
          </p>
          <ol start="2" style="text-align: justify;">
           <li>
            <strong>
             Video Standards
            </strong>
           </li>
          </ol>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Videos need to be watchable, have decent audio, look like they have been filmed to a decent standard and last the maximum of three minutes if you are trying to promote a product or service. If you release a video that looks awful, this will only reflect on the product or service that you are promoting, so make sure that you spend some time on making good videos.
          </p>
          <ol start="3" style="text-align: justify;">
           <li>
            <strong>
             Find Friends
            </strong>
           </li>
          </ol>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           YouTube allows your channel or account to make friends with others, which in the same respect as Twitter allows you to build up a fan base that will be notified every time you add a video. It is important to remember that because friends are public on everyone’s channel, you might then start picking up extra viewers from the friends of your friends that have seen your channel listed on other people’s accounts.
          </p>
          <ol start="4" style="text-align: justify;">
           <li>
            <strong>
             Subscribe To Others
            </strong>
           </li>
          </ol>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           If you subscribe to other YouTube channels that are roughly in the same niche as yours, the chances are they might subscribe back. This means that when ever you release a video, subscribers will get notified and if they then favorite your video, all of their friends and subscribers could see your video as well. Again, a little bit like Twitter, you have to make an effort to encourage other people to find out about you, but once you get going, it will seem much easier as starting a channel is always the hardest part.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="how to promote my youtube channel" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-43" height="301" sizes="(max-width: 547px) 100vw, 547px" src="http://temple-sowerby.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/07/youtube-views-mobile.jpg" srcset="http://temple-sowerby.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/07/youtube-views-mobile.jpg 547w, http://temple-sowerby.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/07/youtube-views-mobile-300x165.jpg 300w" width="547"/>
          </p>
          <ol start="5" style="text-align: justify;">
           <li>
            <strong>
             Promote Your Channel
            </strong>
           </li>
          </ol>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Make sure that you let people know you have a YouTube channel by placing the logo on your website,
           <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Email" rel="nofollow">
            <strong>
             emails
            </strong>
           </a>
           and other forms of online marketing that you might do. To make your YouTube channel work, you have to tell all of your customers so that they then tell other people about your channel. You need quite a few subscribers and friends to make the channel work efficiently, so spend some time telling the world about your fantastic new channel.
          </p>
