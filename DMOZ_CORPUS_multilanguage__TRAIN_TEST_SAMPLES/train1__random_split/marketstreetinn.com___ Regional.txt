         <h1>
          Discover Warm Hospitality in Taylorville, Illinois
         </h1>
         <p>
          Innkeepers Joe and Myrna Hauser welcome you to their historic 1892 Queen Anne Victorian bed and breakfast in central Illinois.
         </p>
         <p>
          You’ll find warm hospitality,
          <a href="breakfast.html" title="Breakfast &amp; Recipes">
           delicious breakfasts
          </a>
          , exceptional amenities and
          <a href="accommodations.html" title="Guest Rooms View All">
           ten inviting guestrooms
          </a>
          at our
          <a href="photos.html" title="Photo Gallery">
           beautiful inn
          </a>
          . In fact, many continue to select Market Street Inn Bed &amp; Breakfast as the “inn of choice” for repeat visits. We are the perfect setting for a delightful vacation,
          <a href="specials.html" title="Specials &amp; Packages">
           special occasion
          </a>
          celebration, or even for your intimate
          <a href="weddings.html" title="Weddings">
           wedding
          </a>
          here in central Illinois.
         </p>

          <a class="expand-collapse-text">

          <p>
           Our
           <a href="main-inn.html" title="Main Inn Rooms">
            Main House
           </a>
           greets visitors with a Victorian wraparound porch overlooking our gardens of 100 hostas and numerous other perennials. The
           <a href="inn.html" title="About the Inn">
            historic home
           </a>
           features a total of six original fireplaces, each with its own unique mantel. Distinct details of ornate woodwork, fretwork, beveled glass windows, pocket doors, six electric fireplaces, antiques and Oriental rugs add to the home’s luxurious personality. Our grand oak staircase leads to eight of our guestrooms, each delightfully decorated and offering modern amenities such as private baths with double-whirlpool tubs, air conditioning, cable TV, and complimentary Wi-Fi.
           <br/>
           The Carriage House offers two guestrooms: one is an ADA-approved, fully accessible unit at ground level with a kitchenette, and the other a deluxe 550 square-foot suite.
          </p>
          <p>
           Our smoke-free and pet-free inn is in downtown Taylorville. Children are welcome in the Carriage House and the Main House’s larger second-floor rooms with prior arrangement. We are
           <a href="directions.html" rel="noopener" target="_blank" title="Directions">
            located
           </a>
           less than 30 miles from both Springfield and Decatur, and convenient to a wide variety of
           <a href="area.html" rel="noopener" target="_blank" title="The Area">
            area activities
           </a>
           . If you are touring the United States, we are less than 21 miles from Route 66, the “Mother Road.”
