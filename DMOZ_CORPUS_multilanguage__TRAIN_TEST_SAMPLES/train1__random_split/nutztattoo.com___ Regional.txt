        <div class="col-md-4">
         <h3>
          They're the Nutz!
         </h3>
         <p>
          With years of experience under their belts, Nutz and Jackie decided to make their shared vision a reality in April 2005 by opening their own tattoo and piercing studio, NUTZ TATTOO, in Ware, Hertfordshire.
         </p>
         <p>
          Nutz and Jackie have been adorning peoples bodies with art and modifications for well over a decade now, with their studio going from strength to strength and Nutz winning several awards for different styles of work.
         </p>
         <p>
          As well as tattooing, Jackie is also a professional body piercer. Be it your first earlobe piercing or something more adventurous, she can cater to your adornment of choice. Her loyal following of customers showcase a wide range of body modifications, and her passion for the perfect piercing.
         </p>
        </div>
        <div class="col-md-4">
         <h3>
          Artists
         </h3>
         <p>
          Both artists have their own unique style, and are comfortable using different styles to give you the tattoo you desire. All of the work undertaken at Nutz Tattoo is customised, one of a kind designs. So each piece is unique. Just like you!
         </p>
         <p>
          Giving a little slice of alternative culture to the lovely picturesque town of Ware, Nutz and Jackie are best friends as well as business partners which gives the studio a more friendly, family feel, making you feel welcome and comfortable.
         </p>
         <p>
          Where? Ware is only 30 mins from London and the same distance again to Cambridge with good public transport and parking so very easy to find.
         </p>
        </div>
        <div class="col-md-4">
         <h3>
          Appointments
         </h3>
         <p>
          If you are interested in getting tattooed by Nutz or Jackie you need to come down to the studio with as much information about your design as you can (pictures, sketches photos etc). You can get the ball rolling with an email though if this is easier. This helps us to give you the best tattoo possible.
         </p>
         <p>
          We only book up work a maximum of 3 months at a time. The customers who we have already started work on, will get first priority to get them finished, then we will only be taking on new work as we finish old pieces. This way, people won't be waiting months (or even years) to get their tattoos finished. It also gives us a chance to do fresh work more often.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
