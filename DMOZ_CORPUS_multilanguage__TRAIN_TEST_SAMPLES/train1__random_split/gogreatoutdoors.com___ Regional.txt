          <h2 class="align-center">
           Fertilization
          </h2>
         </a>
         <p>
          What does your grass need for healthy growth? More than likely you answered sunlight and water, which are both correct answers. However, what's missing from this equation is the fact that any plant life also needs fertilizer, too.
         </p>
         <p>
          Fertilization is what provides your plant life, including grass, trees and shrubs, with the nutrients required to promote healthy development and growth. All of this is what reaches the root system of your plant life and helps it. The bottom line is that this additional service is the difference between your grass just surviving and your grass actually thriving.
         </p>
         <p>
          Because we offer
          <a href="https://www.gogreatoutdoors.com/ann-arbor-organic-lawn-care.php">
           organic lawn care
          </a>
          , you can also feel good knowing we have greener solutions that don't involve harsh chemicals. If you want all of the benefits of fertilization without the repercussions of chemicals, let us take care of your project by offering alternative solutions. Taking this route means that your project will be more Eco-friendly.
         </p>
         <p>
          These additional nutrients don't just make your grass look better, they help your plant life to be able to better fight off potential health threats. Weeds and pests don't stand a chance when your grass is full of vital nutrition. In fact, the right fertilization can even help your plants to do well in poor soil conditions.
         </p>
         <p>
          Fertilizers have been around as long as planting and agriculture has, and its a method that should continue to be implemented. Fail to sign up for this and you are likely to be disappointed with the condition that your grass is in. Why go through the effort of getting your lawn established, only to have it die as the result of a lack of important nutrients?
