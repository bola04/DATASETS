     <h1>
      Welcome to Lindsay McKenna Limited
     </h1>
     <div id="guide">
      <h3 class="notopbottom">
       <a href="purchase/on-line-guidebook.html">
        Electronic Guidebooks
       </a>
      </h3>
      <p class="notop">
       <img align="left" alt="Guidebook" src="images/change/project_guide_sm.jpg" style="margin-right:5px"/>
       Both of our comprehensive 
 	guidebooks, Mastering Change Through Project Management Guide and 
 	The Practitioner's Guide to High Performance Teamwork, are now available in electronic format. More information can be found
       <a href="purchase/on-line-guidebook.html">
        here
       </a>
       .
      </p>
     </div>
     <p>
      We are international experts in change management and high performance teams, working across all five continents with blue 
 	chip corporations, olympic teams and local charities and combine our commercial enterprise with the management of a South African 
 	based not for profit Conservation Company.
     </p>
     <p>
      The ability of a business to deliver change and work through teams remains core to success - this is not a fad but fundamental 
 	to ongoing success in the market. Our approach offers a unique and potent fusion between the people and technical aspects of 
 	delivering change and working through teams.
     </p>
     <p>
      We challenge the ongoing separation in the market place between the people and technical aspects and the acceptance of 
 	software as the way to achieve change as opposed to a tool to use when appropriate.
     </p>
     <p>
      What's offered is a pragmatism rarely witnessed. Back to basics, peeling away the politics, bureaucracy and technology, 
 	that can so often get in the way of personal effectiveness and true progression of the work. Our workshops are based on 
 	client reality with progress of real conflicts, issues, projects and teams. The depth and quality of support material 
 	leaves our clients with concrete takeaways that act as constant reference guides through the journey of change and 
 	the development of a team.
     </p>
