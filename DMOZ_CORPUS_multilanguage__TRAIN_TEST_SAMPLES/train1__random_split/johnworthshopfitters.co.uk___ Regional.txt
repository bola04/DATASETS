       <h1>
        Retail &amp; Commercial Shopfitting in Leicester
       </h1>
       <p>
        John Worth Shopfitters are based in Leicester and provide a full national shop fitting service to various businesses throughout the UK. We have the skill and expertise to refit, re-design and completely transform your shop into a modern and bespoke retail environment. Our dedicated team of UK shop fitters are highly trained in all areas of shopfitting.
       </p>
       <h2>
        Shop Fitters With Experience Operating Nationwide
       </h2>
       <p>
        Traditional, quality shopfitting is the core of our business, however the company does have a wealth of experience in retail fittings. Combining modern materials, traditional craftsmanship and an experienced workforce, we cover all aspects of the national shopfitters industry. This involves complete refits, ceilings, displays, decoration, air conditioning, roofing, signage, shopfittings and flooring throughout retail stores in Leicester,
        <a href="/nottingham.php" title="Shopfitters In Nottingham">
         Nottingham
        </a>

        <a href="/birmingham.php" title="Shopfitters In Birmingham">
         Birmingham
        </a>
        and the entire UK and Ireland.
       </p>
       <p>
        Since we were first established in 1994 we have gained a much respected reputation throughout the UK due to our dedication to providing quality workmanship with all our projects. Our strong focus on customer service has also helped establish our strong reputation. We only use quality assured manufacturers and fully certified plumbers and electricians on our projects so you can be guaranteed of quality workmanship and professionalism every time. We provide our shop fitting services to all types of clients both big and small throughout the UK. You can take a look at some of our current clients by visiting our client list page or take a look at some of our recent projects on our portfolio page.
       </p>
