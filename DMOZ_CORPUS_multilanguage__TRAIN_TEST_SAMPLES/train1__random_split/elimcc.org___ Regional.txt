        <h2 class="entry-title">
         Services &amp; Times
        </h2>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <h3>
         </h3>
         <h3>
          Sunday
         </h3>
         <p>
          8:30 AM –
          <b>
           Worship Practice
          </b>
          <br/>
          9:30 AM –
          <b>
           Prayer
          </b>
          – Please join us for prayer in the Sanctuary each Sunday morning – all are welcome!
          <br/>
          10:00 AM –
          <b>
           Sunday Morning Worship
          </b>
          –  Following the morning worship, our children gather for Children’s Church during the sermon time.   Children are always welcome to sit with their parents in the Adult service.
         </p>
         <h3>
          Wednesday
         </h3>
         <p>
          6:30PM –
          <strong>
           Small Group Meeting
          </strong>
          downstairs in the Community Room.    Come join us!  (use the side entrance of the church).
         </p>
         <h3 align="left">
         </h3>
         <h3 align="left">
          <a href="http://elimcc.org/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/communion.jpg">
           <img alt="Recieving Communion #2" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-649" height="87" src="http://elimcc.org/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/communion.jpg" style="width: 114px;" width="565"/>
          </a>
          <strong>
           Comm
          </strong>
          <strong>
           union
          </strong>
          is held on the first Sunday of each month. Please join us as we partake in this special ordinance!
         </h3>
         <p>
         </p>
         <h3>
          <strong>
           Missions Sunday
          </strong>
          is the first Sunday of each month. This is a time for us to give above and beyond the tithe to missions around the world.
         </h3>
         <p>
          <a href="http://elimcc.org/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/missions.jpg">
           <img alt="missions" class="size-full wp-image-648 aligncenter" height="135" src="http://elimcc.org/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/missions.jpg" style="width: 255px;" width="640"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <h3 align="left">
          <strong>
           Family Fellowship
          </strong>
          (fourth Sunday of each month).
         </h3>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.elimcc.org/church/fellowship.html" style="color: #ff4b33; text-align: -webkit-center;">
           <img alt="" border="0" class="alignleft" height="72" src="http://www.elimcc.org/images/church/family_fellowship.jpg" style="border-style: initial; border-color: initial; border-width: 0px;" width="128"/>
          </a>
          Every month, we get together as a church family and share a meal together.   We call this monthly gathering
          <strong>
           “Family Fellowship”
          </strong>
          and we love to have our visitors stay and eat with us. The best part is, you don’t have to bring anything but yourselves.  This event follows the 10 a.m. Worship Service.
         </p>
         <p align="center">
         </p>
