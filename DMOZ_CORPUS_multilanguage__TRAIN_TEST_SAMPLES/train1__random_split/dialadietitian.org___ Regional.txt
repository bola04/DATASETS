        </a>
        <h1 class="page-header">
         Healthy Eating
        </h1>
        <div class="region region-content">
         <section class="block block-system clearfix" id="block-system-main">
          <article about="/healthy-eating" class="node node-page clearfix" id="node-2" typeof="foaf:Document">
           <header>
            <span class="rdf-meta element-hidden" content="Healthy Eating" property="dc:title">
            </span>
           </header>
           <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
            <div class="field-items">
             <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
              <p>
               <span class="font-size-plus">
                Every day, we make choices about the food we eat and our lifestyles. We can make choices for ourselves and our families that make a real difference to our ability to remain healthy and active now, and enjoy life to its fullest in the future. Healthy eating promotes and supports social, physical, and mental wellbeing for everyone, at all ages and stages of life.
               </span>
              </p>
              <p>
               <span class="font-size-plus">
                To learn more about the healthy eating and nutrition programs and services offered by HealthLink BC's registered dietitians, see
                <a href="dietitian-services">
                 Dietitian Services at HealthLink BC
                </a>
                .
               </span>
              </p>
             </div>
            </div>
           </div>
           <footer>
           </footer>
          </article>
         </section>
         <section class="block block-views clearfix" id="block-views-in-this-section-block">

           Information in this Section
          </h2>
          <div class="view view-in-this-section view-id-in_this_section view-display-id-block information-in-this-section view-dom-id-9afc10caf4cb92e6e6d9aec9d99d7c13">
           <div class="view-content">
            <div class="section-views-row contextual-links-region">
             <h3>
              For Everyone
             </h3>
             <p class="field-content">
              These healthy eating resources can help you meet nutrition recommendations for any age and stage of life, whether you are trying to plan meals for home and at work, are feeding your family, or trying to make lifestyle choices to reduce the risk of chronic disease.
             </p>
             <div class="read-more">
              <a href="/healthy-eating/for-everyone">
               Go To For Everyone
              </a>
             </div>
            </div>
            <div class="section-views-row contextual-links-region">
             <h3>
              For Your Age &amp; Stage
             </h3>
             <p class="field-content">
              Some nutrition recommendations only apply to certain people at certain times during their life. For more specific recommendations for infants and children, women and seniors, click on the link below.
             </p>
             <div class="read-more">
              <a href="/healthy-eating/your-age-and-stage">
               Go To For Your Age and Stage
              </a>
             </div>
            </div>
            <div class="section-views-row contextual-links-region">
             <h3>
              For a Healthy Weight
             </h3>
             <p class="field-content">
              Well-balanced eating and physical activity are the most important things you can do to stay at a healthy weight. These resources can help you get to and maintain the healthiest weight to feel your best.
             </p>
             <div class="read-more">
              <a href="/healthy-eating/healthy-weight">
               Go To For Healthy Weight
              </a>
             </div>
            </div>
            <div class="section-views-row contextual-links-region">
             <h3>
              For Your Condition
             </h3>
             <p class="field-content">
              Healthy eating can help you feel your best, reduce your symptoms and keep you as healthy as possible. These resources can help you make healthy choices while managing chronic disease.
             </p>
             <div class="read-more">
              <a href="/healthy-eating/your-condition">
               Go To For Your Condition
              </a>
             </div>
            </div>
            <div class="section-views-row contextual-links-region">
             <h3>
              For Schools &amp; Communities
             </h3>
             <p class="field-content">
              Making healthy choices easy is an important part of creating healthy eating environments where we live, learn, work and play. These resources provide you with information on nutrition standards for schools and public buildings in B.C.
             </p>
             <div class="read-more">
              <a href="/healthy-eating/schools-and-communities">
               Go To For Schools and Communities
              </a>
             </div>
            </div>
            <div class="section-views-row contextual-links-region">
             <h3>
              For Professionals
             </h3>
             <p class="field-content">
              Many different groups have an important role in planning, promoting, and teaching healthy eating and healthy lifestyle choices. Whether you are a dietitian, school administrator,  or part of another group, these resources can assist you with your plan or program.
             </p>
             <div class="read-more">
              <a href="/healthy-eating/professionals">
               Go To For Professionals
              </a>
             </div>
            </div>
            <div class="section-views-row contextual-links-region">
             <h3>
              Healthy Eating &amp; Nutrition Topics
             </h3>
             <p class="field-content">
              Find healthy eating and nutrition topics that are important to you. Whether you are planning meals for home or work, feeding your family, or trying to make lifestyle choices to reduce the risk of chronic disease, these healthy eating resources can help you feel your best at any age.
             </p>
             <div class="read-more">
              <a href="/healthy-eating/healthy-eating-and-nutrition-topics">
               Go To Healthy Eating and Nutrition Topics
              </a>
