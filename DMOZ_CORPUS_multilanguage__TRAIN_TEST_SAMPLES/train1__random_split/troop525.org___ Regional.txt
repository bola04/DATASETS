            <h2>
             <a href="/node/1" title=" Welcome to Troop 525's Website!">
              Welcome to Troop 525's Website!
             </a>
            </h2>
            <div class="node-content">
             <div class="page-content">
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               <img alt="" height="361" src="/system/files/public/photos/Troopphoto2018.jpg" width="640"/>
               <br/>
              </p>
              <p>
               Welcome to the website of Troop 525 in Peachtree Corners, GA! Chartered with eight boys in August 1984 Troop 525 is boy-led and each is required to hold leadership positions as he advances in rank. The troop meets each Monday night, while school is in session, beginning at 7:30 PM at Simpsonwood United Methodist Church (SUMC)
               <a href="https://maps.google.com/maps?q=4500+Jones+Bridge+Circle,+Peachtree+Corners,+GA+30092&amp;hl=en&amp;sll=32.678125,-83.178297&amp;sspn=4.6785,11.337891&amp;t=h&amp;hnear=4500+Jones+Bridge+Cir,+Norcross,+Gwinnett,+Georgia+30092&amp;z=16" target="_blank" title="Google map showing location of SUMC">
                4500 Jones Bridge Circle, Peachtree Corners, GA 30092
               </a>
               . This site contains information for the scouts, parents, members and volunteers of our troop. Visitors are always welcome. Come join us!
              </p>
              <p>
               Boys who are older than 10, or who have completed the fifth grade can join the Boy Scouting program.  Cub Scouting is for boys in the first through fifth grades, or 7 to 10 years of age. For information about Cub Scouts please contact Pack 525 (pack525cubmaster @ gwinnettbsa dot org).
              </p>

              Seabase 2019
             </a>
            </h2>
            <table border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" width="100%">
             <tr>
              <td class="info">
               Posted by
               <b>

            <div class="node-content">
             <div class="announcement">
              <div class="flexinode-body flexinode-2">
               <div class="flexinode-textarea-3">
                <div class="form-item">
                 <label>
                  Message:
                 </label>
                 <p>
                  Troop 525 will be sending three crews to Seabase Out Island Adventure next year (tentatively late May/early June).  There are currently five remaining slots.  Two of them need to be adult leaders; the other three can be leaders or youth.  If you are interested or have any questions, please contact Robert Gates.
                  <br/>
                  <br/>
                  If you are already signed up to go to Seabase in 2019, please plan on attending a planning meeting on
                  <a href="/node/2348">
                   August 13
                  </a>
                  .
                  <br/>
                  <br/>
                  All leaders will need to be registered with the troop, have an updated BSA Medical form parts ABC, be current in updated YPT training, take the Wilderness First Aid training (WFA), and have current CPR certification.  Also, all participants will need to be able to pass the standard BSA swim test and be certified as a swimmer.  It is recommended that you take the
                  <a href="/node/2347">
                   Fall WFA course
                  </a>
                  , as the Spring course is popular and may fill up.
                  <br/>
                  <br/>
                  Click
                  <a href="http://www.bsaseabase.org/Adventures.aspx">
                   here
                  </a>
                  for more information on Seabase Out Island Adventure.
                 </p>
