     Welcome to my homepage! I wrote this page to promote the screenplays that

 film companies an idea of what I am capable of writing. This webpage was
 also designed to help my fellow screenwriters! That's right, I have included
 some links to various websites that I found to be very helpful!

       My Background

 majors because of budget cuts in the School of Communications.

     <u>
      <b>
       My Qualifications As An Actor/Writer
      </b>
     </u>
    </p>
    <p>

 a child actor (Georgia and South Carolina). I was in the State of South
 Carolina's gifted acting program. I am a member of the American Screenwriters
 Association (ASA). I have completed the video version of Syd Field's Screenwriting
 Workshop. I have studied Lew Hunter's book entitled
     <cite>
      <i>
       Lew Hunter's
 Screenwriting 434
      </i>
     </cite>

 that Lew Hunter teaches at UCLA. I have read countless other books on the art
 of screenwriting.
