         <h5>
          Welcome to Transfer Oil
         </h5>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Transfer Oil S.p.A., with more than 35 years of experience, are today one of the major independent thermoplastic and PTFE hose manufacturers for various applications in the medium, high and very high pressure range.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Since its founding all products manufactured by Transfer Oil are made in Italy.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          With a constant attention for quality and innovation, Transfer Oil continue to grow its strategic partner base and product range to offer solutions for a variety of specialized industries.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Our distribution network in more than 65 countries around the globe is certain to offer quick availability for whatever your demand of thermoplastic hose products may be. With a total capacity of more than 13 million meters (42 million feet) per year, Transfer Oil hose products are divided into four main segments:
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <br/>
          <strong>
           TO HYDRAULIC
          </strong>
          – thermoplastic hose and fitting solutions used in a wide variety of high pressure hydraulic fluid power management applications such as general hydraulic systems for fork-lifts, steering, aerial platforms, cranes, mobile devices, rescue tooling and off-shore techniques, including non-conductive variants. All hoses comply to the relevant ISO – SAE – EN standards in place.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <strong>
           TO INDUSTRIAL
          </strong>
          – thermoplastic and PTFE hose and fitting solutions for a wide variety of industrial applications such as high pressure sewer jetting, greasing, hydrogen &amp; CNG refilling systems, gas and water supply, food industry, paint spray and aggressive fluids, air breathing, horticulture and many more.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <strong>
           TO UHP
          </strong>
          – Multi Spiral Ultra High Pressure hoses and fittings (up to 4000 bar/58000 psi), characterized by a combination of different spiral steel reinforcement layers, perfect for waterjet cutting, tube &amp; tanks cleaning, surface preparation &amp; paint removal, hydro demolition, ships &amp; vessel cleaning, waterblast and general industrial cleaning.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <strong>
           GOMAX
          </strong>
          - thermoplastic hose and fitting solutions for the industry of air conditioning and refrigeration used for suction and discharge lines, and also for pressure switches, manometers and oil lines connection thanks to the world recognized QUADRA Capillary hose system.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Hoses made by Transfer Oil are made to the highest internal standards which have been accredited by ISO 9001:2008 certification. Continuity and innovation are the main keywords that have led to the growth of the company, occupying a leader position in the market. A strong service-minded approach offers daily customers satisfaction, fast and reliable.
         </p>
