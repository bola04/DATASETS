          <h2>
           Welcome to Colbeck &amp; Clarke Inc.
          </h2>
          <p>
           At Colbeck and Clarke, our mandate is to build durable, long-lasting relationships with our clients through hard work and an uncompromising commitment to excellence in customer service. It guides everything we do. It is our belief in this mandate that drives us to deliver a seamless transportation service, to communicate faster and to be more flexible. We understand that no company has the same logistics requirements and customize each shipment to the individual needs of our clients.
          </p>
          <p>
           We offer a full range of freight forwarding solutions for importers and exporters alike. With a global network of partners in over 155 countries you can rest assured that we can assist with your shipments in all major markets. All of our overseas offices share our same corporate beliefs and dedication to outstanding customer service. Our team of dedicated logistics professional will assist you in ensuring your goods are shipped as efficiently as possible while keeping you informed along the journey.
          </p>
          <p>
           We look forward to hearing from you and assisting with your global transportation needs.
