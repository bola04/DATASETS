         </p>

         </p>
         <p>
          To find our exact dates and times for the Adoption Fairs please visit our Petfinder Site at
          <a href="http://www.claringtonpetconnection.petfinder.com/">
           www.claringtonpetconnection.petfinder.com
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          To see a listing and pictures of our adoptable pets please visit our Petfinder Site at
          <a href="http://www.claringtonpetconnection.petfinder.com/">
           www.claringtonpetconnection.petfinder.com
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          To contact us please email
          <a href="mailto:claringtonpetconnection@sympatico.ca">
           claringtonpetconnection@sympatico.ca
          </a>
          or
          <a href="mailto:CanadianDogAdoption@sympatico.ca">
           CanadianDogAdoption@sympatico.ca
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          We do not return long distance calls in order to save costs.
         </p>
         <p>
          We look forward to hearing from you.
          <br/>
          <br/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          WHAT IS INVOLVED IN ADOPTING A DOG OR PUP ?
         </p>
         <p>
          There is an Application to fill out which helps us determine whether or not you fit out adoption criteria at this point in your lives.
         </p>
         <p>
          We are not looking for "
          <em>
           perfect families" or "perfect people"
          </em>
          , we are looking for dedication , an awareness of the work involved in raising a pup or dog, the realization that it is a LIFETIME committment , the desire to do the best you can for a new fourlegged family member, the ability to deal with problems should they arise and a heart's desire to lovingly care for a new family member.
         </p>
         <p>
          After the inital application is done and reviewed by one of our volunteers , then we can proceed with the Adoption Contract itself. This can all be done at the same time so that you and your new family member can leave together that very day you first meet.
         </p>
         <p>
          The Adoption contract is very universal, we require that you give humane treatment to your dog or pup throughout its life. We require that you spay or neuter the pup or dog you choose at the appropriate time
          <em>
           , within 60 days of adoption if it is an adult dog or older pup, or no later than six months of age if it is a baby pup.
          </em>
          We require a copy of the spay or neuter as proof of this procedure being done.
         </p>
         <p>
          We require that should you have to relinquish your pup or dog at any time, that you contact us first. We reserve the right to take back any of our adoptable pups or dogs should you need to re home them.
         </p>
         <p>
          While we strive to help every person and family find the right pup or dog for them , our first priority is to the welfare of the dog and or pup and to that end "
          <em>
           WE RESERVE THE RIGHT TO REFUSE ADOPTION"
          </em>
         </p>
