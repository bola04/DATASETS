          <span style="font-size: 12px;">
           WELCOME
          </span>
         </h4>
         <p class="bodytext">
          Rotary is an international service organization whose purpose is to bring together business and professional leaders in order to provide humanitarian services, encourage high ethical standards in all vocations and help build goodwill and peace in the world.
         </p>
         <p class="bodytext">
          Rotary's actions focus on:
         </p>
         <p class="bodytext">
          *peace and conflict prevention/resolution
         </p>
         <p class="bodytext">
          * disease prevention and treatment
         </p>
         <p class="bodytext">
          * water and sanitation
         </p>
         <p class="bodytext">
          * maternal and child health
         </p>
         <p class="bodytext">
          * basic education and literacy
         </p>
         <p class="bodytext">
          * economic and community development.
         </p>
         <p class="bodytext">
          Belgium and Luxembourg count 273 clubs and around 10.600 members.
         </p>
         <p class="bodytext" style="text-align:center">
