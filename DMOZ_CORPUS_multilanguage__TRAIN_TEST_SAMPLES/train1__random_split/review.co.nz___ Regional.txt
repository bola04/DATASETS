Furthermore we offer admissions consulting for top business schools, graduate programs and colleges. Here is how we are different from other test prep and admissions consulting companies:

             Focus on content
            </strong>

             Curricula designed for top scores
            </strong>
            <br/>

            <strong>
             Best instructors in the industry
            </strong>
            <br/>
            Manhattan Review is extremely selective when hiring its teaching staff. We not only look for individuals who have attained great test results, but also pay special attention that candidates have a passion for teaching and a track record inspiring others.
           </li>
