      <p>
       La Calabria restaurant specialises in rustic Italian cuisine. A family run business, combining authentic Italian cooking with fresh Welsh produce. Many of our dishes have been passed from old Italian cooking traditions, to bring you simple, but tasty dishes. From our homemade pasta, parma ham, to salami’s and a variety of vegetables cooked in many delicious ways.
      </p>
      <p>
       Our restaurant is set in the heart of Welsh countryside, once a derelict barn now converted into a traditional, stylist, country restaurant. You can enjoy a relaxing cappuccino in our café bar, or a large Ice-cream cup on our terraced area. La Calabria is ideal for all occasions, including corporate parties and even wedding receptions.
