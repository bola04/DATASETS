        <h1 class="title" id="page-title">
         Welcome to Energocredit Armenia
        </h1>
        <div class="tabs">
        </div>
        <div class="region region-content">
         <div class="block block-system" id="block-system-main">
          <div class="content">
           <div class="">
            <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
             <div class="field-items">
              <div class="field-item even">
               <p>
               </p>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size:16px;">
                 <strong>
                  <span style="color:#008000;">
                   Energocredit was a dedicated programme of the
                  </span>
                  <u>
                   <a href="http://www.ebrd.org/">
                    <span style="color:#008000;">
                     European Bank for Reconstruction and Development (EBRD)
                    </span>
                   </a>
                   <span style="color:#008000;">
                   </span>
                  </u>
                  <span style="color:#008000;">
                   aimed at supporting sustainable energy projects development in Armenia. Through Energocredit, the EBRD provided USD 125 million to local financial institutions in the Caucasus region between 2007 and 2017. On this website, you can read more about Energocredit’s operations and successes. If you are interested in EBRD’s current efficiency programmes, please visit
                  </span>
                  <a href="https://ebrdgeff.com/">
                   <span style="color:#008000;">
                    https://ebrdgeff.com/
                   </span>
                  </a>
                  <span style="color:#008000;">
                   .
                  </span>
                 </strong>
                </span>
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <p>
                Stay ahead of rising energy prices and the de­mands of an increasingly global economy. Take advantage of the newest technologies and reduce your operational costs. Energocredit supports you to invest into energy efficiency and renewable energy and offers multiple benefits: Get international expertise for free to make the right investments. Reduce your energy costs significantly. Finance our attractive loan from the savings you can achieve. Show your corporate responsibility and use green technology. With this, you will use less energy and bring more power into your business.
               </p>
               <ul>
                <li>
                 <span style="line-height: 1.538em;">
                  transform your energy saving potential into money
                 </span>
                </li>
                <li>
                 <span style="line-height: 1.538em;">
                  turn your
                  <a href="http://www.energocredit.am/en/renewable-energy">
                   renewable energy
                  </a>
                  project ideas into reality
                 </span>
                </li>
                <li>
                 <span style="line-height: 1.538em;">
                  receive
                 </span>
                 <a href="http://www.energocredit.am/en/free-technical-assistance">
                  Free Technical Assistance
                 </a>
                 from our
                 <a href="http://www.energocredit.am/en/contact">
                  Team of Experts
                 </a>
                 who will help you implement your
                 <a href="http://energocredit.am/sites/default/files/list_of_eligible_investments.pdf">
                  Eligible Investments
                 </a>
                </li>
                <li>
                 implement energy efficiency projects and receive 10% or 15% of the loan amount as a cash back
                 <a href="http://www.energocredit.am/en/incentive-payment">
                  Investment Incentive Payment
                 </a>
                </li>
