      <h1>
       NorGeoSpec
      </h1>
      <p>
       NorGeoSpec 2012 the system for certification and specification of geosynthetics and geosynthetic related products.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img align="left" alt="" height="160" src="/data/uploads/bilder/home/ngs-04.jpg" width="120"/>
       NorGeoSpec 2012 is a system where traffic authorities of the member states Finland, Sweden, Norway and Estonia, different test laboratories and SINTEF Certification as the NorGeoSpec certification body works together in a process for certification and specification of geosynthetics and geosynthetic related products.
      </p>
      <p>
       On the initiative and with financial support of the traffic authorities in Finland, Sweden, Norway and Estonia the NorGeoSpec2002 guideline dealing with the separation and filtration functions have been extended to include the reinforcement function.
      </p>
      <p>
       The guidelines are valid for products covered by the following European standards
       <br/>
       <br/>
       EN 13249 Roads and other trafficked areas
       <br/>
       EN 13250 Railways
       <br/>
       EN 13251 Earthworks, foundations and retaining structures
       <br/>
       EN 13252 Drainage systems
       <br/>
       EN 13253 External erosion control systems
       <br/>
       EN 13254 Reservoirs and dams
       <br/>
       EN 13255 Canals
       <br/>
       EN 13256 Tunnel and underground structures
       <br/>
       EN 13257 Solid waste disposals
       <br/>
       EN 13265 Liquid waste containment
       <br/>
       EN 15381 Pavements and asphalt overlays
       <br/>
      </p>
