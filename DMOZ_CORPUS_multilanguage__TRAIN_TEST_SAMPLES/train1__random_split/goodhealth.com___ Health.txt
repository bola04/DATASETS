       <h2>

       </h2>
       <p>
        <a href="https://www.seton.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/googlestorefeature.png">
         <img alt="googlestorefeature" class="alignleft wp-image-18260" height="142" sizes="(max-width: 290px) 100vw, 290px" src="https://www.seton.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/googlestorefeature-640x313.png" srcset="https://www.seton.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/googlestorefeature-640x313.png 640w, https://www.seton.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/googlestorefeature-768x375.png 768w, https://www.seton.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/googlestorefeature-800x391.png 800w, https://www.seton.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/googlestorefeature-300x146.png 300w, https://www.seton.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/googlestorefeature.png 1024w" style="padding: 5px" width="290"/>

       <p class="p1">
        We are making it easier for you to see a provider.  Using the GoodHealth Digital Clinic, you’ll have access to high-quality care by our experienced providers from the convenience of your desktop or mobile device.  For more information, please contact the GoodHealth Team at 512-324-3130.
       </p>
       <h2>
        Why would I want to see a doctor online?
       </h2>
       <p>
        You might choose to access and confirm your eligibility for the digital clinic if:
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         You’re too busy to make it to an in-person appointment
        </li>
        <li>
         You planned on going to an urgent care clinic otherwise
        </li>
        <li>
         You are new to the area and don’t have a primary care doctor yet
        </li>
        <li>
         Your regular primary care doctor isn’t available, but you don’t need to go to the ER
        </li>
       </ul>
       <h2>
        What Illnesses and Injuries Are Commonly Seen through a Digital Clinic?
