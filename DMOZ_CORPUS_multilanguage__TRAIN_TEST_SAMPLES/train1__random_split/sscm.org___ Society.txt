           <h1 class="av-special-heading-tag " itemprop="headline">
            About Us
           </h1>
           <div class="special-heading-border">
            <div class="special-heading-inner-border">
            </div>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="flex_column av_one_full av-animated-generic bottom-to-top flex_column_div av-zero-column-padding first avia-builder-el-3 el_after_av_heading avia-builder-el-last " style="border-radius:0px; ">
           <section class="av_textblock_section " itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="https://schema.org/CreativeWork">
            <div class="avia_textblock " itemprop="text">
             <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_1543" style="width: 310px">
              <img alt="" class="size-medium wp-image-1543" height="190" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="https://sscm.org/dev/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/About-Us-300x190.jpg" srcset="https://sscm.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/About-Us-300x190.jpg 300w, https://sscm.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/About-Us-768x486.jpg 768w, https://sscm.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/About-Us-1030x651.jpg 1030w, https://sscm.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/About-Us-1500x948.jpg 1500w, https://sscm.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/About-Us-705x446.jpg 705w, https://sscm.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/About-Us-450x285.jpg 450w, https://sscm.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/About-Us.jpg 1523w" width="300"/>
              <p class="wp-caption-text">
               The Sisters of Saints Cyril and Methodius
              </p>
             </div>
             <p>
              We, the Sisters of Saints Cyril and Methodius, welcome you! Christ is the heart of our spiritual life together. We believe that the Kingdom is in our midst, nourished by the Eucharist and by the Word of God.
             </p>
             <p>
              Sharing in the Paschal Mystery, we are a presence of Christ in the world through our vowed communal life, joy in the service of the kingdom and spirit of prayerfulness and simplicity.
             </p>
