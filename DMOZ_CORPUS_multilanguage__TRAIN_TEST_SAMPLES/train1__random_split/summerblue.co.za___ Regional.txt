        <div class="col-md-9">
         <p>
          Nelson Mandela Bay, which includes Port Elizabeth, Uitenhage and Despatch, is a successful and growing Municipality, providing affordable accommodation to its tourists, leisure and corporate travellers through its many popular guest house, bed &amp; breakfast and other self-catering establishments.
         </p>
         <p>
          Port Elizabeth with its all-year round warm climate and many local attractions and landmarks, remains an excellent value-for-money holiday centre with quality accommodation. Port Elizabeth is also the host of various national and international sporting and social events including the annual Ironman triathlon. Economic growth ensures increased corporate activity.
         </p>
         <p>
          Summer Blue Guest House is situated in the quiet, up-market beachfront suburb of Summerstrand, Port Elizabeth along with other guest houses, bed &amp; breakfasts and self-catering establishments. We are close to all beachfront amenities offering quality, spacious and private accommodation to leisure and corporate travellers.
         </p>
         <p>
          All rooms have private entrances with secure off-street parking. Summer Blue Guest House is 5 kilometers from the airport and airport transfers can be arranged.
         </p>
         <p>
          Full breakfasts are served in the dining-room. Dinners are by arrangement. All our rooms are semi self-catering.
         </p>
         <p>
          Summer Blue Guest House is conveniently situated close to Hobie, Kings and Pollock Beaches with its various restaurants, McArthur Baths, Boardwalk Casino Entertainment Centre with its restaurants and shops, Bay World Oceanarium, Nelson Mandela Metropolitan University, Nelson Mandela Stadium and Humewood Golf Club.
         </p>
