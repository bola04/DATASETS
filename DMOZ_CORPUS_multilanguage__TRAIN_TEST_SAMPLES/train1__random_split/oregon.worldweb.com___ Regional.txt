       <h2>
        Welcome to Oregon!
       </h2>
       <p>
        Oregon is full of unique and exciting places to visit, ranging from natural volcanic 
 sights to beaches along the Pacific Ocean to a scattering of vineyards.
        <a href="http://www.portland.worldweb.com/">
         Portland
        </a>
        , 
 the state's most populous city, may be one of America's best kept secrets, offering 
 a range of attractions, inlcuding museums, parks and professional sports teams. 
 Near to Portland and bordering
        <a href="http://www.washingtonstate.worldweb.com/">
         Washington
        </a>
        , 
 visitors will find the
        <a href="http://www.columbiagorge-mthood.worldweb.com/">
         Columbia 
 Gorge-Mount Hood
        </a>
        region, which boasts the second largest waterfall in the
        <a href="http://www.usa.worldweb.com/">
         United States
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://www.columbiagorge-mthood.worldweb.com/SightsAttractions/Waterfalls/index.html#L110461">
         Multnomah 
 Falls
        </a>
        . Explore Oregon's 400 mi (640 km) of Pacific coastline or tour the
        <a href="http://www.willamettevalley.worldweb.com/">
         Willamette 
 Valley
        </a>
        , home to
        <a href="http://www.eugene.worldweb.com/">
         Eugene
        </a>
        and the 
 state's capital city,
        <a href="http://www.salem.worldweb.com/">
         Salem
        </a>
        . The
        <a href="http://www.oregoncascades.worldweb.com/">
         Central Cascades
        </a>
        and its 
 largest center,
        <a href="http://www.bend-or.worldweb.com/">
         Bend
        </a>
        , is a perfect 
 base to visit the parks, mountains and rivers of this region. As natural wonders 
 go,
        <a href="http://www.craterlake-klamath.worldweb.com/">
         Crater Lake-Klamath
        </a>
        and
        <a href="http://www.easternoregon.worldweb.com/">
         Eastern Oregon
        </a>
        present 
 a multitude of volcanic attractions, including spectacular
        <a href="http://www.craterlake-klamath.worldweb.com/SightsAttractions/ParksGardens/index.html#L105764">
         Crater 
 Lake National Park
        </a>
        and the
        <a href="http://www.oregoncascades.worldweb.com/LaPineOR/SightsAttractions/ParksGardens/index.html#L116341">
         Newberry 
 National Volcanic Monument
        </a>
        , respectively.
       </p>
