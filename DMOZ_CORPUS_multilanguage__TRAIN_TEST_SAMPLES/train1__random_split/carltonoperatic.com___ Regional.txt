    <h2>
     About us
    </h2>
    <p>
     Carlton Operatic was
     <a href="history.php">
      founded in 1921
     </a>
     and is based in
     <a href="howtofindus.php">
      Netherfield, Nottingham
     </a>
     . We are affiliated to
     <a href="http://www.noda.org.uk">
      NODA
     </a>
     , and we perform at the
     <a href="http://www.trch.co.uk">
      Nottingham Theatre Royal
     </a>
     . Society members also perform in concert during the closed season.
    </p>

         Grease

       </a>

       <a href="2015.php">
        <i>
         Legally Blonde

        <i>
         Sister Act
        </i>
       </a>

      </p>
