            <h2 class="art-postheader">
             <a href="http://www.old-dalby.org.uk/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to Old Dalby">
              Old Dalby
             </a>
            </h2>
            <div class="art-postcontent">
             <!-- article-content -->
             <p>
              <span style="font-family: tahoma,arial,helvetica,sans-serif;">
               Old Dalby is part of the parish of
               <a href="http://broughtonandolddalby.leicestershireparishcouncils.org/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://broughtonandolddalby.leicestershireparishcouncils.org/', 'Old Dalby &amp; Broughton');" target="_blank">
                Old Dalby &amp; Broughton
               </a>
               and lies to the west of Melton Mowbray, Leicestershire close to the Nottinghamshire border.  It is quite a small village with around 1,430 people, including the settlement of Queensway, living in or around the village.
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="font-family: tahoma,arial,helvetica,sans-serif;">
               Old Dalby has a very active community with many
               <a href="http://www.old-dalby.org.uk/?page_id=57">
                groups
               </a>
               such as netball, cricket, choir, church, toddlers, scouts and wine club. The WI was disbanded in 2018. The village also boasts the Old Dalby Singers who rehearse most Tuesdays throughout the year in the Village Hall.
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="font-family: tahoma,arial,helvetica,sans-serif;">
               Our children are educated at the
               <a href="http://www.old-dalby.org.uk/?page_id=64">
                preschool
               </a>
               and
               <a href="http://www.olddalbyschool.org.uk/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.olddalbyschool.org.uk/', 'primary school');" target="_blank">
                primary school
               </a>
               .
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="font-family: tahoma,arial,helvetica,sans-serif;">
               We even have our very own
               <a href="http://www.belvoirbrewery.co.uk/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.belvoirbrewery.co.uk/', 'Belvoir Brewery');" target="_blank">
                Belvoir Brewery
               </a>
               and
               <a href="http://thecrownolddalby.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://thecrownolddalby.com/', 'The Crown Inn');">
                The Crown Inn
               </a>
               .
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="font-family: tahoma,arial,helvetica,sans-serif;">
               Each year the community gets together to put on
               <a href="http://olddalbyday.old-dalby.org.uk/">
                OLD DALBY DAY
               </a>
               , a traditional village fete on the August Bank Holiday Monday. This event has brought thousands of visitors to the village for over three decades who enjoy a varied and exiting array of entertainment and help raise funding for many of the groups mentioned above. In 2018 the car parking for the event was changed making it quite important to consult the website and/or FaceBook page for Old Dalby Day.
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="font-family: tahoma,arial,helvetica,sans-serif;">
               Within this website you will be able to find information about living in Old Dalby.  Enjoy browsing.
              </span>
             </p>
