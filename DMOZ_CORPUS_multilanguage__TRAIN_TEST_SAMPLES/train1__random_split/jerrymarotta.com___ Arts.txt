         <header class="entry-header">
          <h1 class="entry-title">
           Musician/Producer/Composer
          </h1>
         </header>
         <figure class="entry-media entry-image">
          <img alt="" height="1201" sizes="(max-width: 2000px) 100vw, 2000px" src="https://www.jerrymarotta.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/cropped-JerryMarotta-5.jpg" srcset="https://www.jerrymarotta.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/cropped-JerryMarotta-5.jpg 2000w, https://www.jerrymarotta.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/cropped-JerryMarotta-5-300x180.jpg 300w, https://www.jerrymarotta.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/cropped-JerryMarotta-5-768x461.jpg 768w, https://www.jerrymarotta.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/cropped-JerryMarotta-5-1024x615.jpg 1024w" width="2000"/>
         </figure>
         <div class="entry-content">
          <p>
           In addition to drumming,
           <strong>
            Jerry Marotta
           </strong>
           ‘s talents include producing, composing, vocals and various instruments including bass guitar and sax. Jerry divides his time performing and recording with
           <strong>
            <a href="https://www.jerrymarotta.com/marotta-brothers-band/">
             The Marotta Brothers Band
            </a>
           </strong>
           ,
           <a href="https://www.jerrymarotta.com/security-project/">
            <strong>
             Security Project
            </strong>
           </a>
           ,
           <strong>
            <a href="https://www.jerrymarotta.com/the-fragile-fate/">
             The Fragile Fate
            </a>
           </strong>
           ,
           <strong>
            Annie &amp; the Hedonists
           </strong>
           ,
           <strong>
            Reelin’ in the Years
           </strong>
           , and
           <strong>
            <a href="https://www.jerrymarotta.com/soulredemption/">
             Flav Martin
            </a>
           </strong>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           When Jerry’s not on the road you can generally find him behind the drum kit or mixing board at
           <strong>
            Jersville Studio
           </strong>
           in Woodstock, NY or
           <strong>
            Dreamland Recording Studio
           </strong>
           in West Hurley, NY. Future projects include a trio with
           <strong>
            Phil Keaggy
           </strong>
           and
           <strong>
            Tony Levin
           </strong>
           plus producing the upcoming
           <strong>
            Sarah Perrotta
           </strong>
           album.
          </p>
         </div>
