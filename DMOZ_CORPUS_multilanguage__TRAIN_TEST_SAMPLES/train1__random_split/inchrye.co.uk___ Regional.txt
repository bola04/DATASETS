          <h2>
           <strong>
            Welcome To Inchrye Bed and Breakfast
           </strong>
          </h2>
          <p>
           Inchrye Bed &amp; Breakfast is in the setting of a Victorian house built in 1901. We are situated on the shore of the Inverness Firth looking across to the Bonnie Black Isle and Channery Point and are situated at the end of the Village of Ardersier.
          </p>
          <blockquote>
           <p>
            We are a 5 minute drive from Inverness Airport
            <br/>
            which is 2 miles from Inchrye.
           </p>
          </blockquote>
          <p>
           Some of the Local Attractions to name but a few are Fort George, Clava Cairns, Culloden Battlefield and we are also very honoured to be blessed with the presence of a Pod of over Two Hundred Dolphins of which we often see in groups just along from the bottom of our garden. The Fort is also a great viewing point for the Dolphins.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           We at Inchrye feel very privileged to live where we do. There are many many different birds in the area and also we are a 2 minute walk from the common ground which is home to dozens of different varieties of butterflies, rabbits and the occasional roe deer. This is a very pretty walk, which we ourselves take on a daily basis...
           <br/>
           <br/>
           All our Double and Twin Rooms are En-Suite and can be booked for Single Occupancy. The Single Room which is a smaller room and is ideal for a stopover or easy access for the airport has a private bathroom.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Room Rates Range from £32 - £85. Booking can be taken online by using the
           <a href="http://uk-bookings.eviivo.com/eviivo.distribution.presentation/MyWebsite/InchryeIV27QD.home" target="_blank" title="Book Online">
            Book Online Button
           </a>
           , where you can pay using your credit or debit card. Please read your confirmation email down to the bottom as some agents take your card details for reference only which means that you will need to pay in CASH on arrival. This mainly happens when using Laterooms or you can Phone or Email us direct for a room booking.
          </p>
