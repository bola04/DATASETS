               <span class="field-content">
                <a href="/recipes-pyrethrum">
                </a>
                <a class="imagecache imagecache-uc_category imagecache-linked imagecache-uc_category_linked" href="/recipes-pyrethrum">
                 <img alt="" class="imagecache imagecache-uc_category" height="100" src="http://www.ginnysherbs.com/sites/default/files/imagecache/uc_category/original pyrethrum_0.JPG" title="" width="75"/>

                 <p>
                  Spray recipe - coarsely grind dried flower heads. To every firmly packed 1/2 cup of flowers add 1L boiling water.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  Leave to stand until cold, strain, add 1 tsp pure soap.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  Shake well before use. Do not use in extreme heat. It will kill bees, so it is best used in early morning or late evening.
