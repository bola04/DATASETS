          <p>
           <font face="Times New Roman" size="+1">
            Here in my workshop
         in Duluth, Minnesota, USA, I produce professional quality electric
         harps in many styles and sizes- solid body or acoustic/electric,
         lever harps, pedal harps, and chromatic harps. I have developed
         my pickups (piezo transducers) over a period of years specifically
         to amplify harps. They are manufactured here in my shop, so quality
         and consistency is guaranteed. They give any harp a richer, warmer
         tone, a true harp sound.
           </font>
          </p>
          <p>
           <font face="Times New Roman" size="+1">
