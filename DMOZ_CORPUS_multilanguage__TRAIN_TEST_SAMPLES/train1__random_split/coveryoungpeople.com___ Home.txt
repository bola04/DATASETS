             <p>
              Welcome to Cover Young People, a resource dedicated to making health insurance for young adults less complicated to understand.
             </p>
             <p>
              With the Affordable Care Act requirement that all Americans purchase health insurance beginning in 2014, there are many people shopping for medical insurance for the very first time.  Consider the following questions:
             </p>
             <ul>
              <li>
               <span style="font-size: 14px;">
                What is a deductible and how do I calculate my
                <a href="http://coveryoungpeople.com/insurance-basics/how-to-determine-my-health-insurance-out-of-pocket-expenses/" title="How to Determine My Health Insurance Out of Pocket Expenses">
                 out of pocket expenses
                </a>
                ?
               </span>
              </li>
              <li>
               <span style="font-size: 14px;">
                What is a
                <a href="http://coveryoungpeople.com/insurance-basics/what-is-a-preferred-provider-organization-or-ppo/" title="What is a Preferred-Provider Organization or PPO?">
                 PPO
                </a>
                ?
               </span>
              </li>
              <li>
               <span style="font-size: 14px;">
                What is an
                <a href="http://coveryoungpeople.com/insurance-basics/hsa-health-insurance-mean/">
                 HSA
                </a>
                ?
               </span>
              </li>
              <li>
               <span style="font-size: 14px;">
                Should I join a
                <a href="http://coveryoungpeople.com/affordable-care-act/is-a-health-insurance-exchange-something-a-young-person-should-enroll-in/" title="Is a Health Insurance Exchange Something a Young Person Should Enroll In?">
                 health insurance exchange
                </a>
                ?
               </span>
              </li>
              <li>
               <span style="font-size: 14px;">
                What do you do to get health insurance
                <a href="http://coveryoungpeople.com/finding-good-insurance/top-5-ways-to-get-health-insurance-between-jobs/">
                 between jobs
                </a>
                ?
               </span>
              </li>
              <li>
               <span style="font-size: 14px;">
                How long can I stay on my parent’s health insurance plan? (Until your 26th birthday)
                <br/>
               </span>
              </li>
             </ul>
             <p>
              The world of insurance is ever-evolving, but health insurance for young adults has never been easier to attain.  Take a look around; the more educated you are, the more likely you are to have great coverage and financial protection in the case of a medical emergency.
             </p>
             <p>
              Welcome to Cover Young People, where we are helping people enter the world of health insurance.
