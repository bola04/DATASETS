               <h2>
                <a href="/club_services">
                </a>
               </h2>
               <span class="rdf-meta element-hidden" content="" property="dc:title">
               </span>
               <div class="content">
                <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
                 <div class="field-items">
                  <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
                   <p>
                    Membership brings with it many valuable services. The volunteers and staff listed below are here to offer help with your membership, maintenance, and dispute resolution.
                   </p>
                   <ul>
                    <li>
                     <a href="/boardofdirectors">
                      National Board of Directors
                     </a>
                     : Comprised of the Executive Board and the Regional Vice Presidents, these elected leaders determine and enforce club policy.
                    </li>
                    <li>
                     <a href="/staff">
                      National Office Staff
                     </a>
                     : The National Office handles questions about membership, address changes, chapter transfers, and general club policy for individual members. The office is open Monday through Friday between 8:30 AM and 5:00 PM.
                    </li>
                    <li>
                     <a href="/tsa">
                      Technical Service Advisors (TSA)
                     </a>
                     : These experienced technicians can help you with maintenance or repair problems.
                    </li>
                    <li>
                     <a href="/ombudsmen">
                      Ombudsmen
                     </a>
                     : If youve made every effort to resolve a continuing dispute with manufacturers, dealers, repair shops, parts suppliers, or others, our Ombudsmen can serve as an intermediary for you.
                    </li>
                   </ul>
                  </div>
