           </em>
          </span>
         </h2>
         <p align="left" style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="color: #000080; font-size: medium;">
           The Upper Dublin Republican Committee is proud of the contributions and service we provide to our neighbors and our community. We believe that by sharing common goals we can in improve the lives of the people of Upper Dublin.
          </span>
         </p>
         <div align="left">
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="color: #000080; font-size: medium;">
            We are a diverse group of township residents with a wide range of skills and expertise.  We are committed to assuring quality government with conservative values at the local, county and state levels, and better the lives of those we serve.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="color: #000080; font-size: medium;">

             ontact us today!
            </span>
