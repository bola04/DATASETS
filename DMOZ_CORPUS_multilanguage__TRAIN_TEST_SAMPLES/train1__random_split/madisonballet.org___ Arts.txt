           <div class="col sqs-col-4 span-4">
            <div class="sqs-block html-block sqs-block-html" data-block-type="2" id="block-46693cccc8f492a5c42b">
             <div class="sqs-block-content">
              <h1>
               Children's Classes
              </h1>
              <h3>
               Up to age 6
              </h3>
              <p>
               The Children's Division provides students up to age 6 with a solid foundation in ballet fundamentals while also inspiring a life-long love for dance.
              </p>
              <p>
               These classes are a prelude to formal ballet instruction and introduce children to music and movement through a structured progression that stresses creativity and group interaction.
              </p>
             </div>
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="col sqs-col-4 span-4">
            <div class="sqs-block html-block sqs-block-html" data-block-type="2" id="block-a3a10f8715b670ea2d57">
             <div class="sqs-block-content">
              <h1>
               Youth Classes
              </h1>
              <h3>
               Ages 7 &amp; above
              </h3>
              <p>
               The School Division offers five levels of well-coordinated, progressive curriculum emphasizing a strong classical technique and a flow of movement that expresses a sense of energy, freedom, and joy.
              </p>
              <p>
               Students follow a structured sequence of training stages designed to increase their technical skills, stamina, and discipline in accordance with their age and physical development.
              </p>
             </div>
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="col sqs-col-4 span-4">
            <div class="sqs-block html-block sqs-block-html" data-block-type="2" id="block-2712a2ed138da531136c">
             <div class="sqs-block-content">
              <h1>
               Adult Classes
              </h1>
              <h3>
               Attendance is on a drop-in basis to accommodate busy schedules.
              </h3>
              <p>
               Excellence and flexibility are the hallmarks of Open Division classes offered by the School of Madison Ballet.
              </p>
              <p>
               Led by SMB's experienced, professional faculty, classes are scheduled to accommodate demanding work, school, or family commitments.
              </p>
              <p>
               The curriculum provides each student with an enriching education in a stimulating and creative atmosphere.
              </p>
             </div>
