            <p>
             EDGE is an award-winning architecture firm based in Taos, New Mexico and committed to innovation, sustainability and environmentally appropriate design and construction. Our designs are very site- and client-specific, typically with a passive solar orientation and often incorporating alternative materials and independent systems such as photovoltaic electric, rain catchment and grey water re-use. We make every effort to maximize the building’s energy efficiency while creating elegant, unique and stylistically varied structures.
