         <p>
          <span style="font-size:22px">
           <strong>
            The Dereham Carnival - 22nd July
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          Dereham Runners will be hosting a 1 Mile Fun-Run at the start of the Dereham Carnival .
         </p>
         <p>
          100 Places are open to the public for a £1 entry fee via their Website below with proceeds going to the Carnival.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="https://www.derehamcarnival.co.uk">
           https://www.derehamcarnival.co.uk
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          BE QUICK IF YOU WANT TO ENTER.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Important Notes -
          </strong>
          For this year.
         </p>
         <p>
          Runners will NOT be timed or be issued with a Race Number.
         </p>
         <p>
          A Gantry and Timing Clock will be at the finish, so do look at the end if you are interested in your 1 Mile time!
         </p>
         <p>
          There are NO Race Mementos or Trophies, only a Best Fancy Dress Runner.
         </p>
         <p>
          The Run Route will be on the road, no pavement running.
         </p>
         <p>
          No Music devices will be permitted.
         </p>
         <p>
          Age on the day 12+.
         </p>
         <p>
          No Dogs or Prams allowed.
         </p>
