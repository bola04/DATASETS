         <h2>
          Miniature Enthusiasts of Montreal (MEM)
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          We are a group of people who share a common love of 1:12 scale miniatures. We meet monthly on the fourth Thursday and have a monthly workshop. These monthly workshops provide all the material and instructions needed to create a miniature object each month. Each year we have an exhibition and vendor show in Montreal. We will post show information here. We will put up more amazing pictures of the dollhouses and room boxes created by our members and friends.
         </p>
