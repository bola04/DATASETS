         <br/>
         <strong class="FontMed">
          21/6/2018
          <br/>
          Registration Window Opening
         </strong>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         In advance of the Girls League AGM re Registration Support for Clubs and information about the Registration Window opening:
         <br/>
         <br/>
         There will be some slight tweaks to the process next season, with new GDPR also a consideration in this. I have now opened the Registration Process on the Whole Game System season 2018/19, so as soon as your Club Affiliation has been approved by County FA you can then start entering Players. I have not changed the parameters for ID requirements and you will not be required to update new pictures for players who played last season, however I will look to ask for picture updates every two years to keep them current, so a change will be required for the 2019/20 season. I would urge Clubs not to leave it too long and get the Registration process completed as early as possible, remember you MUST have the minimum number of players registered 7 days in advance of the season to ensure your team is eligible and you do not incur a fine.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Last season we were very lenient on fining for breaches of rules. However there will be Club fines issued from 2018/19 season if similar offences occur, so my advice is, do the basics now, do not leave it until last minute!
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Thank you
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Ant
         <br/>
         Registration Secretary
