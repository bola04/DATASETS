        <h3>
         EtherCAT
        </h3>
        is the open real-time Ethernet network originally developed by Beckhoff. EtherCAT sets new standards for
        <a href="/en/performance.html">
         real-time performance
        </a>
        and topology flexibility.
        <br/>
        <a href="/en/why_use_ethercat.htm">
         Why use EtherCAT?
        </a>
       </div>

        <h3>
         EtherCAT Technology Group
        </h3>
        The ETG is a global organization in which OEM, End Users and Technology Providers join forces to support and promote the further technology development.
        <br/>
