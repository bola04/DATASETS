    <h2>
     <span class="style10">
      <em>
       Most of my items are Vintage ~ meaning 
     previously owned. Items are from my personal collection which have either 
     been passed down through the family or items which I've purchased along the 
     way from estates. Provenance will be described where possible. My collection 
     is an array of jewelry by Canadian designers Sherman, Robert Larin, Continental, 
     Butler /5th Avenue Collection and Sarah Coventry, not to mention International 
     designers Coro, Kramer, Art, Chanel, Ciner, Hollycraft, Joseff, Kenneth Lane, 
     Napier, Monet, Trifari, Volupte, Weiss, and Whiting &amp; Davis...to mention 
     a few. You will find rhinestones, bakelite, confetti caboshons, crystal beads, 
     silver, gold, diamonds and semi-precious stones and "fun" stones. 
     Hats from many decades and for every occasion and hatpins from days-gone-by. 
     My assortment of accessories include scarves, gloves, silk lingerie, handkerchiefs, 
     linens, textiles and shoes. Purses &amp; evening bags by Whiting and Davis 
     plus numerous other designers. I have little cocktail purses with masses of 
     beads, rhinestones, sequins and bangles to add to your chic wardrobe. Miscellaneous 
     treasures range from perfume &amp; fragrance bottles (Limoges and Hills Devon 
     Gardens) to crystal bells and Limoges Trinket boxes and James Kent pin dishes. 
     I have a number of Shalimar Perfume Bottles too. If you are looking for Collectibles 
     and Nostalgic Memorabelia, I invite you to make yourself comfortable and browse.
