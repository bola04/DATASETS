         <h2 class="entry-title">
          Home
         </h2>
         <div class="entry-content">
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           Welcome to the First United Methodist Church of Stillwater, Minnesota, at the corner of Greeley and Myrtle. We are a church that is growing in our love of God and in our love of neighbors near and far, and we invite you to come grow with us.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <span style="font-size: 16px;">
            You shall Love the Lord your God
           </span>
           <br/>
           <span style="font-size: 16px;">
            with all your heart, and with all your soul,
           </span>
           <br/>
           <span style="font-size: 16px;">
            and with all your strength,
           </span>
           <br/>
           <span style="font-size: 16px;">
            and with all your mind;
           </span>
           <br/>
           <span style="font-size: 16px;">
            and your Neighbor as Yourself.
           </span>
           <br/>
           ~ Luke 10:27
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <img alt="love God 2" class="alignnone wp-image-259" data-attachment-id="259" data-comments-opened="0" data-image-description="" data-image-meta='{"aperture":"0","credit":"","camera":"","caption":"","created_timestamp":"0","copyright":"","focal_length":"0","iso":"0","shutter_speed":"0","title":"","orientation":"0"}' data-image-title="love God 2" data-large-file="https://i0.wp.com/fumcstillwater.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/love-God-2.jpg?fit=320%2C296" data-medium-file="https://i0.wp.com/fumcstillwater.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/love-God-2.jpg?fit=300%2C278" data-orig-file="https://i0.wp.com/fumcstillwater.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/love-God-2.jpg?fit=320%2C296" data-orig-size="320,296" data-permalink="http://fumcstillwater.com/grow/love-god-2/" data-recalc-dims="1" height="460" sizes="(max-width: 497px) 100vw, 497px" src="https://i0.wp.com/fumcstillwater.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/love-God-2.jpg?resize=497%2C460" srcset="https://i0.wp.com/fumcstillwater.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/love-God-2.jpg?w=320 320w, https://i0.wp.com/fumcstillwater.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/love-God-2.jpg?resize=300%2C278 300w, https://i0.wp.com/fumcstillwater.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/love-God-2.jpg?resize=162%2C150 162w, https://i0.wp.com/fumcstillwater.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/love-God-2.jpg?resize=150%2C139 150w" width="497"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            MUSIC DIRECTOR Needed
           </strong>
           : Our very own church is in search of a creative, versatile music director to lead the Adult Choir. This is a school year, September- May, part time, paid position. Aprox. 10-15 hours a week. Collaborative, supportive work environment. The choir rehearses Wednesday evenings and sings weekly for Sunday morning worship during the school year, Christmas Eve, Easter,and occasional additional services. For more information, contact FUMC office at firstumc@msn.com  or 651-439-1880.
          </p>
