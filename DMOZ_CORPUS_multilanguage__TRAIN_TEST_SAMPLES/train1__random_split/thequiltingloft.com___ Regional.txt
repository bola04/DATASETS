        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          Handbags are more than a necessity to most women. They are used to make a fashion statement, and this explains why most women cannot leave the house without carrying a handbag with them. This does not mean they pick up any other bag; they need to have something that accentuates their fashion and beauty.
         </p>
         <p>
          Handbags come in various shapes, sizes, and colors. This means that you do not need to stick to one bag. In fact, but you should have several bags for different outfits and occasions. Choosing the best handbag is not easy. Thus, here are a variety of things that should be taken care of when making this choice.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Consider Your Physique
         </h2>
         <p>
          <img alt="luxury red handbag" class="alignleft wp-image-128" data-attachment-id="128" data-comments-opened="0" data-image-description="" data-image-meta='{"aperture":"0","credit":"","camera":"","caption":"","created_timestamp":"0","copyright":"","focal_length":"0","iso":"0","shutter_speed":"0","title":"","orientation":"0"}' data-image-title="handbag" data-large-file="https://i1.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/handbag.jpg?fit=293%2C172" data-medium-file="https://i1.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/handbag.jpg?fit=293%2C172" data-orig-file="https://i1.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/handbag.jpg?fit=293%2C172" data-orig-size="293,172" data-permalink="http://www.thequiltingloft.com/choose-right-handbag/handbag/" data-recalc-dims="1" src="https://i1.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/handbag.jpg?resize=184%2C108"/>

If you are somehow plump, see to it that you get something that rets just above your waistline. On the other hand, if you are not so big, do not make a mistake of choosing a bag that is unevenly big. As such, make a point of fitting a handbag before buying and ensure they sit on well on your body structure.
         </p>
         <h2>
          The Color of the Handbag
         </h2>
         <p>
          This is another essential factor to consider when choosing a handbag. First, you need to understand what is in your cabinet and the kind of occasion you are expected to attend. With this fact in mind, choose a handbag that complements your dresses. If you do not have an idea of what works with the outfits in your wardrobe, go for neutral colors that usually go well with any dress.
         </p>
         <h2>
          What You Want to Stuff Inside
         </h2>
         <p>
          Another important criterion to use when choosing a handbag is what you carry on your handbag. If you need to go to school after work, you need a handbag that is big enough to carry your books and some files. If you need something to store your smartphone, buy a purse. Ideally, the amount of things and the place you often visit are of great importance when shopping for a handbag.
         </p>
         <h3>
          Quality
         </h3>
         <p>
          <img alt="lady with a handbag" class="alignright wp-image-130" data-attachment-id="130" data-comments-opened="0" data-image-description="" data-image-meta='{"aperture":"0","credit":"","camera":"","caption":"","created_timestamp":"0","copyright":"","focal_length":"0","iso":"0","shutter_speed":"0","title":"","orientation":"0"}' data-image-title="woman" data-large-file="https://i1.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/woman.jpg?fit=183%2C275" data-medium-file="https://i1.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/woman.jpg?fit=183%2C275" data-orig-file="https://i1.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/woman.jpg?fit=183%2C275" data-orig-size="183,275" data-permalink="http://www.thequiltingloft.com/choose-right-handbag/woman/" data-recalc-dims="1" src="https://i1.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/woman.jpg?resize=123%2C185"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Irrespective, any woman would want a quality handbag. A quality handbag kills two birds with one stone – it lasts long and makes you look fashionable. If you are looking for a handbag for everyday use, the most important thing to look for is the quality. Quality handbags might be costly at first, but they will remain loyal for the next few years.
         </p>
         <p>
          Hence, if you intend to buy a handbag anytime soon, make a point of observing the tips highlighted above. Also, purpose to get the best from
          <a href="https://www.aaahandbags.cn/replica-designer-handbags/">
           shop by designers handbag catalog
          </a>
          . This way, you will do justice to your money.…

          Effect of drunk driving accident
         </h2>

         <h2 class="entry-title">
          <a href="http://www.thequiltingloft.com/important-steps-follow-buying-used-car/" rel="bookmark">
           Important Steps to Follow When Buying a Used Car
          </a>
         </h2>
         <span class="title-divider">
         </span>
        </header>
        <!-- .entry-header -->
        <div class="entry-thumb">
         <a href="http://www.thequiltingloft.com/important-steps-follow-buying-used-car/">

        </div>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          Are you looking to buy a used car? Well, then you are not alone because there are many like you who are also looking to buy used cars. Statistics show that about 40 million cars exchange hands every year. With the high number of choices available, getting the best car can sometimes be a challenge. To help make buying a used car a breeze for you, here are the steps that you can take.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Defining Your Budget
         </h2>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class=" wp-image-70 alignleft" data-attachment-id="70" data-comments-opened="0" data-image-description="" data-image-meta='{"aperture":"0","credit":"","camera":"","caption":"","created_timestamp":"0","copyright":"","focal_length":"0","iso":"0","shutter_speed":"0","title":"","orientation":"0"}' data-image-title="werewert" data-large-file="https://i2.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/werewert.jpeg?fit=525%2C350" data-medium-file="https://i2.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/werewert.jpeg?fit=300%2C200" data-orig-file="https://i2.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/werewert.jpeg?fit=525%2C350" data-orig-size="525,350" data-permalink="http://www.thequiltingloft.com/important-steps-follow-buying-used-car/werewert/" data-recalc-dims="1" sizes="(max-width: 168px) 100vw, 168px" src="https://i2.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/werewert.jpeg?resize=168%2C112" srcset="https://i2.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/werewert.jpeg?resize=300%2C200 300w, https://i2.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/werewert.jpeg?w=525 525w"/>
          This is a rule of thumb when looking for a used car to buy. You need to determine how much you are willing to spend on a car. How you are going to pay for the car is also important. If you have a tight budget, then it is important that you spend less. You will also need to set aside some funds that you can use to repair and service your car once you buy it.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Have a List
         </h2>
         <p>
          The next step is to have a list of used car brands that are known to make good used cars. If you are also looking to save some money, then it is important that you consider a lot of brands. By having a list, chances are high that you are going to get one that fits your budget and one that is good for you.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Vehicle History Report
         </h2>
         <p>
          Before you pay for a used car, it is important that you look at its history report. Unless you are buying it from someone you know, like a friend or a family member, a vehicle history report is very important. If the car you are interested in has a history report that you don’t like, then you should not buy it. Looking at history reports for any vehicle will reveal to you information about the car that is very vital.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Contact the Seller
         </h2>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="wp-image-72 alignright" data-attachment-id="72" data-comments-opened="0" data-image-description="" data-image-meta='{"aperture":"0","credit":"","camera":"","caption":"","created_timestamp":"0","copyright":"","focal_length":"0","iso":"0","shutter_speed":"0","title":"","orientation":"0"}' data-image-title="xdcfvfdwdfg" data-large-file="https://i2.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/xdcfvfdwdfg.jpeg?fit=562%2C350" data-medium-file="https://i2.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/xdcfvfdwdfg.jpeg?fit=300%2C187" data-orig-file="https://i2.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/xdcfvfdwdfg.jpeg?fit=562%2C350" data-orig-size="562,350" data-permalink="http://www.thequiltingloft.com/important-steps-follow-buying-used-car/xdcfvfdwdfg/" data-recalc-dims="1" sizes="(max-width: 196px) 100vw, 196px" src="https://i2.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/xdcfvfdwdfg.jpeg?resize=196%2C122" srcset="https://i2.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/xdcfvfdwdfg.jpeg?resize=300%2C187 300w, https://i2.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/xdcfvfdwdfg.jpeg?w=562 562w"/>
          Once you have looked at the vehicle history report and you are satisfied with it, it’s time to contact the seller. You should not rush to see the car before you call the seller. This will let you establish a relationship between you and the seller. By calling first, you can get to verify the information that you have seen in the ad. This is important because the seller might reveal something about the car that was not in the ad to help you make a good decision. You also need to ensure that you are aware of the coating services that was used for the car to make it look what it is. There are many

         <h2 class="entry-title">
          <a href="http://www.thequiltingloft.com/look-smart-reasons-buy-used-car/" rel="bookmark">
           A look at smart reasons to buy a used car
          </a>
         </h2>
         <span class="title-divider">
         </span>
        </header>
        <!-- .entry-header -->
        <div class="entry-thumb">
         <a href="http://www.thequiltingloft.com/look-smart-reasons-buy-used-car/">
          <img alt="we4rtrhfgdfs" class="attachment-blacklite-full-thumb size-blacklite-full-thumb wp-post-image" data-attachment-id="49" data-comments-opened="0" data-image-description="" data-image-meta='{"aperture":"1.8","credit":"","camera":"NIKON D60","caption":"","created_timestamp":"0","copyright":"","focal_length":"35","iso":"100","shutter_speed":"0.0025","title":"","orientation":"0"}' data-image-title="we4rtrhfgdfs" data-large-file="https://i2.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/we4rtrhfgdfs.jpg?fit=640%2C428" data-medium-file="https://i2.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/we4rtrhfgdfs.jpg?fit=300%2C201" data-orig-file="https://i2.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/we4rtrhfgdfs.jpg?fit=960%2C642" data-orig-size="960,642" data-permalink="http://www.thequiltingloft.com/reasons-youre-waking-sore-neck/we4rtrhfgdfs/" height="514" sizes="(max-width: 768px) 100vw, 768px" src="https://i2.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/we4rtrhfgdfs.jpg?resize=768%2C514" srcset="https://i2.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/we4rtrhfgdfs.jpg?w=960 960w, https://i2.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/we4rtrhfgdfs.jpg?resize=300%2C201 300w, https://i2.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/we4rtrhfgdfs.jpg?resize=768%2C514 768w" width="768"/>
         </a>

        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          The automobile industry is one of the robust and dynamic sectors in the world. Steep competition has sparked the race for energy and road efficient vehicles. New models are subsequently rolled out every other day to meet consumer needs as the old ones sink into an artic oblivion created by insatiable human materialism. Marketers have perfected the art of using promotional strategies to create demand, leading to a world where obtaining a used car isn’t such a bad idea. Below are various reasons to buy a used car and end up with the car you’ve always wanted without any regrets.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Do the depreciation math
         </h2>
         <p>
          <img alt="345tryhfgdfs" class="size-medium wp-image-46 alignright" data-attachment-id="46" data-comments-opened="0" data-image-description="" data-image-meta='{"aperture":"4","credit":"","camera":"NIKON D3100","caption":"","created_timestamp":"0","copyright":"","focal_length":"18","iso":"100","shutter_speed":"0.00625","title":"","orientation":"0"}' data-image-title="345tryhfgdfs" data-large-file="https://i1.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/345tryhfgdfs.jpg?fit=510%2C340" data-medium-file="https://i1.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/345tryhfgdfs.jpg?fit=300%2C200" data-orig-file="https://i1.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/345tryhfgdfs.jpg?fit=510%2C340" data-orig-size="510,340" data-permalink="http://www.thequiltingloft.com/reasons-youre-waking-sore-neck/345tryhfgdfs/" data-recalc-dims="1" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="https://i1.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/345tryhfgdfs.jpg?resize=300%2C200" srcset="https://i1.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/345tryhfgdfs.jpg?resize=300%2C200 300w, https://i1.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/345tryhfgdfs.jpg?w=510 510w"/>
          People don’t pay attention to the depreciation factor whenever they are torn between buying a new and second-hand vehicle. Note that many of the cars that you see in your local or on the online car mart platforms for used vehicles aren’t that old. The wear and tear on them are usually below 20% of their original value. It follows that most of these cars are 80% new. A car that was originally bought at $10,000 has $8,000 worth of road energy by the time they’re put up for sale as used vehicles. This is a good deal considering that the asking price can be as low as $4,000 simply because it’s a second-hand road machine.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Insurance issues
         </h2>
         <p>
          Look at the cost of insurance in the case of a brand new car and then turn your attention that of a used car. You will notice a big difference. Used car insurance premiums are low and easy to process. Most used cars have active accounts with an insurance organization compared to the new ones with no insurance history. There are instances when some of the insurance elements can be dropped. These can be by the national or state policy that guards against dumping of cars in other countries. The cost of dumping can be higher in this age and time when environmental issues are problematic on many fronts.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Mentality shift
         </h2>
         <p>
          <img alt="34tfgdfsda" class=" wp-image-45 alignleft" data-attachment-id="45" data-comments-opened="0" data-image-description="" data-image-meta='{"aperture":"0","credit":"","camera":"","caption":"","created_timestamp":"0","copyright":"","focal_length":"0","iso":"0","shutter_speed":"0","title":"","orientation":"0"}' data-image-title="34tfgdfsda" data-large-file="https://i0.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/34tfgdfsda.jpg?fit=511%2C340" data-medium-file="https://i0.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/34tfgdfsda.jpg?fit=300%2C200" data-orig-file="https://i0.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/34tfgdfsda.jpg?fit=511%2C340" data-orig-size="511,340" data-permalink="http://www.thequiltingloft.com/reasons-youre-waking-sore-neck/34tfgdfsda/" data-recalc-dims="1" sizes="(max-width: 231px) 100vw, 231px" src="https://i0.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/34tfgdfsda.jpg?resize=231%2C154" srcset="https://i0.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/34tfgdfsda.jpg?resize=300%2C200 300w, https://i0.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/34tfgdfsda.jpg?w=511 511w"/>
          A couple of decades ago, cars were expected to have a lifespan of several thousands of kilometers. Technology has changed all these. Stronger and better cars that can double the previously anticipated mileage emerge every day. It follows that there are people who dispose of their cars based on the old mileage threshold not knowing that the newer car breeds can cover more. This gives you the advantage to get a better push for your money. People who are aware of this rarely buy brand new cars because they know how to get more out of a used car.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Registration
         </h2>
         <p>
          You can process a used car’s registration papers within a day. You are less likely to get investigated for buying a second-hand high-end car model. The story changes when you go for a brand new car because some facts about your taxes and professional work undergo scrutiny. Therefore, if you’ve never known that there are smart reasons to buy a used car, then now you have it.…
         </p>

         <span class="title-divider">

        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          Have you ever woken up with a sore neck? Most of us have suffered from this not-so pleasing scenario. A sore neck can be very uncomfortable, and most of all can disrupt your day to day activities. Though getting relief is easy thanks to medications to relieve pain and other measures like massaging, a sore neck can reduce the quality of your life with a margin of over 80%. If you have ever woken up with a sore neck, it is time you double check what could be causing this painful yet stressful ordeal. Below are reasons you’re waking up with a sore neck each morning.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Poor sleeping position
         </h2>
         <p>
          <img alt="q34r5trgdsfad" class="size-medium wp-image-47 alignleft" data-attachment-id="47" data-comments-opened="0" data-image-description="" data-image-meta='{"aperture":"0","credit":"","camera":"","caption":"","created_timestamp":"0","copyright":"","focal_length":"0","iso":"0","shutter_speed":"0","title":"","orientation":"0"}' data-image-title="q34r5trgdsfad" data-large-file="https://i0.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/q34r5trgdsfad.jpg?fit=510%2C340" data-medium-file="https://i0.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/q34r5trgdsfad.jpg?fit=300%2C200" data-orig-file="https://i0.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/q34r5trgdsfad.jpg?fit=510%2C340" data-orig-size="510,340" data-permalink="http://www.thequiltingloft.com/reasons-youre-waking-sore-neck/q34r5trgdsfad/" data-recalc-dims="1" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="https://i0.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/q34r5trgdsfad.jpg?resize=300%2C200" srcset="https://i0.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/q34r5trgdsfad.jpg?resize=300%2C200 300w, https://i0.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/q34r5trgdsfad.jpg?w=510 510w"/>
          Getting a good night’s sleep goes beyond just getting into a bed and sleeping, it starts with the way you position yourself to bed. Poor sleep position accounts for over 70% of all body aches let alone sore necks. Tucking your head in a pillow or placing your head in a not so well position for sleep like bending your neck too much or over-stretching your neck for long periods during the night can lead to a sore neck. Make sure you balance your head properly in a well-propped pillow that is comfortable and chooses a sleep position that is comfortable before falling asleep. This way, you will avoid any neck pains and soreness in the morning.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Sleeping on the wrong mattress
         </h2>
         <p>
          Where you sleep also matters a lot. You are more susceptible to aches and pains if you do not sleep in a good mattress that suits you. This is especially true for those who sleep without pillows. A hard mattress is more likely to support your head throughout the night, but a sinking mattress is more likely to place your head in an awkward position putting your neck at risk. So, get a firm mattress or support your neck with a good pillow. This guarantees you a safe sleep that is free from aches and pains the next day. This is also true for the kind and type of bed you sleep in.
         </p>
         <h2>
          You toss and turn a lot at night
         </h2>
         <p>
          As far as poor sleeping position is bad, not changing your
          <a href="https://www.webmd.com/sleep-disorders/best-sleep-positions">
           sleeping position
          </a>
          is bad too. Sleeping in one position for eight hours straight could have a negative impact on your neck especially if the position you are in, is not favoring your head. Also, tossing and turning could reposition you from a good head position to the worst head position that could lead to soreness in the morning. To, be safe make sure you sleep on a good mattress and limit the number of pillows you use per night. This way, you will avoid putting your head in between pillows lowering your risk of having neck soreness.
          <img alt="34rthfgdfsd" class="size-medium wp-image-44 aligncenter" data-attachment-id="44" data-comments-opened="0" data-image-description="" data-image-meta='{"aperture":"2.8","credit":"","camera":"Canon EOS 5D Mark III","caption":"","created_timestamp":"0","copyright":"","focal_length":"65","iso":"100","shutter_speed":"0.008","title":"","orientation":"0"}' data-image-title="34rthfgdfsd" data-large-file="https://i0.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/34rthfgdfsd.jpg?fit=510%2C340" data-medium-file="https://i0.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/34rthfgdfsd.jpg?fit=300%2C200" data-orig-file="https://i0.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/34rthfgdfsd.jpg?fit=510%2C340" data-orig-size="510,340" data-permalink="http://www.thequiltingloft.com/reasons-youre-waking-sore-neck/34rthfgdfsd/" data-recalc-dims="1" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="https://i0.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/34rthfgdfsd.jpg?resize=300%2C200" srcset="https://i0.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/34rthfgdfsd.jpg?resize=300%2C200 300w, https://i0.wp.com/www.thequiltingloft.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/34rthfgdfsd.jpg?w=510 510w"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Having a good rest is each and everyone’s goal. However, as much as we love sleeping, we must make sure that each night we sleep in a way that is promoting our health. Not caring about the type of bed or mattress you are sleeping on will lead to neck pains and back pains in the future. So, get your bed, pillows, mattresses and your senses in order, and you will be surprised by how neck soreness will be a thing of the past.…
         </p>
