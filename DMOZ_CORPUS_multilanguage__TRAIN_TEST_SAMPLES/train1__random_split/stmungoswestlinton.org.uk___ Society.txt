       <h1 class="page-title entry-title">

       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <a href="http://stmungoswestlinton.dioceseofedinburgh.org/wp-content/uploads/sites/7/2012/11/church-exterior.jpeg">
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://stmungoswestlinton.dioceseofedinburgh.org/wp-content/uploads/sites/7/2012/11/church-exterior.jpeg">
          <img class="wp-image-1020 alignleft size-full" height="237" src="http://stmungoswestlinton.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/sites/7/2018/02/img_0638.jpg" width="166"/>
         </a>
         Welcome to our website; and welcome soon, we hope, to our services and to membership of our community, whether you are here as a resident or whether you come as a visitor. On Sunday mornings our main Service is at 10 am.
        </p>
        <p>
         St Mungo’s is part of the world-wide Anglican Church – part of a very distinct Scottish tradition, but in full communion with the Church of England. It is a congregation of the
         <a href="http://www.scotland.anglican.org/">
          Scottish Episcopal Church
         </a>
         in the
         <a href="http://edinburgh.anglican.org">
          Diocese of Edinburgh
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         Our church is open to all – we welcome everyone, both for worship and for our various social events.
        </p>
        <p>
         There may be times when you feel the need to talk to someone, or maybe you want your child baptised, or are simply curious about Christianity. If so, come and join us on
         <strong>
          Sunday at 10am
         </strong>
         . Or you can always contact us using our contact form. You can also find us on
         <a href="http://facebook.com">
          Facebook
         </a>
         as “St Mungo’s Church”.
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Scottish Charity Number: SC014349
         </em>
        </p>
