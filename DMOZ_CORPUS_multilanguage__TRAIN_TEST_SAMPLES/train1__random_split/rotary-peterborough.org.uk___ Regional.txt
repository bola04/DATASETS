     <h1>
      Rotary Club of Peterborough
     </h1>
     <img class="hidden-xs club-logo pull-right" src="/static/peterborough_rotary_logo.png"/>
     <p>
      Welcome to the Rotary Club of Peterborough. Established in 1931,
     it is the oldest of the of the four Peterborough based clubs.
     </p>
     <p>
      From its commitment to eradicating Polio, through to its support
     of a myriad of
      <a href="/local">
       local
      </a>
      and
      <a href="/global">
       international
      </a>
      charitable causes, Rotary has had a profound
     impact on the lives of many.
     </p>
     <p>
      Hugely supportive of projects involving local youth, Rotary members
     are involved in the mock interview project in addition to others.
     </p>
     <p>
      The club’s events calendar includes, quizzes, golf days and social
     outings.
     </p>
     <p>
      The club meets weekly, rotating between lunch and dinner, and meetings
     usually include a presentation by a guest speaker. Meetings take place at
      <a href="https://www.ihg.com/holidayinn/hotels/gb/en/peterborough/ptrwe/hoteldetail">
       the Holiday Inn West
      </a>
      (lunch at 1pm, evening dinner at 7.30pm).
     </p>
     <p>
      Details of all our upcoming meetings, as well as any events we have
     planned, are always
      <a href="/schedule">
       available from our website
      </a>
      ,
     and you can always
      <a href="mailto:info@rotary-peterborough.org.uk">
       contact us
      </a>
      with any questions.
     </p>
