       <hr style="margin:0 0 12px 0"/>
       <p>
        In 1986, Bill MacWilliam and Dave Paterson had a vision to create a truly great private golf course in the Toronto area.  They dreamed of creating a club that was built for its members, with no outside tournaments, no tee times, no financial surprises and a club that is strictly focused on the player's enjoyment.  A true equity club that cares deeply about ensuring that there is unprecedented value in the annual fees paid by its members, and above all a top class golf facility.
       </p>
       <p>
        The industry said it couldn't be done.
       </p>
       <p>
        29 years later, the founders have done everything they set out to do and more.  St. Andrew's East stands on more than a quarter century of living up to its promises.  The commitment of a core group of management that have been with St. Andrew's East for over 100 years combined is testament to the success of the club.
       </p>
