     <h1>
      Welcome to St George's Court
     </h1>
     <p>
      St George's Court is in the heart of the beautiful Yorkshire countryside and is ideal for exploring Ripon, Harrogate &amp; York as well as the outstanding Yorkshire Dales, Fountains Abbey &amp; Brimham Rocks. This farmhouse B and B offers you the freedom to get away from it all, to relax and unwind in our quality accommodation.
     </p>
     <p>
      As well as being a family home and working farm, we offer ground floor, recently refurbished, comfortable accommodation; all our rooms have a cottage style feel with their own front doors leading out on to the courtyard. The rooms are perfect for that family get together. We even offer a private room for parties and functions.
     </p>
     <p>
      Our working farm is home to pigs, sheep and cattle as well as our chucks working hard to supply the eggs for breakfast, fresh every day.We have a well stocked fishing lake for those of you that fancy your hand at fly fishing along with an abundance of natural wildlife, including pheasant, deer, rabbit, squirrel and the occasional hare.
     </p>
     <p>
      Whether you are looking for a quiet get away or something more active and adventurous, St George's Court is the perfect location.
     </p>
     <p>
      Well behaved dogs are more than welcome to bring their owners along by prior arrangement.
     </p>
     <p>
      We look forward to welcoming you...
     </p>
