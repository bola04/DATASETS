        <p>
         Marple Cottage Surgery is situated on the outskirts of Stockport, and near to the border of the Peak District.
         <a href="staff1.aspx?p=P88006">
          Our staff
         </a>
         share a commitment to high-quality, personal primary health care for our 7,100 patients. We have special interests in diabetes, asthma, heart and other cardiovascular disease, women's health, dermatology, minor surgery and sports injury/medicine.
        </p>
        <p>
         The nursing team have responsibility for
         <a href="clinics-and-services.aspx?p=P88006">
          health screening
         </a>
         and prevention, including asthma, diabetes, heart disease, childhood immunisations and running our in-house weekly anticoagulation clinics.
        </p>
        <p>
         As a training practice we work with GP registrars and also take part in the teaching of medical students from Manchester Medical School.
        </p>
        <h3>
         Award Winning Service
        </h3>
        <p>
         We are a national
         <a href="info.aspx?p=10&amp;pr=P88006">
          award winning practice
         </a>
         , recognised for our commitment and achievements utilising information technologies as a tool to improve clinical standards and services. We are keen to promote our on-line services such as on-line
         <a href="https://patient.emisaccess.co.uk/Account/Login">
          appointment booking
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="https://patient.emisaccess.co.uk/Account/Login">
          repeat prescription
         </a>
         requests and providing
         <a href="https://patient.emisaccess.co.uk/Account/Login">
          online access to your medical records.
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         For more information please refer to our
         <a href="/website/P88006/files/Marple Cottage Surgery Leaflet 11.17.pdf">
          Patient Information Leaflet
         </a>
         .
