       <em>
        Our  goal is to provide our guests with Western North Carolina lodging they will talk about and want to come back to again, and again, and again. Come experience WNC lodging as it was in the 1950’s. We want you to feel at home, away from home. Sit on the front porch and listen to the birds, gather at the Gazebo, grill your dinner out and enjoy our down home atmosphere. In the spring our flowers will amaze you, in the summer enjoy our pleasant mountain air and beautiful forests and waterfalls, in the fall watch the leaves change colors, feel each season change. If you’re looking for Blue Ridge Mountain accommodations, give us a try. We know you’ll leave wanting to come back again.
       </em>

     <p style="text-align: center;">
      <strong>
       <em>
        We are an easy reach to anywhere in WNC, ideally located smack dab between historic downtown Hendersonville to the north and historic Flat Rock to the south. Only 20 minutes to Asheville Regional Airport and 45 minutes to Greenville/Spartanburg Airport. Shop your heart out for antiques, arts and crafts, mountain pottery and local gems. Asheville Outlet Mall is located just 30 mins to fulfill shopping needs. Take day trips to the Great Smoky Mountains, rich in a long human history. Hike DuPont Forest or the Appalachian trail, golf, bike, ride, paddle, fish. Eat ‘down home’ or fine at any of Hendersonville's great restaurants. Catch a play or concert at The Flat Rock Theatre, learn about our town's history, dance or listen to great music from the oldies to the newest and everything in between. You might say we’re a hop, skip, and a jump from everywhere and everything in Western North Carolina. In the heart of it all!.
