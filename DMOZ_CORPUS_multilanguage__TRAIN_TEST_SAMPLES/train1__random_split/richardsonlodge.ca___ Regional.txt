       <h3>
        Welcome
       </h3>
       <p>
        Welcome to the official website for
        <strong>
         Richardson Lodge No. 136 GRC
        </strong>
        in
        <strong>
         York District
        </strong>
        .
        <br/>
        Our lodge was founded in
        <a href="Lodge Buildings.html">
         Cashel
        </a>
        , south-west of Stouffville in 1860.
        <br/>
        Richardson Lodge meets on the 4th Wednesday of most months.
        <br/>
        Prior to the holiday season, our lodge meets on the second Wendesday of December.
        <br/>
        Richardson Lodge does not hold meetings in July or August, as this constitutes our summer break.
       </p>
       <p>
        Richardson Lodge has now been active in the community of Stouffville for 150 years, as of June 15th, 2010.
        <br/>
        We meet at the
        <a href="Lodge Buildings.html">
         Richardson Masonic Hall
        </a>
        , located at 279 Second St. just north of the intersection of Main Street &amp; Ninth Line.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
