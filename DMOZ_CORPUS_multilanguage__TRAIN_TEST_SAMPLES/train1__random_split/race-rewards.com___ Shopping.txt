           •All orders are custom built so you can have it your way!
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span style="color: #ff0000;">
           <strong>
            We know ordering custom awards can be a confusing experience and if you feel more comfortable talking with us over the phone feel free to give us a call, that’s why we’re here!
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <strong>
           Unlike most trophy companies, we do not sell ready made, cookie cutter, trophies. All of our orders are custom built after you tell us what you want. We have many sample pictures on our website to help get your creative juices flowing, from there what you get is up to you. Browse our site to get ideas, then tell us what you want us to build. You can buy any combination of trophies, all in the types, heights, styles, and colors that will fit your event and budget the best. Once you have an idea which awards you would like to use, the next important step to realize is that all of our awards are custom engraved with any information, and graphics. You will NOT be charged more regardless of the amount of information that you would like on each plate.
