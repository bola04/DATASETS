           <h2 class="text-align-center">
            Thank you for attending the
           </h2>
           <h1 class="text-align-center">
            <strong>
             The 32nd Annual "Izzie" Awards Ceremony
            </strong>
           </h1>
           <h2 class="text-align-center">
            which happened on
           </h2>
           <h2 class="text-align-center">
            <strong>
             Monday, March 26th, 2018
            </strong>
            <br/>
            Open: 6pm  |  Reception: 6:30pm  |  Ceremony/Performances: 7pm
           </h2>
           <h3 class="text-align-center">
            at
            <strong>
             Brava Theater Center
            </strong>
            <br/>
            2781 24th Street
            <br/>
            San Francisco, CA  94110
           </h3>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p class="text-align-center">
            <strong>
             Celebrating Dance, Culture, and Legacy!
            </strong>
            <br/>
            <strong>
             Solo and Small Ensemble Performances from
            </strong>
            : Dazaun Soleyn, Zahna Simon, Mahea Uchiyama, and La Tania Baile Flamenco
            <br/>
            <strong>
             Live Music
            </strong>
            : by Terceto Kali
           </p>
           <h2 class="text-align-center">
            Stay tuned for The 33rd Annual "Izzie" Awards Ceremony in 2019!
           </h2>
