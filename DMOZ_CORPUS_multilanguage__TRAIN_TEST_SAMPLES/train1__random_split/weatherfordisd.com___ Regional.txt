           <h1 class="title" id="hp_news_article_3_title" itemprop="name headline">
            <a href="/apps/news/article/888885">
             Verification of Enrollment for Returning Students Begins Tuesday, July 24, 2018
            </a>
           </h1>
          </header>
          <section aria-labelledby="hp_news_article_3_title" class="summary cf" itemprop="description">
           <p>
            Parents/Guardians of students returning to Weatherford ISD for the 2018-2019 school year may go online, starting July 24, 2018, and verify student enrollment information through Skyward Family Access. Once you are logged in through your parent/guardian account, please click on Returning Student Verification and complete the forms for the 2018-2019 school year.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            •	If you are a new student to Weatherford ISD, you have already completed these forms and do not need to continue the verification process.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            •	If you have forgotten your login information, click on the "Forgot your Login/Password?" link on the Skyward home page
            <br/>
            <br/>
            •	If you have never logged in to Skyward, please contact your student's school to get login information.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            The online verification of student enrollment information is for students who are returning to Weatherford ISD for the 2018-2019 school year. Your student’s schedule or homeroom teacher will not be released until the verification forms are completed.
            <br/>
            This is to make sure that we have updated contact information for your student prior to the start of school.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            If you have questions about this process, please contact your campus.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Thank you for helping us have accurate contact information for our families by updating your information!
           </p>
          </section>

           <h1 class="title" id="hp_news_article_4_title" itemprop="name headline">
            <a href="/apps/news/article/887874">
             WISD Education Foundation School Supply Donation Drive
            </a>
           </h1>
          </header>
          <section aria-labelledby="hp_news_article_4_title" class="summary cf" itemprop="description">
           <a class="thumbnail cf" href="/apps/news/article/887874">
            <img alt="" src="https://3.files.edl.io/53ad/18/07/18/163117-4f45a473-e7e6-436d-a6f2-56dfd409b33d.jpg"/>
           </a>
           <p>
            The Weatherford ISD Education Foundation and the Jerry Durant Auto Group are teaming up for the 14th consecutive year to host a school supply and backpack drive for WISD students.  The drive supports the Education Foundation’s School Supply Closet and will take place on Saturday, August 4 from 10 a.m. to 12 p.m. at Jerry’s GMC.  The event features free hot dogs and soft drinks courtesy of Wallace Insurance Agency and a live remote broadcast by 99.5 The Wolf.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            The Foundation’s School Supply Closet provided backpacks and supplies to over 1,200 students annually since 2003.  Weatherford ISD parents needing supplies for their children should contact their campus principal, counselor, or teacher. Requests are handled anonymously by the Education Foundation and supplies are available throughout the school year.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            For more information about the Weatherford ISD Education Foundation’s School Supply Closet, please contact Charlotte LaGrone at 817-598-2956.
           </p>
          </section>
