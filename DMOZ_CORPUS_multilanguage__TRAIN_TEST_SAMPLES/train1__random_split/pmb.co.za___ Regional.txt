      <div>
       Washed by the warm waters of the Indian Ocean, KwaZulu-Natal, with its subtropical coastline, sweeping savannah in the east and magnificent Drakensberg mountain range in the west, generously caters for just about every taste imaginable.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Known as the Kingdom of the Zulu, KwaZulu-Natal is a melting pot of African, European and Indian cultures. KwaZulu-Natal boasts two World Heritage Sites – the Greater St Lucia Wetland Park and the Ukhahlamba Drakensberg Park.
      </div>
      <div id="homeWrap">
       <div id="homeSec">
        <div>
         Traditionally popular with holiday-makers, KwaZulu-Natal is South Africa’s busiest local holiday destination. KwaZulu-Natal is appropriately named “South Africa’s Garden Province”. KwaZulu-Natal's lush, green province forms the east coast of South Africa, from the Mozambique boundary southwards to Port Edward. KwaZulu-Natal's coastline is dotted with small towns, many of which serve as seasonal recreational hubs, with a climate that is humid and subtropical. KwaZulu-Natal has superb beaches of world-class quality, which are to be found along virtually the entire KZN coastline.
        </div>

         KwaZulu-Natal.co.za is a tourism and business network, that caters to different catergories in each area. These areas include Durban, North Coast, Pinetown, South Coast, Midlands, Pietermaritzburg, and Northern Natal. The different catergories are accommodation, entertainment, general business, home improvement, motoring, shopping centers, travel, health and beauty, weddings and functions, arts and crafts, education, property, and more...

                 var fetchHdrSearchSuggestionsRequest = false;                
                 function startHdrSearchSuggestions(input){
                     if(fetchHdrSearchSuggestionsRequest && fetchHdrSearchSuggestionsRequest.readyState !== 4){
                         fetchHdrSearchSuggestionsRequest.abort();
                     }
                     if(input.length > 0){
                         fetchHdrSearchSuggestionsRequest = fetchHdrSearchSuggestions(input);
                     }else{
                         $('#hdrSearchForm .searchTerm .searchTermRes').hide();
                         $('#hdrSearchForm .searchTerm .searchTermRes').html('');
                     }
                 }
         
                 function fetchHdrSearchSuggestions(input){
                     var fetchHdrSearchSuggestionsRequest = $.ajax({
                         type: 'POST',
                         url: '/kznListings.ajax.php?fetchSearchSug=1&resultPageNo=8&hk=$2y$10$wSDSvQ8OjlsGqIwKRX.p2ulwyp.xcPPA4vSe4lGWGTxeNtfdK2qci',
                         data: {
                             term: input
                         },
                         beforeSend: function(){
                             $('#hdrSearchForm .searchTerm .searchTermRes').slideDown();
                             $('#hdrSearchForm .searchTerm .searchTermRes').html('Fetching suggestions...');
                         },error: function(){
                             $('#hdrSearchForm .searchTerm .searchTermRes').html('Could not fetch suggestions, press go for a more advanced search.');
                         },success: function(data){
                             if(data && data !== 'undefined'){
                                 $('#hdrSearchForm .searchTerm .searchTermRes').html(data);
                             }else{
                                 $('#hdrSearchForm .searchTerm .searchTermRes').html('No suggestions found, press go for a more advanced search.');
                             }
                         }
                     });
                     return fetchHdrSearchSuggestionsRequest;
                 };
