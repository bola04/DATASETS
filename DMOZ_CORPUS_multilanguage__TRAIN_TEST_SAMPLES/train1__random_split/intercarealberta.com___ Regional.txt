              Located on 68th  Avenue SW in proximity to Intercare’s Chinook Care Centre, Kingsland Terrace  represents an innovative model of ‘
              <em>
               supportive living’
              </em>
              that   provides care and support services for  younger adults  under 65 years of age.  Kingsland Terrace is home to twenty-four (24) residents in which  six (6) residents live in one of four (4) separate and distinct  residential living environments (also referred to as ’homes’).
             </p>
             <p class="style9">
              Kingsland Terrace  offers programming and services to benefit the care and  social needs of residents residing in each of the four ‘homes’.  This  innovative, 24,000 sq. ft. facility has received  provincial support in the form of an

               Affordable Supportive Living Initiative
              </em>
              (
              <em>
               ASLI)
              </em>
