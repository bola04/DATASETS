            <h1>
             Orlando Catastrophic Injury Attorneys
            </h1>
            <h3>
             Serving Orlando, Winter Park, and all of Florida
            </h3>
            <p>
             Have you been seriously injured in an accident or incident in the greater Orlando area that was caused by the negligence of another? Have you lost a loved one in a wrongful death because of such an accident? Have you or a family member suffered serious health consequences due to medical malpractice on the part of a doctor, surgeon, dentist, therapist, or other health care provider? All of these circumstances can spell tragedy, heartbreak, financial disaster, and adversity for those involved. At Bailey Fisher, PLLC, we understand the long-standing effects that such serious injuries and wrongful deaths can have on individuals and their families. That is why we are devoted exclusively to the practice of personal injury and wrongful death cases and trials, working diligently to obtain the financial compensation you deserve.
            </p>
            <p>
             Each Orlando personal injury attorney at our firm is dedicated to the proposition that you deserve our best effort in seeking to achieve a favorable outcome to your case. That is why we go the extra mile, putting in extra time or effort on your behalf when needed. We make certain that you thoroughly understand what is going on with your case. Our overriding goal is to provide the most effective and compassionate legal care available. When you work with an Orlando personal injury lawyer at our firm, we treat you the way we would want to be treated in similar circumstances.
            </p>
            <p>
             Bailey Fisher is an established traditional law firm with strong ties to the local community. We put the interests of our clients first, giving you the individual attention you deserve and providing the resources of a large firm while maintaining the personalized service of a small firm. As a proven team of trial lawyers, we have over 70 years of combined experience both in and out of the courtroom. One of our founding attorneys, Michael Bailey, is a Florida Bar Board-Certified Civil Trial Lawyer. He is also Certified as a Civil Trial Advocate by the National Board of Trial Advocacy. Our other founding attorney, Jay Fisher, has practiced exclusively in the area of personal injury and wrongful death since March of 1992.
            </p>
