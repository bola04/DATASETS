         NOTICE: The flooding south of us did not effect Copper Harbor at all. We are so fortunate that we had much less rain up here. The roads are open and you can get to Copper Harbor despite the damage to the south. Copper Harbor is 100% up and running so come on UP!

         <b>
          The Brockway Inn
         </b>
         is an owner operated establishment. We are dedicated to providing our guests year round, friendly, efficient service in a unique and truly Northern environment.
        </p>

         <b>
          Accommodations
         </b>
         include six comfortable, clean, cozy, non-smoking rooms situated in Copper Harbor in the shadow of Brockway Mountain. Our motel is a refreshing change from large chain hotels. The rooms all have color TVs with satellite remote and free wireless internet. We accept pets, with some restrictions (
         <a href="http://www.brockwayinn.com/policies/">
          see Pet Policy
         </a>
         ). There is a refrigerator and microwave available in a common area for guest use.
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          We offer
         </b>
         complimentary fresh brewed coffee each morning. In the summer months get your coffee in the shop located in the center of the motel. Please browse this site for more info!

        </p>
