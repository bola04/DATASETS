             <h2 class="title">
              <a class="journal-entry-navigation-current" href="/news/business-spotlight-bexley-bed-breakfast.html">
               BUSINESS SPOTLIGHT: Bexley Bed + Breakfast
              </a>
             </h2>
             <div class="body">
              <p>
               <img alt="" height="220" src="https://gallery.mailchimp.com/a307fdc0518df5b02b5291a58/images/8122f835-aeec-4f97-a475-62ebb83e2185.png" style="text-decoration: none; height: auto !important; -ms-interpolation-mode: bicubic; border-image: none; border-width: 0px;" width="560"/>
               <br/>
               <strong>
                <span style="font-size: 12px;">
                 519 S Drexel Ave, Bexley, OH 43209
                </span>
               </strong>
              </p>
              <p>
               The Bexley Bed + Breakfast is a getaway unlike any other. Owned and operated by Mike and Lesli Mautz, The Bexley B&amp;B features five gorgeous guest rooms complete with free WiFi and a delicious breakfast. While the couple's breathtaking property will certainly draw you in the door, it's their attention to detail and accomodating nature that will beg you to stay. The Bexley Bed + Breakfast, which is a proud member of the
               <a href="http://www.bexleyareachamber.org/" style="color: #2baadf;" target="_blank">
                Bexley Area Chamber of Commerce
               </a>
               , is a must!
               <br/>
               <br/>
               One of their trusted returning customers, Cara, had this to say: "Mike and Lesli Mautz are so warm and so welcoming. The first time I stayed there I felt like they were my friends - and I was even sad to say goodbye. They're incredibly personal and dependable. In May of this year, our daughter graduated from Capital. My husband forgot the tickets in our room, and Mike drove them over to us, and Lesli helped get all of our family members to dinner. It's more than just "Here's you room!" And boy do they love their community. They believe in everything Bexley!"
               <br/>
               <br/>
               To make a reservation, visit
               <a href="https://www.bexleybedandbreakfast.com/" style="color: #2baadf;" target="_blank">
                https://www.bexleybedandbreakfast.com/
               </a>
               .
               <br/>
               <br/>
               If you would like your business given a spotlight in the Bexley Blast, please
               <a href="mailto:smetcalf@bexley.org?subject=Business%20Spotlight%20Opportunity" style="color: #2baadf;" target="_blank">
                send us an email
               </a>
               !
              </p>

              <a class="journal-entry-navigation-current" href="/news/splish-splash-dash-bexley-youth-triathlon.html">
               Splish, Splash, &amp; Dash Bexley Youth Triathlon
              </a>
             </h2>
             <div class="body">
              <p>
               <img alt="" height="220" src="https://gallery.mailchimp.com/a307fdc0518df5b02b5291a58/images/57a66fd7-0d89-424d-afee-c6243789b378.png" style="text-decoration: none; height: 220px; width: 560px; margin: 0px; -ms-interpolation-mode: bicubic; border-image: none; border-width: 0px;" width="560"/>
               <br/>
               Competition Held on Saturday, August 18th
              </p>
              <p dir="ltr" style="color: #202020;">
               It’s National Triathlon Week July 16-22, so just a reminder that our 6th Annual Youth Triathlon is open for
               <a href="https://bit.ly/2zqh0dF" style="color: #2baadf;" target="_blank">
                pre-registration online
               </a>
               and in person at Jeffrey Mansion! The triathlon will require participants to complete a swimming, biking, and running course.  With age divisions ranging from under 6 to 14, varying distances will be selected for each leg that are age appropriate. #TriWeek
               <br/>
               <br/>
               <strong>
                Youth Triathlon Training Camp — (6 - 14 Years Old):
               </strong>
               <br/>
               <em>
                Tuesday &amp; Thursday 8:00 - 9:00 am, $65.00
                <br/>
                July 24 - August 14
               </em>
              </p>
              <p dir="ltr" style="color: #202020;">
               The goal with this program is to prepare for the triathlon. The camp will focus on proper form and technique for all 3 disciplines: swimming, cycling and running. You can get more information on this camp by calling Recreation Department at (614) 559-4300!
              </p>
