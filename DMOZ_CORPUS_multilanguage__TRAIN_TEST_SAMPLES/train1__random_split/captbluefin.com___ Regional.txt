     <p align="left">
      <b>
       <font size="4">
        Deep Sea Fishing charters targeting Cod, 
 Haddock, giant Atlantic Bluefin Tuna, school Bluefin Tuna and Striped Bass from Gloucester, MA aboard the fishing vessel 
 Sandy B. Experience Massachusetts salt water fishing at it's finest.
       </font>
      </b>
     </p>
     <p align="left">
     </p>
     <p class="style35">
      <img alt="Sandy B at the Paint Factory Gloucester, MA" height="600" src="Sandy%20B%20by%20DC%20Pic%202.jpg" style="float: right" width="800"/>
      <span class="style9">
       Welcome aboard 
 			the charter fishing vessel Sandy B!
      </span>
      <a href="captainandcrew/captainandcrew.htm">
       <span class="style23">
        <strong>
         Captain 
 Bluefin and the crew
        </strong>
       </span>
      </a>
      invite you to join us for the 
 Deep Sea charter fishing 
 adventure of a lifetime. Hailing from Gloucester, MA the captain and 
 crew have only one goal: We fully intend to get your adrenaline pumping!!!
     </p>
     <p align="left">
      Bent rods and screaming reels are the norm when you fish with 
 Sandy B Fishing Charters. Built in 2009,  The Sandy B is powered by a 715 HP  Electronic Cummins Diesel engine. Our 
 charter fishing boat is a rocket ship. She is extremely
      <strong>
       fast
      </strong>
      and
      <strong>
       comfortable
      </strong>
      . Her 
 wheelhouse is literally crammed with modern state of the art
      <a class="style34" href="electronics/electronics.html">
       <strong>
        electronics
       </strong>
      </a>
      <span class="style10">
       <span class="style38">
        . We find fish, and we stay on fish. Whether your pleasure is 
 fishing for striped bass, cod fish and haddock, giant Atlantic Bluefin Tuna, or 
 school bluefin tuna, 
 your charter hosts will consistently put you on the bite, and KEEP YOU ON THE BITE. Take 
 a moment to learn a little about our charter fishing vessel here:
       </span>
       <span class="style36">
        <a class="style34" href="sandybboat/sandybboat.html">
         <strong>
          <span class="style39">
           About the Sandy B
          </span>
         </strong>
        </a>
       </span>
       <span class="style38">
        . 
 We are justifiably proud of our boat. We think you will find her to be a 
 superior fishing machine.
       </span>
      </span>
     </p>
     <p align="left">
      When you fish with Sandy B Fishing Charters you 
 are putting the odds in your favor. You'll fish on a  large, stable and modern 
 vessel with state of the art electronics. Your captain and crew are experienced 
 with a proven track record. And you'll have a lot of fun!
     </p>
     <p class="style32">
      We are finally recovering from a wild year 
 			of fishing in 2013. It is hard to believe the year is over. 
 			We'll be spending the winter going over all our fishing our gear and 
 			stocking the freezer and shelves with secret lures and baits. This 
 			past season is barely over, and we are already getting excited for 
 			the 2014 charter fishing year.
     </p>
     <p class="style32">
     </p>
     <p class="style32">
      <strong>
       Spring 2017 news
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p class="style32">
      Haddock fishing is SPECTACULAR!!! There is 
 			currently a 15 Haddock per person limit. Limits are being hit almost 
 			every trip.
     </p>
     <p class="style32">
      Mackeral are just showing up. Lunker striped bass 
 			will be here in the next week or two.
     </p>
     <p class="style32">
      Tuna season will be opening on June 1st.. Now 
 			that the Macks have shown up, the tuna will not be far behind.
     </p>
     <p class="style32">
      As most of you know, NMFS has closed cod fishing. 
 			We are running groundfish trips for haddock and large pollock. We are 
 			also running a lot of groundfish/striper charters.
     </p>
     <p class="style32">
      Give us a call at 978-729-2575 or email:
      <a class="style34" href="mailto:captbluefin@captbluefin.com">
       captbluefin@captbluefin.com
      </a>
      to secure the best 
 			dates.
     </p>
