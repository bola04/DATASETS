              </a>
             </p>
             <div>
              Currently there is a brown algae bloom in the northern Indian River Lagoon and Banana River. The Lagoon is experiencing the same conditions as the 2016 Fish Kill and another kill is possible in the near future.  Keep Brevard Beautiful is working closely with local and state agencies and will respond should there be a need. We are asking all of our volunteers to be on “stand by” to help with the clean up efforts if there is another fish kill.  If you are able and willing to assist either by boat or along the shorelines of the Indian River or Banana River, please register
