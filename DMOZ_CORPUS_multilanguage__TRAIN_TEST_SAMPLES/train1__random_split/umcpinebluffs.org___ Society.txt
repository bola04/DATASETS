      <span class="TitleFont">
       Welcome to The United Methodist Church in Pine Bluffs, WY
      </span>
      <p>
       The addition of your praise to God and presence at any of  our worship services delights us all.  If  you are visiting from out of town, we wish you safe travels.  If you are looking for a church home, you  will find a ready welcome here.
      </p>
      <p>
       Pine  Bluffs United Methodist Church is located in the center of Pine Bluffs, a town  of about 1200 people.  Our farm and ranch  community is in southeast Wyoming, on the Wyoming – Nebraska border,  ten miles north of Colorado, and 40 miles east  of Cheyenne.  Pine Bluffs, Wyoming, is  along Interstate 80 at Wyoming exit 401 and Nebraska exit 1.  For over a century, the Pine Bluffs United  Methodist Church has followed Christ’s ministry of preaching, teaching,  healing, and caring.
      </p>
