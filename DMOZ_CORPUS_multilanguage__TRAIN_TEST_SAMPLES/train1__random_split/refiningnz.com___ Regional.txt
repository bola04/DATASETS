         <p>

         </p>
        </div>
        <div class="col-md-6 intro">
         <p>
          Refining NZ is well regarded as one of South Pacific’s safest and most reliable refineries.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           We are strongly committed to our people, our community, our environment and our economy.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Crude oil bought by our oil company customers (BP, Mobil, Z Energy) is shipped to our deep-water port at Marsden Point, near Whangarei for refining into high quality transport fuels for New Zealand.
         </p>
         <p>
          As a critical part of our national energy infrastructure we are responsible for supplying:
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           around 85% all of the country’s jet fuel
          </li>
          <li>
           around 67% of diesel
          </li>
          <li>
           around 58% of all petrol
          </li>
          <li>
           all fuel oil for ships
          </li>
