     <p align="justify">
      <strong>
       Woody Lodge
      </strong>
      <strong>
       bed and breakfast
      </strong>
      is  situated in an out of village location approached via a private lane close to Prestwold Hall, who provide for corporate events and weddings also Track Days and Driving Experiences.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      The Garden Suite accommodation comprises of an  elegant blue and white ground floor twin/double bedroom, garden room and private  bathroom for comfort and luxury. Completed by its own part of the secluded  garden overlooking the pond and lavender terrace for those sunny moments.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      <img alt="Prestwold Hall near Woody Lodge" class="floatrh" src="images/Prestwold-Hall.jpg"/>
      A gated access leads to your  own entrance into the garden room, whilst the suite itself enjoys the open  rural landscape of Prestwold Hall country estate. Relax and enjoy the view with  your own afternoon tea &amp; coffee making facilities in the garden room, with  books, puzzles, chess and TV and Wifi to add a homely and entertaining atmosphere.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Follow the  bridle path from the garden to explore Prestwold Hall estate, woods and streams, the  haunts of woodpeckers, badgers and foxes make this a delight to bird  watchers, wildlife enthusiasts, walkers and riders.
      <br/>
