              <a class="entry-featured-image-url" href="http://brianstewartracing.ca/top-10-prestigious-races-world/">
               <img alt="Top 10 Most Prestigious Races in the World " height="675" src="http://brianstewartracing.ca/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/maxresdefault-e1519037331465.jpg" width="1080"/>
              </a>
              <h2 class="entry-title">
               <a href="http://brianstewartracing.ca/top-10-prestigious-races-world/">
                Top 10 Most Prestigious Races in the World
               </a>
              </h2>
              <p class="post-meta">
               by
               <span class="author vcard">
                <a href="http://brianstewartracing.ca/author/administrator/" rel="author" title="Posts by Madeline R. Meyer">
                 Madeline R. Meyer
                </a>
               </span>
               |
               <span class="published">
                Feb 19, 2018
               </span>
               |
               <a href="http://brianstewartracing.ca/category/uncategorized/races/" rel="category tag">
                Races
               </a>
              </p>
              <div class="post-content">
               <p>
                <a name="_GoBack">
                </a>
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 There is a big craze around the subject of motorsport and how the action-seekers add the biggest races in the world to their bucket lists. Although racing can seem dangerous, a true racer doesn’t care and is electrified by speed and the title that goes with it.
                </span>
               </p>
               <ol start="10">
                <li>
                 <span style="font-size: large;">
                  <b>
                   Monte Carlo Rally
                  </b>
                 </span>
                </li>
               </ol>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 As an annual season-opener, known as an asphalt rally which is means mostly racing in unpredictable weather conditions, this race is one that is both dangerous and exciting. There are always new challenges and if you’re a good racer, a lower probability of winning. Its first race was held in 1911 and with its usual snowfall, has become a must to race for thrill-seekers from all parts of the world.
                 <br/>
                </span>
               </p>
               <ol start="9">
                <li>
                 <span style="font-size: large;">
                  <b>
                   Pikes Peak Hill Climb
                  </b>
                 </span>
                </li>
               </ol>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 Referred to as ‘
                 <a href="http://brianstewartracing.ca" rel="nofollow">
                  The Race to the Clouds
                 </a>
                 ’. Being the second oldest motorsport race, it is one of the tops in the world. Starting out in 1916, with 153 turns which start at 9.390ft and finishing at 14.115ft, its noticed as one of the highest-altitude races in the world and challenging. Some also refer to it as ‘The Devil’s playground.
                </span>
               </p>
               <ol start="8">
                <li>
                 <span style="font-size: large;">
                  <b>
                   Isle of Man
                  </b>
                 </span>
                </li>
               </ol>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 With its first race kicking off as early as 1907, it’s a race with quite a reputation. Especially one for those seeking thrill and adventure. Choosing to drive in this race is only for those who aren’t afraid to encounter 38 miles of near-death experiences, as there has been many.
                 <br/>
                </span>
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: center;">
                <img alt="" class="alignnone wp-image-34 size-full" height="288" src="http://brianstewartracing.ca/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/Isle-of-Man-TT-The-Game-Trailer-e1519037259900.jpg" width="512"/>
               </p>
               <ol start="7">
                <li>
                 <span style="font-size: large;">
                  <b>
                   24 Hours of Nürburgring
                  </b>
                 </span>
                </li>
               </ol>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 Sounds like a Horror-film and quite an experience as it is referred to as “The Green Hell”. It is a very difficult race but an epic experience at that. With a beautiful scenery of over 25 kilometers in lap length, this is the one to enjoy.
                 <br/>
                </span>
               </p>
               <ol start="6">
                <li>
                 <span style="font-size: large;">
                  <b>
                   Dakar Rally
                  </b>
                 </span>
                </li>
               </ol>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 Known as the Paris-Dakar Rally, this race if an off-road experience that is enjoyed by sports cars, motorcycles and even trucks. Where else in the world can you race with a truck? Dangerous? We think yes.
                </span>
               </p>
               <ol start="5">
                <li>
                 <span style="font-size: large;">
                  <b>
                   Bathurst 1000
                  </b>
                 </span>
                </li>
               </ol>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 On your way to the Aussie land? Be sure to visit the race that every
                 <a href="http://www.bbc.com/news/av/uk-england-hereford-worcester-41901107/racing-driver-with-cerebral-palsy-dreams-of-motorsport-glory" rel="nofollow">
                  racing driver dreams
                 </a>
                 about on the annual Mount Panorama Circuit, situated in New South Wales. It’s an unforgettable V8 super Supercar racing experience.
                </span>
               </p>
               <ol start="4">
                <li>
                 <span style="font-size: large;">
                  <b>
                   Monaco Grand Prix
                  </b>
                 </span>
                </li>
               </ol>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 Known for being quite famous, the F1 Grand Prix has been around since 1929. It is considered one of the most glamorous races. There are even celebrities to be found in the crowds.
                </span>
               </p>
               <p>
                <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-30 size-full" height="529" src="http://brianstewartracing.ca/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/F1-Monaco-GrandPrix-2017.jpg" width="941"/>
               </p>
               <ol start="3">
                <li>
                 <span style="font-size: large;">
                  <b>
                   Daytona 500
                  </b>
                 </span>
                </li>
               </ol>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 This is a race for NASCAR lovers. It is the highest-in-demand stock racing in North America and kicks off as the first of the annual season.
                </span>
               </p>
               <ol start="2">
                <li>
                 <span style="font-size: large;">
                  <b>
                   Indianapolis 500
                  </b>
                 </span>
                </li>
               </ol>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 Starting out in 1911, this famous race started out with balloon races, followed by motorcycle racing and eventually, automobile racing. It is the global pinnacle of American Championship sportscar racing.
                </span>
               </p>
               <ol>
                <li>
                 <span style="font-size: large;">
                  <b>
                   24 Hours of Le Mans
                  </b>
                 </span>
                </li>
               </ol>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 Reaching number one on various best track races in the world, it is recognized as one of the best well-reputable tracks in sports car racing in the world. The race is brutal and just as its name suggests, 24-hours long. There won’t be any rest for these adrenaline junkies.
                 <br/>
                </span>
               </p>
              </div>

             <article class="et_pb_post clearfix post-18 post type-post status-publish format-standard has-post-thumbnail hentry category-f1-drivers" id="post-18">
              <a class="entry-featured-image-url" href="http://brianstewartracing.ca/best-formula-one-drivers-time/">
               <img alt="Best Formula One Drivers of All Time" height="675" src="http://brianstewartracing.ca/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/59b930aa19f92-e1519037108719.jpeg" width="1080"/>
              </a>
              <h2 class="entry-title">
               <a href="http://brianstewartracing.ca/best-formula-one-drivers-time/">
                Best Formula One Drivers of All Time
               </a>
              </h2>
              <p class="post-meta">
               by
               <span class="author vcard">
                <a href="http://brianstewartracing.ca/author/administrator/" rel="author" title="Posts by Madeline R. Meyer">
                 Madeline R. Meyer
                </a>
               </span>
               |
               <span class="published">
                Feb 19, 2018
               </span>
               |
               <a href="http://brianstewartracing.ca/category/f1-drivers/" rel="category tag">
                F1 Drivers
               </a>
              </p>
              <div class="post-content">
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 Over the last 150 years, there has been a massive hype when it comes to Formula One racing. For the drivers of these prestigious and well-engineered cars, there has even been an increase in fame. After all, these are the individuals who put their life at risk each time they step onto the track, get in their F1-machine and break yet another record.
                </span>
               </p>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 With all the thrill and excitement around these superhumans, let’s have a look at the
                 <a href="http://brianstewartracing.ca" rel="nofollow">
                  top drivers
                 </a>
                 in F1-racing.
                 <br/>
                </span>
               </p>
               <p>
                <iframe allow="autoplay; encrypted-media" allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="608" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/9H5L8BPT0ZQ?feature=oembed" width="1080">
                </iframe>
               </p>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 <b>
                  Ayrton Senna
                 </b>
                </span>
               </p>
               <p>
                <a name="_GoBack">
                </a>
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 Topping the list as the number one F1-racer in the world is Ayrton Senna da, Silva. He was a Brazilian racer that won a total of three F1 world championships for the prestigious car brand, McLaren. He is described to be ruthless, determined, brilliant, charismatic and one of the fastest drivers in the history of F1-racing. His strategy was merely believing that he had no choice but to win and it brought him the title of success at a total of 41 wins.
                </span>
               </p>
               <p>
                <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-38 size-medium" height="169" src="http://brianstewartracing.ca/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/Ayrton-Senna-300x169.jpg" width="300"/>
               </p>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 <b>
                  Michael Schumacher
                 </b>
                </span>
               </p>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 His name is recognized all around the world as one of the best. He is also considered the greatest F1 driver that have ever graced the tracks of
                 <a href="https://www.formula1.com/en/championship/races/2017.html" rel="nofollow">
                  F1-racing
                 </a>
                 . He has great talent and skill and can be described as an individual with great work ethic, which was based on speculation and subjectivity.
                </span>
               </p>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 <b>
                  Juan Manuel Fangio
                 </b>
                </span>
               </p>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 As the F1-racer who dominated in the 50’s, he was a man that performed the best of all. His greatest win was in 1940 when won the Grand Prix International, as well as winning the title of world’s best drivers five times. He also kept his record for 47-years until he was beaten by Michael Schumacher.
                </span>
               </p>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 <b>
                  Fernando Alonso
                 </b>
                </span>
               </p>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 Noticed as one of the most skilled drivers, who is still currently racing, he could reach any cars potential, even though the car is not good. He has been compared to Ayrton Senna when it comes to his race-craft, methods, and talent and has been a much-anticipated driver who is set to win many more championships.
                </span>
               </p>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 <b>
                  Jim Clark
                 </b>
                </span>
               </p>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 Jim Clark was one of the greatest natural talented drivers ever seen in motor racing. He had a great work ethic, was respectable and to say the least, a gentleman. He never blocked drivers on the track and always approached it with grace and class. He won the Indianapolis 500, as well as the World title in the same year. He was the only man to ever do so.
                </span>
               </p>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 By looking at the top 5 drivers in F1 history, it’s tough to think how dangerous this sport and these men careers are. That is why referring to them as ‘supermen’ is the most appropriate title one could ever give them.
                </span>
               </p>
              </div>
