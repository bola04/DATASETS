        <div class="col-md-5 col-sm-12">
         <p>
          In 2000 Cheer Camp, LLC was established by
          <strong>
           Meigan Bronstein
          </strong>
          as a cheerleading camp offering tumbling and summer camps for cheerleaders. In 2001, Cheer Camp, LLC expanded and the Cheer Camp (CC) Champs All-Star team was formed.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Over the course of many years, CC CHAMPS has established themselves as one of the top All-Star cheerleading programs in the state of New Jersey.  During the past seasons our cheerleaders attended State, National, International, and World Championships. CC Champs has earned top awards for their performance, tumbling, and creativity.
         </p>
