       <h2>
        Welcome to The Original ZORB New Zealand!
       </h2>
       <hr/>
       <p>
        ZORB New Zealand is the worlds leading ball rolling brand and the original company that started the ball rolling adventure industry. ZORB New Zealand is safe, great value and fun for the whole family.
       </p>
       <br/>
       <p>
        Based in Rotorua, ZORB New Zealand is open 09:00 - 17:00 every day (Til 19:00 in Summer) and offers the most track choices with both WET and DRY Options.
       </p>
       <br/>
       <p>
        Located at 149 Western Road, Rotorua, ZORB New Zealand is close to the adventure and activity hub of Rotorua. If you are travelling by bus, ZORB New Zealand can offer complimentary transport for our international guests. Just call us directly and arrange a pick up from within the Rotorua CBD.
       </p>
       <br/>
       <p>
        ZORB New Zealand offers a range of unique experiences and is always striving to be innovative and can guarantee that a ride with us is “Always Original”.
       </p>
