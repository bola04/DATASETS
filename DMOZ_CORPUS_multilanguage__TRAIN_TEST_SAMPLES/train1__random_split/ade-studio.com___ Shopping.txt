       <h2>
        Photo Retouching
       </h2>
       <p>
        Photo retouch can be subdivided into four categories:
        <em>
         Portrait Retouching, Pageant Retouch, Object or Person Elimination
        </em>
        and
        <em>
         Product Retouching.
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong class="orange">
         Portrait Retouching
        </strong>
        – comprises elimination of cosmetic skin defects, wrinkles, «facelift», make-up correction, squint correction, asymmetry correction, etc. Alongside with cosmetic or physical flaws, some faces have either big mouth or greasy skin, long or short nose, narrow or wide face, a different face angle, hollow cheeks (too skinny), or stoutness.
        <br/>
        One would say that these problems should have been solved by a photographer, but alas, not all of them can be concealed by lens and lighting. Retouching finishes off the triad of tools in artistic portrait imagery which in turn is an essential element of the art of photography.
       </p>
