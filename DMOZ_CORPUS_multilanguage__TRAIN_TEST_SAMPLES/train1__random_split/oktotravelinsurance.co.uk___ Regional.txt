           <h2>
            Travel with Confidence
           </h2>
           <p>
            OK To Travel is designed to help you travel where you want, when you want. Other travel insurers may decline cover based on medical conditions and age but that’s not our style! We aim to provide cover for all ages and all types of medical conditions, including Cancer, Heart Conditions, Diabetes, Stroke, Epilepsy and Mental Health Conditions. Whether you’re looking for a single trip or annual multi trip policy, look no further. Getting a quote takes minutes with our instant Quote and Buy system – it’s quick, simply and totally confidential.
            <strong>
             This policy is only available to residents of UK who have lived in the UK for the last six months and are registered with a UK GP.
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            We can insure your travelling companions too, whether they have medical conditions or not. Being insured on the same specialist OK To Travel policy has many advantages and can protect accompanying friends and family against unforeseen costs resulting from your existing medical conditions, such as cancellation or curtailment of a holiday.
