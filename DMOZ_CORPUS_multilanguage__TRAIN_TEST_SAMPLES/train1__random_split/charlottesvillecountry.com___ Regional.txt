       "The Number One Place to live in America."
      </span>
      So say the poll takers of Fromm's Travel Guide and USA Today. We are truly blessed to be representing real estate in Charlottesville, Virginia, located at the foot of the Blue Ridge Mountains in Central, Virginia, such a becoming part of one of America's prettiest states. The climate, the spectacular scenery and most importantly the eclectic mix of characters that make up the population all combine to make the Charlottesville area, once discovered, nearly impossible to leave. This makes
      <a href="http://charlottesvillecountry.com/charlottesville-country-properties-listings.php" title="Charlotesville Virginia real estate listings">
       investing in Charlottesville real estate
      </a>
      a sure thing for the perfect family setting or the shrewd real estate investor.
     </p>

     <p>
      Here, around Charlottesville, Virginia you might get hung up behind a combine as you wind past hayfields and horse farms on a quiet country road. That's a traffic tie up that will improve your frame of mind as you gaze out at the endless acres of rich, Virginia real estate full of history and heritage. Here you'll enjoy winery tours, steeple chase races,  UVA football and within 20 minutes be completely alone on a mountain stream in the Shenandoah National Park.
     </p>
     <p>
      In and around Charlottesville, Virginia you'll cross paths with captains of industry, film and recording stars, Nobel Prize winning authors and scholars, dairy farmers, horse trainers, veterinarians, jugglers, street musicians, and lots of happy families. (A Readers Digest poll ranked the Charlottesville area as one of the Top 10 places to raise a family in America). Here, you'll also feel welcome into a diverse, well educated population which has assembled from all over the world and found comfort in an open, free thinking society.
     </p>

Hand in hand with the increased appreciation of the situation and amenities of the Charlottesville area is an astounding appreciation of the real estate here. An overriding sentiment echoed by most astute investors says that any real estate owned in the greater Charlottesville, Virginia area will out perform any other investment in their portfolio. Please, come see for yourself what Thomas Jefferson called "The Eden of America" We'd love to help you find your way here!
