       <h2>
        Why Pay a Professional to Write Your CV?
       </h2>
       <p>
        Competition for jobs is fierce. The Office for National Statistics (ONS) reports that UK unemployment is set to increase to 5.4% in 2018 (up from 4.7% in 2017). A slow-down in the supply of jobs made worse by a period of uncertainty while post-Brexit negotiations are carried out.  Being able to stand out from potentially hundreds of other applicants is more important than ever. With this increased competition for jobs, a powerful CV that reaches that all-important shortlist is imperative.
       </p>
       <p>
        CV writing is an intimidating task. Your CV is the first impression a recruiter gets of you and is their first chance to assess whether you would be a good fit for the company. Stand out from the crowd and improve your chances of reaching that top 5% with a high-impact CV and cover letter crafted by one of the UK's leading and longest-running CV writing companies.
       </p>

       <h3>
        What Do Our CV Writers Do?
       </h3>
       <p>
        <b>
         Modernise and optimise your CV while quantifying achievements
        </b>
       </p>
       <p>
        When you write your own CV, you risk putting together a document that looks great to you, but might not warrant a second glance from a recruiter. CV writing styles and formats are constantly changing, particularly following the ever-growing use of Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).  Our in-house team are in tune with current best practice.
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         Create compelling and targeted content
        </b>
       </p>
       <p>
        For most people, crafting a CV is a necessary evil that is just part of the process of trying to get a job. This doesn't mean that you will approach CV development with disdain, but it might mean that you won't feel inclined to go a step further and create a document that will grab the reader's attention.
       </p>
       <p>
        Our expert CV writers can prepare a CV that will offer a compelling read.  Since 2001, we have developed in-depth awareness of industry-specific keywords that will get noticed by both scanning software and the keen eyes of a recruiter.
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         Develop an ongoing job search strategy
        </b>
       </p>
       <p>
        Our Consultants work
        <em>
         with
        </em>
        you, rather than just
        <em>
         for
        </em>
        you to improve your CV. By helping you focus on your career objectives, we will create a 'personal sales document' that clearly and concisely states your experience and skills in a manner that highlights you as a value add for the company.
       </p>
       <p>
        We can also help you prepare for an upcoming interview by asking you insightful questions. This will help create a better CV while supporting you in re-framing your experiences and skills in the context of the position for which you are applying.
       </p>
