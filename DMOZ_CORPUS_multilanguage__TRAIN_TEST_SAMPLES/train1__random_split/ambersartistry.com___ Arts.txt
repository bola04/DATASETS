          <h2 class="entry-title">
           <a href="https://ambersartistrycom.wordpress.com/2017/12/03/welcome-back/" rel="bookmark">
            Welcome to Amber’s Artistry
           </a>
          </h2>
         </header>
         <!-- .entry-header -->
         <div class="entry-summary">
          <p>
           An Artist’s Journey
          </p>
         </div>
         <!-- .entry-summary -->
         <div class="entry-content">
          <p>
           Summer Schedule of Mosaic Classes!  Yippee!  Let’s create, my friends…
          </p>
          <header class="entry-header">
           <h1 class="entry-title">
            Classes
           </h1>
          </header>
          <div class="entry-content">
           <p>
            <strong>
             <span style="color:#000080;">
              June 2 &amp; 3
             </span>
             (Sat 9-1:00, Sun 9-11:00)
             <span style="color:#800080;">
              2-D Outdoor Panel Mosaic
             </span>
            </strong>
            – All inclusive $125
           </p>
           <p>
            Welcome to creating mosaic art that will withstand our weather: rain or shine!  In this course we will create a rectangular panel for hanging in an outdoor setting which you will have the choice of either attaching directly to a wall or by a wire loop hanger.  You are always welcome to bring items you would like to include in your mosaic.  This course is the initial step to learn the panel process, however our next panel class will be the next step in complexity, and we will be working on a 3-D creation.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             June 16 &amp; 17 (Sat 9-2:00, Sun 9-11:00)  Mosaic Turtle, Whale, or other shape
            </strong>
            – All Inclusive $65
           </p>
           <p>
            Let’s create a wonderful and fun piece of art to hang in your home!  In this course we will create a mosaic on a shaped substrate good for hanging indoor.  You can make your animal shape as creative and funky or realistic as you want and I will guide you through the fun.  You are always welcome to bring items you would like to include in your mosaic.
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="eileen-lisa-cathy-beverly-julia" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-23" data-attachment-id="23" data-comments-opened="1" data-image-description="" data-image-meta='{"aperture":"0","credit":"","camera":"","caption":"","created_timestamp":"0","copyright":"","focal_length":"0","iso":"0","shutter_speed":"0","title":"","orientation":"1"}' data-image-title="eileen-lisa-cathy-beverly-julia" data-large-file="https://ambersartistrycom.files.wordpress.com/2017/12/eileen-lisa-cathy-beverly-julia.jpg?w=455" data-medium-file="https://ambersartistrycom.files.wordpress.com/2017/12/eileen-lisa-cathy-beverly-julia.jpg?w=300" data-orig-file="https://ambersartistrycom.files.wordpress.com/2017/12/eileen-lisa-cathy-beverly-julia.jpg" data-orig-size="455,178" data-permalink="https://ambersartistrycom.wordpress.com/2017/12/03/welcome-back/eileen-lisa-cathy-beverly-julia/" sizes="(max-width: 455px) 85vw, 455px" src="https://ambersartistrycom.files.wordpress.com/2017/12/eileen-lisa-cathy-beverly-julia.jpg?w=840" srcset="https://ambersartistrycom.files.wordpress.com/2017/12/eileen-lisa-cathy-beverly-julia.jpg 455w, https://ambersartistrycom.files.wordpress.com/2017/12/eileen-lisa-cathy-beverly-julia.jpg?w=150 150w, https://ambersartistrycom.files.wordpress.com/2017/12/eileen-lisa-cathy-beverly-julia.jpg?w=300 300w"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             <span style="color:#000080;">
              June 30 &amp; July 1
             </span>
             (Sat 9-1:00, Sun 9-11:00)
             <span style="color:#800080;">
              Mosaic Lazy Susan or Breakfast-in-Bed Tray
             </span>
            </strong>
            – All Inclusive $75
           </p>
           <p>
            This fun, all-level mosaic class gives each participant a beautiful and functional mosaic creation to bring home!  Come with a design or create one while in class.  We will be creating designs which are flat, functional, and easy to grout.  These trays and lazy susan designs are heat resistant and will be a centerpiece and conversation piece in your home.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             <span style="color:#000080;">
              July 14 &amp; 15
             </span>
             (Sat 9-3:00, Sun 9-11:00)
             <span style="color:#800080;">
             </span>
            </strong>
            <span style="color:#800080;">
             <strong>
              3-D Panel Mosaic
             </strong>
            </span>
            – All Inclusive $145
           </p>
           <p>
            Welcome to creating mosaic art that has dimension!  In this course we will create a rectangular panel with a three-dimensional design.  Ideas can be tea cups to hold succulents or bird seed; abstract, wavy designs; your imagination is your only limit.  Due to the advanced nature of this course, participants will have to  take one of my beginner/intermediate courses.  These will be good for or hanging in a setting which has protection from direct weather or indoor.  Your panel will have a wire hanger unless direct application to a wall is desired.  You are always welcome to bring items you would like to include in your mosaic.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             <span style="color:#000080;">
              August 11 &amp; 12
             </span>
             (Sat 9-2:00, Sun 9-11:00)
             <span style="color:#800080;">
              “Your Choice” (Shape, Panel, Address Number Plaque)
             </span>
            </strong>
            – All Inclusive $75-125
           </p>
           <p>
            This fun, inspirational course allows the participant to choose their project and create a mosaic address number plaque for their home ($85); two medium shapes from my collection ($75); or, create a large garden panel of any 2-D design ($125).  As always, you  are welcome to bring items you wish to include in your design.
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
