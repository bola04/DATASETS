      <h5 class="boldhead red">
       <span style="color: #333333;">
        Planned Maintenance and Service Engineers in Air Conditioning, Refrigeration, Heating &amp; Plumbing, Ventilation, Electrics &amp; Environmental Control. Also Design &amp; Installation Services. Servicing Bristol, South West England &amp; Wales.
       </span>
      </h5>
      <h2 class="line">
       <span style="color: #ff0000;">
        Heating &amp; Air Conditioning Experts Located in Bristol
       </span>
      </h2>
      <p>
       MFM carry out heating and air conditioning service &amp; maintenance on a wide range of properties. These include Commercial Offices, Industrial Manufacturing Sites, Local Authority Buildings, Public and Private Healthcare facilities. We also cover Government Establishments, Laboratories, Schools and Universities, and all types of retail premises, Hotels and Leisure Complexes in Bristol, South West England and Wales.
      </p>
      <p>
       MFM install and maintain air conditioning equipment from a range of reputable manufacturers that include Airedale, Airwell, Carrier, Daikin, Fujitsu, Hitachi, Mitsubishi, Panasonic, Toshiba, and York.
       <span style="color: #ff0000;">
        <strong>
         MFM are approved
        </strong>
       </span>
       <strong>
        <span style="color: #ff0000;">
         Daikin D1+
        </span>
        installers and
        <span style="color: #ff0000;">
         Mitsubishi Business Solution Partners
        </span>
       </strong>
       , which offers our clients extended 7 year parts warranty on installed and serviced equipment.
      </p>
      <p>
       MFM also offer full installation and maintenance capabilities across all types of commercial boilers and heating plant, including all Gas and Oil Boiler installations.
      </p>
      <p>
       MFM’s skilled mobile workforce is trained to carry out servicing on all types of plant and is fully supported by technical supervision and management. All MFM personnel attend regular training courses to keep them up to date with changing technology and current compliance legislation.
      </p>
      <p>
       MFM can provide Resident and Mobile Operations, providing engineering staff at the client’s premises full time, or specialist technicians who carry out periodic scheduled maintenance activities at sites where a full time presence is not required. Maintenance operations are organised and implemented as a flexible multi-trade approach ranging from one manned operation upwards to meet the client’s required service level.
      </p>
