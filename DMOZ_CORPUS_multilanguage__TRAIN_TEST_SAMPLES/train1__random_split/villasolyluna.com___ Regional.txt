                      <p>
                       Picture you and your loved one standing barefoot on an impeccable white sand beach while the turquoise sea laps at your toes. A flower entwined arbor arching above you exudes delicate scents as you join your lives together.
                      </p>
                      <p>
                       The privacy and beauty of Playa del Secreto makes Villa Sol y Luna an ideal place to celebrate your wedding. The villa offers two unique settings: the wonderfully intimate paved patio around the pool or the stretch of white sand that lies within the property, next to the sea.
                      </p>
                      <p>
                       <strong>
                        The villa rental fee with permission to celebrate a wedding within the property is as follows:
                       </strong>
                      </p>
                      <ol>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-size: 12.16px; line-height: 1.3em;">
                         Rental fee, as a location with no overnight staying.. $ 4,800.00 US
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-size: 12.16px; line-height: 1.3em;">
                         Rental fee for 2-nights including the wedding site fee.. $ 6,050.00 US
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-size: 12.16px; line-height: 1.3em;">
                         Rental fee for 3-nights including the wedding site fee.. $ 7,300.00 US
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-size: 12.16px; line-height: 1.3em;">
                         Rental fee for 4-nights including the wedding site fee.. $ 8,550.00 US
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-size: 12.16px; line-height: 1.3em;">
                         Rental fee for 5-nights including the wedding site fee.. $ 9,800.00 US
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-size: 12.16px; line-height: 1.3em;">
                         Rental fee for 6-nights including the wedding site fee.. $ 11,050.00 US
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-size: 12.16px; line-height: 1.3em;">
                         Rental fee for 7-nights including the wedding site fee.. $ 12,300.00 US
                        </span>
                       </li>
                      </ol>
                      <p>
                       <br/>
                       Note:
                      </p>
                      <ul>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-size: 12.16px; line-height: 1.3em;">
                         16 % VAT plus 3% lodging tax.
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-size: 12.16px; line-height: 1.3em;">
                         Maximum 120 guests at the wedding reception
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-size: 12.16px; line-height: 1.3em;">
                         Price includes 20 people or less sleeping at Sol y Luna
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-size: 12.16px; line-height: 1.3em;">
                         Music volume must be lowered to 40 decibels after 1:00 AM
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-size: 12.16px; line-height: 1.3em;">
                         Additional charges may apply when guest hosts wedding rehearsal dinner, social gathering, family reunions and non-sleeping guest are invited to spend the day at Sol y Luna . Cost of day pass is $ 15 US per person , maximum 10 passes per day.
                        </span>
                       </li>
                      </ul>
                      <p>
                       <span style="font-size: 12.16px; line-height: 1.3em;">
                        <br/>
                        We do not organize the wedding but we can provide you with the contact information of excellent wedding consultants who may help you coordinate medical lab tests, hiring a minister, beauty salon service, facials, massages, live music, catering, table and altar set-up, etc to ensure you will fully enjoy such a special moment.
                        <br/>
                       </span>
                       <em style="font-size: 12.16px; line-height: 1.3em;">
                        <br/>
                        For 50 or less people, Villa Sol y Luna offers a wedding package at an attractive rate of $85.00 USD per person consisting of:
                       </em>
                      </p>
                      <ul>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-size: 12.16px; line-height: 1.3em;">
                         Table set-up and flower center pieces (limited to what we have in stock)
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-size: 12.16px; line-height: 1.3em;">
                         Torches
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-size: 12.16px; line-height: 1.3em;">
                         Sound system
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-size: 12.16px; line-height: 1.3em;">
                         Barman, ice and mixtures. Charged separately: liquor, wine and beers
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-size: 12.16px; line-height: 1.3em;">
                         Dinner reception, dishes to choose from the following
                         <a href="http://www.playadelsecretoweddings.com/images/menu-selection.pdf" target="_blank">
                          menu selection
                         </a>
                         or combination.
                        </span>
                       </li>
                      </ul>
                      <p>
                       <span style="font-size: 12.16px; line-height: 1.3em;">
                        <br/>
                        For weddings over 50 guests you can check our
                        <a href="https://mejoresbanquetes.wixsite.com/villasolyluna" target="_blank">
                         available packages
                        </a>
                        provided by Mejores Banquetes catering company.
