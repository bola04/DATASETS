         <h1>
          Welcome to Bickford Health Care Center
         </h1>
         <h2>
          What Makes Us Different
         </h2>
         <p>
          Sometimes smaller is better. The 48-bed Bickford Health Care Center has been providing quality care for its residents for over sixty years. Since our founding in 1952, our caring staff has offered excellent medical services and therapeutic programs in a warm, intimate setting. We’ve made a few changes over the years, but our philosophy remains the same. At Bickford, we believe our dedication and commitment to providing quality care assures every resident receives prompt and attentive care.
         </p>
         <p>
          Complete our
          <a href="http://www.bickfordhcc.com.php53-4.ord1-1.websitetestlink.com/admission-application/" title="Admission Application">
           online Admissions Form
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Our Commitment
          </strong>
          <br/>
          Bickford Health Care Center is a close knit team of health care providers with a commitment to excellence. Bickford employees are dedicated to making Bickford the most respected and highly recognized long-term care facility in North Central Connecticut.
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Commitment to Quality
           </strong>
           <br/>
           Bickford provides the highest level of short term and long-term care. Each employee contributes to treating the facility and the residents with respect and pride.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Commitment to our Residents
           </strong>
           <br/>
           Bickford is flexible in addressing the individual health and social needs of our residents as if they are a member of our own family.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Commitment to our Employees
           </strong>
           <br/>
           Bickford’s employees are a vital resource who are nurtured and challenged. Bickford provides its employees a work environment that is flexible and personally rewarding, and that enables them to achieve both personal and professional goals.
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <em>
           For more information or to arrange an appointment, please call our Admissions Coordinator at 860-623-4351.
          </em>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Bickford Health Care Center is a 501(c)3 nonprofit corporation, managed by Somerset Health Care Management Group
         </p>
