      <h1 class="style13">
       Friars Mill Bed &amp; Breakfast
      </h1>
      Welcome to Friars Mill Bed and Breakfast in Matfield near Tunbridge Wells.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      As one of the newest Bed and Breakfasts in Matfield we offer recently refurbished accommodation combined with a warm, friendly atmosphere.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Friars Mill Bed and Breakfast in Matfield is 7 miles from Tunbridge Wells in the heart of Kent, 'The Garden of England'. Within easy access of London and the South Coast, Friars Mill is the ideal base for exploring the South East.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      We offer a large deluxe double room with en-suite shower room or a superior double room and a single room which can be booked together as a suite to include its own shower room.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      We welcome families and can provide a cot for babies or extra folding beds for children, but regret that as the accommodation is on the second floor, wheelchair access is not possible.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Our Guests can choose to have either a full English Breakfast, sourced from local producers, or a continental breakfast.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Free WiFi internet access is available for all guests.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      We look forward to hearing from you to discuss your requirements.
