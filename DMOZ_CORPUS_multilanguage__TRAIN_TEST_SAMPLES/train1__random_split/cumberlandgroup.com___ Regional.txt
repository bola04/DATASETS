          <h1>
           Welcome to the Cumberland Insurance Group
          </h1>
          <p>
           Whether your insurance needs are business or personal, The Cumberland Insurance Group can help. With hundreds of professional agents representing us, Cumberland has a local independent agent near you. Founded in 1844, Cumberland today protects more than 100,000 policyholders with quality products and services. We hold an A- (Excellent) financial rating from the A.M. Best Company, the insurance industry's leading rating organization, based on our ability to meet our obligations to our policyholders. Cumberland has built customer relationships by meeting these commitments as we have grown, for over 170 years. Throughout New Jersey, Pennsylvania, Delaware, and Maryland we're here to serve our policyholders.
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            Paul J. Ritter III, President &amp; CEO
           </em>
          </p>
