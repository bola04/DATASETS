      <h4>
       About Le' Pam's House of Creole
      </h4>
      <p class="footer-p">
       We serve the best gumbo in north Houston, but we don't stop there.  We figured we might as well make the best Cajun food in Houston, Texas.  Don't believe us?  Come by and try it for yourself.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       You can fill up on all of your favorite Cajun dishes and sides, right here in Houston.  We have got crawfish etouffee, fried catfish, seafood stuffed bell peppers, and so many more. For our sides, try the red beans and rice, stewed okra, dirty rice, and a few other delights.  Please stop by and taste it for yourself.
      </p>
