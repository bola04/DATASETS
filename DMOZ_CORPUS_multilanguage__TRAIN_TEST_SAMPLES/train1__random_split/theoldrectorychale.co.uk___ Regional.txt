       The Old Rectory Chale is the grade II listed Georgian home of Mary &amp; Nigel Coward. It is located in the village of Chale on the Isle of Wight in an area of outstanding natural beauty and the self catering accommodation is located on the ground and first floors offering wonderful views over the nearby downs. The Isle of Wight has many attractions and beaches, all within a short drive. Access to nearby beaches is good with ample parking. The beach at Compton bay has plenty of sand, good surf and two large cars parks it is an easy 20 min. drive from The Old Rectory.
