        </em>

      <p>
       <strong>
        Answer: 2 Timothy 3:16
        <em>
         "All Scripture is 
 				God-breathed and is useful for teaching, rebuking, correcting, and 
 				training in righteousness."
        </em>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       That is just one of the nearly 600 questions and answers that 
 				children memorize in order to compete with each other through the 
 				ministry of Junior Bible Quiz. Because we believe that all the Bible 
 				is inspired of God and is useful for training, we are happy to be a 
 				part of this fun-filled, exciting children's ministry.
      </p>
      <p>
       JBQ stands for Junior Bible Quiz. JBQ = FUN! In a fast-paced race 
 				to the buzzer, two competing teams try to earn as many points as 
 				they can as they answer questions about the Bible and its teachings. 
 				All questions and answers come from the official Bible Fact-Pak. 
 				Competition is limited to elementary-aged children, but anyone - 
 				child or adult - can earn awards in Junior Bible Quiz.
      </p>
