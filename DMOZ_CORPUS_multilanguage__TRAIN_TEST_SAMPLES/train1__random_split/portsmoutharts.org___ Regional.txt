            <h1>
             <a href="http://www.portsmoutharts.org/home/mv-campground-3-houses/" rel="attachment wp-att-1210">
              <img alt="" class="wp-image-1210 aligncenter" height="227" sizes="(max-width: 341px) 100vw, 341px" src="http://www.portsmoutharts.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/MV-campground-3-houses.jpg" srcset="http://www.portsmoutharts.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/MV-campground-3-houses.jpg 4998w, http://www.portsmoutharts.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/MV-campground-3-houses-300x200.jpg 300w, http://www.portsmoutharts.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/MV-campground-3-houses-768x512.jpg 768w, http://www.portsmoutharts.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/MV-campground-3-houses-1024x683.jpg 1024w" width="341"/>
             </a>
             Trip to Martha’s Vineyard on July 31
            </h1>
            <p style="text-align: left;">
             Sail away with us from New Bedford on Tuesday, July 31. Whether you plan to bring some of your art supplies or camera or just want to enjoy the day, we would love to have you join us. We will be leaving on the SeaStreak Fast Ferry from New Bedford at 9 AM and spend the day doing whatever “floats your boat” and return to New Bedford on the 5:45 ferry. the trip across the water takes one hour. We dock at scenic Oak bluffs and public transportation around the island is quite reliable if you want to visit any of the other well-known towns like Edgartown or Vineyard Haven. If you do plan to bring art supplies, be sure you can transport them yourself conveniently in a small duffel bag or backpack.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: left;">
             Anyone interested in carpooling from Portsmouth to the ferry should be at the PAG parking lot by 7:45 AM or contact Ronnie Newman at 401-486-2824 about carpooling from Barrington or Somerset (off rt 195). There is a parking fee of $20 a day. Everyone should plan to be there by 8:30 a.m.
            </p>
           </div>
           <p>
            The cost for the day trip is $80/75 for members. Registration is required by June 24.
           </p>
           <p>
            Please send your check made out to the Portsmouth Arts Guild with accompanying information with your phone #, email address and whether you are an artist or non-artist to: Portsmouth Arts Guild, PO Box 1181, Bristol, RI 02809
           </p>
           <p>
            If you have any questions please contact Suzanne Lewis at workshops@portsmoutharts.org
