                      <a href="http://stonemadkinsale.blogspot.com/2013/09/amazing-wind-chimes.html">
                       Amazing Wind Chimes
                      </a>
                     </h3>
                     <div class="post-header">
                      <div class="post-header-line-1">
                      </div>
                     </div>
                     <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-1943642443304290105" itemprop="description articleBody">
                      <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
                       <div class="center style49" style="font-family: Geneva, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif, 'Bradley Hand ITC'; font-size: 12px; text-align: center;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                        <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-Duxn9YsQG50/Ui3gjHSbVRI/AAAAAAAAAm4/nOFHDNsHwYc/s1600/koshi-group-720.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                         <img border="0" height="266" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-Duxn9YsQG50/Ui3gjHSbVRI/AAAAAAAAAm4/nOFHDNsHwYc/s1600/koshi-group-720.jpg" width="400"/>
                        </a>
                       </div>
                       <div class="center style49" style="font-family: Geneva, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif, 'Bradley Hand ITC'; font-size: 12px; text-align: center;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <div class="center style49" style="font-family: Geneva, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif, 'Bradley Hand ITC'; font-size: 12px; text-align: center;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <div class="center style49" style="font-family: Geneva, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif, 'Bradley Hand ITC'; font-size: 12px; text-align: center;">
                        Our chimes are authentic musical instruments, an original creation of high quality. Hand made in a workshop at the foot hills of the Pyrenees, each chime is the result of meticulous craftsmanship.
                       </div>
                       <div class="center style49" style="font-family: Geneva, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif, 'Bradley Hand ITC'; font-size: 12px; text-align: center;">
                        Eight chords are welded with silver into the metal plate at the base of the resonance tube. Precise tuning creates a play of clear tones that is rich in
                        <b>
                         overtones
                        </b>
                        . The overtones of the shorter chords gradually dominate and become fundamentals, thus forming a circular tone range.
                        <br/>
                        Move the
                        <b>
                         chime
                        </b>
                        gently holding it by its cord: the crystalline relaxing sound may leave you in quiet wonder. Suspended, it will play with the wind and carry you away in an unexpected melody.
                       </div>
                       <div class="center style49" style="font-family: Geneva, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif, 'Bradley Hand ITC'; font-size: 12px; text-align: center;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <div class="center style49" style="font-family: Geneva, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif, 'Bradley Hand ITC'; font-size: 12px; text-align: center;">
                        Chimes can be bought over the phone and can be shipped world wide.
                       </div>
