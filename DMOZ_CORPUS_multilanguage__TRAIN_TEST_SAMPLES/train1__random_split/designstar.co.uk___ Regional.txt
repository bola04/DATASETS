     Welcome to design-star*
    </h1>
    <p class="mntxt">
     Hello, I am Melanie Cook (design-star*) a Weston-super-Mare based web designer, graphic designer and artist. I offer professional responsive website design, logo design, website graphics, graphic design, character design and animation services combined with artistic flair and technical knowledge since 2001.
     <a href="info-designstar.htm">
      Read more about design-star*
     </a>
    </p>
    <h2>
     Website design
    </h2>
    <p class="mntxt">
     If you need a new website for your online profile I can help create your internet presence. There are a variety of website options available and I can tailor your website design / website project to suit your specific requirements. Read more about
     <a href="web-design.htm">
      web design
     </a>
    </p>
    <h2>
     Logo design
    </h2>
    <p class="mntxt">
     Impact is the key to a memorable logo, a graphic that clearly represents you or your company, organisation, services and products.  You can have a custom logo created and designed specifically for you or choose from
     <a href="logo-designs-for-sale.htm">
      logo designs for sale
     </a>
     . Read more about
     <a href="logo-design.htm">
      logo design
     </a>
    </p>
    <h2>
     Animation
    </h2>
    <p class="mntxt">
     Animation can enhance your website visitors experience and help them to remember your website,  products and  services. Website animation can be fun, subtle or eye catching as well as being interactive. Read more about
     <a href="animation.htm">
      web animation
     </a>
    </p>
    <h2>
     Website graphics
    </h2>
    <p class="mntxt">
     Website graphics on your website can help to capture interest and increase visual impact. Demonstrating concepts, promoting you, your company, your organisation, products, services or simply for added entertainment value. Read more about

      web graphics
