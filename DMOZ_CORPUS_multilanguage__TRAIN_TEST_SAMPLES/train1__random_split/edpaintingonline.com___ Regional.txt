            <p id="tworowarticle">
             <a href="/Exterior-Painting/Exterior-Painting-House-Painters.html" title="Exterior Painting">
              <strong>
               Exterior Painting
              </strong>
             </a>
             <br/>
             Is the exterior of your home peeling or fading?  The thoroughness we bring to our exterior painting will insure a finished product that looks great, and lasts!
            </p>
            <p id="tworowarticle2">
             <a href="/Interior-Painting/Interior-Painting-Professional-House-Painters.html" title="Interior Painting">
              <strong>
               Interior Painting
              </strong>
             </a>
             <br/>
             Colorful, neat, and clean. That's how we leave any interior when the job is done. Our priority is to make sure the homeowner is satisfied!
             <br/>
             <br/>
            </p>
            <p id="tworowarticle">
             <a href="/Decorative-Finish/Decorative-Finishes.html" title="Decorative Finishes">
              <strong>
               Decorative Finish
              </strong>
             </a>
             <br/>
             Venetian Plaster and Marbleizing are only a few of the faux finishes our talented decorative
             <strong>
              painters
             </strong>
             can offer you.
            </p>
            <p id="tworowarticle">
             <a href="/Aluminum-Siding-Painting/Aluminum-Siding-Painters.html" title="Aluminum and Vynil siding painting">
              <strong>
               Aluminum and Vinyl Siding Painting
              </strong>
             </a>
             <br/>
             When painting aluminum or vinyl siding, we know that strong adhesion is the key to a durable, handsome finish.
            </p>
            <p id="tworowarticle">
             <a href="/Wallpaper-Removal/Wallpaper-Removal.html" title="Removing Wallpaper">
              <strong>
               Wallpaper Removal
              </strong>
             </a>
             <br/>
             Wallpaper removal often creates dust, and requires further extensive prep before priming and painting. Rest assured my crew is carefully trained to protect the interior of your house while performing such tasks.
            </p>
            <p id="tworowarticle">
             <a href="/Exterior/power-washing-pressure-washing.html" title="Power Washing, Pressure Wash">
              <strong>
               Power Washing
              </strong>
             </a>
             <br/>
             Given that dirt and mildew constantly gather, your house needs to be pressure washed on a yearly basis to get the most out of your current paint job.  Give us a call and we can easily schedule a time to power wash your home.
