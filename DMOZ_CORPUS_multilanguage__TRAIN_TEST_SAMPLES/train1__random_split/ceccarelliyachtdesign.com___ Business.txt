                   <h2 class="heading-v1-Section fg-text-dark ffb-header-1">
                    Giovanni Ceccarelli
                   </h2>
                   <div class="heading-v1-text-content ff-richtext fg-text-dark ffb-paragraph-2">
                    Driven by a great passion for the sea and boats, Giovanni Ceccarelli was first as a racer and then as a yacht designer.
 He is head of the firm of the same name and has worked on big projects.
 He was principal designer for two America’s Cup challenges and engineering manager for the removal of the Costa Concordia.
 His boats have won numerous offshore sailing world championships.
 Ceccarelli Yacht Design today designs both sailing and motor yachts.
