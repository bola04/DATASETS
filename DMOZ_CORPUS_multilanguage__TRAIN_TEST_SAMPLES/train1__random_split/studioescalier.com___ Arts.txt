       <p>
        <b>
         STUDIO ESCALIER
        </b>
        is a private studio art school and international arts colony in France, founded in 2001. Designed and directed by artists for artists, it is dedicated to the
        <b>
         contemporary classical study
        </b>
        of drawing and painting from life.
       </p>
       <p>
        Now offering courses in English in
        <b>
         the heart of Paris
        </b>
        as well as in
        <b>
         Argenton-Chateau (Poitou-Charentes)
        </b>
        , we are an
        <b>
         international point of contact
        </b>
        for professionals, emerging artists and beginning art students too, attracting artists from more than 45 countries and 40 different US states.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Anyone may apply
        </strong>
        , there are no academic or artistic pre-requisites to apply as a student. It is an open admissions process, based mainly on
        <strong>
         portfolio submission
        </strong>
        – a review of artwork drawn or painted from life.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Admission
        </strong>
        to our Intensive Courses and Special Programs in drawing and painting in Argenton-Chateau — and to our various Paris Programs including our Winter at the Louvre 8-week program — is by
        <strong>
         online application only
        </strong>
        . Entry can be competitive.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Online application is required
        </strong>
        for every course, 4 to 6 months before the first day of class.
        <br/>
        <br class=" sb-br"/>
        <br/>
        <b>
         Our student body includes:
        </b>
        career painters, portraitists, illustrators, concept artists, animators, muralists, college art teachers, architects, sculptors, printmakers, graduate students, undergraduate students, serious beginners, and
        <strong>
         people who simply love to draw and paint the human figure
        </strong>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        To see our complete
        <strong>
         schedule of upcoming courses
        </strong>
        and application deadlines, visit the
        <strong>
         <a href="https://studioescalier.com/course-schedule/" target="_blank" title="Course Schedule">
          COURSE SCHEDULE
         </a>
        </strong>
        page.
       </p>
       <p>
        Every program that is
        <b>
         currently accepting applications
        </b>
        can also be found here on the
        <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
         <b>
          HOME
         </b>
        </span>
        page.
       </p>
       <p>
        Please pay close attention to the
        <strong>
         application deadlines
        </strong>
        below, and follow the links:
