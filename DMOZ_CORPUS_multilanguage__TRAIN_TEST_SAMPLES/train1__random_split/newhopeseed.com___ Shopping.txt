                        <font color="#006600" face="Verdana">
                         Welcome to the New Hope Seed 
         Company!
                        </font>
                       </b>
                      </p>
                      <p align="left" class="MsoNormal">
                       <font color="#006600" face="Verdana">
                        New 
         Hope Seeds is a small, family owned and operated organization located in 
         Tennessee. We specialize in open-pollinated and heirloom vegetable seed 
         varieties that are rare and not readily commercially available.
                        <br/>
                        <br/>
                       </font>
                       <font face="Verdana">
                        <b>
                         <map name="FPMap0">
                          <area coords="5, 4, 129, 89" href="field_corn.html" shape="rect" target="_blank">
                          </area>
                          <area coords="135, 5, 256, 85" href="tomato_main.html" shape="rect" target="_blank">

                          <area coords="8, 93, 127, 186" href="watermelon.html" shape="rect" target="_blank">

                          </area>
                         </map>
                         <img align="right" border="0" height="192" src="melons_tomatoes.jpg" style="border: 1px solid #006600" usemap="#FPMap0" width="263"/>
                        </b>
                       </font>
                       <font color="#006600" face="Verdana">
                        Several 
         of the heirloom vegetable seed varieties that we offer are heirlooms from our own 
         ancestors that have been passed down for many generations in our family. 
         We also work to preserve heirloom and rare open pollinated plant 
         varieties by locating, growing, documenting and offering to gardeners 
         and farmers so they can have a chance to experience the 'goodness' of 
         what our ancestors grew and enjoyed. In turn this helps to greatly 
         insure their continued preservation.
                       </font>
                      </p>
                      <p align="left" class="MsoNormal">
                       <font color="#006600" face="Verdana">
                        We are proud to say that 
         all of the seed we offer at this time are grown and produced here on our 
         farm. Our seeds are rare, and hand produced by our family for your 
         garden. We work hard at researching and locating rarer open-pollinated 
         and heirloom varieties of usable and historical merit. Our goal is to 
         provide you with the best possible quality of open-pollinated and 
         heirloom seeds.
                       </font>
                      </p>
                      <p align="left" class="MsoNormal">
                       <font color="#006600" face="Verdana">
                        <map name="FPMap1">
                         <area coords="5, 7, 133, 85" href="tobacco_plants.html" shape="rect" target="_blank">
                         </area>
                         <area coords="139, 6, 255, 85" href="tobacco_seeds.html" shape="rect" target="_blank">
                         </area>
                         <area coords="5, 91, 261, 191" href="tobacco_plant_specials.html" shape="rect" target="_blank">
                         </area>
                        </map>
                        <img align="left" border="0" height="192" src="tobacco.jpg" style="border: 1px solid #006600" usemap="#FPMap1" width="263"/>
                        Our farm and the 
         surrounding land that we cultivate have been in our family for many generations, dating 
         back to the early 1800's. We are located in the rolling hills 
         of Tennessee where we enjoy a reasonably long growing season and fairly 
         mild winters.
                       </font>
                      </p>
                      <p>
                       <font color="#006600" face="Verdana">
                        Our preservation work has led us beyond open-pollinated and 
               heirloom vegetable seeds and encompasses field crops like tobacco 
               as well. Tobacco farming is deeply rooted in our family’s history 
               and was a primary sources of farm income for us as well as our 
               ancestors until only recently. Our ancestors who settled in 
               Virginia around 1630 and later traveled to North Carolina in the 
               early 1700’s are believed to be our earliest ancestors to have 
               grown tobacco.
                       </font>
                      </p>
                      <p>
                       <font color="#006600" face="Verdana">
                        Our family's rich 
             heritage in farming and growing tobacco here in Tennessee began 
             about 1804 when our ancestors moved from North Carolina into 
             Tennessee and settled a few miles from our present day farm 
             location.
