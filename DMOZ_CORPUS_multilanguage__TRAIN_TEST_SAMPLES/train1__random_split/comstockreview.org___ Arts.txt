            <h2 class="entry-title">
             <a href="http://comstockreview.org/homepage/" rel="bookmark" title="Homepage">
              THE CHAPBOOK CONTEST STARTS AUG. 1ST
             </a>
            </h2>
            <div class="entry-content">
             <p style="text-align: center;">
              <span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">
               <strong>
                BETSY ANDERSON
                <br/>
               </strong>
              </span>
              <span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">
               <strong>

               </strong>

             <p>
              <strong>
               The Comstock Review  presents
               <br/>
               The 2018 Comstock Writers Group
              </strong>
              <strong>
               Chapbook Contest
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              Accepting submission from Aug 1st to Oct. 31st (postmark)
             </p>
             <p>
              First Prize: $1,000 plus publication and 50 author’s copies.
              <br/>
              Entry Fee: $30/chapbook (includes copy of winning chapbook)
              <br/>
              Submissions accepted after August 1 — to postmark October 31, 2017
             </p>
             <p>
              FINAL JUDGE:
              <strong>
               Peggy Miller
              </strong>
              (Comstock Review editor)
             </p>
             <p>
              SEND TO:
              <strong>
               The Comstock Review Chapbook Contest, 4956 St. John Drive, Syracuse, NY 13215
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               OR
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               Submit Online
              </strong>
              using the button below:
              <br/>
              <a href="http://comstockreview.submittable.com/submit" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">
               <img alt="submit" border="0" src="http://manager.submittable.com/Public/Images/submittable-submit-button.png"/>
              </a>
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               New Guideline: The poet must NOT have already WON a previous chapbook contest in
              </strong>
              <em>
               <strong>
                The Comstock Review
               </strong>
              </em>
              <strong>
               .
               <br/>
              </strong>
              <strong>
               <br/>
              </strong>
              <strong>
               The Rules:
               <br/>
              </strong>
              1.   Submissions must be unpublished as a collection, but individual poems may have been previously published in journals.
              <br/>
              2.   Will consider simultaneous submissions as long as the poet notifies us immediately upon acceptance elsewhere.
              <br/>
              3.   Submit
              <strong>
               25-34 pages of poetry, single-spaced
              </strong>
              (1 page=38 lines maximum, including spacing between lines;
              <br/>
              poems may run longer than 1 page). Manuscripts either too short or too long will be disqualified.
              <br/>
              4.   Do not count title page, table of contents, acknowledgments, dedication, or bio in the page length.
              <br/>
              5.   Do not send illustrations, photos, or any other graphics attached to the poems.
              <br/>
              6.   Manuscripts should be paginated and secured with a binder clip; no staples or plastic covers.
              <br/>
              7.   Judging is done without any reference to the poet’s name.  All entries are screened by the editors of
              <em>
               The Comstock Review
              </em>
              .
              <br/>
              8.   Include 2 cover pages: 1 with mss title, poet’s name, address, phone number, and e-mail address; second with mss
              <strong>
               title only
              </strong>
              .
              <br/>
              <strong>
               Poet’s name should not appear on poems.
              </strong>
              List acknowledgments on a separate, removable page.
              <br/>
              9.    Include SASE for results only; mss will not be returned.
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               SEND TO: The Comstock Review Chapbook Contest, 4956 St. John Drive, Syracuse, NY 13215
               <br/>
               Final Judge for 2018 will bePeggy Miller
               <br/>
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              Winner is notified in December.
              <br/>
              Winning chapbooks are saddle-stitched, and available at $12.00 per copy.
             </p>
             <p>
              For ordering details, email
              <strong>
               poetry@comstockreview.org.
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               ($25 plus $2.50 online fees for up to 5 poems):
               <br/>
               No names or contact info on poems.  This info MUST be in cover letter.
              </strong>
