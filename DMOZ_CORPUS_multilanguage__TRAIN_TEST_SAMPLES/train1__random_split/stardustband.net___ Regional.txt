         <strong>
          <em>
           <br/>
           STARDUST
          </em>
         </strong>
         <font face="Arial" size="4">
          is the award winning big band which performs music ranging from the Big Band Era to more recent dance music. If you are in Arkansas, Texas, Louisiana, Mississippi, Tennessee, Missouri or Oklahoma and are looking for a big band for your event you need look no further. The Stardust Big Band rehearses weekly for the most part and performs at least once a month so you know you are getting a group that is tried and true, and always ready to perform. Their library of more than 200 songs  includes Swing, Latin, Rock, Waltzes and Ballads, and arrangements from Glenn Miller, Count Basie, Benny Goodman, Duke Ellington and Woody Herman. Plus the band has pop/rock music dating from the 50's into the 2000's. The membership of this dynamic group consists of musicians from many areas of Arkansas, all of whom aspire to the highest level of musicianship and are dedicated to the perpetuation of the "Big Band" sound. Band members have performed with some of the biggest stars of yesteryear including Tommy Dorsey, Stan Kenton, Glenn Miller, Patti Page and more.
          <strong>
           If you want the best big band/swing band/dance band in the area, this is it.
          </strong>
          <hr align="center" width="50%"/>
          Stardust performs once a month in the Crystal Ballroom of the
          <a href="http://www.arlingtonhotel.com" target="_blank">
           Arlington Hotel in Downtown Hot Springs, AR
          </a>
          .
          <strong>
           Admission is $10 per person
          </strong>
          with no dress code. Most show up in casual or dress casual attire, however feel free to enjoy the time in your best evening wear.
          <a href="http://www.fastnez.net/schedule/" target="_blank">
           See our schedule page for dates and times.
          </a>
          <br/>
          <font size="+1">
           Come to dance or come to listen, you'll be there for a piece of history. Be a part of the music upon which countless memories are built.
           <br/>
          </font>
          <br/>
          <hr align="center" width="50%"/>
          Stardust specializes in music for dances and special occasions. If you are interested in music for concerts, festivals or other events, we recommend our sister group,
          <a href="http://www.arkansasjazzorchestra.com" target="_blank">
           The Arkansas Jazz Orchestra.
          </a>
         </font>
