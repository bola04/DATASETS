          <h1>
           Your Holistic Dental Experience
          </h1>
          <h2>
           Holistic and Functional Dentistry in Escondido, CA
          </h2>
          <p>
           Welcome to fresh flower arrangements, essential oils, hot beverages, scented candles and a truly caring dental team. This is unlike any dental experience you’ve ever had before. That’s because we focus on making each one of your dental visits comfortable and relaxed in every possible way – in a beautiful spa-like environment.
          </p>
          <p>
           Dr. Kerbs holistic philosophy is to approach your dental health from a whole body perspective, looking at the causes to dental problems and finding the solutions to find answers that are long lasting. Dr. Kerbs looks at dentistry from a functional perspective, he looks at nutrition, muscles, bones, joints, soft tissues and teeth, as they all function as a unit. Dr. Kerbs' goal is to provide comprehensive care using the most minimally invasive techniques in order to preserve your natural teeth. Dr. Kerbs practices dentistry using the latest techniques and technology available including the FOTONA laser, Ozone therapy, Dr. Kerbs is a metal free dentist who is IAOMT certified, he uses only the most bio-compatible materials, BPA free, fluoride free and uses every precaution to ensure the safest dental experience possible.
          </p>
          <p>
           Dr. Jeffry S. Kerbs and his dental hygiene team have been trained to provide gentle care with a soft touch. One of our main goals is to minimize your discomfort from treatment. Since we believe in open communication, we will listen to your concerns and answer your questions. Going to the dentist should be a pleasant experience where you feel relaxed and comfortable throughout.
          </p>
          <p>
           We want you to feel heard and understood, so we will always give you clear and honest answers. Together, we can come up with a great plan to accomplish what you desire for your oral health and the beauty of your smile.
          </p>
          <p>
           We also offer complimentary nitrous oxide sedation, warm blankets, massage dental chairs, noise-canceling headphones with your choice of music, warm neck wraps, dark eye shields and paraffin wax hand treatments. Our goal is to make your dental visit one that you enjoy and look forward to repeating.
          </p>
          <p>
           Our well-designed, soothing environment and nurturing team create a custom experience for all whom we are privileged to serve. The care and comfort of our guests, instilling a sense of well-being and fulfilling even your unspoken wishes, is our highest mission – the goal for every experience with Dr. Kerbs.
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            Experience spa-like dentistry with Jeffry Kerbs, DDS. Call us today at 760-746-3663.
           </em>
          </p>
