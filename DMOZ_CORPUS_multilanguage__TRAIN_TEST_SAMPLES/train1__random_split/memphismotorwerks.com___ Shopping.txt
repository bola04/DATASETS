      <p>
       We have been in the auto service business since 1985 and started our own in-house automotive shop in 1998. In every metropolitan area, one automotive service facility stands out above all others; one that has a passion and dedication to superior service for your fine automobile. In the Memphis area, Memphis Motor Werks is that facility!
      </p>
      <p>
       As a recognized dealer alternative for service, all our technicians are ASE certified and 4 of our 5 technicians have attained the level of Certified Master Technician; in addition our technicians all have factory training. We also use genuine or original manufacturer parts, with a large inventory and over-night procurement. We can even email you estimates, invoices and pictures of needed work before the work is performed.
      </p>
