             <h1 class="page-title" itemprop="headline">
              The Change Your Business or Community Needs To GROW!
             </h1>
            </header>
            <section class="entry-content cf" itemprop="articleBody">
             <p>
              The
              <strong>
               Cumberland Empowerment Zone Corporation
              </strong>
              is serious about helping local businesses and communities thrive.
             </p>
             <p>
              Since its inception in 1999, the
              <strong>
               Cumberland Empowerment Zone Corporation has helped attract more than $270 million in public and private funding for the advancement of companies and their employees
              </strong>
              and for the preservation of communities and their neighborhoods.
             </p>
             <p>
              By supporting local businesses and economic development projects directly with initial investments now surpassing more than $27 million, the
              <strong>
               Cumberland Empowerment Zone Corporation
              </strong>
              program continues to be recognized as one of the most successful Empowerment Zones in the nation.
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               To see a map depicting the actual boundaries of the Cumberland Empowerment Zone,
               <a href="http://cezcorp.org/the-zone/">
                click here
