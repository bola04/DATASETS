           </h1>
           <p>
            At SUN Tech we offer career and technical education that gives students the tools they need to succeed in the competitive world of work.  Participating school districts include
            <a href="http://www.lasd.us/" rel="nofollow" title="Lewisburg Area School District">
             Lewisburg
            </a>
            ,
            <a href="http://www.mwsd.cc/" rel="nofollow" title="Midd-West School District">
             Midd-West
            </a>
            ,
            <a href="http://www.mifflinburg.org/" rel="nofollow" title="Mifflinburg Area School District">
             Mifflinburg
            </a>
            ,
            <a href="http://www.seal-pa.org/" rel="nofollow" title="Selinsgrove Area School District">
             Selinsgrove
            </a>
            , and
            <a href="http://www.shikbraves.org/" rel="nofollow" title="Shikellamy School District">
             Shikellamy
            </a>
            .  We also accept students from neighboring districts.  Educational opportunities for adult learners are available as full time day programs or evening courses.  Contact our office for more information.
           </p>
           <p>
            Students who train for a career at SUN Tech know they’re leaving with the
            <em>
             skills and experience
            </em>
            they need to thrive in the working world.
            <strong>
             And employers know it too:
            </strong>
           </p>
           <blockquote>
            <p>
             The students of SUN Tech are eager to learn and adaptable with their training in order to meet the staffing needs of our company.  –  Randy Beiler, General Manager, Pik Rite Inc.
            </p>
           </blockquote>

           <h2>
            Whether Work or College Bound…
           </h2>
           <p>
            Our students train using the latest techniques and cutting-edge, industry standard equipment. SUN Tech students can earn
            <a href="http://www.sun-tech.org/certifications/">
             certifications
            </a>
            which not only boost your résumé but instantly tell employers you’re qualified.
           </p>
           <p>
            Students can also gain valuable on-the-job experience (not to mention earn money) through our
            <a href="http://www.sun-tech.org/students/why-attend/co-op/">
             Co-op program
            </a>
            . Many employers are so impressed with our students that they choose to keep them after their co-op position ends!
           </p>
           <p>
            But SUN Tech isn’t
            <i>
             just
            </i>
            for students who want to go straight to work: a year at SUN Tech is the perfect stepping-stone into
            <a href="http://www.sun-tech.org/students/why-attend/higher-ed/">
             higher education
            </a>
            .  Each of our 19 full-time programs can earn you college credits transferable to a variety of state universities.
           </p>
