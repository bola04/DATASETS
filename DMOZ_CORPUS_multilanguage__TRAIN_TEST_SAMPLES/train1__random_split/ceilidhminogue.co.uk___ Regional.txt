     <h1>
      Ceilidh Minogue - Scottish ceilidh band
     </h1>
     <p align="center" class="intro">
      The band is one of Scotland's best loved ceilidh bands, and features accordion, fiddle, drums and piano. It never fails to make ceilidh, wedding, civil partnership or corporate event a success. 
 The ceilidh band is based in Scotland but plays all over the UK covering cities from Edinburgh and Glasgow  down to London and Manchester as well as abroad. (Most recently Germany, France, Indonesia, Tunisia, Lithuania, Belgium, Azerbaijan, Bahrain)
      <br/>
      We appeared 'live' on the
      <strong>
       BBC Radio Scotland 'Hogmanay' show
      </strong>
      for  8 years from 2008 to 2016. We recently played at the Rudolstadt, Orkney, Newcastleton, Perthshire Amber and Tynemouth Festivals and have been featured in TV dramas like BBC's Monarch of the Glen and STV's Heartless !
      <br/>
      The band was first put together in 1995 and we can provide a 3, 4 or 5 piece line-ups depending on what your wedding, function, civil partnership or budget dictates and we have of course our 'ceilidh horn section'. (For more details on personnel see our
      <a href="lineup.htm">
       line-up page
      </a>
      .)
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Our callers can 'call' all the dances if you wish (see the
      <a href="dances.html">
       ' dances' page
      </a>
      for dance instructions) and we can also provide some disco or background music during intervals.		
 		We can source jazz trios, piano players and other musicians such as the  or the 'Moore 4 Saxophone Quartet' to play at your drinks receptions or during meals, or a piper to play at your wedding. We can even compose original music specially for you on your wedding day.
      <br/>
      You can find out more
      <a href="about.html">
       about the ceilidh band
      </a>
      <a id="about_the_band" name="about_the_band">
      </a>
      and listen to the sound samples from both our albums in the
      <a href="music.html">
       music page
      </a>
      or
      <a href="contact.html">
       Contact us
      </a>
      <a id="contact_the_ceilidh_band" name="contact_the_ceilidh_band">
      </a>
      for more details or a quote.
      <br/>
      We also have £10m Public Liability Insurance and all electrical equipment is PAT tested.
