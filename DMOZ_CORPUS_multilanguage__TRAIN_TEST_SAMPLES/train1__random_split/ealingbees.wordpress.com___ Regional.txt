        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          Ealing and District Beekeepers Association (EDBKA) is a group of experienced beekeepers.
         </p>
         <p>
          We meet on Saturday afternoons at our apiary in Ealing to talk about keeping bees. We run a
          <a href="https://ealingbees.wordpress.com/courses/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">
           beekeeping course for beginners
          </a>
          and hold various events throughout the year.
         </p>
         <p>
          EDBKA is a member of the Federation of Middlesex Beekeepers’ Association and affiliated to the
          <a href="http://www.bbka.org.uk/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">
           British Beekeepers Association (BBKA)
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          If you would like to find out more, please
          <a href="https://ealingbees.wordpress.com/contact-us/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">
           contact us
          </a>
          and subscribe to our website
          <a href="https://ealingbees.wordpress.com/news-2/">
           news
          </a>
          using the buttons below.
         </p>
