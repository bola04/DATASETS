 				James Chambers and Christopher Mills to publish the comic book series
        <i>
         Shadow
 				House
        </i>
        .Each issue featured the ongoing creator-owned features "Nightmark" by
 				Mills and artist Fred Harper and "The Revenant" by Chambers and artist Pat
 				Broderick. The series ran for five issues.
