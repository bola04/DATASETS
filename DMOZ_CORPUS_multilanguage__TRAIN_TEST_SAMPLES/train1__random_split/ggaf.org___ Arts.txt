        DETAILS
       </div>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Nov. 2, 3, &amp; 4, 2018
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Location:
        </strong>
        Seville Square, Pensacola, Florida
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Hours:
        </strong>
        9am - 5pm on Friday and Saturday, 9am – 4pm on Sunday
        <br/>
        Children’s Festival: Saturday &amp; Sunday 10am – 4pm.
       </p>
       <p>
        Admission to the festival is free. Pets are not allowed at the Festival.
        <a href="http://www.ggaf.org/page/visiting-the-festival">
         More Details...
        </a>
       </p>
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="row">
      <div class="four columns">
       <p>
        The Great Gulfcoast Arts Festival is one of the best-regarded, most  popular arts festivals in the United States. The three-day, juried art  show draws more than 200 of the nation's best painters, potters,  sculptors, jewelers, graphic artists, craftsmen and other artists. The Festival is ranked #30 in the nation by
        <em>
         Sunshine Artist
        </em>
        Magazine.
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="four columns">
       <p>
        <strong>
         Live musicians
        </strong>
        hit the right notes from the Main Stage with sounds  ranging from bluegrass to Cajun and blues to jazz and classical.
       </p>
       <p>
        Performing companies, dance schools, community groups, and area folk groups have their time to shine.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Craft artists
        </strong>
        demonstrate traditional arts, crafts and  trades,  including blacksmithing, engraving, spinning, weaving and other skills  in the Heritage Arts Area.
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="four columns">
       <p>
        Children become artists at the
        <strong>
         Children's Arts Festival
        </strong>
        with numerous hands-on art activities. All children's art activities  are free, fun, and geared toward children toddler age and older. The
        <strong>
         Student Art Show
        </strong>
        showcases the talents of both private and public school students of Escambia and Santa Rosa Counties. Over 2,000 pieces of art from elementary, middle and high school students will be on display.
       </p>
       <p>
        From traditional treats at the Heritage Festival to local favorites and regional festival fare, there are
        <strong>
         delicious dining and drink
        </strong>
        choices for every taste at the  Festival!
       </p>
      </div>
     </div>

       <div class="four columns">
        <h4>
         <a href="http://www.ggaf.org/news/heritage-arts-application-2018">
          Call for entries for 2018 Heritage Artists »
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Applications are open for the Heritage Arts area of the Festival. The area is always an extremely popular area offering learning and sharing experiences combined with the unique artistry of objects crafted as they were in times past. Located in the Pensacola Historical Village, GGAF Heritage Arts area is nestled along Zaragosa Street and on the grounds of historical buildings.
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="four columns">
        <h4>
         <a href="http://www.ggaf.org/news/emerging-artists-2018">
          2018 Emerging Artists Announced »
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         The Great Gulfcoast Arts Festival, in partnership with Artel Gallery, announce the artists selected for the Emerging Artists grant program.
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="four columns">
        <h4>
         <a href="http://www.ggaf.org/page/emerging-artists-grants/" target="_self">
          2018 Emerging Artists Applications Open »
         </a>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Applications are open for the GGAF Emerging Artists program. This program seeks artists who have limited experience participating in arts festivals to give them guidance in what is involved in being in an arts festival. Two artists will be chosen to participate in this year’s festival. GGAF will waive fees for the two chosen artists and will provide a mentor to help guide them through the process.

          Details…
         </a>
        </p>

       <h3>
        Poster Design Competition
       </h3>
       <p>
        Each year, the Great Gulfcoast Arts Festival sponsors a design competition to select the official GGAF poster. The design is turned into a limited-edition poster, t-shirts, and other items which will be available for sale at the festival. The poster and shirts have become local collector’s items. The only way to get them is at the Festival. A design by Daniela de Castro Sucre was selected for the Festival Poster for 2017.
       </p>
       <p>
        Creative artists are invited to submit their designs for the 2018 competition. The winning artist receives a $1,000 cash award.
       </p>
