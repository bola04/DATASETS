                Chichester Hockey Club currently has an average yearly membership exceeding 300, comprising playing and social members together with their families. Each Saturday between September and April the Club fields 7 mens teams, 4 ladies teams and 3 junior development teams playing league hockey and U8s, U10s &amp; U12s teams playing tournament hockey. During the week we provide a full coaching programme from Under 8s through to the Ladies and Mens 1st XIs.
