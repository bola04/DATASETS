         <h3>
          <span style="color: #008080;">
           <strong>
            ABOUT THE BICYCLE TOUR NETWORK
           </strong>
          </span>
         </h3>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           The
           <strong>
            BICYCLE TOUR NETWORK
           </strong>
           (BTN) collectively represents hundreds of recreational bicycle tours, events and destinations throughout the world.  From multi-day bicycle events to one-day century rides, from small boutique cycling tours to weekend cycling festivals and from scenic biking destinations to advocacy events the BTN provides a service that matches touring cyclists with the best tours available in the world.
          </span>
         </p>
         <h3>
          <span style="color: #008080;">
           <strong>
            2018 NATIONAL BICYCLE TOURISM CONFERENCE
           </strong>
          </span>
         </h3>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           Annually, the
           <strong>
            BICYCLE TOUR NETWORK
           </strong>
           holds a national conference that allows members to meet and network with each other bicycle event and tourism professionals .  The conference includes lectures, interactive sessions, group discussions and breakouts with a variety of topics related to the successful and safe administration of cycling events and promoting bicycle tourism.  The next
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            <strong>
             <a href="http://www.bicycletournetwork.org/" style="color: #000000; text-decoration: underline;">
              NATIONAL BICYCLE TOURISM CONFERENCE
             </a>
            </strong>
           </span>
           will be held in San Antonio, Texas on November 7-10, 2018.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://biketournetwork.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/room-23334173_10155930024608866_2616558330198358622_o.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="alignnone wp-image-3585" height="429" sizes="(max-width: 900px) 100vw, 900px" src="http://biketournetwork.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/room-23334173_10155930024608866_2616558330198358622_o.jpg" srcset="http://biketournetwork.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/room-23334173_10155930024608866_2616558330198358622_o.jpg 1344w, http://biketournetwork.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/room-23334173_10155930024608866_2616558330198358622_o-300x143.jpg 300w, http://biketournetwork.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/room-23334173_10155930024608866_2616558330198358622_o-768x366.jpg 768w, http://biketournetwork.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/room-23334173_10155930024608866_2616558330198358622_o-1024x488.jpg 1024w, http://biketournetwork.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/room-23334173_10155930024608866_2616558330198358622_o-676x322.jpg 676w" width="900"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           <a href="http://www.bicycletournetwork.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/RFP-2018-NATIONAL-BICYCLE-TOURISM-CONFERENCE.pdf" style="color: #000000;">
            RFP for the 2018 National Bicycle Tourism Conference
           </a>
           is Now Available for potential speakers, presenters, panelists and workshops.  To view and download the PDF, click the image below.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.bicycletournetwork.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/RFP-2018-NATIONAL-BICYCLE-TOURISM-CONFERENCE.pdf">
           <img alt="" class="alignnone wp-image-2474" height="485" src="http://www.bicycletournetwork.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/RFP-Image.png" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://biketournetwork.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/Smoky-Mtns-1.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-1949">
           <img alt="" class="alignnone wp-image-3581" height="411" sizes="(max-width: 900px) 100vw, 900px" src="http://biketournetwork.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/Smoky-Mtns-1.jpg" srcset="http://biketournetwork.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/Smoky-Mtns-1.jpg 1750w, http://biketournetwork.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/Smoky-Mtns-1-300x137.jpg 300w, http://biketournetwork.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/Smoky-Mtns-1-768x351.jpg 768w, http://biketournetwork.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/Smoky-Mtns-1-1024x468.jpg 1024w, http://biketournetwork.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/Smoky-Mtns-1-676x309.jpg 676w" width="900"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           The BTN was formerly known as the National Bicycle Tour Directors Association and was formed over 25 years ago by a small core of ride directors for professional development, marketing and networking purposes. It has grown to include more than 100 organizations that direct nearly 350 bicycle rides and tours across North America. The primary purpose of BTN is to bring together the directors, coordinators, and organizers of multi-day bicycle tours throughout North America, including Canada and Mexico, Europe, and Asia to share ideas, learn ways to improve their events and promote responsible cycling.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://biketournetwork.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/Nebraska-19420597_10211570324605100_4409401381411996828_n.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-1951">
           <img alt="" class="alignnone wp-image-3583" height="675" sizes="(max-width: 900px) 100vw, 900px" src="http://biketournetwork.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/Nebraska-19420597_10211570324605100_4409401381411996828_n.jpg" srcset="http://biketournetwork.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/Nebraska-19420597_10211570324605100_4409401381411996828_n.jpg 960w, http://biketournetwork.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/Nebraska-19420597_10211570324605100_4409401381411996828_n-300x225.jpg 300w, http://biketournetwork.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/Nebraska-19420597_10211570324605100_4409401381411996828_n-768x576.jpg 768w, http://biketournetwork.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/Nebraska-19420597_10211570324605100_4409401381411996828_n-676x507.jpg 676w" width="900"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           When you choose a ride on the
           <strong>
            BICYCLE TOUR NETWORK
           </strong>
           , you know the event is run by organizers who follow the highest standards in the industry. Through the BTN, we share best practices, create benchmarks of quality, and work together to advance bicycling everywhere. Whether it’s a commercial touring company, a nonprofit organization or a fundraising ride, trust that the event you choose through the BTN will provide a great cycling adventure!
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           <a href="http://biketournetwork.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/horsey-13528337_997519157032765_7693207854912485628_o.jpg">
