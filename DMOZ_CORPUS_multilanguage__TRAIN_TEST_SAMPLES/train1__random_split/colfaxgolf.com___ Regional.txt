         <div class="entry-content">
          <p>
           The Colfax Golf Course is a 9-hole layout about 1-mile north of downtown Colfax, WA nestled along the Palouse River.  We are a community-oriented facility catering to all levels of players and people who want a low-key environment for an enjoyable round of golf.  There is a full-service clubhouse with a pro-shop and bar combined with a friendly staff to make your visit a fun experience.
          </p>
