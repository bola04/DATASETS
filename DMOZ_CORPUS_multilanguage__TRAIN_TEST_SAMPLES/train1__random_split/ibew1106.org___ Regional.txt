          MIFA I / Frontier Tentative Agreement Ratified
         </a>
        </h2>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <a href="http://ibew1106.org/2018/07/17/mifa-i-frontier-tentative-agreement-ratified/">

           </span>
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>
        <!-- .entry-meta -->
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          The voting in Michigan, North Carolina, Ohio, South Carolina, and the Buried Service Wire Group has been completed.
         </p>
         <p>
          The collective membership vote in MIFA I voted to approve the tentative agreement. Frontier was notified today, July 13, 2018, that the tentative agreement has been ratified by the membership working under the MIFA I collective bargaining agreement.
         </p>
         <p>
          The Business Managers in MIFA I will be working with Frontier to add the changes into the MIFA I agreement and the appropriate Local Union appendices. Upon the completion of proof reading the final draft, the agreement will be signed and printed for distribution to the membership as soon as possible.
         </p>
         <p>
          We appreciate the memberships support throughout negotiations!
         </p>
         <p>
          Fraternally,
          <br/>
          IBEW Negotiating Team
          <br/>
          IBEW Local Union 71-OH, 289-NC, 1106-MI, 1431-SC
         </p>

        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          The Bargaining Committee is making a recommendation to vote in favor of the Tentative Agreement. The vote will be a collective vote among the 4 Local Unions in MIFA I.
         </p>
         <p>
          Make sure you attend one of these ratification meetings so your vote counts in Michigan.
         </p>
         <p>
          All up to date dues paying Members that work for Frontier will be allowed to attend and vote. The new changes will be read prior to voting. The ballots will be counted at each meeting and recorded.
