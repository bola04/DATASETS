                 <h2 itemprop="name">
                  <a href="/about-helen/latest-news-helen/320-special-called-meeting-2.html" itemprop="url">
                   Special Called Meeting

                 </h2>
                 <dl class="article-info">

                 <p>
                  The City of Helen announces a Special Called Meeting will be held July 31, 2018 at 10:00 a.m. at City Hall, 25 Alpenrosen Strasse. Topic to be discussed will be the setting of the millage rate for 2018 property taxes. For questions, please call 706-878-2733.

                 <p>

                  <a href="/images/helencity/finance/5_Year_History_of_Levy_2018-2.pdf" target="_blank">

                 <h2 itemprop="name">
                  <a href="/about-helen/latest-news-helen/317-helen-is-open.html" itemprop="url">
                   Helen is Open
                  </a>
                 </h2>
                 <dl class="article-info">
                  <dt class="article-info-term">
                   Details
                  </dt>
                  <dd class="category-name">
                   Category:
                   <span itemprop="genre">
                    News
                   </span>
                  </dd>
                  <dd class="modified">
                   <time datetime="2018-06-15T09:10:40-04:00" itemprop="dateModified">
                    Last Updated: Friday, 15 June 2018 09:10
                   </time>
                  </dd>
                 </dl>
                 <p>
                  The City has had numerous calls asking if the City is opened for business. We are happy to announce that the majority of the town was unaffected by the recent flooding of the Chattahoochee River. The river is inside its banks again, and everyone has done a tremendous job cleaning up the affected areas. All roads within the City are currently open including Georgia Highway 75/17 which runs from Helen to Hiawassee. The downtown district was unaffected by the high waters, and businesses are opened and ready for tourist. Others have called concerning the news reports about high bacteria levels in the Chattahoochee River. Please know that these warnings are NOT for the Helen area. The Chattahoochee River in Helen is not experiencing high bacteria levels, and the water is safe for play. We will continue to keep you posted as more information is available. Again, the City and the Chattahoochee River are open and ready for you to come and enjoy them.
                 </p>

                 <h2 itemprop="name">
                  <a href="/about-helen/latest-news-helen/306-time-change-for-commission-and-pdrb-meetings.html" itemprop="url">
                   Time Change for Commission and PDRB Meetings
                  </a>
                 </h2>
                 <dl class="article-info">
                  <dt class="article-info-term">
                   Details
                  </dt>
                  <dd class="category-name">
                   Category:
                   <span itemprop="genre">
                    News
                   </span>
                  </dd>
                  <dd class="modified">
                   <time datetime="2018-03-23T09:23:56-04:00" itemprop="dateModified">
                    Last Updated: Friday, 23 March 2018 09:23
                   </time>
                  </dd>
                 </dl>
                 <div>
                  On March 20, 2018, the Helen City Commission voted to change the Commission Meetings from the first and third Tuesday of the month to a single meeting on the second Tuesday of the month at 10:00 a.m. in the Commission Chambers located at 25 Alpenrosen Strasse.
                 </div>
                 <div>
                  Commission Meetings will be the following dates: April 10, 2018, May 8, 2019, June 12, 2018, July 10, 2018, August 14, 2018, September 11, 2018, October 9, 2018, November 13, 2018, and December 11, 2018.
                 </div>
                 <div>
                  Starting April 1, the Planning Design Review Board Meetings (PDRB) will be moved to the first and third Tuesdays of each month at 9:00 a.m. in the Commission Chambers at City Hall. All Sign and Special Event Permit paperwork will need to be turned into City Hall by the Wednesday prior to the meeting.
                 </div>
                 <div>
                  If you have any questions, please call City Hall at 706-878-2733, Monday through Friday from 8:30 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.
                 </div>
