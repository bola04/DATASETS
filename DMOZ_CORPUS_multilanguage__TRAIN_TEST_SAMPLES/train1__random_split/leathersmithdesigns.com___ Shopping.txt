Our vegetable tanned tooling hides which are selected for top grade are custom finished to our specifications for a smooth even thickness throughout the cowhide.  We stock the cowhide bends, which are the best part of the hide for making belts and other leather accessories.  Besides garment leather we also stock goat skin for wallet interiors and lining.
