         Welcome to Seideman Accounting Group.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Established in 1968, Seideman Accounting Group is a full service San Francisco Bay Area accounting firm. 
 We have 2 offices located in San Mateo and Dublin, California.
 Seideman Accounting Group enjoys lasting relationships based on professional competence and trust because 
 we provide services that are accurate, timely, and informative. 
 Our firm employs the latest in advanced technology and our staff is comprised of highly qualified professionals 
 with a wide variety of experience including domestic and international tax issues.

 accounting/bookkeeping

 international tax assistance.
