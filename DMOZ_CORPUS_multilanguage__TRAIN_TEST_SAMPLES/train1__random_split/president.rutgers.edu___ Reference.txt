        <h1 class="title page-title hidden">
         Office of the President
        </h1>
        <div class="block block-platform" id="block-platform-homequeue-maincontent">
         <div class="content">
          <div about="/content/hprecenthighlightsmain" class="node node-element has-text no-media clearfix" id="node-338" typeof="sioc:Item foaf:Document">
           <div class="content">
            <div class="field field-name-field-header field-type-text field-label-hidden">
             <div class="field-items">
              <div class="field-item even">
               <p>
                Recent Highlights
               </p>
              </div>
             </div>
            </div>
            <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
             <div class="field-items">
              <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
               <div style="display:block;clear:both">
                <p>
                 <img alt="President Barchi and Governor Murphy" class="inline-left" src="/sites/president/files/NR18GovMurphy100Day9611_adj_sm_0.png" title="President Barchi and Governor Murphy" typeof="foaf:Image"/>
                </p>
                <p>
                 <strong>
                  <a href="https://magazine.rutgers.edu/letters/rutgers-delivers">
                   Rutgers Delivers
                  </a>
                 </strong>
                 <br/>
                 <em>
                  (Rutgers Magazine, Spring 2018)
                 </em>
                </p>
                <p>
                 By partnering with the state, Rutgers is fully embracing its role in advancing New Jersey’s prosperity.
                </p>
                <p>
                </p>
                <p>
                 <img alt="Students at Rutgers University Commencement 2018" class="inline-left" src="/sites/president/files/RG18UnivCommencementC0796_sm.png" title="Students at Rutgers University Commencement 2018" typeof="foaf:Image"/>
                </p>
                <p>
                 <strong>
                  <a href="/public-remarks/speeches-and-writings/greetings-university-commencement-2018">
                   President Salutes Rutgers Class of 2018
                  </a>
                 </strong>
                 <br/>
                 <em>
                  (May 13, 2018)
                 </em>
                </p>
                <p>
                 President Barchi told graduates that "the world you are entering is a complicated mix of endless possibilities and seemingly intractable problems. But here’s where you come in."
                </p>
                <p>
                 <img alt="Rabbi Francine Roston talking to the Miller Center benefactor Paul Miller" class="inline-left" src="/sites/president/files/RG18FightHateSympos0444_th.png" title="Rabbi Francine Roston talking to the Miller Center benefactor Paul Miller" typeof="foaf:Image"/>
                </p>
                <p>
                 <strong>
                  <a href="/fighting-hate-while-preserving-freedom-best-practices-forum">
                   Fighting Hate While Preserving Freedom
                  </a>
                 </strong>
                 <br/>
                 <em>
                  (March 27, 2018)
                 </em>
                </p>
                <p>
                 Rutgers presented a daylong symposium, open to all, focused on best practices in confronting hate at houses of worship, online, on campus, and in the broader community.
                </p>
                <p>
                 <img alt="Hand holding a &amp;quot;Nation for Dreamers&amp;quot; sign" class="inline-left" src="/sites/president/files/ss710783152_sb.png" title="Hand holding a &amp;quot;Nation for Dreamers&amp;quot; sign" typeof="foaf:Image"/>
                </p>
                <p>
                 <a href="/public-remarks/letters/one-final-opportunity-advocate-dreamers">
                  <strong>
                   One Final Opportunity to Advocate for Dreamers
                  </strong>
                 </a>
                 <br/>
                 <em>
                  (January 29, 2018)
                 </em>
                </p>
                <p>
                 Congress will be considering DACA over the coming weeks, and Democrats and Republicans are putting aside their differences and seem genuinely poised to take action. We can make sure that happens if we act quickly.
                </p>
               </div>
              </div>
             </div>
            </div>
           </div>
