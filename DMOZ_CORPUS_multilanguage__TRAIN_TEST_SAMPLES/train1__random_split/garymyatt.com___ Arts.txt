             <p>
              Gary Myatt is an artist who paints figurative, painterly canvases and specializes in original, hand-painted murals and trompe l’oeil artwork that has, over the years, complemented a wide variety of decorative schemes.
             </p>
             <p>
              Gary studied Fine Art to postgraduate level at three highly respected art schools in London and now draws from that wealth of experience and acquired knowledge in producing masterly canvases, accomplished murals, and very convincing trompe l’oeil that is capable of withstanding close scrutiny.
             </p>
             <p>
              During his 20 years of professional experience, producing studio-based canvases, and working as both a mural artist and trompe l’oeil artist, Gary has built up an extensive portfolio, including commissions for commercial and residential projects.
             </p>
             <p>
              Gary Myatt is based in London but works throughout the UK and internationally, having undertaken projects throughout Europe, Middle East and India.
             </p>

His work has included a number of large and elaborate, decorative murals and specialist paint effects. I would have no hesitation in recommending him for similar projects and he will always be my first choice for such work.’
