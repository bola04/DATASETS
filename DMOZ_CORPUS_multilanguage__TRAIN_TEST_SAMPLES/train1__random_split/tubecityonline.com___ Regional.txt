      <h2>
       About Us
      </h2>
      <p>
       Tube City Community Media Inc. is a non-profit corporation, chartered in the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania.
      </p>
      <p>
       We encourage McKeesport-area residents, both past and future, both young and old, to become not just consumers, but also community activists and creators of content about the McKeesport area in text, audio, and video form.
      </p>
      <p>
       Tube City Community Media is volunteer driven! We encourage your participation. You can write for the website, host your own radio show, or try your hand at a podcast.
      </p>
      <p>
       And if you have a community announcement or some “good news” to share, and it’s relevant to the McKeesport, Duquesne, Glassport or White Oak areas, we’d like to help you share it.
      </p>
