       <center>
        <h1>
         IMA5 - 5th Conference of the International Marangoni Association
        </h1>
        <font size="+2">
         <strong>
          <i>
           Interfacial Fluid Dynamics and Processes
          </i>
         </strong>
        </font>
        <br/>
        <br/>
       </center>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <img alt="" border="0" height="554" src="Manet_savon.jpg" width="450"/>
        <br/>
        <font size="-2">
         <br/>
         (Édouard Manet,
         <i>
          Les Bulles de savon
         </i>
         , Museu Calouste Gulbenkian, Lisbon)
         <br/>
         <br/>
        </font>
       </p>
       <p>
        The main objective of the 5th Conference of the International Marangoni Association (IMA5) is to provide opportunities for scientists and engineers, working on interfacial fluid dynamics and processes, to exchange their knowledge and ideas, and to renew their friendship.
       </p>
       <p>
        Previous conferences were held in Giessen (2001), Brussels (2004), Gainesville, FL (2006) and the last one in
        <a href="http://www.rs.noda.tus.ac.jp/ima4/" target="blank">
         Tokyo
        </a>
        (2008).
       </p>
       <p>
        The 5th Conference of the International Marangoni Association (IMA5) will take place at the
        <a href="http://www.villafinaly.sorbonne.fr/" target="_blank">
         Villa Finaly
        </a>
        , Florence, Italy.
       </p>
