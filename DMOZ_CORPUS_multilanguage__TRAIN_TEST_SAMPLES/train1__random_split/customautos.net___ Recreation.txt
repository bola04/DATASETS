     <h2>
      <a href="howitbegan.html">
       How it all began...
      </a>
     </h2>
     <br/>
     <br/>
     <div class="cushycms" id="content" style="text-align:justify;padding:3px;">
      Beginning in 1999 as a website to show off my son's truck and the rides of many of his friends, customautos.net has endured through several attempts by hackers to destroy the site.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Today, customautos.net is a personal website to show off our passion for our own cars as well as cars in general.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      From customizing our cars, to performing routine maintenance, we prefer to do as much ourselves as possible. Why? Not because we're cheap or don't trust the dealers and service centers, it's just that we like doing it and it gives us a chance to hone our mechanical skills, learn more about our rides and take ownership in the performance of our vehicles.
