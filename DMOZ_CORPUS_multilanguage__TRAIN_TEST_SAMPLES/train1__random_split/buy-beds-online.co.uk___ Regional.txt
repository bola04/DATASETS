         Performance Products
        </h1>

       <div class="std">
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Opttiuuq is a specialist cricket brand which was established in 1988. The core values of OPTTIUUQ products are: fit for purpose...high quality...value for money...aesthetically pleasing designs. At Opttiuuq we design, produce and sell direct to the consumer.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         We are a low cost operation able to offer great value for money because we perform most of the functions ourselves cutting out unnecessary overheads. Opttiuuq is by far the most competitively priced of all the major cricket brands in the UK.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Superb products coupled with great customer service puts OPTTIUUQ well ahead in a winning position. Our three online sales channels achieve a positive customer feedback rate of over 99.5%.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Many of the staff at Opttiuuq Cricket are ECB qualified cricket coaches and/or are cricket players. We live and breathe cricket – hence we have the knowledge to design fully fit for purpose products. UpfrontCricket.com is a one stop cricket store where you will find every product associated with cricket. Opttiuuq produces the finest range of powerful cricket bats all hand made by experienced craftsmen.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Opttiuuq cricket balls are hand made in the time honoured ways and are amongst the best on the market. Thus Opttiuuq is faithfully able to reproduce that magic sound of leather on willow.  With many cricket products Opttiuuq is leading the way with innovative designs. We were also one of the first brands to introduce a full range of Ladies and girls cricket clothing.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Opttiuuq Cricket produces three different levels/ranges of Cricket products: Qvu, Frontfoot and Upfront. For these austere times we have introduced our Qvu range which is ultra value for money. It is our aim and belief that our Qvu range is the cheapest collection of cricket products in the UK.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         The Qvu range comprises of entry level cricket gear suitable for beginners and occasional cricketers. The Frontfoot range is high quality highly functional performance cricket gear. The Upfront range is cutting edge, technically the most advanced and/or the latest innovation. The Opttiuuq "Upfront" range is a must have for the warrior cricketers.
        </p>
