        <br3 align="center">
         Edvard Munch, Art Histories Great Expressionist of the late 1800's.
         <h2 style="text-align: center;">
          <img alt="" class="size-medium wp-image-653 aligncenter" height="199" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="http://www.edvardmunch.info/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/FE_DA_120427-the-scream415x276-300x199.jpg" srcset="http://www.edvardmunch.info/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/FE_DA_120427-the-scream415x276-300x199.jpg 300w, http://www.edvardmunch.info/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/FE_DA_120427-the-scream415x276.jpg 415w" title="FE_DA_120427-the-scream415x276" width="300"/>
          The Scream
         </h2>
         Edvard Munch made history as a highly talented artist in the late 1800's. He was an expressionist painter and printmaker from Oslo Norway. He was regarded as the pioneer of the amazing expressionist movement. His art work from the late 1800's is the most well known, but his later work is gradually attracting more attention and is a worldwide inspiration. He is most famous for his painting,
         <a href="http://www.edvardmunch.info/more-information/the-scream/">
          The Scream
         </a>
         . There aren't many people that haven't heard of that work of art and it is quite popular with media, advertisements and pop culture today.
 
 We offer three different versions of his biography, a gallery of his
         <a href="http://www.edvardmunch.info/paintings/">
          paintings
         </a>
         &amp; prints, articles, and discussion on Munch and his work.   Here you can learn about by reading a selection of biographies, or you can view some of his hundreds of prints, paintings, sketches, etc. or even learn more by checking out our discussion forums.
