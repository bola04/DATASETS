               <h2 align="center" class="header">
                Welcome to Criminon® International!
               </h2>
               <p align="left">
                <span class="BoldTM">
                 Criminon
                </span>
                (which means “no crime”) is an international non-profit public benefit corporation dedicated to addressing the causes of criminality and restoring the criminal’s self-respect through effective drug detoxification, education and common sense programs. By making them responsible for their actions, people under the Criminal Justice system can become ethical and productive citizens.
               </p>
               <p>
                <span class="BoldTM">
                 Criminon
                </span>
                operates a
                <a href="contact/global-locator.php">
                 network
                </a>
                of groups in 35 countries across the globe, delivering Criminon workshops, courses and programs in jails, prisons, parole and probation offices, courthouses and re-entry facilities, as well as providing
                <a href="programs/training-partnership.php">
                 training
                </a>
                for correctional officers and program personnel.
               </p>
               <p>
                On this site you will see that from Indonesia in the East to Los Angeles in the West , from Great Britain to South Africa,
                <span class="BoldTM">
                 Criminon
                </span>
                groups are working to restore public safety and sane values, assisting local, state and national level jails and prisons as well as the offenders.
               </p>
               <p>
                <span class="BoldTM">
                 Criminon’s
                </span>
                programs address
                <a href="programs/courses-services/understanding-overcoming-addiction.php">
                 Drug Education
                </a>
                and
                <a href="programs/courses-services/drug-rehab.php">
                 Rehabilitation
                </a>
                ,
                <a href="programs/courses-services/personal-integrity.php">
                 Character Building
                </a>
                ,
                <a href="programs/courses-services/the-way-to-happiness.php">
                 Ethical Behavior
                </a>
                ,
                <a href="programs/courses-services/literacy.php">
                 Literacy
                </a>
                ,
                <a href="programs/courses-services/learning-skills-life.php">
                 Education
                </a>
                and
                <a href="programs/courses-services/improving-conditions-life.php">
                 Life and Thinking Skills
                </a>
                including
                <a href="programs/courses-services/ups-downs-life.php">
                 Anger Management
                </a>
                ,
                <a href="programs/courses-services/communication-skills.php">
                 Communication
                </a>
                , and
                <a href="programs/courses-services/successful-parenting-skills.php">
                 Parenting Skills
                </a>
                . Our services are not limited to those in prison,
                <br/>
                but are effective in crime prevention and lowering
                <br/>
                recidivism and in diversionary settings.
               </p>
               <p>
                <span class="BoldTM">
                 Criminon
                </span>
                utilizes the research discoveries made by American author and humanitarian
                <a href="about-us/l-ron-hubbard.php">
                 L. Ron Hubbard
                </a>
                , who personally worked as a Special Officer for the Los Angeles Police Department in the late 1940’s. Mr. Hubbard discovered that every criminal career began with a loss of self-respect. When man could no longer trust himself, only then did he become a real threat to society.
               </p>
               <p>
                As early as 1952, Mr. Hubbard began a criminal rehabilitation program with juvenile delinquents in London, England. After two decades of research developments, the
                <span class="BoldTM">
                 Criminon
                </span>
                program was born.
               </p>
               <p>
                If this is your first visit to the
                <span class="BoldTM">
                 Criminon
                </span>
                web site, we welcome you and please read more on us in the
                <a href="about-us/index.php">
                 "About Us"
                </a>
                section.  You will find out about programs for offenders, training for correctional professionals, materials available to the public, and how you can sponsor someone on a Criminon course.
               </p>
               <p>
                Please feel free to contact
                <span class="BoldTM">
                 Criminon International
                </span>
                at:
                <a href="mailto:info@criminon.org">
                 info@criminon.org
                </a>
                with any questions or requests for more information on starting up
                <span class="BoldTM">
                 Criminon
                </span>
                groups and restoring common sense values to society.
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
