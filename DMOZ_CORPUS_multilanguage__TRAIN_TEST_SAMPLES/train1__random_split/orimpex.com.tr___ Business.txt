      <h2>
       Welcome
      </h2>
      <p>
       Orimpex is a Dutch and Turkish company for manufacturing of Organic Textile. Orimpex’s production facility is located in Izmir (Turkey) in the middle of the Aegan Region. The organic cotton cultivated in this region is famous for its long, soft, strong and organic cotton fibers. We are specialised in the production of baby, women and man apparel. All Orimpex’s cotton products are 100% organic (GOTS certified) and manufactured in its own production facility. Since 2010 we also producce clothes from Bamboo and Tencel.
      </p>
