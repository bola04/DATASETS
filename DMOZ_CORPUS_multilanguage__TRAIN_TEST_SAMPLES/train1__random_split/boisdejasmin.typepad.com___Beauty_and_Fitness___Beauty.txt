          <h2 class="date-header">

          <div class="entry-category-perfume_fragrance_news entry-author-victoria entry-has-title entry-type-post entry" id="entry-6a00d8341c706153ef0167649dd125970b">
           <h3 class="entry-header">
            <a href="http://boisdejasmin.typepad.com/_/2012/04/chandler-burr-the-art-of-scent-exhibit-opening.html">
             Chandler Burr's The Art of Scent Exhibit Opening
            </a>
           </h3>
           <div class="entry-content">
            <input name="zemanta-related" type="hidden" val=""/>
            <div class="entry-body">
             <p>
              <a href="http://www.madmuseum.org/">
               The Museum of Art and Design
              </a>
              in New York and its Curator of Olfactory Art Chandler Burr are going to present their first exhibit
              <em>
               The Art of Scent
              </em>
              starting November 13, 2012. The exhibit will run till January 13 2013. The components of the exhibit are currently being developed.
             </p>
             <p>
              <a href="http://boisdejasmin.typepad.com/_/2010/12/center-of-olfactory-art-at-the-museum-of-art-and-design.html">
               As was announced previously
              </a>
              in the MAD press release, “the first exhibition planned, The Art of Scent, 1889-2011, will allow visitors to experience ten seminal works by some of the greatest scent artists of the late-19th, 20th and early-21st centuries.”
             </p>

          <h2 class="date-header">
           April 03, 2012
          </h2>
          <div class="entry-category-perfume_fragrance_news entry-author-victoria entry-has-title entry-type-post entry" id="entry-6a00d8341c706153ef0167648fa7c5970b">
           <h3 class="entry-header">
            <a href="http://boisdejasmin.typepad.com/_/2012/04/fifi-hall-of-fame-award-2012-gabrielle-coco-chanel.html">
             FiFi Hall of Fame Award 2012 : Gabrielle Coco Chanel
            </a>
           </h3>
           <div class="entry-content">
            <input name="zemanta-related" type="hidden" val=""/>
            <div class="entry-body">
             <p>
              <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://boisdejasmin.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8341c706153ef0167648fa2b5970b-pi">
               <img alt="Cc" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d8341c706153ef0167648fa2b5970b" src="http://boisdejasmin.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8341c706153ef0167648fa2b5970b-500wi" style="width: 500px; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="Cc"/>
              </a>
             </p>
             <p>
              The Fragrance Foundation has announced that
              <strong>
               Gabrielle “Coco” Chanel
              </strong>
              will be inducted into
              <strong>
               The 2012
              </strong>
              <strong>
               FiFi
              </strong>
              <strong>
               Hall of Fame
              </strong>
              during the FiFi Awards on Monday May 21
              <sup>
               st
              </sup>
              <strong>
               .
              </strong>
              As the Fragrance Foundation explains, "The FiFi® Hall of Fame award is voted on by the Foundation’s Board of Directors and is presented to an individual who has brought extraordinary ingenuity, creativity, dedication and inner resources to bear, not only to their company’s success but to the fragrance industry as a whole."
             </p>
             <p>
              " 'It is quite astonishing that in the year 2012, a fragrance introduced in 1921 and simply named Chanel No 5, prevails as one of the most enduring and iconic symbols of style, elegance and modern scent creativity the world has ever known,' says Jill Belasco, Chair of The Fragrance Foundation.'Chanel fragrances have reached near mythical status thanks to the vision of Coco Chanel, her enduring commitment to in-house master perfumery and an uncompromising commitment to luxury and quality. To this day, men and women everywhere delight in wearing fragrances marked by the entwined double C’s of Chanel.' "
             </p>
             <p>
              This year marks the 40
              <sup>
               th
              </sup>
              Anniversary of The FiFi Awards.
             </p>
             <p>
              Via press release
             </p>
             <p>
             </p>
