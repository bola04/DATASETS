          <p>
           <span style="color: #ffffff;">
            <strong>
             Magnatech Business Systems
            </strong>
            , based in Long Island NY, has been providing premium
            <strong>
             quality computer software
            </strong>
            , hardware, and services to the New York/New Jersey/Connecticut area since 1988.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="color: #ffffff;">
            We offer, in plain English, “solutions.” We don’t expect everyone to be a computer geek, that is our job. We listen to a
            <strong>
             business
            </strong>
            or
            <strong>
             computer problem
            </strong>
            and take responsibility for the solution.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="color: #ffffff;">
            How? We offer
            <strong>
             custom programming
            </strong>
            ,
            <strong>
             system integration
            </strong>
            ,
            <strong>
             design
            </strong>
            ,
            <strong>
             electronic mail
            </strong>
            and
            <strong>
             Internet/world wide web integration
            </strong>
            , and
            <strong>
             system administration
            </strong>
            . Generally, we act as a replacement, out-source, or technical support resource for entire IT departments of our clientele.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="color: #ffffff;">
            We specialize in
            <strong>
             multi-user systems
            </strong>
            and the majority of our networks are Unix, Linux, or Windows based. Our chief application development language is
            <strong>
             filePro
            </strong>
            , of which we are one of the premier developers world-wide.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="color: #ffffff;">
            This year, we are celebrating our 30th anniversary. So, please have a look around our website and join us in celebrating!
           </span>
          </p>
