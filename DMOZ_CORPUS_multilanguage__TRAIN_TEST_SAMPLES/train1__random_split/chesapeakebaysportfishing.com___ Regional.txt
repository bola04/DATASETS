         <h1>
          Chesapeake Bay Fishing Charter Trips in Maryland
         </h1>
         <p>
          We pride ourselves on being a first-class
          <a href="/charter-information">
           Chesapeake Bay charter
          </a>
          fishing operation. We have a long list of businesses that have found our fishing services on the Chesapeake Bay very productive for entertaining clients, as well as many private family fishing and cruise charters. Every fishing trip on the Chesapeake Bay aboard the "Net Profits", "Jessie Girl", "Vagabond" or "Retirement Fun" includes all your rods, reels, bait, tackle and most importantly fishing licenses.
 Enjoying a fishing experience on the Chesapeake Bay is as easy as making a phone call. If you desire we can provide everything for a great day of fishing on the Chesapeake Bay from your food and beverages to arranging fish cleaning services at the end of a great day of fishing.
         </p>
         <p>
          We fish seven days a week on the Chesapeake Bay from the Annapolis area, which is conveniently located within minutes to both Baltimore and Washington DC. (April - December). However, custom pickup and drop off points can be arranged anywhere on the Chesapeake Bay or its tributaries for fishing.
         </p>
