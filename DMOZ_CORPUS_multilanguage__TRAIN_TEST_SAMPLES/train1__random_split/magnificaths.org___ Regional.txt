           </h2>
           <p>
            <strong>
             At Magnificat High School, we believe in the POWER OF WOMEN.
            </strong>

            We value their dreams, their drive, their ideas and their potential. As the world evolves and expects more of our young women, we evolve and build upon our strong foundation to prepare our students to reflect Christ in a dynamic and ever-changing World. Our young women graduate from Magnificat empowered to learn, to lead, and to serve in the spirit of Mary's

             Magnificat.
