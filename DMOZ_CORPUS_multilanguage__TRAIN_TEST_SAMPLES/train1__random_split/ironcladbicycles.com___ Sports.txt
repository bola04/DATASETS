          <br/>
          We will always believe bicycles are the most amazing
                   inventions ever, that bicycles are the most efficient
                   transportation available, and there is nothing as much
                   fun as a bicycle... but don't freak out... we have now
                   added electric bicycles to our mix!
          <br/>
          <br/>
          We have studied the electric bike market for over 15
                   years, and with an abundance of caution we have not
                   offered any for sale at our shop until now. What's
                   different now? Now we are able to offer electric bikes
                   with quality components that we are sure we can stand
                   behind.

         <br/>
         Two Years in a Row!
         <br/>
         In 2014, we were first named as one of "America's Best
                 Bike Shops" by the National Bicycle Dealers Association.
                 Since we aren't used to much national attention, we were
                 afraid it might be a fluke, and didn't want to be known
                 as a one hit wonder... So we pledged right then to look
                 for areas to improve, and set our sights higher. It
                 appears that quest has paid off, because we honored to
                 receive the award again this year!
         <br/>
         Our plan is to keep up the momentum, and we have more
                 positive changes planned for this next season... so if
                 you haven't been by in a while, stop by and see what's
                 new!

           IRONCLAD is now an AUTHORIZED FOX SERVICE CENTER!
          </b>
          <br/>
          <img alt="Fox Racing Fork" height="600" src="foxfork.jpg" width="450"/>
          <br/>
          Ironclad Bicycles is proud to announce that we are now
                   authorized as a service center for FOX Racing
                   Suspension forks. In June we sent Corey to a special
                   Fox training seminar on the campus of United Bicycle
                   Institute in Portland. In July we ordered up all the
                   special tools and parts kits. In August we held
                   in-house training sessions for other staff, and now -
                   we are all geared up and ready to go!
          <br/>
          Why is this a big deal? Fox makes the best forks out
                   there, and up until now if there was a problem with a
                   fork, our only option was to remove it, box it up, and
                   send it to Fox... then wait for them to service it,
                   and send it back. This was a process that could mean
                   weeks without your bike... hard to take when the
                   weather is good.
          <br/>
          NOW, we have cut the UPS man out of the picture, and
                   we can service your Fox fork in house... and
                   significantly reduce your down time. We are proud to
                   be the only shop in the area that has the tools,
                   expertise, and parts on hand to do this work!
          <br/>
          <img alt="Cory explains Fox bladder bleeding" height="500" src="foxcorey2.jpg" width="375"/>
          <br/>
          Have some other brand of fork? No sweat... we can
                   probably service that too - just ask!
