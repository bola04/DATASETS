          <p align="left" class="style22">
           Sail  around the beautiful Greek Ionian Islands with Pinnacle Yachts! Our aim at  Pinnacle Yachts, as an independent owner-operated company, is to offer bareboat  yacht charter from Corfu with a degree of flexibility, friendliness and  personal attention which cannot be matched by larger sailing holiday operators.  Our bareboat and skippered charter operation in Corfu, Greece, is run by Colin  Craig.
          </p>
          <p align="left" class="style22">
           By  keeping everything 'in house', our maintenance and yacht preparation standards  are always meticulous.
           <a class="text" href="yachts.html">
            Our yachts
           </a>
           are comfortably equipped to ensure you  have a relaxing and trouble free sailing holiday at an affordable price. We  treat all our clients as individuals and provide a level of personal service  resulting in the yachting holiday you deserve.
          </p>
          <p align="left" class="style22">
           The  Ionian Sea and its numerous islands offer a superb location for a sailing  holiday. Navigation in the area is simple with little tide or current.
           <a class="text" href="area.html">
            The cruising area
           </a>
           is  relatively sheltered and the winds are normally gentle. It is an area of breathtaking  natural beauty, green and lush with safe, clear warm waters. You have plenty of  choice between secluded anchorages and traditional village harbours with tavernas  and friendly locals ready to welcome you ashore.
          </p>
          <p align="left" class="style22">
           Our  base at
           <a class="text" href="travel.html">
            Gouvia Marina, Corfu
           </a>
           , is very  conveniently located, being only around five miles and a short taxi journey  from Corfu airport. Upon your arrival at the marina Colin will meet you and,  once on board, you will be fully briefed on the operation of the yacht and all  its systems, along with details of the Ionian cruising area and weather  information. If required we can demonstrate Mediterranean mooring procedures.
          </p>
          <p align="left" class="style22">
           <a class="text" href="options.html">
            Other options
           </a>
           are available including skippered yacht charter, either to   hone rusty sailing skills or a fully skippered sailing holiday for those with   little or no sailing experience; Sail and stay options - combine sailing in the   Ionian with a stay ashore in Corfu; Flotilla sailing if you prefer a sailing   holiday in company; or you can take an RYA approved sailing course with the Corfu Sea   School.
          </p>
