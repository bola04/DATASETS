      <h1>
       Perisher's iconic hotel
      </h1>
      <h3>
       <em>
        Your complete on snow accommodation experience!
       </em>
      </h3>
      <p>
       Open from the June Long weekend to late September, The Man from Snowy River is Perisher's largest hotel, located in a great location, in the heart of Perisher Resort, just a few hundred metres to the Village 8 Chairlift. We have the only undercover onsite parking in Perisher Valley.
      </p>
      <p>
       Our hotel is suitable for couples and families. Our stunning dining room, with its huge glass facade, looks out over Front Valley. We have warm open fireplaces and two great bars; our cosy lounge bar and iconic pub bar (which has live entertainment most evenings). To help you relax, we have a gym, steam room and an in house beauty salon. Our evening kids club allows parents time to relax, dine together or maybe listen to some music in the bar, whilst the little ones are fed and entertained.
      </p>
      <p>
       Our warm friendly service will complete your Perisher ski experience.
      </p>
