     <p class="txt">
      Cinque Terre Hotels is a tourist web guide to visit Cinque Terre and will help you to find hotel, bed and breakfast, rooms to rent, restaurant, winery or the right beach and the best hiking path to better enjoy your relaxing days in one of the most beutiful places in Italy. Click on where to stay in Cinque Terre and you will be able to find the best solution for lodging in Cinque Terre.
     </p>
     <p class="txt">
      Every property you find on cinqueterrehotels.com is located into the Cinque Terre National Park, so here you will find only accomodation located in one of the five little towns: Monterosso, Vernazza, Corniglia, Manarola and Riomaggiore.
     </p>
     <p class="txt">
      Monterosso, Vernazza, Corniglia, Manarola and Riomaggiore are five little towns immersed in landscapes that are unique of their kind, blending the beauty of the beaches and the crystal clear sea with steep pathways. Like few other places in Italy Cinque Terre is doubly safeguarded: the Protected Marine Area and the National Park. Starting from 1997 Cinque Terre are also named as a world heritage site by UNESCO.
