      We offer wax formulations for the needs of Investment Casting by '
      <i>
       lost wax process'.
      </i>
      Our waxes are used by foundries from aircraft and aerospace industries and defence establishments as well as those making commercial castings.
     </font>

   <font color="#003366">
    <p align="justify">
     <b>
      <font face="Verdana">
       Our waxes are suitable for use on almost all kinds of wax injection machines with reasonable controlling set-up. Besides, our regular customers have also witnessed, over the years, consistency in quality with most economic pricing. These factors have 
 contributed to recognition of our products by Investment Casting foundries in India and abroad.  We have an evaluation report from '
       <i>
        Institut Odlewnictwa'
       </i>
       (Foundry Research Institute, Krakow, Poland) for one of our products, viz.

       -R
      </font>
     </b>
     <b>
      <font face="Verdana">
       by international standards.
      </font>
     </b>
    </p>
    <p align="justify">
     <b>
      <font face="Verdana">
       Present range of our products include pattern-waxes, both filled and unfilled, water emulsifiable pattern waxes, water soluble core wax, sticky and 
 patch waxes. Besides, in house R &amp; D makes it possible for us to put up new 
 products to suit the demands of 'Lost Wax Process' industry. Our quality control and development 
 laboratory is well-equipped for evaluation of physical parameters of waxes like 
 contraction characteristics, surface hardness and mechanical properties, 
 softening temperatures, gravity pouring and pressure injection performances, 
 etc.
