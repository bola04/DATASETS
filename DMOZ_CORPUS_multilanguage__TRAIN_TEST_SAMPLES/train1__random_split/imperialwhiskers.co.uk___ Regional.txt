      </h3>
      <p align="center" class="style1">

      <p align="center" class="style1">
       We happy few decided to organise a whiskers growing competition to raise some money for charities and to prove that it is possible to win the admiring glances of the ladies of the parish with some traditional imperial style side whiskers!
      </p>
      <p align="center" class="style1">
       The competition began on Sunday 27th August 2006, at the White Lion pub, close to Chesham in Buckinghamshire.
      </p>
      <p align="center" class="style1">
       These web pages are intended to raise the profile of the competition and track its progress.
      </p>
