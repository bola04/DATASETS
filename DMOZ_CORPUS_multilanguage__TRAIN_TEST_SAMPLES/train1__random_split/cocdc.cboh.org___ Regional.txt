 <h2>If You Want to Learn to Square Dance, <span style="font-variant:small-caps; font-style:italic; color:black">Keep Reading!</span></h2>
 <p>If you don't know how to dosado yet, you have come to the right place at the right time!  Yes, it's September and the square dance community of Central Ohio is gearing up to start another round of fall classes. Have fun, make new friends and become more physically fit while learning to square dance! <p>No special skills are needed.  It doesn’t cost much (around $6 a dance), meets in smoke- and alcohol-free settings (such as churches, school gyms, parks and rec halls), and is simply good, clean fun.  September is National Square Dance Month and is the best time to get started. Couples, singles and families are welcome, and the first two classes are FREE at most clubs.  We dance in casual clothing and comfortable shoes.  (If you have them, shoes with a smooth sole make dancing just a little easier.)  Nine different modern western square dance clubs in various locations around central Ohio are offering beginner instruction.  Check out the offerings on our <a href=workshops.html>Workshops & Classes</a> page and join the fun! We'll be saving a spot for you in a square!

    <h2>
     Website Changes in Store
    </h2>
    <p>
     After 15 years, the COCDC website will be getting a fresh new look, and
 a new webmaster.  The transition will take place between now and the end
 of the year.  We're still working out the details but check out
     <a href="https://www.columbussquaredance.com">
      www.columbussquaredance.com
     </a>
     ,
 which will be the entry site for the Columbus square dance community.
 We're putting more emphasis on attracting new dancers and are automating
 the dance calendar so that each club can manage the listings of their
 own dance entries.  Ray Owens will be constructing and maintaining the
 new site and I (Rob Stampfli) will be working with Ray to provide for an
 orderly transition.  We're working hard to complete the major work by
 year's end.  So check back with us from time-to-time, and don't hesitate
 to let us know if you have suggestions and/or questions; after all, we're
 doing this for you!
    </p>
    <hr class="halfwidth"/>
    <h2>
     Summer Fun Dances
    </h2>
    <p>
     Now you've graduated from your long winter of dance lessons and can't
 seem to get enough dancing in, we have a little something extra to share
 with you:
 In collaboration with our local clubs, your Council is sponsoring
     <a href="Flyers/2018-COCDC-SummerFunDances.pdf">
      five
      <i>
       free
      </i>
      dances this summer
     </a>
     .**
 Casual attire is fine at these two-hour square-dance-only dances.
 All dancers, both old and new, are welcome.
 So, hone your new-found skills and get to meet and dance to
 some of our talented local callers!  See you in a square.
 (** Optional donations will be accepted for Toys for Tots.)
    </p>

     <h2>
      Our Friendship Dangle Gets a New Look...
     </h2>
     <p>
      Bright yellow with black letters boasting Friendship Hands Across COCDC. Dancers can earn this dangle by attending a club dance at all ten-member clubs during a one-year period.  Now that COCDC has grown and has ten active Plus level clubs, the form has been revised too.  A club officer must sign and date the form at each new club dance you attend.  When you've gotten all ten signatures, give the completed form to a COCDC Council Representative to receive your dangle!   Spread the Friendship of Western Square Dancing throughout Central Ohio!
     </p>

     This Month in Central Ohio Square Dancing

    <div class="thisweek">
     <p>
      Dear Dancers,
      <!--
 <p>Below is a list of opportunities for mainstream or plus level square dancing in Central Ohio for the coming month.  Other dance information (including information about higher level square dances, round dances, and beginner dance instruction) is available on our <a href="current_month.html">monthly calendar</a> and our
 <a href="workshops.html">workshops and classes</a> web pages.
 -->
     </p>
     <p>
      This
      <i>
       Dances of the Month
      </i>
      will be the last that I will routinely send out to everyone. I will continue to forward emails I receive about cancellations and changes in callers, cuers, and locations, along with other dance related information that I believe to be of interest to dancers.
     </p>
     <p>
      The information about scheduled dancers that I have been sending out is readily available on two different websites: this Central Ohio Corporation of Dance Clubs (COCDC) site, www.cocdc.org, and also on the new site that Ray Owens has created,
      <a href="https://www.columbussquaredance.com">
       www.columbussquaredance.com
      </a>
      . You really do not need for me to send out the dance schedule when it is easy to find on either website, and is also available in Cues &amp; Tips magazine and in the annual Central Ohio Western Square &amp; Round Dance Schedule produced and distributed by COCDC.
     </p>
     <p>
      If you have not checked out www.columbussquaredance.com, be sure to take a look. Ray has been busy taking photos and videos at local dances and also at the Ohio and National Square Dance Conventions, and has shared them with all of us. He has added dance related information and links to other dance sites. This new site is colorful and entertaining, and will keep current dancers well informed and hopefully also attract new, younger dancers when they see how much fun we have.
     </p>
     <p>
      Currently, Rob Stampfli is maintaining the COCDC website that he created and has maintained for us for years (www.cocdc.org), but sometime this fall the Central Ohio Corporation of Dance Clubs will be totally transitioning to Ray Owens’ Columbus Square Dance site. We are grateful to Rob for all of the work and the fine job that he has done for COCDC and the Central Ohio dance community over the years. Thank you, Rob!
     </p>
     <p>
      With that being said, below is a list of opportunities for mainstream or plus level square dancing in Central Ohio through July 31st.  Other dance information (including information about higher level square dances, round dances and dance instruction) is available on this COCDC website (www.cocdc.org or www.CentralOhioSquareDancing.com), and also at
      <a href="https://www.columbussquaredance.com">
       www.columbussquaredance.com
      </a>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
      Wednesday, July 25 –
      <span>
       (Location Change)
      </span>
      Daylight Dancers at the Gillie 50+ Center, 2100 Morse Rd, from 2:30 to 4:30 PM.  Caller and cuer: Mark Johnson.
     </p>
     <p>
      Friday, July 27 – Bucks &amp; Does Root Beer Floats Dance at the Gahanna Community Congregational Church, 470 Havens Corners Rd, Gahanna, from 8:00 to 10:30 PM.   (Early rounds at 7:30 PM).
      <span>
       Caller Change:
      </span>
      Mark Patterson.  Cuer: Chuck Berry.
     </p>
     <p>
      <span>
       Canceled:
      </span>

      </del>
     </p>
     <p>
      Keep on dancing!
     </p>
     <p>
      Velma
