       <h1>
        Welcome to the Town of Aylmer
       </h1>
       <p class="vspace">
        Step back in time, to a less hurried pace of life. Hear the hooves of horse and buggy. Take in the mecca of quaint boutiques, fragrant bakeries &amp; historic homes and buildings. Linger a while in one of our beautiful parks. You'll love the esthetic wonders and enticing fragrances you'll encounter along the way. The more you explore our community, the more you'll discover that Aylmer is a treasure trove of exceptional experiences.
       </p>
       <p class="vspace">
        Whether you are here for a day, a life time or to make a business investment, you will find everything you are looking for in Aylmer.
       </p>
       <p class="vspace">
        <em>
         Aylmer
        </em>
        - a great place to live, work and raise a family!
        <br/>
        <em>
         You belong in Aylmer!
        </em>
       </p>
