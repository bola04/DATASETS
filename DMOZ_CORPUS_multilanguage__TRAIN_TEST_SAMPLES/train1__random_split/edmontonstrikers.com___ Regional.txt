             <a href="/organizations/3298/announcements/1401350-OH-CANADA-CONGRATULATIONS-ALOUT-AGAR-">
              OH CANADA! CONGRATULATIONS ALOUT AGAR!
             </a>
            </h3>
            <p class="author">
             by
             <a href="/organizations/3298">
              Edmonton Strikers Soccer Club
             </a>
             ,
             <span class="cached_time">
              <span class="iso8601">
               2018-07-27T09:32:21.000-07:00
              </span>
              July 27 2018, at 09:32 AM PDT
             </span>
            </p>
            <div class="comments">
             (
             <a href="/organizations/3298/announcements/1401350-OH-CANADA-CONGRATULATIONS-ALOUT-AGAR-">
              0 Comments
             </a>
             )
            </div>
            <img alt="image" class="image left" src="/p/announcements/1401350/500/500/1532711068/image.pic"/>
            Another great day for soccer in Edmonton and Alberta.  Edmonton Striker Alout Agar from our U15 girls has been selected to the Concacaf U15 Championship and will represent Canada this August in Florida.  She is the youngest player selected to the Canadian roster, the only player from Edmonton and 1 of 2 players selected from Alberta. We know Alout will make Canada as proud of her as much as her Strikers Coaches, teammates and the rest of her Strikers family are.
            <br/>
            Congratulations to Alout, We wish her the best of luck in the tournament and can’t wait to see her in the Canadian Jersey!
