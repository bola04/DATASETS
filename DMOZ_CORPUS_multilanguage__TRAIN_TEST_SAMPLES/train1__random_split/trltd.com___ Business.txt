          <h1>
           Threat Resolution Ltd
          </h1>
          <h2>
           Solutions for Explosives Risk Management
          </h2>
          <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_592" style="width: 160px">
           <a href="http://trltd.com/cms/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/Dive-RHIB-Aug-13-e1375547674827.jpg">
            <img alt="TRLtd EOD Dive RHIB" class="size-thumbnail wp-image-592 colorbox-4" height="150" src="http://trltd.com/cms/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/Dive-RHIB-Aug-13-150x150.jpg" width="150"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            TRL EOD Dive RHIB
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           Threat Resolution Ltd (TRL) is a provider of explosives risk management services for both land and offshore environments. We aid our clients by filling knowledge and capability gaps within their organisation and the development of explosives ordnance risk management solutions.
          </p>
          <p>
           We specialise in the mitigation of Explosive Remnants of War (ERW) risks and,  the survey for and removal of Unexploded Ordnance (UXO) in a subsea environment, especially in post-conflict areas. TRL services range from desktop study and life-cycle project management to third-party Quality Assurance and Quality Control.
          </p>
          <p>
           The Company operates within the guidelines of International Mine Action Standards (IMAS) in both land and offshore projects.
          </p>
          <p>
           Offshore, we operate to the maritime standards of the United Kingdom and typically, all of our Explosive Ordnance Disposal (EOD) divers are experienced ex-British Royal Navy Clearance Divers.
          </p>
          <p>
           Call us on our 24-hour hotline to discover how TRL can assist in your explosives ordnance risk challenge +44 7976 637 614.
          </p>
