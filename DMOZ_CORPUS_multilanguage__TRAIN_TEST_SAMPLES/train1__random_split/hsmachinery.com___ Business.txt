     <h2>
      H &amp; S Machinery is proud to announce being named Doosan Infracore 2012 Distributor Of The Year
     </h2>
     <p>
      <a href="http://hsmachinery.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/h-s-doosan-award-2012.jpg">
       <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-624" height="386" src="http://hsmachinery.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/h-s-doosan-award-2012.jpg" title="h-s-doosan-award-2012" width="450"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      It is our belief that you can not attain such a lofty award without a service first philosophy ,extremely hard working staff, and most importantly a loyal customer base. When you purchase a machine tool from H &amp; S Machinery it is our promise that you will be treated like you are our only customer. We are not satisfied until you are satisfied. As we look back on the success of the previous year we would like to thank all of our customers from 2011 and prior because we know this award was not just won because of what our customers did for us in 2011 but all of the years before.
     </p>
