            <p>
             <strong>
              Welcome
             </strong>
             to White Diamond Terriers, beautifully located in the  southeastern corner of Washington State, home to a small number of quality AKC  conformation champions. On the following pages, you will find interesting  information and a number of great pictures of our West Highland White and  Norwich Terriers in competition and in the hands of their wonderful owners. At  White Diamond, we focus our breeding efforts on perfection in both conformation  and temperament. We also specialize in professional presentation during AKC  conformation events throughout the United States. Please feel free to contact  us with any questions you may have.
            </p>
            Pictured here are two of my clients' dogs. On the left is CH Wyndbrae's Glenfiddich Gretta owned by Schuyler Pennington and Pam Whiting DVM. On the right, currently ranked among the top Norwich Terriers in the United States is GCH CH Littlefield Dignpop Dashiell of Dungeness owned by Britt Litchford, Elaine Jong and Leandra Little.
