          <h2 class="cen content">
           Getting the Message Across
          </h2>
          This is a mini web site to support a work shop for the Royal College of Nurses Diabetes Forum in Bristol.
 The theme of the forum - '
          <strong>
           Effective diabetes education - getting the message across.
          </strong>
          '
          <p>
           'On topic' web sites are a very effective way to get your message across.
          </p>
          <p>
           This site looks at what's involved in getting your material on the web.
 Its not diabetes specific but there are one or two examples that refer to diabetes.
          </p>
          <p>
           There are DIY options and free alternatives at most stages and links to a number of free resources too, 
 so if you want to know how to setup a website or merely to find out whats involved have a look around.
