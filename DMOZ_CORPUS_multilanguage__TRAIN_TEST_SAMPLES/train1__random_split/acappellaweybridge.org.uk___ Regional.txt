        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          The A Cappella Singers is a small chamber choir based in Weybridge, Surrey.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="IMG_2076" class=" wp-image-142 alignright" height="207" src="http://acappellaweybridge.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/IMG_2076-300x200.jpg" width="311"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Rehearsals take place on Tuesday evenings during term time at St Mary, Oatlands, and we give concerts locally three times a year, including a charity carol concert each Christmas and participating in other occasional fundraising events.
         </p>
         <p>
          We specialize in unaccompanied and accompanied works, using small professional instrumental groups and rising young singers were appropriate. (See previous programmes for the music we enjoy).
         </p>
         <p>
          As a friendly group with members of all ages, we socialise outside rehearsals, including twice yearly parties held at Christmas and in the summer.
         </p>
         <p>
          Regular donations are made to charity from the proceeds of our concerts, and we often choose charities with which our choir members have a personal connection.
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           Welcome to our website, and please use the menu at the top to find out more about us.
          </em>
         </p>
