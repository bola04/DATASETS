        <h1 class="entry-title post-title">
         About
        </h1>
        <div class="post-entry">
         <div>
          <p>
           The
           <strong>
            NORTHERN KITE GROUP
           </strong>
           is for anyone interested in building and flying kites. The Group caters especially for those living in the Yorkshire / Lancashire corridor of towns stretching from York to Liverpool, although it is open to all and has members from all over the UK.
          </p>
          <p>
           The Club has been in continual existence from 1978 and our members have acquired a great deal of experience which is freely shared among members.
          </p>
          <p>
           We have members of all ages, whose skill ranges from complete beginners to those who earn their living from making and selling kites.
          </p>
          <p>
           We meet on the third Sunday of the month at different flying sites in our region. A list of venues is published on this site and also in our quarterly magazine Kite.
          </p>
          <p>
           Many of our members attend Kite Festivals both in the UK and abroad. The Club also has a small library of books available on loan from the Club librarian.
          </p>
          <p>
           Don’t forget to visit our
           <a href="http://www.facebook.com/groups/NorthernKiteGroup/" target="_blank">
            Facebook
           </a>
           page to catch up on all the latest news, events and gossip. See
           <a href="http://www.nkg.org.uk/join" title="Join">
            here
           </a>
           for membership details and an application form.
          </p>
         </div>
