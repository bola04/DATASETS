          <h2>
           Introduction
          </h2>
          <p>
           If you are working as a bartender or planning to start working one day, it is very important that you are fully aware of the responsibilities of being one. It is very well-known just how far reaching the effects, and consequences of alcohol consumption can be.
          </p>
          <p>
           With all this in mind, if you are planning to become a bartender, you should consider enrolling in the RSA certification training course, where you will learn all the important skills and acquire the necessary knowledge in regards to selling and serving drinks responsibly. Throughout this article, we will touch upon some of the most important skills you will learn there.
          </p>
          <h3>
           Managing patrons and problem anticipation
          </h3>
          <p>
           <img alt="22hyiu95" class="alignleft wp-image-147" height="171" src="http://www.crossstitchpatterngall.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/22-150x150.jpg" width="171"/>
           Usually, it is up to the staff to keep a close eye on the customers, who are consuming alcoholic beverages, and their effect on other people in their surroundings. In most cases, potential problems can be easily resolved by being aware of the current situation before it escalates into something serious. One of the skills you will learn and perfect at an RSA training course will involve dealing properly with customers who display severe signs of intoxication, such as abusive language, rowdiness, and acts of violence.
          </p>
          <h3>
           Advising the customers on the content of various drinks
          </h3>
          <p>
           While it may sound surprising, many people are usually completely unaware of what their drinks consist off. Just because the majority of beer bottles are of the same size, does not mean that they contain the same amount of alcohol. The same goes for pretty much every other alcoholic beverage. Keep in mind that a standard glass of wine looks very small when served in a large glass.
          </p>
          <p>
           When serving drinks, try to stick to the “standard glass” drink measurements and make sure to avoid filling up large glasses, otherwise, you can easily end up doubling the standard measure. Keep in mind that spirits can be quite deceiving. If you pour manually, make sure not to fill until it overflows. To avoid this guessing game, try using a standard automatic pourer.
          </p>
          <h3>
           Providing the customers with a range of options
          </h3>
          <p>
           Always try to assist the customers so that they drink within appropriate limits. Also, let them choose the drinks that have a very low alcohol content or none at all. Avoid pushing strong drinks over those that have a lighter alcohol content. Keep in mind that there should always be some alcohol-free options available, such as soft drinks, water, juices, etc. If there is enough space, try setting up a self-service water station for the customers to use.
          </p>
          <h3>
           Providing assistance to intoxicated customers and refusing service
          </h3>
          <p>
           <img alt="33lkfjgij" class="alignright wp-image-148" height="185" src="http://www.crossstitchpatterngall.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/33-150x150.jpg" width="185"/>
           If a customer is clearly intoxicated, you have the right to refuse the service. You should know that there can be dire consequences for both the person who serves alcoholic drinks, as well as the owner of the venue where the event takes place. To avoid these consequences, do not hesitate to refuse a service to an individual who is clearly intoxicated.
          </p>
          <p>
           Instead of serving an alcoholic drink, offer them a non-alcoholic one. Also, remember that it is your responsibility to assist them, as well. When looking for house furniture, there are several factors you should consider. The following are tips for furniture shopping.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Budget
          </h2>
          <p>
           Set a budget on what you can afford to buy. It will save energy and time when you are doing a search on the available options. With a budget in mind, you can go for something that is within the price range of your expenditure. Many online stores sell the same products at different prices. You can get whatever you want at a favorable price.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Research
           <img alt="fcfftt" class="alignright size-thumbnail wp-image-47" height="150" sizes="(max-width: 150px) 100vw, 150px" src="http://www.crossstitchpatterngall.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/vbgbhh-150x150.jpg" srcset="http://www.crossstitchpatterngall.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/vbgbhh-150x150.jpg 150w, http://www.crossstitchpatterngall.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/vbgbhh-300x300.jpg 300w, http://www.crossstitchpatterngall.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/vbgbhh-60x60.jpg 60w" width="150"/>

You obviously have a picture in mind of what you are looking for especially when buying sofas; you know the style and shade you want in the back of your mind. It will assist you in narrowing your search when you know what you are looking for specifically. Making a list of companies that you want to purchase your furniture from can help when comparing them so that you make the right decision.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Reviews
          </h2>
          <p>
           It is important to check in details the companies you have considered on your list. You can do that by finding more from the information on the company’s website. Also, check on customer feedback which is online on the companies’ site. From the reviews, you can know which company has excellent customer service. You can settle on the company to order your furniture from the information, price, and review. Buy from a store that has policies that allow warranties, repairs, and return.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Delivery and installation
          </h2>
          <p>
           <img alt="hvggvgv" class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-45" height="150" sizes="(max-width: 150px) 100vw, 150px" src="http://www.crossstitchpatterngall.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/gbhbh-150x150.jpg" srcset="http://www.crossstitchpatterngall.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/gbhbh-150x150.jpg 150w, http://www.crossstitchpatterngall.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/gbhbh-300x300.jpg 300w, http://www.crossstitchpatterngall.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/gbhbh-60x60.jpg 60w" width="150"/>

Also, ask if the enterprise does installation services especially for things like wardrobes or beds. If they offer these services, there is no reason to run around looking for experts to install your

            furniture
           </a>
           . You can also find out if there are any charges for delivering and installing or is it offered free.

          <h2>
           Skin problems

Using a small amount of coconut oil is important. Or you can use the coconut oil to massage your body before you take a warm shower.

           Cooking
          </h2>
          <p>
           Coconut oil is beneficial when it comes to cooking though many people do not know that. This oil adds flavor to your food. It smells and tastes as if it has been added chutney and curry. If coconut oils stay unused for long, it is advisable to throw it as it develops foul smell and it not safe to use.

           Antibacterial properties
          </h2>

It helps in preventing one from some infections as it kills various bacteria, fungi, and viruses. Therefore dangerous infections like Staphylococcus Aureus and Candida Albicans are destroyed by Lauric Acid.
          </p>

          </p>
