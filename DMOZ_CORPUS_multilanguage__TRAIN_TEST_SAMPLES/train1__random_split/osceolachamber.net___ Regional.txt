                       <span class="article_text">
                        Osceola and South Mississippi County practice a tradition of fine, southern hospitality. This is a friendly, open community where you are always assured of a warm welcome. We take pride in maintaining our agricultural heritage while building a solid industrial base. We are a growing economy with a wealth of assets – mainline rail, interstate and river transportation on our doorstep, and a local airport – an ideal site for business and industry. Our local government is progressive and provides excellent city services.
                       </span>
                      </p>
                      <p>
                       The Osceola/South Mississippi County Chamber of Commerce works with industry, business, and residents to provide quality of life and economic opportunities. Established in 1947, the Chamber has a strong tradition of member volunteerism. Our Board of Directors meets the third Thursday of each month. We proudly preserve our heritage while building our future. You are most welcome to stop by any time or join us at any meetings or events.
                      </p>

                       </a>
