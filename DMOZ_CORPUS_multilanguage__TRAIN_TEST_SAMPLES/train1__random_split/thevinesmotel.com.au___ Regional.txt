         The Vines Motel and Cottages is a family owned and operated AAA 3.5 star rated, recently refurbished motel. The Property boasts 26 spacious rooms and two 3 bedroom cottages attached to the motel. Surrounded by beautiful gardens and only a short stroll to the gorgeous Quart Pot Creek.
