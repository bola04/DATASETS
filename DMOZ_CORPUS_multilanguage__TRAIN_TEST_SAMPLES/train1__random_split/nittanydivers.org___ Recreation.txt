            <h3>
             Nittany Divers SCUBA Still Exists
            </h3>
            <div class="article">
             Contrary to recent reports, the Nittany Divers SCUBA Club continues to remain a recognized student organization by Penn State University.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             Nittany Divers will remain open to all Penn State students interested in SCUBA diving, and will continue to run regular meetings and events for the 2018-2019 academic year and beyond. Our primary focus is fostering a SCUBA community for students at Penn State.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             Being a certified SCUBA diver is not a requirement for membership in Nittany Divers.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             Read more about
             <a href="http://www.nittanydivers.org/news/20180424-scuba-still-exists.php">
              the changes to Nittany Divers
             </a>
             following the University's risk assessment of student organizations.
            </div>
            <div class="posted">
             Posted: Wed Apr 25, 2018 at 12:10 am
             <br/>
             Posted by: Paul Rentschler
            </div>
