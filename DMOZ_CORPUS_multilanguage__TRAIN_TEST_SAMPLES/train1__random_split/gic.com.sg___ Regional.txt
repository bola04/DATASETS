         <h2>
          Sustainable returns above global inflation
         </h2>
         <p>
          We aim to generate sustainable long-term real returns. Over 20 years up to 31 March 2018, we achieved an annualised real rate of return of 3.4% per year. The focus is on disciplined long-term value investing to preserve the international purchasing power of the reserves we manage.
         </p>

          A focus on long-term drivers of return
         </h2>
         <p>
