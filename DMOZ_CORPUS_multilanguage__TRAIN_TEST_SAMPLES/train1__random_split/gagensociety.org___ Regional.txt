                     <h6>
                      SAVE THE DATE!
                     </h6>
                     <h6>
                      Georgia Genealogical Society Meeting - September 22, 2018
                     </h6>
                     <p>
                      Spend a day with David Rencher, Chief Genealogist at FamilySearch! Meeting is being co-sponsored with the Genealogical Society of Henry and Clayton Counties. The meeting will be held at the First Baptist Church in McDonough, GA.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      Check back for more detailed information.
                     </p>
                     <h6>
                     </h6>
                     <h6>
                      News From IGHR
                     </h6>
                     <p>
                      The Georgia Genealogical Society is the proud host of the Institute of Genealogy &amp; Historical Research (IGHR)!
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      <strong>
                       IGHR 2018 will take place Saturday, 2 June 2018-Thursday, 7 June 2018.
                      </strong>
                      <span>
                      </span>
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      <span>
                       <strong>
                        IGHR 2018 Registration closes 6 May 2018!
                       </strong>
                       To view
                      </span>
                      IGHR
                      <span>
                       ’s course offerings for 2018, as well as to learn more about the institute, please visit
                      </span>
                      IGHR
                      <span>
                       ’s website at
                      </span>
                      <a href="http://www.ighr.gagensociety.org">
                       http://www.ighr.gagensociety.org
                      </a>
                      <span>
                       .
                      </span>
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      Established in 1962, IGHR is an annual weeklong institute that allows genealogists––both hobbyists and professionals alike––to study one genealogy topic in depth. Attendees register for one course for the entire week. Courses take place at the University of Georgia Center for Continuing Education &amp; Hotel on the University of Georgia Campus in historic Athens, Georgia.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      IGHR regularly enrolls over 200 attendees from all over the United States––IGHR 2017 had 256 course attendees from 36 states plus Washington D.C. and Ontario, Canada! IGHR faculty is composed of outstanding nationally known genealogy educators.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      <strong>
                       Volunteer with IGHR!
                      </strong>
                     </p>

                      </a>
