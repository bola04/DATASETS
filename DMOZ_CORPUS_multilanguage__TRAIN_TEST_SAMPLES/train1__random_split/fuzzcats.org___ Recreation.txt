             The id tag on the pets protects and reunites them with the owner
            </a>
           </h2>
          </header>
          <!-- .entry-header -->
          <div class="entry-categories">
           <i class="fa fa-bookmark">

           <div class="ecae" style="text-align: justify">
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             May it be a dog or a cat, it is always fascinating to own and play with your pets. But, when you own a pet you also carry with you a responsibility and that is to get your dog registered with the appropriate authorities. In fact, several governments world over have introduced laws which make it mandatory for the owners to register their dogs/pets. In Australia for example, dogs that are more than three months old should be registered with the local Council. For this purpose the Australian Government has passed a law called as Domestic Animals Act 1994. According to the law, the dogs must have
             <a href="https://www.pawz.com.au/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
              dog id tags
             </a>
             around the collar and in addition to this you may also prefer to have a microchip tag.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="https://www.pawz.com.au/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
              <img alt="dog id tags" class="wp-image-97 aligncenter" height="174" sizes="(max-width: 395px) 100vw, 395px" src="https://www.fuzzcats.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/diesel-300x132.jpg" srcset="https://www.fuzzcats.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/diesel-300x132.jpg 300w, https://www.fuzzcats.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/diesel.jpg 599w" title="dog id tags" width="395"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <strong>
              Discount on registration:
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             Imagine a situation where your pet dog is lost; in such a situation when the dog is found, the dog id tags would help the authorities to reunite the dog with you. In the normal course, the owners normally buy pets that are between eight to twelve weeks. According to an article published by the Government of Victoria, Australia, dogs that are more than twelve weeks of age can be desexed. In fact, by doing so, the owners are also given discount on registering the pets.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <strong>
              Customized design:
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             If you are moving to a new location, then you will have to obtain a new registration number in the local council of the said new location. Once your pet is assigned a registration number, you may get the number embossed on a metal sheet. In fact, the dog id tags are made of non-corrosive metals like the aluminum or stainless steel. You find dog id tags are available in different designs and sizes. In fact, the manufacturers of the id tags customize the design depending on the size of your dog.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <strong>
              Some of the uses of id tags:
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             In case of any threat of diseases for the pets, the WA dog id tags would help the authorities in communicating with the owners of the dogs and educate them about the disease. Naturally, this will help the authorities to effectively manage the disease. In addition to this, the data collected from the id tags of dogs helps the authorities in Australia to set up animal refuge shelters at appropriate places. The shelters can be made use of by the owners of the pets. Visit at
             <a href="https://www.pawz.com.au/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
              Pawz
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <strong>
              Do you own more than one pet?
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             In case you own more than one pet, then according to law you will have to register each pet separately. In such cases, dog id tags in WA should be placed around the neck of each pet. Further, if the id tag placed around the neck is lost, you may approach the local Council and get a new tag by paying the appropriate fees.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <strong>
              Details available on id tags:
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             The id tags for pets contain the registration number and also the telephone number of the owner of the pet. It may also contain the zip code and the house number. However, microchipped id tags can be read only by the appropriate authorities by using the suitable equipment. In any case, the id tags on pets help in locating the owner in the earliest possible time. More info at
             <a href="https://www.pawz.com.au/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
              https://www.pawz.com.au/
             </a>
            </p>

           <div class="ecae" style="text-align: justify">
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             Grappling with your
             <a href="http://www.expressfitouts.com.au/">
              Melbourne office fitouts
             </a>
             ? The right kind of fit out plan can make a huge difference in your offices. With good choice of furniture for example, you can differentiate your business from that of your competitors and gain a competitive edge in your area of specialization. This is because customers judge more than just services and professionalism. Customers in the modern business environment are generally quite fickle minded and even the finest of details can make them change their minds about your products and services.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             Sometimes, this can be something as trivial as the kind of the commercial fit outs that you have in your office. So it is important to ensure that you get everything right and that you have plugged in all the holes when it comes to positioning yourself in the competitive markets. Good and trendy Melbourne office fitouts do more than just attract the customers. They also add certain intrinsic value to business. For example, they will help in boosting the productivity, keeping your employees for longer duration of times in the workplace and also increasing the motivation levels and the collaboration in your office.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             When it comes to the commercial office fit outs in Melbourne, there are numerous design trends that you can opt for. Here are some of the most popular Melbourne fit out trends that you can consider when you are planning to transform the look of your office â
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <strong>
              The open plan
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             An open office is one of the leading modern trends in office fit outs. It does way with the old cubicles that traditionally isolate employees from one another in order to give them a sense of privacy when they are working. Instead of seclusion, both the management and the employees are brought together under the open plan office. This trend comes with many advantages. For example, it creates more space in the office in order to accommodate more employees and it also makes the office look more spacious. The other advantage is that it facilitates better communication and collaboration in the office.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <strong>
              Modular plan
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             This is another major trend that when it comes to the modern commercial fit outs. In the modular design of offices, it is all about flexibility. The office walls and the workstations therein are ultra slim and also moveable. It is therefore possible to change the interior look of the office very quickly and without much effort. Like in the open plan of the office, the modular design allows for interconnectedness in the office as it gets rid of the secluded cubicles.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <strong>
              Ergonomic office
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             Ergonomic office design is all the rage these days. Ergonomic designs solve many of the issues and problems that you are likely to face by sitting too long at the office such as injuries, fatigue, back pains and many others. In order to boost the productivity of employees, a lot of business owners now invest in good quality ergonomic furniture.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             With the ergonomic furniture, it is possible to create conditions in which people can work productively as well as safely. The ergonomic designs will minimize fatigue along with injuries in the office environment. The ergonomic chair is always the centerpiece of ergonomic furniture since a lot of our times is spent sitting in the office. The ergonomic chair allows employees to sit in the right posture with knees along with the elbows at 90 degrees while the fleet is horizontally flat on the floor.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             These are some of the main trends that many companies opt for when it comes to the Melbourne office fitouts. You should, however, not be limited in any way. Choose the right theme that goes with your requirements and the brand that you wish to project to your customers.
            </p>

            </p>
            <p style="color: #000000; font-family: Arial, Verdana; font-size: 13.3333px; margin-bottom: 0in; text-align: justify;">
             <strong>
              Food Sanitation
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p style="color: #000000; font-family: Arial, Verdana; font-size: 13.3333px; margin-bottom: 0in; text-align: justify;">
             Humans love food and so do pests. Areas like food storage, kitchens and dining areas are often wrought with cockroaches and mice-coveting crumbs, spoiling food and other things.              Aside from food storage, the entire kitchen and dining area must also be kept clean. Food scraps, trash and crumbs are irresistible to pests so make sure to throw out the trash regularly. Trash bins are also prime targets for pests, so make sure to purchase bins that can be sealed tightly.
            </p>
            <p style="color: #000000; font-family: Arial, Verdana; font-size: 13.3333px; margin-bottom: 0in; text-align: justify;">
             <strong>
              Block All Possible Entrances
             </strong>

            <p style="color: #000000; font-family: Arial, Verdana; font-size: 13.3333px; margin-bottom: 0in; text-align: justify;">
             Even a home that starts out clean and pest-free can become a breeding ground for mice, cockroaches, bugs and mosquitoes if it is accessible. Ants, for example, will always lead you to their entry way if you follow their tracks. Seal holes and cracks as soon as possible.
            </p>
            <p style="color: #000000; font-family: Arial, Verdana; font-size: 13.3333px; margin-bottom: 0in; text-align: justify;">
             Some pests can enter your home through the most obvious manner â the front door. Avoid keeping the doors open for long periods of time. Some entry points are difficult for untrained professionals to see and block, and you may need the help of Gold Coast pest control experts to seal them all.
            </p>
            <p style="color: #000000; font-family: Arial, Verdana; font-size: 13.3333px; margin-bottom: 0in; text-align: justify;">
             <strong>
              Regular Home Maintenance
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p style="color: #000000; font-family: Arial, Verdana; font-size: 13.3333px; margin-bottom: 0in; text-align: justify;">
             Make it a habit to thoroughly clean your home every once in a while. Pests love clutter, so keep everything organized. As long as both parties perform their tasks dutifully, pest problems will be kept to a minimum.
