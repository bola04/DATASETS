          Welcome to Tameling’s Landscape Supply
         </h1>

         <div class="pf-content">
          <figure class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_2692" style="width: 150px">
           <a href="http://www.tamelings.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/th-e1486048883712.jpg">
            <img alt="" class="wp-image-2692 size-thumbnail" height="150" sizes="(max-width: 150px) 100vw, 150px" src="http://www.tamelings.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/th-e1486048883712-150x150.jpg" srcset="http://www.tamelings.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/th-e1486048883712-150x150.jpg 150w, http://www.tamelings.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/th-e1486048883712.jpg 208w" width="150"/>

          <p>
           Since 1947, Tameling Industries has supplied the Chicagoland area with quality landscape materials and products. At Tameling’s we are a one-stop shop for both landscapers and homeowners.  Not only does Tamelings have one of the largest selections of mulch, construction and  decorative stones, but we are one of the largest dealers of Belgard, Rosetta and Unilock products in the Chicagoland area.   Whether you are an homeowner working in your yard, or a certified landscaper, we offer all the products you need to get the job done correctly.
          </p>
          <p>
           Homeowners, do you have questions about how to create your  landscape paradise?   Not matter how big or small your landscape project is, our sales staff will be happy to answer any questions you have and show all the products you need to create your landscape paradise.  Tameling Industries has been helping homeowners and landscapers for over 70 years and has an A+ rating with the Better Business Bureau.
           <a href="http://www.bbb.org/chicago/business-reviews/mulches/tameling-industries-in-willowbrook-il-88289488" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">
            www.bbb.org/chicago/business-reviews/mulches/tameling-industries-in-willowbrook-il-88289488
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Tamelings offers pick-up or delivery of all the products and materials necessary to make your yard a landscaped paradise.  During our busy season, please place your order a couple of days in advance.  We do our best to deliver the material where you want it placed, without causing damage to property and our trucks.  However;  space and the size of our trucks are going to determine where we can safely place the material.  Click on the link to see the sizes of the trucks we use for deliveries and for truck capacities .
           <a href="http://www.tamelings.com/about-us/deliveries/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">
            http://www.tamelings.com/about-us/deliveries/
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Don’t know how big of a pile  a ton of stone or yard of mulch is? Click on the link below and see how big of a pile a ton of stone, yard of mulch and dirt look like.  A good starting point in figuring out how much you need is a yard of much, dirt and ton of stone will cover 100 sqft at 2 inches deep.
           <a href="http://www.tamelings.com/about-us/faq/how-much-will-my-truck-hold/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">
            www.tamelings.com/about-us/faq/how-much-will-my-truck-hold/
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Looking for design ideas.  We have updated our indoor showroom floor with the latest Belgard, Rosetta and Unilock pavers.   In addition to our new showroom floor, we have twenty display racks, installed pathways and patios and outdoor element displays.  All of  displays are labeled with the product name and color. You are more than welcome to stop by after hours to browse our vast selection .  Click on the link below for Tameling’s outdoor displays.
