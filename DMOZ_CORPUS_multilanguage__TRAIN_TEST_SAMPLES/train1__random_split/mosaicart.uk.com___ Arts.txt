          What is mosaic art?
         </strong>

        <li>

        </li>
        Cobbles or pebbles come in many colours, shapes and sizes
        <li>
         We can design a mosaic to suit your requirements or follow a design of pattern specified
        </li>
        <li>
         Mosaics can be any size or shape from 30 centimetres to 30 metres diameter, the choice is yours!
        </li>
        <li>
         Distance is no object, we can design and install a mosaic any where in the World
        </li>
        <li>
         We also specialise in wall and floor tile mosaics, utilising both whole and cracked tiles
