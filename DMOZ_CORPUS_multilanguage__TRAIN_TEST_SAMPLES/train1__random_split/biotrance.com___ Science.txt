       <h3>
        Welcome to Biotrance
       </h3>
       <p align="justify">
        <br/>
        The proteomics market has experienced significant growth in the past few years, mainly due to the increasing need of proteomic outcomes in diagnosis, drug discovery, and various applicable advancements in technologies needed to perform proteomic services. The current market was valued $19.4 billion in 2014 and is expected to grow at a healthy CAGR from 2013 to 2018.
       </p>
       <p align="justify">
        Also, the transformation of the biopharmaceutical industry’s focus from genomics to system biology has enormously increased the importance of proteomics. However, the lack of comprehensive services research pertaining to the proteomics segment has always been a restraint for researchers. Therefore, Biotrance is being conceptualized to bring world class companies offering proteomics solutions under one roof for the prime research centers in India.
       </p>
       <p align="justify">
        At Biotrance, we are committed to the promotion of innovative solutions that are critical to life science research. Our vision is to identify products in emerging technologies that support sophisticated research in protein study. It is our endeavor to be more prompt and innovative than the traditional mode of serving the customers.
       </p>
       <p align="justify">
        We are centric and focused towards the promotion of companies who are majorly in to manufacturing of various life science products and services especially from the Proteomics domain.
       </p>
