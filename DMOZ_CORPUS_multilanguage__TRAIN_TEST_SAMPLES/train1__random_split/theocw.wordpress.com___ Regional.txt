       <p>
        10 years after
        <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/oxfordcycleworkshop/sets/72157608208256922/" target="_blank" title="Memories of Oxford Cycle Workshop">
         Oxford Cycle Workshop
        </a>
        opened it’s doors to offer the cycling community of Oxford support, empowerment and services not available in our beautiful city, we have sold our last recycled bicycle and serviced our last customer’s steed.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Training, events and mobile mechanic services continue
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.ocwt.coop" target="_blank" title="Oxford Cycle Workshop Training Ltd">
         Oxford Cycle Workshop Training
        </a>
        our community owned co-op continue to offer training.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.cycleoxford.com" target="_blank" title="Cycle Oxford CC">
         Cycle Oxford CC
        </a>
        , well know for it’s Oxford cycling social events continue their good work.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="mailto:omcr@btinternet.com">
         Oxford Mobile Cycle Repairs
        </a>
        continue to offer mobile cycle mechanic services.
       </p>
       <p>
        However in the Autumn of 2011 Oxford Cycle Workshop Ltd ceased trading.
       </p>

      <div class="meta clear">

      <div class="entry clear">
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.ocwt.coop" target="_blank" title="Oxford Cycle Workshop Training">
         Oxford Cycle Workshop Training
        </a>
        continues to offer a wide range of services to support, empower and unite the bicycle using community here in Oxford, whether you consider yourself a cyclist or not.
       </p>
       <p>
        Whether you’re looking for tuition in mending a puncture or a professional cycle mechanic qualification Oxford Cycle Workshop Training has
        <a href="http://bookwhen.com/ocwt" target="_blank" title="Course Calendar">
         something for you
        </a>
        , including a growing range of
        <a href="http://www.cycleoxford.com/app" target="_blank" title="Smartphone Apps">
         smartphone apps.
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        They also offer a range of other forms of support, like
        <a href="http://www.ocwt.coop/workshop_access/" target="_blank" title="Workshop Access">
         access to workshop space
        </a>
        , tools and support to fix your own bike.
       </p>
       <span class="embed-youtube" style="text-align:center; display: block;">
        <iframe allowfullscreen="true" class="youtube-player" height="368" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/zDf6C4g66VM?version=3&amp;rel=1&amp;fs=1&amp;autohide=2&amp;showsearch=0&amp;showinfo=1&amp;iv_load_policy=1&amp;wmode=transparent" style="border:0;" type="text/html" width="600">
        </iframe>
       </span>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.ocwt.coop" target="_blank" title="Oxford Cycle Workshop Training">
         Oxford Cycle Workshop Training
        </a>
        is a community owned co-operative with 450 members who keep the organisation connected to the needs of the community it serves. Curious about membership benefits like cargo bike hire, discounts on coffee at Oxfork and Zappi’s Cafe, then
        <a href="http://www.cycleoxford.com/membership" target="_blank" title="Become a Member of OCWT">
         take a look here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        Events is another big part of what Oxford Cycle Workshop Training’s members can get involved with, including Alleycat bicycle treasure hunts, bike polo and bicycle scrabble. Watch these videos to get a flavor, or visit the
        <a href="http://www.cycleoxford.com" target="_blank" title="Cycle Oxford Website">
         Cycle Oxford CC website
        </a>
        for info.

         Oxford Cycle Workshop
        </a>
        opened it’s doors to offer the cycling community of Oxford support, empowerment and services not available in our beautiful city, we have sold our last recycled bicycle and serviced our last customer’s steed.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="" class="alignleft" height="391" src="https://i0.wp.com/farm2.static.flickr.com/1280/4552040905_71422fe027.jpg" title="OCW Shop Front" width="500"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Oxford Cycle Workshop will forever have a very special place in our hearts as a hub of positive energy generating so many incredible projects, events &amp; people who continue to work in Oxford and beyond.
       </p>
       <p>
        We were the first organisation to offer mobile mechanic services in Oxford. A service that
        <a href="https://theocw.wordpress.com/2011/10/20/mobile-cycle-mechanic-services/" title="Mobile Mechanic Services">
         James Dawton
        </a>
        continues to offer our city with the highest standards and quality advice.
       </p>
       <p>
        We were also the first organisation to offer our community training in how to fix and look after their bicycles, a project that grew into the setting up of the vibrant community owned co-operative
        <a href="http://www.ocwt.coop" target="_blank" title="Oxford Cycle Workshop Training">
         Oxford Cycle Workshop Training
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        Oxford Cycle Workshop was also the focal point of a new energy for social cycling events in Oxford, becoming the birth place of
        <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/oxfordcycleworkshop/sets/72157606162403548/" target="_blank" title="Oxford Alleycat Flickr">
         Oxford Alleycats
        </a>
        , Oxford’s
        <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/oxfordcycleworkshop/sets/72157608215660799/" target="_blank" title="Oxford Bike Polo">
         Bike Polo Group
        </a>
        and
        <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/oxfordcycleworkshop/sets/72157625407765393/" target="_blank" title="Bicycle Scrabble">
         Bicycle Scrabble
        </a>
        . All of these event continue to take place regularly, and with the same passion and exuberance thanks to the work of
        <a href="http://www.cycleoxford.com" target="_blank" title="Cycle Oxford">
         Cycle Oxford CC
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        Sadly the one service that has become increasing difficult to offer within Oxford over the last few years is Bicycle Recycling. Difficulty sourcing bikes for recycling and processing bikes at an affordable price have become challenges we were unable to overcome. We wish all those keen to generate recycled bikes for the people of Oxford all the best in that most valuable work.
       </p>
      </div>
