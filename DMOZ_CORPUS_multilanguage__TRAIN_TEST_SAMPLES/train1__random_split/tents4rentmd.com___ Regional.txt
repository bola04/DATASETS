                <p>

                 <strong>
                  We are Sam and Faith Kern of Centreville, Maryland
                 </strong>
                 <strong>
                  .
                 </strong>
                 Sam was born and raised on the Eastern Shore spending a few years on the Western Shore as a teenager helping run his Dad's amusement company. Since Sam was a child, his life- long dream was to become a Police Officer. In 2001 he pursued that dream by moving back to the Eastern Shore and becoming a Police Officer for the Town of Centreville. Due to a decrease in pay when becoming an officer, we decided we needed a little something else for income so in the Spring of 2002 we bought two used tents and hung flyers all over town!
                </p>

We are looking forward to serving you!
