     </h2>
     <h1>
      The Wylie Inn and Conference Center at Endicott College
     </h1>
     <p>
      Situated on a picturesque stretch of the Atlantic Coast on Boston’s   North Shore, the Wylie Inn and Conference Center at Endicott College   offers well appointed, comfortable accommodations with an on-site café   restaurant and bar; state of the art
      <a href="http://www.wyliecenter.com/iacc-conference-center.shtml">
       IACC certified conference center
      </a>
      and an elegant social event venue,
      <a href="http://www.wyliecenter.com/tuppersite/">
       Tupper Manor
      </a>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
      Located just 20 miles north of Boston, in the historic town of Beverly,   the Wylie Inn and Conference Center is ideal for a weekend getaway,   retreat or conference.  This lush 10-acre inn and conference center is   just minutes away from historic downtown Salem, restaurants, scenic Cape   Ann, shopping, museums, and more; all offering visitors to the North   Shore endless sights, sounds, tastes and experiences.
     </p>

Convenient to the entrance of Endicott   College and just 2 miles from the center of Beverly, MA, the Wylie Inn   and Conference Center has made its mark over time as a charming spot for   tourists and a state of the art facility for the business traveler.
