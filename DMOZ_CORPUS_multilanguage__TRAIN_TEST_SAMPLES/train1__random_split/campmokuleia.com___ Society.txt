        <h5>
         E Komo Mai...
        </h5>
        <p>
         Come, You Are Welcome to Camp Mokule`ia! We're a historic beachfront youth camp and adult retreat on Oahu's beautiful North Shore, just 40 minutes from downtown Honolulu, with cabins, private rooms, tent camping, a beach house and more. Camp Mokule`ia's secluded location and attentive staff assures that your kids, students, ohana, company or group will enjoy the rest, recreation and renewal we offer in the spirit of aloha.
        </p>
        <p>
         In the Hawaiian language, Mokule`ia means a place of abundance, which is exactly what we strive to be for all of our guests – a Hawaiian-style place where your spirits, your bellies and your ability to play are all abundantly filled. So please visit us soon, there's always a place for you at Camp Mokule`ia!
        </p>

        </p>

       <div class="grid-4">
        <h5>
         E Wahi Pana...
        </h5>
        <p>
         A Sacred Place, defined by simple beauty and a graceful pace, Camp Mokule`ia is located on the beautiful, pristine North Shore of Oahu at the foot of the Waianae Mountain Range. Our oceanfront location, with more than ¾ of a mile of beach to walk on is home to honu (sea turtles) and the occasional monk seal. It is ideal for swimming, snorkeling, kayaking, surfing, or relaxing to the gentle lap of waves, the splendor of the rising and setting sun, or stargazing in the quiet night. Nearby, there are trails for hiking and biking, a natural area reserve and even the opportunity for skydiving, gliding, and horseback riding.
        </p>
        <p>
         Camp Mokule`ia also structures powerful programs for groups utilizing the stunning environmental setting, our High and Low Challenge Courses, our commitment to sustainability, and our aloha for the wisdom, traditions and history of these islands. So please come and experience why generations of kama'aina have made Camp Mokule`ia their own special place kahakai – by the sea.
        </p>
