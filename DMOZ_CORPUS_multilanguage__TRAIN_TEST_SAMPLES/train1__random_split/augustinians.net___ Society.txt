        <div class="welcomewrap">
         <h2 class="mtop0">
          Welcome to our Website!
         </h2>
         <div class="welcometext">
          <p>
           There are many ways to describe the Augustinian Order, to speak of its character, its history, its mission, its charism. First and foremost, however, the Order is a community of men and women who, in the words of the Rule we profess, seek to
           <em>
            «live together in harmony, being of one mind and one heart on the way to God.»
           </em>
           We are Christians who, captivated by the example and teaching of Saint Augustine, journey together, as we build our house and serve the Lord's people.
          </p>
          <p>
           This site is designed especially for members of the Augustinian Family, and at the same time we hope that other visitors will feel at home and be able to learn something about who we are -- our life, our spirituality, our commitment to building community. And while you are here, you will also discover something about the myriad of ways that we serve others throughout the world.
          </p>
         </div>
