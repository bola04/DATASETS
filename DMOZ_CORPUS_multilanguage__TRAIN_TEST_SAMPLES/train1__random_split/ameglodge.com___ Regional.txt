          <p style="color:white">
           Ameg Lodge Kilimanjaro is based at the foot of Mt. Kilimanjaro in Northern Tanzania.  Set in a 4.5 acre area, it has been constructed with local Tanzanian materials and designed to combine antique African, and modern contemporary styles, creating an articulate and seductive ambiance.
          </p>
          <p style="color:white">
           Ameg Lodge is ideal for those visiting Moshi and its surrounding areas.  It is the perfect base for those taking on the Kilimanjaro Challenge and is indeed popular for the pre-climb night as well as the ‘Celebration Night’ on the return from the Kilimanjaro trek.
          </p>
          <p style="color:white">
           Whilst the lodge is located just a few minutes drive from Moshi Town Centre, the tranquil setting of the Lodge creates an atmosphere of being in the beautiful remote African Bush.  This unique feeling and the special Ameg Lodge touch has made it the preferred choice of accommodation for the business and corporate traveller as well.
          </p>
          <p style="color:white">
           The Lodge is also in close proximity to the Kilimanjaro Christian Medical Centre (KCMC) and the International School Moshi (ISM).
