     <h1>
      Maine Waterfront Real Estate
     </h1>
     <div id="layer1" style="position: absolute; width: 100px; height: 43px; z-index: 1; left: 628px; top: 20px">
      <a href="signup.php">
       <img alt="Sign up for Updates" class="style2" height="42" src="images/signup.png" title="Sign up for Updates" width="139"/>
      </a>
     </div>
     <p>
      <img alt="Maine Waterfront Real Estate" src="images/Milbridge.jpg" style="border-style: solid; border-width: 1px; float: right; height: 241px; width: 400px; margin-left: 5px; margin-right: 5px"/>
      Milbridge 
 		is a small, self-sufficient coastal downeast community with approximately 1400 
 		year round residents. Milbridge is located on the coast in Washington 
 		County Maine. It is the hub for goods and services for the 
 		surrounding downeast communities of Steuben, Cherryfield, Harrington, 
 		Addison, Columbia and Columbia Falls. We have medical facilities, grocery stores, 
 		building and contracting suppliers, banking services, restaurants, and gift 
 		shops.
     </p>
     <p>
      Step back in time and catch your breath. There are no traffic lights 
 		or traffic jams. This area is virtually free of air pollution  and has a 
 		low crime rate. Property real estate taxes are reasonable and the properties are 
 		affordable. There is no greater place to retire seasonally or live 
 		year-round!
     </p>
     <p>
      We have over 75 miles of irregular saltwater shoreline frontage, a class "A" river,  the 
 		Narraguagus, which is famous for its Atlantic salmon and striped 
 		bass. Visit our public harbor with dock and moorings where it is not 
 		uncommon to view seals at play and bald eagles fishing.  Sea kayaking, 
 		sailing, boating, and island exploring add to the recreational 
 		enjoyment.
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="Maine Waterfront Real Estate" height="475" src="images/map.gif" style="float: right" width="325"/>
      Our comfortable summers with average temperatures of 70 degrees and 
 		the gentle sea breeze makes it very pleasant. Winters along the 
 		Downeast coast are cold, but not 
 		harsh. Snowfall average is 30" per year. Milbridge is situated on US Rt 
 		1, 30 miles east of Ellsworth, and 45 miles northeast of
      <a href="http://www.nps.gov/acad/">
       Acadia National Park in Bar Harbor
      </a>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
      Visit our proud coastal Maine town. Stay in our service motel,
      <a href="http://www.redbarnmotel.com/" target="_blank">
       The Red Barn 
 		Motel
      </a>
      , one of the nearby campgrounds or B &amp; Bs.  Enjoy our 
 		five 
 		restaurants:
      <a href="http://www.milbridgehouse.com/">
       The Milbridge House
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="https://www.facebook.com/thewheelhousecafe/" target="_blank">
       The Wheelhouse
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="https://www.facebook.com/vazquezmex/" target="_blank">
       Vazquez 
 		Mexican Takeout
      </a>
      , the
      <a href="https://www.facebook.com/Good-N-Plenty-Buffet-108138033214641/" target="_blank">
       Good' N Plenty Buffet
      </a>
      , and
      <a href="http://www.44-degrees-north.com">
       44 Degrees North
      </a>
      (sorry 
 		... no McDonalds nearby), or visit the
      <a href="http://www.theriverlily.com/">
       Riverlily
      </a>
      , a fabulous gift 
 		shop right in the center of town.
     </p>
     <p>
      Schools: K-6th grade are located in Milbridge.  Grades 7 through 12 
 		are six miles away at
      <a href="http://www.sad37.com/">
       Narraguagus High 
 		School
      </a>
      in Harrington.
      <a href="http://www.umm.maine.edu">
       The 
 		University of Maine
      </a>
      is 26 miles away in Machias.
     </p>
     <p>
      Medical Facilities: In Milbridge we have eye doctor, medical 
 		doctor, dentist, chiropractor, pharmacy,
      <a href="http://dech.org">
       Milbridge Medical Center
      </a>
      , plus 
 		a veterinarian.
      <a href="http://www.harringtonfamilyhealth.org">
       Harrington Family Health Center
      </a>
      in Harrington,
      <a href="http://www.mcmhospital.org/">
       Maine Coast 
 		Memorial Hospital
      </a>
      in Ellsworth,
      <a href="http://www.dech.org">
       Down 
 		East Community Hospital
      </a>
      in Machias, and
      <a href="http://emmc.org">
       Eastern Maine Medical Center
      </a>
      in Bangor.
     </p>
     <p>
      Economy:  Fishing, clamming, lobster fishing, blueberries
      <a href="http://www.maine.gov/local/washington/">
       Washington 
 		County
      </a>
      has the largest concentration of low bush wild blueberries in 
 		the world) and cottage industries such as Christmas wreaths, crafts, 
 		artists, etc.
     </p>
     <p>
      There are local contractors available to clear your house site, drill 
 		your well and build your dream home.  We will be more than happy to help 
 		you with these matters.
     </p>
     <p>
      Check out Robertson Sea Tours (
      <a href="http://www.robertsonseatours.com">
       www.robertsonseatours.com
      </a>
      ) 
 		for whale watching, Puffin and Sea Bird viewing, even Lobster Fishing.
     </p>
     <p>
      There are also extensive trails,
      <a href="http://www.sunrisetrails.com">
       Sunrise Trails
      </a>
      and
      <a href="http://www.mcht.org/index.php">
       Maine Coast Heritage Trust 
 		Trails
      </a>
      , in our area to experience nature and enjoy the uniqueness of 
 		the region.  Such as the
      <a href="http://www.pleasantbay.com">
       Pleasant Bay B&amp;B and Llama Farm
      </a>
      situated on a Farm like setting on 
 		the shores of Peasant Bay, or
      <a href="http://www.oceanspraycottages.com">
       Ocean Spray Cottage
      </a>
      in 
 		Harrington for weekly rentals offering an ideal spot on the rockbound 
 		coast of Harrington.  If camping is your forte, just down the road is 
 		Sunset Point Campground
      <a href="http://www.sunsetpointcampground.com">
       Sunset Point Campground
      </a>
      with direct water access for kayaking, 
 		canoeing and much more. Further to the east is the
      <a href="http://www.blueberrypatchmotel.com">
       Blueberry Patch Motel &amp; 
 		Cabins
      </a>
      with the White House Restaurant next door.
     </p>
     <p>
      Come to Milbridge, Maine and enjoy life in the slow lane.  The way 
 		life should be.
     </p>
