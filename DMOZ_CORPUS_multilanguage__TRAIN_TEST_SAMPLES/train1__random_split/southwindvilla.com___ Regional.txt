       A Southwind Caribbean Vacation
      </h2>
      <p>
       Southwind Villa is a luxury
       <i>
        vacation rental
       </i>
       located in the Mafolie Estates section of St. Thomas in the U.S. Virgin Islands (USVI) overlooking the main harbor of Charlotte Amalie and the beautiful Caribbean. If you are looking for a luxury Caribbean vacation at your own private villa, you've come to the right place!
      </p>
      <blockquote>
       “Caribbean villa rentals provide a vacation experience unlike a hotel or resort.”
      </blockquote>
      <h2>
       Vacation rental luxury and privacy
      </h2>
      <p style="margin-bottom: 0;">
       When you enter Southwind you will find a very private, lush world that is yours alone.
      </p>
      <div class="text-center" style="margin:40px 0">
       <img alt="Southwind Rental Villa aerial view" data-interchange="[images/sw-villa-aerial-480.jpg, (default)], [images/sw-villa-aerial-640.jpg, (medium)]"/>
      </div>
      <p>
       A large open-air terrace, furnished for lounging and dining, overlooks a 20x40 foot private, solar heated swimming pool. The ever-changing harbor view is the perfect spot for your Caribbean vacation rental home. Whether you go out and enjoy the beaches and water sports of St. Thomas and the rest of the U.S. Virgin Islands all day and return for dinner, or just chill and decompress during the whole day, this will be your favorite Caribbean vacation spot.
      </p>
      <h2>
       St. Thomas - US Virgin Island vacation rental paradise
      </h2>
      <p>
       The beautiful cruise ships, the sailboats, and the seaplane provide continuous activity in the blue Caribbean below your private villa. As the sun sets, the lights of town come up and a completely different picture emerges. The cruise ships set sail, all lit up as though for your enjoyment.
      </p>
      <p>
       Begin your adventure with some information about the U.S. Virgin Island
       <a href="about-st-thomas" title="About St Thomas US Virgin Islands">
        St. Thomas

       , or learn just how affordable your luxury Caribbean vacation rental is on our
       <a href="vacation-rental-rates" title="Caribbean vacation villa rental rates">
        villa rates, services, and policies

       page.
      </p>
      <h2>
       Caribbean Villa Rentals
      </h2>
      <p>
       A Caribbean villa rental provides a vacation experience unlike a hotel or resort. Firstly, it gives a family or group of friends complete privacy. You are on your own schedule, not one dictated by the hotel's hours of operation. Caribbean luxury villa rentals offer a setting that only the finest hotels could achieve at a fraction of the cost. Southwind Villa is fully equipped, expertly decorated and furnished for your comfort and enjoyment. Additionally, it is filled with Caribbean artwork and hand painted stencils throughout. From almost every room including the kitchen, one has a view of the harbor of Charlotte Amalie, argueably one of the finest views in the Caribbean.
      </p>
      <div class="text-center" style="margin:40px 0">
       <img alt="Southwind Rental Villa terrace dining view" data-interchange="[images/sw-villa-terrace-480.jpg, (default)], [images/sw-villa-terrace-640.jpg, (medium)]"/>
      </div>
      <p>
       While villa rentals in the Caribbean are abundant on the many islands that exist, we believe that St. Thomas blends the best of all.
      </p>
      <ul id="villa_benefits">
       <li>
        It is a U.S. Territory which has U.S. law, U.S. Postal Service, U.S. currency and direct dial phone service like that of any U.S. state.
       </li>
       <li>
        There is frequent, direct airline service from all the major Eastern U.S. cities: New York, Philadelphia, Boston, D.C., Charlotte, Atlanta, Ft. Lauderdale and Miami.
       </li>
       <li>
        While all Caribbean rental villas offer some beach access, St. Thomas has the beautiful beaches (including world famous Magen's Bay Beach) plus the challenging Mahogany Run 18-hole golf course and a lively city of shops and restaurants all within easy reach. Some of the finest fashion and jewelry duty-free stores are represented on their old cobble stoned streets. Buildings built in the late 1800s from stone ships ballast provide a colorful, quaint setting for high end stores, coffee shops and restaurants.
       </li>
       <li>
        A fleet of sailing and fishing boats for hire, parasailing, snorkeling and wind surfing are some of the many water sports available. Kayaking in the mangroves by day or from Frenchman's Cove at night in the harbor is extremely popular.
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       When one chooses, you return to your private, secluded Caribbean rental villa to enjoy the heated pool, the lush garden setting and the spectacular view. We believe that once you experience your first Caribbean villa rental, you will never settle for a hotel again. It will feel like having your own villa in the Caribbean without the cost or complications.
      </p>
