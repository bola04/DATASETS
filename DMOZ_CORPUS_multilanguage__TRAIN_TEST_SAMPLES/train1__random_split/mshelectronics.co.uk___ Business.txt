      <h1>
       Contract Electronics Manufacturing Services in the UK
      </h1>
      <article class="clearfix post-6 page type-page status-publish hentry" id="post-6" role="article">
       <section class="post-content intro-content">
        <p>
         Located in Wimborne (nr Poole) in Dorset, MSH Electronics Ltd provide a wide range of
         <strong>
          Contract Electronic Manufacturing services (CEM)
         </strong>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         MSH are one of the UK’s leading Contract Electronic Manufacturers and over the past 25 years we have provided our exceptional quality and competitively priced sub contract electronic manufacturing services to a wide range of industry leading companies throughout the whole of the UK.
        </p>
        <p>
         A selection of our most common contract electronics manufacturing services are:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Custom Wire and Cable Assemblies
         </li>
         <li>
          PCB Manufacturing and Assembly Services
         </li>
         <li>
          Electrical Control Panel Manufacturing
         </li>
         <li>
          Module Assembly
         </li>
         <li>
          Electronic Assembly Prototyping
         </li>
         <li>
          Electromechanical Assembly
         </li>
         <li>
          Electronic Prototyping Services
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <br/>
         As contract electronic manufacturers, the completed products MSH produce end up serving Defence, Communications, Automotive, Security, Oil and Business sectors.
        </p>
        <p>
         Our mission is to provide you with a competitive advantage in your served market by providing high quality contract electronics manufacturing at a competitive price, and in return, to become an integral part of your supply chain.
        </p>
        <p>
         Our staff are fully trained and approved to IPC-620*, Classes 1, 2 and 3 and are accustomed to working to the exacting standards and requirements commanded and expected by our customers.
        </p>
        <p>
         The company has a proven record of on time delivery whilst still maintaining the highest quality and conformance of its electronics manufacturing services.
        </p>
        <p>
         * IPC-620 (IPC/WHMA-A-620 requirements for the acceptance for cable and wire harness assemblies)
        </p>
       </section>
      </article>
