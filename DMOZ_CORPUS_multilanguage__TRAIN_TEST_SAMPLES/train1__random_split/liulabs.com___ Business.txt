          <b>
           LIU RESEARCH LABORATORIES, LLC
          </b>
          <font size="2">
           is a research and 
 development company in the fields of pyrometry, gemology, color science and 
 optics with 6 US patents and one patent in pending.
          </font>
          <p>
           <font size="2">
            We provide the most advanced alexandrite effect
            <a href="Spectropyrometer.htm">
             spectropyrometer
            </a>
            for high to ultrahigh temperature measurement from 800 K to 
 100,000K,  and the
            <a href="spectrometer.htm">
             MDIS-f8 multifunction dual integrating sphere spectrometer
            </a>
            for spectral measurement, color measurement,
            <a href="fluorescence_spectroscopy.htm">
             UV fluorescence measurement
            </a>
            ,
            <a href="fluorescence_spectroscopy.htm">
             photoluminescence measurement
            </a>
            , color 
 grading of gemstones, color grading of colored diamonds, color grading of 
 jadeite, and alexandrite effect grading. The spectrometer can measure the 
 fluorescence of liquid and soft tissue easily, and can be used in the 
 biological, 
 biochemical, pharmaceutical and medical research fields, as well as medical 
 examination and other industrial applications.
           </font>
          </p>
          <p>
           <font size="2">
            We provide the state-of-the-art technology and 
 methods obtained from our inventions and innovations 
 by research and development, and the best services of pyrometry, gemology, and 
 optics by knowledge, experience and instrumentation.
