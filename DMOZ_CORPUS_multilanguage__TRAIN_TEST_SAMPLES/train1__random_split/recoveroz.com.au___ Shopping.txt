               <p style="margin-bottom: 1em;">

               </p>

                Our hands-on program takes a comprehensive and intensive approach to addiction recovery. The RecoverOz Urban Rehab offers community-based suburban living in well maintained houses, providing long-term rehabilitation and stability. Our houses are within close proximity to one another and the rehab centre. This allows clients to meet in a group setting during the day to undertake the structured rehabilitation and counselling, and be taken to evening 12 Step meetings at night.
