     <h1 class="h16645" itemprop="headline">
      Welcome
     </h1>
     <span class="txt tx1" itemprop="articleBody">
      <p>
       Our new website provides news, events and information about our church, beliefs and mission. To navigate around this site please click on the red buttons above.
      </p>
      <p>
       Audlem is a small village in south Cheshire, UK, and its Methodist Church is part of the British Methodist Church. Within Audlem there are three churches: the Methodist Church, Audlem Baptist Church and the Parish Church of St James the Great. The churches work together to serve the village. Audlem is part of the Cheshire South Circuit.
      </p>
      <p>
       Though theologically a fairly broad church, Audlem probably tends more towards a liberal than an evangelical perspective. The worship style — including the music — would be considered traditional. The Church strives to do things properly, yet in a relaxed and informal manner. It hopes it is a welcoming and accepting community.
      </p>
     </span>
