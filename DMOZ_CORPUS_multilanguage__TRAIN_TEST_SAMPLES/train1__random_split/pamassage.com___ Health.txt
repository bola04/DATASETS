 611.

 Institute is comprised of 5400 square feet and is equipped with two
 lecture halls, including a Massage Classroom, an Anatomy and Physiology Classroom, four
 clinic rooms, a kitchenette, a student lounge and administrative
 offices.  The Institute is located several yards from the highway,
 which enables the students to enjoy a quiet atmosphere, which is
 quite conducive to learning.  There are ample parking facilities and
 several restaurants that are conveniently located within walking
 distance of the school.
