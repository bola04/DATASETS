            <a href="http://atthewoodscabin.com/welcome-to-at-the-woods-cabin/">
             Welcome to “At The Woods Cabin”
            </a>
           </h2>
           <div class="post-byline">
            Published February 2, 2011 by henrymark
           </div>
           <span class="post-comments-link closed">
            <a href="http://atthewoodscabin.com/welcome-to-at-the-woods-cabin/#respond">
             <i class="fa fa-comment" title="comment icon">
             </i>
             <span>
              0
             </span>
            </a>
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="featured-image">
           <a href="http://atthewoodscabin.com/welcome-to-at-the-woods-cabin/">
            Welcome to “At The Woods Cabin”
            <img alt="At The Woods Cabin" class="attachment-full size-full wp-post-image" height="450" sizes="(max-width: 800px) 100vw, 800px" src="http://atthewoodscabin.com/file/2018/04/At-The-Woods-Cabin-1.jpg" srcset="http://atthewoodscabin.com/file/2018/04/At-The-Woods-Cabin-1.jpg 800w, http://atthewoodscabin.com/file/2018/04/At-The-Woods-Cabin-1-300x169.jpg 300w, http://atthewoodscabin.com/file/2018/04/At-The-Woods-Cabin-1-768x432.jpg 768w" width="800"/>
           </a>
          </div>
          <div class="post-content">
           <p>
            The large cabin welcomes you with full-fledged amenities including fireplace, TV with dish network and all the Pittsburgh channels for the visitors who are a fan of
            <a href="https://www.visitpittsburgh.com/things-to-do/pittsburgh-sports-teams/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
             Pittsburgh sports
            </a>
            or the players, DVD player to enjoy the favorite CD, laundry with washers and dryers, shower with hot bathtub, and the clean and cosy bedrooms for maximum 12 people at the time. The cabin surroundings are worth watching, the sitting area outside the cabin is worth time spending.
           </p>
           <h2>
            <img alt="At The Woods Cabin" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12" height="436" src="http://atthewoodscabin.com/file/2018/04/At-The-Woods-Cabin-3.png" width="779"/>
            About the Facilities
           </h2>
           <ul>
            <li>
             Smoking is not permitted in the cabin, but if you are addicted to smoking then you can smoke out the cabin there is a spacious area to walk freely. But still you are not allowed to through the tobacco pieces anywhere, it is better to trash in the bin.
            </li>
            <li>
             The bedrooms are fully equipped with bed-sheets, pillows, comforts and of course the mattresses. The bed sizes are including, King size bed, Queen size bed, Day bed, full-size futon, full sleeping sofa, and 2 rolling beds are available in the well-built cabin.
            </li>
            <li>
             We offer all the basic cookware and bedding for the maximum comfort and fine experience.
            </li>
            <li>
             <img alt="At The Woods Cabin" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-11" height="173" src="http://atthewoodscabin.com/file/2018/04/At-The-Woods-Cabin-2-300x173.png" width="300"/>
             The kitchen is also well equipped with all the basic needs such as fridge, stove, washing detergent and dishwasher.
            </li>
            <li>
             The laundry room is also a fine with washer and dryer but the cloth washing detergent is not included so it is better to visit with own washing detergent.
            </li>
            <li>
             Outside grill is available for having fun outside the cabin while campfire.
            </li>
           </ul>
           <h2>
            The Coyote and Wolves Reply
           </h2>
           <p>
            The cabin is surrounding with the trees, and grass in the ground that is also worth enjoying if you are affectionate with nature. If you are a Coyote or animal lover then you can ask the cabin owner to hear the response of Coyotes and Wolves which is also worth hearing.
            <a href="https://www.tripadvisor.com/Attraction_Review-g53682-d1929985-Reviews-The_Farmer_s_Inn-Sigel_Pennsylvania.html" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
             The Farmer's Inn is located at the next door
            </a>
            so you can visit that too. We welcome you to our cabin for lodging if you are
            <a href="https://www.discovertheburgh.com/project/plan-a-trip/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
             planning to visit Pittsburgh
            </a>
            and its
            <a href="http://atthewoodscabin.com/things-to-do-in-pittsburgh/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
             other activity areas
            </a>
            .
           </p>
          </div>

           </div>

           <a href="http://atthewoodscabin.com/at-the-woods-campground/">
            At the Woods Campground
            <img alt="At the Woods Campground" class="attachment-full size-full wp-post-image" height="390" sizes="(max-width: 1001px) 100vw, 1001px" src="http://atthewoodscabin.com/file/2018/04/At-the-Woods-Campground-1.png" srcset="http://atthewoodscabin.com/file/2018/04/At-the-Woods-Campground-1.png 1001w, http://atthewoodscabin.com/file/2018/04/At-the-Woods-Campground-1-300x117.png 300w, http://atthewoodscabin.com/file/2018/04/At-the-Woods-Campground-1-768x299.png 768w" width="1001"/>

          </div>
          <div class="post-content">
           <p>
            We offer total 24 campsites, each of them is spacious where you can enjoy with the provided camper and picnic table. The camper is good for the 5 people (pets are not allowed in the Camper) who can sleep easily. The 30 foot Camper is really very adequate for having a better night's sleep after an
            <a href="https://www.tripadvisor.com/ShowTopic-g1-i12357-k10477508-Tired_of_traveling_travel_burnout-Solo_Travel.html" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
             exhausted traveling
            </a>
            . Each site will be provided with a table and a fire ring.
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="At the Woods Campground" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23" height="392" src="http://atthewoodscabin.com/file/2018/04/At-the-Woods-Campground-2.png" width="848"/>
           </p>
           <h2>
            Extra Charges for Facilities
           </h2>
           <p>
            . The camper will be charged $60.00 per night.
           </p>
           <p>
            . The table will be charged $75.00 per night.
           </p>
           <h2>
            Few More Things to Know
           </h2>
           <p>
            Each site has its own fascination and beauty.
            <a href="http://atthewoodscabin.com/things-to-do-in-pittsburgh/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
             Each site offers the charm of nature
            </a>
            in a unique way. You can enjoy the peace and hear the sounds of nature with your loved ones, and family members. The
            <a href="https://www.wikihow.com/Enjoy-Camping" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
             campsite can be enjoyed
            </a>
            with the hammock, guitar, or a favorite book. We also offer the restroom for having a nap in any of the campsites with a fine showers. Everything will be provided neat and clean that you have a good atmosphere to breath. We prefer to provide each and everything of high quality, and we strive to make your each and every second enjoyable and unforgettable.
           </p>

           <h2>
            1. PNC Park
           </h2>
           <p>
            <img alt="Things to Do in Pittsburgh" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-29" height="427" src="http://atthewoodscabin.com/file/2018/04/Things-to-Do-in-Pittsburgh-1.jpg" width="750"/>
            If you visit PNC Park, then you must visit the baseball stadium, it is well constructed, and wherever you have a seat you will get a worth seeing view of nature.             </a>

            3. Mount Washington
           </h2>
           <p>
            <img alt="Things to Do in Pittsburgh" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-31" height="400" src="http://atthewoodscabin.com/file/2018/04/Things-to-Do-in-Pittsburgh-3.png" width="750"/>

To make the visit time well spent, you must visit both the sub and the science center.
