          Welcome!
         </h1>
        </header>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
         </p>
         <div class="content-column two_third">
          Welcome to the home of the Rocky Mountain Outdoor Writers and Photographers! Formed in 1973, Rocky Mountain Outdoor Writers and Photographers, Inc., is an organization of professional and aspiring communicators, dedicated to sharing the outdoor experience. Through writing, photography, broadcast media, and artistic expression, members provide information about the recreational and natural resources found in the Rocky Mountain West.
          <a href="https://rmowp.org/membership/" title="Membership requirements &amp; levels">
           Become a part
          </a>
          of our creative community and:
          <ul>
           <li>
            <a href="https://rmowp.org/annual-conference/" title="Annual conference">
             Attend
            </a>
            the annual conference —
            <strong>
             September 23-26, 2018
            </strong>
            will find us in Alamosa, Colorado.
           </li>
           <li>
            <a href="https://rmowp.org/annual-contest/" title="Annual contest overview">
             Participate
            </a>
            in our annual contest — the deadline for 2018 was
            <strong>
             Monday, June 25
            </strong>
            .
           </li>
           <li>
            <a href="https://rmowp.org/photo-workshop/" title="Photo Workshop – June 24 – 28, 2014">
             Take
            </a>
            a photo workshop —
            <strong>
             June 16-19, 2019
            </strong>
            in
            <strong>
             Rocky Mountain National Park
            </strong>
            , Estes Park, Colorado
            <strong>
             .
            </strong>
           </li>
           <li>
            <a href="https://rmowp.org/scholarship-fund/" title="Scholarship fund">
             Apply
            </a>
            for our scholarship — deadline
            <strong>
             May 30
            </strong>
            each year.
           </li>
           <li>
            <a href="https://rmowp.org/member-area/" title="Member Area">
             Meet
            </a>
            some of our members — see what we’re up to.
           </li>
          </ul>

           We need your inspiration, participation, and support. YOU are the organization!
