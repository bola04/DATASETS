    Paul Dagys, photojournalist and architectural photographer, one of the North Carolina photographers in the Raleigh, North Carolina area,  and research triangle park area, is a photojournalist and artist who has worked on assignment for LIFE, Smithsonian,People, INC., Parents, Computerworld , CFO  and Upside magazines.  He's experienced in art photography, people photography, photojournalism, public relations,  architecture, advertising, family, baby and wedding photography in color, sepia, and black and white  Bamp;W.  Also has experience as a glamour and nude photographer, who has exhibited at  art galleries across the South. Recent travels include Barbados and Thailand. He is located near Raleigh, Durham, and Winston Salem North Carolina, as well as South Carolina, and Richmond, Virginia.   He has a degree in Journalismfrom the University of  Missouri, and received a Regional Artist Project Grant. He has taught travel photography and text and image artwork in the Duke University Short Course Program,  has experience as a glamour and nude photographer, and is available for art photography, people photography, photojournalism, public relations, architecture,  advertising, family, baby and wedding photography in color, sepia, and black and white Bamp;W.
