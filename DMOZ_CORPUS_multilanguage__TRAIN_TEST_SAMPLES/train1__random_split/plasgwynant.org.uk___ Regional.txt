       <h3>
        <strong>
         <span style="color: #ff0000;">
          Plas Gwynant is in it’s 60th year (1958 – 2018)
         </span>
        </strong>
       </h3>
       <p>
        <span style="color: #ff0000;">
         <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lRGqEjnNk-c" rel="noopener" style="color: #ff0000;" target="_blank">
          A video of the first Plas courses in 1958
         </a>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="color: #ff0000;">
        </span>
        Plas Gwynant Residential Outdoor Education Centre is located at the foot of Snowdon (Yr Wyddfa) in North Wales, perfectly situated for a wide range of adventurous outdoor activities on both land and water.
       </p>
       <p>
        We are able to cater for a wide variety of groups up to a maximum size of 54 plus 6 staff.  New facilities mean that groups now do not have to commit to the whole centre and that the centre is also available for groups of 30 students plus up to 4 staff or 20 students plus 2 staff.  Groups can either choose to work together or be independant.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        We provide a welcoming and personal approach to learning.  Plas Gwynant offers unique outdoor experiences within a beautiful and inspirational setting.  Our very experienced and highly qualified staff are committed to providing high quality residential outdoor and adventurous educational courses and will ensure that you have an enjoyable and unforgettable experience.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Hopefully our website will give you a flavour of Plas Gwynant and what we are able to provide, should you require further information or wish to discuss any aspect of what we can provide, then please get in touch with us.
       </p>
