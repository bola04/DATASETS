                  <p align="justify">
                   Dear friends in Christ,
                  </p>
                  <p align="justify">
                   The following article is taken from the International Traditional Catholic Web Blog,
                   <b>
                    <a href="rorate-caeli.blogspot.com">
                     Rorate Caeli
                    </a>
                   </b>
                   .
                  </p>
                  <h4 align="center">
                   <a href="https://rorate-caeli.blogspot.com/2018/07/de-mattei-birth-of-humanae-vitae-in.html">
                    De Mattei: The Birth of Humanae Vitae in light of the Vatican Archives, July 18, 2018
                   </a>
                  </h4>
                  <div style="color:#384c67;height:7px;">
                   ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                  </div>
                  <br/>
                  <p align="justify">
                   <strong>
                    <span style="font-size: 1.15em;color:#28537e">
                     Enthronement Most Rev’d Peter Comensoli as the Ninth Archbishop of Melbourne – St Patrick’s Cathedral, this Wednesday 1st August 2018, at 7 pm:
                    </span>
                   </strong>
                   parishioners are welcome to attend our new Archbishop’s enthronement, but it is essential to book (there is no reserved seating, but a booking will guarantee  access to the Cathedral):
                   <a href="https://www.trybooking.com/book/event?eid=402692&amp;">
                    https://www.trybooking.com/book/event?eid=402692&amp;
                   </a>
                  </p>
                  <p align="justify">
                   <strong>
                    <span style="font-size: 1.15em;color:#28537e">
                     Notre Dame Priory needs your support!
                    </span>
                   </strong>
                   Fr Pius is on the cusp of finalising the purchase of a significant property that will provide both a permanent home, and source of income for the monastery, which now numbers ten members (with others interested in joining). In addition to making a donation, there is now an additional way to help – by investment! Provision has now been made for those who have a self-managed super fund to loan funds and receive a competitive return. For full details of the Jerusalem Estate (the property to which the monastery hopes to relocate), and of how you can support the project, go to
                   <a href="https://www.notredamemonastery.org/jerusalem-estate/">
                    https://www.notredamemonastery.org/jerusalem-estate/
                   </a>
                   .
                   <strong>
                    <span style="font-size: 1.15em;color:#28537e">
                     All are encouraged to make a Novena for a successful settlement, from 6th -14th August, by praying the Litany of Loreto for this intention.
                    </span>
                   </strong>
                  </p>
                  <p align="justify">
                   <strong>
                    <span style="font-size: 1.15em;color:#28537e">
                     Next week we will welcome home for brief family visits, Br Shawn Murphy of the Brisbane Oratory in Formation, and Br Antoninus Samy OP.
                    </span>
                   </strong>
                   There will be a reception after the Solemn Mass next Sunday, 5th August.
                  </p>
                 </article>
                 <article class="article-text-home-v3 col-md-6">
                  <br/>
                  <p align="justify">
                   The Latin Mass Society of Australia has a limited number of places for a
                   <strong>
                    <span style="font-size: 1.15em;color:#28537e">
                     Black Tie dinner in honour of His Eminence, Raymond Cardinal Burke
                    </span>
                   </strong>
                   , to be held at the Melbourne Savage Club on Saturday, 13th October. Further details are available in the flyers in the vestibule. To book, go to:
                   <a href="https://www.trybooking.com/book/event?eid=361678&amp;">
                    https://www.trybooking.com/book/event?eid=361678&amp;
                   </a>
                   . There will be a Parish event (BBQ lunch) with Cardinal Burke on Sunday, 14th October, following Confirmations and Pontifical Mass, at which all will be welcome. The Parish luncheon will be followed by a talk by His Eminence, at which everyone will also be welcome.
                  </p>
                  <p align="justify">
                   <strong>
                    <span style="font-size: 1.15em;color:#28537e">
                     Newman Fellowship:
                    </span>
                   </strong>
                   this Saturday 4th August from 4 pm, we will hold our next gathering at Maryvale, with Br Shawn Murphy (of the Brisbane Oratory in Formation) as our guest speaker. Br Shawn’s topic is Jordan Peterson and Catholic teaching: is he on our side? Br Shawn will speak about Jordan Peterson’s views on the human person, and will evaluate them from the point of view of the Catholic Faith. The talk will open up into a discussion. For more information, please contact Stephen Jury
                   <a href="mailto:sjury07@gmail.com">
                    sjury07@gmail.com
                   </a>
                  </p>
                  <p align="justify">
                   <strong>
                    <span style="font-size: 1.15em;color:#28537e">
                     Christus Rex pilgrimage (26th – 28th October, 2018):
                    </span>
                   </strong>
                   registrations are open for this year’s pilgrimage in honour of Christ the King. For more details, or to register, go to
                   <a href="http://crex.org/wordpress/">
                    http://crex.org/wordpress/
                   </a>
                   .
                  </p>
                  <p align="justify">
                   <strong>
                    <span style="font-size: 1.15em;color:#28537e">
                     First Holy Communion (Sunday, 16th December):
                    </span>
                   </strong>
                   those who wish to have their children make their First Holy Communion (preceded by First Confession) are invited to register before the end of August, by way of an email to the Secretary. The usual age for first Confession and Holy Communion is 7 or 8. Sacramental preparation will commence in September.
                  </p>
