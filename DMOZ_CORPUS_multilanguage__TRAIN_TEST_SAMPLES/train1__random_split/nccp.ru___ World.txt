        <article class="article mp_text">
         <p>
          Novosibirsk Chemical Concentrates Plant (NCCP) is one of the world’s leading manufacturers of the nuclear fuel for NPPs and for research reactors in Russia and abroad. The enterprise is the Russian manufacturer of lithium metal and lithium salts. NCCP is part of Fuel Company TVEL which is affiliated with State Corporation ROSATOM.
         </p>
         <p>
          The enterprise was founded on September 25, 1948. Today, the fuel fabricated by NCCP accounts for about 6% of the total installed capacity of power reactors worldwide.
         </p>
