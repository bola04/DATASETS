        <h1>
         <a href="/mexico-design-magazine/" title="Permalink to Mexico Design magazine">
          Mexico Design magazine
         </a>
        </h1>
       </header>
       <div class="content">
        <figure aria-labelledby="figcaption_attachment_3519" class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_3519" style="width: 772px">
         <img alt="Artwork for Mexico Design magazine." class="size-large wp-image-3519" height="1024" sizes="(max-width: 768px) 100vw, 768px" src="http://www.pauldoeman.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/mexico-design-cover-768x1024.jpg" srcset="http://www.pauldoeman.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/mexico-design-cover-768x1024.jpg 768w, http://www.pauldoeman.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/mexico-design-cover-225x300.jpg 225w, http://www.pauldoeman.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/mexico-design-cover.jpg 884w" width="768"/>
         <figcaption class="wp-caption-text" id="figcaption_attachment_3519">
          Several object drawings on the cover of Mexico Design magazine.
         </figcaption>
        </figure>
        <p>
         I was invited by
         <a href="http://mexicodesign.com/">
          Mexico Design magazine
         </a>
         to create work for the front cover of issue 37, which focused on new designs in eco-friendly transport.
        </p>
        <p>
         I coincided their requirements with my “
         <em>
          <a href="http://www.pauldoeman.com/exhibition-day-244-of-draw-an-object-a-day-for-365-days/">
           Draw an object a day for 365 days project
          </a>
         </em>
         “, drawing a 1960’s children’s scooter, a leaf from a London plain tree and a skateboard.
        </p>
        <p>
         Each drawing was cut-out and layered onto coloured paper.
        </p>
        <p>
         The next issue will contain a 6 page feature on my work, focusing on my drawing an object a day project.
        </p>
        <figure aria-labelledby="figcaption_attachment_3518" class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_3518" style="width: 877px">
         <img alt="The credits page in Mexico Design magazine" class="size-full wp-image-3518" height="873" sizes="(max-width: 873px) 100vw, 873px" src="http://www.pauldoeman.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/paul-credits.jpg" srcset="http://www.pauldoeman.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/paul-credits.jpg 873w, http://www.pauldoeman.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/paul-credits-150x150.jpg 150w, http://www.pauldoeman.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/paul-credits-300x300.jpg 300w" width="873"/>
         <figcaption class="wp-caption-text" id="figcaption_attachment_3518">
          Credits page in Mexico Design magazine.
         </figcaption>
        </figure>
