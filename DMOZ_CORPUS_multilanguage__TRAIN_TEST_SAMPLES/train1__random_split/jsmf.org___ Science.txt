        <h1>
         Welcome to the James S. McDonnell Foundation
        </h1>
        <p class="p-custom">
         Founded in 1950 by aerospace pioneer James S.
                 McDonnell, the Foundation was established to "improve the
                 quality of life," and does so by contributing to the generation
                 of new knowledge through its support of research and
                 scholarship. The Foundation awards grants via the
                 Foundation-initiated, peer-reviewed proposal processes described
                 in the
         <a href="https://www.jsmf.org/apply">
          21st Century
                   Science Initiative
         </a>
         .
        </p>
