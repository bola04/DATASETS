      <h1>
       Welcome to Comcar
      </h1>
      <div class="homeMainContent">
       <p>
        This site is for company car drivers and their employers who are choosing a new car. Our comprehensive database allows you to calculate the company car tax on any UK model. 
 	Configure the vehicle with optional features to see the implications on
        <abbr title="Benefit in kind">
         BIK
        </abbr>
        and CO
        <sub>
         2
        </sub>
        . 
 	You can find tax efficient company cars at all levels by using Comcar's tools, guides and references.
       </p>
      </div>

           </i>

          </a>
          <p>
           If you know exactly which model you want, then start with the tax calculator. 
 							         The Comcar database contains the multitude of car derivatives and their exact 
 							         carbon dioxide emission figures.

         </li>
         <li class="Carchooser">

           Help you identify the lowest taxed cars that fit your key parameters 
 									such as number of doors, seats, acceleration and fuel economy. 
 									Cars ranked in order of the amount of benefit in kind (BIK) tax payable.
          </p>
