           TO CROSS CURRENT DIVERS
          </h3>
          <p class="p1">
           As the only PADI 5 Star Instructor Development Center in London, Ontario, Cross Current Divers has been serving Southwestern Ontario for over 25 years, offering Beginner, Advanced and Professional diver Courses as well as the full suite of Specialty and Technical courses, gear, dive trips and underwater experiences.  Certified Technicians provide service for repairs on your dive cylinders, regulators, BCDs, and exposure suits.
