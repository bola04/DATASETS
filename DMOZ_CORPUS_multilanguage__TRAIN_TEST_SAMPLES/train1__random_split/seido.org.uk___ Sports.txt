            </h2>
            <div class="art-postcontent">

             <div class="art-article">
              <p class="headingsml">
               <span class="Bulletsml">
                Welcome to the UK branch of the World Seido Karate Organisation. Our branch chief is Roger           Thyer-Jones (Jun Shihan, 6th dan)
               </span>
              </p>
              <p align="left">
               <strong>
                Seido
               </strong>
               is a traditional style of karate, formed on  15th October 1976 by Tadashi       Nakamura. Since then Seido has grown  substantially, with clubs in many       countries, including 10 in  the United Kingdom. UK headquarters are in       High Wycombe and are  lead by Roger Thyer-Jones (Sei Shihan (Master) 6th dan).
              </p>
              <p align="left">
               Seido       karate stresses above all the philosophy  that karate should teach       'Technique before Strength, Spirit before  Technique'. If you train with       us, you will undoubtedly become  stronger and develop sound karate       technique, but most of all,  Seido aims to build students with a strong       spirit and attitude  towards karate, and life. This 'non-quitting' spirit       is a modern  interpretation of the bushido (martial way) of the Japanese        samurai.
              </p>
              <p align="left">
               Seido       provides a structured environment for  learning karate. The discipline and       etiquette of a Japanese-based  martial art creates a serious and respectful       atmosphere in the  dojo, and yet there is a genuine friendliness between       students. If  you feel you would like to visit us, please come and join in       and  give it a go - or simply watch a class. We look forward to having you        along! We run events throughout the year including regular gradings, local and national tournaments, beach training and summer camps and socials get-togethers making Seido truly family orientated. We seek to find a place for everyone where difference does not mean deficit.
              </p>
              <p align="left">
               All club instructors have DBS clearance and are First Aid trained. Our insurance through NAKMAS (National Association of Martial Arts Schools) is one of the best you can get. Newcomers classes start throughout 2017 so why not contact your local dojo to discuss starting with us. Just click on the
               <span style="color: #ff0000;">
                Contact
               </span>
               tab above to locate the instructor, telephone number and dojo location. We hope to see you soon.
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               <span style="font-size: medium;">
                <strong>
                 Please note that we have a new Seido Website under development and so this site will not be updated or accept new arrivals.
                </strong>
               </span>
              </p>
