                      <h2>
                       Keystone View
                       <br/>
                       Vision Screeners
                      </h2>
                      <p>
                       Keystone View Vision Screeners test for all of the essential visionary functions including acuity (monocular and binocular), color blindness, peripheral vision, and glare recovery. This series of vision testing equipment can be operated with the remote control or from your PC.  The optional PC software prompts the test question, records and tabulates the answers, prints results and automatically saves the data to a database.
                      </p>
