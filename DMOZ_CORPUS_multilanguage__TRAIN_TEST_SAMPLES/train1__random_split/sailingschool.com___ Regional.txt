        <h1>
         Live aboard sailing school in the Caribbean
        </h1>
        <p style="text-align: justify;padding-right: 30px">
         The School of Ocean Sailing offers live aboard sail training courses. We operate in the Caribbean, in the waters surrounding the United States Virgin Islands. The instructional mission of the School of Ocean Sailing is to teach offshore ocean sailing and ocean navigation in a live aboard setting.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify">
         Your
         <strong>
          sailing school classroom
         </strong>
         is a well founded, romantic, beautiful, fast, and very sea kindly sailing vessel. SAMANA, a 52′ steel offshore sailing ketch, is 63′ LOA, has a 16′ beam, draws 7′, carries 1800 square feet of sail set between two masts, and displaces 34 tons. Built in 1975 in Holland, SAMANA has circumnavigated the globe and has completed several noteworthy offshore passages.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify">
         Captain Larry Wheeler and Letty Wheeler are your sailing school instructors. Both Larry and Letty are professional teachers with more than 25 years of classroom teaching experience and over 25 years of experience in sail training. Larry is a United States Coast Guard Licensed Merchant Marine Officer (USCG).
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify">
         The
         <strong>
          School of Ocean Sailing
         </strong>
         operates winters in the Caribbean Sea surrounding the United States Virgin Islands, and your sailing school class begins in Saint Thomas , USVI. You will be sailing in the Atlantic Ocean and the Caribbean Sea surrounding the United States Virgin Islands (USVI) and the British Virgin Islands (BVI).
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify">
         The curriculum is a rich blend of technical skill, confidence building, and common sense coupled with a spirit of adventure and romance. Instruction centers on the principal objectives underlying the knowledge of ocean sailing, coastal navigation, or celestial navigation.
        </p>
        <h6 style="text-align: justify">
         <em>
          To this end Larry and Letty have dedicated their sailing school, sailing vessel, equipment, sailing routes, and energy.
         </em>
        </h6>
        <p style="text-align: justify">
         The School of Ocean Sailing is able to accommodate beginners just learning to sail a large ocean vessel, to students interested in advanced ocean navigation or celestial navigation.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify">
         The School of Ocean Sailing offers seven day
         <em>
          live aboard
         </em>
         sail training courses. This means you stay aboard the vessel for the full duration of the course. Your course includes all meals, instruction and materials, towels and bedding, toiletries, such as toothpaste and shampoo. No alcohol or smoking is permitted aboard the sail training vessel. Samana is an alcohol, drug and smoke free environment.
        </p>
