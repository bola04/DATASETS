           <b>
            Garage Door Opener Repair and Installation
           </b>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p class="p2">
          <span class="s1">
           There are many types of garage door openers, including electric garage openers, manual garage openers and automatic garage openers. Most electric openers are controlled using a remote.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p class="p2">
          <span class="s1">
           Manual openers, on the other hand, are an advantage even when there’s power shortage. Therefore, it is important to make sure that the opener installed in your door is the right one.
          </span>
         </p>

           <b>
            Garage Door Opener Repair
           </b>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p class="p2">
          <span class="s1">
           Garage door openers fail for various reasons. If the remote control or switch on the wall isn’t operational and burnt fuses, circuit breakers or even garage door motors can cause a door opener to stop working. Therefore, you’ll need to replace the fuse and motor or ensure the circuit breaker is reset.
          </span>
         </p>
