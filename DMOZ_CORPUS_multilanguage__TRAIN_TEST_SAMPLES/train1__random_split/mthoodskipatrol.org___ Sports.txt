      <p>
       <big>
        <strong>
         Welcome to the home of the Mt. Hood Ski Patrol
        </strong>
       </big>
      </p>
      <p class="vspace">
       The Mt. Hood Ski Patrol is a member-driven organization dedicated to rescue, emergency care and public safety for the Mt. Hood recreational community.
      </p>
      <p class="vspace">
       We are composed of nearly 300 patroller volunteers, all dedicated to providing quality rescue and emergency care to alpine and nordic skiers and snowboarders at the following areas:
      </p>
      <p class="vspace">
       <a class="urllink" href="http://www.skihood.com" rel="nofollow">
        Mt. Hood Meadows
       </a>
       <br/>
       <a class="urllink" href="http://www.skibowl.com" rel="nofollow">
        Mt. Hood Skibowl
       </a>
       <br/>
       <a class="urllink" href="http://www.summitskiarea.com" rel="nofollow">
        Summit Ski Area
       </a>
       <br/>
       <a class="urllink" href="http://www.timberlinelodge.com" rel="nofollow">
        Timberline
       </a>
       <br/>
       <a class="urllink" href="http://www.teacupnordic.org/" rel="nofollow">
        Teacup Lake Nordic Ski Area
       </a>
      </p>
      <p class="vspace">
       Primarily, we support weekend and holiday operations at four area ski resorts and along developed backcountry and nordic trails. At two of these resorts, our support extends year-round to summer skiers and mountain bikers!
      </p>
      <p class="vspace">
       We also boast over 120 mountain host volunteers who provide guest services and other assistance at Timberline and Skibowl.
      </p>
