          <p>
           Nearby attractions include hill walking on the stunning mountain ranges of the Scottish Highlands, touring the many quiet country roads, steam engine rides, boat trips, golf, pony trekking, canoeing &amp; sailing, cable car rides, mountain biking, excellent fishing and lots more besides.

           Alan is able to help you visit the less crowded side of Ben Nevis and explore easy or difficult routes to the summit, via the north face of Britain’s Highest peak. For more information on Alan’s mountain adventures, please call on 01397 700451 or check the blog

           </li>
           <li>
            Individuals, couples or small groups can rent twin or four-person rooms for anything upwards of three nights (see next point).
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Minimum length of stay is now three nights.
             <br/>
            </strong>
           </li>
           <li>
            Calluna provides an ideal base for mountaineering and canoe club meets or extended family gatherings.
           </li>
           <li>
            The two apartments with three bedrooms each (8 beds) are ideal for either a large family or two families sharing.
           </li>
           <li>
            The smaller, two-bedroom flat (6 beds), provides enough space for smaller families or two couples on holiday together.
           </li>
           <li>
            The Alpine Loft sleeps 4 people. It is our smallest facility.
           </li>
          </ul>
