            <strong>
             Welcome to the Florida Whips!
            </strong>
            <br/>
            We are a 
 	  not-for-profit statewide equine driving association offering education, 
 	  clinics, pleasure drives, competitions, picnics, social gatherings, and 
 	  other activities.  A "whip" is the term used to describe the driver 
 	  of the carriage, and our whips would be happy to have you join them!  
 	  There are no requirements to become a member other than agreeing to safety 
 	  rules and paying nominal annual dues.  We look forward to driving 
 	  with you and enjoying equines.
