                 <span class="image-block caption-over-image" style="float: left; margin-right: 20px; width:260px;">
                  <img alt="" height="181" id="mce-11039" src="attachments/Image/2-whitetail-deers.jpg" width="260"/>

                 Whitetail Deer Hunting Texas Style
                </span>
               </div>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: small;">
                 The
                 <em>
                  888 Ranch
                 </em>
                 is a working cattle ranch located in the heart of the South Texas Brush Country. Owned and Operated by SLC Land &amp; Cattle Co.
                 <span class="image-block caption-over-image" style="float: right; margin-left: 20px; width:250px;">
                  <img alt="" height="211" id="mce-11429" src="attachments/Image/DSC08468editc_1.jpg" width="250"/>
                 </span>
                 Over the last decade, the ranch has developed and managed its herd of Whitetail deer until it now offers whitetail deer hunting Texas style.  It produces many trophy quality deer that score over 165 B&amp;C. Come for a trophy hunt vacation with us and it will be one you won't forget.
                </span>
               </p>
               <p align="justify">
               </p>
               <p align="justify">
                <span style="font-family: Arial; font-size: large;">
                 <strong>
                  WaterFowl &amp; Upland Game Birds
                 </strong>
                </span>
               </p>
               <p>
                <span style="font-family: Verdana; font-size: small;">
                 For those not interested in whitetail deer hunting, Texas abounds with many types of wildlife. 888 Ranch guests have ample opportunity to enjoy bird viewing of many native and migratory species.
                </span>
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <p>
                <span style="font-family: Verdana;">
                 <span style="font-size: small;">
                  We now offer waterfowl hunting, and it is not uncommon to hear daily reports of large groups of hunters all limiting out, even with the liberal limits allowed inTexas. Plan your Waterfowl and Upland Game Bird Hunting Trip to the
                 </span>
                </span>
                <span style="font-family: Verdana; font-size: small;">
                 ANWR farms near Anahuac, Texas today.
                 <span class="image-block caption-over-image" style="float: right; margin-left: 20px; width:250px;">
                  <img alt="" height="153" id="mce-11512" src="attachments/Image/Waterfowl-hunting.jpg" width="250"/>
                 </span>
                </span>
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <p align="justify">
                <span style="font-family: Arial; font-size: large;">
                 <strong>
                  Quality by Design
                 </strong>
                </span>
               </p>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: small;">
                 Careful breeding and genetics control along with age improve the quality of the Texas whitetail deer hunting on the 888 ranch. These whitetail deer have year round supplemental feeding to improve health and horn size through the winter and the horn growing season.
                </span>
               </p>
