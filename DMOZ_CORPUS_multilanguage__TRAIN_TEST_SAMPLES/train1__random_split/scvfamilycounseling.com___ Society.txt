        <h1 class="CMS-textLines page-title title_fg_fg " id="page-title-2630141332-body_bg-title_fg" style="">
         <span class="textLine1" style="display: block; font-weight: normal;font-style: normal;margin-left: 0px;">
          <span style="font-size:21pt">
           <span style="font-family:font_43_fradm">
            Welcome
           </span>
          </span>
         </span>
        </h1>
        <div class="body_fg_fg" id="content">
         <p>
          <strong>
           <span style="font-size: 12pt">
            <span>
             <span>
              <img alt="" class="ezimage" src="/phpThumb/phpThumb.php?src=/userfiles/1912752/image/Therapy picture Lee(1).jpg&amp;w=200&amp;h=200" style="margin-top: 10px; margin-bottom: 10px; float: left; clear: left; margin-right: 10px"/>
              Welcome to Cornerstone Counseling, serving the Santa Clarita Valley's family counseling needs for over 20 years.
             </span>
            </span>
           </span>
          </strong>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <span style="font-size: 11pt">
           I provide counseling for couples, families, and individuals from a
           <a href="http://www.therapysites.com/sites/scvfamilycounseling.com/ChristianCounselingnearSantaClarita.en.html" style="color:#FFFFFF;">
            Christian perspective.
           </a>
           If you're struggling in your marriage, my goal is to help you resolve the conflict and find the passion and love you had at first.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           I specialize in Christian marriage counseling and my focus is on helping couples better align their relationships with God's plan for marriage. There are other ways to approach marital problems and these methods are utilized with couples when their Christian walk is not the dominant theme in their home. But, it's become clear to me over many years of working with husbands and wives who are struggling in their relationships, that the stronger their focus on God's plan for the marriage, the more powerful and positive the outcome of therapy.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Sometimes, couples have just "forgotten" what they know the
           <a href="http://www.therapysites.com/sites/scvfamilycounseling.com/MyArticles.en.html" style="color:#FFFFFF;">
            Bible teaches about the marriage relationship
           </a>
           and sometimes they never really knew or understood it in the first place. In either case, Scripture is, as one might expect, the best guide to restoring marital harmony.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           But, there's often something blocking a couple's ability to peacefully and lovingly relate to each other as they did when they were dating. After all, the relationship was great in the beginning (in most cases). Difficult situations we've encountered within our families while growing up can have a powerful and detrimental impact on our relationships in the present. Sometimes it's hard to tell where the past ends and the present begins. Therapy can allow you to become what I call a
           <strong>
            "thoughtful responder
           </strong>
           " rather than someone who impulsively
           <em>
            <strong>
             reacts
            </strong>
           </em>
           to current situations in ways dictated by negative past experiences.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           By applying a counseling approach that is biblically-based and focused on practical solutions, I can help you to deal much more effectively with difficult people and situations in your life.
          </span>
          <span style="font-size: 11pt">
           Whether you're simply looking for someone who will
           <em>
            <strong>
             really listen
            </strong>
           </em>
           , dealing with
           <a href="http://www.therapysites.com/sites/scvfamilycounseling.com/ServicesProvided.en.html" style="color:#FFFFFF;">
            sexual addiction/infidelity
           </a>
           , or want to transform and reinvigorate your marriage, I look forward to working with you to achieve your goals.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           If you're interested in personal growth, I can help you uncover your true potential and live the life you've always wanted.
           <br/>
          </span>
          <br/>
          <span style="font-size: 11pt">
           Please call or email me for an individual or couple's session today at
           <strong>
            Cornerstone Counseling
           </strong>
           (661) 297-7255 or
           <a href="mailto:lee@thevine.net" style="color:#FFFFFF;">
            lee@thevine.net
           </a>
          </span>
         </p>
