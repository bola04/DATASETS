         </a>
        </h2>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="meta-sep">

          </span>
         </a>
        </div>
        <!-- .entry-meta -->
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <strong>
          Join Us Tuesday, July 17 at 6:30 pm at the Mascoma Valley High School.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Admission is free!
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Junk 2 Funk (J2F) is a high-energy, confidence-building and performance-based program that emphasizes the importance of working together as a team.  J2F   exposes young people to music and performance art in a non-threatening, novel manner while addressing current environmental issues.  J2F is considered by many educators, parents (and kids!) to be one of the best artist-in-residence programs in New England!  Think STOMP meets Blue Man Group meets the Green Police!
        </p>
        <p>
         Co-hosted with the Enfield and Grafton Town Libraries and supported in part by the Friends of the Canaan Town Library.
        </p>
