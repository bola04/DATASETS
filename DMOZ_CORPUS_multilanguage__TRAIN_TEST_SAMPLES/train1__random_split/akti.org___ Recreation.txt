       <h2>
        Imagine Your Life Without a Knife
       </h2>
       <p>
        <b>
         We are the responsible, credible voice and advocate for the knife community
        </b>
        Working with our diverse, knowledgeable membership, we educate the public, lawmakers, law enforcement and the entire knife community about knives, knife safety, knife laws and knife industry related issues.  We partner with legislators, law enforcement and other organizations to advocate for common sense knife laws and regulations that are both responsible and reasonable, and consistent enforcement.  We believe knives are critically essential and valuable tools in our daily and recreational lives.
       </p>
