     <p>
      A master-planned community, founded in 1956 and incorporated in 1961, Forest Park has grown to be third largest city in Hamilton County, Ohio, with approximately 18,720 residents in 7,900 households. Forest Park is also home to an active community of 350 businesses.
     </p>
