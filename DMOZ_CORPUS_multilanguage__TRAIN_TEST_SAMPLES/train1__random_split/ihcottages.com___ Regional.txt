       Discover the best self catering holiday cottages on the Isle of Skye, Lochalsh and Inverness
      </h2>
      <p>
       Islands &amp; Highlands Cottages offer a wide selection of self catering holiday cottages.  From rustic to luxury, our holiday cottages have something for everyone. Many of them welcome short weekend breaks as well as weekly holidays.
      </p>
      <p>
       The west coast of Scotland has a diverse array of landscapes mixed together with historical sites and excellent activities.  Inverness,  the Isle of Skye and Lochalsh make the perfect holiday destination with rugged coastlines,  towering mountains and picturesque villages. Whilst exploring the area, stay in one of Islands &amp; Highlands Cottages self catering holiday cottages!
      </p>
