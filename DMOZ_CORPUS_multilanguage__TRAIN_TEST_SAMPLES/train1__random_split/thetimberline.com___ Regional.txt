          <center>
           <h1>
            Snowmass Condominium Rentals
           </h1>
           <h2>
            Ski-In Ski-Out on the Slopes of Snowmass, Colorado
           </h2>
          </center>
          <p>
           Timberline Condominiums welcomes you to
           <bold>
            Snowmass Village, Colorado and the heart of the Rockies.  The Aspen/Snowmass area offers something for everyone - world-class skiing, snowboarding, downhill biking, whitewater rafting, culture, dining, shopping, and inspiring views everywhere you turn.
           </bold>
          </p>
          <p>
           The Timberline Condominiums offers slopeside Snowmass condominium rentals. When you stay with us, you'll be just steps  from a variety of festivals, events, and activities that keep Snowmass Village buzzing all year long. And we're just nine miles from  iconic Aspen.  Our condominium suites range from studios, 1, 2, and 3 bedrooms, with
           <a href="/ski-ski-out-condos-snowmass" title="Ski In Ski Out Condos">
            ski-in/ski-out access
           </a>
           and all the conveniences of home.
          </p>
          <p>
           The Timberline offers complimentary Aspen airport transfers and van service within Snowmass Village. Relax in the new slopeside pool and hot tubs, and enjoy dining at our on-site restaurant, The Edge Restaurant &amp; Bar. The Timberline is the perfect location for a weekend getaway, a week-long trip with friends and family, a Rocky Mountain Wedding or Family Reunion or Gathering. We look forward to having you!
          </p>
