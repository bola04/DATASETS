      <h1>
       Welcome to
       <span class="co">
        STUART MODELS
       </span>
      </h1>
      <p>
       We offer sets of castings, machined kits and ready to run models, catering for modellers, collectors or simply those individuals with an eye for great quality. Please navigate around our website to start your journey into your model engineering.
      </p>
      <p>
       Allowing you to machine your own model steam engine our sets of castings come with drawings, with the majority of kits including necessary materials and fixings.
      </p>
      <p>
       For those who do not have their own workshop or machinery we also have a selection of Machined Kits turned, threaded and milled ready for you to finish the model with no more than a set of spanners, small files, screw driver and paint brush.
      </p>
      <p>
       Looking for a perfect gift for a steam model enthusiast? Take a look at our steam model engines carefully assembled and painted in our very own workshop. These Ready To Run Models come with a signed certificate of authentication.
      </p>
      <p>
       Established in 1898, Stuart Models is the oldest company in the world committed to providing you with a great range of model steam engines. Our founding principles of quality, service and commitment, very much to this day remain a key focus of our business.
      </p>
      <p>
       Please use our website to navigate to each of our services and please
       <a href="/p/contact" title="Contact">
        get in touch
       </a>
       if you have any queries you would like our steam model specialists to answer.
      </p>
