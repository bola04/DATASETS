         <p>
          Welcome to the official website of the Shore Haven Resort Inn.  Shore Haven is a family-run 70 room beach area resort in Lauderdale by the Sea, Florida.  We have been managing Shore Haven since 1995.
         </p>
         <p>
          We offer hotel rooms, efficiency apartments and one bedroom apartments with a very complete range of amenities such as free parking, free Wi-Fi internet access, free HBO movie channels, two pools, an outdoor jacuzzi, and a wonderfully landscaped gardens with blooming flowers.  We are located 120 yards from the golden sand beach and we are lucky to have warm weather throughout the year.
         </p>
         <p>
          Lauderdale-by-the-Sea, our town, is an upscale yet laid-back mile-long residential beach community.  Our town is part of the Greater Fort Lauderdale metro area.  Unlike everywhere else in South Florida, the buildings in Lauderdale by the Sea are limited to four stories in height.  Since most of the buildings in our town are only two stories high, this creates a serene Key West style oasis atmosphere in the center of the thriving South Florida.
         </p>
