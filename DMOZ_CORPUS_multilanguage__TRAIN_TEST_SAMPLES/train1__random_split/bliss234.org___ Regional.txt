             </font>
             <br/>
            </font>
           </div>
           <div style="text-align: center;">
            <font face="Georgia, serif" style="font-size: 16px;">
             Our mission is to ensure a safe, secure environment, which provides quality educational opportunities for students, challenging them to become life long achievers and contributing citizens.
            </font>
            <br/>
           </div>
           <font face="Georgia, serif">

             <font face="Georgia, serif" style="font-size: 24px;">
              <br/>
              <font color="#7b3135">
               Belief Statement
              </font>
             </font>
            </b>
            <br/>
           </div>
           <ol>
            <li>
             <font face="Georgia, serif">
              We believe that a SAFE ENVIRONMENT conducive to learning should be provided.
             </font>
            </li>
            <li>
             <font face="Georgia, serif">
              We believe that educators should maintain CLASSROOM DISCIPLINE where students shall accept responsibility and be held accountable for their actions and work.
             </font>
            </li>
            <li>
             <font face="Georgia, serif">
              We believe the BASIC VALUES of honesty, self-discipline, unselfishness, cultural diversity, and respect for authority should be emphasized.
             </font>
            </li>
            <li>
             <font face="Georgia, serif">
              We believe that the basic skills should be taught to help students COMMUNICATE EFFECTIVELY.
             </font>
            </li>
            <li>
             <font face="Georgia, serif">
              We believe that BASIC CURRICULUM must be taught to prepare students for vocational and post-secondary education.
             </font>
            </li>
            <li>
             <font face="Georgia, serif">
              We believe that basic WORKFORCE SKILLS should be taught.
             </font>
            </li>
            <li>
             <font face="Georgia, serif">
              We believe that students and staff should be introduced to current TECHNOLOGY.
             </font>
            </li>
            <li>
             <font face="Georgia, serif">
              We believe students should acquire skills to be RESPONSIBLE, PRODUCTIVE, INFORMED CITIZENS in
             </font>
             <font face="Georgia, serif">
              their homes, schools, communities, and country.
             </font>
            </li>
           </ol>
