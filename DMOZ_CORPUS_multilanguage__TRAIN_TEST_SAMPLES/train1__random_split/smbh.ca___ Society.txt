         <p>
          St. Matthew’s Bracondale House is a privately-owned, non-profit apartment building for individuals 65 and older.
          <a href="https://smbh.ca/accommodation-seniors/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
           Our building
          </a>
          contains 130 one-bedroom and bachelor apartments, and residents have access to our dining room, library, lounges, recreational rooms, and courtyard.
         </p>
         <p>
          Residents and community members are also able to attend
          <a href="https://smbh.ca/menu/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
           communal meals
          </a>
          ,  join yoga and fitness classes, attend parties, and participate in other
          <a href="https://smbh.ca/activities/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
           recreational activities
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          In addition, our organization also manages an 18-unit building for
          <a href="https://smbh.ca/accommodation-families/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
           families
          </a>
          , which we offer through the City of Toronto’s Housing Connections program.
         </p>
