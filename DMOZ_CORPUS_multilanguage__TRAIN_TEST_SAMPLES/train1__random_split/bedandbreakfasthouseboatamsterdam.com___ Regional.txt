          <section class="main_sections">
           <section class="content_section text">
            <h2>
             Houseboat little Amstel
            </h2>
            <p>
             Houseboat Little Amstel is moored along the Amstel River, in the heart of downtown Amsterdam. The boat offers two small rooms, the
             <a href="http://www.littleamstel.com/en/basic-room" rel="nofollow" target="">
              Basic-room
             </a>
             and the
             <a href="http://www.littleamstel.com/en/standard-room" rel="nofollow" target="">
              Standard-room
             </a>
             . Each room has it’s own private entrance, en-suite bathroom and is suitable for maximum two persons, couples or singles.
            </p>
           </section>
           <section class="content_section text">
            <h2>
             The Basic-room &amp; the Standard-room
            </h2>
            <p>
             Although the rooms are small, they are comfortable, because of the smart modern design and all the facilities needed for a short stay like luxurious beds, coffee- and tea making facilities, a fridge, a flatscreen TV, WiFi, bluetooth speaker and a complete bathroom with towels and soap.
            </p>
           </section>
           <section class="content_section text">
            <h2>
             Location &amp; route
            </h2>
            <p>
             The houseboat is located in the historic city centre, just across the luxury 5 star InterContinental Amstel Hotel and 500 metres from metrostop Weesperplein and tramstop Stadhouderskade. Its convenient location makes that all of Amsterdam’s finest attractions and sights are within striking distance, just as a lot of popular restaurants and bars.
            </p>
           </section>
           <section class="content_section text">
            <h2>
             Rates &amp; reservation
            </h2>
            <p>
             To make a reservation request, go to the calendar and click on the date you prefer to check in!
            </p>
            <ul>
             <li>
              <a href="https://littleamstel.stinngo.com/en/basic-room" rel="nofollow" target="">
               Basic-room
              </a>
              — €110
             </li>
             <li>
              <a href="https://littleamstel.stinngo.com/en/standard-room" rel="nofollow" target="">
               Standard-room
              </a>
              — €120
             </li>
            </ul>
            <p>
             When you stay 4 nights or more your room will be cleaned once meanwhile without any extra costs. If you stay 3 nights or less room cleaning is on request and at extra costs.
            </p>
           </section>
           <section class="content_section text">
            <h2>
             Check in &amp; check out
            </h2>
            <p>
             Because there is no reception desk on board of the houseboat you need to check in yourself. The code you need to open your room door is in the confirmation mail. If you need some help, you only have to ring the doorbell which is connected to the mobile phone of the owner. She is always just around the corner and ready to serve you.
            </p>
           </section>
