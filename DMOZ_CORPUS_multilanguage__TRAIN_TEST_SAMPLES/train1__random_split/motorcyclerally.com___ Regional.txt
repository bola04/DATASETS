             <div class="sqs-block-content">
              <h3>
               New owners. New events. Golden Aspen Motorcycle Rally will be better than ever!
              </h3>
              <p>
               For 49 years, the village of Ruidoso in the southern mountains of New Mexico has hosted riders and enthusiasts for the Golden Aspen (Est. 1969) and AspenCash (Est. 1997) motorcycle rallies.
              </p>
              <p>
               In 2018, the father-son team of Humberto and Blayne Maldonado, both long-time Ruidoso residents and Golden Aspen Rally participants and supporters, have taken over from former owners Ron and Jill Andrews.
              </p>
              <p>
               The Maldonados are excited to continue the great tradition of the rallies with new attractions and events, including live music and something for every motorcycle enthusiast.
              </p>
              <h3>
               Mark your calendars now for 5-days of riding, food and music - September 19-23, 2018.
              </h3>
