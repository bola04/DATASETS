                <p>
                 Welcome to Yacht Fractions, we are the only dedicated professional brokerage currently operating in the UK who specialise in the sale of shares in UK based sailing boats shares and Overseas based sailing boat shares and Motor yachts.
                </p>
                <p>
                 Since we started business in 1991 based on our own personal  experiences of
                 <a href="http://yachtfractions.co.uk/Web/about-us/">
                  sharing
                 </a>
                 we have gathered a vast knowledge of the ins and outs of sharing through our function of forming new groups, brokering the sale of hundreds of shares, advising existing groups and talking to a huge number of experienced share owners.
                </p>
                <p>
                 Whether you are
                 <a href="http://yachtfractions.co.uk/Web/how-to-buy-a-share/">
                  buying
                 </a>
                 or
                 <a href="http://yachtfractions.co.uk/Web/how-to-sell-a-share/">
                  selling
                 </a>
                 a share we are here to help you. Whilst we are contracted to the Seller we see our function to ensure that all the share groups we are involved with leave our hands in a healthy state to be enjoyed by their various owners.
                </p>
                <p>
                 Thus whether you are buying or selling  we will always be very willing to discuss any concerns you have or to help you to fill in the gaps in your knowledge.
                </p>
                <p>
                 Throughout your experience with us we aim to give you a very personal and caring service, we will never pressure you or try to alter your own judgement. Our philosophy is that a buyer will only buy what he wants and we respect this. We do however ask you keep us informed of your discussions.
                </p>
                <p>
                 If you are selling; please read our section about the
                 <a href="http://yachtfractions.co.uk/Web/how-to-sell-a-share/">
                  services
                 </a>
                 we will provide .
                </p>
                <p>
                 If you are
                 <a href="http://yachtfractions.co.uk/Web/how-to-buy-a-share/">
                  buying
                 </a>
                 ; please read the section about how we help you buy.
                </p>
                <p>
                 If you would like us to help you buy or sell a
                 <a href="http://yachtfractions.co.uk/Web/boat-buying-and-selling-services/">
                  whole boat
                 </a>
                 we have a range of expertise especially in the Greek area.
                </p>
