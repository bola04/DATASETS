       <h2>
        About Lowara
       </h2>
       <p>
        Xylem’s Lowara brand is leading in providing long term economical solutions for pumping and circulating clean or contaminated water. The main industries served are:
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Residential and commercial building services
         </strong>
         : circulation in heating and cooling systems, domestic hot water, water supply, waste water handling and fire protection solutions
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Public utilities
         </strong>
         : water supply and district heating solutions
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Agriculture
         </strong>
         : irrigation systems solutions
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Aquaculture
         </strong>
         : water transport solutions
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Mining
         </strong>
         : water supply and dewatering solutions
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Oil and gas industry
         </strong>
         : heavy duty water transport solutions
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          General industry
         </strong>
         : water supply, pressure boosting including boiler feeding, washing equipment
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        Lowara pumps, drives and packaged solutions like pressure booster sets and wastewater lifting stations are all developed with low life cycle cost in mind. That means robust, high efficient, easy to install and service products with a long service life.
       </p>
       <p>
        To make it easy to work with Lowara products, we offer a package of tools, including pump selection software and applications, circulator replacement guides and CAD &amp; BIM files online.
       </p>
