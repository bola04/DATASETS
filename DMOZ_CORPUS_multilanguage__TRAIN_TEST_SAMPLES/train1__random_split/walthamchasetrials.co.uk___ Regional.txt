          <td>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Practice Trial
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            Waltham Chase MCC are holding a Practice Trial on FRIDAY 3rd August at Oakridge Copse, Grimstead Rd, Alderbury. (Yellow Arrows from Post Code SP5 3RQ)
           </p>
           <p>
            The venue is open from 1.00pm through to 7.00pm and you can ride for as little or long as you like during these times.
           </p>
           <p>
            We have created a number of new sections with the aid of a digger and carried out a lot of "clearing".
           </p>
           <p>
            There will be sections marked out for you to ride but there will be no marking.
           </p>
           <p>
            All welcome, with day licences available for non-members.
           </p>
           <p>
            Entry fees are £10.00 for adults and £5.00 for youth riders.
           </p>
           <p>
            There is no catering van.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             The St Paul Trophy Trial
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p style="margin: 0px; line-height: normal;">
            <span style="margin: 0px; color: black; font-size: 10pt;">
             The ACU Southern Centre, St Paul Trophy Trial is being hosted by Waltham Chase MCC at Lythe Farm, Limekiln Lane, Ramsdean, on Sunday 5
             <sup>
              th
             </sup>
             August, with a start time of 10.30.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="margin: 0px; color: black; font-size: 10pt;">
             Post code GU32 1QN will get you close and then look out for the “Yellow Arrows”.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="margin: 0px; color: black; font-size: 10pt;">
             All proceeds from this event are donated to the ACU “Ben Fund”, making this a great opportunity to support a worthy charity whilst having a ride at a different venue.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="margin: 0px; color: black; font-size: 10pt;">
             There are classes for all solo riders.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="margin: 0px; color: black; font-size: 10pt;">
             The St Paul Trophy, presented to the best Expert rider, was donated to the Southern Centre ACU back in 1953, some 65years ago, and is engraved with a host of memorable names from the past, including the Rickman’s, Vic Eastwood and more recently Neal Hubbard, Andy Blackman, Brian Francis, Ben Skinner with the current holder being Tom Mcabe.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="margin: 0px; color: black; font-size: 10pt;">
             As always offers to Observe are appreciated in advance.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="margin: 0px; color: black; font-size: 10pt;">
             Sam’s Catering will be on site providing hot and cold refreshments.
            </span>
           </p>
