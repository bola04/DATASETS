     <h1>
      Cheshire Nursery Garden Center and Florist
     </h1>
     <p>
      Cheshire Nursery Garden Center &amp; Florist was established in 1906. It is one of Connecticut's leading garden centers. We carry everything you need from A to Z. Whether you are looking for trees for your yard, flowers for your garden or a centerpiece for your table, our staff will be there to help you. We are proud of our incomparable customer service. We deliver fresh cut flowers daily and nationwide. We have a large selection of trees, annuals, perennials, flowering shrubs, houseplants, mulches, seeds, tools and more!
      <strong>
       Cheshire Nursery – better quality, better service.
      </strong>
     </p>
