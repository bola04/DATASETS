        <p>
         If you are visiting Aberdeen then one of the best and most convenient ways of seeing the city is by taxi. Aberdeen taxis offer affordable fares on long and short journeys, airport transfers and special occasion hire. We can provide a minibus if you are part of a large group. If you have a colleague or competitor visiting your business then we can pick them up from the airport or the railway station and deliver them direct to you or to their accommodation.
        </p>
        <p>
         Aberdeen taxies are available twenty four hours a day seven days a week, we never sleep and are there to take your calls.
        </p>
        <p>
         When it comes to paying for your taxi ride or transfer, companies generally accept cash. When you travel with Aberdeen taxis you are charged by a meter in the car unless a fixed rate has been agreed. If you have chosen airport taxi transfer you will not be charged extra if your plane is delayed because we keep track of its progress. Once you have landed the price includes forty minutes waiting time for you to meet up with the driver. When you are met by our driver in the arrival hall every ten minutes that goes beyond the forty minutes already accounted for is charged at £5. Once you make contact with our driver you will need to pay them the car parking fee before you leave the airport.
        </p>
        <p>
         We offer airport taxi transfers to and from Aberdeen, Glasgow, Edinburgh and Inverness Airports and to and from the city’s railway stations. Our drivers only use high quality vehicles that are cleaned and freshened on a regular basis. In addition to our general taxi and transfer services we offer minibuses, executive cars and a chauffeur service when required. We employ only experienced drivers with a detailed knowledge of the city of Aberdeen and the surrounding area. We accept all major credit cards or you can simply pay the driver in cash at the end of your journey.
        </p>
