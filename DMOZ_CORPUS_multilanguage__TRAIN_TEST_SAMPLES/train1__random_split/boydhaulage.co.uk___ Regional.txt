      <h1>
       Boyd Haulage
      </h1>
      <p>
       Boyd Haulage is a family run fallen stock, animal by-product and food waste collection business serving farms, food processing/retail outlets, and the wider agriculture industry.
      </p>
      <p>
       Operating since 2005 and fully licensed by the Department of Agriculture (DARD) and the Northern Ireland Environment Agency, the Maghera based Boyd Haulage takes pride in providing a fast and efficient service to agriculture clients in Antrim, Derry/Londonderry, Cookstown, Dungannon and throughout County Tyrone.
      </p>
      <p>
       Boyd Haulage were previously contracted as collectors for Ulster Farm By-Products which ceased it services in fallen stock in 2012.
      </p>
      <p>
       Currently Boyd Haulage collects fallen animals, food processing and retail waste on behalf of Foyle Proteins who are part of the Foyle Food Group.
      </p>
      <p>
       Boyd Haulage collects within two working days:
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        All ages and categories of fallen stock
       </li>
       <li>
        Wheelie bins and skips
