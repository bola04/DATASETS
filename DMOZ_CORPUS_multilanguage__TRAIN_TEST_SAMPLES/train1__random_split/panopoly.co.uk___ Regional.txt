         <p style="text-align: left;">
          Panopoly UK specializes in importing giftware for home and garden, children’s gifts and toys.
         </p>
         <p>
          We are proud of our product range and the quality of our service. We are a giftware wholesaler for our registered traders but offer some of our products for retail.
         </p>
         <p>
          There is a very strong animal theme to our range. Among our most popular lines are the famous ‘pot hangers’ which hang on garden pots for indoor or outdoor use. With well over 100 different styles, we have a pot hanger for every taste.
         </p>
         <p>
          We have an extensive range of animal wind chimes and garden decorations such as signs, stakes, ornaments, pond floaters, tree faces and much more. Added recently are ranges of gnome and fairy garden items. Also we have wooden jigsaw puzzles and baby shower gifts.
         </p>
         <p>
          Recently we have added a range of puzzle trinket boxes with a ‘secret key’.
         </p>
         <p>
          Our spring animal mobiles have been a major success. These decorations hang from a ceiling and their happy smiley faces make them a great addition to your child’s room.
          <br/>
          These springy animal baby mobiles are available to trade and retail customers.
         </p>
         <p>
          Our product range is enormous and can be daunting. We have tried to make this site as easy to navigate as possible, but with thousands of different products, it can help to have some advice.
          <br/>
          Please feel free to phone us, we’ll look forward to hearing from you.
         </p>
