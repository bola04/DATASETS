         <h1 class="entry-title">
          Who We Are
         </h1>
        </header>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          The ‘Award Winning’
          <strong>
           <span style="color: orange;">
            Walking Theatre Company
           </span>
          </strong>
          is based in rural
          <span style="color: orange;">
           <strong>
            Argyll
           </strong>
          </span>
          and produce acclaimed work all over Scotland. Our
          <span style="color: orange;">
           <strong>
            unique brand of interactive ‘walking’ theatre
           </strong>
          </span>
          literally takes our audiences on a journey, both physically and creatively, walking through any environment, from forest, Clachan, Kirk yard, garden or school.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Our skilled team of
          <span style="color: orange;">
           <strong>
            professional actors
           </strong>
          </span>
          gently draw audiences into the drama, making a
          <span style="color: orange;">
           <strong>
            fun, interactive spontaneous event!
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          We use theatre in any context, with a roster of
          <span style="color: orange;">
           <strong>
            off-the-shelf plays
           </strong>
          </span>
          including
          <span style="color: orange;">
           <strong>
            Macbeth
           </strong>
          </span>
          ,
          <span style="color: orange;">
           <strong>
            Robin Hood
           </strong>
          </span>
          and
          <span style="color: orange;">
           <strong>
            World War Two cookery demos
           </strong>
          </span>
          , to
          <span style="color: orange;">
           <strong>
            bespoke theatre
           </strong>
          </span>
          that tells a place or a
          <span style="color: orange;">
           <strong>
            community’s story
           </strong>
          </span>
          through its
          <span style="color: orange;">
           <strong>
            history and heritage
           </strong>
          </span>
          , to conference ‘shorts’ and
          <span style="color: orange;">
           <strong>
            training,
           </strong>
          </span>
          and rather
          <span style="color: orange;">
           <strong>
            wicked Murder Mystery parties and events
           </strong>
          </span>
          .
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          As a theatre ‘without walls’,
          <span style="color: orange;">
           <strong>
            your environment becomes our stage
           </strong>
          </span>
          , creating an
          <span style="color: orange;">
           <strong>
            interactive event
           </strong>
          </span>
          full of fun and energy
          <span style="color: orange;">
           <b>
            , suitable for everyone
           </b>
          </span>
          , bringing a place, a time, and a group to life,
          <span style="color: orange;">
           <b>
            no matter where the location
           </b>
          </span>
          , and whatever the weather!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Our work is
          <span style="color: orange;">
           <b>
            high quality
           </b>
          </span>
          , and
          <span style="color: orange;">
           <b>
            low impact on your environment
           </b>
          </span>
          , as we drop in and out with minimum fuss, and high impact in audience engagement, providing a
          <span style="color: orange;">
           <b>
            fantastic experience for all!
           </b>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span style="color: orange;">
           <strong>
            Contact us to find out more, and fly us into your event today!
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://thewalkingtheatrecompany.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/twtc_banner_logo.png">
           <img alt="PageLines- twtc_banner_logo.png" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2768" height="175" src="http://thewalkingtheatrecompany.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/twtc_banner_logo.png" width="152"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="color: #141823; text-align: center;">
          <strong>
           The Walking Theatre Company
          </strong>
          are delighted (and very proud) to have taken
          <br/>
          <strong>
           The Scottish Business Pledge
          </strong>
          !
         </p>
         <p style="color: #141823; text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://thewalkingtheatrecompany.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/Scottish-Business-Pledge-Full-RGB.jpg">
           <img alt="Scottish Business Pledge - Full RGB" class="aligncenter wp-image-5006" height="91" sizes="(max-width: 226px) 100vw, 226px" src="http://thewalkingtheatrecompany.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/Scottish-Business-Pledge-Full-RGB.jpg" srcset="https://thewalkingtheatrecompany.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/Scottish-Business-Pledge-Full-RGB.jpg 1710w, https://thewalkingtheatrecompany.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/Scottish-Business-Pledge-Full-RGB-300x121.jpg 300w, https://thewalkingtheatrecompany.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/Scottish-Business-Pledge-Full-RGB-1024x413.jpg 1024w, https://thewalkingtheatrecompany.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/Scottish-Business-Pledge-Full-RGB-624x251.jpg 624w" width="226"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="color: #141823; text-align: center;">
          <strong>
           The Scottish Business Pledge
          </strong>
          is
          <em>
           “a voluntary commitment made by companies in Scotland to uphold some of the best in modern business practices and to embrace the principles of fairness, equality, opportunity and innovation.”
          </em>
         </p>
         <p style="color: #141823; text-align: center;">
          <span style="color: #141823;">
           Find out more about the
          </span>
          <span style="color: #627aad;">
           #ScotBizPledge
          </span>
          <span style="color: #141823;">
           by visiting
          </span>
          <a href="https://www.facebook.com/l.php?u=https%3A%2F%2Fscottishbusinesspledge.scot%2F&amp;h=MAQGBCAnvAQGoElg2pQi2M-ohpFrhETdbGnzCGAOECOn-LQ&amp;enc=AZMTOm9AlgI362XXJgvw_dTjxbXFnUcTBZdXWuQUnuzzh4qQsh_mQhDNxSSDlfA8HmRPzOJKISibFcrS05fyK6pdk-copJjTS4Ft1kFoJCqv7ou-xqDUW3J5G-X5oOlOoLbPui-LG7Daf0mcELbud_ZIVkrTweCVmrcSpb82N01rdhx23kzG_vPfEOsUlPE29Erbk2bdmD0gE7z8-FGRkINe&amp;s=1" rel="nofollow noopener" style="color: #141823;" target="_blank">
           https://scottishbusinesspledge.scot/
          </a>
         </p>
