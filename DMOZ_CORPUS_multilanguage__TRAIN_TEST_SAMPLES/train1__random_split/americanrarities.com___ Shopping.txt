      <h1 class="homeTitle">
       We Buy Rare Coins, Gold &amp; Silver Bullion and US Paper Currency
      </h1>
      <p class="homeBodyText">
       We regularly purchase US coins, foreign coins, paper money and 
 
 				bullion material from coin collectors across the country.  We buy rare coin collections of all types and 
 
 				sizes, and offer free appraisals on all collections our buyers examine for purchase.
      </p>
      <p class="homeBodyText">
       Being professional coin dealers, our 
 				staff works hard to make the strongest	offer possible 
 
 				on every coin collection we evaluate. Unlike other coin dealers, we do not "cherry pick" collections - 
 				our buyers are interested in the 
 
 				most common and 
 
 				mundane coins, as well as the rarest and most esoteric pieces.
      </p>
      <p class="homeBodyText">
       If you're considering selling your coin 
 
 				collection, 
 
 				bullion or paper money, or are interested in 
 
 				learning more about how selling to American Rarities can benefit you, please
       <a href="/contact-a-rare-coin-buyer">
        contact us
       </a>
       .
      </p>
     </div>
     <div id="midpageContent">
      <h2 id="midpageTitle">
       Don't sell your U.S. or foreign coin collection 
 
 			for less than it is worth.
       <br/>
       Our expert coin buyers are ready to pay you top dollar for your 
 
 			rare coins.
      </h2>
      <p class="bodyText">
       As coin dealers, we are constantly looking 
 
 				to buy from coin collectors to meet our clients' rare coin needs. To facilitate this, we provide
       <strong>
        <a href="/foreign-and-us-rare-coin-values">
         free appraisals
        </a>
       </strong>
       for all rare coin collections we inspect for potential purchase. 
 
 				We are interested in all coins with extra collectible or bullion 
 
 				value - US and world, ancient and modern, common or rare. Our expert buyers are ready to view your collection and make you an 
 
 				immediate, no-obligation offer.
      </p>
      <h2 id="howProcess">
       Our Buying Process
      </h2>
      <h4 class="alignLeft" id="howWeEvaluate">
       How We Appraise Rare Coin Collections
      </h4>
      <p class="bodyText">
       The first step of getting an appraisal and offer on your 
 
 				rare coins is to provide us with information 
 
 				about your collection. It's as easy as emailing us pictures or an 
 
 				inventory list of your coins 
 
 				(partial lists are fine), or just telling us what you have over 
 
 				the phone. One of our expert coin buyers 
 
 				will follow up with you about any questions we may have or further information we need. 
 
 				He will then price your collection, 
 
 				using all available resources to ensure an accurate appraisal.
      </p>
      <p class="bodyText">
       Once the appraisal is completed, we can 
 
 				determine the best way to arrange the purchase of your 
 
 				collection. This involves 
 
 				either a personal meeting with one of our rare coin buyers, or shipping your coins in to us 
 				for sale. In either case, you are guaranteed the highest 
 
 				value possible for your rare coin collection.
      </p>
      <div id="howMontage">
       <img alt="Mixed US and world coins" src="/images/horizontal-mixed-coins.png" title="Our buyers purchase all types of US and world coin collections"/>
      </div>
      <h4 id="homePersonal">
       Personal Visit
      </h4>
      <p class="bodyText">
       In many cases, our rare coin dealers can come to you. Our buyers regularly travel throughout the country attending coin shows, 
 
 				as well as appraising and purchasing collections from private coin collectors just like you. 
 
 				The collection does not need to be organized or presented in any 
 
 				particular manner - we will examine every coin, album or set in 
 
 				turn, in whatever order they may be in. Once our evaluation is complete, we will make you an offer for your coins on the spot. 
 
 				Immediate payment is standard.
      </p>
      <h4 class="alignLeft">
       Insured Mail-In Service
      </h4>
      <p class="bodyText">
       It is not always possible to visit everyone who has coins to sell. Some collections are too small to 
 
 				warrant a visit, or in other cases the travel expense is prohibitive.
      </p>
      <p class="bodyText">
       To address this issue, American Rarities offers a fully insured mail-in service.  You can ship your 
 
 				coins directly to our office, fully insured, and our buyers will appraise your material.  Immediate payment is customary, 
 
 				and if you decide not to sell, we will return your collection at our expense.
      </p>
      <h2>
       We Buy More Than Just Rare Coins
      </h2>
      <p class="bodyText">
       American Rarities also buys gold and silver bullion, US paper currency, fine watches and 
 
 				jewelry, old US stamp collections, and much more.
      </p>
      <p class="bodyText">
       We are collectors at heart, and appreciate more than just rare coins.  If you, 
 
 				or a collector you know, have other collectibles 
 
 				you wish to sell, please advise us as to the nature of your collection.  We will get back to you with detailed information 
 
 				you need to understand the true value of your collectible treasures.
      </p>
      <p class="bodyText">
       If you are ready to sell your rare coin collection, or have questions that need answers, then 
 
 				assistance is just a click or phone call away.  To send us an email, simply fill out
       <a href="/contact-a-rare-coin-buyer">
        this form
       </a>
       to tell us a little 
 
 				about what you have.  You can also reach us, toll free, at
       <strong>
        1-800-622-5680
       </strong>
       .
      </p>
