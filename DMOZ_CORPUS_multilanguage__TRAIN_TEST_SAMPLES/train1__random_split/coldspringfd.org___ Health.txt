          <h2>
           Welcome to Cold Spring Fire Co. No 1
          </h2>
          <p>
           The Cold Spring Fire Company No. 1 is located in the Village of Cold Spring, Putnam County New York.  We proudly protect 3500 people living in an area of 5 square miles.  We operate out of a headquarters located at 154 Main Street that protects a primarily residential area. Our department is a public department whose members are on a volunteer status.  We have an ISO rating of 5.
          </p>
          <p>
           The Cold Spring Fire Company has a total of 125 men and woman and are led by Chief Matt Steltz and President Michael Bowman.  We respond on approximately 150 calls per year providing fire protection to the residents of the Villages of Cold Spring, Nelsonville and the Philipstown/Cold Spring Fire District. For information on joining, call the Firehouse at 845-265-9241 or email at:

         In Memoriam
        </a>
       </h2>
       <div class="entry-meta entry-meta-top">
        <p>
         Wednesday, June 6, 2018
         <span class="posted-in">
          Posted in

       <h2 class="entry-title">
        <a href="http://coldspringfd.org/cold-spring-fire-company-junior-firefighter-academy-to-host-a-parade/" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to Cold Spring Fire Company Junior Firefighter Academy Celebrates 15 Years with Parade">
         Cold Spring Fire Company Junior Firefighter Academy Celebrates 15 Years with Parade
        </a>
       </h2>
       <div class="entry-meta entry-meta-top">
        <p>
         Tuesday, August 1, 2017
         <span class="posted-in">
          Posted in
          <a href="http://coldspringfd.org/http:/coldspringfd.org/juniorfirefighteracademy/uncategorized/" rel="category tag">
           Uncategorized
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </div>
       <!-- .entry-meta-top -->
       <div class="entry-content self-clear">
        <p>
         On Monday, July 24, 2017, the Cold Spring Fire Company reached another milestone by hosting its 15
         <sup>
          th
         </sup>
         Junior Firefighter Academy. Open to 55 local children, the week-long camp allows children a glimpse into what it’s like to be a firefighter. They receive instruction on the history of the fire service, fire prevention, the science of firefighting, CPR and first aid, the importance of physical training and teamwork among other things. The program, co-sponsored by Philipstown Recreation and run by the Cold Spring Fire Company, is free to all who attend, thanks to generous donations from the community.
        </p>
        <p>
         To commemorate the Academy’s 15
         <sup>
          th
         </sup>
         anniversary, graduates of this year’s academy marched in a parade down Main Street on Friday, July 28, 2017. The parade, which started at 5:10 p.m., drew a supportive crowd from the community. After the parade the fire department hosted a large barbecue for  the graduates and their parents.
        </p>
        <p>
         For more information, you can contact the firehouse at 845-265-9241 or visit their website at
         <a href="http://www.coldspringfd.org">
          www.coldspringfd.org
         </a>
        </p>
