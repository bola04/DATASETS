      <h4>
       Pride in independence
      </h4>
      <p>
       Whatever the name above the door, it is difficult to know these days what 
 type of business you are dealing with.  Many funeral directors are now part 
 of multi-national companies, with shareholders and profit as their driving force.
      </p>
      <p>
       James Harper Independent Funeral Directors are the only truly independent funeral directors 
 and a family run business.
      </p>
      <p>
       We, at James Harper, are a dedicated team of experienced funeral personnel.  
 Our aim is to offer you a professional, open and honest service to your 
 individual requirements from the simplest funeral to the grandest.
      </p>
      <p>
       We feel that we have a responsibility to offer you the widest range of products and 
 services available, we expect our customers to ask about cost and quality.
      </p>
     </div>
     <div id="content-mid">
      <h4>
       We understand
      </h4>
      <p>
       We understand that for our customers a funeral is an emotional but special 
 event that can never be repeated.  We will try to accommodate any request 
 in order to make the day a special occasion in memory of your loved one.
      </p>
      <p>
       As a responsible funeral director, we feel very strongly that we are part of 
 the Bromley community and, as such, we are involved in several sponsorship 
 programs that we hope benefit everybody, including the young and the elderly.
