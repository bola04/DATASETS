                     <h2 class="waylaytwo-entry-title" itemprop="headline">
                      <a href="http://www.trebarwith-hotel.co.uk/the-best-attractions-in-newquay/" rel="bookmark">
                       The Best Attractions in Newquay
                      </a>
                     </h2>
                     <div class="waylaytwo-entry-meta waylaytwo-entry-meta-below-title post-meta-inline">
                      <span class="waylaytwo-posted-on waylaytwo-meta">
                       <a href="http://www.trebarwith-hotel.co.uk/the-best-attractions-in-newquay/" rel="bookmark" title="7:09 pm">
                        <time class="waylaytwo-entry-date published" datetime="2018-05-02T19:09:14+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
                         May 2, 2018
                        </time>
                        <time class="waylaytwo-updated" datetime="2018-05-14T08:13:16+00:00" itemprop="dateModified">

                        </time>
                       </a>
                      </span>
                     </div>

                    <div class="waylaytwo-entry-content" itemprop="text">
                     <p>
                      There is more to Newquay than just the sandy beaches. Although the coastal city located in the southwest of London is known for its pristine beaches, there is more to the surfing capital of England than just the main attraction. Let’s dive deep and explore what this
                      <a href="http://www.trebarwith-hotel.co.uk/">
                       beautiful city
                      </a>
                      has to offer.
                     </p>
                     <ol>
                      <li>
                       <h2>
                        <strong>
                         Ahoy Matey
                        </strong>
                       </h2>
                      </li>
                     </ol>
                     <p>
                      Have you ever wanted to live the Pirate life; sail the seven seas and unearth treasure from sunken ships. Well, Newquay has you covered with one of the best immersive Pirate experiences in the world. The Pirate’s quest is one of the best well put together Pirate experience tours that you’ll ever find; the quest has everything, from special effects to trained artists to a complete intense and fun-filled experience. We all know you have a captain Jack Sparrow inside you, waiting for the right time to shine.
                     </p>
                     <p class="responsive-video-wrap wpex-clr">
                      <iframe allow="autoplay; encrypted-media" allowfullscreen="" height="551" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/HX7aoGUIEmo?feature=oembed" width="980">
                      </iframe>
                     </p>
                     <ol start="2">
                      <li>
                       <h2>
                        <strong>
                         Newquay Zoo
                        </strong>
                       </h2>
                      </li>
                     </ol>
                     <p>
                      Zoos are usually an attraction to young families and animal enthusiasts, but we urge everyone who visits the coastal city to check out the Newquay Zoo. The zoo is located in the lakeside gardens that host a sub-tropical climate. The zoo is home to over 130 species and is known for winning quite a few awards over the years.
                     </p>
                     <ol start="3">
                      <li>
                       <h2>
                        <strong>
                         Blue Reef Aquarium
                        </strong>
                       </h2>
                      </li>
                     </ol>
                     <p>
                      With a strong coastline and marine ecosystem; it is no wonder that Newquay has a beautiful underwater aquarium. The south-western coastline of England is along the
                      <a href="https://www.encyclopedia.com/places/oceans-continents-and-polar-regions/oceans-and-continents/atlantic-ocean" rel="nofollow">
                       Atlantic Ocean
                      </a>
                      and houses some of the most beautiful marine life-forms in the world. Travel underneath the blue lagoon to witness some of these mighty creatures at the Blue Reef Aquarium.
                     </p>
                     <p style="text-align: center;">
                      <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-191" height="430" src="http://www.trebarwith-hotel.co.uk/uploaded-files/2018/05/download-4.jpg" width="750"/>
                     </p>
                     <ol start="4">
                      <li>
                       <h2>
                        <strong>
                         Family Day
                        </strong>
                       </h2>
                      </li>
                     </ol>
                     <p>
                      If you have a young family and you’re looking to have fun with the kids but also let your child in you have a little fun as well then head on over to the Holywell Bay Fun Park. The park does not have entry charges; so all you gotta do is walk in and not look back. There are a number of attractions to keep you and your kids’ active throughout the day. Besides having fun attractions, the park also has a restaurant and an ice cream shop.
                     </p>
                     <ol start="5">
                      <li>
                       <h2>
                        <strong>
                         Land Train
                        </strong>
                       </h2>
                      </li>
                     </ol>
                     <p>
                      There is nothing like exploring the city via a bus tour but Newquay has an Ace up their sleeve, and that is their Land train that runs through the city. The land train is the perfect way to get to know the city better.
                     </p>

                       3 Safety Tips for Swimming at the Beach
                      </a>
                     </h2>
                     <div class="waylaytwo-entry-meta waylaytwo-entry-meta-below-title post-meta-inline">
                      <span class="waylaytwo-posted-on waylaytwo-meta">
                       <a href="http://www.trebarwith-hotel.co.uk/3-safety-tips-for-swimming-at-the-beach/" rel="bookmark" title="7:08 pm">
                        <time class="waylaytwo-entry-date published" datetime="2018-05-02T19:08:57+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
                         May 2, 2018
                        </time>
                        <time class="waylaytwo-updated" datetime="2018-05-14T08:13:08+00:00" itemprop="dateModified">

                        </time>
                       </a>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                    </header>
                    <!-- .entry-header -->
                    <div class="waylaytwo-entry-content" itemprop="text">
                     <p>
                      Going to the beach can be one of the best decisions you make. The wind hitting your face and the calm waters hitting your feet; the beach always represents a sense of serenity to everyone. The beach is still a location where things can go horribly wrong, so it is good to take precautions and take necessary steps to deal with things before they get out of hand; this is especially important if you are taking little ones along with you to the beach.
                     </p>
                     <p style="text-align: center;">
                      <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-190" height="366" src="http://www.trebarwith-hotel.co.uk/uploaded-files/2018/05/018766d277f10fc18a1397414c5bb49d.jpg" width="650"/>
                     </p>
                     <ol>
                      <li>
                       <h2>
                        <strong>
                         Don’t mix water and Alcohol
                        </strong>
                       </h2>
                      </li>
                     </ol>
                     <p>
                      Most beaches are filled with pubs and clubs that mix you the perfect drink. And there will be boards on the road asking you not to drink and drive. But it is important for you not to drink and swim. It is really important that you don’t heed to your inner Aquaman and try swimming while drunk; for starters your lack of inhibition and self-control can cause you to drift far away from the shore, making it difficult for people to help you out if the sun begins to set. Another cause of worry is that people find it difficult to perceive depth, which makes it all the riskier as you venture further into the waters.
                     </p>
                     <ol start="2">
                      <li>
                       <h2>
                        <strong>
                         Cramps Cramps Cramps
                        </strong>
                       </h2>
                      </li>
                     </ol>
                     <p>
                      There are a number of reasons that people cramp while
                      <a href="http://www.trebarwith-hotel.co.uk/">
                       swimming
                      </a>
                      ; one of the major reasons is the amount of food that they had recently. The beach is filled with great places to eat which can push you to eat a heavy meal as soon as you reach; we suggest you go for a swim, build up your appetite and then hit the eateries and not the other way around. Once you start cramping, it can go from being a fun day at the beach to a stressful day at the hospital.
                     </p>
                     <p class="responsive-video-wrap wpex-clr">
                      <iframe allow="autoplay; encrypted-media" allowfullscreen="" height="551" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/fhJC7XeXVUE?feature=oembed" width="980">
                      </iframe>
                     </p>
                     <ol start="3">
                      <li>
                       <h2>
                        <strong>
                         Where are you?
                        </strong>
                       </h2>
                      </li>
                     </ol>
                     <p>
                      Unless you have a
                      <a href="http://www.docastaway.com/private-beach-vacations" rel="nofollow">
                       private beach
                      </a>
                      or know a great location that is secluded from the crowds, the beach can quickly become a daunting place filled with countless people. So if you have kids with you, make sure they understand the golden rule of going to the beach. The golden rule of going to the beach is to always be aware of where everyone is. If you have kids, then assign someone to be with them at all times, especially at times when they are going swimming in the waters. If it is the first time on the beach as a young family, then we suggest that you go to the part of the beach that is secured by lifeguards who can help you out, if something goes wrong.
                     </p>
