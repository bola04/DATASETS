                  Hampden-Middlesex-Monroe-Silver Spring (HMMS) is a 501(c)3, non-profit, volunteer based Central PA soccer organization offering a strong character centered soccer program for children and families, developing sportsmanship and athletic skills, promoting
                  <em>
                   The Beautiful Game
                  </em>
                  and making memories that last a lifetime.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  HMMS offers a comprehensive soccer program for children with the following offerings:
                 </p>
                 <ul>
                  <li>
                   Recreational program for children 4 to 18
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   Local, State, Regional and up to National Travel programs for players 8 to 18
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   Eagle FC program for premier advanced level players 8 to 18
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   TOPSoccer program designed for school aged children with special needs who are supported with TOPS Buddies.
                  </li>
                 </ul>
                 <p>
                  The recreational program is Central PA’s largest recreational program with over 1,100 players. The recreational program is the foundation of HMMS and offers play in both the Fall and Spring. As part of the player registration, the recreational teams also participate in the annual Fall Friendship Festival.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  HMMS is also committed in developing those select players who wish to play at the next level through our travel and Eagle FC programs. Our coaching staff is comprised of ex-professional players, college, parochial and high schools coaches. Our Director of Coaching, Gino DiFlorio, consistently attracts the areas top soccer and coaching talent who have competed at the highest levels of competition – locally, regionally and nationally. HMMS consistently has young men and young ladies top ranked teams at the State, Region and National level from our Eagle FC program.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  We attract players from all over Central Pennsylvania including Camp Hill, Carlisle, Chambersburg, Dillsburg, Hanover, Mechanicsburg, New Cumberland, State College, Hershey, Palmyra, Harrisburg, Linglestown, and York.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  As a registered member Eastern Pennsylvania Youth Soccer (EPYSA), our travel teams compete in the highest divisions in the Eastern Development Program (EDP) Futures , and the Central Pennsylvania Youth Soccer League (CPYSL). Our Premier teams play in CPYSL, EDP, Atlantic Premier League (APL), Philadelphia Area Girls Soccer (PAGS), Eastern Regional League (ERL) and National League.
                 </p>
