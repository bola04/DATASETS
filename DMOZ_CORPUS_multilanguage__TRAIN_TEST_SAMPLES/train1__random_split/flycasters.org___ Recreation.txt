              <h2 class="art-postheader">
               <a href="/meetings/programs-info/469-august-program-ken-hanley-fly-fishing-for-bass">
                August Program - Ken Hanley - Fly fishing for BASS
               </a>
              </h2>
             </div>
             <div class="art-postcontent clearfix">
              <div class="art-article">
               <p style="text-align: center;">
                <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
                 <b>
                  <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
                   <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">
                    Largemouth Bass: Building A Productive Strategy …
                   </span>
                  </span>
                 </b>
                 <br/>
                </span>
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                <span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">
                 They’re one of America’s first species ever caught with a fly. Yes indeed -- our flyrodding heritage is deeply connected to the largemouth bass. Ken’s presentation is a celebration of the natural history, tackle selection, and field techniques targeting this great gamefish. From farm ponds to lakes, massive reservoirs and the Delta, largemouth can be found in every county throughout the Golden State.
                </span>
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: center;">
                <img alt="Ken Hanley" height="272" src="/images/Ken_Hanley.jpg" width="207"/>
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                <span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">
                 Ken Hanley has been a fly fishing ambassador since the early 70’s. As a guide and instructor, he’s worked with thousands of students in the field. He was inducted into the FFF Hall of Fame (NCC Chapter) in 2005.
                </span>
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                <span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">
                 He’s the author of seven fly fishing books. He also writes and photographs for both international and regional publications in the outdoor industry. In addition, his outreach to audiences includes videos celebrating both saltwater &amp; freshwater adventures.
                </span>
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                <span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">
                 He’s on Pro Staff with the following sponsors; SAGE Instructor, SIMMS, RIO Products Intl, Daiichi, Renzetti, Dr. Slick, Hatch, and Patagonia
                </span>
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: center;">
                <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
                 <strong>
                  Join us on August 8th
                 </strong>
                 - at the Moreland Apts meeting room - the northeast corner of Payne and Saratoga.  Doors open at 6:30pm and the meeting begins at 7:15
                </span>
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: center;">
                Park across the street and use the crosswalk.  Please lock valuable out of sight - break-ins have occurred.
               </p>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">
                </span>
               </p>
              </div>

              <h2 class="art-postheader">
               <a href="/knowledge/seminars/468-double-haul-casting-clinic-july-28">
                Double Haul Casting Clinic July 28
               </a>
              </h2>
             </div>
             <div class="art-postcontent clearfix">
              <div class="art-article">
               <p style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt; text-align: center;">
                <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
                 <strong>
                  Learn the techniques to master the double haul.
                 </strong>
                </span>
               </p>
               <p style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt; text-align: justify;">
                <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
                 The double haul will add line speed and enable you to punch your fly through the wind, increase your overall distance, and take some of the workload off your rod hand. Learn Tony's
                 <b>
                  secret,
                 </b>
                 "Double" really means "Three".
                </span>
               </p>
               <p style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt; text-align: center;">
                <span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">
                 <img alt="tony yap nn" height="213" src="/images/tony_yap_nn.jpg" width="205"/>
                </span>
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
                 Tony asks that you bring your favorite fly rod, with a reel and weight forward floating line, appropriate leader and tippet and some yarn (no hooks). Bring a notebook and pen to take notes.
                </span>
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
                 <em>
                  <strong>
                   Be sure to bring protective eyewear.
                  </strong>
                 </em>
                 Sunglasses or eyeglasses - just as long as they cover your eyes. This is a requirement, without eye protection, you cannot attend the clinic.
                </span>
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: center;">
                <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">
                 <b>
                  <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
                   Clinic is FREE for members of FLYCASTERS
                  </span>
                 </b>
                </span>
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: center;">
                <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
                 *Parking at the ponds is $6 - payable at the kiosk - they do ticket non-payers
                </span>
               </p>
               <p style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt; text-align: center;">
                <span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: 'Calibri', 'sans-serif';">
                 For more information, contact Mason Seim at
                 <a href="mailto:msseim@aol.com">
                  msseim@aol.com
                 </a>
                </span>
               </p>
              </div>
