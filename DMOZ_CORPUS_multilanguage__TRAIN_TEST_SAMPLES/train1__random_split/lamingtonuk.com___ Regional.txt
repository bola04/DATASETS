       <h2>
        room2 Hometels
       </h2>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        <img alt="room2 Hammersmith" rel="3681" src="/media/2611/room-2-hammersmith-apart-hotel.jpg?width=651px&amp;height=465px" style="width: 651px; height: 465px;"/>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        <span class="notSoSmall">
         <strong>
          What's a hometel?
         </strong>
         It's a place with all the comforts of a home, the flexibility of an airbnb, with the security and services of a hotel.
        </span>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        <span class="notSoSmall">
         <a href="http://www.room2.com/hammersmith" target="_blank" title="room2 Hammersmith">
          <strong>
           room2 Hammersmith
          </strong>
         </a>
         is the first of the room2 hometel collection. Think of it as our Research &amp; Development Lab, a scaled-back version of a full Hometel. It is our hub to continuously test new products and services before taking the best bits to be developed across the rest of the collection.
        </span>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        <span class="notSoSmall">
         With this concept trialed and tested, we have launched the world's first full size hometel in
         <a href="http://www.room2.com/southampton" target="_blank" title="room2 Southampton">
          Southampton
         </a>
         with spectacular views over Queens Park and the harbour. Expect bigger rooms, a longer stay- check in and out at 2pm, and the ability to maintain your diet on your schedule with the use of the kitchens in rooms.
        </span>
       </p>
