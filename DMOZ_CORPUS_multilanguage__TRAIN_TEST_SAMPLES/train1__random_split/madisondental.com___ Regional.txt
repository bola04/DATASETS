           <h1 class="heading">
            Home
           </h1>
           <p>
            Since 1896,
            <strong>
             Madison Dental Group
            </strong>
            has been practicing dentistry in New York City, in fact, our practice is the longest continually running dental practice in Manhattan. Our practice offers a wide range of
            <a href="http://www.madisondental.com/services/general-dentistry/oral-surgery">
             general dental services
            </a>
            , with an emphasis on
            <a href="http://www.madisondental.com/services/cosmetic-dentistry/tooth-whitening">
             cosmetic dentistry
            </a>
            and
            <a href="http://www.madisondental.com/services/implant-dentistry">
             dental implants
            </a>
            . We offer comprehensive dental care, including
            <a href="http://www.madisondental.com/services/cosmetic-dentistry/tooth-whitening#whitening">
             whitening and bleaching
            </a>
            ,
            <a href="http://www.madisondental.com/services/cosmetic-dentistry/porcelain-veneers#porcelain_veneers">
             porcelain veneers
            </a>
            ,
            <a href="http://www.madisondental.com/services/cosmetic-dentistry/composite-bonding#tooth_colored_fillings">
             tooth-colored fillings
            </a>
            ,
            <a href="http://www.madisondental.com/services/cosmetic-dentistry/orthodontics#orthodontics">
             orthodontics
            </a>
            ,
            <a href="http://www.madisondental.com/services/general-dentistry/oral-surgery#crowns">
             crowns
            </a>
            ,
            <a href="http://www.madisondental.com/services/general-dentistry/bridges#bridges">
             bridges
            </a>
            ,
            <a href="http://www.madisondental.com/services/general-dentistry/preventive-dentistry#sealants">
             sealants
            </a>
            and
            <a href="http://www.madisondental.com/services/implant-dentistry">
             dental implants
            </a>
            , as well as general dentistry services such as
            <a href="http://www.madisondental.com/services/general-dentistry/periodontics#endodontics">
             root canals
            </a>
            ,
            <a href="http://www.madisondental.com/services/general-dentistry/oral-surgery#oral_surgery">
             wisdom teeth extraction
            </a>
            and treatment of
            <a href="http://www.madisondental.com/services/general-dentistry/periodontics#periodontics">
             gum disease
            </a>
            and jaw disorders.
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="" class="picRightnoborder2 size-full wp-image-68" height="125" src="http://www.madisondental.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/2eebf70265abc32526a8f0f40815cbf2.jpg" width="129"/>
            <strong>
             We also welcome children to our practice
            </strong>
            , with the goal of making their experience with us a pleasant one. With as little as one trip, you can start your journey toward a happier, healthier smile.
           </p>

            All of MDG's dentists are highly experienced, including our President,

            <a href="http://www.madisondental.com/our-doctors/our-doctors3#dr_mender">

            <a href="http://www.madisondental.com/our-doctors/our-doctors2#dr_harrison">
