              </span>
             </p>
             <p>

               <a href="/practice-areas">

               </a>
               , including:
              </span>
             </p>

               </a>
               <br/>
               <a href="/practice-areas/dependent-support-claims">
                Dependant Support Claims
               </a>
              </span>
             </p>

              <span>
               Our expertise extends to
               <a href="/practice-areas/applications-under-the-substitute-decisions-act">
                Guardianship Applications

               <a href="/practice-areas/powers-of-attorney-disputes">
                Powers of Attorney Disputes
               </a>
