        <a name="AMADOU">
        </a>
        <small>
         With
 their single ‘Bofou Safou’, The Malian duo Amadou &amp; Mariam are back
 with one of their trademark sensual and highly danceable gems. This new
 song is mixing African influences, disco and electro and giving a new
 sheen to Amadou &amp; Mariam music. Following up the electro-dance
 territory explored with producer Damon Albarn on ‘Sabali', it makes
 echo to Francis Bebey or William Oneyeabor who, very early on, started
 mixing traditional music and experimentations.
         <br/>
         <br/>
        </small>
        <small style="font-style: italic; color: rgb(102, 102, 102);">
         Après
 "Welcome to Mali" en 2009 et "Foliila" en 2012, Amadou &amp; Mariam
 reviennent avec un nouvel album "La Confusion", sorti le 22 septembre 2017 chez Because Music.
         <br/>
        </small>
