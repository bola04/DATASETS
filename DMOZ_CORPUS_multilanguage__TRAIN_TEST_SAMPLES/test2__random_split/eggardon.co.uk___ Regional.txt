                             18 different breeds of sheep including North Ronaldsay sheep which are 
 							more rare

 							programme so that there are lambs throughout the 
 							spring and summer into the Autumn. This ensures that 
 							we can give close attention to each and every one. Many of 
 							the sheep will feed from your hand and some will 
 							come for a walk, just like a dog.

 							markings. We  hope to have 
                             some Gloucester Old Spots another British Lop and  
                             large Black very soon.  All have individual characters and just 
 							love visitors, especially if you feed them fruit or 
 							vegetables.

 							throughout their 
 							lives to ensure uncompromised quality, flavour and 
 							succulence.
