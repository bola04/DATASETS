             <h1 class="uk-article-title">
              Welcome to Mercedes-Benz U.S. International, Inc. (MBUSI), Tuscaloosa County, Alabama
             </h1>
             <div>
              <div>
               <img alt="GOAL logo" height="131" src="/images/mbusi/system/GOAL-onwhite.png" style="margin: -14px 16px 0px -12px; float: left;" width="360"/>
              </div>
              <div style="float: right; width: 250px; margin-left: 20px;">
               <p>
                <a charset="utf-8" class="jce-file wf_file" dir="ltr" href="/component/html5flippingbook/publication/2015-impact-report/5" hreflang="en" lang="en" target="_blank">
                 <img alt="2015 ir page01 5d575" class="extra" dir="ltr" onmouseout="this.src='/images/20th-celebration/2015_ir_page01_5d575.jpg';" onmouseover="this.src='/images/20th-celebration/2015_ir_page01_click_overlay_5f164.jpg';" src="/images/20th-celebration/2015_ir_page01_5d575.jpg" style="margin: 0px 10px 3px 0px; float: left;" title="MBUSI Impact Report" usemap="#jcemap"/>
                </a>
               </p>
              </div>
              <p>
               Welcome to Tuscaloosa County Alabama, U.S.A, and the production location of the Mercedes-Benz
               <a class="extra" dir="ltr" href="http://www.mbusa.com/mercedes/vehicles/class/class-GLE/bodystyle-SUV" hreflang="en-GB" lang="en-GB" rel="noreferrer" target="_self" title="View Mercedes-Benz GLE SUV on WWW.MBUSA.COM">
                <i class="uk-icon-external-link">
                </i>
                <strong>
                 GLE SUV
                </strong>
                ,
               </a>
               <a class="extra" dir="ltr" href="https://www.mbusa.com/mercedes/vehicles/class/class-GLS/bodystyle-SUV" hreflang="en-GB" lang="en-GB" rel="noreferrer" target="_self" title="View Mercedes-Benz GLS SUV on WWW.MBUSA.COM">
                <i class="uk-icon-external-link">
                </i>
                <strong>
                 GLS SUV
                </strong>
               </a>
               , the
               <a class="extra" dir="ltr" href="https://www.mbusa.com/mercedes/vehicles/class/class-C/bodystyle-CPE" hreflang="en-GB" lang="en-GB" rel="noreferrer" target="_self" title="View Mercedes-Benz C-Class on WWW.MBUSA.COM">
                <i class="uk-icon-external-link">
                </i>
                <strong>
                 C-Class
                </strong>
               </a>
               for the North American market and the
               <a class="extra" dir="ltr" href="http://www.mbusa.com/mercedes/future/model/model-All_New_2016_GLE_Coupe" hreflang="en-GB" lang="en-GB" rel="noreferrer" target="_self" title="View Mercedes-Benz GLE Coupe on WWW.MBUSA.COM">
                <i class="uk-icon-external-link">
                </i>
                <strong>
                 GLE Coupe
                </strong>
               </a>
               .
              </p>
              <p>
               Mercedes-Benz U.S. International (MBUSI) started with a clean "sheet of paper" in terms of vehicle concept, in the way it would produce Mercedes-Benz quality in the design of the factory and in the establishment of a young corporate culture. The result is Mercedes-Benz vehicles that are continually setting the standard in the industry.
              </p>
              <p>
               We build the GLE SUV, GLS SUV, the C-Class and the GLE Coupe here in Alabama using Mercedes-Benz and MBUSI’s original production system which is a combination of American, Japanese, and German automotive best practices. This process contributes to the quality manufacturing of our products in a way that is safe, efficient, and predictable.
              </p>
              <p>
               With the arrival and build of our Mercedes-Benz vehicles - the dream has become a reality. Make your dreams come true! For more information about purchasing a Mercedes-Benz vehicle, please visit
               <a href="http://www.mbusa.com/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Visit the Mercedes-Benz USA Website">
                <i class="uk-icon-external-link">
                </i>
                <strong>
                 Mercedes-Benz U.S.A.
                </strong>
               </a>
               ,
               <a href="http://www.daimler.com/brands-and-products/our-products/passenger-cars/mercedes-benz" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Visit Daimler AG Website">
                <i class="uk-icon-external-link">
                </i>
                <strong>
                 Daimler AG
                </strong>
               </a>
               , and
               <a href="http://www5.mercedes-benz.com/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Visit the Mercedes-Benz Website">
                <i class="uk-icon-external-link">
                </i>
                <strong>
                 Mercedes-Benz
                </strong>
               </a>
               .
              </p>
