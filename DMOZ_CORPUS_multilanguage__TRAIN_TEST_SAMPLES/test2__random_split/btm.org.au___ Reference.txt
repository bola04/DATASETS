      <div id="opscolumninnerbox">
       <p class="body">
        Ride an authentic Ballarat tram [most over 100 years old] which ran  Ballarat's public transport until 1971. Buy real tickets from our volunteers in  authentic uniforms. Travel as people have been doing since 1887 on 1.3km of original track through the beautiful Botanical  Gardens on the west side of Lake Wendouree. Return trip 20 minutes.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <strong>
         Fares:
        </strong>
        Adult $5, Children $2.
        <br/>
        Also (Hop-on / Hop-off) All Day: Adult $8, Children $4.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <strong>
         Tickets:
        </strong>
        Cash Fares on the tram, EFTPOS at the depot.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <strong>
         When:
        </strong>
        Trams run every Saturday, Sunday, public holiday and during the Victorian  school holidays.  During July and August trams will be running in Wendouree Parade between 11am and 4pm. Our display area at the depot is open from  about 11am on operating days and on Tuesdays between 10am and 5pm subject to volunteer availability.
        <br/>

       </p>
