        Welcome to Lin
        <font face="Times New Roman">

       </p>
       <p class="MsoNormal">
        We have been selling vintage handkerchiefs for many years 
 and have shipped large and small vintage handkerchief orders all over the world.
       </p>
       <p class="MsoNormal">
        Our hankies have been featured in “Country Living” 
 Magazine, we have sold hankies to Boston Public (television show), and have been 
 a major supplier of vintage hankies and vintage linens to My Vintage Baby. We 
 also provided a custom order of vintage hankies for a wedding featured 
 in an issue of “Martha Stewart Weddings” magazine!
       </p>
       <p class="MsoNormal">
        If you are seeking a larger number or group of hankies, 
 please visit our other online shops at
        <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/LinsAntiques?ref=ss_profile">
         <b>
          <i>
           Etsy
          </i>
         </b>
        </a>
        , 
 or
        <i>
         <b>
          <a href="http://www.cyberattic.com/~lin/index.html">
           Cyberattic
          </a>
         </b>
         .
        </i>
       </p>
       <p align="left">
        We are an exclusively online shop with an emphasis on McCoy 
 pottery. We have been active in the antique business for a number of years 
 before deciding to go online. This is our twentieth year as an online business and
 it has been a very wonderful experience! We have "met" many terrific
 new friends.
       </p>
       <p align="left">
        We are avid collectors and dealers of McCoy pottery as evidenced
 by our large McCoy inventory. Due to customer requests, we have expanded our
 inventory to include other potteries such as Shawnee and Frankoma (and sneaked
 in some jewelry and other collectible items!).
       </p>
       <p align="left">
        Please come in and look around. Browse through our large
 inventory here and at our other online shop at
        <i>
         <a href="http://www.cyberattic.com/~lin/index.html">
          Cyberattic
         </a>
        </i>
        and our new shop at
        <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/LinsAntiques?ref=ss_profile">
         etsy
        </a>
        ! Our 
 etsy shop has many vintage hanky handkerchief lots that are not listed in our 
 other online shops. We hope you enjoy your visit!
