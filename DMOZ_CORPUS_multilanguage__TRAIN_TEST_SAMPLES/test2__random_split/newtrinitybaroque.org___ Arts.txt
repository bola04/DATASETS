              <font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="-1">
               by New Trinity Baroque's Artistic Director, Predrag Gosta
              </font>

             <p>
              <font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="-1">
               <img align="right" border="0" height="200" hspace="5" src="images/artists/600-PredragGosta-PhotoByPiaRabeaVornholt-July2009-DSC_5448-COLOR.jpg" width="300"/>
              </font>
              <font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="-1">
               For the past 20 years New Trinity Baroque has delighted audiences in Atlanta and further with its attractive programs, brilliantly performed on period instruments. The critics agree that New Trinity Baroque concerts are of consistently high quality. Described as "artistically satisfying" and "simply dazzling" (The Atlanta Journal-Constitution), New Trinity Baroque has become not only "Atlanta's most adventurous early music ensemble," but also "one of America's premier period groups."
              </font>
             </p>
             <p>
              <font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="-1">
               New Trinity Baroque remains commited in presenting outstanding programs of music written by some of the greatest composers of the Baroque period, and feature some of the brightest stars of early music today, using period instruments, stylistically appropriate voices, and historically informed techniques. Starting on September 23, he 2017/18 season will bring three concerts, which we hope that will delight you as well as entertain you.
              </font>
             </p>
             <p>
              <font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="-1">
