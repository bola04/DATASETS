          Moana 
           Beach Tourist Park
         </font>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p class="paragraph">
        Moana Beach Tourist Park is located less than 40 Kilometres south of the city of Adelaide.  It is an ideal base for exploring the many attractions along the Fleurieu Peninsula, including Kangaroo Island, and right on the doorstep of the McLaren Vale Wine Region famous around the world for award winning wines.
       </p>
       <p class="paragraph">
        Our Park is adjacent to Moana Beach, a beautiful beach popular for surfing enthusiasts and those wanting to pack up the car and drive right up to the water's edge for a fun day with the family. The beach is also patrolled by the Lifesaving Club over the summer months, and safe enough for tiny tots to swim with their parents in the shallows.
       </p>
       <p class="paragraph">
        We strive to be always friendly, offer sparkling clean amenities, and with fully self-contained Cabins as well as powered camping Sites we can provide an ideal environment to relax and revitalise your mind &amp; body.  With larger Sites and spacious grounds, bigger vehicles like 5th Wheelers, Buses &amp; longer vans are easily manoeuvred into position without encroaching onto the neighbouring sites.
       </p>
       <p class="paragraph">
        Come for a short visit to share the experience of Moana Beach Tourist Park, and we guarantee that you will want to extend your stay.  Most of our Guests do!
       </p>
       <p class="paragraph">
        Our Park was voted top 5 in South Australia for the recent "Australia's Best Holiday Park" competition by Turu.
       </p>
