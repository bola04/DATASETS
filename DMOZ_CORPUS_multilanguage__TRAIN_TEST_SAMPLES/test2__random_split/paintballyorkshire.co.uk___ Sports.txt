       <h1>
        Harrogate Paintball Centre
       </h1>
       <p>
        Harrogate’s only paintball venue and Yorkshire’s premier woodland site, we are committed to giving each and every customer a fun and memorable experience.
       </p>
       <p>
        With 10 exciting games zones in 50 acres of woodland we cater for up to 300 paintballers – and we’re just 20 minutes or less from Leeds, York and Harrogate!
       </p>
       <p>
        Established more than 20 years, our professional and friendly staff’s expertise gives customers a great time, at great value for money and with no hidden charges.
       </p>
       <p>
        High quality equipment and high staff to guest ratio means we can ensure a safe, secure environment at all times, allowing customers to really enjoy their experience. PLEASE BE AWARE that our goggles DO NOT accommodate all styles of spectacles, especially large or wide frames, hence we may not be able to provide you with goggles that fit over your spectacles.
       </p>
       <p>
        For younger guests, we offer low impact paintballing using smaller pellets to maintain the range whilst reducing the impact; all the adrenaline rush so the kids can have just as much fun and no worries for parents!
       </p>
       <p>
        And paintball is not all we do - try your hand at anything from archery, crossbows, airsoft sniper and pistol range to battling in a mini-tank!
       </p>
       <p>
        We arrange activities for corporate events or parties, as well as offering team building exercises, and stay open until dusk for a fun evening experience. Our services and activities can be tailored to suit you, providing catering for your party if required, although you are welcome to supply your party's food yourself.
       </p>
       <p>
        All our activities are fully insured and risk assessed for your peace of mind.
       </p>
       <p>
        To find out more, or to book your activity today, call, email or book online and let us give you a fun-filled and action-packed day to remember.
       </p>
