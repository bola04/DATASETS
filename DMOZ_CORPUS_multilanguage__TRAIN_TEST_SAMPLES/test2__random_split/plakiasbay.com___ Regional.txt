            <h2>
             Plakias Bay Hotel
            </h2>
            <p>
             600 meters from the village of Plakias at the end of the long sandy beach is the Plakias Bay Hotel in its peaceful surroundings with a mountain behind it and a view of the sea at the front. The beautiful turquoise water and golden sand 50 meters in front of the hotel invite you into their embrace.
            </p>
            <p>
             Around us are footpaths in between thyme bushes or through olive groves taking you to caves and other small beaches or to surrounding villages.
            </p>
            <p>
             10 minutes on foot , the village of Plakias offers a doctor, chemists, shops, supermarket, hairdressers, a laundry, taverns and restaurants, bars, coffee houses, bus service, rent car offices and money exchanges, taxi offices and tour offices offering excursions and boat trips.
            </p>
            <p>
             You will want nothing more.
            </p>

           <div class="right">
            <h2>
             Family Atmosphere
            </h2>
            <p>
             Waiting for you is the turquoise sea and days full of "What you want to do" instead of "What you have to do"..
            </p>
            <p>
             That's what holidays are all about!! Isn't it?
            </p>
            <p>
             At dusk before the evening meal, in the shaded garden, Aris and Natasa serve cool drinks or homemade wine (Aris has a passion for his vineyard) and an assortment of traditional Greek culinary delights to be sampled. It is the time to wind down and watch the sun redden and sink slowly into the sea. Twinkling lights appear on the mountains as the surrounding villages prepare for nightime. In the distance you can hear the far off sounds of children not wanting to leave the beach. Overhead high in the sky, a plane passes by. Listen to the sound of the tinkling bells of the goats returning hame from the mountains behind, and birds twittering in their evening nests. Peace descends around you.
            </p>
            <p>
             At night nothing moves around (except for the sea and bouzuki, the dog, who does his duty guarding us from imaginary enemies!!) No passers by, no cars, no bars, NOTHING.
            </p>
            <p>
             Children go to sleep and parents sit quietly drinking a beer or some wine and talk amongst themselves. Some play cards or backgammon.
            </p>
            <p>
             The days pass by so quickly.
            </p>

            <h2>
             Natural Geography
            </h2>
            <p>
             The south cast of the county of Rethymno is named "Gialia". Along with the inland is the center of South Crete. Continuous beaches surrounded by high hills and in between, green valleys filled with flowers and aroma.
            </p>
            <p>
             Between the high mountains the gorges Kourtaliotis, Kotsifou and Mesa gorge allow the access between the north and the south compiling a special landscape.
            </p>
            <p>
             Getting down from the mountains you meet, olive farms, small hills, streams, rare flowers and herbs. All these changes in the landscape end in magnificent beaches with cristal clear waters.
            </p>
            <h2>
             Environment Climate
            </h2>
            <p>
             The area of Gialia has a special dry and warm climate (mediterannean). In the winter sot winds from Africa heat the atmosphere living the sun to reach the freely Plakias and the nearby villages. The sun in this area is 92%.
            </p>
            <p>
             In the summer the north winds from the mountains bring cool breezes. The cleanliness and brightness ofthe atmosphere is very tense and control the human feelings.
            </p>

           <h2>
            Cretan Diet
           </h2>
           <p>
            Olive oil, weet, milk, cheese, meat, vegetables, fruit: everything in Cretan cuisine is simple, clear, organic and natural.
           </p>
           <p>
            The gastronomy of the Cretans was based on local grown products. These provide a full diet which today is proven to provide good health and long living.
           </p>
           <p>
            The secret is not based on complicated recipes but in fresh and organic materials. This is truth in the whole of the island with small variations depending the area.
           </p>
           <p>
            Everywhere in Crete we consume raw vegetables in the form of a salad or boiled served with olive oil and fresh lemon juice.
           </p>
           <p>
            The healthy and low fat diet along with the amazing taste of the food of Cretan cuisine is internationally recognised.
           </p>
