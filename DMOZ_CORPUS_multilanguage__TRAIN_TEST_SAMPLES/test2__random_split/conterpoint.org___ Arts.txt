    <h2 align="center" style="text-align:center">
     <i>
      <span style="color:black">
       Registration
       <u1:p>
       </u1:p>
      </span>
     </i>
     <span style="color:black">
      <o:p>
      </o:p>
     </span>
    </h2>
    <p class="MsoNormal" style="mso-margin-top-alt:auto;mso-margin-bottom-alt:auto">
     <span style="color:black">
      Pre-registration is $50 until May 31,
           2016. Registration at the door will be $60 for the weekend.
           Small children (up to age 6) in the keeping of their parents
           are admitted free of charge, and do not get copies of
           convention publications. Junior memberships for children aged
           7-12 are $10 for the weekend. Single day rates will be
           available at the door. But of course, we want you to come for
           the whole weekend and experience all the fun and festivities
           we have planned!
      <br/>
      <br/>
      <span class="SpellE">
       Conterpoint
      </span>
      is also offering a
           Premier Membership. In addition to a listing in the program
           book, Premier Memberships include a convention T-shirt (please
           let us know your size) and a package of other goodies (to be
           determined, but may include a bottle of water, buttons,
           bookmarks and shaker eggs). The Premier Membership costs $100
           and will be offered up through May 31, 2016 (the standard pre-
      <span class="SpellE">
       reg
      </span>
      deadline). If you have already
           pre-registered for
      <span class="SpellE">
       Conterpoint
      </span>
      ,
           you can upgrade for an additional $50 to the Premier
           Membership.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Supporting Memberships are available for $20. Supporting
           Members will get a copy of the convention songbook and program
           book.
     </span>
    </p>
    <p class="MsoNormal" style="mso-margin-top-alt:auto;mso-margin-bottom-alt:auto">
     <span style="color:black">
      <br/>
     </span>
     In addition to the convention program book/songbook, all
         full adult members (including at-the-door), premiere members,
         and supporting members will get a copy of a CD produced by
         Harold Stein featuring tracks from
     <span class="SpellE">
      Conterpoint's
     </span>
     guests and next year's Contata guests.

    <h2 align="center" style="text-align:center">
     <i>
      <span style="color:black">
       Songbook
       <o:p>
       </o:p>
      </span>
     </i>
    </h2>
    <p class="MsoNormal" style="mso-margin-top-alt:auto;mso-margin-bottom-alt:auto">
     <span class="SpellE">
      <span style="color:black">
       Conterpoint will
             again feature a combined program book and songbook. All
             attending and supporting members (with the exception of
             children under 6) will receive a copy, and contributors will
             receive a complimentary copy.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       The songbook portion is being produced by Gary McGath. Songs
             can be originals or parodies. Parodies should credit the
             source tune and songwriter. Chords are preferred if
             available, whether as a lead sheet, a text file in Chordpro
             format, or similar presentations. Sheet music for original
             tunes is welcome if available, sound files should be
             provided if you would like a tune transcribed.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Submissions can be sent to conterpointbook atsymbol mcgath
             DOT com. The deadline for songbook submissions is May 16th.
      </span>
     </span>

    <h2 align="center" style="text-align:center">
     <i>
      <span style="color:black">
       Song Contest
       <u1:p>
       </u1:p>
      </span>
     </i>
     <span style="color:black">
      <o:p>
      </o:p>
     </span>
    </h2>
    <p class="MsoNormal" style="mso-margin-top-alt:auto;mso-margin-bottom-alt:auto">
     <span style="color:black">
      Our song contest topic will be "The Final
           Frontier", in honor of the upcoming 50th anniversary of the
           premiere of Star Trek. Entries must be new songs written since
           the end of last year’s Northeast Filk Conventions (i.e. after
           June 23rd, 2015). Songs set to original tunes and parodies of
           existing tunes are both acceptable.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      All entries must be performed at the convention. If you cannot
           attend (or prefer not to perform) someone else may present
           your entry.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      A copy of the lyrics should be provided for the use of our
           judges. Songs are judged on the quality of the lyrics and
           appropriateness to the topic, not on the quality of the
           performance.
