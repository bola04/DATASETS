            <header>
             <h1>
              Welcome to Lowton St. Mary’s CE Primary School
             </h1>
            </header>
            <section class="entry">
             <p>
             </p>
             <p style="text-align: center;">
              <img alt="" class="alignnone wp-image-1944" height="333" sizes="(max-width: 500px) 100vw, 500px" src="https://lowtonstmarysceprimary.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/2018-06-LSM-Head-1487-July-2018-300x200.jpg" srcset="https://lowtonstmarysceprimary.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/2018-06-LSM-Head-1487-July-2018-300x200.jpg 300w, https://lowtonstmarysceprimary.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/2018-06-LSM-Head-1487-July-2018.jpg 630w" width="500"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              Lowton St Mary’s has been educating children for over a hundred years, and has been constantly developing to ensure every pupil is provided with the best possible environment in which to learn. As a Church of England school our approach has a strong Christian ethos, and this is reflected in both the curriculum we teach and the moral values we impart to our children.
             </p>
             <p>
              Understanding the needs of our community is vital to maintaining a strong relationship between home and school, so we actively encourage parents and children alike to share their views. In this way we can ensure Lowton St Mary’s continues to prosper and remains a productive place for everyone.
             </p>
             <p>
              Our website is designed to offer a broad introduction to the school community, and we welcome the opportunity to talk about all aspects of being at Lowton St Mary’s. Please do not hesitate to contact us if you wish to do so.
             </p>
             <p>
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               Mr D. Sherriff
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               Headteacher
              </strong>
             </p>
