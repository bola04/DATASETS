        <br/>
        Just one kilometre outside the village is the lighthouse built on the southern point of the cliff.The Revera complex is situated between the village of Keri and the lighthouse and is ideal for couples or families that are looking for a traditional holiday in isolated relaxing surroundings with fantastic views.
        <br/>
        Just outside the village, (1 km) you will find the Lighthouse of the Ionian Islands which is built on the Southern point of the cliff with an unbelievable view and here you can witness the most famous sunset in Greece. In between the village and the lighthouse you will find our Traditional stone studios and apartments "Revera".
        <br/>
        The travellers who come here, are seeking more than a typical vacation.
        <br/>
        They come to experience a timeless culture, hike to ancient monasteries, walk on donkey paths thousands of years old with panoramic views, visit mountain chapels, try to capture the colours and scenery with their paints, photograph the wild flowers that are always miraculously blooming, walk the village's steps, ready to greet everyone that passes them by and follow the trail through the olive groves and singing cicadas to the light house...
        <br/>
        On the large terrace which is beautifully decorated with flowers you can relax with a coffee, a drink or a light snack while enjoying summer moments and excellent views.
        <br/>
        The impeccable, friendly service will make you love this place and recommend it to your friends as well. The beautiful studios and apartments are furnished with antiques that will take you back in time and give you an understanding of how the traditional way of life was in old Zakynthos and the Village of Keri.
