            <strong>
             With the wonders of the Web...
            </strong>
            We want to keep you informed every way possible. To let you know that "We are here to serve you." - just a few words, but we think it says quite a bit more than that. The TOHLE FUNERAL HOME staff is committed to your family's total care. Please stop by, call or e-mail our funeral home to have any questions answered regarding planning a funeral service in advance, funeral ceremonies in general, price information, estimated costs or just to see a friendly face. TOHLE FUNERAL HOME can be reached by phone 24 hours a day 7 days a week (without voice mail, answering service or menus to select from). A funeral director will answer your call at 773-685-4400.
           </p>
           <p style="line-height: 1.2em;">
            <strong>
             Dedicated service to your family...
            </strong>
            <br/>
            Relating to the needs of area families takes a special kind of understanding; one which is nurtured over years of knowing what is most important. At TOHLE FUNERAL HOME, our understanding of family care comes from over sixty years growing up with our community, and responding to each family's needs as if they were our own. The following are just some of the services we offer:
            <br/>
            1. Advanced funeral planning through funeral trusts and life insurance.
            <br/>
            2. Maintaining a compassionate staff available 24 hours a day 7 days a week.
            <br/>
            3. Constantly improving our facilities and services for increased family comfort.
            <br/>
            4. Offering burial, cremation and alternative funeral services
            <br/>
            5. Convenient adjacent parking, disability access, elevator and private family restrooms.
            <br/>
            6. Serving all faiths, ethnic and cultural lifestyles.
           </p>
           <p style="line-height: 1.2em;">
            Our dedication to customer service and protecting the feelings of our families has made us Chicago's premier funeral home. We pride ourselves on treating your family as a one of our own and carry out all of your wishes in remembering a loved one. With being a family business, we concentrate on helping people properly say goodbye to their loved ones. We provide a wide variety of services to meet all of your needs. We are here to take the burden off of you and your families so you can begin grieving. We believe in celebrating the life that our client lived and assisting their loved ones with the proper goodbye. Our funeral services in Chicago are simply the best.
            <br/>
            Personal service has made us a reliable Chicago funeral home. Our sensitivity and attention to detail has helped us stand the test of time and become the go to place for all of Chicago's funeral services. We have a wide variety of urns, caskets and cremation options available at our Chicago funeral home. We will customize the funeral services to meet the family's needs. We provide a wide variety of services that make last wishes a realistic wish. Please take a few moments and see the variety of services we provide to honor the lifetime of the people you love. We are a business that operates on family values and pride ourselves on helping our families with one of life's greatest challenges. We look forward to hearing from you.
