           <div class="et_pb_text et_pb_module et_pb_bg_layout_light et_pb_text_align_left et_pb_text_0">
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <img alt="liweddingofficiant" class="aligncenter wp-image-2458" height="377" src="http://www.churchofancientways.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/LIweddingofficiant.jpg" width="537"/>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <center>
             <sub>
              Patty and Larry Wonderfully Wed at
              <em>
               <strong>
                <a href="https://www.eastwindlongisland.com/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">
                 East Winds Long Island
                </a>
               </strong>
              </em>
              !
             </sub>
            </center>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <strong>
              The wedding ceremony shouldn’t be the thing that you and your guests have to suffer through to get to the good part. I promise you a meaningful, enjoyable, ceremony that everyone will remember for years to come.
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             If you already expect that the ceremony will be absolutely amazing… You are in the right place! For many the wedding day is all about emotionally participating in the experience. If you are starting out thinking that the ceremony is the part that you’re not going to absolutely love… it is our goal to have the moment you become married live in your memory, as the best part… of the best day of your lives.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             Looking for Long Island Wedding Officiants &amp; Ministers, JOP or information on marriages in Nassau and Suffolk? My name is April, and I lead a team of fabulous officiants who perform ceremonies all over Long Island and in the Tri-State area! My associates and I offer 21st Century Wordsmithing, original writing, for all of life’s most important occasions. You say you like it short and sweet, yet meaningful? We can do that! The modern couple often see their day as more about being married than getting married. The same way they are attracted to clean lines and open spaces with an uncluttered beauty, they want a ceremony that is simple, short, yet elegant. The desire is to make promises that will still matter in years to come and move forward! The question is would you like that with or without original writing?
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             We can include all the aspects of a traditional ceremony that are important to you and leave out the things that are not. Experience lets us blend cultures and faiths seamlessly, while bringing life to Civil Ceremonies. By listening and caring about your input we keep everyone emotionally and intellectually present with a “real” and “natural” feeling to our ceremonies. This site has many sample ceremonies that include original writing, videos, reading selections, along with all the practical things that all couple’s need. Please feel free to explore and enjoy!
            </p>
            <h3 style="text-align: center;">
             Choosing us as your wedding officiant, takes all the worries out of planning the perfect wedding ceremony.
            </h3>
            <p>
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2700" height="78" src="http://www.churchofancientways.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/divider.png" width="500"/>
             <br/>
             ~ Civil Marriage Ceremonies, Justice of the Peace Style, Fusion Weddings
             <br/>
             ~ Traditional, Alternative, Spiritual, Religious, Interfaith, Bilingual, Spanish
             <br/>
             ~ Minister and Rabbi Team Available
             <br/>
             ~ Quality Service, Reasonable Rates
             <br/>
             ~ Recommended and Trusted by Top Locations
             <br/>
             ~ High Quality PA System Included
             <br/>
             ~ Fully Legal in all parts of NY, No ULC or Online Ordinations
             <br/>
             ~ Serving the New York City, Long Island, Tristate area
             <br/>
             ~ Elopement Specials, Midweek Signature Service
             <br/>
             I am primarily a Long Island Wedding Officiant, but I do go anywhere and everywhere. Whether you are getting married near or far, please feel free to explore this website. It is intended to be a resource for couples and officiants alike.
            </p>
