      <p align="LEFT">
       <span style="font-size: medium; color: #888888;">
        <strong>
         <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">
          REMEMBERING CARPATHIA, TITANIC's HEROIC RESCUE SHIP
         </span>
        </strong>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p align="LEFT">
       <span style="font-size: small; color: #888888;">
        100th anniversary of the loss of RMS Carpathia, off the south Coast of Ireland, on 17th July 1918
       </span>
      </p>
      <p align="LEFT">
       <span style="color: #888888;">
        <strong>
         <span style="font-size: small; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">
          <br/>
         </span>
        </strong>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p align="LEFT">
       <span style="color: #888888;">
        <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: small;">
         <strong>
          We are delighted to be working with Titanic Belfast to commemorate the 100th anniversary of the loss of RMS Carpathia, Titanic's rescue ship
         </strong>
        </span>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p align="LEFT">
       <span style="color: #888888;">
        <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: small;">
         <strong>
          <br/>
         </strong>
        </span>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p align="LEFT">
       <span style="font-size: small; color: #888888;">
        To mark this important and special occasion we will be meeting onboard SS Nomadic, hamilton dry dock, in Titanic Quarter, Belfast on Tuesday 17th July 2018.  At 8.30am there will be a short welcome by the Society followed by special readings from newspaper reports of the 'day' telling the story of not only the dramatic rescue by Captain Rostron and his crew, of the surviviors from the Titanic disaster in 1912, taken from the perspective of those on board Carpathia, but also a short passage marking the torpedo attack, and subsequent loss of Carpathia with the sad loss of five of her crew...in the last days of the first world war.
       </span>
      </p>
      <p align="LEFT">
       <span style="font-size: small; color: #888888;">
        <br/>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p align="LEFT">
       <span style="font-size: small; color: #888888;">
        All Society members and their guests are welcome to attend - please be on board Nomadic for 8.15am and the event will start at 8.30 shapp.
       </span>
      </p>
      <p align="LEFT">
       <span style="font-size: small; color: #888888;">
        <br/>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p align="LEFT">
       <span style="font-size: small; color: #888888;">
        There will be refreshments provided afterwards on Board Nomadic (second class accomodation). There will be a small 'Carpathia' exhibition in the foyer of Titanic Belfast which you can visit afterwards.
       </span>
      </p>
      <p align="LEFT">
       <span style="color: #888888;">
        <strong>
         <span style="font-size: small;">
          <br/>
         </span>
        </strong>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p align="LEFT">
       <span style="color: #888888;">
        <strong>
         <span style="font-size: small;">
          Special Guest - Janet Rostron (Barnett),who is making her second visit to Belfast this year to help us with the Carpathia anniversary events (Janet is the
          <span>
           great-granddaughter of Carpathia's Captain)
          </span>
         </span>
        </strong>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p align="LEFT">
       <span style="color: #888888;">
        <strong>
