        <h2 class="post-title entry-title">
         About
        </h2>
       </div>
       <div class="post-content clear">
        <p>
         We are the Plymouth State University Gaming Club. With a membership of over 40, our members are students, staff, and community members from around the Plymouth area. Our mission is to bring gaming to the people. Be it role playing games, video games, board games, miniature wargames, or collectible card games. We now even include Anime and Japanese Culture. We cover them all. If it’s a game, we want to see it played.
        </p>
        <p>
         Our meetings are every Thursday and Saturday, from 7pm to midnight at Plymouth State University. Meetings are in HUB room 119, upstairs next to the print shop. You don’t have to be a member or a student to be part of a good time at gaming club, and we welcome anyone who is interested to join us in enjoying your favorite games. If you are curious about gaming club, please come check us out some night!
        </p>
        <p>
         Don’t forget, GAME ON!
        </p>
