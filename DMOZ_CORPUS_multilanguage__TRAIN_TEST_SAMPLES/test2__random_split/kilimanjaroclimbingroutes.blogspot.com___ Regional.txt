Rongai is highly recommended, especially for those with less backpacking experience. There are different options to complete the climb in 5, 6, or 7 days in Alpine trekking expeditions.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <a href="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-AEs97fp7PkU/WpQ-D1YA0tI/AAAAAAAACRw/IJGkB_HHReo0PsKbNAFy6RmGpwliGEZVQCLcBGAs/s1600/Rongai%2Broute%2BMap%2BKilimanjaro.jpg" imageanchor="1">

                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      Climbing Mount Kilimanjaro tours is the dream to thousands of people across the world because this is the current top Africa tourist attraction World Award winner in 2017. Summit Kilimanjaro to Uhuru peak is determination of every climber, so hiking to Kibo Volcano peak through Rongai route have 2 options:
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <a href="http://www.kili-tanzanitesafaris.com/rongairouteitinerar.htm">
                       Rongai route option 1:
                      </a>
                      <b>
                       Drive from Moshi through Marangu, Rombo Villages
                      </b>
                      , Tarakea town bordering Ilassit/Loitokok Kenya to Nalemoru gate Rongai...Begin trekking through wilderness rain forest to Simba Camp, Second day continue trekking through scenic beauty wilderness trails to Second Cave, climb to Kikelelwa Camp, continue climbing to Mawenzi Tarn hut, hike to Kibo hut and struggle to summit to Uhuru peak. Advantages of this kilimanjaro trail is that it is easier and have better way to acclimatize and you get best view of Mawenzi peak, Second highest Volcano rugged peak.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       Rongai route option 2
                      </b>
                      : Drive from Moshi after JRO arrival, through Marangu, Rombo Villages, Tarakea town bordering Ilassit/Loitokok Kenya to Nalemoru gate Rongai...Begin trekking through wilderness rain forest to Simba Camp, do simple leisure trekking to Second cave, continue trekking to third cave, climb to School hut and then hike to Kibo Summit Kilimanjaro Snow peak. This is somehow more challenging than first option above. All you experience wilderness experience, how ever School hut trail it is more natural with responsible travel ethics and you escape crowd of climbers, less tourists.

                      <br/>

                       How do you train to climb Mount Kilimanjaro?  Where to acclimatize....
                      </a>
                     </h3>
                     <div class="post-header">
                      <div class="post-header-line-1">
                      </div>

                     <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-6376021888288231332" itemprop="articleBody">
                      Actually Mount Kilimanjaro is one of 7 Summit World highest peaks of each continent, Kilimanjaro is highest Mountain in Africa. Kilimanjaro climbing is easier and Non Technical
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      How do you train to climb Mount Kilimanjaro?...The answer is simple, you do simple training which is merely exercises before days to begin trekking. Exercises recommended to take are jogging, running and gym exercises. Aim of exercise is to make your body muscles become used to stretching, to increase lungs capacity to store oxygen and breath. Further more, change mind set and prepare for mountaineering challenges in Tanzania.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      This are travel tips and advise prepared by

                      and

                       certificate of summit.

                      <br/>
                      <a href="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-b86fRWH8Hok/Wobv9-EYYQI/AAAAAAAACPk/CIU8QVOZcUQWVmj2VgU-Wh1hmicvypVFwCLcBGAs/s1600/Amboseli-Kenya%2BKilimanjaro%2B-%2BCopy.jpg" imageanchor="1">

                      </a>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      Guides explain ways to acclimatize...
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      First of all understand what is acclimatization. This is to make body become used to local weather/climate of the destination. You need to do some minor activities so that body adapt to new condition such as challenge Kilimanjaro trekking adventure. One day before or two days before a climber can hike small hills, runs or doing small exercises. Second, is to have enough time to ascend up Kilimanjaro, it is recommended to choose Machame route 7 days or Lemosho 7 days travel package etc. Choose Kilimanjaro route which has best acclimatization terrain like Machame or Lemosho. During Kilimanjaro trekking expeditions, drink enough water, usually 3 - 5 liters per day.

                     <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                      <a href="http://kilimanjaroclimbingroutes.blogspot.com/2018/01/things-to-do-in-short-time-to-see-all.html">
                       Things to do in Short time to see all Mount Kilimanjaro without climb include Shira Trek Lemosho 2 days
                      </a>
                     </h3>
                     <div class="post-header">
                      <div class="post-header-line-1">
                      </div>
                     </div>
                     <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-98369607290450992" itemprop="articleBody">
                      Mount Kilimanjaro attractions which can be done without climb or extreme adventure include waterfall tours, Lake Challa, coffee tours, Short trek Lemosho route Londorosi gate and more...
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <a href="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-ttak1_E2tho/WkziW7QZqUI/AAAAAAAACGk/dmXixdOSTIk7fxbUiD-lk08UhVpgmdHyACLcBGAs/s1600/mount-kilimanjaro.jpg" imageanchor="1">
                       <img border="0" data-original-height="567" data-original-width="850" height="213" src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-ttak1_E2tho/WkziW7QZqUI/AAAAAAAACGk/dmXixdOSTIk7fxbUiD-lk08UhVpgmdHyACLcBGAs/s320/mount-kilimanjaro.jpg" width="320"/>
                      </a>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       Mount Kilimanjaro attractions in low slopes of Mount Kilimanjaro and near Moshi are:
                       <br/>
                      </b>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      A -Travel to Moshi and you can swim in the soothing warm waters of the Kikuletwa Hot springs. It’s a beautiful location with crystal-clear waters – and the perfect way to recover from a lengthy, mountainous trek.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      B - On the south-eastern slopes of Mount Kilimanjaro lies Lake Chala, a crater lake set against a stunning volcanic backdrop. The dramatic creation of the area belies its tranquility and timeless beauty.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      C- Materuni Waterfalls and Coffee tours in and out of Hotels in Moshi
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <a href="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-MtvdWxnO4hU/WkziMCArbzI/AAAAAAAACGg/B22qu5CDze8RmdJTMefV8lKPMF7gTJdTwCLcBGAs/s1600/Waterfalls-Materuni%2BMoshi.jpg" imageanchor="1">
                       <img border="0" data-original-height="768" data-original-width="1024" height="240" src="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-MtvdWxnO4hU/WkziMCArbzI/AAAAAAAACGg/B22qu5CDze8RmdJTMefV8lKPMF7gTJdTwCLcBGAs/s320/Waterfalls-Materuni%2BMoshi.jpg" width="320"/>
                      </a>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       Active adventures are 2 days Shira trekking and Marangu day hike to Maundi Crater or up to Mandara and back.
                       <br/>
                      </b>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      Mount Kilimanjaro Day climbing trip is a rewarding sightseeing tour to see attractions in Mount Kilimanjaro through Marangu gate. Nature forest has wild animals, birds, scenery and waterfalls.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      Our Guide and Driver will drive with you to Mount Kilimanjaro National Park gate and on a clear day (No cloud cover) our driver will stop at different places where you can take good pictures of Mt. Kilimanjaro. The driver and the guide will be there to answer any question you might have regarding Mt. Kilimanjaro, Chagga tribe, German old buildings, Missionary, Culture and the area around the great Mountain.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      Register at Marangu gate and start the walk to Mandara hut. You will walk for about 3-4 hours to the first hut (Mandara hut) and you will arrive there in the afternoon.  You can possibly trek to Maundi Crater to see a lot more volcano and beyond.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      Then you begin descend down to Marangu gate, at Marangu gate you visit waterfalls and chagga German caves made to host chiefs and warriors.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      Day tour Kilimanjaro trek ecotourism and wildlife tour Shira Lemosho
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      4-5Hours Ascend and 3 descend
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      After breakfast, meet guide and transfer to West Kilimanjaro to Londorosi Gate with 2250m. Our 4 x 4 Car will drive through Nature Kilimanjaro forest –rain forest to Mountain Morrum barrier (3720m), then you begin hike to heath and moorland. You continue to rim of Shira Plateau. Shira plateau is most beautiful point to see a lot of Kilimanjaro attractions like Volcano rocks, glaciers and Snow view. Shira is a middle way of climbing Kilimanjaro through Machame route, Umbwe route, and Lemosho and Northern circuit.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      You will explore Shira Needle, Shira Cathedral and Volcano rock formations, take photos and then descend back to Morrum barrier where driver will take you back to Arusha.

                     <div class="post-header">

                      Kilimanjaro trekking information, Kilimanjaro routes and trip booking

                      Mount Kilimanjaro is Worldwide known Natural wonder as well as Africa highest peak Mountain. Mount Kilimanjaro is largest free standing Mountain in the World and has 3 volcano cones. Kilimanjaro Volcano peaks are Shira, Mawenzi and Kibo.Kibo peak is the highest and is covered by Snow throughout the year.

                      </a>
