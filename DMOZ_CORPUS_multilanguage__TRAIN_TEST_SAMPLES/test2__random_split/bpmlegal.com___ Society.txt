      <p>
       We hope this collection of web pages will help you answer your
       questions about your
       <a href="overview.html">
        intellectual property
       </a>
       rights
       - in other words, "who owns what's in your head?" The pages you
       can access from this home page will cover
       <a href="pat.html">
        patents,
       </a>
       <a href="tm.html">
        trademarks,
       </a>
       and
       <a href="copyrt.html">
        copyrights.
       </a>
       If you're not sure which you
       need, our
       <a href="overview.html">
        intellectual property overview page
       </a>
       will help.
      </p>
      <p>
       You can view
       <a href="xappat.html">
        some patents
       </a>
       our clients have
       obtained in a variety of fields, check out some
       <a href="tmrep.html">
        trademarks
       we've registered
       </a>
       , or look at a few
       <a href="weird.html">
        weird and
       wonderful patents
       </a>

       thoughts about your invention down on paper, there's a

        disclosure
       form
       </a>
       for you to download and use, and we have
       <a href="patfees.html">
        Patent
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="tmfees.html">
        Trademark
       </a>
       and
       <a href="copyfees.html">
        Copyright
       </a>
       budget estimators with the procedures and costs of filing patent,
       trademark and copyright applications.
      </p>
      <p>

      <h3 align="center">
       About Brown &amp; Michaels, PC
      </h3>
      <p>
       <a href="bio.html">
        BROWN &amp; MICHAELS'
       </a>
       office is located in
       Ithaca in the Finger Lakes region of central New York State. Since 1980,
       we have represented individual inventors, research institutions, high tech
       start-ups and established companies, both locally and world-wide.
      </p>
      <p>
       The inventors we represent
       <a href="xappat.html">
        have received patents
       </a>
       on cell lines, chemical processing methods, electronics, energy systems,
       medical diagnostics, furniture, software, tools, and vaccines (and this is
       just a sample). We are highly responsive to inventors and deliver high
       quality work at very reasonable rates.
      </p>
