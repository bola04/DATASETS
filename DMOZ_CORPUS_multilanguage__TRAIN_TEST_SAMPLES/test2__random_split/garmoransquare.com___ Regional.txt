            <p>
             ...it consists of four luxury self-catering apartments situated in Mingarry, Moidart, Lochaber on the picturesque west coast of the Scottish Highlands and gateway to Ardnmaurchan. Garmoran Square is just 1 hour from Fort William, Ben Nevis and Nevis Range. Also within easy reach of Mull, Skye and the small Isles of Eigg, Muck and Rhum.
            </p>
            <p>
             Opened in 2006, the apartments have been designed to Visit Scotland’s high standards. They combine a traditional Highland setting with modern day comfort and chic. The elegant apartments have stunning mountainous views across Loch Shiel to Ben Resipol. All accommodation is on the ground floor, bedrooms all are en-suite, each apartment has a log burner or open fire and pets are welocme by previous arrnagement. Sleep soundly, in super king beds with 4 inch toppers and awake to the peace and quiet of rural life, ready to explore all the glorious things our wonderful area has to offer.
