        <h2>
         Home
        </h2>
        <div class="entry">
         <p>
          <img alt="sign" class="size-full wp-image-87 alignright" data-lazy-src="https://eagle-house.com/wp-content/uploads//2009/07/sign.jpg" height="225" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="300"/>
         </p>
         <noscript>
          <img alt="sign" class="size-full wp-image-87 alignright" height="225" src="https://eagle-house.com/wp-content/uploads//2009/07/sign.jpg" width="300"/>
         </noscript>
         <strong>
          Welcome to the EAGLE CENTRE HOUSE
         </strong>
         <strong>
          Bed and Breakfast, located in Eagle, Wisconsin,
         </strong>
         <p>
          Eagle Centre House Bed and Breakfast is an authentic replica of an 1846 Greek Revival style stagecoach inn,
          <strong>
           situated in the scenic Southern Kettle Moraine.
          </strong>
          We offer comfortable, romantic B&amp;B lodging in Eagle near
          <strong>
           Old World Wisconsin Outdoor Museum
          </strong>
          <strong>
           and the Southern Kettle Moraine State Forest.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          From the front parlor to third floor bedrooms, you will enjoy the splendid collection of carefully assembled period antiques. For pure relaxing pleasure, two second floor chambers feature double whirlpool tubs and fireplaces.
          <strong>
           All four spacious guest chambers have a private adjoining bath.  Groups of 5 couples or up to 14 individuals may be comfortably accommodated by accessing another, fifth bedchamber also with private bath. (Not pictured, please inquire.)
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           A full, candlelight breakfast is included
          </strong>
          and served  in the dining room adding to the romantic  journey into the past as breakfast is served on “Tea-Leaf” Ironstone china (c. 1855 plus). Take your time, eat hearty. It will be a memorable experience.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="bird-bath" class="alignright size-full wp-image-85" data-lazy-src="https://eagle-house.com/wp-content/uploads//2009/07/bird-bath.jpg" height="225" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="300"/>
         </p>
