        About Court Hotels
       </h1>
       <img alt="" class="attachment-featured size-featured wp-post-image" height="454" sizes="(max-width: 705px) 100vw, 705px" src="http://www.courthotel.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/sites/617/2017/10/court-hotel.jpg" srcset="http://www.courthotel.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/sites/617/2017/10/court-hotel.jpg 1182w, http://www.courthotel.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/sites/617/2017/10/court-hotel-300x193.jpg 300w, http://www.courthotel.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/sites/617/2017/10/court-hotel-768x495.jpg 768w, http://www.courthotel.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/sites/617/2017/10/court-hotel-1024x660.jpg 1024w" width="705"/>
       <p>
        Court Hotels can be found all over the world. It is one of the most prestigious and popular chain of hotels. If you are traveling with family or friends and you want your stay to be pleasant, the hotel you will be staying in will help influence that.
       </p>
       <p>
        But what are the signs of a great hotel?
       </p>
       <h2>
        Signs of a great hotel
       </h2>
       <p>
        There are many signs that will tell you if a hotel is good or not. It is important for you to know these so you can look for them as you search for the best hotel to book. These signs should be evident in review sites. If you know what to look for, you can easily filter the good hotels from the bad.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9" height="665" sizes="(max-width: 999px) 100vw, 999px" src="http://www.courthotel.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/sites/617/2017/10/Bright-Living-room.jpg" srcset="http://www.courthotel.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/sites/617/2017/10/Bright-Living-room.jpg 999w, http://www.courthotel.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/sites/617/2017/10/Bright-Living-room-300x200.jpg 300w, http://www.courthotel.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/sites/617/2017/10/Bright-Living-room-768x511.jpg 768w" width="999"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        So what signs should you be looking for when searching for the best hotel?
       </p>
       <p>
        First of all, look for reviews about the staff. They should be warm, friendly, accommodating, and professional. In the end, it does not matter if you are in a luxurious hotel. If the staff are disrespectful and unaccommodating, you will not enjoy your stay. Fortunately, Court Hotels are serious about training all their hotel staff to be courteous and accommodating to all their guests. They are trained, not just to do their job, but to go the extra mile especially during times of emergencies.
       </p>
       <p>
        Another sign of a good hotel is when they are knowledgeable of the local community. This can be very helpful for the guests. The staff should be able to help them arrange for transportation or tickets – whatever is necessary. They should also know what to recommend based on what their guests need – like what are the best places to visit for a couple on their honeymoon or a family with small children.
       </p>
       <p>
        When it comes to the physical appearance, the hotel should look clean and organized. The sheets should be replaced, the washrooms and everything else should be clean. Nobody wants to stay in a hotel that looks dirty and cluttered. A well-decorated hotel makes it very appealing. The guests should feel like they are home.
       </p>
       <p>
        Location is also very important. This is why Court Hotels take extra care in choosing where every hotel is located. It was to be accessible and near the popular tourist hotspots. If it cannot be within walking distance of a famous location, it has to be near the road or terminal to make the commute easy.
       </p>
       <p>
        These are the qualities that you need to look for if you want to stay in the best hotels. To help you with your search, start with Court Hotels. There is bound to be one in the place you are visiting.
       </p>
