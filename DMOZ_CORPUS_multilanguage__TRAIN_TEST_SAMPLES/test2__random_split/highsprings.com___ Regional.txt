          <p class="margin-tb-20">
           The people of High Springs welcome you to experience the charm of our small town. Voted the "Friendliest Small Town" in Florida, High Springs beckons you to discover History, Adventure, Recreation and Family Fun!
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Located in beautiful north Florida halfway between Gainesville and Lake City, our area offers delights for the nature lover, sportsman, and historian. Canoe our pristine rivers, dive or splash into a crystal clear spring, shop till you find that item you never thought you would find, or just relax with an ice cream and admire one of our many historic buildings. Stroll our tree-lined streets and stop to enjoy our fine eateries, browse to your heart's content through our antique, specialty and gift shops. At the end of the day soothe yourself in our Spas then rest in one of our cozy Bed &amp; Breakfast or Country Inns.
