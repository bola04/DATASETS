     <h1>
      Milk Delivery Software, Dairy Software and Milk Software, UK  Milk Round Software
     </h1>
     Milk Round Software are a UK based developers of dairy software systems, having developed milk software since 1988. MRS has over 1500 users all over the UK from Cornwall in the south to Aberdeen in the north including many in Ireland, Guernsey and The Falklands!  Milk Round Software has developed the MRS for Windows to deliver the very best of systems and service to the dairy trade (including Van Sales and Organic Vegetable distribution). Use of the Windows system frees you completely for the choice of printers. Opinion is unanimous. All users are very pleased with all aspects of our milk delivery software. The system is available for rental at £100+vat or purchase £375+vat. Updates and Demonstration Systems are available free of charge from our web site on the Internet. If you need help in downloading or installing your system update or demonstration you can call us on Tel 0161 408 5022 or 01430 888055 (will be patched through to office). You are no longer reliant on your system supplier to send updates - you can get these when you decide. Whilst our milk software was written specifically as invoice printing and milk delivery software, it is also suitable for paper rounds and van sales. If you wish to contact Milk Round Software Users join the forum and ask them yourself. Our dairy software comes with unlimited free telephone support - at a time when you want it (weekends, evenings etc we take support of our milk software seriously). It does help to be connected to the internet. In the very rare event you should need a panic update we can supply it very quickly.  From the dairy to the doorstep, we offer a fully inclusive dairy software system, which allows for easy reporting as effectively as a customers hand written note. Our milk delivery software is effective not only for milk, but other rounds from newspapers to window cleaning.
