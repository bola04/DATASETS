     <h1 title="Welcome To IDF Ltd">
      Welcome To IDF Ltd
     </h1>
     Based in Chesham, Buckinghamshire, IDF Ltd offer sub-contract sheet metal fabrication, laser cutting and Mig/Tig welding services to industry across the South of England..
     <h2 style="color:red" title="New For 2016">
      New
     </h2>
     We have upgraded our facilities at IDF LTD &amp; are pleased to now offer in house machining, milling &amp; turning services with the acquisition of a Harrison M390 center lathe &amp; a XYZ Proto Trak CNC turret milling machine along with a highly skilled machinist.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     We are pleased to quote or start your work immediately.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     If you feel this would be of interest to you please do not hesitate to contact us.
     <h2 title="Large Capacity Laser Cutting">
      Large Capacity Laser Cutting
     </h2>
     New to our capacity list is the Prima Platino 1530 Laser cutting system which offers a large 3m x 1.5m cutting bed, allowing us to both carry out our own sheetmetal cutting requirements as well as offer laser cutting services directly to customers.
     <h2 title="Sheetmetal Fabrication">
      Sheetmetal Fabrication
     </h2>
     Our dedicated facilities are fully equipped for fabrication of both sheet and plate metal upto 16mm thick in mild steel (5mm aluminium &amp; 8mm stainless steel) based around state of the art our Amada HFT 100-3 CNC folding machine. Our experience ranges from heavy fabrication for the construction industry for use in concrete pilings to fine limit sheet metal enclosures, panels and brackets for the electronics, communication and lift industries.
     <h2 title="Experience">
      Experience
     </h2>
     IDF Ltd was established in 1990 and currently employs 6 highly 
                     skilled time served engineers. We have carried out work for 
                     a wide range of customers across the retail/supermarkets, marine, medical, 
                     electronics and general engineering &amp; 
                 manufacturing sectors.
     <h2 title="Finishing">
      Finishing
     </h2>
     We have close ties with a local finisher for powder coating 
                     and are happy to provide a one stop shop service for our customers 
                     finishing requirements including plating, anodising, cleaning 
                 and painting.
     <h2 title="Further Interest">
      Further Interest
     </h2>
     If you have an interest in our services, please feel free 
                 to call for an informal chat or send us details of our requirements.
