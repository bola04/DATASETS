      <p>
       Firwood Cottage is a delightful, self-catering pine chalet on the edge of the village of Nethybridge, adjoining the RSPB Abernethy Forest Nature Reserve.
      </p>
      <p>
       Just a short walk from the village, local shops and hotels, the chalet sleeps up to 4 people, is ecologically built from local Caledonian Pine, and is warm, comfortable and attractively furnished.
      </p>
      <p>
       The chalet is insulated and furnished to a very high standard, making it warm and comfortable. There are two bedrooms – one double and one bunkroom. All bedding and towels are provided. There is a shower room with shower, W.C and washbasin, a kitchen/dining room and lounge area with fully equipped kitchen area, cooker, fridge, microwave, etc. TV, radio and a small library of books and maps of the surrounding area are also provided. We also provide use of a washing machine and deep freeze. Very effective electric heaters heat the chalet and there is instant hot water.
      </p>
      <p>
       Outside there is a peaceful patio area and gardens adjoining the forest nature reserve, which is a magnet for wildlife –
       <a href="http://www.firwoodcottage.co.uk/index.php?page=wildlife">
        see list
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       Off-road parking is provided and there is a secure store for bicycles, skis, etc.
      </p>
      <p>
       Janet and Stewart Taylor have lived in Nethybridge since 1976. For over 30 years, Stewart was the manager of the RSPB Abernethy Reserve. He is now retired, undertaking a little contract work and lots of voluntary wildlife recording. He will be happy to advise on suitable areas for wildlife and birdwatching. Apart from birds, he has a good local knowledge of flowering plants, butterflies, dragonflies, mammals, etc.
      </p>
