         <div class="entry-content">
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           Both the Ironmen A and B sides opened up their 2017 seasons over the weekend. The A side traveled to Sackville to play against Mount Allison. Mount Allison put up a strong defensive effort but ultimately a second half offensive surge secured a 36-5 win for the Ironmen. The B side visited the Loyalists home field for a NBRU Mens’s B league match. The Ironmen looked strong but fell to the Loyalists with a final score of 34-7.

            </a>
           </h2>
           <div class="entry-meta">
            Posted in
            <a href="http://www.ironmenrugby.com/?cat=1" rel="category">

         <div class="entry-content">
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           The UNB Ironmen B side have wont the Toks Akpata trophy for a second year in a row with a big 35-15 victory over Mount Allison. Mount Allison put up a strong fight to the very last minute of the game but the Ironmen were able to hang on to secure their spot in the maritime championships that will take place in November 12th in Nova Scotia. The Ironmen A side will play STU on November 5th at 11am at college field for the A side provincial championship followed by the UNB women’s team playing at 1pm against STU as well for their championship, good luck to both teams!
          </p>

Playing a late game on the UNBSJ turf, the Ironmen fought hard to claw back late in the game to close the score. Both teams had possession in the last 10, however the Ironmen dropped the game 30-22 to the Seawolves.
