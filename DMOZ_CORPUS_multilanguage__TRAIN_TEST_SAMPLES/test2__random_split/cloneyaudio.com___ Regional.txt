          <h1 class="text-align-center">
           Welcome to Cloney Audio
          </h1>
          <p>
           Back when Sergent Pepper was a hit across the globe, Cloney Audio were already well established as Ireland’s Number One destination for Hi-Fi sound in the home!
          </p>
          <p>
           November 1966 Noel Cloney opened Irelands first real Hi-Fi Store – no portable audio, TV’s or cheap all in one systems – just the best sound around at a price music lovers  could afford.
           <br/>
           During the late Sixtie’s and beyond Noel became exclusive Irish distributors for many of the industry’s most famous names including Bowers &amp; Wilkins Loudspeakers (1968) and Arcam (1977).
