     <p>
      The Maidenhead Steam Navigation Company is the leading riverboat company, established for more than thirty years, at the upper end of the  market, covering the upper reaches of the Thames.
     </p>
     <p>
      Our aim is to offer first class facilities aboard
      <a href="../fleet">
       vessels
      </a>
      that combine  style with practicality, cruising through the most picturesque stretches  of the river.
     </p>
     <p>
      Throughout the year our airy but centrally-heated
      <a href="../fleet">
       boats
      </a>
      cater for hundreds  of private and corporate functions. Dinners, anniversaries, wedding  receptions, birthday parties, film shoots and conferences - the list  and variety is endless.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="../events">
       Events
      </a>
      afloat offer a unique atmosphere in which to entertain or impress.  We have a large number of embarkation points between Henley, Windsor and Walton-on-Thames.  Our
      <a href="../fleet">
       boats
      </a>
      are the preferred choice of all the top class hotels in the  area and, providing in some way clients are using their facilities,  pick ups from Oakley Court to Cliveden are easy to arrange.
     </p>
     <p>
      We have a  wide variety of
      <a href="../fleet/entertainment.php">
       entertainment
      </a>
      contacts that we can call upon to give your event that 'finishing touch'. You will  find a more varied selection of tried and tested artistes and musicians  under this section. All entertainers come highly recommended and the sales team will  be pleased to listen to your brief and come up with the right
      <a href="../fleet/entertainment.php">
       entertainer
      </a>
      for your occasion.
     </p>
     <p>
      The
      <a href="../fleet/georgian.php">
       'Georgian'
      </a>
      has a boardroom ambience and, as such, serves as the perfect venue for
      <a href="../events/conferences.php">
       meetings, conferences and presentations
      </a>
      . Flexible layouts, seating configurations and a full range of audio-visual equipment are available. All our
      <a href="../fleet">
       boats
      </a>
      are built to the highest specifications and operated to the most professional standards. From the moment you first make
      <a href="../contact.php">
       contact
      </a>
      you will find our office and operating staff friendly, courteous and efficient.
