         <p>
          Looking for a great motivational speaker? Whether you need help motivating your employees, or you want to educate your elementary school students with a fun and entertaining presentation, Randy Judkins can help! Randy is a seasoned, professional, new-vaudeville stage performer, a visually engaging motivational speaker, a highly interactive team-building facilitator, an educator who entertains, a coach for presenters of all kinds, and a tour guide.
         </p>
         <p>
          Randy’s unique blend of humor and storytelling is tailored to any event, anywhere. Whether it is a company retreat, team-building day, party, school assembly, picnic, festival, trade show, or workshop, Randy will give a presentation that is both educational and inspirational! Topics include self-esteem and anti-bullying for middle school and high school students, as well as team building, creativity, and change for corporate employees. Best of all, every topic is approached with Randy’s trademark warmth and gentle humor.
         </p>
         <p>
          Randy is currently working on his goal of performing in every town in the state of Maine, and he also regularly travels to locations outside the Maine area. So far he has presented in over 24 states, Canada and Europe. Call or email Randy today to schedule a presentation for your event.
