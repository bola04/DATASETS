        <p>
         Welcome. My name is Estelle and since the very start of my interest
             in Complementary Therapies in 1996 with learning all about Reflexology,
             I have seen this wonderful expansion of natural healing therapies.
             It is my privilege to be part of this lovely holistic movement towards
             better health, which incorporates true healing from within. Healing
             which starts with a desire from a place within ourselves for positive
             change and wholeness, which radiates out to all levels of our being
             and humanity itself.
        </p>
        <p>
         I have over 10 years experience as a Holistic Therapist. It is my
             objective to provide thoroughly effective and professional healing
             therapies, in a relaxed, unhurried and confidential environment. I
             work with sensitivity, bringing together my knowledge and caring approach
             to individual health and well-being.
