           Welcome
          </a>
         </h2>
        </div>

        <div class="postcontent">
         <p>
          Welcome to the web site for the Isle of Wight Symphony Orchestra Society.
         </p>
         <p>
          The IWSO is a well-established non-professional Symphony Orchestra on  the Isle of Wight. The Orchestra performs music from a varied  repertoire, usually at the Medina Theatre in Newport. This web site  provides programme information for past (and future) concerts.
         </p>

         <h2 class="posttitle">
          <a href="http://www.iwso.info/2018/02/spring-concert-news/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to Spring concert news">
           Spring concert news
          </a>
         </h2>
         <div class="postmetatop">
          Posted Feb.17, 2018 under
          <a href="http://www.iwso.info/category/news/" rel="category tag">
           News
          </a>
         </div>
         <!-- /postmetatop -->
        </div>
        <!-- /posttop -->
        <div class="postcontent">
         <p>
          On the 17th of March the Isle of Wight Symphony Orchestra will be putting on the third concert in their current season, at Medina Theatre in Newport on the Isle of Wight.
         </p>
         <p>
          The highlight of the evening will be Walton’s Cello Concerto, performed by Jamal Aliyev – who in 2017 gave his solo debut performance for the BBC Proms at the Royal Albert Hall with the BBC Concert Orchestra.
         </p>
         <p>
          The concert will be opened with Greig’s Norwegian Dances, with Dvorák’s Fourth Symphony providing the second half of the evening’s music.
         </p>
         <p>
          Tickets are selling fast, but some are still available from the Medina Theatre Box Office on 01983 823884, or online via
          <a href="https://www.iwight.com/theatrebookings/ticket/diary/mte336">
           the Isle of Wight Council’s website.
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          If you’d like to help spread the word, please download and distribute a poster.
         </p>

         <h2 class="posttitle">
          <a href="http://www.iwso.info/2017/12/happy-christmas-and-a-musical-new-year/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to Happy Christmas – and a musical New Year!">
           Happy Christmas – and a musical New Year!
          </a>
         </h2>
         <div class="postmetatop">
          Posted Dec.05, 2017 under
          <a href="http://www.iwso.info/category/news/" rel="category tag">
           News
          </a>
         </div>
         <!-- /postmetatop -->
        </div>
        <!-- /posttop -->
        <div class="postcontent">
         <p>
          If you’re looking for a gift idea for Christmas, why not tickets for the Isle of Wight Symphony Orchestra’s New Year concert? Available now from Medina Theatre box office on 01983 823884 or
          <a href="https://www.iwight.com/theatrebookings/ticket/diary/mte335">
           online via the Isle of Wight Council booking system
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          On the 20th of January, warm up a cold winter’s night with music from Shostakovitch, Ireland and Elgar.
         </p>
         <p>
          The concert will begin with Ireland’s Epic March, before the first half concludes with Shostakovitch’s first Symphony.
         </p>
         <p>
          After the interval we welcome back to the Island violinist Melina Mandozzi, playing Elgar’s Violin Concerto in B minor.
         </p>
         <p>
          If you’d like to help spread the word, please download the poster below.
         </p>

         <h2 class="posttitle">
          <a href="http://www.iwso.info/2017/10/dont-miss-out-first-concert-of-new-season-fast-approaching/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to Don’t miss out – first concert of new season fast approaching">
           Don’t miss out – first concert of new season fast approaching
          </a>
         </h2>
         <div class="postmetatop">
          Posted Oct.26, 2017 under
          <a href="http://www.iwso.info/category/news/" rel="category tag">
           News
          </a>
         </div>
         <!-- /postmetatop -->
        </div>
        <!-- /posttop -->
        <div class="postcontent">
         <p>
          Tickets for the first concert in the Isle of Wight Symphony Orchestra’s new season are still available from Medina Theatre, either in person (weekdays 7.00am – 9.00pm, weekends 9.00am – 5.00pm) or online at any time via
          <a href="https://www.iwight.com/theatrebookings/ticket/diary/mte308">
           the Isle of Wight Council’s booking system
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          The concert features Gliere’s Harp Concerto, performed by soloist Oliver Wass, along with the Masquerade Suite by Khachaturian, Glinka’s Orchestra Fantasia – Kamarinskaya, and Tchaikovsky’s 6th Symphony – “Pathetique”.
         </p>
         <p>
          If you’d like to help spread the word, please download and distribute a poster.
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_528" style="width: 330px">
          <a href="http://www.iwso.info/wp-content/uploads/2017-11-Poster.pdf" rel="attachment wp-att-528">

          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Click the image to download a copy of the November 2017 IWSO poster
          </p>
         </div>

       <div class="post post-512 type-post status-publish format-standard hentry category-news" id="post-512">
        <div class="posttop">
         <h2 class="posttitle">
          <a href="http://www.iwso.info/2017/07/new-season-fast-approaching/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to New Season Fast Approaching">
           New Season Fast Approaching
          </a>
         </h2>
         <div class="postmetatop">
          Posted Jul.06, 2017 under
          <a href="http://www.iwso.info/category/news/" rel="category tag">
           News
          </a>
         </div>
         <!-- /postmetatop -->
        </div>
        <!-- /posttop -->
        <div class="postcontent">
         <p>
          Once again we find ourselves at the end of another concert season; I hope you have enjoyed the concerts.
         </p>
         <p>
          A big thank you to those of you who have been season ticket holders and I hope you will consider being a season ticket holder again for next season. Full details on the dates and music for the 2017/2018 season can be
          <a href="http://www.iwso.info/programmes/">
           found on our Programmes page
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          For those of you who are not season ticket holders, but think you might be interested in becoming one, then we do have some spaces.
         </p>
         <p>
          I am pleased to announce that ticket prices are being kept the same as for the 2016/17 season, so that the box office price will be £14.50 and the season ticket price £13 per concert. Season ticket holders also get a saving on their programme (normal cost £1.50, season ticket holders £1).
         </p>
         <p>
          This means that season ticket holders will benefit by £2 per concert as well as knowing they have their chosen seat reserved for the whole season. Please note that if you book online for individual concerts, the Medina Theatre will charge you a booking fee of £1
          <strong>
           per ticket
          </strong>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          Audience numbers were very good for the last season with all concerts sold out, so a season ticket is the best way of ensuring your seat.
         </p>
         <p>
          If you would like to renew your season ticket then I would ask you to complete the
          <a href="http://www.iwso.info/wp-content/uploads/season-reply-slip-1.pdf">
           attached form
          </a>
          and return it to me by post (
          <strong>
           with a S.A.E
          </strong>
          .). For new season ticket holders it is probably best if you contact me either by phone or email so that I can tell you which seats are available.
         </p>
         <p>
          If there are any questions, please do not hesitate to contact me.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Tim Isard (Chairman)
          </strong>
          –
          <a href="mailto:chairman@iwso.info">
           chairman@iwso.info
          </a>
          – 01983 400074
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.iwso.info/2017/07/end-of-one-season-but-time-to-get-ready-for-the-next/season-reply-slip-2/" rel="attachment wp-att-513">
           Download the Season Ticket reply slip.
          </a>
         </p>
