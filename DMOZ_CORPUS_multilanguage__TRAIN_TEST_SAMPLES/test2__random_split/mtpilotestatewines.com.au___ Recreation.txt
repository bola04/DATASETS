     <p>
      <img align="right" alt="Welcome to Mt Pilot Estate Wines" class="margin_left" height="375" src="images/image_welcome.jpg" width="273"/>
      Mt Pilot Estate is a small,  family owned boutique vineyard and winery committed to the production of  premium quality wines of rare distinction.
     </p>
     <p>
      The vineyard, situated at Mt Pilot in North East  Victoria Australia, is managed to produce low yields of wine grapes, through a minimal  water regime, to encourage intense flavour and colour development in the fruit  prior to harvest and wine making.
     </p>
     <p>
      To protect the integrity and consistency of our  wine, all of the grapes are Mt Pilot Estate grown. Our well regarded wine  maker, Marc Scalzo monitors the wine making process very carefully. The fruit is  given the benefit of time with each vintage aged in premium quality French and  American Oak barrels for two years, thus allowing time for tannins to mature  and bring out the full flavour, fruit concentration and complexity of a truly  handcrafted, premium wine. Our wine varities include viognier Chardonnay, Shiraz, Cabernet Sauvignon and Durif.
     </p>
     <p>
      Mt Pilot Estate wines are now available at many top  restaurants throughout North East Victoria, Sydney and Melbourne and in a  select number of quality boutique food and wine shops and specialist bottle  shops.
