        <h1>

        </h1>
        <p>
         <strong>
          The Baltimore Mandolin Orchestra, under the direction of Kristin Turner, is a Baltimore, Maryland-based ensemble  of mandolins, mandolas, mandocellos, mandobasses, and classical guitars.  We play an eclectic mix of classical music, marches, rags, show tunes,and a  variety of other traditional compositions for mandolin orchestra.  Please explore our site and find out more about us!
         </strong>
