        <p>
         The Aikido UMN is focused on introducing the art of Aikido to the University of Minnesota community and offers year-round practice. We look forward to seeing you on the mat!
        </p>

         <strong>
          New members
         </strong>
         , please visit the
         <a href="https://umnaikido.wordpress.com/new-members/">
          new members page
         </a>
         for information on getting started and FAQs.
        </p>
        <p>
         Our practice schedule is listed above and on the membership page.
        </p>
        <p>
         If you have any questions or comments,  our email is
         <a href="mailto:umnaikido@gmail.com">
          umnaikido@gmail.com
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         You can also get in touch with or follow us via
         <a href="http://www.facebook.com/pages/University-of-Minnesota-Aikido-Club/151783601662?sk=wall" target="_blank">
          Facebook
         </a>
         and
         <a href="https://twitter.com/umnaikido" target="_blank">
          Twitter
         </a>
         . We look forward to hearing from you!
