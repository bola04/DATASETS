         <h1>
          First Unitarian Universalist Church of Winnipeg
         </h1>
         <p>
          Welcome to our site - we are glad you are here! Ours is a vibrant, intentionally-inclusive community that welcomes you regardless of your gender identity, economic circumstance, sexual orientation, age, race, ethnicity, or national origin.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="/worship-and-music/upcoming-services">
           Sunday services
          </a>
          are held year-round at 10:30 a.m., followed by refreshments and conversation. We have a dynamic program of
          <a href="/learning/children-and-youth">
           Religious Exploration
          </a>
          for children and youth, and child care is available. Information about getting here:
          <a href="/home/map-and-parking">
           Get here! Map, parking, transit, and bike information
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          We welcome you, and look forward to sharing the adventure that is life and the spiritual journey. Feel free to
          <a href="/contact/contact-the-church-office?view=responsive">
           Contact Us
          </a>
          with questions or for further information.
