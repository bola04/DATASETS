           </div>
           <div class="clear">

           <p class="img">
            <img src="./images/DSC00014.jpg"/>
           </p>
           <p class="first_para">
            Yee Keung Industrial Company Limited (YKI) was founded in 1972 as a mold maker and contract manufacturer in Hong Kong. The factory was relocated to China in 1989 for continuous expansion. Today, YKI specializes in plastic injection mold making, precision plastic injection molding, and contract manufacturing businesses with production plant (Spectrum Precision Molding &amp; Mfg. Ltd.) located in Zhongshan, China. Accredited with ISO9001:2008, TS16949:2009, &amp; ISO14001:2004, YKI now serves customers in Automotive, Electronics, and Consumer product industries with excellent reputation.
           </p>
