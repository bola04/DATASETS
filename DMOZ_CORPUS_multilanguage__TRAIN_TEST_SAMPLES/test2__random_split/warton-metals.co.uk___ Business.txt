           Over 3 Decades of British Manufacturing Excellence
          </h3>
          <p>
           Founded in 1979, Warton Metals has gained an enviable reputation for its range of innovative Solder Pastes, High Purity Bar Solder and Solid Solder Wire products, Cored Solder Wires, Liquid Fluxes, Rework Gels and Cleaning products - designed to meet the changing demands of both industry and the environment.
          </p>
          <p>
           Product technologies include: no clean, VOC Free, halogen free, rosin /colophony free, lead free along with a full range of traditional leaded products to suit all applications.
          </p>
          <p>
           Warton supply products and services for: electronics assembly, telecoms, automotive, industrial soldering, micro component, plumbing, hardware, retail, wholesale and hobbyist markets.
          </p>
          <p>
           Warton offers full product traceability and manufacturing to BS EN ISO 9001:2015. RoHS, REACH and Conflict compliance documentation are available.
          </p>
          <p>
           Warton Metals supply direct, through our UK based technical sales department, or through our global network of distributors. We currently have distributors in France, Spain, Sweden, Czech Republic, Bulgaria, Poland, Netherlands and Russia and would welcome more Technical Distributors.

           Warton respond to many enquiries and appreciate that every user of solder has an individual requirement to suit their company. Warton provides innovative, quality solder products, at competitive prices.
          </p>
