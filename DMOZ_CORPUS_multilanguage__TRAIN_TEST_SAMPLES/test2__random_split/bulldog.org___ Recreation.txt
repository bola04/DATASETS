 handler is to provide comfort and companionship by sharing the dog with
 the patients in hospitals, nursing homes, and other institutions and wherever
 else the Therapy Dog is needed. This is done in a way that increases emotional
 well being, promotes healing, and improves the quality of life for the
 people being visited and the staff that cares for these people. TDI dogs
