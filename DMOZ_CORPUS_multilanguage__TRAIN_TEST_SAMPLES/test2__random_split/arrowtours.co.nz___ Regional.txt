            <h1>
             Fantastic Cruise Ship Excursions from Port Of Tauranga
            </h1>
            <p>
             Are you looking for a cruise ship shore excursion that will show you the best of Tauranga, Rotorua or Hobbiton  on your New Zealand tour? If yes, then book our Tauranga Cruise Ship Tour now and let Roger and his expert local guides share the fabulous Bay of Plenty region with you. Highly rated by TripAdvisor just have a look at what travellers say when they’ve been on our Tauranga shore excursion. Let us guide you through the best attractions, gushing Geysers, hot springs and bubbling mud pools, Maori culture only available in Rotorua.
            </p>
            <h2>
             Enjoy Small Group Tours In Tauranga &amp; Rotorua:
            </h2>
            <p>
             We specialise in arranging small group tours in Tauranga &amp; Rotorua that are tailor-made for a day you will long remember. We offer small group customized cruise ship day tours from Port of Tauranga allowing visitors more freedom &amp; independence. Our top rated shore excursions from Tauranga help you explore the major attractions in Rotorua, Tauranga and Mt Maunganaui encompassing the picturesque geothermal sites showcasing maori culture at either Te Puia or Whakarewarewa or the amazind lanscape and bubbling mud pools walk at Waimangu Valley
            </p>
           </div>
           <div class="small-12 medium-6 large-6 columns">
            <h2>
             Customised Day Tours For Tauranga Cruise Ship Passengers:
            </h2>
            <p>
             Our day tours are the best for Tauranga cruise ship passengers. On our day tours visitors can witness some of the most wonderful highlights of Tauranga &amp; Maunganui and also enjoy a trip to the geothermal wonderland of Rotorua. Our
             <strong>
              Cruise Ship Excursions from Port of Tauranga
             </strong>
             to Rotorua encompasses experiencing the phenomenon of Wai-o-Tapu , Te Puia and Whakarewarewa thermal valley .
            </p>
            <h2>
             Hobbiton Tours from Tauranga:
            </h2>
            <p>
             Are you a Lord of the Rings fan? If yes, then you will definitely find our
             <strong>
              Hobbiton tour from Tauranga
             </strong>
             interesting. The film site is just outside a town known as Matamata which is less than one hour drive from Tauranga nestled amongst the lush farmland of the Waikato district. Enjoy an informative tour around the movie set hearing about everything about the making of such a blockbuster movie, enjoy coffee and lunch and browse the amazing gift store on site.
            </p>
            <p>
             We will be there with you every step of the way to make sure you maximize your time in our backyard &amp; make this a major highlight of your New Zealand vacation experience. For any help, we are always available at +64 274493 293.
            </p>
