             <h3 class="widgettitle">
              WELCOME!
             </h3>
             <p>
              <strong>
               Hi welcome to Melissa King Photography!!!
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              I have been fascinated since my early childhood with camera’s, computers and for that matter technology in general. I believe that I have my dad to thank for that, he has always given me the support that I needed and the use of his camera and computers. I am now a professional photographer/designer, a collage graduate (multimedia internet development), a devoted wife and mother of two.  I love all different types of photography from family portraits, newborn session's, scenery portraits, to stock photography. The key to my success is my stock photography, I sell many of my photos on the internet through companies like
              <a href="http://www.shutterstock.com/gallery.mhtml?id=85">
               Shutterstock
              </a>
              ,
              <a href="http://www.fotolia.com/?&amp;utm_source=affiliation&amp;utm_medium=affiliation&amp;utm_content=4364">
               Fotolia
              </a>
              , and
              <a href="http://www.dreamstime.com/Melking_portfolio_pg1">
               Dreamstime
              </a>
              but my love for children has always taken me back to my newborn sessions.  You know spending just that little bit of extra time capturing the first moments of a child's life on film to ensure those times of joy will always be remembered for years to come gives me such a feeling of fulfillment and really makes it all worth while.
             </p>
             <p>
              In the future I would really love to build my stock photography portfolio by capturing more natural lifestyle photography with professional, and amateur models alike. I am also pursuing family and child portraits with my main focus on newborn photography. I really love the camera and I feel in order to capture that winning photo you also have to make the model love the camera as well.
             </p>
             <p>
              My Specialties include: Photography, graphic design, webdesign, and digital retouching using Adobe Photoshop
             </p>
             <p>
              Originally I am from Eastport, NL but I now live and work from home in Wabush, NL
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               Life is not the amount of breaths you take, Its the photos that take your breath away.
              </strong>
             </p>
            </li>
           </ul>
