           <div class="entry-content">
            <h2 class="blog-title">
             <a href="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/3-tips-to-help-your-family-better-enjoy-the-summer/" title="Permalink to 3 Tips to Help your Family Better Enjoy the Summer">
              3 Tips to Help your Family Better Enjoy the Summer
             </a>
            </h2>
            <div class="entry-meta">
             <span class="date">
              <a href="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/3-tips-to-help-your-family-better-enjoy-the-summer/" title="3 Tips to Help your Family Better Enjoy the Summer">
               <time datetime="2018-07-10T15:24:52+00:00">
                <span class="date-month">
                 July 10, 2018
                </span>
               </time>
              </a>
             </span>
             <span class="author">
              <a href="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/author/admin/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Sandra Robinette">
               Sandra Robinette
              </a>
             </span>
             <span class="category">
              <a href="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/category/blog/" rel="category tag">
               Blog
              </a>
             </span>
            </div>
            <p>
             Summer is a time to slow down, relax, and spend some quality time with your loved ones. But the scorching heat, pesky mosquitoes, and long days without school can make it hard to find new ways to enjoy the summer with your family.
            </p>
            <p>
             These three tips will help you and your family have the best summer you’ve ever had. You’ll wish it could last all year!
            </p>
            <h3>
             <strong>
              Beat the Heat
             </strong>
            </h3>
            <h3>
             <strong>
              <img alt="Grass on a hot day" class="size-medium wp-image-84 alignright" height="225" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/iStock_000001319145Medium-300x225.jpg" srcset="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/iStock_000001319145Medium-300x225.jpg 300w, http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/iStock_000001319145Medium-768x576.jpg 768w, http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/iStock_000001319145Medium-1024x768.jpg 1024w, http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/iStock_000001319145Medium-380x285.jpg 380w, http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/iStock_000001319145Medium-285x214.jpg 285w, http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/iStock_000001319145Medium.jpg 1600w" width="300"/>
             </strong>
            </h3>
            <p>
             As summer goes on, the weather just keeps getting hotter and hotter. Depending on where you live, you might even be dealing with temperatures of 100 degrees or above. Once August rolls around, you’ll be desperate to find ways to beat the heat while still have summer fun with your family.
            </p>
            <p>
             Think about joining your neighborhood pool or buying a book of guest passes for those extra hot days where you just have to splash around in some water. Or, if you have friends with pools at their homes, offer to bring over lunch or dinner in exchange for a fun day at the pool. And of course, always wear and regularly reapply sunscreen and make sure everyone is
             <a href="https://www.health.harvard.edu/staying-healthy/the-importance-of-staying-hydrated">
              staying hydrated
             </a>
             with plenty of water.
            </p>
            <p>
             When it gets so hot that you just can’t imagine spending the day outside, see what family friendly movies are playing at your local theater. It’ll be a special treat for your kids, and you’ll get to enjoy the A.C. for a couple hours at the same time.
            </p>
            <h3>
             <strong>
              Control those Pesky Pests
             </strong>
            </h3>
            <p>
             Mosquitoes have their heyday in the summer, and they can completely infest your property and ruin an otherwise perfect summer day. You might be grilling out in your back yard or enjoying a summer evening on your deck when suddenly, you realize that you and your family are being eaten up by mosquitoes.
            </p>
            <p>
             The first thing you should do is walk around your property and get rid of any stagnant water. Sitting water is the perfect breeding ground for mosquitoes, and you’ll find them in pet water dishes, outdoor toys, inflatable pools, and more. Dump it all, and make sure to do so any time it rains.
            </p>
            <p>
             If you don’t want to cover yourself and your kids with chemical mosquito repellent, you can try dabbing a few drops of essential oil – lavender and peppermint oil have been shown to repel mosquitoes, and they smell great – around your ankles, wrists, and any other exposed skin. Stay away from using them on your face and be careful not to rub any in your eyes.
            </p>
            <p>
             Citronella candles and torches will keep some of the mosquitoes away, but fans are even more effective. Keep an electric fan or two outside and turn them on any time you’re enjoying the outdoors.
            </p>
            <p>
             When it comes to completely getting rid of mosquitoes, hiring a
             <a href="https://collierville.mosquitosquad.com/">
              professional mosquito control service
             </a>
             is the only way to make sure they’re gone for good. Mosquito Squad will treat your property to make sure that you and your family can enjoy the outdoors all summer long, without worrying about getting covered in itchy mosquito bites.             </h2>
            <div class="entry-meta">
             <span class="date">
              <a href="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/choosing-the-best-rv-insurance/" title="Choosing The Best RV Insurance">
               <time datetime="2017-08-23T18:54:44+00:00">

                </span>
               </time>
              </a>
             </span>
             <span class="author">
              <a href="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/author/admin/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Sandra Robinette">

             </span>
             <span class="category">

            <p>
             When you decide to invest in a recreational vehicle, it can be a significant cost expense. Regardless of whether you decide on a simple trailer, or go with a stand-alone motorized RV unit, they can cost tens of thousands of dollars. Deciding on the right insurance plan for your RV can be the difference between a simple accident and a major pain that could result in tons of damages. In some cases, it may be necessary to have insurance coverage on your RV, regardless of the type.
            </p>
            <p>
             <img alt="InsuranceDoctorRV" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-63" height="683" sizes="(max-width: 1024px) 100vw, 1024px" src="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/InsuranceDoctorRV-1024x683.jpg" srcset="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/InsuranceDoctorRV-1024x683.jpg 1024w, http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/InsuranceDoctorRV-300x200.jpg 300w, http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/InsuranceDoctorRV-768x512.jpg 768w, http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/InsuranceDoctorRV-570x380.jpg 570w, http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/InsuranceDoctorRV-380x254.jpg 380w, http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/InsuranceDoctorRV-285x190.jpg 285w" width="1024"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             There are some simple car insurance terms that are thrown around, such as :
            </p>
            <ol>
             <li>
              <h4>
               Collision/Comprehensive/Liability
              </h4>
             </li>
             <li>
              <h4>
               Deductible
              </h4>
             </li>
             <li>
              <h4>
               Premium
              </h4>
             </li>
             <li>
              <h4>
               Seasonality Coverage
              </h4>
             </li>
            </ol>
            <p>
            </p>
            <ul>
             <li>
              The first set of terms describes the various types of damages you may encounter. A collision is fairly straightforward, and covers repairs that are caused by hitting another vehicle, individual, or an object. Comprehensive insurance is also known as “Act of God” insurance – because it covers accidents from fires, thefts, nature, animals, and a variety of other non-fault accidents. The last term, liability, is used to describe damages that are covered to people, vehicles, and other property that you are responsible for.
             </li>
            </ul>
            <p>
            </p>
            <ul>
             <li>
              Your deductible, based on your budget and needs, varies, but it is the amount that you pay to the auto shop for your claim, before your insurance company will start to cover the rest. If you have a $500 deductible, for example, and your damage claim came to a total of $12,000, then you would be responsible for the first $500, and your insurance company would cover the remaining $11,500. The higher your deductible, the less you pay for monthly premiums, however, if you were to be in an accident and file a claim, you would obviously be paying a higher limit before your insurance would kick in, and vice versa.
             </li>
            </ul>
            <p>
            </p>
            <ul>
             <li>
              Premium is simply slang for the monthly amount you pay to your insurance company for coverage. This varies based on the different coverages you have (collision, comprehensive, liability), and a few other factors such as your deductible amount.
             </li>
            </ul>
            <p>
            </p>
            <ul>
             <li>
              Seasonality Coverage is for those who only use their RV’s during specific months of the year. For example, if you tend to take your vacations during the Summer time, you may want to ask about seasonal coverage for just the Summer months. During the other months of the year, you would be without insurance.
             </li>
            </ul>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             Many times, your typical auto insurance company will have a separate department for recreational vehicle insurance, and a whole team dedicated to handling these types of claims. It is extremely important that you take the time to thoroughly review and research the many different insurance options before you decide on pursuing one. Every insurance company has different policies and regulations for how they can handle certain situations, and you may choose differently based on other’s experiences.
            </p>
            <p>
             It is also important to note that if your recreational vehicle has many attachments such as a satellite dish, TV antenna, a balcony umbrella, or anything else, they are more than likely not covered by the insurance package. This is something, depending on the item, that you may want to bring up with your company to see if there are other options for covering these attachments.
            </p>
            <p>
             Depending on how often you use your RV, your insurance needs may vary, so it is always vital that you do your research! If you are a part-time RV user who goes on family vacations once a year, that will be a significant factor when choosing insurance. On the other hand, if you are a full-time RV user and are constantly in the vehicle, then you more than likely will want to pursue some type of full-time coverage. There are many options for choosing the right insurance, whether you use a major corporation, or a smaller, more-locally based company. In my case, with a full-time motorized RV, I actually ended up going with a local company, Insurance Doctor:
             <a href="http://www.myinsurancedoctor.com/chesapeake/">
              http://www.myinsurancedoctor.com/chesapeake/
             </a>
             , who was able to answer all of the questions I had and gave me an excellent, competitive quote. I’ve had to use insurance a few times, and so far, there has been nothing but pleasant experiences dealing with  Insurance Doctor. They are one of the top auto insurance companies in Chesapeake VA, which is ironically the home of major insurance company GEICO!
            </p>
            <p>
             Another important thing to keep in mind when choosing insurance for your RV is the various emergencies that you may encounter during your trips. It may be worthwhile to invest in a form of health insurance that can transport you to and from hospitals if you were to have a health emergency. There are many medical insurance plans available in this very tailored service industry. A few of the big names in this industry are companies such as Skymed and MASA.
            </p>

              7 things you should have in your RV
             </a>
            </h2>
            <div class="entry-meta">
             <span class="date">
              <a href="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/7-things-you-should-have-in-your-rv/" title="7 things you should have in your RV">
               <time datetime="2017-05-11T17:01:16+00:00">
                <span class="date-month">
                 May 11, 2017
                </span>
               </time>
              </a>
             </span>
             <span class="author">
              <a href="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/author/admin/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Sandra Robinette">
               Sandra Robinette
              </a>
             </span>
             <span class="category">
              <a href="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/category/blog/" rel="category tag">
               Blog
              </a>
              ,
              <a href="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/category/tips/" rel="category tag">
               Tips
              </a>
             </span>
            </div>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-40" height="320" sizes="(max-width: 762px) 100vw, 762px" src="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/Blog4.jpg" srcset="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/Blog4.jpg 762w, http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/Blog4-300x126.jpg 300w" width="762"/>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             In an RV, space is a major concern. You get into a dilemma in deciding what to take and what not to take. Here are some things you should always carry in your RV.
            </p>
            <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
             1. Roadside assistance card
            </h3>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             It is a very important thing to carry in an RV. If your RV breaks down in the middle of the road, then you can call the roadside assistance people, like AAA, to fix your RV. If you don’t have such card, you should get one.
            </p>
            <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
             2. Portable folding propane grill
            </h3>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             As you will be going out camping in an RV, you must take a portable grill. You will be able to have BBQ parties in the midst of nature. This grill can be used for many years.
            </p>
            <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
             3. Folding table
            </h3>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             You won’t find a table at your campsite. So, you should take one with you. There are many folding tables in the market. You should choose something lightweight so that it’s easy to carry. You can use the table for meals, games, etc. You should look for something that is weather resistant. So, you won’t need to run to get the table when it rains.
            </p>
            <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
             4. Brass Y valve
            </h3>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             This will allow you to get an extra connection for water. Most campsites have only one connection. So this valve will give you an extra line to wash your hands and cars. You can also use it to wet your partner and have some fun.
            </p>
            <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
             5. Internet connection
            </h3>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             We stay online every day. Whether it’s for work or using the social network, internet connectivity is a must. RVs cannot give you unlimited internet. So, you should sign up with a good carrier before starting your journey.
            </p>
            <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
             6. A vacuum
            </h3>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             As you will be staying in campsites most of the time, there will be dirt, mud, and bugs. You need a vacuum to clean your RV to get rid of all these. You will find rechargeable and cordless vacuum in the market.
            </p>
            <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
             7. Folding Step
            </h3>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             A folding step can be very useful. It can be used to get inside your RV in case the ground is not level. You can use it to reach things or for cleaning and repair works as well.
            </p>
            <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
             8. Portable dehumidifier
            </h3>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             It is very useful for controlling the humidity of your RV. Make sure it is small and portable so that you can easily carry it around.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             When you are traveling in an RV, make sure you carry all these things with you. It will make your journey very comfortable.
            </p>

            </h2>

             <span class="date">
              <a href="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/5-reasons-you-should-live-in-an-rv-when-you-are-young/" title="5 reasons you should live in an RV when you are young">

               </time>

             <span class="author">
              <a href="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/author/admin/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Sandra Robinette">

              </a>
             </span>
             <span class="category">
              <a href="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/category/blog/" rel="category tag">

              ,
              <a href="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/category/information/" rel="category tag">

            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-37" height="320" sizes="(max-width: 762px) 100vw, 762px" src="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/bLOG3.jpg" srcset="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/bLOG3.jpg 762w, http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/bLOG3-300x126.jpg 300w" width="762"/>

            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             When you are young, you should explore new things in life. Living in an RV is such an experience. Though it’s small it can give you lots of benefits. Some of the benefits are mentioned below.
            </p>
            <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
             They are affordable
            </h3>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             Mortgage rates are high. So, when you are in your early 20s, you are still in college or have just entered the job field. You might not be able to afford such high mortgage payments every month with your part time jobs. So, RV is a cost effective option. You can buy an RV, live there till you finish your college, and then sell it off. By that time you would save money for your other major expenses.

            </h3>

            </p>
            <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
             A good tool to pay off your student loan
            </h3>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             The money you save from living in an RV can be used to pay off your student debt. RVs are a lot cheaper than living in a house. If you own an RV, you won’t need to pay house rent or mortgage. You only need to pay for the parking.
            </p>

             <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-35" height="400" sizes="(max-width: 762px) 100vw, 762px" src="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/IMG5.jpg" srcset="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/IMG5.jpg 762w, http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/IMG5-300x157.jpg 300w" width="762"/>

            <h2 class="blog-title">
             <a href="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/3-ways-you-can-keep-your-rv-safe/" title="Permalink to 3 ways you can keep your RV safe">
              3 ways you can keep your RV safe
             </a>
            </h2>
            <div class="entry-meta">
             <span class="date">
              <a href="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/3-ways-you-can-keep-your-rv-safe/" title="3 ways you can keep your RV safe">
               <time datetime="2017-05-11T16:50:10+00:00">
                <span class="date-month">
                 May 11, 2017
                </span>
               </time>
              </a>
             </span>
             <span class="author">
              <a href="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/author/admin/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Sandra Robinette">
               Sandra Robinette
              </a>
             </span>
             <span class="category">
              <a href="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/category/blog/" rel="category tag">
               Blog
              </a>
              ,
              <a href="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/category/safety/" rel="category tag">
               Safety
              </a>
             </span>
            </div>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-32" height="320" sizes="(max-width: 762px) 100vw, 762px" src="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/BLog2.jpg" srcset="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/BLog2.jpg 762w, http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/BLog2-300x126.jpg 300w" width="762"/>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             RVs can be an easy target for thieves. They can simply drive away with your valuable things inside. The crime rate is increasing and so you should ensure that your RV is safe. Here are some ways you can keep your RV safe.
            </p>
            <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-31" height="400" sizes="(max-width: 762px) 100vw, 762px" src="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/img4.jpg" srcset="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/img4.jpg 762w, http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/img4-300x157.jpg 300w" width="762"/>
            </h3>
            <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
             Park it in a safe place
            </h3>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             When you get to the campsite, find a place where you can park your RV safely. Don’t position your RV in a way that it is easy to drive off. Park it in such a way that you have to turn your RV in order to get out of the place. This way, thieves will need more time to escape and they will probably skip the plan.
            </p>
            <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
             Secure your trailer properly
            </h3>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             You should lock the doors and windows of your trailer. There are different types of locks available. Padlock and a cylinder lock are the most popular ones. Some cylinder locks have special keys that you cannot duplicate. You can get a boot to cover your wheel.
            </p>
            <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-30" height="400" sizes="(max-width: 762px) 100vw, 762px" src="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/img3.jpg" srcset="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/img3.jpg 762w, http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/img3-300x157.jpg 300w" width="762"/>
            </h3>
            <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
             Find the proper security system
            </h3>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             You should have the right security system installed in your RV. There are many options, like alarms, motion detecting lights, a deadbolt, etc. Motion detector lights can detect people, animals or cars. When something is detected, the light turns on and stays on for some time. Then when the motion is not detected anymore, the light shuts off. The modern RVs have motion detectors installed already.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             You should implement these security features in your RV if you want to keep it safe from the thieves. RVs are expensive, so spend some money on its security systems.
            </p>

            <div class="entry-meta">

               <time datetime="2017-05-11T16:24:39+00:00">
                <span class="date-month">
                 May 11, 2017
                </span>
               </time>
              </a>
             </span>
             <span class="author">

             <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-27" height="320" sizes="(max-width: 762px) 100vw, 762px" src="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/Blog1.jpg" srcset="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/Blog1.jpg 762w, http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/Blog1-300x126.jpg 300w" width="762"/>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             RV travel is very popular. It is a wonderful way to spend time with family. It leads you to a world of freedom, fun, and flexibility. You can travel on your schedule and create your own travel plan. You can sleep at the end of the day.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <strong>
              Here are five reasons to buy an RV.
             </strong>
            </p>
            <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
             It’s another home
            </h3>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             In an RV, you can pack all your stuff and get off for the weekend somewhere nice. You can explore the countrysides. You will have all the amenities you have at home. You won’t need to spend time in booking flights and hotel rooms. You can travel and sleep at the same place if you have an RV.
            </p>
            <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-21" height="400" sizes="(max-width: 762px) 100vw, 762px" src="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/IMG1.jpg" srcset="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/IMG1.jpg 762w, http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/IMG1-300x157.jpg 300w" width="762"/>
            </h3>
            <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
             Change your routine
            </h3>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             You can escape your normal routine by traveling in RV. Sometimes it’s important to come out of your normal routine to enjoy and mix with people. It gives you a break from your routine life. You can take your pets along with you as well.
            </p>
            <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
             Make new friends
            </h3>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             When you take your RV to a camping site, you will meet new people and make friends. You will meet people on the way as well. This is not possible when you stay in a hotel.
            </p>
            <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
             See the world
            </h3>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             If you travel in an RV, you can sleep in the middle of a national park if you want to. You can sit under the open sky and see the beautiful stars. It’s an experience you won’t get without RV.
            </p>
            <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-22" height="400" sizes="(max-width: 762px) 100vw, 762px" src="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/IMG2.jpg" srcset="http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/IMG2.jpg 762w, http://eagleshotlakerv.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/IMG2-300x157.jpg 300w" width="762"/>
            </h3>
            <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
             Save money
            </h3>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             You won’t need to pay for the air ticket or hotel room when you travel. So, you will save a lot of money. You can also prepare healthy meals instead of eating in restaurants.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             So, if you are looking for a different experience, travel in an RV. You will see the world in a different way and get lots of new memorable experiences.
            </p>
