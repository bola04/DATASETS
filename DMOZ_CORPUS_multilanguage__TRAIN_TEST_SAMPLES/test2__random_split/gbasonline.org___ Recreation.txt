         <h1 class="title">
          Home
         </h1>
        </section>
        <p>
         The Green Bay Aquarium Society (GBAS) is a non-profit educational organization dedicated to the aquarium hobby.  Our meetings are open to the general public.  Members need not live in Green Bay, or even in Wisconsin!
        </p>
        <h2>
         Aquatic Pet Rehoming:
        </h2>
        <p>
         GBAS has partnered with the University of Wisconsin-Extension and the Wisconsin Sea Grant to help keep exotic species out of Wisconsin’s waters.  If you have an aquatic pet (freshwater or saltwater) that you can no longer keep,
         <strong>
          never
         </strong>
         release that pet into the environment.  Instead, fill out and submit our
         <a href="http://www.gbasonline.org/home/aquatic-pet-surrender-form/">
          aquatic pet surrender form
         </a>
         and we will make arrangements with you to take that pet and find it a new home.  We also accept aquatic plants and corals.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Next Meeting:
        </h2>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           7 pm Wednesday September 12, 2018
          </strong>
         </span>
         <br/>
         1000 Islands Environmental Center
         <br/>
         1000 Beaulieu Ct., Kaukauna, Wisconsin 54130
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          You need not be a member to attend.  All are welcome.
         </strong>
        </p>
