     <section class="body-entry blue light regular anchor-mission" id="anchor-60">
      <div class="container rel">
       <div class="content-wrapper center-text full">
        <h2 class="headline">
        </h2>
        <p>
         Generational poverty can be broken. We've been doing it for more than 25 years.
        </p>
        <h6>
         Children born into poverty are at risk for dropping out of school, substance abuse, incarceration and teen parenting. We provide a salaried, professional mentor, called a Friend, for their entire childhood, from kindergarten through graduation. 12.5 years. No matter what.
        </h6>
       </div>
      </div>
     </section>
     <section class="body-entry grey light double " style="background-image:url('https://friendsofthechildren.org/uploads/page-content/_browserWidth/home-method-bg.jpg');">
      <div class="gradient">
      </div>
      <div class="container rel">
       <div class="content-wrapper center-text">
        <h5 class="rubrik">
         Our Method
        </h5>
       </div>
       <div class="content-wrapper center-text full">
        <h2 class="headline">
        </h2>
        <p>
         We select children facing the highest risks. We help them change their stories.
        </p>
       </div>
      </div>
     </section>
     <section class="body-entry white dark regular ">
      <div class="container rel">
       <div class="col-3">
        <h4 style="text-align: center;">
         <img class=" img-responsive responsive--sq150" sizes="150px" src="/imager/uploads/page-content/logos/221/Home-method-icon-left_92f2fb4ef51d5a174031abf75351998a.png" srcset="

        </h4>
        <h3 style="text-align: center;">
         83% of our youth graduate high school.
        </h3>
        <hr/>
        <p style="text-align: center;">

        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="col-3">
        <h4 style="text-align: center;">
         <img class=" img-responsive responsive--sq150" sizes="150px" src="/imager/uploads/page-content/logos/222/Home-method-icon-middle_22eb602d9085bea6e5b9c11dc22adc0c.png" srcset="

         93% avoid the juvenile justice system.
        </h3>
        <hr/>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Although 50% have parents who were incarcerated.
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="col-3 last">
        <h4 style="text-align: center;">
         <img class=" img-responsive responsive--sq150" sizes="150px" src="/imager/uploads/page-content/logos/223/home-method-icon-right_92f2fb4ef51d5a174031abf75351998a.png" srcset="

        </h4>
        <h3 style="text-align: center;">
         98% avoid early parenting.
        </h3>
        <hr/>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Although 85% were born to a teen parent.
        </p>
