                     <h3>
                      <b>
                       <font color="#FFFF00" size="5">
                        McVille is the community for you!!
                       </font>
                      </b>
                     </h3>
                     <p>
                      <font color="#FFFFFF" size="4">
                       McVille has a number of recreational facilities.  We offer an excellent municipal 9-hole grass-green golf course. Our paved bike path leads to the McVille Dam Facility located on the east edge of the City. Here you can fish, swim, camp and small-hp Boat. We have beautifully manicured baseball and football fields. A running track is located at the football field.
                      </font>
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      <font color="#FFFFFF" size="4">
                       Our community has well-lit and paved streets. Residents enjoy activities performed at the Dakota Prairie School located on the north end of our community. The business district boasts a new restaurant facility and several businesses to meet your needs. Our health facilities take care of all your personal needs.
                      </font>
                     </p>
