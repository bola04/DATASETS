       New website promotion opportunity for Accommodations industry
      </h3>
      <p>
       <i>
        January 2018
       </i>
       <br/>
       <a href="https://accommodationdirect.nz/add-new-listing/">
        <img src="http://webdesignpros.co.nz/wp-content/uploads/logo7.png" style="max-width:344px;height:auto"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Promoting accommodation provider’s websites and businesses can be difficult since it is a very competitive market. Sure you can get ‘free’ listings on the many of the big international OTA websites such as Booking.com, Expedia, Orbitz, AirBnB, Travelocity, etc. but they typically don’t allow their users to promote their own websites and they handle the bookings with hefty commissions so it’s far from free. So the people who brought you Web Design Pros now bring you
       <strong>
        AccommodationDirect.nz
       </strong>
       , a
       <a href="https://accommodationdirect.nz/">
        New Zealand accommodations listings website
       </a>
       . And to get the ball rolling we’re giving away listings for FREE! Once we achieve our target number of listings the free offer will be over but we plan to keep annual listing fees very low and have zero commissions. We allow and encourage operators to promote their own websites and booking systems of their choice. At a later date we may also offer our own booking engine at a low cost. But right now the listings are free so get in quick!
