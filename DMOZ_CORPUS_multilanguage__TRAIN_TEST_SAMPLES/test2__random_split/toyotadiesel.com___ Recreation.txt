This is the result of a monumental amount of research and development implementing electrical engineers and CAD systems designers to achieve an OEM Toyota quality product. To the end user, this means there is no “splicing” or “re-wiring” of the vehicle’s electrical system. The results are substantially increased reliability and provide the added benefit of being a visually cleaner installation.
