      <h2 class="wsite-content-title">
       <font color="#8c48b7">
        <strong>
         building visitors
        </strong>
       </font>
       <br/>
      </h2>
      <div class="paragraph">
       <font size="4">
        The Caroline County Public School system has begun deploying a new visitor management system to all of our county school buildings. The Raptor system will better allow us to track visitors in our schools and provide us with a safer, more monitored environment for our students and staff.
       </font>
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <font size="4">
        Upon entering a Caroline County School, visitors will be asked to present a valid state-issued ID (driver’s license, state id card), which will be scanned into the system. Upon reading the information, Raptor will check national and local databases to identify those individuals that should not be allowed in our schools. Once entry is approved, Raptor will issue a badge that identifies the visitor, the date, and the purpose of his/her visit. The visitor badges must be returned to the office at the conclusion of the visit so that they can be logged out of the system.
       </font>
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <font size="4">
        The safety of our students is our highest priority and the Raptor visitor system provides a consistent way to aid in keeping away people who may present a danger to our students. Therefore, all visitors to the county schools must be scanned into the system and obtain a visitor badge.
       </font>
       <br/>
       <font size="4">
        If you are interested in learning more about the visitor management system, please visit the Raptor Technologies Website (
        <a href="http://www.raptortech.com">
         www.raptortech.com
        </a>
        ).
       </font>

       <font color="#8c48b7">
        <strong>
         dismissal time
        </strong>
       </font>
       <br/>
      </h2>
      <div class="paragraph">
       <font size="4">
        Federalsburg Elementary School offers 3 convenient options for your child's dismissal daily.  There are 3 exits:
        <br/>
        (1) bus riders at the main entrance of the building;
        <br/>
        (2) car riders via the teachers' parking lot by the baseball fields; and
        <br/>
        (3) walkers via the Academy Avenue door exit.
        <br/>
        All exits are strictly monitored by school staff for your children's safety.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Please notify the school,
        <strong>
         <em>
          in writing
         </em>
        </strong>
        , if you will need your child dismissed at 3:40 p.m. via the walker exit or the car rider exit.  Thank you!
        <br/>
        <br/>
        We ask that you
        <strong>
         DO NOT
        </strong>
        call the school office to make end of the day transportation changes after 3:15 p.m.
        <em>
         <strong>
          and
         </strong>
        </em>
        if at all possible, teachers and staff ask for your cooperation in not picking your child up from school between 3:15 and 3:40 p.m. unless you have an appointment to attend or, of course, a family emergency.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Due to safety concerns we are not going to allow parents to wait outside by the buses to pick up their child at dismissal time. Also, we will not be able to have parents wait for their students in the lobby or just outside the front doors as it creates congestion and confusion during a busy time when your child's safe departure is our only concern.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Thank you in partnering with us in creating a calm, smoothly run daily departure for all of our students.
