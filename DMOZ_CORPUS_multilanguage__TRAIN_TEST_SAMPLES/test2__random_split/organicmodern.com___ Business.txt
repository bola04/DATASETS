             united states. Simple sketches and open dialog with clients and builders 
             provide the basis for design. Extent of services include permitted 
             drawings to installed furnishings. We are a California Licensed architect
 			with experience in residential, commercial, religious, institutional, and
 			industrial design.

         <p>
          <font face="Tw Cen MT, Arial" size="3">
           Our eventual goal is the bring 
             together an open studio, with graphic designers, architects, landscapers, 
             artists, contractors, and business people. Imagine a two story building 
             along a coastal highway with a view of the ocean. On the first floor 
             is a gallery/bistro. There is a glass wall separating gallery from 
             a foundry and wood shop, where craftsman make all things, from furniture 
             to jewelry. Beautiful, wonderful things that need people.
          </font>
         </p>
