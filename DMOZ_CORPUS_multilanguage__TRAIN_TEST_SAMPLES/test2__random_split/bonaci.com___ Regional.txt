            <h2>
             Welcome to Bonaci Jewelers
            </h2>
           </span>
          </center>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Bonaci Fine Jewelers is Kent’s longest operating jeweler serving the City of Kent.  Our goal is to provide the finest quality jewelry that will last a life time, even future generations! We are proudly part of the Kent Community since the early 1960’s and value the trust of our clients.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          We are proud to feature:
          <br/>
          • Hand-selected Ideal Cut Diamonds
          <br/>
          • Custom designed jewelry
          <br/>
          • A wide selection of diamond jewelry, engagement sets, anniversary bands, and diamond earrings and pendants
          <br/>
          • An exclusive array of fine colored gemstone jewelry
          <br/>
          • A unique collection of estate jewelry
          <br/>
          • On-site appraisals
          <br/>
          • Jewelry Reapir
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Bonaci Fine Jewelers is a member of the Independent Jewelers Organization (IJO), which offers us extensive buying opportunities which we then pass on to you, our customers. The organization promotes consumer protection through continuing education, offering seminars to its jewelers on both Gemology and Industry Trends. In addition to IJO, we are also proudly involved with Rotary International.
