               </h2>
               <p>

The course is renowned for its lush, authentic Florida landscape where the fairways are lined with mature oaks, pine trees and historic palms trees. You will find the incredible conditioning on our golf course makes it as undeniably beautiful as it is challenging.
               </p>

Rockledge Country Club’s traditional feel and routing make for an enjoyable walking course if you choose to walk.  Five sets of tees provide ample options for playability and length in this par 71 layout.
