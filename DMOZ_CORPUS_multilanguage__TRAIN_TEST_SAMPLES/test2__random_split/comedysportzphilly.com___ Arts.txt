               <h2>
                <strong>
                 Comedy club.Improv school. Business solution.
                </strong>
               </h2>
               <p>
                At CSz Philly, we are experts when it comes to comedy and improvisation. We should be since we've been doing it for a quarter century! In the classroom we
                <a href="https://d-m-0fsm.squarespace.com/improv-classes-1b">
                 teach
                </a>
                our students how to use improv to entertain and excite audiences. In the boardroom, we teach professionals how to communicate, listen, and take risks using
                <a href="https://d-m-0fsm.squarespace.com/corporate-teambuilding">
                 Applied Improvisation
                </a>
                . On stage we entertain
                <em>
                 everyone
                </em>
                with hysterical comedy shows including
                <strong>
                 <a href="https://d-m-0fsm.squarespace.com/comedysportz">
                  ComedySportz
                 </a>
                </strong>
                , Philadelphias longest-running show.
               </p>
               <p>
                CSz Philadelphia is located at The Adrienne Theater in the
                <a href="http://www.visitphilly.com/museums-attractions/philadelphia/rittenhouse-square-park/" target="_blank">
                 Rittenhouse
                </a>
                section of Center City Philadelphia, across from the famed
                <a href="http://filmadelphia.org/nowshowing/" target="_blank">
                 Roxy Movie Theater
                </a>
                and Helium Comedy Club.
               </p>
               <p>
                CSz Philadelphia is a part of
                <a href="http://www.cszworldwide.com/" target="_blank">
                 CSz Worldwide
                </a>
                , a global network of companies for organizations needing entertainment, training, and events marketing solutions.
               </p>
