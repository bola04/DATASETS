            </strong>

           <p>

            <a href="http://kknag.com/business-verticals/expanded-polystyrene-india/">
             EPS
            </a>
            in India, i.e., Expanded Polystyrene. Our facilities include the most modern, vacuum-assisted, German moulding machines, and we use the latest technologies. EPS India – We have one of the largest EPS processing capacities in India.  EPS is a light weight, completely recyclable material that has various applications in packaging and insulation.

           <strong>
            Expanded Polypropylene (EPP)
           </strong>
           <p>
            We were the first company to process Expanded Polypropylene in India and have now established facilities for this process in Pune.
            <a href="http://kknag.com/business-verticals/expanded-polypropylene-epp/">
             EPP
            </a>
            is a comparatively newer plastic foam suited for high-end applications in the automobile industry and for packaging of very sensitive and expensive products. It is more durable than EPS (expanded polystyrene) and has good recovery after dynamic and static loading.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a class="button medium color" href="http://kknag.com/business-verticals/expanded-polypropylene-epp">

             Rotational Moulding
            </a>

       <p>
        K. K. Nag Pvt. Ltd is a plastic processing company, headquartered in Pune, India. We are known for having pioneered various plastic processes in India. From the concept to final product, everything is done by us in-house, including designing, prototyping and tool making.  EPS India – K. K. Nag Pvt. Ltd was established in 1965 and it pioneered the application and use of fiberglass in India.
       </p>
       <p>
        Today, K. K. Nag Pvt. Ltd is a multi-location company with manufacturing facilities located in Western and Southern India. We are involved with several plastic processes including Expanded Polystyrene i.e. EPS or Thermocol, Expanded Polypropylene i.e. EPP, Other Foamed Plastics, RotoMoulding or
        <a href="http://kknag.com/business-verticals/rotational-moulding/rotationally-moulded-industrial-tanks-water-tanks/">
         Rotational Moulding
        </a>
        , Vacuum Forming, Injection Moulding and Blow Moulding.

        <strong>

          Thermocol
         </strong>
        </a>
        in India. EPS (expanded polystyrene) i.e. Thermocol Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) is one of the most economical and secure packaging materials available in India; and is used all over the world to protect goods from damage during transit. EPS (expanded polystyrene) i.e. Thermocol (EPS or Expanded Polystyrene) is also used to make internal components for the air-conditioning and refrigeration industries. NagMagic squatting slabs are a favourite among many overseas clients, especially for natural disasters and for emergency deployment. Over the period of time we have developed the expertise and are the best among the manufacturers of Thermocol, i.e. EPS in India.
