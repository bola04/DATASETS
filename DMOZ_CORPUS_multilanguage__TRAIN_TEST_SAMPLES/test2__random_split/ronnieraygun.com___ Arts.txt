     <div class="left with-sidebar post-530 page type-page status-publish" id="page-container">
      <h1 style="text-align: center;">
       <strong>
        <span style="color: #ff6600;">
         Denver’s Most “Unique” Eighties Tribute Band!
        </span>
       </strong>
      </h1>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <span style="color: #ff6600;">
        <strong>
         unique |yoōˈnēk|adjective – being the only one of its kind; unlike anything else
        </strong>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/nDUGQB5CBDU" width="560">
       </iframe>
      </p>
      <h4 style="text-align: left;">
       Even though Denver has its share of Eighties Tribute bands, Ronnie Raygun is the only one to sport the wildest costumes.
       <a href="http://ronnieraygun.com/ronnie-raygun-eighties-tribute-band-2/">
        <img alt="" class="alignleft wp-image-849" data-attachment-id="849" data-comments-opened="0" data-image-description="&lt;p&gt;Ronnie Raygun | Corporate Band | Eighties Band&lt;/p&gt;
 " data-image-meta='{"aperture":"11","credit":"Robbie Wright","camera":"NIKON D810","caption":"","created_timestamp":"1456062620","copyright":"Robbie Wright     Photography","focal_length":"16","iso":"125","shutter_speed":"0.005","title":"","orientation":"1"}' data-image-title="Ronnie Raygun | Eighties Tribute Band | Hi-Res" data-large-file="http://ronnieraygun.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/Ronnie-Raygun-Eighties-Tribute-Band-684x1024.jpg" data-medium-file="http://ronnieraygun.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/Ronnie-Raygun-Eighties-Tribute-Band-200x300.jpg" data-orig-file="http://ronnieraygun.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/Ronnie-Raygun-Eighties-Tribute-Band.jpg" data-orig-size="1538,2304" data-permalink="http://ronnieraygun.com/hi-res-photos-promo/ronnie-raygun-eighties-tribute-band-2/" height="599" sizes="(max-width: 400px) 100vw, 400px" src="http://ronnieraygun.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/Ronnie-Raygun-Eighties-Tribute-Band-684x1024.jpg" srcset="http://ronnieraygun.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/Ronnie-Raygun-Eighties-Tribute-Band-684x1024.jpg 684w, http://ronnieraygun.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/Ronnie-Raygun-Eighties-Tribute-Band-200x300.jpg 200w, http://ronnieraygun.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/Ronnie-Raygun-Eighties-Tribute-Band-768x1151.jpg 768w, http://ronnieraygun.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/Ronnie-Raygun-Eighties-Tribute-Band-50x75.jpg 50w, http://ronnieraygun.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/Ronnie-Raygun-Eighties-Tribute-Band.jpg 1538w" width="400"/>
       </a>
      </h4>
      <p style="text-align: left;">
       This is something they started in 1998 when they rolled into Denver as
       <a href="http://www.boogiemachine.com" target="_blank">
        Boogie Machine
       </a>
       , The Super 70’s Disco Dynamo. The giant afros, bell-bottoms and platform shoes were something to behold as people craned their necks to get another look.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: left;">
       In 2001, Ronnie Raygun carried on the tradition of big, out-of-this-world wigs with a full complement of spandex, fingerless gloves, Vans and Wayfarer sunglasses.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: left;">
       <a href="http://ronnieraygun.com/about-2/version-4/">
        <img class="alignleft wp-image-916 size-medium" data-attachment-id="916" data-comments-opened="0" data-image-description="" data-image-meta='{"aperture":"5","credit":"","camera":"NIKON D5300","caption":"","created_timestamp":"1434829879","copyright":"","focal_length":"23","iso":"400","shutter_speed":"0.01","title":"Version 4","orientation":"1"}' data-image-title="Boogie Machine" data-large-file="http://ronnieraygun.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Boogie-Machine-Disco-1024x819.jpg" data-medium-file="http://ronnieraygun.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Boogie-Machine-Disco-300x240.jpg" data-orig-file="http://ronnieraygun.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Boogie-Machine-Disco-e1469080773221.jpg" data-orig-size="1500,1200" data-permalink="http://ronnieraygun.com/about-2/version-4/" height="240" src="http://ronnieraygun.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Boogie-Machine-Disco-300x240.jpg" width="300"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: left;">
       Totally bitchin’ retro dude! This ain’t no disco. this ain’t no foolin’ around.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: left;">
       Giant sticky hair abounds and the New Wave beat throbs in your cranium, direct from the time when music and fashion were larger than life. Feel free to cruise on up to that wave and say, “Hey bud, let’s party!”
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h2>
      </h2>
      <h2>
      </h2>
      <h2>
      </h2>
      <h4 style="text-align: center;">
       <span style="color: #ff6600;">
        Want the party without the costumes?
       </span>
       <span style="color: #ff6600;">
        We can do that too.
       </span>
      </h4>
