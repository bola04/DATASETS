         <h1>
          Introduction.
         </h1>
         <div style="float: right; margin: 2px 2px 2px 2px;">
          <script type="text/javascript">

         <p>
          This guide is divided in 17 sections where we will explain step by step all 
 	the information you need to purchase real estate as an investment in Buenos Aires, 
 	Argentina.
         </p>
         <p>
          The sections are ordered in a 
 	sequential order. We explain each step in a comprehensive way, making you 
 	easier to understand the whole process of buying property in Buenos Aires.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="relocating_vs__investing.htm">
           Section 1: Relocating vs. 
 	Investing in Real Estate
          </a>
          , here we will describe 
 	the main issues you should take in mind when buying real estate property in 
 		Buenos Aires.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="searching_properties.htm">
           Section 2: Searching properties in 
 	Buenos Aires
          </a>
          , explains the distinctive 
 	features of a real estate search in Buenos Aires, and 
 	provides the best sources to find the property you are looking for.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="making_apointments.htm">
           Section 3: Making Appointments
          </a>
          and
          <a href="visiting.htm">
           iSection 4: Visiting the apartment
          </a>
          give tips how to make an 
 	appointment to see the apartment, and things you should take in mind when visiting different properties 
 		in Buenos Aires.
         </p>
         <p>
          From
          <a href="reserving_an_apartment.htm">
           Section 5: Making a Reservation Offer
          </a>
          we start to 
 	concrete the operation, which first step would be to Make and Offer on an 
 	apartmet and how to pay 
 	the  reservation fee.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="commisions.htm">
           Section 6 - Realtor Commisions
          </a>
          explains the 
 	commission that Buenos Aires realtors charge for real estate operations.
         </p>
         <p>
          In
          <a href="money_transfer.htm">
           Section 7 Tranfering the money to 
 	Argentina
          </a>
          we cover one of 
 	the more complex parts of the buying process which is to transfer the money to 
 	Argentina in order to do the payment.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="tax_id.htm">
           Section 8: How to get a Tax ID
          </a>
          provides step by step 
 	information on how to get Tax ID Number in Argentina (You will need it for Money 
 	Transfer, signing the Escritura and to open a bank account).
         </p>
         <p>
          In
          <a href="escribano.htm">
           Section 9: Escribano
          </a>
          describes the 
 	role and importance of having a good Escribano Público (Notary) .
         </p>
         <p>
          We explain in
          <a href="boleto_-_purchase_contract.htm">
           Section 10: Boleto - Pruchase 
 	Contract
          </a>
          what is the 
 	"Boleto" or property purchase contract.
         </p>
         <p>
          In
          <a href="escritura.htm">
           Section 11: The Escritura
          </a>
          we will cover the Escritura 
 	signature and the contents of the escritura itself.
         </p>
         <p>
          The
          <a href="bank_account.htm">
           Section 12: Opening a Bank Account in Argentina
          </a>
          explains how to open a Bank 
 	Account in Argentina, in order to receive rent payments, and be able to 
 	pay services and taxes of the apartment using internet.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="renting.htm">
           Section 13: Renting the Apartment
          </a>
          lists the major short term real estate agencies 
 	in Buenos Aires, and details the 
 	usual conditions.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="property_tax.htm">
           Section 14: Property Tax
          </a>
          and
          <a href="revenues_tax.htm">
           Section15: Income Tax
          </a>
          make a review of the taxes you would have to pay related to the property 
 	and the revenues it will generate in the future.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="buying_furniture.htm">
           Section 16: Buying Furniture
          </a>
          shows a sample inventory of furniture and other electronic 
 	equipments for the apartment and estimative costs.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="insurances.htm">
           Section 17: Insurance
          </a>
          explains the importance to hire an individual fire insurance 
 	additional to the one the building already has
         </p>
