    <div>
     <font class="size12 Arial12" color="#000000" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">
      Many people first learn about John "Mad Jack" Fuller in association with the follies he built in and around Brightling, Sussex, England. These structures stand in testimony to Fuller's desire to be remembered long after his death.
      <br/>
     </font>
    </div>
    <div>
     <font class="size12 Arial12" color="#000000" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">
      <br/>
     </font>
    </div>
    <div>
     <font class="size12 Arial12" color="#000000" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">
      But the follies are  not the most important legacy he created. He was a philanthropist, a patron of the arts and sciences and a founding member of the Royal Institution.
      <br/>
     </font>
    </div>
    <div>
     <font class="size12 Arial12" color="#000000" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">
      <br/>
     </font>
    </div>
    <div>
     <font class="size12 Arial12" color="#000000" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">
      Jack Fuller  is immortalized in the lasting gifts he bestowed upon the community: the
     </font>
     <font class="size12 Arial12" color="#000000" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">
      <a href="fullerianprofessors.html" target="_self">
       Fullerian Professorships
      </a>
     </font>
     <font class="size12 Arial12" color="#000000" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">
      ,  the
     </font>
     <font class="size12 Arial12" color="#000000" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">
      <a href="EastbourneLifeboat.html" target="_self">
       Eastbourne Lifeboat
      </a>
     </font>
     <font class="size12 Arial12" color="#000000" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">
      ,  the
     </font>
     <font class="size12 Arial12" color="#000000" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">
      <a href="Faith.html" target="_self">
       barrel organ &amp; bells
      </a>
     </font>
     <font class="size12 Arial12" color="#000000" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">
      at St Thomas a Becket Church and
     </font>
     <font class="size12 Arial12" color="#000000" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">
      <a href="Bodiam.html" target="_self">
       Bodiam Castle
      </a>
     </font>
     <font class="size12 Arial12" color="#000000" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">
      which he bought to save from demolition.
      <br/>
     </font>
    </div>
    <div>
     <font class="size12 Arial12" color="#000000" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">
      <br/>
     </font>
    </div>
    <div>
     <font class="size12 Arial12" color="#000000" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">
      The Fullers of Sussex were wealthy ironmasters and owned vast amounts of property, including sugar plantations in Jamaica.
      <br/>
     </font>
    </div>
    <div>
     <font class="size12 Arial12" color="#000000" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">
      <br/>
     </font>
    </div>
    <div>
     <font class="size12 Arial12" color="#000000" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">
      Fuller, being a Squire,  a Member of Parliament , and a captain in the Sussex Yeomanry Cavalry was well connected and is associated with many
     </font>
     <font class="size12 Arial12" color="#000000" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">
      <a href="friends2.html" target="_self">
       notable figures
      </a>
     </font>
     <font class="size12 Arial12" color="#000000" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">
      of his day.
      <br/>
     </font>
    </div>
   </div>

      </a>

    <div>
     <font class="size10 Helvetica10" color="#000000" face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif">

    <div>
     <font class="size10 Helvetica10" color="#000000" face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif">
      Heard the term insulin, dinosaur, thesaurus, genetics or anorexia nervosa? You can thank a Fullerian Professor. Faraday's electric motor, Dewar's thermos flask and Roget's slide rule were also created by scientists who were funded through Fuller's gifts to the Royal Institution.
      <br/>
     </font>
    </div>
    <div>
     <font class="size10 Helvetica10" color="#000000" face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif">
