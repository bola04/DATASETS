         <div class="entry-content">
          <p>
           Mental Health America of Greater Tarrant County cares about the mental wellness within the community.  We strive to enhance mental health and improve the lives of those impacted by mental health.  We believe in building awareness and education in our community.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            We work hard to make our community healthier and happier.
            <br/>
            <em>
             -Healing Hearts and Minds Together
            </em>
            <br/>
           </strong>
          </p>

           <strong>
            LOSS (5677).

           As many of you know, North Texas Giving Day is a concentrated effort to support the many organizations that make our community so wonderful.  at On North Texas Giving Day, you chose to enhance the health of the community and improve those lives impacted by mental illness. Every gift directly supports MHA programs. Follow MHA on Facebook or go to mhatc.org to learn more about upcoming events and how to get involved.

            North Texas Giving Day 2018 will be………. September 20, 2018!
