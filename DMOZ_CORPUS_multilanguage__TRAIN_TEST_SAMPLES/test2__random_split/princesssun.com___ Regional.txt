          <h2>
           Indulge in blissfully sunny holidays at Princess Sun Hotel
          </h2>
         </div>
         <div class="main-content-simple__content">
          <p>
           Princess Sun Hotel offers a perfect mix of comfort and leisure in Kiotari, lying on the southeastern coast of Rhodes. With delightful swimming pools and waterslides, exciting recreational activities for children and adults, a great spa and wellness centre and 214 newly refurbished rooms, this
           <strong>
            all-inclusive 4-star hotel
           </strong>
           welcomes guests in a warm and friendly ambience, ideal
           <strong>
            for families and couples
           </strong>
           .
          </p>
          <h3>
           All-inclusive holidays in Rhodes
          </h3>
          <p>
           If you are looking for
           <a href="https://www.princesssun.com/rooms-suites/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
            quality accommodation in Rhodes Greece
           </a>
           , Princess Sun is
           <strong>
            among the best hotels in Kiotari
           </strong>
           . It is situated on a small hill, surrounded by a charming Greek mountain landscape, and boasts panoramic sea view all over Kiotari bay. Featuring a variety of facilities and services, including tempting food and drink options available all day, a lively Animation Team, a range of spa treatments and massages, evening entertainment, as well as a professionally-run Mini Club, Princess Sun makes a dream setting for both children and adults to enjoy a relaxing
           <a href="https://www.princesssun.com/location/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
            holiday in south Rhodes
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <div class="main-content-simple__content__more" data-more-content="">
           <p>
            Princess Sun Hotel features five different accommodation types for all tastes and budgets. From the classic Double Rooms with mountain or sea view to the spacious Family Rooms and the newly refurbished Economy Rooms, guests may select the room that meets their needs and are ensured a totally relaxing stay in well-appointed spaces with modern decoration.
           </p>
           <p>
            With all those amenities and all-inclusive services provided, along with the friendly service by the attentive staff, Princess Sun is a great value for money hotel for your summer holidays in Rhodes, Greece.
           </p>
           <h2>
            Rhodes beach hotel for relaxing family holidays
           </h2>
           <p>
            Princess Sun provides an excellent range of leisure facilities and thoughtful services. It offers a mesmerizing private beach setting on Galouni Beach. Our free shuttle buses offer rides to the beach (800 meters distance) every 30 minutes, where you can enjoy the crystal clear blue waters, do water sports and of course taste meals and snacks at our all-inclusive beach Restaurant and Bar.
           </p>
           <p>
            Kids will be glad to discover the hotel’s Mini Club, where our professional staff will introduce them to fun activities and games for moments of creativity and inspiration. In the meantime parents can enjoy quality time, take advantage of the free spa facilities and unwind by the pool. Our little friends will have the time of their lives at the children’s pool with the waterslides. They will also have the chance to select their favourite food from the kid’s menu.
            <br/>
            Can you think of anything better for your
            <a href="https://www.princesssun.com/children-family/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
             family holidays in south Rhodes
            </a>
            ?
           </p>
           <p>
            We look forward to welcoming you to Princess Sun Hotel in Kiotari, Rhodes, a member of the emblematic H Hotels Collection.
           </p>
