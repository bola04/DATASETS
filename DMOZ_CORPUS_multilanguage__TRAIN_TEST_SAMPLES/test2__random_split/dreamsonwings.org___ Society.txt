          <h2 style="text-align: center;">

           </span>

          <h1 style="text-align: center;">
           Have A Full Day Of Fun On Us!
          </h1>
          <p>
           When a child is faced with a serious illness it affects the entire family. Dreams On Wings is designed to give those children and their families a day to forget about the issues they’re dealing with and do something few people get to do—FLY!!
          </p>
          <p>
           Through the generosity of numerous individuals and organizations, the Dreams On Wings program provides a full day of entertainment-Limo rides, games, food, drink, clowns, and FLYING!!
          </p>
          <p>
           You will be flown in one of a variety of aircraft donated by our highly qualified volunteer pilots. From a single engine Cessna to a helicopter to a business jet, you will have the opportunity to experience the thrills that pilots love. You will be taken on a 45 minute flight around the city of Birmingham. And the best part of it is that your immediate family gets to go, too!
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://dreamsonwings.org/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/DOW-Article.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">
            Civil Air Patrol Dreams On Wings Article
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            Dreams On Wings was the recipient of the 2008 Teamwork Award in the Public Benefit Flying Awards given by the National Aeronautic Association and the Air Care Alliance.
           </em>
          </p>
