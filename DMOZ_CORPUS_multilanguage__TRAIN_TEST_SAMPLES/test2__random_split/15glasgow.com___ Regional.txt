              <p>
               Welcome to 15Glasgow, the award-winning boutique bed &amp; breakfast in Glasgow. Situated on the edge of the city centre, we are also a stone’s throw away from the heart of the west end. Whether you are in town for a concert or here on business, we are the visitor’s choice for luxury accommodation with super king-size beds, large, modern ensuites, and a hearty, traditional breakfast.
              </p>
              <p>
               Our grand old 19th century terrace home, features original fireplaces, intricate cornicing, working wooden shutters coupled with spectacular modern conveniences. Book any one of our five themed rooms for a comfortable, quiet place to rest.
              </p>
              <p>
               During your stay, you will have access to the private residents’ garden opposite. We’re a dog-friendly bed &amp; breakfast so you’re more than welcome to let your dog use the private garden too. If you are driving into the city, do let us know because parking is limited in our private car park at the rear of the house.
              </p>
              <p>
               Experience the best of Glasgow – art galleries, museums, parks, shopping and an abundance of restaurants just minutes away. When you stay with us at 15Glasgow, the city centre and the bustling west end, are at your doorstep.
              </p>

               To book us for private events, call +44 (0)141 332 1263 with your enquiry.
