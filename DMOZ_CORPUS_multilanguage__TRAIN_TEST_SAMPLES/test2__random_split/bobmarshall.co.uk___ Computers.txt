     <h1>
      Bob Marshall - Historical reconstruction artist &amp; 3D modelmaker
     </h1>
     <div class="hpwrapper">
      <div class="hpitem">
       <p>
        I am a freelance artist based in East Lothian in Scotland. I produce detailed architectural illustrations and historical reconstructions using digital techniques; computer-based 3D modelling and animation.
       </p>
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="hpwrapper">
      <div class="hpitem">
       <p>
        My work is used in books, interpretive signage, and in video and interactive multimedia programmes for exhibitions, museums and visitor attractions. My clients include The Royal Collection Trust, Historic Environment Scotland, Historic Royal Palaces, English Heritage and Glasgow School of Art.
       </p>
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="hpwrapper">
      <div class="hpitem">
       <p>
        Illustrated historical reconstructions are the main focus of my work, but I produce other complex visualisations and animations as well. Please
        <a href="illustrations.asp">
         browse some of my work
        </a>
        and
        <a href="contact.asp">
         drop me a line
        </a>
        if you have any questions.
       </p>
