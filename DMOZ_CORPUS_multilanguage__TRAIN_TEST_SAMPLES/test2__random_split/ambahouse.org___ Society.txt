      <h1>
       <b>
        Amba House of Traditions
       </b>
      </h1>
      <img align="right" src="images/hands_praying.jpg" style="margin-right:25px; margin-bottom:50px;"/>
      <p>
       Where cherished scriptural traditions of India are brought directly to you.
      </p>
      <p>
       "I do not wish to be considered any higher than any other sadhaks. I feel like I am a fellow traveller (sahacharee) that is accompanying, helping and being there for those sadhaks that reach out to me. When they no longer need me they will go their way. Being very alive to the kingdom of the Divine, I am not interested in establishing any swami/guru/baba/ amma dom for myself. I feel very much a part not apart from the Whole."
      </p>
      <p>
       Your journey is yours Alone
       <br/>
       The Pathway is your making
       <br/>
       But as you go, there by your side
       <br/>
       Hush! Footsteps accompany.
      </p>
      <p>
       Matching your gait, one by one
       <br/>
       A skip, a jump, a turn or drag
       <br/>
       Leading you to YOUR DIVINE CORE
       <br/>
       Coating with love an oft bitter-sweet truth
       <br/>
       Hidden in the words from Beyond
      </p>
      <p>
       Engaged in the never ending churn
       <br/>
       Of connecting the All Around
       <br/>
       To the steady Rod within.
      </p>
      <p>
       The One whose raiment keeps changing
       <br/>
       Custom ordered by you, none other
       <br/>
       That human companion
       <br/>
       Stepping no more,
       <br/>
       In silence will remain behind
       <br/>
       Only when you rendezvous
       <br/>
       With your Eternal Kind
      </p>
      <h2>
       Sahachari Vasantiji
      </h2>
      <p>
      </p>
