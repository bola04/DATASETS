     <font color="#666666" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">
      I 
     am a designer and builder of custom
      <font size="3">
       <strong>
        solid body
       </strong>
      </font>
      ,
      <strong>
       <font size="3">
        semi acoustic
       </font>
      </strong>
      and
      <font size="3">
       <strong>
        acoustic
       </strong>
      </font>
      instruments. The instruments I design can be within any scale length. Through 
     detailed discussion with every client, every job that goes through this shop 
     is custom designed, planned and
      <font size="3">
       <strong>
        hand built
       </strong>
      </font>
      strictly for the end user, the client.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Over the years I have developed a construction style that allows me the ability 
     and freedom to adapt and combine any series of elements that might appear 
     on any instrument into one personal project. Each project is voiced through 
     selection of woods, dimensions,
      <font size="3">
       <strong>
        electronics
       </strong>
      </font>
      , 
     shapes and bracing to suit the needs of every client.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Every neck I build has a dozen elements that I have control over and allow 
     me to create the perfect neck for each player. As with all aspects of my work, 
     all necks are made individually.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Our electronics are as advanced as any in this field. My rigs vary from basic, 
     to over the top with tonal flexibly. We build
      <font size="3">
       <strong>
        custom 
     pickups
       </strong>
      </font>
      for any application.
      <br/>
      I use fine and rare materials or highly figured woods if requested. Complicated 
     artistic inlay is frequent. We do all sorts of odd and unusual combinations 
     of custom work and have come to be known as the last shop, and the best shop 
     that will actually attempt the instrument that a client has in mind.
      <br/>
