          <p>
           <span style="color: #703e1f;">
            <strong>
             Glenkirk B&amp;B
            </strong>
           </span>
           in the village of
           <span style="color: #703e1f;">
            <strong>
             Drumnadrochit, Loch Ness
            </strong>
           </span>
           offers the weary traveller comfortable homely bed and breakfast holiday accommodation of the highest quality set in delightfully scenic Scottish surroundings.
          </p>
          <p>
           A
           <strong>
            <span style="color: #703e1f;">
             warm welcome
            </span>
           </strong>
           awaits all our guests in our Scottish Tourist Board
           <span style="color: #703e1f;">
            <strong>
             4 Star
            </strong>
           </span>
           bed and breakfast accommodation.
          </p>
          <p>
           Glenkirk,
           <span style="color: #703e1f;">
            <strong>
             once the village church
            </strong>
           </span>
           , became redundant 30 years ago and is now the home of Ross &amp; Fiona Urquhart, who have transformed it into
           <span style="color: #703e1f;">
            <strong>
             one of the most unique
            </strong>
           </span>
           Bed and Breakfasts in the Highlands of Scotland.
           <a href="https://www.facebook.com/Glenkirkbedandbreakfast?ref=hl">
