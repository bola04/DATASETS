          Hunting with AGASSIZ OUTFITTERS in Manitoba’s World Famous Interlake Region for a huge
          <a href="http://agassizoutfitters.com/manitoba-black-bear-hunts/information-rates/" title="Manitoba Black Bear Outfitter">
           Manitoba Black Bear
          </a>
          or in beautiful Northern Manitoba on one of our remote fly-in
          <a href="http://agassizoutfitters.com/manitoba-black-bear-hunts/information-rates/" title="Manitoba Black Bear Outfitter">
           Manitoba Moose Hunts.
          </a>
          Our professional hunting guides and outfitters have the experience that gets you “front and center” with the big ones! The Interlake Region, where forest meets agricultural land, with numerous lakes, rivers, creeks, swamps and marsh areas makes it some of the best trophy
          <strong>
           Manitoba Black Bear Hunting
          </strong>
          in the world! Our SPRING and FALL Canadian wilderness BLACK BEAR HUNTS cover over 3000 square miles of prime wilderness. This area also offers an abundance of color phase BLACK BEARS, which include chocolate, cinnamon and blonde. Most years BLACK BEARS over 21″ B&amp;C and over 700 lbs are taken. AGASSIZ OUTFITTERS also has exclusive rights to a large remote hunting area in Northern Manitoba, approximately 120 air miles north of Thompson and this area is only accessible by float plane. This area is pristine and virtually untouched as far as hunting pressure, which explains why MOOSE in the high 190’s to low 200 class are harvested most years, due to the vast wilderness and no pressure in this area. This is one of the best MOOSE areas MANITOBA has to offer and we cater to both rifle and archery hunters.
          <p>
           Sitting by the fire enjoying the Northern Lights, listening to the wolves howl, fishing and hunting in this remote and pristine part of Northern Manitoba is a hunting experience that memories are made of!
          </p>
          <p>
           A big plus to hunting Manitoba’s Interlake Region and Northern Manitoba Regions with AGASSIZ OUTFITTERS, is the ability to take a wolf or coyote on any open big game tag in the fall. This does not fill the tag. Our hunters have several opportunities each year to take wolves and some real trophies have been taken.
          </p>
          <p>
           Or try your luck at our MANITOBA “World Class” WATERFOWL HUNTS, which are located in the heart of the Central Flyway. These World-Class MANITOBA WATERFOWL HUNTS are in a class of their own! Our area is the first stop (resting &amp; feeding areas) for most Northern birds migrating to their wintering grounds. At AGASSIZ OUTFITTERS, you will experience a hunt like no other. Divers such as: Redheads, Bluebills, Canvasbacks, Goldeneyes, etc… and puddleducks such as : Mallards, Pintails, Gadwalls, Teal, Woodducks, etc.. , Greater and lesser Canada Geese, Snow &amp; Blue Geese make it a waterfowl hunters paradise. Over pot holes, lakes, rivers or grain fields we can satisfy your crave for some excellent waterfowl hunting. As an added bonus, our ruffed grouse is second to none and tops off to make an incredible hunting package put into one for those that choose to add another bird species to their hunt.
          </p>
          <p>
           Come out and sink your line and hook and try our World Class MANITOBA Fishing Adventures. With endless miles of shoreline, un-touched bodies of water and the number of species, makes MANITOBA a favorite destination for most. Families from all over North America have witnessed the spectacular walleye and northern pike fishing our area has to offer. Manitoba Walleye over 28” and northern pike over 41”, when the fishing is peak, is never un-common. Our professional licensed Manitoba fishing guides work many lakes and rivers pending species preferred. Guests of AGASSIZ OUTFITTERS will also witness a mouth watering prepared shore lunch of their catch cooked up to perfection. Fishing on a lake just minutes from camp or the historic Cedar Lake areas and experiencing these great fisheries ,will leave you breathless.
          </p>
          <p>
           AGASSIZ OUTFITTERS is one of MANITOBA’S premier road accessible and fly-in HUNTING &amp; FISHING camps. The camps have some great amenities with many years of history and have something unique to offer everyone – all served up by your hosts, Rick &amp; Colleen Liske &amp; family. While at their camps, you’ll experience home cooking at it’s finest with their own specialty dishes which are requested year after year.
          </p>
          <p>
           Make your next MANITOBA BLACK BEAR HUNT, MANITOBA MOOSE HUNT, MANITOBA WATERFOWL HUNTING trip or World Class MANITOBA Fishing Adventure an AGASSIZ OUTFITTERS experience!
          </p>
