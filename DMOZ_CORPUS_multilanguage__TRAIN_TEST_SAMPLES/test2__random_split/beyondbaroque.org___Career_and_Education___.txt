 Carlos Segovia Heart of Compassion Scholarship!
              </strong>
              <center>
               <br/>
               <br/>
               <a href="http://beyondbaroque.org/gallery.html">
                <img alt="" src="img2018/poetaward.jpg" width="250"/>

               <br/>
               <br/>
               <p>
                Each year, Beyond Baroque partners with Get Lit to award a $3,000 scholarship to one graduating high school poet to be used towards college expenses. It is given to writers who show exceptional generosity and kindness in their communities to honor the memory of Carlos Segovia, a late poet, Get Lit Player &amp; U.S marine.
                <a href="https://getlit.org/2018/05/872/" target="_blank">
                 More information
