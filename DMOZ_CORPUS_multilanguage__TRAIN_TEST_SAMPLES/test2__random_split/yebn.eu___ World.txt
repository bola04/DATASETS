              <h1>
               Welcome to the new Young European Biotech Network website!
              </h1>
              <span style="font-size: 14px">
               Are you a student or early-stage researcher in the Life Sciences and Biotechnology? Are you an organisation that would like to get in touch with proactive young scientist? If yes, then this website is for you.
 
 The Young European Biotech Network (YEBN) promotes Biotechnology and the Life Sciences in Europe. The purpose of YEBN is to exchange information and to build up international collaborations within the young European Life Science community. On this website you will find a lot of useful information, e.g. about career pathways for young scientists and how to foster the interaction between the Bio-industry and academia. At the discussion forums you can join young scientists from YEBN’s six national member organisations and individual members from by now fourteen European countries. YEBN represent the needs of young Biotechnologists and Life Science researchers in Europe – a chance for you to make your voice heard.
 
 Don’t wait, become part of the Young European Biotech Network!
              </span>
