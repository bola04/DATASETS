         <p>
          <b>
           CAVANAUGH EDITORIAL SERVICES
          </b>
          is a professional freelance copyediting and
 proofreading company with extensive experience in the publishing industry. Cavanaugh
 Editorial boasts more than ten years of freelance copyediting and proofreading experience
 and three years of in-house publishing experience. Our editorial service spans
 from academic publications to fiction novels and children's books. We are adept
 at editing and proofreading books and journals with displayed mathematics, complex
 legal, scientific, and medical terminology, and have extensive editorial experience
 in many subject areas, including the social sciences. Please visit the
          <a href="completed.html">
           <font color="#660000">
            <u>
             Projects
            </u>
           </font>
          </a>
          page
 for a sampling of the diverse subjects and genres we copyedit and proofread.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <h2>
          Publishing Experience
         </h2>
         <p>
          As the owner of Cavanaugh Editorial, Lori Cavanaughs three years of in-house production and editorial experience equips us with extensive knowledge of the various aspects of the publishing process.  As an in-house editor, Lori completed a range of tasks, including administration, page proof proofreading, and scheduling, all of which add to our understanding of the publishing industry as a whole. Specifically, Lori has editorial production experience at a world-renowned social science publisher; editorial experience at a large reference book publisher affiliated with accredited organizations such as the American Council on Education (ACE) and the Educational Resources Information Center (ERIC); and experience performing legal text codification, including electronic formatting of supplements. For a complete listing of our in-house editing and proofreading history, visit the
          <a href="resume.html">
           <font color="#660000">
            <u>
             Resume
            </u>
           </font>
          </a>
          page of this site.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <h2>
          Technical Expertise
         </h2>
         <p>
          In an effort to meet the dynamic needs of the overall publishing industry
   and the individual needs of each client, we have a thorough understanding of
   all of the major style manuals in use, including the American Psychological
   Association (APA), American Medical Association (AMA), Modern Language Association
   (MLA), the Chicago Manual of Style (CMS), and individual house styles. We also
   have experience with many different styles of typesetting code and page layout.
         </p>
         <p>
          As the industry continues the transition into the electronic world, it is our
   goal to remain current with the latest computer applications. We have extensive
   electronic publishing experience and regularly use Adobe Acrobat (PDF), Pagemaker,
   and Microsoft Word, including the Track Changes, Comments, and Styles (electronic
   tagging) features. Additionally, we regularly use FTP server applications to
   exchange large files and graphics.
         </p>
         <p>
