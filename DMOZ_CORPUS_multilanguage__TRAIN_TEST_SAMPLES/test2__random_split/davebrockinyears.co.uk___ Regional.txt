          <img align="right" alt="" border="1" height="350" hspace="2" src="images/dave-b11.jpg" vspace="2" width="250"/>
          Welcome to the Official Website of Dave Bs Rockin Years.
         </p>
         <p>
          Dave B is a South East based multi tribute artist playing across the UK in various Theatre Shows and with his band or solo.
               Currently starring in hit show Rock n Roll Paradise as Ricky Nelson and Big Bopper and fronting his very own show launched in March 2017 Travelin Man The Ricky Nelson Rock n Roll Show.
               Dave B also fronts rockin band Dave Bs Good Rockin Tonite.
         </p>
         <p>
          Dave Bs authentic solo or band tribute's of Ricky Nelson, Shakin Stevens, Buddy Holly and 50s/60s Elvis can be booked via the contact page.
               As seen on BBC TV, in London's West End (Buddy The Musical) Theatre's across the Uk and heard on radio stations across Europe.
          <br/>
          What are you waiting for? Enjoy the website and keep rockin.
          <br/>
          Dave B.
