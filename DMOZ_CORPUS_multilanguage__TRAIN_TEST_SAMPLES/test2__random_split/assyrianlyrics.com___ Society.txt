       <h2 class="push-up--h2">
        Welcome to Assyrin Lyrics
       </h2>
       <div class="flat-top push-down">
        <p>
         This website is created with the hope of getting more Assyrians to listen and understand Assyrian song lyrics. We created this website along with
         <a href="http://qeenatha.com" target="_blank">
          Qeenatha.com
         </a>
         , where you can listen to a large collection of Assyrian songs - together we wish to keep our beloved music alive.
        </p>
        <p>
         Select one of the letters from the side, or from below to navigate through the names of the various Assyrian artists available.
         <br/>
         You can also choose to browse songs starting by a certain letter.
        </p>
        <p>
         Click on
         <a href="/contact">
          Contact us
         </a>
         if you have any general enquiries or have any suggestions.
        </p>
