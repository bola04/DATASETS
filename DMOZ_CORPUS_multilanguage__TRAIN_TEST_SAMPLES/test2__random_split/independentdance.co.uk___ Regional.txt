        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Welcome to Independent Dance
        </h1>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         Independent Dance (ID) is an artist-led organisation working responsively to support and fuel dance artists in all roles, at all stages of their career, and of all physicalities.
        </p>
        <p>
         We nurture a practice-led, peer-supported international community of dance artists, with a particular focus on inclusive body-based research enriched by cross-disciplinary and diverse approaches.
        </p>
        <p>
         Often working with internationally renowned artists, our programme of classes and workshops, complemented by projects and events, invites artists to deepen enquiry and exchange ideas.
        </p>
        <div class="three-columns">
         <div class="column column-1">
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           <span class="editor-notes">
            .column 1
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.independentdance.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/beeca-hi-res-mairi-taylor.jpg">
            <img alt="" class="alignnone wp-image-6035 size-full" height="5906" src="http://www.independentdance.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/beeca-hi-res-mairi-taylor.jpg" title="Mike Harwood" width="5906"/>
           </a>
           <a href="http://www.independentdance.co.uk/programmepage/activities/">
            <img alt="" class="alignnone wp-image-6037 size-full" height="3936" sizes="(max-width: 5904px) 100vw, 5904px" src="http://www.independentdance.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/beeca-hi-res-mairi-taylor-4x6.jpg" srcset="http://www.independentdance.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/beeca-hi-res-mairi-taylor-4x6.jpg 5904w, http://www.independentdance.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/beeca-hi-res-mairi-taylor-4x6-300x200.jpg 300w, http://www.independentdance.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/beeca-hi-res-mairi-taylor-4x6-768x512.jpg 768w, http://www.independentdance.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/beeca-hi-res-mairi-taylor-4x6-1024x683.jpg 1024w, http://www.independentdance.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/beeca-hi-res-mairi-taylor-4x6-600x400.jpg 600w" title="Image by Mairi Taylor" width="5904"/>

          </p>
          <h3 style="text-align: left;">
           Autumn at Independent Dance
          </h3>
          <p>
           Booking now open
          </p>
          <p>
           Our Autumn season starts with a week of
           <a href="http://www.independentdance.co.uk/programmepage/exchanges/parlour-conversations/">
            Parlour Conversations
           </a>
           , a series of informal conversations about current practice and its context, alongside our annual
           <a href="http://www.independentdance.co.uk/programmepage/activities/id-graduate-open-day/">
            ID Graduate Day
           </a>
           and
           <a href="http://www.independentdance.co.uk/he-round-table/">
            Higher Education Round Table.
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           We will have three workshops with
           <a href="http://www.independentdance.co.uk/author/claire-cunningham/">
            Claire Cunningham
           </a>
           ,
           <a href="http://www.independentdance.co.uk/material-for-the-spine/">
            Otto Ramstad
           </a>
           and Rebecca Mackenzie’s
           <a href="http://www.independentdance.co.uk/programmepage/activities/book-club-for-dancers/">
            Book Club for Dancers
           </a>
           before WinLab closes the year.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.independentdance.co.uk/author/marina-collard/">
            Marina Collard
           </a>
           starts the
           <a href="http://www.independentdance.co.uk/programmepage/classes/morning-class/">
            Morning Classes
           </a>
           of the season and
           <a href="http://www.independentdance.co.uk/author/claudio-girard/">
            Claudio Girard
           </a>
           leads the first
           <a href="http://www.independentdance.co.uk/programmepage/classes/monday-night-improvisation/">
            Monday Night Improvisation
           </a>
           of the autumn.
           <br/>
           <a href="http://www.independentdance.co.uk/author/rosalie-wahlfrid/">
            Rosalie Wahlfrid-Shaw
           </a>
           and
           <a href="http://www.independentdance.co.uk/author/adriana-pegorer/">
            Adriana Pegorer
           </a>
           will be leading a week of morning class each for the first time at ID.
          </p>
          <p>
           Here is a handy summary of the
           <a href="http://independentdance.cmail19.com/t/ViewEmail/j/E01CC8E8D22423942540EF23F30FEDED/F01C3AEBE4BC76DBC5EC08CADFFC107B" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
            autumn season
           </a>
           in e-flyer form!
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="column column-2">
          <p>
           <span class="editor-notes">
            column 2
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.independentdance.co.uk/invitation-to-attend/">
            <img alt="" class="alignnone wp-image-5999 size-medium" height="200" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="http://www.independentdance.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/Claire-Cunningham-in-Beyond-the-Breakwater-©-Paul-Blakemore-courtesy-Situations--300x200.jpg" srcset="http://www.independentdance.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/Claire-Cunningham-in-Beyond-the-Breakwater-©-Paul-Blakemore-courtesy-Situations--300x200.jpg 300w, http://www.independentdance.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/Claire-Cunningham-in-Beyond-the-Breakwater-©-Paul-Blakemore-courtesy-Situations--768x512.jpg 768w, http://www.independentdance.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/Claire-Cunningham-in-Beyond-the-Breakwater-©-Paul-Blakemore-courtesy-Situations--1024x683.jpg 1024w, http://www.independentdance.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/Claire-Cunningham-in-Beyond-the-Breakwater-©-Paul-Blakemore-courtesy-Situations--600x400.jpg 600w" title=" Image by Paul Blakemore courtesy Situations" width="300"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <h3>
           Workshop:
           <em>
            Invitation to Attend
           </em>
           <br/>
           with Claire Cunningham
          </h3>
          <p>
           Sunday 16 September | 11am – 5pm
          </p>
          <p>
           In our first workshop of the season,
           <a href="http://www.independentdance.co.uk/author/claire-cunningham/">
            Claire Cunningham
           </a>
           will share methods and scores central to her work, opening up dialogues about where her choreographic practice based around attending/attention and perception, and practices learned from normative-bodied artists, meets with concepts of Crip politics – such as Crip time – and the phenomenology/lived experience of disability.
          </p>
          <p>
           The workshop is open to people of diverse physicalities and sensory modalities, the work will use structured improvisation and allows everyone to engage at their own chosen levels of energy, pace and scale of movement.
          </p>
          <p>
           If you have any specific access requirements please let us know.
          </p>
          <p>
           For more information and to book click
           <a href="http://www.independentdance.co.uk/invitation-to-attend/">
            here
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
         </div>

          <h3>
           <strong>
            MA/MFA Creative Practice:

            MA/MFA Creative Practice: Dance Professional Practice
           </a>

          </p>
