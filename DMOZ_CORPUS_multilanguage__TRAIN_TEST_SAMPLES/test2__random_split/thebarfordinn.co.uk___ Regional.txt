         <h1>
          Vince,  Karen and staff welcome you to The Barford Inn
         </h1>
         <img align="right" height="803" src="images/posters2018.jpg" style="margin-left:" width="600"/>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <font face="Verdana, Helvetica, sans-serif">
           Situated between Salisbury 
               and Shaftesbury in the heart of Wiltshire, in the picturesque village 
               of Barford St. Martin, the Barford Inn is a sixteenth century Coaching 
               Inn that used to brew its own beer in what is now the beautiful 
               restaurant. The Grade II listed building also has a bar and 4 en-suite 
               bedrooms.
          </font>
         </p>
         <p>
          <font face="Verdana, Helvetica, sans-serif">
           Relax outside in our 
               large beer garden, or sit in our courtyard, which has a full awning 
               and patio heaters.
          </font>
         </p>
         <p>
          <font face="Verdana, Helvetica, sans-serif">
           The Barford Inn is 
               an ideal base to enjoy all the attractions of Salisbury and the 
               local area while enjoying the peace and quiet of an old English 
           Pub. The area is also wonderful for walking and fishing.
          </font>
          <br/>
         </p>
