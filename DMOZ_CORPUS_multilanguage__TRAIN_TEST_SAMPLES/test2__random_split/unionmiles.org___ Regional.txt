           <h1>
            Welcome To Union Miles Development Corporation.
           </h1>
           <p>
            The Union Miles Development Corporation’s mission is to “improve the quality of life within Union Miles service area through community engagement, housing and economic development.” We accomplish our mission through providing a variety of programs and services. One of these aspects is community building through neighborhood beautification and community engagement. Utilizing a culturally relevant approach to produce creative products while telling stories in the tradition of the African Griot. creating deeper connections between people, place and time.
