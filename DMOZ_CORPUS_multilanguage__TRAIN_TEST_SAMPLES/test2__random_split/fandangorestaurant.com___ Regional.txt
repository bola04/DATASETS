           </h1>
          </header>

          </a>
          <div class="entry-content">
           <p>
            <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-105" height="1000" sizes="(max-width: 1450px) 100vw, 1450px" src="http://fandangorestaurant.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/fandango-night-shot.jpg" srcset="http://fandangorestaurant.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/fandango-night-shot.jpg 1450w, http://fandangorestaurant.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/fandango-night-shot-300x206.jpg 300w, http://fandangorestaurant.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/fandango-night-shot-1024x706.jpg 1024w" width="1450"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Welcome to Fandango Restaurant, located on the beautiful Monterey Peninsula in the heart of Pacific Grove, California. The family-owned restaurant, conveniently located near Monterey and Carmel, stands for a heritage of good taste, craftsmanship of award winning cuisine and 25 years of tradition.
           </p>
           <p>
            Fandango, serving Monterey and Carmel, has become a favorite restaurant for locals and visitors alike. We specialize in creating a unique and enjoyable fine dining experience, presenting a
            <a href="http://fandangorestaurant.com/menus/lunch/" title="Lunch">
             lunch
            </a>
            ,
            <a href="http://fandangorestaurant.com/menus/dinner/" title="Dinner">
             dinner
            </a>
            and
            <a href="http://fandangorestaurant.com/menus/brunch/" title="Sunday Brunch">
             Sunday Brunch
            </a>
            menu that features Mediterranean and European style cuisine. We offer inventive dishes inspired from ingredients around the globe including fresh seafood and hearty pastas, mouth-watering steaks and our signature rack of lamb.
           </p>
           <p>
            Whether it’s for your private
            <a href="http://fandangorestaurant.com/special-events/" title="Special Events">
             event
            </a>
            , wedding rehearsal dinner,
            <a href="http://fandangorestaurant.com/banquets/" title="Banquets">
             banquet
            </a>
            or for that special occasion for just the two of you, Fandango Restaurant will prepare a tantalizing meal perfectly paired with our extensive
            <a href="http://fandangorestaurant.com/wines/whites/">
             wines selections
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <p>
            Let Fandango’s delectable dishes and attentive service transport you to a European Bistro without leaving your own backyard! Call to make your reservation today
            <strong>
             831-372-3456
            </strong>
            or
            <a href="http://fandangorestaurant.com/reservations/" title="Find a Table">
             book online
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <p>
            Fandango offers gift certificates for any amount. They can be mailed or picked up at Fandango.
           </p>
