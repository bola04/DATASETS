               <div class="sqs-block-content">
                <h1>
                 Brand new thinking
                </h1>
               </div>
              </div>
              <div class="row sqs-row">
               <div class="col sqs-col-6 span-6">
                <div class="sqs-block html-block sqs-block-html" data-block-type="2" id="block-yui_3_17_2_14_1518556698365_58809">
                 <div class="sqs-block-content">
                  <p>
                   At the heart of any great design is a great idea. An idea that captures your attention – and doesn’t let it go. It has the power to entertain and inform you, to inspire and teach you. It can change the way we behave and it can bring people together.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   It takes great thinking, great people and great processes to deliver great work. Our clients have kept coming back for more than 20 years because they know we aspire to all three.
                  </p>
                 </div>
                </div>
               </div>
               <div class="col sqs-col-6 span-6">
                <div class="sqs-block html-block sqs-block-html" data-block-type="2" id="block-yui_3_17_2_14_1518556698365_59756">
                 <div class="sqs-block-content">
                  <p>
                   Sure we like to win awards – who doesn't? – and we love what we do, but a satisfied client and a successful result are our real motivation. That's how we know we've done our job.
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   Whatever your product or service we're here to share it with the world. We'll find that special detail that sits quietly waiting to be discovered and then we'll make it sing. It's really pretty simple. Sometimes it just takes some brand new thinking.
                  </p>
                 </div>
