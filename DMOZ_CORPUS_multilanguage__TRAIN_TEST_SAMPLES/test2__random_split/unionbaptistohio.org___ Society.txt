      <h1>
       Welcome to Union Baptist Church
      </h1>
      <hr/>
      <p class="auto-style1">
       Union Baptist Church is warm with Christian fellowship and evangelistic 
 fervor in the context of strong doctrinal preaching. Our preaching reveals our 
 belief that preaching that is consistant with historic systematic theology and 
 consistant with sound Biblical theology is the preaching that produces spiritual 
 Christians that enjoy the presence of God in their lives.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       It is with the same sentiment that the number of children does not make a 
 family the number of attendees do not make the church. The church is the Lord's assembly. He is the head. When we welcome you we are inviting you to 
 visit with us as we assemble as the Lord's assembly to worship and serve His 
 person and His work.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       We begin our service with a "call to worship" 
 not to mimic any tradition but to consciously attempt to bring ourselves 
 from the cares of the world to focus our hearts and minds on Jesus. We want 
 our not so formal worship time to focus on God who has reveled Himself to us 
 in the person of His Son, Jesus, by His Holy Spirit.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       We pray that all visitors will find a warm welcome at Union Baptist.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <strong>
        For the youth;
       </strong>
       We have AWANA on Wednesday evening beside a reguler 
 schedule for youth and children.
       <br/>
       <br/>

        For our fellowship:
       </strong>
       We have at least one meeting per month that is

 Churches. The Garbc is a national/intenational association of independant
