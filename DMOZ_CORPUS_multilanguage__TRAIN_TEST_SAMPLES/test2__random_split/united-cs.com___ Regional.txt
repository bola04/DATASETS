           <h1 class="marginBottom rtecenter">
            <strong>
             Yet Another Beat the Peak for July 23
            </strong>
           </h1>
           <div class="_5pbx userContent _3576" data-ft='{"tn":"K"}' id="js_1g">
            <p>
             <span aria-live="polite" class="fbPhotosPhotoCaption" data-ft='{"tn":"K"}' id="fbPhotoSnowliftCaption" tabindex="0">
              <span class="hasCaption">
               <img alt="" height="292" name="public://resize/images/Beat" peak="" peak-300x292.jpg="" src="/sites/unitedcs/files/resize/images/Beat%20the%20Peak/Beat%20the%20Peak-300x292.jpg" style="margin-left: 12px; margin-right: 12px; float: right; width: 300px; height: 292px;" the="" width="300"/>
              </span>
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size:12pt">
              <span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>
               <span style='font-family:"Calibri",sans-serif'>
                <span style="color:black">
                 Beat the Peak from 3:30-5:30 p.m. Monday (7-23)! During this time, don't use ovens, stoves, dishwashers and dryers. Set AC to 78. Together, we save!
                </span>
               </span>
              </span>
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             United Cooperative Services members can cut usage and save energy dollars by signing up for Beat the Peak, an early-warning system that tells consumers when energy prices peak, and how they can avoid paying peak prices for power. Lower demand at peak time lowers the co-op’s overall cost for power. As a nonprofit entity, United then passes the energy savings on to members. The more people participate, the more energy and energy dollars are conserved.
            </p>
            <p>
             The program is voluntary and benefits all co-op members. Individual members will see neither a charge increase for non-participation, nor a conservation usage credit for those who choose to partake. Click
             <a href="/content/beat-peak-sign-sheet">
              HERE FOR MORE.
             </a>
            </p>

                <h2 class="marginBottom rtecenter">
                 <strong>
                  Let the Sun Shine
                 </strong>
                </h2>
                <div class="_5pbx userContent _3576" data-ft='{"tn":"K"}' id="js_1g">
                 <p>
                  <span aria-live="polite" class="fbPhotosPhotoCaption" data-ft='{"tn":"K"}' id="fbPhotoSnowliftCaption" tabindex="0">
                   <span class="hasCaption">
                    <img alt="" beat="" height="300" jpg="" knockout-300x300.jpg="" logo="" name="public://resize/images/Beat" peak="" solar="" src="/sites/unitedcs/files/resize/images/COMMUNITY%20SOLAR%20LOGO%20knockout-300x300.jpg" style="margin-left: 12px; margin-right: 12px; float: right; width: 300px; height: 300px;" the="" width="300"/>
                   </span>
                  </span>
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <span aria-live="polite" class="fbPhotosPhotoCaption" data-ft='{"tn":"K"}' tabindex="0">
                   <span class="hasCaption">
                    Are you a member who has thought about installing solar, but just didn't have the space or the cash? Sign up for United's Community Solar program! We take care of maintaining the system, you get benefits of green energy and a whole lot more. It's a win-win for our members.
                   </span>
                  </span>
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  To join, you must be a United member in good standing on a general service single-phase rate class with an average monthly net energy consumption above 500 kWh. There is no upfront cost to join, no contract to sign, no insurance required and the potential savings begin the first year members sign up for the project.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  So what are you waiting for?
                  <a href="/content/contact-us">
                   Call your local United office
                  </a>
                  and ask to sign up for community solar today! Click
                  <a href="/content/united-community-solar">
                   HERE TO LEARN MORE
                  </a>
                  .
                 </p>

                <hr/>
                <h2 class="rtecenter" style="margin-right: 0in; margin-left: 0in;">
                 Enroll in Rush Hour Rewards and Reap the Energy Savings
                </h2>
                <p style="margin-right:0in; margin-left:0in">
                </p>
                <p>
                 <img alt="" and="" height="150" name="public://resize/images/Nest" neste-263x150.jpg="" src="/sites/unitedcs/files/resize/images/Nest%20and%20NestE-263x150.jpg" style="width: 263px; height: 150px; margin-left: 12px; margin-right: 12px; float: left;" width="263"/>
                </p>
                <p>
                 For an automated response to beating the peak, some members also may wish to participate in United’s Rush Hour Rewards program by purchasing a Nest thermostat and signing up.
                </p>
                <p>
                 The Nest thermostat can help members use less energy during the rush hour by cooling their homes down ahead of the peak and then slightly adjusting the temperature up a few degrees later on when it’s needed. However, members are still in control and can manually change the temperature at any time if they start feeling uncomfortable.
                </p>
                <p>
                 After signing up, United gives members a $50 bill credit after the first summer. They’ll earn $15 for each summer month they are enrolled up to $60 a year for each Nest Learning Thermostat in their homes.
                 <a href="/content/nest">
                  Click here for details.
                 </a>
                </p>

                <h2 class="rtecenter">
                </h2>
                <h2 class="rtecenter">
                 Members Get Valuable Discounts on Furnace Filters through United
                </h2>
                <p>
                 <a href="http://www.filterchange.coop/?affid=TX164">
                  <img alt="" height="175" name="public://resize/images/filterChangeCoop" src="/sites/unitedcs/files/resize/images/filterChangeCoop%2050%25off-500x175.jpg" style="border-width: 0px; border-style: solid; margin-left: 10px; margin-right: 10px; width: 500px; height: 175px; float: right;" width="500"/>
                 </a>
                </p>
                <p>
                 Changing your furnace or air conditioner filter regularly saves wear and tear on valuable equipment. What most members aren’t necessarily familiar with is that heating and cooling costs can be reduced by as much as 15 percent by regularly practicing this simple task.
                </p>
                <p>
                 Through a unique new service called
                 <a class="ext" href="http://www.filterchange.coop/?affid=TX164" target="_blank">
                  FilterChange.coop
                 </a>
                 , United members now have a means of obtaining filters at discounted rates and without the need for wasting gas on a trip to the store.
                </p>
                <p>
                 FilterChange.coop is an easy way to order filters online at a discount and delivered free to your door. In addition to saving energy by helping HVAC systems run at optimal levels, FilterChange.coop can save members up to 50 percent.
                 <a href="/content/filterchangecoop">
