     <h3>
      Home
     </h3>
     <h4>
      Manufacturers of high integrity Carbon, Low Alloy, Manganese, Stainless, Duplex Steels and Cr Irons
     </h4>
     <p>
      <br/>
      H.I. Quality Steel Castings Ltd is a successful modern well equipped company that is committed to ongoing capital investment, career training and development. The high integrity castings we produce are exported world wide, they are used in the most demanding industrial applications. Very close metallurgical controls are in place to ensure that Â chemical and mechanical properties meet with our customers requirements.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      The company is located in Chesterfield Derbyshire. Steel castings are manufactured within a weight range of a few kgs to 9000 kgs allowing us to offer a vast range of component weights and sizes.
     </p>
     <p>
      The high integrity castings produced have served the world wide engineering industry for over 50 years.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Both sites manufacture all grades of
      <strong>
       Carbon Steels, Low Alloy Steels, Austenitic Manganese Steels, Corrosion and Heat Resisting Ferritic, Austenitic and Duplex Stainless Steels
      </strong>
      .
      <br/>
      <br/>
      The company's commitment to Quality Management systems and constant product improvement has provided us with the vehicle to enter into markets that require a high degree of casting integrity and material control.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Our customers are our valued partners and it is of paramount importance that we are able to offer a first class product, manufactured in the most efficient and cost effective manner to ensure that commercial advantage and product dependability result in increased business opportunities for both parties.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Ongoing skills training and career development, combined with continued investment in modern manufacturing techniques and technologies, provide a strong platform for our future development and reinforce our position as the leading steel foundry in the UK.
     </p>
