       <h4>
        The Celebrated California Bandit
       </h4>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.dianagabaldon.com/books/essays-forwards-intros/murieta/">
         <img alt="murieta-cover" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-8041" src="http://www.dianagabaldon.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2011/01/murieta-cover-193x300.jpg" width="90"/>
        </a>
        Diana Gabaldon has written a Forward for a new edition of THE LIFE AND ADVENTURES OF JOAQUIN MURIETA: THE CELEBRATED CALIFORNIA BANDIT, by John Rollin Ridge, a novel that was first published in 1854. The family of Joaquin Murieta was murdered by white settlers, which caused him to seek vengeance; this character was the inspiration for Zorro, the Lone Ranger, and Batman, all characters we know and love today.
       </p>

        This new edition by Penguin Classics was released in the U.S.A. on July 10, 2018. Copies signed by Diana are available from The Poisoned Pen bookstore, too.

          More information.

      <div class="post_content" id="next_book">
       <h4>
        When Will the Next Book Be Out?
       </h4>
       <hr/>
       <p>
        <b>
         Will there be a ninth major Outlander novel?
        </b>
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         Yes…
         <a href="http://www.dianagabaldon.com/books/outlander-series/book-nine-outlander-series/">
          GO TELL THE BEES THAT I AM GONE
         </a>
         (nicknamed “BEES”)
        </b>
        is the ninth book in my OUTLANDER series of major novels, which I am writing (and doing the research for) now.
       </p>
       <p>
        BEES follows
        <a href="http://www.dianagabaldon.com/books/outlander-series/written_in_my_own_hearts_blood/">
         WRITTEN IN MY OWN HEART’S BLOOD,
        </a>
        aka “MOBY,” the eighth novel in my OUTLANDER series, which was first published in the U.S. in 2014.
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         Excerpts (aka “Daily Lines”)
        </b>
        from—and information about—BEES are posted on
        <a href="http://www.dianagabaldon.com/books/outlander-series/book-nine-outlander-series/">
         my BEES official webpage
        </a>
        as they are released. Also, I often post the same excerpts on my official social media pages and accounts.
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         Do I know exactly when BEES will be finished? No.
        </b>
        I am working on it now;
        <a href="http://www.dianagabaldon.com/2016/09/want-to-watch-me-write-preview/">
         my books take at least 3-4 years to research and write.
        </a>
        I am hoping to finish writing BEES by the end of 2018, but no publication date is set yet by myself or my publisher. A publication date will be posted on this website when known.
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         About reposting my excerpts
        </b>
        from any of my books and short fiction, I’d appreciate it if you would not copy the excerpts and post them yourself all over the internet.
        <b>
         Instead, please do pass on the links to my excerpts (“Daily Lines”) to anyone you think might be interested.
        </b>
        Thanks!
       </p>

       <h4>
        Diana's Fan Fiction Policy
       </h4>
       <div class="post_content">
        <p>
         You know, I’m very flattered that some of you enjoy the books so much  that you feel inspired to engage with the writing in a more personal way than  most readers do.
