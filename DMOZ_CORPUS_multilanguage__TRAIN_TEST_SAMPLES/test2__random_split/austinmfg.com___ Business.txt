                <div class="col-2">
                 <h1 class="smaller">
                  Complete Contract Manufacturing and Assembly
                 </h1>
                 <p>
                  Providing a full spectrum of world-class
                  <strong>
                   electronics manufacturing
                  </strong>
                  services (EMS), from prototyping through full scale production including, turnkey box builds, engineering and advanced testing solutions. AMS specializes in low-to-medium volume, high-mix assemblies for IP critical and complex applications.
                 </p>
                 <h2>
                  Electronics Manufacturing, Engineering &amp; System  Assembly
                 </h2>
                 <p class="inner">
                  <img alt="Electronics Manufacturing, Engineering and System  Assembly" class="margin1home" height="160" src="images/electronics-manufacturing.jpg" title="Electronics Manufacturing" width="280"/>
                  Experts in the manufacturing of printed circuit board assemblies (PCBAs) utilizing automated, leading-edge surface mount technology (SMT) and pin-through-hole (PTH) technologies. Providing engineering supported design-for-manufacturability DFM) and test (DFT), through complete PCBA integration into
                  <strong>
                   high level electronics systems and electromechanical assemblies
                  </strong>
                  .
                 </p>
                 <h2>
                  Material, Supply Chain &amp; Program Management
                 </h2>
                 <p class="inner">
                  <img alt="Material, Supply Chain and Program Management" class="margin1home" height="160" src="images/material-supply.jpg" title="long term supplier partnerships" width="280"/>
                  Comprehensive component and raw material purchasing capabilities managed by knowledgeable and experienced purchasing  and planning professionals. Robust MRP/ERP systems and a strong financial framework help leverage long term supplier partnerships, in order to ensure success throughout the entire manufacturing and product life cycle.
                 </p>
                 <h2>
                  Worldwide Logistics &amp; Fulfillment
                 </h2>
                 <p class="inner">
                  <img alt="Worldwide Logistics and Fulfillment" class="margin1home" height="160" src="images/worldwide-logistics.jpg" title="Inventory management" width="280"/>
                  Inventory management dedicated to streamlining the client's operations. Providing seamless component-level to finished goods warehousing and shipping. Proven testing, kitting, and worry-free final customer fulfillment services as well as offering versatile and customized warranty, repair and refurbish programs.
                 </p>
