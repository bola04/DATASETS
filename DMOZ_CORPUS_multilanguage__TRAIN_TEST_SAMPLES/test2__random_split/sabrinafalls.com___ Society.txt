         <p>
          Sabrina Sigal Falls is a professional harpist who plays harp music for weddings, special events and funerals throughout the Indianapolis and central Indiana area.
         </p>
         <p>
          She is a Certified Music Practitioner (CMP), having completed the Music for Healing and Transition Program, a National Educational Certification Program for musicians who wish to become music practitioners. As a CMP, Sabrina frequently plays live therapeutic harp music at the bedside in hospitals, hospices, or homes.
         </p>
         <p>
          Please click on the Facebook icon below to see Sabrina playing at various events or
          <a href="https://sabrinafalls.wordpress.com/contact-sabrina/" title="Contact Sabrina">
           contact Sabrina
          </a>
          about playing for your event!
         </p>
