          AAA Approved Eugene, OR Hotel
         </h1>
         <div id="fact-box">
          <span>
           Check in: 4:00 PM
           <br/>
           Check out: 11:00 AM
           <br/>
           59 Rooms
          </span>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Free Wi-Fi
           </li>
           <li>
            Complimentary Breakfast
           </li>
           <li>
            Hot Tub
           </li>
           <li>
            Business Center
           </li>
           <li>
            Fitness Center
           </li>
          </ul>
         </div>
         Discover the beauty of the Pacific Northwest at our AAA approved hotel in downtown Eugene. Offering modern amenities, complimentary breakfast and a family friendly environment, our property is ideal for business and leisure travelers alike. From affordable rates to a convenient downtown location near the University of Oregon, you'll find it all at the Campus Inn &amp; Suites Eugene Downtown.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <strong>
          Disclaimer:
         </strong>
         <ul>
          <li>
           As a matter of business routine, Campus Inn &amp; Suites will secure a confirmation on your credit card (24-hours in advance of your arrival) for the total of a 1-night stay (including tax).  At check-in we will use the authorization to close out your billing or you may change your form of payment.
          </li>
          <li>
           At arrival, we require a $200.00 security deposit if you choose to pay with a debit or cash versus a credit card.
          </li>
         </ul>
