      <h2>
       Equipment, parts and service
      </h2>
      <p>
       Power Zone is the leading full service supplier of equipment, parts, and repairs for gasoline and battery powered lawn care, tree care, and construction tools in the New River Valley.
      </p>
      <p>
       Our service department is staffed with technicians that are factory-trained on all the products we sell as well as many brands sold by others. We offer high-priority service scheduling for equipment purchased from Power Zone, striving to offer 1-day turn-around to commercial customers and 7 days or less to residential customers.
      </p>
      <p>
       Warranty service is provided for machines purchased from us and for our brands purchased from mass retailers such as Home Depot, Lowes, Sears. Buy from us when you can, but we do service what others sell. We welcome all customers!
