          <h2 style="text-align: center;">
           Join us on Idaho’s Famous “River of No Return” multi-day whitewater rafting trips
          </h2>
          <p>
           The Main Salmon River, running through the heart of Idaho wilderness, is a classic river. Broad, sandy beaches, roller coaster and rock garden rapids,riverside wildlife such as moose and big horn sheep,emerald waters, hot springs, ranch sites from days of yore and one of the deepest canyon in the world combine to form an unparalleled setting for a wilderness rafting adventure.
          </p>
          <p>
           All of our trips feature the best guides in the business including those who have cut their guiding teeth on rivers throughout the western United States only to return to “The River Of No Return” The Main Salmon River. Our adventure also included the finest river cuisine imaginable, and then some; Full complements of personal camping gear; state of the art equipment, including self bailing rafts, one and two persons inflatable kayaks and coast guard approved life jackets; smooth and hassle free logistics; and an absolute dedication to your safety and enjoyment. Simply put there is no better way to experience the grand escape then with River Time Guide Service. We are an owner operated multi-day wilderness rafting outfitter. The benefit of this, is that we take great pride in providing our guest with personal attention needed to create the best wilderness trip possible. And while safety is our number one concern, your comfort and creating an environment in which you can relax and have fun is what we strive for.
