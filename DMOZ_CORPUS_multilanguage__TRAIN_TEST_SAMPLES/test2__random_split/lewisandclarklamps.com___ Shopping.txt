       Lewis+Clark is a studio, workshop and gallery in Columbia, South Carolina, featuring handmade post-modernist lamps, robots, and other hand-crafted art created by Clark Ellefson. The lamps are uniquely crafted of fine art papers, hand-worked steel, glass accents and fine hand-finished wood. Robots and other art is created from discarded metal and other materials. The gallery in the Congaree Vista also features examples of Lewis+Clark's award-winning contemporary furniture designs.
