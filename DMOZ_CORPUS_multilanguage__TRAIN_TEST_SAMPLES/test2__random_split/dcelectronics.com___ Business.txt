        <h2>
         Wire Harness and Cable Assembly Manufacturing: Process Driven. Customer Centered
        </h2>
        <div class="columnMainTextBlock">
         <div class="columnMainText">
          <p>
           ADCE (Amphenol DC Electronics) specializes in the design, engineering and manufacturing of custom wire harnesses, cable assemblies and related sub-assemblies. Founded in 1979 by the Cianciulli Family, ADCE built a reputation of uncompromising quality, innovation and delivery excellence that is second to none. As an
           <a href="http://www.amphenol.com">
            Amphenol
           </a>
           company, the Family foundation has been reinforced and expanded like never before. Servicing our diverse customers worldwide from our state of the art manufacturing facilities in Silicon Valley and Nogales Mexico, ADCE is now a Global wire harness and cable assembly leader with access to expertise and resources on six continents.
          </p>
          <p>
           ADCE has over thirty years of experience and innovation with custom wire harnesses and electronics cable assemblies. This industry knowledge gives us an unparalleled advantage in meeting our customers’ every need. Amphenol DC Electronics, Inc. ADCE is known for quick and comprehensive customer service as well as consistency in producing products that are manufactured to rigorous
           <a href="/about-custom-cable-assembly/quality">
            quality
           </a>
           standards and demanding production schedules.
          </p>
          <p>
           All custom wire harnesses and cable assemblies produced at our world class manufacturing facility in Silicon Valley are ISO 13485 certified and UL/CSA safety-approved. Our site includes a Class 7, Class 5 Clean Work Area and an ESD Safe Manufacturing Area.
           <a href="/contact">
            Contact
           </a>
           us today to get your wire harness manufacturing under way!
          </p>
         </div>
