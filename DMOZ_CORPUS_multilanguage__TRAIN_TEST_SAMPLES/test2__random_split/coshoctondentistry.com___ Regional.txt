       <h2>
        Local Dentists, World Class Smiles.
       </h2>
       <p>
        When you visit our office, your smile is our top priority.
        <a href="/meet-dr-brian-dunlap">
         Drs. Brian Dunlap
        </a>
        and
        <a href="/meet-dr-patrick-laaper">
         Patrick Laaper
        </a>
        and their entire
        <a href="/meet-the-staff">
         team
        </a>
        are dedicated to providing you with the personalized, gentle care that you deserve. We want to educate you on the choices you have. Many patients have found we offer a wide variety of quality choices without having to leave town in search of dental specialists.
       </p>
       <p>
        Part of our commitment to serving our patients includes providing information that helps them to make more informed decisions about their oral health needs. This website is a resource we hope you’ll find both useful and interesting.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <h3>
        Treatment Options
       </h3>
       <ul>
        <li>
         <a href="/dental-implants">
          Dental implants
         </a>
         offer simple single-tooth replacement, as well as comprehensive denture stabilization depending on your needs.
        </li>
        <li>
         <a href="/cosmetic-dentistry">
          Cosmetics
         </a>
         including
         <a href="/cerec">
          CEREC®
         </a>
         same-day crowns and veneers have been extremely well received and give a long lasting, durable smile.
        </li>
        <li>
         Orthodontics including conventional and
         <a href="/clear-aligners">
          ClearCorrect®
         </a>
         clear aligners offered in several varieties.
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        We genuinely hope this site is helpful to you in learning more about our practice, dental conditions and treatments, dental recommendations, and more.
       </p>
       <p>
        Please browse through our website and be sure to contact our
        <a href="/coshocton-office-location">
         Coshocton dental office
        </a>
        if you have any questions or would like to schedule an appointment.
       </p>
