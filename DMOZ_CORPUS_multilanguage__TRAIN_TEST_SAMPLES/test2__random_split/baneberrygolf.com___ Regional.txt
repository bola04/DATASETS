        <p align="center" style="font-size: 16px;">

        </p>
        <p align="center">
         <span style="font-weight: bold;">
          Great Golf
         </span>
         – Set on beautiful Douglas Lake in the historic Smokey Mountains, our 6700 yard golf course offers a relaxing country setting. With 4 sets of tees, the course is suitable for all skill levels. Guests of the resort enjoy unlimited golf.
        </p>
        <p align="center">
         <span style="font-weight: bold;">
          Comfortable Surroundings
         </span>
         – Enjoy a private villa or a room in the Inn. Our clubhouse features a fully-stocked pro shop, restaurant and bar, as well as a deck situated near our first tee. Enjoy the stars on your private balcony or patio, or prepare a meal in your room’s kitchen. Hit the swimming pool or the driving range to work on your stroke.
        </p>
        <p align="center">
         <span style="font-weight: bold;">
          Nearby Attractions
         </span>
         – When not enjoying the links, guests can explore Pigeon Forge, Gatlinburg, Dollywood, and the city of Knoxville – all are within an hour of the resort.
        </p>
