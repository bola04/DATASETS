                 <h3 class="p1">
                  Parri Estate
                 </h3>
                 <p class="p1">
                  Parri Estate is a family owned business.  Our aim is to produce wines that are a unique reflection of the region in which they are grown and the focus is very much on producing high quality estate grown wines.
                 </p>
                 <p class="p1">
                  The Kaurna people, traditional owners of the Southern Fleurieu Peninsula used the word “Parri” to describe the rivers, creeks and gullies that surrounded them.
                 </p>
                 <h3 class="p1">
                  Mc Laren Vale
                 </h3>
                 <p class="p1">
                  Located approx 35km from Adelaide, Mc Laren Vale is one of Australia's more reowned wine regions, famous for producing some of the best wines in the world. Its Mediterranean climate  with long warm days, and short cool nights, makes the region perfect for producing varieties like Shiraz, Cabernet Sauvignon and Grenache.
                 </p>
                 <p class="p1">
                  Parri Estate’s 14 acre Ingoldby Road vineyard is planted with Shiraz, Grenache and Cabernet Sauvignon. The property is just 50 metres above sea level and cool gully winds occur in summer slowing the ripening period down during vintage to produce full flavoured wines of high quality.
                 </p>
