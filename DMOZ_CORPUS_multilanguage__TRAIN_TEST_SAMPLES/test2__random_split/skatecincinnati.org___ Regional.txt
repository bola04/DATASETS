        <p>
         <span style="line-height: 21.45px;">

         </span>

       <h2 class="entry-title">
        <a href="http://skatecincinnati.org/summer-freestyle-program-available-starting-june-3rd/" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to Summer Freestyle Program available starting June 3rd">
         Summer Freestyle Program available starting June 3rd
        </a>
       </h2>
       <div class="entry-meta">
        <span class="entry-date">
         <a href="http://skatecincinnati.org/summer-freestyle-program-available-starting-june-3rd/" rel="bookmark" title="4:06 am">
          May 22, 2018
         </a>
        </span>
        <span class="entry-author">
         <a href="http://skatecincinnati.org/author/skatecincinnati/" rel="author" title="View all posts by skatecincinnati">
          skatecincinnati
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         FSCC is partnering with the coaches of Northern Kentucky to offer a Summer Freestyle program.  The program will include freestyle ice as well as on- and off-ice classes.  On weekdays our ice time starts at 10:15 and the program ends at 1:30.  Two freestyle ice sessions will also be available on Sundays starting at 4:25.  For more details on sessions, pricing and registration please see the
         <a href="http://skatecincinnati.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/Summer-Program-Brochure-2018v5.pdf">
          Summer Program Brochure-2018v5
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         When using the on-line registration,
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          please enter “Summer” in the class search window for easy access to the Summer offerings.
         </span>
        </p>
       </div>
      </article>

         Join the Figure Skating Club of Cincinnati!
        </a>
       </h2>
       <div class="entry-meta">
        <span class="entry-date">
         <a href="http://skatecincinnati.org/join-the-figure-skating-club-of-cincinnati/" rel="bookmark" title="7:14 pm">
          September 1, 2017
         </a>
        </span>
        <span class="entry-author">
         <a href="http://skatecincinnati.org/author/skatecincinnati/" rel="author" title="View all posts by skatecincinnati">
          skatecincinnati
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         We want you to know why we love the Figure Skating Club of Cincinnati!  Are you ready to soar toward your skating dream?  We are told that we are trustworthy, knowledgeable and fun!   Kids can’t wait to get to classes or take the podium with friends at competitions like Skate Cincinnati, Solo Dance Nationals or National Showcase.  Adults love getting together to prepare for next year’s Adult Nationals!  Hockey moms and dads are thrilled to have their kids learn power skills from our experienced, certified professionals in our Cincinnati Skating School.   Skaters of all ages are motivated in creative ways to perfect their skills, and work side by side in our skating club and wonderful city of Cincinnati.  Our club is over 50 years old and as our program continues to grow, we want to make it easy for our skaters to not only start but to continue with us through completion of skating goals and a lifelong love of skating!
        </p>
        <p>
         Look under the Club Information tab to learn more!
        </p>
       </div>
