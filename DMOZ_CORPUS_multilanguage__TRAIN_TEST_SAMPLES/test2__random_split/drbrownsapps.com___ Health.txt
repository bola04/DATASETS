       <p>
        DrBrownsApps.com is hosted by
        <a href="http://www.childrenstreatmentcenter4autism.com/">
         The Children’s Treatment Center for Autism and other Childhood Developmental Disorders
        </a>
        . At the Center we use Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA), which is recommended by the
        <a href="http://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/autism-spectrum-disorders-asd/index.shtml#part_145442">
         National Institute of Mental Health
        </a>
        , and the Surgeon General, as an essential therapy for children with autism or other developmental disorders. The desire to aid parents and care givers has led us on a path to develop a simple to use, but immensely powerful tool in helping a student build their skill sets.
       </p>
       <p>
        We have designed a set of apps to get your student interested in learning! The entire basis of of our app and core philosophy is that when scientifically proven clinical methods meets touch devices there is an instant increase in interest shown by students. The children of tomorrow will have access to methods that increase their desire to learn through their own natural playful minds and mixed with the DTT process can yield amazing results.
       </p>
       <p>
        We have built our app design on over 40 years of Dr. Brown’s psychological therapist experience in a clinical setting. This experience and setting has shown that Discrete Trial Training is the best way for a child to learn a skill. These results have been repeatedly proven via double blind scientific testing as one of the best ways to ensure proper learning that your child will hold onto and move forward with. This Discrete Trial Training enables the student to build upon a skill set over time by increasing set variables such as the number of choices, inter interval time, and correct / incorrect choice selections. There are dozens of ways for a user to customize the DTT experience to help your student grow!
       </p>
       <p>
        These apps apply DTT in the form of needed skills to navigate the world such as : Colors, Shapes, Letters, Numbers, Words, People, Animals, Time, Bully and Dyslexia options for Numbers and Letters! We have also combined all these into a single app, DTT Pro.
