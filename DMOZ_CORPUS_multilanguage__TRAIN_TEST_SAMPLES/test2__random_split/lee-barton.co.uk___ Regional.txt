               <div style="text-align: center;">
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 <strong>
                  Surrounded by glorious North Devon countryside, Lee Court and Lee Meadow
                 </strong>
                </span>
                <br/>
                <span style="font-size: large;">
                 <strong>
                  offer luxury self-catering cottage holidays on a working farm
                 </strong>
                </span>
               </div>
               <p>
                <img alt="Lee Meadow and Lee Court, North Devon" height="419" src="/images/cottages.jpg" style="margin-top: 8px; margin-right: 0px; margin-left: 12px; float: right;" title="Lee Meadow and Lee Court, North Devon" width="500"/>
                <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
                 <strong>
                  <img alt="AA 4 Star Self Catering" height="79" src="/images/aa4star.jpg" style="margin-top: 4px; margin-right: 8px; margin-left: 0px; float: left;" title="AA 4 Star Self Catering" width="50"/>
                 </strong>
                </span>
                <img alt="Enjoy England Gold Award" height="79" src="/images/enjoyengland.jpg" style="margin-top: 4px; margin-right: 12px; margin-left: 0px; float: left;" title="Enjoy England Gold Award" width="56"/>
                The cottages are
                <strong>
                 AA 4-star rated
                </strong>
                and gained the 2009/10
                <strong>
                 Enjoy England Gold Award
                </strong>
                for exceptional quality of accommodation and customer service. They form part of the Lee Barton farm, which includes a 17th century farmhouse, situated 2 miles from the village of High Bickington in rural North Devon.
               </p>
               <p>
                Available all year, both cottages offer self catering holiday accommodation with outstanding views of the countryside and are ideally situated for exploring the stunning coasts of North Devon and the wild beauty of Exmoor.
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 Accommodation in brief:
                </strong>
               </p>
               <p>
                <a href="/lee-court">
                 Lee Court
                </a>
               </p>
               <ul>
                <li>
                 <strong>
                  Sleeps up to 6
                 </strong>
                 in 3 bedrooms (2 kingsize, 1 twin which can be linked to make a superking)
                </li>
                <li>
                 Large kitchen and dining room, and spacious lounge
                </li>
                <li>
                 Ground floor toilet
                </li>
                <li>
                 Family bathroom
                </li>
                <li>
                 Private hot tub
                </li>
               </ul>
               <p>
                <a href="/lee-meadow">
                 Lee Meadow
                </a>
               </p>
               <ul>
                <li>
                 <strong>
                  Sleeps up to 4
                 </strong>
                 in 2 bedrooms (1 kingsize, 1 twin)
                </li>
                <li>
                 Large kitchen and dining room, and spacious lounge
                </li>
                <li>
                 Ground floor toilet
                </li>
                <li>
                 Family bathroom
                </li>
                <li>
                 Private hot tub
                </li>
               </ul>
               <p>
                <img alt="Getting to know the farm animals at Lee Barton" height="290" src="/images/008b.jpg" style="margin-top: 4px; margin-right: 16px; margin-left: 0px; float: left;" title="Getting to know the farm animals at Lee Barton" width="400"/>
               </p>
               <p>
                During your holiday at Lee Barton you can:
               </p>
               <ul>
                <li>
                 <strong>
                  Take a tour of the farm
                 </strong>
                 , meet the animals and see the cows being milked
                </li>
                <li>
                 Use the
                 <strong>
                  children's play area
                 </strong>
                 , including swings and trampoline
                </li>
                <li>
                 <strong>
                  Feed the fish
                 </strong>
                 in a large securely fenced pond
                </li>
                <li>
                 Experience a homemade
                 <strong>
                  Devon cream tea
                 </strong>
                </li>
               </ul>
               <p>
                North Devon is a wonderful area to visit at any time of the year. Whether you want to go on an adventure or to simply relax, there are so many beautiful places to explore and so many activities to take part in that you may not want to leave at all!
               </p>
