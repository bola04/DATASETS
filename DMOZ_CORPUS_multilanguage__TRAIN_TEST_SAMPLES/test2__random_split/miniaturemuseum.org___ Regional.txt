    <p align="left">
     <font size="4">
      Welcome to the "virtual" home of the
 Miniature Museum of Greater St. Louis.  Our museum opened June 27,
 2001 and
 features dollhouse miniatures in all scales.  The museum has a
 gift shop
 and a library devoted to miniatures.  The second floor is OPEN and
 ready for visitors.  Come and see all the beautiful things on the
 second floor.  If you aren't able to tackle the stairs, there is a
 DVD of seven dollhouses that you can look at in the library.
