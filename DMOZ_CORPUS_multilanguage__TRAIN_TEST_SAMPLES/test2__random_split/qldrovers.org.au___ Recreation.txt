                <h2 class="entry-title">
                 <a href="http://qldrovers.org.au/intro-to-rovers-workshop-suncoast-region/">
                  Intro to Rovers Workshop – Suncoast Region
                 </a>
                </h2>
                <p class="post-meta">
                 by
                 <span class="author vcard">
                  <a href="http://qldrovers.org.au/author/qldrovers/" rel="author" title="Posts by IT Chair">
                   IT Chair
                  </a>

                 |
                 <span class="published">

                 <a href="http://qldrovers.org.au/category/news/" rel="category tag">

                <div class="post-content">
                 <p>
                  Introduction to Rovers Workshops are back, proudly presented by QLD Rovers. The aim of the Workshop is to allow the Rovers in Suncoast Region to get together, ask any questions they have about what is Rovering, how they can improve their Crews, and allow the District and Region teams to also come along and meet the Rovers in their areas.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  The Workshop is free to attend, and will commence at 6:30pm. The formal part of the evening will conclude about 8:30/9pm, with tea/coffee afterwards to mingle, get to know each other, and ask any other questions you may have.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  Don’t forget to register your attendance on the below link, so we know how many people to expect and cater for.
