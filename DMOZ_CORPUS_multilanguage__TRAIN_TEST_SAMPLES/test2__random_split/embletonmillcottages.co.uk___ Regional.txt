      </h1>

       </p>
      </div>
      <p>
       Situated ¼ mile south of Embleton village, on the edge of our traditional family farm Furrow Cottage is an ideal base for exploring the
       <a href="/northumberland.php">
        Northumberland countryside and coastline
       </a>
       . Watch the sun rise over the sea and set over the Cheviot Hills.
      </p>
      <blockquote>
       <p>
        Lovely cottage in a lovely setting.
       </p>
       <cite>
        The Formans
       </cite>
      </blockquote>
      <p>
       Embleton is a great base from which to explore the local beaches and castles, all situated in a designated
       <strong>
        Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty
       </strong>
       .  Embleton Mill is within easy distance of:
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        The country town of Alnwick, with its
        <a href="http://www.alnwickcastle.com/">
         castle
        </a>
        and the new
        <a href="http://www.alnwickgarden.com/">
         Alnwick Garden
        </a>
        .
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="http://www.howickhallgardens.org.uk/">
         Howick Hall
        </a>
        with its wonderful garden and tearoom.
       </li>
       <li>
        The picturesque fishing harbour of
        <a href="http://www.seahouses.org/">
         Seahouses
        </a>
        to the north where you can catch a boat to visit the
        <a href="http://www.farne-islands.com/">
         Farne Islands
        </a>
        , famous for their puffins and seals.
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       Walk on unspoilt beaches, lunch in coastal pubs and discover the wide open spaces of the Cheviots. Let the majesty of
       <a href="http://www.bamburghcastle.com/">
        Bamburgh
       </a>
       inspire you or breathe the spiritual isolation of the
       <a href="http://www.nationaltrust.org.uk/main/w-vh/w-visits/w-findaplace/w-lindisfarnecastle/">
        Holy Island of Lindisfarne
       </a>
       . Explore the Borders or simply relax in our superbly appointed cottages. The choice is yours.
      </p>
      <p>
       Can I bring my dog? Yes, we are happy to accept one small dog per booking. For further info on bringing a small dog please ask the
       <a href="https://www.northumbrian-cottages.info/northumberland-coast/craster-embleton-area/furrow-cottage-uk3252">
        booking agent…
       </a>
      </p>
