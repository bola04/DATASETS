     <h2>
      Our Mission
     </h2>
     <p>
      To promote the scientific and educational interests of those engaged in the energy industry and to foster cooperative action in advancing the common purposes 
 	of its members and to promote activities designed to enable the industry to be conducted with the greatest economy and efficiency.
     </p>
     <p style="font-size:16px">
      The Baltimore Chapter of the Association of Energy Engineers was chartered on December 3rd, 1998, as one the most dynamic, informative, and dedicated professional 
 		organizations in the country, The Association of Energy Engineers is well recognized all over the world for its commitment in saving, promoting, educating and divulgating the unpredictable world 
 		of energy
     </p>
