       <section class="intro">
        <article>
         <h1>
          Easy Travel. Rewarding Play.
          <br/>
          It’s All Here. You Should Be, Too!
         </h1>
         <figure>
          <img alt="Mini map of Wisconsin" src="img/wi-icon.png"/>
         </figure>
         <p>
          Make yourself at home. No matter what you're here for we have something special for you. Just
                             an hour from Milwaukee, Madison and Green Bay, and located at the base of one of the
                             country's largest inland lakes, Fond du Lac is the perfect location for a fantastic getaway.
                             Known for its world-class fishing, windsurfing, snowmobiling and ice fishing, Lake Winnebago
                             is a four-season playground right in our own backyard.
         </p>
         <div class="mobileMore js_mobileMore">
          <p>
           But the Fond du Lac area is much more than just a magnificent lake. Some of Mother
                                 Nature's finest work can be seen through our great golf courses, beautiful roads,
                                 idyllic towns and hiking and biking trails. This area is a hunter's paradise with our
                                 healthy population of deer, turkey, waterfowl and pheasant. Our resurgent downtown is
                                 full of fun boutiques, rich offerings of museums and galleries, restaurants that are
                                 sure to satisfy your inner gourmand and accommodations to meet every need and
                                 budget.
          </p>
          <p>
           Fond du Lac - we're closer than you think. So C'mon In, and stay a while. You'll be glad
                                 you did.
          </p>
         </div>
