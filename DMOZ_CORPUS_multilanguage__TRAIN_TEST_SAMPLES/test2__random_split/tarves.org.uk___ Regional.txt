          <h2 class="post-titulo" id="post-7461">
           Family Fun Day at Haddo – 12th August
          </h2>
          <p class="postmeta">
           29, July 2018 at 4:52 pm · Filed under
           <a href="https://methlick.wordpress.com/category/notices/" rel="category tag">
            Notices
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           On 12th of August the National Trust for Scotland will be holding a family fun day at Haddo. There will be lots of activities for the whole family, which include: live wrestling, stalls, inflatables, crafts, music, bird show, pets’ corner and child friendly tours. The cost of the event is £5 per child, which will allow unlimited access to all activities. Come along and enjoy a day of fun in the surroundings of a stunning, historic mansion.
          </p>

          <h2 class="post-titulo" id="post-7412">
           Water Scarcity – Use        Water Wisely
          </h2>
          <p>
           <span style="margin:0;color:#3d596d;font-family:'Arial',          'sans-serif';font-size:10pt;">
            From Scottish Water –
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="margin:0;color:#1f497d;font-family:'Arial',          'sans-serif';font-size:10pt;">
            Simple steps customers can take to          use water efficiently include:
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="margin:0;color:#3d596d;font-family:'Arial',          'sans-serif';font-size:10pt;">
            Using a bucket of water instead of          a hose to wash your car or only washing windscreens and          headlights and brake lights
           </span>
          </p>
          <ul type="disc">
           <li style="margin:0;color:#1f497d;line-height:14.4pt;font-family:'Arial',          'sans-serif';font-size:10pt;font-style:normal;font-weight:400;">
            <span style="margin:0;font-family:'Arial',            'sans-serif';font-size:10pt;">
             Using a watering can instead of            a hose to water gardens and plants
            </span>
           </li>
           <li style="margin:0;color:#1f497d;line-height:14.4pt;font-family:'Arial',          'sans-serif';font-size:10pt;font-style:normal;font-weight:400;">
            <span style="margin:0;font-family:'Arial',            'sans-serif';font-size:10pt;">
             Taking a shower instead of a            bath and making it a short shower
            </span>
           </li>
           <li style="margin:0;color:#1f497d;line-height:14.4pt;font-family:'Arial',          'sans-serif';font-size:10pt;font-style:normal;font-weight:400;">
            <span style="margin:0;font-family:'Arial',            'sans-serif';font-size:10pt;">
             Only using washing machines or            dishwashers fully loaded
            </span>
           </li>
           <li style="margin:0;color:#1f497d;line-height:14.4pt;font-family:'Arial',          'sans-serif';font-size:10pt;font-style:normal;font-weight:400;">
            <span style="margin:0;font-family:'Arial',            'sans-serif';font-size:10pt;">
             Turning off the tap when            brushing teeth and shaving
            </span>
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
           <span style="margin:0;color:#3d596d;font-family:'Arial',          'sans-serif';font-size:10pt;">
            Update from Aberdeenshire Council          –
