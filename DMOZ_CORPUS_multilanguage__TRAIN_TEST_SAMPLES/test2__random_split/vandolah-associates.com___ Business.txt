             Hummus
            </a>
           </h3>
          </header>
          <!-- .entry-header -->
          <div class="entry-content">
           <p>
            <img alt="Hummus Chickpeas Appetizer Cold Appetizers" src="https://cdn.pixabay.com/photo/2015/06/17/15/57/hummus-812675__340.jpg"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            I had a dream last night where I had been planning our dinner for the evening. First off, it was sad I was dreaming about planning dinner. Secondly, in my fantasy, my child was little again and to my surprise was asking for hummus. What’s hummus? It disturbs me to do a bit of research on the topic and it all began with a simple definition.
           </p>
           <p>
            Once”Googling” the term hummus (also spelled houmous, hommus, or humus), I have discovered that hummus is a dip made up mainly of sesame seed paste and chickpea paste.
           </p>
           <p>
            Being frugal, I was amazed at just how economical this versatile dip could be! You can create your own hummus by soaking dried chickpeas overnight and then simmering them for about one hour. Once they are cooked, then you grind them in a food processor or hand mixer with a little olive oil and lemon juice. You might also add some of the chickpea broth till you’ve got the desirable”dip-like” consistency. You may use hummus as a dip for veggies, tortilla chips or flatbread which makes this an incredibly versatile staple!
           </p>
           <p>
            Hummus can be extremely rich in protein, iron and fiber that makes it a real vegetarian’s dream. Create a spicy hummus by adding chopped jalapenos and serve with chopped taco shells. Make an Italian hummus with the addition of fresh basil and chopped tomatoes. Stuff pita shells with hummus, add a little lettuce and make a tasty alternative to the ordinary peanut butter and jelly. The options are endless!
           </p>
          </div>

           <h3 class="entry-title">
            <a href="http://vandolah-associates.com/2018/05/30/breathtaking-photos/" rel="bookmark">
             Breathtaking Photos
            </a>
           </h3>
          </header>
          <!-- .entry-header -->
          <div class="entry-content">
           <p>
            <img alt="Owl, Bird, Feather, Eagle Owl, Animals" src="https://cdn.pixabay.com/photo/2018/04/22/12/48/owl-3340957__340.jpg"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            There’s not one rule of thumb to follow when it comes to creating a stunning and professional photograph. The only thing to understand when you first begin in photography is that it is going to take a while before you have the ability to take stunning shots.
           </p>
           <p>
            However, without general guidelines you may wander off the trail quite readily. This is why we’ve opted to supply you a list of items to focus on. If you follow it you will have the ability to concentrate your efforts on learning how to take expert shots.
           </p>
           <p>
            If you take your time to assess the magnificent photographs captured by your colleagues, you will begin to discover certain patterns. There’s a special one that emerges from the heap of the miscellaneous others – unique perspective.
           </p>
           <p>
            You can easily observe that a few of the pictures of world-famous architectural masterpieces are only more magnificent than others. Why? Because a photographer chose an interesting place to take photos from.
           </p>
           <p>
            Practice Composition
           </p>
           <p>
            Every terrific photo follows the principles of terrific composition. You should examine your photo like were a tic-tac-toe (3×3) board. If you assess the work of your colleagues, you’ll quickly discover that they put interesting items on the intersection of those lines.
           </p>
           <p>
            This bit requires a whole lot of practice. After some time, you may develop an instinct to set the items of your photography in these areas.
           </p>
           <p>
            Play with Lighting
           </p>
           <p>
            Lighting is also among the factors that plays a vital role in the making of a gorgeous photograph. If you’re a beginner photographer, you need to begin by learning a few tricks, like when to place the object behind and facing the light source, how to leverage light to highlight something on the photograph, etc..
           </p>
           <p>
            If you prefer to shoot photos of landscapes and town scenes, consider focusing your photography attempts on taking pictures during the golden hours. If you take photos inside, you’ll need to invest into some light equipment to play with.
           </p>
           <p>
            Photo Editing Is Essential
           </p>
           <p>
            All the stunning photos which were recorded in the modern history of photography have been tampered with. Lightroom and Photoshop can make a stunning picture from the normal and”meh” photographs. You should definitely start post processing your photographs if you would like to wind up with diamonds in your hands.
           </p>
           <p>
            On the other hand, many photographers do not have time or just don’t want to become involved with image editing. If you belong to this group of individuals, you can outsource your picture editing to professionals with years of expertise in image post processing program.
           </p>
           <p>
            Start Learning Exposure
           </p>
           <p>
            We’ve saved the toughest for the end – vulnerability. As it is possible to control the exposure with ISO, aperture and shutter speed, you’ll have a lot to learn about how all them affect your picture, and eventually have the ability to take extraordinary photos.
           </p>
           <p>
            Learning exposure is quite important because you will find yourself taking photos in tricky lighting situations, and that is where your knowledge will truly shine. If you wish to learn how exposure affects your photographs, load them into any image editing software and check the histogram.
           </p>
           <p>
            And bear in mind, nothing beats raw expertise and continuous practice.
           </p>

           <h3 class="entry-title">
            <a href="http://vandolah-associates.com/2018/05/30/good-for-weight-loss/" rel="bookmark">
             Good For Weight Loss?
            </a>
           </h3>
          </header>
          <!-- .entry-header -->
          <div class="entry-content">
           <p>
            <img alt="Racquetball Sports Exercise Man Competitio" src="https://cdn.pixabay.com/photo/2013/03/04/06/18/racquetball-89823__340.jpg"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            The only way to keep a healthy weight is to burn off more calories than we take in. Even though the evidence clearly supports this reality, it will not prevent us from trying to find shortcuts. Superfoods, fad diets, and fat-burning pills guarantee real and lasting results which never seem to materialize. It’s no surprise that Americans are tipping the scales in the wrong direction.
           </p>
           <p>
            As we mentioned at the start, expending more calories than you eat is the only way to win the battle of the bulge. The issue, however, is that only a small part of the U.S. population is physically active enough to function at a net calorie shortage on a regular basis.
           </p>
           <p>
            Why Racquetball Works
           </p>
           <p>
            While it true that any aerobic exercise can help you burn the pounds, racquetball is among the very best for several simple explanations. First and most important, it is fun! We can’t overstate the importance of finding a workout routine that’s challenging and enjoyable. Too many adults skip their everyday workout as it’s just plain dull. At the end of the day, the promise of a slimmer waistline isn’t sufficient to get you to do something that you do not wish to do.
           </p>
           <p>
            The Benefits
           </p>
           <p>
            A fast-paced sport which may be played alone or with friends, indoors or outside, racquetball checks all of the boxes when it comes to a fun, exciting, versatile action. Additionally, it does not hurt that the game works wonders for your waist. According to the specialists, it’s likely to burn 700 calories an hour playing the game — based upon your weight and intensity level. That is more than tennis, basketball, weight training, and many kinds of aerobics.
           </p>
           <p>
            Getting Started
           </p>
           <p>
            If you’re searching for somewhere to play, check out your local gym. With over 5 million regular players at the U.S., it should not be difficult for you to find a court in your area. Based on the facility, they might even allow you to borrow a racquet and balls. In terms of the rules of this game, they are rather easy and straightforward and can often be learned in just a few sessions.
           </p>
           <p>
            If losing weight is your goal, you should play for one hour, three times each week. Because hitting the ball to yourself for 180 minutes per week would be dull, we strongly recommend that you play with a buddy. If you can find a spouse of the exact same ability/experience degree, fantastic. However, you might also benefit if your spouse is significantly better than you because you’ll be forced to push yourself to the limits, burning off plenty of extra calories in the procedure.
           </p>
           <p>
            Racquetball is an exciting game that may help you to lose weight and increase your overall fitness while having a good deal of fun.
           </p>

          <div class="entry-content">
           <p>
            A individual’s treatment options and prognosis is dependent on the particular type and progression of the breast cancer.
            <br/>
            Determining Type
            <br/>
            Most breast cancers are carcinomas, which start in the cells which line cells and organs. More specifically, they tend to be adenocarcinomas, which start in the milk ducts or lobules of milk-producing glands. Less common forms are sarcomas, which start from the cells of connective tissue, fat, or muscle.
            <br/>
            When the cancer is described as “in situ,” it means that it hasn’t spread. If it is called invasive or infiltrating, it means the cancer has invaded the surrounding breast tissue.
            <br/>
            An important piece of advice, a breast cancer’s grade determines how quickly it’s likely to grow and spread. A grade is decided by checking the cancer cells under a microscope to find out how much the cancer cells look like normal cells. A lesser tier number typically means the cancer is slower-growing and less likely to spread. A higher grade number refers to a faster-growing cancer. The grade helps predict prognosis in addition to helps determine which treatments may work best.
            <br/>
            Ductal carcinoma in situ (DCIS) is a noninvasive or pre-invasive breast cancer. Since DCIS has not spread, it’s the easiest form of cancer to treat successfully.
            <br/>
            Lobular carcinoma in situ (LCIS), while the title sounds like cancer, isn’t actually a cancer. In this form, cells that look like cancer cells grow in the lobules of the milk-producing glands but they don’t propagate throughout the lobular wall.
            <br/>
            Invasive ductal carcinoma (IDC) is the most common kind of breast cancer. It starts in a milk duct, spreads through the wall of the duct and invades the fatty tissue of the breast.
            <br/>
            Invasive lobular carcinoma (ILC) starts in the lobules (milk-producing glands) and spreads into outside tissue. These special kinds are often named after specific features that were identified under the microscope. These sub-types include adenoid cystic carcinoma, low-grade adenosquamous carcinoma, medullary carcinoma, mucinous carcinoma, papillary carcinoma, tubular carcinoma, metaplastic carcinoma,
            <a href="http://centurianexterminators.com">
             Rats in Attic
            </a>
            , micropapillary carcinoma, and mixed carcinoma (which has features of both ILC and IDC).
           </p>

           <p>
            Less Common Types
            <br/>
            There are a couple of kinds of breast cancer that occur but are more rare. Inflammatory breast cancer (invasive) accounts for approximately one to three percent of all breast cancers. Another, rarer, kind of breast cancer is Paget disease of the breast, which starts in the breast ducts and spreads to the skin of the nipple and areola. There is also Phyllodes tumor, which can be rare breast tumors that develop in the stroma (connective tissue) of the breast. Finally, there’s angiosarcoma, which starts in the cells that line lymph vessels or blood vessels, but rarely happens in the breasts.
