         <h1>
          Welcome to Kirkwood National Golf Club &amp; Cottages
         </h1>
         <div class="specialbox">
          <span class="title">
           Two Great Courses, One Great Experience.
          </span>
          <p>
           We’ve teamed up with Mallard Pointe Golf Course at Sardis, MS and are offering a tremendous membership opportunity.
          </p>
          <p>
           Members can enjoy benefits of their membership at both courses for the same low price.
          </p>
          <p>
           Contact the Pro Shop at either course for more information.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="post-5 page type-page status-publish hentry" id="post-5">
          <div class="entry-content">
           <p>
            Kirkwood National Golf Club &amp; Cottages is situated in The Hills Region of north Mississippi, just south of historic
            <a href="http://www.visithollysprings.com" target="_blank">
             Holly Springs
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <p>
            Conveniently located just 35 miles southeast of Memphis, Kirkwood National is only thirty minutes north of Oxford, home of the University of Mississippi, and an hour east of Tunica.  Kirkwood National is the perfect choice for first-class golf, meeting and banquet facilities and accommodations.
           </p>
           <p>
            You will thoroughly enjoy the wooded sanctuary of Kirkwood National, hidden enough to be private, close enough to be convenient; where what you discover will bring you back.
           </p>
           <p>
            Locate Us:
            <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?q=kirkwood+national+277+Palmer+Lane+Holly+Springs,+Mississippi+38635&amp;hl=en&amp;client=firefox-a&amp;fb=1&amp;gl=us&amp;hq=kirkwood+national+277+Palmer+Lane+Holly+Springs,+Mississippi+38635&amp;cid=0,0,8520757666737879033&amp;t=m&amp;z=16&amp;iwloc=A" target="_blank">
             Map to Kirkwood National
            </a>
           </p>
