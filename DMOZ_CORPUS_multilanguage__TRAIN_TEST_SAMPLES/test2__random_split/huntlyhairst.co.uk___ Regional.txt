            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 18px;">
              The  Hairst is a unique annual harvest festival taking place in Huntly. This family event, established in 2002,  celebrates the food and traditions of
     Aberdeenshire.
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 18px;">
              It has something for everyone, from an extended
              <a href="/event-details/" title="The Juicy Details">
               Farmers Market
              </a>
              with over 50 stalls showcasing the
     best of local produce to cookery demonstrations and
              <a href="/competitions/" title="-Competitions-">
               competitions
              </a>
              , including
              <a href="/world-stovies-championship/" title="World Stovies Championships">
               The World Stovies Championship
              </a>
              .  There will also be farm tours and exhibitions and the very successful annual Tin Hut Supper.
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 18px;">
              <span style="line-height: 1.5;">
               Huntly's
               <a href="http://www.roomtorun.uk/" target="_blank" title="http://www.roomtorun.uk/">
                Room to Run
               </a>
               event is
     also taking place on the same day.
              </span>
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="line-height: 1.5; font-size: 18px;">
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 18px;">
              The Festival is organised by a group of volunteers who give their time to promote the produce and producers of Aberdeenshire and provide an enjoyable event
     for local residents and visitors.
             </span>
            </p>
