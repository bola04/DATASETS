        <h1 class="heading-1">
         Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) Services
        </h1>
        <img class="img-responsive img-fullbleed" id="ctl00_ContentPlaceHolderBody_imgWideArticle" src="/uploadedImages/wwwintertekcom/Services/Testing_and_Analysis/_Images/Header_NDT%20theme%20page%20_760x192_Bulb%203.jpg?n=1469"/>
        <p class="lead-para">
         Intertek provides non-destructive testing (NDT), materials testing and welding services for a diverse range of industries.
        </p>
        <p>
         We help you ensure the reliability and safety of your products, equipment or plant assets with our world-class services in non-destructive testing (NDT),
         <a href="/non-destructive-testing/materials-testing/" title="materials testing">
          materials testing
         </a>
         and
         <a href="/non-destructive-testing/welding-services/" title="welding quality">
          welding quality
         </a>
         . We understand the processes involved throughout the entire product cycle  from design and manufacture to operation and maintenance - and the frequent need for rapid turnaround times. Our services can support production processes, quality control, and regulatory compliance as well as new construction, pipelines, plant maintenance and scheduled shutdown inspection.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Our vast expertise and knowledge in NDT and materials testing means we can select the right techniques and procedures to detect defects and irregularities in your products, equipment, production facilities or plant assets and provide you with necessary data to assist you in making informed decisions. Intertek can help you avoid the potential for catastrophic consequences and financial losses with early detection of problems before they cause damage, operating inefficiencies or in-service failure.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         In addition to our non-destructive testing and materials testing, we provide a complete welding support service to help you with your welding requirements, comply with international and national codes and standards, and to enter new markets with our welder training and welder qualification.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <b>
          Interteks global services
         </b>
         <br/>
         Intertek works with a diverse range of industry sectors including oil and gas, aerospace, power generation, petrochemical, automotive, rail, motorsport, general engineering, fabrication and construction. Wherever your company is based and whichever sector, our global reach means we can support you with our comprehensive range of NDT services that include:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <a href="/non-destructive-testing/materials-testing/" title="Materials testing">
           Materials testing
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="/non-destructive-testing/ndt-specialist/ " title="Specialist NDT services">
           Specialist NDT services
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="/testing/non-destructive-testing/welding-services/" title="Welding Services">
           Welding Services
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="/non-destructive-testing/consulting/" title="Non-destructive consulting ">
           Non-destructive consulting
          </a>
          <br/>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <b>
          Our skilled and experienced Non-Destructive Testing labs and personnel
         </b>
         <br/>
         Interteks has independent accredited laboratories (
         <a href="https://www.ukas.com/wp-content/uploads/schedule_uploads/00002/0084Testing%20Multiple.pdf" target="_blank" title="UKAS accredited in the UK">
          UKAS accredited in the UK
         </a>
         ) and highly skilled and
         <a href="/testing/non-destructive-testing/level-2-3-consulting/" title="certified personnel">
          certified personnel
         </a>
         holding qualifications with the following industry bodies:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          PCN - Personnel Certification in Non Destructive Testing
         </li>
         <li>
          ASNT - American Society of Non-destructive Testing
         </li>
         <li>
          API  American Petroleum Institute
         </li>
         <li>
          AWS - American Welding Society
         </li>
         <li>
          ACCP  ASNT Central Certification Programme
          <br/>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <b>
          The benefits of choosing Intertek for non-destructive testing, materials testing and welding
         </b>
         <br/>
         By partnering with Intertek, we will deliver real value for your company by protecting your brand and reputation and by ensuring the safe and efficient operation of your products, equipment and plant assets. We help you save time and money by avoiding the costs and loss of revenue due to product, equipment and asset defects or failure, all done with the minimum of disruption to your production processes and schedules.
        </p>
