We are a growing church made up of people from all walks of life from different nationalities backgrounds and cultures. We enjoy being a diverse community all sharing in a common goal of making Jesus Christ known in our community. We believe the Bible is God's word and that His message is to be spread all over the world. Therefore we believe that we are called as a church to live for His glory and make His 'good news' the gospel known at home and abroad. If you are wanting to know more about what the bible teaches about the good news of Jesus Christ.  In our morning services, we aim to explain simply how you too can know Jesus Christ as your Saviour.
