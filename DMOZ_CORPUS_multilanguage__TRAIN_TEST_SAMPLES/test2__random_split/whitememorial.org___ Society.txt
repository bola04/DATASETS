          </strong>

         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           From the Office of the General Assembly of the PC(USA):
          </strong>
          <a href="https://www.pcusa.org/site_media/media/uploads/oga/pdf/jhn_statement_child_separation_at_borders_061618.pdf" rel="noopener" target="_blank">

          </a>
          <br/>

           Faith Leaders’ Statement on Family Separation
