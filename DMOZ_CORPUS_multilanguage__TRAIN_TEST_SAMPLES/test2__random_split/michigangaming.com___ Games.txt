                        <p style="text-align: justify;">
                         The Pokagon Band of Potawatomi Indians issued the following press release on July 21 announcing that Matthew Wesaw was elected as its Tribal Council Chairman during the Tribeâ€™s general election on Saturday, July 14. During the election, Kelly Curran was elected Secretary, Colin Wesaw as Elders Representative, and Gary Morseau, Sr. as Member-at-Large.
                        </p>
                        <p style="text-align: justify;">
                         <img alt="" src="http://www.michigangaming.com/images/matt-wesaw-200x300.jpg" style="border-width: 1px; border-style: solid; margin-left: 20px; margin-right: 20px; width: 125px; float: left; height: 188px;"/>
                         As Chairman, Wesaw will also serve as Chairman and CEO of the Pokagon Gaming Authority Board, which oversees the Bandâ€™s casino properties including Four Winds New BuffaloÂ®, Four Winds HartfordÂ®, Four Winds DowagiacÂ® and Four Winds South BendÂ®. This marks his third successful election as Chairman, the first time 2008 and again in 2012. In October 2013, he announced his retirement from the Council to become the Executive Director of the Michigan Department of Civil Rights. At that time, current Chairman John P. Warren was chosen in a special election to serve out the remainder of the term and was re-elected in 2015.
                        </p>
                        <p style="text-align: justify;">
                         Other members of the 11-member Tribal Council who were not up for re-election are Vice-Chairman Bob Moody, and Members-at-Large Steve Winchester, Becky Price, Andy Jackson, and Alex Wesaw.

                        <p style="text-align: justify;">
                         The Nottawaseppi Huron Band of the Potawatomi, the owners of FireKeepers Casino Hotel, announced the expansion of its hotel on Monday, July 23. A second guest tower is being built to accommodate an increasing demand for rooms. The goal of the expansion is to attract more patrons from throughout the Midwest, along with new and larger conventions and business meetings.
                        </p>
                        <p style="text-align: justify;">
                         Occupancy at the hotel has exceeded 95 percent for the last year, making it a challenge for retail guests and larger group businesses to reserve rooms, according to the tribe. FireKeepers Casino Hotel opened its 243-room hotel tower in December 2012.
                        </p>
                        <p style="text-align: justify;">
                         "Today's announcement is the first step in the process, as we secure architects and construction management," Jamie Stuck, chairman of the NHBP Tribal Council, said in a
                         <a href="https://firekeeperscasino.com/news/firekeepers-casino-hotel-and-its-owners-the-nottawaseppi-huron-band-of-the">
                          press release
                         </a>
                         . "This is a key step in our Strategic Plan for FireKeepers to provide for future generations of our tribe. With more rooms and potentially more meeting space, FireKeepers will be able to market and attract guests from greater distances, bringing new visitors to the area and boosting the local economy."
                        </p>
                        <p style="text-align: justify;">
                         FireKeepers has yet to announce a groundbreaking date, along with details including its budget, additional jobs, number of rooms, and amenities. Additional information will become available as the project progresses, the company said.

                         <strong>
                          BEST CONSUMER-SERVICE TECHNOLOGY
                         </strong>
                         <br/>
                         This category concerns technology that directly touches the customers. Whether it is an enhanced kiosk, a new player tracking system, reservations system, parking management system, or any other guest-friendly device, this technology impacts the experience of the customer.
                        </p>
                        <p style="text-align: justify;">
                         <strong>
                          BEST PRODUCTIVITY-ENHANCEMENT TECHNOLOGY
                         </strong>
                         <br/>
                         This category describes a technology that makes a job or task easier and more efficient. Examples could be an online accounting system, better technology for printing tickets on cashless slots, or an employee communications device that allows a property to better explain its programs to its workers.
                        </p>

                         <strong>
                          BEST SLOT PRODUCT
                         </strong>
                         <br/>
                         Very simply, this product is the judges' favorite new slot product. It can be a new game or a traditional game that has been updated within the past 12 months. What makes this machine or game a step forward technologically?
                        </p>

                         <strong>
                          BEST TABLE-GAME PRODUCT OR INNOVATION
                         </strong>
                         <br/>
                         The growth of table games continues to occur around the world and makes it important to recognize innovative developments in this area. In this category, nominations can be made for table games or any product related to table games.
                        </p>
                        <p style="text-align: justify;">

                          BEST INTERACTIVE PRODUCT
                         </strong>
                         <br/>
                         Recognizing emerging technology, nominations are open to products that electronically connect customers with land-based or online casinos or gaming platforms.
